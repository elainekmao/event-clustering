SUBJECT: NEPALI CANDIDATE FOR HIGH REPRESENTATIVE OF

LEAST DEVELOPED COUNTRIES

1. This is an action request. Please see Para 4.

2. The Embassy has received a diplomatic note, dated

February 13, from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs

requesting USG support for the Government of Nepal\'s

candidate for the position of UN Secretary General High

Representative for Least Developed Countries, Land-

locked Developing Nations and Small Island Developing

States. A copy of the diplomatic note, along with the

curriculum vitae of Kul Chandra Gautam, Nepal\'s proposed

candidate, have been faxed to SA/INS. The text of the

diplomatic note follows below.

3. Begin text of diplomatic note:

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs, His Majesty\'s

Government of Nepal, presents its compliments to the

Embassy of the United States of America in Kathmandu and

has the honour to inform that His Majesty\'s Government

of Nepal is interested to promote its candidate Mr. Kul

Chandra Gautam, Assistant Secretary-General and Deputy

Executive Director, UNICEF, for the recent established

post of UN Secretary General High Representative in the

direction of Least Developed Countries, Land-Locked

Developing Nations and Small Island Developing States.

While appreciating the valuable support extended by the

government of USA for promoting and safeguarding the

interest of the LDCs in many international forums, the

Ministry has the further honour to enclose herewith the

bio-data of Mr. K.C. Gautam to be transmitted to its

highest destination for having the valuable support of

the esteemed government of USA.

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs avails itself of this

opportunity to renew to the Embassy of the United States

of America the assurances of its highest consideration.

February 13, 2002

End text of diplomatic note.

4. Embassy requests Department\'s guidance in responding

to the MFA\'s note.
