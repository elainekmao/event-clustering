1. (C) POPE JOHN PAUL II DISPATCHED PIO CARDINAL LAGHI TO

MEET ISRAELI PRIME MINISTER SHARON AND PALESTINIAN

AUTHORITY CHAIRMAN ARAFAT ON JUNE 2. VATICAN MIDDLE EAST

DIRECTOR MONS. GIOVANNI D\'ANIELLO ACCOMPANIED LAGHI.

CHARGE CALLED ON D\'ANIELLO JUNE 6 FOR READ-OUT OF TRIP.

D\'ANIELLO SAID THAT SOLE PURPOSE OF THE TRIP WAS TO EXPRESS

THE POPE\'S DESIRE THAT THE VIOLENCE STOP. D\'ANIELLO SAID

THAT THE VATICAN DID NOT PROPOSE ANY NEW POLITICAL

SOLUTIONS TO THE ARAB/ISRAELI CONFLICT, AND DID NOT RAISE

ITS DESIRE TO SEE AN INTERNATIONAL FORCE DISPATCHED TO

ISRAEL AND THE TERRITORIES. CHARGE ASKED ABOUT LAGHI\'S

STATEMENTS WHICH ISRAELI CONTACTS INTERPRET AS JUDGING

SETTLEMENT EXPANSION AS THE MORAL EQUIVALENT OF THE SUICIDE

BOMBINGS. D\'ANIELLO SAID THAT LAGHI\'S INTENT WAS TO SAY

THAT BOTH THE VIOLENCE, AND THE "PROVOCATION" TO VIOLENCE,

MUST END. D\'ANIELLO ADDED VERY LITTLE INSIGHT TO THE

VISIT, BUT DID SAY THAT THE VATICAN HAD NO PLANS TO

UNDERTAKE A SECOND MISSION.

2. (C) IN A FOLLOW-UP CONVERSATION, ISRAEL EMBASSY DCM ZVI

TAL GAVE A/DCM AN EARFUL OF HIS EMBASSY\'S VIEWS ON THE

DETERIORATION OF RELATIONS WITH THE HOLY SEE. RECITING A

NUMBER OF SLIGHTS DATING BACK TO NOVEMBER 2000, TAL SAID A

PATTERN HAS EMERGED OF THE VATICAN BACKING OFF FROM ITS

PREVIOUS COMMITMENT TO CATHOLIC-JEWISH DIALOGUE. BEGINNING

WITH THE VATICAN\'S REFUSAL TO COORDINATE WITH THE ISRAEL

EMBASSY A COMMEMORATION OF THE ANNIVERSARY OF THE PONTIFF\'S

HISTORIC VISIT TO THE HOLY LANDS, THROUGH A SERIES OF MINOR

SLIGHTS (LIKE NOT RECEIVING VISITING KNESSET MEMBERS) TO

THE QLACK OF BALANCEQ IN LAGHI\'S PUBLIC COMMENTARY THIS

WEEK, TAL ASSERTED THAT THE VATICAN HAS BEEN CLOSING DOORS

ON DIALOGUE WITH THE GOI. TAL SHARED WITH A/DCM A COPY OF

THE POPE\'S LETTER TO PM SHARON (FAXED TO EUR/WE), NOTING

THAT EVEN THAT MISSIVE CONTAINED THE QCODE WORD JUSTICEQ,

TO REFER TO A PERCEIVED VATICAN TILT TOWARDS THE

PALESTINIANS. THE ISRAEL EMBASSY IS NOT AT ALL HAPPY WITH

THE STATE OF RELATIONS, AND IF FEARFUL THAT THINGS COULD

GET WORSE. TAL SAID HIS SOURCES INDICATE SECRETARY OF

STATE CARDINAL SODANO IS THE FORCE BEHIND THESE

DEVELOPMENTS. (NOTE: SODANO IS PM EQUIVALENT AND REGARDED

BY SOME AS A POSSIBLE PAPAL SUCCESSOR).

3. (C) COMMENT: POST TOO HAS NOTED THAT THE VATICAN\'S

TILT TOWARD THE PALESTINIANS HAS BECOME SOMEWHAT MORE

EVIDENT. THERE ARE A NUMBER OF POSSIBLE EXPLANATIONS FOR

THIS. WE BELIEVE IT REFLECTS IN LARGE PART THE VATICAN\'S

UNDERSTANDABLE NEED TO MIND THE INTERESTS OF CHRISTIANS IN

THE HOLY LANDS. IT MAY ALSO REFLECT A VATICAN POLICY TO

SUPPORT ARAFAT ON THE GROUNDS THAT THE EVIL YOU KNOW IS

BETTER THAN THE EVIL YOU DON\'T. IT MAY REFLECT RESIDUAL

ANTI-SEMITIC ATTITUDES THAT STILL EXIST AMONG SOME WITHIN

THE VATICAN. IT ALSO MAY REFLECT SODANO\'S OWN POLITICAL

MANEUVERING TOWARDS GREATER INFLUENCE, PERHAPS EVEN THE

PAPACY, IN THE WANING DAYS OF JP II\'S PONTIFICATE.
