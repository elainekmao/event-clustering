SUBJECT: JORDAN: WORLD FOOD SUMMIT BIOTECH SYMPOSIUM

REF: STATE 107335

Embassy delivered Secretary Veneman\'s letter (reftel) to

Agriculture Minister Mahmoud Duwairi, who welcomed the

invitation to the agricultural biotechnology symposium.

Duwairi will be staying at the Sheraton in Rome. Duwairi can

be contacted there directly or through delegation member

Jihad Abu Mushref. Alternately, Duwairi can be contacted

through the Jordanian embassy in Rome, either through

Ambassador Omar Rifai or Mr. Muwafaq al Ajlouni, who will

also be part of the Jordanian delegation to the World Food

Summit.
