SUBJECT: SEX AND THE CITY -- HANOI STYLE

1. (U) SUMMARY. THE COMMUNIST PARTY OF VIETNAM (CPV) AND

GOVERNMENT OF VIETNAM (GVN) ADMIT THAT PROSTITUTION IS A

MAJOR PROBLEM FOR THE COUNTRY. DISCOS, KARAOKE BARS,

MASSAGE PARLORS, AND OTHER "FUN" LOCATIONS EVEN IN STAID

HANOI INCREASINGLY OFFER PROFESSIONAL OR SEMI-PROFESSIONAL

SEX SERVICES FOR SALE. APART FROM LAMENTING THE PROBLEM,

OFFICIALS APPEAR TO DO LITTLE TO REMEDY THIS SOCIAL EVIL --

PARTLY DUE TO CORRUPTION BUT PERHAPS MORE IMPORTANTLY

BECAUSE THEY CANNOT BE SURE WHETHER THE PUBLIC WOULD RATHER

CONTINUE TO ENJOY SUCH NEWFOUND FREEDOMS OR WELCOME A

CLAMPDOWN AND RETURN TO "SOCIALIST MORALITY."

UNFORTUNATELY, THE HEALTH IMPLICATIONS OF THE GROWING SEX

INDUSTRY ARE NO JOKE, WITH HIV/AIDS INFECTIONS RISING

STEADILY (ALTHOUGH OFFICIALS CONTINUE TO MAINTAIN THAT THE

LARGE MAJORITY OF INFECTIONS STEM FROM INTRAVENOUS DRUG

USE). TRAFFICKING IN PERSONS RELATED TO PROSTITUTION ALSO

REMAINS A SERIOUS ISSUE OF CONCERN TO THE GVN, ALTHOUGH MORE

IN VIETNAM DUE TO THE EXPORT OF WOMEN THAN TO THE DOMESTIC

MARKET (REFTEL). THE BOTTOM LINE IS THAT HANOI NIGHTLIFE

AND SOCIETY INCREASINGLY RESEMBLE THE REST OF SOUTHEAST

ASIAN CITIES -- ESPECIALLY HO CHI MINH CITY. END SUMMARY.

2. (U) THE COMMUNIQUE ISSUED AT THE END OF THE SEVENTH

PLENUM OF THE COMMUNIST PARTY OF VIETNAM (CPV) 9TH CONGRESS

ON NOVEMBER 9 ADMITTED "URGENT SOCIAL PROBLEMS" THAT NEED TO

BE ADDRESSED, NOTABLY "SOCIAL EVILS SUCH AS DRUG ADDICTION,

PROSTITUTION, AND CRIME" AS WELL AS CORRUPTION. SIMILARLY,

PRIME MINISTER PHAN VAN KHAI, IN OPENING REMARKS TO THE

SECOND SESSION OF THE 11TH NATIONAL ASSEMBLY ON NOVEMBER 12,

REFERRED TO "URGENT SOCIAL ISSUES" AND POINTED IN PARTICULAR

TO "DRUG ADDICTION, PROSTITUTION AND CRIME." NEITHER ARE

BRAND-NEW THEMES FOR SENIOR CPV AND GVN OFFICIALS, BUT THEIR

PROMINENCE IN THESE KEYNOTE ADDRESSES -- AS WELL AS THE

TOTAL ABSENCE OF PROPOSED SOLUTIONS -- REFLECT THE NOW "IN

YOUR FACE," ENDEMIC NATURE OF THESE PROBLEMS IN THE VIETNAM

OF THE 21ST CENTURY.

3. (U) HANOI HAS A REPUTATION OF BEING THE DOUR

BUREAUCRATIC OLDER BROTHER OF THE MORE FLASHY HO CHI MINH

CITY; THIS CHARACTERIZATION IS NOT FAR OFF THE MARK. BUT

OVER THE PAST DECADE, WITH RISING INCOMES, GROWING PERSONAL

FREEDOMS, AND DIMINISHING PUBLIC SECURITY INTRUSIONS INTO

PERSONAL LIVES, AT LEAST MANY HANOI RESIDENTS HAVE AGAIN --

OR FOR THE FIRST TIME -- BEEN ABLE TO INDULGE IN RANGE OF

SEXUAL LICENSE THAT WOULD MAKE HO CHI MINH AND HIS

4. (U) AT LEAST THREE DISCOS IN HANOI CATER TO THE WELL-

HEELED TWENTY- AND THIRTY-SOMETHING VIETNAMESE OF HANOI;

ALL, INTERESTINGLY ENOUGH, SPORT ENGLISH NAMES: "NEW

CENTURY," "MAGIC MOO," AND "SPARK." ANOTHER, "APOCALYPSE

NOW," CATERS PRIMARILY TO A WESTERN CROWD BUT HAS A SIZEABLE

AND APPARENTLY INCREASING PERCENTAGE OF LOCALS. COGNAC --

AT PRICES THAT IN THE COURSE OF AN EVENING COULD EASILY EAT

UP THE ANNUAL PER CAPITA INCOME OF THE AVERAGE VIETNAMESE --

AND BEER ARE THE BEVERAGES OF CHOICE. CONVENTIONAL WISDOM

HOLDS THAT "NEW CENTURY" IS ALSO A POPULAR SITE FOR DRUG

PURCHASES AND EVEN USAGE, ESPECIALLY OF METHAMPHETAMINES.

7. (U) PROBABLY BECAUSE SINGING CAN BE A LONELY BUSINESS,

MANY KARAOKE BARS EMPLOY A STABLE OF USUALLY VERY GOOD-

LOOKING "HOSTESSES," WHOSE SINGING ABILITIES VARY

"KARAOKE OM" -- CUDDLE KARAOKE. THE HOSTESSES ARE GENEROUS

WITH THEIR ENTHUSIASM FOR OTHERWISE UNTALENTED RENDITIONS OF

POPULAR TUNES, BUT ALSO WILLING -- NAY, EAGER -- TO KEEP THE

MALE SINGERS WARM AGAINST THE CHILL OF AIR CONDITIONED ROOMS

IN THE HANOI HEAT. MOST, ACCORDING TO ANECDOTAL EVIDENCE,

ARE ALSO QUITE WILLING TO GET OFF WORK EARLY TO GO ON TO

OTHER NOCTURNAL ACTIVITIES, IN EXCHANGE FOR CERTAIN MONETARY

COMPENSATIONS.

8. (U) A REAL BOON FOR HANOI RESIDENTS WITH BAD BACKS OR

STIFF NECKS, INTER ALIA, HAS BEEN THE PROLIFERATION OVER THE

PAST SEVERAL YEARS OF AT LEAST HUNDREDS OF MASSAGE CENTERS,

OFTEN HIGHLIGHTING THE FACT THAT THEY OFFER "THAI-STYLE"

MASSAGES. (ODDLY, THE VIETNAMESE TERM "XOA BOP" IS

GRADUALLY GIVING WAY IN THE LINGUA FRANCA AS WELL AS ON

SIGNS TO A MORE VIET-ENGLISH "MASSAT" OR THE STRAIGHTFORWARD

USE OF THE ENGLISH WORD.) AN ASTONISHINGLY HIGH PERCENTAGE

OF THESE MASSEUSES APPEAR TO BE ATTRACTIVE YOUNG LADIES,

MANY OF WHOM CLAIM TO BE FROM OUT OF TOWN. (FOR SOME

WITH THE CLIENT AFTERWARDS. HOWEVER, SOME MANAGERS

10. (U) WHERE ARE ALL THESE NEWLY FORMED COUPLES HEADING?

TO ONE OF THE SEEMINGLY UBIQUITOUS "NHA NGHI" -- REST HOUSES

-- THAT DOT THE URBAN (AND MORE AND MORE, EVEN THE RURAL)

LANDSCAPE. AGAIN, MOST ARE SMALL ESTABLISHMENTS, PRIVATELY

OWNED, AND ASK FEW QUESTIONS OF THEIR GUESTS, UNLIKE HOTELS

AND GUESTHOUSES, WHICH USUALLY REQUIRE THE DEPOSIT OF A

11. (U) ANECDOTAL EVIDENCE -- INCLUDING FROM THE USUALLY

RELIABLE STAPLE OF POLOFFS EVERYWHERE, TAXI DRIVERS --

INDICATE THAT THE COST OF SHORT-TIME SEXUAL SERVICES RANGE

FROM 100,000 VIETNAMESE DONG (USD 7) UPWARD. VIETNAMESE MEN

CALL STEPPING OUT FOR ONE OF THESE EXTRAMARITAL LIAISONS

"EATING PHO" (THE CLASSIC VIETNAMESE NOODLE SOUP), IN

CONTRAST TO "EATING RICE" AT HOME. AS IN OTHER ASIAN

CULTURES, MANY HANOI MEN TEND TO GO OFF AND "EAT PHO" ALONG

WITH THEIR BUDDIES, RATHER THAN ON THEIR OWN, OFTEN AFTER AN

EVENING IN A BEER HALL AND/OR KARAOKE AND/OR MASSAGE. WHILE

OF COURSE MANY -- PERHAPS EVEN MOST -- HANOI MEN MAY NEVER

ENGAGE IN THIS STILL ILLEGAL PASTIME, RANDOM SAMPLES SUGGEST

THAT MANY MALES DO SO ABOUT ONCE A MONTH, AND THAT THE FIRST

SEXUAL EXPERIENCE OF MOST HANOI MEN PROBABLY TAKES ONE OF

THESE FORMS.

12. (U) HANOI\'S NUMEROUS SMALL LAKES, WHICH USUALLY HAVE

ATTRACTIVE PARKS AROUND THEM, OFFER CHARMING LOCATIONS FOR

BOTH PRO- AND NON-PRO SEXUAL ACTIVITIES. THE LARGE LAKESIDE

IT\'S NO JOKE, HOWEVER

13. (U) ACCORDING TO A RECENT REPORT IN THE OFFICIAL

MEDIA, THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH HAS PREDICTED THAT THE NUMBER

OF PEOPLE IN VIETNAM INFECTED WITH HIV/AIDS WILL REACH

200,000 BY 2005. ALREADY, ACCORDING TO THIS REPORT, 154,244

PEOPLE ARE INFECTED, 23,605 CASES WITH FULL-BLOWN AIDS, AND

20,089 ON THE VERGE OF DEATH. THE NATIONAL AIDS STANDING

BUREAU OFFICIALLY HAS ACKNOWLEDGED ONLY 56,495 HIV CARRIERS

(13,000 OF WHOM WERE IDENTIFIED SO FAR IN 2002), AS WELL AS

A TOTAL OF 4,649 DEATHS (1,096 IN 2002) AND 8,450 CURRENT

AIDS PATIENTS, ACCORDING TO ANOTHER REPORT IN THE OFFICIAL

MEDIA. MOST GVN OFFICIALS CONTINUE TO MAINTAIN THAT THE

LARGE MAJORITY OF INFECTIONS STEM FROM INTRAVENOUS DRUG USE,

ALTHOUGH SOME EXPERTS BELIEVE THAT THIS TREND IS NOW

SHIFTING IN FAVOR OF SEXUALLY TRANSMITTED INFECTIONS. GVN

14. (U) AS NOTED IN REFTEL SUBMISSION TO THE ANNUAL ANTI-

TRAFFICKING IN PERSONS REPORT, TRAFFICKING IN VIETNAM

REMAINS AN ISSUE OF CONCERN TO THE GVN AND OTHER OBSERVERS,

INCLUDING THE USG. HOWEVER, MOST TRAFFICKING IN WOMEN

APPEARS TO BE AIMED AT FOREIGN AUDIENCES, ALTHOUGH SOME

TRAFFICKING FROM RURAL TO URBAN AREAS FOR THE PURPOSES OF

PROSTITUTION EXISTS. ECONOMIC INCENTIVES ARE MORE LIKELY

FACTORS TO INDUCE YOUNG WOMEN VOLUNTARILY INTO PROFESSIONAL

OR SEMI-PROFESSIONAL PROSTITUTION DOMESTICALLY, HOWEVER.

WITHIN HANOI, THERE DOES NOT APPEAR TO BE THE CHILD SEX

PROSTITUTION SO ABHORRENT IN OTHER NEAR-BY COUNTRIES.

COMMENT

-------

15. (U) NO RELIABLE ESTIMATES EXIST ABOUT HOW MANY PEOPLE

ARE ENGAGED -- FULL-TIME, PART-TIME, FREE-LANCE, EMPLOYED --

IN PROSTITUTION IN HANOI THESE DAYS, BUT THE NUMBERS APPEAR

TO BE GROWING. HANOI STILL (AS FAR AS EMBASSY KNOWS) HAS

NOTHING LIKE DE FACTO BROTHELS OR BANGKOK-STYLE "MASSAGE

PARLORS," BUT THE RANGE OF OTHER OPTIONS HAS DEVELOPED

MARKEDLY AND IS CLEARLY MEETING SOME MARKET DEMANDS. THE

RESPONSE OF POLICE AND SECURITY OFFICIALS TO ALL OF THESE

TRENDS APPEARS WEAK, EVEN INDIFFERENT. THE CLEAR

IMPLICATION IS THAT, AS ELSEWHERE IN SOUTHEAST ASIA,

CORRUPTION IS EFFECTIVE IN KEEPING POLICE AWAY AND OFF OF

THE BACKS OF THESE DEMIMONDE ENTREPRENEURS. IT IS NO WONDER

THAT THE GVN AND CPV FEEL COMPELLED TO ADMIT THE GROWING

PREVALENCE OF SOCIAL EVILS. WHAT THEY MAY NOT YET HAVE A

NIGHTLIFE AND SOCIETY INCREASINGLY RESEMBLE THOSE OF OTHER

SOUTHEAST ASIAN CITIES -- ESPECIALLY HO CHI MINH CITY.
