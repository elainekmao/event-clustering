SUBJECT: MDC WILL ONLY TALK TRANSITION

REF: HARARE 721

CLASSIFIED BY JOSEPH G. SULLIVAN FOR REASONS 1.5B/D

1. (C) SUMMARY: THE MDC SHARPLY DEFINED ITS WILLINGNESS

TO ENGAGE IN FACILITATED DIALOGUE WITH ZANU-PF AS

LIMITED TO A TRANSITIONAL FORMULA TOWARD NEW ELECTIONS

AND REJECTED A UNITY GOVERNMENT. THIS PUTS THEM ON A

CONFLICTING COURSE WITH ZANU-PF WHICH SEEKS TO FORCE MDC

AND THE NATION TO ACCEPT MUGABE\'S LEGITIMACY AS

PRESIDENT. WE URGE DIRECT AND INDIRECT US EFFORTS TO

HIGHLIGHT AND PRESS FOR AN END TO STATE-SPONSORED

VIOLENT RETRIBUTION AGAINST OPPOSITION SUPPORTERS. END

SUMMARY.

2. (C) MDC MP DAVID COLTART ASKED TO SEE THE BRITISH

HIGH COMMISSIONER AND THE AMERICAN AMBASSADOR THE

EVENING OF MARCH 19 TO BRIEF ON RESULTS OF THE MDC

NATIONAL EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE MEETING HELD THAT DAY.

COLTART SAID THAT THE COMMITTEE WAS UNITED IN OPPOSITION

TO MDC PARTICIPATION IN A NATIONAL UNITY GOVERNMENT.

GRASSROOTS SENTIMENT WAS EXTREMELY STRONG BECAUSE PEOPLE

IN THE COUNTRYSIDE WERE BEING BEATEN UP AND KILLED BY

ZANU-PF, THE ARMED FORCES AND THE POLICE FOR HAVING

SUPPORTED THE MDC. THE REQUIREMENT TO PUBLISH THE NAMES

AND ADDRESSES OF MDC POLLING AGENTS WAS NOW BEING USED

AS A WAY TO PERSECUTE AND PUNISH MDC SUPPORTERS.

COLTART SAID THAT SHOULD THE MDC LEADERSHIP SEEK TO

PARTICIPATE IN A UNITY GOVERNMENT, THEIR FOLLOWERS WOULD

REVOLT. MDC ANALYSIS WAS THAT PRESIDENTS MUGABE WAS

SEEKING TO BUY TIME BY ENTERING "RECONCILIATION TALKS"

IN THE HOPE THAT PRESSURE ON HIM WOULD BE RELIEVED AND

HE WOULD GET SOME FOOD, FOREIGN EXCHANGE AND FUEL

ASSISTANCE IN RETURN. MDC BELIEVED THAT PRESIDENT MBEKI

HAD HIS OWN REASONS FOR BUYING TIME TO PUT OFF A CRISIS

AND DIMINISH PRESSURE ON SOUTH AFRICA TO RESOLVE THE

3. (C) COLTART SAID THAT MDC WOULD ENTER INTO

NEGOTIATIONS BEING ENCOURAGED BY THE SOUTH AFRICAN

PRESIDENTS ONLY IF THE NEGOTIATIONS WERE BASED ON THE

FOLLOWING PRINCIPLES:

-- THEY WOULD BE HELD UNDER THE MEDIATION OF AN

ACCEPTABLE NEUTRAL AFRICAN. THE MDC LATER TOLD THE

NIGERIAN HC THAT THEY COULD NOT ACCEPT CHIEF SHONEKAN\'S

DESIGNATION IN THAT ROLE BECAUSE OF HIS ACCEPTANCE OF

THE ELECTION RESULT. PRESIDENT OBASSANJO INSTEAD NAMED

PROFESSOR ADEDEJE(SPELLING ?), WHO SERVED FOR TEN YEARS

AS REPRESENTATIVE TO THE ECONOMIC COMMISSION FOR AFRICA

IN ADDIS.)

-- MBEKI AND OBASSANJO MUST COMMIT TO A FINITE, TIME-

LIMITED NEGOTIATION.

-- THE GOZ MUST RELEASE ALL MDC SUPPORTERS ARRESTED

DURING THE ELECTION CAMPAIGN AND SUSPEND PROSECUTION OF

MDC LEADERS.

-- THE OBJECTIVE OF NEGOTIATIONS IS NOT A UNITY

GOVERNMENT, BUT A GOVERNMENT OF TRANSITION FOR NO MORE

THAN SIX MONTHS LEADING TO NEW ELECTIONS HELD UNDER

UNITED NATIONS OR COMMONWEALTH AUSPICES AND HELD UNDER

THE NORMS AND STANDARDS FOR ELECTIONS SET FORTH BY THE

SADC-PARLIAMENTARY FORUM. (COLTART, A LAWYER, SAID THAT

MUGABE HAD FREQUENTLY DEMONSTRATED THAT HE COULD DECREE

-- MINISTERS IN SUCH A TRANSITIONAL GOVERNMENT SHOULD BE

AND SECURITY ACT SPECIFICALLY PROHIBITED "STAY-AWAYS"

7. ACTION REQUESTS: 1.) WE RECOMMEND THAT WE DO

EVERYTHING POSSIBLE TO HIGHLIGHT AND BRING PRESSURE
