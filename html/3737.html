SUBJECT: CONGEN MEETING WITH THICH HUYEN QUANG

1. (U) CLASSIFIED BY EMI L. YAMAUCHI, CG; REASON 1.5(B)

2. (SBU) SUMMARY: CONSUL GENERAL AND POLOFF VISITED THICH

HUYEN THQ (THQ) AT THE PHUOC QUANG PAGODA IN QUANG NGAI

PROVINCE ON SEPTEMBER 6. THQ WAS CANDID AND FEISTY IN

DESCRIBING HIS HEALTH AND THE CIRCUMSTANCES OF HIS

CONFINEMENT. HE CRITICIZED THE GVN\'S "CONDUCT OF ELECTIONS

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 HANOI 02255 01 OF 02 170852Z

AND POLICIES ON HUMAN RIGHTS AND RELIGIOUS FREEDOM."

ACCORDING TO THQ, HE WOULD BE WILLING "TO CONSIDER" MOVING

BACK TO HIS HOME PROVINCE OF BINH DINH ONCE THE GVN

PRESENTS HIM WITH AN OFFICIAL DOCUMENT DETAILING WHY HE HAS

BEEN DETAINED, THAT HE HAS FULFILLED THE TERMS OF HIS

DETENTION, AND THAT HE IS FREE TO TRAVEL. POST UNDERSTANDS

THAT GVN AUTHORITIES REFUSED A EUROPEAN PARLIAMENT

DELEGATION\'S REQUEST FOR A MEETING WITH THQ DURING THIS

SAME TIMEFRAME. END SUMMARY.

3. (U) ON SEPTEMBER 6, CONGENOFFS MET WITH THQ IN A ROOM

WITH A LONG TABLE, CHAIRS, AND BOOKCASES, SITUATED ACROSS A

COURTYARD FROM THE MAIN PAGODA. A POLICE STATION STANDS

OPPOSITE THE PAGODA\'S OUTER GATE, ABOUT 200 FEET AWAY. AT

ONE END OF THE ROOM WERE A BED AND SOME OTHER SMALL

FURNISHINGS. PROVINCIAL ESCORTS FROM THE EXTERNAL

RELATIONS OFFICE AND QUANG NGAI\'S "COMMITTEE ON RELIGIOUS

MANAGEMENT" ATTENDED.

4. (SBU) WHILE THQ SAID HIS HEALTH WAS DETERIORATING (DUE

MOSTLY TO OLD AGE, HE NOTED RUEFULLY), HE APPEARED TO BE IN

GOOD HEALTH FOR A MAN OF 83 YEARS. HE GREETED CONGENOFFS

STANDING UNASSISTED IN A DRIVING RAIN, SAT UPRIGHT

THROUGHOUT THE 50-MINUTE MEETING, AND ROSE SPRYLY TO SEE

OFF HIS VISITORS. HE WAS ALERT AND COHERENT AT ALL TIMES,

BUT APPEARED HARD OF HEARING (ALTHOUGH THIS COULD HAVE BEEN

BECAUSE OF THE HARD RAIN AND WIND). THQ\'S PRIMARY MEDICAL

COMPLAINTS WERE AN INCREASING LOSS OF SENSATION IN HIS

RIGHT HAND FINGERS OVER THE PAST TWO YEARS, AND EPISODES OF

HIGH BLOOD PRESSURE. FROM TIME TO TIME, HE EXPERIENCED

SOME NUMBNESS ON THE RIGHT SIDE OF HIS BODY. STILL, HE

NOTED HIS AILMENTS DID NOT KEEP HIM FROM WRITING BOOKS ON

BUDDHIST RITUALS. HE SAID HE RECEIVED REGULAR MEDICAL CARE

AND MEDICATIONS AND HAD NEVER BEEN DENIED PERMISSION TO

VISIT A DOCTOR.

5. (SBU) WHILE THE MAJORITY OF HIS TRIPS OUTSIDE THE

PAGODA WERE FOR MEDICAL ATTENTION, THQ MENTIONED TWO LOCAL

TRIPS WITHIN THE LAST YEAR TO MY LAI AND THE DUNG QUAT

INDUSTRIAL ZONE. IN WHAT MAY HAVE BEEN STAGED FOR OUR

BENEFIT, THE QUANG NGAI RELIGIOUS MANAGEMENT COMMITTEE

ESCORT REMINDED THQ THAT HE HAD JUST GONE TO QUANG NGAI

TOWN THE DAY BEFORE TO VISIT WITH A FELLOW MONK. THQ SAID

HE LED A NORMAL LIFE, READING RELIGIOUS TRACTS AND

RECEIVING REGULAR VISITORS. LATER ON, HE SAID HE HAD VERY

FEW VISITORS, ASIDE FROM THE YOUNG NUNS AND MONKS WHO CAME

FOR A FEW HOURS EACH DAY TO COOK AND CLEAN. THESE YOUNG

FOLLOWERS ROTATED ABOUT EVERY 15 DAYS. THQ LIVES ALONE,

WITH NO ACCESS TO TELEPHONE OR MAIL. HE HAD BEEN INFORMED

OF CONGENOFFS\' UPCOMING VISIT BY THE LOCAL AUTHORITIES.

6. (U) REGARDING THE BASIS FOR HIS CONFINEMENT, THICH

HUYEN QUANG EMPHASIZED THAT HE WAS UNDER DETENTION FOR

RELIGIOUS - NOT POLITICAL - REASONS. HE IS UNABLE TO LEAVE

THE PAGODA WITHOUT POLICE PERMISSION. THROUGHOUT THE

VISIT, THQ REFERRED TO THE FACT THAT HE HAD BEEN DETAINED

BY THE "FRENCH COLONIAL GOVERNMENT FOR THE FIRST TIME MORE

THAN 30 YEARS AGO." HE SAID HE WAS UNAWARE OF THE REASONS

FOR HIS CURRENT DETENTION AND HAD NEVER SEEN AN OFFICIAL

DOCUMENT DETAILING THE CHARGES AND SENTENCE. ACCORDING TO

THQ, NO ONE HAD EVER EXPLAINED THE CONDITIONS UNDER WHICH

HE COULD TRAVEL/MOVE. THUS, WHENEVER HE WAS INVITED TO GO

ELSEWHERE, HE TOLD PEOPLE HE COULD NOT LEAVE. OTHER

PAGODAS WERE HESITANT TO INVITE THQ BECAUSE THEY KNEW HE

COULD NOT MOVE.

7. (U) THE RELIGIOUS MANAGEMENT COMMITTEE ESCORT

INTERRUPTED TO SAY THAT THQ HAD BEEN OFFERED AN OPPORTUNITY

TO RETURN TO BINH DINH PROVINCE (HIS HOME PROVINCE) LAST

YEAR, BUT "HAD CHANGED HIS MIND." THQ CONTRADICTED HIM,

POINTING OUT THAT HE HAD MERELY ASKED TO SEE HIS OFFICIAL

CHARGING DOCUMENT, TO KNOW THE REASONS FOR HIS ARREST AND

LENGTH OF DETENTION "BEFORE MAKING UP HIS MIND." THQ MADE

IT CLEAR THAT HE COULD NOT COMMIT TO A MOVE UNTIL HE HAD

SEEN OFFICIAL PAPERS DETAILING THE REASONS FOR HIS

DETENTION, CERTIFICATION THAT HE HAD COMPLETED THE

DETENTION SUCCESSFULLY AND HAD BEHAVED CORRECTLY, AND

CONFIRMATION OF HIS FREEDOM TO TRAVEL.

8. (SBU) THICH HUYEN QUANG STATED THERE HAD BEEN NO

(POSITIVE) CHANGES IN VIETNAM WITH REGARD TO ELECTIONS,

RELIGIOUS FREEDOM, OR HUMAN RIGHTS. HE SAID THE GVN HAD

NOT ALLOWED THE UNIFIED BUDDHIST CHURCH OF VIETNAM (UBCV)

TO OPERATE AFTER 1975, AND THAT FEWER PEOPLE NATIONWIDE

WENT TO WORSHIP. WHEN ASKED HOW HE REGARDED THOSE WHO

BELONGED TO THE GVN-RECOGNIZED BUDDHIST CHURCH, THQ

9. (SBU) THE PROVINCIAL ESCORTS ATTEMPTED TO INTERRUPT

WHEN THE QUESTIONS AND ANSWERS STRAYED "TOO FAR FROM

GENERAL CONCERN ABOUT THICH HUYEN QUANG\'S HEALTH." BUT

CONGENOFFS AND THQ PRESSED ON. AT THE END, THE ESCORTS

ENCIRCLED THQ\'S CHAIR TO SPEED UP THE FAREWELLS. THQ SAID

HE WOULD WELCOME A RETURN VISIT.

10. (C) COMMENT: THIS WAS CONGENOFFS\' FIRST MEETING WITH

THICH HUYEN QUANG. WHILE WE FOUND HIM TO BE SOMEBODY THAT

WE WOULD LIKE TO GET TO KNOW BETTER, WE WERE CONCERNED THAT

HE SEEMED TO DWELL ON THE DISTANT PAST -- WEARING THE PRIDE

ON HIS SLEEVE OF HAVING BEEN ARRESTED MORE THAN 30 YEARS

AGO "UNDER THE FRENCH COLONIALISTS" AND DECRYING THE

OUTLAWING OF THE UBCV IN 1975. HE DID NOT WISH TO BE DRAWN

INTO A DISCUSSION ABOUT FUTURE ACTIVITIES, ASPIRATIONS,

GOALS, SO THE HISTORY LESSON WAS INTERESTING, BUT DID NOT

NECESSARILY ADD TO OUR KNOWLEDGE.

11. (C) ON THE OTHER HAND, WHEN CONGENOFFS RAISED THE

NAMES OF TWO BUDDHIST FOLLOWERS WHO HAVE VISITED THQ IN THE

PAST AND WHO ARE CURRENT CONGEN CONTACTS -- THICH NHAT

THIEN AND A NUN WHO DESCRIBES HERSELF AS THQ\'S GRANDNIECE -

- THQ\'S LANGUAGE INDICATED THAT HE HAD RESERVATIONS ABOUT

THE FORMER. THQ DESCRIBED THICH NHAT THIEN AS "SOMEBODY I

HAVE WORKED WITH IN THE PAST." AS FOR HIS GRANDNIECE, HE

SAID THAT HE DID NOT KNOW WHERE SHE WAS, OR WHAT SHE WAS

DOING BECAUSE SHE WAS "USUALLY VERY BUSY."

12. (SBU) POST NOTES THAT ACCORDING TO A PRESS RELEASE

FROM THE "QUE ME" ACTION FOR DEMOCRACY IN VIETNAM/VIETNAM

COMMITTEE ON HUMAN RIGHTS, HEADQUARTERED IN FRANCE, A

EUROPEAN PARLIAMENTARY DELEGATION VISITING VIETNAM DURING

SEPTEMBER 7-11, WAS DENIED MEETINGS WITH THICH HUYEN QUANG,

THICH QUANG DO, AND FATHER NGUYEN VAN LY. POST ALSO NOTES

THAT QUANG NGAI PROVINCIAL OFFICIALS DID NOT USE THE EXCUSE

OF TORRENTIAL RAINS AND FLOODED ROADS TO CANCEL OUR

APPOINTMENT, AS IS OFTEN DONE IN THESE SORTS OF SITUATIONS.

HOWEVER, PROVINCIAL OFFICIALS DID TRY TO KEEP THE MEETING

BRIEF. THE CHAIRMAN OF THE PROVINCIAL PEOPLE\'S COMMITTEE,

NGUYEN KIM HIEU, PERHAPS DOWNPLAYING THICH HUYEN QUANG\'S

STATURE, HAD TOLD CONGENOFFS EARLIER IN THE DAY THAT THQ

WAS SIMPLY AN OLD MAN "WHOSE WAY OF THINKING DOES NOT AGREE

WITH OURS. WE WOULD JUST LIKE HIM TO GO BACK TO HIS HOME

PROVINCE." END COMMENT.
