SUBJECT: ITALY ON WASSENAAR HOS: NOT TO WORRY

UNCLASSIFIED

REF: (A) MCBRIDE-VANSON 3/22 E-MAIL (B) VIENNA 449

1. (U) ITALY SEES NO REMAINING SUBSTANTIVE WASSENAAR

ARRANGEMENT HEAD OF SECRETARIAT SUCCESSION ISSUE, MFA\'S

NONPROLIFERATION COORDINATOR UGO DE MOHR TOLD ECONOFF ON

MARCH 22. ITALY ACCEPTS THAT THE SWEDISH CANDIDATE,

AMBASSADOR DANIELSSON, WILL BE THE NEXT HOS, AND DE MOHR SAID

ITALY IS FULLY SUPPORTIVE.

2. (SBU) DE MOHR ALLOWED THAT THERE MAY HAVE BEEN SOME RECENT

"MISCOMMUNICATION" ABOUT ITALY\'S POSITION ON THE HOS SALARY

QUESTION. ITALY AGREES THAT THE NEXT HOS SHOULD HAVE A

COMPENSATION PACKAGE EQUIVALENT TO A D2 RANK ON THE UNITED

NATIONS PERSONNEL SCALE. DE MOHR, HOWEVER, INSISTS THAT NO

REFERENCE BE MADE TO A D2 RANK IN THE FORMAL LETTER OF

APPOINTMENT OF THE HOS BECAUSE (1) WASSENAAR IS NOT PART OF

THE UN SYSTEM AND (2) REFERENCE TO A D2 RANK WOULD SEEM TO

THE WORLD TO BE A DOWNGRADING OF THE POSITION. HE POINTED

OUT THAT THE LETTER OF APPOINTMENT PRESENTED FOUR YEAR AGO TO

INCUMBENT HOS LAURIOLA MADE NO REFERENCE TO RANK. LAURIOLA,

LIKE DANIELSSON, CARRIES THE PERSONAL RANK OF AMBASSADOR, A

RANK DE MOHR BELIEVES LENDS USEFUL PRESTIGE TO THE HOS

POSITION. ECONOFF COUNTERED THAT, IF THE POSITION CARRIED NO

EXPRESS RANK WHEN AMBASSADOR LAURIOLA OCCUPIED IT, IT COULD

HARDLY BE "DOWNGRADED" NOW.

3. (SBU) REPEATEDLY CHARACTERIZING THE HOS COMPENSATION

PACKAGE AS A MINOR ISSUE THAT SHOULD NOT BE ALLOWED TO

INTERFERE WITH THE ENHANCED IMPORTANCE OF WASSENAAR IN THE

POST 9-11 WORLD, DE MOHR NEVERTHELESS BELABORED THE MATTER.

HE COMPLAINED THAT THE TURKISH AND UKRAINIAN PLENARY CHAIRS

HAD NOT CIRCULATED A GOI LETTER OBJECTING TO THE DOWNGRADING

OF THE HOS POSITION. ("THE TURKS," HE SAID, "LIED TO THE

UKRAINIANS FOR REASONS I WON\'T GO INTO.") HE ALLEGED THAT

THE DRAFT COMPENSATION PACKAGE HAD IMPROPERLY BEEN SHOWED TO

A HOS CANDIDATE BEFORE BEING APPROVED BY THE MEMBERSHIP. HE

DOUBTED THAT WASSENAAR WILL REALIZE A SAVINGS WITH THE NEW

CONTRACT BECAUSE THE NEW HOS MAY BE ENTITLED TO ADDITIONAL

BENEFITS (SEPARATION ALLOWANCE, ETC.) THAT CANNOT BE FULLY

CALCULATED UNTIL HE SUBMITS REQUISITE PAPERWORK. HE,

HOWEVER, CONCLUDED THE SUBJECT ON AN UPBEAT NOTE, SAYING

"LET\'S NOT MAKE PROBLEMS THAT DON\'T EXIST" AS THE USG AND GOI

PREPARE TO WORK CLOSELY TOGETHER (AS WASSENAAR PLENARY CHAIR

AND EXPERTS COMMITTEE CHAIR RESPECTIVELY).

4. (SBU) COMMENT. WE WON\'T PRESUME TO JUDGE WHETHER DE

MOHR\'S LATEST HOS FOOT-DRAGGING REFLECTS FURTHER

LAURIOLA-SAGA INTRIGUE OR WHETHER HE REALLY FEELS THE HOS

WOULD BE DIMINISHED BY AN EXPRESS D2 RANK. IF APPROPRIATE AS

A WASSENAAR ADMINISTRATIVE MATTER, THE PATH OF LEAST

RESISTANCE WOULD BE TO SIMPLY DELETE ANY REFERENCE TO RANK IN

THE HOS LETTER OF APPOINTMENT. DE MOHR MAINTAINS THAT\'S ALL

HE WANTS AND, NOTWITHSTANDING ALL THAT HAS GONE BEFORE IN THE

HOS MATTER, WE HAVE NEVER KNOWN HIM TO BE OVERTLY

DISINGENUOUS.
