SUBJECT: HOLOCAUST ENVOY\'S VATICAN FAREWELL CALL

REF: 01 VATICAN 06619

CLASSIFIED BY: AMBASSADOR NICHOLSON. REASONS 1.5 (B,D)

1. (C) SUMMARY: U.S. SPECIAL ENVOY FOR HOLOCAUST ISSUES

AMBASSADOR BINDENAGEL MET WITH VATICAN OFFICIALS APRIL 26 FOR

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 VATICA 02229 01 OF 02 081737Z

A LAST UPDATE ON THE ISSUE OF ACCESS TO PONTIFICAL ARCHIVES

FOR RESEARCH RELATED TO THE SHOAH AND THE PONTIFICATE OF PIUS

XII. HE MET SEPARATELY WITH VATICAN DFM MSGR. MIGLIORE AND

CARDINAL KASPAR, HEAD OF THE VATICAN\'S COMMISSION FOR

RELIGIOUS RELATIONS WITH JUDAISM. BOTH NOTED GRADUAL

HEADWAY, YET ALSO ACKNOWLEDGED THE SETBACK OF THE 2001

BREAKDOWN OF THE JEWISH-VATICAN COMMISSION TO STUDY PIUS

XII\'S WWII ROLE. KASPAR NARRATED HIS EFFORTS TO FOUND A NEW

COMMISSION BASED ON COLLABORATION BETWEEN ACADEMIC

INSTITUTIONS THAT WOULD MARGINALIZE INVOLVEMENT BY THE

VATICAN AND THE WORLD JEWISH CONGRESS. KASPAR AND MIGLIORE

DENOUNCED A RESURGENT ANTI-SEMITISM, NURTURED IN THEIR

OPINION BY THE MIDDLE EAST CRISIS, AND ALSO VOICED CONCERN AT

THE APRIL 21 FRENCH ELECTION RESULTS. END SUMMARY.

2. (U) AMBASSADOR J.D. BINDENAGEL, SPECIAL ENVOY FOR

HOLOCAUST ISSUES, MET SEPARATELY APRIL 26 AT THE VATICAN WITH

DFM CELESTINO MIGLIORE AND CARDINAL WALTAR KASPAR, HEAD OF

THE COMMISION FOR RELIGIOUS RELATIONS WITH JUDAISM. POLOFF

ATTENDED BOTH MEETINGS AS NOTETAKER; MSGR. FUMAGELLI

ACCOMPANIED CARDINAL KASPAR.

3. (C) CARDINAL KASPAR NOTED THAT THE ACTS OF THE BERLIN

NUNCIATURE UP TO 1939, COVERING THE REIGN OF PIUS XI, WOULD

BE ACCESSIBLE IN 2003. ALTHOUGH THESE WOULD NOT INCLUDE

DOCUMENTS FROM THE PAPACY OF PIUS XII, THEY HAVE SPECIAL

RELEVANCE GIVEN THE ROLE OF EUGENIO PACELLI, THE FUTURE PIUS

XII, AS NUNCIO IN BERLIN AND LATER SECRETARY OF STATE UNDER

PIUS XI. YET KASPAR STATED HE WAS UNABLE TO ACCELERATE THE

TIMETABLE FOR OPENING THE VATICAN ARCHIVES FOR THE REIGN OF

PIUS XII, CURRENTLY ANTICIPATED ABOUT 2010 (REF). NOT ONLY

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 VATICA 02229 01 OF 02 081737Z

WERE THOSE ARCHIVES IN EXTREME DISORDER -- A "DURCHEINANDER"

HE DECLARED IN GERMAN -- AND IN NEED OF PAINSTAKING REVIEW,

BUT THESE ARCHIVES WERE THE DOMAIN OF THE VATICAN SECRETARIAT

AND NOT THE COUNCIL FOR RELATIONS WITH THE JEWS. HE REMARKED

THE SECRETARIAT HOLDS NO FEAR OF THE HISTORICAL VERDICT,

HOWEVER WORK MUST PROCEED METHODICALLY. KASPAR COMMENTED THAT

SINCE HISTORICAL INVESTIGATION ENTAILED MAKING JUDGMENTS AND

NOT SIMPLY ASCERTAINING FACTS, THERE WOULD ALWAYS BE SOME

DISAGREEMENT. EARLIER MIGLIORE, TOO, HAD SPOKEN SIMILARLY,

OPINING THAT IDEOLOGICAL CONCERNS WERE GUIDING MANY

RESEARCHERS. MIGLIORE DECRIED THE RECENT COSTA-GRAVAS FILM

"AMEN" FOR ITS LACK OF CONCERN FOR HISTORICAL TRUTH.

4. (C) KASPAR LAMENTED THE 2001 BREAKDOWN OF THE

JEWISH-VATICAN COMMISSION ESTABLISHED IN 1999 TO ASSESS THE

12 VOLUME SET OF HOLY SEE ARCHIVAL MATERIALS RELATING TO THE

WWII PAPACY OF PIUS XII AND THE HOLOCAUST. THAT COMMISSION

LOOKED GOOD ON PAPER, HE STATED, YET ITS EXECUTION WAS DEEPLY

FLAWED, CITING THE LACK OF SHARED CRITERIA, THE WANT OF

MUTUAL TRUST, AND THE UNHELPFUL RECOURSE BY INDIVIDUAL

PARTICIPANTS TO THE MEDIA. HE ALSO FAULTED THE COMMISSION

FOR ITS FAILURE TO INCLUDE A EUROPEAN HISTORIAN, GIVEN THAT

THE HOLOCAUST WAS FUNDAMENTALLY A EUROPEAN AFFAIR. KASPAR

DISAVOWED ANY BLAME FOR THE BREAKDOWN, BUT EMPHASIZED THE

COMMISSION WAS FRAGILE FROM THE START. KASPAR HAS SINCE

SOUGHT TO ESTABLISH A NEW COMMISSION THAT WOULD PROCEED ON AN

ACADEMIC PLANE, AS A COOPERATIVE EFFORT BETWEEN TEL AVIV AND

FRANKFURT UNIVERSITIES, IN PLACE OF THE PREVIOUS

VATICAN-WORLD JEWISH CONGRESS DRIVEN COMMISSION. HIS PROJECT

FOUNDERED, ACCORDING TO KASPAR, WHEN TEL AVIV SOUGHT NEW

CONDITIONS BEYOND KASPAR\'S AUTHORIZATION, MOST NOTABLY THE

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 04 VATICA 02229 01 OF 02 081737Z

INCLUSION OF NEW YORK UNIVERSITY, ALLEGEDLY DUE TO PRESSURE

BY TEL AVIV\'S U.S. FINANCIAL BACKERS.

5. (C) BOTH KASPAR AND MIGLIORE DECRIED THE APRIL 21

ELECTORAL SUCCESS OF FRENCH NATIONAL LEADER JEAN-MARIE LE

PEN, A REPUTED ANTI-SEMITE. KASPAR, IN PARTICULAR, REPEATEDLY

DECLARED HIS REVULSION, AND DELIVERED AN IMPASSIONED

DENUNCIATION OF ANTI-SEMITISM, IN WHICH HE REITERATED THE

CENTRALITY OF JUDAISM TO CHRISTIANITY -- "OUR ELDER BROTHER

IN THE FAITH OF ABRAHAM" IN THE WORDS OF JOHN PAUL II -- AND

OF JUDAISM TO EUROPEAN CULTURE. "WITHOUT JUDAISM YOU CANNOT

EVEN SPEAK OF CULTURE IN BERLIN AND VIENNA," KASPAR REMARKED,

ADDED THAT IN HIS YOUTH HE HIMSELF HAD BEEN ABSORBED BY THE

THOUGHT OF JEWISH PHILOSOPHER MARTIN BUBER.

6. (C) MIGLIORE LINKED RESURGENT EUROPEAN ANTI-SEMITISM TO

FALLOUT FROM DEVELOPMENTS IN THE MIDDLE EAST, A VIEW KASPAR

SHARED IN PRINCIPLE, ALTHOUGH THE CARDINAL TOOK EXPLICIT CARE

TO CONDEMN PALESTINIAN HOMICIDAL BOMBERS AS WELL AS ISRAELI

ACTIONS IN BETHLEHEM.

7. (C) COMMENT: DFM MIGLIORE\'S REMARKS REVEAL THAT THE

VATICAN MFA NOW VIEWS THE ARCHIVAL DISPUTE AS A QUESTION OF

SECONDARY IMPORTANCE, A NUISANCE THAT PALES IN RELATION TO
