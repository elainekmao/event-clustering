SUBJECT: PUBLIC DIPLOMACY ACTION PLAN: WMD- JORDAN

REF: STATE 109724

Classified By: PAO DANA SHELL SMITH, REASONS 5 (B & D)

1. EMBASSY AMMAN AGREES THAT SUBJECT PLAN IS AN IMPORTANT

GOAL. THE GOJ ALREADY HAS A VERY HELPFUL POSITION ON THE

ISSUE OF WMD AND WE DO NOT BELIEVE THAT PROACTIVE PUBLIC

ENGAGEMENT ON THE ISSUE WILL BE USEFUL WITH OUR AUDIENCE.

GIVEN THE CURRENT POLITICAL REALITIES IN THE REGION, PUBLIC

ENGAGEMENT ON WMD HERE WILL ONLY ELICIT NEGATIVE REACTIONS

EMPHASIZING THAT ISRAEL MAINTAINS AN ACTIVE NUCLEAR PROGRAM.

ADDITIONALLY, THE TOPIC WILL BE SEEN AS JUSTIFICATION FOR AN

ATTACK ON IRAQ. EMBASSY AMMAN WILL, HOWEVER, BE PLEASED TO

HIGHLIGHT THE WMD WEBSITE ON OUR HOMEPAGE.
