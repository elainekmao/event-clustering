SUMMARY

-------

1. (C) WILFRED MHANDA, LEADER OF THE ZIMBABWE LIBERATORS\'

PLATFORM, A MEMBERSHIP ORGANIZATION SEEKING TO RESTORE THE

GOOD NAME OF WAR VETERANS, TOLD CHARGE AND POLITICAL

SECTION CHIEF ON FEBRUARY 8 THAT VERY FEW EX-COMBATANTS ARE

INVOLVED IN THE GOVERNMENT\'S EFFORTS TO COW OPPOSITION

SUPPORTERS, THE JUDICIARY, AND THE INDEPENDENT MEDIA. THE

MAN LEADING THE GOVERNMENT\'S CAMPAIGN OF TERROR, ZANU-PF MP

CHENJERAI HUNZVI, IS NOT A GENUINE WAR VETERAN AND DOES NOT

REPRESENT THE INTERESTS OF MOST EX-COMBATANTS, ACCORDING TO

MHANDA. SENIOR MILITARY OFFICERS HAVE TOLD MHANDA THAT

HUNZVI COULD BE BROUGHT UNDER CONTROL IN FIVE MINUTES,

SHOULD THE GOVERNMENT DECIDE TO DO SO. END SUMMARY.

2. (C) WILFRED MHANDA, LEADER OF THE ZIMBABWE LIBERATORS\'

PLATFORM, A MEMBERSHIP ASSOCIATION OF WAR VETERANS, CALLED

ON THE CHARGE ON FEBRUARY 8. MHANDA WAS JOINED BY ADVISOR

HAPPYSON NENJI. CHARGE WAS ACCOMPANIED BY POLITICAL

SECTION CHIEF.

ZIMBABWE LIBERATORS\' PLATFORM

-----------------------------

3. (C) MHANDA SAID THE ZIMBABWE LIBERATORS\' PLATFORM WAS

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 HARARE 00695 01 OF 02 091230Z

FORMED TO WORK FOR IMPLEMENTATION OF THE FREEDOMS

ZIMBABWEANS FOUGHT FOR DURING THE WAR OF LIBERATION.

SPECIFICALLY, THE ORGANIZATION IS STRIVING TO RESTORE THE

GOOD NAME OF WAR VETERANS, TO ASSIST THEM IN BECOMING

ECONOMICALLY SUSTAINABLE, REDUCING THEIR VULNERABILITY TO

POLITICAL MANIPULATION, PRESSING FOR THE RESTORATION OF THE

RULE OF LAW AND DEVELOPMENT OF A NON-RACIAL DEMOCRACY.

APPROXIMATELY 500 EX-COMBATANTS HAVE PAID THEIR MEMBERSHIP

DUES IN THE ZLP, AND MHANDA FELT CONFIDENT THAT THAT NUMBER

WOULD INCREASE. NOTING THAT HIS ORGANIZATION CURRENTLY

CANNOT AFFORD OFFICE SPACE, MHANDA MADE A PITCH FOR

INSTITUTIONAL SUPPORT, NOTING THAT, WITHOUT SUCH RESOURCES,

GROUPS LIKE HIS CANNOT PROVIDE AN EFFECTIVE ALTERNATIVE

VOICE AND CANNOT HOPE TO COMPETE FOR POLITICAL SPACE WITH A

GOVERNMENT WITH FULL ACCESS TO THE STATE MACHINERY.

VERY FEW WAR VETS INVOLVED IN MUGABE\'S VIOLENT CAMPAIGN

--------------------------------------------- ----------

4. (C) THE CHARGE SOLICITED MHANDA\'S ASSESSMENT OF THE

ZIMBABWE NATIONAL LIBERATION WAR VETERANS ASSOCIATION

(ZNLWVA) LED BY CHENJERAI "HITLER" HUNZVI, NOTING THAT

HUNZVI AND HIS BAND OF WAR VETERANS HAVE PLAYED A LARGE

ROLE IN BLACKENING ZIMBABWE\'S INTERNATIONAL IMAGE. THE

ZNLWVA, MHANDA REPLIED, IS NO LONGER A FUNCTIONING

ORGANIZATION. IT HAS CEASED TO SERVE THE PURPOSE FOR WHICH

IT WAS FOUNDED -- REPRESENTING THE INTERESTS OF EX-

COMBATANTS -- BUT, RATHER, HAS BEEN HIJACKED BY PRESIDENT

MUGABE TO BE USED AS A ZANU-PF MILITIA. HUNZVI, MHANDA

CLAIMED, IS NOT HIMSELF A GENUINE EX-COMBATANT. HUNZVI HAS

NO INDEPENDENT SUPPORT BASE AND, MHANDA ASSERTED, HAS BEEN

GIVEN LITTLE ROOM FOR MANEUVER BY MUGABE. HUNZVI\'S PRIMARY

VALUE LIES IN GIVING PRESIDENT MUGABE PLAUSIBLE DENIABILITY

BY SERVING AS THE PUBLIC FACE OF THE GOVERNMENT\'S CAMPAIGN

OF VIOLENCE AND INTIMIDATION. ACCORDING TO MHANDA, THE

DECISION TO INVADE COMMERCIAL FARMS AND TO COW OPPOSITION

SUPPORTERS WAS MADE BY PRESIDENT MUGABE AND HIS MILITARY

SERVICE CHIEFS, NOT BY HUNZVI.

5. (C) MHANDA INSISTED THAT VERY FEW OF ZIMBABWE\'S

APPROXIMATELY 40,000 WAR VETERANS ARE WORKING WITH HUNZVI

AND HIS THUGS. ABOUT HALF OF THE EX-COMBATANTS ARE STILL

SERVING IN THE ARMY, WHILE MOST OF THE REMAINING 20,000 ARE

GAINFULLY EMPLOYED ELSEWHERE IN THE PUBLIC AND PRIVATE

SECTORS. MHANDA ESTIMATED THAT FEWER THAN 100 GENUINE

LIBERATION WAR VETERANS, IN ADDITION TO LARGER NUMBERS OF

ZANU-PF THUGS, ARE RESPONSIBLE FOR CARRYING OUT THE

GOVERNMENT\'S CAMPAIGN OF VIOLENCE AND INTIMIDATION AGAINST

OPPOSITION SUPPORTERS, THE JUDICIARY, AND THE INDEPENDENT

MEDIA, AS WELL AS OCCUPYING COMMERCIAL FARMS. THE MILITARY

SERVICE CHIEFS HAVE TOLD MHANDA THEY COULD BRING HUNZVI

UNDER CONTROL IN FIVE MINUTES. THE FACT THAT THEY HAVE NOT

DONE SO, MHANDA SURMISED, CONFIRMS THAT HUNZVI AND HIS

SHOCK TROOPS ARE CONTINUING TO SERVE A USEFUL ROLE FOR

ZANU-PF. FURTHER EVIDENCE THAT HUNZVI AND THE "WAR

VETERANS" ARE LITTLE MORE THAN PAWNS, OUR INTERLOCUTOR

COMMENT

-------

6. (C) WE FOUND MHANDA TO BE BRIGHT AND ARTICULATE, AND

EMBARRASSED THAT THE REPUTATION OF "WAR VETERANS" HAS BEEN

DRAGGED THROUGH THE MUD. HE WAS AT GREAT PAINS TO EXPLAIN

THAT HITLER HUNZVI AND MOST OF THOSE UNDER HIS LEADERSHIP,

NEVER FOUGHT IN THE LIBERATION WAR. THE SUBSTANCE OF

MHANDA\'S CONTENTION HAS BEEN WIDELY ACCEPTED IN ZIMBABWE,

BUT THE ZLP LEADER WAS TROUBLED THAT BOTH DOMESTIC AND

INTERNATIONAL MEDIA CONTINUE TO DESCRIBE HUNZVI AND HIS

HENCHMEN AS WAR VETERANS. MHANDA WAS CLEARLY CONTEMPTUOUS

OF HUNZVI, AND WE CONSIDER CREDIBLE HIS DECLARATION THAT

THE ZANU-PF MP DOES NOT SPEAK FOR THE MAJORITY OF WAR

VETERANS BUT, IN FACT, HAS DESTROYED THE ORGANIZATION SET

UP TO REPRESENT THE INTERESTS OF EX-COMBATANTS.

7. (C) IT IS OBVIOUS THAT HUNZVI HAS AN IMPORTANT

CONSTITUENCY OF ONE -- PRESIDENT MUGABE. THERE IS

SIGNIFICANT EVIDENCE TO SUGGEST THAT THE PRESIDENT HAS

GIVEN HUNZVI GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS WITH CARTE BLANCHE ON

TACTICS TO DO WHAT IS NECESSARY TO ENSURE THE RULING PARTY

WINS PARLIAMENTARY BY-ELECTIONS AND THE BIGGEST PRIZE OF

ALL: THE 2002 PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION. HUNZVI CLEARLY HAS

TAKEN ON THAT RESPONSIBILITY WITH PATHOLOGICAL ENTHUSIASM,

EVIDENT IN HIS SUPERVISION OF -- AND PERSONAL PARTICIPATION

IN -- TERROR CAMPAIGNS AGAINST OPPOSITION SUPPORTERS IN

ADVANCE OF BY-ELECTIONS IN MARONDERA AND BIKITA. HUNZVI
