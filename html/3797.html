1. (C) SUMMARY: ON SEPTEMBER 23, PARLIAMENTARY DEFENSE

COMMITTEE PRESIDENT LUIGI RAMPONI TOLD DCM THAT HE WAS

CONFIDENT THAT UNSC MANDATED USE OF FORCE IN IRAQ WOULD HAVE

STRONG, ACROSS THE BOARD SUPPORT IN THE CHAMBER. UNILATERAL

OR LIMITED COALITION ACTION WOULD, HOWEVER, NOT BE SUPPORTED

BY THE CENTER-LEFT OPPOSITION. RAMPONI SAID THAT ALPINI

TROOP DEPLOYMENT TO OEF WAS BEYOND THE SCOPE OF DEPLOYMENT

LEGISLATION PASSED LAST FALL, AND HE COULD NOT PREDICT THE

EXTENT OF BIPARTISAN SUPPORT IN PARLIAMENT FOR SUCH A

DEPLOYMENT. END SUMMARY

2. (C) IN ANTICIPATION OF PARLIAMENTARY (CHAMBER OF

DEPUTIES) DEBATE ON IRAQ SCHEDULED FOR SEPTEMBER 25, DCM

REINFORCED WITH RAMPONI USG VIEWS ON THE IMPORTANCE OF

DISARMING SADDAM HUSSEIN, AND URGED RAMPONI TO DO WHAT HE

COULD TO SOLIDIFY SUPPORT IN PARLIAMENT. RAMPONI, A MEMBER

OF THE JUNIOR COALITION PARTNER NATIONAL ALLIANCE PARTY, SAID

THAT HE AGREED WITH THE USG POSITION, AND THAT THERE WAS

AGREEMENT FROM ACROSS THE ITALIAN POLITICAL SPECTRUM ON THE

IMPORTANCE OF KEEPING WMD FROM SADDAM. RAMPONI WILL MAKE A

SPEECH IN THE CHAMBER DURING THE SEPTEMBER 25 DEBATES, IN

WHICH HE WILL DRAW COMPARISONS BETWEEN THE CURRENT PROBLEMS

WITH IRAQ AND THE PRE 9/11 SITUATION IN AFGHANISTAN. HE SAID

THAT HE WOULD CITE MULTIPLE VIOLATIONS OF SECURITY COUNCIL

RESOLUTIONS BY AFGHANISTAN IN 2000 AND 2001. THE RESOLUTIONS

HAD CALLED FOR, INTER ALIA, HALTING AID TO TERRORIST

ORGANIZATIONS AND EXTRADITING OSAMA BIN LADEN. HAD THE IC

BEEN MORE RESOLUTE IN ENFORCING THOSE RESOLUTIONS, SAID

RAMPONI, "WE WOULD NOT HAVE HAD A 9/11."

3. (C) RAMPONI SAID THAT HE SUPPORTS THE STRONG POSITION

THE U.S. HAS TAKEN IN ORDER TO PUSH THE UN TO ACTION AND TO

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 ROME 04625 241753Z

"GET SOME ANSWERS" FROM SADDAM. IF INSPECTIONS WORK, AND THE

IC HAS TOTAL ASSURANCE THAT IT HAS SADDAM\'S WEAPONS

PRODUCTION CAPABILITIES UNDER CONTROL, THEN PERHAPS WE CAN

AVOID WAR. IF SADDAM DOES NOT COOPERATE, THE UNSC MUST

REACT. THE USE OF FORCE UNDER A UN BANNER WOULD RECEIVE THE

SAME LEVEL OF SUPPORT IN THE ITALIAN PARLIAMENT AS GIVEN TO

OEF. HOWEVER, CONTINUED RAMPONI, IF THE U.S. GOES IT ALONE

OR WITH A SMALL COALITION, IT WOULD BE VERY HARD TO SECURE

SUPPORT FROM THE MINORITY CENTER-LEFT.

4. (C) TURNING TO THE U.S. REQUEST FOR A BATTALION OF THE

GOI\'S ALPINI TROOPS TO BE DEPLOYED TO OEF, THE DCM REITERATED

THAT THE ALPINI WERE CAREFULLY SELECTED FOR THEIR EXPERTISE

BY CENTCOM STAFF. FURTHER, OUR REQUEST FOR THE ALPINI TROOPS

IS NOT LINKED TO ANY POSSIBLE U.S. DEPLOYMENT TO IRAQ.

RATHER, INTERNATIONAL PARTICIPATION IN OEF GROUND FORCE

DEPLOYMENT HAS DIMINISHED, TO THE POINT WHERE SOON ALMOST ALL

GROUND FORCES IN OEF WOULD BE U.S. THE ALPINI DEPLOYMENT

WOULD HELP CORRECT THAT SITUATION AND MEET PREVIOUSLY

IDENTIFIED OEF REQUIREMENTS.

5. (C) RAMPONI SAID THAT THE ALPINI WERE WELL TRAINED IN

MOUNTAIN COMBAT TACTICS, BUT NOT IN COUNTERTERRORISM

TECHNIQUES. HE SAID THAT THERE WOULD BE SOME DEBATE IN

PARLIAMENT ABOUT THE DEPLOYMENT SINCE IT WOULD GO BEYOND THE

SCOPE OF OEF SUPPORT LEGISLATION PASSED LAST FALL. RAMPONI

COULD NOT PREDICT THE LEVEL OF SUPPORT IN PARLIAMENT FOR SUCH

A DEPLOYMENT.

6. (C) COMMENT. WE CONTINUE TO ANTICIPATE SUFFICIENT

PARLIAMENTARY SUPPORT FOR ALPINI DEPLOYMENT. OUR
