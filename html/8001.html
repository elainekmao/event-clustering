SUBJECT: NEXT STEPS ON FINANCIAL AGREEMENT

REF: A. (A) STATE 123461

B. (B) ANKARA 3018

(SBU) A/DCM passed Ref A answers regarding the $1 billion

financial assistance package to Treasury Deputy U/S Aydin

Karaoz May 12. Karaoz said that, based on the May 9

Babacan-Taylor phone conversation, he understood the USG

would prepare and present a draft financial agreement, after

which the GOT would send a technical team to Washington to

discuss it. A/DCM said his understanding was that U/S Taylor

had undertaken to get back to Minister Babacan early this

week about next steps, and that all concerned seemed to agree

that technical discussions would take place in Washington.
