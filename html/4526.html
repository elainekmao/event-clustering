SUBJECT: FIAT: WORKER PROTESTS CONTINUE; SICILIAN SOLUTION

IN SIGHT?

1. (SBU) SUMMARY: FIAT AUTO, TRADE UNIONS AND THE BERLUSCONI

GOVERNMENT CONTINUE THEIR POSTURING AROUND THE AUTOMAKER\'S

PLAN TO CUT COSTS BY IDLING THREE PLANTS AND LAYING OFF OVER

8,000 EMPLOYEES. ALTHOUGH FIAT\'S LENDERS APPEAR TO BE THE

ONLY ONES CONTENT WITH THE PLAN AS CONCEIVED, WORKER

ACTIVISM, POPULAR ATTENTION -- AND GOVERNMENT MEDIATION --

IS FOCUSED ON SICILY, WHERE CHRONICALLY HIGH UNEMPLOYMENT

AND THE SPECTRE OF A RESURGENT MAFIA MAGNIFY THE SOCIAL

IMPACT OF 1800 LAYOFFS AT FIAT\'S TERMINI IMERESE PLANT.

WITH THE ENTIRE SICILIAN PARLIAMENTARY DELEGATION HAILING

FROM THE PARTIES IN PM BERLUSCONI\'S COALITION, THE

GOVERNMENT IS DETERMINED TO PREVENT THE PLANT\'S PERMANENT

CLOSURE. AT ITS HEART, THE WRANGLING IS NOT ABOUT THE

RESTRUCTURING PLAN\'S IMMEDIATE IMPACT; IT\'S ABOUT COMPETING

VISIONS FOR THE LONG-TERM HEALTH AND DIVERSITY OF THE

ITALIAN ECONOMY. FOR MANY ITALIANS, THE FIAT CRISIS HAS

BECOME A LEITMOTIF FOR THE FUTURE SHAPE OF THEIR INDUSTRIAL

SECTOR AND LABOR MARKET -- AND WHAT ONE SEES DEPENDS VERY

MUCH ON WHICH END OF THE PENINSULA ONE LIVES. END SUMMARY

2. (U) SOME OBSERVERS (INCLUDING US) HAVE LIKENED THE

MANEUVERING AROUND THE RESTRUCTURING PLAN TO A HIGH-STAKES

POKER GAME. BUT IN RECENT WEEKS, IT\'S LOOKED MUCH MORE LIKE

A GAME OF CHICKEN, WITH EACH PARTY SPEEDING TOWARD THE

OTHERS, ACCELERATOR JAMMED TO THE FLOOR, HOPING TO FORCE THE

OTHER PARTIES OFF THE ROAD. THE GOVERNMENT AVERTED A MAJOR

COLLISION AT A NOVEMBER 25 "MEDIATION" SESSION (TO WHICH IT

HAD CONVOKED THE PARTIES) BY PERSUADING FIAT TO POSTPONE ITS

PLANNED LAYOFF OF 7600 WORKERS FOR 10 DAYS. THE

POSTPONEMENT, DEMANDED BY LABOR AS A PRECONDITION FOR

FURTHER TALKS, SETS THE STAGE FOR FURTHER DISCUSSIONS OF A

MODIFIED FIAT RESTRUCTURING PLAN.

3. (SBU) ALTHOUGH THE ENTIRE PLAN OSTENSIBLY WILL BE UNDER

REVIEW, IT\'S CLEAR THE GOVERNMENT\'S MAIN OBJECTIVE WILL BE

TO HAMMER OUT AN AGREEMENT THAT PRESERVES MOST, IF NOT ALL,

THE 1800 JOBS AT FIAT\'S PLANT IN SICILY. BEHIND THE SCENES,

EVEN LABOR SEEMS TO AGREE WITH THIS APPROACH; UNION

OFFICIALS IN MILAN HAVE TOLD US THEY EXPECT MOST OF THE

WORKERS IDLED FROM AN ALFA ROMEO PLANT NEARBY TO QUICKLY

FIND OTHER JOBS IF, AS EXPECTED, THE PLANT IS PERMANENTLY

CLOSED. AT THE NOVEMBER 25 SESSION, FIAT OFFICERS

REPORTEDLY PROPOSED MODIFICATIONS UNDER WHICH THE SICILIAN

PLANT, SITE OF THE MOST DRAMATIC PROTESTS, WOULD REOPEN IN

SIX MONTHS AFTER RETOOLING TO PRODUCE A NEW VERSION OF THE

PUNTO -- PRODUCTION THAT WOULD BE SHIFTED FROM FIAT\'S

MIRAFIORI PLANT IN TURIN.

4. (U) IN THIS AND FUTURE PASSES OF CHICKEN, EACH OF THE

DRIVERS HAS SOME HEAVY MUSCLE RIDING SHOTGUN. THE UNION

CONFEDERATION LEADERS CAN POINT TO THE THOUSANDS OF FIAT

WORKERS, THEIR FAMILIES AND OTHER SUPPORTERS WHO HAVE STAGED

A ROLLING SERIES OF STRIKES AND OTHER ACTIONS TO DRAMATIZE

THE IMPACT OF THE LAYOFFS. THOUSANDS OF FIAT WORKERS AND

THEIR FAMILIES NOVEMBER 12-15 BLOCKED PALERMO\'S AIRPORT AND

TRAIN STATION, THE MESSINA FERRY TERMINALS AND SICILIAN

HIGHWAYS, SNARLING TRAFFIC, PROMPTING WIDESPREAD DELAYS AND

BRINGING MUCH OF THE REGION/ISLAND TO A STANDSTILL. WORKERS

FROM THE ALFA ROMEO PLANT OUTSIDE MILAN SHUT DOWN MALPENSA

AIRPORT FOR AN HOUR NOVEMBER 21 AND BLOCKED A MAJOR FREEWAY

NOVEMBER 25. STRIKES HAVE IDLED ALL OF FIAT\'S PLANTS

SEVERAL TIMES. OBSERVERS POINT TO THE DIFFERENT ATMOSPHERE

AT THE VARIOUS FIAT PLANTS AFFECTED: AT THE ALFA PLANT

OUTSIDE MILAN AND AT FIAT\'S HEADQUARTERS IN TURIN, THE MOOD

IS ONE OF ANGER; AT THE SICILIAN PLANT, IT IS ONE OF

DESPERATION. METALWORKERS HAVE STAGED A SECTOR-WIDE

SOLIDARITY ACTION, AND ONE OF THE METALWORKER FEDERATION

LEADERS TOLD US TALKS WERE UNDERWAY TO ORGANIZE A SOLIDARITY

STRIKE AT FIAT\'S OTHER EUROPEAN PLANTS.

5. (U) ON MANAGEMENT\'S SIDE, FIAT AUTO\'S LENDORS AREN\'T JUST

ALONG FOR THE RIDE; THEY\'VE LEANED ACROSS AND ADDED THEIR

FOOT TO THE ACCELERATOR. UNINTERESTED IN EITHER PROVIDING

FURTHER LOANS OR BECOMING FIAT SHAREHOLDERS BY BUYING BACK

THEIR DEBT, THE BANKS CONTINUE TO PRESS FOR MAXIMUM COST

SAVINGS. THE COMPANY HAD A MUCH-PUBLICIZED MEETING WITH ITS

MAJOR LENDORS NOVEMBER 21. AFTERWARD, IT DECLARED THAT IT

WAS WILLING TO ADJUST THE DETAILS OF ITS RESTRUCTURING

PROPOSAL BUT COULD NOT ACCEPT ANY CHANGES THAT REDUCED THE

PROJECTED COST SAVINGS (AT LEAST 2 BILLION EUROS).

6. (U) THE BERLUSCONI GOVERNMENT\'S CAR IS ALSO CROWDED,

JAMMED AS IT IS WITH THE ENTIRE SICILIAN PARLIAMENTARY

DELEGATION (NOT TO MENTION SEVERAL OF THE CABINET\'S MOST

7. (SBU) AT THEIR HEART, THE NEGOTIATIONS ARE ABOUT THE

SICILIAN PLANT\'S LONG-TERM PROSPECTS; THIS IS WHERE TERMINI

IMERESE BECOMES A LEITMOTIF FOR FIAT\'S -- AND MORE BROADLY,

MANUFACTURING\'S -- FUTURE IN ITALY. FOR MANAGEMENT, THE

PLANT\'S IDLING IS AN ACCOUNTING DECISION THAT WILL RESTORE

THE FIRM TO COMPETITIVENESS AND/OR MAKE IT MORE ATTRACTIVE

TO GM (FIAT HAS AN OPTION TO SELL GM A CONTROLLING STAKE IN

THE COMPANY IN 2004). FIAT HAS SAID IT WILL REOPEN THE

PLANT IN 12 MONTHS IF SALES OF THE MODEL MANUFACTURED THERE

IMPROVE. THE TRADE UNION CONFEDERATIONS ARGUE THE COMPANY

HAS NOT PRESENTED A COMPREHENSIVE RESTRUCTURING PLAN, WITH

NEW INVESTMENT IN DESIGN AND PRODUCTION, TO DEMONSTRATE THAT

IT PLANS TO REOPEN THE PLANT AND MAINTAIN CURRENT EMPLOYMENT

LEVELS. THIS REFLECTS THE UNION\'S INTEREST IN MAINTAINING A

VIABLE, INNOVATIVE AUTOMOBILE INDUSTRY IN ITALY, ONE IN

WHICH TRADITIONAL ITALIAN STRENGTHS IN DESIGN AND

PRODUCTIVITY WOULD OUTWEIGH LOWER LABOR COSTS OFFSHORE.

FIAT\'S ACQUISITION BY GM, THEY FEAR, WILL LEAD TO A STEADY

DECLINE OF MANUFACTURING JOBS AS THE "GM COLOSSUS" SHIFTS

PRODUCTION TO PLANTS OUTSIDE ITALY.

8. (U) FOR THE GOVERNMENT, SUBSTANTIAL JOB LOSS IS THE

BRIGHT RED LINE; HOW EMPLOYMENT IS MAINTAINED IS LESS

IMPORTANT. CURRENTLY, THREE OPTIONS ARE UNDER PUBLIC

DISCUSSION:

-- RETOOLING THE PLANT AND PRODUCING NEW MODELS;

-- SELLING THE PLANT TO ANOTHER AUTO MANUFACTURER;

-- CONVERTING THE PLANT TO ANOTHER INDUSTRIAL USE (THE

SICILIAN REGIONAL PRESIDENT HAS SUGGESTED A CONTAINER

TERMINAL, THOUGH IT IS DIFFICULT TO SEE SUCH A FACILITY

EMPLOYING SIMILAR NUMBERS OF WORKERS).

9. (U) THE GOVERNMENT HAS SEVERAL TOOLS TO PROMOTE AND

ACHIEVE ANY OF THESE ALTERNATIVES. THE FIRST IS A SPECIAL

DEVELOPMENT FUND FOR SOUTHERN ITALY, WHICH THE GOVERNMENT

CAN DISPENSE TO OFFSET DEVELOPMENT COSTS (TO RETOOL THE

PLANT OR HELP FINANCE NEW MANUFACTURING ON THE SITE, FOR

EXAMPLE). IT MAY ALSO EMPLOY A VARIETY OF DIFFERENT ACCORDS

WHICH PROVIDE, WITH THE AGREEMENT OF LABOR, SPECIAL TERMS OF

EMPLOYMENT (FOR EXAMPLE, WAGE REDUCTIONS OR INCREASED SHIFT

WORK); IN ESSENCE THESE ACCORDS CARVE OUT EXCEPTIONS TO THE

NATIONAL LABOR CONTRACTS, OFFERING EMPLOYERS BETTER TERMS IN

EXCHANGE FOR CREATING JOBS IN HIGH-UNEMPLOYMENT AREAS.

10. (U) IT\'S UNCLEAR WHICH OF THESE REMEDIES, IF ANY, THE

GOVERNMENT WILL CHOOSE TO EMPLOY. THE MOST LIKELY SCENARIO

IS A PROMPT DECLARATION OF THE REQUIRED "STATE OF

BENEFITS IN EARLY DECEMBER (WHICH SHOULD ALLOW FIAT TO BEGIN

REALIZING THE COST SAVINGS IT NEEDS TO MEET ITS LENDORS\'

TERMS), FOLLOWED BY MORE LENGTHY NEGOTIATIONS TO DETERMINE

THE PLANT\'S FUTURE.

11. (SBU) COMMENT: WE SHARE ONE CONTACT\'S VIEW THAT THE

INTENSE FOCUS ON THE TERMINI IMERESE PLANTS SAYS A GREAT

DEAL ABOUT HOW ITALIANS VIEW -- OR CHOOSE NOT TO VIEW --

SOME OF THE UNCOMFORTABLE REALITIES OF THEIR CHANGING

ECONOMY AND LABOR MARKET. "THERE HAS BEEN INTENSE PRESS

COVERAGE OF WHAT AMOUNTS TO 6,000 JOBS, AND VIRTUALLY NONE

ON THE REGULARIZATION (UNDER WHICH 700,000 PREVIOUSLY

ILLEGAL IMMIGRANTS RECEIVED LEGAL STATUS)," HE NOTED.

PERHAPS THAT\'S BECAUSE MOST OF THE IMMIGRANTS ARE ALREADY

FIXTURES OF LOCAL ECONOMIES IN NORTHERN AND CENTRAL ITALY, A

PHENOMENON MANY HERE WOULD PREFER NOT TO ACKNOWLEDGE. MANY

NORTHERN ITALIANS APPEAR ALREADY TO HAVE ACCEPTED THAT A

DIFFERENT FIAT -- LEANER, PERHAPS MEANER AND NO LONGER

LOCALLY-OWNED -- WILL EMERGE FROM THIS RESTRUCTURING

PROCESS. THEIR SOUTHERN COMPATRIOTS, WITH FEWER ECONOMIC

OPTIONS, ARE MUCH MORE FOCUSED ON THE IMMEDIATE

CONSEQUENCES.
