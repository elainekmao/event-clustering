SUBJECT: MEET U.S. FOLLOW-UP: JORDAN

REF: USDOC 3592

1. (u) Embassy appreciates DOC feedback from the first MEET

U.S. conference. Regarding the proposed issue areas for

future MEET U.S. programs listed reftel, we believe it will

be easy to find a solid group of qualified applicants for

virtually any sector-specific program. Reftel suggestions

for education services, health technologies, irrigation and

water treatment technologies, airport and port management,

hotel management, and hospitality/tourism would all be

welcome programs in Jordan. We might expand the irrigation

proposal to include environmental technologies more broadly

(in keeping with the Environmental provisions of the

Jordan-U.S. FTA), and would add banking/financial services

and health sector services/hospital management to the list.

2. (u) Regarding format options, Embassy would be happy to

continue recruitment of participants for group programs. We

would also be willing to support an internship program as

outlined reftel. Support for either, as noted reftel, would

be dependent on available MEPI funding.

3. (u) Embassy looks forward to working with DOC on the

MEET U.S. program as it develops.

GNEHM
