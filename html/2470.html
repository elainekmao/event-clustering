SUBJECT: RUTELLI STILL "LEADER" OF ITALY,S CENTER-LEFT --

BUT THE COALITION CANNOT HIDE ITS DISARRAY

REF: 01 ROME 6496

CLASSIFIED BY: POLMILCOUNS GARY ROBBINS, REASONS 1.5 (B) AND (D)

1. (SBU) SUMMARY: AFTER PUBLIC SQUABBLING WHICH

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 ROME 00610 01 OF 02 071804Z

UNDERSCORED THE DISARRAY IN ITALY\'S CENTER LEFT, PRESIDENT OF

THE DAISY ALLIANCE FRANCESCO RUTELLI REMAINS LEADER OF THE

CENTER-LEFT OLIVE TREE COALITION, AT LEAST FOR NOW. THE

COALITION EXPOSED ITS UNEASY PARTNERSHIP, HOWEVER, BY

CREATING A BOARD COMPOSED OF ALL COALITION PARTY LEADERS TO

OVERSEE RUTELLI. END SUMMARY.

2. (U) ON JANUARY 30, THE OLIVE TREE DIRECTORATE HELD A

SIX-HOUR MEETING TO DISCUSS FRANCESCO RUTELLI\'S ROLE AS

LEADER OF THE CENTER-LEFT COALITION. RUTELLI HAD BEEN

PUBLICLY CRITICIZED BY HIS "ALLIES" FOLLOWING SELECTION OF

ITALY\'S REPRESENTATIVES TO THE EU CONVENTION. THE TWO

PARLIAMENTARY REPRESENTATIVES TO THE EU CONVENTION ARE NAMED

BY THE PRESIDENTS OF THE TWO CHAMBERS. CHAMBER OF DEPUTIES

PRESIDENT PIER FERDINANDO CASINI CHOSE CHRISTIAN DEMOCRATIC

CENTER-CHRISTIAN DEMOCRATIC UNION (CCD-CDU) DEPUTY MARCO

FOLLINI AND SENATE PRESIDENT MARCELLO PERA CHOSE ITALIAN

RENEWAL (RI, PART OF THE DAISY ALLIANCE) SENATOR LAMBERTO

DINI. DEMOCRATS OF THE LEFT (DS) PRESIDENT MASSIMO D\'ALEMA

WAS NOT SELECTED, AND DS MEMBERS ACCUSED RUTELLI OF

REPRESENTING HIS OWN DAISY ALLIANCE BY FAVORING DINI OVER

D\'ALEMA, RATHER THAN CONSULTING WITH THE OLIVE TREE COALITION

ON A COALITION REPRESENTATIVE. (ALTHOUGH THE TWO CHAMBER

PRESIDENTS CHOOSE THE DELEGATES AUTONOMOUSLY, THERE IS

USUALLY BACKSTAGE DISCUSSION ON PREFERENCES.)

3. (SBU) AT THE JANUARY 30 MEETING, RUTELLI WAS ALLOWED TO

REMAIN LEADER OF THE OPPOSITION UNTIL THE OLIVE TREE\'S

CONVENTION IN THE FALL. COALITION MEMBERS MADE CLEAR,

HOWEVER, THAT DURING THE DAISY CONGRESS SCHEDULED FOR APRIL,

RUTELLI MUST CHOOSE BETWEEN REMAINING THE TITULAR LEADER OF

CONFIDENTIAL

THE CENTER-LEFT OR PRESIDENT OF THE DAISY ALLIANCE. IT WAS

ALSO DECIDED THAT A DIRECTORATE COMPOSED OF COALITION PARTY

LEADERS WOULD REPLACE THE POSITION OF DEPUTY COALITION LEADER

HELD BY DS SECRETARY PIERO FASSINO -- A CLEAR INDICATION THAT

THE COALITION WANTED TO EXERT GREATER CONTROL OVER RUTELLI\'S

LEADERSHIP.

4. (U) SEVERAL DAYS LATER, ON FEBRUARY 2, THE OLIVE TREE

COALITION ORGANIZED A RALLY TO DENOUNCE THE JUSTICE POLICIES

OF THE BERLUSCONI GOVERNMENT. THE RALLY WAS MEANT TO

RELAUNCH THE COALITION FOLLOWING THE LATEST DS-DAISY CRISIS.

MOVIE DIRECTOR NANNI MORETTI (A FAMOUS DIRECTOR, LEFTIST

INTELLECTUAL, WITH CLOSE TIES TO THE DS), INVITED TO ADDRESS

THE RALLY, UNEXPECTEDLY ATTACKED OLIVE TREE LEADERS, FOCUSING

HIS IRE MAINLY ON HIS ONE-TIME DS FRIENDS. MORETTI CHARGED

THE GROUP WITH PUTTING TOGETHER A &USELESS GATHERING.8

ADDRESSING THE HAPLESS LEADERS, HE CONDEMNED THEM FOR DOING

NOTHING RIGHT AND FAILING UTTERLY AT SELF-REFLECTION AND

SELF-CRITICISM. &WITH SUCH LEADERS,8 MORETTI CONTINUED,

&WE WILL NEVER WIN. IT WILL TAKE TWO OR THREE GENERATIONS

TO BEAT BERLUSCONI.8 MORETTI\'S STINGING DENUNCIATIONS

RECEIVED WIDESPREAD MEDIA COVERAGE, RUBBING SALT IN THE

LEFT\'S (SELF-INFLICTED) WOUNDS. THE DS, SEEKING TO AVOID

FURTHER PUBLIC DISPLAY OF DIRTY LAUNDRY, PROPOSED THAT

MORETTI AND OTHER INTELLECTUALS COME TOGETHER TO GIVE

SUGGESTIONS FOR A MORE QUALIFIED AND EFFICACIOUS OPPOSITION.

A MEETING HAS BEEN SCHEDULED FOR FEBRUARY 22; THE DS\'S PIERO

FASSINO MET WITH MORETTI FEBRUARY 7 TO DISCUSS IT FURTHER.

5. (C) COMMENT: OVER THE PAST FEW MONTHS, THE CENTER-LEFT

COALITION HAS REGISTERED MANY INTERNAL -) BUT NOT SO QUIET

-- DISPUTES. RUTELLI WAS NAMED LEADER OF THE OLIVE TREE

COALITION AND FASSINO THE COALITION\'S DEPUTY STRICTLY FOR

CAMPAIGN PURPOSES. AFTER THE ELECTIONS, IT BECAME

INCREASINGLY CLEAR (AND INCREASINGLY PUBLIC) THAT RUTELLI\'S

LEADERSHIP WAS LITTLE BUT A USEFUL ELECTORAL FICTION; HE

PRESENTED HIMSELF AS LEADER OF THE OPPOSITION, AND EVEN

D\'ALEMA, IN A COUPLE OF INTERVIEWS, GRACIOUSLY CONCEDED HIM

THE TITLE. BUT EVIDENCE POINTED TO THE CONTRARY. DURING

PARLIAMENTARY DEBATES AND VOTES, RUTELLI THEORETICALLY SPOKE

FOR THE OLIVE TREE -- WHILE THE DS SPOKE FOR ITSELF. IN EVEN

THE SYMBOLIC, IF IMPORTANT, ACT OF EXPRESSING CONDOLENCES TO

OUR THEN-CHARGE AFTER SEPTEMBER 11, WE SAW EVIDENCE OF SHARP

ELBOWS BETWEEN RUTELLI AND D,ALEMA, WITH EACH EVENTUALLY

COMING TO THE EMBASSY SEPARATELY TO EXPRESS SYMPATHY AND

SUPPORT.

6. (C) COMMENT, CONTINUED: THE CENTER-LEFT KNOWS IT HAS A

PROBLEM ON ITS HANDS. SUGGESTING IT FACES POLITICAL

EXTINCTION IS CERTAINLY OVERSTATING THE POINT, BUT THE

COALITION AND ITS MEMBER PARTIES STAND LITTLE CHANCE OF BEING

MORE THAN A NATTERING CROWD ON THE EDGE OF ITALIAN POLITICS

UNLESS THEY CAN PULL TOGETHER. IDEOLOGICAL DIFFERENCES

BETWEEN THE GROUPS MAKE IT UNLIKELY THAT THEY WILL FIND THAT
