SUBJECT: NETANYAHU IN ROME: SUCCESSFUL FIRST TRIP AS FM

REF: TEL AVIV 7148

CLASSIFIED BY: POL MC THOMAS COUNTRYMAN FOR REASONS 1.5 (B) AND (D)

1. (C) SUMMARY: ROME WAS ISRAEL FM NETANYAHU\'S FIRST STOP IN

HIS FIRST EUROPEAN TOUR SINCE HE ASSUMED THE FUNCTIONS OF FM.

BOTH SIDES PLACED EMPHASIS ON THE GREATER BALANCE IN ITALY\'S

APPROACH TO THE MIDDLE EAST SINCE BERLUSCONI TOOK OFFICE,

THOUGH THE ITALIANS MADE CLEAR TO NETANYAHU THAT THEY WILL

NOT SEVER ALL CONTACT WITH ARAFAT. NETANYAHU BELIEVES THAT

THE EU\'S PUBLIC STATEMENTS ON THE CONFLICT WILL BE MORE

EVENHANDED DURING ITALY\'S EU PRESIDENCY THE SECOND HALF OF

2003, THOUGH HE HAD NO ENTHUSIASM FOR THE EU ROLE WITHIN THE

QUARTET. SENIOR ITALIAN OFFICIALS REITERATED THEIR BELIEF IN

THE ROLE OF THE QUARTET AND THEIR COMMITMENT TO THE "ROAD

MAP" LEADING TO THE CREATION OF A PALESTINIAN STATE EXISTING

IN PEACE AND SECURITY NEXT TO THE STATE OF ISRAEL. END

SUMMARY.

2. (U) NETANYAHU HAD A BUSY TWO-DAY VISIT TO ROME DEC 18-19

WHERE HE MET WITH PM BERLUSCONI, FM FRATTINI, MOD MARTINO,

DEPUTY PM FINI, PRESIDENT OF THE SENATE PERA, AND PRESIDENT

OF THE HOUSE CASINI. AMBASSADOR SEMBLER HOSTED A DINNER FOR

NETANYAHU, A LONG TIME ACQUAINTANCE, DEC 18. AT ALL THESE

EVENTS, AND IN HIS PUBLIC COMMENTS, NETANYAHU\'S CORE TALKING

POINTS TRACKED CLOSELY WITH HIS RECENT CAMPAIGN SPEECHES AND

WITH THE PRESENTATION HE GAVE TO CODEL PRICE/DAVIS (REFTEL):

THE ISSUE IS NOT ISRAEL\'S OCCUPATION, BUT THE WORLDWIDE

ISLAMIC TERRORISM; ISRAEL HAS BEEN TOO RESTRAINED IN NOT

EXILING ARAFAT; PALESTINIANS CAN HAVE SELF-GOVERNMENT, BUT

NEVER A STATE, ETC.

3. (C) OUR ITALIAN AND ISRAELI SOURCES TOLD US THAT LITTLE

NEW INFORMATION WAS DISCUSSED IN THE MEETINGS WHICH THEY

DESCRIBED NONETHELESS AS "USEFUL" FOR BOTH SIDES. LUCA

FERRARI, THE MFA\'S OFFICE DIRECTOR FOR ISRAEL AND PALESTINIAN

TERRITORIES WHO ATTENDED THE MEETING WITH FM FRATTINI,

DESCRIBED NETANYAHU\'S SPEECH AS "A LECTURE AT THE AMERICAN

ENTREPRISE INSTITUTE," FULL OF HIS USUAL "CAMPAIGN PITCH" AND

A CLEARLY STATED POSITION THAT ISRAEL\'S PROBLEM IS PART OF A

WORLDWIDE MUSLIM TERRORISM PROBLEM.

4. (C) NETANYAHU TOLD AMB. SEMBLER THAT ISRAEL DEEPLY

APPRECIATED THE DRAMATIC CHANGE IN ITALY\'S RELATIONS WITH

ISRAEL SINCE BERLUSCONI TOOK OFFICE, AND THE MAJOR REDUCTION

IN ARAFAT\'S STANDING WITH ITALY IN THE LAST EIGHT MONTHS. TO

ALL HIS ITALIAN INTERLOCUTORS, NETANYAHU SAID THAT, IN THE

MIDDLE EAST, ISRAEL IS THOUGHT OF AS THE "SMALL SATAN", THE

U.S. BEING THE "BIG SATAN." NOW, ADDED NETANYAHU, ITALY IS

BECOMING THE "MEDIUM SATAN." FERRARI SAID THE NEXT DAY THAT

THIS REMARK OF NETANYAHU\'S HAD HIT THE RIGHT CHORD WITH

ITALIAN OFFICIALS, WHO ARE AWARE OF THE RISKS BEING RUN BY

ITALY\'S ACTIVITIES TO DISRUPT TERRORIST OPERATIONS. FERRARI

ADDED THAT NETANYAHU TOLD FRATTINI THAT ITALY IS ONE OF THE

FEW RELIABLE PARTNERS ISRAEL HAS WITHIN THE EU. NETANYAHU

WAS PARTICULARLY IMPRESSED WITH MOD MARTINO FOR HIS PROFOUND

KNOWLEDGE OF THE WHOLE WORLD AND HIS COMMITMENT TO ACTIVELY

COMBAT TERRORISM.

5. (C) THE ISRAELIS ARE CONFIDENT THAT THE ITALIAN PRESIDENCY

OF THE EU, DURING THE SECOND HALF OF 2003, WILL BE FAR MORE

BALANCED THAN THE CURRENT DANISH PRESIDENCY. IN FACT, EHUD

GOL, THE AMBASSADOR OF ISRAEL IN ROME, HAS BEEN LOBBYING THE

MFA AND WRITING OP-EDS AFTER EVERY SINGLE DANISH STATEMENT ON

THE MIDDLE EAST. FERRARI HOWEVER DID NOT HIDE HIS ANGER AT

THE "UTTERLY NONPROFESSIONAL" BEHAVIOR OF THE ISRAELI EMBASSY

EARLIER THIS MONTH WHEN IT QUOTED VERBATIM BERLUSCONI\'S

STATEMENT, IN A PRIVATE MEETING WITH ISRAELI PRESIDENT KATSAV

DEC 11, THAT THE CHIGI (ITALY\'S PRIME MINISTERIAL PALACE) HAD

NUMEROUS NOT ONLY IN THE OPPOSITION, BUT WITHIN BERLUSCONI\'S

THAT THE ISRAELIS NEVER EVEN APOLOGIZED FOR THE GAFFE.
