1. (U) As per instructions REFTEL, we passed along to the

Ministry of Information and Communications Technology (MOICT)

our intention to nominate James Carroll for reelection to the

Radio Regulations Board. MOICT Adviser Roger Guichard told

us that Minister of ICT Fawaz Zu\'bi and MOICT Adviser Afran

Jamil Hennin will attend this year\'s conference in Marrakech.

Guichard also told us that Zu\'bi and Hennin would be "happy

and eager to cooperate" with the U.S. at the conference and

would like to meet with the U.S. delegation to further

discuss such cooperation.
