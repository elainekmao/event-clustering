ITALIAN

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION ON THIS SPECIFIC

EXPORT, HE PASSED OUR REQUEST TO THE JOINT

CHIEF OF STAFF DEFENSE OFFICE, THE COMPETENT

ITALIAN AUTHORITY, FOR A FORMAL REPLY.

EMBASSY OFFICERS HAVE ATTEMPTED TO CONTACT

ATP A NUMBER OF TIMES, VIA PHONE AND FAX, BUT

HAVE BEEN UNABLE TO REACH THE COMPANY.

EMBASSY ALSO SENT A NOTE DIRECTLY TO THE

ITALIAN JOINT CHIEF OF STAFF DEFENSE OFFICE
