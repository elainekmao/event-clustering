SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION: WAR ON IRAQ, DEMOCRACY IN THE ARAB

WORLD, MEPI, AND ISRAEL

1. SUMMARY: DEMOCRACY IN THE ARAB WORLD, THE MIDDLE EAST

PARTNERSHIP INITIATIVE (MEPI), AND THE ANTICIPATED WAR ON IRAQ

WERE THE PRIMARY FOCUS OF UAE AND REGIONAL MEDIA. UNDER THE PEN

NAME OF "IBN AL DEIRA," A COLUMNIST IN "AL KHALEEJ" HOPES THAT

THE U.S. DIPLOMACY WILL RECOVER ITS \'SPIRIT,\' AND THE VOICE OF

WISDOM WILL BE HEARD AGAIN. IN "AL ITTIHAD," COLUMNIST DR.

SHAMLAN AL EISA DEMANDS THAT GCC STATES ADOPT A CLEAR AND PUBLIC

POLICY TOWARDS THE U.S. "AL BAYAN" URGES ARABS TO UTILIZE THE

INCREASING AMERICAN OPPOSITION TO THE WAR ON IRAQ, AND SAY \'NO\'

TO WHAT HE DESCRIBES AS ANOTHER STUPID WAR. AT THE SAME TIME, IN

"AL ITTIHAD," UAE INTELLECTUAL DR. ANWAR GHARGHASH BELIEVES MEPI

WILL BE LOST AMONG THE HATRED FOR UNLIMITED AMERICAN SUPPORT TO

ISRAEL, WASHINGTON\'S IMPATIENCE WITH THE REGION, AND THE CULTURAL

CHALLENGES OF THE ARAB WORLD. ALSO IN "AL ITTIHAD," UAE

COLUMNIST ABDULLAH RASHEED MOCKS THE REACTION OF ARABS TO

ISRAEL\'S BAN OF TWO ARAB KNESSET MEMBERS FROM PARTICIPATING IN

THE UPCOMING ELECTIONS (NOTE: OBVIOUSLY WRITTEN PRIOR TO THE

ISRAELI COURT\'S OVERTURNING OF THIS DECISION, END NOTE.). END

SUMMARY.

2. COMMENTING ON THE MEETING OF ASSISTANT SECRETARY OF STATE

RICHARD HAASS WITH THE CROWN PRINCE OF DUBAI, A COLUMNIST USING

THE NAME "IBN AL DEIRA" WROTE IN SHARJAH-BASED PAN-ARAB "AL

KHALEEJ ON 1/12:

"... THE MAJOR PROBLEM WITH AMERICAN DIPLOMACY PRESENTLY IS THAT

IT PERMITS MILITARY AND SECURITY SOLUTIONS THAT DO NOT UNDERSTAND

THE PSYCHOLOGY OF THE REGION AND THAT TREAT ARABS AS AN USELESS

QUANTITY LACKING CIVILIZATION. WE HOPE THAT U.S. DIPLOMACY WILL

RECOVER ITS \'SPIRIT\', THE ONE WHICH INSPIRES US AND TO WHICH WE

WANT TO LISTEN. WE HOPE THAT THE VOICE OF WISDOM WILL RISE

BEFORE IT IS LOST TO IMPERIALISM AND A NEW MCCARTHYISM."

3. CRITICIZING WHAT HE DESCRIBES AS THE DOUBLE STANDARDS OF GCC

COUNTRIES, COLUMNIST DR. SHAMLAN YOUSUF AL EISA WROTE IN ABU

DHABI-BASED SEMI-OFFICIAL "AL ITTIHAD" ON 1/12:

"... THE PUBLIC POSITIONS OF THE GCC STATES DEMONSTRATE THAT THEY

ARE AGAINST THE WAR ON IRAQ AND ANY REGIME CHANCE BY FORCE. SOME

GCC OFFICIALS EVEN DECLARED A WEEK AGO OPPOSITION TO THE USE OF

THEIR BASES TO ATTACK IRAQ. CONVERSELY, IN PRACTICE, GCC STATES

HAVE AMERICAN BASES AND FACILITIES USED BY THE U.S. WHENEVER IT

WANTS ... THIS UNCLEAR AND VAGUE STANCE TOWARDS THE WEST IS NOT

NEW AND HAS BEEN THE PRACTICE FOR DECADES. IT IS ACCEPTED BY THE

U.S., THE WEST, AND GCC STATES ALIKE. THEREFORE, WE DEMAND THAT

THE GCC STATES ADOPT A CLEAR, FRANK, AND PUBLIC POLICY TOWARDS

THE U.S. MOREOVER, THEY MUST ANNOUNCE THEIR CLEAR SUPPORT TO THE

U.S. BECAUSE THEIR INTERESTS REQUIRE IT. THE INTERNAL PROBLEMS

ARE MERELY THE RESPONSIBILITY OF THE GOVERNMENTS, SINCE THEY

CREATE THEM THROUGH THEIR OWN ACTIONS."

4. UNDER THE HEADLINE "NO -- TO ANOTHER STUPID WAR", DUBAI-BASED

BUSINESS-ORIENTED "AL BAYAN" EDITORIALIZED ON 1/13:

"... ARABS SHOULD UTILIZE THE FLOW OF PEACE ADVOCATES AND

VOLUNTEERS COMING FROM EUROPE AND THE U.S. TO FORM A HUMAN SHIELD

PROTECTING IRAQI CITIES FROM ANY AMERICAN ATTACK. ARABS MUST

TAKE A COURAGEOUS, UNITED, AND UNANIMOUS STANCE THAT SAYS \'NO\' TO

ANOTHER STUPID WAR, THE PRICE OF WHICH, IN THE END, WILL BE PAID

BY THE IRAQI PEOPLE ALONE. THE IRAQI PEOPLE HAVE BEEN SUFFERING

FOR THE LAST 12 YEARS UNDER UNFAIR INTERNATIONAL SANCTIONS."

5. UNDER THE HEADLINE "WASHINGTON CAN NOT LEAD THE CHANGE TO

DEMOCRACY", UAE COLUMNIST DR. ANWAR MOHAMMED GHARGASH COMMENTED

ON MEPI IN ABU DHABI-BASED SEMI-OFFICIAL "AL ITTIHAD" ON 1/13:

"THE U.S. CAN NOT LEAD THE CHANGE TO DEMOCRACY IN THE REGION.

MOREOVER, SECRETARY OF STATE COLIN POWELL\'S INITIATIVE WILL NOT

SUCCEED IN CHANGING THE MIDDLE EAST TO AN OASIS OF DIVERSITY RUN

BY ELECTIONS TO PEACEFUL TRANSITION OF POWER ... IN THIS CONTEXT,

I MUST MENTION THE THREE MAJOR REASONS THAT PREVENT WASHINGTON

FROM LEADING THIS PROCESS. THE FIRST ONE IS ITS INFINITE SUPPORT

TO SHARON AND HIS SUPPRESSION (OF THE PALESTINIANS), WHICH

RESULTS IN THE LOSS OF ITS CREDIBILITY AND ANY (PERCEPTION OF)

NEUTRALITY IN THE REGION ..."

"SECOND, ON THE OTHER HAND, WE FIND THAT THE DEMOCRATIC CHANGE IS

A COMPLETE PROCESS, WHICH REQUIRES MANY YEARS AND SIGNIFICANT

EFFORT. WASHINGTON IS NOT PATIENT ENOUGH TO LEAD THE CHANGE

TALKED ABOUT BY U.S. THEORISTS ... OBSERVERS OF U.S. FOREIGN

POLICY BASICALLY REALIZE THAT THIS POLICY IS CRISIS MANAGEMENT.

THUS, WASHINGTON HASTILY LOSES CONCENTRATION AND ENTHUSIASM. THE

U.S. WILL DISCOVER THAT THE PRIORITIES OF ITS ACTIVITIES ARE

DISORGANIZED TO DEAL WITH THIS NEW URGENT CRISIS ..."

"THE THIRD REASON IS ... WASHINGTON\'S PROJECTS WILL FACE THE

SITUATION ON THE GROUND THAT WARNS US OF OUR HISTORICAL

EXPERIENCE, WHICH WILL MAKE A CHANGE TO DEMOCRACY DIFFICULT AND

FULL OF INTERNAL PROBLEMS. OUR CULTURE IS PARAMOUNT (BUT HAS) A

LONG HISTORY OF SUPPRESSION AND ORGANIZED VIOLENCE ... HOWEVER,

THIS DOES NOT WIPE OUT THE IMPORTANCE OF SUCH A CHANGE AND THE

NECESSITY FOR ITS IMMEDIATE START IN THE REGION, AND THROUGH A

CLEAR BELIEF THAT WITHOUT THIS CHANGE, THE FUTURE WILL BE

BLURRED."

6. COMMENTING ON ARAB REACTION TO ISRAEL\'S BAN OF ARAB KNESSET

MEMBERS AZMI BISHARA AND AHMED AL TIBI FROM PARTICIPATION IN THE

UPCOMING ELECTIONS (PRIOR TO THE COURT DECISION OVERTURNING THIS

MOVE), UAE COLUMNIST ABDULLAH RASHEED WROTE IN "AL ITTIHAD" ON

1/13:

"300 MILLION ARABS EXPRESS ANGER BECAUSE ISRAEL BANNED AL TIBI

AND BISHARA FROM PARTICIPATING IN THE KNESSET ELECTIONS ON

JANUARY 28 ... SOME ARABS DO NOT KNOW THE MEANING OF DEMOCRACY,

SOME HAVE NEVER EVER HEARD OF THIS WORD .... AND THE MAJORITY IS

CERTAIN THAT ANY ANIMAL IN THE WEST HAS MORE HUMAN RIGHTS THAN

300 MILLION ARABS EVEN DARE SEEK. SOME ARABS EVEN CONSIDER TALK

OF DEMOCRACY AS A SUMMARY OF ALL THE HORRIFYING THINGS PROHIBITED

IN THEIR WORLD. IN SOME ARAB COUNTRIES TALKING ABOUT DEMOCRACY

IS SIMILAR TO POSSESSING OPIUM OR OTHER TYPES OF DRUGS, WHERE AS

IN OTHER SOCIETIES THE PENALTY FOR TALKING ABOUT DEMOCRACY IS

MORE THAN PROSTITUTION OR POSSESSING HASHISH OR ANY KIND OF DRUGS

AND HALLUCINATION PILLS. NEVERTHELESS, WE BLAME ISRAEL FOR NOT

GIVING DEMOCRACY TO AL TIBI AND BISHARA. WE SHOULD BE ASHAMED."
