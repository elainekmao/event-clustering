SUBJECT: ZIMBABWE LABOR FEDERATION LEADER SEEKS TO SEVER

LABOR\'S TIES TO THE OPPOSITION MDC

REF: 00 HARARE 3680

CLASSIFIED BY CHARGE D\' AFFAIRES EARL M. IRVING FOR

REASONS 1.5 (B/D).

1. (C) SUMMARY: THE ZIMBABWE CONGRESS OF TRADE UNIONS

(ZCTU) WILL HOLD ITS FIVE-YEAR CONGRESS IN FEBRUARY.

THE CONGRESS WILL FOCUS ON ADMINISTRATIVE ISSUES, AND

ECONOMIC AND SOCIAL TOPICS. THE ISSUE OF ZCTU TIES TO

THE OPPOSITION MOVEMENT FOR DEMOCRATIC CHANGE (MDC) WILL

NOT BE A FORMAL AGENDA ITEM, BUT WILL BE INDIRECTLY

RAISED VIA THE PROPOSAL THAT LEADERS OF POLITICAL

PARTIES CANNOT ALSO BE MEMBERS OF THE ZCTU EXECUTIVE

COUNCIL. WE BELIEVE THAT IF THE ACTING SECRETARY-

GENERAL OF THE ZCTU, WHO IS WIDELY CONSIDERED TO BE PRO-

ZANU-PF, IS ELECTED AS SECRETARY-GENERAL, HE WILL SEEK

TO SEVER ALL TIES BETWEEN THE ZCTU AND THE MDC. END

SUMMARY.

2. (U) ON JANUARY 10, LABOFF MET WITH THE ACTING

SECRETARY-GENERAL OF THE ZCTU, ISIDORE ZINDOGA, TO

SIPDIS

DISCUSS THE UPCOMING CONGRESS TO BE HELD FEBRUARY 23-25

IN MASVINGO. THE ZCTU IS AN UMBRELLA LABOR ORGANIZATION

COMPRISING 31 AFFILIATED UNIONS. THE OPPOSITION MDC HAD

ITS ROOTS IN THE ZCTU, AND MUCH OF THE MDC LEADERSHIP

STILL FORMALLY HOLDS POSITIONS IN THE ZCTU.

3. (SBU) LABOFF DURING THE MEETING QUERIED ZINDOGA ON

CONFIDENTIAL

THE AGENDA OF THE CONGRESS, AND SPECIFICALLY IF THE

CONGRESS WOULD DISCUSS THE RELATIONSHIP BETWEEN THE ZCTU

AND THE MDC. ZINDOGA STATED THAT THE CONGRESS WAS GOING

TO DEAL PRINCIPALLY WITH UNION ADMINISTRATIVE ISSUES, AS

WELL AS ECONOMIC AND SOCIAL POLICY ISSUES. HE TOLD US

THERE WILL BE FOUR PRINCIPAL AGENDA ITEMS:

A) ELECTING THE EXECUTIVE AND GENERAL COUNCILS OF THE

ZCTU FOR THE NEXT FIVE-YEAR PERIOD. THE EXECUTIVE

COUNCIL CONSISTS OF A SECRETARY-GENERAL, TWO ASSISTANT

SECRETARY-GENERALS, FOUR PRESIDENTS, AND A TREASURER.

SIPDIS

THE GENERAL COUNCIL INCLUDES THE EXECUTIVE COUNCIL AND

TEN OTHER MEMBERS FROM INDIVIDUAL UNIONS. THE GENERAL

COUNCIL IS CONVENED IN LIEU OF A CONGRESS WHENEVER THERE

ARE ACTIONS TO BE VOTED ON THAT THE EXECUTIVE COUNCIL

PROPOSES TAKING;

B) REVIEWING THE ZCTU\'S FINANCIAL BASE WITH THE GOAL OF

ENSURING THAT THE ORGANIZATION WILL HAVE A SOUND

FINANCIAL FOOTING FOR THE NEXT FIVE YEARS;

C) DETERMINING THE ZCTU\'S POSITIONS ON ECONOMIC AND

SOCIAL POLICIES, IN ORDER TO PROTECT AND FURTHER WORKERS

INTERESTS;

D) FINDING WAYS TO STRENGTHEN THE ZCTU AND ITS

AFFILIATED UNIONS IN THE CONTEXT OF ZIMBABWE\'S SEVERE

ECONOMIC DECLINE.

4. (SBU) ZINDOGA STATED THAT ONLY UNDER THE LAST AGENDA

ITEM WOULD THE ZCTU\'S RELATIONSHIP WITH THE MDC COME UP.

HE WAS EMPHATIC THAT THERE IS NO LINKAGE BETWEEN THE

ZCTU AND THE MDC, DESPITE THE CENTRAL ROLE THAT THE ZCTU

HAD IN THE FORMATION OF THE MDC. ACCORDING TO ZINDOGA,

A GROUP OF CIVIL SOCIETY ORGANIZATIONS WAS RESPONSIBLE

FOR THE FORMATION OF THE MDC. HE STATED THAT SOME

MEMBERS OF THE ZCTU, INDEPENDENT OF THEIR AFFILIATION

WITH THE ORGANIZATION, BECAME HEAVILY INVOLVED IN THE

MDC. ZINDOGA ADDED THAT THE CONGRESS WILL ADDRESS THIS

PERCEPTION OF A ZCTU CONNECTION TO THE MDC BY DISCUSSING

HOW THE ZCTU CAN BEST STRENGTHEN ITSELF AS AN

INDEPENDENT LABOR ORGANIZATION. ZINDOGA\'S OWN VIEW IS

THAT UNION MEMBERS SUPPORT DIFFERENT POLITICAL PARTIES,

THEREFORE, THE ZCTU MUST KEEP ITSELF ABOVE THE POLITICAL

FRAY BY REMAINING INDEPENDENT OF ANY LINKAGE, OR

PERCEPTION OF A LINKAGE, WITH ANY SPECIFIC POLITICAL

PARTY.

5. (SBU) ZINDOGA STATED WHEN THE CONGRESS CONSIDERS THE

ELECTION OF THE NEXT EXECUTIVE COUNCIL, IT WILL ALSO

ADDRESS WHETHER A POLITICAL PARTY LEADER OR EXECUTIVE

CAN BE A MEMBER OF THE EXECUTIVE COUNCIL AT THE SAME

TIME. HIS OPINION IS THAT THE CONGRESS SHOULD DISALLOW

THIS FOR TWO REASONS: THE ZCTU WISHES TO REMAIN AN

ORGANIZATION INDEPENDENT OF POLITICAL PARTIES AND THEIR

INFLUENCE, AND BEING A MEMBER OF THE EXECUTIVE COUNCIL

IS A FULL-TIME RESPONSIBILITY THAT REQUIRES MEMBERS TO

REFTEL), HE PRESENTS A CONVINCING IMAGE AS A CONCERNED

TRADE UNIONIST AND DOES NOT COME ACROSS AS A RULING

PARTY HACK. HOWEVER, ZINDOGA\'S INSISTENCE THAT THERE IS

NO LINKAGE OF ANY KIND BETWEEN ZCTU AND THE MDC IS

HARDLY TENABLE, AS THE OPPOSITION PARTY DEFINITELY HAD

7. (C) COMMENT CONTINUED: THE CONGRESS AGENDA ITEM

STATING THAT MEMBERS OF THE ZCTU EXECUTIVE COUNCIL

CANNOT BE IN LEADERSHIP POSITIONS OF POLITICAL PARTIES

IS CLEARLY AIMED AT REMOVING MDC LEADERS SUCH AS MORGAN

TSVANGIRAI (PRESIDENT OF THE MDC WHO IS ON PAID LEAVE AS

SIPDIS

THE ZCTU SECRETARY-GENERAL) AND ISAAC MATONGO (NATIONAL

CHAIRMAN OF THE MDC AND AN ACTING PRESIDENT OF THE ZCTU)

FROM THE ZCTU LEADERSHIP. WE STRONGLY SUSPECT THAT

ZINDOGA MAY BE DOING THE BIDDING OF ZANU-PF IN HIS

CURRENT POSITION, WHICH WOULD BE GLAD TO SEE THE TIES

BETWEEN THE ZCTU AND THE MDC SEVERED. IF ZINDOGA IS

ELECTED SECRETARY-GENERAL AND SUCCEEDS AT SEVERING THE

TIES, IT WOULD BE A SEVERE BLOW TO THE MDC\'S

ORGANIZATIONAL STRENGTH, AND ITS ABILITY TO MOUNT A

NATIONAL CAMPAIGN IN THE 2002 PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION.

END COMMENT.
