SUBJECT: STATUS OF JORDAN\'S ACCESSION TO UN CT CONVENTIONS

REF: SECSTATE 125639

1. Poloff delivered Ref points to Aiyman Aamri, Acting

Director of the International Organizations section of the

Foreign Ministry, on June 30. Poloff explained that the U.S.

has now ratified all twelve UN CT Conventions and pressed

Aamri on the GOJ\'s progress in ratifying the four remaining

UN CT Conventions that Jordan is not a party to. Aamri said

that Jordan has signed but not ratified three of these

Conventions: the Suppression of Terrorist Financing, the

Suppression of Unlawful Acts against Maritime Navigation and

the subsequent Protocol on Acts against the Safety of Fixed

Platforms, and the 1979 Convention on the Physical Protection

of Nuclear Materials. Aamri told Poloff that the GOJ

continues to study the Convention on the Suppression of

Terrorist Bombing and added that the Convention was not

presently open to signing.
