1. THIS MESSAGE HAS BEEN CLEARED BY AND INCLUDES

CONTRIBUTIONS FROM CONSULATES FLORENCE, MILAN, AND NAPLES.

2. SUMMARY: AS REPORTED REF A, THE GOI HAS ONLY RECENTLY

STARTED TO COORDINATE GOVERNMENT-WIDE ITS PREPARATION FOR

THE WSSD NEXT SEPTEMBER. HOWEVER, ACCORDING TO MFA, ITALY

WILL WORK HARD TO ACHIEVE CONSENSUS WITHIN THE EU ON A

POSITION WHICH WILL NOT LEAD TO FRACTURES WITH THE U.S.

POSITION. POST OBJECTIVES: 1) SUPPORTING ITALIAN LOCAL AND

REGIONAL EFFORTS ON SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT BY IVP\'S, PARKS

COOPERATION, ROUNDTABLES, AND PRESENTATIONS. 2)

ENCOURAGING GOI TO SUPPORT U.S. OBJECTIVES SUCH AS GOOD

GOVERNANCE, PUBLIC-PRIVATE PARTICIPATION, BIOTECHNOLOGY IN

SUSTAINABLE AGRICULTURE, AND CLIMATE FRIENDLY TECHNOLOGIES.

SUPPORTING U.S. OBJECTIVES FOR THE MONTERREY CONFERENCE ON

FINANCING FOR DEVELOPMENT IS PART OF MISSION\'S WSSD

STRATEGY. THIS CABLE DESCRIBES POST ACTION PLAN. END

SUMMARY.
