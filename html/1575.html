SUBJECT: FOREIGN MINISTER TAURAN\'S VISIT TO IRAN

1. (U) CLASSIFIED BY CDA JOSEPH MERANTE FOR REASONS 1.5

(A) AND (D) .

2. (C) CHARGE CALLED ON VATICAN DIRECTOR OF MIDDLE

EASTERN AFFAIRS MONS. GIANNI D\'ANIELLO ON MARCH 12 TO

DISCUSS FOREIGN MINISTER TAURAN\'S MARCH 3-8 TRIP TO IRAN.

D\'ANIELLO, WHO ACCOMPANIED THE FOREIGN MINISTER,

CATEGORIZED THE VISIT AS "HIGHLY SUCCESSFUL". HE SAID

THE MAIN PURPOSE OF THE VISIT WAS TO LOBBY FOR INCREASED

RELIGIOUS FREEDOM FOR CATHOLICS, AS WELL AS FOR OTHER

CHRISTIANS IN IRAN. ACCORDING TO D\'ANIELLO, THE LARGEST

GROUPS IN COMMUNION WITH ROME ARE THE CHALDEANS, SOME

MELKITES, AND EUROPEANS WHO WOULD BE MEMBERS OF THE LATIN

CHURCH. TWO OTHER MAIN BODIES OF CHRISTIANS ON WHOSE

BEHALF THE VATICAN WANTED TO SPEAK ARE THE ASSYRIAN

CHURCH OF THE EAST, AND THE SYRIAN ORTHODOX CHURCH.

D\'ANIELLO SAID THAT IN GENERAL, RELIGIOUS FREEDOM FOR

THESE CHRISTIAN GROUPS WAS NOT GREATLY CURTAILED, AND THE

IRANIAN GOVERNMENT PROMISED EVEN GREATER LATITUDE. HE

SAID THAT HE AND TAURAN FOUND STATEMENTS BY GOVERNMENT

OFFICIALS INDICATIVE OF AN EVOLVING OPENNESS ON THE PART

OF IRAN, BUT ADDED THAT THE VATICAN IS STILL UNSURE IF WE

CAN EXPECT CONTINUED OPENNESS OR A RETURN TO MORE

REACTIONARY POLICIES.

3. (C) D\'ANIELLO SAID THAT TAURAN RAISED THE SITUATION OF

THE IRANIAN JEWS WHO WERE SENTENCED FOR SPYING FOR ISRAEL

DIRECTLY IN A PRIVATE CONVERSATION WITH PRESIDENT

KHATAMI. HE SAID THE PRESIDENT WAS NONCOMMITTAL.

HOWEVER, ON THE ISSUE OF THE MIDDLE EAST PEACE PROCESS,

D\'ANIELLO SAID THAT IRAN AGREES WITH THE VATICAN, THAT

THE VIOLENCE MUST END AND THAT THE TWO PARTIES RESUME

NEGOTIATIONS. D\'ANIELLO USED THIS OPPORTUNITY TO SAY

THAT HE "WASN\'T SPEAKING OFFICIALLY" BUT THAT THE VATICAN

IS HAVING INCREASED CONCERNS THAT IN AN ULTIMATE

AGREEMENT BETWEEN THE TWO SIDES, THE CHRISTIANS OF

JERUSALEM WILL BE "LEFT OUT". HE ASKED THAT WE PAY

ATTENTION TO THE NEEDS OF ALL THREE RELIGIOUS GROUPS IN

ANY DISCUSSIONS IN WHICH THE U.S MIGHT TAKE PART. (NOTE:

THIS DIM VIEW OF THE MEPP WAS ALSO REFLECTED IN A 3/11

DINNER CONVERSATION ACTING DCM HAD WITH ISRAELI EMBASSY

DCM. THE ISRAELI DCM RELATED THAT THE VATICAN HAD

REBUFFED THE ISRAELI EMBASSY\'S ATTEMPTS TO SECURE A

LOCATION IN VATICAN CITY TO HOLD AN EVENT COMMEMORATING

THE LST ANNIVERSARY OF THE POPE\'S VISIT, SAYING THAT THE

HOLY SEE DID NOT "VIEW THAT VISIT AS A SUCCESS.")

4. (C) CHARGE CALLED AUSTRIAN DCM LATER IN THE DAY TO GET

READOUT OF FM TAURAN\'S MORNING BRIEFING ON THE TRIP TO EU

EMBASSIES. ACCORDING TO AUSTRIAN DCM, TAURAN SAID HE

RAISED 1) IMPORTATION OF BIBLES; 2) THE NEED FOR A

JUDICIAL STATUS FOR THE CATHOLIC CHURCH IN IRAN; 3)

RETURN OF SOME PROPERTIES CONFISCATED AFTER THE 1979

REVOLUTION. HE ALSO TOLD THE EU AMBASSADORS THAT KHATAMI

SAID THE SENTENCES OF THE IRANIAN JEWS WERE "FINAL", BUT

"EARLY PARDON" SHOULD NOT BE RULED OUT.

5. (C) D\'ANIELLO ALSO USED THE VISIT TO PRESENT A NOTE

VERBALE ON THE CASE OF ZIVKOVICH AND ALPERIN VS. THE

IOR.(SEPTEL TO FOLLOW, NOTE FAXED TO EUR/WE, L/DL 3/12).

6. (C) COMMENT: A MAJOR FOREIGN POLICY GOAL OF THE

VATICAN IS PROTECTION OF THE CHRISTIANS IN ISLAMIC

COUNTRIES. THUS, IN THOSE ISLAMIC COUNTRIES, SUCH AS

IRAN AND IRAQ, IN WHICH THE VATICAN BELIEVES THE STATUS

OF CHRISTIANS IS NOT GREATLY COMPROMISED, WE SHOULD

EXPECT VATICAN ENGAGEMENT AND SUPPORT. MERANTE
