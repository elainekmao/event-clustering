SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED ON THREE INSTRUMENTS RELATED TO

THE PROTECTION OF CHILDREN

REF: STATE 126276

Classified by A/Polcouns Philip Kaplan for reason 1.5 (b) and

(d).

1. (C) We delivered reftel demarche May 15 to Gokcen Kaya,

MFA Department Head for the United Nations. Kaya noted the

Optional Protocol to the Convention on the Rights of the

Child on the Involvement of Children in Armed Conflict is on

the Turkish Parliament\'s agenda for ratification during this

session. MFA will advise when Parliament ratifies. Turkey

has already ratified ILO Convention No. 182 and the Protocol

on the Sale of Children, Child Prostitution and Child

Pornography.

2. (C) In response to Kaya\'s query whether the U.S. would

ratify the Convention on the Rights of the Child, we

explained that, unlike in other countries, U.S. state and

local jurisdictions have primary authority over this issue

and that U.S. law and custom already afford children

protection.
