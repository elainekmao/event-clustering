1.(U) SENATOR, I WOULD LIKE TO CONGRATULATE YOU ON YOUR

APPOINTMENT AS THE PRESIDENT\'S SPECIAL ENVOY FOR PEACE IN

SUDAN. THE DIFFICULT SITUATION IN THAT COUNTRY IS ONE OF

THE MOST PRESSING ISSUES FOR THE VATICAN ON THE AFRICAN

CONTINENT.

CONFIDENTIAL

2.(SBU) BECAUSE OF THE VATICAN\'S DEEP INTEREST IN THE

CONFLICT IN SUDAN, I WOULD LIKE TO INVITE YOU TO PAY A

VISIT DURING YOUR UPCOMING TRAVEL TO DISCUSS THE

SITUATION WITH THE VATICAN\'S FOREIGN MINISTER ARCHBISHOP

TAURAN. THE SUDAN IS AN AREA IN WHICH THE UNITED STATES

AND THE VATICAN SHARE MUTUAL CONCERNS AND OBJECTIVESNLS.

3.(C) MY EMBASSY HAS ENGAGED THE VATICAN ON THE SUDAN

ISSUE OVER THE LAST YEAR. IN 2000, FOR INSTANCE, WHEN

SUDAN WAS A CANDIDATE FOR A UN SECURITY COUNCIL SEAT,

EMBASSY VATICAN APPROACHED THE VATICAN FOREIGN MINISTER

AND ASKED HIM TO USE THE VATICAN\'S INFLUENCE IN CERTAIN

COUNTRIES TO DEFEAT THE SUDANESE CANDIDACIES. VATICAN

NUNCIOS (AMBASSADORS) IN SEVERAL KEY COUNTRIES MET WITH

THEIR HOST COUNTRY FOREIGN MINISTERS AND PRESENTED THE

CASE. THE SUDANESE CANDIDACY WAS DEFEATED, AND THE

VATICAN PLAYED AN IMPORTANT ROLE.

4.(C) EMBASSY VATICAN HAS ALSO BEEN INVOLVED IN

DEVELOPING CONTACTS AND SOURCES THAT CAN BE USEFUL TO

YOUR MISSION. ARCHBISHOP FITZGERALD, THE DIRECTOR OF THE

PONTIFICAL COUNCIL FOR INTERELIGIOUS DIALOG AND AN EXPERT

ON ISLAM, HAS MAINTAINED CONTACTS WITH ISLAMIC LEADERS IN

SUDAN AND IS AN EXPERT ON THE SITUATION. FABRIZIO

RICARDI, A LEADER OF THE SANT\'EGIDIO COMMUNITY (THE

VATICAN NGO WHICH NEGOTIATED THE MOZAMBIQUE PEACE TREATY)

HAS BEEN TRAVELLING TO THE SUDAN ON A REGULAR BASIS. THE

COMBONI FATHERS, A MAJOR CATHOLIC MISSIONARY ORDER WHICH

IS BASED IN ROME AND THE ITALIAN CITY OF PESARO, HAS A

LARGE PRESENCE IN SOUTHERN SUDAN.

5.(SBU) IF YOUR SCHEDULE PERMITS, I WOULD RECOMMEND A

DISCUSSION WITH THE VATICAN FOREIGN MINISTER ON NEXT

STEPS IN THE SUDAN. I WOULD ALSO USE THE OPPORTUNITY OF

YOUR VISIT TO INTRODUCE YOU AND YOUR STAFF TO VATICAN AND

OTHER OFFICIALS WHO MIGHT BE ABLE TO ASSIST YOU IN YOUR

MISSION.

6. (SBU) I HOPE YOU WILL BE ABLE TO STOP IN VATICAN CITY,

I WOULD GREATLY APPRECIATE HEARING DIRECTLY FROM YOU ON

WAYS I CAN BETTER ENGAGE THE VATICAN IN PURSUIT OF YOUR

OBJECTIVES. NICHOLSON
