1. (U) AMBASSADOR-AT-LARGE FOR WAR CRIMES ISSUES

PIERRE-RICHARD PROSPER AND SPECIAL ASSISTANT TO THE PRESIDENT

AND NSC SR. DIRECTOR FOR AFRICAN AFFAIRS DR. JENDAYI FRAZER

MET WITH THE PRESIDENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE CONGO (ROC),

DENIS SASSOU-NGUESSO, ON JUNE 13 IN ROME, WHERE HE WAS

ATTENDING A WORLD FOOD SUMMIT. POLOFF RAFIK MANSOUR AND

S/WCI JONATHAN CROCK WERE ALSO PRESENT. AMB. PROSPER

EXPLAINED THE LAUNCHING OF THE REWARDS FOR JUSTICE CAMPAIGN

IN NAIROBI, KENYA TO APPREHEND PERSONS INDICTED FOR GENOCIDE

BY THE INTERNATIONAL CRIMINAL TRIBUNAL FOR RWANDA (ICTR)

THROUGH A REWARD OF UP TO $5 MILLION, AND THE PLANNED

EXPANSION OF THE CAMPAIGN TO THE CONGO REGION IN JULY (SEE

REFTELS).

2. (C) AMB. PROSPER REQUESTED SASSOU-NGUESSO,S SUPPORT FOR

APPREHENDING PERSONS INDICTED FOR GENOCIDE BY ICTR,

SUGGESTING A CAMPAIGN TO ADVERTISE THE REWARDS FOR JUSTICE

PROGRAM AND A VISIT TO THE ROC. AMB. PROSPER GAVE

SASSOU-NGUESSO THE NAMES OF THREE FUGITIVES WHO ARE REPORTED

TO BE IN THE ROC: AUGUSTIN BIZIMANA, JEAN-BAPTISTE GATETE,

AND IDELPHONSE HATEGEKIMANA. PRES. SASSOU-NGUESSO STATED

THAT &AS WITH ANY OTHER GENOCIDE,8 HE IS TOTALLY IN SUPPORT

OF APPREHENDING THE PERPETRATORS AND WOULD COOPERATE VERY

OPENLY. HE WELCOMED A VISIT BY AMB. PROSPER TO BRAZZAVILLE

AND COMMENTED THAT WITH THE PHOTOGRAPHS OF THESE FUGITIVES

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 ROME 03119 261427Z

THEY CAN BE FOUND IN HIS SMALL COUNTRY. HE REQUESTED THAT

THE NAMES OF THESE FUGITIVES ALONG WITH ANY FURTHER

INFORMATION THE USG HAS BE PRESENTED BY THE U.S. AMBASSADOR

TO THE ROC (S/WCI WILL PROVIDE TO AMBASSADOR SEPTEL).

3. (C) PRES. SASSOU-NGUESSO EMPHASIZED THE NEED TO SOLVE THE

REFUGEE PROBLEM TO SECURE STABILITY IN THE REGION AND PREVENT

A CIRCLE OF VIOLENCE. HE STATED THAT 12,000 HUTU REFUGEES

FROM RWANDA HAVE DOUBLED THE POPULATION OF THE NORTHERN

REGION. THEY ARE LIVING IN VERY DIFFICULT CONDITIONS AND ARE

BEING IGNORED BY THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY, HE SAID.

4. (C) AMB. PROSPER SAID THE KEY TO THE PROBLEM IS SOLVING

THE ISSUE OF THE GENOCIDAIRES. DR. FRAZER STRESSED THE

IMPORTANCE OF APPREHENDING ICTR INDICTEES WHO ARE FUELING THE

ONGOING CONFLICT. THESE FUGITIVES FORM THE LEADERSHIP OF THE

ARMY FOR THE LIBERATION OF RWANDA (ALIR) AND ARE EFFECTIVELY

HOLDING LOWER-LEVEL COMBATANTS HOSTAGE. DR. FRAZER STATED

THAT RWANDAN PRESIDENT PAUL KAGAME HAS GIVEN ASSURANCES THAT

THE RANK AND FILE CAN BE REPATRIATED IN RWANDA, BUT WE MUST

FIRST DEAL WITH THE LEADERS.
