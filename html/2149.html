SUBJECT: MFA PERMANENT SECRETARY HOLDS FORTH ON

ELECTION OBSERVATION, OTHER ISSUES

CLASSIFIED BY DCM REWHITEHEAD DUE TO 1.5 (B) AND (D).

1. (C) SUMMARY. ON NOVEMBER 6, AMBASSADOR PAID AN

INITIAL COURTESY CALL ON WILLARD CHIWEWE, PERMANENT

SECRETARY (AND THIRD-RANKING OFFICER) AT THE MINISTRY OF

SIPDIS

FOREIGN AFFAIRS. AFTER INITIAL EXCHANGES ON THE WAR

AGAINST TERRORISM AND GOZ COOPERATION IN PROTECTING

AMCITS AFFECTED BY THE FAST-TRACK LAND ACQUISITION

PROGRAM, THE SUBJECT TURNED TO ELECTION OBSERVATION AND

THE ROLE OF IFES. CHIWEWE REACTED WITH A RAMBLING

LECTURE ABOUT SOVEREIGNTY AND INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY

MOTIVES THAT AT MOMENTS BORDERED ON THE PARANOID. THE

MEETING CONCLUDED ON A GENTLER NOTE, WITH CHIWEWE

DESCRIBING HIS LOVE OF THE LAND AND HIS SHORTCOMINGS IN

THE ART OF DIPLOMATIC DISCOURSE, WITH WHICH WE HEARTILY

AGREE. END SUMMARY.

-------------------------------------

THE WAR ON TERROR AND SECURITY ISSUES

-------------------------------------

2. (SBU) THE AMBASSADOR OPENED THE EXCHANGE BY RAISING

SOME OF THE SAME ISSUES DISCUSSED WITH PRESIDENT MUGABE

DURING THE PRESENTATION OF CREDENTIALS IN OCTOBER. HE

EXPRESSED USG SATISFACTION WITH THE GOOD COOPERATION OF

THE POLICE AND CIO ON TERRORISM RELATED ISSUES. HE

OBSERVED THAT IN AN EARLIER MEETING, SPEAKER OF THE

PARLIAMENT MNANGAGWA HAD URGED THAT THE U.S. CONCLUDE

THE STRUGGLE AGAINST TERRORISM QUICKLY. OBVIOUSLY, WE

WOULD LIKE TO SEE SUCH AN OUTCOME, BUT BY ITS NATURE THE

INTERNATIONAL APPROACH TO THIS COMPLEX PROBLEM MUST BY

THOROUGH AND NOT DEPEND ON INSTANT SOLUTIONS. THE

AMBASSADOR CITED THE UN COUNTERTERRORISM CONVENTIONS

THAT THE GOZ HAS NOT YET RATIFIED AND EXPRESSED OUR HOPE

THAT THE MFA CAN MOVE QUICKLY TO GET THESE BEFORE

PARLIAMENT, SINCE THEY ARE THE BUILDING BLOCKS OF THE

INTERNATIONAL INFRASTRUCTURE TO THWART TERRORISM.

3. (SBU) THE AMBASSADOR NOTED THAT THE EMBASSY HAD NOT

-----------------

AMCIT COOPERATION

-----------------

4. (U) THE AMBASSADOR SAID WE ALSO APPRECIATED RECENT

EFFORTS BY THE MFA TO ASSIST AMERICAN CITIZENS WHOSE

DWELLINGS AND PROPERTY HAD BEEN ADVERSELY AFFECTED BY

EVENTS IN THE COUNTRY. EFFECTIVE INTERVENTION BY THE

MFA HAD HELPED RESOLVE THESE CASES. CHIWEWE REMAINED

SPHINX-LIKE.

-----------------------------

ELECTION OBSERVATION AND IFES

-----------------------------

5. (SBU) THE AMBASSADOR NEXT RAISED THE ISSUE OF

ELECTION OBSERVATION SPECIFICALLY AS IT PERTAINED TO

IFES. HE REFERRED TO THE EXCELLENT INTERNATIONAL

REPUTATION (AND INTERNATIONAL CONTACTS) THAT IFES ENJOYS

AND BRIEFLY DESCRIBED THE ABORTIVE ATTEMPT BY IFES STAFF

TO UNDERTAKE AN INITIAL ELECTIONS ASSESSMENT. HE

OBSERVED THAT A DEFINITIVE GOZ ANSWER ON WHEN IFES CAN

BECOME INVOLVED WOULD BE POSITIVE. HE ADDED THAT IFES

WAS INTERESTED IN AN OBSERVATION RATHER THAN MONITORING

ROLE.

6. (C) THE NEW SUBJECT MATTER ANIMATED CHIWEWE

CONSIDERABLY. HE LAUNCHED INTO A LONG AND AT TIME

PARANOID LECTURE QUESTIONING THE EAGERNESS OF THE

INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY TO BECOME INVOLVED IN ZIMBABWEAN

ELECTIONS, WHICH THE GOZ TOOK TO BE INTERFERENCE. HE

CITED THE "COINCIDENCE" THAT THE EU HAD "DEMANDED" --

BUT NOW WERE REDUCED TO "REQUESTING" -- A SIMILAR

RESPONSE. THIS HAD TO DO WITH THE OPPOSITION PARTY THAT

HAD ATTRACTED INTERNATIONAL INTEREST AND WAS BEING

"NURSED BY DOMINANT INTERESTS". THE GOZ VIEWED SUCH

REQUESTS AGAINST THIS BACKDROP.

7. (C) THE AMBASSADOR RESPONDED THAT WE BELIEVE THAT

ZIMBABWEANS HAVE THE RIGHT TO CHOOSE THEIR OWN

GOVERNMENT. AMERICANS DON\'T VOTE HERE, BUT THEY DO

RETAIN STRONG INTEREST IN UNIVERSAL DEMOCRATIC

PRINCIPLES. CHIWEWE WAS OFF TO THE RACES AGAIN, ARGUING

THAT AMERICAN REBELLION AGAINST THE COLONIAL CROWN MEANT

THAT OUR TWO COUNTRIES COME FROM THE SAME IDEOLOGICAL

ORIGIN. ZIMBABWE WANTS TO BE ON THE SIDE OF THOSE WHO

PROMOTE DEMOCRACY AND HOLD FAIR LAW AND THE PRINCIPLES

OF JUSTICE DEAR. WE SHOULD HAVE NO DOUBT THAT GOZ

LEADERSHIP, AND PRESIDENT MUGABE AND HIS PARTY, ARE

COMMITTED TO FREE AND FAIR ELECTIONS.

8. (C) CHIWEWE FILIBUSTERED AT LENGTH ABOUT ZIMBABWE\'S

HISTORY OF ELECTIONS SINCE 1980, EMPHASIZING THAT THEY

HAD RECEIVED A CLEAN BILL OF HEALTH DURING THE

MARXIST/LENINIST PERIOD FROM 1980-1991. HE SNORTED THAT

SINCE 1999 A LOT OF CASH HAD BEEN INVESTED IN A NEW

PARTY, AND THE INVESTORS WERE ANXIOUS THAT THE

"POLITICAL ELECTIONS GUARANTEE SOLID DIVIDENDS". HE

CITED THE WESTMINSTER FOUNDATION AS A CHIEF CULPRIT AND

ASKED HOW ZIMBABWE COULD EXPECT HONEST OBSERVERS FROM

SUCH BIASED GROUPS.

9. (C) CHIWEWE SAID THAT THE IFES AND EU REQUESTS HAD

TO BE EXAMINED FROM THIS OPTIC. HE EXPLAINED THAT "THE

CONTEXT IS NOT OBJECTIVE". WHY IS THE U.S. TAKING

SIDES INSTEAD OF PLAYING THE HONEST BROKER, AS IT HAD AT

LANCASTER HOUSE? THE GOZ FEARED PRE-JUDGMENT. HE

CONCLUDED BY STRESSING THAT THE GOZ HAD ALWAYS INVITED

OBSERVERS, SO WHY WERE THE USUAL GUESTS ASKING FOR AN

INVITATION THIS TIME AROUND? ZIMBABWE HAD NO ELECTORAL

SKELETONS IN THE CUPBOARD. SOMETIMES THE INTERNATIONAL

COMMUNITY CREATES CONDITIONS TO FORCE OTHERS TO ASK

SOMETHING, AND THEREBY CREATE CONDITIONS TO CONDEMN.

THE EU WAS USING ARTICLE 96 CONSULTATIONS TO TRY AND

FORCE ZIMBABWE TO AGREE TO EU DEMANDS. THIS ASSUMED

EUROPEAN AUTHORITY OVER ZIMBABWEANS AND REMINDED THEM OF

THE SAD PAST.

------------------

CHIWEWE ON CHIWEWE

------------------

10. (C) CHIWEWE LIGHTENED UP CONSIDERABLY ON THE SUBJECT

OF HIMSELF. HE VOLUNTEERED THAT HE SPOKE FRANKLY. SOME

CALLED HIS HONEST STYLE RUDE OR EVEN AGGRESSIVE, BUT IT

HAD SERVED HIM WELL IN HIS ROLE AS A PEACEMAKER IN THE

RENAMO/FRELIMO RAPPROCHEMENT. HE NOTED OUT OF THE BLUE

THAT HE WAS UNREACHABLE 99 PERCENT OF THE TIME ON

WEEKENDS BECAUSE BE WAS GARDENING AT HIS RURAL HOME IS

MASVINGO. HE CONTINUED THAT HE SPOKE NOT AS A

BUREAUCRAT, BUT AS "SOME SORT OF SCIENTIST". AS A

CLOSER, HE PROCLAIMED THAT THERE WAS FERTILE GROUND FOR

RICH BILATERAL RELATIONS BETWEEN ZIMBABWE AND THE U.S.,

AND EVEN ZIMBABWE AND THE UK.

-------

COMMENT

-------

11. (C) THE MEETING WAS AT TIMES AMUSING, IF NOT

PARTICULARLY ENLIGHTENING. CHIWEWE\'S ROLE AS FRONT MAN

IN DEALING WITH THE EU ON ARTICLE 8 CONSULTATIONS IS

PROBABLY AS GOOD AN EXPLANATION AS ANY FOR THE EU MOVING

TO ARTICLE 96. HE IS CLEARLY A MAN WHO RELISHES HIS

ROLE AS CEREBUS GUARDING THE GATES FOR ZANU-PF, AND IS

DETERMINED NOT TO YIELD THIS SINECURE WITHOUT A TUSSLE.
