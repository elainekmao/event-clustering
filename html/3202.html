SUBJECT: SUBJECT: NIGERIA REQUESTS U.S. TO DELAY

RECOGNITION OF RAVALOMANANA UNTIL AFTER OAU/AU MEETING

REF: ABUJA 1849 AND PREVIOUS

CLASSIFIED BY CDA ANDREWS. REASON: 1.5(B)

1.(C) National Security Advisor Aliyu Mohammed June 25

requested the U.S. to delay acceptance of Ravalomanana\'s

accession until after the OAU/AU Durban convention in early

July. Aliyu said that, knowing the views of the U.S. and most

Western countries would help the OAU reach a decision

favorable to Ravalomanana. But announcements by the U.S. and

others that appear to pre-empt the OAU/AU would create

difficulties.

ANDREWS
