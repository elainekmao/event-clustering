INTRODUCTION

1. (U) SUMMARY: THE HOLY SEE ACTIVELY SEEKS TO INFLUENCE

THE POLICIES OF INTERNATIONAL ORGANIZATIONS AND INSTITUTIONS

AND PARTICIPATES INTENSELY IN THE MOST IMPORTANT OF THESE TO

PROMOTE ITS OWN AGENDA, PARTICULARLY ITS POSITION ON

SEXUALITY AND THE TRADITIONAL FAMILY. THE HOLY SEE WILL BE

A U.S. ALLY ON ISSUES THAT DEFEND "HUMAN DIGNITY," SUCH AS

TRAFFICKING, PEACEKEEPING, DEVELOPMENT AID, AND HUMAN

RIGHTS. IT COULD BE A POWERFUL OPPONENT ON OTHERS SUCH AS

ASPECTS OF REPRODUCTIVE RIGHTS. U.S. MISSIONS TO

INTERNATIONAL ORGANIZATIONS ARE WELL SERVED WHEN THEY

CULTIVATE AND MAINTAIN GOOD RELATIONS WITH HOLY SEE

REPRESENTATIVES, WORKING TOGETHER WHERE OUR POSITIONS ARE

COMPLEMENTARY, AND ENSURING U.S. POSITIONS ARE UNDERSTOOD

WHERE VIEWS MAY DIVERGE. END SUMMARY.

THE HOLY SEE\'S MULTILATERAL FOREIGN POLICY

------------------------------------------

2. (U) THE HOLY SEE IS AN ACTIVE PARTICIPANT IN A BROAD

RANGE OF INTERNATIONAL ORGANIZATIONS -- TYPICALLY AS AN

"OBSERVER" -- AND WORKS WITHIN THESE FORA TO ADVANCE

PRINCIPLES CENTRAL TO THE CHURCH\'S WORLD VIEW: PRIORITY OF

THE HUMAN PERSON; THE PROMOTION AND, WHEN NECESSARY, THE

DEFENSE OF PEACE; SUPPORT FOR DEMOCRACY BUILDING

INITIATIVES; AND CONSTRUCTION OF AN INTERNATIONAL ORDER

BASED ON JUSTICE AND HUMAN RIGHTS. FM TAURAN RECENTLY

RESTATED THE HOLY SEE\'S BASIC MESSAGE TO UN MEMBER STATES:

ALL NATIONS ARE EQUAL, WITH EQUAL DIGNITY AND RIGHT TO

DEFENSE OF IDENTITY AND AUTONOMY; NATIONS MUST ACT IN

SOLIDARITY (I.E., ACCORDING TO RECIPROCAL MORAL OBLIGATIONS

NOT ONLY TO TREAT EACH OTHER WITH RESPECT BUT TO SEE THAT

PEOPLES\' BASIC NEEDS ARE MET). THE HOLY SEE REGARDS THE

UNITED NATIONS SYSTEM AS AN IDEAL FORUM AND PRIVILEGED

PLATFORM TO ADVANCE THESE PRINCIPLES MULTILATERALLY. AS A

RESULT, IT HAS TENDED TO LOOK THE OTHER WAY WHEN FACED WITH

THE UN\'S FAILINGS, PARTICULARLY ITS BUREAUCRATIC

DEFICIENCIES. IT IS UNLIKELY TO PUBLICLY SUPPORT U.S.

CRITIQUES OF THE UN. HOWEVER, THE HOLY SEE WILL NOT

HESITATE TO CRITICIZE THE UN AND INTERNATIONAL ORGANIZATIONS

DIRECTLY WHEN THESE ENGAGE IN A PERCEIVED ASSAULT ON THE

HOLY SEE\'S CORE PRINCIPLES.

3. (U) IN OPTING FOR PERMANENT OBSERVER STATUS RATHER THAN

FULL MEMBERSHIP IN MOST ORGANIZATIONS, THE HOLY SEE HAS

SOUGHT TO EMPHASIZE ITS MORAL, RATHER THAN POLITICAL,

AUTHORITY, AND ITS UNIQUE INTERNATIONAL JURIDICAL STATUS.

THE HOLY SEE THEREFORE OPERATES FROM A DOCTRINAL AND

PRINCIPLE-BASED PERSPECTIVE WITHIN INTERNATIONAL

ORGANIZATIONS TO MAINTAIN ITS REPUTATION OF BEING "SUPER

PARTES" - A DISINTERESTED MORAL AGENT ACTING FOR THE GOOD OF

HUMANITY. THE HOLY SEE IS RELUCTANT TO ENGAGE IN PURELY

"POLITICAL" MATTERS, SUCH AS THE UNSC VOTE ON SUDAN\'S

MEMBERSHIP, EVEN TO THE EXTENT OF ESCHEWING ITS VOTE ON SUCH

MATTERS IN FORA WHERE IT HAS A VOTE. THE HOLY SEE PREFERS

NOT TO ENGAGE ON OPERATIONAL ISSUES SUCH AS CANDIDACIES FOR

INTERNATIONAL ORGANIZATIONS, PREFERRING TO BE IN THE

ORGANIZATION BUT NOT OF IT. IN THE SUDAN CASE, IT DID TAKE

AN ACTIVE BUT BEHIND THE SCENES ROLE IN DISCUSSING WITH

OTHER UNSC DELEGATES THE INAPPROPRIATENESS OF SUDAN AS A

UNSC MEMBER.

4. (SBU) SOME WITHIN THE CHURCH HIERARCHY HAVE QUESTIONED

WHETHER THE HOLY SEE WOULD BE BETTER SERVED BY FULL VOTING

MEMBERSHIP IN MORE ORGANIZATIONS, BUT AS BISHOP MARCHETTO OF

THE COUNCIL FOR THE PASTORAL CARE OF MIGRANTS AND TRAVELERS

RECENTLY POINTED OUT TO US, OBSRVER STATUS GIVES THE HOLY

SEE THE BEST OF BOTH ORLDS: IT CAN PARTICIPATE IN

DISCUSSIONS AS ACTVELY AS ANY MEMBER, BUT RETAINS THE

ABILITY TO SAY ABOVE THE FRAY AND "SAY THINGS THAT NEED TO

BESAID."

5. (U) THE HOLY SEE\'S PRINCIPLES TRANSATE INTO A SERIES

OF STRONGLY HELD POSITIONS ON SSUES OF PRIORITY INTEREST TO

THE UNITED STATES. FOR EXAMPLE, THE HOLY SEE BELIEVES THAT

WAR MUST ALWAYS BE REFUTED AND PRIORITY GIVEN TO NEGOTIATION

AND THE USE OF JURIDICAL MEANS FOR CONFLICT RESOLUTION. IT

WILL THEREFORE OPPOSE THE USE OF FORCE UNLESS THE

REQUIREMENTS OF ITS JUST WAR DOCTRINE ARE FULFILLED, AS WAS

THE CASE IN THE LIBERATION OF KUWAIT, BUT NOT IN THE

CONTINUED MILITARY ACTION AGAINST IRAQ (REFTEL). THE HOLY

SEE ALSO USES MULTILATERAL DIPLOMACY TO PROTECT HUMAN LIFE

FROM CONCEPTION TO NATURAL DEATH, AS WELL AS TO PROMOTE ITS

6. (U) IN MANY CASES, U.S. AND VATICAN VIEWS ARE

COMPATIBLE, AND HOLY SEE DIPLOMATS CAN BE VALUABLE ALLIES.

WHERE THE USG SEEKS TO PROMOTE HUMAN VALUES AND RELIEVE

HUMAN SUFFERING BY MEDIATING CONFLICTS, COMBATING DISEASE,

AND PROTECTING HUMAN RIGHTS, THE HOLY SEE WILL BE A STRONG

PARTNER. THE HOLY SEE PERCEIVES A CLOSER THAN EVER

CONVERGENCE WITH THE USG IN SEVERAL AREAS. THE MOST

UNAMBIGUOUS EXAMPLE WOULD BE THE RECENT COORDINATION OF

POSITIONS IN SUPPORT OF A UN CONVENTION TO BAN ALL FORMS OF

HUMAN CLONING, AN EFFORT THAT WAS EXTREMELY WELL-RECEIVED AT

THE HIGHEST LEVELS IN THE CURIA. POST IS ALSO ENGAGED WITH

RELEVANT CURIA OFFICES IN A DIALOGUE TO EXPLORE QUESTIONS OF

FOOD SECURITY IN THE DEVELOPING WORLD AND THE POSITIVE

CONTRIBUTION TO BE MADE BY GMOS. WHILE THE HOLY SEE HAS YET

TO BE CONVINCED THAT USG COMMERCIAL INTERESTS ARE NOT THE

DRIVING MOTIVATION FOR OUR SUPPORT FOR GMO\'S, THEY ARE

WILLING TO DISCUSS GMOS WITH AN OPEN MIND AS A MEANS OF

INCREASING FOOD SECURITY. IF WE CAN MAKE OUR CASE, THE HOLY

SEE COULD BECOME A POWERFUL BOOSTER IN THE FAO AND THE

COUNCIL OF EUROPE TO BUILD SUPPORT FOR GMO\'S AS A MEANS TO

ALLEVIATE HUNGER. IF, HOWEVER, THE HOLY SEE WERE TO

CONCLUDE THAT GMOS ARE IMMORAL, IT COULD COMPLICATE OUR

EFFORTS TO PROMOTE U.S. AGRICULTURAL INTERESTS AND

CONTRIBUTE TO THE ALLEVIATION OF HUNGER THROUGH GMO\'S.

7. (U) SOME WITHIN THE HOLY SEE BELIEVE THAT A FEW WESTERN

NATIONS AND NGOS HAVE HIJACKED THE UN TO PROMOTE FEMINIST

AND HOMOSEXUAL AGENDAS THAT REFLECT MINORITY POSITIONS

WITHIN THE NGO\'S HOME COUNTRIES. AT THE CAIRO CONFERENCE,

AS WELL AS IN BEIJING IN 1995, THE HOLY SEE FORMED EFFECTIVE

ALLIANCES WITH CONSERVATIVE SOUTH AMERICAN AND ISLAMIC

STATES SUCH AS GUATEMALA AND LIBYA IN SUPPORT OF ITS

POSITION. THE STRATEGY WAS SUCCESSFUL AND WE EXPECT IT TO

BE USED AGAIN. ACCORDING TO FM TAURAN, THE HOLY SEE\'S BASIC

POSITION IS THAT A FAMILY IS CONSTITUTED OF A MAN AND A

WOMAN IN A PERMANENT RELATIONSHIP, AND THAT HUMAN NATURE AND

UNIVERSAL RIGHTS EXIST AND ARE SANCTIONED IN THE "GREAT

DOCUMENTS AND CONVENTIONS THAT REGULATE THE LIFE OF THE

INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY." TAURAN SAID THE HOLY SEE WOULD

CONTINUE TO PROMOTE A CONSERVATIVE JUDEO-CHRISTIAN MORALITY

OF SEXUAL ABSTINENCE OUTSIDE OF LEGAL MARRIAGE, FIDELITY

WITHIN MARRIAGE, AND OPPOSITION TO ARTIFICIAL FORMS OF

CONTRACEPTION, ABORTION AND THE DEATH PENALTY. OBVIOUSLY,

THIS APPROACH LEADS THE HOLY SEE TO BALKS AT WIDELY

SANCTIONED APPROACHES ON SOME ISSUES SUCH AS CONDOM USE TO

PREVENT HIV/AIDS IN AFRICA, PREFERRING TO FOCUS ON

EDUCATION, ABSTINENCE, AND TREATMENT. THE HOLY SEE HAS ALSO

LOBBIED AGAINST REPRODUCTIVE HEALTH INITIATIVES SUCH AS

ACCESS TO CONTRACEPTION AND ABORTION FOR WOMEN IN REFUGEE

CAMPS DURING RECENT CONFLICTS, AND WILL CONTINUE TO ADVOCATE

VIGOROUSLY TO PROMOTE ITS VIEWS ON REPRODUCTIVE ISSUES.

COMMENT

-------

8. (SBU) THE HOLY SEE WILL ACTIVELY CULTIVATE ALLIANCES

WITH ANY STATE OR ORGANIZATION THAT FOSTERS TRADITIONAL

EXPRESSIONS OF THE FAMILY, CONSERVATIVE SEXUAL AND

AND ETHICAL PRINCIPALS THAT RECOGNIZE AND PROTECT HUMAN LIFE

FROM CONCEPTION TO NATURAL DEATH. THE HOLY SEE WILL BE

AGGRESSIVE IN FOSTERING SUCH ALLIANCES, AS WHEN IT SOLICITED

THE SUPPORT OF MOSLEM STATES DURING RECENT UN CONFERENCES.

OFFICIALS, ARE NOT MONOLITHIC IN VIEW, AND CAN BE CONVINCED

BY MORAL ARGUMENTS. TO GAIN THEIR SUPPORT, EMBASSIES SHOULD

SEEK TO CONVEY TO HOLY SEE REPRESENTATIVES HOW OUR POLICIES

CAN CONTRIBUTE TO ADVANCING THE PRINCIPLES THEY HOLD DEAR.
