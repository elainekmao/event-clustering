SUBJECT: REQUEST FOR DVC ON "ISLAM IN A MULTICULTURAL

SOCIETY"

1. SUMMARY: PAS AMMAN REQUESTS IIP SUPPORT IN RECRUITING AN

AMERICAN EXPERT FOR AN ARABIC LANGUAGE 90 MINUTE DVC PROGRAM

ON "CULTURAL DIVERSITY AND ISLAM." THE PROGRAM WOULD BE

CONDUCTED DURING THE HOLY MONTH OF RAMADAN. OUR AUDIENCE

WOULD INCLUDE STUDENTS AND PROFESSORS FROM ISLAMIC SHARIA

SCHOOLS AT JORDANIAN UNIVERSITIES, INCLUDING RECENTLY

RETURNED IV PARTICIPANTS, MEMBERS OF THE ISLAMIC THOUGHT

CENTER AND THE CENTER FOR INTERFAITH STUDIES. WE WOULD

PREFER TO HOLD THIS PROGRAM ON NOVEMBER 20 OR NOVEMBER 25 AT

1730 GMT.

2. GIVEN THE SENSITIVITIES AND THE POLITICAL TENSIONS IN THE

REGION, WE ASK THAT PROGRAM FOCUS ON CULTURAL DIVERSITY,

COEXISTENCE, RELIGIOUS TOLERANCE IN THE U.S. AND THE

MISCONCEPTIONS THAT HAVE RISEN IN THE ARAB WORK ON THE U.S.

VIEWS OF ISLAM AND MUSLIMS, PARTICULARLY AFTER THE SEPTEMBER

11. THE SPEAKER SHOULD FOCUS ON THE ROLE MUSLIMS PLAYS IN

THE U.S. SOCIETY, POLITICALLY, SOCIALLY AND ECONOMICALLY.

3. THE AMERICAN PARTICIPANT SHOULD SPEAK ARABIC AND BE AN

EXPERT ON ISLAM. WE RECOMMEND THE FOLLOWING POSSIBLE

SPEAKERS: IMAM YAHYA HENDI, MUSLIM CHAPLAIN AT GEORGETOWN

UNIVERSITY; DR. KHALID YEHIA BLANSKHIP, A SCHOLAR AT THE

UNIVERSITY OF UTAH; MS. SHARIFA AL KHATIB, FOUNDER AND VICE

PRESIDENT OF THE NORTH AMERICAN COUNCIL FOR MUSLIM WOMEN.

4. WE REQUEST THAT THE SPEAKER MAKE A 20 MINUTE

PRESENTATION, THEN TAKE QUESTIONS FROM THE AUDIENCE.

5. CONTROL OFFICER FOR THIS PROGRAM IS CAO DONNA WINTON,

TEL: 962-6-5920101, EXT. 2115; FAX; 962-6-5920121. PLEASE

ADVISE.

GNEHM
