1. DESPITE PRECIPITOUSLY DECLINING RESOURCES FOR

DEVELOPMENT, THE BOARD APPROVED COUNTRY PROGRAMMES FOR

RWANDA, ZAMBIA AND BOLIVIA, WITH A TOTAL COMMITMENT OF USD

(U.S. DOLLARS) 116.3 MILLION. A COUNTRY STRATEGY OUTLINE

(CSO) WAS APPROVED FOR ETHIOPIA. BUDGET INCREASES FOR THE

DOMINICAN REPUBLIC, HAITI, LAOS AND RWANDA, VALUED AT USD

14.6 MILLION, PREVIOUSLY APPROVED BY THE EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR,

WERE ENDORSED BY THE BOARD.

2. PROTRACTED RELIEF AND RECOVERY (PRRO) OPERATIONS FOR

AZERBAIJAN, ALGERIA, ANGOLA (ON THE CONDITION OF REVISIONS)

AND COLOMBIA, VALUED AT USD $28.6 MILLION, WERE ALSO

APPROVED. THE BOARD ENDORSED A BUDGET INCREASE OF USD 1.44

MILLION FOR BANGLADESH (ROHINGYA REFUGEES), AS PREVIOUSLY

APPROVED BY THE EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR. THE NEW EMOP FOR THE

PALESTINIAN TERRITORY WAS DISCUSSED AT LENGTH BY THE BOARD.

3. A NUMBER OF WFP OPERATIONS ARE FACING IMMEDIATE PIPELINE

BREAKS. WITH A SCARCE (NON-U.S.) RESOURCING PICTURE AND WITH

A MAJOR SOUTHERN AFRICA HUMANITARIAN CRISIS ON THE HORIZON,

WE FORESEE A "TRIAL BY FIRE" SUMMER FOR THE NEW EXECUTIVE

DIRECTOR AND HIS TEAM. END SUMMARY.

21. THE JOINT INSPECTION UNIT (JIU) WAS ESTABLISHED BY THE

UNGA IN 1966. IT EVALUATES WHETHER ACTIVITIES UNDERTAKEN BY

"PARTICIPATING ORGANIZATIONS" ARE CARRIED OUT IN THE MOST

ECONOMICAL MANNER, ENSURING THAT OPTIMUM USE IS MADE OF

UP SYSTEM FOR JOINT INSPECTION UNIT (JIU) REPORTS. THE BOARD

METHODS AND PRACTICES IN HANDLING OVERSIGHT REPORTS," OF

PALESTINIAN TERRITORY

---------------------

22. UNDER OTHER BUSINESS A NUMBER OF ISLAMIC COUNTRIES,

PAKISTAN, SYRIA, EGYPT, IRAQ, SAUDI ARABIA, ETC., ASK FOR A

FULL BRIEFING ON WFP\'S RESPONSE (ACTUAL AND PLANNED) IN THE

PALESTINIAN TERRITORY, CITING IN THEIR VIEW AN EXTREME

HUMANITARIAN CRISIS AMONG THE PALESTINIAN CIVILIAN

POPULATION.

23. WFP ANNOUNCED THAT IT HAD CONSOLIDATED A PREVIOUSLY

ISSUED EMERGENCY EMOP OPERATION (10072) AND A PROTRACTED

RELIEF AND RECOVERY (PRRO) OPERATION (6214) UNDER A NEW EMOP

(10190) FROM MAY-DECEMBER 2002 TO ASSIST 500,000

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 04 ROME 02648 05 OF 06 311353Z

BENEFICIARIES, VALUED AT USD 18.29 MILLION (41, 217 MTS).

THE EMOP WILL TARGET THE MOST VULNERABLE AMONG THE NON-

EXPECTANT/NURSING WOMEN, THE ELDERLY AND PATIENTS IN
