SUBJECT: ZIMBABWE BANK DIRECTOR PROVIDES SOME HOPE FOR

MORE STEADY FUEL SUPPLIES

REFS: A) HARARE 5350 (NOTAL), B) HARARE 4999 (NOTAL)

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 HARARE 05397 01 OF 02 261404Z

CLASSIFIED BY DCM EARL M. IRVING, REASON 1.5 (B), (D)

1. (C) SUMMARY: DCM AND POLOFF CALLED ON GIDEON GONO,

DIRECTOR OF THE COMMERCIAL BANK OF ZIMBABWE (CBZ),

SEPTEMBER 25 TO GET AN UPDATE ON ZIMBABWE\'S FUEL

SITUATION. AS REPORTED REF B, THE CBZ, AND GONO

PERSONALLY, HAS BEEN INSTRUMENTAL IN KEEPING FUEL

FLOWING INTO ZIMBABWE UNDER THE MOST ARDUOUS OF

CIRCUMSTANCES. GONO OFFERED SOME HOPE THAT THE FUEL

CRISIS HAS PEAKED, AND THAT THE NATIONAL OIL COMPANY OF

ZIMBABWE (NOCZIM) PARASTATAL THAT HAS CAUSED MUCH OF THE

MISERY WILL LOSE ITS PROCUREMENT MONOPOLY BY THE END OF

THE YEAR. END SUMMARY.

2. (C) DCM AND POLOFF CALLED ON GIDEON GONO, MANAGING

DIRECTOR AND CEO OF THE COMMERCIAL BANK OF ZIMBABWE

(CBZ), SEPTEMBER 25 TO GET AN UPDATE ON ZIMBABWE\'S FUEL

SITUATION. GONO OUTLINED THE CBZ\'S ROLE IN SECURING NEW

SOURCES OF FUEL EARLIER THIS YEAR WHEN ZIMBABWE\'S

TRADITIONAL CREDITORS TURNED OFF THE TAPS. WHEN NOCZIM

MISMANAGEMENT AND FOREIGN EXCHANGE SHORTFALLS LED TO

CRITICAL FUEL SHORTAGES IN FEBRUARY, GONO EMBARKED ON A

SERIES OF TRIPS TO LIBYA, EGYPT, DUBAI, AND KUWAIT. THE

FIRST THREE DECLINED TO HELP GONO, BUT KUWAIT\'S

INTERNATIONAL PETROLEUM GROUP (IPG) OFFERED SOME HOPE.

GONO ACCOMPANIED PRESIDENT MUGABE AND THEN ENERGY

MINISTER ENOS CHIKOWORE TO KUWAIT IN MARCH, AND IPG SET

UP A US$30 MILLION REVOLVING CREDIT FACILITY FOR FUEL,

ACCORDING TO GONO. AT IPG\'S REQUEST, CBZ GUARANTEED THE

DEAL ON A COMMERCIAL BASIS; IN EXCHANGE, NOCZIM

CONFIDENTIAL

DEPOSITED ITS CASH WITH THE CBZ. THE DEAL PROVIDED SOME

TEMPORARY RELIEF TO ZIMBABWE\'S FUEL WOES.

3. (C) NEW PROBLEMS SOON SURFACED, HOWEVER, WHEN THE

GOVERNMENT\'S SPECIAL FUEL BOND WAS ONLY 50 PERCENT

SUBSCRIBED. ZIMBABWE\'S FOREIGN FUEL SUPPLIERS BECAME

VERY NERVOUS WHEN THEY SAW THAT NOCZIM COULD NOT

CONVINCE LOCAL BANKS TO BUY THE BONDS. THE FOREIGN

SUPPLIERS STARTED TO PUT THE SQUEEZE ON NOCZIM AND CBZ,

AND FUEL SUPPLIES STARTED TO DWINDLE AGAIN. THE

GOVERNMENT BECAME DISENCHANTED WITH CBZ, AND ENDED ITS

FINANCIAL ADVISORY CONTRACT WITH THE BANK IN JULY. WHEN

THE NEW ENERGY MINISTER, SYDNEY SEKERAMAYI, COULD NOT

FIND NEW FUEL SUPPLIERS FOR NOCZIM, HE SENT AN "S.O.S."

TO CBZ IN AUGUST, GONO SAID (REF B). THE CBZ CONVINCED

BRITISH PETROLEUM, WHICH HAS THE LARGEST RETAIL NETWORK

IN ZIMBABWE, TO RELEASE FUEL FROM ITS STORAGE FACILITY

IN GWERU. WITHIN TWO WEEKS, GONO HAD EASED THE FUEL

CRISIS AGAIN.

4. (C) GONO HAS SINCE INVITED FUEL SUPPLIERS, SUCH AS IPG

AND SOUTH AFRICA\'S ENGEN AND SASOL, TO COME MEET MUGABE

PERSONALLY IN AN EFFORT TO RESTORE CONFIDENCE IN

ZIMBABWE. THIS LED TO THE NEW FUEL DEAL REPORTED REF A

BETWEEN NOCZIM, THE ZIMBABWE MINISTRY OF MINES AND

ENERGY, SOUTH AFRICA\'S ENGEN (26 PERCENT STAKEHOLDER IN

CBZ, AND ITSELF 80 PERCENT OWNED BY PETRONAS OF

MALAYSIA), CBZ, AND METROPOLITAN BANK. GONO ELABORATED

THAT THE DEAL INVOLVES A US$50 MILLION REVOLVING CREDIT

FACILITY THAT IS TIED TO EXPORT EARNINGS ON SPECIFIC

COMMODITIES, SUCH AS COTTON AND TOBACCO. THE CREDIT

SCHEME INCLUDES A $25 MILLION 90-DAY AND A $25 MILLION

360-DAY REVOLVING CREDIT LINE (REF A). CBZ HELPED TO

NEGOTIATE THE 360-DAY FACILITY WITH MALAYSIA DIRECTLY,

AND IT HAS HELPED TO RESTART THE IPG FACILITY, WHICH HAD

BEEN SUSPENDED.

5. (C) ON THE FATE OF NOCZIM, GONO STATED THAT IT IS ON

ITS WAY TO BECOMING SOLELY A REGULATING BODY WITH NO

PROCUREMENT AUTHORITY. ACCORDING TO THE GOZ\'S CURRENT

THINKING, ZIMBABWE\'S FUEL IMPORTERS WOULD HAVE THE

FREEDOM TO BUY FUEL DIRECTLY AT MARKET PRICES AND CUT

OUT THE NOCZIM MIDDLEMAN. THE GOVERNMENT WOULD STILL

HAVE A HAND IN DETERMINING FUEL PRICES, USING THE SOUTH

AFRICAN GOVERNMENT\'S FORMULA (NFI) THAT WOULD ENSURE NO

LOSSES TO THE STATE OR SUPPLIERS. NOCZIM\'S STAFF IS TO

BE REDUCED FROM 200 TO 15, AND IT IS TO MOVE OUT OF ITS

SEVEN-STORY HEADQUARTERS, GONO STATED. WHEN ASKED HOW

LONG NOCZIM HAD LEFT, GONO FELT THAT IT WOULD BE

EFFECTIVELY DISSOLVED BY THE END OF THE YEAR, BUT NOTED

COYLY THAT THERE WERE STILL POLITICAL OBSTACLES TO

OVERCOME. IN ANY CASE, GONO FELT THAT HE WOULD BE ABLE

TO MAINTAIN THE REPRIEVE FROM ZIMBABWE\'S FUEL TROUBLES

FOR AT LEAST THE NEXT THREE TO FOUR MONTHS.

6. (C) COMMENT: GONO WAS MORE CIRCUMSPECT WITH US THAN

IN HIS DINNER CONVERSATION A FEW WEEKS AGO IN WHICH HE

DESCRIBED ENERGY MINISTER SEKERAMAYI SENDING LETTERS TO

NOCZIM REQUESTING A CUT OF ANY NEW FUEL DEAL (REF B).

THIS IS LIKELY THE "POLITICAL OBSTACLE" THAT GONO WAS

REFERRING TO. EVEN IF GOVERNMENT OFFICIALS CONTINUE

SKIMMING OFF OF THE COUNTRY\'S FUEL TROUBLES, IT APPEARS

THAT ZIMBABWE HAS SECURED ENOUGH FOREIGN FINANCIAL

BACKING TO TEMPORARILY STAVE OFF THE CONSTANT FUEL

DISRUPTIONS THAT HAVE PLAGUED IT FOR THE LAST TEN

MONTHS. QUEUES AT LOCAL PETROL OUTLETS HAVE BEEN

NOTICEABLY SHORTER SINCE THE SEPTEMBER 22 FUEL DEAL WAS

ANNOUNCED. WHETHER ZIMBABWE HAS FOUND A MORE LASTING

SOLUTION WILL DEPEND ON THE POLITICAL WILL OF MUGABE AND

THE ENERGY MINISTER TO DISSOLVE THE CORRUPT AND BADLY

MANAGED NOCZIM. THAT MAY PROVE DIFFICULT IF THE NEW

ENERGY MINISTER AND OTHERS LIKE HIM CANNOT WEAN

THEMSELVES FROM THE BLOATED PARASTATAL.

7. (C) BIO NOTE: GONO WAS BORN IN BUHERA, MANICALAND

PROVINCE, ZIMBABWE ON NOVEMBER 29, 1959. HE IS MARRIED

AND HAS FOUR CHILDREN AGES 18, 16 (TWIN GIRLS), AND 4.

HE DESCRIBES HIMSELF IN HORATIO ALGER TERMS, STARTING

OUT AS A TEA-MAKER/CLEANER AT A LARGE COMPANY, WHO USED

HIS PAYCHECK TO PUT HIMSELF THROUGH SCHOOL FROM GRADE

SCHOOL TO UNIVERSITY. GONO HINTED THAT PRESIDENT MUGABE

RECENTLY SOUNDED HIM OUT FOR A CABINET APPOINTMENT,

WHICH HE TURNED DOWN ("THE HARDEST DECISION OF MY

LIFE"). THE BANK MANAGING DIRECTOR CLAIMS HE HAS NO

POLITICAL AMBITIONS ("YET") BUT DOES WANT TO GALVANIZE A

REPUTATION AS A CORPORATE TURNAROUND EXPERT, WHICH HE
