CLASSIFIED BY: ECMIN SCOTT KILNER FOR REASONS 1.5 (B) AND (D).

1. (C) EMBASSY DELIVERED REFPOINTS TO ATTILIO IANNUCCI, THE

MFA\'S DEPUTY DIRECTOR GENERAL FOR DEVELOPMENT ASSISTANCE.

IANNUCCI SHARED THAT THE GOI WAS "THINKING ALONG THE SAME

LINES" AS THE USG ON PLANNING HUMANITARIAN ASSISTANCE IN THE

EVENT OF A WAR WITH IRAQ. IANNUCCI SAID THAT ITALY IS

ALREADY WORKING CLOSELY WITH THE IOM, ICRC AND WFP.

FURTHERMORE, THE GOI IS PREPARED "TO TAKE DECISIONS WITHIN

HOURS" AS TO WHAT SPECIFIC CONTINGENCIES WOULD REQUIRE

ITALIAN EMERGENCY ASSISTANCE AND THROUGH WHICH INTERNATIONAL

ORGANIZATIONS SUCH ASSISTANCE SHOULD BE DELIVERED. IANNUCCI

WAS NOT ABLE TO QUANTIFY THE GOI\'S COMMITMENT, BUT DREW A

PARALLEL TO AFGHANISTAN, WHERE ITALY, AFTER INITIALLY

STEPPING UP TO THE PLATE AT THE TOKYO DONOR\'S CONFERENCE,

WOUND UP EXCEEDING ITS PLEDGE OF EUROS 47.3 MILLION FOR THE

YEAR (REF B).
