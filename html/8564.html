SUBJECT: KUWAIT\'S NEW CABINET, PENDING ELECTIONS

1. In order to run in the National Assembly elections

scheduled for July 5, four members of the Cabinet have

resigned, as required by the Constitution: Talal al-Ayyar

(Electricity and Water, Social Affairs and Labor), Fahd

Dhaisan al-Mai al-Azmi (Public Works, Housing), Ahmed Baqer

(Justice, Awqaf and Islamic Affairs), and Salah Khorsheed

(Commerce and Industry).

2. The Constitution also requires the Cabinet to include at

least one elected member of the National Assembly. To meet

this requirement, MP Rashed al-Hjailan, who is not running

for reelection, has been appointed to the Cabinet. All

these changes took effect June 3.

3. The new Cabinet will be short-lived, because the

National Assembly elections are sure to trigger a reshuffle.
