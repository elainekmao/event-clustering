SUBJECT: VIETNAM ON AN ASEAN TROIKA APPROACH TO BURMA

REF: SECSTATE 185162

1. POLCOUNS AND POLOFF PRESENTED REFTEL POINTS 3 OCTOBER

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 02 HANOI 02225 050421Z

TO MFA ASEAN DEPARTMENT DEPUTY DIRECTOR NGUYEN HUU CHI. CHI

SAID THAT, AFTER MEETING WITH UN SECRETARY GENERAL KOFI

ANNAN, VIETNAMESE FOREIGN MINISTER NGUYEN DY NIEN CONSULTED

CLOSELY AT THE UNGA WITH THE THAI AND BRUNEI FOREIGN

MINISTERS ON THE IDEA OF ACTIVATING THE ASEAN TROIKA ON

BURMA. NIEN THEN HAD "CANDID AND FRIENDLY" TALKS WITH THE

BURMESE FOREIGN MINISTER. BASED ON THESE DISCUSSIONS, THE

TROIKA WAS OF THE VIEW THAT THE SITUATION IN BURMA IS "NOT

ONE THAT AFFECTS OVERALL ASEAN STABILITY," AND GETTING THE

TROIKA INVOLVED IS NOT NOW NECESSARY. (NOTE: CHI INITIALLY

CHARACTERIZED THIS AS A GROUP DECISION, BUT LATER NOTED THAT

VIETNAM AS CHAIR MADE THE CALL ON BEHALF OF THE TROIKA. END

NOTE.)

2. CHI CONTINUED THAT THE RULES OF ENGAGEMENT FOR THE

TROIKA LAID OUT AT THE JULY BANGKOK MINISTERIAL SPECIFIED

THAT THE TROIKA SHOULD BE CONVENED WITH THE CONSENSUS OF ALL

ASEAN MEMBERS, BUT AS YET NO SUCH CONSENSUS EXISTS ON BURMA.

ASEAN WOULD, OF COURSE, CONVENE THE TROIKA IF BURMA WERE TO

REQUEST IT; HOWEVER, BURMA HAS JUST RECENTLY SENT A LETTER

TO ALL THE ASEAN COUNTRIES ASKING FOR NO OUTSIDE,

SPECIFICALLY TROIKA, INTERVENTION IN ITS INTERNAL AFFAIRS.

3. CHI TOLD POLOFFS HE HAS NO INFORMATION ON A VISIT TO

HANOI BY UN SPECIAL ENVOY ON BURMA RAZALI ISMAIL. AS

VIETNAM HAS NO MANDATE TO ACTIVATE THE TROIKA UNILATERALLY,

IT HAS NO AUTHORITY TO WORK WITH RAZALI, AND THERE HAVE BEEN

NO CONTACTS OF WHICH CHI IS AWARE.

4. POLCOUNS SUGGESTED IT COULD BE USEFUL FOR VIETNAM AND

OTHER ASEAN COUNTRIES TO APPROACH BURMA AND TRY TO NUDGE THE

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 03 HANOI 02225 050421Z

REGIME IN A POSITIVE DIRECTION, RATHER THAN WAITING FOR A

BURMESE REQUEST. CHI SAID THIS WAS ALREADY DONE, AT THE

UNGA. HE ACKNOWLEDGED THAT SOME OF THE ASEAN COUNTRIES,

SUCH AS THAILAND, FAVOR FURTHER ACTION, BUT THAILAND HAS

AGREED TO FOLLOW VIETNAM\'S LEAD. ANY ASEAN APPROACH TO

BURMA WILL BE MADE THROUGH VIETNAM. (NOTE: CHI\'S ATTITUDE

SUGGESTED VIETNAM BELIEVES IT DID ITS DUTY IN THAT REGARD AT

THE UNGA. END NOTE.)

5. COMMENT: THE APPARENT DECISION NOT TO PRESS THE IDEA

OF TROIKA INVOLVEMENT IN BURMA WITH ANY VIGOR CLEARLY HAS

VIETNAM\'S IMPRINT ON IT. THE RESULTS OF THIS FIRST TEST OF

HANOI\'S WILL AS ASC AND TROIKA CHAIR ARE NOT SURPRISING,

DEMONSTRATING VIETNAM\'S EVER-CAUTIOUS NATURE AND AVERSION TO

THE TYPE OF OUTSIDE INVOLVEMENT IT WOULD BE LOATHE TO ACCEPT

IN A SIMILAR CIRCUMSTANCE. EMBASSY WILL FOLLOW UP ON THE

ISSUE WITH OTHER ASEAN EMBASSIES TO GAIN THEIR PERSPECTIVES;

FOR NOW, HOWEVER, OUR HUNCH IS THAT NOT MUCH SHOULD BE

EXPECTED FROM THE TROIKA ON BURMA WHILE VIETNAM IS ITS

CHAIR. END COMMENT.
