YOUR EXCELLENCY, IN RESPONSE TO YOUR FAX WHICH I

RECEIVED AT 9:30 PM TODAY ENCLOSING PROPOSALS BY THE

REBEL SERB LEADERSHIP IN KNIN, I STATE THE FOLLOWING:

1. IT IS SURPRISING THAT YOU MAKE NO MENTION OF

UNCRO -- THE UNITED NATIONS\' PEACE KEEPING FORCES,

WHICH UNDER THAT NAME GOT THE MANDATE IN THE REPUBLIC

OF CROATIA BY A UN RESOLUTION, WHILE YOU MENTION THE

KNIN LEADERSHIP AND RSK;

2. WE FIND IT UNACCEPTABLE THAT UNCRO BE DEPLOYED ONLY

ALONG THE BORDER TOWARDS THE BIHAC POCKET. OUR DEMAND

WAS AND STILL IS THAT UNCRO TAKE OVER THE CONTROL OF

THE INTERNATIONALLY RECOGNIZED BORDERS BETWEEN THE
