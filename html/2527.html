SUBJECT: MFA ON ASEAN PHUKET MEETING --

- VIETNAM WANTS ASEAN "TERRORISM FREE"

REF: A. BANGKOK 1461 B. FBIS SEP20020225000071

1. (SBU) SUMMARY: FM NIEN MADE A STRONG PITCH FOR A

"TERRORISM-FREE" ASEAN REGION WITHIN 15 YEARS AT THE RECENT

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 02 HANOI 00430 270810Z

PHUKET INFORMAL DISCUSSIONS. THE FOREIGN MINISTERS AGREED

IN PRINCIPLE ON STRENGTHENING COUNTER-TERRORISM EFFORTS

THROUGH ENHANCED LAW ENFORCEMENT COOPERATION, IMPROVED

INFORMATION SHARING, AND OTHER MULTI-LATERAL EFFORTS SUCH AS

CONTINUED MONITORING OF SUSPECT FINANCIAL TRANSFERS AND THE

TRANSIT OF SUSPECT INDIVIDUALS WITHIN AND THROUGH THE

REGION. FM NIEN ALSO LINKED THE REDUCTION OF TERRORIST

THREATS TO THE NEED FOR POVERTY ALLEVIATION. END SUMMARY.

2. (SBU) TERRORIST FREE ZONE: ACCORDING TO MFA ASEAN

DEPARTMENT OFFICIAL LE CHI DUNG, FOREIGN MINISTER NGYUEN DY

NIEN AT THE FEBRUARY 21 ASEAN FOREIGN MINISTERS\' MEETING IN

PHUKET URGED HIS COLLEAGUES TO STRIVE TO ERADICATE TERRORISM

WITHIN ASEAN OVER THE NEXT 10-15 YEARS. FM NIEN PUSHED FOR

SUCH A BENCHMARK SO THAT ALL THE ASEAN MEMBERS HAD A COMMON

LONG-TERM GOAL TOWARD WHICH TO STRIVE WHEN COMBATING

TERRORISM, DUNG CLAIMED, NOTING THAT VIETNAM WANTS FUTURE

ASEAN COUNTER-TERRORISM EFFORTS TO BE MULTILATERAL IN FOCUS.

DUNG ADDED THAT FM NIEN ENVISIONED THE FOREIGN MINISTRIES OF

ASEAN MEMBERS LEADING THE COORDINATION OF FUTURE COUNTER

TERRORIST EFFORTS. FM NIEN ALSO REPORTEDLY SUGGESTED THAT

ASEAN MEMBERS\' NATIONAL POLICE FORCES COOPERATE IN SHARING

INFORMATION AND TACTICS THAT HAVE PROVEN SUCCESSFUL IN

COMBATING TERRORISM WITHIN THEIR OWN COUNTRIES. IN RESPONSE

TO A QUESTION, DUNG WAS CAREFUL TO STATE THAT THIS WOULD NOT

NECESSARILY INVOLVE SHARING OF INTELLIGENCE, BUT RATHER

SHARING USEFUL AND PRACTICAL EXPERIENCE.

3. (SBU) IMPLEMENTATION: VIETNAM FORESEES NEW CONCRETE

COUNTER-TERRORISM MEASURES TO MATERIALIZE BY THIS SUMMER,

DUNG NOTED. DURING UPCOMING ASEAN MEETINGS IN BRUNEI AND

UNCLASSIFIED

KUALA LUMPUR, SPECIALISTS WILL COME TOGETHER TO DISCUSS

FUTURE LAW-ENFORCEMENT AND MFA COOPERATION IN GREATER

DETAIL. DUNG EXPRESSED VIETNAM\'S HOPE THAT, DURING THESE

MEETINGS, STRONGER CONCRETE MEASURES WILL BE AGREED UPON IN

ORDER TO BEGIN IMPLEMENTATION.

4. (SBU) POVERTY ALLIEVIATION: HOWEVER, FM NIEN\'S PRIMARY

FOCUS FOR COMBATING INTERNATIONAL TERRORISM CENTERED ON

POVERTY ALLEVIATION, ACCORDING TO DUNG. FM NIEN ARGUED THAT

MANY OF THE RANKS OF TERRORIST CELLS FOUND IN OTHER ASEAN

STATES, NOTABLY INDONESIA AND MALAYSIA, WERE FILLED WITH THE

POOREST SEGMENTS OF SOCIETY. HE ALSO REPORTEDLY ARGUED THAT

THE PEOPLE ATTRACTED TO TERRORISM WERE DRAWN TO THESE GROUPS

DUE TO LACK OF ECONOMIC OPPORTUNITIES AS WELL AS

DISGRUNTLEMENT WITH THEIR GOVERNMENTS DUE TO THEIR POOR

ECONOMIC STATUS. DUNG SAID THAT FM NIEN HAD ARGUED THAT THE

BEST MEANS OF COMBATING TERRORISM WAS TO IMPROVE ECONOMIC

OPPORTUNITIES FOR THESE POTENTIAL RECRUITS/MILITANTS, AND,

IN SO DOING, LIMIT THE APPEAL OF TERRORISM. DUNG CLAIMED

THAT FM NIEN FIRMLY BELIEVES THAT IMPROVED ECONOMIC

OPPORTUNITIES FOR THE POOREST PEOPLES OF ASEAN, COMBINED

WITH A COORDINATED LAW ENFORCEMENT APPROACH, WILL LIMIT THE

GROWTH OF TERRORISM WITHIN ASEAN.

5. (SBU) COMMENT: FM NIEN\'S FOCUS ON IMPROVING THE

ECONOMIC SITUATION OF ASEAN\'S POOREST CITIZENS SHOULD NOT BE

SURPRISING, GIVEN THAT VIETNAM HAS BEEN AT THE FOREFRONT OF

SHAPING THE DISCUSSIONS ABOUT SHRINKING THE "DEVELOPMENT

GAP" AMONG ASEAN MEMBER STATES. VIETNAM\'S APPARENT

WILLINGNESS WITHIN ASEAN TO SPEAK OUT FORCEFULLY ON COUNTER-

TERRORISM IS NONETHELESS A WELCOME DEVELOPMENT.
