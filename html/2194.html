SUBJECT: PUBLIC SECURITY PUBLISHES BOOK AGAINST BIN LADEN

1. (U) EMBASSY HAS OBTAINED A COPY OF A BOOK PUBLISHED (IN

VIETNAMESE) BY THE PEOPLE\'S PUBLIC SECURITY PRESS SOMETIME

SINCE SEPTEMBER 11 ENTITLED "THE LIFE OF THE INTERNATIONAL

TERRORIST OSAMA BIN LADEN (MANY SECRET THINGS)." IT

INCLUDES PHOTOS OF THE SUSPECTED TERRORISTS CONNECTED WITH

THE SEPTEMBER 11 HIJACKINGS, ACCOUNTS OF BIN LADEN\'S PAST

LIFE, APPARENTLY FACTUAL ACCOUNTS OF HIS ACTIVITIES SINCE

UNCLASSIFIED

1979, AN ORGANIZATIONAL CHART PLACING HIM DIRECTLY OVER AL

QAEDA WITH LINKS TO OTHER GROUPS AS WELL AS COMMITTEES ON

RELIGION, DIPLOMATIC RELATIONS, INFORMATION, AND FINANCE

WHICH HAVE "BASES" (OR "FOUNDATIONS") IN COUNTRIES/PLACES

INCLUDING THE CANADA, US, FRANCE, GERMANY, SPAIN, NORWAY,

SWEDEN, NETHERLANDS, BELGIUM, CHECHNYA, ALBANIA, MAURITIUS,

KAZAKHSTAN, UZBEKISTAN, TAJIKISTAN, ALGERIA, TUNISIA, MALI,

NIGERIA, CONGO, UGANDA, ETHIOPIA, KENYA, TANZANIA, SOMALIA,

ERITREA, OMAN, QATAR, SUDAN, EGYPT, LIBYA, IRAN, LEBANON,

JORDAN. AFGHANISTAN, PAKISTAN, INDIA, CHINA, BANGLADESH,

BURMA, PHILIPPINES, MALAYSIA, AND INDONESIA. IT PROVIDES

WHAT IT DESCRIBES AS INFORMATION FROM A JUNE 1999 ARREST

WARRANT FOR BIN LADEN, AS WELL AS THE TEXT OF PRESIDENT

BUSH\'S SEPTEMBER 20 REMARKS TO CONGRESS. IT ALSO DISCUSSES

THE TALIBAN.

2. (U) EMBASSY CONTACTS INDICATE THAT THERE HAS BEEN A

BRISK SALE IN THIS PUBLICATION, AND IT CONTINUES TO BE

AVAILABLE FOR SALE.

3. (U) COMMENT: IT IS NOTEWORTHY THAT THE PUBLIC SECURITY

APPARATUS WOULD PUBLISH WHAT APPEARS TO BE A STRAIGHT-

FORWARD AND CRITICAL ACCOUNT OF BIN LADEN AND HIS ROLE IN

INTERNATIONAL TERRORISM. THIS INITIATIVE IS HELPFUL IN

GETTING THE WORD OUT TO THE VIETNAMESE PEOPLE ABOUT THIS

SOURCE OF INTERNATIONAL TERRORISM. EMBASSY DOES NOT HAVE

THE RESOURCES TO TRANSLATE THE ENTIRE TEXT BUT WILL POUCH A

COPY TO THE DEPARTMENT IF REQUESTED.
