CLASSIFIED BY: POLITICAL MINISTER-COUNSELOR TOM COUNTRYMAN FOR

REASONS 1.5 (B) AND (D)

1. (C) MFA OFFICIALS IN THE DISARMAMENT BUREAU APOLOGIZED FOR

THEIR DELAY IN RESPONDING TO REFTEL QUERIES AND HAVE NOW

SHARED WITH US THE POINTS BELOW.

2. (C) STATUS OF DISCUSSIONS WITH RUSSIANS: THE GOI MET WITH

THE GOR AT THE POLITICAL DIRECTORS LEVEL IN MOSCOW JULY

15-16, HAD AN INFORMAL MEETING IN OTTAWA ON SEPT. 26, AND

ANOTHER MEETING IN MOSCOW ON OCT. 14. THE LATEST MEETING

INVOLVED THE NATIONAL MUNITIONS AGENCY, THE MINISTRY OF

ATOMIC ENERGY (MINATOM), AND AMBASSADOR LYSSENKO AT THE MID.

THE GOI SUBMITTED TO THE GOR A DRAFT TEXT FOR A "FRAMEWORK

AGREEMENT" AND HOPES TO START NEGOTIATIONS SOON. THE

NATIONAL MUNITIONS AGENCY SUGGESTED THAT ITALY TAKE THE LEAD

FOR THE CW DESTRUCTION PLAN PROJECT IN POCHEP. THE MINATOM

WAS NOT YET PREPARED TO MAKE SPECIFIC PROPOSALS.

3. (C) GUIDELINES AND PRACTICAL STEPS FOR IMPLEMENTATION:

THE GOI, THROUGH THE FRAMEWORK AGREEMENT, IS SEEKING TO

TRANSPOSE INTO A LEGALLY BINDING TEXT THE POLITICAL

COMMITMENTS CONTAINED IN THE KANANASKIS GUIDELINES. IN

PARTICULAR, THE GOI ASSIGNS GREAT RELEVANCE TO ISSUES RELATED

TO LIABILITY, TAXATION, FISCAL ASPECTS, AND PRIVILEGES AND

IMMUNITIES WHICH WOULD BE GRANTED TO REPRESENTATIVES OF ITALY

WORKING ON SPECIFIC PROJECTS.

4. (C) TASK FORCE: THE GOI ESTABLISHED A TASK FORCE, LED BY

THE MFA AND INCLUDING ALL RELEVANT AGENCIES AND MINISTRIES,

IN PARTICULAR THE MINISTRY OF PRODUCTIVE ACTIVITIES. THE

TASK FORCE IS CURRENTLY FOCUSING ON THE DESTRUCTION OF CW AND

THE DECOMMISSIONING OF NUCLEAR SUBMARINES. THE TASK FORCE

HAS UNDERSCORED THAT ITALY\'S FINANCIAL CONTRIBUTION SHOULD BE

LINKED TO SPECIFIC AND DETAILED PROJECTS.

5. (C) CHEMICAL WEAPONS: SCHUCH\'YE (GAS PIPELINE): ITALY IS

CURRENTLY INVOLVED IN THE CONSTRUCTION OF A GAS PIPELINE IN

ORDER TO SUPPLY ENERGY TO THE DESTRUCTION FACILITY AND THE

TOWN OF SCHUCH\'YE. THE GOI IS EXPLORING THE POSSIBILITY OF

EXTENDING THE PIPELINE.

6. (C) CHEMICAL WEAPONS: POCHEP (DE-TOXIFICATION OF NERVE

AGENTS): THE RUSSIANS HAVE PROPOSED IN PRINCIPLE THAT THE

ITALIANS TAKE THE LEAD IN THIS PROJECT, WHICH WOULD PROVIDE

FOR THE DETOXIFICATION OF NERVE AGENTS, WITH SUBSEQUENT

TRANSFER TO THE SCHUCH\'YE DESTRUCTION FACILITY. ITALY IS

STILL AWAITING SPECIFIC RUSSIAN PROPOSALS.

7. (C) NUCLEAR SUBMARINES: NO SPECIFIC PROJECT PROPOSAL HAS

BEEN SUBMITTED TO ITALY. THE GOI HAS ASKED RUSSIA FOR MORE

DETAILS ON THE SAEDA BAY PROJECT. THE POSSIBLE SUPPLY OF

HEAVY CRANES IS BEING CONSIDERED.
