SUBJECT: UAEG ASSURES HIGH-LEVEL PARTICIPATION FOR UN

HOSTED CONSULTATIONS ON IRAQ\'S RECONSTRUCTION

REF: STATE 163448

1. (U) Classified by Acting DCM Thomas E. Williams,

for reasons 1.5 (b) and (d).

2. (U) This is an action request; please see

paragraph 4.

3. (C) Acting DCM raised reftel points 6/14 with UAE

Ministry of Foreign Affairs Assistant Undersecretary

for Political Affairs Abdullah Rashid Al-Nuami, who

confirmed that he would attend the June 24 United

Nations technical consultations along with UAE

Ministry of Finance Assistant Undersecretary for

Revenue and Budget Khalid Al-Bustani. Other UAE

officials may also be planning to attend; we will

inform Washington agencies of the full delegation

ASAP.

4. (C) Comment and action request: As a member of the

informal working group on Iraq\'s reconstruction, the

UAEG is strongly committed to Iraq\'s economic recovery

and is looking to play a useful role. Al-Nuami would

appreciate the opportunity to meet appropriate U.S.

officials on the sidelines of the United Nations

meetings, including Undersecretary Alan Larson and DoD

Comptroller Dr. Dov Zakheim, to further discuss UAE

financial and humanitarian support to Baghdad, as well

as broader Iraq political and security issues. Al-

Nuami indicated that he might be interested in a short

side-trip to Washington should bilaterals not be

convenient on the margins of the U.N. conference. If

so, we suggest that Al-Nuami also meet with new NEA

Deputy Assistant Secretary Philo Dibble. End comment

and action request.

Wahba
