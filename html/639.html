INTRODUCTION

------------

1. (U) THIS IS ONE IN A SERIES OF REPORTS OF

INTERVIEWS WITH WAR CRIMES VICTIMS CONDUCTED IN

LATE 1993 IN MAOCA, BOSNIA-HERCEGOVINA, BY A

DART/ZAGREB FIELD REP AND AN AMCIT IRC REP WHILE

THEY WERE IN THE AREA ASSESSING HUMANITARIAN

ASSISTANCE NEEDS.

-------------------

WITNESS INFORMATION

-------------------

2. (LOU) ZEJCIR OSMIC (STRICTLY PROTECT), HEREAFTER

REFERRED TO AS THE WITNESS, IS A BOSNIAN MUSLIM

FROM THE BRCKO MUNICIPALITY. HE WAS BORN ON MAY

19, 1945, AND WAS A CIVILIAN SHOP ASSISTANT AT THE

MERKUR COMPANY IN BRCKO AT THE TIME OF HIS ARREST

BY BOSNIAN SERB FORCES ON MAY 1, 1992.

---------------------------------

---------------------------------

3. (U) THE WITNESS, A SHOP ASSISTANT IN BRCKO, WAS

VISITING HIS BROTHER\'S HOME IN BRCKO ON MAY 1, 1992.

HE AND HIS BROTHER SAW MANY SERB SOLDIERS IN BLACK

UNIFORMS WHO SAID THEY WERE "ARKAN\'S MEN." THE
