SUBJECT: GOT Intends to Participate in Kimberley

Process

Ref: State 208989

Oya Karakas, MFA Head of Department for Multilateral

Economic Affairs, told Econoff July 22 that the GOT

intends to participate in the Kimberley Process

governing trade in rough diamonds (reftel). However, it

has not completed domestic procedures necessary to

implement the Process\' certification scheme, which may

require a Council of Ministers decree or new

legislation. Neither measure will be implemented before

the July 31 end of the "period of tolerance". Econoff

encouraged Karakas to communicate Turkey\'s intentions to

the Kimberley Process Chair, and Karakas agreed to do

this. Deutsch
