SUBJECT: GOVERNMENT GAZETTES LIST OF 804 FARMS FOR

COMPULSORY ACQUISITION

REF: A). HARARE 2820

B). HARARE 1914 (NOTAL)

CLASSIFIED BY DEPUTY CHIEF OF MISSION EARL M. IRVING FOR

REASONS 1.5 (B) AND (D).

1. (U) ON JUNE 2 THE GOVERNMENT OF ZIMBABWE PLACED A

NOTICE IN AN EXTRAORDINARY EDITION OF THE "GOVERNMENT

GAZETTE," ANNOUNCING ITS INTENTION TO ACQUIRE COMPULSORILY

804 FARMS FOR RESETTLEMENT. THE ANNOUNCEMENT ADVISED

FARMERS WISHING TO CONTEST THE ACQUISITIONS TO LODGE

THEIR OBJECTIONS IN WRITING WITH THE MINISTER OF LANDS

AND AGRICULTURE BY JULY 2. THEREAFTER, FARMERS HAVE AN

ADDITIONAL POSSIBILITY FOR APPEAL THROUGH THE PROVINCIAL

ADMINISTRATIVE COURTS, AS THEY WILL FOR ANY SUBSEQUENT

DISAGREEMENTS ON COMPENSATION. IF NO OBJECTIONS ARE

CONFIDENTIAL

LODGED OR THE APPEAL PROCESS HAS BEEN EXHAUSTED, THE

EFFECTIVE OWNERSHIP OF THE FARM/LAND IN QUESTION

TRANSFERS FROM THE FARMER TO THE STATE, ONCE THE STATE

ISSUES AN ACQUISITION ORDER. THE TITLE DEED, HOWEVER,

IS NOT TRANSFERRED UNTIL A BILL OF SALE IS EXECUTED

PURSUANT TO A NEGOTIATED PRICE.

2. (C) COMMERCIAL FARMERS\' UNION PRESIDENT TIM HENWOOD

TOLD US JUNE 2 THAT NO FARMER IN HIS RIGHT MIND WOULD

RISK HIS LIFE, AT THIS TIME OF RAMPANT INTIMIDATION, BY

RAISING OBJECTIONS TO THE GOVERNMENT\'S MOVE.

3. (C) COMMENT: THE AMENDED ENABLING LEGISLATION (REF

A), COUPLED WITH THE GAZETTING OF THE LIST OF FARMS,

CHANGES THE RULES OF THE GAME AND EFFECTIVELY SCUTTLES

THE UNITED NATIONS-LED EFFORT TO WORK OUT A MECHANISM TO

FUND ZIMBABWE\'S LAND REFORM PROGRAM AHEAD OF THIS STEP.

THE DONORS NOW WILL HAVE TO STAND BACK AND ASSESS HOW

BEST TO RESPOND TO THE ZIMBABWEAN GOVERNMENT\'S ACTIONS.

END COMMENT.
