SUBJECT: DUTCH ATTENDANCE AT ICG MEETING ON BURMA

REF: SECSTATE 200930

The Dutch will be represented at the ICG meeting on Burma by

a member of their UN Mission in New York. A representative

from the Hague will not/not attend the meeting. MFA

South-East Asia policy advisor Roeland Kollen noted to PolOff

that because of staffing constraints, the Dutch UN mission

plans to send an officer from within their political section.

However, he will communicate reftel request for high-level

participation to their UN Mission in New York.
