SUBJECT: RAED HIJAZI DEATH PENALTY CONVICTION UPHELD

1. (U) The State Security Court on January 5 upheld the death

penalty conviction of Jordanian-American Raed Hijazi for

conspiring to bomb tourist sites in Jordan during the

Millennium celebrations. An Embassy Legal contact has

confirmed press reports that Hijazi will be able to appeal

the decision to the Court of Cassation (Supreme Court). If

Hijazi\'s death sentence is upheld through the appeals

process, King Abdullah also has the power to commute the

sentence to life imprisonment. Should Hijazi decide to

appeal the State Security Court\'s decision, the appeals

process will likely take several months. We will continue to

follow this case and will report any new developments as they

occur.
