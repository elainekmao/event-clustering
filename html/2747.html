1. (U) EMBASSY ROME CONCLUDES THAT IT WOULD BE INAPPROPRIATE

TO DELIVER REF A DEMARCHE TO ITALY. THE GOI IS ALREADY

MAKING SIGNIFICANT FINANCIAL, MILITARY, AND TROOP

CONTRIBUTIONS TO OEF, THE STABILIZATION OF THE BALKANS, ISAF,

THE RECONSTRUCTION OF AFGHANISTAN, AND THE PROTECTION OF THE

FORMER KING OF AFGHANISTAN, IN ADDITION TO CONTRIBUTIONS TO

OTHER MULTILATERAL AND PEACEKEEPING MISSIONS (REF B).

---

OEF

---

2. (U) ITALY HAS COMMITTED SIGNIFICANT ASSETS FOR OPERATION

ENDURING FREEDOM (OEF), INCLUDING: ITALY\'S ONE AIRCRAFT

CARRIER "GARIBALDI", EIGHT HARRIER JETS AND SIX TORNADO

RECONNAISSANCE AIRCRAFT. THE ITALIAN MILITARY HAS DEPLOYED

ONE OF THE FOUR LARGEST FORCES IN THE COALITION SUPPORTING

OEF, INCLUDING OVER 2,500 MILITARY PERSONNEL, AND IS ONE OF

THE FEW COALITION ALLIES TO MOVE ITS ASSETS TO THE THEATER

UNASSISTED. ADDITIONALLY, WITHIN DAYS OF THE EVENTS OF

SEPTEMBER 11, ITALIAN AUTHORITIES FACILITATED, SIMPLIFIED, OR

OTHERWISE EXPEDITED EXISTING PROCEDURES GOVERNING THE TRANSIT

OVER ITALIAN TERRITORY AND OVERFLIGHT OVER ITALIAN AIRSPACE

OF US FORCES ENGAGED IN OEF. THESE ACTIONS WERE TAKEN

WITHOUT PROMPTING BY US OFFICIALS AND WERE CLEARLY THE RESULT

OF DIRECTION FROM THE HIGHEST LEVELS OF THE GOI. THE GOI HAS

MADE ITS MILITARY FACILITIES READILY AVAILABLE FOR OEF

DEPLOYMENTS TO THE THEATER, A CONTRIBUTION THE US HAS MADE

ISAF

----

3. (C) ITALY HAS DEPLOYED ABOUT 400 PERSONNEL TO THE

INTERNATIONAL SECURITY ASSISTANCE FORCE (ISAF) TO HELP

STABILIZE THE SITUATION IN KABUL. THE GOI RECENTLY AGREED TO

EXTEND ITS COMMITMENT TO ISAF WHEN ISAF\'S ORIGINAL MANDATE

WAS EXTENDED DESPITE ITALY\'S EXTENSIVE TROOP COMMITMENTS

ELSEWHERE. ISAF HAS ESTABLISHED TWO QUICK-REACTION UNITS OF

PLATOON SIZE. ONE OF THESE UNITS WILL BE MADE UP ENTIRELY OF

ITALIAN CARABINIERI. THESE UNITS WILL BE DEDICATED TO

ASSISTING THE FORMER KING OF AFGHANISTAN IN THE EVENT OF

TROUBLE.

RECONSTRUCTION OF AFGHANISTAN

-----------------------------

4. (U) AT THE TOKYO AFGHAN RECONSTRUCTION CONFERENCE, ITALY

MADE AN INITIAL PLEDGE OF 40-45 MILLION EUROS (ABOUT 36-40

MILLION DOLLARS). ITALY HAS ALSO COMMITTED SOME 35 MILLION

EUROS (ABOUT 31 MILLION DOLLARS) TO BILATERAL ASSISTANCE TO

AFGHANISTAN. SOME OF THE BILATERAL EFFORTS ITALY EXPECTS TO

UNDERTAKE IN AFGHANISTAN INCLUDE SUPPORT FOR THE

RE-ESTABLISHMENT OF AFGHAN MINISTRIES, COOPERATION IN POLICE

INTERNATIONAL ORGANIZATIONS OPERATING IN THE AREA. THE GOI

PLANS TO ASSIST IN CULTURAL REHABILITATION AND REVITALIZATION

OF AFGHANISTAN\'S JUDICIAL PROGRAMS.

BALKANS

--------

7. (U) ITALY IS THE SECOND LARGEST TROOP CONTRIBUTOR IN THE

BALKANS, WITH ABOUT 7,500 SERVING IN BOSNIA (SFOR) AND KOSOVO

(KFOR), AND ANOTHER 1,200 AS PART OF COMZWEST IN ALBANIA (REF

D). ITALIAN CARABINIERI CONSTITUTE MORE THAN HALF OF THE

MULTINATIONAL SPECIALIZED UNITS (MSUS) NEEDED FOR

LAW-ENFORCEMENT TASKS IN BOSNIA. THE GOI IS ONE OF THE

LEADING BILATERAL PROVIDERS OF LAW ENFORCEMENT AID TO ALBANIA

WHERE THE DROP IN CLANDESTINE IMMIGRATION IS LARGELY DUE TO

ITALIAN POLICE ASSISTANCE (REF E).

8. (U) ITALY COMMANDS COMZWEST, ONE OF FIVE SECTORS IN KOSOVO

CONTROLLED BY KFOR. AN ITALIAN GENERAL (GENERAL CABIGIOSU)

ABLY COMMANDED KFOR FROM OCTOBER 2000-APRIL 2001, AND ANOTHER

ITALIAN IS SCHEDULED TO TAKE COMMAND OF KFOR IN OCTOBER 2002.

9. (U) THE GOI WAS AMONG THE FIRST TO OFFER TROOPS (750) FOR

TASK FORCE ESSENTIAL HARVEST TO RECOVER WEAPONS FROM ARMED

GROUPS IN MACEDONIA IN 2001. ITALIAN SOLDIERS ARE CURRENTLY

ENGAGED IN TASK FORCE FOX (TFF), WHOSE MISSION IS TO PROVIDE

SECURITY FOR OSCE OBSERVERS IN MACEDONIA. ITALY HAD OFFERED

TO TAKE COMMAND OF TFF THIS SPRING, HOWEVER GERMANY AGREED TO

RETAIN COMMAND UNTIL JUNE.

ITALY IS DOING ITS BEST

-----------------------

10. (U) ITALY CONTINUES TO DEMONSTRATE ITS FIRM COMMITMENT TO

THE GLOBAL WAR ON TERRORISM, TO THE NATO ALLIANCE AND TO

OTHER AVENUES FOR FOSTERING SECURITY IN EUROPE AND BEYOND,

INCLUDING PARTICIPATION IN BOTH UN AND NON-UN PEACEKEEPING

FORCES THROUGHOUT THE WORLD. SINCE THE SEPTEMBER 11

TERRORIST ATTACKS, THE GOI HAS BEEN A FULL PARTNER WITH THE

US IN ANTI-TERRORISM MEASURES IN THE DIPLOMATIC,

INTELLIGENCE, LAW ENFORCEMENT, FINANCIAL CONTROL AND OTHER

AREAS. THE MOST VISIBLE DISPLAY OF THIS COMMITMENT WAS THE

DISPATCH OF TROOPS IN SUPPORT OF OEF, A DEPLOYMENT ENDORSED

BY NEARLY 90% OF THE ITALIAN PARLIAMENT DESPITE THE

CONSIDERABLE STRAIN THAT ITALY\'S ACTIVE MILITARY ROLE PLACES

ON THE ITALIAN BUDGET (REF B).

11. (C) GIVEN ITALY\'S SUBSTANTIAL CONTRIBUTIONS TO ASSISTING

THE COALITION IN THE FIGHT AGAINST TERRORISM AS WELL AS TO

STABILIZE SE EUROPE, A FURTHER APPROACH AS OUTLINED IN REF A

WOULD NOT ONLY BE UNLIKELY TO SUCCEED BUT WOULD CONVEY AN

INAPPROPRIATE IMPRESSION: THAT THE USG LACKS APPRECIATION FOR

ITALY\'S CONTRIBUTIONS.
