1. THIS IS AN ACTION CABLE. SEE PARA 9.

2. (C) SUMMARY: ON FEB 11, EMBOFFS CALLED ON COUNSELOR

GIORGIO DI PIETROGIACOMO, MFA\'S OFFICE DIRECTOR FOR SOUTH

AMERICA, TO EXCHANGE INFORMATION ON DISCUSSIONS WITH

ARGENTINE FOREIGN MINISTER RUCKAUF IN BOTH WASHINGTON AND

ROME. ITALY HAS SIGNIFICANT INTERESTS IN ARGENTINA BUT IS

NOT PREPARED TO PROVIDE BILATERAL ASSISTANCE UNTIL THE GOA

SATISFIES IMF CRITERIA. MEANWHILE, THE GOI IS PROVIDING AID

TO ITALIANS LIVING IN ARGENTINA, SHORING UP SECURITY AT ITS

MISSION, AND NOMINATING A PROMINENT FINANCE OFFICIAL TO AN

INTERNATIONAL "FRIENDS OF ARGENTINA" COMMITTEE. END SUMMARY.

3. (U) ITALY\'S INTEREST IN ARGENTINA STEMS FROM THE FACT

THAT ABOUT ONE-THIRD OF ARGENTINA\'S CITIZENS ARE OF ITALIAN

ORIGIN, PIETROGIACOMO SAID. ITALIAN PASSPORT HOLDERS

NUMBERED 610,000, WHILE THOSE WITH THE RIGHT TO ACQUIRE

ITALIAN CITIZENSHIP WERE ESTIMATED AT 10-15 MILLION. ITALY

MAINTAINS AN EMBASSY AND NINE CONSULATES IN ARGENTINA. IN A

MEETING BETWEEN RUCKAUF AND THE HEAD OF ITALY\'S CONFERENCE OF

REGIONAL PRESIDENTS (WHO ARE ROUGHLY EQUIVALENT TO U.S.

GOVERNORS), BOTH SIDES AGREED THAT THEY SHOULD SEEK TO

IMPROVE THE LOT OF ITALIANS (AND ARGENTINES) IN ARGENTINA,

NOT FACILITATE IMMIGRATION.

4. (C) BECAUSE ITALY IS SIGNIFICANTLY EXPOSED IN

ARGENTINA, THE GOI IS CONCERNED THAT ALL INTERNATIONAL

INVESTMENTS BE TREATED EQUITABLY AND IN A TRANSPARENT MANNER.

SACE, EQUIVALENT TO THE U.S. EXPORT-IMPORT BANK, HAS 675M

USD IN EXPOSURE. OF THIS, 103M OF PAYMENTS COMING DUE IN

NEXT FEW MONTHS. SACE VOTED ON FEB 4 TO RESCHEDULE THESE

PAYMENTS. IN RESPONSE TO THE GOI\'S QUERY ABOUT THE NEW

GOVERNMENT RULES ON BANKRUPTCY, RUCKAUF RESPONDED THAT THEY

INTENDED TO REVISE THEM. IN OUR FEB 8 CONVERSATION,

PIETROGIACOMO REPORTED HAVING RECEIVED INFORMATION THAT THEY

WERE DOING SO.

5. (C) PIETROGIACOMO SAID THAT THE GOI HAD OFFERED

ARGENTINA ITALY\'S SUPPORT. AT PRESENT, HOWEVER, THE GOI

AGREED WITH THE IMF\'S TOUGH LINE THAT ARGENTINA\'S CURRENT

ECONOMIC PLAN IS INSUFFICIENT TO JUSTIFY EITHER BILATERAL OR

MULTILATERAL ASSISTANCE. ONCE ARGENTINA HAS AN AGREEMENT

WITH THE IMF AND PROVIDES THE GOI WITH A DETAILED REFORM

PLAN, A BILATERAL AID PACKAGE WOULD BE CONSIDERED.

6. (U) IN JANUARY, ITALY INCREASED A FUND IT MAINTAINS FOR

HELPING ITS CITIZENS RESIDENT ABROAD. OF 20M EUROS NOW

ALLOTTED TO THE FUND, 10M EUROS WERE SLOTTED FOR ITALIANS IN

ARGENTINA. THIS MONEY WOULD COVER BASIC NEEDS FOR THE POOR

AND PROVIDE SUPPORT FOR ESTABLISHING SMALL AND MEDIUM

ENTERPRISES THAT COULD CREATE NEW JOBS. THE GOI HAS ALSO

SENT MEDICINE TO ARGENTINA.

7. (C) BECAUSE THE GOI IS CONCERNED ABOUT THE SECURITY

RISKS INHERENT IN THE TUMULTUOUS POLITICAL ENVIRONMENT, IT

HAS INCREASED PRECAUTIONS AT ITS FACILITIES IN ARGENTINA.

PIETROGIACOMO NOTED THAT, FORTUNATELY, MILITARY TAKEOVER IS

NO LONGER AN OPTION FOR A TROUBLED LATIN AMERICAN COUNTRY.

8. (C) RUCKAUF REQUESTED THAT ITALY BE REPRESENTED IN THE
