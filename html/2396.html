SUBJECT: PROGRAM EVALUATION: DVC WITH ITALIAN NATIONAL

COUNCIL ON ECONOMICS AND LABOR (CNEL), NOVEMBER 27, 2001

1. DESCRIPTION OF ACTIVITY: A DVC ON ECONOMIC ISSUES

ORGANIZED IN COLLABORATION BETWEEN PA ROME AND THE ITALIAN

NATIONAL COUNCIL ON ECONOMICS AND LABOR (CNEL),

A CONSULTING BODY TO THE PARLIAMENT AND TO THE ITALIAN

GOVERNMENT ON ECONOMIC AND

LABOR ISSUES. PA MILAN WAS "PASSIVE" ON THE PROGRAM.

2. DATES: FIRST QUARTER, FY-2002; WEDNESDAY, NOVEMBER 27,

2001.

3. JUSTIFICATION/OBJECTIVE: CNEL IS ITALY\'S HIGHEST-LEVEL

ECONOMIC RESEARCH INSTITUTE

AND IS OFFICIALLY A COMPONENT OF THE ITALIAN GOVERNMENT.

THE NEW PRESIDENT IS TRYING

TO MODERNIZE AND INTERNATIONALIZE THE INSTITUTION AND

APPROACHED PA WITH A PROPOSAL

TO CO-HOST A SERIES OF DVC PROGRAMS ON GLOBALIZATION. THIS

FIRST PROGRAM WAS

ORIGINALLY DESIGNED TO LOOK AT GLOBALIZATION OF MARKETS;

HOWEVER, SINCE SEPTEMBER 11,

WE DECIDED INSTEAD TO FOCUS ON THE STATE OF THE U.S. ECONOMY

AND THE IMPACT THE ATTACKS

HAVE HAD ON BOTH U.S. CONSUMERS AND PRODUCERS.

4. MPP UMBRELLA THEMES: PROMOTING ECONOMIC PROSPERITY,

SUBTHEME: GLOBAL

ECONOMIC GROWTH; COOPERATION ON GLOBAL ISSUES, SUBTHEME:

TERRORISM

5. AUDIENCES REACHED: ITALIAN LABOR EXPERTS, GOVERNMENT

OFFICIALS, BUSINESSPERSONS

AND ECONOMISTS, INTERNATIONAL DIPLOMATS, JOURNALISTS.

6. RESULT/IMPACT: EXCELLENT. THE DVC WAS ENTITLED

"ECONOMICS AFTER SEPTEMBER 11: MORE STATE, LESS MARKET?"

THE THREE U.S. SPEAKERS, RON WEXLER, INTERNATIONAL ECONOMIST

FROM MERRILL LYNCH, WILLIAM CANIS OF THE MANUFACTURERS

INSTITUTE, THE NATIONAL MANUFACTURERS ASSOCIATION, AND
