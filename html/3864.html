SUBJECT: GALILEO DISCUSSION WITH ALENIA\'S VIRIGLIO

REF: ROME 04292

1. (C) SUMMARY: EST SECTION RECENTLY MET WITH GIUSEPPE

VIRIGLIO, CEO FOR THE SPACE DIVISION OF ITALIAN AEROSPACE

COMPANY ALENIA SPAZIO. THE EUROPEAN GALILEO GLOBAL

NAVIGATIONAL SATELLITE SYSTEM WAS DISCUSSED IN THE CONTEXT OF

RECENT USG-GOI DISCUSSIONS ON GPS-GALILEO INTEROPERABILITY.

VIRIGLIO PROVIDED SOME INTERESTING INSIGHTS INTO THE ITALIAN

DECISIONMAKING PROCESS ON GALILEO, BUT HE ALSO RESPONDED IN A

MUCH LESS SYMPATHETIC MANNER TO USG CONCERNS ABOUT THE

OVERLAY OF ONE OF THE GPS MILITARY SIGNALS (M-CODE) BY THE

PROPOSED GALILEO PUBLIC REGULATED SERVICE (PRS). END SUMMARY.

2. (C) ESTOFFS BROACHED THE ISSUE OF GALILEO BY EXPRESSING

THEIR REGRET THAT THE TIMING OF THE LATE JULY 2002 USG GPS

DELEGATION VISIT (SUBJECT OF REFTEL) CONFLICTED WITH

VIRIGLIO\'S TRAVEL TO THE FARNBOROUGH AIR SHOW. THEY BRIEFED

VIRIGLIO ON THE GPS DELEGATION\'S MESSAGE TO THE GOI ON USG

POLICY TOWARD GPS-GALILEO COOPERATION. IN PARTICULAR,

ESTOFFS STRESSED THE USG\'S OPPOSITION TO THE OVERLAY OF THE

M-CODE BY PRS. ALTHOUGH VIRIGLIO DID NOT SEEM TO HAVE AN

INSTITUTIONAL STAKE IN PRS, HE DID NOT SEEM PARTICULARLY

SYMPATHETIC TO USG CONCERNS ABOUT THE POTENTIAL RISKS TO NATO

WARFIGHTING CAPABILITIES. HE INTIMATED THAT FRANCE IS THE

ONLY GALILEO PARTNER INSISTING ON OVERLAYING THE M-CODE, BUT

HE ALSO EXPRESSED SOME SYMPATHY FOR THE VIEW THAT THE US

SHOULD BE PREVENTED FROM BEING ABLE TO UNILATERALLY JAM PRS

UNDER ANY CIRCUMSTANCE AND LARGELY DISMISSED USG ASSESSMENTS

OF THE RISKS TO NATO.

3. (C) WHEN ESTOFFS NOTED USG CONCERNS ABOUT THE POSSIBLE

INCLUSION OF CHINA AS A NON-EUROPEAN PARTNER IN GALILEO,

VIRIGLIO BECAME SOMEWHAT DEFENSIVE. HE RESPONDED THAT THE

EUROPEANS WERE FORCED TO SEEK NON-EUROPEAN CUSTOMERS, SUCH AS

THE CHINESE, BECAUSE THE UNITED STATES, BY INSISTING THAT

GALILEO SHOULD BE A CIVILIAN-USE ONLY SYSTEM, HAD DRASTICALLY

REDUCED THE POOL OF POTENTIAL EUROPEAN INSTITUTIONAL USERS

(THE VARIOUS EU MINISTRIES OF DEFENSE) THAT COULD CONTRIBUTE

TO GALILEO DEVELOPMENT. (EMBASSY COMMENT: ESTOFFS VAINLY

TRIED TO POINT OUT THE INACCURACIES IN VIRIGLIO\'S DESCRIPTION

OF THE EVOLUTION OF GALILEO AS A CIVILIAN-ONLY SYSTEM. IT

SEEMED TO BE MORE A CASE OF SELF-RATIONALIZATION.) HE WAS

UNMOVED BY ESTOFFS EFFORTS TO POINT OUT LEGITIMATE USG

SECURITY CONCERNS BY THE POSSIBILITY OF CHINA DEVELOPING

WEAPONS THAT COULD USE PRS FOR TARGETING.

4. (C) VIRIGLIO PROVIDED SOME INSIGHTS INTO THE ITALIAN

DECISION-MAKING PROCESS ON GALILEO. HE CONFIRMED THAT

COUNSELOR RENATO LIBASSI, A SPECIAL ADVISOR TO THE MINISTER

OF TRANSPORTATION, IS THE KEY GOI OFFICIAL FOR GALILEO, AS

THE CHAIRMAN OF THE GOI\'S INTERAGENCY WORKING GROUP ON

GALILEO. VIRIGLIO DESCRIBED LIBASSI AS HAVING THE MOST

COMPREHENSIVE UNDERSTANDING OF GALILEO POLICY WITHIN THE GOI.

RATHER THAN BEING A POLICY-MAKER, HOWEVER, LIBASSI IS MORE

OF COORDINATOR FOR THE ITALIAN GALILEO POLICY PROCESS.

LIBASSI IS THE PRINCIPLE GOI INTERLOCUTOR WITH THE EUROPEAN

COMMISSION (EC) AND THE OTHER GALILEO PARTNERS ON POLICY

ISSUES, AND HE MAKES SURE THAT ALL THE RELEVANT GOI AGENCIES

ARE ON THE SAME SHEET OF MUSIC FOR GALILEO POLICY. THE

ITALIAN SPACE AGENCY (ASI), HOWEVER, IS THE GOI FOCAL POINT
