SUBJECT: JORDAN MFA ON SECRETARY POWELL\'S FEBRUARY 14 UNSC

ADDRESS

REF: SECSTATE 44466

Classified By: PolCouns Doug Silliman for reasons 1.5 (B)(D)

1. (C) PolCouns February 16 presented Ali al-Ayed, personal

assistant to Foreign Minister Marwan Muasher, with a copy of

the Secretary\'s February 14 address before the UNSC on Iraq.

Ayed said he had called up a text of the Secretary\'s speech

from the Department web site and had already read it. Ayed

commented that Jordanian UN PermRep Prince Zaid bin Raad

would address the February 18 debate on Iraq, although he did

not know precisely what Prince Zaid would say. PolCouns

suggested that Prince Zaid\'s remarks should focus on Iraq\'s

failure to implement numerous UNSCRs over the past 12 years.
