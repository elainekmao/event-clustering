1. (U) Classified by Political-Military Counselor Stuart

Brown. Reasons 1.5 (b) and (d).

2. (C) On April 23, Polmiloff delivered reftel demarche to

MFA/Disarmament Second Secretary Esra Dogan. Dogan thanked

us for sharing the USG approach with regard to the April 29

"European Defense Summit," which will include France,

Germany, Belgium and Luxembourg. She indicated that the GOT

shares our concern and that Ankara also does not want to see

any summit statement that contradicts the fundamental

principles of the Berlin-plus arrangements recently agreed

between NATO and the EU. Dogan asked that we share with the

GOT the four participants\' reaction to our demarche as well

as provide Ankara with a readout of the summit\'s outcome and

any follow-up USG conversations or actions on this matter.
