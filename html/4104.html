SUBJECT: ASSASSINATION OF U.S. DIPLOMAT IN AMMAN 10/28:

SITREP 3

REF: (A) AMMAN 6265 (B) AMMAN 6261

Classified By: DCM Gregory L. Berry. Reasons 1.5 (b,d).

1. (C) The GOJ\'s investigation of this morning\'s murder of

USAID employee Larry Foley continues. As reported

previously, cooperation from the GID and PSD has been

excellent.

2. (S/NF) Investigation: According to investigators working

the case, the assailant used a small caliber 7mm handgun to

fire six shots at close range, killing Mr. Foley instantly.

Witnesses reported seeing a lone gunman fleeing up the street

following the shooting, where he appeared to join another

unidentified male. Both men were then seen getting into a

dark colored car which then departed the area. PSD and GID

are working intently and cooperatively to further identify

the make and model of the car, and the identities of the men

seen leaving the scene. Additional details on the status of

the investigation will be sent septel.

3. (S/NF) Security: Additional GID and PSD security

personnel have been deployed around the Embassy, ancillary

USG facilities, embassy residences, the American Community

School, and areas where members of the American community are

known to congregate. The PSD has increased the threat level

in the country to the highest degree possible, and has

assigned additional marked police cars, roving patrols, and

plain clothes personnel to these tasks. Working-level PSD

and GID officials have been tasked to pursue leads from

intelligence sources within the Palestinian, Iraqi, and

radical Islamic communities to learn possible identities of

the culprits.

4. (U) At 1230 local time, the Ambassador convoked a town

hall meeting for all Embassy employees, both U.S. and

Jordanian, to review the day\'s tragic events and provide

information to the Embassy community on steps the GOJ is

taking to protect American personnel and apprehend the

perpertrator(s). At 1630 local time, the Ambassador held a

similar meeting for Embassy family members who were not able

to attend the 1230 gathering.

5. (C) At 1400 local time, the Chief of Royal Protocol,

Faisel al-Fayez informed the Ambassador that King Abdullah

wished to pay a condolence call to Virginia Foley on October

29.

6. (C) At 1430 local time, Polcouns spoke with NEA Staff

Assistant Wurr in Doha and conveyed that Virginia Foley has

accepted NEA A/S Burns\' offer to come to Amman and accompany

her and Larry Foley\'s casket back to the U.S. The Embassy

understands that A/S Burns has milair, but may face problems

obtaining Saudi air clearances. We are looking into

commercial connections from Doha to Amman as an alternative.

In either case, we are planning for A/S Burns to arrive in

Amman sometime on 10/29.

7. (C) At 1515 local time, DCM talked with NEA/EX Director

Hudson about arranging milair to carry Larry Foley\'s body

from Amman to the U.S. Current plans envision a departure

from Amman on Wednesday morning, 10/30.
