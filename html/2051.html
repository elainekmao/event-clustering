SUBJECT: TFUS01: KUWAIT\'S FOREIGN MINISTER: "BIN LADEN KNOWS

NOTHING BUT TERRORISM"

REF: KUWAIT 3077

-------

SUMMARY

-------

1. KUWAIT\'S DE FACTO PRIME MINISTER AND FOREIGN MINISTER,

SHAYKH SABAH AL-AHMAD, CONVOKED THE EDITORS-IN-CHIEF OF THE

KUWAITI NEWSPAPERS AND MAGAZINES OCTOBER 10 TO DISCUSS THE

GOVERNMENT\'S VIEW OF THE CURRENT SITUATION. SHAYKH SABAH\'S

STATEMENTS WERE CARRIED OCTOBER 11 BY ALL PAPERS ON THE

FRONT PAGE. BELOW ARE KEY EXCERPTS, INCLUDING HIS

REAFFIRMATION OF KUWAIT\'S STRONG SUPPORT FOR THE

INTERNATIONAL COALITION AGAINST TERRORISM AND PRAISE FOR

PRESIDENT BUSH\'S STATEMENT RECOGNIZING "THE PALESTINIAN

STATE." THE FOREIGN MINISTER STATED THAT BIN LADEN DOES NOT

KNOW PALESTINE AND DOES NOT CARE FOR THE PALESTINIANS,

OBSERVING, "HE KNOWS NOTHING BUT TERRORISM."

2. PARA 8 REPORTS THE STATEMENTS OF DR. MOHAMMED AL-SABAH,

KUWAIT\'S MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOREIGN AFFAIRS, UPON HIS

RETURN OCTOBER 10 FROM THE OIC CONFERENCE IN DOHA. DR.

MOHAMMED AL-SABAH STRESSED THAT THE OIC FOREIGN MINISTERS

HAD REFUSED TO MIX THE CONCEPT OF TERRORISM -- TARGETING

UNARMED INNOCENT CIVILIANS TO ACHIEVE POLITICAL GOALS --

WITH THE LEGITIMACY OF NATIONAL EFFORTS TO COMBAT FOREIGN

OCCUPATION AND IMPERIALISM.

---------------------------

FOREIGN MINISTER TO EDITORS

---------------------------

3. AL-QAEDA SPOKESMAN TO BE STRIPPED OF CITIZENSHIP: THE

FOREIGN MINISTER ANNOUNCED THAT KUWAIT WILL STRIP SULEIMAN

BU GHEITH, THE KUWAITI IMAM WHO IS THE SPOKESMAN OF AL-

QAEDA, OF HIS CITIZENSHIP. (SEE REFTEL.) "THIS IS THE LEAST

WE CAN DO TO MAINTAIN KUWAIT\'S SECURITY FOR THIS PERSON IS

MORE AFGHAN THAN KUWAITI AND HE DOES NOT REPRESENT THE

PEOPLE OF KUWAIT." THE FOREIGN MINISTER DESCRIBED BU GHAITH

AS COMMITTING "TREASON AND CRIMINAL ACTS."

4. KUWAIT\'S STAND ON TERRORISM: THE FOREIGN MINISTER SAID

THAT KUWAIT WAS ONE OF THE FIRST COUNTRIES TO REJECT ALL

FORMS OF TERRORISM, RECALLING KUWAIT\'S OWN EXPERIENCE WITH

TERRORISM IN THE 1980S. "KUWAIT SUPPORTS THE WAR AGAINST

TERRORISM AND TERRORISTS WHEREVER THEY MAY BE AND WE

ENCOURAGE ALL TO ERADICATE THIS PHENOMENON. ULTIMATELY, WE

ARE MUSLIMS AND ISLAM IS A RELIGION OF PEACE. A TRUE MUSLIM

IS PEACEFUL IN WORDS AND DEEDS." HE STRESSED THAT TERRORISM

IS UNISLAMIC. "WE SUPPORT COMBATING THOSE WHO SUPPORT

TERRORISM OR HARBOR TERRORISTS OR OFFER ANY SUPPORT FOR

TERRORISM." HE ALSO STRESSED THAT KUWAIT IS AGAINST

TERRORISM, NOT PEOPLE, OR COMMUNITIES, OR RELIGIONS. "WE

SUPPORT THE INTERNATIONAL COALITION WHOSE AIM IS TO

ERADICATE TERRORISM." HE SAID THE CAMPAIGN AGAINST

TERRORISM IS BASED ON UN RESOLUTIONS AND OTHER INTERNATIONAL

AGREEMENTS, ADDING "KUWAIT IS BOUND" TO ABIDE BY THESE

INSTRUMENTS.

5. KUWAIT\'S RELATIONS WITH THE UNITED STATES: DURING THE

OCTOBER 13 VISIT OF DEFENSE UNDER SECRETARY DONALD FEITH,

SHAYKH SABAH RECOUNTED, "THE UNITED STATES DID NOT ASK FOR

ANYTHING EXCEPT THE EXCHANGE OF INFORMATION WITHIN THE

FRAMEWORK OF COMBATING TERRORISM. WE HAVE ASSURED ALL

AMERICAN OFFICIALS WHOM WE HAVE MET THAT WE ARE AGAINST

TERRORISM AND THAT WE HAVE NOT FORGOTTEN AMERICA\'S POSITION

IN SUPPORT OF US IN 1990 WHEN KUWAIT WAS INVADED. WE HAVE

NOT FORGOTTEN THAT WE HAVE AN AGREEMENT WITH THEM."

6. KUWAIT\'S VIEW OF U.S. ROLE IN PEACE PROCESS: SHAYKH

SABAH CITED AN ARABIC PROVERB WHICH MEANS \'THERE IS A SILVER

LINING IN EVERY CLOUD.\' "AMERICA HAS NOW BEGUN TO LOOK WITH

INTEREST AT THE PALESTINIAN ISSUE. WE EXPRESSED OUR HOPES

DURING FEITH\'S VISIT THAT AMERICAN OFFICIALS WOULD KEEP IN

MIND THAT THEY ARE RESPONSIBLE BEFORE THE WORLD FOR THE

PALESTINIAN CAUSE AND THAT THE PALESTINIANS HAVE SUFFERED A

GREAT INJUSTICE." SHAYKH SABAH ALSO CALLED ON THE AMERICAN

ADMINISTRATION TO UNDERTAKE POSITIVE AND PRACTICAL MEASURES

TO SOLVE THE PALESTINIAN ISSUE AND TO ESTABLISH A

PALESTINIAN STATE. HE TOLD FEITH: "SOLVING THE PALESTINIAN

ISSUE IS ALSO PART OF COMBATING TERRORISM."

7. SHAYKH SABAH PRAISED PRESIDENT BUSH\'S STATEMENT

RECOGNIZING "THE PALESTINIAN STATE." HE EXPLAINED THAT

THERE WAS AN AMERICAN INITIATIVE WITH REGARDS TO THE

PALESTINIAN STATE THAT WAS UNDERWAY IN COOPERATION WITH

SAUDI ARABIA BUT THE SEPTEMBER 11 TERRORIST ATTACKS

POSTPONED THIS INITIATIVE. HE SAID, "I DON\'T KNOW THE

DETAILS OF THIS INITIATIVE." HE ALSO SAID THAT SAUDI ARABIA

HAD PRESSURED THE ADMINISTRATION TO FIND AN AMERICAN

SOLUTION TO THE PROBLEM. HE ADDED THAT BIN LADEN DOES NOT

KNOW PALESTINE AND DOES NOT CARE FOR THE PALESTINIANS,

OBSERVING, "HE KNOWS NOTHING BUT TERRORISM."

8. KUWAIT\'S REGULATION OF ISLAMIC CHARITIES: THE FOREIGN

MINISTER ALSO TALKED IN DETAIL ABOUT THE GOVERNMENT\'S

CONSULTATIONS WITH ISLAMIC CHARITIES AND PLANS TO REGULATE

THE COLLECTION AND DISTRIBUTION OF CHARITABLE ASSISTANCE.

KUWAIT ON OIC STATEMENT

-----------------------

9. NO LINK BETWEEN TERRORISM AND LEGITIMATE RESISTANCE:

THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOREIGN AFFAIRS, DR. MOHAMMED AL-

SABAH, SPOKE TO THE KUWAITI PRESS OCTOBER 10 UPON HIS RETURN

FROM THE OIC CONFERENCE IN DOHA. HE STRESSED THAT THE OIC

FOREIGN MINISTERS HAD REFUSED TO CONFUSE THE CONCEPT OF

TERRORISM -- TARGETING UNARMED INNOCENT CIVILIANS TO ACHIEVE

POLITICAL GOALS -- WITH THE LEGITIMACY OF NATIONAL EFFORTS

TO COMBAT FOREIGN OCCUPATION AND IMPERIALISM. WITH REGARD

TO ATTACKS AGAINST MUSLIM OR ARAB STATES, DR. MOHAMMED AL-

SABAH SAID: "ANY SUCH ATTACK WOULD BE UNDER THE UMBRELLA OF

INTERNATIONAL LEGITIMACY, AND THERE IS AN IMPLICIT CONSENSUS

THAT IT IS THE RIGHT OF THE UNITED STATES AND BRITAIN TO

DEFEND THEMSELVES UNDER ARTICLE 51 OF THE UN CHARTER.

THEREFORE, THE MILITARY ACTS IN AFGHANISTAN WERE NOT

DENOUNCED."

10. DR. MOHAMED AL-SABAH ADDED: "AT THE SAME TIME,

HOWEVER, WE HAVE DEMANDED THAT ISRAEL DOES NOT USE THE

EXCUSE OF COMBATING TERRORISM TO ATTACK ARAB

COUNTRIES...THERE WILL BE AN INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON

COMBATING TERRORISM WHICH WILL DESIGNATE SPECIFIC CONCEPTS

(OF TERRORISM)."
