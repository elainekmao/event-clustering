1. (C) CROATIA TOOK SOME TIME TO WARM TO THE IDEA OF THE

ADRIATIC CHARTER. BECAUSE OF ITS FEAR OF BEING TRAPPED IN A

NEW BALKAN ALLIANCE AND ALSO BECAUSE OF AN ARROGANT

ASSUMPTION THAT ITS MORE ADVANCED LEVEL OF ECONOMIC

DEVELOPMENT MADE IT A BETTER NATO CANDIDATE, CROATIA RESISTED

TYING ITS FORTUNES TO ALBANIA AND MACEDONIA. BUT CROATIA HAS

SINCE BECOME AN ENTHUSIASTIC SUPPORTER OF THE CHARTER. THE

GOVERNMENT AND ESPECIALLY FM PICULA HAVE TAKEN US AT OUR WORD

THAT EACH CANDIDATE WILL BE EVALUATED ON AN INDIVIDUAL BASIS.

THEY ALSO REALIZE THAT BY SIGNING, CROATIA CAN DEMONSTRATE

ITS COMMITMENT TO WORKING COOPERATIVELY WITH OTHER

PROSPECTIVE ALLIES. FINALLY, PICULA HOPES THAT THE CHARTER

WILL ENSURE THAT THE U.S. REMAINS COMMITTED TO FUTURE

EXPANSION.

2. (C) WE EXPECT THAT THE SECRETARY\'S SCHEDULE WILL ALLOW

VERY LITTLE TIME FOR SUBSTANTIVE CONVERSATION WITH FM PICULA.

THERE ARE, HOWEVER, A NUMBER OF ISSUES WHICH MAY COME UP IN

WHICH THE SECRETARY\'S ENGAGEMENT WOULD BE VERY HELPFUL.

--------------------------------

UN-FROZEN ASSETS FROM YUGOSLAVIA

--------------------------------

3. (C) CROATIA CONTINUES TO OBJECT TO OUR FORMULA TO

UN-BLOCK FUNDS FROZEN IN THE U.S. BY MILOSEVIC-ERA SANCTIONS.

POINT TO MAKE:

- CROATIA NEEDS TO PUT THIS ISSUE IN THE PAST, AS OTHER

SUCCESSOR STATES HAVE, AND ACCEPT THE TRANSFER OF FUNDS.

--------------------

ARTICLE 98 AGREEMENT

--------------------

4. (C) CROATIA HAS NOT BEGUN TO NEGOTIATE AN ARTICLE 98

AGREEMENT, AND MILITARY ASSISTANCE ESSENTIAL TO CROATIA\'S

MODERNIZATION AND INTEROPERABILITY GOALS IS IN JEOPARDY.

POINT TO MAKE:

- URGE YOU TO BEGIN NEGOTIATIONS FOR AN ARTICLE 98 AGREEMENT

AS SOON AS POSSIBLE. THE JULY 1 DEADLINE IS LOOMING AND

CROATIA MUST NOT COUNT ON A WAIVER.

----------------

ICTY COOPERATION

----------------

5. (C) CROATIA MET THE TRIBUNAL\'S RECENT DEADLINES FOR

COOPERATION, BUT ONLY AT THE LAST MINUTE AND ONLY UNDER HEAVY

PRESSURE. THE INDICTMENT WAS SERVED ON GENERAL BOBETKO,

REQUESTS FOR DOCUMENTS ARE ON THE WAY TO BEING FULFILLED, AND

FUGITIVE ICTY INDICTEE IVICA RAJIC HAS BEEN APPREHENDED.

POINTS TO MAKE:

- PROMPT AND FULL COOPERATION WITH ICTY IS ESSENTIAL AND

NON-NEGOTIABLE AND IS A LITMUS TEST FOR CROATIA\'S READINESS

TO ADVANCE IN THE PROCESS OF INTEGRATION INTO NATO AND THE

EU.

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 04 ZAGREB 00954 01 OF 02 301022Z

- URGE YOU TO REDOUBLE YOUR EFFORTS TO APPREHEND ICTY

FUGITIVE ANTE GOTOVINA.

---------------

REFUGEE RETURNS

---------------

6. (C) CROATIA HAS NOT DONE ENOUGH ON THIS ISSUE. DESPITE

PROGRESS ON RECONSTRUCTION OF WAR-DAMAGED HOUSES, THE

GOVERNMENT HAS FAILED TO MEET DEADLINES ON EVICTIONS OF

THOUSANDS OF CROAT "SQUATTERS" IN HOMES BELONGING TO ETHNIC

SERBS WHO FLED DURING THE WAR, NOR HAS IT MET COMMITMENTS TO

COMPENSATE OWNERS. NOW THE RACAN GOVERNMENT IS RESISTING

ENGAGEMENT WITH THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY ON THIS ISSUE.

POINT TO MAKE:

- IT IS THE CONSENSUS OF THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY THAT

YOUR GOVERNMENT IS NOT DOING ENOUGH TO SUPPORT REFUGEE

RETURNS. THIS IS A SERIOUS ISSUE, AND UNTIL IT IS ADDRESSED

IN A SERIOUS, SYSTEMATIC WAY, IT BLOCKS CROATIA\'S INTEGRATION

INTO EURO-ATLANTIC INSTITUTIONS.

7. (C) CROATIA HAS BEEN A SOLID ALLY IN THE WAR ON

TERRORISM, AND HAS MADE IMPORTANT CONTRIBUTIONS. CROATIA\'S

ONLY MILITARY UNIT CERTIFIED AS NATO-INTEROPERABLE (AN MP

PLATOON) IS NOW DEPLOYED IN AFGHANISTAN. CROATIA REFUSED TO

BECOME A MEMBER OF THE COALITION ON IRAQ, PREFERRING TO SCORE

POINTS WITH THE FRENCH TO SUPPORT ITS EU MEMBERSHIP BID.

CROATIA HAS OFFERED IN-KIND ASSISTANCE TO IRAQ AND IS

CONSIDERING OTHER CONTRIBUTIONS, BUT THIS IS AN ELECTION

YEAR, AND THE RACAN GOVERNMENT HAS NOT BEEN WILLING TO SWIM

THE TIDE AGAINST PUBLIC OPINION TO SUPPORT OUR APPROACH.

OFFICIALS SEEK TO KEEP CROATIA\'S COOPERATION ON TERRORISM

DISTINCT FROM ITS RESISTANCE ON IRAQ.

POINT TO MAKE

- WE APPRECIATE CROATIA\'S IMPORTANT CONTRIBUTIONS TO THE WAR

ON TERRORISM. WE HOPE CROATIA WILL RESPOND POSITIVELY TO

CONCRETE REQUESTS FOR SUPPORT TO ACTIVITIES IN IRAQ.

----------------------------------

PROGRESS TOWARD MEMBERSHIP IN NATO

----------------------------------

8. (C) IN THE THREE YEARS SINCE THE CURRENT COALITION

DEFEATED THE NATIONALIST REGIME LED BY TUDJMAN\'S HDZ,

CROATIA\'S STATUS CHANGED FROM BEING OUTSIDE PFP TO THAT OF

SERIOUS ASPIRANT IN NATO\'S MEMBERSHIP ACTION PLAN. PROGRESS

HAS BEEN UNEVEN AND, IN SOME AREAS, DISAPPOINTING. AT

CROATIA\'S FIRST FORMAL MAP REVIEW IN BRUSSELS ON MAY 8,
