SUBJECT: JORDANIAN ISLAMISTS\' RELATIONSHIP WITH HAMAS

REF: A) AMMAN 8495; B) JERUSALEM 2594

CLASSIFIED BY AMBASSADOR WILLIAM J. BURNS FOR REASONS

1.5 (B) AND (D)

1. (C) SUMMARY: THE JORDANIAN MUSLIM BROTHERHOOD (MB)

HAS BEEN OUTSPOKEN IN ITS PUBLIC PROTESTS OVER THE

KILLING OF HAMAS MILITARY LEADERS IMAD AND ADEL

AWADULLAH AND HAS SUGGESTED PALESTINIAN AUTHORITY

COMPLICITY IN THEIR DEATHS. THE MB OPENLY DECLARES ITS

CLOSE TIES WITH HAMAS, BUT PRIVATELY, THE RELATIONSHIP

APPEARS MORE COMPLICATED. END SUMMARY.

---------------------------------------------

LOCAL ISLAMIST RESPONSE TO AWADULLAH KILLINGS

---------------------------------------------

2. (U) THE MUSLIM BROTHERHOOD (MB) AND THE MB-

AFFILIATED POLITICAL PARTY, THE ISLAMIC ACTION FRONT

(IAF), HAVE PUBLICLY PROTESTED IN A NUMBER OF WAYS THE

KILLINGS BY ISRAELI SECURITY FORCES OF HAMAS LEADERS

IMAD AND ADEL AWADALLAH.

- THE MB ORGANIZED A PUBLIC MEETING SEPTEMBER 13 TO

DENOUNCE THE KILLINGS AND CALL FOR AN END TO PEACE

AGREEMENTS WITH ISRAEL.

- THE MB AND IAF ISSUED A PUBLIC STATEMENT CALLING FOR

THE PALESTINIAN AUTHORITY TO END SECURITY COOPERATION

WITH ISRAEL.

- JORDANIAN ISLAMIST NEWSPAPER AL-SABEEL PRINTED A

POSTER AND A FOUR-PAGE INSERT SEPTEMBER 15 DEDICATED TO

THE AWADULLAH KILLINGS.

- THE MB TRIED TO STAGE TWO RALLIES IN AL BAQA\'A AND AL

WIHDAT REFUGEE CAMPS SEPTEMBER 18 BUT WAS REFUSED

PERMISSION. THE JORDAN TIMES REPORTED THAT MB

CONTROLLER GENERAL ABDUL MAJEED AL-THUNAYBAT AND

PROMINENT MB MEMBER HAMZEH MANSOUR, SPEAKING AT FRIDAY

PRAYERS IN THE CAMPS, CALLED FOR HAMAS TO AVENGE THE

AWADULLAH MURDERS. THEY ALSO CALLED FOR JIHAD (HOLY

WAR) AGAINST ISRAEL TO "LIBERATE" JERUSALEM AND THE

PALESTINIAN TERRITORIES, WITH THE ASSISTANCE OF THE PA.

--------------------------------------

SUMMARY OF ISLAMISTS\' PRESS STATEMENTS

--------------------------------------

3. (U) IN THE IAF STATEMENT, REPORTED SEPTEMBER 14 IN

THE JORDAN TIMES:

- THE IAF CALLED THE AWADULLAH KILLINGS "ONE MORE LINK

IN A LONG CHAIN OF ISRAELI CRIMES AGAINST THE PEOPLE OF

PALESTINE."

- IT CALLED ON THE PALESTINIAN NATIONAL AUTHORITY TO

RELEASE ALL POLITICAL DETAINEES, INCLUDING HAMAS

MEMBERS, TO ABROGATE THE OSLO ACCORDS AND TO ALLOW

RESISTANCE GROUPS TO STRUGGLE AGAINST ISRAELI

OCCUPATION.

- IT CALLED ON EGYPT AND JORDAN TO END THEIR PEACE

ACCORDS WITH ISRAEL.

4. (U) JORDANIAN ISLAMIST NEWSPAPER AL-SABEEL\'S FOUR

PAGE SPECIAL INSERT SEPTEMBER 15 ABOUT THE KILLINGS

INCLUDED:

- AN INTERVIEW WITH AMER AWADULLAH, THE BROTHER OF THE

TWO SLAIN HAMAS LEADERS, WHO IMPLIED PA COMPLICITY IN

THE DEATHS.

- AN ARTICLE QUESTIONING THE DENIAL OF JIBRIL AL-

RUJOUB, COMMANDER OF THE PALESTINIAN PREVENTATIVE

SECURITY UNIT, OF ANY CONNECTION TO THE KILLINGS.

- AN ARTICLE CRITICIZING THE PA FOR BANNING ANY MENTION

IN PALESTINIAN PAPERS OF HAMAS\' CALL FOR A STRIKE IN

MEMORY OF THE AWADULLAH BROTHERS.

5. (U) WEEKLY NEWSPAPER AL-HADATH ON SEPTEMBER 14 RAN

AN AFP REPORT OF AN MB STATEMENT, IN WHICH THE MB HINTS

MORE STRONGLY AT PA COMPLICITY:

- THE MB DOUBTED "THE ZIONIST VERSION OF THE MARTYRDOM"

OF HAMAS MILITARY LEADERS IMAD AND ADEL AWADALLAH.

- IT QUESTIONED THE TIMING OF THE KILLINGS DURING SMEC

DENNIS ROSS\' VISIT.

- IT SUGGESTED THE PA "MIGHT HAVE OFFERED THE HEADS OF

IMAD AND ADEL TO GAIN A FLEXIBILITY FROM NETANYAHU, SO

THAT HE WOULD ACCEPT THE US PROPOSAL" FOR A 13 PERCENT

ISRAELI WITHDRAWAL FROM THE WEST BANK.

6. (U) AN UNNAMED \'HIGH PNA OFFICIAL\', QUOTED IN

SENSATIONALIST WEEKLY PUBLICATION AL-SHIHAN SEPTEMBER

17, RETURNED THE ACCUSATIONS:

- THE SOURCE CALLED THE JORDANIAN MB "IMPERIALIST

STOOGES" WHO ARE "WORKING AGAINST ARAB, NATIONALIST AND

PALESTINIAN CAUSES".

- HE ACCUSED THE MB OF FOLLOWING INSTRUCTIONS FROM ITS

INTERNATIONAL LEADERSHIP (WHICH HE SAID JUST MOVED FROM

GERMANY TO THE U.S.) TO ADOPT ISRAELI SECURITY\'S

VERSION OF THE AWADULLAH KILLINGS WHICH IMPLICATES THE

PA.

- HE ACCUSED THE MB OF SPREADING THESE LIES TO ISOLATE

THE PA BY DRIVING A WEDGE BETWEEN IT AND THE

PALESTINIAN PEOPLE.

THE AL-SHIHAN ARTICLE ALSO QUOTED MB CONTROLLER GENERAL

ABDUL MAJEED AL-THUNAYBAT, TRYING TO CALM THE

SITUATION, SAYING HE DOES NOT WANT A WAR OF WORDS WITH

THE PA.

----------------------------------

MB PICKS HAMAS OVER THE PA ANY DAY

----------------------------------

7. (C) THE MB HAS A LONG-TIME, PUBLIC AFFILIATION WITH

HAMAS, DESPITE ITS DECLARED POLICY AGAINST THE USE OF

VIOLENCE (REF B). VIOLENT ACTS PERPETRATED BY HAMAS

AGAINST ISRAELIS ARE, AS ISLAMIST MP ABDUL MAJEED

AQTASH TOLD POLOFF SEPTEMBER 15, "A REVOLUTIONIST

RESPONSE TO ISRAELI INJUSTICE, KILLING, OCCUPATION AND

DESTRUCTION OF THE PALESTINIAN INFRASTRUCTURE." AQTASH

CALLED HAMAS "THE MILITARY WING FOR ALL MUSLIMS, NOT

JUST THE MB".

8. (U) IN AN INTERVIEW PUBLISHED IN FILASTIN AL-

MUSLIMAH (THE DATE OF THE INTERVIEW WAS NOT GIVEN BUT

APPEARS TO BE EARLY AUGUST), THE INTERVIEWER ASKED

JORDANIAN MB LEADER THUNAYBAT TO DEFINE THE

RELATIONSHIP BETWEEN THE MB AND HAMAS. THUNAYBAT

RESPONDED, "WE CONSIDER OURSELVES ONE PEOPLE AND THUS

ONE MOVEMENT...WE HAVE THE SAME CONCERNS AND THE SAME

CAUSE...WE PRAY FOR THEM AND STRETCH OUR HANDS OUT TO

THEM IN RESISTING AGAINST THE OCCUPATION AND IN WARDING

OFF THE HARM FROM THE SONS OF OUR PEOPLE IN PALESTINE.

AS FOR THE BROTHERS IN HAMAS, THEY ARE THE VANGUARD OF

JIHAD IN OCCUPIED PALESTINE. JIHAD IS ONE OF THE

DUTIES IMPOSED UPON US BY GOD ALMIGHTY, AND IT IS ONE

OF THE PILLARS ON WHICH THE MUSLIM BROTHERHOOD MOVEMENT

WAS FOUNDED. WE BACK AND SUPPORT OUR BROTHERS IN THIS

BLESSED MOVEMENT WITH ALL THE POWER WE HAVE AND WILL

ALL THE MATERIAL, MORAL AND INFORMATION SUPPORT WE CAN

GIVE THEM."

9. (C) THUNAYBAT GIVES A ROSIER VIEW OF THE

RELATIONSHIP BETWEEN THE MB AND HAMAS IN THIS PUBLIC

STATEMENT THAN MB MEMBER AND INTELLECTUAL IBRAHIM

GHARAIBEH (STRICTLY PROTECT) DESCRIBED TO POLOFF IN A

CONVERSATION TWO MONTHS AGO. GHARAIBEH, EDITOR OF THE

ISLAMIC MAGAZINE AL-UMMAH, TOLD POLOFF JULY 14 THAT THE

MB HAD DECIDED TO CEASE ALL RELATIONS WITH HAMAS UNTIL

HAMAS RESOLVES A POWER STRUGGLE BETWEEN THE LEADERS

INSIDE THE OCCUPIED TERRITORIES AND OUTSIDE.

10. (C) GHARAIBEH SAID THE PALESTINIAN MB APPROACHED

THE JORDANIAN MB IN THE 1980\'S FOR ASSISTANCE TO FORM

HAMAS. THEY HAD AN AGREEMENT THAT HAMAS WOULD ONLY

FUNCTION WITHIN PALESTINE AND REMAIN COMPLETELY

SEPARATE FROM THE JORDANIAN MB, ALTHOUGH THE JORDANIAN

MB WOULD PROVIDE SUPPORT (WHAT KIND OF SUPPORT WAS NOT

CLARIFIED). WHEN "GOVERNMENTS STARTED TO HARASS

HAMAS", IT MOVED SOME OFFICES OUTSIDE OF THE OCCUPIED

TERRITORIES FOR SELF-PRESERVATION. ACCORDING TO

GHARAIBEH, THESE OUTSIDE LEADERS, SUCH AS KHALID

MISHA\'AL IN JORDAN, BEGAN COMPETING WITH THE INTERNAL

LEADERS, LIKE SHEIKH YASSIN, FOR CONTROL OVER THE

ORGANIZATION. GHARAIBEH SAID SHEIKH YASSIN HAS ASKED

THE MB TO IGNORE DIRECTIVES FROM THE EXTERNAL LEADERS.

11. (C) GHARAIBEH SAID THE JORDANIAN MB OPPOSES HAMAS

RECEIVING DECISIONS FROM OUTSIDE THE TERRITORIES. IT

WANTS IT TO BE A PURELY PALESTINIAN ORGANIZATION AND

INDEPENDENT FROM ANY OUTSIDE SOURCE, INCLUDING THE

JORDANIAN MB. HE SAID THE MB DOES NOT WANT HAMAS TO

BECOME LIKE NAYEF HAWATMA\'S DEMOCRATIC FRONT FOR THE

LIBERATION OF PALESTINE (DFLP) WHICH, ACCORDING TO

GHARAIBEH, IS ACTIVE IN SYRIA, BUT HAS FEW FOLLOWERS IN

THE PALESTINIAN TERRITORIES.

12. (C) COMMENT: THE JORDANIAN REGIME, COGNIZANT OF

THE WIDESPREAD PUBLIC SUPPORT FOR THE POLICIES OF BOTH

THE MB AND HAMAS, SEES NO ADVANTAGE IN OPENLY

CRITICIZING THE MB OVER ITS DECLARATIONS OF SUPPORT FOR

HAMAS. IN THE MIND OF MOST JORDANIANS, TO BE ANTI-

HAMAS IS TO BE ANTI-PALESTINIAN - A MONIKER THE REGIME

SEEKS TO AVOID. THE GOJ DOES, HOWEVER, CONTINUE TO

MONITOR CAREFULLY LOCAL HAMAS LEADERS - THEIR CONTACTS,

STATEMENTS AND ACTIVITIES - AND DOES NOT SHY AWAY FROM

THREATENING HAMAS MEMBERS WHEN THEY HAVE OVERSTEPPED

GOJ-IMPOSED REDLINES. END COMMENT.
