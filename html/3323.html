SUBJECT: NGOS AFFILIATED WITH THE UN: SECTION 102

(PEDOPHILIA) CERTIFICATION

REF: SECSTATE 113678

1. SUMMARY: IN RESPONSE TO REFTEL, THE U.S. MISSION/ROME

HAS OBTAINED LISTS OF NGOS AFFILIATED WITH THE U.N. FOOD

AND AGRICULTURE ORGANIZATION (FAO), THE WORLD FOOD

PROGRAM (WFP) AND THE INTERNATIONAL FUND FOR AGRICULTURE

AND DEVELOPMENT (IFAD). THE U.S. MISSION HAS REVIEWED

THESE LISTS AND HAS DETERMINED THAT, TO THE BEST OF ITS

KNOWLEDGE, NONE OF THESE NGOS PROMOTE OR CONDONE, OR SEEK

THE LEGALIZATION OF PEDOPHILIA, OR INCLUDE AS A

SUBSIDIARY OR MEMBER ANY SUCH ORGANIZATION. END SUMMARY.

2. THE U.S. MISSION CONTACTED, VIA TELEPHONE AND FAX,

THE APPROPRIATE PERSONNEL IN THE THREE ROME-BASED U.N.

ORGANIZATIONS LISTED IN REFTEL TO DISCUSS SECTION 102

REQUIREMENTS. IN ADDITION EACH ORGANIZATION WAS

REQUESTED, IN WRITING, TO PROVIDE BOTH A CERTIFICATION

AND A LIST OF NGOS THAT HAVE AFFILIATIONS WITH THEM.

RESPONSES FROM EACH ORGANIZATION ARE DISCUSSED BELOW:

3. FAO:THE MISSION HAS RECEIVED A LETTER FROM NORA

MCKEON, OFFICER-IN-CHARGE OF FAO\'S RESOURCES AND

STRATEGIC PARTNERSHIPS UNIT, TCDS, TECHNICAL COOPERATION

DEPARTMENT TRANSMITTING A LIST OF ALL NGOS THAT HAVE

FORMAL RELATIONS WITH FAO. THIS LIST CONSISTS OF 16 NGOS

WITH "CONSULTATIVE STATUS", 65 NGOS WITH "SPECIALIZED

CONSULTATIVE STATUS", AND 120 NGOS WITH "LIAISON STATUS."

THE LETTER ASSURES THAT, TO THE BEST OF FAO\'S KNOWLEDGE,

NONE OF THESE ORGANIZATIONS PROMOTE, CONDONE OR SEEK THE

LEGALIZATION OF PEDOPHILIA, OR HAVE ANY SUBSIDIARIES OR

MEMBER GROUPS THAT DO.

4. WFP: THE MISSION HAS RECEIVED A LETTER FROM REBECCA

DOYLE, CHIEF OF THE RESOURCES MOBILIZATION SERVICE

(AMERICAS, AUSTRALIA AND ASIA) STATING THAT, TO THE BEST

OF HER KNOWLEDGE, NONE OF THE NGOS THAT WFP IS AFFILIATED

WITH PROMOTE, CONDONE OR SEEK THE LEGALIZATION OF

PEDOPHILIA, NOR HAVE ANY SUBSIDIARIES OR MEMBER GROUPS

WHICH DO. ATTACHED TO THE LETTER IS A LIST OF 19 NGOS

THAT ARE WFPS\' MAJOR IMPLEMENTING PARTNERS (I.E. A LIST

OF ALL NGOS WITH WHICH WFP HAS A GLOBAL MEMORANDUM OF

UNDERSTANDING).

5. IFAD: THE MISSION HAS RECEIVED A LETTER FROM ALAN

PRIEN, SECRETARY OF IFAD, TRANSMITTING A LIST OF 494 NGOS

COLLABORATING WITH IFAD. THIS LETTER FURTHER STATES THAT

IFAD CONFIRMS, TO THE BEST OF ITS KNOWLEDGE, THAT NONE OF

THESE ORGANIZATIONS PROMOTE, CONDONE OR SEEK THE

LEGALIZATION OF PEDOPHILIA OR HAVE ANY SUBSIDIARIES OR

MEMBER GROUPS THAT DO.

6. UPON RECEIPT OF LETTERS FROM THE ABOVE THREE

ORGANIZATIONS, MISSION HAS REVIEWED THE LISTS OF NGOS AND

DETERMINED THAT, TO THE BEST OF ITS KNOWLEDGE, NONE OF

THESE NGOS PROMOTE OR CONDONE, OR SEEK THE LEGALIZATION

OF PEDOPHILIA, OR INCLUDE AS A SUBSIDIARY OR MEMBER ANY

SUCH ORGANIZATION. THE LETTERS AND LIST OF NGOS PROVIDED

BY EACH ORGANIZATION WILL BE SENT TO IO/SHA VIA PRIORITY

MAIL ON JULY 17, 2002. POINT OF CONTACT FOR THIS ISSUE AT

U.S. MISSION IN ROME IS LUCY TAMLYN.

7. APPROXIMATELY 5 HOURS OF STAFF TIME WAS DEVOTED TO

COMPLYING WITH THIS REQUEST. HEILEMAN
