SUBJECT: ZANU-PF INSIDER: THE COUNTRY IS UNDER

INFORMAL SANCTIONS

CLASSIFIED BY CHARGE D\'AFFAIRES EARL M. IRVING FOR

REASONS 1.5 (B) AND (D).

SUMMARY

-------

1. (C) AN ACADEMIC WITH VERY CLOSE TIES TO ZANU-PF

LEADERSHIP CIRCLES, CLEVER MUMBENGEGWI, TOLD US APRIL 2

THAT THE ZIMBABWEAN GOVERNMENT BELIEVES IT IS OPERATING

UNDER AN INFORMAL INTERNATIONAL SANCTIONS REGIME. THE

GOVERNMENT PERCEIVES THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY AS

BEING BIASED IN FAVOR OF THE OPPOSITION MOVEMENT FOR

DEMOCRATIC CHANGE (MDC), A PARTY HE DISMISSED AS

INEFFECTIVE AND BOUND FOR OBLIVION. HE BELIEVED THE

WEST WAS WILLING TO ENGAGE MEANINGFULLY WITH ZIMBABWE

ONLY WHEN PRESIDENT MUGABE IS OFF THE SCENE. HE

CONFIRMED TENSIONS BETWEEN THE GOVERNMENT AND THE WHITE

BUSINESS COMMUNITY, ATTRIBUTING THEM TO AN AGENDA OF

WRECKING THE ECONOMY TO THE LATTER. HE DEFENDED THE

GOVERNMENT\'S POLICY OF NOT DEVALUING THE CURRENCY,

SAYING THAT TO DO SO WOULD HURT THE MAJORITY OF

ZIMBABWEANS WHO ARE DEPENDENT ON CHEAP IMPORTS. HE SAW

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 HARARE 01275 01 OF 03 091226Z

RESOLUTION OF THE LAND REFORM PROBLEM AS THE PANACEA FOR

ZIMBABWE\'S POLITICAL TROUBLES, NOTING THAT THE

GOVERNMENT COULD SORT OUT QUICKLY ANCILLARY DIFFICULTIES

ASSOCIATED WITH THE FAST TRACK RESETTLEMENT EFFORT. HE

THOUGHT THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY HAS A KEY ROLE TO

PLAY IN RESTORING ZIMBABWE TO ECONOMIC HEALTH, BUT

DOUBTED IT WOULD MAKE ANY MOVE TO HELP PRIOR TO THE

PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION SCHEDULED FOR 2002. WE BELIEVE

MUMBENGEGWI\'S COMMENTS REFLECT THE MAINSTREAM THINKING

OF ZANU-PF -- PARANOID, ISOLATIONIST, ECONOMICALLY

UNSOUND AND MARKED BY CONSPIRACY THEORIES. IF THE

INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY WANTS TO REVERSE ZIMBABWE\'S

RAPID ECONOMIC AND POLITICAL DECLINE, IT MUST DO A

BETTER JOB OF GETTING AND SUSTAINING THE GOVERNMENT\'S

ATTENTION. END SUMMARY.

--------------

US VERSUS THEM

--------------

2. (C) ZANU-PF INSIDER AND DEAN OF THE UNIVERSITY OF

ZIMBABWE\'S FACULTY OF SOCIAL STUDIES CLEVER MUMBENGEGWI

(HIS BROTHER SAMUEL IS THE MINISTER OF EDUCATION, SPORT

AND CULTURE, AND HIS BROTHER SIMBARASHE IS THE

ZIMBABWEAN HIGH COMMISSIONER IN LONDON) TOLD US APRIL 2

THAT IT WAS THE GOVERNMENT\'S WIDELY-HELD PERCEPTION THAT

ZIMBABWE WAS UNDER UNOFFICIAL SANCTIONS, WITH THE

PROSPECT OF THEIR BEING LIFTED SOON CLOSE TO NIL. HE

THOUGHT THAT FOREIGN INVESTORS AND THE INTERNATIONAL

FINANCIAL INSTITUTIONS WERE ON THE SIDELINES AND WAITING

FOR THE PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION TO TAKE PLACE BEFORE

ENGAGING ZIMBABWE MEANINGFULLY ONCE AGAIN.

3. (C) THE PERCEPTION IS THAT THE INTERNATIONAL

COMMUNITY WAS SITTING WITH ITS ARMS CROSSED WATCHING

ZIMBABWE COLLAPSE, ASSUMING THAT IT IS A BASKET CASE

LIKE OTHER AFRICAN COUNTRIES. THE POSITION OF THE

INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY TO FOSTER THE DOWNFALL AND

REMOVAL OF THE RULING PARTY IMPINGES ON ZIMBABWE\'S

ECONOMIC HEALTH; IT IS NOT SOLELY A DOMESTIC ISSUE. THE

INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY\'S FOCUS ON A PERSONALITY, AND

ITS WITHHOLDING OF ASSISTANCE UNTIL THAT PERSONALITY IS

CHANGED ONLY GETS US INTO A POLITICAL PROBLEM, HE

AVERRED.

4. (C) MUMBENGEGWI CONTINUED THAT THE GOVERNMENT WANTED

TO USE THE ISSUE OF LAND REFORM TO BEGIN SOLVING

DOMESTIC PROBLEMS ONCE AND FOR ALL, AS IT WAS AN ISSUE

AROUND WHICH MOST ZIMBABWEANS COULD RALLY. ONCE

COMPENSATION FOR LAND COULD BE ARRANGED, THE GOVERNMENT

COULD QUICKLY TAKE CARE OF THE OTHER THINGS, SUCH AS

FARM INVASIONS, IN A SHORT PERIOD OF TIME. THE REASON

WHY THERE IS NO GOING BACK ON THE LAND ISSUE IS THAT

POLITICIANS HAVE SOLD THE PROGRAM TO THE NATION.

ALTHOUGH HE AGREED THAT THEY OUGHT TO HAVE SOMETHING

ELSE TO OFFER, IT WOULD COST ZANU-PF POLITICALLY IF IT

THE RHODESIAN LEGACY LIVES ON

5. (C) THE SOCIAL STUDIES DEAN STATED THAT THERE WAS A

LACK OF TRUST BETWEEN THE GOVERNMENT AND THE WHITE

BUSINESS COMMUNITY BECAUSE THAT COMMUNITY WAS DOING ITS

BEST IT COULD TO DESTROY THE COUNTRY\'S ECONOMY IN ORDER

TO BRING DOWN THE CURRENT GOVERNMENT. WHITE

BUSINESSMEN, HE ALLEGED, ARE DIRECTING SUBSTANTIAL

PORTIONS OF THEIR FOREIGN EXCHANGE INFLOWS "THROUGH

OTHER CHANNELS," BRINGING ONLY A SMALL FRACTION OF THEIR

EARNINGS VIA OFFICIAL CHANNELS INTO ZIMBABWE. THE

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 HARARE 01275 02 OF 03 091226Z

PRIVATE SECTOR WAS MYOPIC AS TO WHERE ITS INTERESTS LIE

AND SHORT-TERM IN ITS OUTLOOK, NOT INTERESTED IN THE

GOOD OF THE COUNTRY, ITS GOVERNMENT OR ITS BLACK

CITIZENS. THIS VIEW IS WIDELY HELD AND ACCEPTED AS TRUE

BY THE GOVERNMENT AND ITS LEADERSHIP, HE STATED.

6. (C) HE LECTURED US WITH A STRAIGHT FACE THAT FOR

YEARS BUSINESSPERSONS HAVE LIVED OFF THE TAXPAYER

(NOTWITHSTANDING THE FACT THAT BUSINESSES, VIA COMPANY

AND PERSONAL INCOME TAXES, PAY OVER HALF THE NATIONAL

TAX BILL), BUYING UP GOVERNMENT PAPER AND DRIVING UP

INTEREST RATES, SO THAT THEY AND THE BANKS CAN GET THE

HIGH YIELDS, THEREBY PLACING THE GOVERNMENT IN ITS

CURRENT DEBT TRAP. THERE\'S A SHORT-TERM PRICE TO BE

BORNE IN TURNING AROUND THE ECONOMY, HE OFFERED, AND THE

COST SHOULD BE BORNE BY THE POPULATION IN GENERAL, AND

NOT JUST THE GOVERNMENT. "WE HAVE TO START SOMEWHERE,"

HE SHRUGGED; "SOMEONE WILL ALWAYS CALL FOUL." HE SAID

THAT THE NATION IS IN CRISIS AND IT IS TIME TO STAND

TOGETHER AND NOT TRY TO SPLIT THE NATION, SOMETHING THE

DONOR COMMUNITY IS CLEARLY TRYING TO DO. THE WHITE

BUSINESS COMMUNITY, ON THE OTHER HAND, IS OF THE VIEW

THAT "YOU CAUSED THIS MESS, YOU GET OUT OF POWER."

7. (C) TURNING TO THE REASON WHY THE GOVERNMENT STUCK

TO A DYSFUNCTIONAL A FIXED EXCHANGE RATE, MUMBENGEGWI

EXPLAINED THAT ZIMBABWE IS EXTREMELY IMPORT DEPENDENT;

EVEN MANY OF ITS EXPORTS CONTAIN A HIGH PERCENTAGE OF

IMPORTED CONTENT. ALLOWING THE ZIMBABWE DOLLAR TO FLOAT

TO ITS MARKET VALUE WOULD INCREASE THE PRICE OF INPUTS.

EXPORTERS, WHO HAVE ACCESS TO FOREIGN CURRENCY, FAVOR

DEVALUATION. HOWEVER, BECAUSE THEY ARE A MINORITY OF

THE POPULATION (WHEREAS IMPORTS IMPACT A MAJORITY OF THE

POPULATION), THE POLITICAL PRESSURE THEY BRING TO BEAR

TO DEVALUE IS NOT AS INTENSE. IT IS THE GOVERNMENT\'S

SINCERE VIEW THAT COUNTRIES THAT HAVE DEVALUED THEIR

CURRENCIES OR IMPLEMENTED STRUCTURAL ADJUSTMENT HAVE NOT

DONE BETTER THAN THOSE THAT HAVE NOT DONE SO, HE

CLAIMED. HE COMPLAINED THAT THE LAST TIME THE

GOVERNMENT DEVALUED THE CURRENCY FOR 38.5 TO 55 TO THE

U.S. DOLLAR, THERE WAS NO COMMENSURATE IMPROVEMENT IN

EXPORT SECTOR PERFORMANCE. THE GOVERNMENT, HE HELD,

COULD DEVALUE THE CURRENCY BY 1000 PERCENT -- A MOVE

THAT WOULD WRECK THE IMPORT SECTOR -- AND STILL GET NO

RESPONSE. EXPORTERS ARE SIMPLY SEEKING WINDFALL GAINS,

HE ALLEGED, WHICH THEY ARE GETTING IN THE FOREIGN

EXCHANGE BLACK MARKET.

-------

COMMENT

-------

9. (C) IF WE PART FROM THE PREMISE THAT PERCEPTION IS

REALITY, THEN THE U.S.-EDUCATED MUMBENGEGWI GIVES A

DISTRESSING ACCOUNT OF HIS COUNTRY\'S GOVERNANCE: THE

MINDS OF ITS LEADERSHIP ARE IN A PRISON OF ECONOMIC

OBSCURANTISM AND CONSPIRACY THEORIES. WE HAVE NO DOUBT

THAT THE SOCIAL SCIENCE PROFESSOR\'S EPISTEMOLOGY

REPRESENTS THE MAINSTREAM THINKING OF THE RULING PARTY.

UNFORTUNATELY, THE CHASM BETWEEN THIS PERCEPTION AND

REALITY IS WIDE, AND CORRECTIVE ACTION WITH ANY HOPE OF

DOING GOOD IS THUS FAR OUT OF GRASP. BETWEEN NOW AND

THE PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION, REAL DAMAGE TO ZIMBABWE WILL

CONTINUE TO OCCUR AT AN UNSUSTAINABLE RATE, AS THE PARTY

FAITHFUL IGNORE THE CARNAGE AND PLAY FOLLOW-THE-LEADER

TO NEVER-NEVER LAND. IF THE WEST OR THE DONORS WANT TO

CHANGE THIS PATTERN THEY MUST DO A BETTER JOB OF GETTING

-- AND SUSTAINING -- THE GOVERNMENT\'S ATTENTION. WE ARE

CONVINCED NOW THAT ONLY STEPS WHICH AFFECT PRESIDENT

MUGABE AND HIS CRONIES PERSONALLY -- SUCH AS RESTRICTING

THEIR INTERNATIONAL TRAVEL AND FREEZING THEIR ASSETS --

CAN HALT ZIMBABWE\'S SLIDE TOWARD ECONOMIC AND POLITICAL

COLLAPSE. END COMMENT.
