SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION: MORE ON POWELL\'S UNSC SPEECH AND MEPI

1. SUMMARY: LOCAL NEWSPAPERS CONTINUED TO REPORT EXTENSIVELY ON

SECRETARY POWELL\'S SPEECH AT THE UNSC. EDITORIALS IN THE ARABIC

SIPDIS

DAILIES NOTE THAT THE U.S. IS PLANNING TO ATTACK IRAQ IN ORDER TO

CONTROL ARAB AND IRAQI RESOURCES, MAINLY OIL. THE EDITORIALS

ALSO CLAIM THAT THE ATTACK WOULD RESULT IN RESHAPING THE ENTIRE

REGION ACCORDING TO AMERICAN AND ISRAELI INTERESTS. MEANWHILE,

AN AL BAYAN EDITORIAL CALLED ON ARABS TO WAKE UP BEFORE IT IS TOO

LATE. END SUMMARY.

2. COMMENT: FOLLOWING THE SECRETARY\'S PRESENTATION AT THE UN,

AL-ITTIHAD, THE SEMI-OFFICIAL ARABIC DAILY MOST LIKELY TO MIRROR

OFFICIAL THINKING, HAS BEEN MARKEDLY LESS NEGATIVE REGARDING USG

POLICIES TOWARDS IRAQ THAN THE OTHER, MORE DOGMATIC LOCAL ARABIC

DAILIES. AL-ITTIHAD IS RUNNING COMMENTARIES AND COVERAGE

CRITICAL OF THE IRAQI REGIME, MANY OF WHICH LARGELY BLAME THE

CURRENT CRISIS ON SADDAM HUSSEIN AND NOT THE US ADMINISTRATION.

END COMMENT.

3. UNDER THE HEADLINE, "RESHAPING THE REGION," ABU DHABI-BASED

PAN-ARAB DAILY "AKHBAR AL-ARAB" 2/8 EDITORIALIZED:

"WITH A RUDE FRANKNESS AND CLEAR WORDS, SECRETARY OF STATE COLIN

POWELL DEFINED THE REAL GOALS FOR HIS NATION\'S IMPULSE TO ATTACK

IRAQ, THE REARRANGEMENT OF THE MIDDLE EAST IN ACCORDANCE TO U.S.

INTERESTS. THE OBJECTIVES OF INVADING AND OCCUPYING IRAQ ARE NOT

VIRTUAL ASSUMPTIONS ANYMORE. SECRETARY POWELL HAS DISCARDED THE

FAADE OF A DOVE AND JOINED THE HAWKS HAS PLACED THE IMMINENT WAR

ON TRACK. WE HOPE THAT POWELL\'S FRANKNESS WOULD PERSUADE THE

DOUBTFUL AND DECEIVED WITH GOOD AMERICAN INTENTIONS. THE GOAL OF

THE WAR GOES BEYOND IRAQ AND IS ONLY THE BEGINNING OF A LAUNCHING

PAD TO RESHAPE THE REGION, ITS BORDERS, AND ITS REGIMES, IN

ACCORDANCE WITH AMERICAN INTERESTS WITH CONSIDERATION FOR ISRAELI

ASPIRATIONS AND AMBITIONS TO DOMINATE THE REGION AND CONTROL ITS

RESOURCES..."

4. THE EDITOR-IN-CHIEF OF THE DUBAI-BASED BUSINESS-ORIENTED "AL-

BAYAN" ON 2/8 WROTE IN A FRONT-PAGE EDITORIAL:

"... IRAQ MUST KNOW THAT IT IS USING ITS SPARE TIME. THE RESULT

IS BEING DECIDED. POWELL\'S SPEECH BEFORE THE UNSC WAS A WAR

MESSAGE AND MUST BE UNDERSTOOD BY IRAQ AS SUCH ... THE COUNTDOWN

FOR THE WAR IS AWAITING THE COMPLETED ARRIVAL OF AMERICAN TROOPS

AFTER TWO WEEKS ... THE WAR THIS TIME WILL TAKE PLACE ON IRAQI

SOIL AND IS UNDOUBTEDLY DIFFERENT FROM PREVIOUS IRAQI WARS, THE

GULF WAR, AND THE WAR WITH IRAN, WHERE MOST OF THE BATTLES

OCCURRED OUTSIDE IRAQ. IT MEANS DESTROYING EVERYTHING ON THE

GROUND, AND IRAQ WILL NOT BE ABLE TO STAND IN THE FACE OF AN

AMERICAN ARSENAL EQUIPPED WITH THE MOST SOPHISTICATED AND MODERN

MACHINES ... THE U.S. SECRETARY OF STATE SAYS THAT THE WAR ON

IRAQ WOULD CAUSE SOME TROUBLE BUT THAT ALL TROUBLES WOULD BE

EASED WITH OIL. THE SUCCESS IN IRAQ WILL INCREASE AMERICA\'S

APPETITE TO SWALLOW THE REST OF THE CAKE. AMERICA\'S AMBITIONS

AND AGENDA ARE NOT SECRET ANYMORE. THEY STATE IT PUBLICLY IN ALL

MEDIA. THEIR SUCCESS IN IRAQ AND THE FALL OF BAGHDAD WILL LEAD

INTO RESHAPING THE REGION FUNDAMENTALLY AND POSITIVELY."

5. SHARJAH-BASED PAN-ARAB DAILY "AL-KHALEEJ" EDITORIALIZED ON

2/8:

"IS THERE A COMMON FACTOR THAT LINKS THE PARTNERSHIP INITIATIVE

FOR DEMOCRACY PROPOSED BY WASHINGTON FOR THE SO-CALLED REFORM OF

THE ARAB WORLD AND THE AGGRESSION WASHINGTON AND LONDON ARE

PLANNING TO WAGE AGAINST IRAQ? THE GOAL IS ONE: RESHAPING THE

ARAB REGION FUNDAMENTALLY ACCORDING TO U.S. INTERESTS INCLUDING

THOSE OF ISRAEL ... AMERICA WILL TURN IRAQ IN PARTICULAR AND THE

ARAB NATIONS IN GENERAL INTO EARTHENWARE WHERE THE POTTER CAN

DECIDE THEIR FORM, SHAPE, SIZE AND WEIGHT. THE ISSUE IS NOT OF

ALLEGED WEAPONS OF MASS DESTRUCTION WHICH AMERICA, BRITAIN, AND

ISRAEL WANT TO ELIMINATE BUT TO TAKE CONTROL OF ARAB DESTINY AND

REDRAW IT TO SUIT THEIR NEW COLONIAL GREED..."

6. TAKING A TOUGH LINE AGAINST THE US, AN "AL BAYAN" EDITORIAL

COMMENTED ON 2/9:

"ARAB REACTION TO POWELL\'S PRESENTATION ADDED FRESH EVIDENCE OF

THE ARAB REGIME\'S WEAKNESS IN FRONT OF THE US OFFENCE TO

DISMEMBER AND REMAKE THE MIDDLE EAST ... WE HEARD NO BOLD ARAB

REACTION TO POWELL\'S THREATS TO ATTACK IRAQ AND HIS FABRICATED

EVIDENCE ON BAGHDAD\'S ALLEGED WEAPONS OF MASS DESTRUCTION. IT IS

REGRETTABLE THAT THERE WAS NO ARAB REACTION TO EXPOSE POWELL\'S

DRAMA TO INVADE IRAQ. UNFORTUNATELY, REACTIONS FROM WESTERN

CAPITOLS (MOSCOW, PARIS, AND BERLIN), ALTHOUGH NOT IN DEFENCE OF

IRAQ OR ARABS, WERE STRONGER AND MORE DECISIVE THAN THOSE OF ARAB

CAPITALS BECAUSE THEIR INTERESTS ARE VULNERABLE TO THE \'COWBOY\'

INVASION (PLANNED) BY THE U.S. ... WHILE THESE COUNTRIES ARE

TRYING TO HAMPER THE US WAR WAGON TO PROTECT THEIR OWN INTERESTS

IN THE REGION, THE ARABS ARE INVOLVED IN SELLING THE US PROPOSAL

TO IRAQI PRESIDENT SADDAM HUSSEIN TO EXILE TO AVERT WAR."
