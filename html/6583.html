SUBJECT: NOTMAR AND NOTAM DELIVERED TO GOT

REF: SECSTATE 72914

1. Economic Officer delivered Reftel NOTMAR to MFA Chief of

Maritime Affairs Cagatay Erciyes, and reftel NOTAM to MFA

Chief of Aviation Affairs Bekir Uysal on March 20. Uysal

asked if the U.S. could provide coordinates to more

specifically define "Eastern Mediterranean Sea." Both Uysal

and Erciyes said they would immediately inform all relevant

authorities in Turkey. NOTMAR and NOTAM were also delivered

to the Turkish General Staff, Turkish Air Force Command, and

Turkish Naval Forces Command by the Defense Attache\'s Office

via normal military channels.
