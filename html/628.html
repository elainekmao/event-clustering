SUBJECT: TFSROL: AMBASSADOR GALBRAITH MEETS WITH

"KRAJINA PRESIDENT" GORAN HADZIC

REF: ZAGREB 0073

1. ON JANUARY 6, DURING THE VISIT OF UN AMBASSADOR

ALBRIGHT TO SECTOR EAST REPORTED REFTEL, AMBASSADOR

GALBRAITH MET FOR APPROXIMATELY THIRTY MINUTES WITH

CONFIDENTIAL

CONFIDENTIAL

GORAN HADZIC, THE LAME DUCK "PRESIDENT" OF THE

"REPUBLIC OF THE SERBIAN KRAJINA" (RSK) AND MILAN

ILIC, THE HEAD OF THE LOCAL "REGIONAL COUNCIL."

REGIONAL COUNCILS ARE SAID TO BE THE LOCAL

LEGISLATIVE BODIES IN THE SERB-OCCUPIED TERRITORIES

OF CROATIA. THE MEETING TOOK PLACE IN A MUNICIPAL

BUILDING AT THE BOROVO FACTORY OUTSIDE VUKOVAR.

AMBASSADOR ALBRIGHT AND ASSISTANT SECRETARY SHATTUCK

AND THEIR PARTY ALSO ATTENDED THE MEETING, BUT DID

NOT PARTICIPATE. EMBASSY CON/POL OFFICER WAS

NOTETAKER. THE MEETING WAS AGREED TO AFTER THE LOCAL

SERBS THREATENED TO OBSTRUCT THE VISIT OF THE

ALBRIGHT PARTY TO THE OVCARA GRAVE SITE UNLESS THEY

HAD AN OPPORTUNITY TO MEET THE GROUP. THE BRIEF

MEETING FOLLOWED THE VISIT TO THE GRAVE SITE.

(NOTE: OVCARA IS SAID TO BE THE SITE OF A MASS GRAVE

WHERE THE BODIES OF UP TO THREE HUNDRED PEOPLE TAKEN

BY THE SERBS FROM THE VUKOVAR HOSPITAL ARE BURIED.

END NOTE.)

2. AFTER WELCOMING THE GROUP, HADZIC COMPLAINED THAT

THE "RSK" WAS A "SMALL AND POOR" COUNTRY WHICH CANNOT

GET THE TRUTH TO THE WORLD. THE SERBS ARE BLAMED FOR

EVERYTHING, HE SAID, EVEN THE DESTRUCTION OF THE OLD

BRIDGE IN MOSTAR. IT IS NOT TRUE, HE ADDED, THAT

EVERYTHING THE SERBS DO IS BAD AND EVERYTHING THE

CROATS DO IS GOOD, "JUST AS IT WOULD BE A LIE IF I

TOLD YOU EVERYTHING THE SERBS DID WAS GOOD." HADZIC

SAID THE SERBS HAD FOUGHT TO DEFEND THEIR HOMES AFTER

THE WAR WAS STARTED BY CROATS WHO KILLED OR SET FIRE

TO EVERYTHING WHICH BELONGED TO THE SERBS. HE

CONCLUDED BY SEEKING THE GROUPS\' OBSERVATIONS HAVING

JUST VISITED THE OVCARA GRAVE SITE.

3. AMBASSADOR GALBRAITH RESPONDED THAT, AS

"AMBASSADOR TO THIS COUNTRY," HE WANTED TO EXPRESS

THE UNITED STATES\' COMMITMENT TO HUMAN RIGHTS FOR

"ALL OF THE PEOPLES OF FORMER YUGOSLAVIA." NOTING

THAT THE OVCARA SITE HAD MADE A STRONG IMPRESSION ON

ALL MEMBERS OF THE GROUP, HE EXPRESSED THE

DETERMINATION OF THE U.S. THAT THOSE RESPONSIBLE FOR

WAR CRIMES "ON ALL SIDES" WILL BE HELD ACCOUNTABLE.

HE THEN DETAILED U.S. POLICY TOWARDS THE REGION,

SPECIFICALLY NOTING OUR SUPPORT FOR THE TERRITORIAL

INTEGRITY OF CROATIA AND THE DEMAND THAT THE HUMAN

RIGHTS OF ALL GROUPS, INCLUDING THE SERBIAN

COMMUNITY, BE PROTECTED "IN FACT AS WELL AS IN LAW."

ASKED WHY ATTENTION WAS FOCUSED ON CRIMES IN THE

FORMER YUGOSLAVIA? HE ADDED, HOWEVER, THAT HE WAS

WILLING TO SEE SERB CRIMINALS FACE THE TRIBUNAL AND

EVEN UNDERSTOOD THAT SERBS MAY NEED TO BE THE FIRST

TRIED, "BUT NOT ONLY SERBS SHOULD BE TRIED."

5. MILAN ILIC, HEAD OF THE REGIONAL COUNCIL THEN

DELIVERED A SPEECH FULL OF BOMBAST, COMPLETE WITH

QUOTATIONS FROM MARX (AND ELIHU ROOT), WHILE

WAVING IN THE AIR BOOKS ALLEGEDLY PROVING CROATIAN

GUILT FOR THE WAR. HE STATED FIRMLY THAT SERBS WOULD

NEVER ACCEPT BEING PART OF CROATIA AGAIN BECAUSE

"THAT WOULD MEAN THE END OF SERBS\' EXISTENCE."

6. ASKED ABOUT THE RETURN OF DISPLACED PERSONS TO

THEIR HOMES IN THE KRAJINA, HADZIC SAID THAT THOSE

NOT GUILTY OF WAR CRIMES (ADDING "WHILE MOST CROATS

ARE, A FEW ARE NOT") CAN RETURN AS SOON AS THEY

ACKNOWLEDGE THE KRAJINA\'S SOVEREIGNTY AND BECOME

"RSK" CITIZENS. HADZIC QUIPPED THAT U.S. AND RSK

POLICIES WERE NEARLY IDENTICAL, SINCE THE KRAJINA

ALSO SUPPORTS CROATIA\'S TERRITORIAL INTEGRITY "WITHIN

ITS CURRENT BOUNDARIES."

7. CONCLUDING THE MEETING, AMBASSADOR GALBRAITH SAID

THAT HE FELT IT COULD MARK THE BEGINNING OF A

DIALOGUE WITH THE SERBIAN COMMUNITY IN THE KRAJINA.

TO ARRANGE A VISIT TO SECTOR EAST FOR A CONGRESSIONAL

STAFF DELEGATION. WHEN UNPROFOR WENT TO THE "KRAJINA
