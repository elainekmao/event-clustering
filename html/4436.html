START MEMO NOMINATING-JUSTIFICATION:

Telecommunication has now become a very important

part of Maldivian life as the country spread over a

vast area of sea with inhabited islands situated far

apart.

Currently there is only one telecommunications

provider in the Maldives, "Deeraagu" a joint

collaboration between the Maldivian government and

the Cable and Wireless Co. of U.K. The

Telecommunications Department is keen to introduce

competition by allowing other telecom operations and

internet service providers to operate in the

Maldives. However, they lack sufficient experience

in handling issues and introduce transparent

regulatory and other principles of legislative

control.

Mr. Amir\'s position has thus become very vital. He

was one of the key people who handled the Y2K

problem in the Maldives. As stated earlier Mr. Amir

needs exposure to other developed countries to

establish a level playing fielding for investments

by telecom companies. An exposure to the U.S. where

he could visit telecom and ISP regulatory bodies and

meet with relevant authorities to observe and

consult would help Maldives to provide a healthy

competitive climate where even U.S. telecom and ISP

providers could compete.

END MEMO NOMINATING-JUSTIFICATION:
