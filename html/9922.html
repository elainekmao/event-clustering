SUBJECT: MEETING WITH MFA ON BASAYEV PRENOTIFICATION AND

FSAT REPORT

REF: A. A) STATE 224095

B. B) STATE 197548

1. (U) CLASSSIFIED BY ECONOMIC COUNSELOR SCOT MARCIEL FOR

REASONS 1.5 (B), (D).

2. (SBU) ON AUGUST 5, EMBOFFS MET WITH MFA DEPUTY DIRECTOR

GENERAL FOR RESEARCH AND INTELLIGENCE HAKAN OKCAL TO EXECUTE

REFTEL INSTRUCTIONS.

3. (S) EMBOFFS DELIVERED REF A TALKING POINTS EMPHASIZING THE

CLOSE HOLD. AS EXPECTED, OKCAL NOTED THAT THE GOT WOULD NEED

UN APPROVAL OF THE DESIGNATION BEFORE ISSUING A

LEGALLY-BINDING DECREE. ACCORDING TO OKCAL THIS IS A

SOMEWHAT CUMBERSOME PROCESS BUT IS NECESSARY TO ENSURE THAT

COURTS TAKE ACTION AGAINST DESIGNATED INDIVIDUALS. IN THE

MEANTIME, OKCAL UNDERTOOK TO SHARE THE INFORMATION WITH

APPROPRIATE AGENCIES WHO WOULD CHECK THEIR FILES AND

DATABASES FOR EVIDENCE OF BASAYEV ASSETS OR ACTIVITIES IN

TURKEY.

4. (C) OKCAL COMMENTED ON THE RECURRING FRICTIONS BETWEEN

TURKISH AND RUSSIAN AUTHORITIES OVER ALLEGED TURKISH SUPPORT

FOR CHECHEN GROUPS. OKCAL PUT THE NUMBER OF TURKS OF CHECHEN

OR CIRCASSIAN ORIGIN AT 4 OR 5 MILLION BUT CLAIMED THAT THE

GOT HAD ACTED DECISIVELY TO CLOSE DOWN TURKISH CITIZENS\'

FUNDING OF CHECHEN TERRORISTS AND CHECHEN REBELS ACCESS TO

TURKEY. THE GOR, ESPECIALLY MILITARY AND SECURITY OFFICIALS,

OCCASIONALLY ACCUSE THE GOT OF NOT BEING TOUGH ENOUGH ON THIS

ISSUE BUT OKCAL DENIED THESE ACCUSATIONS AND NOTED THE GOT

HAD SIMILAR CONCERNS ABOUT RUSSIAN POLICY VIS-A-VIS THE PKK.

5. (U) AS REQUESTED IN REF B, EMBOFFS PASSED ON THE FSAT

REPORT TO OKCAL. OKCAL THANKED THE USG FOR THE REPORT AND

SAID HE WOULD REVIEW IT AND DISCUSS WITH OTHER GOVERNMENT

AGENCIES HAVING RESPONSIBILITY FOR COMBATTING FINANCIAL

CRIMES.

DEUTSCH
