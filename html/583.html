SUMMARY

-------

1. KRIVOKUCA MURDER UNDER INTENSE INVESTIGATION.

UNPROFOR FALLS SHORT IN REQUEST FOR CHRISTMAS

VISITS TO SECTOR SOUTH. SPLIT SUMMONS 29 EX-JNA

TO ANSWER FOR WAR CRIMES. NEW TRADE LAW PAVES WAY

FOR CONCESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT. END SUMMARY.

------------------------------------------

MURDER OF LABOR ACTIVIST GETS TOP PRIORITY

------------------------------------------

2. THE INVESTIGATION OF THE DECEMBER 17 MURDER OF

FORMER LABOR UNION LEADER AND POLITICAL ACTIVIST

MILAN KRIVOKUCA IS SAID TO BE RECEIVING PRIORITY

UNCLASSIFIED

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 03 ZAGREB 02816 01 OF 02 241219Z

ATTENTION FROM ZAGREB POLICE OFFICIALS. NO

DETAILS OF THE OFFICIAL INQUIRY HAVE BEEN MADE

PUBLIC. COLLEAGUES FROM THE SOCIAL DEMOCRATIC

UNION, A NEW POLITICAL PARTY WITH WHICH KRIVOKUCA

WAS ASSOCIATED, HAVE ASKED FOR A THOROUGH

INVESTIGATION TO DETERMINE IF POLITICAL MOTIVES

WERE INVOLVED. THE SPLIT WEEKLY "NEDJELJNA

DALMACIJA" RAISED THE QUESTION ON DECEMBER 24 OF

WHETHER KRAJINA SERBS MIGHT HAVE BEEN THE

CULPRITS. KRIVOKUCA, A CROATIAN OF SERBIAN

NATIONALITY, REPORTEDLY HAD SOUGHT UNPROFOR

COOPERATION TO HELP RESTART POLITICAL CONTACTS

WITH SERBIAN MODERATES IN THE SERBIAN-CONTROLLED

PARTS OF THE UNPAS.

-----------------------------------------

UNPROFOR FAILS WITH CHRISTMAS VISITS PLAN

-----------------------------------------

3. THE ZADAR CITY ASSEMBLY HAS COMPLAINED THAT

UNPROFOR FAILED TO ARRANGE THE VISIT OF CROATIAN

DISPLACED PERSONS TO THEIR VILLAGES AND CHURCHES

IN UN-CONTROLEED SECTOR SOUTH. UNPROFOR

-

REPORTEDLY ARRANGED VISITS TO ONLY THREE LOCATIONS

IN ALL OF SECTOR SOUTH. UNPROFOR ALSO

ACKNOWLEDGED THAT, OF THE SEVERAL HUNDRED CATHOLIC

CHURCHES WHICH USED TO EXIST IN SECTOR SOUTH, ONLY

THREE REMAIN, ONE EACH IN OBROVAC, BENKOVAC, AND

NOVIGRAD.

PARLIAMENT PASSES ECON CONCESSIONS LAW

--------------------------------------

5. THE CROATIAN SABOR PASSED A LAW ON DECEMBER 23

REGULATING CONCESSIONS FOR NATURAL RESOURCE

DEVELOPMENT AND OTHER SPECIALIZED PROJECTS. AMONG

ITS PROVISIONS IS THE RIGHT FOR BOTH CROATIAN AND
