3. (SBU) SUMMARY. THE ROLE OF SCIENCE IN THE ITALIAN

BIOTECHNOLOGY DEBATE IS GROWING, THANKS IN PART TO EMBASSY

EFFORTS. IN THE LAST TEN DAYS, TWO DEBATES FEATURING LEADING

U.S. AND ITALIAN SCIENTISTS HAVE BEEN WELL COVERED BY THE

MEDIA AND HAVE THROWN INTO QUESTION THE UNSCIENTIFIC

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 03 ROME 02999 01 OF 05 191328Z

FOUNDATIONS USED BY AGMIN ALEMANNO TO POSIT HIS "TIME FOR

CHOICES" WHITE PAPER. ALEMANNO\'S TEAM IS REGARDED WITH

SKEPTICISM AND EVEN CONTEMPT BY THE ITALIAN SCIENTIFIC

COMMUNITY. AT THE MINISTER\'S JUNE 4 STAGE-MANAGED PUBLIC

ROLL-OUT OF HIS WHITE PAPER, OUTDATED AND SUPERSEDED RESEARCH

WAS PRESENTED AS GOSPEL, SECONDED BY A THEOLOGIAN, AND

COMMENTED ON FROM THE FLOOR BY A PRESELECTED GROUP OF

PRO-ALEMANNO, ANTI-GMO PROPONENTS.

4. (SBU) SUMMARY CONTINUED. OTHER ELEMENTS OF THE GOI

APPEAR MORE AND MORE WILLING TO ENDORSE SCIENCE AND RESEARCH

AS A WAY TO OPPOSE THE ALEMANNO LUDDITES AND THEIR ANTI-GMO

PROPOSALS. THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, IN ON-THE-RECORD

ADDRESSES AT THE DG AND U/S LEVELS, ENDORSED THE NEED FOR

SCIENCE-BASED POLICY, STRONG U.S.-ITALIAN RESEARCH

COOPERATION, AND USING THAT COOPERATION TO REOPEN A BROADER

U.S.-EU DIALOGUE DURING THE 2003 ITALIAN PRESIDENCY. THE PM\'S

NATIONAL BIOTECHNOLOGY COMMISSION PRESIDENT HAS SPOKEN

SIMILARLY, AND TOLD US WE SHOULD EXPECT ENDORSEMENT OF THE

PRINCIPLES OF COOPERATION DOCUMENT DESCRIBED REF A FROM THE

PM\'S OFFICE BY THE END OF JUNE. LAST, BUT BY NO MEANS LEAST,

THE PRIME MINISTER, CONTINUING TO FIND HIS WAY WITH CAUTION,

HAS SAID THAT ITALY MUST REMAIN OPEN TO RESEARCH, AND LOOK TO

THE EXPERIENCE OF OTHER COUNTRIES. THIS IS AN OPPORTUNITY WE

SHOULD NOT FAIL TO EXPLOIT. SEE EMBASSY ANALYSIS STARTING

PARA 16.END SUMMARY.

RAISING THE BAR ON FEAR-MONGERING?

5. (U) THE ITALIAN SCIENTIFIC COMMUNITY HAS REMAINED

STEADFASTLY PRO-BIOTECHNOLOGY, BUT HAS BEEN RELUCTANT TO

OPPOSE ALEMANNO\'S PROPOSED POLICY, AND THEY ARE TRYING TO

HELP EXPOSE THE SHABBY SCIENTIFIC UNDERPINNINGS OF THE

AGRICULTURE MINISTER\'S WHITE PAPER BY CONDUCTING THEIR OWN

PROGRAMS WHICH SHOW THE BENEFITS OF THE TECHNOLOGY.

BIOTECHNOLOGY.
