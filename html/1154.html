SUBJECT: ANTI-TRAFFICKING WORKSHOP IN KIEV

REF: (A) STATE 111094; (B) STATE 108676

1. WE WERE INFORMED BY THE DUTCH FOREIGN AFFAIRS

MINISTRY TODAY THAT MR. H.F. VAN DRUNEN OF THE DUTCH

EMBASSY IN UKRAINE WILL BE PARTICIPATING IN THE ANTI-

TRAFFICKING WORKSHOP ON JUNE 21-23. THE MFA NOTED THAT

NO OTHER DUTCH OFFICIALS WILL BE AVAILABLE BECAUSE THE

APPROPRIATE PERSONS WILL BE ATTENDING THE OSCE MEETING

ON TRAFFICKING IN HUMAN BEINGS IN VIENNA ON JUNE 19

(REF B).

2. ACCORDING TO THE MFA, MS. M. PECHACZEK OF THE

FOREIGN MINISTRY\'S DEPARTMENT FOR WOMEN AND DEVELOPMENT

WILL ATTEND THE OSCE MEETING, AS WELL AS A

REPRESENTATIVE FROM THE DUTCH JUSTICE MINISTRY.
