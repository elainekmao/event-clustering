1. (C) SUMMARY: UNDER SECRETARY OF DEFENSE FOR POLICY

DOUGLAS FEITH MET WITH MOD FAHIM KHAN IN KABUL ON 29

SEPTEMBER. MOD FAHIM MET WITH THE U.S. DELEGATION IN

PRESIDENT KARZAI\'S OFFICE AND MADE THE FOLLOWING KEY POINTS

DURING THE DISCUSSION:

(A) MOD SAID HE SHARES U.S. OBJECTIVES FOR PEACE AND

STABILITY FOR AFGHANISTAN.

(B) COMMITTED TO PROVIDING THE RESOURCES AND SUPPORT FOR AN

AFGHAN NATIONAL ARMY.

(C) RECOGNIZE AND SUPPORT U.S.-LED ANA PROGRAM.

(D) GREATER RECONSTRUCTION ASSISTANCE NEEDED FOR SUCCESSFUL

DEMOBILIZATION/DISARMAMENT.

2. (C) USDP CONVEYED THE FOLLOWING KEY POINTS:

(A) BUILDING ANA IS INTEGRAL TO CREATING A PROSPEROUS AND

STABLE AFGHANISTAN.

(B) "AFGHAN NATIONAL ARMY" AS US OFFICIALS ARE USING THE

TERM, REFERS TO U.S.-LED TRAINING EFFORT; NOT TO AFGHAN MOD

PROGRAM.

(C) MOD NEEDS TO ASSIGN MISSIONS TO THE GRADUATING BATTALIONS

AND TO EQUIP THEM.

(D) RECOGNIZE IMPORTANT LINK BETWEEN ECONOMIC AND SECURITY

RECONSTRUCTION.

CONFIDENTIAL

(E) NEED TO PRESERVE LEGITIMATE AFGHAN POLITICAL PROCESSES.

(F) GREAT OPPORTUNITY FOR MOD FAHIM TO PLAY A UNIFYING ROLE

FOR THE COUNTRY DURING PEACETIME.

3. (C) MOD FAHIM STRIKES A NATIONAL CHORD:

-- MOD FAHIM NOTED THE SUCCESSFUL MILITARY ALLIANCE OF THE

U.S. AND HIS FORCES AND THEIR ABILITY TO OVERCOME HARDSHIPS

TOGETHER.

-- HE AFFIRMED THE MOD\'S WILLINGNESS TO PROVIDE RECRUITS AND

RESOURCES FOR THE ANA. BUT NOTED THAT U.S. SUPPORT WOULD BE

WELCOME GIVEN THE MOD\'S LIMITED CAPABILITIES.

-- HE SAID HE AGREED WITH USDP FEITH THAT ANA REFERS TO

US-LED TRAINING EFFORT, AND THAT ANA SHOULD NOT REFER TO THE

ARMIES OF THE REGIONAL LEADERS.

-- COMMANDER AHMAD SHAH MASOUD AND HIS SUPPORTERS LONG CALLED

FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF CENTRAL AUTHORITY AND GOVERNANCE.

-- TOGETHER WITH LTG BARYALAI, THE MOD IS WORKING TO

ESTABLISH ANA RECRUITING CRITERIA.

-- NATIONAL MISSION AND NATIONAL MAKE-UP FOR THE ANA IS THE

ONLY VIABLE WAY AHEAD.

-- GRADUATED ANA BATTALIONS SHOULD CONDUCT COUNTERINSURGENCY

OPERATIONS, PROJECT THE CENTRAL AUTHORITY\'S POWER IN THE

REGIONS, AND SERVE TO DIMINISH REGIONAL FORCE PRESENCE AND

LEGITIMACY.

-- TRAINING ANA FORCES IN THE REGIONS WOULD HELP DIMINISH THE

INFLUENCE OF REGIONAL MILITIAS.

-- THE SLOW PACE OF ECONOMIC RECONSTRUCTION WILL HAMPER

DEMOBILIZATION/DISARMAMENT/REINTEGRATION EFFORTS.

-- THE AFGHAN PEOPLE ARE TIRED OF WAR.

-- THE PROBLEMS AND CHALLENGES BROUGHT ON BY 23 YEARS OF WAR

REGIONAL ARMIES.

-- HE ENCOURAGED THE MOD TO CONSIDER WAYS THE AFGHAN
