SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION: WAR IN IRAQ POWS CIVILIAN CASUALTIES

1. SUMMARY: THE WAR IN IRAQ, AN ALLEGED U.S. DOUBLE STANDARD ON

POWS, CIVILIAN CASUALTIES, AND THE "OUR PEOPLE IN IRAQ" CAMPAIGN

DOMINATED COVERAGE IN UAE MEDIA. IN AN "AL KHALEEJ" ARTICLE, DR.

HASSAN MADAN ALLEGED A DOUBLE STANDARD IN U.S. POLICY TOWARDS

POWS. UNDER THE HEADLINE "THE DILEMMA AND FEAR," AN "AL BAYAN"

EDITORIAL NOTES THAT U.S. FORCES ARE PLANNING TO USE BANNED

WEAPONS, WHILE ANOTHER COLUMNIST COMPARES PRESIDENT BUSH TO WW2

NAZI LEADERS. END SUMMARY.

2. DUBAI TV FINISHED A 12-HOUR TELETHON EARLY MORNING 3/26 AFTER

RAISING NEARLY 49 MILLION AED ($14 MILLION) FOR HUMANITARIAN AID

TO IRAQIS, FEATURING PROMINENT EMIRATIS AS WELL AS EGYPTIAN AND

SYRIAN FILM STARS. ALL VOICED STRONG OPPOSITION TO THE WAR, WITH

ONE UAE OFFICIAL CALLING ON " THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY TO STOP

THIS AGGRESSION" AND ON "THE AMERICAN AND BRITISH AMBASSADORS TO

REPORT THE FEELINGS OF THE PUBLIC HONESTLY."

3. UNDER THE HEADLINE "THE CASUALTIES OF WAR," DUBAI-BASED

ENGLISH-LANGUAGE DAILY "GULF NEWS" EDITORIALIZED 3/26:

"... YET SOME DAYS LATER, POCKETS OF RESISTANCE ARE STILL BEING

MET AND COUNTERED WITH GREAT DIFFICULTY. EXPECTATIONS OF HORDES

OF GLEEFUL IRAQIS WELCOMING THE \'LIBERATING TROOPS\' WITH BOUQUETS

AND SWEETMEATS HAVE FAILED TO MATERIALISE, AS HAVE THE SO-CALLED

WEAPONS OF MASS DESTRUCTION ALLEGEDLY HELD BY THE IRAQIS ..."

4. UNDER THE HEADLINE "THE DILEMMA AND FEAR," DUBAI-BASED

BUSINESS-ORIENTED "AL BAYAN" EDITORIALIZED 3/26:

"... DURING THIS DILEMMA (FOR THE U.S. MILITARY), IT IS NOT FAR

FROM TRUTH THAT U.S. FORCES MIGHT USE \'DESTRUCTIVE METHODS\'

OUTSIDE NORMAL WAR PLANNING. SOME REPORTERS MENTIONED YESTERDAY

THAT THE U.S. FORCES USED CLUSTER BOMBS ON BASRA ... WHAT IS

FRIGHTENING IS THAT THE AMERICANS HAVE BEGUN TO CONSIDER USING

MILITARILY BANNED MATERIAL, AND IF THAT HAPPENS, THE \'SHOCK AND

AWE\' WILL NOT ONLY HIT IRAQ AND THE ARABS BUT THE WORLD AS A

WHOLE."

5. COMMENTING ON THE U.S. PLANS TO REMOLD THE REGION, SHARJAH-

BASED PAN-ARAB DAILY "AL-KHALEEJ" EDITORIALIZED 3/26:

"... IN PALESTINE, A WAR OF LIQUIDATION HAS BEEN WAGED USING

AMERICAN WEAPONS. IN IRAQ, AN AMERICAN-BRITISH WAR OF

LIQUIDATION IS BEING WAGED AGAINST THE IRAQI PEOPLE USING ISRAELI

WEAPONS ... THE REGION IS BEING REMOLDED WITH ARAB BLOOD WHILE

ARABS ARE WATCHING AND COUNTING THE MISSILES THAT FALL ON IRAQI

PEOPLE, JUST AS BEFORE WITH THE MISSILES THAT FELL AND ARE STILL

FALLING ON THE PALESTINIANS."

6. DESCRIBING THE IMAGE BUSH PAINTS FOR THE U.S., UAE COLUMNIST

AYSHA SULTAN WROTE IN "AL BAYAN" 3/25:

"... THE PICTURE THAT PRESIDENT BUSH DRAWS FOR THE U.S. IS AN

EXTREMELY UGLY IMAGE OF A COWBOY DRUNK WITH THE IDEA OF

TYRANNICAL POWER. THIS HAS LED HIM TO IMAGINE THAT HE CAN DO

WHATEVER HE WANTS TO WHOMEVER. AMERICANS THINK THAT THE ARAB

WORLD AND IN PARTICULAR, THE ISLAMIC WORLD, ARE REGIONS INHABITED

BY SAVAGE ARAB TERRORISTS, WHO NEED TO BE ELIMINATED TO REALIZE

THE PROPHECY AND STABILITY OF AMERICA AND THE WELL-BEING OF

AMERICANS ... OH PEOPLE OF IRAQ: YOU ARE TURNING, ON BEHALF OF

THE (ARAB) NATION, A DANGEROUS CORNER; AN IDEA CONFRONTING A

TANK; CULTURE CONFRONTING DEATH; CIVILIZATION CONFRONTING A

TOMAHAWK; AND A HUMAN BEING FACING EXPULSION. YOU ARE STANDING

LIKE AN ARROW IN THE DESERT, LIKE PALESTINE AND BEIRUT BEFORE

YOU, AND JUST AS OTHER CAPITALS WILL STAND IN THE FUTURE ..."

7. ALLEGING A U.S. DOUBLE STANDARD IN THE TREATMENT OF POWS, UAE

COLUMNIST DR.HASSAN MADAN WROTE IN "AL KHALEEJ" 3/26:

"... THE U.S. IN PARTICULAR SHOULD BE THE LAST TO DISCUSS RESPECT

OF THE GENEVA CONVENTION REGARDING POWS. THE U.S. IGNORED AND IS

STILL IGNORING ALL THE OBJECTIONS OF THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY

AND HUMAN RIGHTS ORGANIZATIONS IN THE TREATMENT OF THE ARABS AND

NON-ARAB PRISONERS SHIPPED TO GUANTANAMO BAY IN CUBA. THEIR

TREATMENT IS A HUMILIATION OF ALL HUMAN DIGNITY ..."

8 COMPARING GEORGE BUSH TO NAZI LEADER HERMAN GOERING, A

COLUMNIST NICKNAMED ABU KHALDOUN WROTE IN "AL BAYAN" 3/26:

"... DURING THE TRIAL OF THE NAZI LEADER HERMAN GOERING (HITLER\'S

DEPUTY), HE WAS ASKED: DID THE PEOPLE OF GERMANY WANT THE WAR?

HE ANSWERED: THE NORMAL MAN IN THE STREET AND ALL PEOPLE OF ALL

NATIONS DO NOT WANT WAR, BUT IT IS THE POLITICAL LEADERS WHO

DECIDE A NATION\'S POLICY ... PRESIDENT GEORGE BUSH, IN HIS

CAMPAIGN TO PREPARE FOR THE INVASION OF IRAQ, FOLLOWED THIS SAME

METHOD REVEALED BY THE NAZI LEADER IN THE NEUREMBERG TRIAL. HE

ANNOUNCED FROM THE FIRST DAY OF MOBILIZATION: \'WHOEVER IS NOT

WITH US IS AGAINST US\' AND CREATED A SITUATION OF ANXIETY AND

FEAR AMONG THE AMERICAN PEOPLE ..."

9 MARIAM ABDULLAH WROTE IN HER DAILY COLUMN IN "AL-BAYAN" 3/26

"... IRAQ\'S ACCEPTANCE OF THE CHALLENGE (OF THE WAR) WAS THE

GREATEST AND MOST WONDERFUL OPTION THAT HELPED TO RESTORE PART OF

OUR WOUNDED DIGNITY AND ALLOWED US A RARE OPPORTUNITY TO SEE A

VITAL PART OF OUR (ARAB) ENTITY RESISTING, FACE THE AGGRESSORS,

AND REJECT A DRINK FROM THE CUP OF HUMILIATION ..."
