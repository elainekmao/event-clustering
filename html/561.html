SUMMARY

-------

1. WE REPORT HEREIN FOR THE RECORD RECENT PUBLIC

REMARKS BY UNPROFOR CIVILIAN AFFAIRS DIRECTOR CEDRIC

THORNBERRY REGARDING THE SITUATION IN THE CROATIAN

UNPA\'S AND PINK ZONES. REFERING TO SECTORS EAST AND

SOUTH, THORNBERRY DECLARED, "GANGS OF TERRORISTS ROAM

THE AREA, THERE ARE POLICE FORCES WHO DO NOT POLICE,

PROSECUTORS WHO DO NOT PROSECUTE, AND COURTS THAT ARE

AFRAID TO SIT." AT A MEETING WITH AUTHORITIES IN

UNCLASSIFIED

UNCLASSIFIED

KNIN, UNPROFOR REASSERTED ITS AUTHORITY TO CONTROL

BORDER CROSSING POINTS BUT FAILED TO OBTAIN SERBIAN

AGREEMENT TO DRAW BACK FROM FIVE BRIDGES. UNPROFOR

ESTIMATES THAT THERE ARE AT LEAST 5,000 ARMED,

UNIFORMED PERSONNEL IN SECTOR EAST AT THE MOMENT; IN

CLEAR VIOLATION OF THE VANCE PLAN.

2. THORNBERRY WARNED THAT IF THE LOCAL SERBIAN

LEADERS DO NOT ACCEPT THE BASIC ELEMENTS OF

RESOLUTION 762, WHICH CALLS FOR A STEP-BY-STEP

RESTORATION OF CROATIAN AUTHORITY IN THE PINK ZONES,

THE SECURITY COUNCIL WILL HAVE TO BE INFORMED. HE

STATED THAT UNPROFOR HAS NO INTENTION OF BEING AN

ACCESSORY TO THE CONSOLIDATION OF THE RULE OF THE

PINK ZONES BY AUTHORITIES OTHER THAN THOSE STATED IN

THE RESOLUTION.

3. THORNBERRY CONDEMNED THOSE RESPONSIBLE FOR

PUTTING A REPORTED FIFTEEN TONS OF EXPLOSIVES IN THE

CENTRAL LOWER GALLERY OF THE PERUCA DAM. AFTER

FAILING TO OBTAIN MAPS AND DIAGRAMS FROM THE KNIN

AUTHORITIES, UNPROFOR SENT IN A TEAM OF BRITISH

DEMOLITIONS EXPERTS. RECENT PRESS REPORTS INDICATE

THAT THE MINES HAVE NOW BEEN "NEUTRALIZED." END

SUMMARY.

THE UNPROFOR PRESS BRIEFING HELD ON NOVEMBER 5 IN

BELGRADE BY CEDRIC THORNBERRY, DIRECTOR OF CIVILIAN

DURING THE WINTER. IN ONE AREA NEIGHBORING SECTOR

WEST, UNPROFOR HAS PROVIDED INFORMATION RELATING TO

RESOLUTION 780 TO THE WAR CRIMES COMMISSION, WHICH

WAS SCHEDULED TO MEET YESTERDAY FOR THE FIRST TIME.

7. IN SECTORS EAST AND SOUTH, THE TREND TOWARD

ANARCHY CONTINUES. GANGS OF TERRORISTS ROAM THE

AREA, THERE ARE POLICE FORCES WHO DO NOT POLICE,

PROSECUTORS WHO DO NOT PROSECUTE, AND COURTS THAT ARE

SERB-CONTROLLED TERRITORIES, BUT THERE ARE ALSO SOME

AGAINST SERBS LIVING IN CROAT-CONTROLLED AREAS. IN A

WAY, THERE HAS BEEN SOME IMPROVEMENT, AS THERE ARE

FEWER MURDERS THAN BEFORE.

8. ALONG THE EDGES OF SECTOR SOUTH, SOME CEASE-FIRE

VIOLATIONS, MAINLY BY THE CROATIAN ARMY, ARE PROBABLY

PROVOCATIONS. AS THERE HAS STILL NOT BEEN

DISARMAMANT OF THE SERB MILITIA, THE CROATIAN ARMY

HAS, IN SOME CASES, MOVED BACK UP FROM THE WITHDRAWAL

LINE, TO THE CONFRONTATION LINE, AND THE RELATIVELY

HEAVILY ARMED SERB MILITIA HAVE ALSO MOVED UP TO THAT

LINE.

MASLENICA BRIDGE STILL ON HOLD:

-------------------------------

10. THE REBUILDING OF MASLENICA BRIDGE, LOCATED IN A

PINK ZONE, HAS THE AGREEMENT OF ALL SIDES, BUT NO ONE

SEEMS TO WANT TO "ROLL UP THEIR SLEEVES AND GET TO

WORK" ON THE PROJECT. IT MUST MOVE AHEAD AS SOON AS

POSSIBLE.

------------------------------------------

5,000 UNAUTHORIZED "POLICE" IN SECTOR EAST

------------------------------------------

11. SECTOR EAST HAS BEEN RATHER QUIET LATELY. AN

ACCOUNT PUBLISHED IN THE BELGRADE PRESS ON EVENTS

AROUND BATINA BRIDGE CAUSED THORNBERRY\'S AMAZEMENT,

AND THE CONCLUSION THAT "IF YOU DON\'T HAVE ANY FACTS,

GO UP TO THE CRAZIEST PERSON AROUND AND PUBLISH WHAT

HE SAYS."

12. ON A MORE SERIOUS NOTE, UNPROFOR ESTIMATES THERE

ARE AT LEAST 5,000 ARMED, UNIFORMED PERSONNEL IN

SECTOR EAST AT THE MOMENT; IN CLEAR VIOLATION OF THE

VANCE PLAN. UNPROFOR IS NOT SURE WHO IS IN CONTROL

OF ALL OF THESE ELEMENTS. "PERHAPS UNPROFOR BELGRADE

COULD ORGANIZE A TOUR FOR JOURNALISTS SO THEY COULD

SEE FOR THEMSELVES? - SECTOR EAST IS QUITE CLOSE TO

BELGRADE." (NOTE: PRESS REPORTS INDICATE THAT IN

FACT A VISIT BY JOURNALISTS IS NOW BEING ARRANGED.)

DISCUSSION. WITH REGARD TO RESOLUTION 769, WHICH

REQUIRES UNPROFOR TO CONTROL THE CROSSING POINTS FROM

THE UNITED NATIONS PROTECTED AREAS INTO OTHER PARTS

OF THE FORMER YUGOSLAVIA, THE KNIN AUTHORITIES AGREED

TO MOVE THEIR CHECKPOINTS BACK CA. 500 METRES FROM

THE UNPROFOR CHECKPOINTS AT THE BOUNDARIES, EXCEPT ON

FIVE BRIDGES; THESE ARE STILL UNDER NEGOTIATION.

TALKS ON THE DISARMING OF THE SPECIAL MILITIA WERE

NOT FRUITFUL AND DISCUSSIONS WILL CONTINUE.

REGARDING RESOLUTION 762 ON THE PINK ZONES, WHICH

CALLS FOR A STEP-BY-STEP RESTORATION OF CROATIAN
