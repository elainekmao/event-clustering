SUBJECT: TFIZ01: GOC STATEMENT ON THE FALL OF IRAQI REGIME

1. ON APRIL 11, THE GOVERNMENT OF CROATIA ISSUED A

STATEMENT COMMENTING ON THE FALL OF BAGHDAD AND THE

COLLAPSE OF THE IRAQI REGIME. THE STATEMENT (FULL TEXT

BELOW) HAILS THE END OF THE REGIME AS A CHANCE FOR THE

FUTURE OF THE IRAQI PEOPLE, AS WELL AS FOR THE ADVANCEMENT

OF PEACE AND STABILITY IN THE REGION. THE STATEMENT ALSO

REITERATES CROATIA\'S WILLINGNESS TO CONTRIBUTE TO

RECONSTRUCTION AND CALLS FOR A UN ROLE IN ASSURING HUMAN

RIGHTS PROTECTION IN POST-CONFLICT IRAQ.

2. BEGIN TEXT OF GOC STATEMENT:

THE GOVERNMENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF CROATIA ASSESSES THE FALL

OF SADDAM HUSSEIN\'S DICTATORSHIP REGIME AS A GOOD CHANCE

FOR THE FUTURE OF THE IRAQI PEOPLE, AS WELL AS FOR THE

ADVANCEMENT OF PEACE AND STABILITY IN THE WIDER REGION.

THE GOVERNMENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF CROATIA HOPES THAT

MILITARY OPERATIONS, AS WELL AS THE SUFFERING OF IRAQI

CITIZENS, ALLIANCE SOLDIERS, JOURNALISTS, AND OTHER

VICTIMS, WILL SOON BE OVER.

IN THE CURRENT CIRCUMSTANCES IN IRAQ, THE ESTABLISHMENT OF

PUBLIC PEACE AND ORDER, THE TRUE PROTECTION OF HUMAN

RIGHTS, AND THE DELIVERY OF HUMANITARIAN ASSISTANCE IN

WHICH THE UN SHOULD HAVE AN IMPORTANT ROLE, IS OF CRUCIAL

IMPORTANCE. THE GOVERNMENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF CROATIA

EXPECTS IRAQ TO BE CONSTITUTED AS A SOVEREIGN COUNTRY WITH

THE RULE OF LAW, WHICH WILL ENABLE A PEACEFUL AND DIGNIFIED

LIFE TO ALL ITS CITIZENS AS SOON AS POSSIBLE.

THE GOVERNMENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF CROATIA PASSED A DECISION

ON APRIL 3, 2002 ON SENDING HUMANITARIAN ASSISTANCE TO

IRAQ\'S WAR-AFFECTED POPULATION IN THE AMOUNT OF 20 MILLION

KUNA, AND ARRANGEMENTS ABOUT ITS SPEEDY DELIVERY AND

DISTRIBUTION ARE CURRENTLY BEING DISCUSSED. THE GOVERNMENT

REITERATES THE WILLINGNESS OF THE REPUBLIC OF CROATIA TO

PARTICIPATE IN REMOVING THE CONSEQUENCES OF THE WAR AND IN

THE ECONOMIC RECONSTRUCTION OF IRAQ.

END TEXT.
