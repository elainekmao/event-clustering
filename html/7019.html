SUBJECT: TFIZ01: ITALY ECHOING OUR MESSAGE ON IRAQI WAR

CRIMES

REF: SECSTATE 81638

1. (SBU) ON MARCH 31, POL MC DELIVERED REFTEL POINTS TO

CLAUDIO BISOGNERO, THE MFA\'S DEPUTY POLITICAL DIRECTOR. ON

APRIL 1, ECON MC DELIVERED REFTEL POINTS TO ANTONIO BADINI

AND LUIGI MACCOTTA, THE MFA\'S DIRECTOR GENERAL FOR MIDDLE

EASTERN AFFAIRS AND THE MFA\'S OFFICE DIRECTOR FOR IRAQ,

RESPECTIVELY, AND URGED THEM TO HAVE SENIOR GOI OFFICIALS

SPEAK PUBLICLY AGAINST THE IRAQI REGIME\'S ATROCITIES. BADINI

WELCOMED OUR REQUEST AND SAID HE AND BISOGNERO WOULD ASK FM

FRATTINI TO MENTION IRAQI WAR CRIMES IN THE SPEECH HE IS

SCHEDULED TO GIVE APRIL 2 TO THE PARLIAMENT\'S JOINT FOREIGN

AFFAIRS/DEFENSE COMMITTEES ABOUT HUMANITARIAN AID TO IRAQ.

BISOGNERO CALLED EMBASSY LATER ON APRIL 1 TO CONFIRM THAT

THESE SENTENCES HAD BEEN ADDED TO FRATTINI\'S APRIL 2 SPEECH.

2. (SBU) BADINI SAID HE WOULD ENCOURAGE SENIOR GOI TO SPEAK

PUBLICLY ABOUT IRAQI WAR CRIMES AT OTHER OPPORTUNITIES IN

ORDER TO EDUCATE ITALIAN PUBLIC OPINION AND SHOW THAT THE

STABILIZATION PHASE IN POST-CONFLICT IRAQ, A PRIORITY OF THE

ITALIAN TASK FORCE ON IRAQ\'S RECONSTRUCTION, WILL BE CRUCIAL

IN PREVENTING SIMILAR CRIMES.
