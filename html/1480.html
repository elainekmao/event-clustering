SUBJECT: BRITISH INSTRUCTIONS WITH RESPECT TO THE DEATH

OF PRESIDENT KABILA

CLASSIFIED BY CHARGE D\'AFFAIRES EARL M. IRVING FOR

REASONS 1.5 B AND D.

1. (C) BRITISH DEPUTY HIGH COMMISSIONER IAN HAY-

CAMPBELL (PROTECT) SHARED THE FOLLOWING INSTRUCTION,

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 HARARE 00353 01 OF 02 181550Z

CLASSIFIED "RESTRICTED," THAT HE HAD RECEIVED FROM

LONDON DATED JANUARY 18 WITH RESPECT TO THE DEATH OF

PRESIDENT KABILA. THE BRITISH ARE PLAYING CATCH-UP

HERE AND HAVE BEEN UNABLE TO SECURE AN APPOINTMENT

WITH ANYONE AT THE MINISTRY OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS TO

DELIVER THE DEMARCHE. WE TRANSMIT IT FOR DEPARTMENT\'S

INFORMATION. WITH THE POSSIBLE EXCEPTION OF THE

INSTRUCTION TO KAMPALA AND KIGALI TO SEND A CLEAR

MESSAGE TO MUGABE THAT THEY WILL NOT SEEK TO IMPOSE A

NEW LEADER IN KINSHASA, THE POSITIONS LONDON TAKES

APPEAR TO ADHERE CLOSELY TO OUR OWN.

2. (C) BEGIN TEXT:

SUMMARY

THE PARALYSIS IN KINSHASA PROVIDES THE EXTERNAL PLAYERS

WITH AN OPPORTUNITY TO TAKE LUSAKA FORWARD.

INSTRUCTIONS TO ENCOURAGE THEM TO SEIZE THE INITIATIVE.

DETAIL

WE ARE TRYING TO ASSESS THE IMPLICATIONS OF EVENTS IN

KINSHASA ON THE INTERNAL POLITICAL SITUATION, LUSAKA AND

MONUC DEPLOYMENT. BUT WITH NO FORMAL STATEMENT FROM

GDRC ABOUT KABILA\'S DEATH AND NO POLICY STATEMENT FROM

THE NEW LEADERSHIP, IT IS TOO EARLY TO MAKE SENSIBLE

JUDGEMENTS. WE WILL PROVIDE A CONSIDERED ASSESSMENT

ONCE THE DUST HAS SETTLED.

WHILE A VACUUM REMAINS IN KINSHASA, THERE IS A DANGER

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 HARARE 00353 01 OF 02 181550Z

THAT LUSAKA PARTIES MIGHT TRY TO TAKE ADVANTAGE. WE

MUST URGE THEM NOT TO DO SO. RATHER, THEY SHOULD SEIZE

THE OPPORTUNITY TO DISCUSS WAYS OF MAKING PROGRESS ON

THE IMPLEMENTATION OF LUSAKA AND THE KAMPALA AND HARARE

DISENGAGEMENT PLANS. THERE ARE ALREADY INDICATIONS OF

MOVEMENT IN THIS DIRECTION. MUGABE AND NUJOMA ARE

REPORTED TO BE PLANNING AN EARLY SUMMIT; THE

AFRICA/FRANCE SUMMIT IN YAOUNDE WILL DISCUSS THE CRISIS

(THOUGH WITH MOST OF THE KEY PLAYERS LIKELY TO BE

ABSENT, IT IS DIFFICULT TO SEE WHAT THEY CAN ACHIEVE);

RWANDA AND UGANDA HAVE ISSUED STATEMENTS URGING THE DRC

TO IMPLEMENT LUSAKA.

WE MUST CONTINUE TO ENCOURAGE THE NON-CONGOLESE

BELLIGERENTS TO DO ALL THEY CAN TO MAKE PROGRESS.

ACTION ADDRESSEES SHOULD SEEK AN EARLY OPPORTUNITY TO

MAKE THE FOLLOWING POINTS AT A SUITABLY SENIOR LEVEL:

- WE ARE LOOKING FOR NEW LEADERSHIP IN KINSHASA TO

RESTORE STABILITY,

- WILL URGE THEM TO BRING ABOUT THE CONDITIONS FOR MONUC

TO DEPLOY,

- CONCERNED THAT NONE OF THE PARTIES SEEK TO TAKE PROFIT

MILITARILY FROM THE UNCERTAINTY IN KINSHASA,

- HOPE YOU WILL DO ALL YOU CAN TO DEFUSE MILITARY

TENSIONS AND GET IMPLEMENTATION OF LUSAKA UNDERWAY,

- THERE IS A REAL OPPORTUNITY TO LAUNCH NATIONAL DEBATE

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 04 HARARE 00353 01 OF 02 181550Z

AS ENVISAGED IN LUSAKA.

FOR KAMPALA AND KIGALI ONLY:

- HOPE YOU WILL SEND A CLEAR MESSAGE TO MUGABE AND DOS

SANTOS THAT YOU WILL NOT SEEK TO IMPOSE A NEW LEADER IN

KINSHASA,

- ENCOURAGE YOU TO ENGAGE CONSTRUCTIVELY IN TAKING

FORWARD IMPLEMENTATION OF DISENGAGEMENT PLAN.

FOR HARARE, WINDHOEK AND LUANDA ONLY:

- AN EARLY MEETING OF ALLIES VITAL. HOPE YOU WILL ALSO

HAVE EARLIER CONTACTS WITH UGANDA/RWANDA.

COOK

END TEXT.
