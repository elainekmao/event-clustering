1. (U) THIS IS AN ACTION REQUEST. PLEASE SEE PARAGRAPH 5.

2. (C) ITALIAN PRIME MINISTER SILVIO BERLUSCONI WILL

RECOMMEND TO PARLIAMENT THAT ITALY SEND TROOPS TO AFGHANISTAN

IN SUPPORT OF OPERATION ENDURING FREEDOM, ACCORDING TO

BERLUSCONI\'S TOP ADVISOR, GIOVANNI CASTELLANETA. IN A

MEETING WITH AMBASSADOR SEMBLER AND A/DCM ON AUGUST 1 TO

FOLLOW-UP ON A JULY 31 DISCUSSION (REFTEL A), CASTELLANETA

SAID THE GOI WOULD LIKE US INPUT ON TWO OPTIONS THAT ARE

BEING DISCUSSED BY THE PM\'S OFFICE, THE CHIEF OF DEFENSE AND

THE ITALIAN LIAISON CELL IN TAMPA. UNDER OPTION 1, ITALY

WOULD SEND 1,000 ADDITIONAL TROOPS TO KFOR TO FREE UP US

TROOPS FOR OEF. THIS COULD BE DONE IMMEDIATELY, WITHOUT

PARLIAMENTARY REVIEW. UNDER OPTION 2, ITALY WOULD SEND

TROOPS (EITHER COMBAT OR SPECIAL OPERATIONS FORCES) TO

AFGHANISTAN. THIS WOULD INVOLVE PARLIAMENTARY CONSULTATIONS,

IF NOT A VOTE, WHICH WOULD MEAN WAITING FOR PARLIAMENT\'S

RETURN IN MID-SEPTEMBER. IF THE USG PREFERS OPTION 2, THE

GOI NEEDS MORE INFORMATION ABOUT THE TYPE OF MISSION

ANTICIPATED IN ORDER TO DETERMINE WHICH TROOPS SHOULD BE

DEPLOYED, CASTELLANETA SAID.

3. (C) NOTING THAT THE US WOULD LIKELY BE RELUCTANT TO SET

OFF SUSPICIONS THAT WE ARE WITHDRAWING SUPPORT FROM BALKANS

OPERATIONS, THE AMBASSADOR SAID OPTION 2 MAY BE PREFERRED.

HE ASKED WHETHER PARLIAMENTARY REVIEW IS LEGALLY NECESSARY,

OR MORE OF A POLITICAL REQUIREMENT. CASTELLANETA CONFIRMED

THAT THERE IS A REAL POLITICAL, NOT LEGAL, NEED TO CONSULT

WITH PARLIAMENT, BUT PREDICTED THAT THE PROCESS WOULD LIKELY

LEAD TO STRONGER PUBLIC AND POLITICAL SUPPORT FOR DEPLOYING

ITALIAN TROOPS. BERLUSCONI WOULD RAISE THE REQUEST WITH

PARLIAMENT IN MID-SEPTEMBER, AND TROOPS COULD BE DEPLOYED IN

THE FALL, BEFORE THE AFGHAN WINTER BEGINS, HE SAID.

4. (C) CASTELLANETA SAID AMBASSADOR SALLEO, WHO RECENTLY MET

WITH PENTAGON OFFICIALS, HAD REPORTED STRONG REGARD WITHIN

DOD FOR THE ALPINI BRIGADE. AGAIN, CASTELLANETA URGED THAT

WE PROVIDE MORE PRECISE INFORMATION ABOUT THE MISSION SO THAT

THE GOI CAN DETERMINE WHICH TROOPS WOULD BE MOST APPROPRIATE

TO SEND. THE AMBASSADOR ASSURED HIM THAT THE EMBASSY WILL

SHARE THE DETAILS WE OBTAIN FROM WASHINGTON, BUT EMPHASIZED

THAT TAMPA IS THE CENTRAL POINT FOR COORDINATING DISCUSSIONS

ABOUT OPERATIONAL DETAILS, SUCH AS WHICH TYPE OF FORCES ARE

NEEDED AND WHEN.

5. (C) ACTION REQUEST. PLEASE INFORM EMBASSY WHICH OPTION IS

PREFERRED: BACKFILLING TROOPS IN THE BALKANS (OPTION 1) OR

DEPLOYING TROOPS TO AFGHANISTAN (OPTION 2). A RESPONSE

BEFORE MONDAY, AUGUST 5 WOULD ALLOW U/S BOLTON TO DELIVER THE

USG RESPONSE TO SENIOR GOI OFFICIALS, INCLUDING CASTELLANETA,

WHILE HE IS IN ROME. IF OPTION 2 IS PREFERRED, AS WE EXPECT,

PLEASE PROVIDE AVAILABLE DETAILS REGARDING THE EXPECTED

MISSION IN ORDER TO HELP THE ITALIANS IDENTIFY THE

APPROPRIATE TROOPS TO DEPLOY. WE WOULD ALSO APPRECIATE

FURTHER INFORMATION REGARDING WHICH TROOPS ARE ROTATING OUT

AND WHEN FROM OTHER CONTRIBUTING COUNTRIES.
