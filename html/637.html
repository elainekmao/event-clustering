SUBJECT: THE LTTE: WHAT WE ARE SAYING AND WHO WE

ARE SAYING IT TO

REFS: A) 93 STATE 107728 B) 93 COLOMBO 1850

1. (U) THIS IS AN ACTION REQUEST--PLEASE SEE PARA 9.

2. (C) SUMMARY. POST IS WORKING THROUGH

INTERMEDIARIES TO SEND THE MESSAGE THAT THE

LIBERATION TIGERS OF TAMIL EELAM (LTTE) SHOULD

BECOME CONSTRUCTIVELY ENGAGED IN FINDING A PEACEFUL

SOLUTION TO SRI LANKA\'S ETHNIC CONFLICT. WE ARE

CALLING ON THE LTTE AS WELL AS THE GOVERNMENT TO

FOCUS INITIALLY ON MODEST CONFIDENCE-BUILDING

MEASURES, RATHER THAN BROAD POLITICAL ISSUES. USG

POLICY CONTINUES TO FAVOR A PEACEFUL POLITICAL

SETTLEMENT WHICH PRESERVES SRI LANKA\'S TERRITORIAL

INTEGRITY AND PROVIDES APPROPRIATE GUARANTEES FOR

MINORITY RIGHTS. END SUMMARY.

3. (C) WE CONTINUE TO WORK THROUGH INTERMEDIARIES

TO GET THE TALKING POINTS CONTAINED REF A (REPEATED

PARA 8) ACROSS TO THE LIBERATION TIGERS OF TAMIL

EELAM (LTTE). WE HAVE GIVEN THE MESSAGE TO A NUMBER

OF PROMINENT CITIZENS OF THE LTTE-CONTROLLED JAFFNA

PENINSULA WHO HAVE PASSED THROUGH COLOMBO IN RECENT

MONTHS. THEY INCLUDE ACADEMICIANS FROM THE

UNIVERSITY OF JAFFNA, PROMINENT MEMBERS OF THE

CHRISTIAN CLERGY AND A LEADING DOCTOR FROM JAFFNA

HOSPITAL. MOST RECENTLY, THE AMBASSADOR MET THE

GOVERNMENT AGENT FROM JAFFNA, A TAMIL PUBLIC SERVANT

WHO MAINTAINS A CLOSE WORKING RELATIONSHIP WITH THE

TIGERS. WE ALSO DELIVERED OUR POINTS TO A

JAFFNA-BOUND DELEGATION FROM THE UK-BASED "QUAKER

PEACE AND SERVICE" NGO.

4. (C) THE EFFECTIVENESS OF INTERMEDIARIES IS

LIMITED BY THE RECLUSIVE NATURE OF THE LTTE

LEADERSHIP AND THE UNDERSTANDABLE RELUCTANCE OF

JAFFNA RESIDENTS TO MAKE WAVES WITH THE TIGERS.

NEVERTHELESS, WE CONTINUE TO BELIEVE THAT IN THE

ABSENCE OF DIRECT USG CONTACT WITH THE TIGERS, THIS

IS THE BEST ALTERNATIVE FOR GETTING OUR MESSAGE

ACROSS. WE PLAN TO INTENSIFY THESE EFFORTS BY

SEEKING OUT SPECIFIC INDIVIDUALS WHO MAY HAVE ACCESS

TO (AND PERHAPS INFLUENCE WITH) THE LTTE. THESE

INCLUDE THE CATHOLIC BISHOP OF JAFFNA AND THE VICE

CHANCELLOR OF JAFFNA UNIVERSITY.

5. (C) IN ADDITION TO THE TALKING POINTS CONTAINED

REF A, WE ARE EMPHASIZING THE NEED TO BUILD

CREDIBILITY PRIOR TO ANY SUBSTANTIVE DISCUSSION OF

LARGER POLITICAL ISSUES. IN OUR VIEW, THERE IS

CURRENTLY SUCH A LARGE RESERVOIR OF SUSPICION AND

MISTRUST BETWEEN THE GOVERNMENT AND THE LTTE THAT

HASTY NEGOTIATIONS BETWEEN THE TWO PARTIES COULD

ACTUALLY SET THE PEACE PROCESS BACK. PUBLIC

POSTURING ASIDE, NEITHER THE GOVERNMENT NOR THE LTTE

IS INTERESTED IN TALKS RIGHT NOW, UNLESS IT IS TO

DISCUSS THE TERMS OF THE OTHER SIDE\'S CAPITULATION.

6. (C) WE BELIEVE THAT ALL PARTIES--GOVERNMENT,

LTTE, AND NON-LTTE TAMILS--SHOULD FOCUS INITIALLY ON

PRACTICAL PROBLEM-SOLVING, LEAVING ASIDE THE LARGER

POLITICAL ISSUES. (A NOTE OF CAUTION: WHILE WE

RECOGNIZE THAT THE LTTE IS THE ONLY TAMIL PLAYER

THAT MATTERS MILITARILY, WE DO NOT ACCEPT THE LTTE

AS THE SOLE SPOKESMAN OF THE TAMIL PEOPLE; AN

EVENTUAL POLITICAL SOLUTION WILL HAVE TO TAKE INTO

ACCOUNT THE NON-TIGER TAMILS AS WELL AS THE LTTE.)

THESE CONFIDENCE-BUILDING MEASURES SHOULD BE

DESIGNED TO DEMONSTRATE A SERIOUS COMMITMENT TO

FINDING A POLITICAL SOLUTION AND UPHOLDING ANY

EVENTUAL AGREEMENT. WE ARE, FOR EXAMPLE,

ENCOURAGING THE GSL TO PUT INTO PRACTICE ITS

COMMITMENT TO DEVOLVE POWER AT THE PROVINCIAL AND

LOCAL LEVELS.

7. (LOU WITH THE ABOVE IN MIND, WE PLAN TO ADD THE

FOLOWING POINTS TO OUR "DIALOGUE" WITH THE LTTE:

0 WE ENCOURAGE THE LTTE TO DEMONSTRATE THAT IT IS

SERIOUS ABOUT FINDING A POLITICAL SOLUTION TO THE

WAR. THIS COULD INCLUDE, FOR EXAMPLE, GOODWILL

GESTURES SUCH AS PRISONER RELEASES, ALLOWING FREE

MOVEMENT OF CIVILIANS BETWEEN GOVERNMENT AND

LTTE-CONTROLLED AREAS, OR A TEMPORARY, UNILATERAL

CEASEFIRE.

0 WE ENCOURAGE THE LTTE AND THE GOVERNMENT TO

SEARCH FOR AREAS OF POTENTIAL AGREEMENT WHICH COULD

BE NEGOTIATED PRIOR TO ANY DISCUSSION OF LARGER

POLITICAL ISSUES. THESE COULD BE USED TO BUILD THE

CONFIDENCE NECESSARY FOR MORE COMPREHENSIVE FUTURE

AGREEMENTS.

8. (LOLL) THE NEW TALKING POINTS WILL BE

INCORPORATED INTO THE POINTS WE HAVE BEEN USING

SINCE LAST YEAR:

0 THE U.S. SUPPORTS A POLITICAL SOLUTION TO SRI

LANKA\'S ETHNIC PROBLEMS WHICH PRESERVES SRI LANKA AS

ONE COUNTRY AND PROVIDES MINORITIES WITH PROTECTION

AND DIGNITY.

0 SRI LANKANS OF ALL ETHNIC GROUPS AND POLITICAL

PERSUASIONS NEED TO WORK OUT AMONG THEMSELVES THE

CONSTITUTIONAL ARRANGEMENTS WHICH WILL RESOLVE THIS

TRAGIC CONFLICT WHILE PRESERVING THE INTEGRITY OF

SRI LANKA AND THE RIGHT OF ITS PEOPLE TO LIVE IN

POLITICAL AND ECONOMIC FREEDOM.

0 BOTH SIDES MUST BE WILLING TO DISCUSS ALL

RELEVANT ISSUES AND SEEK COMPROMISE. NOTHING OF

IMPORTANCE SHOULD BE LEFT OFF THE TABLE.

0 WE HAVE SPOKEN OUT VIGOROUSLY AGAINST GSL HUMAN

RIGHTS ABUSES, AND CONTINUE TO WORK WITH THE

GOVERNMENT TO IMPROVE ITS PERFORMANCE.

0 WE ALSO CONDEMN HUMAN RIGHTS ABUSES BY OTHERS,

NOTABLY THE LTTE. WE CONDEMN IN THE STRONGEST TERMS

THE LTTE\'S ATTACKS ON INNOCENT MUSLIM AND SINHALESE

CIVILIANS.

9. (C) POST RECOMMENDS CONTINUATION OF OUR CURRENT

POLICY OF RESTRICTING SUBSTANTIVE CONTACTS WITH THE

LTTE AND LTTE SYMPATHIZERS TO EMBASSY COLOMBO AND

THE DEPARTMENT. IF OTHER MISSIONS ARE APPROACHED BY

LTTE REPRESENTATIVES OR PERSONS CLAIMING TO SPEAK ON

BEHALF OF THE LTTE, WE ASK THAT THEY SEEK GUIDANCE

FROM THE DEPARTMENT. HOWEVER, SHOULD THE USDEL IN

GENEVA BE APPROACHED IN THE COURSE OF THE ONGOING

UNHRC MEETING BY TAMIL EXPATRIATE GROUPS SEEKING TO

PLEAD THEIR CAUSE, WE WOULD RECOMMEND THAT THE

DEPARTMENT AUTHORIZE THE USDEL TO MAKE USE OF THE

ABOVE TALKING POINTS.

SCHAFFER
