SUBJECT: READOUT FROM ASEAN STANDING COMMITTEE MEETING ON US-ASEAN DIALOGUE

1. THIS IS AN ACTION CABLE. SEE PARAS 3-4.

2. MFA ASEAN DEPARTMENT DEPUTY DIRECTOR NGUYEN HOANG AN

GAVE POLCOUNS AND POLOFF A READOUT NOV. 1 ON THE "STATUS

REPORT ON ASEAN-U.S. RELATIONS" WHICH WAS DISCUSSED AT THE

OCT 9-11 34TH ASEAN STANDING COMMITTEE MEETING IN HANOI. AN

ALSO PRESENTED FOLLOW-UP REQUESTS FROM THAT MEETING. A COPY

OF THE REPORT FOLLOWS.

BEGIN TEXT:

THE FIRST MEETING OF THE 34TH ASEAN STANDING COMMITTEE (ASC

1/34) HANOI, 9-11 OCTOBER 2000

STATUS REPORT OF THE ASEAN-UNITED STATES OF AMERICA

RELATIONS

I. SUMMARY RECORD OF THE PMC-PLUS-ONE SESSION WITH THE

UNITED STATES:

THE PMC-PLUS-ONE SESSION WITH THE US WAS HELD ON 28 JULY

2000 IN BANGKOK. THE SUMMARY RECORD OF THE MEETING HAS BEEN

FINALIZED.

II. DEVELOPMENT COOPERATION:

1) ESTABLISHMENT OF THE CENTRE FOR THE ASSESSMENT AND

PROMOTION OF ENVIRONMENTALLY SOUND TECHNOLOGIES IN ASEAN:

THE ASEAN SECRETARIAT AND THE US-ASIA ENVIRONMENTAL

PARTNERSHIP (AEP) ARE WORKING TOWARD THE FINALIZATION OF THE

TOR FOR THE CONSULTANTS TO UNDERTAKE THE FEASIBILITY STUDY

ON THE ESTABLISHMENT OF THE CENTRE IN ASEAN. THIS CENTRE

WILL ACT AS A CLEARINGHOUSE FOR CLEAN TECHNOLOGIES AND

ESPECIALLY FOR SMES. THE DRAFT TOR FOR THE CONSULTANTS TO

UNDERTAKE THE FEASIBILITY STUDY ON THE ESTABLISHMENT OF THE

CENTRE IN ASEAN WAS CIRCULATED TO ASEAN SUB-COMMITTEE ON

ENVIRONMENT (ASOEN) FOCAL POINTS FOR THEIR COMMENTS. THE

ASEAN SECRETARIAT WAS REQUESTED TO CONTINUE WORKING TOGETHER

WITH THE US-ASIA ENVIRONMENTAL PARTNERSHIP (US-AEP) IN

FINALIZING THE TOR.

2) ASEAN-US COOPERATION IN ENVIRONMENT: AT THE RECENT PMC

MEETING, THE US ANNOUNCED THE INTENTION TO PROVIDE ANOTHER

US$3.5 MILLION OF THE EAST ASIA AND PACIFIC ENVIRONMENTAL

INITIATIVE FUND IN A BROAD RANGE OF NEW PROJECTS, INVOLVING

FOREST MANAGEMENT, FOREST FIRE AND HAZE CONTROL,

PRESERVATION OF CORAL REEFS, PROTECTION OF BIODIVERSITY.

ASEAN WOULD LIKE TO SEEK CLARIFICATION FROM THE US ON USING

THIS CONTRIBUTION AND WOULD LIKE TO REQUEST THE ASSISTANCE

TO ASEAN BE CHANNELD THROUGH ASEAN MECHANISMS.

3) ASEAN-US COOPERATION ON SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY: AT THE

15TH ASEAN-US DIALOGUE MEETING HELD ON 24-25 MAY IN KUALA

LUMPUR, ASEAN AND THE US RECOGNIZED THAT THE ADVANCES IN

SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY AND IN HUMAN RESOURCES DEVELOPMENT

HAD BEEN CRUCIAL TO SOCIO-ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT AND DISCUSSED

EXISTING AND PLANNED AREAS OF COOPERATION IN BIOTECHNOLOGY,

FOOD SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY, METEOROLOGY AND PHYSICS,

MICROELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY, MATERIAL

SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY, NON-CONVENTIONAL ENERGY RESEARCH,

MARINE SCIENCES AND SPACE TECHNOLOGY AND APPLICATIONS.

ASEAN SUGGESTED US SUPPORT FOR ASEAN\'S INITIATIVES SUCH AS

THE ASEAN VIRTUAL UNIVERSITY OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY,

ASEAN SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY NETWORK, SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY

MANAGEMENT THROUGH TECHNICAL ASSISTANCE, AND CONTINUED US

COLLABORATION IN THE AREAS OF EDUCATION, R&D AND QUALITY

SYSTEMS, WHICH WERE CRITICAL ELEMENTS FOR THE PROMOTION OF

SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY AND HUMAN RESOURCES DEVELOPMENT.

ASEAN WOULD LIKE TO HAVE MORE INFORMATION ON THE

IMPLEMENTATION.

4) HRD SUPPORT FROM THE US-ASEAN BUSINESS COUNCIL: THE US-

ASEAN BUSINESS COUNCIL HAS CREATED THE ASEAN-US CENTRE FOR

TECHNOLOGY COOPERATION (CTC) TO PROVIDE BUSINESS-LED

TRAINING AND TECHNOLOGY EXCHANGE PROGRAMMES TO SUPPORT ASEAN

COUNTRIES IN HRD, WITH A PARTICULAR FOCUS ON NEW ASEAN

COUNTRIES. THE ASEAN PROGRAMME WILL BE MANAGED BY THE

REGIONAL CENTRE IN MANILA. PROGRAMMING WILL BE JOINTLY

UNDERTAKEN BY ASEAN AND THE US-ASEAN BUSINESS COUNCIL AND

IMPLEMENTED BY USING PRIVATE SECTOR FUNDING AS A PRIMARY

SOURCE. AEM AND US BUSINESS COUNCIL HAVE MET RECENTLY

DURING THE AEM MEETING.

5) ASSISTANCE TO THE ASEAN CENTRE FOR COMBATING

TRANSNATIONAL CRIME (ACTC): THE US HAS OFFERED TO PROVIDE

TEMPORARY DEVELOPMENT ASSISTANCE AND COMPUTER EQUIPMENT FOR

THE ACTC ONCE THE AGREEMENT TO ESTABLISH THE CENTRE HAS BEEN

SIGNED IN ORDER TO SUPPORT THE DEVELOPMENT OF A REGIONAL

DATABASE ON TRANSNATIONAL CRIME. DECISION ON THE

ESTABLISHMENT OF THE ACTC IS UNDER DISCUSSION AMONG ASEAN

COUNTRIES. ON TRANSNATIONAL ISSUES, THE US HAS ALSO

EXPRESSED INTEREST IN CONTINUING TO COLLABORATE WITH ASEAN

IN COMBATING TRANSNATIONAL CRIME, IN THE COOPERATION ON

NARCOTICS THROUGH REGIONAL ACTIVITIES, AND IN THE

COOPERATIVE PROGRAMMES TO ADDRESS THE HIV-AIDS PROBLEM.

ASEAN WOULD LIKE TO HAVE MORE INFORMATION ON THE

IMPLEMENTATION.

6) THE US HAS ATTENDED THE US-ACCSQ (ASEAN CONSULTATIVE

COMMITTEE ON STANDARDS AND QUALITY) CONSULTATION ON

STANDARDS AND CONFORMANCE IN BALI IN AUGUST 2000 TO EXPLORE

POSSIBLE AREAS FOR COOPERATION.

7) THE US HAS CONVEYED A DISCUSSION PAPER TO ASEAN PROPOSING

TO INVIGORATE THE US-ASEAN DIALOGUE IN TRADE AND INVESTMENT.

THE 32ND AEM IN CHIANG MAI HAS AGREED TO HOLD A DIALOGUE ON

TRADE AND INVESTMENT WITH THE USTR BACK-TO-BACK WITH SEOM IN

MAY 2001 IN SIEM REAP, CAMBODIA.

III. ASEAN-USDA EXCHANGE OF LETTERS ON COOPERATION IN FOOD,

AGRICULTURE AND FORESTRY AND IN RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND

POVERTY ALLEVIATION:

THE ASEAN SECRETARIAT HAS TRANSMITTED THE EXCHANGE LETTER

BETWEEN THE SECVRETARY-GENERAL OF ASEAN AND THE US SECRETARY

OF AGRICULTURE TO THE US AND IS AWAITING RESPONSE FROM THE

US DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE, TO FORMALIZE USDA\'S COMMITMENT

TO PROVIDE TECHNICAL ASSISTANCE TO ASEAN FOR THE DEVELOPMENT

OF INDUSTRIES RELATED TO THE REGION\'S FOOD, AGRICULTURE,

LIVESTOCK, FISHERIES AND POVERTY ALLEVIATION.

END TEXT.

3. AN MADE THE FOLLOWING ADDITIONAL COMMENTS/QUESTIONS ON

THE TEXT:

-- ENVIRONMENTAL COOPERATION: AN NOTED THAT THERE HAS

BEEN NO FURTHER INFORMATION ON THIS OFFER SINCE THE JULY PMC

AND ASKED FOR MORE DETAIL. SPECIFICALLY, AN SAID, ASEAN

WOULD LIKE TO KNOW IF THE PROPOSED ADDITIONAL FUNDS WOULD BE

USED FOR REGIONAL PROJECTS ONLY, OR ALSO BILATERAL PROJECTS.

ASEAN ALSO WANTS TO KNOW HOW FUNDS WOULD BE CHANNELED, AND

WOULD LIKE TO PROPOSE THE ESTABLISHMENT OF A U.S.-ASEAN

FUND, SUCH AS ASEAN HAS WITH OTHER DIALOGUE PARTNERS, TO

FACILITATE PROJECT IMPLEMENTATION. AN NOTED THAT THE

ESTABLISHMENT OF SUCH A FUND WOULD MAKE THE U.S. A "FULL

PARTNER" OF ASEAN.

-- S&T COOPERATION: AN EMPHASIZED THAT ASEAN WOULD

LIKE TO PROPOSE THE U.S. SUPPORT ASEAN EDUCATIONAL SYSTEMS.

-- TRANSNATIONAL CRIME: AN NOTED THAT ASEAN IS NOW

FINALIZING A MOU ON THE ESTABLISHMENT OF THE ACTC. WHEN THE

MOU IS COMPLETE, ASEAN WILL WANT TO MAKE USE OF THE OFFERED

US ASSISTANCE. AN WAS NOT ABLE TO COMMENT ON WHEN THE MOU

MIGHT BE COMPLETE.

-- AGRICULTURAL COOPERATION: AN NOTED THAT THERE HAS

BEEN NO RESPONSE TO ASEAN\'S LETTER AND ASKED FOR FEEDBACK

FROM USDA.
