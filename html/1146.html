SUBJECT: UNHCR REGIONAL REPRESENTATIVES ATTEMPTS TO

BROKER A DEAL FOR THE CUBANS\' RELEASE

REF: HARARE 3164

CLASSIFIED BY AMBASSADOR TOM MCDONALD FOR REASONS 1.5

B/D.

1. (C) ON JUNE 14 LUSAKA-BASED UNITED NATIONS HIGH

COMMISSIONER FOR REFUGEES (UNHCR) REGIONAL

REPRESENTATIVE OLUSEYI BAJULAIYE TOLD AMBASSADOR

MCDONALD THAT HE HAD MET WITH A NUMBER OF OFFICIALS IN

HARARE WITH RESPECT TO THE CASES OF CUBAN DOCTORS LEONEL

CORDOVA RODRIGUEZ AND NORIS DEL CARMEN PENA MARTINEZ.

HIS FIRST CALL WAS ON THE REFUGEE COMMISSIONER, MR. I.

T. MUKHARO, TO WHOM HE PROTESTED THE CONDITIONS UNDER

WHICH THE CUBANS WERE BEING HELD. DOCTORS CORDOVA AND

PENA HAVE NO MATTRESS AND RECEIVE ONLY ONE MEAL A DAY,

ACCORDING TO MICHIEL SALOMONS, THE UNHCR LAWYER WHO HAD

VISITED THEM EARLIER ON JUNE 14. THE CUBANS ARE SLEEPING

ON BLANKETS ON A HARD, COLD, CEMENT FLOOR IN THE

SOUTHERN HEMISPHERE WINTER. SALOMONS WAS ALLOWED ONLY

TO LEAVE MONEY FOR FOOD AND TOILETRIES, BUT NO BLANKETS

OR A MATTRESS. THE GOROMONZI POLICE EXPLAINED TO

SALOMONS THAT PERSONS IN REMAND STATUS WERE PROHIBITED

FROM SLEEPING ON A MATTRESS, AS IT COULD BE USED TO

CONCEAL WEAPONS.

2. (C) BAJULAIYE REPORTED THAT HE HAD SCHEDULED A

MEETING AT THE MINISTRY OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS WITH LEGAL

AND CONSULAR AFFAIRS HEAD, DR. ELITA SAKUPWANYA, WHO WAS

CALLED AWAY TO BRIEF FOREIGN MINISTER MUDENGE ALMOST AS

SOON AS THE MEETING BEGAN. THE UNHCR REPRESENTATIVE

ALSO SPOKE WITH THE CUBAN AMBASSADOR TO ZIMBABWE,

RODOLFO SARRACINO, WHO ASSURED THE FORMER THAT HIS

GOVERNMENT HAD NO OBJECTION TO THE DOCTORS LEAVING FOR A

THIRD COUNTRY, POSSIBLY CANADA, AND THAT HE HAD SENT A

DIPLOMATIC NOTE TO THAT EFFECT TO THE ZIMBABWEAN

MINISTRY OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS. BAJULAIYE SAID THAT THE

ZIMBABWEANS FELT THEMSELVES BOXED, UNDER TREMENDOUS

PRESSURE FROM THE CUBANS, THE UNHCR AND THE USG. ASKED

WHAT WAS THE STATUS OF THE CUBAN DOCTORS UNDER

ZIMBABWEAN LAW, BAJULAIYE EXPLAINED THAT THE ZIMBABWEAN

REFUGEE COMMITTEE HAD DECIDED ON JUNE 8 THAT IT "NEITHER

RECOGNIZED NOR REJECTED" REFUGEE STATUS FOR THE CUBANS.

3. (C) THE UNHCR REPRESENTATIVE WANTED TO PRESENT THE

ZIMBABWEANS WITH A DEAL. HE WANTED TO PROPOSE THAT THE

GOZ HAND OVER CUSTODY OF THE CUBANS IMMEDIATELY TO UNHCR

FOR RESETTLEMENT. THE RESETTLEMENT COUNTRY WOULD BE

CANADA, ALTHOUGH CANADIAN HIGH COMMISSIONER JAMES WALL

HAD BEEN VERY CAUTIOUS IN A CONVERSATION WITH BAJULAIYE

THE AFTERNOON OF JUNE 14. THE CANADIAN DIPLOMAT

HIS DECISION TO THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS.
