SUBJECT: Nepal Seeks Bilateral Air Services Agreement

Nepal seeks a bilateral Air Services Agreement with the

U.S., the MFA informed Post in a diplomatic note dated

April 15. The text of the dipnote follows.

Begin Text:

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of His Majesty\'s

Government of Nepal presents its compliments to the

Embassy of the United States of America in Kathmandu and

has the honor to inform the latter that His Majesty\'s

Government of Nepal has decided to conclude bilateral Air

Services Agreement at mutually agreed date and venue.

The Ministry would appreciate it if the esteemed Embassy

could kindly convey the above matter to the authorities

concerned of the Government of the United States of

America.

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of His Majesty\'s

Government of Nepal avails itself of this opportunity to

renew to the Embassy of the United States of America in

Kathmandu the assurances of its highest consideration.

End Text.

MALINOWSKI
