1. (C) SUMMARY: ISADORE ZINDOGA, WHO WAS

ADMINISTRATIVELY PROMOTED TO SECRETARY GENERAL OF THE

ZIMBABWE CONGRESS OF TRADE UNIONS (ZCTU) IN APRIL AFTER

MORGAN TSVANGIRAI TOOK A LEAVE OF ABSENCE FROM THE

POSITION TO LEAD THE MOVEMENT FOR DEMOCRATIC CHANGE

(MDC), IS WIDELY ACCUSED BY MANY UNIONISTS OF PURPOSELY

FAILING TO LEAD THE ZCTU AND RENDERING THE ORGANIZATION

INEFFECTIVE. ZINDOGA\'S LOYALTY TO THE RULING PARTY,

ZANU-PF, IS COMMON KNOWLEDGE, AND HE IS RUMORED TO BE A

FORMER (SOME SAY CURRENT) AGENT OF THE CENTRAL

INTELLIGENCE ORGANIZATION (CIO). WHETHER OR NOT ZINDOGA

IS GUILTY OF SUBVERSION, THE FACT IS THAT THE ZCTU HAS

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 HARARE 03680 01 OF 05 070943Z

FALLEN DEAFLY SILENT UNDER HIS LEADERSHIP. REGULAR ZCTU

ACTIVITIES, INCLUDING LABOR WORKSHOPS AND TRADITIONAL

RECRUITING ACTIVITIES, ARE AT A STANDSTILL. IN THE RUN-

UP TO THE JUNE 24-25 PARLIAMENTARY ELECTIONS, THE ZCTU

FAILED TO JOIN CIVIL SOCIETY IN CONDEMNING PRE-ELECTION

VIOLENCE AND ENCOURAGING PEOPLE TO VOTE. ISAAC MATONGO,

PRESIDENT OF THE ZCTU AND CHAIRMAN OF THE MDC, IS

CONFIDENT THAT UNIONISTS WILL VOTE ZINDOGA OUT OF OFFICE

AT THE NEXT ANNUAL CONGRESS, WHICH MATONGO HOPES WILL

TAKE PLACE IN SEPTEMBER 2000. UNTIL THAT HAPPENS, WE DO

NOT EXPECT THE ZCTU TO RE-EMERGE AS A CHAMPION OF

WORKERS\' RIGHTS AND A VOCAL STAKEHOLDER IN DISCUSSIONS

WITH BUSINESS AND GOVERNMENT ON ECONOMIC REFORM. END

SUMMARY.

6. (C) ACCORDING TO MATONGO AND MAKORE, ZINDOGA HAS

TELL US THAT ZINDOGA NO LONGER MEETS REGULARLY WITH

REPRESENTATIVES FROM THE ZCTU\'S 35 MEMBER UNIONS, AND

THE ZCTU\'S SIX OPERATIONAL DEPARTMENTS.
