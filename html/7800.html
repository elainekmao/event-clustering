SUBJECT: UAEG SIGNS DOUBLE TAXATION AGREEMENTS WITH 65

COUNTRIES; SEEKS TREATY WITH USG

REF: 02 STATE 16509

1. (U) ADDRESSING A SEMINAR ON LEGAL AND TAX ISSUES

RELATING TO FOREIGN INVESTMENT ON 5/4, UAE MINISTRY OF

FINANCE UNDERSECRETARY FOR BUDGET AND REVENUE KHALID AL-

BUSTANI CONFIRMED THAT THE UAEG HAS SIGNED BILATERAL

AGREEMENTS WITH 65 COUNTRIES ON AVOIDANCE OF DOUBLE TAXATION

AND INCOME TAX. THESE COUNTRIES INCLUDE A NUMBER OF

EUROPEAN UNION NATIONS, CANADA, INDIA, AND OTHER MAJOR

TRADING PARTNERS. HE NOTED THAT THE UAEG HAD NOT/NOT

CONCLUDED A DOUBLE TAXATION TREATY WITH THE UNITED STATES,

BUT SUCH AN AGREEMENT REMAINS A PRIORITY FOR THE UAEG.

2. (U) AL-BUSTANI HIGHLIGHTED THE UAE\'S DOUBLE TAXATION

AGREEMENT WITH GERMANY, IN PARTICULAR, WHICH WAS SIGNED IN

1996 AND APPLIED RETROACTIVELY FROM 1992. HE SAID THAT THE

TAX SITUATION OF UAE INVESTORS IN GERMANY HAS IMPROVED

CONSIDERABLY -- PREFERENTIAL TREATMENT ON DIVIDENDS IS

LIMITED TO 5 PERCENT AND REQUIRED TAX WITHHOLDINGS HAVE BEEN

REDUCED TO ZERO. A REPRESENTATIVE FROM THE GERMAN EMBASSY

NOTED THAT HIS COUNTRY HAS BENEFITED FROM THE INCREASE IN

UAE INVESTMENT IN GERMANY SINCE THE CONCLUSION OF THE DOUBLE

TAXATION AGREEMENT.

3. (SBU) COMMENT: UAEG OFFICIALS HAVE ASKED TIME AND AGAIN

FOR A MEETING TO DISCUSS THE MODALITIES OF CONCLUDING A

DOUBLE TAXATION AGREEMENT WITH THE USG. SUCH A TREATY IS A

HIGH-PRIORITY FOR THE UAEG, AND WAS RAISED WITH ASSISTANT

SECRETARY FOR ECONOMIC AFFAIRS TONY WAYNE AT THE FIRST

SIPDIS

U.S.-UAE STRATEGIC DIALOGUE IN DECEMBER (SEE REF). WE WOULD

APPRECIATE GUIDANCE FROM TREASURY\'S OFFICE OF INTERNATIONAL

TAX COUNSEL AS TO WHETHER SUCH AN AGREEMENT WOULD BE

FEASIBLE, AND IF U.S. NEGOTIATORS WOULD WELCOME

CONSULTATIONS WITH UAE OFFICIALS IN WASHINGTON TO DISCUSS

THE MATTER FURTHER. END COMMENT.
