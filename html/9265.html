SUBJECT: DUTCH MFA ON SENIOR OSCE REPRESENTATIVE FOR MOLDOVA

REF: STATE 197225

Classified By: CLASSIFIED BY POLITICAL COUNSELOR MARY DALY FOR REASONS

1.5 (B AND D).

(C) POLOFF DELIVERED REFTEL POINTS TO WILLEMIJN KALLENBERG,

DUTCH MFA OSCE TASK FORCE POLICY ADVISOR FOR MOLDOVA, ON JULY

9. KALLENBERG SAID THE DUTCH APPRECIATED RECEIVING U.S.

VIEWS ON APPOINTING A SENIOR REPRESENTATIVE FOR MOLDOVA AND

SAID THAT THEY WERE ALSO LOOKING TO SELECT A HIGH-RANKING

INDIVIDUAL SUCH AS A FORMER MINISTER OR PRESIDENT FOR THE

JOB. DUTCH THINKING IS TO FIND THE RIGHT PERSON (IDEALLY A

RUSSIAN SPEAKER) AND HOLD THEM IN RESERVE TO DEPLOY AT THE

RIGHT MOMENT IN THE PROCESS. KALLENBERG SAID THE DUTCH WOULD

APPRECIATE ANY U.S. RECOMMENDATIONS OF PARTICULAR INDIVIDUALS

FOR THE ROLE.
