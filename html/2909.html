SUBJECT: ITALY SAYS IRAN MAY REALLY NEED SPEED BOATS

CLASSIFIED BY: CLASSIFIED BY ECOUNS KAREN MILLIKEN; REASON 1.5 B/D.

1. (C) ACTION REQUEST AND SUMMARY. ITALY WILL INVESTIGATE

REFTEL POTENTIAL SALE OF HIGH-SPEED BOATS TO IRAN BUT, AT

LEAST PRELIMINARILY, DOES NOT NECESSARILY AGREE THAT THE

SALES WOULD EITHER VIOLATE ITALIAN LAW OR WOULD COME WITHIN

THE PURVIEW OF WASSENAAR\'S MUNITIONS LIST. MFA\'S

NONPROLIFERATION COORDINATOR SAYS THAT ITALY WOULD NOT ALLOW

THE SALES IF IT FOUND THE BOATS TO BE WARSHIPS. ON THE OTHER

HAND, HE SAID, IRAN HAS A LOT OF COAST TO PATROL AND SOME

NEIGHBORS WHO NEED WATCHING. SEE ACTION REQUEST PARA 4. END

ACTION REQUEST AND SUMMARY.

2. (C) THE GOI WILL INVESTIGATE REFTEL\'S POTENTIAL BOAT AND

BOAT ENGINE SALES TO IRAN IN THE CONTEXT OF APPLICABLE

NONPROLIFERATION OBLIGATIONS AND INTERESTS, MFA\'S

NONPROLIFERATION COORDINATOR UGO DE MOHR TOLD ECONOFF ON MAY

8. DE MOHR EXPLAINED THAT ITALY, NOTWITHSTANDING ITS VAST

TRADE RELATIONS WITH IRAN, STEADFASTLY EMBARGOES SALES TO

IRAN OF WEAPONS AND SENSITIVE DUAL USE GOODS AND

TECHNOLOGIES. REVIEWING REFTEL TALKING POINTS, HOWEVER, HE

SAID HE COULD NOT AGREE THAT THE SALES WOULD NECESSARILY

EITHER VIOLATE ITALIAN LAW OR REQUIRE ACTIVATION OF THE

WASSENAAR PROCESS.

3. (C) DE MOHR SAID THAT ITALIAN LAW REQUIRES A WEAPONS/

SENSITIVE DUAL USE EMBARGO AGAINST IRAN UNDER "CATCH HUMAN

RIGHTS" PROVISIONS UNTIL IRAN COMPLIES WITH APPLICABLE UNITED

NATIONS RESOLUTIONS BUT ADDED, "I AM NOT IN A POSITION TO

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 ROME 02257 091415Z

SAY, HOWEVER, THAT THE PROPOSED SALES ARE CONTROLLED, THAT

THEY SHOULD BE CONSIDERED AS INTENDED FOR MILITARY PURPOSES."

HE SAID MFA WOULD TALK TO THE COMPANIES SPECIFIED REFTEL --

FABBIO BUZZI DESIGN AND ISOTTA FRANCHINI -- AND SHARE ITS

FINDINGS WITH POST.

4. (C) IN CLOSING, DE MOHR NOTED THAT "ANYTHING, EVEN A PAIR

OF SHOES, CAN BE INTERPRETED AS DUAL USE. IRAN YOU KNOW HAS

A LOT OF COAST TO PATROL AND SOME NEIGHBORS WHO BEAR

WATCHING." ACTION REQUEST. DE MOHR WAS NOT THE RECIPIENT OF

LAST YEAR\'S DEMARCHE IN WHICH WE ASKED MFA TO PREVAIL UPON

THE IRANIANS TO STEP UP MONITORING OF OIL TANKERS IN ITS

WATERS FOR POSSIBLE SMUGGLING OF IRAQI OIL IN CONTRAVENTION

OF UN SANCTIONS. ITALY CARRIED THAT MESSAGE TO IRAN, AND

IRAN EVIDENTLY DID INCREASE SURVEILLANCE OF ITS WATERS. WE

EXPECT THAT OTHER MFA OFFICES, THE IRANIANS, OR THE COMPANIES

INVOLVED WILL POSIT THIS KIND OF PATROLLING FUNCTION AS A

LEGITIMATE END-USE FOR THESE SPEED BOATS. WE WOULD

APPRECIATE BENEFIT OF DEPARTMENT\'S COUNTER-ARGUMENTS.
