SUBJECT: RENAMO KILLS MORE CIVILIANS IN NORTHERN

ZIMBABWE

REF: A. HARARE 2478 (NOTAL), B. 88 HARRE 6792

1. (U) ACCORDING TO LOCAL PRESS REPORTS, ON THE NIGHT OF

JULY 1, RENAMO KILLED FOUR CIVILIANS IN AN ATTACK IN THE

RUSHINGA AREA OF NORTHERN ZIMBABWE. THE ATTACK5S SHOT

DEAD A 27 YEAR OLD FARMER AND THEN BURNED DOWN THE HUT

CONTAINING HIS WIFE AND TWO CHILDREN, AGED FIVE AND TWO

YEARS OLD. AN EARLIER ATTACK IN THE SAME AREA WAS

REPORTED REF A.

-

2. (C) POLOFF SPOKE ON JULY12 WITH FATHER HANSKO OF

MARYMOUNT MISSION ABOUT THE REFAMO ATTACK. HANSKO SAI

THAT THE JULY 1 ATTACK TOOK PLACE SOME 10 KM INSIDE THE

BORDER WITH MOZAMBIQUE AND SOME 40 KM FROM THE MISSION.

AN EARLIER ATTACK, PERHAPS THAT REPORTED REF A, TOOK

PLACE ONLY 1.5 KM FROM THE MISSION. HANSKO SAID, HOWEVER,

THAT SECURITY AT THE MISSION IS NOT A PROBLEM. SOME 100

PEOPLE ARE NOW LIVING NEARBY IN ORDER TO TAKE ADVANTAGE

OF THE PROTECTION AFFORDED BY THE POLICE ENCAMPED THERE

(REF B). THE GOVERNMENT HAS BEEN "GENTLY" ENCOURAGING

PEOPLE FROM ISOLATED BORDER SETTLEMENTS TO SEEK

PROTECTION AT NIGHT AT SUCH GUARDED POINTS AS PART OF

ITS "VILLAGIZATION" PROGRAM. THE GOVERNMENT IS ALREADY

PROVIDING THOSE WHO AGREE TO SO GATHER WITH FOOD AID.

HANSKO BELIEVES THE GOVERNMENT\'S PROGRAM TO BE WELL

ADVISED. HE ESTIMATES THAT HALF OF THE POPULATION EAST

OF MARYMOUNT TO THE BORDER HAS FLED SINCE RENAMO BEGAN

HITTING THE AREA. HENSKO ADDED THAT WHILE HE IS HOPEFUL

THAT THE SIGNS OF MOVEMENT TOWARD NEGOTIATIONS IN

MOZAMBIQUE WILL LEAD TO REAL PEACE, HE IS AFRAID THAT

IN ANY LEADUP TO TALKS, RENAMO WILL SEEK TO INCREASE

ITS LEVERAGE OVER THE GOZ BY INCREASING ITS ATTACKS

IN ZIMBABWE.

LARSEN
