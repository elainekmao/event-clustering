SUBJECT: UAE MINISTER OF INFORMATION GIVES GREEN LIGHT

FOR MIDDLE EAST TELEVISION NETWORK

1. SUMMARY: I CALLED ON UAE MININFO AND PRESIDENTIAL

SON SHAYKH ABDULLAH BIN ZAYED ON DECEMBER 30 TO DISCUSS

THE PROPOSED MIDDLE EAST TELEVISION NETWORK (METN). THE

MINISTER WELCOMED BOTH THE IDEA OF THE NEW STATION AND

THE PROSPECT OF LOCATING IT IN THE UAE, ESPECIALLY IN

ABU DHABI. SHOULD THE UAE BE THE SITE CHOSEN BY THE

BBG, MY MISSION AND I LOOK FORWARD TO WORKING CLOSELY

WITH YOUR TEAM TO BRING THIS PROJECT TO FRUITION. WE

AWAIT A DETAILED PROPOSAL IN ORDER TO ASSIST YOU AS

FULLY AS POSSIBLE. END SUMMARY

2. FURTHER TO EXPLORATORY SITE VISITS TO THE UAE BY IBB

STAFF, I CALLED ON UAE MININFO SHAYKH ABDULLAH DECEMBER

30 TO EXPLAIN THE CONCEPT OF THE METN AND GAUGE HIS

REACTION TO THE POSSIBILITY OF LOCATING IT IN ABU DHABI

OR DUBAI. THE MINISTER INDICATED THAT HE HAD BEEN WELL-

BRIEFED ON THE EARLIER VISITS BY AHMAD BLOUSHI, HEAD OF

EMIRATES MEDIA INC. (EMI). HE ASKED WHAT SORT OF

PROGRAMMING THE NETWORK WOULD BROADCAST. I EXPLAINED

THAT THE PLAN FOR THE METN WAS STILL IN ITS FORMATIVE

STAGES BUT THAT IT ENVISIONS A MIXTURE OF SIXTY PERCENT

NEWS, FORTY PERCENT FEATURES IN AN ALL-ARABIC SEVEN-DAY,

24-HOUR FORMAT.

--------------------------------------------- ----------

"WE ARE PROUD TO BE A PART OF IT:" MINISTER WELCOMES TV

PROPOSAL

--------------------------------------------- ----------

3. THE MINISTER WELCOMED THE IDEA ENTHUSIASTICALLY,

NOTING THAT THE NEWS OPERATION OF EMIRATES MEDIA WOULD

SOON BE VACATING ITS OLD STUDIOS IN ABU DHABI, LEAVING

THEM AVAILABLE FOR A NEW TENANT. HE ADDED THAT IN FOUR

YEARS, THE EMI TELEVISION OPERATION WOULD MOVE TO NEW

PREMISES IN ABU DHABI, LEAVING ITS ENTIRE SITE, WITH SIX

TELEVISION STUDIOS, FREE. HE ALSO MENTIONED THAT EMI

HAS PREMISES IN DUBAI MEDIA CITY WHICH BBG COULD USE IN

THE FUTURE IF THEY WANT TO BUILD A FACILITY THERE. WHEN

I ASKED HIM IF THE UAEG WOULD BE "COMFORTABLE" WITH THE

IDEA OF HOSTING SUCH A STATION AND WITH ITS POLITICAL

OVERTONES, HE REPLIED UNHESITATINGLY: "YES, OF COURSE.

WE ARE NOT LOOKING FOR THE FINANCIAL ELEMENT. I AM

LOOKING FOR COOPERATION BETWEEN TWO ENTITIES. WE WILL

BOTH BENEFIT." HE ADDED: "WE FEEL PROUD TO BE PART OF

THIS. THIS IS NOT A THING WE WANT TO DO BEHIND THE

SCENES; WE WANT IT TO BE PUBLIC."

4. FOR MY PART, I AM CONFIDENT THAT WE HAVE THE UAEG\'S

POLITICAL SUPPORT FOR THE PROPOSED TV NETWORK. SHOULD

THE UAE BE THE SITE CHOSEN BY THE BBG, MY MISSION AND I

LOOK FORWARD TO WORKING CLOSELY WITH YOUR TEAM TO BRING

THIS PROJECT TO FRUITION. WE AWAIT A DETAILED PROPOSAL

IN ORDER TO ASSIST YOU AS FULLY AS POSSIBLE. WITH BEST

REGARDS. MARCELLE WAHBA
