SUMMARY

-------

1. (C) A REPRESENTATIVE OF THE CHURCH OF SCIENTOLOGY IN

ITALY VISITED US 3/25, SEEKING FURTHER USG HELP IN BEHALF OF

TWO SCIENTOLOGY MEMBERS CURRENTLY IMPRISONED IN EGYPT. HE

HOPED FURTHER USG HELP WOULD BE DECISIVE AND SWAY GOE

AUTHORITIES TO DEPORT THE TWO. END SUMMARY.

2. (C) FABIO AMICARELLI, PUBLIC AFFAIRS DIRECTOR FOR

SCIENTOLOGY (ITALY), VISITED EMBASSY 3/25 TO REQUEST USG HELP

ON BEHALF OF CHURCH OF SCIENTOLOGY MEMBERS MAHMOUD MASARWA

AND WIFE (WAFAA YASIM), ARRESTED IN CAIRO 12/24/01 (REFTEL).

ACCORDING TO AMICARELLI, MASARWA IS AN ISRAELI ARAB, MUSLIM

BY BIRTH AND SCIENTOLOGIST BY CONVICTION. YASIM IS

PALESTINIAN AND HOLDS PALESTINIAN AUTHORITY AND JORDANIAN

TRAVEL DOCUMENTS. BOTH ARE MEMBERS OF THE TEL AVIV

SCIENTOLOGY COMMUNITY.

3. (SBU) AMICARELLI IS BASED IN MILAN. HIS INVOLVEMENT WITH

THIS MATTER STEMS FROM HIS POSITION AS SCIENTOLOGY\'S PUBLIC

AFFAIRS DIRECTOR IN ITALY, MASARWA\'S LEGAL RESIDENCE IN MILAN

(SINCE 1999), AND MASARWA\'S EMPLOYMENT BY MILAN-BASED NEW ERA

PUBLICATIONS ITALY (PUBLISHING HOUSE LINKED TO SCIENTOLOGY).

MASARWA/YASIM WENT TO EGYPT TO PROMOTE "DIANETICS," AUTHORED

BY SCIENTOLOGY FOUNDER L.RON HUBBARD. THE BOOK, ACCORDING TO

AMICARELLI, HAD BEEN CLEARED BY EGYPTIAN CENSORS AND IS NOT

RELIGIOUS IN NATURE.

4. (C) AMICARELLI ATTRIBUTES THE ARREST TO DISINFORMATION ON

SCIENTOLOGY EMANATING FROM GERMANY AND FRANCE WHICH, HE

SUSPECTS, PROVOKED A NERVOUS REACTION AMONG EGYPTIAN CENSORS,

WHO HAD SECOND THOUGHTS ABOUT HAVING APPROVED "DIANETICS."

IT WAS NOT UNTIL THREE WEEKS AFTER THEIR ARREST THAT MASARWA

AND YASIM WERE CHARGED WITH CONTEMPT OF RELIGION. A 60-DAY

INVESTIGATIVE HOLDING PERIOD ENDED 2/26/02, AT WHICH TIME

PROSECUTORS SOUGHT A 45-DAY EXTENSION. THE COURT GRANTED AN

EXTENSION, BUT LIMITED IT TO 30 DAYS, I.E., A DEADLINE THAT

IS IMMINENT. AT THE END OF THIS PERIOD, AMICARELLI SAID,

EGYPTIAN AUTHORITIES WILL EITHER HAVE TO REQUEST A FURTHER

EXTENSION OF THE HOLDING PERIOD, SEND THE CASE TO TRIAL, OR

FREE (AND PRESUMABLY DEPORT) THE TWO.

5. (C) AMICARELLI SAID FOREIGN INTERVENTION HELPED BRING

THIS MATTER TO THE ATTENTION OF HIGHER GOE AUTHORITIES, FOR

WHOM IT REPRESENTS AN EMBARRASSMENT. INFORMAL GOI INTEREST,

HE SAID, WAS PROBABLY STIMULATED BY THE ACTIVE INVOLVEMENT OF

EMBASSY CAIRO HUMAN RIGHTS OFFICER MICHELLE DUNNE. HE SAID

HE HOPED TO PROD OFFICIAL GOI INTERVENTION (BASED ON THE

EFFORTS OF AN ITALIAN PUBLISHER TO PROMOTE COMMERCIAL SALES

IN EGYPT) THAT COULD SWAY EGYPTIAN AUTHORITIES TO RELEASE OR

DEPORT THE TWO WHEN THE CURRENT 30-DAY HOLDING PERIOD ENDS.

6. (C) COMMENT: WHILE AMICARELLI UNDERSTANDS THE USG HAS

LIMITED GROUNDS ON WHICH TO INTERVENE (AS MASARWA AND YASIM

ARE NOT AMCITS OR RESIDENT IN THE U.S., AND THEIR EMPLOYER IS

NOT AN AMERICAN FIRM), HE NEVERTHELESS ATTRIBUTES TO THE U.S.

AN INFLUENTIAL ROLE IN EGYPT THAT COULD BE DECISIVE. (AMONG

OTHER DOCUMENTS, HE LEFT US A 2/22/02 LETTER ON THE CASE FROM

HOUSE INTERNATIONAL RELATIONS COMMITTEE MEMBER REP. BENJAMIN

GILMAN TO EGYPTIAN AMBASSADOR NABIL FAHMY.) AMICARELLI HOPED
