1. (C) THE CROATIAN POLICE ARE TRYING TO CONFIRM THE

CREDIBILITY OF INFORMATION INDICATING THAT ICTY INDICTEE ANTE

GOTOVINA WAS HIDING IN BOSNIA, NEAR THE CROATIAN BORDER, IN

JANUARY 2003. THAT IS WHAT BORIS SPUDIC, ASSISTANT HEAD OF

CROATIAN CRIMINAL POLICE TOLD RSO DURING AN MEETING -- HELD

AT SPUDIC\'S REQUEST -- LATE ON FEBRUARY 7. SPUDIC SAID THAT

HE ALSO WAS PASSING THIS INFORMATION TO THE BRITISH AND OTHER

WESTERN EUROPEAN CONTACTS. SPUDIC WOULD NOT COMMENT ON THE

IDENTITY OR RELIABILITY OF THE SOURCE, BUT OFFERED TO

RECONTACT HIM IF WE FOUND THE MATERIAL USEFUL AND ACCURATE.

2. (C) BEGIN TEXT OF CROATIAN POLICE INFORMATION (AS

PROVIDED):

CROATIAN POLICE HAS INFORMATION ON THE MOVEMENT OF ANTE

GOTOVINA IN RELATION OF KUPRES AND LJUBUSKI, FEDERATION

BOSNIA AND HERCEGOVINA.

BY INFORMATION, GOTOVINA WAS DURING JANUARY ON SEVERAL

OCCASIONS, TOGETHER WITH HIS ESCORT, SPOTTED IN CAFE BAR ON

GAS STATION IN PLACE VELJACI, ON ROAD VRGORAC - VITINA, ON

ROUTE TO VISIT RETIRED HVO GENERAL AND EX-PRESIDENT OF B&H

HDZ PARTY ANTI JELAVIC, WHO POSSES THE HOUSE ON HERCEGOVINIAN

SIDE OF THE STATE BORDER, IN DIRECTION TOWARDS PLACE

PODPROLOG AND CROATIAN BORDER.

BY INFORMATION PROVIDED BY SOURCE, GOTOVINA FREQUENTLY IS

STAYING AT THE NEWLY OPENED HOTEL ABOVE KUPRES, WHOSE OWNERS

ARE VINKO ZULJEVIC "KLICA", AND SLAVEN PETRICEVIC, EX MEMBERS

COMMENT

4. (C) THIS IS THE FIRST TIME SINCE GOTOVINA\'S DISAPPEARANCE

18 MONTHS AGO THAT THE CROATIAN INTERIOR MINISTRY HAS PASSED

US INFORMATION ON THE ICTY FUGITIVE. IT COMES AT A TIME WHEN

THE GOC FINDS ITSELF UNDER MOUNTING PRESSURE FROM THE

PROSECUTOR\'S OFFICE AT THE HAGUE TRIBUNAL TO DEMONSTRATE THAT

IT IS TAKING ACTIVE MEASURES TO FIND GOTOVINA.

5. (C) WE DEFER TO EMBASSY SARAJEVO AND OTHERS TO ASSESS THE

QUALITY OF THE GOC INFORMATION AND WOULD WELCOME ANY ANALYSIS

THAT WE CAN PASS TO SPUDIC ON HIS LEAD. WE NOTE THAT VINKO

ZULJEVIC "KLICA" IS IMPRISONED IN ZAGREB PENDING TRIAL FOR

THE JUNE 2002 MURDER OF A ALLEGED CROATIAN INTELLIGENCE

AGENCY INFORMANT, VESELIN MARINOV. "KLICA," A BOSNIAN CROAT,

IS A WELL KNOWN CROATIAN MAFIA FIGURE, WHO REPORTEDLY SERVED

IN THE NOTORIOUS "CONVICTS" BRIGADE OF ICTY INDICTEE MLADEN

NALETILIC TUTA AND HVO GENERAL IVAN ANDABAK IN BOSNIA DURING

THE WAR.
