1. (C) SUMMARY. IN ADDITION TO ASSET SEIZURE AND

TRAVEL SANCTIONS FOCUSED ON A SMALL NUMBER OF TOP GOZ

OFFICIALS IMPLICATED IN POLITICAL VIOLENCE AND THE

BREAKDOWN OF THE RULE OF LAW (REF A), THE HARARE COUNTRY

TEAM HAS ALSO EXPLORED WHAT VIABLE TECHNOLOGY/DUAL USE

SANCTIONS MIGHT BE CONSIDERED. IDENTIFICATION OF ANY

MEANINGFUL SANCTIONS THAT WOULD NOT ADVERSELY AFFECT

CRITICAL POPULATION GROUPS HAS BEEN DIFFICULT. THE LOW-

TECH NATURE OF ZIMBABWE\'S ECONOMY, THE ZIMBABWE DEFENSE

FORCE\'S (ZDF) DEPENDENCE ON (FORMER) EAST-BLOC HARDWARE,

AND THE LACK OF INFORMATION ON COMMERCIAL LINKS BETWEEN

PARASTATAL/ZANU-PF COMPANIES AND U.S. SUPPLIERS ARE

IMPEDIMENTS TO TIGHTLY FOCUSED SANCTIONS THAT COULD

PINCH THE ZANU-PF RINGLEADERS WITHOUT INFLICTING SERIOUS

COLLATERAL DAMAGE ON THE ECONOMY.

2. (C) SUMMARY CONTINUED. WE HAVE IDENTIFIED A SHORT

LIST OF OUTMODED U.S. EQUIPMENT WITHIN THE ZDF INVENTORY

THAT COULD BE TARGETED, BUT TO MINIMAL EFFECT. WE WILL

FORWARD A LIST OF ZANU-PF-OWNED COMPANIES TO THE

DEPARTMENT TO DETERMINE IF THERE IS INFORMATION IN

WASHINGTON OR ELSEWHERE ON LINKS TO U.S. SUPPLIERS. UN

STUDIES ON EXPLOITATION OF THE DEMOCRATIC REPUBLIC OF

CONGO\'S (DRC) RESOURCES MAY OFFER ANOTHER, MORE

INTERNATIONAL APPROACH. EVEN HERE WE WILL NEED TO TREAD

CAREFULLY, SINCE MANY PARASTATAL/ZANU-PF FIRMS DEAL IN

CONSUMER OR SERVICE PRODUCTS. SANCTIONS ON THESE WOULD

RISK GENERATING UNEMPLOYMENT AND FURTHER SLOWDOWN OF THE

ZIMBABWEAN ECONOMY, CREATING THE IMPRESSION AMONG

ZIMBABWEANS THAT THE U.S. DOES NOT DIFFERENTIATE BETWEEN

BABY AND BATH. END SUMMARY.

BACKGROUND

----------

3. (C) THE EMBASSY\'S CAREFUL PREPARATIONS FOR Y2K

REVEALED INTERESTING INSIGHTS INTO ZIMBABWE\'S ECONOMY.

BECAUSE OF INTERNATIONAL SANCTIONS IMPOSED DURING THE

UDI PERIOD, ZIMBABWE WAS FORCED TO BECOME AS SELF-

RELIANT AS POSSIBLE. ONE RESULT WAS A RELATIVELY LOW-

TECH APPROACH TO BOTH THE MANUFACTURING AND SERVICE

SECTORS. GLOBAL TECHNOLOGY MARCHED FORWARD BUT ZIMBABWE

DID NOT. MUCH OF ITS TECHNOLOGY AND MANUFACTURING

REMAINS TECHNOLOGICALLY STUCK IN THE 1960\'S, 1970\'S OR

1980\'S, DEPENDING UPON THE SECTOR IN QUESTION. THIS

REMAINS TRUE OF THE MILITARY ESTABLISHMENT AS WELL --

OUR Y2K SURVEY INDICATED THAT THERE WERE LESS THAN 100

COMPUTERS IN THE MOD, ALMOST ALL OF THESE STAND-ALONES

AND MANY NOT COMPATIBLE.

4. (C) SINCE INDEPENDENCE, SOME SECTORS HAVE MODERNIZED

DUE TO FOREIGN INVESTMENT AND THE AVAILABILITY OF UP-TO-

DATE TECHNOLOGY. FOREIGN INVESTORS LAUNCHED SEVERAL

WORLD CLASS MINING OPERATIONS (SOME NOW CLOSED). THE

COMMERCIAL FARMING SECTOR, WHICH IMPORTS MANY OF ITS

CHEMICAL AND MECHANICAL INPUTS, ALSO TAKES A BACK SEAT

TO NO ONE. HOWEVER, BOTH OF THESE SECTORS HAVE SUFFERED

SHARP DOWNTURNS DUE TO UNREALISTIC EXCHANGE RATES, THE

CONCOMITANT UNAVAILABILITY OF FOREX, AND UNENLIGHTENED

GOZ POLICIES ACROSS THE BOARD. ALREADY ON THE ROPES,

THESE SECTORS WILL BE FURTHER WEAKENED BY EXTERNAL

SANCTIONS. THE MEDICAL SECTOR ALSO ENJOYED SIGNIFICANT

TECHNOLOGICAL ADVANCES, ALTHOUGH MANY OF THESE ARE IN

MILITARY SANCTIONS

------------------

5. (C) IN RECENT YEARS THE ZDF HAS SHOPPED FOR MOST OF

ITS MILITARY HARDWARE IN EITHER CHINA OR THE FORMER EAST

BLOC. ACQUISITIONS FROM THE WEST HAVE BEEN INCREASINGLY

RARE. THE ZDF MAINTAINS IN ITS INVENTORY A FEW

VENERABLE AIRCRAFT OF U.S. MANUFACTURE, INCLUDING TWELVE

BELL 412 JET RANGERS, EIGHT DAKOTA FIXED-WING

TRANSPORTS, AND TWO CESSNA 235\'S. VERY FEW OF THESE ARE

CURRENTLY AIRWORTHY DUE TO A LACK OF SPARE PARTS.

SENIOR ZDF OFFICERS HAVE RECENTLY TOLD US THAT THEY

WOULD LIKE TO GET THIS EQUIPMENT ALOFT, PREFERABLY WITH

U.S. ASSISTANCE. HOWEVER, THE ROOT OF THE PROBLEM IS

MONEY RATHER THAN TECHNOLOGY, SINCE SPARES FOR ALL OF

THESE AIRCRAFT ARE READILY AVAILABLE ON THE OPEN MARKET

WITHOUT END USER CERTIFICATION, ON A CASH BASIS. THE

ZDF\'S BUDGET CRUNCH, AND THE DECISION BORN OF NECESSITY

6. (C) ZIMBABWE MILITARY INDUSTRIES (ZMI), ANOTHER

VESTIGE OF THE UDI POLICY OF SELF-RELIANCE, MANUFACTURE

A RANGE OF MILITARY EQUIPMENT. OUTPUT IS TAILORED FOR

THE ZDF AND OTHER SECURITY SERVICES. UNIFORMS AND KIT

ARE THE MOST SIGNIFICANT ITEMS. LIGHT WEAPONRY

PRODUCTION INCLUDES HAND GRENADES, SMALL ARMS MUNITIONS,

40 MM ROCKET LAUNCHERS AND ROCKETS, 60 MM MORTAR TUBES

AND SHELLS, 81 MM AND 120 MM MORTAR SHELLS. OTHER

WEAPONS SYSTEMS AND HEAVY MUNITIONS ARE ALL IMPORTED.

WE HAVE BEEN ABLE TO IDENTIFY ONLY ONE U.S. LINK TO THE

ZMI IMPORT/EXPORT CYCLE, THE EXPORT OF APPROXIMATELY

20,000 ROUNDS OF SMALL CALIBER HUNTING AMMUNITION TO A

CALIFORNIA BUYER.

-------------

OTHER OPTIONS

-------------

7. (C) BOTH SENIOR MILITARY AND ZANU-PF OFFICIALS, IN

ADDITION TO THE RULING PARTY ITSELF, SHARE OWNERSHIP IN

A DIVERSE BUSINESS EMPIRE THAT RANGES FROM

TRANSPORTATION TO TOURISM TO FINANCE TO MINING AND

MANUFACTURE TO TELECOMMUNICATIONS. WE HAVE ACQUIRED A

USEFUL STUDY DONE IN 2000 THAT LISTS THESE HOLDINGS AND

THAT REVEALS SOME OF THE ZANU-PF OFFICIALS INVOLVED

BEHIND THE SCENES, INCLUDING IN VENTURES IN THE DRC.

8. (C) FOCUSING ON THE DRC ECONOMIC CONNECTIONS

(TECHNOLOGICALLY OR OTHERWISE) SHOULD BE CONSIDERED,

SINCE THE REVENUES FROM ZIMBABWEAN VENTURES THERE FLOW

INTO THE PRIVATE ACCOUNTS OF ZANU-PF INSIDERS AND NOT

THE ZIMBABWEAN TREASURY. THE LOSS OF THESE REVENUES

WOULD HAVE LITTLE EFFECT ON THE ZIMBABWEAN ECONOMY BUT

WOULD BE FELT DIRECTLY BY MUGABE CRONIES. OSLEG LTD., A

HOLDING COMPANY, BOASTS ZDF FORCE COMMANDER ZVINAVASHE,

MOD PERMANENT SECRETARY TRUST MAPOSA, AND SPEAKER OF THE

PARLIAMENT MNANGAGWA ON ITS BOARD OF DIRECTORS. OSLEG

HAS INTERESTS IN A RANGE OF DRC MINING AND TIMBER

VENTURES: COSLEG, ORYX ZIMCOM, PETRA DIAMONDS LTD.,

COMIEX CONGO SARL, SENGAMINES/MIBA, TSHIBUA KIMBERLITE

DEPOSITS, AND SENGA-SENGA ALLUVIAL MINE.

9. (C) WE WILL PROVIDE AVAILABLE DOCUMENTATION TO AF/S

TO DETERMINE IF IT IS POSSIBLE TO USE COMMERCE OR

TREASURY DATABASES TO ESTABLISH U.S. LINKAGES WITH ZANU-

PF BUSINESS INTERESTS LOCATED IN ZIMBABWE OR DRC. THIS

MAY BE THE BEST HOPE OF IDENTIFYING TECHNOLOGIES OR

OTHER INSTRUMENTS THAT COULD GIVE TECHNOLOGY SANCTIONS

TEETH. CROSS-REFERENCING WITH UN STUDIES THAT TRACE THE

OUTSIDE EXPLOITATION OF DRC NATURAL RESOURCES MAY ALSO

REVEAL ADDITIONAL LINKAGES AND PROVIDE THE IMPETUS TO

PUSH FOR INTERNATIONAL RATHER THAN BILATERAL MEASURES.

----------------------------

LIKELY RESULTS ON GOZ POLICY

----------------------------

10. (C) UNLESS SIGNIFICANT LINKAGES BETWEEN ZANU-PF/ZDF

COMPANIES CAN BE ESTABLISHED, COUNTRY TEAM DOES NOT

BELIEVE THAT TECHNOLOGY/DUAL-USE SANCTIONS WOULD HAVE A

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 04 HARARE 02567 02 OF 03 301519Z

MEASURABLE IMPACT ON PRESIDENT MUGABE AND HIS INNER

CIRCLE, OR RESULT IN A CHANGE IN GOZ POLICY. MUGABE

WOULD UNDOUBTEDLY REACT LOUDLY TO THE ANNOUNCEMENT OF

SANCTIONS, HARMFUL OR NOT, AND ATTEMPT TO BEND THESE TO

HIS OWN PROPAGANDA PURPOSES. WE DO NOT ANTICIPATE

MEANINGFUL RETALIATORY MEASURES, ALTHOUGH THE GOZ COULD

THEORETICALLY TAKE ACTION AGAINST THE APPROXIMATELY 45

AMERICAN COMPANIES OR THEIR REPRESENTATIVES OPERATING

HERE.

-------

COMMENT

-------

11. (C) ECONOMIC/TECHNOLOGY SANCTIONS LEVIED ON

ZIMBABWE MUST BE CAREFULLY FOCUSED TO AVOID COLLATERAL

DAMAGE TO AN ALREADY IMPLODING ECONOMY. IN RECENT

REMARKS TO THE PRESS IN SOUTH AFRICA, MDC LEADER MORGAN

TSVANGARAI REPEATED THAT AT THIS TIME HE OPPOSES

SIPDIS

INTERNATIONAL (ECONOMIC) SANCTIONS, SINCE THE ZIMBABWEAN

ECONOMY CANNOT BEAR THE STRAIN. IT IS OUR VIEW THAT

TECHNOLOGY OR ECONOMIC SANCTIONS THAT HAVE THE POTENTIAL

OR EFFECT OF CREATING ADDITIONAL UNEMPLOYMENT AND

ERODING ECONOMIC ACTIVITY WOULD BE SEEN BY MOST

ZIMBABWEANS AS SPITEFUL RATHER THAN CONSTRUCTIVE.
