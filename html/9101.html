SUBJECT: TRIAL OF ACCUSED IN FOLEY ASSASSINATION POSTPONED

AGAIN

1. (U) Local press reported on July 1 that the trial of five

men (six additional suspects are being tried in absentia)

accused of murdering US diplomat Laurence Foley has been

postponed for a week. One of the accused, Libyan national

Salem Said Bin Sewid, requested the State Security Court

release his current attorney and appoint him an attorney from

the Jordan Bar Association (JBA). The President of the JBA,

who is representing co-defendant Yasser Fathi, asked the

court for some time to examine Sewid\'s case to see if he

might represent both defendants. The Presiding Judge granted

him a week. The trial should resume on July 8.
