SUBJECT: UNHCR TO PROPOSE RESETTLING CUBAN DOCTORS IN

UNSPECIFIED THIRD COUNTRY

REF: A). WHITE/HEBRON TELCON 6/20/00

B). HARARE 3323

CLASSIFIED BY AMBASSADOR TOM MCDONALD FOR REASONS 1.5

B/D.

1. (C) UNHCR REGIONAL REPRESENTATIVE OLUSEYI BAJULAIYE

TOLD AMBASSADOR MCDONALD ON JUNE 20 THAT HE WOULD BE

DELIVERING THAT DAY A LETTER FROM UNITED NATIONS DEPUTY

HIGH COMMISSIONER FOR REFUGEES SOREN JESSEN-PETERSEN TO

ZIMBABWEAN VICE PRESIDENT JOSEPH MSIKA THROUGH THE

MINISTRY OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS. THE LETTER WILL PRESENT A

CONCRETE SOLUTION TO THE PROBLEM OF THE CUBAN DOCTORS,

AND STATE THAT THE UNHCR HAS IDENTIFIED A COUNTRY FOR

THEIR IMMEDIATE RESETTLEMENT. BAJULAIYE WOULD NOT

IDENTIFY THE COUNTRY BEFORE HE HAD PROPOSED IT TO THE

ZIMBABWEAN GOVERNMENT. HE SAID THAT HE HOPED THAT THE

ZIMBABWEANS WOULD REACT QUICKLY AND POSITIVELY, AND

COUNTED ON HIS PERSONAL FRIENDSHIP WITH ZIMBABWEAN

MINISTRY OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS PERMANENT SECRETARY

MACHIVENYIKA MAPURANGA TO HELP LUBRICATE THE CUBANS\'

RELEASE PROCESS. HOWEVER, HE ALSO ACKNOWLEDGED THE

GOVERNMENT\'S DISTRACTION WITH THE PARLIAMENTARY

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 HARARE 03344 201559Z

ELECTION, A FACT THAT COULD REQUIRE THE CUBANS TO EXTEND

THEIR STAY IN ZIMBABWE.

2. (C) THE REGIONAL UNHCR REPRESENTATIVE ASSURED

AMBASSADOR MCDONALD THAT HIS STAFF VISITED THE CUBANS

EVERY DAY. SPEAKING "OFF THE RECORD," BAJULAIYE

(PROTECT) SAID THAT THE ZIMBABWEAN GOVERNMENT WOULD NOT

FORCIBLY REPATRIATE THE CUBANS, BUT RATHER WANTED THEM

TO "STEW" FOR A WHILE. BY MAKING THE CUBANS SUFFER, THE

ZIMBABWEAN GOVERNMENT WOULD SEND A SIGNAL TO OTHER

WOULD-BE DEFECTORS REGARDING THEIR CHOICE OF ZIMBABWE AS

A JUMPING OFF POINT.

3. (C) CONSUL HANDED OVER THE CUBANS PASSPORTS TO

BAJULAIYE ALONG WITH A FEW OTHER PERSONAL BELONGINGS.

EMBASSY SOUGHT GUIDANCE FROM DEPARTMENT (REFTEL B) AS TO

THE ADVISABILITY OF HANDING OVER THE CUBANS PASSPORTS TO

UNHCR TO FACILITATE THEIR TRAVEL TO A THIRD COUNTRY FOR

RESETTLEMENT. DEPARTMENT (PRM\'S CRAIG WHITE) CONCURRED

THAT EMBASSY SHOULD HAND OVER THE CUBANS\' PASSPORTS TO

UNHCR, AS THAT ORGANIZATION WAS OFFICIALLY RESPONSIBLE

FOR THE CUBANS (REF A).
