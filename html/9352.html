SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION: AL KHALEEJ EDITORIAL ON IRAQ

1. SUMMARY: AN EDITORIAL IN THE SHARJAH-BASED PAN-ARAB DAILY

"AL-KHALEEJ" DESCRIBED THE SITUATION OF U.S. TROOPS IN IRAQ AS "A

COSTLY DILEMMA." THE EDITORIAL CLAIMED THAT THE US LAUNCHED THE

WAR BASED ON "DECEPTION, FORGERY, AND LIES" AND THAT THE U.S. HAS

"BEGUN TO HARVEST THE RESULT OF ITS DEEDS." THE EDITORIAL

FURTHER ASSERTED THAT THE UNANTICIPATED PROBLEMS IN IRAQ EXPLAIN

IS WHY THE U.S. ADMINISTRATION WANTS "TO GET OTHER NATIONS

INVOLVED IN THIS QUAGMIRE." END SUMMARY.

2. UNDER THE HEADLINE "A QUAGMIRE OF LIES," SHARJAH-BASED PAN-

ARAB DAILY "AL-KHALEEJ" 7/12 EDITORIALIZED:

"THREE AMERICAN SITUATIONS IN THE LAST TWENTY FOUR HOURS CONFIRM

THAT THE U.S. IS BEGINNING TO FEEL ITS OCCUPATION OF IRAQ HAS

BECOME A COSTLY DILEMMA ... THE FIRST SITUATION WAS PRESIDENT

BUSH\'S DECLARATION THAT HIS NATION IS FACING A SECURITY PROBLEM

IN IRAQ. THE SECOND SITATION WAS SECRETARY POWELL\'S ANNOUNCEMENT

THAT HIS COUNTRY IS DISCUSSING WITH ITS ALLIES A ROLE THAT NATO

CAN PLAY IN IRAQ. THE THIRD SITUATION WAS THE UNANIMOUS APPROVAL

BY THE U.S. SENATE OF A RESOLUTION ENCOURAGING THE AMERICAN

ADMINISTRATION TO SEEK NATO AND UN\'S ASSISTANCE TO MAINTAIN PEACE

AND SECURITY IN IRAQ ..."

"... THE AMERICAN DILEMMA HAS BEGUN TO TAKE ON A DOMESTIC

DIMENSION FOLLOWING THE REVELATION OF A SCANDAL OF LIES USED BY

U.S. ADMINISTRATION TO JUSTIFY ITS AGGRESSION AGAINST IRAQ BASED

ON CLAIMS THAT IRAQ POSSESSES WMD. ALSO, THE BAD PLANNING OF

THE WAR AND ITS CONSEQUENCES HAVE ELICITED SHARP CRITICISMS FROM

CONGRESS AND SENIOR AMERICAN OFFICIALS .... THOSE WHO LAUNCHED

THEIR WAR THROUGH DECEPTION, FORGERY, AND LIES HAVE BEGUN TO

HARVEST THE RESULT OF THEIR DEEDS. THEY ARE DROWNING IN A

QUAGMIRE OF LIES AND ARE TRYING TO GET OTHERS INVOLVED IN THIS."
