SUBJECT: KURDS, AND PKK, CONTINUE TO SPREAD WEST

REF: ADANA 0468

1. CONFIDENTIAL - ENTIRE TEXT

2. RECENT REPORTING FROM ADANA AND OTHER POSTS IN

TURKEY HAS FOCUSSED ON THE MASS MIGRATION OF KURDS

FROM THE SOUTHEAST INTO THE LARGE CITIES OF THE WEST.

AN ITEM IN THURSDAY\'S ADANA PRESS SUMMARY SHOWS HOW

KURDISH MIGRATION IS ALSO PUTTING THE SQUEEZE ON

TURKISH RESIDENTS IN SMALLER TOWNS BORDERING ON THE

TRADITIONAL KURDISH AREAS -- AND BRINGING WITH IT THE

PKK.

3. ACCORDING TO A "MILLIYET" ITEM ON SEPTEMBER 9

(PARA 5, REFTEL), PKK MILITANTS ATTACKED THE

SUMERBANK (A GOVENMENT-OWNED COOPERATIVE) YARN MILL

IN THE TOWN OF PERTEK ON THE NIGHT OF SEPT 6, KILLING

THE MILL\'S PLANNING CHIEF AND A SECURITY GUARD. THE

MILITANTS THEN TORCHED THE MILL. SECURITY TROOPS

REPORTEDLY ARRIVED AS THE MILITANTS WERE LEAVING, AND

KILLED ONE. THE REST PRESUMABLY ESCAPED. PERTEK, A

TOWN IN TUNCELI PROVINCE ABOUT HALFWAY BETWEEN

TUNCELI AND ELAZIG ON THE NORTH SHORE OF THE KEBAN

RESERVOIR, HAS BEEN ALMOST WHOLLY POPULATED BY ETHNIC

TURKS SINCE THE ARMENIAN RESIDENTS WERE REMOVED OR

EXTERMINATED DURING WORLD WAR I. THE MOTHER OF ONE

OF CONSULATE ADANA\'S EMPLOYEES HALES FROM PERTEK, AND

THE EMPLOYEE KNOWS THE TOWN, AND WAS A DISTANT

RELATIVE OF ONE OF THE MEN KILLED IN THE YARN MILL

ATTACK.

4. ACCORDING TO THE EMPLOYEE\'S RELATIVES, THERE HAD

BEEN NO SIGNS OF KURDISH UPRISING VIOLENCE IN THE

TOWN UNTIL THIS SUMMER, AND THE ATTACK ON THE MILL

WAS THE FIRST ATTACK EVER INSIDE THE TOWN. THE ONLY

OTHER PREVIOUS INCIDENT, ALSO THIS SUMMER, INVOLVED

GUNMEN -- PRESUMABLY KURDISH MILITANTS -- FIRING DOWN

ON A NEARBY JANDARMA STATION FROM SURROUNDING HILLS.

THE ATTACK ON THE MILL, HOWEVER, WAS DIRECTED AGAINST

A GOVERNMENT-OWNED FACTORY, AND INCIDENTALLY, THE

ONLY MAJOR EMPLOYER IN TOWN.

5. FROM THE TIME OF THE ARMENIAN REMOVALS UNTIL THE

LAST 4 OR SO YEARS, PERTEK HAD REMAINED ALMOST

EXCLUSIVELY TURKISH. IN THE LAST 4 YEARS, LARGE

NUMBERS OF ZAZA KURDS FROM TUNCELI HAVE MOVED INTO

THE TOWN, BOUGHT PROPERTY AND BUILT HOUSES, ALL

APPARENTLY HOPING TO ESCAPE THE VIOLENCE IN AND

AROUND TUNCELI. THE NUMBERS OF KURDS AND TURKS IN

PERTEK ARE NOW ABOUT EQUAL, OR PERHAPS EVEN WEIGHTED

SLIGHTLY IN FAVOR OF THE KURDS. THE TWO GROUPS DO

NOT MIX, AND KEEP THEIR DISTANCE FROM EACH OTHER.

KURDISH MEN DO NOT, FOR EXAMPLE, GO TO TURKISH TEA

HOUSES, AND VICE VERSA.

6. EVEN BEFORE THE PKK FOLLOWED THE KURDISH

MIGRANTS, MANY ETHNIC TURKS HAD ALREADY LEFT PERTEK,

CITING THE OTHER PROBLEMS THE KURDISH MIGRANTS

BROUGHT WITH THEM. HOWEVER, OUR EMPLOYEE\'S RELATIVES

HAVE RESISTED, HAVING THEIR ROOTS AND BUSINESSES IN

TOWN. IT WILL BEAR WATCHING WHETHER THIS ATTACK, AND

THE RESULTING JOBLESSNESS, WILL ACCELERATE THE

TURKISH FLIGHT FROM SMALL TOWNS LIKE PERTEK.

7. COMMENT: IN THE PAST MONTH OR SO, THE TURKISH

PRESS HAS REPORTED GREAT GOVERNMENT SUCCESS IN THE

SOUTHEAST, CITING FIGURES OF OVER 1000 PKK

FATALITIES. THESE REPORTS ARE PRESUMABLY BASED ON

TGS PRESS RELEASES, WHICH PROBABLY HAVE MORE

PROPAGANDA THAN NEWS VALUE. BUT WHILE THE TGS IS

CLAIMING VICTORY IN THE AREAS AROUND HAKKARI, SIRNAK,

AND DOGUBEYAZIT, THE PKK CONTINUES TO STRIKE IN OTHER

PARTS OF THE SOUTHEAST. AS THIS AND OTHER ATTACKS

SHOW, THE MILITANTS CAN EVEN VENTURE INTO NEW AREAS.

8. FURTHER COMMENT: ALTHOUGH THE PRESS REPORTS

REFERRED TO THE ATTACKERS AS PKK MILITANTS, AND THERE

IS NO EVIDENCE TO THE CONTRARY, TUNCELI PROVINCE HAS

BEEN A BASE FOR THE TURKISH WORKERS AND PEASANTS

LIBERATION ARMY (TIKKO). IT IS POSSIBLE THAT TIKKO

ELEMENTS CARRIED OUT THE ATTACK, BUT THE LARGER AND

MORE NOTORIOUS PKK GOT THE CREDIT. END COMMENTS.
