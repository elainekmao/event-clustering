SUBJECT: NIGERIA: OBASANJO TO VISIT CHINA

Classified by Ambassador Howard F. Jeter. Reason: 1.5(b).

1. (C) Mission has learned that the much-discussed but

never-before-scheduled State Visit to China of Nigerian

President Olusegun Obasanjo is now slated for late August.

President Obasanjo plans to leave Nigeria on August 25, and

depart China on August 29, arriving in Nigeria on August 30.

2. (U) Chinese nationals have long been active in

manufacturing in Nigeria, notably in textiles and plastic

products. More recently, a wider range of Chinese (and other

East Asian origin) low-cost consumer goods has become

available in Nigeria -- often to the consterntion of domestic

manufacturers who cannot compete effectively. Chinese firms

in the engineering sector (onshore seismic operations in

oil-producing areas and large civil engineering projects in

Abuja (CCECC) and telecommunications are also establishing

strong profiles here. The Chinese have also sought to

establish a presence in the sale of defense-related articles.
