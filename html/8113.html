SUBJECT: MARTINO OUTLINES DEPLOYMENT TO IRAQ FOR PARLIAMENT

1. SUMMARY: ITALIAN MOD MARTINO TOLD PARLIAMENT MAY 14 THAT

A CONTINGENT OF ABOUT 3000 MILITARY PERSONNEL WILL BE

DEPLOYED AND OPERATIONAL IN IRAQ BY MID-JULY. TWO MINESWEEPS

THAT ARE ALREADY IN THEATER WILL BECOME OPERATIONAL SOONER.

AN ADVANCE TEAM WILL DEPART FOR IRAQ WITHIN DAYS TO ASSESS

ON-THE-GROUND NEEDS FOR THE TROOPS. PARLIAMENT APPROVED

MILITARY SUPPORT IN AN APRIL 15 VOTE ON ITALY,S

CONTRIBUTIONS TO IRAQ RECONSTRUCTION, AND THE GOI IS FUNDING

THE DEPLOYMENT THROUGH AN EXPEDITED PROCESS. THE TROOPS ARE

SEPARATE FROM THE UNIT OF CARABINIERI THAT DEPARTED MAY 15

FOR IRAQ TO PROTECT THE ITALIAN RED CROSS FIELD HOSPITAL IN

BAGHDAD, ACCORDING TO MARTINO. THE CENTER-RIGHT COALITION

PRAISED MARTINO FOR KEEPING PARLIAMENT FULLY INFORMED AND

APPROVED THE MISSION. THE CENTER-LEFT OPPOSITION CRITICIZED

THE GOI FOR ITS SUBSERVIENCE TO THE U.S. END SUMMARY.

MISSION IS HUMANITARIAN, PARLIAMENT TO APPROVE FUNDING ONLY

--------------------------------------------- --------------

2. MOD MARTINO APPEARED BEFORE A JOINT SESSION OF THE SENATE

AND CHAMBER OF DEPUTIES FOREIGN AFFAIRS AND DEFENSE

COMMITTEES MAY 14 TO BRIEF ON THE PLANNED DEPLOYMENT OF

ITALIAN TROOPS TO IRAQ. HE STRESSED THAT THE MISSION -- CODE

NAMED "ANCIENT BABYLONIA" -- IS FOR HUMANITARIAN AND

RECONSTRUCTION PURPOSES, AND TO PROVIDE DIRECT SUPPORT TO THE

LOCAL POPULATION. HE STATED THAT IT IS EXPECTED TO LAST SIX

MONTHS LEAVING, HOWEVER, THE DOOR OPEN FOR A POSSIBLE

EXTENSION. HE REMARKED THAT THERE IS NO NEED FOR A

PARLIAMENTARY VOTE AT THIS TIME BECAUSE THE MISSION HAD

ALREADY BEEN AUTHORIZED BY PARLIAMENT IN ITS APRIL 15 VOTE,

BUT SAID PARLIAMENT WILL HAVE TO APPROVE FUNDING. THE GOI IS

PROVIDING IMMEDIATE FUNDING THROUGH A DECREE LAW THAT WILL

SOON BE PRESENTED TO PARLIAMENT FOR ITS DISCUSSION AND FINAL

APPROVAL. MARTINO DID NOT INDICATE WHEN THE GOI WOULD

PRESENT THE DECREE, BUT SAID IT WILL INCLUDE CIVIL

RECONSTRUCTION PROJECTS THAT ARE BEING COORDINATED BY THE

MFA. (NOTE: UNDER ITALY,S CONSTITUTION, DECREE LAWS ARE

TOOLS FOR URGENT MATTERS; THEY ARE EFFECTIVE IMMEDIATELY AND

VALID FOR 60 DAYS. PARLIAMENT HAS 60 DAYS TO DISCUSS, AMEND

AND APPROVE A DECREE LAW OR IT EXPIRES. TO THE BEST OF OUR

KNOWLEDGE, THIS NEVER HAPPENS WITH DECREES INVOLVING FUNDING

FOR INTERNATIONAL PEACE MISSIONS. END NOTE.)

ITALIANS WITH THE BRITISH; WHO COMMANDS?

--------------------------------------------- --------

3. MARTINO TOLD PARLIAMENT THAT ITALY\'S AREA OF

RESPONSIBILITY WILL BE IN THE SOUTHERN BRITISH SECTOR. THE

ITALIAN COMMANDER, WHO WILL BE BASED IN BASRA AT THE BRITISH

DIVISIONAL COMMAND, WILL ENSURE THAT ITALIAN TROOPS ARE USED

EXCLUSIVELY TO IMPLEMENT THE ASSIGNED MISSION APPROVED BY THE

ITALIAN GOVERNMENT, WHICH WILL HAVE DISCRETION TO CHOOSE

EXCEPTIONS OR DIFFERENT USES. HE SAID THE TROOPS WILL BE

SUBJECT TO THE ITALIAN MILITARY CODE OF WAR AS IT WAS

RECENTLY AMENDED AND ASSURED PARLIAMENT THAT THEY WILL

EXERCISE THE LOWEST POSSIBLE USE OF FORCE, IN COMPLIANCE WITH

INTERNATIONAL LAW AND ITALIAN LAWS AND REGULATIONS. THE

TROOPS WILL BE UNDER THE OPERATIONAL COMMAND OF THE ITALIAN

CHIEF OF DEFENSE STAFF, "BUT THE BRITISH DIVISIONAL COMMAND

HEADQUARTERED IN BASRA WILL HAVE OPERATIONAL CONTROL OF THE

INTERNATIONAL CONTINGENTS, INCLUDING THE ITALIANS, IN THE

ENTIRE SOUTHERN SECTOR."

MARTINO EXPLAINS TASKS...

-------------------------

4. MARTINO CONFIRMED FM FRATTINI,S APRIL 15 STATEMENTS TO

PARLIAMENT, NOTING THAT THE ITALIAN CONTINGENT WILL BE TASKED

WITH CREATING AND MAINTAINING A SECURE ENVIRONMENT,

CONTRIBUTING TO PUBLIC ORDER AND MILITARY POLICE UNITS,

SUPPORTING DEMINING ACTIVITIES, DETECTING BIOLOGICAL AND

CHEMICAL SUBSTANCES, PROVIDING HEALTH ASSISTANCE, PROVIDING

AIRPORT MANAGEMENT CAPABILITIES, SUPPORTING OHRA ACTIVITIES,

AND RESTORING INFRASTRUCTURE. THE TROOPS WILL HAVE TO ENGAGE

IN RECONNAISSANCE MISSIONS AND PATROLS, BUT THEIR PRESENCE ON

THE GROUND "WILL BE AS DISCREET AS POSSIBLE" AND THE

"INTERVENTION AND IMMEDIATE RESPONSE TO POSSIBLE SITUATIONS

OF DANGER WILL BE GUIDED BY THE INTELLIGENCE SERVICES AND

WILL BE BASED ON THE CONTINGENT\'S HIGH MOBILITY, FLEXIBILITY,

AND DYNAMISM, AS WELL AS ON HELICOPTERS."
