SUBJECT: UAEG WILL CONSIDER MULTILATERAL OPEN SKIES REGIME

REF: STATE 25364

1. (U) Classified by DCM Richard Albright for reasons

1.5 (b) and (d).

2. (C) Econchief delivered reftel demarche on the benefits

of joining a multilateral open skies aviation regime on

2/22 to UAE Ministry of Communications Assistant

Undersecretary for Civil Aviation Mohamed Yahya

Al-Suweidi. Al-Suweidi responded, "at first glance, it is

not a bad idea," but said he must study further the

information provided before taking a position. (Comment:

The UAEG has signed, or is in the process of signing,

bilateral open skies agreements with nearly every country

listed in reftel as a party to the multilateral regime,

which may limit the perception of the benefits of

joining. End comment.)

3. (C) Al-Suweidi is aware that the multilateral open

skies regime may be a topic at the next U.S.-GCC Economic

Dialogue, and said he will be prepared to discuss it with

others in the GCC beforehand.
