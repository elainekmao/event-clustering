SUBJECT: THE NEST OF EVIL: SERBS INTENSIFY ATROCITIES IN

REF: ZAGREB 0572

-------

SUMMARY

-------

1. THE USE OF EXTREME VIOLENCE AGAINST NON-SERBS REMAINING

IN THE BANJA LUKA AREA OF BOSNIA-HERZEGOVINA HAS

ACCELERATED IN RECENT WEEKS. RECENT ARRIVALS FROM THE

REGION HAVE PROVIDED NUMEROUS EYEWITNESS ACCOUNTS OF

EGREGIOUS EXAMPLES OF ETHNIC CLEANSING OF THE NON-SERBIAN

POPULATION. THESE INCLUDE WIDESPREAD KILLINGS, RAPES,

BEATINGS, DESTRUCTION OF HOMES, ROBBERIES, HARASSMENT, AND

FORCED FIRINGS DESIGNED TO EXPEL ALL NON-SERBS FROM THE

AREA. END SUMMARY.

-

----------

BACKGROUND

----------

2. REFCOORD AND POLOFF VISITED GASINCI REFUGEE COLLECTIVE

CENTER IN EASTERN CROATIA ON MARCH 23 TO INTERVIEW RECENT

ARRIVALS FROM BANJA LUKA, A CITY IN NORTH-CENTRAL BOSNIA

THAT HAS SEEN TWO YEARS OF SYSTEMATIC ATROCITIES DIRECTED

AT NON-SERBS. BY ALL ACCOUNTS, THIS HARASSMENT HAS

INTENSIFIED SUBSTANTIALLY DURING THE PAST FEW MONTHS.

3. AT GASINCI ALONE, REFUGEES FROM BANJA LUKA ARRIVE AT

THE RATE OF APPROXIMATELY 50 PER WEEK VIA CONVOYS UNDER

THE AUSPICES OF THE INTERNATIONAL COMMITTEE OF THE RED

CROSS. MANY ARRIVE IN TATTERED CLOTHING AND CLEARLY

SHOWING EVIDENCE OF EXTREME STRESS. DESPITE THE SEVERITY

AND RECENCY OF THEIR TRAUMA, MOST WERE WILLING OPENLY TO

DESCRIBE THE HORRORS THEY HAD EXPERIENCED OR WITNESSED.

THE EXCEPTION WAS ONE MAN WHO CAREFULLY LIMITED HIS

REMARKS AS HE FEARED RETRIBUTION BY THE "SERBIAN MAFIA,"

EVEN IF HE WERE RESETTLED OVERSEAS.

------------------

A LITANY OF CRIMES

------------------

4. REFUGEES RECOUNTED THE FOLLOWING EYEWITNESS EXAMPLES OF

ABUSE:

A) TWO MUSLIM BROTHERS WERE MERCILESSLY BEATEN BY

UNIFORMED SOLDIERS WITH BATS FASHIONED FROM WOODEN PLANKS.

THEIR ASSAILANTS WERE KNOWN TO THEM; THEY HAD FORMERLY

LIFTED WEIGHTS AND PLAYED SOCCER TOGETHER.

B) A GYPSY WAS BEATEN WITH RIFLE BUTTS FOLLOWING AN

ID CARD CHECK WHICH REVEALED THAT HE WAS NOT A SERB.

UNCLASSIFIED

UNCLASSIFIED

C) AN ELDERLY WOMAN FROM THE VILLAGE OF SEHER

RECOUNTED THREE CONSECUTIVE NIGHTS OF HORROR. THE FIRST

NIGHT A GRENADE WAS THROWN IN FRONT OF A HOUSE. THE NEXT

NIGHT FIVE MEN IN MILITARY UNIFORMS TORTURED A NEIGHBOR IN

HIS HOUSE FOR FOUR HOURS, CUTTING OFF BOTH HIS EARS. THE

THIRD NIGHT APPROXIMATELY TEN SOLDIERS CAME TO ANOTHER

FAMILY IN THE NEIGHBORHOOD, RAPED AND TORTURED THE THREE

DAUGHTERS, AND BEAT THE FATHER TO DEATH. THE THREE

DAUGHTERS ARE REPORTED TO HAVE DIED LATER IN THE HOSPITAL.

-

D) TEENAGE GIRLS WERE ADVISED BY SERBIAN FRIENDS

THAT THEIR NAMES WERE ON LISTS OF THOSE TO BE RAPED.

THESE FRIENDS THEN SHELTERED THEM IN THEIR HOMES FOR A FEW

DAYS TO PROTECT THEM.

E) IN ONE SECTION OF BANJA LUKA, CROATIAN HOUSES

WERE BLOWN UP EVERY EVENING AT THE SAME HOUR OVER A PERIOD

OF SEVERAL MONTHS. THIS WAS SO RELIABLE A PRACTICE THAT

SOME WENT OUTSIDE AT THIS HOUR TO SEE WHOSE HOUSE WOULD BE

DESTROYED THAT EVENING.

SWEDEN. HER ASSAILANTS WERE, THEY SAID, INCENSED THAT

THEY HAD BEEN DENIED AN OPPORTUNITY TO TORTURE OR KILL HIM

FIRST.
