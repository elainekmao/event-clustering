SUBJECT: UN SANCTIONS: DEMARCHE ON JORDANIAN REQUEST TO ADD

TO 1267 COMMITTEE LIST

REF: A. BERRY/SUTPHIN E-MAILS

B. SECSTATE 149439

Classified By: PolCouns Doug Silliman for reasons 1.5 (B) and (D)

1. (C) Following up on earlier Embassy approaches to the

GOJ (ref a), PolCouns on August 6 covered ref b points on

Jordan\'s request to add 9 names to the 1267 Committee List

with Ali al-Ayed, Personal Assistant to Foreign Minister

Muasher. PolCouns explained that the USG would like Jordan

to submit additional identifying information for each

individual on the list, as well as a summary of the reasons

why the GOJ believes they are members of al-Qaeda. He asked

that the Jordanian Mission ask that the 1267 Committee not

circulate the current list, then re-submit a more complete

list to the 1267 Committee after consulting with the USG.

2. (C) Ayed took the points and promised to pass them on to

the Jordanian Mission in New York. He also agreed that

informal GOJ-USG consultation on the appropriate information

to add to the 1267 request was a good idea.

3. (C) COMMENT: Post greatly appreciates the background

information provided in ref b.
