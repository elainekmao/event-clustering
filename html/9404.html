E.O. 12958: DECL: 07-15-13

TAGS: PREL, PGOV, CE, IZ, External Relations

SUBJECT: Iraq: Dipnote announces imminent suspension

of Embassy operations

Ref: Colombo 1214

(U) Classified by Donald A. Camp, Charge d\'Affaires.

Reasons 1.5 (b, d).

1. (U) This is an Action Request -- Please see

Para three.

2. (U) Post received a circular diplomatic note on

July 15 from the Iraqi Embassy to Sri Lanka stating that

it was suspending operations "until further notice" on

August 1. The dipnote states that this action is being

taken "upon the instructions of the American Civil

Administration."

3. (C) ACTION REQUEST: As requested Reftel, please

provide us response to two requests from Sri Lankan

Foreign Secretary on background and rationale for this

action - GSL has expressed concerns that the closure of

the Iraqi Embassy will have a detrimental effect on Sri

Lanka\'s trade relationship with Iraq. Mission would

like to be responsive in addressing GSL questions on

this matter. END ACTION REQUEST.

CAMP
