SUBJECT: NIGERIA: CENTRAL BANK WILL ACT ON NINTH LIST

REF: STATE 36362

EconOff delivered reftel information February 27 to Central

Bank Deputy Governor Ernest Ebi, who is responsible for

International Operations. Ebi responded March 1 that the CBN

would add the list of names per reftel to its terrorist watch

list, which is sent to all domestic banking institutions.

Banks are required to report to the CBN if accounts

associated with persons on the watch list are identified. To

date, no banks have identified accounts held by names on the

list.
