TO SARAJEVO WAS A "ONE-OFF DEAL", AND THAT THIS

THE DELIVERY OF FUEL THROUGH FEDERATION-HELD

TERRITORY.

POLICY IN MOSTAR ON ILLEGAL CROSSINGS

-------------------------------------

6. DURING AN EU MEETING IN MOSTAR ON JAN 21, AN

AGREEMENT WAS SIGNED BY BOTH THE CROATIAN AND

BOSNIAC SIDES OF MOSTAR REGARDING THE HANDLING OF

PEOPLE WHO ILLEGALLY CROSS THE CONFRONTATION LINE

BETWEEN EAST AND WEST MOSTAR. THE NEW POLICY

DIFFERENTIATES BETWEEN WOMEN, CHILDREN AND MEN

WHO ARE NOT OF MILITARY AGE, AND MEN OF MILITARY

AGE AND SOLDIERS. FOR THOSE IN THE FORMER

CATEGORY THE FOLLOWING PROCEDURE IS TO BE

FOLLOWED: A) AFTER ARREST THE WEU POLICE WILL BE

INFORMED. IF NO OTHER CRIMINAL ACT HAS BEEN

COMMITTED, THE PERSON MAY ONLY BE HELD FOR UP TO

2 HOURS, AND THEN MUST BE TAKEN IMMEDIATELY TO

THEIR OWN SIDE; B) IF THE ARRESTED PERSON HAS

COMMITTED ANY OTHER CRIMINAL ACT, THE WEU POLICE

WILL BE INFORMED AND ALLOWED TO MONITOR THE

SUBSEQUENT LEGAL PROCEEDINGS. THE DETAINED

VODOVOD TAKES OVER OPERATION OF LOWER WATER PLANT

--------------------------------------------- ----

8. ON JAN 27, THE PROTOCOL WAS SIGNED THAT

TRANSFERRED THE LOWER SITE OF THE IRC (SOROS-

FUNDED) WATER TREATMENT PLANT TO SARAJEVO\'S WATER

UPDATE ON WFP FOOD PIPELINE

---------------------------

9. WFP ANNOUNCED THAT MANY OF THE MAIN

WAREHOUSES IN B-H ARE EXPERIENCING A "HAND TO

MOUTH" DISTRIBUTION PROBLEM. FORTUNATELY, THE

COVER SHORTAGES IN THE HI-QUALITY FOODS INCLUDING

SUGAR, MEAT, FISH, AND CHEESE. WFP REPORTED THAT

FOOD STOCKS SHOULD BE SUFFICIENT FOR B-H THROUGH

MARCH AND APRIL. HOWEVER, WFP IS CONCERNED ABOUT

THE LACK OF PLEDGES FROM MAY ON. IMMEDIATE
