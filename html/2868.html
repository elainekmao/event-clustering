SUBJECT: DEMARCHE ON BOYCOTT OF U.S. AND ISRAELI PRODUCTS

DELIVERED TO JORDAN

REF: (A) STATE 84703 (B) AMMAN 2093

1. (SBU) On May 6, Poloff conveyed reftel points on the

boycott issue to Ali Al-Ayed of Foreign Minister Muasher\'s

Private Office. Al-Ayed offered no substantive comment but

promised to convey the message to FM Muasher who is currently

in Washington with King Abdullah and other senior members of

the GOJ.

2. (SBU) Comment: Post continues to support business

community efforts to find appropriate responses to popular

calls to boycott U.S. products and services (ref b). The

local Amercian Chamber of Commerce is working quietly to

ensure that opinion-makers in the media and elsewhere are

aware of the potential costs of a boycott to Jordan. The GOJ

fully recognizes the potential negative consequences an

anti-American boycott could have and will do what it can to

ensure that one does not materialize. The Jordanians are

hoping for progress on the political front that would defuse

the calls for a boycott and other steps promoted by

anti-normalizers.
