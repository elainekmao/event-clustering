SUBJECT: UAEG UP-TO-DATE ON PALESTINIAN AID COMMITMENT

Ref: SECSTATE 181425

1. (U) Classified by Ambassador Marcelle M. Wahba for reasons

1.5 (b) and (d).

2. (C) During a meeting on June 25, the Ambassador delivered

Secretary Powell\'s oral message to MFA Minstate Shaykh Hamdan

SIPDIS

Bin Zayid, stressing the need for the UAEG to fulfil the

commitment it made at the 2002 Beirut Summit to provide the

Palestinian Authority budgetary support. The Ambassador noted

to Hamdan that our records reflect that the UAEG has thus far

paid 60 percent of its commitment as of June 1. Hamdan

undertook to review the payment schedule of the UAEG\'s pledge

and stated that his office would provide us with a report on its

status.

3. (C) On July 2, Hamdan\'s Deputy Office Director Khadim Al

Darei reported to Poloff that the UAEG has completely paid its

Beirut 2002 $21 million pledge to the Palestinian Authority. Al

Dharei noted that the UAEG pledged an additional $21 million to

the Palestinian Authority in Sharm Al Sheikh in March 2003,

which the UAEG intends to pay in full by October 2003 as per the

terms of the pledge.

4. (C) COMMENT: We are confident that the UAEG will fulfill

its assistance to the Palestinian Authority as promised. These

official pledges reflect only a portion of assistance provided

to the Palestinian people. We note that President Zayed and the

UAE Red Crescent Authority have also made significant

contributions to the Occupied Territories and likely will

continue to do so in the future. END COMMENT.

WAHBA
