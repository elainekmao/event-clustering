SUBJECT: ITALIAN BOVINES POSITIVE TO

BOLDENONE HORMONE

1. MORE THAN 20,000 BOVINES, ALMOST

EXCLUSIVELY CALVES, WERE FOUND POSITIVE TO

BOLDENONE STEROID HORMONES AND SEIZED IN 50

CATTLE FARMS BY THE ANTI-ADULTERATION UNITS

OF ITALY\'S CARABINIERI.

2. THE INVESTIGATION WAS STARTED IN DECEMBER

2001 WITHIN THE NATIONAL RESIDUE CONTROL

PROGRAM CONDUCTED BY THE ITALIAN VETERINARIAN

SERVICE OF MINISTRY OF HEALTH. THE

OPERATION, WITH RAIDS AT FARMS,

FEED-PRODUCTION COMPANIES AND VETERINARY

PRACTICES IN SEVERAL NORTHERN ITALIAN

REGIONS, LOMBARDIA, PIEMONTE, VENETO, FRIULI,

EMILIA-ROMAGNA, STILL CONTINUES AND COULD

INVOLVE MORE ANIMAL AND PEOPLE.

ACCORDING TO THE ITALIAN VETERINARIAN

SERVICE, BOLDENONE WAS IMPORTED FROM THE U.S.

AND ILLEGALLY USED TO INCREASE THE SIZE OF

MUSCLES. ONCE TEST IS CONFIRMED POSITIVE,

ANIMALS WILL BE DESTROYED. CONFIRMATION

TESTS HAVE BEEN DONE FOR MANY ANIMALS AND

RESULTS WERE POSITIVE. HOWEVER, ANIMALS HAVE

BOLDENONE COULD ALSO BE NATURALLY PRESENT IN

ADMINISTRATIVE CRIME, THE CHARGE OF

CONSIDERED A CRIMINAL ACTION AND INVOLVED

4. NO CONNECTION EXISTS WITH AN HORMONE
