SUBJECT: NIGERIA: OBASANJO GRATEFUL FOR U.S. SUPPORT ON

IKEJA TRAGEDY

1. (U) Due to President Obasanjo\'s heavy schedule

prior to his February 26 early morning departure

(0530) for the Commonwealth Meeting in Australia,

Ambassador Jeter was unable to personally deliver

President Bush\'s letter announcing EOD assistance

with the clean-up in the aftermath of the Ikeja

cantonment explosion.

2. (U) The Ambassador did, however, deliver the letter

to Chief of Protocol Coker who promised to give it to

Obasanjo at the earliest possible moment. Later that

evening, the Ambassador received a phone call from

President Obasanjo\'s Political Advisor stating that

the President had seen the letter and wished to

express his deep gratitude for American support.

JETER
