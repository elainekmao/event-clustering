CLASSIFIED BY CHARGE D\'AFFAIRES EARL M. IRVING FOR

REASONS: 1.5 (B) AND (D).

1. (C) THE DRC JOINT POLITICAL COMMITTEE MEETING IS

TAKING PLACE IN HARARE TODAY, APRIL 4, ACCORDING TO

BERNARD TAKAVARASHA, THE UNDER SECRETARY FOR AFRICA IN

ZIMBABWE\'S MINISTRY OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS. AMONG THE

ATTENDEES ARE FOREIGN MINISTER MUDENGE AND ZIMBABWE\'S

SPECIAL ENVOY TO THE DRC DAVID HAMADZIRIPI. TAKAVARASHA

DID NOT KNOW THE MEETING\'S AGENDA, BUT SURMISED THAT IT

WOULD BE USED TO SET THE DATE FOR THE NEXT JMC MEETING.

THE UNDER SECRETARY WAS UNFAMILIAR WITH REPORTS THAT 300

ZIMBABWEAN SOLDIERS HAD BEEN WITHDRAWN FROM MBANDAKA.
