1. (C) Operation Northern Watch POLAD met with Iraqi Turkmen

Front (ITF) Ankara rep and Executive Committee member Dr.

Mustafa Ziya November 29. Ziya shared the text (see para 3)

of the opposition conference steering committee\'s response to

the ITF\'s complaint letter of November 5 (ref B). ziya noted

that the Kurdistan Democratic Party (KDP) and the Supreme

Council for Islamic Revolution in Iraq (SCIRI) continued to

oppose granting ITF requests. He said that the ITF had

agreed to participate in the London conference after ITF

Chairman Sanan Aga received a letter from four senior USG

official encouraging its participation. Ziya added that the

ITF now had only one demand - that the pro-KDP Turkmen cited

ref A not be counted against the 6 percent of the seats

reserved for Turkmen (which, with the addition of the

independents, is now closer to 4 percent, he claimed). "Let

them come as part of the KDP group, or as independents," he

said. Ziya hoped that, with USG help, this problem could be

solved. We stressed that the ITF should not expect USG

intervention on this point. Ziya then assured ONW POLAD that

ITF reps in London would continue to negotiate this with the

steering committee.

2. (C) Ziya told us that the KDP and SCIRI had a "secret"

agreement to support each other\'s positions at the

conference, including on the KDP\'s draft constitution. With

a total of 113 seats, Ziya said, the KDP and SCIRI would

drive the outcome. Ziya worried that if the KDP draft were

approved, the federal structure would separate 4 million

Sunnis from the south/center of Iraq, leaving the

southern/central federal unit with 80 percent Shi\'a. Ziya

also promised us an advanced copy of the ITF\'s presentation

for the conference, which he expected would win approval from

90 percent of the delegates.

3. (C) Text of Steering Committee Reply to ITF (As translated

Begin Text:

Preparation of the Iraqi Opposition Congress, London, 20/11/02

ITF Responsibilities

We received your letter on the Opposition Congress and we

analyzed your requests in detail during our meetings in a

positive manner. Please find below our suggestions on your

requests.

1. All documents, statements and participation of branches,

emerging after the opposition congress will be considered

seriously by the preparation committee.

2. Concerning your request on participating the preparation

activities; As you also know, the six person\'s group decided

on the August Washington meeting, not to enlarge the

preparation committee beyond their group and also to finance

the congress all by their own. The commission is a technical

committee whose activities will have an end immediately with

the start of the congress. The participation of any group

might be considered as an example for the others and our

former experiences have proved that large preparation

activities, participated by 20 groups or more are mostly

resulted nor practical neither beneficial.

3. The participation rates of the 1992 Salahadeen congress

have been taken essential to the participation of the

political, national and religious trends for the coming

congress. As there are no clear statistics about the

structure and population rates of the Iraqi community, the

determined participation rates might appear to be unfair.

All of us feel treated unjustly about this case. It is hard

to change these rates at the moment since a change in favor

of one group will affect the others. Actually the

participation rates will not affect the results of the

congress because the results will depend on political

conciliation, not on votes. all documents will be presented

as drafts, open to discussion.

4. In order to ensure your participation to this congress

which is the hope of all Iraqi people, the commission

increased the number of the Turkmen delegation to 15 persons,

including 8 persons from the ITF. This means a majority for

the Turkmen participation.

We expect you positive contribution to this very important

congress which is expected forwardly by the Iraqi community

and also the whole district.

The Preparation Committee

End Text.
