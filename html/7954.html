SUMMARY

-------

1. (C) ICTY ZAGREB RESREP THOMAS OSORIO TOLD US MAY 5 THAT

THE GOC HAS IMPROVED COOPERATION IN THE MONTH FOLLOWING THE

VISIT OF OTP CHIEF PROSECUTOR DEL PONTE TO ZAGREB. THE GOC

LARGELY HAS FULFILLED OUTSTANDING DOCUMENT REQUESTS AND

APPEARED SOMEWHAT MORE PROACTIVE IN THE GOTOVINA CASE -- IT

IS SET TO ISSUE A REWARD FOR HIS ARREST ON MAY 10. THE IVICA

RAJIC TRANSFER TO ICTY CUSTODY APPEARED TO BE MOVING AHEAD AS

WELL AS THE GOC INVESTIGATION AGAINST THOSE THAT SHELTERED

AND SUPPORTED HIM AND OTHER CROATIAN PIFWCS. THE ONLY BLACK

CLOUD FOR OSORIO WAS DEL PONTE\'S POOR HANDLING OF A ZAGREB

DIPLOMATIC CORPS REQUEST FOR AN "ICTY ISSUES CHECKLIST."

DESPITE OSORIO\'S WARNINGS, DEL PONTE CIRCULATED A

POORLY-CRAFTED DOCUMENT THAT -- IF LEAKED -- COULD CLOUD

GOC-ICTY RELATIONS. END SUMMARY

TO THE CROATIAN SUPREME COURT, BUT LEGAL EXPERTS TOLD US THE

APPEAL HAS NO CHANCE OF PROSPERING. RAJIC SHOULD BE IN ICTY

CUSTODY NO LATER THAN THE END OF MAY.

6. (C) OSTOJIC AND BAJIC TOLD US THAT THE GOC STILL IS

PIECING TOGETHER THE COMPLEX NETWORK ESTABLISHED BY THE

FORMER HDZ GOVERNMENT THAT PROTECTED IVICA RAJIC AND OTHER

ICTY PIFWCS. THEY PLAN TO INDICT ALL THOSE INVOLVED IN THE

ILLEGAL "OPERATION HAAG," CODE NAME FOR PROGRAM THAT

SHELTERED PIFWCS AND RAN INTELLIGENCE OPERATIONS AGAINST

ICTY, SFOR, AND OTHERS (REF A).

7. (C) OSTOJIC TOLD US THAT THE EFFORT TO PROTECT RAJIC WAS

MASTERFUL: WHEN HIS SPECIAL POLICE UNIT BEGAN ITS

INVESTIGATION IN MID-2002, THERE WAS NO RECORD IN THE GOC TO

INDICATE THAT AN IVICA RAJIC OFFICIALLY EVER EXISTED IN

CROATIA. CRITICALLY, ALL COPIES OF RAJIC\'S FINGERPRINT FILES

HAD BEEN DESTROYED. OSTOJIC SAID THAT THE POLICE WERE ABLE

TO GET A COPY OF RAJIC\'S FINGERPRINTS FROM BIH GOVERNMENT

AUTHORITIES WITHOUT TIPPING THEIR HAND TO THE ONGOING

INVESTIGATION. WITHOUT THOSE PRINTS, THEY NEVER WOULD HAVE

BEEN ABLE TO IDENTIFY CONCLUSIVELY THE PIFWC, WHO, WHEN

ARRESTED, WAS LIVING IN A SUBURB OF SPLIT UNDER THE NAME OF

JAKOV KOVAC.

SUBMISSION SHOULD BE SHORTER AND STRICTLY FACTUAL, OUTLINING

REQUESTS.

10. (C) OSORIO SAID HE SHARED OUR VIEW (AS DID THE LOCAL OSCE

MISSION, WHICH ALSO WAS GIVEN AN ADVANCE COPY OF THE

"NON-PAPER"), AND HELD OFF ON DELIVERING THE DOCUMENT TO THE

LOCAL EU TROIKA, DESPITE EXPLICIT INSTRUCTIONS FROM DEL

PONTE. HE TOLD US THAT ON APRIL 29 HE CALLED DEL PONTE TO

EXPRESS HIS RESERVATIONS. HE SAID THAT SHE ACKNOWLEDGED THAT

THE DOCUMENT WAS FLAWED, BUT ORDERED HIM TO GIVE IT TO THE

ZAGREB EU TROIKA AND THE U.S. EMBASSY ANYWAY; SHE PERSONALLY

HAD GIVEN THE DOCUMENT TO SOLANA, PATTEN AND PRODI THE

PREVIOUS DAY DURING A VISIT TO BRUSSELS. SHE TOLD OSORIO

"WE\'LL GET IT RIGHT NEXT TIME." OSORIO SAID THAT HE WAS

CONVINCED THAT THE GOC WOULD GET ITS HANDS ON THE DOCUMENT,

WHICH IT WOULD LEAK TO THE PRESS -- AT A TIME OF ITS CHOOSING

-- TO DEMONSTRATE THAT THE OTP IS NOT SINCERE IN ITS EFFORTS

TO BUILD A WORKING RELATIONSHIP WITH THE GOC
