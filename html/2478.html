A) THE ISTITUTO NAZIONALE PER IL COMMERCIO ESTERO, OR ICE (ITALIAN

TRADE COMMISSION) WEBSITE PERMITS ONLINE USERS TO REGISTER AT NO COST

FOR THEIR NEW EXPORT CLUB SERVICE. ONCE REGISTERED, MEMBERS ARE ABLE

TO CONSULT, ONLINE GRATIS, PRELIMINARY INFORMATION ON TRADE LEADS,

INTERNATIONAL TENDERS, INTERNATIONAL FINANCING OPPORTUNITIES, AND

OTHER SUBJECTS OF INTEREST, BUT MUST PAY 1.5 EUROS OR 3 CREDITS OFF

THEIR SERVICE CARDS (DISCUSSED IN PART D) TO VIEW COMPLETE CONTACT

INFORMATION. INFORMATION ON THE SITE IS UPDATED CONSTANTLY AS NEW

LEADS ARRIVE. MEMBERS MAY ALSO REQUEST REGULAR NOTIFICATION OF

TENDERS, INTERNATIONAL FINANCING, AND PREVIEWS OF MAJOR PROJECTS IN

SPECIFIC AREAS OF THE WORLD, IN PARTICULAR, INDUSTRY SECTORS TO BE

SENT TO THEIR E-MAIL ADDRESS AS THEY BECOME KNOWN OR AS AN ANNUAL

SUBSCRIPTION. THE FEE SCHEDULE IS AS FOLLOWS: UP TO 10

COUNTRIES/INDUSTRY SECTORS, 50 EUROS (USD 45); FROM 10 TO 30

COUNTRIES/INDUSTRY SECTORS, 100 EUROS (USD 90); AND OVER 30

COMPANIES/INDUSTRY

SECTORS, 155 EUROS (USD 138). IT SHOULD BE NOTED THAT ICE\'S FOCUS IS

ON SMALL- AND MEDIUM-SIZED ENTERPRISES (SMES). THUS, IT MAY DEAL WIT

MAJOR PROJECTS ONLY AS THEY RELATE TO SUBCONTRACTING.

PRIOR TO OCTOBER 2001, COMPLETE INFORMATION WAS AVAILABLE TO ANYONE

WHO CONSULTED THE WEBSITE. BECAUSE THE SERVICE IS NOW ORGANIZED IN A

DIFFERENT MANNER, IT IS NOT YET CLEAR HOW SUCCESSFUL THE INITIATIVE

IS. ICE IS ABLE TO TRACK THE NUMBER OF VISITS TO THE SITE, HOWEVER,

AS WELL AS THE NUMBER OF COMPANIES THAT REGISTER FOR EACH LEAD OR

TENDER. RESULTS WILL PROBABLY BE MADE PUBLIC IN THE FUTURE.

B) THE SOCIETA ITALIANA PER LE IMPRESE ALL\'ESTERO, OR SIMEST (ITALIAN

SOCIETY FOR OVERSEAS FIRMS), A SEPARATE ENTITY FROM ICE, IS THE

ITALIAN FINANCIAL INSTITUTION FOR THE DEVELOPMENT AND PROMOTION OF

ITALIAN BUSINESS ABROAD, IS A LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANY CONTROLLED BY

THE MINISTRY FOR FOREIGN TRADE. ITS SHAREHOLDERS INCLUDE BANKS,

BUSINESSES AND TRADE ASSOCIATIONS.

UNDER A MARKET PENETRATION PROGRAM, SIMEST ASSISTS ITALIAN COMPANIES

IN ESTABLISHING A PERMANENT PRESENCE AND PROMOTES MARKETING ACTIVITIE

IN NON-EU MARKETS. FINANCING IS AVAILABLE FOR PERMANENT FACILITIES

SUCH AS REPRESENTATIVE OFFICES, SALES OUTLETS AND NETWORKS, CUSTOMER

ASSISTANCE CENTERS, WAREHOUSES, DEPOSITS, AND SHOWROOMS. PROGRAMS FO

THE STRENGTHENING OF EXISTING STRUCTURES ARE ALSO ELIGIBLE. PROJECT

MUST BE IMPLEMENTED WITHIN TWO YEARS.

TYPES OF EXPENSES THAT MAY BE COVERED INCLUDE SETTING UP AND

MAINTAINING REPRESENTATIVE OFFICES, CONDUCTING MARKET RESEARCH,

PROMOTIONS, ADVERTISING AND DEMONSTRATIONS, AND CUSTOMER SERVICE

ACTIVITIES. IN CASES WHERE FINANCING IS REQUESTED TO IMPROVE OR

ENLARGE EXISTING FACILITIES, THE PROJECT IS ELIGIBLE WHERE THERE IS A

SUBSTANTIAL INCREASE IN COST FOR PERMANENT STRUCTURES AND/OR NEW

STAFF.

FINANCING COVERS UP TO 85 PERCENT OF THE COMPANY\'S PROJECT EXPENSES,

MILLION EUROS (ABOUT USD 1.8 MILLION), OR 3 MILLION EUROS IN THE CASE

OF A CONSORTIUM OF SMES THAT IS IMPLEMENTING THE PROJECT DIRECTLY.

THE REPAYMENT PERIOD MUST NOT EXCEED SEVEN YEARS FROM THE DATE THE

LOAN CONTRACT IS SIGNED. COMPANIES THAT HAVE BEEN APPROVED FOR

FINANCING HAVE 2 YEARS TO MEET THEIR PROPOSAL TARGET AND PAY ONLY

INTEREST DURING THAT TIME.

C) ORIGINALLY, THERE WAS AN ICE SUBSCRIPTION SERVICE FOR 400.000

ITALIAN LIRE (ABOUT USD 200) THAT ENTITLED SUBSCRIBERS TO A 30 PERCEN

DISCOUNT ON ALL SERVICES. THERE WAS NO CHARGE FOR REPORTS, STUDIES

AND STATISTICS, ONLY FOR ASSISTED SERVICES.

THE ARE TWO TYPES OF ASSISTED SERVICES: SPECIALIZED AND PERSONALIZED

ALTHOUGH THESE NAMES ARE CONSIDERED AMBIGUOUS AND WILL PROBABLY CHANG

IN THE FUTURE. SPECIALIZED SERVICES ARE DOCUMENTS, STUDIES, AND

CONSULTATIONS THAT ARE SOLD AT FIXED PRICES. PERSONALIZED SERVICE

PRICES ARE ESTIMATED BASED ON PERSONNEL-HOURS AND COUNTRY.

ICE HAS DEVELOPED A LIST OF CITIES WHERE IT HAS OFFICES, DEFINING

THESE CITIES AS BEING IN DEVELOPING, NEWLY INDUSTRIALIZED, AND

INDUSTRIALIZED COUNTRIES. WHEN ICE WAS QUESTIONED ON HOW THE CRITERI

FOR THESE CATEGORIES WERE DETERMINED, IT INDICATED THAT THERE WAS NO

HARD AND FAST RULE. SOME OF THESE ARE FAIRLY OBVIOUS, BUT OTHERS SEE

RATHER ARBITRARY. MONTREAL AND AMSTERDAM ARE ON THE "NEWLY

INDUSTRIALIZED LIST," FOR EXAMPLE. IT SEEMS THAT, BECAUSE THE LISTS

ARE USED TO CALCULATE COSTS, COST OF LIVING IS THE BOTTOM LINE. COST

PER PERSON/DAY FOR ICE SERVICES IS AS FOLLOWS: IN DEVELOPING

COUNTRIES, 260 EUROS (ABOUT 260 USD); IN NEWLY INDUSTRIALIZED

COUNTRIES, 300 EUROS (ABOUT 300 USD); AND IN INDUSTRIALIZED COUNTRIES

360 EUROS (ABOUT USD). SERVICES IN COUNTRIES NOT ON THE LIST HAVE TH

SAME COST AS THEIR SO-CALLED COUNTRY OF JURISDICTION.

THE DOWNSIDE OF CHARGING FOR PRODUCTS THAT ONCE WERE FREE OF CHARGE,

ICE OFFICIALS SAY, IS THE DECREASE IN DEMAND FOR THESE PRODUCTS.

HOWEVER, THESE PRODUCTS ARE VIEWED AS A NEW SOURCE OF REVENUE, WHICH,

IT IS HOPED, WILL INCREASE OVER TIME. BECAUSE THE PROGRAM IS STILL

QUITE NEW, IT IS DIFFICULT TO GAUGE SUCCESS AT THIS TIME.

ICE IS NOT ENGAGED IN SERIOUS COST-RECOVERY (ALTHOUGH IT HAS TAKEN

D) THE ICE PRE-PAID "SERVICE CARD" IS AVAILABLE IN THREE CATEGORIES

AND IS VALID FOR ONE YEAR FROM THE DATE OF ISSUE. EACH LEVEL IS WORT

A CERTAIN NUMBER OF POINTS THAT CAN BE USED TO PURCHASE ON-LINE

SERVICES (TRADE LEADS, INFORMATION ON TENDERS, PURCHASE OF STUDIES,

DOCUMENTS, AND STATISTICS) AND OFFERS DISCOUNTS ON PERSONALIZED

SERVICES AT ANY OF THE ICE OFFICES THROUGHOUT THE WORLD. THE BASIC

LEVEL IS WORTH 250 POINTS, OFFERS A 10 PERCENT DISCOUNT, AND COSTS 13

EUROS (ABOUT USD 125). SUPER IS WORTH 500 POINTS, OFFERS A 15 PERCEN

DISCOUNT, AND COSTS 230 EUROS (ABOUT USD 220). GLOBAL IS WORTH 1000

POINTS, OFFERS A 25 PERCENT DISCOUNT, AND COSTS 400 EUROS (ABOUT USD

380).

CARDS CAN BE OBTAINED ONLINE USING A CREDIT CARD OR BY MAILING OR

FAXING THE REQUEST FORM TO ICE\'S OFFICE. CUSTOMERS CAN PURCHASE

DOCUMENTS AND REPORTS ONLINE OR MAY REQUEST BOUND COPIES OF THESE

MATERIALS FROM ICE FOR A HIGHER PRICE. THE PROGRAM IS STILL NEW

(INITIATED IN OCTOBER 2001), BUT IT SEEMS THAT FORMER SUBSCRIPTION

HOLDERS ARE BUYING THE CARDS. ICE WILL HAVE A BETTER IDEA IN THE

FUTURE AS TO WHETHER THE NEW PROGRAM HAS DRAWN NEW CUSTOMERS.
