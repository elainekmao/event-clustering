SUBJECT: Mission conveys points on strengthening the

NPT in wake of North Korea\'s decision to withdraw

Ref: State 42535

1. (U) This message is Sensitive but Unclassified.

2. (SBU) Per Reftel, Mission has conveyed the points on

strengthening the Treaty on the Nonproliferation of

Nuclear Weapons (NPT) to the Sri Lankan Government in

light of North Korea\'s decision to withdraw from the

treaty. The GSL has not yet responded to the points.
