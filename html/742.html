2. SAKIC RUMOR: (FOR CHRIS HOH FROM RICK HOLTZAPPLE) I

HAVE NOT BEEN ABLE TO REACH CICAK TO SEE WHAT HE HAS

HEARD, BUT I ASKED SLAVKO GOLDSTEIN (A MEMBER OF THE

CROATIAN HELSINKI COMMITTEE\'S BOARD, A CLOSE ASSOCIATE OF

CICAK, AND A PROMINENT MEMBER OF THE ZAGREB JEWISH

COMMUNITY) IF HE HAD HEARD ANY RUMORS THAT SAKIC HAD

SURFACED IN CROATIA. GOLDSTEIN SAID HE HAD NOT HEARD ANY

SUCH RUMORS, AND WOULD CERTAINLY HAVE HEARD THEM IF THEY

WERE CIRCULATING IN ZAGREB. (NOTE: IF CICAK WERE

SPREADING THESE RUMORS, GOLDSTEIN WOULD ALMOST CERTAINLY

HAVE HEARD THEM.)

3. GOLDSTEIN, AND OTHERS I ASKED WHO FOLLOW THE PRESS

CLOSELY, RECALLED THAT SAKIC GAVE AN INTERVIEW IN BUENOS

AIRES TO A LOCAL JOURNALIST WHO ACCOMPANIED TUDJMAN ON HIS

TRIP TO ARGENTINA A FEW MONTHS BACK. (I GATHER SAKIC IS

NOT EXACTLY IN HIDING IN ARGENTINA; HE CLAIMS HE HAS

POWERFUL FRIENDS IN THE ARMY AND LEGISLATURE.) IN THAT

INTERVIEW, SAKIC SAID THAT HE WOULD LIKE TO VISIT CROATIA,

BUT THAT HE KNEW IT WOULD BE AN IMPOLITIC THING TO DO,

BECAUSE OF HIS PAST. BUT SAKIC SAID, "I HOPE THAT THE

BEGINNING OF NEXT YEAR I WILL BE ABLE TO COME AND STAY.

WE WILL SEE." GOLDSTEIN SAID HE ALSO VAGUELY RECALLED A

SUBSEQUENT PRESS REPORT OF AN (UNCONFIRMED) COMMENT BY

CROATIAN HEALTH MINISTER HEBRANG THAT THERE WAS NO REASON

SAKIC SHOULD NOT TRAVEL TO CROATIA, SINCE HE HAD NEVER

BEEN CONVICTED OF WAR CRIMES. BUT GOLDSTEIN SAID THAT

THIS COMMENT WAS AN UNOFFICIAL STATEMENT, AND HE WAS NOT

EVEN SURE HEBRANG HAD REALLY SAID IT. (NOTE: HEBRANG\'S

FATHER, A LEADING PARTISAN, WAS PART OF A PRISONER

EXCHANGE ORGANIZED BY SAKIC IN WWII.)

4. OUR FBIS FSN ALSO TOLD ME THAT HE THOUGHT TANJUG HAD

RECENTLY REPORTED THAT SAKIC HAD COME TO ZAGREB. POSSIBLY

THAT IS THE SOURCE OF THE RUMOR THAT SAKIC HAS NOW COME TO

ZAGREB.

5. BY THE WAY, IT IS MY UNDERSTANDING THAT THE COMMANDANT

OF JASENOVAC WAS A MAN NAMED MAX LUBURIC (WHOSE SISTER IS

MARRIED TO SAKIC), AND SAKIC WAS THE CAMP\'S DEPUTY

COMMANDER (AND CHIEF EXECUTIONER).
