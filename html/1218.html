SUBJECT: ZIMBABWE\'S NEW CABINET: MUGABE REARRANGES THE

DECK CHAIRS ON THE TITANIC

CLASSIFIED BY CHARGE D\'AFFAIRES EARL M. IRVING FOR

REASONS 1.5 (B) AND (D).

-------

SUMMARY

-------

1. (C) PRESIDENT MUGABE ANNOUNCED HIS NEW 19 MEMBER

(DOWN FROM 27 MEMBERS) CABINET CONSISTING OF 19 MEMBER

ON JULY 15. A NUMBER OF THE OLD GUARD, INCLUDING WOMEN,

WERE JETTISONED IN FAVOR OF TECHNOCRATS AND PARTY

LOYALISTS. OF PARTICULAR NOTE IS THE FACT THAT THE

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 HARARE 03910 01 OF 04 171528Z

RUTHLESS FORMER MINISTER OF JUSTICE, LEGAL AND

PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS EMMERSON MNANGAGWA GOT NO CABINET

POST, ALTHOUGH HE MAY GET THE NOD TO BE SPEAKER OF

PARLIAMENT WHERE HE WILL TAKE A HARD LINE AGAINST THE

OPPOSITION MOVEMENT FOR DEMOCRATIC CHANGE (MDC). THE

TWO KEY ECONOMIC MINISTERS, NKOSANA MOYO AND SIMBA

MAKONI, WILL BE ATTRACTIVE AMBASSADORS FOR ZIMBABWE TO

THE WORLD, ALTHOUGH THEIR MARGIN FOR MANEUVER LIKELY

WILL BE ABRIDGED BY PRESIDENT MUGABE. THE ONE FEMALE

MINISTER, JOYCE MUJURU, HAS LITTLE INTEREST IN WOMEN\'S

ISSUES AND PROBABLY OWES HER POSITION TO THE POWER

WIELDED BY HER HUSBAND, FORMER ARMY COMMANDER SOLOMON

MUJURU. THE SOCIAL MINISTRIES ARE IN THE HANDS OF WEAK

AND LESS THAN EFFECTIVE MINISTERS, WITH THE EXCEPTION OF

INFORMATION AND PUBLICITY MINISTER JONATHAN MOYO, WHOSE

OFFICE\'S PLACEMENT WITHIN THE PRESIDENCY GIVES IT A

DISTINCTLY IDEOLOGICAL CAST. THE SECURITY MINISTRIES

WENT TO TRUSTED MUGABE ALLIES. HOME AFFAIRS, THE LEAST

SENSITIVE OF THESE, WENT TO A NDEBELE, JOHN NKOMO, WHO

IS NOT VIEWED AS A CREDIBLE REPRESENTATIVE OF HIS ETHNIC

GROUP IN MATABELELAND. SOME HERE ARGUE THAT THIS MIXED

BAG IS A "PLACE HOLDER" CABINET, AWAITING THE RETURN OF

THE OLD GUARD IN 2002. BE THAT AS IT MAY, WE DO NOT SEE

IN ITS COMPOSITION A SUFFICIENT DEPARTURE FROM THE

PROBLEMS THAT HAVE PLAGUED THE MUGABE GOVERNMENT TO

DATE. END SUMMARY.

THE ECONOMIC MINISTRIES

-----------------------

6. (C) FOR PURPOSES OF ANALYSIS WE COULD DIVIDE THE

CABINET PORTFOLIO INTO THREE AREAS: ECONOMIC, SOCIAL AND

SECURITY. ON THE ECONOMIC SIDE WE HAVE A MIXED BAG.

FAILED BANKER (AND SON-IN-LAW OF THE LATE VICE PRESIDENT

JOSHUA NKOMO), FRANCIS NHEMA, HEADS UP THE ENVIRONMENT

AND TOURISM PORTFOLIO, WHICH MANY HERE EXPECTED WOULD GO

TO THE FORMER DEPUTY MINISTER OF MINES ENVIRONMENT AND

TOURISM EDWARD CHINDORI-CHININGA. NHEMA TAKES THE HELM

OF A MINISTRY THAT OFFERS GREAT POSSIBILITY FOR

CORRUPTION. AT THE SAME TIME, HE FACES AT LEAST ONE

CHARGE OF FRAUD PENDING AGAINST HIM IN THE COURTS.

RESPECTED BUSINESSMAN AND FORMER SADC SECRETARY GENERAL

THE SOCIAL MINISTRIES

---------------------

7. (C) THE SOCIAL SECTOR HAS A COLLECTION OF WEAK

MINISTERS WITH THE EXCEPTION OF JONATHAN MOYO, ZANU-PF\'S

SPLENETIC SPIN DOCTOR DURING THE FAILED CONSTITUTIONAL

REVIEW PROCESS AND RECENT PARLIAMENTARY ELECTION, WHO

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 HARARE 03910 03 OF 04 171529Z

HAS BEEN NAMED MINSTER OF STATE FOR INFORMATION AND

PUBLICITY. THE MINISTRY NORMALLY SHOULD HAVE TECHNICAL

REGULATORY OVERSIGHT OF COMMUNICATIONS IN THE COUNTRY,

BUT HAVING BEEN MOVED INTO THE PRESIDENT\'S OFFICE GIVES

THE PORTFOLIO AN IDEOLOGICAL CAST. OUR FEAR IS THAT NO

ONE WILL BE ABLE TO SILENCE HIM. HE MAY SHOOT DOWN ANY

GOOD ECONOMIC IDEAS THAT MINISTERS MAKONI AND MOYO MAY

HAVE AND COULD POISON RELATIONS WITH THE DONOR

COMMUNITY. TIMOTHY "KILLJOY" STAMPS WILL CONTINUE TO

PRESIDE OVER THE DECLINING MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND CHILD

WELFARE. IGNATIUS CHOMBO AS HEAD OF LOCAL GOVERNMENT,

PUBLIC WORKS AND NATIONAL HOUSING IS ALSO PERCEIVED AS A

NONENTITY. HERBERT MURERWA AS MINISTER OF HIGHER

EDUCATION AND TECHNOLOGY PROBABLY WILL CONTINUE IN HIS

PATTERN OF A DECENT BUT INEFFECTIVE CABINET MEMBER;

HOWEVER A MARGINALLY MORE EFFECTIVE MINISTER IN THIS

LESS POLITICAL PORTFOLIO. BORDER GEZI, AN OPPORTUNISTIC

LIGHTWEIGHT WHO LED FARM INVASIONS, THE POLITICAL

REEDUCATION EXERCISE, AND VOTER INTIMIDATION AS GOVERNOR

OF MASHONALAND CENTRAL PROVINCE, WAS REWARDED WITH THE

POST OF MINISTER OF YOUTH DEVELOPMENT, GENDER AND

EMPLOYMENT CREATION, A MINISTRY WITHOUT TEETH AND NOT

INVOLVED IN POLICY MAKING. THIS HAS BEEN THE MINISTRY

IN WHICH CIVIL SERVANTS ESSENTIALLY DID ZANU-PF PARTY

WORK AND COULD BE CALLED TO ACCOUNT BY THE MDC FOR HOW

IT SPENT ITS BUDGET ALLOTMENT. THE SOLE WOMAN APPOINTED

TO THE CABINET IS JOYCE MUJURU, RETAINING HER POST AS

MINISTER OF RURAL RESOURCES AND WATER DEVELOPMENT. THE

APPOINTMENT APPEARS TO BE A NOD TO HER POWERFUL HUSBAND,

FORMER ARMY COMMANDER SOLOMON MUJURU, CONSIDERED HERE AS

A KINGMAKER. MUJURU HAS SHOWN NO INTEREST IN PROMOTING

RIGHTS ACTIVISTS. FOREIGN MINISTER STANISLAUS MUDENGE,

WHO IS ARROGANT BUT NO SHINING STAR, ALSO RETAINED HIS

POSITION.

COMMENT

-------

9. (C) THE COMMENT WE HAVE HEARD MOST OFTEN ABOUT THE

NEW CABINET IS THAT IT IS A MIXED BAG. IT LOOKS

CREDIBLE, PARTICULARLY IN THE PERSONS OF SIMBA MAKONI

AND NKOSANA MOYO WHO WILL BE ATTRACTIVE AMBASSADORS FROM

ZIMBABWE TO THE WORLD. THERE IS TALENT IN KEY AREAS AND

PATRONAGE IN MANY OTHERS. NEVERTHELESS, THEY ARE ALL

TAKING ORDERS FROM PRESIDENT MUGABE, MEANING THAT WE

WILL SEE THE SAME RUINOUS POLICIES OVER THE NEXT COUPLE

OF YEARS. SOME OF OUR CONTACTS FEAR THAT THE NEW

CABINET IS COMPOSED OF "PLACE HOLDERS," PEOPLE WHO WILL

KEEP THE SEATS WARM UNTIL THE PRESIDENTIAL ELECTIONS OF

2002 AND THE POTENTIAL RETURN OF SOME OF THE OLD GUARD.

THE BOTTOM LINE IS THAT WE DO NOT SEE A SUFFICIENT

DEPARTURE IN THE NEW LINE-UP FROM THE PROBLEMS THAT HAVE

CHARACTERIZED THE GOVERNMENT TO DATE.
