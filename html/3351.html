SUBJECT: RESPONDING TO ISRAELI CHARGES ABOUT UNRWA: MESSAGE

DELIVERED TO DEPUTY UNRWA COMGEN KAREN ABUZAYD

REF: (A) STATE 140505 (B) JERUSALEM 2282

Classified By: DCM Gregory L. Berry. Reasons 1.5 (b,d).

1. (C) On July 23, A/Polcouns (in the absence from post of

Refcoord) conveyed ref A para 4 points to UNRWA Deputy

Commissioner General Karen AbuZayd. AbuZayd made careful

note of the points. Her only substantive comment was that

the message called for UNRWA to sever its ties to youth

centers in the West Bank only and not in Gaza. To do this

would pose fewer problems as the West Bank youth centers,

unlike those in Gaza, are not located in UNRWA facilities.
