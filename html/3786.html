SUBJECT: EMBASSY AMMAN\'S PARTICIPATION IN CAIRO CONFERENCE

ON ENGAGING ISLAM

REF: A. STATE 173741

B. CAIRO 9441

1. Embassy Amman requests country clearance for Political

Counselor Douglas A. Silliman and Public Affairs Officer

Haynes Mahoney to participate in the Conference on Engaging

Islam in Cairo October 2-3, 2002.

2. Mr. Silliman and Mr. Mahoney will arrive at Cairo

International Airport (CAI) on Tuesday, October 1 at 1130 on

Royal Jordanian flight 505. Please advise whether Embassy

will meet and assist or whether Mr. Silliman and Mr. Mahoney

will need to get a taxi in from the airport.

Mr. Silliman will depart CAI on Friday, October 4 at 1230 on

RJ flight 506.

Mr. Mahoney will depart CAI on Saturday, October 5 at 1230 on

RJ flight 506.

Please make hotel reservations for Mr. Silliman for the

nights of October 1-3. Please make hotel reservations for

Mr. Mahoney for the nights of October 1-4.

3. Embassy Amman will pay all expenses (travel and per diem)

for Mr. Silliman\'s travel, as well as the expenses for Mr.

Mahoney\'s travel and one additional day of per diem.

4. Mr. Silliman and Mr. Mahoney have Top Secret security

clearances.

5. Mr. Silliman and Mr. Mahoney do not/not wish to be

included in tour plans to the pyramids on the morning of

October 2.

GNEHM
