SUBJECT: HUMAN RIGHTS REPORT DELIVERED TO MFA

REF: A. SECSTATE 81443

B. AMMAN 1432

1. (U) Per instruction in reftel A, on March 31 PolCouns and

PolOff delivered the 2002 Country Report on Human Rights

Practices to Ali al-Ayedd, Assistant to the Foreign Minister.

PolCouns noted the report was less positive than the

previous year\'s, but thanked Ayed for the MFA\'s increased

dialogue on human rights issues during the past year, and

complimented its decision to establish a human rights

directorate (reftel B). Ayed said the MFA would review the

report thoroughly and follow up with us.
