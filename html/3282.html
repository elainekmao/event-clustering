SUBJECT: ITALY ON UNIFIL: REDUCE IT BUT DON\'T DISMANTLE IT

1. (C) POL OFF DISCUSSED THE GOI\'S ASSESSMENT OF UNIFIL WITH

GIUSEPPE PERRONE, THE MFA\'S DESK OFFICER FOR LEBANON AND

ISRAEL AND DEPUTY DIRECTOR FOR THE MIDDLE EAST OFFICE.

PERRONE SAID THAT THE GOI IS PLEASED WITH UNIFIL AND ITS

MISSION SO FAR. GIVEN THE WITHDRAWAL OF ISRAEL FROM LEBANON

IN 2000, ITALY AGREES WITH THE UNSC PLAN OF PROGRESSIVE

REDUCTION IN UNIFIL UNITS REACHING A FORCE OF 2,000 UNITS OF

ALL RANKS BY JULY 2002. WHEREAS THE GOI APPROVES OF A

REASONABLE REDUCTION IN UNIFIL FORCES TO REFLECT THE PRESENT

NEEDS, IT IS AGAINST THE DISMANTLING OF UNIFIL. UNIFIL

SHOULD CONTINUE TO MAINTAIN A SUFFICIENT FORCE NOT TO

JEOPARDIZE STABILITY IN THE REGION, STRESSED PERRONE.
