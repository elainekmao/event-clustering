SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION: MARCH 7 UN SECURITY COUNCIL

DELIBERATIONS, ISRAEL, AND THE OCCUPIED TERRITORIES

1. SUMMARY: DELIBERATIONS AT THE SECURITY COUNCIL AND RECENT

ESCALATIONS IN THE OCCUPIED TERRITORIES RECEIVED PROMINENT

COVERAGE IN LOCAL MEDIA. NEWS REPORTING AND EDITORIALS WIDELY

ASSERT THAT THE U.S. IS DETERMINED TO ATTACK IRAQ REGARDLESS OF

THE OUTCOME OF THE VOTING AT THE UN. AN "AL KHALEEJ" EDITORIAL

CLAIMED THAT THE U.S. IS PLANNING TO DIVIDE IRAQ, AND OTHER ARAB

COUNTRIES LATER, INTO ETHNIC AND SECTARIAN ENTITIES. OTHER

EDITORIALS CRITICIZED THE MILD U.S. REACTION TOWARDS ISRAELI

KILLINGS OF PALESTINIAN CIVILIANS. END SUMMARY.

2. LOCAL AND REGIONAL PRINT AND BROADCAST MEDIA FEATURED

COVERAGE AND REACTION TO RECENT REMARKS ON IRAQ BY SECRETARY

POWELL AT THE CENTER FOR STRATEGIC AND INTERNATIONAL STUDIES AND

PRESIDENT BUSH DURING THE MARCH 6 WHITE HOUSE PRESS CONFERENCE.

ABU DHABI TV, AL JAZEERA, THE MIDDLE EAST BROADCAST CENTER (MBC),

AND NEW ARABIC NEWS CHANNEL, AL ARABIYYA, CARRIED LIVE COVERAGE

OF THE MARCH 7TH UNSC DELIBERATIONS WITH COMMENTARY IN ADDITION

TO ANALYSIS OF SECRETARY POWELL\'S AND PRESIDENT BUSH\'S RECENT

REMARKS.

3. COMMENTING ON ALLEGED U.S. PLANS FOR IRAQ AFTER THE

ANTICIPATED ATTACKS, SHARJAH-BASED PAN-ARAB "AL KHALEEJ"

EDITORIALIZED ON 3/09:

"... AMERICAN OFFICIALS REVEALED THAT IRAQ WILL BE DIVIDED INTO

THREE AREAS: NORTHERN, MIDDLE, AND SOUTHERN. THESE WILL BE

UNDER THE SUPERVISION OF THREE AMERICANS: THE PREVIOUS AMERICAN

AMBASSADOR TO YEMEN, BARBARA BODINE, AND TWO RETIRED U.S.

GENERALS. THIS ... WILL ALSO DIVIDE IT INTO ETHNIC AND SECTARIAN

ENTITIES, WHICH WILL LEAD TO A CIVIL WAR THAT WILL DESTROY

WHATEVER IS LEFT OF IRAQ. THIS IS A SAMPLE OF WHAT AWAITS OTHER

ARAB COUNTRIES, AS IT IS A ZIONIST PLAN, DRAFTED FOR THE AMERICAN

STRATEGY IN THE MIDDLE EAST FOR THE PERIOD AFTER 9/11, IN ORDER

TO RESHAPE THE REGION GEOGRAPHICALLY AND POLITICALLY IN

ACCORDANCE WITH THE AMERICAN AND ISRAELI INTERESTS ..."

4. COMMENTING ON THE MARCH 7TH UN SECURITY COUNCIL MEETING, "AL

KHALEEJ" EDITORIALIZED ON 3/08:

"... FRANCE, RUSSIA, CHINA, GERMANY, AND ALL ARAB AND ISLAMIC

SUMMITS SAY: \'NO TO WAR.\' PEOPLE AROUND THE WORLD HAD AND ARE

STILL HAVING ANTI-WAR DEMONSTRATIONS. DESPITE ALL THIS, THE ONLY

THING THAT THE U.S. CAN THINK OF IS FABRICATING EXCUSES FOR ITS

ATTACKS AGAINST IRAQ. THE MEANING OF ALL THIS WAS SUMMARIZED BY

THE SYRIAN FOREIGN MINISTER WHEN HE SAID: \'IT\'S JUST ARMED

BURGLARY\' AND NOTHING ELSE. GEORGE BUSH\'S REMARKS (INDICATING)

NO REQUIREMENT FOR INTERNATIONAL AUTHORIZATION CONFIRM THIS. THE

BURGLARY BEGINS WITH THE ASSASSINATION OF INTERNATIONAL

LEGITIMACY, WHICH HAS ALWAYS BEEN THE CASE IN ARAB ISSUES. WHAT

IS HAPPENING IN PALESTINE AND WILL HAPPEN IN IRAQ ARE GOOD

EXAMPLES OF THAT."

5. UNDER THE HEADLINE \'BUSH\'S COWBOY MENTALITY,\' DUBAI-BASED

BUSINESS-ORIENTED "AL BAYAN" EDITORIALIZED ON 3/08:

"... PRESIDENT BUSH ANNOUNCED: \'IF WE NEED TO TAKE ANY ACTION

AGAINST IRAQ, WE DEFINITELY WILL, WITHOUT UN APPROVAL.\' THE

RIGHT TRANSLATION OF THIS IS: IF THE UN DOES NOT ABIDE BY OUR

ORDERS AND DOES NOT APPROVE THE KILLING OF IRAQ, THEN THERE IS NO

NEED FOR IT (THE UNITED NATIONS) AND EVERY MEMBER SHOULD JUST

LEAVE NEW YORK AND GO BACK TO WHERE S/HE CAME FROM ..."

6. COMMENTING ON THE U.S. SILENCE TOWARDS THE SITUATION IN

PALESTINE, ABU DHABI-BASED PAN-ARAB DAILY "AKHBAR AL ARAB"

EDITORIALIZED ON 3/08:

"... THE U.S. SILENCE TOWARDS THE CRIMES COMMITTED BY THE BUTCHER

SHARON AND THE GANG OF KILLERS AROUND HIM MUST BE CONSIDERED

COLLUSION. IN FACT, CLOSING THE EYE FROM THE BLOODSHED TAKING

PLACE IN PALESTINE REPRESENTS AN ACTUAL PARTICIPATION IN THE

CONTINUOUS CRIME AGAINST OUR BROTHERS IN OCCUPIED PALESTINE ..."

7. COMMENTING ON THE SUICIDE OPERATION IN HAIFA, "AL KHALEEJ"

EDITORIALIZED ON 3/07:

"... THE HEROIC OPERATION IN HAIFA PROVED WITH NO DOUBT THAT ALL

ISRAELI GAMBLES ARE ALWAYS DOOMED TO FAILURE AND THAT NO

COLONIALIST WILL BE SAFE OR SECURE AS LONG AS THE OCCUPATION AND

ATTACKS ARE STILL GOING ON ..."

8. DESCRIBING THE UAE INITIATIVE AS \'THE SAFE EXIT,\' ABU DHABI-

BASED SEMI-GOVERNMENT "AL ITTIHAD" EDITORIALIZED ON 3/07:

"... IN RESPECT TO THE GIVEN FACTS, THE UAE INITIATIVE IS THE

MOST PRACTICAL PROPOSAL AND PRECIOUS ADVISE THAT COULD SAVE FACE

FOR ALL PARTIES. IT REPRESENTS A SAFE EXIT, WHICH WILL RESCUE

EVERYONE FROM THE CRISIS. IT IS ALSO THE ONLY WAY TO REMOVE THE

SPECTER OF WAR AND DESTRUCTION FROM IRAQ."

9. COMMENTING ON THE SITUATION IN PALESTINE AND THE

INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY\'S ATTITUDE TOWARDS SUICIDE BOMBINGS, "AL

BAYAN" EDITORIALIZED ON 3/07:

"... IT WON\'T BE A SURPRISE IF WE ARE TOLD THAT THE BUTCHER

SHARON HAS ORDERED MORE MASSACRES AND MORE PALESTINIAN TRANSFERS

AND BANISHMENTS. ARABS\' PREOCCUPATION WITH THE IRAQI CRISIS

SHOULD NOT MAKE THEM INADVERTENT OF THE ISRAELI CRIMES. AN

UNIFIED ARAB MOVE TO PROTECT AND SAVE THE PALESTINIANS FROM DAILY

ANNIHILATION IS NEEDED."

10. COMPARING THE RESOLUTIONS OF THE RECENT OIC SUMMIT AND NAM

CONFERENCE, "AL KHALEEJ" EDITORIALIZED ON 3/06:

"... THE TIME HAS COME FOR THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY\'S

SEPARATION BETWEEN \'EVIL\', SYMBOLIZED BY U.S. AND ITS ALLIES, AND

\'GOOD\', SYMBOLIZED BY PROMOTERS OF PEACE SUCH AS FRANCE, RUSSIA,

GERMANY, AND THEIR ALLIES, IN ORDER TO CREATE A NEW MULTI-POLAR

WORLD. THE BATTLE IS STILL AT ITS BEGINNING, SO THE ARABS AND

MUSLIMS ARE REQUIRED TO HAVE A SAY IN ACTIONS AND NOT IN WORDS

ONLY."

11. CRITICIZING THE OIC SUMMIT\'S RESOLUTIONS, "AL ITTIHAD"

EDITORIALIZED ON 3/06:

"... THE DOHA SUMMIT FOLLOWED THE FOOTSTEPS OF ALL OTHER ARAB AND

ISLAMIC SUMMITS IN ANNOUNCING MANY ENTHUSIASTIC RESOLUTIONS

WITHOUT A SOLUTION FOR THE COMPLICATED AND DANGEROUS ISSUES THAT

MIGHT AFFECT THE ARAB AND ISLAMIC WORLD ..."
