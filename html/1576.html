1. (C) SUMMARY: THE HOLY SEE DOES NOT SHARE THE

POLITICAL VIEWS OF THE BISHOP OF MOSTAR AND OTHER

CROAT-NATIONALIST CLERGY IN B-H AND IT IS DOING THE

UTMOST WITHIN THE TRADITIONAL LIMITS OF THE CHURCH

TO CHANGE THEIR POLITICAL BEHAVIOR. OUR

INTERLOCUTORS BELIEVE THE CLERGY\'S VALID CONCERNS ON

BEHALF OF THEIR FLOCK WERE REFLECTED IN MARCH 2

LETTER CARDINAL PULJIC SENT PRESIDENT BUSH. FOR

NOW, THE HOLY SEE\'S EFFORTS TO EFFECT CHANGE IN THE

HERZEGOVINIAN CLERGY ARE FOCUSED ON THE NUNCIO IN

SARAJEVO. END SUMMARY.

2. (C) A/DCM FOLLOWED UP ON REF A EXCHANGE IN A

3/9 MEETING WITH VATICAN BALKANS DIRECTOR MSGR.

JOSEPH MARINO. IN THAT CONVERSATION, MARINO SAID

THAT HE HAD ALREADY HEARD ON THE ISSUE OF THE BISHOP

OF MOSTAR FROM SRSG JACQUES KLEIN 3/5. FRENCH AMB.

TO THE HOLY SEE DEJAMMET ALSO CALLED 2/8. MARINO

SAID THIS WAS CLEARLY HIS "ISSUE DU JOUR" AND THAT

HE HAD PREPARED A PAPER FOR FM TAURAN UPON THE

LATTER\'S RETURN FROM IRAN. MARINO TOOK A/DCM\'S

POINT THAT BISHOP PERIC HAD OVERSTEPPED IN CALLING

FOR A UNILATERAL REVISION OF DAYTON AND IN PUBLICLY

SUPPORTING CONVICTED WAR CRIMINALS, BUT HE OPINED

THAT THE CROATS HAVE BEEN SAYING FOR MANY MONTHS

THAT THEIR LEGITIMATE INTERESTS HAVE BEEN LARGELY

IGNORED BY THE I/C. WHEN A/DCM PRESSED FOR THE HOLY

SEE TO DO SOMETHING TO REIN-IN THE BISHOP, MARINO

EXPLAINED THAT THERE WAS VERY LITTLE THE HOLY SEE

COULD DO OTHER THAN PULL THE BISHOP ASIDE FOR A

QUIET TALK (NOTE: SEE PARAS 5-6 FOR FURTHER

BACKGROUND ON THE LIMITS FACED BY THE HOLY SEE IN

DEALING WITH POLITICAL MATTERS INSIDE THE CHURCH.

FYI: TO THE BEST OF OUR KNOWLEDGE, NEITHER PERIC NOR

3. (C) A/DCM FOLLOWED UP REF C INSTRUCTION ON 2/13,

MEETING AGAIN WITH MSGR. MARINO. AFTER HEARING THE

TALKING POINTS, MARINO ASSURED US THAT THE VATICAN

HAS HEARD THE SAME MESSAGE FROM A NUMBER OF SOURCES

AND THAT IT WILL DO EVERYTHING IN ITS POWER TO TRY

TO CHANGE THE HERZEGOVINA CLERGY\'S BEHAVIOR. HE

POINTED OUT THAT THE HOLY SEE IS ON RECORD

SUPPORTING A DEMOCRATIC AND MULTIETHNIC B-H, AND

THAT THIS POSITION HAS NOT CHANGED. HE SAID THAT

THE NUNCIO IS ACTIVELY ENGAGED UNDER INSTRUCTION

FROM THE HOLY SEE, HAS ALREADY SPOKEN WITH CARDINAL

PULJIC, AND WILL LIKELY SPEAK WITH BISHOP PERIC

SOON. HE NOTED THAT PULJIC HAD SENT A LETTER TO

PRESIDENT BUSH MARCH 2, AND THOUGHT THAT A HIGH-

LEVEL RESPONSE MIGHT BE A GOOD WAY FOR THE USG TO

REINFORCE THE NUNCIO\'S REPRESENTATIONS.

4. (C) A/DCM PRESSED ON THE POSSIBILITY OF A PUBLIC

STATEMENT FROM THE HOLY SEE; IF NOT A DENUNCIATION

OF THE MOSTAR BISHOP THEN AT LEAST A RESTATEMENT OF

RELEVANT PASSAGES FROM THE PONTIFF\'S NEW YEARS

5. (SBU) CATHOLIC BISHOPS ENJOY A UNIQUE AUTONOMY

WITHIN THEIR JURISDICTIONS. AS LONG AS THEY DO NOT

FALL INTO THEOLOGICAL, MORAL OR SPIRITUAL ERROR,

THERE IS LITTLE THE POPE AND VATICAN OFFICIALS CAN

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 VATICA 01419 02 OF 02 131649Z

DO TO CORRECT A BISHOP\'S POLITICAL/POLITICAL

INDISCRETIONS, EVEN WHEN THEY HAVE THE INCLINATION

TO DO SO. THE POPE IS THE SOLE JUDGE OF BISHOPS,

BUT HAS "JURIDICAL COMPETENCE" ONLY IN SPIRITUAL OR

ECCLESIASTICAL MATTERS. IN THE LAST 100 YEARS,

CATHOLIC BISHOPS HAVE HELD A VARIETY OF POLITICAL

POSITIONS RANGING FROM NEAR-MARXIST SOCIALISM TO

HARD-LINE FASCISM, WITHOUT BEING DISCIPLINED BY THE

HOLY SEE. THE POLITICAL POLICY OF POPES AND THE

VATICAN GOVERNMENT TENDS TO BE CENTRIST, BUT IT HAS

NEITHER THE AUTHORITY NOR, USUALLY, THE WILL TO

PROMOTE ONE POLITICAL EXPRESSION OVER ANOTHER.

6. (SBU) FURTHERMORE, UNDER THE PRINCIPLE KNOWN

AS SUBSIDIARITY IT IS VATICAN POLICY NOT TO CARRY

OUT AT A HIGHER LEVEL WHAT CAN BE SUCCESSFULLY

ACHIEVED AT A LOWER LEVEL. IF THE VATICAN WERE TO

HAVE CONCERNS ABOUT DIVISIVE NATIONALISTIC REMARKS

MADE BY AN OTHERWISE THEOLOGICALLY AND MORALLY

FAITHFUL CATHOLIC BISHOP, IT COULD BEGIN AN

INTERVENTION THROUGH THE LOCAL CATHOLIC HIERARCHY: A

NEIGHBORING BISHOP, THEN A SENIOR BISHOP, THEN

PERHAPS THE PAPAL NUNCIO. THIS APPROACH HAS WORKED

IN THE PAST WHERE THE BISHOPS\' COUNCIL IS LARGE, AND

CONTAINS BISHOPS WITH POLITICAL VIEWS SYMPATHETIC TO

THOSE OF THE HOLY SEE (NOTE: APPARENTLY NOT THE CASE

IN B-H) .

7. (C) THE CATHOLIC CHURCH IN CROATIA AND B-H IS A

FIERCELY LOYAL SUPPORTER OF THE PRESENT POPE AND OF

THE VATICAN. IT IS UNLIKELY THAT THE HOLY SEE WOULD

WISH TO PUBLICLY CHASTISE A CROATIAN BISHOP,

APPOINTED BY POPE JOHN PAUL II, FOR EXCEEDING THE

BOUNDS OF POLITICAL PRUDENCE. POST HAS OVER PAST

MONTHS DISCUSSED WITH THE VATICAN AS WELL AS WITH

OTHER CONTACTS POSSIBLE AVENUES TO TAME THE MOSTAR

BISHOP\'S MORE DESTRUCTIVE NATIONALIST IMPULSES.

POST WOULD BE INTERESTED IN WHETHER THE BISHOPS OF

THE CROAT BISHOPS CONFERENCE SUPPORT PERIC\'S

ACTIVITY. FOR NOW, WE ARE SEEKING TO DEVELOP AN

INTER-RELIGIOUS DIALOGUE (REF D) WITH THE

SANT\'EGIDIO COMMUNITY, IN LARGE PART TO TRY TO BRING

PERIC OUT OF THE CROAT-NATIONALIST POLITICAL

WILDERNESS.
