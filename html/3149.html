Embassy verifies that the Department of State possesses

information of concern with respect to the Royal Nepal Army

Sri Nath Battalion of the First Brigade as of this date (Ref

A). The Department also understands that the Royal Nepal

Army is currently investigating the allegations at issue.

Embassy verifies that the Department of State possesses no

verifiable information of gross violations of human rights by

the other battalions in the First Brigade, or by 2nd, 3rd,

4th, 5th, 6th, 7th, 8th, 9th, or 10th Brigade, or by the RNA

School.
