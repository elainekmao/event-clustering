SUBJECT: ZIMBABWE OPENS AN EMBASSY IN TRIPOLI

CLASSIFIED BY AMBASSADOR TOM MCDONALD FOR REASONS 1.5

(B) AND (D) .

1. (C) EGYPTIAN EMBASSY COUNSELLOR (DCM) KADRI ABDEL-

MOTTALEB TOLD DCM NOVEMBER 4 THAT ZIMBABWE HAS OPENED AN

EMBASSY IN TRIPOLI. THE AMBASSADOR, JOHN MVUNDURA, LEFT

IN EARLY OCTOBER TO ASSUME CHARGE. THE REMAINING 2-3

MEMBERS OF THE EMBASSY STAFF FLEW TO TRIPOLI VIA CAIRO

TWO WEEKS LATER, CITING LACK OF FOREIGN EXCHANGE AS THE

CAUSE OF DELAY IN THEIR TRAVEL.

2. (C) COMMENT: IT IS CLEAR TO US THAT, EVEN IN AN ERA

OF EXTREMELY SCARCE FOREIGN EXCHANGE AND TIGHT BUDGETS,

WITH DIPLOMATS ABROAD GOING UNPAID FOR MONTHS AT A TIME

OR FINDING THEMSELVES STRANDED AT THE END OF THEIR

TOURS, ZIMBABWE VALUES ITS RELATIONSHIP WITH COLONEL

QADHAFI OVER OTHER, MORE PRESSING SOCIAL AND POLITICAL

DEMANDS. WE SUSPECT THE LIBYAN HEAD OF STATE EITHER

GIFTED OR LOANED THE ZIMBABWEAN GOVERNMENT THE FUNDS FOR

THE PURCHASE OF THE PROPERTIES WHERE ITS CHANCERY AND

AMBASSADOR ARE HOUSED. END COMMENT.

MCDONALD
