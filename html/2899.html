SUBJECT: JORDAN: PARTICIPATION IN TRANSSHIPMENT ENFORCEMENT

CONFERENCE

REF: A. STATE 63013

B. AMMAN 1540

C. STATE 43883

1. Jordanian Customs Director General Mahmoud Qteishat

confirmed May 6 that his agency would send three

representatives to the Barcelona conference on transshipment:

-- Faisal Ahmad al-Sarhan, Director of Inspection

-- Fadi Baker Makhlouf, Director of the al-Omary Customs House

-- Mohammad Fayez Obeidat, Head of International Cooperation

Section

2. This seems to be an appropriate mix of officials

responsible for policy and enforcement. Customs has

coordinated its representation with the Ministry of Foreign

Affairs.

3. Post is booking flights and will provide per diem and

finalize travel arrangements upon receipt of fiscal data. We

understand that Department will make hotel reservations at

the Grand Marina Hotel in Barcelona.
