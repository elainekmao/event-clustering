1. (C) DESPITE INTENSIFYING BUDGET PRESSURES, DEFENSE

SPENDING FOR 2003 WILL NOT BE CUT, FRANCESCO TRUPIANO,

DIPLOMATIC ADVISOR TO DEFENSE MINISTER MARTINO, TOLD ACTING

ECONOMIC COUNSELOR ON OCTOBER 31. TRUPIANO CONFIRMED THAT

ITALIAN PRIME MINISTER BERLUSCONI HAS REJECTED FINANCE

MINISTER TREMONTI\'S RECOMMENDATION TO REDUCE DEFENSE

SPENDING, AND ALREADY HAS REAFFIRMED WITH LORD ROBERTSON THAT

ITALY WILL ABIDE BY ITS DEFENSE COMMITMENTS, PARTICULARLY IN

UPGRADES AND PROCUREMENT.

2. (U) THE DRAFT BUDGET, WHICH IS BEFORE PARLIAMENT,

INCREASES DEFENSE SPENDING BY .3 PERCENT IN NOMINAL TERMS,

OVER LAST YEAR\'S LEVELS. HOWEVER, AFTER ACCOUNTING FOR

PREDICTED 2003 INFLATION OF 1.9 PERCENT, THE BUDGET WOULD BE

REDUCED IN REAL TERMS BY AS MUCH AS 1.6 PERCENT.

3. (C) TRUPIANO SAID THE EXACT DEFENSE SPENDING LEVELS ARE

STILL UNDER NEGOTIATION. HE REMAINS UNCONVINCED, HOWEVER,

THAT THE MOD\'S BUDGET WILL INCREASE SUFFICIENTLY TO COVER ALL

THE "FIXED COSTS", SUCH AS MILITARY PAY, WHICH ARE INCREASING

AS ITALY SHIFTS FROM A CONSCRIPT TO A PROFESSIONAL FORCE. HE

ALSO PREDICTED THAT SPENDING FOR SPECIFIC DEFENSE PROGRAMS

WILL BE SHIFTED EVEN MORE TO OTHER MINISTERIAL BUDGETS.

INDEED, GABRIELE CHECCHIA, DIPLOMATIC ADVISOR TO PRODUCTIVE

ACTIVITIES MINISTER MARZANO, ALSO PRESENT WHEN TRUPIANO TOLD

US OF THE PM\'S DECISION, COMMENTED (SOMEWHAT SARDONICALLY)

THAT THE MPA\'S PROGRAMS WILL THEREFORE "RECEIVE AN EVEN

GREATER CUT."

4. (C) COMMENT: DEFENSE PROCUREMENT IS FUNDED THROUGH

MULTIPLE APPROPRIATIONS, INCLUDING THOSE FOR THE MINISTRY OF

PRODUCTIVE ACTIVITIES (I.E. INDUSTRY). DEFMIN MARTINO IS IN

FACT TRYING TO SHIFT MORE OF THE EUROFIGHTER COSTS TO THE

MPA, WHICH DOES NOT WISH TO FUND THE PROGRAM BUT ALREADY IS

RESPONSIBLE FOR SOME OF ITS FINANCIAL OBLIGATIONS. IF THE MPA

PICKS UP MORE OF THE EUROFIGHTER FINANCIAL BURDEN,

NON-DEFENSE RELATED PROGRAMS WILL CERTAINLY BE AFFECTED. WE

ALSO HAVE HEARD THAT MARTINO IS TRYING TO SHIFT DEFENSE

SPENDING TO OTHER MINISTRIES BESIDES THE MPA. END COMMENT.

SPECULATION ON TREMONTI\'S FUTURE

--------------------------------

5. (C) RUMORS ARE RAMPANT THAT FINANCE MINISTER TREMONTI

PROFFERED HIS RESIGNATION AFTER THE PM\'S REJECTION OF THE

PROPOSED DEFENSE CUTS. A JOURNALIST WITH ITALY\'S LEADING

NEWSPAPER CORRIERE DELLA SERA TOLD POLMINCOUNS THAT TREMONTI

CONFIRMED IN AN INTERVIEW THAT HE HAD OFFERED HIS

RESIGNATION, BUT IT HAD BEEN REJECTED. WHEN CLEARING THE

ARTICLE PRIOR TO PUBLICATION, HOWEVER, TREMONTI CUT THE

EXCHANGE FROM THE STORY. IN RESPONSE, CORRIERE RAN A CRYPTIC

EDITORIAL ALLUDING TO THE RESIGNATION AND ACCUSING TREMONTI

OF "INTELLECTUAL ARROGANCE."

6. (C) ALTHOUGH BERLUSCONI HAS CHALLENGED TREMONTI, THE PM

CLEARLY STILL NEEDS HIM IN PLACE. INTERNATIONALLY, THE

FINANCE MINISTER IS ONE OF THE MOST VISIBLE AND IMPORTANT

FIGURES IN THE ITALIAN CABINET. MOREOVER, THE PM COULD

ILL-AFFORD THE RESIGNATION OF TREMONTI IN THE MIDST OF THE

BUDGET BATTLE. CABINET MINISTERS AND PARTY MEMBERS ALREADY

ARE THROWING THEIR WEIGHT ABOUT TO ENSURE THAT FAVORED

PROGRAMS ARE PRESERVED. A VACANT FINANCE MINISTER SEAT COULD

TRIGGER A POLITICAL FREE-FOR-ALL AND MAKE EVEN MORE DIFFICULT

PASSAGE OF A BUDGET BY YEAR\'S END, AS REQUIRED BY LAW.

7. (C) ADDITIONALLY, AN OPEN FINANCE MINISTER SLOT WOULD

RENEW DEBATE ABOUT CABINET REALIGNMENT, A CONTENTIOUS ISSUE

THAT, WE ARE TOLD, SIMMERS ONLY SLIGHTLY BELOW THE SURFACE IN

THE BERLUSCONI GOVERNMENT AND MAY PLAY A ROLE IN THE

CONTINUING DELAY OF THE APPOINTMENT OF A FOREIGN MINISTER, AS

WELL. A TREMONTI RESIGNATION ALSO WOULD EXACERBATE FRICTION

WITHIN THE RULING COALITION. TREMONTI OFTEN SMOOTHES THE WAY

BETWEEN BERLUSCONI\'S FORZA ITALIA AND BOSSI\'S NORTHERN LEAGUE

PARTIES. BOSSI HAS IMMEDIATELY AND PUBLICLY DECLARED THAT

TREMONTI "WILL NOT BE TOUCHED... NOT THE MAN (WITH WHOM) THE

LEAGUE MADE A DEAL TO CREATE THE RULING COALITION."
