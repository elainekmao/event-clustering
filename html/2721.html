SUBJECT: AMBASSADOR GALVANIZES SUPPORT FOR STRICT IPR

1. SUMMARY. AMBASSADOR MEL SEMBLER HOSTED A RECEPTION,

SPONSORED BY COPYRIGHT INDUSTRY ASSOCIATIONS, TO SOLIDIFY

SUPPORT FOR THE STRICT APPLICATION OF ITALY\'S NEW ANTI-

PIRACY LAW. BOTH THE AMBASSADOR AND DR. MAURO MASI,

REPRESENTING THE ITALIAN PRIME MINISTER, ARTICULATED A

SHARED INTEREST IN A MORE STRINGENT INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

RIGHTS (IPR) REGIME. THE NINETY KEY INDUSTRY AND PUBLIC

SECTOR IPR CONTACTS EXPRESSED THEIR SOLIDARITY AND

DEDICATION TO CHANGING ITALY\'S PERMISSIVENESS TOWARD IPR

VIOLATIONS. THE AMBASSADOR MADE STRONG ARGUMENTS WHY

ITALIAN POLICYMAKERS AND LAW ENFORCEMENT OFFICIALS SHOULD

VIGOROUSLY OPPOSE PIRACY. OUR ITALIAN COUNTERPARTS WERE

ALSO FORTHCOMING WITH COMMITMENTS TO COLLABORATE IN THE

FILM AND IN LAW ENFORCEMENT ARENAS. END SUMMARY.

2. ON MARCH 26, AMBASSADOR SEMBLER HOSTED A RECEPTION AT

VILLA TAVERNA THAT BROUGHT TOGETHER FOR THE FIRST TIME

REPRESENTATIVES OF ALL OF THE KEY ORGANIZATIONS INVOLVED IN

ITALY\'S ONGOING EFFORT TO ENFORCE ITS NEW ANTI-PIRACY LAW

(REFTEL). WHILE PIRACY IN ITALY HAS DIMINISHED SINCE THE

LAW REQUIRING STIFFER CRIMINAL PENALTIES ENTERED INTO

EFFECT, COPYRIGHT INDUSTRIES STILL LOSE AN ESTIMATED HALF-

BILLION USD ANNUALLY IN ITALY, WHERE PIRACY RATES IN EXCESS

OF TWENTY PERCENT ARE AMONG THE HIGHEST IN WESTERN EUROPE.

DR. MASI REPRESENTED THE OFFICE OF THE PRIME MINISTER AND

THE SIAE, THE COLLECTION AGENCY RESPONSIBLE FOR THE

STICKERING OF VARIOUS COPYRIGHT-PROTECTED PRODUCTS. ALSO

PRESENT WERE ITALIAN PARLIAMENTARIANS RESPONSIBLE FOR THE

PASSAGE OF THE LAW, LAW ENFORCEMENT OFFICIALS FROM THE

FINANCE POLICE, CARABINIERI, CUSTOMS, AS WELL AS

PROSECUTORS, JUDGES AND ATTORNEYS. THE PRIVATE SECTOR WAS

REPRESENTED BOTH BY THE SPONSORING ASSOCIATIONS FROM THE

MUSIC, FILM/VIDEO, AND SOFTWARE INDUSTRIES, AND BY

REPRESENTATIVES OF INDIVIDUAL COMPANIES FROM THESE SECTORS.

3. THE EVENT WAS ORGANIZED AS A CELEBRATION OF THE

SUCCESSFUL COMPLETION OF THE IPR INDUSTRY\'S 2001 ROAD SHOW.

THE ROAD SHOW, WHICH WAS ENDORSED BY THE EMBASSY, PROVIDED

IN-DEPTH TRAINING FOR LAW ENFORCEMENT OFFICIALS ACROSS

ITALY. THE TRAINING PROGRAM FOCUSED ON EXPLAINING THE NEW

LAW, ARTICLE BY ARTICLE, AND ITS ENFORCEMENT IMPLICATIONS

FOR ALL RELEVANT PLAYERS.

4. DURING THE EVENING, THE AMBASSADOR SPOKE WITH DR.

GIANNI MASSARO, PRESIDENT OF ANICA, THE ASSOCIATION OF

ITALIAN FILM PRODUCERS, ABOUT THE NEED TO MAKE GOOD ON

PRIVATE COPY PAYMENTS WORTH USD 2.5 MILLION ANNUALLY THAT

ARE OWED TO THE MPAA, THE MOTION PICTURE ASSOCIATION OF

AMERICA. DR. MASSARO SAID THAT HE WOULD IMMEDIATELY CALL A

MEETING OF ANICA\'S MEMBERS TO DISCUSS THIS ISSUE.

5. NOT ABLE TO ATTEND WAS BRIGADIER GENERAL NINO DI

PAOLO, CHIEF OF STAFF OF THE FINANCE POLICE. WHEN ECONOFFS

MET WITH HIM THE FOLLOWING DAY, THE GENERAL SAID THAT HE

VIEWS ANTI-PIRACY AS A CRUCIAL ELEMENT IN THE FIGHT AGAINST

ORGANIZED CRIME NOT ONLY IN ITALY, BUT INTERNATIONALLY. HE

ADDED THAT HIS ORGANIZATION, FOR ITS PART, WAS EAGER TO

COLLABORATE ON THE RANGE OF INTERNATIONAL LAW ENFORCEMENT

PROBLEMS, FROM IPR TO TERRORISM.

6. THE INDUSTRY ASSOCIATIONS GAVE THE EMBASSY STRONG

POSITIVE FEEDBACK ABOUT THE EVENT. THE BUSINESS SOFTWARE

ALLIANCE REPRESENTATIVE EXPRESSED OPTIMISM ABOUT USING

RENEWED WORKING-LEVEL CONTACTS WITH OFFICIALS OF THE SIAE

TO HELP RESOLVE OUTSTANDING STICKERING ISSUES. ONE

REPRESENTATIVE FROM A PAY-TELEVISION PROVIDER REMARKED THAT

SHE COULD NOT HAVE HEARD A BETTER MESSAGE THAN THAT WHICH

THE AMBASSADOR GAVE.

7. COMMENT. WHILE THERE IS MORE TO BE DONE TO MOVE THE

IPR ENFORCEMENT AGENDA FORWARD, AND WE WILL MONITOR THE

PLEDGES MADE TO SEE THAT THERE IS CONCRETE FOLLOW-THROUGH,

IT IS ALSO CLEAR THAT MUCH HAS BEEN ACCOMPLISHED. THE

EMBASSY\'S COOPERATION WITH INDUSTRY AND THE PRIVATE SECTOR

HAS BEGUN TO PAY DIVIDENDS. THE EVENING AT VILLA TAVERNA

MARKED THE FIRST TIME THAT WE HAVE BEEN ABLE TO BRING

TOGETHER SO MANY KEY PLAYERS WHO SHARE AWARENESS OF THE

IMPORTANCE OF IPR PROTECTION. THE EVENT HELPED US TO

GALVANIZE GROWING CONSENSUS ABOUT THE SERIOUSNESS OF THE

PROBLEM AND THE COMMITMENT TO FIND AND IMPLEMENT REAL

SOLUTIONS. END COMMENT.
