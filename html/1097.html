1. (C) ON JUNE 1 AMBASSADOR AND ECONOFF MET WITH

ISAAC TAKAWIRA, MANAGING DIRECTOR OF BARCLAYS BANK,

AND WHOM WE REGARD AS AN EXCELLENT, WELL CONNECTED

AND INFORMED INTERLOCUTOR, TO DISCUSS ZIMBABWE\'S

SITUATION AND OUTLOOK. THE BANKER OPENED HIS SERIOUS

DIALOGUE BY STATING HOW WORRIED HE WAS ABOUT THE

CONSEQUENCES, BOTH INTERNAL AND INTERNATIONALLY, OF

THE LAND ISSUE AND RESETTLEMENT PROCESS AS CURRENTLY

BEING HANDLED BY GOVERNMENT. CALLING IT A DISASTER,

HE STATED IT WAS NOT WELL THOUGHT OUT AND WOULD

NEGATIVELY AFFECT AGRICULTURAL PRODUCTIVITY, LEAVE

THE RESETTLED POPULACE MORE DESTITUTE IN THE FUTURE,

AND DESTROY THE CHANCES OF INTERNATIONAL SUPPORT FOR

THE PROGRAM. (COMMENT: TAKAWIRA IS ALSO A LARGE-

SCALE TOBACCO FARMER IN HIS OWN RIGHT, EMPLOYING 2000

FARM WORKERS. HIS PROPERTY HAS NOT BEEN AFFECTED BY

THE RECENT INVASIONS. CLOSELY AFFILIATED WITH THE

RULING PARTY AND MOVING AT EASE IN THE HIGHEST RANKS

OF THE GOVERNMENT IS LIKELY A CONTRIBUTING FACTOR TO

THIS. TOBACCO FARMING GIVES TAKWIRA A GOOD SENSE OF

THE SENTIMENT OF A SIZABLE SEGMENT OF THE POPULACE.

END COMENT.)

2. (C) THE MANAGING DIRECTOR ALSO TOLD US THAT HIS

BANK WAS RELATIVELY WELL-POSITIONED REGARDING ITS

EXPOSURE TO THE FARMING INDUSTRY, WHICH IS SUFFERING

ITS WORSE FINANCIAL CRISIS SINCE INDEPENDENCE. WHEN

HE TOOK OVER THE HELM IN 1992, BARCLAY\'S FARM-LENDING

BOOK CONSTITUTED 39 PERCENT OF TOTAL LOANS. TODAY

THAT FIGURE IS DOWN TO 19 PERCENT AND IS HEAVILY

RESERVED AGAINST. THE SHRINKAGE WAS DUE TO HIS

DECISION EIGHT YEARS AGO TO WRITE NO NEW FARM

BUSINESS, AS HE KNEW EVEN BACK THEN THAT THE

UNRESOLVED LAND ISSUE WAS A MAJOR PROBLEM WAITING TO

HAPPEN. THOUGH HIS PRESCIENCE WAS VINDICATED, HIS

SENSE OF SHAME AND LOSS AT WHAT HAS OCCURRED WAS

EVIDENT AND HEARTFELT. THE BULK OF THE BANK\'S

BUSINESS THESE DAYS IS LENDING TO CORPORATES, WITH

SUCH LOANS MAKING UP 65 TO 70 PERCENT OF THE TOTAL.

3. (C) TAKAWIRA ALSO TOLD US THAT HE HAD RECENTLY

POINTED OUT TO THE ATTORNEY GENERAL A POTENTIAL

PROBLEM WITH THE LAND ACQUISITION PROGRAM AS

CURRENTLY STRUCTURED. HE FURTHER EXPLAINED THAT THE

COMPENSATION PROCEDURES IN THE JUST-SIGNED

LEGISLATION WOULD PAY THE FARMERS IN IOU\'S (25

PERCENT OF THE VALUE OF IMPROVEMENTS ON SEIZURE, 25

PERCENT TWO YEARS LATER AND THE BALANCE FOUR OR FIVE

YEARS OUT, SEE REFTEL A) WHICH ONLY CARRY A 25

PERCENT INTEREST RATE. HIS BANK\'S LOWEST RATE IS

CURRENTLY 60.5 PERCENT, AND A COURT CASE WOULD SURELY

DECIDE FOR THE PLAINTIFFS THAT THEY WERE

UNDERCOMPENSATED AND DIRECT THE GOZ TO PAY MARKET

RATES. THIS LEGALLY CHALENGABLE DISPARITY WOULD ONLY

MAKE A BAD PROGRAM WORSE, HE OPINED.

4. (C) ON THE ELECTIONS, TAKAWIRA REVEALED A

PERSONAL BELIEF THAT THE OPPOSITION MOVEMENT FOR

DEMOCRATIC CHANGE (MDC) PARTY MAY DO MUCH BETTER IN

THE POLLS THAN IS WIDELY ASSUMED. HE POINTEDLY TOLD

US STORIES OF CONVERSATIONS HE RECENTLY HAD WITH

RELATIVES OF HIS (AN UPPER RANK MILITARY OFFICER AND

TWO ELDERLY AUNTS), AS WELL AS THE SENTIMENT OF HIS

2000 FARM WORKERS. SUCCINCTLY PUT, HIS MESSAGE WAS

THAT THESE DAYS EVERYONE IN THE COUNTRY IS FOR ZANU-

PF -- UP UNTIL ELECTION DAY. AN 80 OR 90 SEAT MDC

WIN WOULD NOT SURPIRSE HIM, AND HE ASKED OUR

ASSURANCE THAT WE WOULD SUPPORT THE NEW GOVERNEMNT

WERE THIS TO OCCUR.

5. (C) ON OTHER TOPICS, NOTABLY THE FOREX SHORTAGE,

HE CONFIRMED THAT MANY COMMERCIAL OPERATIONS WERE ON

THE VERGE OF SHUTDOWN DUE TO THEIR INABILITY TO PAY

INTERNATIONAL SUPPLIERS OR EVEN THEIR PARENT

COMPANIES. FOR THE PAST FOUR MONTHS THE ZIMBABWEAN

COMPANIES HAD BEEN LIVING ON THE CREDIT AND SUPPORT

OF THEIR HISTORIC FRIENDS AND RELATIONS, BUT THESE

ARE ABOUT EXHAUSTED, WITH DIRE CONSEQUENCES. AS A

SINGLE EXAMPLE HE CITED ZIMBABWE\'S LARGEST GOLD

PRODUCER WHICH CURRENTLY OPERATES EIGHT MINES, BUT
