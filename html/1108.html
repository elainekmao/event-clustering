SUBJECT: DUTCH COUNTERNARCOTICS INITIATIVES IN SOUTH

AMERICA AND CARIBBEAN

REF: A. THE HAGUE 03679

B. THE HAGUE 04123

C. SECSTATE 98868

1. PURSUANT TO REF C, THE FOLLOWING IS A SHORT SUMMARY OF

RECENT DUTCH COUNTERNARCOTICS INITIATIVES IN SOUTH AMERICA

AND THE CARIBBEAN SINCE LAST POST SUBMISSION IN DECEMBER

1999.

2. PERU: THE NETHERLANDS HAS PROVIDED PERU WITH

APPROXIMATELY 10,000,000 (TEN MILLION) DOLLARS IN DEBT RELIEF

AS PLEDGED AT THE PERU DONOR CONFERENCE, WHICH IS TO BE USED

FOR COUNTERNARCOTICS PROGRAMS. THIS DEBT RELIEF WAS

PREVIOUSLY REPORTED AS PLEDGED (REF B).

3. CARIBBEAN: THE NETHERLANDS, ALONG WITH EU PARTNERS,

SUPPORT THE PROJECT MANAGEMENT OFFICE (PMO) IN BARBADOS AND

CONTINUES TO ENCOURAGE REGIONAL MARITIME COOPERATION IN THE

REGION (REFS A AND B).
