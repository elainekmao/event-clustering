1. (C) SUMMARY: BETWEEN JANUARY 15 AND 18, ASSISTANT

SECRETARY LORNE CRANER AND HIS DELEGATION MET WITH A

SIPDIS

DETERIORATING HUMAN RIGHTS SITUATION ON THE GROUND. THE
