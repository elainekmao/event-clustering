SUBJECT: JORDAN: MEDIA REACTION ON IRAQ, QUADRIPARTITE

MEETING

SUMMARY

NEWS REPORTS IN JORDAN\'S DAILIES ON JULY 16 COVERED THE

KING\'S INTERVIEW WITH NBC IN WHICH HE REAFFIRMED THAT

THERE IS NO UNDERSTANDING WITH THE U.S. TO USE JORDAN

AS A BASE FOR AN ATTACK ON IRAQ, THE VISIT ORGANIZED

FOR JOURNALISTS TO JORDAN\'S AIR BASE IN THE EAST TO

CONFIRM THAT THERE ARE NO U.S. MILITARY PERSONNEL

THERE, AN INTERVIEW WITH THE IRAQI AMBASSADOR IN AMMAN,

AND REPORTS THAT THE U.S. WOULD ASK ARAB MINISTERS AT

THE QUADRIPARTITE MEETING TO "MARGINALIZE ARAFAT AS

MUCH AS POSSIBLE."

EDITORIAL WRITERS COMMENTED ON A NUMBER OF DOMESTIC

SUBJECTS, AND A FEW WROTE AGAINST THE ANTICIPATED

MILITARY STRIKE AGAINST IRAQ, WARNING OF A POPULAR

REACTION AGAINST THE U.S.

NEWS REPORTS

THE LEAD STORY IN ALL JORDANIAN DAILIES ON JULY 16

COVERED KING ABDULLAH\'S INTERVIEW WITH NBC, IN WHICH HE

REAFFIRMED, ACCORDING TO THE REPORTS, THAT THERE IS NO

UNDERSTANDING WITH WASHINGTON REGARDING A STRIKE

AGAINST IRAQ, AND REITERATED THE WARNING THAT STRIKING

IRAQ OR PARTITIONING IT WOULD LEAD TO A CATASTROPHE.

DAILIES ALSO REPORTED THAT JOURNALISTS WERE ALLOWED TO

VISIT THE MUWAFFAQ AL-SALTI AIRBASE IN AZRAQ (EAST OF

JORDAN) TO CONFIRM THAT THERE ARE NO U.S. TROOPS THERE.

ASSABEEL (INDEPENDENT WEEKLY REFLECTING THE VIEWS OF

THE ISLAMIC ACTION FRONT) INTERVIEWED THE IRAQI

AMBASSADOR IN AMMAN, WHO SAID THAT HIS COUNTRY IS FULLY

PREPARED, EVEN THOUGH IT HAS NO WEAPONS OF MASS

DESTRUCTION. IN A POSSIBLE REFERENCE TO REPORTS ABOUT

A JORDANIAN ROLE IN THE MILITARY ACTION, THE AMBASSADOR

SAID: "WE KNOW FROM WHERE THE WIND WILL BLOW, AND WE

KNOW FROM WHERE THE HYENAS WILL COME TO GNAW OUR FLESH,

BUT IRAQ IS LIKE A WOUNDED WOLF THAT SLEEPS WITH HIS

EYES HALF SHUT, AND IT WOULD NOT BE AFFECTED BY

BETRAYAL."

COVERAGE OF THE QUADRIPARTITE COMMITTEE MEETING

HIGHLIGHTED DIFFERENT ASPECTS, FROM A SAUDI PAPER BEING

SUBMITTED BY THE MINISTERS OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS OF JORDAN

AND EGYPT, TO THE "AMERICAN INSTRUCTIONS TO MARGINALIZE

ARAFAT AS MUCH AS POSSIBLE, AFTER WASHINGTON REALIZED

THE DIFFICULTY OF OUSTING HIM."

EDITORIAL COMMENTARIES

-- NO TO THE AMERICAN STRIKE AGAINST IRAQ

THE LEADER COMMENTARY IN CENTER-LEFT, INFLUENTIAL

ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR ON JULY 16 READ: "JORDAN DENIES

REPEATEDLY AND EMPHATICALLY THE PRESENCE OF AMERICAN

FORCES ON ITS TERRITORY, AND IT OPENS ITS AIR BASES AND

MILITARY INSTALLATIONS TO FOREIGN JOURNALISTS TO

CONFIRM FOR THEMSELVES THE FALSEHOOD OF MEDIA REPORTS

IN THIS CONCERN. AT THE SAME TIME, JORDAN WARNS

AGAINST THE FOLLY OF THE UNITED STATES IN BEING DRIVEN

SOLELY BY THE LOGIC OF INIQUITOUS POWER, AND IT WARNS

AGAINST THE NEGATIVE CONSEQUENCES TO THE WHOLE REGION

SHOULD WASHINGTON CARRY OUT ITS THREATS."

-- THE WORLD IS NOT A CHESS SET

DR. NABIL AL-SHARIF, CHIEF EDITOR OF CENTER-LEFT,

INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR WROTE ON JULY 16:

"THE AMERICAN GIANT HAS SUFFERED FROM HIS WOUNDED PRIDE

SINCE THE EVENTS OF SEPTEMBER 11, SO HE SEEKS TO

RESTORE HIS PRESTIGE BY IMPOSING HIS HEGEMONY ON THE

WORLD AND PLAYING SCISSORS AND PASTE WITH THE WORLD MAP

AS A CHILD DOES ON HIS COMPUTER. THE REMAINING MONTHS

OF THIS SUMMER MAY BRING FIRE WHOSE HEAT IS BEYOND

IMAGINATION. BUT THE REALITY OF LIFE ON THIS PLANET IS

ALSO PREGNANT WITH SURPRISES TO TYRANNICAL POWERS THAT

ARE BLINDED BY THEIR POWER TO THE POINT WHERE THEY

CANNOT DISTINGUISH BETWEEN FACTS AND WISHFUL THINKING.

THE WORLD IS NOT A CHESS SET, AND PEOPLE ARE NOT

PAWNS."

-- SHOULD IRAQ BE ATTACKED

ATEF AL-JOLANI, CHIEF EDITOR OF ASSABEEL (INDEPENDENT

WEEKLY REFLECTING THE VIEWS OF THE ISLAMIC ACTION

FRONT) WROTE ON JULY 16: "ALL ARAB STATES HAVE DECLARED

OPENLY THEIR OPPOSITION TO THE PROSPECTIVE AMERICAN

AGGRESSION AGAINST IRAQ, BUT THE U.S. HAS TURNED A DEAF

EAR AND AFFIRMED ITS INTENTION NOT ONLY TO CARRY OUT

ITS AGGRESSION, BUT ALSO TO VIOLATE THE SOVEREIGNTY OF

ARAB STATES IN COURSE OF THIS ATTACK WITHOUT SEEKING

THE PERMISSION OF THESE STATES. AS FOR THE ARAB PEOPLE,

WHOSE HEARTS HAVE ALWAYS BEEN WITH THE CAUSE OF THEIR

NATION, THEIR SWORDS HAVE REMAINED DYSFUNCTIONAL TO A

POINT THAT MADE AMERICAN OFFICIALS DISCOUNT THEM. THEY

ARE NOW CALLED UPON TO PROVE TO THE HAWKS OF THE U.S.

THE ERROR OF THEIR CALCULATIONS, WHICH HAVE TOTALLY

DISMISSED THE ARAB STREET, AND TO CONFIRM TO THE U.S.

AND ITS ALLIES THAT PEOPLE CAN BE SURPRISING." GNEHM
