SUMMARY

-------

1. (C) THE GOC IS REACTING TO IC PRESSURE ON REFUGEE RETURNS.

MINISTER FOR PUBLIC WORKS AND RECONSTRUCTION AND BUILDING,

RADIMIR CACIC, HELD A HASTILY ARRANGED MEETING ON FEBRUARY 4

WITH THE DIPLOMATIC COMMUNITY TO PRESENT HIS MINISTRY,S

PROGRAM ON REFUGEE RETURN. THE EVENT AT THE SHERATON HOTEL

WAS HEAVILY ATTENDED BY TELEVISION AND PRINT MEDIA. THE

MINISTER WAS COMBATIVE. MUCH OF WHAT CACIC SAID WAS OLD NEWS

AND FILLED WITH QUESTIONABLE FIGURES ABOUT THE GOVERNMENT,S

EFFORTS THUS FAR. HE DID, HOWEVER, ANNOUNCE A NEW INITIATIVE

AIMED AT RESTORING LOST OCCUPANCY AND TENANCY RIGHTS. HE

CHASTISED THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY FOR ITS LACK OF

FINANCIAL SUPPORT WHILE, AT THE SAME TIME, CRITICIZED THE GOC

AT EVERY OPPORTUNITY FOR ITS SHORTCOMINGS ON RETURN-RELATED

ISSUES.

2. (C) MANY IC OFFICIALS AGREED THAT CACIC,S PRESENTATION

WAS LONG ON STYLE BUT SHORT ON SUBSTANCE. THE HASTY EVENT

OBVIOUSLY WAS DESIGNED TO PUSH BACK STRONGER IC PRESSURE ON

THE GOC ON RETURNS. THE GOC MISSED ANOTHER OPPORTUNITY TO

CONVINCE THE IC THAT IT IS WILLING TO MAKE MEANINGFUL

PROGRESS ON RETURNS AND TO WORK WITH THE IC IN DOING SO. THAT

SAID, THE GOC\'S PUSHING BACK IS SOFTENING SOME IC RESOLVE,

GOC LAYS OUT RED CARPET TREATMENT

---------------------------------

3. (C) IN WHAT MANY IC OFFICIALS CONSIDERED AN UNUSUAL VENUE

TO HOLD A MEETING ON REFUGEE RETURNS, GOC OFFICIALS, LED BY

CACIC, HOSTED ALMOST ONE HUNDRED REPRESENTATIVES FROM THE

DIPLOMATIC COMMUNITY AT THE SHERATON HOTEL. TELEVISION AND

PRINT MEDIA PRESENCE WAS EXTENSIVE. CACIC USED THE OCCASION

TO REVIEW THE GOVERNMENT,S PERFORMANCE ON REFUGEE-RELATED

ISSUES SINCE COMING TO POWER IN 2000 AND TO HIGHLIGHT NEW GOC

PLANS TO ACCELERATE THE RETURN OF REFUGEES. HE WANTED THIS

EVENT TO BE UP CLOSE AND PERSONAL AND ACTED MORE LIKE A

LECTURER THAN MINISTER. HIS GOAL WAS TO SHOW THAT THE GOC

REGARDED THE ISSUE OF RETURNS WITH GREAT IMPORTANCE.

CACIC: I,M IN CHARGE

---------------------

4. (C) CACIC,S DEMEANOR THROUGHOUT THE PRESENTATION WAS

INTENDED TO DISPEL ANY NOTION THAT ANYONE ELSE BESIDE HIMSELF

WAS RESPONSIBLE FOR REFUGEE-RELATED MATTERS. CACIC PRESENTED

HIS DEPUTY AND ASSISTANT MINISTERS DIRECTLY RESPONSIBLE FOR

REFUGEE RETURNS, BUT DID MOST OF THE TALKING. HE ASSUMED

DIRECT RESPONSIBILITY FOR THE GOC,S RECORD ON RETURNS OVER

THE PAST THREE YEARS. HE REMINDED THE AUDIENCE THAT HIS

MINISTRY WAS HAMPERED BY LEGISLATION PASSED BY THE PREVIOUS

GOVERNMENT. ONLY LAST FALL WERE THE MOST CRUCIAL PARTS OF

THIS LEGISLATION AMENDED, WHICH, HE SAID, HELPED REMOVE ALL

OF THE LEGISLATIVE BARRIERS TO RETURN. HE SAID HE WAS NOT

SATISFIED WITH RESULTS TO DATE; HE QUESTIONED HIS ASSISTANT

MINISTERS ABOUT THE LACK OF PROGRESS AND FOUND MANY OF THEIR

RESPONSES TO BE UNACCEPTABLE. ON THE OTHER HAND, SOME OF

THEIR "JUSTIFICATIONS," AS HE REFERRED TO THEM, DEALT WITH

FINANCIAL AND RESOURCE CONSTRAINTS OF THE GOVERNMENT THAT

WERE OUT OF THEIR CONTROL.

EU MEMBERSHIP AT STAKE

----------------------

5. (C) CACIC INSISTED THAT THE DISCONTENT IN THE

INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY REFLECTED A LACK OF INFORMATION. HE

SAID HE INTENDS TO HAVE A SERIES OF MEETINGS WITH THE IC TO

SHARE INFORMATION AND AVOID FUTURE MISUNDERSTANDINGS. THIS

IS IMPORTANT, HE SAID, BECAUSE CROATIA WILL SOON SUBMIT ITS

APPLICATION TO JOIN THE EU AND CROATIA IS HELPED BY GETTING

MORE INFORMATION OUT. HIS TOP PRIORITIES ARE RECONSTRUCTION

AND PROPERTY RETURNS. SINCE 2000 THERE HAS BEEN A CHANGE IN

THE GOC,S ATTITUDE COMPARED TO THE PREVIOUS GOVERNMENT,

CACIC SAID: THE PREVIOUS GOVERNMENT HELPED ONLY ETHNIC CROATS

WHILE THE CURRENT GOVERNMENT HELPS ALL OF ITS CITIZENS.

7. (C) CACIC SAID HE EXPECTED GREATER UNHCR INVOLVEMENT

DURING WHAT HE SAID WOULD BE A CRITICAL YEAR FOR RETURNS (REF

A). THIS WAS BECAUSE THREE-FOURTHS OF THE GOVERNMENT,S

RECONSTRUCTION EFFORTS WILL BENEFIT RETURNING SERBS. HE SAID

HE REGRETTED THAT IT TOOK SO LONG FOR THE PROGRAMS TO HAVE A

DIRECT IMPACT ON THE SERB POPULATION. THE GOC PLANS TO ISSUE

7,500 DECISIONS FOR RECONSTRUCTION THIS YEAR, WHICH WILL MAKE

IT POSSIBLE FOR AT LEAST 20,000 PEOPLE TO RETURN IF THEY

CHOOSE TO DO SO ONCE THEIR HOUSES HAVE BEEN COMPLETED. HE

CLAIMED THAT SINCE SEPTEMBER 2002, ALMOST 940 PROPERTIES HAVE

BEEN REPOSSESSED. THE GOC PLANS TO RETURN ALL HOMES TO THEIR

RIGHTFUL OWNERS BY THE END OF 2003.

NEW INITIATIVE TO RESTORE TENANCY RIGHTS

----------------------------------------

8. (C) CACIC,S DECLARATION THAT THE GOC WOULD SEEK TO

RESTORE LOST OCCUPANCY AND TENANCY RIGHTS SPARKED THE

GREATEST ATTENTION FROM HIS AUDIENCE. HE SAID THAT IN THE

AREAS OF SPECIAL STATE CONCERN ("ASSC" -- WAR-AFFECTED AREAS)

ALL CITIZENS WERE VICTIMS OF THE WAR AND HAVE A RIGHT TO

RETURN, TO QUALIFY FOR HOUSING CARE AND TO PURCHASE THEIR

FORMER RIGHTS. FOR PEOPLE WHO LIVED OUTSIDE THE ASSC (THAT

IS MOST FORMER OTR HOLDERS), THEY WILL BE INCLUDED IN THE

GOC,S PROGRAM ON PUBLICLY FUNDED HOUSING CONSTRUCTION. A

NEW LAW WILL BE ENACTED THAT WILL CALL FOR THE FORMULATION OF

LISTS TO RESOLVE THE ISSUE OF WHO CAN QUALIFY FOR SUCH

ASSISTANCE.

9. (C) UNDER THE PROGRAM, THE MINISTER EXPLAINED, THERE WOULD

BE TWO POSSIBILITIES: OWNERSHIP AND RENT. UNDER THE

OWNERSHIP PROGRAM, LOANS WILL BE OFFERED WHICH THE OWNERS

WILL HAVE TO REPAY OVER A CERTAIN TIME PERIOD. THE RENT

PROGRAM WILL INCLUDE PROVISIONS THAT WILL ALLOW THE PERSON TO

PAY RENT FOR A REQUIRED PERIOD OF TIME, AFTER WHICH HE WILL

BECOME THE OWNER OF THE PROPERTY. CACIC ENVISIONED A 4-YEAR

PERIOD TO IMPLEMENT THE PROGRAM. FORMER TENANCY RIGHT

HOLDERS WILL BE GIVEN A ONE-YEAR DEADLINE TO APPLY AND 2 TO 3

YEARS FOR THE PROPERTIES TO BE BUILT. THE GOC WILL EARMARK

FUNDS FOR THE PROGRAM BY THE END OF THE YEAR, AND CACIC SAID

IT EXPECTS THE PROGRAM WILL BE COMPLETED BY 2006.

10. (C) CACIC PREDICTED THAT THERE WOULD NOT BE A HIGH NUMBER

OF APPLICATIONS FROM SERBS CURRENTLY LIVING IN

SERBIA-MONTENEGRO AND BIH. THESE ARE THE POPULATIONS THE IC

WANTED TO RETURN TO CROATIA. MANY OF THE SERBS THAT WOULD

QUALIFY FOR THE PROGRAM DO NOT WANT TO RETURN TO CROATIA BUT

WANT THE OPPORTUNITY TO SELL ANY PROPERTY PROVIDED TO THEM,

AN OUTCOME STRONGLY OPPOSED BY THE GOC. THE PROGRAM AS

OUTLINED BY CACIC IS DESIGNED SPECIFICALLY FOR THOSE WHO

REALLY WANT TO RETURN. HE STATED THAT THE GOC DID NOT

CURRENTLY HAVE THE MONEY TO INITIATE THE PROGRAM BUT WOULD

RESOLVE THE FUNDING ISSUE SOON. IT HAD TO IN ORDER TO

RESOLVE THE ISSUE OF OCCUPANCY AND TENANCY RIGHTS ONCE AND

FOR ALL. HE STRESSED THAT PEOPLE WILL NOT BE ABLE TO SELL

THE PROPERTIES OBTAINED THROUGH THE PROGRAM.

CONTENTIOUS QUESTION AND ANSWER PERIOD

--------------------------------------

11. (C) A BRIEF QUESTION AND ANSWER PERIOD FOLLOWED CACIC,S

REMARKS. THE MINISTER BECAME IRRITATED AND CONFRONTATIONAL

WHEN OSCE HEAD PETER SEMNEBY ASKED FOR SPECIFIC INFORMATION

ABOUT THE TENANCY RIGHTS INITIATIVE, NOTABLY WHEN WOULD THE

ONE-YEAR DEADLINE BEGIN FOR FORMER TENANCY RIGHTS HOLDERS.

CACIC RESPONDED THAT HE WOULD WAIT FOR THE INTERNATIONAL

COMMUNITY TO TELL HIM WHAT TO DO. THIS WAY, THE IC COULD NOT

SAY THAT THE GOC SET A SHORT DEADLINE THAT WAS DISCRIMINATORY

AGAINST SERBS WHO DID NOT RESIDE IN CROATIA. CACIC SAID HE

WOULD SIGN ANY PROPOSAL THE IC SUBMITTED TO HIM, BUT THEN HE

DID NOT WANT TO HEAR COMPLAINTS AFTERWARDS.

12. (C) OTHER LEADING IC REPS RAISED QUESTIONS ABOUT RENTAL

COMPENSATION FOR CURRENT OWNERS WHO ARE NOT OCCUPYING THEIR

PROPERTIES AND FOR DAMAGE RESULTING FROM THE LOOTING OF

PROPERTIES BY TEMPORARY OCCUPANTS AS THEY VACATE THEM. CACIC

REPLIED THAT THE GOC WILL PROVIDE RENTAL COMPENSATION TO

THOSE WHO QUALIFY UNDER THE LAW BUT HE DID NOT SAY WHEN IT

WOULD BEGIN. AS FOR LOOTING, CACIC SAID THE GOC DID NOT HAVE

THE MONEY TO COMPENSATE EVERYONE WHOSE PROPERTY HAD BEEN

LOOTED. HE EXPRESSED SURPRISE THAT CROATIA WAS BEING ASKED

TO PROVIDE SUCH COMPENSATION WHEN NO OTHER COUNTRY IN THE

REGION WAS ASKED TO DO SO.

INCREASING THE STAKES

---------------------

13. (C) IC PRESSURE ON THE GOC TO STEP UP TO THE RETURNS

ISSUE HAS INCREASED RECENTLY IN WAYS IT APPARENTLY CANNOT

IGNORE. WE NEED TO JUDGE THE GOC BY ITS ACTIONS AND BY

RESULTS ON THE GROUND, NOT BY RHETORIC OR DECLARATIONS. SO

FAR, WHAT IS CLEAR -- CACIC\'S PERSONAL LEAD MADE THIS CLEAR

-- IS THAT MORE CAREFULLY "MANAGING" THE RETURNS ISSUE WITH

THE IC IS PART OF THE GOC\'S EFFORT TO MAINTAIN MOMENTUM

TOWARD THE EU AND NATO. IT WAS LESS OBVIOUS THAT ACTUALLY

SOLVING THIS BIG PROBLEM IS ON THE GOC\'S AGENDA -- MOST OF

WHAT CACIC TALKED ABOUT WAS EITHER OLD HAT TO THOSE WHO WORK

THESE ISSUES OR INSUFFICIENTLY DETAILED TO BE GROUNDS FOR

CONFIDENCE THAT THE GOC IS TURNING OVER A NEW LEAF.

14. (C) THIS EVENT WAS ONLY THE LATEST SIGN THAT THE

17. (C) WE KNOW THERE IS AN INTENSE DEBATE WITHIN THE GOC ON

HOW TO HANDLE THE INCREASED IC PRESSURE. PM RACAN IS SAID TO

FAVOR SOME RESPONSIVENESS, BUT DPM GRANIC, THE MFA, CACIC\'S

PRESSURE. WE WILL WORK TO REINFORCE THE PRESSURE HERE. WE
