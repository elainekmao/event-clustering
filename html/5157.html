SUBJECT: (C) PAYA VISITS POPE; FM TAURAN MAY RECIPROCATE

REF: A) FROWICK/NIGRO EMAIL JANUARY 10 B) HAVANA 01235

CLASSIFIED BY: POLOFF GHFROWICK REASON EO 12958 1.5 (B,D).

1. (C) VATICAN CARIBBEAN AFFAIRS DIRECTOR MONS. GIORGIO

LINGUA TOLD US JANUARY 9 THAT THE HOLY SEE CONTINUES TO BE

CAUTIOUS ABOUT "PRESSING THE ENVELOPE" FOR GREATER CHURCH

FREEDOMS IN CUBA. HE BELIEVED THAT THE CUBAN GOVERNMENT

WANTS TO CHANGE, BUT, LIKE THE CHINESE, ARE FEARFUL OF LOSING

CONTROL. HE SAID THE POPE, WHO GREETED VARELA PROJECT AND

CHRISTIAN LIBERATION MOVEMENT FOUNDER OSVALDO PAYA AT THE END

OF A GENERAL AUDIENCE JANUARY 8 (REF A), WOULD NOT MEET

PRIVATELY WITH HIM, AS SPECULATED IN THE PRESS, BECAUSE THE

CHURCH DID NOT WISH TO PROVOKE A NEGATIVE REACTION FROM THE

CUBAN GOVERNMENT. PAYA REPORTEDLY EXPRESSED SATISFACTION

WITH THE BRIEF ENCOUNTER, CLAIMING "ONE CANNOT ASPIRE FOR

MORE IN AN IMPROVISED TRIP WITHOUT A FIXED AGENDA."

2. (C) IN HIS CONVERSATIONS WITH THE CUBAN EMBASSY TO THE

HOLY SEE, LINGUA SAYS HE HAS PRESSED THE CUBANS TO CULTIVATE

PAYA AS A "SOFT OPPOSITIONIST." ACCORDING TO LINGUA, THE

CUBAN EMBASSY CONSISTENTLY REPLIES BY DOWNPLAYING THE

INFLUENCE OF THE OPPOSITION. LINGUA SAID PAYA WAS A

COMMITTED CATHOLIC WHO WISHES TO WORK FOR CHANGE WITHIN

CUBA\'S SYSTEM. HE TOLD US THAT WHEN THE CASTRO GOVERNMENT

REBUFFED THE VARELA PROJECT WITH THE REFERENDUM REAFFIRMING

THE SOCIALIST CONSTITUTION, PAYA TOLD THE CHURCH HE WAS HAPPY

THAT HIS APPROACH OF "USING THE CONSTITUTION ITSELF" HAD BEEN

"REAFFIRMED."

3. (C) QUERIED ON WHETHER THE CHURCH WAS CONTENT WITH THE

PACE OF CHANGE IN CUBA, LINGUA TOLD US THE CHURCH ASSIDUOUSLY

AVOIDS POLITICAL ACTIVISM, SO AS TO PROTECT SPACE FOR ITS

PASTORAL WORK. HE TOLD US THE GOC HAD COMPLAINED ON OCCASION

ABOUT THE CHURCH\'S LIBRARIES, AND SOMETIMES IT REFUSED OR

RESTRICTED VISAS FOR CLERGY, BUT LINGUA DID NOT SIGNAL THAT

THE CHURCH WOULD TAKE THOSE RESTRICTIONS AS SERIOUSLY AS THEY

HAVE TAKEN THE RUSSIANS\' SIMILAR ACTIONS (NOTE: THE POPE

HIMSELF DECRIED THE RUSSIAN VISA REFUSALS IN HIS ANNUAL

ADDRESS TO THE DIPLOMATIC CORPS JANUARY 13).

4. (C) LINGUA MENTIONED THAT THIS MONTH MARKS THE FIVE YEAR

ANNIVERSARY OF THE POPE\'S GROUNDBREAKING VISIT TO CUBA. HE

TOLD US (PROTECT) THAT THE GOC HAD INVITED THE POPE AND/OR FM

TAURAN TO VISIT THE ISLAND TO TAKE PLACE IN COMMEMORATIVE

EVENTS. LINGUA BELIEVED TAURAN WOULD ACCEPT THE INVITATION,

BUT WOULD KEEP THE VISIT LOW-KEY AND TRY TO COMBINE IT WITH

PASTORAL VISITS TO CUBA\'S BELEAGUERED BISHOPS. EMBASSY WILL

CERTAINLY ENCOURAGE TAURAN TO MEET WITH PAYA AND OTHER

DISSIDENTS IF SUCH A VISIT WERE TO TAKE PLACE. WE NOTE FROM

LOCAL PRESS ACCOUNTS AS WELL AS REF B THAT CUBAN CARDINAL

JAIME ORTEGA RECENTLY LAMENTED PUBLICLY IN CUBA THE LACK OF

PROGRESS SINCE THE POPE\'S VISIT, ASSERTING THAT THERE WAS NO

SUBSTANTIAL CHANGE SINCE 1998, THAT SOCIAL-POLITICAL SPACE

REMAINED LIMITED, AND THAT THE CHURCH CONTINUED TO BE

IGNORED.
