SUBJECT: ORAL MESSAGE FROM SECRETARY POWELL TO

ARAB LEAGUE FOREIGN MINISTERS

REF: State 224109

1. (U) Classified by Ambassador Marcelle M. Wahba,

Reasons 1.5 (B) and (D).

2. (C) The Ambassador used the opportunity of an August 4

telcon with de facto Foreign Minister Sheikh Hamdan Bin

Zayid Al-Nahyan, who is out of the country, to deliver a

brief summary of the Secretary\'s oral message related to

Iraq and the Roadmap to Arab League Foreign Ministers (see

reftel).

3. (C) Sh. Hamdan said the UAEG is doing everything

possible to support the situation in Iraq, as well as to

support the Iraqi Governing Council (GC) and the Coalition.

He told the Ambassador that the international community

should realize that there is no alternative to the current

process in Iraq, but that there is a real need to work

together to ensure a successful transition of power in

Iraq. The Ambassador urged the UAEG to back the current

process, saying it would be helpful for public opinion if

there were a public expression of UAEG support for the GC.

Sh. Hamdan concurred, but added that such a public

statement from the UAEG in support of the process would

also convey the importance of moving as rapidly as possible

to form a permanent form of government. He asked the

Ambassador if the US would have any problem with that

approach. The Ambassador told Sh. Hamdan that reference to

a careful transition to a permanent government in Iraq,

coupled with a public recognition of the GC\'s interim role,

would be a balanced statement. Sh. Hamdan requested that

the Embassy send the Secretary\'s talking points to the MFA

to allow his government to consult about issuing a public

statement. The points were delivered before COB Monday.

WAHBA
