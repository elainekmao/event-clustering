SUBJECT: UN FLASH APPEAL FOR HUMANITARIAN RESPONSE IN IRAQ

REF: STATE 89192

1. (U) As requested ref, refcoord contacted UN Resident

Coordinator Christine McNab to ensure that UN officials in

Jordan have raised the UN flash appeal with GOJ officials.

McNab said that UN Humanitarian Coordinator for Iraq Ramiro

Lopes da Silva will be in Jordan April 11-13 and will brief

senior GOJ officials on the appeal at that time.

2. (SBU) Although King Abdullah on April 9 directed the GOJ

"to take all necessary measures to launch a national campaign

to extend humanitarian aid and secure essential medical and

food supplies for the Iraqi people," we do not anticipate

that this campaign will include a contribution to the UN

flash appeal. Instead, the GOJ is likely to contribute

in-kind medical and technical assistance. The Foreign

Minister and Minister of Planning will launch the campaign

later today (April 10); we will report GOJ plans septel.
