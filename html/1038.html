SUBJECT: VIETNAM IDEOLOGICALLY COMMITTED TO STATE-LED ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT, WARY OF DANGERS OF GLOBALIZATION

REF: A) HANOI 637 B) HANOI 232

(U) CLASSIFIED BY POLITICAL COUNSELOR JAMES BRUNO;

REASONS 1.5 (B) AND (D).

SUMMARY

-------

1. (C) SENIOR LEADERS RECENTLY HAVE EMPHASIZED FAMILIAR

IDEOLOGICAL THEMES ABOUT THE COMMUNIST PARTY\'S PREEMINENT

ROLE IN ALL MAJOR POLITICAL AND ECONOMIC DECISION-MAKING.

A FOCUS OF THIS CENTER-LED GOVERNING STYLE IS THE NEED FOR A

STRONG STATE SECTOR TO GUIDE ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT. IN THE

VIEW OF VIETNAM\'S IDEOLOGUES, MARKET REFORMS MUST BE

INCREMENTAL AND SUPPLEMENTAL TO THE STATE-LED BACKBONE OF

THE ECONOMY. RECENT ARTICLES HAVE STRESSED THAT SOCIALIST

VIETNAM\'S HIGHEST PRIORITIES MUST ALWAYS BE TO REDUCE

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 HANOI 00686 01 OF 07 050821Z

POVERTY AND HUNGER. WHILE THESE ARE LAUDABLE GOALS,

VIETNAM\'S COMMAND-STYLE ECONOMIC AND SOCIAL SERVICE SYSTEM

IS SEEN, IN LARGE PART, AS THE MECHANISM TO ACHIEVE THIS,

RATHER THAN ALLOWING GREATER ECONOMIC AND POLITICAL FREEDOM

FOLLOWING A MARKET-ORIENTED PARADIGM. IN THE WORDS OF THE

PRIME MINISTER, VIETNAM IS A POOR COUNTRY THAT "CANNOT

COMPETE" WITH RICH COUNTRIES. VIETNAM CONSTANTLY ARGUES IT

NEEDS MORE AID, DESPITE HAVING AID COMMITMENTS AND PLEDGES

TOTALING 28 PERCENT OF GDP.

2. (C) THOUGH THE PROCESS OF GLOBALIZATION IS ACKNOWLEDGED

TO BE INEVITABLE, SENIOR SRV OFFICIALS SEE IT AS LARGELY

NEGATIVE. THIS IS HIGHLY IRONIC, BECAUSE CERTAIN SECTIONS

OF THE COUNTRY, ESPECIALLY HO CHI MINH CITY, ARE PROFOUNDLY

BENEFITING FROM GROWING TRADE WITH THE REST OF THE WORLD.

IN FACT, MOST VIETNAMESE SEE THE LEADERSHIP\'S MARXIST

SLOGANS AS IRRELEVANT TO THEIR DAILY LIVES. BUT MANY

LEADERS IN HANOI ARE PROFOUNDLY FEARFUL OF HOW THE WEST, AND

PARTICULARLY THE U.S., MAY TAKE ADVANTAGE OF VIETNAM\'S

"WEAKNESSES." THEY SEE UNBRIDLED ECONOMIC REFORM AND

LIBERALIZATION AS DESTABILIZING AND REFER TO SCIENCE AND

TECHNOLOGY AS TOOLS THAT INTERNATIONAL "IMPERIALISM" USES TO

SUBJUGATE POOR COUNTRIES. THEY ALSO FEAR WESTERN EFFORTS TO

IMPOSE "MILITARY SOLUTIONS" IN OTHER COUNTRIES, SUCH AS IN

KOSOVO OR EAST TIMOR. THOUGH THE COUNTRY\'S CONSERVATIVE

IDEOLOGUES CHAMPION THESE VIEWS MOST VIGOROUSLY, IN RECENT

MONTHS EVEN MORE TECHNOCRATIC MEMBERS SUCH AS THE PRIME

MINISTER AND FOREIGN MINISTER HAVE ECHOED THEM. WE SENSE

VIETNAMESE LEADERS ARE EMBOLDENED BY THE STRONG CRITICISM OF

FREE TRADE AT THE SEATTLE WTO AND THE BANGKOK UNCTAD

MEETINGS. THIS HEIGHTENED IDEOLOGICAL RHETORIC SUGGESTS

CONFIDENTIAL

MAJOR ECONOMIC REFORMS ARE UNLIKELY BEFORE NEXT SPRING\'S

PARTY CONGRESS. END SUMMARY.

THE ROLE OF IDEOLOGY IN VIETNAM

-------------------------------

3. (C) THIS IS THE SECOND OF A TWO-PART SERIES ANALYZING THE

IDEOLOGICAL THEMES HIGHLIGHTED IN RECENT SPEECHES AND

PRONOUNCEMENTS BY COMMUNIST PARTY (CPV) LEADERS. THESE

CABLES ANALYZE THEMES RAISED BY PARTY GENERAL SECRETARY LE

KHA PHIEU IN HIS SPEECH ON THE 70TH ANNIVERSARY OF THE

FOUNDING OF THE CPV; BY POLITBURO MEMBERS PHAM THE DUYET AND

NGUYEN PHU TRONG AND PARTY IDEOLOGISTS NHI LE AND NGUYEN

KHANH IN ARTICLES IN THE OFFICIAL COMMUNIST PARTY

THEORETICAL JOURNAL; AND BY PRIME MINISTER PHAN VAN KHAI AND

FOREIGN MINISTER NGUYEN DY NIEN IN MEETINGS WITH FOREIGN

LEADERS. THIS CABLE FOCUSES ON THE CPV\'S CONTINUING

ADHERENCE TO CENTRALLY DIRECTED ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT AND TO

THE POLITICAL, ECONOMIC AND TECHNOLOGICAL DANGERS VIETNAM\'S

LEADERS SEE IN GLOBALIZATION. REF A FOCUSED ON THE

COMMUNIST PARTY\'S ROLE IN GUIDING THE VIETNAMESE STATE.

4. (C) WHILE THESE IDEOLOGICAL PRINCIPLES HAVE BEEN GETTING

INCREASED ATTENTION IN RECENT SPEECHES AND STATEMENTS, IT IS

IMPORTANT TO NOTE THAT AMONG THE POPULATION AT LARGE,

MARXIST-LENINIST IDEOLOGY HAS FEW FOLLOWERS IN VIETNAM. THE

AVERAGE PERSON ON THE STREET FINDS MUCH OF THIS TO BE

"BACKGROUND NOISE" THAT IS IRRELEVANT TO HIS OR HER DAILY

LIFE. IN HO CHI MINH CITY, IN PARTICULAR, MANY PEOPLE ARE

QUICK TO VOICE THEIR DISDAIN FOR THE EMPTY SHIBBOLETHS THAT

ARE SPOUTED BY PARTY IDEOLOGUES. THE PROCESSES OF "DE

FACTO" GLOBALIZATION AND INTERNATIONAL TRADE ARE MOST

ADVANCED IN THE SOUTH, AND PARTY AND GOVERNMENT LEADERS FROM

THAT REGION ARE, AS A GROUP, MUCH MORE OPEN TO ENGAGEMENT

WITH THE REST OF THE WORLD AND ATTUNED TO THE BENEFITS OF

GLOBALIZATION. HOWEVER, IT WOULD BE A MISTAKE TO DISMISS

THE IDEOLOGICAL MANTRAS OF THE PARTY AND GOVERNMENT AS ONLY

EMPTY SLOGANS. MARXIST-LENINIST IDEOLOGY REMAINS IMPORTANT

IN VIETNAM BECAUSE MANY OF THE SENIOR LEADERS EITHER

CONTINUE TO BE TRUE BELIEVERS -- OR THEY HIDE BEHIND THE

RHETORIC AS AN EXCUSE FOR NOT PRESSING FORWARD BRISKLY ON

REFORM. IDEOLOGY IN VIETNAM IS MOST IMPORTANT FOR WHAT IT

SAYS ABOUT THE NARROW CUPOLA OF LEADERS WHO HOLD THE REINS

OF ULTIMATE POLITICAL POWER.

IDEOLOGISTS STRESS STATE-LED DEVELOPMENT AS NECESSARY

FOR SOCIAL STABILITY; MARKET REFORMS GRADUAL AND MEASURED

--------------------------------------------- ------------

5. (C) FROM THEIR OWN PERSPECTIVE, THE COMMUNIST PARTY

LEADERS OF VIETNAM FACE A PROFOUND ECONOMIC DILEMMA: HOW TO

OPEN UP THE ECONOMY AND BENEFIT FROM INCREASED TRADE AND

INVESTMENT WITHOUT RISKING LOSS OF SOCIAL STABILITY AND THE

POTENTIAL LOSS OF THEIR MONOPOLY ON POWER. WE HAVE HEARD

FROM MANY GOVERNMENT OFFICIALS THAT THE DEBATE IN VIETNAM IS

NOT ABOUT WHETHER OR NOT TO MOVE FORWARD -- BUT AT WHAT PACE

TO DO SO. MANY OF VIETNAM\'S SENIOR LEADERS HAVE AN INHERENT

CAUTION ABOUT PROCEEDING "TOO RAPIDLY" WITH ECONOMIC

LIBERALIZATION AND MARKET REFORMS. IN THE ABSENCE OF A

CONSENSUS TO MOVE FORWARD BOLDLY ON REFORM, THE PARTY

PRESERVES ITS PATERNALISTIC, STATE-LED ECONOMIC MODEL THAT

GRADUALLY INTRODUCES GREATER "MARKET CHARACTERISTICS" IN A

CONTROLLED, MEASURED WAY.

6. (C) IN HIS SPEECH COMMEMORATING THE 70TH ANNIVERSARY OF

THE CPV IN FEBRUARY, GENERAL SECRETARY PHIEU EXTOLLED

SOCIALISM AS THE MEANS FOR DEVELOPMENT IN VIETNAM. HE SAID

VIETNAM MUST BUILD "A HIGHLY DEVELOPED ECONOMY WITH MODERN

PRODUCTION FORCES AND WITH STATE OWNERSHIP OF ALL KEY

PRODUCTION AREAS." VIETNAM WILL DEVELOP ALL "ECONOMIC

SECTORS, INCLUDING STATE ENTERPRISES, COOPERATIVES, FAMILY

BUSINESSES, FARMS, PRIVATE COMPANIES, AND JOINT VENTURES

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 04 HANOI 00686 02 OF 07 050822Z

WITH FOREIGN PARTNERS." HE CONTINUED THAT THE STATE ECONOMY

AND THE COOPERATIVE-BASED ECONOMY WILL FORM "A FOUNDATION

FOR OUR NATIONAL ECONOMY." FURTHER PARROTING THE 1992

CONSTITUTION, HE ADDED THAT ALL ECONOMIC SECTORS WILL BE

EQUAL. PHIEU SAID, "IT IS TOTALLY CORRECT WHEN OUR PARTY

EMPHASIZES AND CONSOLIDATES THE LEADING ROLE OF THE STATE

ECONOMY." HE SAID THE STATE SECTOR SHOULD "SERVE AS A

CENTER TO ACCELERATE THE DEVELOPMENT OF OTHER ECONOMIC

SECTORS, MAINTAIN STABILITY DURING COMPLICATED

CIRCUMSTANCES, AND IMPLEMENT THE MARKET-ORIENTED ECONOMY

UNDER STATE MANAGEMENT WITH A SOCIALIST ORIENTATION."

7. (C) PHIEU FURTHER NOTES THAT ELIMINATING HUNGER AND

REDUCING POVERTY ARE KEY ECONOMIC PRIORITIES. BY LAUDING

THE SUCCESSES OF VIETNAM\'S ANTI-POVERTY EFFORTS THUS FAR,

PHIEU SEEMS TO BE ARGUING FOR CONTINUING A FUNDAMENTALLY

CENTRALLY PLANNED MODEL OF POVERTY REDUCTION, RATHER THAN

SEEKING TO PROMOTE PROSPERITY THROUGH GREATER ECONOMIC AND

POLITICAL FREEDOM. (COMMENT: ADMITTEDLY THIS MAY IMPACT

DIFFERENT REGIONS AND SECTORS DIFFERENTLY. END COMMENT.)

PHIEU GOES ON TO URGE THAT VIETNAM EXPAND ITS "ECONOMIC

COOPERATION" WITH OTHER COUNTRIES AND PRODUCE NEW PRODUCTS

OF HIGH QUALITY SO THAT VIETNAM CAN COMPETE WITH OTHER

COUNTRIES. HOWEVER, THE PRIMACY OF THE STATE SECTOR IS

CLEAR IN HIS ECONOMIC WORLDVIEW.

8. (C) SIMILARLY, IN A MARCH ARTICLE OUTLINING VIETNAM\'S

HUMAN RIGHTS POLICY IN THE PARTY NEWSPAPER "THE PEOPLE"

("NHAN DAN"), PARTY FUNCTIONARY PHAM VAN KHANH STRESSED THE

IMPORTANCE OF STATE EFFORTS TO REDUCE POVERTY. "THE MOST

FUNDAMENTAL HUMAN RIGHTS ISSUE IN VIETNAM IS TO MAKE IT

POSSIBLE FOR THE PEOPLE TO HAVE ADEQUATE FOOD, CLOTHES AND

ACCESS TO EDUCATION." KHANH WROTE THAT "PAYING ATTENTION TO

ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT TO RAISE THE ENTIRE PEOPLE\'S LIVING

STANDARDS, IMPLEMENTING POLICIES ON HUNGER ELIMINATION, AND

POVERTY REDUCTION ARE REALLY THE PRIMARY CONCERN OF THE

PARTY AND STATE OF VIETNAM." KHANH SAYS THE SRV IS

PERFECTING ITS SOCIAL SERVICES SYSTEM AND DEVELOPING A

SOCIAL WELFARE NETWORK TO GUARANTEE THESE RIGHTS.

9. (C) COMMENT: VIETNAM HAS RECEIVED GENEROUS SUPPORT AND

STRONG ENCOURAGEMENT FROM THE IFI\'S AND BILATERAL DONORS FOR

THE SUCCESSES OF ITS LARGELY STATE-LED APPROACH TO POVERTY

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 HANOI 00686 03 OF 07 050822Z

REDUCTION. DURING THE VISIT OF WORLD BANK PRESIDENT

WOLFENSOHN IN MARCH -- AND THROUGHOUT VIETNAM\'S ONGOING

ENGAGEMENT WITH LOCAL WORLD BANK, ASIAN DEVELOPMENT BANK,

AND UN AGENCY OFFICIALS -- VIETNAM GETS CONSISTENTLY HIGH

MARKS FOR ITS SUCCESSES IN REDUCING THE POVERTY RATE. GIVEN

THIS ONGOING CHORUS OF PRAISE FROM MANY INTERNATIONAL

DONORS, WHICH IS SUPPORTED TANGIBLY WITH PLEDGES OF MORE

THAN $2 BILLION ANNUALLY IN MULTILATERAL AND BILATERAL

ASSISTANCE, NO WONDER THE GVN FEELS JUSTIFIED IN "STAYING

THE COURSE" WITH A HEAVILY STATE-LED MACROECONOMIC APPROACH

THAT EMPHASIZES POVERTY REDUCTION AND WHICH IS BASED IN

LARGE PART ON ABUNDANT AID FLOWS. END COMMENT.

PLAYING POKER WITH A BAD HAND:

WE NEED AID AND SPECIAL TREATMENT

---------------------------------

10. (C) VIETNAMESE LEADERS ROUTINELY EMPHASIZE THAT VIETNAM

IS A POOR, UNDERDEVELOPED COUNTRY THAT IS APPROACHING GLOBAL

TRADE FROM A POSITION OF WEAKNESS. PHIEU WAS CANDID IN

ADMITTING THAT VIETNAM MUST BE "VIGILANT" IN PROCEEDING WITH

ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT. HE SAID THE SRV FACES "NUMEROUS

OPPONENTS FROM MORE DEVELOPED ECONOMIES, STRONGER POWERS AND

BETTER POSITIONS." OVERALL, THE TONE IS THAT VIETNAM MUST

BE CAUTIOUS AND CAREFUL, LEST IT BE TAKEN ADVANTAGE OF IN

RELATIONS WITH STRONGER POWERS.

11. (C) WHILE DISCUSSING THE U.S.-VIETNAM BILATERAL TRADE

AGREEMENT (BTA) WITH VISITING SECDEF COHEN, PRIME MINISTER

KHAI WAS VERY POINTED IN SAYING THAT VIETNAM CANNOT COMPETE

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 04 HANOI 00686 03 OF 07 050822Z

WITH OTHER COUNTRIES SUCH AS THE U.S. HE SAID THAT, WITHIN

THE BILATERAL TRADE AGREEMENT NEGOTIATIONS, THERE MUST BE

PROVISIONS THAT TAKE INTO ACCOUNT "THE REALITY THAT VIETNAM

IS AN UNDERDEVELOPED COUNTRY." KHAI FURTHER MADE A PITCH

FOR INCREASED ASSISTANCE TO VIETNAM, SAYING THE U.S. "CANNOT

UNDERSTAND THE FULL DIFFICULTIES WE ARE FACING." KHAI MADE

AN APPEAL BASED ON VIETNAM\'S LOSSES OF "MORE THAN 3 MILLION

PERSONS," BOTH MILITARY AND CIVILIAN, DURING WARTIME. HE

SAID THE GVN CONTINUES TO PROVIDE ASSISTANCE TO 1.2 MILLION

FAMILIES WHO LOST RELATIVES DURING THE WAR, AS WELL AS TO

THOSE WHO ALLEGEDLY WERE HARMED BY "CHEMICALS" DURING THE

WAR.

12. (C) POLITBURO MEMBER NGUYEN PHU TRONG, CURRENTLY HEAD OF

THE HANOI PARTY COMMITTEE, RAISED THE THEME OF VIETNAM\'S

WEAKNESS IN A RECENT ARTICLE IN "COMMUNIST REVIEW." TRONG

OBSERVED, "THE NATIONAL AND CLASS STRUGGLES ARE PROCEEDING

IN A VERY INTENSE AND FIERCE MANNER. THE SCIENTIFIC AND

TECHNOLOGICAL REVOLUTION IS DEVELOPING TEMPESTUOUSLY.

MEANWHILE, OUR CAPACITY FOR COMPREHENSION AND INTELLECTUAL

LEVEL ARE LIMITED." TRONG FEARS THAT "HOSTILE FORCES ARE

TRYING TO ATTACK AND UNDERMINE OUR PEOPLE\'S REVOLUTIONARY

CAUSE BY ALL MEANS. THEY HAVE MADE EVERY EFFORT TO

MISREPRESENT AND SLANDER OUR PARTY AND STATE AND DIRECTLY

STRIKE AT THE IDEOLOGICAL SYSTEM, POLITICAL PROGRAM AND

POLITICAL LINE OF OUR PARTY IN AN ATTEMPT TO CAUSE OUR PARTY

TO DISINTEGRATE FROM ITS ROOTS AND FROM WITHIN."

THE DANGERS OF GLOBALIZATION

----------------------------

13. (C) REINFORCING THE NEED FOR A STRONG STATE SECTOR AND

THE RISKS OF ECONOMIC REFORM, IN THE EYES OF SRV LEADERS,

ARE THEIR FEARS ABOUT THE HARMFUL EFFECTS OF GLOBALIZATION.

ON REPEATED OCCASIONS VIETNAMESE LEADERS HAVE VOICED THEIR

CONCERNS ABOUT THE INSTABILITY BROUGHT TO THE REGION BY THE

EAST ASIAN FINANCIAL CRISIS. PARTICULARLY ATTENTION-GETTING

IN HANOI WAS THE IMPACT OF THE REGIONAL CRISIS ON THE

POLITICAL STABILITY OF THE FORMERLY DE FACTO ONE-PARTY STATE

OF INDONESIA. ALSO OF CONCERN WAS THE IMPACT OF THE CRISIS

IN SETTING BACK POVERTY REDUCTION EFFORTS IN THAT COUNTRY

AND OTHERS.

CONFIDENTIAL

14. (C) IN RECENT SPEECHES AND STATEMENTS, SENIOR LEADERS

HAVE SPOKEN OF GLOBALIZATION IN HIGHLY AMBIVALENT WAYS.

THOUGH SEEN AS AN "INEVITABLE" PROCESS, GLOBALIZATION IS

VIEWED AS A LARGELY NEGATIVE PHENOMENON THAT MUST BE MANAGED

CAREFULLY. (COMMENT: THIS IS IN CONTRAST TO HOW MANY IN THE

WEST SEE VIETNAM, BELIEVING THAT GLOBALIZATION PROVIDES

ENORMOUS OPPORTUNITIES FOR A POOR COUNTRY WITH GREAT HUMAN

RESOURCES, LIKE VIETNAM, TO MAKE QUICK PROGRESS

DEVELOPMENTALLY. END COMMENT.)

15. (C) AT THE MEETING OF THE UNITED NATIONS CONFERENCE ON

TRADE AND DEVELOPMENT (UNCTAD) IN BANGKOK FEBRUARY 13, PM

KHAI NOTED THAT THE TRENDS OF GLOBALIZATION AND

REGIONALIZATION OFFER BOTH OPPORTUNITIES AND CHALLENGES FOR

UNDERDEVELOPED COUNTRIES LIKE VIETNAM. KHAI SAID NO

NATIONAL ECONOMY "CAN EXIST INDEPENDENTLY AMIDST THE GROWING

INTERDEPENDENCE OF NATIONAL ECONOMIES." IN ORDER FOR POOR

COUNTRIES TO BE ABLE TO PARTICIPATE IN THE GLOBAL ECONOMY,

THEY NEED SPECIAL TREATMENT. HE OPINED THAT GLOBALIZATION

REQUIRES THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY "TO PAY SPECIAL

ATTENTION TO MAPPING OUT SOLUTIONS THAT BENEFIT POOR AND

BACKWARD COUNTRIES." HE SAID DEVELOPING COUNTRIES ARE "IN

GREAT NEED OF ACTIVE ASSISTANCE FROM DEVELOPED COUNTRIES TO

COMPREHENSIVELY AND EQUALLY ENGAGE IN INTERNATIONAL ECONOMIC

RELATIONS." HE SAID WTO DEVELOPED MEMBERS "SHOULD GIVE

PRIORITY ... TO (CREATING) FAVORABLE CONDITIONS FOR THOSE

COUNTRIES THAT ARE NEGOTIATING FOR WTO MEMBERSHIP." THE

AGENDA OF THE WTO SHOULD CREATE "A MORE FAVORABLE

INTERNATIONAL COOPERATION ENVIRONMENT" AND ALLOW THEM TO

"ENJOY SPECIAL PRIORITY (TREATMENT) WITHOUT ANY KIND OF

CONFIDENTIAL

DISCRIMINATION."

16. (C) IN HIS 70TH ANNIVERSARY SPEECH, GENERAL SECRETARY

PHIEU OUTLINED A PROFOUNDLY NEGATIVE VIEW OF GLOBALIZATION.

AFTER TRACING THE HISTORICAL ROLE OF IMPERIALISM IN

SUBJUGATING PEOPLES IN THE DEVELOPING WORLD, PHIEU GOES ON

TO STATE THAT IMPERIALISM STILL EXISTS, BUT IT HAS ADOPTED

DIFFERENT FORMS. "IMPERIALIST FORCES HAVE EXPANDED THE

WORLD MARKET EVERYWHERE FOR MAXIMUM PROFIT. AT THE SAME

TIME, COUNTRIES WHICH HAVE REGAINED INDEPENDENCE RECENTLY

AFTER A LONG PERIOD OF SLAVERY ARE POOR, BACKWARD, AND

LOOKING FOR WAYS TO DEVELOP." HE SAID THEY NEED CAPITAL,

SCIENCE, HIGH TECHNOLOGY, MANAGEMENT EXPERIENCE,

INTELLECTUALS, AND SKILLED WORKERS. BUT, HE ADDS, "THESE

THINGS ARE IMPOSSIBLE FOR THEM TO OBTAIN UNDER THE

DOMINATION OF IMPERIALISM." PHIEU SAYS THAT DEVELOPING

COUNTRIES MUST DEVISE NEW STRATEGIES "TO DEAL WITH NEW AND

UNFAIR TRICKS PLAYED ON THEM BY THEIR OPPONENTS." (COMMENT:

EVEN IF THIS IS LARGELY IDEOLOGICAL BOILERPLATE FOR THE

PARTY FAITHFUL, IT IS REVEALING FOR ITS EXCESSIVE CONCERN,

EVEN PARANOIA, ABOUT THE HARMFUL EFFECTS OF ENGAGING THE

WORLD. PHIEU\'S VIEW IS THAT GLOBALIZATION IS A ZERO-SUM

GAME, AND VIETNAM IS LIKELY TO GET THE SHORT END OF THE

STICK. IN SEVERAL PLACES, HE VIRTUALLY EQUATES

GLOBALIZATION AND IMPERIALISM. END COMMENT.)

17. (C) TAKING THEIR CUES FROM PARTY LEADERS, GOVERNMENT

OFFICIALS LIKEWISE ARE SOUNDING THESE NOTES, IF NOT AS

HARSHLY AS PHIEU. FOREIGN MINISTER NIEN, ON HIS FIRST

OFFICIAL TRIP TO JAPAN MARCH 10 AFTER ASSUMING HIS POST THE

MONTH BEFORE, URGED JAPANESE FOREIGN MINISTER KONO TO

HIGHLIGHT THE DETRIMENTAL ASPECTS OF ECONOMIC GLOBALIZATION

WHEN JAPAN HOSTS THE GROUP OF EIGHT MEETING IN JULY,

ACCORDING TO A JAPANESE MFA OFFICIAL QUOTED BY THE KYODO

NEWS SERVICE. IN THE SAME WAY, DEPUTY NATIONAL ASSEMBLY

CHAIRMAN VU DINH CU SPOKE OUT MARCH 6 IN THE COMMUNIST PARTY

MOUTHPIECE "NHAN DAN" ("THE PEOPLE") ABOUT THE IMPACT

GLOBALIZATION WOULD HAVE ON VIETNAM. CU SAID "RAPID OPENING

OF THE GLOBAL MARKET" WOULD LEAD TO MASSIVE BANKRUPTCY OF

VIETNAMESE COMPANIES THAT ARE WEAK IN FINANCIAL,

TECHNOLOGICAL AND MANAGEMENT CAPABILITIES. THIS WOULD

RESULT IN GREATLY INCREASED UNEMPLOYMENT, HE SAID.

GLOBALIZATION CREATES MORE POOR PEOPLE

--------------------------------------

18. (C) PRIME MINISTER KHAI TOOK THIS THEME ONE STEP FURTHER

WHEN HE TOLD VISITING SECDEF COHEN MARCH 13 THAT

GLOBALIZATION WILL INCREASE THE NUMBER OF POOR PEOPLE. KHAI

SAID GLOBALIZATION AND INTEGRATION ARE "STILL CONTROVERSIAL

ISSUES" IN VIETNAM. SPEAKING OF HIS ATTENDANCE AT THE

UNCTAD CONFERENCE IN BANGKOK, KHAI SAID THERE WAS A LOT OF

CONTROVERSY BETWEEN RICH AND POOR COUNTRIES, PARTICULARLY

OVER HOW TO BRIDGE THE GAP OF WEALTH BETWEEN THEM. HE ADDED

THAT VIETNAM RECOGNIZES GLOBALIZATION IS "THE INTERNATIONAL

TENDENCY, WHICH WE CANNOT AVOID." BUT HE SAID EACH COUNTRY

NEEDS TO CHOOSE ITS OWN APPROACH TO DEVELOP ITSELF AND RAISE

LIVING STANDARDS.

19. (C) COMMENT: IT IS STRIKING HOW NEGATIVELY EVEN

VIETNAM\'S MORE TECHNOCRATIC/REFORMIST LEADERS, SUCH AS KHAI,

SEE THE PROCESS OF GLOBALIZATION. THEIR WARINESS REINFORCES

THEIR CAUTION, AND CAUSES THE LEADERSHIP AS A GROUP TO FAVOR

SHORT-TERM STABILITY RATHER THAN OPEN UP THE ECONOMY IN WAYS

THAT WOULD PAY LONG-TERM REWARDS. HOWEVER, THE CHALLENGES

ARE DAUNTING: MORE THAN ONE MILLION NEW JOBS NEED TO BE

CREATED EVERY YEAR IN THIS EXTRAORDINARILY YOUNG COUNTRY IN

ORDER TO KEEP UP WITH POPULATION GROWTH. END COMMENT.

20. (C) PREDICTABLY, IN HIS 70TH ANNIVERSARY SPEECH, PHIEU

TOUCHED ON THIS THEME AS WELL. HE STATED, "WHEN IMPERIALISM

ACCELERATES THE LIBERALIZATION OF WORLD TRADE, SERVICE AND

INVESTMENT, RICH COUNTRIES WILL BECOME RICHER, AND THE

A SCREED AGAINST TECHNOLOGY

---------------------------

21. (C) THE MORE IDEOLOGICAL MEMBERS OF THE POLITBURO, SUCH

AS PHIEU AND TRONG, ALSO HAVE RAILED AGAINST DELETERIOUS

ASPECTS OF SCIENTIFIC AND TECHNOLOGICAL ADVANCES. PHIEU\'S

70TH ANNIVERSARY SPEECH WAS THE MOST JARRING, THOUGH THERE

HAVE BEEN ECHOES ELSEWHERE AS WELL (SUCH AS BY TRONG, CITED

PARA 11 ABOVE). COMING DEEP OUT OF LEFT FIELD, PHIEU

CLAIMED THAT "CAPITALISM AND IMPERIALISM WERE BROUGHT TO

LIFE BY THE DEVELOPMENT OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY." HE GOES

ON TO NOTE, POSITIVELY, THAT SCIENTIFIC AND TECHNOLOGICAL

ACHIEVEMENTS IN THE 21ST CENTURY CAN HELP BRING ABOUT BETTER

AND MORE CIVILIZED LIVING CONDITIONS. BUT HERE AGAIN THE

OVERRIDING TONE IS ONE OF CONCERN: "PEOPLE ARE STILL

WORRIED." HISTORY HAS SHOWN, PHIEU SAID, THAT "A HIGHER

LEVEL OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY WILL BRING ABOUT A HIGHER

AND MORE SEVERE LEVEL OF WAR AND ECONOMIC CRISIS."

22. (C) COMMENT: IN A UNIQUE VARIATION ON THE CLASSIC

THEORY, PHIEU SEEMS TO BELIEVE IN AN "INVISIBLE HAND" THAT

IS SOMEHOW AT WORK IN THE GLOBAL TRADING SYSTEM AND

TECHNOLOGICAL TRANSFER TO ATTEMPT TO SUBVERT THE INTERESTS

OF VIETNAM. END COMMENT.

---------------------------------

THE DANGERS OF A MONO-POLAR WORLD

---------------------------------

23. (C) THESE CONCERNS ABOUT THE HARMFUL ASPECTS OF

GLOBALIZATION ECONOMICALLY AND TECHNOLOGICALLY ARE ALSO

REFLECTED IN VIETNAMESE LEADERS\' VIEWS OF INTERNATIONAL

RELATIONS. PHIEU SAID, "AFTER THE COLLAPSE OF THE SOVIET

26. (C) COMMENT: WHEN ONE ANALYZES RECENT SPEECHES AND

STATEMENTS BY VIETNAMESE LEADERS, ONE IS STRUCK BY THE

STRONG UNDERCURRENT OF IDEOLOGICAL RHETORIC. AT ONE LEVEL,

THIS CAN BE ASCRIBED TO VIETNAM\'S "PRIMARY SEASON" IN THE

RUN-UP TO THE ELECTIONS FOR NEXT SPRING\'S NINTH PARTY

CONGRESS. HOWEVER, THIS RATCHETING UP OF IDEOLOGICAL THEMES

SHOULD NOT BE DISMISSED LIGHTLY. WHILE AT A SURFACE LEVEL

VIETNAM\'S LEADERS SAY THEY DO NOT WANT TO FOCUS ON THE

LEGACY OF THE WAR-TIME YEARS, THE HISTORY OF CONFLICT THREE

DECADES AGO CONTINUES TO PROFOUNDLY SHAPE THE WORLDVIEW OF

THE COUNTRY\'S SENIOR LEADERS. MANY OF THEM ARE FEARFUL AND

SUSPICIOUS OF WESTERN, AND ESPECIALLY AMERICAN, INTENTIONS.

27. (C) COMMENT (CONT\'D): AS WE CONTINUE TO PRESS FORWARD

SEEKING A BILATERAL TRADE AGREEMENT AND OTHER MAJOR REFORMS,

WE NEED TO REMEMBER THAT MANY SENIOR LEADERS ARE DEEPLY

DISTRUSTFUL OF THE U.S. THEIR FUNDAMENTAL CAUTION AND

CONSENSUS-ORIENTED NATURE, COMBINED WITH A GENUINE FEAR OF

BEING HOODWINKED BY THE WEST, COMBINE TO DISCOURAGE MAJOR

PROGRESS ON ECONOMIC REFORM. THOUGH WE HAVE MADE GREAT

STRIDES IN BUILDING CONSTRUCTIVE RELATIONS WITH WORKING-

LEVEL SRV OFFICIALS AND INCREASING THEIR APPRECIATION FOR

THE BENEFITS OF ENGAGING THE GLOBAL COMMUNITY, THE SECRETIVE

AND INSULAR POLITBURO IS ANOTHER STORY. IN THE SEAT OF

ULTIMATE POLITICAL POWER, PARTY VETERANS CONTINUE TO USE

IDEOLOGICAL SLOGANS TO RESIST MOVING FORWARD ON REFORM.
