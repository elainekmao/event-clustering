SUBJECT: STEP FORWARD FOR CYBER-SECURITY IN ITALY

REFS: A) ROME 02533 B) ROME 03437

1. LESS THAN THREE MONTHS AFTER OUR BILATERAL

INITIATIVE ON THE SUBJECT, THE GOI IS TAKING A STEP

FORWARD TO BETTER PROTECT KEY GOVERNMENT COMPUTER

SYSTEMS FROM ATTACK (REF A). POST HAS LEARNED THAT

ITALY IS CREATING A COMMITTEE TO COORDINATE AND LEAD

PUBLIC SECTOR INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (IT) PROTECTION

ACTIVITIES. THE COMMITTEE WILL BE A JOINT-VENTURE

BETWEEN THE INNOVATION MINISTRY AND THE COMMUNICATIONS
