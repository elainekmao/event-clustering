SUBJECT: KUWAITI GOVT FUNDING HAMAS: "NOT ONE PENNY"

(U) CLASSIFIED BY THE AMBASSADOR. REASON 1.5. D.

1. (C) DURING A CALL BY CONGRESSMAN ROHRABACHER ON FONMIN

SABAH TODAY, THE CONGRESSMAN TOLD SHAYKH SABAH THAT CHAIRMAN ARAFAT

HAD TOLD A SENIOR CONGRESSMAN YESTERDAY IN WASHINGTON THAT THE

KUWAITI GOVERNMENT WAS PROVIDING FUNDING FOR HAMAS.

2. (C) SHAYKH SABAH RESPONDED WITH SOME EMOTION, UNDERLINING THAT HE

COULD ASSURE US THAT "NOT ONE PENNY" OF KUWAITI GOVERNMENT FUNDS HAD

GONE TO HAMAS. HE ADDED, HOWEVER, THAT HE COULD PROVIDE NO SUCH

ASSURANCE OF KUWAITI PRIVATE SUPPORT FOR HAMAS, WHICH COULD NOT REALLY

BE CONTROLLED BY THE GOK. HE NOTED THAT KUWAITIS PROVIDED FUNDING NOT

SO MUCH OUT OF SYMPATHY FOR HAMAS AS OUT OF HATRED FOR ARAFAT FOR HIS

STAND DURING THE GULF WAR.

3. (C) COMMENT: AS WE REPORTED DURING SHAYKH YASSIN\'S VISIT

HERE EARLIER THIS YEAR, CONSIDERABLE KUWAITI PRIVATE FUND RAISING

FOR HAMAS DOES OCCUR, BUT WE ALSO HAVE NO EVIDENCE OF OFFICIAL

FUNDS GOING TO HIM. (IT IS WORTH NOTING ALSO, HOWEVER, THAT SHAYKH

SABAH IS NO FRIEND OF HAMAS AND TRIED UNSUCCESSFULLY TO BLOCK

YASSIN\'S VISIT.)
