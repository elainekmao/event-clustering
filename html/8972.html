SUBJECT: SHAYKH ZAYID BESTOWS THE EMIRATES\'

MILITARY ORDER ON CENTCOM COMMANDER

GENERAL FRANKS

1. (U) Classified by Ambassador Marcelle M. Wahba

for reasons 1.5 (B) and (D).

2. (SBU) UAE President Shaykh Zayid bin Sultan Al-

Nahyan conferred upon CENTCOM Commander General

Tommy R. Franks the Emirates Military Order of

First Class in a meeting on June 24 at Al-Bahr

Palace in Abu Dhabi. General Franks is the first

U.S. official Shaykh Zayid has met with since the

March 2002 visit of Vice President Cheney. The

indomitable Zayid was in fine form with General

Franks, drawing upon his core religious values and

fundamental life philosophies in a brief but very

warm discussion. Zayid looked hale and hearty;

many of his 19 sons, including Chief of Staff

Muhammad, MFA Minstate Hamdan, InfoMin Abdullah and

Deputy Prime Minister Sultan, were in attendance.

General Franks was joined by Ambassador Wahba,

Ambassador Litt, and an accompanying delegation

from Franks\' staff and the Embassy.

3. (C) General Franks thanked Shaykh Zayid for the

UAE\'s generous humanitarian contributions to both

Afghanistan and Iraq. He praised Zayid\'s

leadership in developing the UAE into a model to be

emulated in the region, noting that while Zayid

used the last 30 years to build his country, others

had spent that time destroying their countries.

Zayid spoke with pride about the UAE\'s progress,

noting that 30 years ago, the government had to

bring in tens of thousands of foreigners to teach

Emirati children. With the literacy rate now at

over 90 percent, Emiratis themselves are taking up

the teaching profession.

4. (C) Referring to the May 12 terror bombings in

Riyadh, Zayid denounced those who called themselves

Muslims but engaged in abominable acts. As Zayid

put it, these "brutal beasts" do not represent

Islam and should be cast out of the faith.

5. (C) COMMENT: This largely ceremonial meeting

was a testament to the great admiration and respect

felt for General Franks and the U.S. by Shaykh

Zayid and the senior Emirati leadership. END

COMMENT.

WAHBA
