3. MISSION\'S OVERRIDING GOALS

-----------------------------

MISSION\'S OVERRIDING GOALS, AS SET FORTH IN REFTEL:

-- MEETING EMERGENCY AND LONGER-TERM HUMANITARIAN

RELIEF AND REFUGEE PROTECTION NEEDS IN CROATIA AND

BOSNIA;

-

-- A HALT TO MILITARY SKIRMISHES, AND THE AVOIDANCE OF

FULL-SCALE WAR, IN AND OVER THE KRAJINA, AND THE

PEACEFUL REINTEGRATION OF THE UNPAS INTO CROATIA;

-- AN END TO HUMAN RIGHTS ABUSES BY BOSNIAN CROAT

FORCES AND THE EARLY PROSECUTION OF PERSONS RESPONSIBLE

FOR COMMITTING WAR CRIMES;

-- A MORE DEMOCRATIC CROATIA, MARKED BY A VIGOROUSLY

PLURALISTIC SYSTEM, FREE MEDIA, THE RULE OF LAW, AND

RESPECT FOR THE RIGHTS AND DIGNITY OF MINORITIES;

-- PROVIDING THE HIGHEST STANDARD OF CONSULAR SERVICE,

CONFIDENTIAL

CONFIDENTIAL

PARTICULARLY IN AREAS RELATED TO THE HUMANITARIAN

TRAGEDY IN EX-YUGOSLAVIA, INCLUDING VISAS, PAROLE,

REFUGEE PROCESSING, AND MEDICAL AND OTHER HUMANITARIAN

EVACUATION.

-- AN ECONOMICALLY TRANSFORMED CROATIA, WHICH REFLECTS

PRIVATE OWNERSHIP, FREE MARKETS, A STABLE CURRENCY, AND

MINIMAL BARRIERS TO TRADE AND INVESTMENT;

-- STEADILY INCREASING SALES OF NON-MILITARY U.S. GOODS

AND SERVICES IN CROATIA.

-- IN ADDITION (AND NOT IN REFTEL), THE MISSION\'S

RESPONSIBILITY TO REPRESENT THE U.S. IN THE CONDUCT AND

SUPPORT OF THE REINTEGRATION TALKS BETWEEN CROATIA AND

ITS SECESSIONIST SERBIAN MINORITY HAS BECOME OUR MAJOR

PRIORITY
