SUBJECT: USG OPPOSITION TO EU PROPOSAL TO

MODIFY GRAIN IMPORT REGIME DISCUSSED WITH

BELGIAN TRADE MINISTRY

1. ITALY\'S AGMIN PARTICIPANT IN THE 133

COMMITTEE MEETING AND SUBSEQUENT SPECIAL

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 02 ROME 03422 111258Z

COMMITTEE ON AGRICULTURE REPORTS THAT THE

COMMISSION\'S PROPOSAL TO RECTIFY THE MARGIN

OF PREFERENCE FOR GRAINS WAS SOLIDLY

REJECTED BY MOST, IF NOT ALL DELEGATIONS,

ALBEIT FOR DIFFERENT REASONS. ITALY EXPECTS

THAT THE COMMISSION WILL HAVE TO GO BACK TO

THE DRAWING BOARD.

2. ITALY PREFERS TO MAINTAIN THE CURRENT

MARGIN OF PREFERENCE SYSTEM WHILE TAKING

SEPARATE ACTION AGAINST GRAIN IMPORTS FROM

THE BLACK SEA REGION. ITALY SEES THIS AS AN

EXPEDIENT AND VIABLE OPTION GIVEN NEITHER

UKRAINE NOR RUSSIA ARE WTO MEMBERS. TARIFF

RATE QUOTAS ARE NOT THE ANSWER ACCORDING TO

OUR CONTACT. THIS IS CONSISTENT WITH

ITALY\'S STATUS AS A LARGE NET IMPORTER OF

HIGH-QUALITY WHEAT.

3. WITH RESPECT TO RICE, ITALY BELIEVES THE

EU MADE A MISTAKE AT THE END OF THE URUGUAY

ROUND WHEN IT CALCULATED THE IMPORT DUTY FOR

RICE ON A PADDY BASIS RATHER THAN ON A ROUGH

BASIS, AND THAT THIS ERROR SHOULD BE

CORRECTED. THAT SAID, ITALY TOLD THE 133

COMMITTEE THAT RECTIFICATION OF THE RICE

DUTY SHOULD NOT BE TIED TO ACTION ON THE MOP

FOR GRAINS. SEMBLER
