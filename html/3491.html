SUBJECT: AMBASSADOR TALKS TRADE, STEEL AND BIOTECH WITH

VICE MINISTER URSO

UNCLASSIFIED

1. (U) THE AMBASSADOR MET WITH VICE MINISTER FOR FOREIGN

TRADE URSO ON AUGUST 8. AFTER DISCUSSION OF THE CONTAINER

SECURITY INITIATIVE (SEPTEL), THE TWO DISCUSSED THE STATUS OF

SEVERAL KEY TRADE ISSUES.

2. (U) FAST TRACK: URSO WAS PLEASED WITH THE PASSAGE OF THE

FAST TRACK LEGISLATION IN THE U.S., CALLING IT "VERY GOOD

NEWS." HE EXPRESSED THE HOPE THAT THE GREATER FLEXIBILITY

WOULD FACILITATE WTO NEGOTIATIONS.

3. (SBU) FSC: URSO HOPED THAT MODIFICATIONS OF U.S. FSC

LEGISLATION COULD BE FINISHED SOON. HE WAS CONCERNED THAT

PRESSURE WAS BUILDING FOR A DECISION ON RETALIATION. THE

AMBASSADOR RECALLED THAT THE U.S. HAD CHANGED OUR LEGISLATION

ONCE ALREADY, IN ORDER TO MEET THE CONCERNS OF OUR TRADING

PARTNERS. WE HAD LOST THE WTO CASE AND WOULD COMPLY WITH THE

WTO RULING.

4. (SBU) STEEL: URSO TOLD THE AMBASSADOR THAT HE HAD WRITTEN

TO COMMISSIONER LAMY TO EXPRESS DISAPPOINTMENT THAT NO

ITALIAN STEEL EXEMPTIONS HAVE YET BEEN GRANTED BY THE U.S.,

AND DECLARED THAT "THIS IS A SERIOUS ISSUE FOR US.8 HE

NOTED THAT OTHER EUROPEAN COUNTRIES HAVE RECEIVED STEEL

EXEMPTIONS. THE AMBASSADOR ASKED URSO TO SEND A LIST OF

ITALIAN STEEL EXEMPTION REQUESTS, SO THAT WE COULD ENSURE

THAT USTR HAS THIS INFORMATION.

5. (SBU) GMOS: URSO DECLARED THAT, IN HIS VIEW, SO LONG AS

GMOS WERE LABELED, AND THERE WERE TRACEABILITY STANDARDS,

THERE WAS NO REASON TO KEEP GMOS OUT. THE AMBASSADOR

RESPONDED THAT AGRICULTURE MINISTER ALEMANNO HAD A VERY

DIFFERENT POLICY. HE HAD DECLARED A ZERO THRESHOLD FOR GMOS.

ALEMANNO POINTS TO PUBLIC OPINION POLLS TO DEMONSTRATE THAT

THE MAJORITY OF ITALIANS FAVOR HIS ANTI-GMO POLICY. URSO

NOTED THAT HE AND MINISTER ALEMANNO WERE OF THE SAME PARTY,

AND HAD KNOWN EACH OTHER FOR 30 YEARS. HOWEVER, HE

EMPHASIZED, THEY DISAGREED ON THIS ISSUE. &IF I WERE THE

MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE, I WOULD END AGRICULTURAL SUBSIDIES

AND OPEN OUR BORDERS TO ALL AGRICULTURE IMPORTS, PARTICULARLY

FROM THE THIRD WORLD. THIS WOULD ALLOW DEVELOPING COUNTRIES

TO GROW ECONOMICALLY AND AFFORD OUR NON-AGRICULTURAL

EXPORTS.8 URSO NOTED THAT HE WAS WRITING A BOOK ABOUT HIS

TRADE VIEWS, WHICH WOULD BE PUBLISHED LATER IN THE YEAR.

6. (SBU) COMMENT: VICE MINISTER URSO IS A SENIOR OFFICIAL

OF THE AN, CLOSE TO DEPUTY PRIME MINISTER FINI. AGRICULTURE

MINISTER ALEMANNO, ALSO OF AN, COMES FROM THE RIVAL WING OF

THE PARTY AND IS A PROTEGE OF LAZIO PROVINCE GOVERNOR

STORACE.
