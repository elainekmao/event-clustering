SUBJECT: INITIATION OF COMMERCIAL SUPPLY LOC

Classified by DCM Robert S. Deutsch; reasons 1.5 b and d.

(C) Ambassador spoke to MFA U/S Ziyal 1830 local April 4.

Ziyal said there was no impediment to commercial supplies and

civilian trucks moving to Habur Gate and no problem with

crossing the border into Northern Iraq. Based on that

information, Ambassador informed Task Force North that they

could initiate the movement of trucks and supplies into Iraq.

Embassy will monitor the first movements of trucks to make

sure there are no hitches in the transport of supplies.
