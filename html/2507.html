SUBJECT: PROPOSED ECONOMIC/FINANCIAL SYSTEM REGULATORY

CHANGES

REF: 01 ROME 2847

1. (SBU) SUMMARY: THE ITALIAN PARLIAMENT IS

CONSIDERING DRAFT LEGISLATION TO CHANGE THE NATURE OF

ITALY\'S INDEPENDENT GOVERNMENTAL AUTHORITIES. WHILE

ONLY IN ITS EARLIEST STAGES WITHIN THE PARLIAMENT, THE

PROPOSAL WOULD TURN THE BANK OF ITALY (ITALY\'S CENTRAL

BANK) INTO A RESEARCH INSTITUTION AND TRANSFER ITS

FUNCTIONS TO INDEPENDENT AUTHORITIES. THE ITALIAN

SECURITIES, INSURANCE AND PENSION FUND REGULATORS WOULD

BE ABOLISHED AND REPLACED BY INDEPENDENT AUTHORITIES.

THIS COULD MEAN A DANGEROUS WEAKENING OF REGULATORY

OVERSIGHT, IF APPROVED, AND HAVE IMPLICATIONS FOR U.S.

BANKS AND OTHER FIRMS. WE WILL WATCH CAREFULLY AS THE

PROPOSAL DEVELOPS. END SUMMARY.

PARLIAMENTARY PROPOSAL

----------------------

2. (SBU) ON JANUARY 24 BRUNO TABACCI, PRESIDENT OF THE

PRODUCTIVE ACTIVITIES COMMISSION (AND MEMBER OF CCD-

CDU: DEMOCRATIC CHRISTIAN CENTER/UNITED CHRISTIAN

DEMOCRATS - PART OF THE GOVERNMENT COALITION) PRESENTED

DRAFT LEGISLATION TO REFORM ITALY\'S INDEPENDENT

AUTHORITIES. IF APPROVED, THE LEGISLATION WOULD

CONSTITUTE A MAJOR REVOLUTION IN THE STRUCTURE OF

ITALY\'S ECONOMIC REGULATORY SYSTEM. ARTICLE 4 OF THE

BILL WOULD ABOLISH CONSOB (ITALY\'S SEC EQUIVALENT),

ISVAP (THE SUPERVISORY BODY FOR THE INSURANCE SECTOR),

COVIP (THE SUPERVISORY BODY FOR PENSION FUNDS) AND

CREATE A NEW AUTHORITY TO SUPERVISE FINANCIAL MARKETS.

THIS NEW AUTHORITY WOULD STRIP FROM THE BANK OF ITALY

(BOI - ITALY\'S CENTRAL BANK) ITS BANK SUPERVISION

POWERS. THE ANTITRUST AUTHORITY WOULD TAKE OVER THE

BOI\'S CONTROL OF FINANCIAL SECTOR COMPETITION. THE BANK

OF ITALY WOULD EXIST ONLY AS A RESEARCH OFFICE.

3. (SBU) THE SAME BILL PROPOSES THE TRANSFER OF

COMMUNICATIONS AUTHORITY FUNCTIONS TO A NEW AUTHORITY

FOR INFORMATION AND THE TRANSFER OF ENERGY AUTHORITY

FUNCTIONS TO A NEW AUTHORITY FOR NETWORK SERVICES.

POLITICAL DEBATE

----------------

4. (SBU) TABACCI\'S PROPOSAL TRIGGERED STRONG OPPOSITION

FROM THE PARTY OF DEPUTY PRIME MINISTER FINI (NATIONAL

ALLIANCE (AN) - ALSO WITHIN THE GOVERNMENT COALITION).

THE PRESIDENT OF THE PUBLIC WORKS COMMISSION, PIETRO

ARMANI (AN), TERMED THE PROPOSAL "UNACCEPTABLE". THE

DEPUTY MINISTER OF PRODUCTIVE ACTIVITIES ADOLFO URSO

(AN) OPPOSED ANY CHANGE IN BOI FUNCTIONS AND POWERS.

FORMER PRESIDENT COSSIGA SAID THAT EFFECTIVE OVERSIGHT

IS A CRUCIAL ISSUE AND IT WAS GOOD THAT TABACCI RAISED

IT. COSSIGA NOTED, HOWEVER, THAT ONLY IN SIX OF THE 12

COUNTRIES OF EURO AREA (GREECE, IRELAND, ITALY,

NETHERLANDS, PORTUGAL AND SPAIN) DO CENTRAL BANKS

5. (SBU) CENTER-LEFT LEADERS ENRICO LETTA (FORMER

PARLIAMENTARIANS TO SUPPORT IT AS WELL.

6. (SBU) MINISTER OF PUBLIC ADMINISTRATION FRATTINI NOW

HAS THE RIGHT TO PROPOSE ALTERNATIVE LEGISLATION. HE

COULD INSIST THAT THERE BE NO CHANGE IN THE POWERS OF

THE BANK OF ITALY. BY THE END OF FEBRUARY, THE

GOVERNMENT COMMISSION IN CHARGE OF REFORMING THE

AUTHORITIES WILL PRESENT ITS FINAL REPORT.

OPERATIONS OF THESE INTERMEDIARIES. THE BOI IS ALSO

RESPONSIBLE FOR ENSURING COMPETITION IN THE BANKING

SECTOR, IN COORDINATION WITH THE ANTITRUST AUTHORITY.
