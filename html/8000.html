SUBJECT: IRAQ: TURKISH DELEGATION TO TRAVEL TO IRAQ TO

MEET WITH KDP/PUK, TURKMEN

REF: ANKARA 2982

(U) Classified by Acting Deputy Chief of Mission Scot

Marciel. Reasons: 1.5 (B and D).

1. (C) MFA Deputy Director General for Middle East Affairs

Safak Gokturk informed us May 12 that Turkey intends to send

a three-person "specialized" team to northern Iraq (Mosul,

Irbil and Kirkuk) to meet with KDP, PUK and Turkmen

leadership. The team is planning to fly from Ankara (via

Turkish Airlines) to either Diyarbakir or Mardin (plans still

being coordinated) and cross the Habur Gate (via ground

transportation) into northern Iraq May 14 in the morning

(Ankara time). The MFA already has informed Ankara-based KDP

and PUK reps, and asked if Barzani and Talabani would be

available. Gokturk said the team also intends to travel to

Baghdad to meet with newly arrived TU embassy staff.

2. (C) The team consists of:

--AMB Selim Karaosmanoglu: MFA Director General for the

Balkans. MFA\'s "political coordinator for Iraq." Formerly

Turkey\'s ambassador to Iraq before Mehmet Akat (who served

before current Amb. Osman Paksut).

--AMB Ecvet Tezcan: MFA Director General for Intelligence

and Research (INR). Former TU Amb. in Baku and DDG for INR

(when INR had the lead for northern Iraq issues).

--Kerim Uras: MFA Department Head for Iraq.

3. (C) Gokturk asked us to convey this information to US

military authorities in Iraq. He promised to provide

additional details (e.g. automobile license plates,

personnel, equipment, etc.) as the MFA had done when Turkish

Amb. Paksut\'s group crossed Habur Gate last week (reftel).
