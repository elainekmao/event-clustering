2. ON THE DAY AFTER U.S. RECOGNITION OF THE

REPUBLICS OF CROATIA AND SLOVENIA, WE BELIEVE IT

WOULD BE HELPFUL TO CATCH OUR BREATH AND TAKE AN

ACCOUNTING OF WHERE WE ARE, HOW WE GOT TO THIS POINT

AND SOME EARLY IMPRESSIONS ON HOW WE SEE OUR

RELATIONS DEVELOPING WITH THESE NEW EUROPEAN

CONFIDENTIAL

CONFIDENTIAL

NATIONS. IN THE CASE OF SLOVENIA, OUR VIEW IS THAT,

WITHOUT STRONG, OUTWARD-LOOKING LEADERSHIP, THE TINY

REPUBLIC COULD CONCEIVABLY FALL OFF THE RADAR SCREEN

(ONCE THE YUGOSLAV CRISIS PASSES WHICH WILL OBVIOUSLY

NOT OCCUR ANYTIME SOON). DESTINED TO FALL PREY TO A

GERMAN-AUSTRIAN-ITALIAN SUZERAINTY BY VIRTUE OF ITS

SIZE AND GEOGRAPHY, SLOVENES ARE ALREADY EXPRESSING

PREVIOUSLY INDETECTABLE ANGST OVER DOMINATION BY

THEIR POWERFUL NEIGHBORS. REGRETTABLY, WE CONCLUDE

THAT THIS ECONOMIC TURN OF EVENTS IS UNAVOIDABLE. WE

WOULD ALSO NOT BE SURPRISED IF SLOVENIA TOOK THE LEAD

IN TRYING TO REBUILD YUGOSLAV ECONOMIC LINKAGES.

WITH SLOVENIA NOT POSING A THREAT TO ITS NEIGHBORS,

ITS NASCENT DEMOCRACY NOT AT RISK AND NO HUMAN RIGHTS

PROBLEMS, THE U.S. HAS NO STRATEGIC INTERESTS AT

STAKE.

3. CROATIA, ON THE OTHER HAND, WILL REMAIN OF KEEN

INTEREST TO U.S. POLICY-MAKERS. CROATIAN-SERBIAN

RELATIONS COULD WELL BE THE KEY ISSUE IN THE BALKANS

FOR YEARS TO COME. ALWAYS SLOW OFF THE MARK

VIS-A-VIS THE SLOVENES, THE CROATS WERE LESS PREPARED

FOR THIS HISTORIC STEP. ITS DEMOCRACY IS MORE

FRAGILE THAN THAT OF ITS NORTHERN NEIGHBOR, WITH

MAJOR ISSUES OF PRESS FREEDOMS, A FAIR AND IMPARTIAL

LEGAL SYSTEM, AND TROUBLESOME DRIFT OF THE POLITICAL

CENTER OF GRAVITY TO THE RIGHT. CROATIAN PRESIDENT

TUDJMAN, AS THE MAN WHO DELIVERED ON INTERNATIONAL

RECOGNITION, SEEMS A SURE BET FOR A SECOND TERM. HE

WILL FACE THE TOUGH TASK OF DISMANTLING, OR LEAST

DIMINISHING THE IMPACT, OF A SECURITY AND MILITARY

SLOVENIA: FROM LEADING ROLE TO BIT PLAYER

-----------------------------------------

4. WHEN THE HISTORY OF THE BREAKUP OF YUGOSLAVIA IS

FINALLY WRITTEN, ONE OF THE THE SMALLEST REPUBLICS IN

THE FORMER FEDERATION WILL BE IDENTIFIED AS HAVING

PLAYED THE KEY ROLE IN MOVING YUGOSLAVIA WESTWARD

TOWARD DEMOCRACY, A TREND WHICH WOULD ULTIMATELY SET

THE STAGE FOR THE COUNTRY\'S DEMISE. WITH ITS

CONFIDENTIAL

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 ZAGREB 00527 02 OF 07 091303Z

REPUBLIC-LEVEL ELECTIONS IN 1990, SLOVENIA CONDUCTED

THE FIRST FREE BALLOTING IN YUGOSLAVIA, A WATERSHED

EVENT THAT WOULD ULTIMATELY LEAD TO CALLS IN THE

OTHER REPUBLICS FOR FREE ELECTIONS. IN A DOGGED AND

DETERMINED FASHION WHICH FLOWS NATURALLY FROM THE

SLOVENE NATIONAL CHARACTER, THE NORTHERN REPUBLIC SET

A COURSE TOWARD DEMOCRACY BUT ALSO AWAY FROM

YUGOSLAVIA AND SIMPLY REFUSED TO BE TURNED AROUND.

5. TO REACH ITS GOAL, SLOVENIA FORGED AN ALLIANCE OF

CONVENIENCE WITH CROATIA TO CHALLENGE A RECALCITRANT,

CENTRALIST FEDERAL SYSTEM. WHETHER THE INTENTION

FROM THE BEGINNING WAS TO BREAK UP YUGOSLAVIA AS IT

THEN EXISTED WILL NEVER BE KNOWN (WE SUSPECT THAT IT

ALWAYS WAS). SLOVENE HISTORY WILL RECALL THAT

BELGRADE WAS NEVER WILLING TO ENTERTAIN THE IDEA OF

RESTRUCTURING YUGOSLAVIA INTO THE MORE DEMOCRATIC,

LOOSE CONFEDERATION OF SOVEREIGN STATES PROPOSED BY

SLOVENIA AND SUPPORTED BY ITS NOMINAL ALLY, CROATIA.

THE HOLLOW TALKS THAT WERE HELD THROUGHOUT THE LATTER

HALF OF 1990 AND THE FIRST HALF OF 1991 BETWEEN THE

SIX REPUBLICS LED INEXORABLY TO WHERE SLOVENIA WANTED

TO BE FROM THE BEGINNING, THE DECLARATION OF AN

INDEPENDENT AND SOVEREIGN REPUBLIC IN JUNE, 1991.

6. ODDLY, SINCE ITS INDEPENDENCE DECLARATION AND

BRIEF WAR OF "LIBERATION", SLOVENIA\'S ROLE IN THE

CONTINUING UNRAVELLING OF YUGOSLAVIA HAS DIMINISHED

TO THAT COMMENSURATE WITH ITS SIZE. WITH BROAD

RECOGNITION IN THE BAG, THE SLOVENES\' INTEREST IN THE

BRUSSELS PROCESS IS NOW CONFINED TO SUCCESSION STATE

7. ALTHOUGH CURRENTLY STRAPPED IN A POLITICAL

STRAITJACKET AND UNABLE TO BREAK FREE, DEMOCRACY IS

ALIVE AND WELL IN SLOVENIA. AT SOME POINT, A NEW

ELECTION LAW WILL BE PASSED BY THE SLOVENE ASSEMBLY

AND A NEW GOVERNMENT COMMITTED TO DEMOCRATIC

PRINCIPLES AND A MARKET ECONOMY WILL BE SELECTED TO

RUN THE COUNTRY.

-

8. WITH DEMOCRACY ITSELF NOT AT RISK OR ENDANGERED,

SLOVENES ARE OBSESSIVELY CONCERNED WITH WHAT KIND OF

COUNTRY AN INDEPENDENT SLOVENIA WILL BECOME.

DEMONSTRATING AN ANGST NOT PREVIOUSLY NOTICEABLE,

SLOVENE OFFICIALS AND ORDINARY CITIZENS HAVE

CONFIDENTIAL

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 ZAGREB 00527 03 OF 07 091304Z

EXPRESSED DEEP SUSPICION OF THE GROWING INFLUENCE OF

GERMANY, EVEN NOTING THE SUDDEN INFLUX OF GERMAN

MARKS USED IN THE PURCHASE OF PREVIOUSLY SOCIALLY-

OWNED APARTMENTS.

9. ULTIMATELY SLOVENIA\'S DESTINY WILL BE DETERMINED

BY ITS LOCATION. WITH NO HINTERLAND TO SPEAK OF, WE

ENVISION A SLOVENIA THAT WILL OF NECESSITY BECOME

"REGIONALIZED." THE INDUSTRIALIZED NORTH (MARIBOR)

WILL NEED LARGE CAPITAL INVESTMENTS TO MODERNIZE AND

TRANSFORM ITS FACTORIES AND PLANTS. VIRTUALLY A

SUBURB OF AUSTRIA\'S SECOND LARGEST CITY (GRAZ),

NORTHERN SLOVENIA, WHICH IS ALREADY SEEKING MORE

LOCAL AUTONOMY FROM A FARAWAY (IN THE SLOVENE

CONTEXT) LJUBLJANA, WILL ALMOST BY INERTIA FALL INTO

THE SPHERE OF INFLUENCE OF SOUTHERN AUSTRIA. TO THE

WEST, PARTICULARLY ISTRIA, ITALY IS KING. SLOVENES

(AND CROATS, TOO) FROM THAT REGION DO NOT HESITATE IN

IDENTIFYING WITH THEIR ITALIAN ROOTS. THIS WEEK,

SLOVENIAN (AND CROATIAN) POLITICAL PARTIES MET WITH

PARTIES FROM ACROSS THE BORDER TO ANNOUNCE THAT THEY

WOULD SEEK TO "REGIONALIZE" THEIR ACTIVITIES AND

PURSUE COMMON POLITICAL GOALS.

10. IF SLOVENIA IS TO MAINTAIN SOME CONTROL OVER ITS

ECONOMIC DESTINY, RESTORING SOME SEMBLANCE OF THE

YUGOSLAV MARKET WOULD BE THE MOST EFFECTIVE WAY.

FROM THE BEGINNING, IDEOLOGY HAS PLAYED LITTLE OR NO

ROLE IN THE DRIVE FOR INDEPENDENCE. ONCE A WAY OUT

OF THE CURRENT POLITICAL AND MILITARY MORASS HAS BEEN

CHARTED, WE COULD ENVISION THE SLOVENES WORKING

CONFIDENTIAL

SERBIA AND THE OTHER FORMER YUGOSLAV REPUBLICS.

UNLIKE CROATIA, THOSE REPUBLICS\' ECONOMIES ARE MUCH

MORE COMPLEMENTARY TO, NOT COMPETITIVE WITH, THE

SLOVENE ECONOMY.

U.S.-SLOVENE RELATIONS

----------------------

11. AS PRESIDENT KUCAN TOLD THE CG ON APRIL 8 WHEN

HE PRESENTED OUR LETTER OF RECOGNITION, SLOVENIA

EXPECTS TO HAVE THE MOST CORDIAL RELATIONS WITH THE

U.S. FROM THE START. WE SEE NO REASON TO QUESTION

THAT ASSESSMENT. BUT, AGAIN, GIVEN THE NEW

INDEPENDENT COUNTRY\'S SIZE AND LOCATION, WE SEE NO

VITAL OR STRATEGIC U.S. INTERESTS. SLOVENIA HAS NO

HUMAN RIGHTS PROBLEMS, POSES NO THREAT TO ITS

NEIGHBORS, AND HAS NO LARGE, INFLUENTIAL EMIGRE

CONFIDENTIAL

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 ZAGREB 00527 04 OF 07 091305Z

COMMUNITY IN THE U.S. ITS DOMESTIC MARKET IS

INHERENTLY SMALL AND MAJOR U.S. INVESTMENT POTENTIAL

WILL BE LIMITED.

12. WITHOUT AN OUTWARD LOOKING LEADER WITH A

EUROPEAN VIEW (E.G., SOMEONE LIKE JANEZ DRNOVSEK, THE

FORMER FEDERAL PRESIDENT), SLOVENIA COULD BECOME AN

INSULAR, INWARD LOOKING COUNTRY, FEARFUL OF THE KIND

OF CHANGE EUROPEAN INTEGRATION THREATENS TO BRING TO

THE TWELVE. BECAUSE OF ITS SIZE, SLOVENE LEADERS,

WITHOUT EXCEPTION, UNDERSTAND FULLY THE LIMITED

INFLUENCE THEY WILL HAVE ON THE IMPORTANT ISSUES OF

THE DAY. BUT, LEFT WITHOUT A LEADER WITH VISION,

SLOVENIA COULD FIND ITSELF MARGINALIZED. THE ODDEST

TWIST OF FATE WOULD BE FOR THE REPUBLIC THAT TOOK THE

FIRST STEP DOWN THE ROAD TO DEMOCRACY AND EUROPE

COULD WELL END UP ON THE OUTSKIRTS OF EUROPE, A

VICTIM OF ITS SIZE AND GEOGRAPHY.

-

CROATIA: A FATE OF ETERNAL STRUGGLE WITH SERBIA

--------------------------------------------- --

13. IN CONTRAST, IF SLOVENIA\'S FATE IS ULTIMATELY

TIED TO ITS GEOGRAPHY, THEN CROATIA\'S IS CONNECTED TO

ITS HISTORIC ENTANGLEMENT WITH SERBIA, REINFORCED BY

THE VOLATILE ISSUE OF THE STATUS OF CROATIA\'S LARGE

SERBIAN MINORITY. CROATIAN-SERBIAN RELATIONS WILL BE

THE KEY ISSUE IN THE BALKANS FOR YEARS TO COME. AND,

AS THE CURRENT TROUBLES IN BOSNIA CLEARLY SHOW, THE

STATE OF THOSE RELATIONS HAS CONSEQUENCES BEYOND THE

BORDERS OF BOTH REPUBLICS.

KEENLY AWARE THAT THEIR DOMESTIC POLITICAL AND

SECURITY SITUATION WAS MUCH MORE TROUBLESOME THAN

THEIR NORTHERN NEIGHBOR, CROATIA\'A LEADERS

NEVERTHELESS CHARTED A VOLATILE COURSE THAT COMBINED

SHARP ANTI-SERBIAN RHETORIC WITH AN APPARENT FAILURE

TO ANTICIPATE AND ADEQUATELY PREPARE FOR THE (IN THE

VIEW OF CROATS EMPLOYING HINDSIGHT) INEVITABLE

SERBIAN UPRISING. ALTHOUGH GIVEN HIGH MARKS FOR

BRINGING CROATIA INTERNATIONAL RECOGNITION, MANY

CROATS OFFER THIS UNPREPAREDNESS AS THE ESSENTIAL

REASON FOR TUDJMAN\'S FALLING POPULARITY.

THE FRAGILE STATE OF DEMOCRACY

CONFIDENTIAL

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 ZAGREB 00527 05 OF 07 091306Z

------------------------------

15. AS WE HAVE BEEN REGULARLY REPORTING, CROATIA\'S

SECURITY WOES THREATEN TO UNDERMINE THE SHAKY STATE

OF THE YOUNG DEMOCRACY OF THE REPUBLIC. FIRST, THE

POLITICAL CENTER HAS MOVED DECIDEDLY TO THE RIGHT,

WITH EVEN SOCIALIST AND LIBERAL PARTIES TAKING A HARD

LINE ON REGAINING "LOST" TERRITORY. SECOND,

NEWSPAPERS AND MAGAZINES THAT HAVE ATTEMPTED TO

EXHIBIT ANY DEGREE OF OBJECTIVITY HAVE FOUND

THEMSELVES FACING DECLINING CIRCULATION. THIRD, THE

TELEVISION CHANNELS SPOUT AN UNABASHEDLY

PRO-GOVERNMENT LINE THAT, FRANKLY, MAY HAVE MORE TO

DO WITH HOW THEY VIEW THEIR AUDIENCE THAN ACTUAL

DIRECTIVES FROM THE GOVERNMENT. (NEVERTHELESS, THE

LEADERSHIP OF CROATIAN TV REMAINS FIRMLY ENTRENCHED

IN THE HANDS OF TUDJMAN CRONIES.) FINALLY, MORE AND

MORE WE ARE RECEIVING REPORTS THAT EVEN CROATS ARE

RELUCTANT TO SEEK HELP FROM POLICE OFFICIALS WHEN

THEY ARE THE VICTIMS OF ORDINARY CRIMES.

16. IT\'S THIS LATTER POINT THAT TROUBLES CROATS THE

MOST. MANY PEOPLE ARE NOT CONVINCED THAT IT WILL BE

EASY TO RETURN CROATIA TO A STATE OF LAW, ORDER AND

RESPECT FOR BASIC CONSTITUTIONAL RIGHTS. THE

TENTACLES OF A PERVASIVE AND INTRUSIVE SECURITY

APPARATUS UNDER THE CONTROL OF THE FORMER COMMUNIST

SECURITY OFFICIAL AND TUDJMAN ALLY JOSIP MANOLIC

REACHES FAR AND WIDE. IT\'S NOT JUST A QUESTION OF

GETTING THE GENIE BACK IN THE BOTTLE AS MUCH AS IT IS

FINDING A BOTTLE LARGE ENOUGH TO RESTRAIN AND CONTAIN

CONFIDENTIAL

THE MONSTER. A SECURITY NETWORK THAT MANY CONSIDERED

TO BE INDISPENSABLE IN TIME OF CONFLICT MAY LIVE ON

LONG AFTER THE CONFLICT ENDS, THREATENING TO STRANGLE

THE VERY DEMOCRACY IT WAS DESIGNED TO PROTECT.

TUDJMAN: FORWARD TO A DEMOCRATIC FUTURE, WITH A

LITTLE HELP FROM FRIENDS

17. CRITICS OF THE CURRENT GOVERNMENT--HUMAN RIGHTS

ACTIVISTS, OPPOSITION POLITICAL FIGURES, SERBIAN

LEADERS AND OTHERS--ALL AGREE THAT IN THE SHORT TERM,

ABSENT A DRAMATIC TURN OF EVENTS, PRESIDENT FRANJO
