SUBJECT: ZAGREB UPDATE, NOVEMBER 25, 1991

-

1. (U) SUMMARY: PRESIDENT TUDJMAN WENT ON

CROATIAN TELEVISION YESTERDAY TO DENOUNCE

ALLEGED RIGHTIST AND JNA COVERT PLOTS TO BRING

DOWN HIS GOVERNMENT. THE EVACUATION OF JNA

INSTALLATIONS IN THE ZAGREB AREA HAS BEGUN,

THOUGH NOT ALTOGETHER SMOOTHLY. SHELLING IN

OSIJEK WAS REPORTED TODAY. A CONTACT IN

DUBROVNIK CLAIMS THE JNA AIMS TO PRESSURE THE

CIVILIAN POPULATION OUT IN ORDER TO TAKE THE

CITY. REFUGEES CONTINUE TO FLOOD INTO ZAGREB

AND ELSEWHERE, AND STORIES OF HUMANITARIAN

ABUSES ARE MOUNTING. END SUMMARY

-

TUDJMAN: "THE FINAL PHASE" OF THE BATTLE

-----------------------------------------

2. (U) CROATIAN PRESIDENT TUDJMAN APPEARED ON

CROATIAN TELEVISION SUNDAY EVENING, NOVEMBER 24,

CONFIDENTIAL

CONFIDENTIAL

TO DECLARE THAT "THE BATTLE" FOR CROATIAN

INDEPENDENCE AND INTERNATIONAL RECOGNITION "HAS

TAKEN ON DRAMATIC PROPORTIONS AS IT ENTERS ITS

FINAL PHASE." TUDJMAN DISCUSSED HIS HOPES FOR A

U.N. PEACE-KEEPING FORCE, SAYING THAT

DIFFERENCES STILL EXISTED ON DEFINING THE

"CRISIS AREAS" TO WHICH SUCH A FORCE WOULD BE

DEPLOYED, AND WARNING THAT THE JNA AND SERBIA

WOULD SEEK TO CONSOLIDATE THEIR TERRORITORIAL

GAINS BEFORE THE PEACE-KEEPING FORCE COULD

ARRIVE. HE ASSERTED THAT WITH THE WEST NOW

READY "TO ACT MORE DECISIVELY, THE SERBIAN SIDE

HAS UNDERTAKEN NEW DECEITS."

-

3. (U) TUDJMAN DEVOTED MUCH OF HIS SPEECH TO A

DESCRIPTION OF WHAT HE TERMED "EVIDENT PROOF"

THAT BOTH CROATIAN RIGHTIST EXTREMISTS, IN THE

FORM OF THE CROATIAN PARTY OF THE RIGHT (HDS)

AND ITS ARRESTED LEADER, DOBROSLAV PARAGA, AND

THE INTELLIGENCE BRANCH OF THE YUGOSLAV ARMY

(KOS) WERE ENGAGED IN PLOTS TO OVERTHROW THE

CROATIAN GOVERNMENT, ALTHOUGH TUDJMAN SEEMED TO

SUGGEST THAT PARAGA WAS BEING UNWITTINGLY USED

BY KOS RATHER THAN BEING IN ACTIVE COLLUSION

WITH IT. TUDJMAN CRITICIZED PARAGA AND HIS

PARTY FOR REVIVING FASCIST SYMBOLS WHICH

DISCREDITED CROATIA.

-

PROOF" WAS NOT VERY SPECIFIC, AND WHEN WE RAISED

THE ISSUE THE FOLLOWING DAY DURING A MEETING

PARAGA REMAINS JAILED

---------------------

5. (U) PARAGA\'S DETENTION HAS BEEN EXTENDED

THIRTY DAYS PENDING PREPARATION OF THE CASE

AGAINST HIM. PARAGA\'S DEPUTY, MILAN VUKOVIC,

WAS RELEASED OVER THE WEEKEND, AND IMMEDIATELY

ANNOUNCED THAT HE, ALONG WITH PARAGA, WOULD

CONTINUE A HUNGER STRIKE UNTIL PARAGA\'S

RELEASE.

6. (C) VIRTUALLY ALL OF CROATIA\'S OPPOSITION

PARTIES ISSUED STATEMENTS CRITICAL OF THE

GOVERNMENT\'S ARREST OF PARAGA, ARGUING THAT

PARAGA SHOULD BE DEALT WITH BY POLITICAL RATHER

CONFIDENTIAL

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 ZAGREB 01658 02 OF 05 251749Z

THAN LEGAL MEANS. AT LEAST SOME CROATIANS BOTH

IN AND OUT OF GOVERNMENT ARE TAKING THIS STANCE

OF OPPOSITION SOLIDARITY WITH A GRAIN OF SALT,

HOWEVER; AS ONE JOURNALIST POINTED OUT, MANY IN

THE OPPOSITION WERE CRITICIZING TUDJMAN ONLY

LAST WEEK FOR NOT DEALING MORE FORCEFULLY WITH

PARAGA.

-

7. (C) THERE HAVE BEEN DAILY GATHERINGS SINCE

SATURDAY OF ABOUT ONE HUNDRED PERSONS, INCLUDING

SOME OF PARAGA\'S ARMED TROOPS, IN ZAGREB\'S MAIN

SQUARE TO PROTEST PARAGA\'S ARREST. THESE

PROTESTS ARE REPORTEDLY SLATED TO OCCUR DAILY

UNTIL HIS RELEASE. TUDJMAN AIDE SARINIC CLAIMED

TO US THAT "THE MAJORITY" OF THE FORCES LOYAL TO

PARAGA (HE PUT THEM AT "LESS THAN ONE THOUSAND")

ARE ACCEPTING INTEGRATION INTO REGULAR CROATIAN

FORCES. MOST OTHER CROATIAN SOURCES CONSIDER

THIS PREDICTION UNREALISTICALLY OPTIMISTIC.

INAUSPICIOUS START FOR LATEST CEASE-FIRE

----------------------------------------

9. (C) ACCORDING TO EC MONITORS, HEAVY JNA

SHELLING OCCURRED OVER THE WEEKEND IN KARLOVAC,

ZADAR, AND OSIJEK, AND DID NOT STOP ENTIRELY

WITH THE BEGINNING OF THE NEW CEASE-FIRE

EFFECTIVE NOVEMBER 22 AT 1800 LT. THE EC

SPOKESMAN SAID CROATIAN FORCES DEMOLISHED THE

MASLENICA BRIDGE (25 KM NORTHEAST OF ZADAR) THE

NIGHT OF NOVEMBER 20-21, RESULTING IN SOME

THIRTY DEATHS.

10. (C) DURING OUR CALL ON SARINIC AT THE

PRESIDENCY MONDAY MORNING, SARINIC RECEIVED WORD

THAT THE JNA WAS SHELLING OSIJEK. SARINIC

CALLED DEFENSE MINISTER KADIJEVIC\'S OFFICE TO

PROTEST THE APPARENT CEASE-FIRE VIOLATION, AND

LATER TOLD US IN DISGUST THAT THE JNA CLAIMED

THE CROATIANS THEMSELVES WERE SHELLING OSIJEK.

SITUATION IN DUBROVNIK

11. (C) SLOBODAN LANG, PRESIDENT OF THE

CROATIAN-AMERICAN FRIENDSHIP SOCIETY AND A

LONG-TIME CONSULATE CONTACT, HAS BEEN

TELEPHONING US NIGHTLY FROM THE DUBROVNIK AREA

WITH UPDATES. (HE APPARENTLY HAS ACCESS TO

COMMUNICATIONS FACILITIES BROUGHT IN BY ONE OF

THE RELIEF GROUPS.) LANG SAYS THE JNA IS

ATTEMPTING TO INTIMIDATE THE CIVILIAN POPULATION

TO ABANDON DUBROVNIK BY USING A COMBINATION OF

THREATS (LANG SAID YET ANOTHER JNA "ULTIMATUM"

EXPIRED AT 1100 THIS MORNING), SHELLING, AND

SPORADIC ADMISSION OF RELIEF VESSELS TO TAKE

PEOPLE OUT. THE AIM, ACCORDING TO LANG, IS TO

REDUCE THE POPULATION TO WHAT CAN BE CLAIMED TO

BE "USTASI HOLD-OUTS" AND THEN TO TAKE THE CITY.

-

12. (C) LANG SAID HE IS CONCENTRATING HIS

EFFORTS ON PRACTICAL MEASURES AIMED AT WINNING

JNA OBSERVANCE OF INTERNATIONAL HUMANITARIAN

CONVENTIONS. FOR EXAMPLE, THE JNA IS BEING

ASKED TO ALLOW LOCAL WOMEN RESIDENTS TO

ACCOMPANY THEM WHEN THEY ENTER HOMES TO SEARCH

FOR WEAPONS IN AN EFFORT TO MONITOR THEIR

BEHAVIOR. SOME TROOPS ARE DISCIPLINED, HE SAID;

OTHERS, NOTABLY THE RESERVISTS, "HAVE NEVER

HEARD OF THE GENEVA CONVENTIONS AND DON\'T

CARE." BASED ON HIS DEALINGS THERE, LANG

BELIEVES THAT SOME POTENTIAL EXISTS FOR

OBTAINING COOPERATION FROM MONTENEGRO TO ASSURE

THE SECURITY OF DUBROVNIK.

THE HORRORS OF WAR

------------------

14. (U) REFUGEES FROM VUKOVAR AND ELSEWHERE

CONTINUED TO POUR INTO ZAGREB OVER THE WEEKEND.

WE ARE NOW HEARING FIGURES OF 100,000 REFUGEES

IN ZAGREB ITSELF (THOUGH MOST OF THESE WILL

QUICKLY BE MOVED ELSEWHERE) AND AS MANY AS HALF

A MILLION IN CROATIA AS A WHOLE. THE REFUGEES

FROM VUKOVAR TELL WRENCHING STORIES OF MONTHS

SPENT IN UNDERGROUND SHELTERS, WITH DIMINISHING

SUPPLIES, NO NEWS OF THEIR FAMILIES, AND FINALLY

OF EMERGING TO FIND THEIR CITY COMPLETELY

DEMOLISHED AROUND THEM.

-

15. (U) THE REFUGEES\' MOVING TESTIMONY, THE

ABSOLUTENESS OF VUKOVAR\'S DESTRUCTION, AND

CONTINUAL RUMORS OF ATROCITIES STILL NOT

CONFIDENTIAL

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 ZAGREB 01658 04 OF 05 251752Z

UNCOVERED HAVE RAISED PUBLIC EMOTION ABOUT THE

WAR TO A HIGH PITCH. CROATIAN ANGER WAS FURTHER

STOKED BY THE BBC BROADCAST OF FOOTAGE FROM

VUKOVAR OF SERBIAN IRREGULARS MARCHING

VICTORIOUSLY THROUGH THE STREETS SINGING

(UNBEKNOWNST TO THE BRITISH CORRESPONDENT) AN

OLD "CHETNIK" SONG OF WHICH THE WORDS WERE

CLEARLY HEARD: "THERE\'LL BE MEAT; THERE\'LL BE

MEAT; WE\'LL SLAUGHTER CROATS." THE JNA

ALLEGATION THAT CROATS HAD MASSACRED SERBIAN

CHILDREN IN VUKOVAR, PUBLISHED BY REUTERS AND

LATER RETRACTED, IS BEING CITED AS FURTHER PROOF

OF SERBIAN PERFIDY.

-

16. (U) AT THE SAME TIME, THE CROATIAN PRESS

HAS REPORTED AN ALLEGED MASSACRE BY SERBIAN

IRREGULARS OF 35 CIVILIANS FROM THE CROATIAN

VILLAGE OF SKABRNJA, NEAR ZADAR. ACCORDING TO

PRESS REPORTS, THE BODIES WERE TURNED OVER BY

THE JNA TO THE ZADAR MEDICAL CENTER, WHICH

ESTABLISHED THAT THE BODIES BORE SIGNS OF

"MASSACRE." THE EC MONITORS\' SPOKESMAN TOLD US

TODAY THAT THE EC GROUP REGARDS THE CASE AS A

"SERIOUS ONE MERITING INVESTIGATION," BUT THAT

THE JNA HAS DENIED PERMISSION TO EC MONITORS TO

VISIT THE AREA.

17. (U) WE ALSO UNDERSTAND FROM THE EC

MONITORS\' GROUP THAT EFFORTS TO CONFIRM THE

STATUS OF PERSONS MISSING FROM THE EVACUATION OF

THE WOUNDED FROM VUKOVAR\'S HOSPITAL CONTINUE.
