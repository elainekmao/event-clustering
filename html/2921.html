SUBJECT: RUSSIANS IN ROME SAY ORTHODOX/VATICAN TENSIONS ARE

AT HIGHEST LEVEL IN YEARS

CLASSIFIED BY: AMBASSADOR JIM NICHOLSON FOR REASONS 1.5 (A) AND (D).

1. (C) SUMMARY: OVER THE COURSE OF THE LAST FEW WEEKS, DCM

MET SEPARATELY WITH FATHER MARCEL GUARNIZO, PRESIDENT OF

THE AID TO THE CHURCH IN RUSSIA, FATHER FILIPP OF THE RUSSIAN

ORTHODOX CHURCH IN ROME, AND DIMITRI SHTODIN, DCM AT THE

CONFIDENTIAL

RUSSIAN EMBASSY TO THE HOLY SEE. ALL ASSERT THAT

RUSSIAN/VATICAN RELATIONS ARE AT A 10-YEAR LOW. FATHER

MARCEL BLAMES IT ON THE INCREASING INSECURITY OF PATRIARCH

ALEXI AND RUSSIAN ORTHODOX "FOREIGN MINISTER" KYRILL. FATHER

FILIPP STATES THAT THE PROBLEM IS THE ROMAN CATHOLIC CHURCH\'S

"AGGRESSIVE" PROSELYTISM, WHILE SHTODIN SEES IT AS A

MANIFESTATION OF AN ETHNIC RUSSIAN/ETHNIC POLE CONFRONTATION

WITHIN THE RUSSIAN FEDERATION. NONE OF THE THREE SEE ANY

SHORT-TERM IMPROVEMENT. END SUMMARY

THE VIEW OF THE RUSSIAN CHURCH IN ROME

2. (C) THE RUSSIAN ORTHODOX CHURCH IN ROME WAS A SMALL,

DISCREET CONGREGATION UNTIL THE LATE 1980\'S. FOUNDED BY

"WHITE RUSSIANS", IT USE TO EASILY ACCOMMODATE ITS LESS THAN

300 MEMBERS IN ITS CHURCH LOCATED IN THE CENTER OF ROME.

HOWEVER, BECAUSE OF THE INFLUX OF SERBS, RUSSIANS, AND

UKRAINIANS INTO ROME SINCE THE EARLY 1990\'S, THERE WERE OVER

1,000 PEOPLE AT ORTHODOX EASTER SUNDAY MASS ON MAY 5, WITH

THE OVERFLOW CROWD FILLING THE SURROUNDING STREETS. THE

CONGREGATION HOPES TO HAVE A SECOND CHURCH BUILDING ON THE

RUSSIAN EMBASSY COMPOUND IN 2003, WHERE A LARGE CHURCH IS

CURRENTLY BEING CONSTRUCTED. THERE ARE THREE PRIESTS AT THE

CHURCH, FATHER FILIPP, FATHER MIKAIL, AND FATHER ALEKSEY. DCM

MET MOST RECENTLY WITH FATHER FILIPP ON MAY 6 TO DISCUSS HIS

PARTICIPATION IN EMBASSY\'S MAY 15-16 ANTI TRAFFICKING

CONFERENCE. FATHER FILIPP AGAIN USED THE OPPORTUNITY TO

COMPLAIN ABOUT VATICAN ACTIONS IN RUSSIA. HE SAID THAT THERE

WILL BE NO IMPROVEMENT IN RELATIONS UNTIL POPE JOHN PAUL II

"LEAVES THE SCENE". HE SAID THAT THE CREATION OF CATHOLIC

DIOCESE IN RUSSIA WAS A GREAT "INSULT" TO THE RUSSIAN CHURCH.

WHEN ASKED IF THESE WERE NOT NECESSARY TO PROVIDE PASTORAL

CARE FOR CATHOLICS LIVING IN RUSSIA, HE REPLIED THAT THIS

ARGUMENT IS A VATICAN "LIE" AND COVER FOR AGGRESSIVE

PROSTYELATION OF RUSSIANS. HE DESCRIBED RELATIONS AS WORSE

THAN ANYTIME IN THE LAST 20 YEARS.

VIEW OF THE EMBASSY OF RUSSIA TO THE HOLY SEE

3. (C) DURING SEVERAL ENCOUNTERS WITH RUSSIAN EMBASSY DCM

SHTODIN, DCM RAISED STATE OF RUSSIAN/VATICAN RELATIONS.

SHTODIN, WHO VOLUNTEERED THAT HE IS A NON BELIEVER AND DID

NOT FEEL PARTICULARLY CLOSE TO THE ORTHODOX CHURCH, AGREED

ENTIRELY WITH THE SENTIMENTS VOICED BY FATHER FILIPP. HE SAW

NOT ONLY THE CREATION OF DIOCESES AS AN AFFRONT, BUT WHAT HE

DESCRIBED AS THE RECENT VATICAN "VIRTUAL INVASION" OF MOSCOW.

ACCORDING TO SHTODIN, THE RUSSIANS WERE INFORMED THAT THE

VATICAN WOULD DO A LIVE VIDEO LINK FOR THE POPE WITH

SEMINARIANS AT THE CATHEDRAL IN MOSCOW. THE ARCHBISHOP OF

MOSCOW, AGAIN ACCORDING TO SHTODIN, MADE IT A PUBLIC EVENT.

SHTODIN SEES BOTH ETHNICITY AND POLITICS AT WORK ON THE

VATICAN SIDE. HE SAID THAT A MAJORITY OF ROMAN CATHOLICS IN

RUSSIA ARE ETHNIC POLES, AND THAT THOSE IN THE VATICAN WHO

WANT TO RETARD PROGRESS IN VATICAN/RUSSIA RELATIONS ARE

TELLING THE POPE THAT HIS CO-NATIONALS IN RUSSIA ARE BEING

PERSECUTED AND THE VATICAN SHOULD THEREFORE TAKE AN

AGGRESSIVE STAND. HE SINGLED OUT PRIME MINISTER SODANO

AS BEHIND THESE ACTIONS, NOT BECAUSE HE\'S CONCERNED WITH

POLISH CATHOLICS IN RUSSIA, BUT TO GAIN SUPPORT IN

ANTICIPATION OF THE NEXT CONCLAVE AMONG CARDINALS LOYAL TO

JOHN PAUL.

A ROMAN CATHOLIC\'S VIEW

4. (C) FATHER MARCEL, PRESIDENT OF AN ORGANIZATION CALLED

"AID TO THE CHURCH IN RUSSIA", CALLED ON DCM ON MAY 1.

HE SAID THAT PERSECUTION OF ROMAN CATHOLICS IN RUSSIA IS AT

ITS WORST LEVEL SINCE THE FALL OF COMMUNISM. HE CLAIMED THAT

THERE IS NO PROSELYTIZING, BUT IF AN ETHNIC RUSSIAN WANTS TO

INVESTIGATE CATHOLICISM "HE MUST BE RECEIVED". MARCEL

STRESSED THAT TO CONTINUE TO PROVIDE PASTORAL SUPPORT FOR

ROMAN CATHOLICS IN RUSSIA, IT IS NECESSARY FOR FOREIGN

BISHOPS AND PRIESTS TO BE ALLOWED TO ENTER THE COUNTRY. HE

MADE A STRONG PLEA FOR PRESIDENT BUSH TO MEET ARCHBISHOP

TADEUSZ KONDRUSIEWICZ OF MOSCOW DURING THE PRESIDENT\'S

UPCOMING TRIP TO RUSSIA.

COMMENT: POST WOULD APPRECIATE HEARING FROM EMBASSY MOSCOW

IF SHTODIN\'S PERSPECTIVE IS SHARED BY MANY IN THE GOI.

EMBASSY VATICAN IS CONVINCED THAT THE HOLY SEE HAS LEGITIMATE

PASTORAL CONCERNS IN RUSSIA WHICH NECESSITATE THE PRESENCE OF

FOREIGN PRIESTS. THAT SAID, THERE IS SOME INDICATION THAT

THE VATICAN IS CALLING FOR A GREATER NUMBER OF PRIESTS PER

CATHOLIC CONGREGANTS THAN IS THE NORM IN WESTERN EUROPEAN

COUNTRIES. AGAIN, EMBASSY MOSCOW\'S VIEW WOULD BE WELCOMED.
