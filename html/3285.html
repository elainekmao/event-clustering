SUBJECT: ITALY SPLIT ON BIOTECH TRACEABILITY AND LABELING,

STILL THINKING ABOUT EU CHEMICALS REFORM

REF: A. STATE 98591

B. STATE 52550

1. THIS IS AN ACTION MESSAGE FOR DEPT.; SEE PARA 8. ESTCOUNS

MET WITH MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT EU AFFAIRS OFFICERS ON MAY

30 TO DISCUSS THE EU PROPOSALS FOR TRACEABILITY AND LABELING

OF GMO EVENTS, AND TO DISCUSS ITALIAN VIEWS TOWARD EU

CHEMICALS REFORM.

2. FINDING THE RIGHT GOI MINISTRY WITH WHICH TO DISCUSS

TRACEABILITY AND LABELING WAS A CHALLENGE. WE WERE REBUFFED

BY MFA, FOREIGN TRADE, AGRICULTURE, HEALTH AND EU AFFAIRS.

ALL POINTED US TO THE MINISTRY OF GOVERNMENT (MOG), WHICH HAS

HANDLED THE ACCOUNT WITHOUT MUCH ENTHUSIASM, MUCH PROFILE, OR

MUCH ATTENTION BEYOND THE TECHNICAL LEVEL.

3. HEAD OF MOE\'S EU AFFAIRS OFFICE GIULIANA GASPARINI TOLD

EST COUNS THAT THE GOI HAD NOT TAKEN A CONSOLIDATED POSITION

ON THE TRACEABILITY AND LABELING PROPOSALS BECAUSE OF THE NOW

FAMILIAR SPLIT BETWEEN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND THE

MINISTRY OF AGRICULTURE. ACCORDING TO GASPARINI, HEALTH

OPPOSES BIOTECH TRACEABILITY UNLESS HEALTH ISSUES (POSSIBLE

ALLERGENS, CHANGED NUTRITIONAL COMPOSITION) ARE INTRODUCED BY

THE BIOTECH PRODUCT. SHE SAID AGRICULTURE BELIEVES THAT THE

PROPOSED TRACEABILITY RULES DO NOT GO FAR ENOUGH (WE KNOW

THAT MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE ALEMANNO LOBBIED HEAVILY TO EU

PARLIAMENTARIANS BEFORE THE EP VOTE), AND SHOULD INCLUDE

TRACEABILITY THROUGH ANIMAL PRODUCTS WHEN BIOTECH FEED IS

INVOLVED. THE MINISTRIES AGREE THAT LABELING IS NECESSARY TO

RESPOND TO CONSUMER DEMANDS.

4. WITHIN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT ITSELF, A SIMILAR SPLIT

EXISTS. CONSULTANT SPECIALISTS WITHIN MOE FROM THE FORMER

AMATO GOVERNMENT ARE WORKING ON TRACEABILITY AND LABELING.

THESE INCLUDE FORMER GREEN PEACE ANTI-GMO ACTIVISTS WHO SEE

BURDENSOME AND EXACTING T AND L AS A WAY TO BLOCK GMOS IN

ITALY AND THE EU, ACCORDING TO GASPARINI. A MORE MODERATE

FACTION WITHIN MOE, LED BY DG FOR INTERNATIONAL AFFAIRS CLINI

AND INCLUDING GASPARINI HERSELF, ARE ARGUING FOR REALISTIC

RULES THAT CAN BE IMPLEMENTED. GASPARINI EXPLAINED THAT THIS

WOULD INCLUDE "REASONABLE" THRESHOLDS FOR ADVENTITIOUS

PRESENCE (AP) (BY WHICH IS MEANT 0.3-0.7 PERCENT FOR FOODS

AND FEEDS) AND SOME ACCOMMODATION ON BIOTECH PRODUCTS LIKE

OILS WHICH ARE INDISTINGUISHABLE FROM CONVENTIONAL PRODUCTS

AFTER PROCESSING. THE ISSUE OF AP IN SEEDS FOR PLANTING IS

ESPECIALLY DIFFICULT FOR ITALY BECAUSE THE EU PROPOSALS DO

NOT SPECIFICALLY ADDRESS SEEDS. CONSEQUENTLY, THE MINISTRY

OF AGRICULTURE HAS BEEN ABLE TO ARGUE ITS ZERO THRESHOLD

PROPOSAL AS A MEMBER STATE\'S RIGHT TO SET STANDARDS AND RULES

WHERE NONE ARE PROVIDED BY BRUSSELS.

5. GASPARINI SAID THAT THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT IS MOVING

CAUTIOUSLY TO FIND A BRIDGE FROM ITALIAN VIEWS THROUGH THE EU

PROPOSALS TO A MORE "OPEN" APPROACH TO BIOTECHNOLOGY BASED ON

SCIENCE. SHE DID NOT EXPECT MUCH CLARIFICATION OF ITALIAN

POSITIONS IN THE NEAR TERM, NOTING THAT THE SPANISH

PRESIDENCY HAS DECIDED NOT TO DEAL FURTHER WITH

BIOTECHNOLOGY, AND THAT THE DANES HAD SET THE FIRST

ENVIRONMENT COUNCIL MEETING OF THEIR PRESIDENCY FOR

MID-OCTOBER.

6. COMMENT: THE TRACEABILITY AND LABELING ISSUE IS MAKING ITS

WAY SLOWLY UPWARD FROM TECHNICIAN-BUREAUCRATS WHO REMAIN

STEADFASTLY ANTI-BIOTECH. MOE REPRESENTATIVES AT A RECENT

MEETING CONVENED BY THE TECHNICAL SECRETARIAT AT THE EU

AFFAIRS MINISTRY DID NOT CLEAR THEIR VIEWS WITH THE

MINISTER\'S OR DG\'S OFFICE AND JOINED THE AG MINISTRY ON

ARGUING THAT THE EU PROPOSALS WERE TOO LIBERAL. LOWER

RANKING MOE OFFICIALS ARE RELUCTANT TO SPEAK AGAINST THE

MEMBER STATE "CONSENSUS" AT TECHNICAL MEETINGS IN BRUSSELS.

AT THE DIRECTOR GENERAL LEVEL, THERE IS GROWING RECOGNITION

WITHIN THE MOE THAT A COHERENT POLICY IS REQUIRED FOR THE

LONG TERM. END COMMENT.

EU CHEMICALS REFORM ON THE SLOW TRACK

-------------------------------------

7. GIORGIO SUBINI, WITHIN THE SAME MOE OFFICE, IS THE

TECHNICAL EXPERT PARTICIPATING IN THE EU CHEMICAL REFORM

PROCESS. WHEN REMINDED ABOUT U.S. VIEWS (REF B), SUBINI SAID

HE UNDERSTOOD OUR CONCERNS ABOUT COSTS, SINCE THESE WERE THE

CONCERNS OF SMALL- AND MEDIUM-SIZED BUSINESSES IN ITALY. HE

ADMITTED THAT HE HAD YET TO BEGIN DISCUSSIONS WITH ITALIAN

INDUSTRY, THOUGH HE PLANS TO DO SO IN THE NEAR FUTURE. HE

ALSO SAID THAT HE DID NOT BELIEVE THE TECHNICAL DISCUSSIONS

WERE PROCEEDING VERY QUICKLY FROM THE CONCEPTUAL PROBLEM OF

MANAGING 100,000 CHEMICALS FROM THE "POLLUTER PAYS" PRINCIPLE

TO PRACTICAL WORK.

8. COMMENT AND ACTION REQUEST: THE SIZE AND ECONOMIC

IMPORTANCE OF ITALY\'S CHEMICAL INDUSTRY (WITH A U.S.

INVESTMENT OF USD 2.3 BILLION), AND THE LACK OF SIGNIFICANT

OUTREACH BY MOE, MAY CREATE AN OPPORTUNITY. WE WOULD LIKE TO
