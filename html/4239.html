START MEMO NOMINATING-JUSTIFICATION:

Ms. Ranawaka has served for ten years as the Assistant

Director of the National Intellectual Property Office

(NIPO) and, according to the current director, has good

prospects for becoming the head of the office in the

future. The existence and enforcement of strong IPR

laws are critical to Sri Lanka\'s economic development,

especially if it hopes to take advantage of the

burgeoning IT industry. IPR issues are central to many

American investors and to the USG.

According to the current director of NIPO, Ms. Ranawaka

is a dedicated officer who will have an increasing

influence on IPR issues. An exposure to the American

scene through the participation in MRP on "Protection

of Intellectual Property Rights" would provide her with

a broader perspective of the issues involving IPR and

related fields and impress upon her the centrality of

these issues to foreign investment in Sri Lanka. This

will also provide an opportunity to establish

institutional linkages.

END MEMO NOMINATING-JUSTIFICATION:
