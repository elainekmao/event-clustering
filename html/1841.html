1. (C) SUMMARY: VATICAN FM TAURAN CONVOKED CHARGE EVENING

OF AUGUST 2 TO DISCUSS THE POPE\'S AUDIENCE WITH PA

PRESIDENT ARAFAT EARLIER IN THE DAY. TAURAN SAID THE POPE

WAS "EXTREMELY WORRIED" AT THE PERSISTENCE AND SCOPE OF

VIOLENCE IN THE HOLY LANDS; ARAFAT TOLD THE POPE THAT HE

WANTS MONITORS NOW, TO HELP BUILD CONFIDENCE AND BREAK THE

CYCLE OF VIOLENCE. ARAFAT WOULD ACCEPT ANY SORT OF

MONITORS, INCLUDING JUST U.S. ONES. THE POPE WISHED TO

CONVEY TO PRESIDENT BUSH HIS PERSONAL WORRY AT THE STATE OF

THINGS AND TO URGE THE PRESIDENT TO INTERVENE WITH THE GOI

TO PERMIT THE "THE MECHANISM OF OBSERVATION TO START."

TAURAN ALSO SHARED WITH US THE POPE\'S PRESS STATEMENT FROM

THE ARAFAT MEETING (EMBASSY INFORMAL TRANSLATION AT PARA

THREE) AND HIS OWN PERSONAL VIEWS ON THE SITUATION. END

SUMMARY.

2. (C) AT 7:00 THE EVENING OF AUGUST 2, VATICAN FM

EQUIVALENT JEAN LOUIS TAURAN CONVOKED CHARGE AND A/DCM TO

DISCUSS THE MEETING BETWEEN THE POPE AND PALESTINIAN

AUTHORITY PRESIDENT ARAFAT EARLIER ON THE DAY. TAURAN HAD

HAD A CONCURRENT MEETING WITH ARAFAT\'S DELEGATION,

INCLUDING MEMBERS OF ARAFAT\'S NEGOTIATING TEAM. TAURAN

UNDERSCORED THE VATICAN\'S VIEW THAT THE SITUATION IS QUITE

SERIOUS, AND THEIR VIEW THAT THE VIOLENCE HAD CROSSED AN

IMPORTANT AND WORRISOME THRESHOLD AT THE WAILING WALL

EARLIER IN THE WEEK. (NOTE: A VATICAN PRIORITY IN THE MEPP

IS PROTECTION OF THE HOLY PLACES). TAURAN SAID THE POPE

WANTS THE PRESIDENT TO KNOW ARAFAT IS READY TO ACCEPT ANY

TYPE OF OBSERVERS IN ORDER TO BREAK THE CYCLE OF VIOLENCE.

THE POPE SPECIFICALLY ASKED THAT PRESIDENT BUSH EXERCISE

PRESSURE ON THE GOI, IN ORDER TO PERMIT "THE MECHANISM OF

OBSERVATION TO START" AS SOON AS POSSIBLE. THE POPE

BELIEVED PRESIDENT BUSH "HOLDS THE KEY TO THIS."

RESPONDING TO CHARGE\'S QUERY, TAURAN UNDERSCORED THAT

ARAFAT TOLD THEM HE WAS WILLING TO ACCEPT INTERNATIONAL

OBSERVERS OF ANY SORT -- INCLUDING JUST U.S. ONES.

2. (C) TAURAN ALSO SAID EGYPTIAN FM MOUSSA RECENTLY

CALLED AND SAID THAT THE SITUATION WAS "VERY SERIOUS,"

WHICH TAURAN TOOK TO MEAN THAT THE POSSIBILITY OF THE

VIOLENCE SPREADING WAS GROWING. IN RESPONSE TO CHARGE\'S

QUESTION, TAURAN ADMITTED THAT THE VATICAN HAS NOT HAD ANY

RECENT CONTACTS WITH THE ISRAELI SIDE. (NOTE: TAURAN

DIDN\'T EVEN REMEMBER THE ISRAELI HOLY SEE AMBASSADOR\'S NAME

-- AN INDICATION OF HOW DISTANT THE VATICAN NOW IS FROM THE

GOI). TAURAN REMINDED US THAT HE HAD FIRST MENTIONED THE

NEED FOR AN INTERNATIONAL OBSERVER FORCE LAST NOVEMBER.

WHILE THE VATICAN SUPPORTS THE MITCHELL PLAN, TAURAN

BELIEVED THE ONLY WAY TO STOP THE VIOLENCE NOW IS WITH THE

HELP OF THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY (IE. AN OBSERVER

FORCE).

3. (C) IN A SIDE NOTE, TAURAN, WHO HAD TOLD US LAST YEAR

THAT HE THOUGHT ARAFAT WAS "LOSING IT" BOTH MENTALLY AND

PHYSICALLY, NOW OBSERVED ARAFAT TO BE MENTALLY SHARP AND

ENGAGED, WHILE PERHAPS DETERIORATING PHYSICALLY WITH

PARKINSON\'S-LIKE SYMPTOMS.

POPE JOHN PAUL II RECEIVED MR. YASSER ARAFAT, PRESIDENT OF

THE PALESTINIAN AUTHORITY, AT HIS RESIDENCE AT CASTEL

GANDOLFO THIS MORNING.

THE MEETING FOCUSED ON THE STATUS OF THE PEACE PROCESS AND

THE PERSISTENT SITUATION OF UNPRECEDENTED VIOLENCE THAT

CONTINUES TO PRODUCE VICTIMS, ABOVE ALL AMONG THE

DEFENSELESS CIVILIAN POPULATION, AND THAT DOES NOT EVEN

SPARE THE MOST HOLY PLACES.

HIS HOLINESS, WHILE EXPRESSING HIS SYMPATHY FOR THE MANY

VICTIMS OF THE ONGOING CLASHES, FIRMLY STRESSED THE

ABSOLUTE NECESSITY

OF ENDING EVERY FORM OF VIOLENCE, BOTH

DIRECT ACTION AND RETALIATION, AND THAT WITH THE ASSISTANCE

OF THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY, THE HOPED FOR NEGOTIATIONS

WHICH ARE THE SOLE MEANS CAPABLE OF GIVING HOPE FOR

ACHIEVING PEACE, WILL BEGIN.

END TEXT.
