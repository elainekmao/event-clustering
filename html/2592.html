SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION ZIMBABWE DECIDES; HARARE

1. AS ZIMBABWEANS HEAD FOR THE POLLS, EDITORIALS IN

THE LEADING NEWSPAPERS TAKE ONE LAST SHOT AT PERSUADING

VOTERS TO SUPPORT ONE OF THE TWO LEADING CANDIDATES.

EXCERPTS:

2. UNDER HEADLINE "D-DAY FOR ZIMBABWE," THE MARCH 9

EDITION OF THE GOVERNMENT-CONTROLLED DAILY "THE

HERALD" CARRIED THE FOLLOWING EDITORIAL ON PAGE

ONE:

"TODAY IS D-DAY, AS ZIMBABWEANS GO TO THE POLLS TO

DECIDE THEIR POLITICAL AND ECONOMIC FUTURE IN THIS

WATERSHED PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION. THIS IS NO

ORDINARY PLEBISCITE TO CHOOSE WHO WILL LEAD THIS

COUNTRY FOR THE NEXT SIX YEARS. IT IS IN FACT, A

CRUCIAL DEFINING MOMENT WHICH WILL DETERMINE THE

DIRECTION WHICH THIS NATION WILL TAKE IN TERMS OF

ITS SOVEREIGNTY. THE RESULT OF THIS ELECTION WILL

ALSO HAVE WIDER IMPLICATIONS ON THE POLITICAL AND

ECONOMIC LANDSCAPE OF SOUTHERN AFRICA AND THE REST

OF THE CONTINENT. AFTER THE ELECTION, ZIMBABWE

WILL NEVER BE THE SAME. IT WILL EITHER EMERGE AS A

TRULY INDEPENDENT AFRICAN STATE IN CHARGE OF ITS

OWN POLITICAL AND ECONOMIC DESTINY OR A SEEMINGLY

PROSPEROUS COUNTRY WHERE THE SOCIAL AND ECONOMIC

DIVIDE BETWEEN WHITE AND BLACK WILL BE PERPETUATED.

.OPTIONS CONFRONTING MOST ZIMBABWEANS AT THIS POINT

IN TIME ARE WHETHER THEY WANT TO CONTINUE BEING

GOOD DOCILE AFRICANS THAT ARE PAMPERED BY THE NORTH

TO TAKE THE BULL BY ITS HORNS AND TAKE CHARGE OF

. . .THE EUROPEAN UNION AND THE UNITED STATES

IMPOSED SURGICAL SANCTIONS AGAINST PRESIDENT MUGABE

AND THREATENED TO FREEZE HIS ALLEGED OVERSEAS

COMBINED MIGHT OF EUROPE AND AMERICA HAS FAILED TO

DAMPEN HIS SPIRIT, WHICH HAS GROWN STRONGER

INSTEAD. . . HISTORY WILL REMEMBER HIM FOR HIS

ABILITY TO SACRIFICE HIS GOOD NAME FOR THE SAKE OF

THE PEOPLE OF THIS COUNTRY. . .

. . .PRESIDENT MUGABE WILL INDEED GO DOWN IN

HISTORY AS A TRUE CHAMPION OF AFRICAN LIBERATION

WHO WENT AGAINST THE MIGHT OF THE WEST AND RISKED

RELEGATION TO A PARIAH IN ORDER TO DELIVER HIS

PEOPLE TO THE PROMISED LAND. AS ZIMBABWEANS GO TO

THE POLLS TODAY AND TOMORROW, THEY DO SO IN DIGNITY

AND PRIDE. THEIR VOTE WILL SEND A CLEAR AND

RESOUNDING MESSAGE TO THE REST OF THE WORLD THAT WE

ARE A SOVEREIGN NATION WITH THE DETERMINATION NOT

ONLY TO SURVIVE, BUT SUCCEED AGAINST ALL ODDS. THE

TRUST AND CONFIDENCE THAT WILL BE GIVEN BY THE

PEOPLE SHOULD SPUR THE PRESIDENT TO COMPLETE THE

TASK THAT WAS STARTED IN THE 1960S, TO RESTORE

FULLY THE COUNTRY, BACK TO ITS PEOPLE. . ."
