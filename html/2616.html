2. (C) SUMMARY: GVN ACTIONS HINDERED CONTACT

BETWEEN RELIGIOUS LEADERS IT DID NOT HAND PICK AND THE

U.S. COMMISSION FOR INTERNATIONAL RELIGIOUS FREEDOM\'S

(USCIRF) DELEGATION. CIRCUMSTANCES, INCLUDING A

PACKED ITINERARY, SELECTIVE COMPLIANCE WITH SCHEDULE

CHANGE REQUESTS, PLAINCLOTHES TAILS, AND POORLY

EXPLAINED DETOURS, COMBINED TO MAKE UNOFFICIAL

MEETINGS DIFFICULT. THE GVN\'S ESSENTIALLY UNDISGUISED

EFFORTS TO CONTROL THE DELEGATION LEFT A WORSE

IMPRESSION OF VIETNAM THAN THE CONTENTS OF THE

MEETINGS THE GVN SEEMS TO HAVE FEARED SO MUCH. END

SUMMARY.

PLAYING GAMES WITH THE SCHEDULE

-------------------------------

3. (C) DESPITE USCIRF REQUESTS IN WRITING, SENT

BEFORE THE DELEGATION LEFT THE UNITED STATES, THEIR

VIETNAMESE HOSTS, THE VIETNAM - USA SOCIETY, AN OFFICE

OF THE VIETNAM UNION OF FRIENDSHIP ORGANIZATIONS

(VUFO), REFUSED TO ELIMINATE ANY OF THE 26 ENGAGEMENTS

IT SCHEDULED OVER FIVE DAYS. VUFO ALSO REBUFFED A

SERIES OF ORAL REQUESTS TO STREAMLINE THE SCHEDULE

MADE BY THE DELEGATION, POLOFF, AND (SEPTEL) CONGEN

POLOFF. ON FEBRUARY 22, TRAN VAN DANG, GENERAL

SECRETARY OF THE VIETNAM FATHERLAND FRONT, VUFO\'S

SIPDIS

PARENT ORGANIZATION, "CONGRATULATED" THE DELEGATION ON

ITS "ABUNDANT" SCHEDULE, BUT SAID THAT IT WOULD BE TOO

COMPLICATED TO CHANGE. LATER, IN AN ASIDE TO POLOFF,

THE DELEGATION\'S HOST, HOANG CONG THUY, GENERAL

CONFIDENTIAL

SECRETARY OF THE VIETNAM - USA SOCIETY, SAID THAT HIS

SIPDIS

BOSS HAD TOLD HIM THERE COULD BE NO CHANGES. VUFO DID

COMPLY WITH AN EARLY REQUEST THAT THE DELEGATION TRIED

TO REVERSE AFTER IT REALIZED THAT THE CHANGE MEANT AT

MEETINGS. THEY ALSO DECIDED TO SIMPLY REFUSE TO

ATTEND EVENTS THEY HAD ASKED VUFO TO CANCEL.

MOTORBIKE TAILS

---------------

4. (C) THE DELEGATION HAD TWO UNOFFICIAL MEETINGS

(SEPTEL/REF D) IN HANOI, BUT PLAINCLOTHESMEN ON

MOTORBIKES FOLLOWED THE DELEGATION\'S EMBASSY VEHICLE

TO BOTH. THE FIRST MEETING, ON FEBRUARY 21, WAS AT

ONE PILLAR PAGODA NEAR HO CHI MINH\'S MAUSOLEUM. TWO

PLAINCLOTHESMEN DISMOUNTED FROM THEIR MOTORBIKES AND

FOLLOWED THE DELEGATION AND ACCOMPANYING EMBOFFS ONTO

THE PAGODA\'S GROUNDS ON FOOT. THE DELEGATION SPOKE

WITH A MONK IN ONE OF THE PAGODA\'S PUBLIC ROOMS FOR

ABOUT AN HOUR. EACH PLAINCLOTHESMEN CAME IN ONCE FOR

A FEW MINUTES AT A TIME, OSTENSIBLY TO PRAY AND MAKE

AN OFFERING WHILE STANDING CLOSE ENOUGH TO EAVESDROP

EASILY. THE MONK, RECOUNTING PROBLEMS WITH THE

DISTRICT GOVERNMENT (SEPTEL/REF D), SEEMED TO PAY

LITTLE ATTENTION TO THE LISTENERS.

ANOTHER TAIL AND THE PHONE CALL THAT SAID IT ALL

5. (C) ON FEBRUARY 22, USCIRF EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR

STEVEN MCFARLAND, ANALYST JOHN TAI, AND EMBOFFS PAID A

CALL ON PASTOR VINH OF THE EVANGELICAL CHURCH OF

VIETNAM (ECVN). PLAINCLOTHESMEN IN TOW, THE

DELEGATION AND EMBOFFS DROPPED BY PASTOR VINH\'S CHURCH

ON FEBRUARY 20 TO TRY TO MEET WITH HIM. PASTOR VINH

ASKED THAT THEY SEND A WRITTEN REQUEST FOR A MEETING.

THE DELEGATION COMPLIED AND HE CALLED TO ACCEPT THAT

AFTERNOON. AFTER THE DELEGATION AND EMBOFFS RETURNED

TO THE CHURCH ON FEBRUARY 22, PASTOR VINH SAID THAT

THE POLICE HAD CONTACTED HIM IMMEDIATELY AFTER THE

DELEGATION LEFT ON FEBRUARY 20 AND ASKED HIM WHAT HE

HAD DISCUSSED WITH THE DELEGATION. HE SAID THAT HE

HAD NOT SAID ANYTHING TO THE DELEGATION, BUT TOLD THE

POLICE THAT HE INTENDED TO MEET WITH THE DELEGATION

BEFORE SUNDAY. A MAN AND A WOMAN ON A MOTORBIKE

FOLLOWED THE DELEGATION FROM THEIR HOTEL TO THE ECVN

THE HOST AS PEST

6. (C) THUY SUBSEQUENTLY ASKED POLOFF ON TWO OR

THREE OCCASIONS IF THERE WERE GOING TO BE "OTHER"

OUTSIDE MEETINGS, ONCE WINKING AT POLOFF. ON FEBRUARY

23, WHILE THE DELEGATION WAS IN HUE, THUY AGAIN CALLED

POLOFF TO ASK FOR CONGEN POLOFF\'S PHONE NUMBER (EVEN

7. (C) THUY AND POLOFF HAD SEVERAL CONVERSATIONS

ABOUT WHETHER THE DELEGATION WOULD USE A GVN PROVIDED

VEHICLE OR EMBASSY AND CONGEN VEHICLES, PARTICULARLY

FOR A TRIP FROM HO CHI MINH CITY TO AN GIANG PROVINCE.

THUY PERSISTENTLY PUSHED FOR VUFO TO PROVIDE ALL THE

VEHICLES NEEDED TO ACCOMMODATE THE DELEGATION, EMBASSY

TRANSLATOR, CONGEN STAFF, AND VUFO\'S TWO HANDLERS. HE

EXPRESSED NO INTEREST IN CONGEN\'S OFFER TO PROVIDE TWO

VEHICLES, EVEN THOUGH HE AND HIS VUFO SUPERIORS

REPEATEDLY ASKED, FIRST THE DELEGATION, THEN THE

EMBASSY, IF THEY WOULD PAY VUFO\'S TRAVEL EXPENSES FOR

THE TRIP.

THE FIASCO MOVES SOUTH

----------------------

7. (C) SEPTEL DETAILS THE GVN\'S MISHANDLING OF THE

USCIRF DELEGATION IN HUE, AN GIANG, AND HO CHI MINH

CITY. POLICE CONFINED AT LEAST TWO INDIVIDUALS TO

THEIR HOMES TEMPORARILY. TWO OTHERS REPORTEDLY LOST

PHONE SERVICE AFTER AGREEING TO MEET USCIRF. WITH THE

DELEGATION IN A CONGEN VEHICLE AND VUFO IN ANOTHER,

VARIOUS EXASPERATING CIRCUMSTANCES PROLONGED THE TRIP

TO AND FROM AN GIANG. POLICE GATHERED CONSPICUOUSLY

OUTSIDE PLACES WHERE THE DELEGATION HAD OR MIGHT HAVE

HAD UNOFFICIAL MEETINGS, INCLUDING THE CONSUL

GENERAL\'S RESIDENCE. FINALLY, VUFO\'S THUY BECAME

INCREASINGLY UNPLEASANT AS THE VISIT WORE ON.

8. (U) THE USCIRF DELEGATION WAS UNABLE TO REVIEW

THIS CABLE BEFORE THEY DEPARTED VIETNAM.
