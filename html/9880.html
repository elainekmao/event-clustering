SUBJECT: U/S JARALLAH ON CIC REPRESENTATIVE

REF: A. STATE 206458 B. KUWAIT 03266

1. (U) This is an action request. Please see para. 3.

2. (C) The Ambassador raised the issue of Kuwait\'s

designation of a Council for International Coordination (CIC)

representative with MFA U/S Khaled Al-Jarallah during their

July 27 meeting. Jarallah said that Kuwaiti participation

would be "difficult" because the GOK had no official presence

on the ground in Iraq. When reminded by the Ambassador that

the GOK does have Ministry of Interior officials there as

part of Task Force Hope, Jarallah indicated Kuwait should be

willing to designate a representative, as long as security

could be assured. (Note: Kuwait currently has 7 Ministry of

Interior and 2 Ministry of Defense personnel attached to Task

Force Hope. All are there under the auspices of coalition

forces, to search for Kuwaitis missing since the Iraqi

occupation. End Note.)

2. (C) Action Request: Post requests department\'s assistance

in ascertaining what living and security arrangements will be

made for CIC representatives, so we may pass this on for GOK

consideration.

3. (U) Baghdad minimize considered.
