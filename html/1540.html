SUBJECT: UNENTHUSIASTIC ZIMBABWE RULING PARTY TRYING TO

RIDE OUT MUGABE\'S STORM

REFTEL: 00 HARARE 7134

CLASSIFIED BY CHARGE D\'AFFAIRES EARL IRVING FOR REASONS:

1.5 (B) AND (D).

1. (C) SUMMARY: THE MAJORITY OF ZIMBABWE\'S RULING ZANU-

PF PARTY MEMBERS ARE UNHAPPY WITH PRESIDENT MUGABE AND

THE WAY HE IS RUNNING THE COUNTRY, BUT THEY WILL NOT

OFFER MORE THAN PASSIVE RESISTANCE TO HIS DESTRUCTIVE

POLICIES, OUR CONTACTS TELL US. THERE IS NOT LIKELY TO

BE MUCH ENTHUSIASM FOR CAMPAIGNING FOR MUGABE IN THE

UPCOMING PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION, ACCORDING TO A FORMER

ZANU-PF MP. THE ONLY ENTHUSIASTIC CAMPAIGNERS -- AND

POTENTIAL MUGABE SUCCESSORS -- WILL BE THOSE WHO HAVE

BENEFITED RECENTLY FROM ZANU-PF\'S REWARD SYSTEM. A SNAP

PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION IS UNLIKELY BECAUSE IT IS TOO

RISKY FOR MUGABE, ACCORDING TO OUR SOURCES. MEANWHILE,

SOME REFORM-MINDED "YOUNG TURKS" ARE ATTEMPTING TO

MODERATE THE PARTY\'S MORE OUTLANDISH SWIPES AT THE RULE

OF LAW. FOR MOST ZANU-PF MEMBERS, HOWEVER, SURVIVAL IS

THE BYWORD OF THE DAY. END SUMMARY.

2. (C) ON FEBRUARY 15, POLOFF MET WITH MICHAEL MATAURE,

A FORMER ZANU-PF MP FOR CHIMANIMANI AND CURRENT HEAD OF

A LOCAL NGO THAT FOCUSES ON PARLIAMENTARY ISSUES AND

WORKS WITH MPS. (COMMENT: TOWARD THE END OF HIS TENURE

IN THE LAST PARLIAMENT, MATAURE WAS AN OUTSPOKEN CRITIC

OF ZANU-PF\'S TOP-DOWN STRUCTURE AND ATTEMPTS TO QUASH

INTERNAL DISSENT. END COMMENT.) MATAURE DESCRIBED THE

CURRENT ATMOSPHERE IN PARLIAMENT AS "POISONED" SINCE

MOST MPS ON BOTH SIDES OF THE AISLE SEE ISSUES ONLY

THROUGH A POLITICAL PRISM. (NOTE: THIS SENTIMENT WAS

ALSO EXPRESSED BY WEBSTER SHAMU, ZANU-PF MP FOR CHEGUTU,

IN A CONVERSATION WITH POLOFF FEBRUARY 13. END NOTE.)

MATAURE REPORTED THAT LITTLE PROGRESS HAS BEEN MADE IN

THE IMPEACHMENT EFFORT AGAINST PRESIDENT MUGABE BECAUSE

THE ZANU-PF CHAIR IS USING ALL MANNER OF DELAYING

TACTICS TO DRAG OUT THE PROCEEDINGS. NO HEARINGS TO

INVESTIGATE THE CHARGES AGAINST THE PRESIDENT HAVE BEEN

SCHEDULED, HE ADDED.

ZANU-PF REMAINS QUIETLY DISGRUNTLED

-----------------------------------

3. (C) MATAURE CONTINUED BY DESCRIBING ZANU-PF AS A

"DISGRUNTLED" AND "DIVIDED" PARTY, THE RECENT STRING OF

BY-ELECTION VICTORIES NOTWITHSTANDING. THERE IS A

"SILENT MAJORITY" OF ZANU-PF MEMBERS WHO DO NOT APPROVE

OF PRESIDENT MUGABE\'S VIOLENT TACTICS TO COW OPPONENTS

AND WANT HIM TO LEAVE POWER, BUT THEY FEEL THEY HAVE TO

KEEP QUIET ON THE SUBJECT OR THEIR CAREERS WILL MEET AN

4. (C) PAUL MANGWANA, A YOUNG ZANU-PF MP FOR KADOMA

6. (C) THE LEVEL OF UNHAPPINESS IN ZANU-PF WITH MUGABE

IS LIKELY TO TRANSLATE INTO A LACK OF ENTHUSIASM FOR

MUGABE\'S PRESIDENTIAL CAMPAIGN, MATAURE THEORIZED. IT

IS TELLING, HE SAID, HOW LITTLE CABINET MEMBERS AND

OTHER ZANU-PF MEMBERS HAVE VOICED PUBLIC SUPPORT FOR

MUGABE SINCE THE DECEMBER PARTY CONGRESS (REFTEL). THE

FEW WHO DO ACTIVELY CAMPAIGN FOR MUGABE ARE THE ONES TO

BE VIEWED AS MUGABE\'S POTENTIAL SUCCESSORS, MATAURE

ASSERTED, EVEN CALLING THEM THE PRESIDENT\'S "RUNNING

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 HARARE 00816 02 OF 02 231136Z

MATES." YOUTH DEVELOPMENT MINISTER BORDER GEZI, LOCAL

GOVERNMENT MINISTER IGNATIUS CHOMBO, AND SPEAKER OF

PARLIAMENT EMMERSON MNANGAGWA ARE POTENTIAL "RUNNING

MATES" BECAUSE OF THEIR UNSWERVING LOYALTY TO MUGABE.

THE PRESIDENT VALUES LOYALTY ABOVE ALL ELSE, MATAURE

ARGUED, AND DOLES OUT PATRONAGE BASED ON THAT

ALLEGIANCE. THE NEEDS OF THIS REWARD SYSTEM AND THE

PARTY ARE "PARAMOUNT" BEFORE THE NEEDS OF THE COUNTRY,

HE OPINED.

7. (C) WHEN ASKED ABOUT THE POSSIBILITY OF A SNAP

PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION THIS YEAR, MATAURE DESCRIBED THAT

SCENARIO AS VERY RISKY FOR MUGABE, AND THEREFORE,

UNLIKELY. THE PRESIDENT IS SO UNPOPULAR RIGHT NOW, HE

WOULD HAVE TO DEPLOY 250,000 PEOPLE ACROSS THE COUNTRY

TO CANVASS FOR HIM, AND ZANU-PF SIMPLY DOES NOT HAVE THE

RESOURCES TO MOUNT SUCH A CAMPAIGN, THE FORMER MP

STATED. THE ONLY WAY MUGABE COULD WIN IS IF THERE WAS A

THREE- OR FOUR-WAY RACE AMONG HIGH-PROFILE CANDIDATES

THAT ESSENTIALLY SPLIT THE OPPOSITION VOTE, MATAURE

SUGGESTED, AND THAT DOES NOT APPEAR LIKELY AT THIS TIME.

BY APRIL 1, 2002. THOSE WHO ARE NOT WILLING TO TRY TO

THE SCENES ARE LYING LOW UNTIL THE STORM BLOWS OVER.
