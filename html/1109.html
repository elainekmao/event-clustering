SUBJECT: DUTCH GOVERNMENT NOTIFIED OF SOESTERBERG

FACILITIES CLOSURE

REF: SECSTATE 100360

CLASSIFIED BY POLCOUNS STEPHEN R. KELLY; REASONS 1.5 (B,D).

1.(C) EMBASSY DELIVERED REFTEL NOTIFICATION TO THE

NETHERLANDS MFA ON 6/5. TO DATE, THE MFA HAS MADE NO COMMENT

ON THE NOTIFICATION.

2.(SBU) COMMENT IN OTHER FORA ABOUT PREVIOUSLY HELD INFORMAL

CONSULTATIONS REGARDING THE SOESTERBERG FACILITY TERMINATION

HAS BEEN LIMITED. ON MAY 17, IN A WRITTEN RESPONSE TO A

LETTER EARLIER SUBMITTED TO THE MOD BY VVD DEFENSE

SPOKERPERSON THEO VAN DEN DOEL, STATE SECRETARY FOR DEFENSE

HENK VAN HOOF INFORMED THE PARLIAMENT THAT HE HAD BEEN

"INFORMED ORALLY BY THE U.S. ON MAY 4 THAT THE COB STATUS OF

SOESTERBERG AIR BASE HAD EXPIRED. THE PROMISE WAS MADE THAT

THIS WOULD SOON BE CONFIRMED IN WRITING. NOW THAT THE COB

STATUS HAS EXPIRED, THE NUMBER OF FLIGHT MOVEMENTS RESERVED

FOR JET FIGHTERS HAS BEEN CANCELLED...SINCE THE AIRBASE WILL

REMAIN OPEN, THE REQUIRED AND EXISTING FACILITIES TO KEEP IT

OPEN MUST BE MAINTAINED. THE BULK OF THE U.S. AIR FORCE\'S

FACILITIES AND INFRASTRUCTURE HAS BEEN GIVEN ANOTHER

DESTINATION SINCE THE AMERICAN UNIT LEFT. SOESTERBERG AIR

BASE WILL REMAIN THE HOME BASE OF TWO (DUTCH) TRANSPORT

HELICOPTER SQUADRONS AND A RESERVE BASE FOR DUTCH F-16\'S."
