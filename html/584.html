2. IZETBEGOVIC FOREIGN POLICY ADVISOR HAJRUDIN SOMUN

HAS PASSED TO US A VIDEOTAPE DEALING WITH THE

SITUATION IN B-H AND REPORTED WAR CRIMES. THE TAPE

INCLUDES ON-SITE DESCRIPTIONS OF ATROCITIES COMMITTED

IN VARIOUS B-H LOCALES. SPEAKERS ARE NON-PROFESSIONAL

LOCAL RESIDENTS WHO APPEAR TO HAVE WITNESSED CERTAIN

OF THE CRIMES. SOME VICTIMS ARE ALSO INTERVIEWED AND

THEIR INJURIES GRAPHICALLY DISPLAYED. MOST OF THE

FOOTAGE IS ON-SCREEN-DATED AS EARLY SUMMER 1992.

WHILE THE TAPE IS NOT WELL EDITED, AND INCLUDES

EXTENSIVE, CLOSE-UP COVERAGE OF MEDICAL OPERATIONS,

LARGE PARTS OF IT MIGHT NONETHELESS BE OF USE TO

MEMBERS OF THE COMMISSION OR THEIR STAFF PERSONALLY

UNFAMILIAR WITH THE TERRAIN AND ATMOSPHERICS OF

WAR-TORN BOSNIA. ITS AMATEUR QUALITY ADDS TO ITS

CREDIBILITY. IT IS ENTIRELY IN SERBO-CROATIAN.

-

3. SOMUN SAID WE COULD MAKE ANY APPROPRIATE USE OF

THE TAPE WE WISHED. IF MISSION BELIEVES THAT THIS

TAPE WOULD BE OF USE TO BASSIOUNI OR OTHER COMMISSION

MEMBERS OR STAFF, PLEASE ADVISE AND WE WILL SEND IT

BY "OVERNIGHT" EXPRESS.
