SUBJECT: SANT\'EGIDIO/UNITA TALKING PEACE

REF: 01VATICAN 4030

CLASSIFIED BY: CHARGE MERANTE, REASON E.O. 12958 1.5 (B,D)

1. (C) IN AN ASIDE TO POLOFF BEFORE A 12/20/01 MEETING WITH

S/E DANFORTH TO DISCUSS SUDAN PEACE INITIATIVES (REPORTED

SEPTEL), SANT\'EGIDIO SPIRITUAL DIRECTOR AND AFRICA HAND DON

MATTEO ZUPPI RELATED HIS RECENT CONVERSATIONS WITH UNITA

LEADER SAVIMBI AND LOCAL UNITA REP. ZUPPI, A FLUENT

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 VATICA 00057 041304Z

PORTUGUESE SPEAKER WITH LONG EXPERIENCE IN AFRICA, HAS BEEN

ACTIVELY PRESSING UNITA CONTACTS TO OFFER A MEANINGFUL

CEASEFIRE SINCE BEFORE HIS APPEAL FOR USG SUPPORT (REPORTED

REFTEL) SOME MONTHS AGO. HE TOLD POLOFF THAT SAVIMBI HAD

INDICATED TO HIM DURING A PHONE CONVERSATION TWO WEEKS BEFORE

CHRISTMAS THAT UNITA, AS A CONFIDENCE BUILDING MEASURE, WOULD

OFFER A UNILATERAL THREE-MONTH CEASEFIRE STARTING AROUND

CHRISTMAS. ACCORDING TO ZUPPI, SAVIMBI ONLY NEEDED TO

CONSULT WITH HIS "GENERALS" BEFORE ANNOUNCING IT.

2. (C) ZUPPI SAID HE LATER SPOKE WITH THE UNITA

REPRESENTATIVE IN ROME, WHO TOLD HIM THAT IN A PRE-CHRISTMAS

TELEVISION INTERVIEW ANGOLAN PRESIDENT CHISSANO HAD MADE

SCATHING REMARKS ABOUT DRIVING THE UNITA REBELS INTO THE SEA,

THUS MAKING SAVIMBI\'S OFFER HARDER TO CARRY OUT. ZUPPI

SPECULATED THAT BOTH CHISSANO AND SAVIMBI WERE MORE

"PRIMITIVE" THAN HAD BEEN HIS INTERLOCUTORS IN MOZAMBIQUE,

AND THAT THEIR RHETORIC FREQUENTLY HAMPERS MOVEMENT TOWARD A

PEACEFUL SETTLEMENT. NEVERTHELESS, MATTEO AVERRED, WITH A

LITTLE PUSH FROM OUTSIDE HE BELIEVED THE ANGOLANS COULD

DUPLICATE THE MOZAMBIQUE EXPERIENCE (I.E. AN OFF-SITE

DIALOGUE LEADING TO A PEACE AGREEMENT, MEDIATED BY

SANT\'EGIDIO).

3. (C) QUERIED ABOUT THE ANGOLAN CATHOLIC BISHOPS\' RECENT

CALLS FOR A PEACE DIALOGUE, ZUPPI RESPONDED THAT THEY WERE

WELL MEANING BUT EQUALLY UNSOPHISTICATED, AND WEAK. HE NOTED

THAT THEY DIDN\'T HAVE ANYTHING TO OFFER BEYOND THEIR APPEAL;

WHAT THEY NEEDED WAS AN ACTION PLAN.

4. (C) COMMENT: AFTER REFTEL CONVERSATION, WE WERE

PLEASANTLY SURPRISED TO HEAR THAT DON MATTEO HAD SPOKEN

DIRECTLY WITH SAVIMBI ABOUT A UNILATERAL CEASEFIRE. WE ASKED

WHETHER THE THREE- MONTH TIMEFRAME MIGHT HAVE BEEN TOO

AMBITIOUS; ZUPPI APPEARED TO US PATIENT, BUT FIRM IN HIS

INTENT TO KEEP PROBING SAVIMBI FOR WHAT HE CALLED A

MEANINGFUL GESTURE. WE LEAVE IT TO THOSE MORE FULLY

KNOWLEDGEABLE OF THE POLITICAL DYNAMICS OF THE PARTIES TO

DECIDE WHETHER THESE CONVERSATIONS POINT TO THE POSSIBILITY

OF A BREAKTHROUGH -- ALTHOUGH WE HASTEN TO ADD IT HAS BEEN

MORE THAN A WEEK SINCE CHRISTMAS AND WE HAVE NOT SEEN ANY

REPORT OF AN ANGOLA CEASEFIRE.
