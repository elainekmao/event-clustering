1. FOLLOWING IS TEXT OF A LETTER TO THE PRESIDENT DATED

FEBRUARY 4, 2003 FROM TONY P. HALL, AMBASSADOR, U.S.

MISSION TO THE UN AGENCIES, ROME. BEGIN QUOTE:

THE PRESIDENT

THE WHITE HOUSE

WASHINGTON, DC 20500

DEAR MR. PRESIDENT:

I WANT TO THANK YOU FOR YOUR COMMITMENT AND DEDICATION TO

FEEDING THE HUNGRY AND HELPING THOSE LESS FORTUNATE. MY

WHOLE STAFF AND I WERE GREATLY ENCOURAGED BY YOUR STATEMENT

IN THE STATE OF THE UNION ABOUT HOW GENEROUS AMERICA IS IN

FEEDING THE WORLD. IT IS SOMETHING I SAY ALL THE TIME.

AS YOU KNOW, YOUR PROPOSAL FOR A FAMINE FUND AND THE

EMERGENCY PLAN FOR AIDS RELIEF COULD NOT COME AT A BETTER

TIME. MORE THAN 38 MILLION PEOPLE ARE THREATENED BY

STARVATION IN AFRICA ALONE, AND THAT SITUATION IS EVEN MORE

COMPLEX BECAUSE OF THE AIDS PANDEMIC. IN ALL OF MY YEARS

WORKING TO FIGHT HUNGER, I HAVE NEVER SEEN SO MANY SERIOUS

CRISES DEMANDING SO MUCH ASSISTANCE AT ONCE. I AM HEADED

BACK TO ETHIOPIA WHERE I EXPECT TO FIND CHILDREN STARVING,

AS I DID WHEN I WENT IN 1984.

I KNOW THERE ARE MANY THINGS THAT DEMAND YOUR TIME AND

ATTENTION, BUT I WANTED TO LET YOU KNOW HOW MUCH I

APPRECIATE YOUR HUMANITARIAN EFFORTS. WE WILL CONTINUE TO

DO OUR BEST TO PUT INTO ACTION AMERICA\'S COMMITMENT TO

ALLEVIATE HUNGER AND BUILD HOPE, THROUGH COMMUNICATING AND

DEMONSTRATING AMERICA\'S COMPASSION FOR PEOPLE IN NEED.

WHILE I HOPE THAT OUR ALLIES IN THE G-8 RESPOND TO YOUR

INITIATIVE AND CREATE OTHER FAMINE FUNDS, EVEN IF YOU DON\'T

GET CREDIT ON EARTH, YOU WILL IN HEAVEN.

THANK YOU AGAIN FOR ALL THAT YOU\'RE DOING ON BEHALF OF THE

POOR AND HUNGRY. GOD BLESS.

SINCERELY, TONY P. HALL AMBASSADOR. END QUOTE.

2.ORIGINAL SIGNED LETTER WILL FOLLOW.
