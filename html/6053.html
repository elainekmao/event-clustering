SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION: ARAB SUMMIT UAE PLAN FOR IRAQ

1. SUMMARY: THE ARAB SUMMIT IN SHARM EL-SHEIKH RECEIVED PRIMARY

AND EXTENSIVE COVERAGE IN THE LOCAL MEDIA. NEWS REPORTING AND

EDITORIALS CRITICIZED THE ARAB SUMMIT FOR NOT INCLUDING SHEIKH

ZAYID\'S PLAN FOR IRAQ IN ITS FINAL RESOLUTION. EDITORIALS

CRITICIZED THE ARAB LEADERS FOR NOT CONSIDERING OR DEBATING

SHEIKH ZAYID\'S PLAN FOR IRAQ AS A POSSIBLE WAY OUT OF THE CURRENT

IMPASSE. AN "AL KHALEEJ" EDITORIAL STRONGLY CRITICIZED SECRETARY

POWELL\'S REQUEST OF THE ARAB SUMMIT TO ADVISE SADDAM TO SEEK

EXILE AS A SHAMELESS DIPLOMATIC PLOY. END SUMMARY.

2. CRITICIZING ARAB ATTITUDES TOWARDS THE UAE\'S INITIATIVE FOR

IRAQ PRESENTED TO THE ARAB SUMMIT, ABU DHABI-BASED SEMI-

GOVERNMENT "AL-ITTIHAD" EDITORIALIZED ON 3/02:

"... ZAYED ADVISED THE ARAB LEADERS OF THE EXPECTED EARTHQUAKE,

AND, WHILE IN DIRE NEED OF COUNSEL, THEY HAVE BEEN NEGLECTING ALL

ADVICE DURING THE PREVIOUS DECADES, NOT REALIZING THE GRAVE

CONCEQUENCES OF THEIR ACTIONS, WHICH CAUSED ALL (PREVIOUS) CRISES

IN THE FIRST PLACE ..."

3. REMARKING ON THE DECISIONS MADE AT AND THE U.S. ATTITUDE

TOWARDS THE ARAB SUMMIT, SHARJAH-BASED PAN-ARAB "AL KHALEEJ"

EDITORIALIZED ON 3/02:

"... GIVEN THE U.S. ATTITUDE OVERLOOKING THE INTERNATIONAL ANTI-

WAR MOOD, AND ITS INSISTANCE ON CHANGING THE FACTS AS A

JUSTIFICATION FOR ITS ATTACKS, IS IT POSSIBLE THAT THEY WILL TAKE

INTO CONSIDERATION THE DECISIONS MADE BY AN ARAB SUMMIT IN SHARM

AL SHEIKH, WHILE REALIZING THAT THE ARABS WERE NEVER SERIOUS

ABOUT ANY OF THEIR DECISIONS?"

4. COMMENTING ON THE UAE\'S INITIATIVE ANNOUNCED TO THE ARAB

SUMMIT, ABU DHABI-BASED PAN-ARAB DAILY "AKHBAR AL ARAB"

EDITORIALIZED ON 3/02:

"... THE UAE\'S INITIATIVE CALLING ON SADDAM TO STEP DOWN AND

LEAVE IRAQ WITH INTERNATIONAL GUARANTEES TO PROTECT HIS DIGNITY

IS A PRICE THAT SHOULD BE PAID TO AVERT WAR AND IS THE CHEAPEST

PRICE TO SAVE THE PEOPLE, THE COUNTRY, AND THE WHOLE REGION. THE

CONCEQUENCES OF ANY OTHER CHOICE ARE WELL-KNOWN BECAUSE THE U.S.

IS DETERMINED ON PLAYING TO THE VERY END TO TOPPLE THE IRAQI

REGIME."

5. COMMENTING ON THE ARAB SUMMIT AND THE AMERICAN DISDAIN FOR

IT, "AL KHALEEJ" EDITORIALIZED ON 3/01:

"... THE IRAQIS ARE THREATENED BY ANOTHER \'TRANSFER\' WHEN

DISCUSSING THE EFFECTS OF THE ANTICIPATED BUSH-BLAIR WAR THAT

COULD LEAD TO THE EMIGRATION OF TWO MILLION IRAQIS. ACCORDING TO

STATISTICS, IT MIGHT ALSO CAUSE THE DEATHS AND INJURIES OF

ANOTHER MILLION AS A RESULT OF THE AMERICAN PLANS TO BOMB IRAQ

WITH THOUSANDS OF MISSILES. THE WHITE HOUSE IS PLANNING ON

TRANSFORMING IRAQ FROM AN ARAB COUNTRY INTO AN AMERICAN TERRITORY

IN THE HEART OF THE ARAB WORLD AND THEN APPLYING THIS "MODEL" IN

THE REGION ... THE LEAST THAT COULD BE SAID ABOUT THE

INSTRUCTIONS SUBMITTED TO THE ARAB SUMMIT BY COLIN POWELL, U.S.

SECRETARY OF STATE, IS THAT IT REPRESENTS A SHAMELESS DIPLOMATIC

SIPDIS

PLOY TO IMPOSE CERTAIN OPINIONS ON THE SUMMIT. THIS MUST URGE

THE ARABS TO TAKE A STRONG STAND IN THE SUMMIT FOLLOWING U.S.

DISDAIN OF ARABS AND ANYTHING THEY REPRESENT ... THE SUMMIT IS

REQUESTED TO ASSERT TO THE U.S THAT THE ARABS HAVE REACHED A

POINT WHERE THEY KNOW WHAT WORKS BEST FOR THEM. WHAT IS REQUIRED

IS A UNIFIED ARAB ATTITUDE AND NOT A RAMBLING STAND FED BY THE

U.S TO ACHIEVE ITS GOALS AND THOSE OF ISRAEL. WITHOUT THIS, THE

CHANCE WILL BE LOST, JUST AS OTHERS BEFORE IT, AND THERE WILL BE

NO NEED FOR ANY SUMMITS SINCE THE U.S. WILL BE POSITIONED IN THE

ARAB REGION CONTROLLING EVERYTHING. THE CURRENT IRAQI CRISIS,

AFTER THE PALESTINIAN CRISIS, WILL PAVE THE WAY FOR FURTHER

CRISES IN THE REGION."

6. NOTING SADDAM\'S ATTITUDE TOWARDS THE ANTICIPATED WAR, EDITOR-

IN-CHIEF OF "AL-BAYAN" KHALED MUHAMMAD AHMAD WROTE ON 3/01:

"... AT A TIME WHEN THE WHOLE WORLD, AND THE ARAB WORLD IN

PARTICULAR, IS BUSY WITH THE ANTICIPATED U.S. WAR AGAINST IRAQ,

IRAQI NEWSPAPERS PUBLISHED \'SADDAM\'S ORDERS\' TO HIGHLY RANKED

OFFICIALS AND SENIOR MILITARY OFFICERS TO LOSE WEIGHT OR ELSE

LOSE PART OF THEIR WAGES AND OTHER PRIVALAGES ... SADDAM HAS

PUSHED THE ARABS INTO MANY WARS; THE WAR WITH IRAN WHICH ENDED

WITH IRAQ\'S DEFEAT, AND \'THE MOTHER OF ALL BATTLES\' WHICH ENDED

WITH THE COMPLETE COLLAPSE OF IRAQ. HOW DOES SADDAM WANT TO END

THIS THIRD WAR, WHICH SEEMS VERY IMMINENT? ARABS ARE HELPLESS

AND ARE EVEN UNABLE TO STOP IT!!"
