SUBJECT: LEFTIST MAGAZINE CLAIMS PKK "WILL HIT THE U.S. ARMY"

1. (C) WE SET OUT BELOW IN SOME DETAIL AN ARTICLE WHICH

APPEARED IN THE LEFTIST WEEKLY "2000\'E DOGRU" (TOWARDS THE YEAR

2000) MAY 30. WE DO SO NOT BECAUSE WE BELIEVE IT NECESSARILY

IS ACCURATE (IT CONTAINS A NUMEBER OF OBVIOUS ERRORS, INCLUDING

THE CLAIM THAT THE PKK ACTIVELY OPPOSED SADDAM), BUT BECAUSE IT

IS REFLECTIVE OF ELITE, URBAN, LEFTIST THOUGHT IN TURKEY. THE

GIST OF THE ARTICLE IS THAT, ASSUMING IRAQI KURDISH LEADERS

REACH A POLITICAL ACCOMODATION WITH BAGHDAD, THEN U.S. FORCES

WILL BECOME LEGITIMATE TARGETS IF THEY DO NOT WITHDRAW. THE

ARTICLE WOULD APPEAR TO CONTAIN NO CURRENT THREAT.

2. (U) BEGIN TEXT.

THE PKK IS THOUGHT TO HAVE PLAYED AN ACTIVE ROLE IN THE KURDISH

REBELLION IN NORTHERN IRAQ. WHEN THE "CHAHS" REVOLTED AGAINST

THE IRAQI REGIME, MANY PKK FIGHTERS WERE IN NORTHERN IRAQ.

SINCE THE ARRIVAL OF U.S. FORCES IN THE AREA, PKK FORCES HAVE

VANISHED. "2000\'E DOGRU" VISITED A PKK CAMP IN NOTHERN IRAQ TO

TALK WITH LEADERS WHO TOOK PART IN THE UPRISING.

WE FIRST ASKED "WHY HAVE (PKK FIGHTERS) NOT BEEN SEEN?" THE

REPLY WAS "IT IS ONLY A MATTER OF TIME BEFORE WE CLASH WITH THE

U.S. WE ARE GAZING AT EACH OTHER DOWN THE BARREL OF A GUN, AND

WE MUST BE CAUTIOUS."

WE REACHED THE CAMPSITE, CALLED \'BEHIND SEVEN MOUNTAINS,\' AFTER

A LONG DRIVE AND A TWO-AND-A-HALF HOUR WALK. THE PKK MILITANTS

ASKED US NOT TO MENTION THEIR NAMES UNNECESSARILY, CITING THE

EXAMPLE OF LEADER ABDULLAH OZCALAN, WHO CRITICIZED EXAGGERATED

USE OF HIS NAME IN SONGS AT THE 4TH PKK CONVENTION...

PKK COMMANDER BOTAN DISCUSSED THE GROUP\'S STAND IN THE

REBELLION. "AFTER 22 DAYS OF FIGHTING WE BEGAN RETREATING FOR

PSYCHOLOGICAL REASONS. SOME PESHMERGES WHO SAW THE GROWING

PANIC ESCAPED AHEAD OF THE CIVILIANS. WE TRIED TO STOP THEM.

OUR WOMEN SHAMED SOME INTO STAYING, SAYING "WE WOMEN ARE GOING

TO FIGHT. AREN\'T YOU ASHAMED TO RUN?" WE FOUGHT SADDAM\'S

TROOPS UNTIL THE LAST MOMENT AND THEN ESCAPED.

PKK LEADERS DENIED REPORTS THE PKK RECENTLY OPENED FIRE AT A

U.S. HELICOPTER AND LOST NINE OF ITS MILITANTS WHEN THE CRAFT

RETURNED FIRE. THEY ADDED, HOWEVER, "IT IS ONLY A MATTER OF

TIME BEFORE WE CLASH WITH THE AMERICANS," AND SAID THEY HAD

HEARD FROM THE KDP (KURDISH DEMOCRATIC PARTY) THAT AMERICANS

FEAR POSSIBLE PKK ATTACKS.

WE ASKED IF THE PKK COULD WIN A FIGHT WITH THE U.S. THE

LEADERS SAID "THE ISSUE IS NOT TO WIN OR LOSE. WE EXIST TO

STRUGGLE AGAINST IMPERIALISM AND COLONIALISM. IF THE U.S.

OCCUPATION CONTINUES, WE WILL STRUGGLE AGAINST IT. IT WOULD BE

OUR GREATEST ANTI-IMPERIALIST STRUGGLE. KURDISTAN HAS THE

POLITICAL POTENTIAL AND THE ORGANIZED MILITARY POWER TO

OVERWHELM IMPERIALISM IN THE MIDDLE EAST. WE INVITE ALL

PARTIES WHO FAVOR REVOLUTION IN THE MIDDLE EAST TO FIGHT. WE

SUPPORT ANY INITIATIVE IN THIS RESPECT."

THE PKK CLAIMS THE U.S. HAS SETTLED IN BOTH SOUTHERN AND

NORTHERN KURDISTAN. IT ADVOCATES TAKING UP THE STRUGGLE

AGAINST IMPERILISM ON BOTH FRONTS BECAUSE U.S. OCCUPATION IS

THE COMMON PROBLEM OF KURDS, TURKS, ARABS, AND IRANIANS. A

MIDDLE-EAST REVOLUTION COULD SOLVE THIS PROBLEM. "WE WOULD

LIKE TO CREATE A KURDISTAN THAT WOULD BE THE CENTER OF AN

ANTI-IMPERIALIST WAR."

THE U.S. TRIES TO MAKE PEOPLE (IN NORTHERN IRAQ) BELIEVE ITS

MOTIVE IN COMING TO THE REGION IS THEIR RESCUE - AND THE KDP

AND YNK SUPPORT THIS. IN SOUTHERN KURDISTAN THE U.S. IS TRYING

TO WIN POPULAR SUPPORT BY MEETING PEOPLE\'S NEEDS, INCLUDING

THEIR NEED FOR FOOD. THE US, HOWEVER, WILL DESTROY PEOPLE IN

THIS REGION JUST AS IT ONCE DID IN THE PHILIPPINES.

THE U.S. ALREADY HAS THE PEOPLE OF SOUTHERN KURDISTAN IN ITS

POLITICAL, MILITARY, AND CULTURAL GRIP. IT LEASHES THEM LIKE

DOGS, USING THE RUSE OF \'ASSISTANCE\' TO BIND THEM.

WE DO NOT WANT TO BE UNDER THE CONTROL OF SADDAM, THE TURKISH

STATE, ANY OTHER REGIONAL POWER, OR THE U.S. WHAT THE U.S.

DOES AT PRESENT IS OCCUPY SOUTHERN KURDISTAN. WE WOULD LIKE

LEGAL GROUNDS TO HIT THE U.S. IF THE SO-CALLED REPRESENTATIVES

OF SOUTHERN KURDISTAN REACH AGREEMENT WITH THE IRAQI

ADMINISTRATION, THE U.S. WILL HAVE NO REASON TO REMAIN IN

KURDISTAN, AND WE WILL STRIKE.

WE CONSIDER THE KDP THE ARMED GANG OF THE U.S. AND ITS ALLIES.

A NEW MARXIST-LENINIST PARTY HAS FORMED IN SOUTHERN KURDISTAN.

IT IS ATTRACTING MANY SUPPORTERS AND WE, THE PKK, WILL THROW

OUR FULL STRENGTH BEHIND IT.

END TEXT.

ABRAMOWITZ
