SUBJECT: IWC MEETING - ITALY UNLIKELY TO VOTE BUT STILL

PLANS TO ATTEND

REFTEL: STATE 190306

1. ON OCTOBER 4, POST PRESENTED REFTEL DEMARCHE TO SILVIA

DE BERTOLDI, ASSISTANT TO GIUSEPPE AMBROSIO, THE ITALIAN IWC

COMMISSIONER. DE BERTOLDI WAS CONFIDENT THAT ITALY WOULD

HAVE BEEN ABLE TO VOTE AT THE IWC SPECIAL MEETING SINCE THE

FISHERIES DIRECTOR GENERAL HAD SIGNED THE IWC-ARREARS

PAYMENT DECREE ON SEPT. 30. UNFORTUNATELY, DURING A FOLLOW-

UP CONVERSATION, DE BERTOLDI TOLD US OCT 8 THAT THE ITALIAN

BUREAUCRACY WOULD TAKE MUCH LONGER THAN EXPECTED AND THAT

THE GOI WOULD NOT BE ABLE TO PAY ITS 2001 ASSESSMENT BEFORE

THE OCT. 14-17 MEETING.

2. DE BERTOLDI WAS TOLD BY THE IWC SECRETARIAT THAT A

"SIGNED PROMISE TO PAY" WOULD NOT BE SUFFICIENT FOR VOTING,

BUT THAT A GOI CHECK COULD BE ACCEPTED. SINCE "BRINGING A

GOI CHECK" IMPLIES INCLUSION OF A TREASURY MINISTRY OFFICER

IN THE DELEGATION, THE CHANCE THAT ITALY WILL VOTE IS LOW.

DE BERTOLDI TOLD US THAT IF ITALY COULD NOT VOTE, THE GOI

WILL PROBABLY NOT ATTEND THE MEETING, BECAUSE OF A

UNDERSTANDABLE EMBARRASSMENT. HOWEVER, WHEN WE EMPHASIZED

THE IMPORTANCE OF ITALY\'S PARTICIPATION TO ESTABLISH A

QUORUM SO THAT THE BOWHEAD QUOTA COULD BE ADOPTED, DE

BERTOLDI TOLD US THAT THE GOI WOULD BE PRESENT.

3. THE GOI WILL BE REPRESENTED BY DE BERTOLDI, AND PROF.

NOTARBARTOLO DI SCIARRA (THE FORMER COMMISSIONER) - AND

LIKELY A TREASURY MINISTRY OFFICER.
