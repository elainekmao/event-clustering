SUBJECT: AFGHANISTAN: ALL SYSTEMS (STILL) GO FOR FORMER

KING\'S RETURN

REF: ROME 1774

CLASSIFIED BY: POL MINCOUNS TOM COUNTRYMAN, REASONS 1.5 (B) AND (D).

1. (C) SUMMARY: THE SON-IN-LAW TO THE FORMER AFGHAN KING

SAID THREATS AND DISTURBANCES WILL NOT DETER THE KING AND HIS

PARTY FROM THEIR RETURN TO AFGHANISTAN. THEY ARE PLEASED

WITH ARRANGEMENTS BEING MADE BY ITALY IN CONSULTATION WITH

THE AIA, THEY REMAIN IN CLOSE TOUCH WITH CHAIRMAN KARZAI, AND

ALL SYSTEMS ARE GO FOR THE RETURN. END SUMMARY.

2. (C) IN A MARCH 9 CONVERSATION WITH POLOFF, GENERAL ABDUL

WALI, SON-IN-LAW AND ADVISER TO FORMER AFGHAN KING ZAHIR

SHAH, SAID SECURITY ARRANGEMENTS AND ALL OTHER LOGISTICAL

PREPARATIONS FOR THE KING\'S APRIL 17 RETURN ARE WELL UNDERWAY

AND ARE SATISFACTORY TO THE KING AND HIS ENTOURAGE.

EVERYTHING IS MOVING FORWARD APPROPRIATELY, WALI SAID, AND

THE GROUP IS ANTICIPATING THEIR ARRIVAL IN KABUL. WALI\'S

BROTHER, WALI SHAH, WHO WILL ALSO JOIN THE KING\'S HOMECOMING,

AND EXECUTIVE SECRETARY HAMED SIDIQ MADE THE SAME POINT TO

POLOFF IN SEPARATE CONVERSATIONS.

3. (C) WALI HAD CONCLUDED A CONVERSATION WITH MFA SPECIAL

COORDINATOR FOR AFGHANISTAN ENRICO DI MAIO SHORTLY BEFORE

POLOFF\'S CALL, AND SAID DI MAIO HAD REFERRED TO ITALY\'S APRIL

16 GENERAL STRIKE BUT HAD NOT CHANGED THE DATE FOR THE KING\'S

DEPARTURE. (COMMENT: WALI WAS REFRESHINGLY CAREFUL ABOUT

NOT STATING THE EXPECTED DATE OF THE KING\'S DEPARTURE ON THE

TELEPHONE -- A FIRST SINCE THIS PROCESS BEGAN.) DI MAIO HAD

DISCUSSED DIFFERENT "STAGING AREAS" WITH WALI, TIMING FOR

DEPARTURE, AND THE ARRIVAL OF AIA CHAIRMAN KARZAI, FINALIZING

DETAILS FOR THE RETURN.

4. (C) AS NOTED REFTEL, DI MAIO TOLD WALI THE GOI WOULD

COORDINATE THE KING\'S SECURITY UNTIL ZAHIR SHAH LANDS IN

KABUL, BUT THAT GOI SECURITY EXPERTS WOULD REMAIN IN KABUL

FOR SOME TIME THEREAFTER TO ADVISE AFGHAN PROTECTIVE FORCES.

POLOFF ASKED IF THIS ARRANGEMENT WAS SATISFACTORY, AND WALI

SAID YES. WAS THE KING ALSO SATISFIED? "YES, FULLY," WALI

REPLIED. WALI ELABORATED THAT THE PROTECTION WOULD BE

PROVIDED BY AFGHANS TRAINED BY "DIFFERENT EUROPEAN

COUNTRIES," INCLUDING ITALY. "THEY ARE AFGHANS WE SELECTED,"

HE SAID, "BUT THE REAL SELECTION WILL NOT TAKE PLACE UNTIL I

AM IN AFGHANISTAN AND CHOOSE THEM MYSELF." WALI WOULD SELECT

"PEOPLE OF PROVEN LOYALTY," BUT NOT ONLY PASHTUNS. "THEY

WILL BE LARGELY PASHTUNS BECAUSE PASHTUNS ARE THE LARGEST

ETHNIC GROUP," HE OBSERVED.

5. (C) WALI REPORTED THE KING TO BE STILL LOOKING FORWARD

TO HIS RETURN AND LARGELY UNFAZED BY THE DELAYS. ALTHOUGH

ZAHIR SHAH HAD A "SLIGHT INFLUENZA" AND WAS TAKING

ANTIBIOTICS, THERE WOULD BE NO FURTHER DELAY OF HIS RETURN,

"BARRING SOMETHING UNFORESEEN." WALI EXPRESSED THE HOPE THAT

THE SITUATION IN THE MIDDLE EAST WOULD EASE, AS THAT

INCREASES OVERALL DANGER, BUT AGAIN ASSERTED THAT "DELAYING

IS OVER." POLOFF NOTED THE RECENT ATTEMPT AGAINST DEFENSE

MINISTER FAHIM. WALI EXPRESSED DISAPPOINTMENT THAT THE

ATTACK HAD OCCURRED, BUT SAID THE BOMBINGS AND DISTURBANCES

WERE "NOT IMPORTANT" IN THE CONTEXT OF THE KING\'S RETURN. IF

THEY WERE MEANT TO DETER ZAHIR SHAH, "THEN SOMEONE MADE A

MISTAKE ANALYZING THE KING\'S CHARACTER," WALI ASSERTED

STRONGLY. "HIS MAJESTY HAS A WILL OF IRON." ZAHIR SHAH WILL

RETURN, WALI CONCLUDED FORCEFULLY.

6. (C) DESPITE HIS CARE IN DISCUSSING THE DATE FOR THE

KING\'S RETURN, WALI TOLD POLOFF CHAIRMAN KARZAI WOULD ARRIVE

IN ROME ON APRIL 16 IN THE AFTERNOON, WALI BELIEVED STILL

USING THE LOANED AIRCRAFT OF THE AGHA KHAN. FOREIGN MINISTER

ABDULLAH, CIVIL AVIATION MINISTER "AND OUR FRIEND" ZALMAI

RASSOUL, AND RECONSTRUCTION MINISTER FARHANG WOULD JOIN

KARZAI, AS WALI UNDERSTOOD. THE KING AND WALI REMAINED IN

CLOSE COMMUNICATION WITH CHAIRMAN KARZAI AND LOOKED FORWARD

TO PROVIDING HIM THEIR SUPPORT. UNPROMPTED, WALI OBSERVED

THAT KARZAI WAS DOING A GOOD JOB AS CHAIRMAN.

7. (C) ASKED IF THE AFGHANS HAD ANY PARTICULAR CONCERNS OR

ANYTHING THAT THEY WANTED OF THE U.S. TO FACILITATE THE

KING\'S RETURN, WALI SAID "ONLY THE CONTINUED AND UNDIMINISHED

ATTENTION OF THE GOVERNMENT OF THE UNITED STATES FOR THE

COUNTRY OF AFGHANISTAN." HE AGAIN SAID LOGISTICS, SECURITY

AND OTHER ARRANGEMENTS FOR THE KING\'S RETURN WERE ON TRACK

AND THEY LOOKED FORWARD TO THEIR APPROACHING RETURN TO KABUL.
