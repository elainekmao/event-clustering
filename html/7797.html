SUBJECT: EXTRANCHECK: PRE-LICENSE CHECK REQUEST FOR D307110

REF: USDOC 1988

1. REFTEL REQUESTED POST CONDUCT A PRE-LICENSE CHECK ON

SAFEWATER CHEMICALS FOR THE EXPORT OF ELEVEN NICKEL-LINED PUMPS

FOR USE IN THE MANUFACTURE OF CHEMICALS USED IN THE OIL AND GAS

INDUSTRY.

2. ON APRIL 29, 2003, COMMERCIAL OFFICER MARY O\'BRIEN MET WITH

MARK CONNER OF AL JABER ENERGY SERVICES, WHO IS THE PROJECT

MANAGER FOR THE SAFEWATER PROJECT. (NOTE: COMOFF O\'BRIEN HAD

PREVIOUSLY MET WITH MR. CONNER IN MARCH 2003 REGARDING THE NEED

FOR EXPORT LICENSES FOR THE SAFEWATER FACILITY.) AL JABER GROUP,

OF WHICH AL JABER ENERGY SERVICES IS A PART, IS A HUGE

CONGLOMERATE INDUSTRIAL FIRM HERE IN THE UAE WITH BUSINESSES

INVOLVED IN GENERAL CONTRACTING; MARINE WORKS; IRON, STEEL,

REBAR, AND ALUMINUM FABRICATION; ASPHALT PRODUCTION; AND HEAVY

EQUIPMENT SALES. IN 2001, AL JABER\'S GROSS REVENUE WAS $410

MILLION. THEIR MAIN FACILITY CONSISTS OF 500,000 SQUARE METERS

ON THE SEASIDE OF MUSSAFAH, ABU DHABI\'S NEW INDUSTRIAL AREA.

3. AL JABER HAS RECENTLY BEGUN CONSTRUCTION ON A CHLOR-ALKALI

PLANT THAT IS ABOUT A TEN MINUTE DRIVE WITHIN MUSSAFAH FROM AL

JABER\'S MAIN FACILITY WHERE MR. CONNER\'S OFFICE IS LOCATED. THIS

FACILITY WILL MANUFACTURE HYDROCHLORIC ACID, SODIUM HYPOCHLORITE

(BLEACH), AND CAUSTIC SODA. THESE PRODUCTS ARE EXTENSIVELY USED

IN OIL WELLS AND OTHER INDUSTRIAL PROCESSES SUCH AS WATER

PURIFICATION, PAPER MILLS, AND ALUMINUM (SPELLED ALUMINIUM HERE)

PROCESSING. IN FACT, WHEN COMMOFF WENT TO THE CONSTRUCTION SITE,

THERE ARE A PAPER MILL AND AN ALUMINUM EXTRUSION FACILITY VISIBLE

FROM THE PLOT WHERE THE PLANT IS BEING BUILT.

4. SAFEWATER HAS PURCHASED AROUND $9 MILLION OF OTHER U.S.

EQUIPMENT FOR THIS PROJECT, MOST OF WHICH WILL COME FROM IONICS

IN WATERTOWN, MA. THIS EQUIPMENT DOES NOT REQUIRE EXPORT

LICENSES BASED ON INFORMATION MR. CONNER HAS RECEIVED FROM

IONICS.

5. BECAUSE OF THE HIGHLY CAUSTIC NATURE OF THE SUBSTANCES BEING

PRODUCED THE FACILITY WILL REQUIRE THE ELEVEN NICKEL-LINED PUMPS

LISTED ON THIS LICENSE. IN ADDITION, IN ORDER TO PRODUCE CAUSTIC

SODA, SAFEWATER WILL UTILIZE AN EVAPORATOR WHICH INCORPORATES A

NICKEL-LINED PUMP ALSO FROM GOULDS. THAT PUMP IS THE SUBJECT OF

EXPORT LICENSE D305336. THE PUMP WILL BE SENT TO GEA IN FRANCE,

INCORPORATED INTO THE EVAPORATOR AND THEN EXPORTED TO THE UAE.

6. ON MAY 3, 2003, COMMOFF O\'BRIEN SPOKE WITH AHMED SOBHI,

SALES MANAGER, DAFF TRADING, ABU DHABI. MR SOBHI SAID THAT DAFF

TRADING HAS BEEN THE AGENT FOR GOULDS PUMPS IN THE UAE FOR OVER

15 YEARS. HE SAID THAT WHEN DAFF SENDS A PURCHASE ORDER TO

GOULDS, DAFF IS REQUIRED TO IDENTIFY THE END USER AND END-USE TO

GOULDS. SAFEWATER PLACED A PURCHASE ORDER FOR 25 PUMPS

ALTOGETHER WITH DAFF. IN THE QUOTE TO SAFEWATER DAFF INFORMED

SAFEWATER THAT AN EXPORT LICENSE WAS REQUIRED FOR THE EXPORT OF

THE ELEVEN NICKEL-LINED PUMPS. MR. SOBHI EXPLAINED TO COMOFF

THAT THE PUMPS WILL BE DELIVERED TO THE PORT HERE IN THE UAE AND

THEN CLEARED THROUGH CUSTOMS BY DAFF. THE PUMPS WILL THEN BE

DELIVERED TO SAFEWATER\'S FACILITY IN MUSSAFAH. MR. SOBHI KNOWS

THE RESTRICTIONS ON EXPORTING ALL OF GOULDS\' PRODUCTS TO

SANCTIONED COUNTRIES AND IS AWARE DAFF MUST ABIDE BY ALL

CONDITIONS THAT MIGHT BE IMPOSED ON THE LICENSE.

7. AS A SIDE NOTE, THE UAE HAS BEGUN THE REGISTRATION PROCESS

FOR CHEMICAL COMPANIES IN THE UAE. THEREFORE IF ANY OF THE

CHEMICALS PRODUCED AT SAFEWATER\'S FACILITY ARE SUBJECT TO CWC

VERIFICATION, THE MECHANISM FOR REGISTRATION AND REPORTING IS

UNDERWAY.

8. RECOMMENDATION: COMMOFF RECOMMENDS DAFF TRADING AND

SAFEWATER CHEMICALS AS RELIABLE RECIPIENTS OF CONTROLLED U.S.

ORIGIN COMMODITIES AND TECHNOLOGY.
