SUBJECT: THACI SCENESETTER: LEADER LOOKING FOR APPROVAL

1. (U) CLASSIFIED BY AMBASSADOR JOHN K. MENZIES, COM, FOR

REASONS 1.5 (B&D)

SUMMARY

-------

2. (C) DEMOCRATIC PARTY OF KOSOVO (PDK) PRESIDENT HASHIM

THACI IS CONCERNED THAT WASHINGTON IS NEITHER MUCH INTERESTED

IN NOR FRIENDLY TOWARDS HIM, AND WILL SEEK DEMONSTRATIONS OF

APPROVAL, LARGELY TO BOLSTER HIS IMAGE IN KOSOVO, BUT ALSO

BECAUSE HE WANTS THE RECOGNITION. HIS POLITICAL STRENGTH IN

KOSOVO A BIT RAGGED, THACI MAY BE MORE OPEN TO ENCOURAGEMENT

TO BE MORE CONSTRUCTIVE, PARTICULARLY IN REACHING OUT TO

KOSOVO SERBS. IN HIS MEETINGS IN WASHINGTON, WE SHOULD ALSO

PUSH HIM TO DO HIS PART BEFORE AND AFTER THE NOVEMBER

ELECTIONS TO CONSOLIDATE DEMOCRACY IN KOSOVO. END SUMMARY.

POLITICIAN IN SEARCH OF...

--------------------------

3. (C) AS PDK PRESIDENT THACI LEAVES KOSOVO FOR THE U.S.

SEPTEMBER 11, HE IS A POLITICIAN WHOSE RHYTHM HAS BEEN BROKEN

BOTH BY HIS COMPATRIOTS\' PREFERENCE FOR MOVING PAST THE WAR

AND FOR POLITICAL MODERATION, AND BY AN ACUTE AWARENESS OF

POLITICAL CHANGES IN WASHINGTON. NEVER ONE TO LOOK TO OTHER

EUROPEAN CAPITALS, THACI NOW FEARS THAT HIS STOCK HAS FALLEN

IN U.S. POLICY CIRCLES. A MAJOR REASON FOR HIS TRIP, HE HAS

IMPLIED, IS TO GAUGE JUST HOW DEEP THE DISENCHANTMENT WITH

HIM GOES THERE.

...APPROVAL

-----------

4. (C) SOMEWHAT COUNTER-INTUITIVELY PERHAPS, IT IS THACI WHO

HAS MOST RELIED ON A PRESUMED "ALLIANCE" WITH THE U.S. TO

BOLSTER HIS POLITICAL FORTUNES. HIS POLITICAL FORTUNES IN

KOSOVO HAVE SAGGED. DEMOCRATIC LEAGUE OF KOSOVO (LDK)

PRESIDENT RUGOVA HAS SOME 12 YEARS OF POLITICAL INVOLVEMENT

AND LEADERSHIP IN KOSOVO, AS WELL AS STILL VERY STRONG

POPULAR SENTIMENT IN HIS FAVOR. THACI WILL ALWAYS FALL SHORT

OF RUGOVA,S STATURE AS A FATHER FIGURE FOR THE K-ALBANIANS.

AT A PRISTINA CULTURAL EVENT SEPTEMBER 8, RUGOVA APPEARED

RELAXED AND CONNECTED WELL WITH THE AUDIENCE. IN CONTRAST,

THACI FELL FLAT IN HIS SPEECH.

5. (C) ALLIANCE FOR THE FUTURE OF KOSOVO (AAK) PRESIDENT

RAMUSH HARADINAJ CAN POINT TO HIS OWN WAR RECORD WHEN HE

NEEDS TO, BUT HAS NOT SAT ON HIS LAURELS, INSTEAD

ENERGETICALLY PURSUING A RANGE OF POLITICAL ALLIANCES AND

BROADENING THE APPEAL OF HIS COALITION. SPECULATION IS RIFE

THAT THE AAK WILL SURPASS THE PDK ON NOVEMBER 17 AND BECOME

KOSOVO,S SECOND POLITICAL FORCE AND REAL OPPOSITION. AS THE

BRITISH COM PUT IT RECENTLY, THACI COULD BECOME "YESTERDAY,S

MAN" IN KOSOVO.

6. (C) BOTH RUGOVA AND HARADINAJ ARE EAGER TO COOPERATE AND

MAINTAIN GOOD RELATIONS WITH THE U.S., OF COURSE, BUT NEITHER

NEEDS US IN THE WAY THACI, WHO REMAINS PERSONALLY VESTED IN

HAVING "LIBERATED" KOSOVO, SEEMS TO DO. HE WILL BE ANXIOUS,

AS THE CAMPAIGN SEASON STARTS IN KOSOVO, TO PORTRAY HIMSELF

AS PLUGGED INTO THE USG IN WASHINGTON AND UN IN NEW YORK. HE

WILL SEEK A PUBLIC POLITICAL EMBRACE FROM U.S. OFFICIALS.

...SUPPORT

----------

7. (C) THACI AND OTHER PDK LEADERS HAVE NOT DONE MUCH EITHER

TO REDEFINE THEMSELVES IDEOLOGICALLY OR TO RE-ENERGIZE THE

PARTY ORGANIZATION FOLLOWING THEIR DISAPPOINTING, BUT NOT

WHOLLY UNEXPECTED, SHOWING LAST OCTOBER. RUMORS OF

INFIGHTING HAVE INCREASED DURING THE YEAR (ALTHOUGH THACI WAS

ABLE IN DECEMBER AND JANUARY TO DISAPPEAR TO THE U.S. FOR 6

WEEKS WITHOUT APPARENT CONCERN OVER A COUP D\'ETAT). MORE

THAN ONCE, THE PRESS STATEMENTS AND PUBLIC POSITIONS OF PDK

OFFICIALS HAVE BEEN AT VARIANCE, HIGHLIGHTING SHAKY CENTRAL

CONTROL. MOREOVER, THE PDK HAS SEVERAL WELL-KNOWN AND MEDIA

SAVVY HIGH OFFICERS, MORE THAN ONE OF WHOM MAY SEEK AN EVEN

HIGHER PROFILE IN THE PARTY. ANOTHER DISAPPOINTING SHOWING

IN THE NOVEMBER ELECTIONS COULD SHARPEN THE INTERNAL

CHALLENGE TO THACI.

...ENCOURAGEMENT TO DO RIGHT

----------------------------

8. (C) DESPITE HIS POLITICAL WOES, HOWEVER, THACI IS SHOWING

SOME OPENNESS TO POSITIVE STEPS ON THE KOSOVO SCENE. FOR

EXAMPLE, IN A RECENT DISCUSSION WITH THE COM, HE AGREED TO

MEET WITH THE K-SERB LEADER MOMCILO TRAJKOVIC, PROVIDED USOP

ASSISTED IN FACILITATING AND PROVIDING A VENUE. THE KOSOVAR

PRESS IS REPORTING ON THE INVITATION OF SERBIAN PM DJINDJIC

FOR THACI TO VISIT BELGRADE. EARLIER THIS SUMMER, WHEN

DJINDJIC SUGGESTED ORALLY THAT HE WOULD LIKE TO MET WITH BOTH

THACI AND KOSOVO PROTECTION CORPS (KPC) COMMANDER AGIM CEKU,

THACI EXPRESSED SOME WILLINGNESS TO CONSIDER A MEETING ON

NEUTRAL GROUND. WE DO NOT KNOW IF HE WILL GO TO BELGRADE (HE

MAY SUSPECT A POLITICAL OR JUDICIAL TRAP), BUT HE HAS

ACKNOWLEDGED RECEIVING THE SERBIAN PM\'S LATEST, AND MORE

OFFICIAL, INVITATION.

9. (C) INTERESTINGLY, THOSE WHO ATTEND MEETINGS OF THE

INTERIM ADVISORY COUNCIL (IAC) KNOW THAT IN PRIVATE, THACI IS

COMFORTABLE AND GETS ALONG WELL WITH SERB OBSERVER RADA

TRAJKOVIC, JOKING WITH HER EVEN IN SERBIAN. PRECISELY

BECAUSE OF HIS KLA LEADERSHIP DURING THE WAR, THACI IS

POLITICALLY SAFER IN REACHING OUT TO KOSOVO SERBS THAN IS

RUGOVA. THE LATTER WAS MORE THAN ONCE PORTRAYED AS "SOFT ON

SERBIA", AND FINDS THIS HIS MOST SIGNIFICANT POLITICAL

VULNERABILITY. THACI CANNOT BE ACCUSED OF COMPLICITY. WE

BELIEVE THAT HE RECOGNIZES THIS AND MAY SEEK TO USE DEALINGS

WITH THE SERBS TO RAISE HIS PROFILE -- AND GAIN OUR APPROVAL.

WE SHOULD ENCOURAGE HIM TO UNDERTAKE CONSTRUCTIVE MOVES IN

THIS DIRECTION, SINCE THIS IS AN AREA IN WHICH HE AND HIS

FOLLOWERS CAN DO A LOT TO IMPROVE THE CLIMATE FOR

RECONCILIATION AND RETURNS.

10. (C) IN THE REGIONAL CONTEXT, AS EVENTS IN THE FYROM

DEGENERATED DURING THE SPRING, THACI TOOK THE MOST

CONSTRUCTIVE PUBLIC LINE FOR SOME TIME (WHATEVER HE OR HIS

COHORTS MAY HAVE DONE LESS PUBLICLY), OFFERING POLITICAL

SUPPORT TO HIS OLD MENTOR ARBEN XHAFERI, PRESIDENT OF THE

ALBANIAN DEMOCRATIC PARTY (PDSH) IN FYROM AND MOST PROMINENT

OF THE ALBANIANS IN PARTICIPATING IN GOVERNMENT THERE. THACI

MAINTAINED THIS SUPPORT DESPITE CRITICISM FROM WITHIN HIS OWN

PARTY AS TO HIS LOYALTY TO THE ALBANIAN CAUSE.

...A FUTURE

-----------

11. (C) THACI TOLD THE COM RECENTLY THAT VIOLENCE HAS

TAPERED OFF IN KOSOVO. THERE ARE STILL OCCASIONAL REPORTS OF

ATTACKS OR INSTANCES OF INTIMIDATION AGAINST LDK ACTIVISTS IN

PARTICULAR, HOWEVER, AND THACI KNOWS WELL THAT PDK IS ALWAYS

THE FIRST SUSPECT. IN OUR PRIVATE DISCUSSIONS, THACI IS

ALREADY WARNING OF LDK POLITICAL DOMINANCE, WHICH HE FEARS

WILL BECOME "ONE-PARTY RULE." USG OFFICIALS MAY WISH TO

ENCOURAGE HIM TO ENCOURAGE CONTINUED REDUCTION OF VIOLENCE,

TO BEHAVE CONSTRUCTIVELY DURING THE CAMPAIGN, AND TO DO HIS

PART, NO MATTER WHAT THE ELECTORAL OUTCOME, TO CONSOLIDATE

DEMOCRACY IN KOSOVO.

12. (U) BELGRADE MINIMIZE CONSIDERED.
