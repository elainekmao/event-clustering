SUBJECT: Z-4: GOC\'S OBJECTIONS ARE DEEP AND FIRM

-------

SUMMARY

-------

1. (LOU) THE GOC\'S EXPRESSED WILLINGNESS TO

"CONSIDER" THE Z-4 PLAN HAS HELPED CROATIA PRESENT A

"REASONABLE" FACE THAT CONTRASTS FAVORABLY WITH THE

KRAJINA SERBS\' INTRANSIGENCE. NEVERTHELESS, CROATIAN

OBJECTIONS TO VARIOUS ELEMENTS OF THE PLAN ARE DEEPLY

HELD. A POINT-BY-POINT REVIEW IN THE HEAVILY

GOVERNMENT-INFLUENCED "VJESNIK" FEBRUARY 1, WHICH

ESSENTIALLY EVISCERATES ELEMENTS OF THE PLAN, APPEARS

INDICATIVE. END SUMMARY.

2. (LOU) AFTER THE Z-4 GROUP\'S JANUARY 30 PRESENTATION

OF ITS PLAN FOR POLITICAL REINTEGRATION OF THE SERB-

CONTROLLED AREAS OF CROATIA, THE CROATIAN GOVERNMENT

EXPRESSED ITS WILLINGNESS TO REVIEW THE PLAN AND TO

ACCEPT IT AS A BASIS FOR NEGOTIATION. IN LIGHT OF THE

GOC\'S KNOWLEDGE THAT THE "RSK WOULD FIND THE PLAN

UNACCEPTABLE EVEN IF IT HAD AGREED TO RECEIVE A COPY OF

3. (LOU) THE ORAL REACTIONS OF CROATIAN LEADERS,

KRAJINA SERBS.
