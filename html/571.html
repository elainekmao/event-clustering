1. (U) THIS IS ONE IN A SERIES OF REPORTS ON

SPECIFIC AND GROSS HUMAN RIGHTS ABUSES IN BOSNIA AND

HERCEGOVINA (B-H) BASED ON PERSONAL INTERVIEWS WITH

THE SUBJECTS NAMED. THE SUBJECT IN THIS REPORT

AGREED TO SPEAK ONLY AFTER RECEIVING ASSURANCES THAT

HIS NAME WOULD NOT/NOT BE USED PUBLICLY FOR FEAR THAT

THAT WOULD ENDANGER RELATIVES STILL UNDER SERBIAN

DETENTION IN B-H.
