SUBJECT: ITALY AGREES ON LEVERAGING POLITICS OF AFGHAN AID

REF: A. STATE 141043

B. STATE 142782

1. (SBU) A/DCM DELIVERED REF POINTS TO MFA POLITICAL

DIRECTOR, GIANCARLO ARAGONA. ARAGONA NOTED THAT MOST OF

ITALY\'S AFGHAN AID CONTRIBUTIONS ARE CHANNELED THROUGH

INTERNATIONAL ORGANIZATIONS (IOS) RATHER THAN DISPERSED

DIRECTLY OR THROUGH NGOS. CAUTIONING THAT SUCH IOS CAN BE A

CUMBERSOME MECHANISM OF DISBURSEMENT, HE URGED THE USG TO

JOIN THE GOI IN IMPRESSING UPON THEM THE IMPORTANCE OF

QUICKLY GENERATING TANGIBLE BENEFITS FOR WHICH CREDIT WILL GO

TO THE ATA. HE PROMISED TO TRY TO SPEED ITALIAN

DISBURSEMENTS.

COOPERATIVE APPROACH

--------------------

2. (SBU) A/DCM MET ON AUGUST 8 WITH MFA POLITICAL DIRECTOR

GIANCARLO ARAGONA, HIS DEPUTY, CLAUDIO BISOGNIERO, AND

SPECIAL COORDINATOR FOR AFGHANISTAN ENRICO DE MAIO TO DISCUSS

AFGHAN AID (AND OTHER ISSUES REPORTED SEPTEL). A/DCM

RECOGNIZED ITALY\'S EXISTING FINANCIAL CONTRIBUTIONS TO

SPECIFICALLY WHETHER THE USD SEVEN MILLION ITALY PLEDGED TO

THE WORLD BANK\'S AFGHAN RECONSTRUCTION TRUST FUND HAD BEEN

DISPERSED. HE UNDERSCORED THE NEED TO GET ASSISTANCE TO

AFGHANISTAN RAPIDLY.

WE NEED TO MOVE FURTHER DOWN THE FUNDING PIPELINE

--------------------------------------------- ----

3. (SBU) ARAGONA "FULLY AGREED" WITH WHAT HE CALLED "A SOUND

POLITICAL OBJECTIVE" OF ENHANCING THE ATA AND KARZAI\'S

CREDIBILITY AT THIS CRITICAL JUNCTURE. HE NOTED THAT ITALY

PLEDGED USD 47 MILLION TO AFGHANISTAN IN TOKYO. ALL OF IT,

HE SAID, HAS NOW BEEN COMMITTED AND THE CHANNELS FOR ITS

DISBURSEMENT IDENTIFIED. HE CAUTIONED, HOWEVER, THAT SINCE

MOST OF THE AID IS GOING THROUGH IOS (ARAGONA MENTIONED THE

WORLD BANK AND UNDP IN PARTICULAR), HE UNDERSTOOD THE

DIFFICULTY OF ENSURING THAT KARZAI AND THE ATA GET CREDIT FOR

THE ENSUING PROJECTS AND PROGRAMS. ARAGONA PROMISED TO MAKE

THE POINT TO IOS THAT EMPHASIS MUST BE GIVEN TO RAISING THE

PROFILE OF THE ATA. HE URGED THE USG DO SO AS WELL,

SUGGESTING THAT DONOR COUNTRIES SHOULD UNDERSCORE THE

IMPERATIVE OF USING ASSISTANCE TO ENSURE THAT TANGIBLE

PROJECTS ARE SEEN SOON AND ARE LINKED IN THE PUBLIC MIND TO

THE FUNCTIONING OF THE ATA. ARAGONA PROMISED THAT THE GOI

WOULD REVIEW ITALY\'S COMMITMENTS IN ORDER TO IDENTIFY WAYS TO

STRENGTHEN THE ATA AND STABILIZE AFGHANISTAN.

4. (SBU) SPECIAL COORDINATOR FOR AFGHANISTAN ENRICO DE MAIO

POINTED OUT THAT ITALY HAS ALREADY GIVEN HALF OF ITS TOKYO

PLEDGE. HE HAS REQUESTED THE OFFICE OF COOPERATION TO

PROVIDE AN UPDATE ON THE STATUS OF AFGHAN AID AND EXPECTS TO

HAVE MORE DETAILS IN THE COMING DAYS. DE MAIO ADDED THAT

DONORS SHOULD DO MORE THAN ADVISE THE IOS TO ENSURE PROJECTS

REFLECT CREDIT ON THE ATA; "WE SHOULD INSTRUCT THEM" TO WORK

MORE QUICKLY AND TO EMPHASIZE THE CENTRAL GOVERNMENT\'S ROLE.

HE SUGGESTED DONORS NEGOTIATE CONDITIONALITIES TO THEIR

FUNDING WHICH STIPULATE RAPID DELIVERABLES LINKED TO THE ATA.

COMMENT

-------

5. (SBU) THE ITALIANS TAKE OUR PRINCIPAL POINTS AND ARE

COMMITTED TO REVIEWING THEIR ASSISTANCE TO LEVERAGE ITS

POLITICAL VALUE FOR THE ATA -- SOONER, RATHER THAN LATER.

POST SECONDS THE GOI SUGGESTION THAT WE AND OTHER DONORS

DRIVE THE MESSAGE HOME TO THE IOS AS WELL.

6. (U) MINIMIZE CONSIDERED - KABUL
