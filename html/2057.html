SUBJECT: HANOI MUSLIM COMMUNITY RETICENT ON RECENT EVENTS

REF: STATE 175484

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 02 HANOI 02658 121200Z

1. (U) HANOI\'S TINY VIETNAMESE MUSLIM COMMUNITY (NO MORE

THAN A COUPLE OF DOZEN PEOPLE) HAS NOT NOTICEABLY REACTED TO

THE SEPTEMBER 11 TERRORIST ACTS IN THE US AND SUBSEQUENT

INTERNATIONAL RESPONSE UNDER OPERATION ENDURING FREEDOM,

ACCORDING TO THE RESIDENT MANAGER OF HANOI\'S SOLE MOSQUE.

THE OFFICIAL NOTED THAT SERVICES AT THE MOSQUE AND

DISCUSSION BEFORE AND DURING THE SERVICE STEER AWAY FROM

POLITICAL SUBJECTS AND THUS HAVE NOT ADDRESSED TERRORISM OR

INTERNATIONAL RESPONSES TO IT. HE ADDED THAT MUSLIMS HERE

HAD NONETHELESS THOUGHT ABOUT OFFERING CONDOLENCES TO THE

VICTIMS OF THE SEPTEMBER 11 ATTACK BUT WERE UNSURE TO WHOM

THEY SHOULD ADDRESS THEIR SYMPATHIES.

2. (U) POL/C AND POLOFF VISITED THE MOSQUE ON OCTOBER 12

SHORTLY AFTER THE USUAL FRIDAY PRAYERS, WHICH ABOUT 100

PEOPLE USUALLY ATTEND, THE OFFICIAL SAID. MOST ARE FOREIGN

MUSLIMS WHO ARE IN HANOI AS DIPLOMATS OR BUSINESS PEOPLE,

AND INCLUDE IRANIANS AND IRAQIS. FOUR OR FIVE AFGHANS ALSO

ATTEND; THEY ARE IN HANOI TEACHING MUSLIM CHILDREN SUBJECTS

SUCH AS ARABIC AT A SPECIAL SCHOOL AT THE LIBYAN EMBASSY.

(NOTE: WHEN POL/C MET WITH MPS OFFICIALS HANDLING FOREIGN

EMBASSIES ON OCTOBER 1, THEY WERE UNAWARE OF ANY AFGHANS IN

COUNTRY. END NOTE) THE OFFICIAL SAID THAT NONE OF THE

MUSLIMS HE KNEW IN ANY WAY SUPPORTS OSAMA BIN LADEN OR HIS

FOLLOWERS. POL/C LEFT A COPY OF THE EMBASSY PUBLIC AFFAIRS

SECTION\'S MOST RECENT "RESPONSE TO TERRORISM" FOR THE

MOSQUE\'S REFERENCE.

3. (U) THE STATE-RUN RELIGIOUS PUBLISHING HOUSE ISSUED THE

FIRST-EVER KORAN IN VIETNAMESE IN 2000 AND DISTRIBUTED

COPIES FREE OF CHARGE TO ALL VIETNAMESE MUSLIM FAMILIES, THE

UNCLASSIFIED

OFFICIAL SAID. THERE IS SOME TALK ABOUT A SECOND EDITION,

ALTHOUGH HE WAS NOT CLEAR WHY. COPIES ARE NOT/NOT AVAILABLE

FOR PURCHASE. PREVIOUSLY, HE SAID, VIETNAMESE MUSLIMS WERE

DEPENDENT ON USING A FEW COPIES OF THE KORAN IN ARABIC, OR

ON ENGLISH OR FRENCH EDITIONS BROUGHT IN BY FOREIGN MUSLIMS.

HE NOTED SOME OCCASIONAL INTEREST IN ISLAM BY VIETNAMESE

FROM NON-MUSLIM FAMILIES; THEY CAN STUDY AT LEAST THE

RUDIMENTS OF ISLAM AT THE MOSQUE AND SOME HAVE BECOME

MUSLIM, HE CLAIMED. NONE OF THE CURRENT VIETNAMESE MUSLIMS

IN HANOI HAD EVER UNDERTAKEN THE HADJ TO MECCA, HE NOTED

REGRETFULLY, ALTHOUGH ABOUT ONE HUNDRED GO EACH YEAR FROM

THE MORE POPULOUS CHAM MUSLIM COMMUNITIES IN SOUTHERN

VIETNAM. (NOTE: PREVIOUS REPORTING SUGGESTED 35 TO 40

VIETNAMESE MUSLIMS MAKE THE HADJ EACH YEAR. END NOTE.)

WHEN ASKED, HE CITED COST, RATHER THAN LACK OF GVN

PERMISSION, AS THE MAIN BARRIER TO THE HADJ.

4. (U) COMMENT: ALL OF VIETNAM\'S OFFICIALLY RECOGNIZED

RELIGIOUS ENTITIES TEND TO KEEP THEIR SERVICES AS PURELY

RELIGIOUS AS POSSIBLE AND TO AVOID POLITICAL OR REMOTELY

CONTROVERSIAL SUBJECTS. IT IS NOT SURPRISING THAT THERE HAS

BEEN LITTLE IF ANY OPEN DISCUSSION OF RECENT EVENTS WITHIN

THIS COMMUNITY.
