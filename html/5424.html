SUBJECT: PARLIAMENTARIAN REQUESTS INFO ON GUANTANAMO

DETAINEE

REF: ISTANBUL 118

1. (U) This is an action request.

2. (SBU) Mehmet Elkatmis, chairman of the Parliamentary Human

Rights Committee, handed Poloff January 29 a copy of the

letter reported reftel and asked for a response. Embassy

requests guidance on how to respond to the inquiry. Embassy

would also appreciate any updated general guidance pertaining

to detainees at Guantanamo Bay Naval Base on issues such as:

conditions for detainees; visits by ICRC or other

organizations; visits by foreign government officials;

information released government-to-government regarding

detainees; and the legal status of detainees.
