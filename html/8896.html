SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION: POWELL\'S VISIT IRAQ

1. SUMMARY: ISSUES FEATURED IN THE LOCAL MEDIA INCLUDED THE WEF

MEETING IN JORDAN, U.S. EFFORTS TO ACTIVATE PEACE BETWEEN THE

PALESTINIANS AND ISRAELIS, AND IRAQ. A COLUMNIST WARNED AGAINST

THE WEF, SAYING THAT IT WAS FIRST INITIATED BY SHIMON PERES AND

NOTING THAT THE ROOT OF THE MIDDLE EAST PROBLEM IS THE (ISRAELI)

OCCUPATION. AN EDITORIAL IN "AL-KHALEEJ" WARNED AGAINST ALLEGED

ISRAELI AMBITIONS IN IRAQ, URGING ALL ARABS TO COUNTER THEM. A

COLUMNIST IN "AL-ITTIHAD" ASSERTED THAT THE ABSENCE OF DEMOCRACY

AND THE FLOURISHING OF POLITICAL ISLAM ARE THE LEADING FACTORS

THAT HAVE PREVENTED ARAB WOMEN FROM FULL PARTICIPATION IN

POLITICAL LIFE. END SUMMARY.

2. UNDER THE HEADLINE \'THE BURIAL OF THE MIDDLE EAST ECONOMIC

PROJECT," PSEUDONYMOUS COLUMNIST IBN AL-DEIRA WROTE IN SHARJAH-

BASED PAN-ARAB DAILY "AL-KHALEEJ" 6/21:

"THE DAVOS INTERNATIONAL ECONOMIC FORUM CONVENES AT THIS TIME IN

AN ARAB COUNTRY ON THE BANKS OF THE DEAD SEA, FOLLOWING ISRAELI

EXPANSIONIST POLICIES\' FREEZE OF ALL ECONOMIC MEETINGS OF THIS

NATURE DURING THE LAST SIX YEARS... WHAT\'S NEW ABOUT THIS

EXCEPTIONAL MEETING ON THE DEAD SEA IS THAT THE PROMOTER OF THIS

INITIATIVE IS NOT SHIMON PERES BUT THE AMERICAN PRESIDENT GEORGE

BUSH, WHO IS DROWNING IN IRAQ\'S SHIFTING SANDS, AND WHO IS LOST

IN THE NARROW STREETS OF A CONFUSING MAP FOR A ROAD TOWARDS PEACE

IN PALESTINE. THE OBJECTIVE CONDITIONS FOR THE SUCCESS OF ANY

ECONOMIC PROJECT IN THE MIDDLE EAST CONTINUE TO BE ABSENT AND

BURIED BENEATH PALESTINIAN ASHES AND BODIES DESTROYED BY SHARON\'S

TANKS AND PLANES...THE ARAB CITIZEN DOES NOT NEED ANYONE TO

PROVOKE HIM AGAINST U.S. POLICIES IN THE MIDDLE EAST. SHARON IS

CAPABLE OF DOING SO, AND DREAMING OF ECONOMIC CONFERENCES WILL DO

NO GOOD FOR THE DEAD SEA MEETING. THE ROOTS OF TERRORISM ARE THE

OCCUPATION."

3. UNDER THE HEADLINE, "ISRAEL IS IN IRAQ," "AL-KHALEEJ"

EDITORIALIZED 6/21:

"THE DANGER THAT THREATENS IRAQ AND THE REST OF THE ARAB REGION

IS NOT ONLY REPRESENTED BY THE AMERICAN MILITARY OCCUPATION, BUT

ALSO BY ISRAELI SECURITY AND ECONOMIC ATTEMPTS TO PENETRATE INTO

THE IRAQI ARENA THROUGH THE OCCUPATION.... IRAQ HAS BECOME AN

OCCUPIED COUNTRY LACKING INDEPENDENCE AND SOVEREIGNTY, AND ITS

PEOPLE LACKING FREEDOM, WHILE ITS DESTINY AND FUTURE WILL BE

DEFINED ACCORDING TO THE AMERICAN WILL. THEREFORE, THE MISSION

OF COUNTERING ISRAELI EXPANSION INTO IRAQ IS NOT THE

RESPONSIBILITY OF THE IRAQI PEOPLE ALONE. THE ARAB COUNTRIES ARE

REQUIRED TO WAKE UP FROM THEIR STATE OF UNCONSCIOUSNESS TO

CONFRONT AN IMMINENT DANGER..."

4. KUWAITI COLUMNIST DR. SHAMLAN YOUSUF AL-EISSA WROTE IN SEMI-

OFFICIAL ABU DHABI-BASED ARABIC DAILY "AL-ITTIHAD" 6/22:

"...WHY HAVE ARAB WOMEN NOT HAD THEIR RIGHTS DESPITE LONG YEARS

OF STRUGGLE? WE BELIEVE THERE ARE MANY REASONS FOR THIS, WHICH

WE WOULD NOT FIND APPROPRIATE TO MENTION HERE. HOWEVER, WE CAN

SAY THAT THE BACKWARDNESS OF ARAB SOCIETIES, THE ABSENCE OF FREE

DEMOCRATIC REGIMES, THE PREVALENCE OF TOTALITARIAN PARTIES AND

THE FLOURISHING OF POLITICAL ISLAM IN THE LAST FEW YEARS ARE ALL

FACTORS THAT HELPED TO FOIL THE WOMEN\'S MOVEMENT IN THE ARAB

WORLD..."
