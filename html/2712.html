SUBJECT: VATICAN\'S GROWING CONCERN WITH MIDDLE EAST

DEVELOPMENTS

CLASSIFIED BY: AMBASSADOR JIM NICHOLSON FOR REASONS 1.5 (A) AND (D).

1.(C) FOREIGN MINISTER TAURAN CONVOKED AMBASSADOR NICHOLSON

TODAY, APRIL 3 AND PRESENTED A NOTE VERBALE (FAXED TO

EUR/WE). TAURAN SAID THAT THE "ARAB STREETS ARE BOILING",

ESPECIALLY IN CAIRO AND JORDAN. THE VATICAN FEARS TERRORIST

ATTACKS AROUND THE WORLD AND A PETROLEUM CRISIS. THE FOREIGN

MINISTER STRESSED THAT ISRAEL HAS A RIGHT TO PROVIDE SECURITY

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 VATICA 01674 050925Z

FOR ITS PEOPLE, BUT ITS CURRENT ACTIONS ARE DISPROPORTIONATE.

HE REMARKED THAT HE FINDS IT DIFFICULT TO BELIEVE THAT THE

ISRAELIS DON\'T REALIZE THE IMPACT THE TELEVISION IMAGES OF

THEIR ATTACKS ARE HAVING ON THE ARAB WORLD. TAURAN AGAIN

CALLED FOR "PEACEMAKERS" ON THE SPOT, ALTHOUGH HE ADMITTED

THAT IT IS DIFFICULT BECAUSE THERE ARE NO FRONT LINES TO

SEPARATE.

2.(C) THE AMBASSADOR AND TAURAN ALSO DISCUSSED THE CURRENT

SITUATION OF A CONVENT OF THE SISTERS OF SAINT BRIDGET IN

BETHLEHEM. IT IS CURRENTLY BEING OCCUPIED BY PALESTINIAN

GUNMEN WHO ARE USING THE NUNS AS HUMAN SHIELDS. IN ADDITION

TO SEVEN NUNS OF DIVERSE NATIONALITY (INCLUDING AN AMCIT)

THERE ARE 200 PEOPLE WHO HAVE SOUGHT REFUGE IN THE CONVENT.

TAURAN SAID THAT HE SPOKE WITH THE ISRAELI DCM YESTERDAY TO

URGE THE ISRAELIS NOT TO SHELL THE CONVENT (SAINT MARY\'S

HOUSE), AS IT WOULD TURN INTO A MASSACRE. HE ALSO REQUESTED

OUR ASSISTANCE IN OBTAINING ACCESS FOR THE RED CROSS TO

DELIVER FOOD, WHICH HE SAYS IS CURRENTLY BEING DENIED BY

ISRAEL. NICHOLSON
