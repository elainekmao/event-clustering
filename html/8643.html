SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION: SHARM AL SHEIKH AND AQABA SUMMITS WMD

IN IRAQ ISRAEL ATTACKS HAMAS LEADERS

1. SUMMARY: THE SHARM AL SHEIKH AND AQABA SUMMITS, THE US

"FAILURE" TO FIND WMD IN IRAQ, AND ISRAEL\'S ATTACK ON HAMAS

LEADERS GENERATED EXTENSIVE COVERAGE IN LOCAL AND REGIONAL PRINT

AND SATELLITE MEDIA. AN "AL ITTIHAD" EDITORIAL COMMENTED ON

PRESIDENT BUSH\'S "ANNOYANCE" AT THE ASSASSINATION ATTEMPT ON

HAMAS LEADER ABDULAZIZ AL RANTEESI YESTERDAY. UNDER THE "AL

ITTIHAD" HEADLINE, "FUTURE OF IRAQ," DR. SHAMLAN AL EISSA

COMMENTED ON THE CURRENT SITUATION IN IRAQ. ANOTHER "AL ITTIHAD"

EDITORIAL CLAIMED THAT THE U.S. "FAILURE" TO FIND WMD IN IRAQ IS

A "STORM" IN THE FACE OF PRESIDENT BUSH AND PM BLAIR. AN "AL

KHALEEJ" EDITORIAL ASSERTED THAT THE U.S. IS NOT ALLOWING OTHER

PARTIES TO PARTICIPATE IN THE IMPLEMENTATION OF THE ROADMAP. END

SUMMARY.

2. DUBAI-BASED BUSINESS-ORIENTED ARABIC DAILY "AL BAYAN" CARRIED

A TWO-PART INTERVIEW WITH FORMER IRAQI REPRESENTATIVE TO THE UN

MOHAMMED AL DOURI. DURING HIS INTERVIEW, HE CRITICIZED THE

PREVIOUS REGIME AND ALL THE CRIMES COMMITTED DURING THAT ERA.

MR. AL DOURI ALSO CLAIMED THAT ALL THAT IS HAPPENING IN IRAQ,

INCLUDING THE LOOTING, WAS PLANNED BY THE U.S. AND THAT U.S.

POLICY IN IRAQ MIGHT LEAD TO A CIVIL WAR.

3. COMMENTING ON THE ASSASSINATION ATTEMPT OF ABDULAZIZ AL

RANTEESI YESTERDAY AND ON PRESIDENT BUSH\'S "ANNOYANCE" TOWARDS

WHAT HAPPENED, ABU DHABI-BASED SEMI-GOVERNMENT "AL ITTIHAD"

EDITORIALIZED 6/11:

"... THE GOAL BEHIND THIS ATTEMPT IS OBVIOUS ... THAT IS WHY IT

DESERVES THE AMERICAN ANGER THAT WAS RIGHT ON TIME ... ISRAEL

SHOULD STOP ITS POLICY OF DELUDING THE WORLD AND SHOULD REALIZE

THAT THE TIME HAS COME TO REALLY JOIN A SERIOUS AND JUST PEACE

PROCESS WHICH HAS LONG BEEN AWAITED IN THE REGION."

4. CRITICIZING ISRAEL\'S ASSASSINATION ATTEMPT ON AL RANTEESI,

SHARJAH-BASED PAN-ARAB DAILY "AL KHALEEJ" EDITORIALIZED 6/11:

"... THERE IS NO SOLUTION WITH THESE ASSASSINS. AFTER 55 YEARS

OF \'ATTEMPTING\' THEM, IT IS TIME FOR THE CONCERNED PEOPLE IN

PALESTINE AND THE ARAB COUNTRIES TO STOP THESE \'ATTEMPTS\' AND

\'TESTS\' THAT MIGHT LEAD TO THE SURRENDER OF THE WHOLE REGION, NOT

ONLY PALESTINE, TO THOSE \'NAZIS\' AND ALL WHO SUPPORT THEM AND

COVER UP FOR THEIR MASSACRES ..."

5. UNDER THE HEADLINE, "WHO WILL STOP SHARON," ABU DHABI-BASED

PAN-ARAB DAILY "AKHBAR AL ARAB" EDITORIALIZED 6/11:

"... THE GOVERNMENT OF SHARON WILL NOT MISS AN OPPORTUNITY TO

EMPHASIZE ITS HOSTILITY TOWARDS PEACE AND ITS INSISTENCE ON

BURYING ANY CHANCE FOR THIS PROCESS (PEACE). THE OBLIGATION OF

THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY IS TO EXPRESS ITS REFUSAL OF THIS

GOVERNMENT\'S HOSTILE AND IMMORAL BEHAVIOR ..."

6. UNDER THE HEADLINE, "MOTHER OF SCANDALS," "AL KHALEEJ"

EDITORIALIZED 6/10:

"... THE SCANDALS OF ATTACKS AND OCCUPATION WILL NOT END, THE

WEAPONS ISSUE IS A LIE, THE UNNEEDED DEMOCRACY, HANDING OVER THE

POSTPONED ADMINISTRATION TO IRAQIS, THE OIL THAT IS BEING STOLEN,

AND THE INSECURITY AND INSTABILITY ARE ALL INITIAL INDICATIONS OF

THE \'MOTHER OF SCANDALS\'."

7. UNDER THE HEADLINE, "FUTURE OF IRAQ," DR. SHAMLAN AL EISSA

WROTE IN "AL ITTIHAD" ON 6/08:

"... NO ONE CAN DENY THAT THE U.S. TROOPS OCCUPYING IRAQ HAVE

PARTICIPATED LARGELY IN THE DETERIORATION OF THE SITUATION ...

MOST IMPORTANTLY IS THE POLITICAL FUMBLING OF THE U.S. IN IRAQ

THROUGH THE GOVERNOR OF BAGHDAD ... WHAT ARE THE OPTIONS FOR THE

IRAQI PEOPLE AFTER ITS LIBERATION? THE IRAQI PEOPLE AND THE

NATIONAL POWERS ARE STILL IN CONFLICT REGARDING A CLEAR WORK

PROGRAM, THAT IS WHY IT WILL BE A MESS IF THE AMERICANS LEAVE

IRAQ BEFORE STARTING A NEW FOUNDATION OPERATING IN DEMOCRACY,

DIVERSITY, AND RESPECT FOR HUMAN RIGHTS ... THE IRAQI PEOPLE

SHOULD INSIST ON REQUESTING THAT THE OCCUPYING TROOPS ORGANIZE

THE SITUATION IN IRAQ ... THE GULF COUNTRIES HAVE A BIG

RESPONSIBILITY IN HELPING THE IRAQI PEOPLE OVERCOME THEIR CRISIS.

HUMANITARIAN AID AND PARTICIPATING IN RECONSTRUCTION ARE TWO

POSITIVE STEPS, BUT WHAT IS ALSO NEEDED IS THAT THE GULF

COUNTRIES, THROUGH THEIR SATELLITE TV CHANNELS, SHOULD CALM

THINGS DOWN AND NOT MOBILIZE THE ARAB STREET AGAINST THE U.S ..."

8. COMMENTING ON THE U.S. TAKING SOLE RESPONSIBILITY OF THE

ROADMAP, "AL KHALEEJ" EDITORIALIZED 6/07:

"... FOR THE U.S. TO BE THE ONLY DECISION MAKER, THIS WILL NOT

FULFILL THE DESIRED LASTING SOLUTION AND FAIR RESPONSIBILITY IN

THE LEAST, SINCE THE CREDIBILITY OF THE AMERICAN ADMINISTRATION

MINIMIZES ANY HOPE OF A JUST SOLUTION. AGAIN THE U.S.,

\'VICTORIOUS\' IN IRAQ, IMPOSED ITS REGIONAL AGENDA BY DOMINATING

THE DECISION FOR A SOLUTION TO THE PALESTINIAN CAUSE ON THE BASIS

OF GUARANTEEING THE \'ZIONIST COUNTRY\'S\' SECURITY, EVEN IF THIS

MEANS CREATING A PALESTINIAN CIVIL WAR SEEDED AT BOTH THE SHARM

AL SHEIKH AND AQABA SUMMITS."

9. URGING THAT ALL CONFLICTS AMONG PALESTINIANS BE OVERCOME, "AL-

BAYAN" EDITORIALIZED 6/07:

"... WE KNOW THAT THERE ARE REGIONAL AND INTERNATIONAL

CIRCUMSTANCES THAT ARE AGAINST PALESTINIAN AND ARAB BENEFITS,

WHICH IS WHY ALL PALESTINIAN SIDES SHOULD REACH A UNIFIED VISION

IN ORDER TO OVERCOME ALL CHALLENGES AND OBSTACLES THAT THE

OCCUPATION IS TRYING TO PUT BEFORE THE PALESTINIAN PEOPLE ..."

10. COMMENTING ON THE U.S. "FAILURE" TO FIND WMD IN IRAQ AS A

"STRONG" IN BUSH AND BLAIR\'S FACES BUT ASSERTING THAT THE ISSUE

FOR THE IRAQI PEOPLE WAS NOT WMD BUT GETTING RID OF THE REGIME,

"AL ITTIHAD" EDITORIALIZED ON 6/06:

"... ALTHOUGH BUSH AND BLAIR FAILED TO FIND EVIDENCE TO JUSTIFY

THEIR WAR, THE IRAQI PEOPLE ARE SCORING SUCCESS EACH DAY BY

REVEALING THE CRIMES OF THE PREVIOUS REGIME ... SO THE ISSUE FOR

THE IRAQI PEOPLE WAS NOT FINDING THE WMD ALTHOUGH THEY WERE ITS

FIRST VICTIMS, BUT THE ISSUE WAS GETTING RID OF THE REGIME THAT

MADE IRAQ A PRISON ..."

11. UNDER THE HEADLINE "NOT FOR PEACE," "AL KHALEEJ"

EDITORIALIZED 6/05:

"... THROUGH A THOROUGH READING OF WHAT WAS ANNOUNCED FOLLOWING

THE SHARM AL SHEIKH AND AQABA SUMMITS, WE COULD SIMPLY UNDERSTAND

THE INDICATION THAT THE WAR AGAINST TERRORISM- WHATEVER ITS

MOTIVES AND REASONS- MEANS THE PALESTINIAN RESISTANCE AND NO ONE

ELSE, AND WHICH MEANS THAT WHAT ISRAEL COMMITS AGAINST THE

PALESTINIAN PEOPLE DOES NOT FALL UNDER THIS DESCRIPTION

(TERRORISM) ... PRESIDENT BUSH\'S CONCERN IS NOT PEACE WITHIN ITS

TRUE MEANING, BUT HIS CONCERN IS TO INSURE AMERICAN PEACE IN THE

REGION AFTER OCCUPYING IRAQ, IN ADDITION TO GUARANTEEING ISRAEL\'S

SECURITY EVEN IF THROUGH A PALESTINIAN CIVIL WAR, AND ALSO

PLACING SYRIA AND LEBANON ON THE SHELF UNTIL THEIR TURN COMES

FOLLOWING THE END OF THE PALESTINIAN RESISTANCE, SINCE THE

REQUEST IS THE SAME: ERASING ANY RESISTANCE AND ANY STAND THAT

MAY STOP AGREEMENTS WITH ZIONIST CONDITIONS."
