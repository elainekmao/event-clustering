DEAR MS. HEILEMAN,

IN REPLY TO YOUR FAX OF YESTERDAY, I AM WRITING TO

CERTIFY THAT NO UNIDROIT PROJECTS HAVE THE PRIMARY, OR

INDEED THE SECONDARY PURPOSE OF PROVIDING ANY DIRECT OR

INDIRECT BENEFITS TO THE PLO OR SWAPO OR TO OTHER

ACTIVITIES MENTIONED IN THE STATE AUTHORIZATION ACT.

NEITHER HAS UNIDROIT ENGAGED IN ANY EXTERNAL BORROWINGS

IN 1984 OR SUCCESSIVE FINANCIAL YEARS AND I CAN CERTIFY

THAT NONE OF THE UNITED STATES CONTRIBUTION TO UNIDROIT

FOR THE 2003 FISCAL YEAR (PROPOSED EURO 110,000.00) WILL

BE USED FOR THE UNITED STATES SHARE OF INTEREST COSTS AS

NO SUCH COSTS HAVE BEEN IN INCURRED.

PLEASE DO NOT HESITATE TO INFORM ME SHOULD YOU REQUIRE

ANY FURTHER INFORMATION.

YOURS SINCERELY,

WALTER RODINO

DEPUTY SECRETARY GENERAL. END TEXT. HALL
