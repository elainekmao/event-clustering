SUBJECT: GOT TERMINATES OPERATION NORTHERN WATCH

(U) Classified by Deputy Chief of Mission Robert Deutsch for

reasons 1.5 (B) and (D).

(C) On March 22, MFA Director General for the Americas Amb.

Banguoglu called in DCM to deliver formal notification that

the GOT has decided to terminate Operation Northern Watch.

Banguoglu requested that all civilian and military personnel,

forces and assets connected with the operation be redeployed

in a reasonable period of time in close coordination with the

Turkish General Staff. Banguoglu stated that MFA had

informed TGS of the content of the diplomatic note notifying

us of the GOT\'s decision.

2. Begin text MFA note:

2003/ALGY/111929

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Turkey

presents its compliments to the Embassies of the United

Kingdom and the United States of America and with reference

to paragraph (j) of the Exchange of Letters between Turkey

and the USA dated December 25, 1996 and the Ministry\'s Note

of March 31, 1997, has the honor to inform the esteemed

Embassies that the Government of Turkey has decided to

terminate Operation Northern Watch as of March 21, 2003, upon

the commencement of hostilities toward Iraq.

It is kindly requested that all military and civilian forces

and assets participating in Operation Northern Watch

activities be duly redeployed out of Turkey in close

coordination with the Turkish General Staff.

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Turkey

avails itself of this opportunity to renew to the Embassies

of the United Kingdom and the United States of America the

assurances of its highest consideration.

Ankara, March 21, 2003

End text MFA note.
