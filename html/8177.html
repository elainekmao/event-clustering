SUBJECT: (S) TERRORIST FINANCING: REQUEST

FOR RELEASABLE INTEL PACKAGE ON HUMAN APPEAL

INTERNATIONAL

REF: STATE 133205

1. (U) CLASSIFIED BY CHARGE RICHARD ALBRIGHT

FOR REASONS 1.5 (B,D).

2. (S/NF) Because of the sensitivities

associated with Human Appeal International

(HAI), we have requested an appointment with

de facto Foreign Minister Shaykh Hamdan Bin

Zayid to deliver reftel points. Hamdan, our

primary leadership-level contact on terrorist

financing issues, is currently traveling

outside the UAE.

3. (S/NF) However, in anticipation of his

likely response to our demarche, we would

appreciate receiving (via ORCA channels if

necessary) a releasable intel package which we

may provide to the UAEG in support of our

request that they crack down on Human Appeal.

The information on Ayoub Ali and Boudella Al-

Haj is specific and compelling, but we have

no/no direct supporting information with

regard to HAI\'s funding of Hamas. If we want

the UAEG to block HAI funding of Hamas, it

would be helpful to give them evidence that

such funding has occurred in the past and is

continuing, including the specific

entities/accounts belonging to Hamas into

which HAI funds are flowing. Similarly,

details with regard to the Al-Rashid Trust\'s

continuing operations in the UAE would be most

helpful. We note that the UAE Central Bank

has already ordered financial institutions

here -- at our request -- to freeze any

accounts associated with the Al-Rashid Trust.

4. (S/NF) When we went to the UAE to request

their partnership with us in closing Al-

Barakat operations here we were able to submit

to them a lengthy intel package outlining the

reasons why we believed that organization was

involved in terrorist financing. A similar

package on HAI will demonstrate that we are

motivated by solid fact and that we are

serious about having the UAEG take appropriate

action to block HAI funding of terrorist

groups. A strong intel package should give

the Emirati authorities the compelling

information they will need to intiate their

own investigation and take the necessary steps

to ensure HAI is not funding -- wittingly or

unwittingly -- terrorist groups.

Albright
