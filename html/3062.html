SUBJECT: AA/S WOOD HOLDS WIDE-RANGING DISCUSSIONS

-- IN HANOI

REF: HANOI 1332

1. (SBU) SUMMARY: DURING A MAY 20-22 VISIT TO HANOI,

IO AA/S WOOD DISCUSSED COUNTERTERRORISM, THE

INTERNATIONAL CRIMINAL COURT (ICC), NATIONAL ASSEMBLY

REFORM, THE MONTAGNARDS, MULTILATERAL AFFAIRS, AND THE

MIDDLE EAST WITH GVN AND COMMUNIST PARTY OF VIETNAM

(CPV) OFFICIALS, STUDENTS FROM THE MINISTRY OF FOREIGN

AFFAIRS\' INSTITUTE OF INTERNATIONAL AFFAIRS (MFA/IIR),

AND UN OFFICIALS. VIETNAMESE INTERLOCUTORS WERE

SUPPORTIVE OF THE INTERNATIONAL CAMPAIGN AGAINST

TERRORISM, BUT SAID IT SHOULD BE CONDUCTED WITHIN THE UN

FRAMEWORK. THE GVN, WITH THE ASSISTANCE OF THE UN, IS

MOVING AHEAD TO PROFESSIONALIZE THE NATIONAL ASSEMBLY.

THE GVN IS "STUDYING" THE ICC, BUT APPEARS GENERALLY TO

AGREE WITH THE US VIEW. THE GVN DOES NOT SUPPORT

SUICIDE BOMBINGS IN ISRAEL, BUT HAS "GREAT SYMPATHY FOR

THE PALESTINIANS. VIETNAMESE OFFICIALS APPEALED FOR USG

SUPPORT IN THEIR EFFORTS TO OBTAIN A SEAT ON THE UN

SECURITY COUNCIL IN 2008. END SUMMARY

2. (U) DURING HIS VISIT TO HANOI, IO AA/S WOOD MET

WITH NGUYEN SI DUNG, DIRECTOR OF THE NATIONAL ASSEMBLY

DEPARTMENT\'S FSI. END NOTE)

---------------------------------------

NATIONAL ASSEMBLY REFORM MOVING FORWARD

---------------------------------------

3. (U) THE NA\'S DUNG TOLD AA/S WOOD THE NA HAS A "VERY

COOPERATIVE" RELATIONSHIP WITH UNDP AND IS GRATEFUL FOR

ITS ASSISTANCE IN THE GOOD GOVERNANCE PROJECT. THIS

PROJECT IS HELPING VIETNAM DEVELOP A MORE TRANSPARENT

DECISION-MAKING PROCESS, DUNG CLAIMED. DUNG DESCRIBED

THE NA AS "THE MOST IMPORTANT INSTITUTION OF DEMOCRACY

IN VIETNAM." THE PROJECT IS ALSO SENDING GVN OFFICIALS

ABROAD FOR TRAINING AND TO LEARN HOW LEGISLATURES IN

OTHER COUNTRIES FUNCTION. IMPORTANT ACTIVITIES HAVE

INCLUDED A CD ROM CONTAINING INFORMATION ON VIETNAM\'S

LEGAL STRUCTURE AND THE CREATION OF PORTABLE LEGAL

LIBRARIES. THESE EFFORTS ARE INTENDED TO CREATE A MORE

TRANSPARENT GOVERNMENT, DUNG SAID. HE ALSO NOTED THAT,

WHILE ECONOMIC REFORMS ARE THE CORNERSTONE OF THE GVN\'S

AGENDA, HE HAS PUSHED THE VIEW THAT POLITICAL REFORMS

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 04 HANOI 01405 01 OF 03 030638Z

ARE A KEY COMPLEMENT TO THIS EFFORT. HE POINTED OUT

THAT, FOR THE FIRST TIME, "THE RULE OF LAW IS

ACKNOWLEDGED" IN THE COUNTRY\'S CONSTITUTION. ALSO, THE

CONCEPT OF THE GOVERNMENT\'S "LIMITS OF POWER IS

RECOGNIZED."

4. (U) REFORMING THE BUDGET PROCESS IS A KEY COMPONENT

OF PUBLIC ADMINISTRATION REFORM, ACCORDING TO BUI DUC

THU, NA DEPUTY DIRECTOR, DEPARTMENT OF ECONOMY AND

BUDGET. HE ADMITTED THE PRESENT STRUCTURE IS

"CUMBERSOME AND NOT VERY GOOD." ALSO, THE PRESENT

SYSTEM IS TOO TOLERANT OF CORRUPTION. THU DISCUSSED THE

GVN\'S PLANS TO DEVOLVE MORE BUDGETARY RESPONSIBILITY TO

THE PROVINCIAL LEVEL. ALSO, PLANS ARE UNDERWAY TO

REVISE THE BUDGET LAW TO DEFINE MORE CLEARLY

RESPONSIBILITY AND ACCOUNTABILITY. THESE IMPROVEMENTS

WILL REQUIRE ENHANCED CAPACITY AMONG NA MEMBERS AND

PROVINCIAL PEOPLE\'S COUNCILS, THU CONCLUDED.

5. (U) IN CONCLUDING THE MEETING, DUNG EXPRESSED

SUPPORT FOR THE INTERNATIONAL CAMPAIGN AGAINST TERRORISM

AND SAID HE HOPED THE USG WOULD SUPPORT VIETNAM IN ITS

BID FOR ELECTION TO THE UN SECURITY COUNCIL IN 2008.

COUNTRY REPRESENTATIVE AND UN RESIDENT COORDINATOR

JORDAN RYAN TOLD AA/S WOOD THAT, HAVING RETURNED TO

HANOI FOR A SECOND TOUR (HIS FIRST TOUR WAS 1993-1996),

HE WAS STRUCK BY THE DIFFERENCES. SPECIFICALLY, HE

NOTED THAT, DURING HIS FIRST TOUR, THERE WAS ALMOST NO

DISSENT AND TOPICS SUCH AS HUMAN RIGHTS WERE NOT

DISCUSSED OPENLY. THERE IS STILL A LONG WAY TO GO, BUT

AT LEAST THE TOPIC IS ON THE GVN\'S AGENDA. MR. RYAN IS

WORKING FOR MORE COLLABORATION AMONG THE UN "FAMILY" IN

VIETNAM AND POINTED TO THE RECENT MEETING AMONG UNICEF,

UNDP, AND UNFPA AS A SIGN OF PROGRESS.

7. (SBU) IN RESPONSE TO A QUESTION FROM AA/S WOOD,

RYAN ALSO SPOKE ABOUT THE SERIOUS HIV/AIDS PROBLEM AND

THE CONNECTION WITH INTRAVENOUS DRUG USE. HE

COMPLIMENTED THE USG FOR ITS HELP THROUGH USAID AND CDC,

BUT LAMENTED THE FACT THAT THERE ARE TWO "COMPETING"

ORGANIZATIONS WITHIN THE GVN\'S MINISTRY OF HEALTH. RYAN

SAID THAT STATISTICS INDICATED 1.3 PERCENT OF NEW ARMY

RECRUITS ARE HIV-INFECTED. ABOUT 60 PERCENT OF HIV

CASES ARE RELATED TO DRUG INJECTING. MR. RYAN SAID MORE

HIGH LEVEL COMMITMENT FROM THE GVN IS NEEDED.

8. (SBU) VU AN SON, UNHCR HANOI OFFICER-IN-CHARGE,

TOLD AA/S WOOD THAT UNHCR REGIONAL REPRESENTATIVE ASSADI

HOPES TO VISIT THE CENTRAL HIGHLANDS NEXT MONTH. THE

905 MONTAGNARDS WHO FLED VIETNAM ARE BEING PROCESSED FOR

RESETTLEMENT IN THE U.S. AMBASSADOR BURGHARDT NOTED

REPORTS THAT THE CAMBODIAN GOVERNMENT RECENTLY SEALED

THE BORDER AND TURNED BACK ABOUT 200 MONTAGNARDS. SON

THE FIGURE HE HEARD WAS 500. SON OPINED THAT ULTIMATELY

THE BEST SOLUTION IS TO IMPROVE LOCAL ECONOMIC AND

SOCIAL CONDITIONS.

-------------------------------------------

CPV: AGAINST TERRORISM, NOT SURE ABOUT ICC

-------------------------------------------

9. (SBU) DIRECTOR NHIEN TOLD AA/S WOOD THE CPV\'S VIEW

IS CLEAR: "WE FIGHT TERRORISM IN ANY FORM." HOWEVER,

THE CPV ALSO BELIEVES ANY OPERATIONS AGAINST TERRORISTS

SHOULD BE "IN ACCORDANCE WITH INTERNATIONAL LAW AND THE

UN FRAMEWORK." NHIEN SAID VIETNAM HAS BEEN PLEASED TO

ASSIST THE USG. THE GVN HAS RESPONDED ON A TIMELY BASIS

TO REQUESTS FROM THE US EMBASSY REGARDING LISTS OF

TERRORIST ORGANIZATIONS AND BANK ACCOUNTS THAT COULD BE

POTENTIALLY USED BY TERRORISTS. FORTUNATELY, NO

TERRORIST ACTIVITY HAS BEEN FOUND YET, HE SAID. NHIEN

NOTED THAT THE ISSUE OF TERRORISM CUTS BOTH WAYS, AND HE

APPEALED TO AA/S WOOD TO CARRY THE MESSAGE BACK THAT THE

GVN ALSO WANTS THE USG TO DEAL STRONGLY WITH WHAT HE

VIETNAM." AA/S WOOD SAID HE WOULD CONVEY THIS MESSAGE

10. (SBU) CONCERNING THE ICC, AA/S WOOD EXPLAINED THE

USG\'S CONCERNS AND ASKED FOR VIETNAM\'S SUPPORT FOR

ARTICLE 98 AGREEMENTS AS AN ALTERNATIVE PROCESS. NHIEN

DID NOT COMMENT DIRECTLY ON ARTICLE 98 SUPPORT, BUT

ADMITTED THAT, WHILE THERE HAVE BEEN INTERNATIONAL

TRIBUNALS IN THE PAST, HE WAS NOT VERY FAMILIAR WITH NOR

DID HE FULLY UNDERSTAND THE ICC CONCEPT.

MFA/IO: AGAINST TERRORISM, SUPPORTIVE OF PALESTINIANS

--------------------------------------------- ---------

12. (SBU) MFA/IO DIRECTOR HUYEN TOLD AA/S WOOD THAT,

WHILE THE GVN IS AGAINST TERRORISM AND SUPPORTS THE

INTERNATIONAL CAMPAIGN, ALL ACTIVITIES DIRECTED AGAINST

TERRORISM "SHOULD CONFORM WITH THE UN CHARTER AND

INTERNATIONAL LAW." HUYEN SAID THE GVN HAS RATIFIED

"FIVE" TERRORIST CONVENTIONS AND WOULD LIKE TO RATIFY

"THE MAJORITY, IN THE NEAR FUTURE." (NOTE: PER THE

GVN\'S UNSCR 1373 SUBMISSION, THE GVN HAS ONLY RATIFIED

FOUR AND IS NOW CONSIDERING A FIFTH. END NOTE) HUYEN

ALSO REQUESTED USG SUPPORT FOR ACTIONS AGAINST "THOSE

VIETNAMESE AND OTHER GROUPS PLOTTING TERRORIST

ACTIVITIES AGAINST VIETNAM FROM THE U.S." AA/S WOOD

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 03 HANOI 01405 03 OF 03 030639Z

TOLD HUYEN SAID THE USG WILL NOT TOLERATE ANY FORM OF

TERRORISM ANYWHERE. HE ALSO EXPRESSED APPRECIATION FOR

VIETNAM\'S PLEDGE OF USD 300,000 IN MATERIAL ASSISTANCE

FOR AFGHAN RECONSTRUCTION.

13. (SBU) CONCERNING THE MIDDLE EAST, HUYEN SAID

VIETNAM IS "VERY WORRIED." VIETNAM DOES NOT SUPPORT

SUICIDE BOMBING, BUT UNDERSTANDS THOSE PALESTINIANS

UNDER OCCUPATION ARE ACTING "OUT OF DESPAIR-THEY HAVE NO

OTHER MEANS." HUYEN FURTHER STATED "WE CONDEMN ALL

VIOLENCE THAT KILLS PEOPLE." SHE URGED THE USG TO

CONTINUE ITS EFFORTS TO "ACCELERATE THE PEACE PROCESS"

AND BRING ABOUT A TWO-STATE SOLUTION-"THE ONLY WAY TO

SOLVE THE PROBLEM," SHE OPINED. AA/S WOOD ASKED HUYEN

TO RESIST UNBALANCED POSITIONS ON MIDDLE EAST

RESOLUTIONS.

14. (SBU) ON THE ICC, HUYEN SAID THE GVN\'S VIEW IS

THOSE WHO COMMIT CRIMES NEED TO BE HELD ACCOUNTABLE.

HOWEVER, THE ICC CONCEPT IS "NEW TO US AND WE ARE

STUDYING IT." SHE NOTED VIETNAM\'S CONCERN OVER POSSIBLE

SOVEREIGNTY ISSUES CONCERNING THE ICC.

15. (U) AA/S WOOD HAS CLEARED THIS MESSAGE. PORTER
