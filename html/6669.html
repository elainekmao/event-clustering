Consular

--------

We have been told by the Saudi Embassy in Kuwait that

individual American citizens wishing to apply for visas

to the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia should go the Saudi

Embassy in Kuwait during business hours and apply in

person. Applicants are required to complete a visa

application form and provide passport-size photographs

and copies of their valid passports and Kuwaiti residency

permits. We have been told that gratis "emergency

transit visas" will be issued to individual applicants,

provided they have valid travel documents. There have

been

no requests for assistance in obtaining Saudi visas

Kuwaiti immigration reports all quiet at the main POE

on the Saudi border this morning. There are no

"stranded" Americans. The Consular team from Dhahran

that was at Khafji on the Saudi side of the border to

assist Americans left yesterday after

we agreed their presence was no longer required. We are

in close

touch with Kuwait Immigration and they have our emergency

contact numbers if any Americans do need assistance.

The remaining two Amcits who had contacted the Embassy

for departure assistance are no longer interested. They

have made their arrangements. No pending cases.
