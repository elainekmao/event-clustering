SUBJECT: ANONYMOUS THREAT LETTER

Sensitive but unclassified - protect accordingly.

1. (SBU) Colombo British High Commission (BHC) counterpart

contacted RSO yesterday to report receipt of an anonymous

threat letter addressed to Americans and British in Sri

Lanka. Presumably, letters were sent to both the BHC and

the Embassy, but we have not yet received our letter. The

BHC has already forwarded their letter to the Sri Lanka

Police for investigation.

BEGIN TEXT -

WARNING TO THE AMERICANS AND THE BRITISH LIVING IN SRI

LANKA.

WE MUSLIMS IN SRI LANKA CONDEMN THE THREAT OF ATTACKING

IRAQ. THE DAY YOU INVADE IRAQ WE WILL KIDNAP THE AMERICANS

AND BRITISH FAMILIES LIVING IN SRI LANKA.

LIKE PREVIOUSLY WE WILL NOT PROTEST BUT WE WILL DO ALL WHAT

AL-QAEDA AND OSAMA BIN LADEN WILL ORDER US TO DO.

WE ARE FOLLOWING AL-QAEDA AND OSAMA BIN LADEN. WE WILL NOT

KEEP ANY ROOM FOR THE BRITISH/AMERICAN FORCES TO ATTACK OR

STRIKE OR INVADE IRAQ, AT ANY COST.

WE ARE BOLD TO SAY OUR MUSLIM POLITICAL PARTIES ARE ALLIED

OR JOINTLY WORKING WITH AL-QAEDA ORGANIZATION LED BY OSAMA

BIN LADEN.

ONCE WE GET OUR SEPARATE STATE AND A MUSLIM PORT IN SRI

LANKA WE WILL DO THE NEEDFUL.

END TEXT.

2. (SBU) Whereas we take all threats seriously, we also see

an endless number of Sri Lankan-based anonymous poison pen

letters. We generally view these with a good deal of

skepticism. We are reporting this one however, because it

threatens kidnapping family members. We continue to believe

the threat is minimal. We will nonetheless continue to

monitor related additional developments and threats closely.

Amselem
