SUMMARY

-------

1. (C) ZIMBABWE\'S INCREASINGLY DESPERATE RULING PARTY,

ZANU-PF HAS RESORTED TO VIOLENCE, BOTH RHETORICAL AND

PHYSICAL TO COMBAT ITS ERODING POPULARITY. AS THREATS

ISSUED BY ZANU-PF ESCALATE, SO DO INCIDENTS OF

HARASSMENT AND MAYHEM AND THE DANGER THAT LESS STABLE

ELEMENTS IN ZANU-PF WILL INTERPRET THEM AS A GREEN LIGHT

FOR ANYTHING GOES. ONE POLICEMAN WAS KILLED APRIL 3 ON

A FARM OCCUPIED BY WAR VETERANS, WHOSE OWNER WAS

SEVERELY BEATEN. THE GOVERNMENT-CONTROLLED PRESS HAS

COLLABORATED IN THE GOVERNMENT\'S EFFORT TO SOW CONFUSION

AND, SOME OBSERVERS SPECULATE, SET THE STAGE FOR A

CRACKDOWN OR EVEN A STATE OF EMERGENCY. THE MILITARY

AND SECURITY FORCES WILL BE THE FINAL ARBITERS OF THE

TRANSITION. IN THE EYE OF THE STORM IS ROBERT MUGABE,

CONFIDENTIAL

WHOM ARCHBISHOP TUTU RECENTLY DUBBED A CARICATURE OF AN

AFRICAN LEADER. THE PRESIDENT SINCERELY BELIEVES THE

BRITISH, ZIMBABWEAN WHITES AND THE WEST IN GENERAL ARE

TO BLAME FOR HIS SETBACKS DUE TO THEIR COLLUSIVE,

TARGETED EFFORTS. HE SEEMS SERENELY UNAWARE OF THE

SERIOUSNESS OF THE SITUATION OF HIS PARTY AND COUNTRY.

WHILE SOME SORT OF BRIDGE BETWEEN GOVERNMENT AND

OPPOSITION NEEDS TO BE BUILT, IT IS IMPROBABLE THAT THAT

CAN BE ACCOMPLISHED PRIOR TO THE ELECTION. MUGABE MAY

WELCOME A VISIT BY A SENIOR AMERICAN OFFICIAL WHOSE

PRIVATE MESSAGE WOULD BE ONE OF RESTRAINT. AT THE SAME

TIME WE MUST WORK WITH THE SOUTH AFRICANS, WHO SET THE

STANDARD FOR PEACEFUL TRANSITION IN AFRICA, AND THE

INTERNATIONAL BUSINESS SECTOR TO PRESS FOR DOMESTIC

ORDER AND A PEACEFUL WAY FORWARD IN ZIMBABWE. END

COMMENT.

THE SECURITY FORCES AS ARBITERS OF EVENTS

-----------------------------------------

9. (C) WHERE THINGS WILL GO DEPENDS ON THE SECURITY

FORCES, AND WHETHER THEY WILL OBEY ORDERS TO INSTITUTE

MARTIAL LAW OR A STATE OF EMERGENCY WHEN THE PRESIDENT

DECIDES THE COUNTRY HAS BECOME OTHERWISE UNGOVERNABLE.

AT THE MOMENT WE HAVE INDICATIONS THAT BOTH THE ARMY AND

ESPECIALLY THE POLICE HAVE A NUMBER OF MDC SUPPORTERS.

INDEED, THE POOR POLICEMAN WHO MADE THE ULTIMATE

SACRIFICE ON A MARONDERA FARM WAS KILLED BECAUSE THE WAR

VETERANS THOUGHT THAT WAS WHERE HIS SYMPATHIES LAID.

THE 13,000 OR SO TROOPS IN THE CONGO HAVE HAD THEIR PER

DIEMS CUT, AND TO ADD INSULT TO INJURY THEY HAVE NOT

BEEN PAID IN FOUR MONTHS. WE THINK THAT WHILE THE

PRESIDENT SINCERELY WISHES TO BRING THE TROOPS HOME

BECAUSE OF DIMINISHING RETURNS AND THE COST TO THE

COUNTRY, THIS IS NOT THE MOMENT TO DO SO. HE DOES NOT

WANT A NUMBER OF DISGRUNTLED (AND PROBABLY UNEMPLOYED)

MEN BACK IN ZIMBABWE TO SWELL THE RANKS OF THE

OPPOSITION.

10. (C) SENIOR MILITARY FIGURES ARE WORRIED ABOUT

MORGAN TSVANGIRAI WINNING OVER 76 SEATS IN PARLIAMENT,

THE MAJORITY. UNLIKE PRESIDENT WADE IN SENEGAL,

TSVANGIRAI WAS NEVER PART OF THE GOVERNMENT AND IS

RECOMMENDATIONS

---------------

12. (C) UNDER THESE CIRCUMSTANCES, OUR ABILITY TO

INFLUENCE EVENTS IS VERY LIMITED. NEVERTHELESS, WE

BELIEVE MUGABE IS LIKELY TO LISTEN, AND PERHAPS HEED,

PRIVATE MESSAGES OF RESTRAINT FROM SENIOR USG OFFICIALS

IN THE RUN-UP TO AND AFTER THE ELECTION, PARTICULARLY IF

THESE MEETINGS ARE CAST AS "CONSULTATIONS" WITH THE

ZIMBABWEAN HEAD OF STATE. WE HAVE BEEN URGED TO REMAIN

ENGAGED WITH MUGABE AND HIS GOVERNMENT BY PEOPLE BOTH

INSIDE AND OUTSIDE THE RULING PARTY, WHO SENSE THAT THE

GOVERNMENT WILL ONLY BECOME MORE REPRESSIVE IF IT

PERCEIVES ITSELF AS UNDER ATTACK FROM THE WEST. A

SECONDARY MESSAGE OUR SENIOR OFFICIAL MIGHT DELIVER IS

THAT ZIMBABWE HAS INTERNATIONAL OBLIGATIONS IN THE DROC,

WHICH IT RECONFIRMED IN NEW YORK IN JANUARY, AND IN THE

SOUTHERN AFRICAN SUB-CONTINENT, WHICH ITS VOLATILE

DOMESTIC POLITICS ARE HINDERING. WE SHOULD URGE

ZIMBABWE AT EVERY LEVEL TO GET ITS HOUSE IN ORDER

THROUGH PEACEFUL, DEMOCRATIC ELECTIONS, AND GET BACK ON

ZIMBABWE BY A SENIOR AMERICAN OFFICIAL WOULD BE HELPFUL.

13. (C) IN ADDITION TO THE BILATERAL RELATIONSHIP,

THERE ARE OTHER AVENUES OF COMMUNICATION AND INFLUENCE

THAT WE COULD DRAW UPON. FROM MULTIPLE POINTS, THE USG

THE PRIVATE SECTOR, TO DIALOGUE WITH THEIR ZIMBABWEAN

COUNTERPARTS. THE SOUTH AFRICANS EXECUTED A MODEL FOR

TO ZIMBABWE. AT THE SAME TIME WE COULD MORE PRO-

ZIMBABWE TO RAISE THEIR CONCERNS WITH THE ZIMBABWEAN

GOVERNMENT AND THE OPPOSITION. THOUGH TO DATE THE

PRIVATE SECTOR\'S DEGREE OF INFLUENCE WITH THE GOVERNMENT

HAS BEEN SECONDARY, THE JOBS, TAXES AND UNDERPINNING

THEY PROVIDE ARGUE FOR A MORE VISIBLE SEAT AT THE TABLE.
