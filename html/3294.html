SUBJECT: ITALY COMMITS ITSELF TO RESEARCH COOPERATION IN

AGRICULTURE BIOTECHNOLOGY

REFS: A) ROME 1965 B) ROME 2999 C) ROCK-MULENEX TELCOM JULY

01

1. THIS IS AN ACTION REQUEST, SEEKING OES RESPONSE TO REFS

A AND B. SEE PARAGRAPH 4.

2. EMBASSY PROVIDES INFORMAL TRANSLATION OF A LETTER SENT

TO ESTCOUNS FROM ANTONIO CATRICALA, SECRETARY GENERAL OF THE

PRESIDENCY OF THE COUNCIL OF MINISTERS. THE LETTER IS DATED

JUNE 24 BUT WAS RECEIVED ON JULY 1.

BEGINNING OF INFORMAL EMBASSY TRANSLATION:

IN REFERENCE TO YOUR LETTER TO U/S LETTA SENT ON APRIL 18

AND SIGNED ALSO BY PROF. LEONARDO SANTI, I CAN CONFIRM, ALSO

WITH U/S LETTA\'S APPROVAL, THE INTEREST AND COMMITMENT OF

THE ITALIAN GOVERNMENT FOR A FRUITFUL COOPERATION WITH THE

U.S. ON BIOTECHNOLOGY RESEARCH.

I REVIEWED THE PROPOSED TOPICS AND BELIEVE THAT THE AGREED

ISSUES COULD REPRESENT CONCRETE INITIATIVES.

IN THE BIOTECHNOLOGY FIELD, THE PRESIDENCY OF THE COUNCIL OF

MINISTERS IS SUPPORTING A DEVELOPING PLAN, WHICH WILL BE

CARRIED OUT BY A WORKING GROUP WITHIN THE NATIONAL COMMITTEE

FOR BIOSAFETY AND BIOTECHNOLOGY. THE WORKING GROUP WILL

INCLUDE THE PRESIDENCY, THE INTERESTED MINISTRIES, THE

REGIONS AND THE COMPANY SVILUPPO ITALIA (ITALIAN

THE PROGRAM WILL HAVE TO TAKE INTO CONSIDERATION, IN A

PRIORITY WAY, THE NATIONAL AND EUROPEAN NEEDS, BUT IT SHOULD

ALSO EVALUATE, IN AN HARMONIC WAY, THE INDISPENSABLE TOOLS

FOR THE WHOLE DEVELOPMENT OF BIOTECHNOLOGY INCLUDING THOSE

SUBJECTS MENTIONED IN YOUR LETTER.

WITHIN THE PRIORITIES TO BE CONSIDERED:

-THE FIGHT AGAINST BIOTERRORISM

-ENVIRONMENTAL REMEDIATION TECHNOLOGIES

-BIOTECHNOLOGY CONTRIBUTION TO FOOD QUALITY

-BUILDING DEVELOPING WORLD CAPACITY TO EVALUATE

BIOTECHNOLOGIES.

I BELIEVE THAT THE ACTIVE U.S.-ITALY COOPERATION COULD

CONTRIBUTE TO REACHING THE COMMON OBJECTIVES OF OUR TWO

COUNTRIES. WISHING YOU A GOOD WORK, I SEND YOU MY BEST

REGARDS.

END OF EMBASSY INFORMAL TRANSLATION.

3. COMMENT: THE LETTER, COMING FROM THE PRIME MINISTER\'S

OFFICE, IS FORMAL GOI APPROVAL FOR BILATERAL COOPERATION ON

BIOTECHNOLOGY. THE LETTER REFLECTS A DECISION WITHIN THE

PRIME MINISTER\'S OFFICE TO USE SCIENCE AS A LEVER TO CREATE

MORE ROOM FOR BIOTECHNOLOGY OPTIONS, ESPECIALLY WITH REGARD

TO THE MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE\'S POSITION AGAINST

COMMERCIALIZATION OF AGROBIOTECHNOLOGY. AS REPORTED IN REF

A, THE GOI HAS ALREADY NAMED EXPERTS (31 IN TOTAL) FOR EACH

OF TEN SUBJECT AREAS OF MUTUAL INTEREST AND PROPOSED A

BILATERAL EXPERTS MEETING ON AGRICULTURAL BIOTECHNOLOGY TO

UNCLASSIFIED

BE HELD LATER THIS YEAR. WE BELIEVE THIS IS THE CORRECT,

NEXT STEP IN OUR EFFORT TO RAISE THE BAR ON THE QUALITY OF

SCIENCE NEEDED TO UNDERPIN ITALIAN POLICY ON BIOTECHNOLOGY.

4. ACTION REQUEST: EMBASSY ASKS OES APPROVAL OF THE

BILATERAL COOPERATION CONCEPT. SECOND, WE NEED YOUR SUPPORT

FOR AN INTERAGENCY EXPERTS MEETING IN THE FALL IN ORDER TO

EXPLORE SPECIFIC AREAS FOR COOPERATION. SPECIFICALLY WE

ENVISION BIOTERRORISM AND PLANT VACCINES; ENVIRONMENTAL

REMEDIATION; AGRICULTURE BIOTECHNOLOGY AND FOOD QUALITY;

RENEWABLE INDUSTRIAL FOOD SAFETY; NANOBIOTECHNOLOGY; AND

DEVELOPING COUNTRIES USE OF BIOTECHNOLOGY. PRIOR TO THE

MEETING, WE WILL NEED TO DECIDE WHETHER THE ACTIVITIES ARE

ADEQUATELY COVERED UNDER THE EXISTING SANDT AGREEMENT AND

ITS ANNEXES, OR WHETHER A NEW ADMINISTRATIVE ARRANGEMENT,

E.G. LIKE THAT OF THE U.S.-EU BIOTECH TASK FORCE, WILL BE

MOST EFFECTIVE. END OF ACTION REQUEST.
