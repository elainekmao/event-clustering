SUBJECT: UAE REQUESTS INFORMATION ON EXBS TRAINING

REF: ABU DHABI 2876

1. (U) This is an action message. Please see para 3.

2. (U) In June 2003, UAEG officals met with an

interagency USG team in the UAE to develop a roadmap

for EXBS training (reftel). The UAEG MFA has requested

further information about the training programs

scheduled to start in October 2003. They would like to

know:

I. Number of UAE participants permitted to attend the

training sessions held either domestically or abroad.

II. Number of UAE participants permitted to

participate in the workshops either domestically or

abroad.

III. What facilities and materials does Washington

require to hold training sessions in the UAE?

IV. Will the UAEG need to pay fees for the sessions?

Who will bear expenses such as food, accommodation,

transportation, and paying trainers\' fees?

3. (U) ACTION REQUESTED: Please advise on how to

respond to the UAEG request.
