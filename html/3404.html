SUBJECT: NEPAL\'S CROWN PRINCESS GIVES BIRTH TO SON

REF: KATHMANDU 1413 (NOTAL)

At approximately 1:45 p.m. on July 31, Nepal\'s Crown Princess

Hemani Rajya Laxmi Devi Shah gave birth to a son. In

accordance with Hindu custom, the child\'s name will be chosen

in a ceremony after 11 days. Crown Prince Paras and his wife

already have one child, a girl born in December 2000. The

boy, their first son, is now second in line to inherit

Nepal\'s throne (Reftel).
