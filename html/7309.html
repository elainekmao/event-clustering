SUBJECT: Sri Lanka/Maldives: EU intervention on

bilateral Article 98 agreements

Ref: State 92117

(U) Classified by Lewis Amselem, Deputy Chief of

Mission. Reasons 1.5 (b,d)

1. (C) Per Reftel, Mission has no information re

European Union intervention against the bilateral

U.S./Sri Lanka Article 98 agreement prior to the

November 22, 2002, signing of the agreement. There are

indications, however, that the Germans and Dutch

approached the GSL through the Sri Lankan ambassador in

Brussels about the issue -- but to no avail.

2. (C) In regards to the Maldives, Mission has no reason

to believe that there has been any intervention by the

EU. Mission expects the non-surrender agreement with

the Maldives to be finalized soon.

3. (U) Minimize considered.
