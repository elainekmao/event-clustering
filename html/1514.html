WHAT PRICE A PRO-ZANU-PF UNION LEADERSHIP?

------------------------------------------

1. (C) ON FEBRUARY 1, CHARGE, ACCOMPANIED BY POLOFF AND

LABOFF, MET WITH BRIAN RAFTOPOULOS (PROTECT), A RESEARCH

FELLOW IN THE INSTITUTE OF DEVELOPMENT STUDIES AT THE

UNIVERSITY OF ZIMBABWE, AND AN INSIGHTFUL CONTACT

REGARDING THE LOCAL POLITICAL SCENE. RAFTOPOULOS TOLD

US THAT HE HAS NO DOUBTS THAT ZANU-PF IS TRYING TO BUY

THE ELECTION OF A MORE FRIENDLY LEADERSHIP AT THE

ZIMBABWE CONGRESS OF TRADE UNIONS (ZCTU), IN ORDER TO

NEUTRALIZE LABOR AS A RICH SOURCE OF ORGANIZATIONAL

SUPPORT FOR THE MOVEMENT FOR DEMOCRATIC CHANGE (MDC).

HE HAS HEARD FOR EXAMPLE, THAT THE ZANU-PF/GOZ HAS PAID

THE ARREARAGES OF TEN UNIONS AFFILIATED WITH THE ZCTU,

WHICH ALLOWS THEM TO PARTICIPATE IN THE UPCOMING

CONGRESS FEBRUARY 23-25 IN MASVINGO AND GIVES THEM

VOTING PRIVILEGES. THE FEBRUARY 1-7 EDITION OF THE

INDEPENDENT "FINANCIAL GAZETTE" DESCRIBED A ZANU-PF/GOZ

PLOT TO RIG THE UPCOMING CONGRESS ELECTIONS, INCLUDING

THE EXISTENCE OF A SLUSH FUND TO BE USED TOWARDS THIS

END AND CONTROLLED BY THE GOZ CENTRAL INTELLIGENCE

WHY NOT JUST MANHANDLE THE ZCTU?

--------------------------------

2. (C) RAFTOPOULOS ALSO BELIEVES THAT THE RULING PARTY

WILL SEND WAR VETERANS TO DEMONSTRATE OUTSIDE THE

CONGRESS VENUE IN MASVINGO, BUT THAT THE PARTY WILL NOT TRY

HEAVY-HANDED INTIMIDATION TACTICS LIKE THOSE USED IN THE

RECENT PARLIAMENTARY BY-ELECTIONS. THE UNION MEMBERSHIP

OF THE ZCTU WOULD NOT BE INTIMIDATED BY THESE TACTICS,

HE ADDED, AND THEY WOULD LIKELY BACKFIRE AND LEAD TO THE

ELECTION OF PRO-MDC LEADERS. RAFTOPOULOS EXPECTS THAT

ZANU-PF WILL STEP UP EFFORTS TO BRIBE INDIVIDUAL UNION

LEADERS AS THE CONGRESS APPROACHES, AS WELL AS RUN

STORIES IN THE MEDIA ON HOW THE MDC IS TRYING TO CONTROL

THE ZCTU FOR ITS OWN SELFISH AND NEFARIOUS PURPOSES.

THE JANUARY 31 EDITION OF THE GOZ-CONTROLLED "THE

HERALD" RAN A FEATURE STORY ENTITLED "ZCTU: TRADE

UNIONISM RISKS BEING SACRIFICED ON THE PEDESTAL OF

POLITICAL GREED." THE STORY CLAIMED THAT THE TRADE

UNION MOVEMENT WOULD SUFFER IF ITS MEMBERSHIP ALLOWS IT

TO BE AFFILIATED WITH THE MDC.

CONGRESS OUTCOME IS CRITICAL TO THE MDC

3. (C) RAFTOPOULOS MADE CLEAR THAT THE MDC\'S FUTURE

SUCCESS AS A POLITICAL PARTY DEPENDS ON CONTINUED

SUPPORT FROM THE ZCTU. ANY ATTEMPT AT MASS ACTION BY

THE MDC REQUIRES THE SUPPORT AND ORGANIZING RESOURCES OF

THE ZCTU. SIMILARLY, RAFTOPOULOS BELIEVES THAT THE MDC

WOULD NOT BE ABLE TO RUN AN EFFECTIVE NATIONWIDE

PRESIDENTIAL CAMPAIGN IN 2002 WITHOUT THE ZCTU. IT IS

FOR THIS REASON, HE ADDED, THAT ZANU-PF IS MAKING A

CONCERTED EFFORT TO BREAK THE TIES BETWEEN THE MDC AND

THE ZCTU.

4. (C) COMMENT: GIVEN THE HISTORY OF ZANU-PF, WE HAVE

LITTLE DOUBT THAT THE RULING PARTY IS ATTEMPTING TO

MANIPULATE AND/OR BUY THE ELECTION OF A SYMPATHETIC ZCTU

LEADERSHIP. SEPARATING THE MDC FROM ITS TRADE UNION

BASE IS A CENTRAL ELEMENT OF ZANU-PF\'S "URBAN STRATEGY"

FOR THE 2002 PRESIDENTIAL ELECTIONS. THE MDC IS TAKING

THIS CHALLENGE VERY SERIOUSLY, AND IS ORGANIZING ITS OWN

SUPPORTERS WITHIN THE UNIONS TO COUNTER THE ATTACK. THE

OUTCOME OF THIS STRUGGLE WILL DEPEND ON WHETHER THE PRO-

MDC UNION LEADERS PRESENT A SINGLE SLATE OF CANDIDATES

FOR THE ZCTU LEADERSHIP, AS WELL AS HOW EFFECTIVE THE

RULING PARTY IS AT BUYING THE VOTES OF UNION LEADERS.

END COMMENT.
