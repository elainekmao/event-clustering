SUBJECT: ZIMBABWE CABINET MINISTER KILLED IN CAR

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 HARARE 01470 301345Z

ACCIDENT LEAVES GAPING HOLE IN RULING PARTY

CLASSIFIED BY CHARGE D\'AFFAIRES EARL IRVING FOR REASONS:

1.5 (B) AND (D).

1. (U) YOUTH DEVELOPMENT, GENDER AND EMPLOYMENT

CREATION MINISTER BORDER GEZI WAS KILLED IN AN APRIL 28

CAR ACCIDENT ON THE HARARE-MASVINGO ROAD. GEZI\'S DRIVER

WAS ALSO KILLED, AND HIS AIDE WAS SERIOUSLY INJURED. A

TIRE BLOWOUT APPARENTLY CAUSED GEZI\'S OFFICIAL MERCEDES

TO SWERVE OFF THE HIGHWAY AND INTO A LARGE TREE. THE

APRIL 29 EDITION OF THE GOVERNMENT-INFLUENCED "SUNDAY

MAIL" SHOWED GEZI\'S CAR AS A BADLY CRUMPLED WRECK, WITH

THE LEFT HALF OF THE CAR, WHERE GEZI WAS SEATED, MOSTLY

SHEARED AWAY. GEZI HAD BEEN ON HIS WAY TO MASVINGO

TOWN, WHERE HE WAS DUE TO PRESENT ZIM$1.6 MILLION

(US$16,000) TO LOCAL OFFICIALS FOR DEVELOPMENT PROJECTS

(NOTE: A MAYORAL ELECTION IS SCHEDULED IN MASVINGO FOR

MAY 12-13. END NOTE.) PRESIDENT MUGABE SAID A FULL

POLICE INVESTIGATION WOULD BE CARRIED OUT. THE APRIL 30

EDITION OF THE GOVERNMENT-CONTROLLED "THE HERALD" SAID

THE ZANU-PF POLITBURO WOULD MEET TODAY (APRIL 30) TO

DECIDE WHETHER GEZI WOULD BE GRANTED NATIONAL HERO

STATUS AND BURIED IN HEROES\' ACRE.

2. (C) THE SUDDEN DEATH OF GEZI CREATES A GAPING HOLE,

NOT ONLY IN CABINET, BUT ALSO IN THE RULING ZANU-PF

STRUCTURE. GEZI WAS A POLITBURO MEMBER AND ZANU-PF\'S

NATIONAL POLITICAL COMMISSAR, AND AS SUCH, WAS

RESPONSIBLE FOR ORGANIZING PARTY ELECTIONS AND HAD LED

THE RESTRUCTURING OF THE PARTY\'S PROVINCIAL EXECUTIVES.

THE 36-YEAR-OLD MINISTER WAS KNOWN AS ONE OF THE PARTY\'S

MOST ENERGETIC PROMOTERS, AND HAS BEEN CREDITED WITH

REJUVENATING THE PARTY AFTER ITS ELECTORAL SETBACKS LAST

YEAR. IN HIS PREVIOUS POST AS GOVERNOR OF MASHONALAND

CENTRAL PROVINCE, GEZI LED THE CAMPAIGN OF TERROR

AGAINST OPPOSITION SUPPORTERS IN HIS PROVINCE IN THE

RUN-UP TO THE JUNE 2000 PARLIAMENTARY ELECTIONS.

3. (C) GEZI\'S DEATH WILL ALSO RESULT IN A BY-ELECTION

FOR HIS BINDURA PARLIAMENTARY SEAT. THE CONSTITUENCY

NORTH OF HARARE IN MASHONALAND CENTRAL WAS ONE OF THE

MOST BRUTALIZED CONSTITUENCIES IN LAST YEAR\'S ELECTION,

AND OPPOSITION SUPPORTERS ARE STILL ROUTINELY HARASSED

AND BEATEN THERE. DESPITE THE VIOLENCE, GEZI WAS

ELECTED BY A MARGIN OF ONLY 2,000 VOTES, THE ONLY

MASHONALAND CENTRAL ELECTION WHERE THE MDC CAME EVEN

REMOTELY CLOSE TO WINNING. THE BY-ELECTION, WHENEVER IT

IS HELD, WILL UNDOUBTEDLY BE A VIOLENT AND BLOODY ONE.

4. (C) COMMENT: ALTHOUGH IT\'S NOT CLEAR WHO WILL FILL

GEZI\'S SHOES, MT. DARWIN SOUTH MP SAVIOR "TYSON"

KASUKUWERE APPEARS WELL-POSITIONED FOR THE JOB.

KASUKUWERE WAS APPOINTED TO THE 30-MEMBER POLITBURO IN

FEBRUARY THIS YEAR AND GIVEN THE PARTY\'S PORTFOLIO OF

DEPUTY SECRETARY FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS. THE YOUTHFUL

KASUKUWERE HAS A REPUTATION FOR ZEALOUSLY ATTACKING

PARTY OPPONENTS -- BOTH FIGURATIVELY AND LITERALLY --

AND IS JUST THE SORT OF PERSON MUGABE WOULD PROBABLY

LIKE TO HAVE AS YOUTH DEVELOPMENT MINISTER. GEZI IS

BELIEVED TO HAVE USED MINISTRY RESOURCES FOR

ESTABLISHING "TRAINING CENTERS" FOR ZANU-PF YOUTH AND
