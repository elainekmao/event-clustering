SUBJECT: AUSTRALIA GROUP SPRAYER NON-PAPER SHARED WITH GOT

REF: STATE 106333

(U) Classified by Acting Economic Counselor Robert Silverman

for reasons 1.5 B, D and E.

(C) Econoff delivered reftel non-paper on the U.S. proposal

to control certain aerosol sprayers in the Australia Group to

Tevfik Mengu, Director General for Agreements at the Foreign

Trade Undersecretariat, and to Mehmet Gucuk, MFA Deputy

Director General for Bilateral Economic Affairs. Neither had

immediate comment on the proposal, but both stated that they

would share the document with relevant experts in their

ministries and pass any comments to Embassy.
