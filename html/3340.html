CLASSIFIED BY: CHARGE D. BRENT HARDT FOR REASONS E.O.12958 1.5 (B), (D)

1. (C) IN THE ABSENCE OF ARCHBISHOP CLAUDIO MARIA CELLI,

THE VATICAN\'S MAIN CHINA HAND, POLOFFS ON 07/19 MET TO

DISCUSS REFEMAIL POINTS WITH VATICAN CHINA DESKOFF, MSGR.

GIANFRANCO ROTA-GRAZIOSI. ROTA-GRAZIOSI RECOMMENDED THREE

POSSIBLE APPROACHES FOR AMBASSADOR HANFORD TO PUT FORWARD TO

HELP UNBLOCK PRC/HOLY SEE DIALOGUE DURING HIS UPCOMING TRIP

TO BEIJING. AVERRING THAT NOT MUCH WILL HAPPEN BEFORE THE

CCP PARTY CONFERENCE IN OCTOBER, ROTA-GRAZIOSI SEEMED

RESIGNED TO THE LIKELIHOOD THAT YEARS WOULD PASS BEFORE

PRC/HOLY SEE DIALOGUE WOULD BEAR FRUIT. HE HELD LITTLE HOPE

THAT BEIJING COULD BE PERSUADED OFFICIALLY TO REOPEN DIALOGUE

WITH THE HOLY SEE, MUCH LESS TO CONCLUDE A SINO-VATICAN

AGREEMENT BEFORE PRC PRESIDENT JIANG VISITS THE U.S. IN THE

FALL.

2. (C) ROTA-GRAZIOSI WAS CAUTIOUSLY OPTIMISTIC, HOWEVER, ON

THREE LIMITED STEPS THAT THE DRL/IRF DELEGATION MIGHT URGE

THE PRC TO TAKE DURING THE DRL/IRF LATE JULY VISIT TO

BEIJING. FOR EXAMPLE THE PRC MIGHT HOST AN OFFICIAL

DELEGATION FROM THE HOLY SEE. HE SAID THAT THE HOLY SEE HAS

PROPOSED ON NUMEROUS OCCASIONS TO THE PRC THAT AN ENVOY BE

ALLOWED TO MAKE AN OFFICIAL VISIT TO CHINA TO TRAVEL TO

VARIOUS DIOCESES AND HOLD MEETINGS WITH CLERGY FROM BOTH THE

OFFICIAL CHURCH AND THE UNDERGROUND CHURCH. IN THE PAST,

CARDINAL ETCHEGARRAY HAS VISITED, BUT ONLY AS A "PRIVATE

PERSON." HE MUSED THAT AN OFFICIAL HOLY SEE VISITOR COULD BE

QITHER CARDINAL OR BISHOP RANK, AND MIGHT EVEN CELEBRATE A

MASS AT A CHINESE OFFICIAL CATHEDRAL "AS A MARK OF

SOLIDARITY." ROTA-GRAZIOSI BELIEVED A LESS HIGH-PROFILE MOVE

MIGHT BE PRC SUPPORT FOR WHAT THE POPE HAS CALLED A "CULTURAL

DIALOGUE BETWEEN THE HOLY SEE AND CHINA," ROTA-GRAZIOSI SAID

THE HOLY SEE WOULD BE HAPPY TO COLLABORATE WITH BEIJING ON

SOMETHING LIKE AN ART SHOW OF VATICAN MUSEUM PIECES, OR, MORE

AMBITIOUSLY, A SYMPOSIUM TO DISCUSS VATICAN/CHINESE HISTORY.

HE DID UNDERSCORE THAT IN THE PAST THE PRC HAD REFUSED TO

ACCEPT EVEN A SINGLE WORK OF ART FROM THE VATICAN MUSEUM AS

PART OF ANOTHER ART SHOW. FINALLY, HE NOTED THAT IT WOULD

CERTAINLY HELP FOR THE U.S. DELEGATION TO URGE THE PRC TO

REOPEN ITS DIALOGUE WITH THE HOLY SEE, WHICH COULD START EVEN

VIA INFORMAL INTERMEDIARIES SUCH AS THE U.S., ITALY, SPAIN,

GERMANY, OR OTHER INTERESTED PARTIES.

3. (C) COMMENT: ROTA-GRAZIOSI WAS SPEAKING FOR HIMSELF AND,

CHARACTERISTICALLY CAUTIOUS AS A VATICAN OFFICIAL, WAS

OBVIOUSLY NOT COMFORTABLE "BRAINSTORMING" FOR POLOFFS. IT

IS NOT CERTAIN THAT ARCHBISHOP CELLI WOULD ENDORSE ANY OR ALL

OF THESE IDEAS, SAVE THE LAST POINT. THE HOLY SEE HAS

CONSISTANTLY EXPRESSED ITS APPRECIATION TO US FOR THE WORK OF

DRL/IRF AND FOR THE PRESIDENT\'S PERSONAL INTERVENTIONS ON

THIS ISSUE IN BEIJING.
