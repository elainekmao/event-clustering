SUBJECT: COMMITMENTS RY FONMIN GRANIC

1. FOREIGN MINISTER GRANIC CALLED ME AT 7:00

P.M., MAY 15, TO REPORT THE FOLLOWING:

-- CROATIA WILL BE READY TO SIGN THE STATUS OF

FORCES AGREEMENT (SOFA) WITH THE UN IN ONE

HOUR. ONLY TWO WORDS OF DIFFERENCE REMAIN AND

THESE ARE NOT IMPORTANT.

-

-- COLONEL HARAMBASIC, THE SERB MILITARY LEADER

IN WESTERN SLAVONIA, HAS BEEN RELEASED FROM

DETENTION AND IS AT HIS HOME. THE GOC HAS HAD

USEFUL CONVERSATIONS WITH HIM.

-- TUDJMAN HAS GRANTED AMNESTY TO 46 SERBS WHO

HAD BEEN ARRESTED FOR WAR CRIMES.

-- TUDJMAN HAS ISSUED AN ORDER TO HAVE ALL

FORCES THAT ENTERED THE ZONE OF SEPARATION AFTER

MAY 1 TO WITHDRAW BY 5:00 P.M. LOCAL TIME, MAY

16.
