DESK OFFICERS IN TOWN, POLOFF WAS ABLE TO IDENTIFY A DESK

OFFICER ABLE TO ACCEPT REF A DEMARCHE ON UNGA PRIORITIES.

MONSIGNOR OSVALDO NEVES, STANDING IN SINCE AUGUST 26 FOR

OUTGOING VATICAN DESKOFF WITH RESPONSIBILITY FOR UN

AFFAIRS, RECEIVED REFTEL POINTS WITH LITTLE SUBSTANTIVE

COMMENT. HE INDICATED THAT HE WILL TAKE HIS LEAD FROM

HOLY SEE PERMANENT OBSERVER ARCHBISHOP MARTINO IN

PREPARING THE HOLY SEE\'S POSITIONS FOR THE UNGA. THE

LATTER IS CURRENTLY LEADING THE HOLY SEE DELEGATION AT

THE WSSD IN JOHANNESBURG.

2. NEVES HAD REVIEWED THE TALKING POINTS WE HAD FAXED,

AND COMMENTED THAT THE HOLY SEE WOULD BE PARTICULARLY

INTERESTED IN COLLABORATING ON OUR THIRD POINT REGARDING

A "NEW PARTNERSHIP" BETWEEN DEVELOPED AND DEVELOPING

COUNTRIES. (NOTE: OUR VIEWS TRACK VERY CLOSELY WITH THE

HOLY SEE\'S EMPHASIS ON "SOLIDARITY," WHICH IMPLIES

ASSISTANCE FOR GREATER CAPACITY BUILDING IN DEVELOPING

COUNTRIES, END NOTE.) THROUGHOUT THE UNGA, AS AT THE

CURRENT WSSD (REF B) THE HOLY SEE WILL FOLLOW ITS

LEITMOTIF THAT "PEOPLE COME FIRST" IN ASSESSING ITS

SUPPORT FOR U.S. POSITIONS.
