SUBJECT: SERBIAN DEMOCRATIC FORUM INAUGURATES ITS

BUREAU FOR REFUGEES AND HUMAN RIGHTS.

-

1. ON MARCH 19, THE SERBIAN DEMOCRATIC FORUM

(SDF) INTRODUCED TO THE PUBLIC ITS BUREAU FOR

REFUGEES, HUMAN RIGHTS AND RIGHTS OF NATIONAL

COMMUNITIES. THE CENTRAL BUREAU IS LOCATED IN

ZAGREB. BRANCH OFFICES WILL BE OPENED SOON IN

KARLOVAC, SISAK, RIJEKA AND PULA. LATER, AFTER

THE DEPLOYMENT OF THE UN PEACEKEEPING FORCES ON

THE TERRITORY UNDER JNA OCCUPATION, SDF INTENDS TO

OPEN BRANCH OFFICES IN THAT REGION, AS WELL AS

SPLIT, ZADAR AND OSIJEK.

2. APPROXIMATELY 40 PERSONS (INCLUDING CONSULATE

FSN POL. ANALYST) ATTENDED THE PUBLIC OPENING OF

THE BUREAU FOR REFUGEES. PRESIDENT OF SDF,

MILORAD PUPOVAC, WELCOMED THE GUESTS, ESPECIALLY

MS PATRICIA KOHL, REPRESENTATIVE OF THE

UNCLASSIFIED

UNCLASSIFIED

INTERNATIONAL RED CROSS. IN HIS SPEECH, PUPOVAC

EXPRESSED HIS REGRET THAT ALTHOUGH INVITATIONS

WERE SENT, REPRESENTATIVES OF THE GOVERNMENT

COMMITTEES FOR HUMAN RIGHTS DID NOT COME TO THE

RECEPTION.

3. IN HIS SPEECH, PUPOVAC POINTED OUT THE

CROATIAN GOVERNMENT IS DRAWING THE WRONG

CONCLUSION ABOUT THE ROLE OF THE BUREAU. HOWEVER,

PUPOVAC HOPES THAT THIS WILL BE CORRECTED AND THE

SDF BUREAU WILL BE ABLE TO COOPERATE WITH THE

GOVERNMENT\'S OFFICE FOR INTERNATIONAL RELATIONS.

THE GOAL OF THE SDF BUREAU IS NOT TO CREATE A

PARALLEL BODY TO THE GOVERNMENT OFFICE, BUT TO

COOPERATE AND TO HELP. THE OBJECTIVE OF THE

BUREAU IS TO COLLECT DATA ON REFUGEES, DISPLACED

PERSONS AND PRISONERS IN ORDER TO PRESENT THEM TO

LOCAL AND INTERNATIONAL ORGANS. THEREFORE IT IS

NECESSARY THAT REPRESENTATIVES OF MINORITIES,

CROATIAN GOVERNMENT, EC AND UN BE REPRESENTED IN

THE BUREAU.

-

4. THE SDF TOOK THIS OPPORTUNITY TO RELEASE THE

FIRST ISSUE OF ITS BULLETIN IN WHICH CASES OF

HUMAN RIGHTS VIOLATIONS ARE MADE PUBLIC. THE

BULLETIN INCLUDES A LIST OF NAMES OF MISSING

PERSONS. THESE LISTS DO NOT APPLY ONLY TO SERBS,

BUT TO ALL PEOPLE REGARDLESS OF THEIR NATIONALITY.

(COPY OF BULLETIN BEING POUCHED TO EUR/EEY AND

AMEMBASSY BEI4-$3).
