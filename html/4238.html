START MEMO NOMINATING-JUSTIFICATION:

As a professor at Kotelawala Defence Academy for more

than ten years she has had a string influence on the

world view of a generation of Sri Lankan military

officers. In her position, Ms. Panagoda teaches

international relations and law, including human

rights. These are both critical areas to the Sri

Lankan military\'s efforts to modernize itself and its

outlook. The human rights performance of the Sri

Lankan military has been of great concern to the United

States Embassy and will be critical to any hopes of

eventual peace. Though Ms. Panagoda visited Minnesota

on a two-week self-funded program on human rights in

2000, this has given her only a limited exposure. A

broad ranging IV on Foreign Policy and Human Rights

Issues would help push forward this mission\'s goal of

improving the human rights record of the Sri Lankan

government and giving it a broader outlook on the

world.

END MEMO NOMINATING-JUSTIFICATION:
