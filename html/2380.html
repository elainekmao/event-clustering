SUBJECT: SANT\'EGIDIO WANTS VOTE ON ITS ECOSOC CANDIDACY

1. (SBU) THIS IS AN ACTION MESSAGE. SANT\'EGIDIO

INTERNATIONAL RELATIONS SPECIALIST MARIO GIRO CALLED DCM

01/10 TO SEEK USG SUPPORT IN FORWARDING THE SANT\'EGIDIO

CANDIDACY TO BE AN OBSERVER NGO AT THE ECONOMIC AND SOCIAL

COMMITTEE OF THE UN (ECOSOC). GIRO EXPLAINED THAT

SANT\'EGIDIO BELIEVES IT HAS THE SUPPORT OF THE GREAT

MAJORITY OF COUNTRIES SITTING ON THE "NGO COMMISSION" OF THE

ECOSOC, INCLUDING OF THE UNITED STATES. GIRO AVERRED THAT

ALGERIA OBJECTS TO THE SANT\'EGIDIO CANDIDACY, AND HAS

SUCCESSFULLY USED PARLIAMENTARY MANEUVERS TO POSTPONE THE

VOTE. GIRO SOUGHT SUPPORT FROM THE US DEL FOR MOVING THE

SANT\'EGIDIO CANDIDACY TO A VOTE DURING THE UPCOMING SESSION

OF THE "NGO COMMISSION" STARTING 01/14/02. IF THEIR

CANDIDACY WERE TO BE BROUGHT TO A VOTE, GIRO WAS CONFIDENT

SANT\'EGIDIO WOULD PREVAIL.

2. (SBU) IN A PHONE CONVERSATION WITH MONS. DOMINIQUE

MAMBERTI, THE HOLY SEE DESK OFFICER WHO COVERS UN ISSUES,

POLOFF ASKED WHETHER THIS CANDIDACY IS ON THE HOLY SEE\'S

RADAR SCREEN. MAMBERTI WAS FAMILIAR WITH SANT\'EGIDIO\'S

PROBLEMS IN ALGERIA, BUT KNOW OF NO OBJECTIONS TO ITS ECOSOC

CANDIDACY IN THE HOLY SEE. AFTER ALL, HE SAID, "THEY ARE

NOT SEEKING A PLACE ON THE SECURITY COUNCIL!"

3. (SBU) COMMENT: SANT\'EGIDIO IS A RELIABLE INTERLOCUTOR OF

THIS EMBASSY, AND ONE WHICH WE HAVE COLLABORATED WITH IN

AREAS AS DIVERSE AS FUNDING ITS INTER-COMMUNAL EDUCATION

EFFORTS IN KOSOVO AND SUPPORTING ITS EFFORTS A MEDIATING

PEACE IN MOZAMBIQUE. WE ARE WORKING CLOSELY WITH

SANT\'EGIDIO TO SEE WHETHER THEY MIGHT CONTRIBUTE TO PEACE IN

SUDAN. WHICH IS NOT TO SAY THAT SANT\'EGIDIO HAS NEVER MADE

A FALSE STEP -- ALGERIA BEING A CASE IN POINT. IN THE MID-

1990S SANT\'EGIDIO GOT INTO HOT WATER IN ALGERIA BY

ORGANIZING A MEETING WITH ALGERIAN OPPOSITION WITHOUT

COORDINATING ITS "BOOTLEG DIPLOMACY" EITHER WITH THE GOA OR
