1. CONFIDENTIAL -- ENTIRE TEXT

-------

SUMMARY

-------

2. WHILE THE CROATIAN POLITICAL OPPOSITION HAS MOSTLY

FALLEN IN BEHIND THE GOVERNMENT\'S POSITION ON TERMINATING

THE UNPROFOR MANDATE HERE, SOME PARTIES, INCLUDING THE

SERBIAN PEOPLE\'S PARTY (SNS), THE ISTRIAN DEMOCRATIC PARTY

(IDS) AND THE CROATIAN INDEPENDENT DEMOCRATS (HND) HAVE

POINTED OUT THE DANGERS THAT THE GOC DECISION ON UNPROFOR

WILL MEAN FOR CROATIA. THESE THREE PARTIES HAVE NOT

BOUGHT INTO THE NATIONALISTIC RHETORIC THAT HAS BEEN

ECHOED BY THE OTHER OPPOSITION PARTIES AND HDZ, AND WHICH

HAS CLOUDED THE PROPOSALS FOR AUTONOMY FOUND IN THE Z-4

PLAN. HND OFFICIALS CONSIDER THE Z-4 PLAN TO BE A SOUND

BASIS FOR FURTHER TALKS. THE IDS CHAMPIONS REGIONAL

AUTONOMY, BUT IS CONCERNED THAT THE Z-4 PLAN WOULD GRANT

AUTONOMY TO SERBS WHILE OTHER ETHNIC GROUPS MUST LIVE WITH

CENTRALIZED AUTHORITY IN ZAGREB. END SUMMARY.

3. ON FEBRUARY 22 THE AMBASSADOR MET WITH STIPE MESIC,

PRESIDENT OF THE CROATIAN INDEPENDENT DEMOCRATS (HND) WHO

SAID THAT HE CONSIDERS THE Z-4 PLAN TO BE A SOUND BASIS

FOR FURTHER TALKS. BROAD AUTONOMY AND DEMILITARIZATION,

HE BELIEVES, ARE A NECESSARY CONFIDENCE BUILDING MEASURE

FOR SERBS TO AGREE TO REINTEGRATION. HE CITED EVIDENCE OF

CROATIAN WAR CRIMES -- THE PLANNED DESTRUCTION OF SERB

HOUSES AND THE INABILITY OF THE POLICE TO FIND THE

PERPETRATORS, AS WELL AS THE ALLEGED INVOLVEMENT OF

TUDJMAN\'S CHIEF BODYGUARD IN THE MURDER OF A SERB FAMILY

IN THE ZAGREB SUBURBS -- AS A MAJOR FACTOR IN SERB

DISTRUST OF THE GOC. HE SAID THAT THE GOC ATTEMPT TO

LIMIT THE RIGHT TO DUAL CITIZENSHIP TO CROATS ONLY IS

UNWORKABLE, THAT RIGHT MUST BE APPLIED TO SERBS, ITALIANS,

AND OTHER MINORITIES ON AN EQUAL BASIS.

4. MESIC THINKS TUDJMAN WILL CONSIDER A REVISED UNPROFOR

MANDATE (UNDER A DIFFERENT NAME) WHEN HE REALIZES THAT

MILOSEVIC IS PLAYING TRICKS. MESIC SAID THAT TUDJMAN\'S

VISION OF AN ETHNICALLY PURE GREATER CROATIA IS

FUNDAMENTALLY AT ODDS WITH MILOSEVIC\'S GREATER SERBIA.

MESIC THOUGHT THAT A NEW MANDATE WOULD BUILD TRUST BETWEEN

SERBS AND CROATS AND FACILITATE REINTEGRATION IF IT

PROVIDED A SECURE ENVIRONMENT FOR CROATIA\'S SERBS AND THE

RETURN OF DISPLACED PERSONS, AS WELL AS ESTABLISHED

CONTROL OF CROATIA\'S BORDERS. HE VIEWED TERMINATION OF

THE MANDATE AS A POINTLESS MOVE WHICH WOULD SERVE TO UNDO

ALL THE GAINS ACHIEVED UNDER THE ECONOMIC CONFIDENCE

BUILDING MEASURES AGREEMENT BETWEEN ZAGREB AND KNIN.

5. IN ONE OF THE FEW FAVORABLE PRESS ARTICLES, PUBLISHED

IN THE WEEKLY "GLOBUS," HND VICE PRESIDENT JOSIP MANOLIC

WROTE THAT Z-4 IS THE "MOST SERIOUS INTERNATIONAL DOCUMENT

OFFERING A SOLUTION TO THE CRISIS IN CROATIA." HE WAS

CRITICAL OF THE GOC FOR NOT PROPOSING THEIR OWN PLAN,

WHICH WAS EMPHASIZED BY MESIC WHEN HE TOLD THE AMBASSADOR

THAT THE GOC SHOULD HAVE HAD SOMETHING TO FILL THE VOID

WHEN THEY CANCELLED THE UNPROFOR MANDATE. MANOLIC

CRITICIZED THE VIEW THAT Z-4 WOULD CREATE A STATE WITHIN A

STATE, WRITING THAT, IN HIS OPINION, CONSTITUTIONAL LAW

GIVES THE SERBS THE RIGHT TO HAVE ADMINISTRATIVE AND COURT

AUTHORITY AS WELL AS PARLIAMENTARY INSTITUTIONS TO PASS

LEGISLATION ON ISSUES OF LOCAL AUTONOMY.

CROATIAN PEASANTS PARTY (HSS)

-----------------------------

9. THE HSS TYPIFIES THE STANCE ASSUMED BY MOST OF THE

REST OF THE OPPOSITION. NEW HSS PRESIDENT ZLATKO TOMCIC,

IN A CALL ON THE AMBASSADOR NOVEMBER 8, SAID THAT THE HSS

SUPPORTED THE GOC DECISION TO TERMINATE THE UNPROFOR

MANDATE, BUT THEY WOULD SUPPORT A REVISION OF THE MANDATE

(UNDER A DIFFERENT NAME) IF IT PROVIDED GUARANTEES TO

CONTROL CROATIA\'S BORDERS. TOMCIC SAID THAT IN GENERAL

THE HSS SUPPORTS THE Z-4 PLAN AS A BASIS FOR DISCUSSION

BUT HAD SERIOUS RESERVATIONS, PARTICULARLY ON THE ISSUE OF

TIES TO SERBIA. TOMCIC CLAIMED THE HSS WAS IN FAVOR OF

ADMINISTRATIVE AUTONOMY FOR THE SERBS, BUT WHEN ASKED BY

THE AMBASSADOR IF THIS MEANT THAT SERBS COULD PURCHASE,

FOR EXAMPLE, SCHOOL TEXTBOOKS FROM SERBIA, TOMCIC

ANSWERED, "ABSOLUTELY NOT." A FLUSTERED SILENCE ENSUED

COMMENT

-------

10. THE ATTITUDE OF THE HND AND IDS (AND SNS) -- THIRD,

AND, ROUGHLY, SIXTH LARGEST PARTIES IN THE SABOR -- IS A

SMALL HOPEFUL SIGN THAT ATTITUDES AMONG THE OPPOSITION\'S

CONSTITUENCIES HAVE NOT SOLIDIFIED AGAINST SERB AUTONOMY

AND THE Z-4. THERE ARE AT LEAST A FEW VOICES CHALLENGING

TUDJMAN\'S RATHER UNREALISTIC ASSESSMENT OF WHAT UNPROFOR\'S

DEPARTURE WILL MEAN FOR CROATIA.
