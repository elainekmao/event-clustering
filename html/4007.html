SUBJECT: GOI AGREES WITH USG ON NEED FOR SERBIAN ELECTION

REFORM

REF: STATE 202845

CLASSIFIED BY: POLITICAL MINISTER COUNSELOR TOM COUNTRYMAN

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 ROME 05093 180852Z

1. (C) ON OCTOBER 16, MFA BALKAN OFFICE DIRECTOR MAURIZIO

MASSARI TOLD POLOFF THAT THE GOI AGREED COMPLETELY WITH USG

THE USG VIEW EXPRESSED REFTEL THAT THE IC MUST PUT PRESSURE

ON SERB PM DJINDJIC TO RECONVENE PARLIAMENT IN ORDER TO AMEND

THE ELECTORAL LAW IN LINE WITH ODIHR RECOMMENDATIONS.

MASSARI HAD JUST RETURNED FORM A MEETING IN BRUSSELS ON

OCTOBER 15 DURING WHICH HE HAD WORKED WITH HIS EU MEMBER

STATE COUNTERPARTS TO CRAFT HIREP SOLANA\'S TALKING POINTS FOR

HIS CALL TO DJINDJIC REFERRED TO IN REFTEL.

2. (C) MASSARI SAID THAT DJINDJIC\'S POINTS WERE STRONG, BUT

PERHAPS NOT AS STRONG AS HE WOULD HAVE WANTED. NEGOTIATION

AT 15 IN THE EU TENDS TO MOVE STATEMENTS TOWARDS THE "LEAST

COMMON DENOMINATOR," CONTINUED MASSARI, BUT NONETHELESS, THE

POINTS WILL COMMUNICATE TO DJINDJIC THE EU\'S VIEW THAT SERB

ELECTION LAWS SHOULD FALL INTO LINE WITH OSCE/ODIHR

RECOMMENDATIONS ASAP.

3. (C) THE GOI PLANS TO DELIVER ITS OWN MESSAGE THROUGH ITS

EMBASSY IN BELGRADE TO DJINDJIC AND OTHER SERB LEADERS. THE

STATEMENT WAS STILL IN DRAFT FORM, SO MASSARI WAS NOT

SPECIFIC ON THE WORDING OR RECIPIENTS OTHER THAN DJINDJIC,

BUT THE DEMARCHE "WOULD BE STRONGER" THAN THE ONE DELIVERED

BY SOLANA. MASSARI PROMISED POLOFF A READOUT OF THE SERB

REACTION TO THE DEMARCHE AS SOON AS IT WAS AVAILABLE.
