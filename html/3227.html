SUBJECT: ITALY: REACTION TO THE PRESIDENT\'S JUNE 24 SPEECH

ON THE MIDDLE EAST

REF: SECSTATE 122320

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 ROME 03217 051609Z

CLASSIFIED BY: ACTING POL MC GARY ROBBINS FOR REASONS 1.5 (B) AND (D)

1. (U) SUMMARY: THE GOI INITIALLY EXPRESSED GENERAL SUPPORT

FOR THE PRESIDENT\'S JUNE 24 SPEECH ON THE MIDDLE EAST

TEMPERED WITH CONCERN OVER ARAFAT\'S ROLE. AFTER THE G8

SUMMIT, WHERE PM AND ACTING FM BERLUSCONI HAD A CHANCE TO

SPEAK WITH PRESIDENT BUSH AND WAS REPORTEDLY "OVERWHELMED" BY

THE EVIDENCE LINKING ARAFAT AND THE PA TO TERRORISM,

BERLUSCONI CHANGED HIS STAND ON ARAFAT. HE HAS NOW SUGGESTED

THAT ARAFAT STEP DOWN AS LEADER OF THE PA. END SUMMARY.

2. (U) MFA SOURCES EXPRESSED SATISFACTION WITH THE

PRESIDENT\'S JUNE 24 SPEECH ON THE MIDDLE EAST, SAYING IT

REFLECTED US WILLINGNESS TO COMMIT TO REACTIVATING THE PEACE

PROCESS IN THE REGION. MFA SOURCES ALSO NOTED THE POINTS OF

CONVERGENCE WITH THE EU\'S POSITION AT THE EU COUNCIL IN

SEVILLE JUNE 21-22: TWO STATES, STRUGGLE AGAINST TERRORISM,

AND END OF OCCUPATION.

3. (U) MFA SOURCES AGREED THAT IT WAS IMPORTANT AND URGENT TO

HOLD ELECTIONS IN THE PALESTINIAN TERRITORIES. THEY STRESSED

HOWEVER THAT ONLY THE PALESTINIANS SHOULD BE ALLOWED TO

CHOOSE THEIR LEADERSHIP. SPEAKING PUBLICLY ON JUNE 25,

ALFREDO MANTICA, THE MFA\'S UNDER SECRETARY FOR AFRICA AND THE

MIDDLE EAST, SAID "UNDOUBTEDLY, FOR THE AMERICANS TO CHOOSE

ARAFAT AS A CONDITION FOR NON-PEACE IS A RISK. THIS WILL

CREATE DIFFICULTIES FOR BUSH WITH MANY EUROPEAN GOVERNMENTS

WHO HAVE A DIFFERENT OPINION. EVEN WE ITALIANS HAVE

REITERATED THAT ARAFAT IS THE LEGITIMATE REPRESENTATIVE OF

THE PALESTINIAN POPULATION AND THAT ONLY THE PALESTINIANS CAN

CHOSE THEIR REPRESENTATIVE".

4. (U) BERLUSCONI ECHOED MANTICA\'S VIEWS RIGHT BEFORE THE

BEGINNING OF THE G8 SUMMIT IN KANANASKIS. SPEAKING TO

JOURNALISTS JUNE 26, HE SAID: "ARAFAT CERTAINLY IS AND WILL

CONTINUE TO BE THE ELECTED LEADER OF THE PALESTINIANS." IN

RESPONSE TO A SPECIFIC QUESTION HOWEVER, BERLUSCONI SAID HE

WOULD BE ABLE TO SAY WHETHER ARAFAT IS AN OBSTACLE THAT MUST

BE REMOVED ONLY AFTER SPEAKING WITH PRESIDENT BUSH. ONE DAY

LATER, AFTER THE DINNER WITH PRESIDENT BUSH AND BEING

REPORTEDLY OVERWHELMED BY THE INFORMATION LINKING ARAFAT AND

THE PA TO TERRORISM, BERLUSCONI INVITED ARAFAT TO STEP ASIDE:

"IF I WERE ARAFAT, I WOULD MAKE THE GREAT GESTURE THAT WOULD

CONSECRATE HIM TO HISTORY AS A MAN WHO DID EVERYTHING FOR HIS

COUNTRY." BERLUSCONI WAS REPORTEDLY THE ONLY EUROPEAN LEADER

TO STATE THAT ARAFAT SHOULD STEP DOWN FOR THE GOOD OF THE

PALESTINIANS.

5. (C) COMMENT: WHY SUCH A METAMORPHOSIS IN BERLUSCONI\'S

POSITION? IT IS POSSIBLE THAT HE WAS INDEED PERSUADED BY

EVIDENCE LINKING ARAFAT TO TERRORISM. IT IS MORE LIKELY,

HOWEVER, THAT BERLUSCONI SAW IN THIS AN OPPORTUNITY TO BE

SUPPORTIVE OF THE US WHEN SOME OTHER EU LEADERS, AND SOME KEY

OFFICIALS WITHIN HIS OWN MFA, HAVE RESERVATIONS ABOUT THE US

POSITION ON ARAFAT. BERLUSCONI, WHO IS STILL ACTING FM,

CONTINUES TO SEEK OPPORTUNITIES TO DEMONSTRATE THAT HE IS ONE

OF OUR GREATEST SUPPORTERS IN THE EU. END COMMENT.
