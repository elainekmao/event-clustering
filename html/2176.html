1. (C) AMBASSADOR NICHOLSON ATTENDED DINNER HOSTED BY

AMBASSADOR OF CANADA, WILFRED GUY LICARI, IHO OF CANADIAN

ASSISTANT DEPUTY MINISTER PAUL DUBOIS ON NOVEMBER 5.

DUBOIS TOLD THE AMBASSADOR THAT IT WAS VERY UNFORTUNATE

THAT WHEN PRESIDENT BUSH SPOKE TO CONGRESS IN THE

AFTERMATH OF THE SEPTEMBER 11 ATTACK, HE FAILED TO

MENTION CANADA AMONG THOSE NATIONS PROVIDING ASSISTANCE

TO THE UNITED STATES.

2. (C) COMMENT: DUBOIS STRESSED THAT CANADA IS FULLY

BEHIND U.S. EFFORTS TO DESTROY TERRORISM, BUT DID WANT TO

MAKE THE POINT THAT FAILING TO MENTION CANADA AS AN

IMPORTANT PARTNER WAS AN OVERSIGHT WHICH WAS VIEWED AS A

MAJOR SLIGHT. NICHOLSON
