SUBJECT: DEALING WITH ROBERT MUGABE, THE CONSUMMATE

SUMMARY

-------

1. (C) OVER THE NEXT YEAR, PRESIDENT MUGABE WILL FACE

HIS GREATEST CHALLENGE IN 20 YEARS TO STAYING IN POWER.

A PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION, IN WHICH MUGABE INTENDS TO RUN,

IS SLATED FOR EARLY 2002. HE MUST CONTEND WITH A

SIZABLE AND RELATIVELY UNIFIED POLITICAL OPPOSITION, A

COSTLY AND UNPOPULAR INTERVENTION IN THE CONGO,

MALCONTENTS IN HIS RULING PARTY, AND HIS OWN PLUMMETING

POPULARITY ACROSS ZIMBABWE. MUGABE IS INTRANSIGENT,

HOWEVER, AND WILL USE ALMOST ANY MEANS NECESSARY,

INCLUDING WIDESPREAD VIOLENCE, TO ENSURE HIS CONTINUED

GRIP ON THE COUNTRY. THE PRESIDENT APPEARS WILLING TO

SACRIFICE THE ECONOMY IN ORDER TO PURSUE HIS LAND

RESETTLEMENT PROGRAM -- THE ONLY CREDIBLE PLATFORM THE

RULING PARTY HAS LEFT -- AND PUNISH HIS ENEMIES,

PARTICULARLY WHITE FARMERS. THE PRESIDENT\'S HENCHMEN

HAVE ATTACKED AND ATTEMPTED TO UNDERMINE EVERY SEGMENT

OF SOCIETY, INCLUDING THE INDEPENDENT JUDICIARY AND THE

MEDIA, THAT APPEARS OPPOSED TO HIS POLICIES. THE

PRESIDENT DOES NOT REACT WELL TO INTERNATIONAL

CRITICISM, SO ANY ATTEMPT TO INFLUENCE OR MODERATE HIS

THINKING SHOULD BE PUT IN TERMS OF BENEFITS TO HIS

CONTINUED RULE AND SHOULD INVOLVE THE REGIONAL LEADERS

TO WHOM HE STILL LISTENS. U.S. INTERESTS LIE IN

BOLSTERING THE DEMOCRATIC INSTITUTIONS THAT ARE UNDER

SIEGE AND PREVENTING BOTH A SOCIAL EXPLOSION AND

ECONOMIC IMPLOSION THAT WOULD HAVE SERIOUS REGIONAL

IMPLICATIONS. ZIMBABWE, WITH ITS DEMOCRATIC

INSTITUTIONS, RESILIENT MARKET ECONOMY AND INDUSTRIAL

BASE, RESPECTED JUDICIARY, AND THE BEST-EDUCATED WORK

FORCE IN AFRICA, MATTERS AND MERITS OUR TIMELY AND

CREATIVE INTERVENTION IN ORDER TO PRESERVE THESE. END

SUMMARY.

----------------

A PITCHED BATTLE

----------------

2. (C) 2001 WILL BE A CRITICAL YEAR FOR ZIMBABWE AS THE

COUNTRY\'S TWO POLITICAL PARTIES SQUARE OFF TO WAGE WHAT

PROMISES TO BE A PITCHED BATTLE TO SECURE THE PRESIDENCY

IN 2002. ALTHOUGH NOT FORMALLY CONFIRMED, ROBERT MUGABE

ALMOST CERTAINLY WILL BE THE CANDIDATE FOR THE RULING

ZANU-PF AND MORGAN TSVANGIRAI WILL HEAD UP THE

OPPOSITION MOVEMENT FOR DEMOCRATIC CHANGE (MDC) TICKET.

AT THIS WRITING THERE IS ONE TRUISM IN ZIMBABWEAN

POLITICS: ZANU-PF CANNOT WIN ON A LEVEL PLAYING FIELD.

A HELEN SUZMAN FOUNDATION POLL RELEASED IN OCTOBER 2000

SHOWED SUPPORT FOR MUGABE AT ITS LOWEST POST-

INDEPENDENCE EBB, PROVIDING A NATIONAL SUPPORT RATING OF

ONLY 13 PERCENT, WITH A 6 PERCENT URBAN AND 17 PERCENT

RURAL SPLIT.

PROTRACTED LIBERATION STRUGGLE DURING THE 1960S AND

1970S, A CIVIL WAR IN THE EARLY 1980S AND TWO MAJOR

ENSURED UNSWERVING SUPPORT OF HIS POLITICAL PROGRAM AND

IF THOSE IN CABINET FELT ANY REMORSE, WE SAW NO EVIDENCE

OF IT. THIS INCREASINGLY FREQUENT SUSPENSION OF THE

MUGABE WILL NOT BE PUSHED OFF THE SCENE

---------------------------------------

10. (C) MUGABE WILL NOT GO GENTLY INTO THAT GOOD NIGHT

OF RETIREMENT. WE SERIOUSLY DOUBT THAT IT IS POSSIBLE TO

CONVINCE HIM TO DO SO. EVEN IF WE WERE ABLE TO SCRAPE

TOGETHER THE CONSIDERABLE AMOUNT OF MONEY TO FINANCE A

GENEROUS "GOLDEN PARACHUTE" FOR HIM TO LEAVE THE SCENE,

WE DO NOT THINK MUGABE WOULD FULFILL THE TERMS OF THE

BARGAIN. NOR, IN OUR OPINION, IN HIS TIME LEFT ON STAGE

WILL HE LISTEN TO OR HEED WELL-INTENTIONED ADVICE COMING

FROM THOSE HE DOES NOT CONSIDER ABSOLUTE BACKERS. FOR

THOSE WITH LESS THAN WHOLEHEARTED SUPPORT, THE BEST WAY

TO MAKE MUGABE LISTEN IS TO APPEAL TO HIS DESIRE TO STAY

IN POWER. HE WILL AT LEAST CONSIDER ANY PROPOSAL THAT

IS COUCHED IN TERMS OF PROLONGING HIS POLITICAL

SURVIVAL. TRUE TO THEIR SELF-INTEREST, THE DEFENSE

FORCES WILL CONTINUE THEIR UNWAVERING AND OBSEQUIOUS
