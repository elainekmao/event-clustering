SUBJECT: FACILITATING THE WORK OF IRAQ INSPECTION TEAMS IN

JORDAN

REF: CPA BAGHDAD 041713Z JUN 03

1. Embassy Amman welcomes the visit of an Iraqi MFA

inspection team to Amman and will be happy to facilitate the

work of the team, in coordination with the UK Embassy in

Amman. Post will name a specific liaison officer for the

Iraqi MFA team when we have specific travel dates to Amman,

given the demands on the Embassy to cover upcoming Jordanian

Parliamentary elections (June 17) and the World Economic

Forum (Davos) Extraordinary Conference at the Dead Sea (June

21-13). In the meantime, CPA should communicate with

PolCouns Doug Silliman (e-mails - sillimanda@state.gov

(unclassified) or sillimandsms@state.sgov.gov (classified)).

GNEHM
