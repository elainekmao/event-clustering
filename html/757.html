"ARSK" ATTACK ON BIHAC LIMITED

------------------------------

2. ON JULY 25, POLOFFS MET WITH UNPF CHIEF OF STAFF

GENERAL KOLSTEREN TO DISCUSS THE "ARSK" OFFENSIVE IN BIHAC

AND THE POTENTIAL FOR GOC REACTION. KOLSTEREN TOLD US

THAT THE UN\'S ANALYSIS OF THE SITUATION IN BIHAC LEADS TO

THE CONCLUSION THAT THE OBJECTIVE OF THE "ARSK" OPERATION

IS ONLY TO RETAKE AREAS OF THE POCKET HELD BY ABDIC PRIOR

TO HIS EXPULSION BY THE GOBH V CORPS LAST YEAR. ATTACKS

BY THE BSA ARE BELIEVED TO BE ONLY DIVERSIONARY. "ARSK-"

ABDIC ACTION WOULD CREATE A RUFFER ZONE TO PROTECT SERB

VILLAGES FROM ATTACKS BY GOBH FORCES, WHICH HE RLAMED FOR

PRECIPITATING THE ATTACKS. HE SAID THAT GORH MILITARY

COMMAND LOCATED V CORPS HQ IN DOWNTOWN BIHAC IN A

DISINGENUOUS EFFORT TO DRAW NATO AIRSTRIKES ON ATTACKING

SERBS.

3. MEANWHILE, UN SENIOR POLITICAL ADVISOR ALMSTROM TOLD

US JULY 25 THAT THE LEVEL OF FIGHTING IN BIHAC SEEMED TO

HAVE DECREASED SOMEWHAT. CONTRARY TO KOLSTEREN, ALMSTROM

SAID THAT UN ANALYSTS ARE QUESTIONING "WHETHER THE BOSNIAN

SERBS" ARE INVOLVED OR NOT; ALMSTROM ECHOED KOLSTEREN\'S

COMMENTS THAT THE CURRENT CONFRONTATION LINE LOOKS VERY

MUCH LIKE THE AREA THAT ABDIC FORCES CONTROLLED ABOUT A

YEAR AGO AND SPECULATED THAT THE ARSK MIGHT BE HELPING

ABDIC TO REGAIN LOST GROUND IN RETURN FOR HIS COOPERATION

NOW AND IN THE PAST. KOLSTEREN AND ALMSTROM BOTH DOUBTED

THAT THE ARSK WOULD PRESS ITS ATTACK BECAUSE THEY MUST

RECOGNIZE THE CROATIAN THREAT. (ALMSTROM ADDED THAT ALL

FORCES IN THE AREA HAD SCRUPULOUSLY AVOIDED ACTIVITY IN

THE SAFE ZONE, I.E., BIHAC TOWN ITSELF).

--------------------------------

GOC WILL RESPOND BEFORE NOVEMBER

--------------------------------

4. IN KOLSTEREN\'S OPINION, THE GOC COULD USE ATTACKS ON

BIHAC AS AN EXCUSE TO RETAKE THE "RSK" BUT HAD NO

INDICATIONS THAT ANY ASSAULT WAS IMMINENT. HE SAID THAT

THE HV COULD BEGIN AN ATTACK INTO SECTOR SOUTH FROM THE

FREE CROATIA AND BOSNIA IN A FEW DAYS OR WEEKS, BUT WOULD

DEFINITELY MOVE BEFORE THE END OF THE UNCRO MANDATE IN

NOVEMBER AND THE ONSET OF WINTER. HE SAID THAT THE LONGER

THE GOC WAITS TO ATTACK, THE LONGER "ARSK" MILITARY

COMMANDER GENERAL MRKSIC HAS TO STRENGTHEN HIS FORCES. IF

THE GOC DECIDES TO ATTACK, HE TOLD US THAT HE THINKS

MILOSEVIC WILL DO NOTHING TO IN DEFENSE OF THE "RSK"

SERBS AND BOSNIAKS GUILTY OF WAR CRIMES

---------------------------------------

7. KOLSTEREN STRESSED THAT THE UN MUST REMAIN IMPARTIAL.

IN THE CASE OF THE BSA ATTACKS ON SREBRENICA, HE SAID THAT

DUTCH SOLDIERS COULD ONLY CONFIRM THE FEW CASES OF

ATROCITIES THAT THEY WITNESSED. HE CHARGED THAT VIOLENCE

BETWEEN RIVAL MUSLIM GROUPS (THOSE THAT WANTED TO

SURRENDER AND THOSE THAT DIDN\'T) WAS ALSO RESPONSIBLE FOR

SITUATION IN ZEPA

-----------------

8. ALMSTROM DENIED BROADCAST NEWS REPORTS THAT ZEPA HAD

FINALLY "FALLEN" TO THE SERBS. HE SAID THE SITUATION

THERE REMAINED "CONFUSING." HE ADMITTED THE UKRAINIANS

WERE HUNKERED DOWN, TAKING MORTAR FIRE FROM BOTH (I.E.

MUSLIM AND SERB) SIDES AND UNABLE TO DO MUCH OBSERVING.

B-H FORCES WERE PUTTING UP A STIFF RESISTANCE; MLADIC WAS

CURIOUSLY RELUCTANT TO MOVE IN AND FINISH THE JOB, BUT HE

MUST ALSO BE CHAFING, HAVING CLAIMED ALMOST A WEEK AGO

THAT HIS ARMY HAD TAKEN CONTROL OF ZEPA. ALMSTROM

REPORTED VARIOUS "RUMORS" THAT THERE WAS SOME AGREEMENT

UNDER WHICH UNPROFOR WOULD COLLECT WEAPONS IN ZEPA IN

RETURN FOR WHICH THE CIVILIANS COULD BE RELEASED BUT

AKASHI WAS OF THE FIRM VIEW THAT VULNERABLE CIVILIANS;

MUST BE ALLOWED TO LEAVE, PERIOD, WITHOUT ANY

PRECONDITIONS.
