INTRODUCTION AND SUMMARY

------------------------

1. (U) THIS IS ONE IN A SERIES OF REPORTS OF

SPECIFIC AND GROSS HUMAN RIGHTS ABUSES IN BOSNIA AND

HERCEGOVINA (B-H) BASED ON PERSONAL INTERVIEWS WITH

THE SUBJECTS NAMED. THE SUBJECTS IN THIS REPORT

AGREED TO SPEAK ONLY AFTER RECEIVING ASSURANCES THAT

THEIR NAMES WOULD NOT/NOT BE USED PUBLICLY FOR FEAR

THAT THAT WOULD ENDANGER THE LIVES OF FAMILY MEMBERS

STILL IN SERBIAN-CONTROLLED AREAS IN B-H.

2. (LOU) NURIJA BESIC IS A 27-YEAR-OLD BUTCHER FROM

KOZARAC. JUSUF ARIFAGIC IS A 31-YEAR-OLD MECHANIC

FROM KOZARAC. HUSEIN CUSKIC IS A 35-YEAR-OLD BUILDER

FROM TRNOPOLJE. SUAD BAJRIC IS A 40-YEAR-OLD TEXTILE

WORKER FROM KAMICANI. HASIB ELKAZ IS A 52-YEAR-OLD

BLUE-COLLAR WORKER FROM KOZARAC.

3. (U) EACH OF THESE SUBJECTS IS IDENTIFIED BELOW BY

HIS INITIALS. ALL FIVE ARE BOSNIAN MUSLIMS

TEMPORARILY IN THE KARLOVAC TRANSIT FACILITY FOR

EX-DETAINEES. THEY WERE INTERNED IN KERATERM CAMP

FOR ROUGHLY TWO MONTHS, FROM MID-JUNE TO EARLY AUGUST.

THEY IDENTIFIED VARIOUS SENIOR OFFICERS AND GUARDS AT

THE CAMP. ALL FIVE SUBJECTS CLAIM TO HAVE WITNESSED

SEVERE BEATINGS, TORTURE, AND KILLINGS. TWO OF THESE

SUBJECTS PROVIDED CORROBORATING TESTIMONY REGARDING

THE MASS KILLING AT KERATERM ON JULY 24 (REFTEL).

ONE SUBJECT PROVIDED NOTEWORTHY SECOND-HAND TESTIMONY

FROM A CLOSE RELATIVE WHO HAD WITNESSED ELABORATE

STEPS TAKEN TO DISGUISE THE CONTENTS OF A MASS GRAVE

IN THE VILLAGE OF TOMARSICA NEAR OMARSKA (PARA 16,

BELOW). THE SUBJECTS WERE INTERVIEWED SEPARATELY ON

OCTOBER 9, 10, AND 13 IN KARLOVAC AND EACH WAS JUDGED

CREDIBLE. END INTRODUCTION AND SUMMARY.

5. (U) J.A. AND H.E. IDENTIFIED THREE PERSONS WHO

WERE SENIOR OFFICERS AT KERATERM. ALTHOUGH THEY

DISAGREED ON THE PRECISE POSITIONS OF THESE THREE

OFFICERS, J.A. AND H.E. IDENTIFIED THE SAME THREE

INDIVIDUALS. J.A. IDENTIFIED DRASKO DOSEN (NICKNAMED

KAJIN) AS THE COMMANDER OF KERATERM AT LEAST FROM

DOSEN WAS WANTED BY THE CROATS FOR CRIMES HE HAD

6. (U) J.A. IDENTIFIED KOMJENOVIC (FNU - POSSIBLY

YEARS OLD AND USED TO BE THE HEAD OF THE CELPAK PAPER

FACTORY IN PRIJEDOR. H.E. SAID SIKIRIC OFTEN WATCHED

THE GUARDS BEAT THE PRISONERS.

MASS MURDER JULY 24

-------------------

14. (U) N.B. PROVIDED SEPARATE TESTIMONY REGARDING

THE JULY 24 MASS MURDER OF OVER ONE HUNDRED PRISONERS

(REFTEL). PRISONERS WERE KEPT IN FOUR SEPARATE ROOMS

AT KERATERM. N.B. WAS IN ROOM TWO. ROOM THREE IS

WHERE PRISONERS WERE MOST SEVERELY TORTURED AND WHERE

THE MASSACRE OCCURRED. THE DETAILS OF N.B.\'S STORY

AGREE WITH THE ACCOUNTS GIVEN IN REFTEL. FROM A

WINDOW IN ROOM TWO, N.B. SAW THE GUARD SHIFT CHANGE

ON SCHEDULE AT SIX PM. BUT THEN IT CHANGED AGAIN TWO

HOURS LATER. MORE GUARDS WERE STATIONED IN FRONT OF

THE ROOMS. N.B. SAID THE GUARDS HAD AUTOMATIC RIFLES.

WHEN THE DISTURBANCES STARTED BECAUSE OF THE

INSUFFERABLE CONDITIONS IN THE ROOM, THE GUARDS FIRED

A FEW SHOTS. N.B. HEARD A VOICE SHOUT, "DON\'T SHOOT

WITHOUT AN ORDER FROM KOLA," (KOMJENOVIC). WHEN THE

RIOTING GREW WORSE. N.B. HEARD KOMJENOVIC SAY,

"FIRE|" N.B. HEARD RAPID GUNFIRE AND SCREAMS.

15. (U) THE FOLLOWING DAY GUARDS CHOSE TWO PRISONERS

FROM ROOMS ONE AND TWO EACH TO REMOVE THE DEAD.

THESE PRISONERS COUNTED 99 DEAD AND 42 WOUNDED, N.B.

REPORTED. THEY WERE ORDERED TO PUT THE WOUNDED ON

THE SAME TRUCK AS THE DEAD. THE TRUCK WAS LABELED

PRIJEDOR AUTOTRANSPORT. NONE OF THE WOUNDED OR THE

DRIVER OF THE TRUCK (ANOTHER PRISONER) WAS SEEN AGAIN.
