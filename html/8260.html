SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION: ROADMAP IRAQ

1. SUMMARY: THE ISRAELI CABINET\'S ACCEPTANCE OF THE ROADMAP,

THE RECONSTRUCTION OF IRAQ, AND THE LIFTING OF UN SANCTIONS ON

IRAQ DOMINATED COVERAGE IN UAE PRINT AND ELECTRONIC MEDIA. AN

"AL ITTIHAD" EDITORIAL CLAIMED THAT BOTH \'AL QAEDA\' AND ISRAEL

ARE SIMILAR IN THEIR AIMS AND GOALS. AN "AL KHALEEJ" EDITORIAL

ALLEGED THAT THE U.S. IS NOT SERIOUS ABOUT IMPLEMENTING THE

ROADMAP. ANOTHER "AL KHALEEJ" EDITORIAL ASSERTED THAT ANTI-

AMERICAN ATTITUDES ARE ON THE RISE IN IRAQ. END SUMMARY.

2. A/S LINCOLN BLOOMFIELD\'S VISIT TO THE UAE RECEIVED PROMINENT

COVERAGE IN LOCAL PRINT AND ELECTRONIC MEDIA. SPECIAL COVERAGE

WAS GIVEN TO HIS MEETING WITH SHEIKH MOHAMED BIN RASHED AL

MAKTOUM, DUBAI\'S CROWN PRINCE AND UAE MINISTER OF DEFENSE. THE

PUBLIC AFFAIRS OFFICE IN ABU DHABI FACILITATED A EXCLUSIVE

TELEVISION INTERVIEW WITH \'AL ARABIYYA\' SATELLITE TV CHANNEL THAT

WAS AIRED IN ITS ENTIRETY ON 5/26 AT 21:30 (17:30 GMT). DURING

THE INTERVIEW, A/S BLOOMFIELD COMMENTED ON MANY ISSUES, INCLUDING

THE WAR ON TERRORISM, REGIONAL COOPERATION AND STABILITY, U.S.

EFFORTS TO RESTORE STABILITY IN IRAQ, AND THE IMPLEMENTATION OF

THE ROADMAP. PAO ALSO FACILITATED AN EXCLUSIVE PRINT INTERVIEW

WITH DUBAI-BASED BUSINESS-ORIENTED ARABIC DAILY "AL-BAYAN" THAT

LARGELY FOCUSED ON IRAQ. THIS INTERVIEW WILL BE PRINTED LATER IN

THE WEEK.

3. ON 5/26, EXPATRIATE-ORIENTED ENGLISH DAILY "GULF NEWS"

REPRINTED A LOS ANGELES TIMES-WASHINGTON POST NEWS SERVICE

ARTICLE BY SULAIMAN AL HATTLAN ENTITLED, "FOCUS ON TODAY\'S

REALITIES." COMMENT: THE REPRINT IS UNIQUE IN THAT THE ARTICLE

CONTAINS POINTS VERY CRITICAL OF ARAB AND RELIGIOUS LEADERS SUCH

AS:

--"IT IS TIME FOR ARAB INTELLECTUALS TO SAY PUBLICLY WHAT THEY

HAVE LONG BEEN ACKNOWLEDGING PRIVATELY: MOST ARAB LEADERS HAVE

FAILED THEIR PEOPLE."

--"THE FUTURE, IN THE ARAB WORLD, HAS ALWAYS BEEN GOD\'S BUSINESS.

BUT THIS HASN\'T TAKEN US ANYWHERE. LOOK AT OUR SOCIAL FAILURES:

SEVERE GENDER SEGREGATION AND THE LACK OF RESPECT FOR WOMEN HAVE

CREATED PARALYSED SOCIETIES. THE PRACTICE OF ART AND MUSIC HAS

BECOME A SIN IN MANY ARAB COUNTRIES. THE EDUCATIONAL SYSTEM HAS

BECOME A SOURCE OF IGNORANCE RATHER THAN KNOWLEDGE. AS A RESULT,

YOUNG ARABS TODAY LIVE UNDER A SIEGE OF POLITICAL, SOCIAL, AND

RELIGIOUS TABOOS."

--"WE MUST LET GO OF THIS DISTORTED VERSION OF HISTORY, WHICH

GLORIFIES ONLY WHAT OUR ANCESTORS CONTRIBUTED TO THE ANCIENT

WORLD\'S CIVILIZATIONS AND BLAMES OUR CURRENT PROBLEMS ON THE

INFLUENCE OF OUTSIDERS. INSTEAD, WE HAVE TO FOCUS ON TODAY\'S

REALITY OF ECONOMIC CHALLENGES, POVERTY, LACK OF FREEDOM, AND,

INDEED, LACK OF HOPE."

--"UNLESS WE BEGIN AN AUTHENTIC REVISIONIST DIALOGUE ABOUT WHAT

REALLY WENT WRONG IN OUR POLITICAL AND SOCIAL EXPERIENCE DURING

THE PAST CENTURY OR SO, THE OTHER SADDAMS IN OUR SOCIETIES -

CORRUPT POLITICAL AND EDUCATIONAL SYSTEMS - WILL CONTINUE TO

PRODUCE DISASTROUS RESULTS." END COMMENT.

4 CLAIMING THAT SIMILARITIES EXIST BETWEEN \'AL QAEDA\' AND

ISRAEL, ABU DHABI-BASED SEMI-GOVERNMENT "AL ITTIHAD"

EDITORIALIZED 5/26:

"... THE RESULT THAT BOTH \'AL QAEDA\' AND ISRAEL ARE SEEKING FROM

THESE CONTINUOUS ATTACKS AGAINST HUMANITY IS TO DISMANTLE THE

NETWORK OF HEAVENLY COMMUNITY VALUES AND TO KEEP THE MIDDLE EAST

ECONOMICALLY, RELIGIOUSLY, AND POLITICALLY BOILING IN ORDER TO

FREEZE PEACE ..."

5 ASSERTING THAT ANTI-AMERICAN ATTITUDES ARE ON THE RISE IN

IRAQ, SHARJAH-BASED PAN-ARAB DAILY "AL KHALEEJ" EDITORIALIZED

5/26:

"... THE VOICES OF OPPOSITION ARE NOTING THAT THEY HAVE BEEN

DECEIVED AND THAT ALL THEY AGREED UPON WITH THE AMERICANS DIFFERS

FROM REALITY, THEY HAVE DISCOVERED THAT WINTER AND SUMMER DO NOT

MEET ... THIS IS WHAT THE IRAQI PEOPLE FEEL, AND THE POLITICAL

POWERS ARE STARING TO FEEL, AND WHEN BOTH THE IRAQI PEOPLE AND

SINCERE POWERS UNITE UNDER ONE NATIONAL PRINCIPLE, THEN LIBERTY

WILL RETURN, AND OCCUPATION WILL BE GONE."

6 ALLEGING THAT THE U.S. IS NOT SERIOUS ABOUT IMPLEMENTING THE

ROADMAP, "AL KHALEEJ" EDITORIALIZED 5/25:

"... IF AMERICAN GUARANTEES FOR ISRAEL INCLUDE GUARANTEEING ITS

SECURITY, SAFETY, AND SUPERIORITY, THEN THE GUARANTEES GIVEN TO

THE PALESTINIANS ARE NOT UNDERSTANDABLE. IF ISRAEL IS GOING TO

GET EVERYTHING IT WANTS, SOME OF WHICH INCLUDES CORE RIGHTS OF

THE PALESTINIANS, THEN WHAT WILL BE LEFT FOR THE PALESTINIANS?

... THE PALESTINIAN PEOPLE, AS WELL AS THE ARABS, ARE FACING A

CRITICAL AND DANGEROUS PHASE DUE TO THE CRUSHING AMERICAN POWER

ALLIED WITH EXPANDING, RACIST, ZIONIST ATTACKS. BUT RESISTANCE,

HOLDING ONTO THEIR RIGHTS, AND POSSESSING THE WILL TO FIGHT WILL

PREVENT (THE PALESTINIANS FROM) FALLING INTO THE TRAP SET TO

CAPTURE THE PALESTINIANS AND THEIR RIGHTS."

7 COMMENTING ON THE UN RESOLUTION TO LIFT SANCTIONS ON IRAQ,

"AL KHALEEJ" EDITORIALIZED 5/23:

"... BUSH LAUGHS SECRETLY AT EVERYONE SINCE HE IS THE ONE WHO

DEVOURED IRAQ AND WILL DEVOUR ITS RESOURCES, ONLY RETURNING THEM

TO THE IRAQIS BIT-BY-BIT. HE WILL DETERMINE HOW THEY SHOULD

LIVE, WHAT THEY SHOULD EAT, HOW THEY SHOULD BE EDUCATED, WHAT

ARMY SHOULD THEY BUILD, AND WHICH RELIGION SHOULD THEY FOLLOW ...

THE QUESTION THAT ARISES IS, IF LIFTING SANCTIONS ON IRAQ TOOK 13

YEARS AND ENDED IN ITS OCCUPATION, THEN HOW LONG WILL IT TAKE TO

END THE OCCUPATION? ..."
