SUBJECT: ZIMBABWE: EDITOR OF INDEPENDENT "DAILY NEWS"

ARRESTED

REF: HARARE 2402

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 HARARE 02411 151239Z

CLASSIFIED BY POLITICAL OFFICER TODD FAULK FOR REASONS

1.5 (B) AND (D).

(C) ACCORDING TO SEVERAL EMBASSY SOURCES, POLICE

ARRESTED GEOFF NYAROTA, THE EDITOR OF THE INDEPENDENT

"DAILY NEWS" NEWSPAPER, AT HIS HOME ABOUT 1AM ON THE

MORNING OF AUGUST 15. AS OF MID-DAY AUGUST 15,

NYAROTA WAS STILL BEING HELD AT HARARE\'S CENTRAL

POLICE STATION. PROMINENT OPPOSITIONIST ATTORNEY

BRIAN KAGORO AND THE "DAILY NEWS" NEWS EDITOR ARE

REPORTEDLY AT THE POLICE STATION WITH NYAROTA TO

REPRESENT HIM. OUR CONTACTS DO NOT KNOW WITH WHAT

NYAROTA HAS BEEN CHARGED, IF HE HAS BEEN CHARGED AT

ALL. OUR CONTACTS SPECULATE THAT THE POLICE ARE ANGRY

WITH NYAROTA FOR AUGUST 14\'S HEADLINE STORY ENTITLED

"POLICE VEHICLES USED IN FARM LOOTING SPREE," AND ARE

QUESTIONING HIM ABOUT IT (SEE REFTEL FOR A REPORT OF

THE LOOTING SPREE). WE ARE FOLLOWING THE SITUATION

CLOSELY AND WILL REPORT ONCE WE KNOW MORE.
