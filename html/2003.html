SUBJECT: TFUS1: MEDIA REPORTING ON TERRORIST ATTACKS

REF: STATE 161795

1. MAINSTREAM NEWSPAPERS CONTINUE TO CARRY OPINION

PIECES AND OP-ED ARTICLES FOCUSING ON THE SEPTEMBER 11

BOMBINGS OF THE WORLD TRADE CENTER AND THE

PENTAGON. THESE ARTICLES BOTH REFLECT AND LEAD

ZIMBABWEAN POPULAR OPINION ON THE ATTACKS. IN

GENERAL, THE ZIMBABWEAN MOOD IS THAT THE ATTACKS

WERE HORRIBLE AND SENSELESS ASSAULTS ON INNOCENT

PEOPLE. EXPRESSIONS OF CONDOLENCE HAVE BEEN

POURING IN TO THE U.S. MISSION IN ZIMBABWE. AT

THE SAME TIME, THERE IS AN UNDERCURRENT OF PUBLIC

OPINION ALONG THE LINES OF ".THE U.S. MUST EXPECT

TO PAY SOME PRICE FOR ITS SINS AND SELF-ASSUMED

ROLE OF GLOBAL POLICEMAN." ZIMBABWEANS ARE ALSO

CONCERNED ABOUT THE U.S. RESPONSE TO THE ATTACKS

AND, TRUE TO THEIR NATURE, GENERALLY ENDORSE

PATIENCE AND PRUDENCE. ARTICLE EXCERPTS FOLLOW:

2. UNDER HEADLINE \'U. S. MUST SEARCH DEEP WITHIN ITS

CONSCIENCE," THE SEPTEMBER 20 EDITION OF THE

INDEPENDENT WEEKLY "THE FINANCIAL GAZETTE"

CARRIED THE FOLLOWING OPINION PIECE AUTHORED BY

MAGGIE MZUMARA, A ZIMBABWEAN JOURNALIST, WRITER

AND SOCIAL COMMENTATOR CURRENTLY BASED IN NEW

YORK:

". . .(AMERICA) IS A NATION HUMBLED, A NATION

ROBBED,

AND A NATION OUTRAGED. TRUE, ALL THAT. BUT AT

SOME POINT AMERICA HAS TO STOP DWELLING ON ONLY

\'WHAT\' HAS HAPPENED AND \'HOW\' IT HAS HAPPENED AND

LOOK AT SOME FACTS. . . TO CONCENTRATE ONLY ON

AND TO STOP AT \'SMOKING OUT BIN LADEN FROM HIS

HIDING HOLES\' AND WIPING AWAY ALL THOSE WHO

\'HARBOR TERRORISTS\' IS AT BEST INCOMPLETE AND AT

WORST MISSING THE POINT. I AM NOT SAYING AMERICA

IS NOT JUSTIFIED IN SEEKING JUSTICE. IT IS AND

IF THE JUSTICE TO THE AMERICANS INCLUDES

RETALIATING MILITARILY, SO BE IT. BUT THAT KIND

OF JUSTICE WILL AND CAN ONLY BE MEANINGFUL IF IT

IS ACCOMPANIED BY A DEEPER LOOK AT THE ROOT

CAUSES OF THE FEELINGS BEHIND LAST WEEK\'S

CATASTROPHE. . . BUT AMERICA HAS THREE

WEAKNESSES. THREE MAJOR VICES: RUTHLESSNESS,

SELFISHNESS AND ARROGANCE. AMERICA IS ARROGANT.

SO ARROGANT IN FACT THAT IT IS NOW, AS IN THE

PAST, DESPERATELY PROTECTING ITS INNOCENCE BY

REFUSING TO LOOK AT THE FACTS. AMERICA AS A

NATION IS SO BLINDED BY ARROGANCE IT FAILS TO

RESPECT THAT WHICH IS DIFFERENT FROM ITSELF, BE

IT RELIGION, WAY OF LIFE, SOCIAL OR POLITICAL

SYSTEM. . .\' IT HAS TO BE AMERICAN OR ELSE ITS

NOTHING\' SEEMS TO BE THE SWEEPING FEELING HERE.

IN ITS SELFISH AND RUTHLESS PURSUIT AND

PROTECTION OF ITS INTERESTS, AMERICA HAS

DESTROYED CULTURES, ENSLAVED OTHERS AND COMMITTED

BIGOTRY. AS A RESULT OF ITS THREE WEAKNESSES,

THERE IS ALONG LIST OF GRIEVANCES AGAINST

AMERICANS KNOWN AND RECOGNIZED BY MANY WHO HAVE

STUDIED THE POLITICAL HISTORY OF THIS OTHERWISE

GREAT NATION. GRIEVANCES LIKE AGGRESSIVE WESTERN

EXPANSIONISM AND MANIPULATION OF COUNTRIES AND
