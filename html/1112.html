SUBJECT: DUTCH DISCOURAGED ABOUT EU EFFORT TO DEFINE FRY

WHITE LIST

REF: SECSTATE 103909

CLASSIFIED BY POLCOUNS STEPHEN R. KELLY; REASONS 1.5 (B,D).

1.(C) SUMMARY: DUTCH MFA OFFICIALS ARE DISCOURAGED AT THE

VERY SLOW AND POOR QUALITY EU EFFORT TO DEFINE A FRY WHITE

LIST TO BUTTRESS THE SANCTIONS REGIME. THEY NOTE A CLEAR

LACK OF FRENCH, ITALIAN AND GREEK EFFORT TO MEET EU POLITICAL

COMMITMENTS TO DEFINE A MEANINGFUL LIST AND EXPECT THE END

PRODUCT TO BE OF QUESTIONABLE REAL ENFORCEMNT VALUE.

NEVERTHELESS, THEY SAY THEY ARE AT LEAST RELIEVED THAT THE EU

IS NOT NOW ARGUING ABOUT HOW TO DISMANTLE THE "ENERGY FOR

DEMOCRACY" PROGRAM. THEY CAUTION THAT, SHOULD THE TENSION IN

BELGRADE SOON SUBSIDE, STRONG EU MEMBER STATE PRESSURE TO

WATER DOWN FINANCIAL SANCTION EFFORTS AND THE OIL DELIVERY

BAN WILL START REAPPEARING IN EU PROCEEDINGS. END SUMMARY.

2.(C) IN 6/5 DISCUSSIONS WITH MFA BALKANS OFFICE AND EU

AFFAIRS OFFICIALS, THE DUTCH EXPRESSED A BROAD

DISSATISFACTION WITH THE EU\'S EFFORT TO DEFINE A FRY

SANCTIONS "WHITE LIST." THEY CONCUR THAT THE EUROPEAN

COMMISSION IS SEVERELY UNDER-RESOURCED TO PERFORM THE TASK,

BUT SEE THAT STAFFING OBSTACLE AS SECONDARY TO GENERAL EU

MEMBER STATE RELUCTANCE TO PROVIDE ENFORCEMENT MECHANISMS

THAT MATCH POLITICAL COMMITMENTS. MFA EU COORDINATION

OFFICIALS STATED THAT, WHILE IT IS WORTH NOTING THAT THE EU

AT LEAST IS EMBARKING ON A SANCTIONS REGIME, THERE IS STILL

BROAD TENSION BETWEEN MANY MEMBER STATES AND THE COMMISSION

OVER THE QUESTION OF WITH WHOM GENERAL ENFORCEMENT

IMPLEMENTATION POWERS LIE. TO THIS THEY ADD THAT THERE IS

LITTLE REAL CONSENSUS TO ADD TEETH TO THE POLITICAL

STATEMENTS ABOUT THE EU FRY SANCTIONS REGIME. THE DUTCH

REPORT A CONTINUING CORE OF UK/NL RESOLVE TO PUSH FOR A

COMMISSION-LED EU SANCTIONS REGIME THAT FINANCIALLY PRESSURES

MILOSEVIC. ON THE OTHER HAND, THEY ARE TO QUICK TO NOTE

THEIR EFFORTS ARE OFFSET BY BROAD EU SLUGGISHNESS ON THE

QUESTION AND ANIMOSITY TO SUCH STEPS BY PARIS, ROME AND

ATHENS.

THE DUTCH ALSO FREELY CONCEDE THAT EVEN THEY LACK THE

PERSONNEL TO DO MUCH MORE ON SANCTIONS ENFORCEMENT THAN THEY

ALREADY DO. THEY SAY THAT THEY CANNOT EASILY SURGE BEYOND

THE 3-4 FULL-TIME EQUIVALENTS THAT THEY JOINTLY DEDICATE TO

THE EFFORT BETWEEN THE FOREIGN, FINANCE AND COMMERCE

MINISTRIES.

3.(C) AS A CASE IN POINT, DUTCH OFFICIALS HIGHLIGHT THE

DUTCH AND UK STRONG VETTING EFFORTS ON THE WHITE LIST. THE UK

HAS DEMARCHED EU STATES TO WORK HARD TO MEET THE JUNE 30

DEADLINE AND PRESENT CREDIBLE CANDIDATE BUSINESSES. THE DUTCH

SAY THAT THEY HAVE ONLY SUBMITTED TWO NAMES FOR COMMISSION

CONSIDERATION FOR THE WHITE LIST. UNDERCUTTING THIS

SERIOUSNESS OF APPROACH, THEY REPORT THAT STOCKHOLM HAS

SUBMITTED A SLATE OF 2,500 TO 3,000 CANDIDATE COMPANIES WHILE

PARIS\' SUBMISSIONS WERE MEASURED IN WEIGHT, COMING IN AT

25-30 KILOS.

4.(C) ACCORDINGLY, THE DUTCH PREDICT THAT THE JUNE 20 REVIEW

MEETING SET TO ORGANIZE THE JUNE 30 ANNOUNCEMENT LIKELY WILL

OVERSEE COMPILATION OF A POORLY VETTED LIST THAT MEETS THE EU

POLITICAL COMMITMENT TO DEFINE SUCH A PRODUCT, BUT WHICH WILL

LACK CREDIBILITY AS A REAL ENFORCEMENT TOOL.

5.(C) SEVERAL MFA OFFICIALS FAMILIAR WITH RECENT EU

DELIBERATIONS ON THE BALKANS AND SANCTIONS HAVE MADE CLEAR

THE LIMITS THAT THEY PERCEIVE THE EU IS REACHING ON BALKANS

SANCTIONS. THEY COMMENT THAT, WHILE EU LEADERS POLITICALLY

WOULD FEEL VULNERABLE IF THEY SAID NOTHING IN THE FACE OF

BELGRADE\'S ONGOING REPRESSION, THERE IS STILL NO BROAD EU

RESOLVE TO MATCH WORDS WITH ENFORCEMENT DEEDS. THEY ADD THAT

UNTIL THAT RESOLVE MATERIALIZES IT IS NOT WORTH THE EFFORT OF

TRYING TO SECOND PERSONNEL TO THE COMMISSION OR CHIP IN FROM

SCARCE NATIONAL RESOURCES WITH VETTING THE WHITE LIST DRAFT.

6.(C) COMMENT: DESPITE THE GENUINE DUTCH DISCOURAGEMENT OVER

DEFINING THE WHITE LIST, THEY STILL CAUTIOUSLY IMPLY THAT THE

U.S. MIGHT CONSIDER BEING GLAD FOR WHAT WE HAVE: THAT IS,

THE MOMENTARY ABSENCE OF AN ALL-OUT EU DRIVE TO DISMANTLE THE

EXISTING FRY SANCTIONS REGIME. THEY SIGNAL THAT WE SHOULD

NOT UNDERESTIMATE HOW CLOSE TO THE SURFACE THOSE SENTIMENTS

ARE IN EUROPE. IT IS PROBABLY WORTH NOTING THAT THE DUTCH

ALSO SEEM TO BE TIRING OF WADING UPSTREAM AGAINST A STRONG

CURRENT OF FRENCH, ITALIAN AND GREEK NEGATIVE SENTIMENT. IT

IS CLEAR THAT, IN DISCUSSIONS OF EU FRY SANCTIONS

DELIBERATIONS, THEY ALSO ARE TAKING PAINS TO DEFLECT

ANTICIPATED U.S. PRESSURE AWAY FROM THE HAGUE. END COMMENT.
