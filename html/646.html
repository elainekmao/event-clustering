SUBJECT: CONTACTS WITH THE PKK

REF: A) STATE 72605 (NOTAL); B) NICOSIA 1373 (NOTAL);

1. SECRET - ENTIRE TEXT.

2. WE APPRECIATE DEPARTMENT GUIDANCE (REF A)

REGARDING OFFICIAL USG CONTACTS WITH THE PKK. IN OUR

VIEW, IT IS LIKELY THAT PKK REPRESENTATIVES WILL

CONTINUE, IF NOT EXPAND, THEIR ATTEMPTS TO MEET WITH

U.S. AND OTHER WESTERN EMBASSY OFFICIALS TO PLEAD

THEIR CASE. IN VIEW, HOWEVER, OF THE ASSASSINATION IN

CYPRUS OF THE LOCAL "KURDISTAN SOLIDARITY COMMITTEE"

CHAIRMAN SHORTLY AFTER MEETING WITH EMBASSY NICOSIA

POLOFF (REFTEL B)--AND THE FACT THAT BOTH THIS MEETING

AND AN EARLIER ONE INVOLVING CONGEN ISTANBUL SAW US

UNEXPECTEDLY CONFRONTED WITH PERSONS PURPORTING TO

SPEAK FOR THE PKK--WE HAVE INSTRUCTED OUR EMBASSY

STAFF AND CONSTITUENT POSTS HERE IN TURKEY TO TURN

ASIDE, FOR THE MOMENT, ANY NEW APPROACHES FROM PERSONS

PURPORTING TO SPEAK FOR THE PKK. WE RECOMMEND THAT

THE DEPARTMENT ISSUE FORMAL GUIDANCE IN THIS REGARD TO

POSTS THROUGHOUT EUROPE, URGING THAT THESE POSTS ADOPT

THE SAME APPROACH FOR THE TIME BEING.

BARKLEY
