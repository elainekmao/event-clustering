SUBJECT: ZIMBABWE\'S NEW LABOR LEADERS PURSUE TRADITIONAL

LABOR CONCERNS WHILE CONTINUING SUPPORT OF THE MDC

REFTEL: HARARE 1054

CLASSIFIED BY CHARGE D\' AFFAIRES EARL M. IRVING FOR

REASONS 1.5 (B/D).

-------

SUMMARY

-------

1. (C) ON MARCH 21, LABOFF MET WITH THE NEW SECRETARY-

GENERAL OF THE ZIMBABWE CONGRESS OF TRADE UNIONS (ZCTU),

WELLINGTON CHIBHEBHE, AND THE NEW PRESIDENT, LOVEMORE

MATOMBO, BOTH EXPERIENCED AND ARTICULATE LABOR

ORGANIZERS, WHO INDICATED THAT THEY INTEND TO

AGGRESSIVELY ENGAGE THE GOVERNMENT OF ZIMBABWE (GOZ) ON

A RANGE OF TRADITIONAL LABOR ISSUES. THESE NEW LABOR

LEADERS WOULD LIKE TO SEE PASSAGE OF A HARMONIZED LABOR

BILL ACCEPTABLE TO LABOR, ESTABLISHMENT OF THE NECESSARY

CONDITIONS FOR LABOR TO CONSIDER ENTERING A SOCIAL

CONTRACT WITH GOVERNMENT AND BUSINESS, LABOR INPUT IN

THE PRIVATIZATION OF PARASTATALS, AND GOZ FUNDING FOR

ZCTU WORKPLACE HIV/AIDS EDUCATION PROGRAMS. CHIBHEBHE

AND MATOMBO BOTH SAID THAT THERE WAS NO TRUTH TO A

RECENT NEWSPAPER ARTICLE THAT STATED NEW ELECTIONS FOR

THE LABOR LEADERSHIP WOULD BE REQUIRED (REFTEL),

CLAIMING IT WAS PART OF THE GOZ/ZANU-PF\'S ONGOING

ATTEMPTS TO GAIN CONTROL OVER THE LABOR MOVEMENT.

ALTHOUGH BOTH MEN REPEATED THEIR PUBLIC PROCLAMATIONS

THAT THE ZCTU IS AN INDEPENDENT ORGANIZATION, THEY

ACKNOWLEDGED THAT THE ZCTU MEMBERSHIP STRONGLY SUPPORTS

THE MDC AND THE ORGANIZATION WILL HAVE A KEY ROLE TO

PLAY IN THE UPCOMING PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION. END

SUMMARY.

----------------------------------

LOBBYING FOR PRO-LABOR LEGISLATION

----------------------------------

2. (C) MATOMBO SPOKE EXTENSIVELY ON THE HARMONIZED LABOR

BILL THAT IS CURRENTLY BEING CONSIDERED BY PARLIAMENT.

THIS BILL HAS BEEN GATHERING DUST FOR THE LAST NINE

YEARS, AFTER BEING DRAFTED WITH THE INPUT OF THE

INTERNATIONAL LABOR ORGANIZATION (ILO). ACCORDING TO

MATOMBO, THE GOZ HAS MADE SUCH SIGNIFICANT CHANGES TO

THE LABOR BILL, AS IT DID WITH THE 1999 DRAFT ZIMBABWE

CONSTITUTION, THAT THE ZCTU OPPOSES IT IN ITS CURRENT

FORM. HE INDICATED THAT THE MINISTRY OF LABOR, UNDER

THE EXISTING BILL, WOULD HAVE POWERS TO DECERTIFY

UNIONS, A FACT THAT WOULD IN EFFECT MAKE THE UNIONS

SUBSERVIENT TO THE GOZ. RATHER THAN TREATING ALL

WORKERS EQUALLY, WHICH THE ZCTU STRONGLY WANTS IN A

HARMONIZED LABOR LAW, THE BILL WOULD CONTINUE TO TREAT

CIVIL SERVANTS DIFFERENTLY FROM OTHER UNIONIZED WORKERS.

PARASTATAL WORKERS WOULD BE DEEMED ESSENTIAL, AND

THEREFORE PROHIBITED FROM ENGAGING IN INDUSTRIAL

ACTIONS. THE ZCTU IS ACTIVELY OPPOSING THE GOZ EFFORT

TO "FAST-TRACK" THIS BILL THROUGH PARLIAMENT BY

INTENSIVELY LOBBYING MEMBERS OF PARLIAMENT FROM BOTH THE

LABOR WILL SUCCEED IN THIS EFFORT GIVEN THE ZANU-PF

CONDITIONS NOT RIGHT FOR A SOCIAL CONTRACT

------------------------------------------

3. (C) CHIBHEBHE SPOKE AT LENGTH ABOUT WHY THE ZCTU

CANNOT AT THIS TIME ENTER INTO NEGOTIATIONS FOR A SOCIAL

CONTRACT WITH THE GOZ AND BUSINESS. ONE LABOR

PRECONDITION FOR SUCH TALKS IS AGREEMENT ON A BASIC

MINIMUM WAGE FOR ALL WORKERS THAT IS ABOVE THE POVERTY

DATUM LINE FOR A FAMILY OF FOUR. THE GOZ HAS THUS FAR

REFUSED TO AGREE TO THIS. ANOTHER PRECONDITION IS THAT

AGREEMENT ON AN ACCEPTABLE NATIONAL MINIMUM WAGE BY

LABOR SHOULD NOT PRECLUDE THE RIGHT OF UNIONS TO ENTER

INTO INDUSTRIAL ACTIONS, WHICH IS WHAT THE GOZ WANTS.

LASTLY CHIBHEBHE STATED THAT A RETURN TO THE RULE OF LAW

IS A CRITICAL PRECONDITION, NOTING THAT LABOR IS

EXTREMELY CONCERNED BY THE CURRENT LAWLESSNESS IN THE

COUNTRY. HE CONTINUED THAT AS A RESULT OF THE ZCTU ROLE

IN FOUNDING THE OPPOSITION MOVEMENT FOR DEMOCRATIC

CHANGE (MDC), THE GOZ AND ZANU-PF VIEW LABOR AS PART OF

PRIVATIZATION MUST BE TRANSPARENT

---------------------------------

4. (C) MATOMBO THEN TURNED TO THE ZCTU POSITION ON

PRIVATIZATION OF GOZ-OWNED PARASTATALS. HE RECOGNIZED

THAT PRIVATIZATION IS NECESSARY AND THAT GLOBALIZATION

AND FOREIGN INVESTMENT ARE INEVITABLE TRENDS THAT WILL

PLAY A ROLE IN THIS PROCESS. HOWEVER, THE ZCTU IS

OPPOSED TO THE WAY PRIVATIZATION IS CURRENTLY BEING

HANDLED BY THE GOZ. MATOMBO STATED THAT THE GOZ IS NOT

CONSULTING WITH THE UNIONS THAT REPRESENT PARASTATAL

WORKERS, AS IT SHOULD. OF EQUAL CONCERN IS THE FACT

THAT PRIVATIZATION IS CURRENTLY BEING CONDUCTED TO

BENEFIT A CLIQUE OF POWERFUL PEOPLE CONNECTED TO ZANU-

PF, HE CLAIMED. THE "INDIGENIZATION PROCESS" ESPOUSED

BY THE GOZ TO CREATE BLACK OWNERS OF PRIVATIZED

PARASTATALS, HE SAID, IS JUST A COVER FOR HANDING OVER

THESE COMPANIES TO A FEW POWERFUL ZANU-PF SUPPORTERS.

THE NEW ZCTU LEADERSHIP INTENDS TO ENGAGE THE GOZ ON

THIS ISSUE AND WILL LOBBY FOR A TRANSPARENT

PRIVATIZATION PROCESS IN WHICH LABOR WILL HAVE A ROLE IN

ADDRESSING THE HIV/AIDS EPIDEMIC

--------------------------------

5. (C) CHIBHEBHE AND MATOMBO BOTH ACKNOWLEDGED THAT THE

HIV/AIDS EPIDEMIC IS A CRITICAL ISSUE THAT LABOR MUST

ADDRESS. THEY BOTH HAVE SEEN THE RAVAGES OF THE

EPIDEMIC FIRSTHAND THROUGH THE DEATHS OF WORKERS.

MATOMBO CRITICIZED THE GOZ, AND PRESIDENT MUGABE

PERSONALLY, FOR NOT DOING MORE TO COMBAT THIS PROBLEM.

LABOR\'S POSITION IS THAT THE EPIDEMIC SHOULD BE DECLARED

A NATIONAL CATASTROPHE AND THERE SHOULD BE AN URGENT

CAMPAIGN INITIATED BY THE GOZ TO ADDRESS IT ON MULTIPLE

FRONTS. THE ZCTU HAS AN OFFICE DEDICATED TO HIV/AIDS

EDUCATION OF ITS MEMBERSHIP, BUT IS HAMPERED BY LIMITED

RESOURCES. CHIBHEBHE STATED THAT THE ZCTU IS GOING TO

REQUEST THAT THE GOZ PROVIDE SOME OF THE MONEY

CONTROLLED BY THE NATIONAL AIDS COUNCIL, RAISED THROUGH

AN AIDS TAX ON WORKERS\' SALARIES, TO THE ZCTU SO THAT IT

CAN EXPAND ITS EFFORTS AGAINST THE EPIDEMIC. MATOMBO

ADDED THAT LABOR RECOGNIZES THAT UNIONS CAN PLAY A VITAL

ROLE IN HIV/AIDS EDUCATION BECAUSE ITS ORGANIZATION IS

DISPERSED BROADLY THROUGHOUT SOCIETY.

--------------------

SCHISMS IN THE ZCTU?

--------------------

6. (C) MATOMBO AND CHIBHEBHE DENIED THAT THERE ARE

DIVISIONS WITHIN THE LABOR FEDERATION. THEY

SPECIFICALLY REFERRED TO A RECENT ARTICLE IN THE GOZ-

CONTROLLED "THE SUNDAY MAIL" AS A ZANU-PF PROPAGANDA

THAT WAS FULL OF LIES (REFTEL). CHIBHEBHE ADDED THAT

THE GOZ/ZANU-PF ARE STILL SMARTING FROM THEIR CANDIDATES

BEING SHUT OUT IN THE ELECTIONS FOR THE NEW ZCTU

EXECUTIVE COUNCIL. THEY ACCUSED THE FORMER ACTING ZCTU

SECRETARY-GENERAL (AND ZANU-PF CANDIDATE FOR SECRETARY-

SIPDIS

GENERAL AT THE LAST CONGRESS), ISIDORE ZINDOGA, OF

LEADING THE EFFORT TO DISCREDIT THE NEW LEADERSHIP.

MATOMBO REVEALED THAT AT THE FIRST FULL ZCTU GENERAL

COUNCIL MEETING ON MARCH 17, THERE WAS NOT A DISSENTING

VOICE AGAINST THE NEWLY ELECTED LEADERSHIP. HE ALSO

POINTED OUT THAT THE VERY PEOPLE (ZINDOGA AND COMPANY)

WHO ARE NOW SAYING THAT THE CONGRESS WAS TAINTED WERE

THE ONES WHO ORGANIZED IT.

7. (C) CHIBHEBHE AND MATOMBO BOTH REPEATED THEIR PUBLIC

PROCLAMATIONS THAT THE ZCTU IS NOT AFFILIATED WITH ANY

POLITICAL PARTY AND WILL FOLLOW ITS OWN INDEPENDENT NON-

PARTISAN APPROACH IN PURSUING WORKERS\' INTERESTS.

LABOFF ASKED HOW THIS POSITION MESHED WITH THE ZCTU\'S

CREATION OF THE MDC. CHIBHEBHE RESPONDED THAT THE ZCTU

HAD TO TAKE THIS POSITION, BECAUSE IF THE MDC WERE TO

FORM THE NEXT GOVERNMENT, THIS INDEPENDENCE WOULD ALLOW

LABOR TO CONTINUE TO AGGRESSIVELY PURSUE WORKER

INTERESTS. HOWEVER, MATOMBO QUICKLY ADDED THAT IT WAS

NO SECRET THAT THE VAST MAJORITY OF WORKERS SUPPORT THE

MDC, AND WILL VOTE FOR ITS PRESIDENTIAL CANDIDATE IN THE

NEXT ELECTION. HE SAID THAT ZANU-PF\'S CURRENT URBAN

CAMPAIGN OF "GOING INTO THE TOWNSHIPS AND BEATING UP

WORKERS" IS ONLY HARDENING THE SUPPORT FOR THE MDC AMONG

WORKERS.

COMMENT

-------

8. (C) THE NEW SECRETARY-GENERAL AND PRESIDENT OF THE

ZCTU COME ACROSS AS HIGHLY ARTICULATE LEADERS. THEIR

ABILITIES WILL CLEARLY SERVE THEM WELL IN FURTHERING THE

INTERESTS OF THE ZCTU MEMBERSHIP AT A TIME OF ECONOMIC

PAGE 03 HARARE 01096 03 OF 03 221148Z

COLLAPSE AND POLITICAL CONFRONTATION. CHIBHEBHE AND

MATOMBO BARELY CONCEALED THE FACT THAT THEY ARE STRONGLY

PRO-MDC. IGNORING THEIR OWN PROTESTATIONS OF THE

APOLITICAL NATURE OF THE ZCTU, THEY FREELY ACKNOWLEDGED

THAT THE FEBRUARY ZCTU CONGRESS RESULTED IN THE ELECTION

OF A PRO-MDC ZCTU EXECUTIVE. THEIR COMMENTS REGARDING

THE COST TO LABOR OF THE GOZ\'S MISMANAGEMENT OF THE

ECONOMY (JUST ONE EXAMPLE BEING THAT AN ESTIMATED 20

PERCENT OF UNION WORKERS HAVE LOST THEIR JOBS IN THE

LAST 12 MONTHS), COMBINED WITH THE ZANU-PF CAMPAIGN OF

REPRESSION DIRECTED AT UNION ORGANIZERS AND WORKERS,

CLEARLY PUTS THE ZCTU IN OPPOSITION TO PRESIDENT MUGABE.

WE BELIEVE THE NEW ZCTU EXECUTIVE WILL USE THE LABOR

MOVEMENTS\' ORGANIZATIONAL STRUCTURES TO HELP DELIVER THE

VOTE FOR THE MDC CANDIDATE IN THE UPCOMING PRESIDENTIAL

ELECTIONS. END COMMENT.
