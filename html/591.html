2. AN EXPATRIATE STAFFER FROM UNHCR\'S BANJA LUKA

OFFICE (STRICTLY PROTECT) DESCRIBED FOR US THE

WORSENING SITUATION IN NORTHWEST BOSNIA DEC. 30.

THE UNHCR STAFFER SAID THE LOCAL SERBIAN FORCES HAD

INTENSIFIED THEIR HARASSMENT OF BOTH BOSNIAN MUSLIMS

AND CROATS SINCE EARLY DECEMBER. IN BANJA LUKA,

UNHCR AND ICRC STAFFERS SEEING AND HEARING NUMEROUS

ARMED SERBIAN GROUPS SHOOTING UP HOUSES, APARTMENT

BUILDINGS, AND STORES AT RANDOM, KILLING AND WOUNDING

PEOPLE OF ALL AGES EVERYDAY. THE SERBS ALSO WERE

LOOTING HOMES AND STORES AND DESTROYING MOST

ABANDONED MUSLIM AND CROAT BUILDINGS. THE VIOLENCE

AND DESTRUCTION IS MUCH WORSE IN THE SURROUNDING

COUNTRYSIDE. UNHCR-ZAGREB RECALLED ITS EXPAT STAFF

FROM BANJA LUKA A FEW DAYS BEFORE CHRISTMAS FOR A

TEMPORARY, BUT INDEFINITE, PERIOD. ALTHOUGH NO RPT

NO CREDIBLE, SPECIFIC DEATH THREATS HAD BEEN MADE

AGAINST THE EXPAT STAFF, INDIVIDUAL ARMED SERBS HAVE

BEEN MORE DIFFICULT TO DEAL WITH SINCE MID-DECEMBER,

ESPECIALLY OUTSIDE OF THE TOWN. A SERB COLONEL

RECENTLY JOKED THAT IF THE UN SECURITY COUNCIL

DECIDED WESTERN FORCES SHOULD ENFORCE THE NO FLY

RESOLUTION AND ATTACKED THE AIRFIELD NEAR BANJA LUKA

OR OTHER MILITARY TARGETS, THE SERBS HAD 60,000

3. ON DEC. 30, 1992, AN EXPATRIATE STAFFER FROM

UNHCR\'S BANJA LUKA OFFICE (STRICTLY PROTECT) MET AT

UNHCR ZAGREB HEADQUARTERS WITH TDY POLOFF AND HEAD OF

OFDA DART TEAM . THE EXPAT HAS PREVIOUSLY WORKED FOR

AN INTERNATIONAL ORGANIZATION IN SOUTHEAST ASIA AND

AN AMERICAN PVO IN CENTRAL AMERICA. THE STAFFER SAID

THE FOUR MONTHS SHE WORKED IN BANJA LUKA (SEPTEMBER -

DECEMBER) SEEMED LIKE FOUR YEARS. "IT WAS MUCH WORSE

THAN THE WAR IN EL SALVADOR." ACKNOWLEDGING

DISAGREEMENT WITH USG POLICIES IN EL SALVADOR, THE

UNHCR STAFFER SAID THE SERB FORCES\' KILLING, RANDOM

VIOLENCE AND VICIOUS TREATMENT OF BOSNIAN MUSLIMS AND

CROATS IN THE SO-CALLED SERBIAN REPUBLIC OF BOSNIA

WAS "EVEN WORSE THAN THE ATROCITIES OF THE SALVADORAN

DEATH SQUADS."

SERB HARASSMENT INTENSIFIES

---------------------------

4. THE UNHCR STAFFER SAID THE SERBIAN FORCES SEEMED

TO HAVE REDUCED THEIR KILLING AND MISTREATMENT OF

MUSLIMS AND CROATS IN SEPTEMBER FOR SOME UNKNOWN

REASON. SHE SPECULATED THAT THEY HAD TEMPORARILY

BACKED OFF AFTER THE INTERNATIONAL OUTCRY AT SERBIAN

MASSACRES AND DEATH CAMPS DURING THE SUMMER AND EARLY

AUTUMN. HOWEVER, IN EARLY NOVEMBER THE SERBS RESUMED

THEIR PREVIOUS SEVERE HARASSMENT OF BOTH ETHNIC

GROUPS. THE KILLING, VIOLENCE, AND DESTRUCTION

INCREASED FURTHER IN EARLY DECEMBER. THE NON-SERBIAN

POPULATION IS THOROUGHLY TERRORIZED, AS ARE SOME LONG

RESIDENT SERBS WHO HAVE REFUSED TO BE "MOBILIZED"

AGAINST THE MUSLIMS AND CROATS. WHILE THE STAFFER

HAD NOT RPT NOT HEARD OF ANY NEW LARGE SCALE

MASSACRES SINCE SEPTEMBER, THE VARIOUS ARMED SERBIAN

GROUPS KILLED OR WOUNDED PEOPLE SINGLY OR IN SMALL

NUMBERS EVERY DAY, OFTEN SEEMINGLY AT RANDOM. THE

VIOLENCE WAS MUCH WORSE IN THE COUNTRYSIDE AROUND

BANJA LUKA, ESPECIALLY WEST AND NORTH OF TOWN. UNHCR

HAD ONLY THREE EXPAT AND NINE LOCAL EMPLOYEES IN

BANJA LUKA WITH ONLY A FEW VEHICLES, SO THEY COULD

NOT ACCURATELY ESTIMATE CASUALTIES THROUGHOUT THE

REGION. THE STAFFER SAID ICRC HAD NINE EXPATS AND 15

LOCAL EMPLOYEES AND A NUMBER OF VEHICLES IN BANJA

LUKA, BUT SHE DOUBTED ICRC HAD SUFFICIENT FREEDOM OF

MOVEMENT TO ESTIMATE CASUALTIES EITHER.

UNHCR EXPATS BACK FROM BANJA LUKA A FEW DAYS BEFORE

CHRISTMAS FOR A "TEMPORARY PERIOD." THE BANJA LUKA

OFFICE REMAINS OFFICIALLY OPEN AND LOCAL STAFF

CONTINUE TRYING TO WORK. THE EXPAT STAFF HAS HAD

SIGNIFICANTLY MORE DIFFICULTY WORKING OVER THE LAST

TWO MONTHS. FOR EXAMPLE, SERBIAN "COLONELS" IN BANJA

LUKA TOLD THE UNHCR STAFF THAT THEY DID NOT NEED

PERMISSION TO LEAVE TOWN TO OBSERVE HOW UNHCR-

PROVIDED FOOD WAS BEING DISTRIBUTED OR MISUSED. BUT

JUST OUTSIDE OF TOWN, SERBIAN GUARDS AT A CHECKPOINT

WOULD REFUSE THEM PERMISSION TO PROCEED AT ALL OR

ONLY AFTER LONG, NASTY ARGUMENTS AND INCREASINGLY

FREQUENT WAVING AROUND OF RIFLES AND PISTOLS. THE

STAFFER SAID THAT THE THREE EXPATS BECAME VERY

FRUSTRATED AS IT WAS NEARLY IMPOSSIBLE TO DO

MEANINGFUL WORK, EVEN ON AN IRREGULAR BASIS. MORE

IMPORTANTLY, SERBIAN "HASSLING" OF UNHCR STAFFERS WAS

SERBS READY FOR MORE WAR CRIMES

6. A SERBIAN "OFFICER" AT BANJA LUKA TOLD THE UNHCR

STAFFER THAT THEY WOULD COUNTER THREATS OF U.S. OR

OTHER AIR STRIKES ON THE AIRFIELD NEAR BANJA LUKA OR

OTHER MILITARY TARGETS BY DEPLOYING SOME OF THE

60,000 MUSLIMS AND CROATS IN AND AROUND BANJA LUKA AS

A HUMAN SHIELD. THE STAFFER IS CONVINCED THE OFFICER

WAS DEADLY SERIOUS.

MORE FORCES BUT POORER DISCIPLINE

---------------------------------

8. THE STAFFER SAID THAT IN THE LAST TWO MONTHS

THERE HAS BEEN A LARGE INCREASE IN THE NUMBER OF

SERBIAN MILITARY AND PARA-MILITARY PERSONNEL IN BANJA

LUKA. THE NEWLY ARRIVED "SOLDIERS" OFTEN SEEM TO

OPERATE AT CROSS-PURPOSES, AND SOME HEADQUARTERS AND

UNITS ARE FEUDING WITH OTHERS. THE MEN WEAR A

VARIETY OF UNIFORMS. GUESSING FROM SHOULDER PATCHES

(WHICH MANY DO NOT WEAR), NEW MEN ARE FROM ABOUT 12

MAJOR UNITS OR ORGANIZATIONS, APPARENTLY INCLUDING

MANY WITH ADMIN OR SUPPORT-TYPE ACTIVITIES.

DISCIPLINE IN MANY GROUPS IS POOR AND GETTING WORSE.

CONFIDENTIAL

CONFIDENTIAL

THE PARA-MILITARY GROUPS ARE THE WORST AND ARE

GETTING MORE NUMEROUS AS SOME "ARMY" UNITS MOVE OUT

OF TOWN NEARER THE AIRFIELD OR TOWARDS THE SOUTHERN

AND WESTERN FRONTS. MANY OF THE NEW GROUPS

APPARENTLY ARE FROM SERBIA. IT IS DANGEROUS JUST TO

WALK DOWN THE STREET OR EVEN BE IN A BUILDING. THE

SERBS ALSO ARE DRAFTING BOSNIAN SERB POLICEMEN OUT OF

THE TOWN AND SURROUNDING AREAS, SO CRIME RATES ARE

RISING. THE POLICEMEN AT LEAST PROTECTED SOME OF THE

SERBIAN CIVILIANS, NOW THEY TOO ARE BEING VICTIMIZED

BY THEIR "PROTECTORS."

MUSLIM RESISTANCE LIMITED TO HIT AND RUN

----------------------------------------

9. THERE WAS NO LARGE-SCALE ORGANIZED MILITARY

OPPOSITION TO THE SERBIAN FORCES AS FAR AS THE UNHCR

KNEW. THERE ARE NUMEROUS SMALL SKIRMISHES AND

FIREFIGHTS OUTSIDE OF BANJA LUKA, ESPECIALLY IN THE

WEST, THAT ADD TO THE TOLL OF DEAD AND WOUNDED AND

DISPLACED. LOCAL SERBIAN FORCES CLAIMED "MUSLIM

FUNDAMENTALISTS AND MERCENARIES" ARE RESPONSIBLE FOR

ATROCITIES . SERBIAN OFFICERS ARE STILL SHOWING

VISITING FOREIGN OFFICIALS AND JOURNALISTS GRUESOME

PICTURES OF BUCKETS OF CHOPPED OFF HEADS AND PILES OF

HEADS AND OTHER GORY SCENES WHICH THEY CLAIM ARE THE

RESULT OF MUSLIM ATTACKS ON SERBS.
