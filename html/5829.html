SUBJECT: (C) OIL FOR JORDAN

Classified By: (U) AMBASSADOR RICHARD H. JONES; REASON: 1.5 (B,D)

1. (C) MFA MinState Dr. Mohammed al-Sabah told Ambassador

February 19 that he had recently informed Jordanian Foreign

Minister Marwan al-Muasher that Kuwait is prepared to provide

Jordan up to 20,000 bpd of oil in the event that war cuts off

Iraqi oil to Jordan. He understands the UAE will provide the

same amount. Muasher told him that Saudi Arabia will provide

50,000 bpd on terms not specified. Dr. Mohammed indicated

Kuwait\'s oil would be provided at a "substantial discount,"

but not free.
