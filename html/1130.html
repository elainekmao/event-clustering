SUBJECT: DUTCH READY FOR POST-ELECTION SUPPORT TO

DJUKANOVIC

REF: A. SECSTATE 109696

B. SECSTATE 107487 EXDIS (NOTAL)

CLASSIFIED BY A/POLCOUNS S. REID; REASONS 1.5 (B,D).

1.(C) A/POLCOUNS DELIVERED REF.A DEMARCHE TO ACTING EUR

A/S-EQUIVALENT MARTIN LAK AND MFA MONTENEGRO DESK OFFICER

NORBERT BOTH ON 6/9. LAK RECALLED FM VAN AARTSEN\'S

EXPRESSIONS OF CONCERN OVER MONTENEGRO TO SECRETARY ALBRIGHT

AT THE FLORENCE NAC (REF. B). HE ALSO SAID THAT THE DUTCH

CHARGE IN BELGRADE\'S REPORTING OF EVENTS IN MONTENEGRO

TRACKED WITH REF. A ASSESSMENT. CONFIRMING THAT THE

NETHERLANDS MFA SHARES U.S. CONCERNS ABOUT RISING TENSIONS IN

THE RUN-UP TO THE 6/11 BALLOTING, LAK AGREED THAT PROMPT

PUBLIC SUPPORT FOR DJUKANOVIC FOLLOWING THE ANNOUNCEMENT OF

THE ELECTION OUTCOME WAS A PRUDENT STEP GIVEN POSSIBLE

MILOSEVIC PROVOCATIONS. HE ADDED THAT, WHILE SUPPORTIVE

REMARKS WOULD BE PREPARED FOR POSSIBLE USE WITH THE PRESS BY

FM VAN AARTSEN ON 6/12, THE DUTCH WOULD ALSO PUSH FOR AN

EARLY EU PRESIDENCY STATEMENT OF SUPPORT FOR DJUKANOVIC.

FINALLY, HE PREDICTED A PROMINENT PLACE FOR "ANTICIPATED

POSITIVE NEWS ON THE ELECTIONS" IN A 6/13 EU GAC STATEMENT.

2. (C) LAK NOTED THE DUTCH WERE PLEASED WITH THE SIZEABLE

ODIHR ELECTION MONITORING DEPLOYMENT IN MONTENEGRO. HE ADDED

THAT THE DUTCH ALSO WERE BUOYED THAT, SO FAR, BELGRADE\'S

ALLIES HAD NOT CALLED FOR A BOYCOTT OF THE VOTE. "IT WOULD

BE VERY IMPORTANT FOR THE CAUSE OF FRY DEMOCRACY FOR BELGRADE

AND ITS ALLIES TO LOSE A FAIR VOTE," LAK SUMMARIZED, "IT

MIGHT JUST SPUR THE SERB OPPOSITION FORWARD SOME MORE.

DJUKANOVIC\'S EXAMPLE IS VERY IMPORTANT AS A PICTURE OF WHAT

THE FRY COULD BE IF IT WANTED TO BE SO."
