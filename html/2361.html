SUBJECT: THERE HE GOES AGAIN: WHITHER RUGGIERO, II?

REF: 2001 ROME 6573

CLASSIFIED BY: DCM WILLIAM P. POPE, REASONS 1.5 (B) AND (D).

1. (C) SUMMARY: ITALY\'S FOREIGN MINISTER HAS AGAIN

GENERATED TURMOIL BY PUBLICLY CHASTISING HIS FELLOW CABINET

PAGE 02 ROME 00067 070638Z

MEMBERS -- AND SOME SUGGEST EVEN HIS PRIME MINISTER -- FOR

FAILING TO BE SUFFICIENTLY VOCAL IN THEIR SUPPORT OF THE

LAUNCH OF THE EURO. IS RUGGIERO SEEKING HIS OWN PREMATURE

OUSTER? IS HE SIMPLY TRYING TO ENSURE THAT THE GOI REMAINS

FIRMLY ON THE EU TRACK IN PREPARATION FOR ITS UPCOMING TURN

AT THE PRESIDENCY? WE AREN\'T SURE WHAT RUGGIERO IS UP TO,

BUT FOR THE TIME BEING IT APPEARS PM BERLUSCONI HAS CHOSEN TO

KEEP PEACE IN THE FAMILY, AT LEAST LONG ENOUGH TO ALLOW THE

STRONGEST FEELINGS TO DIE DOWN. HE HAS, HOWEVER, SUMMONED

RUGGIERO FOR A "TETE-A-TETE" ON JANUARY 7. END SUMMARY.

2. (SBU) ITALIAN FOREIGN MINISTER RENATO RUGGIERO HAS AGAIN

SPOKEN OUT FRANKLY -- TOO FRANKLY FOR THE LIKING OF SOME OF

HIS MINISTERIAL COLLEAGUES. RUGGIERO WAS WIDELY QUOTED IN

THE MEDIA FOLLOWING THE INAUGURATION OF THE EURO AS

CRITICIZING NOT ONLY HIS FELLOW CABINET MEMBERS, BUT EVEN

(THIS INDIRECT "CRITICISM" IS OPEN TO INTERPRETATION) PRIME

MINISTER BERLUSCONI AS BEING LUKEWARM IN THEIR SUPPORT OF THE

EURO AND THUS IN THEIR SUPPORT OF EUROPE. RUGGIERO\'S

STATEMENTS DREW THE IMMEDIATE IRE OF, ESPECIALLY, FINANCE

MINISTER TREMONTI (FORZA ITALIA), MINISTER FOR DEVOLUTION

BOSSI (LEGA NORD), AND MINISTER FOR EUROPEAN AFFAIRS

BUTTIGLIONE (CHRISTIAN DEMOCRATIC UNION). BOSSI INTENSIFIED

HIS (RELATIVELY) MILD COMMENTS REPORTED REFTEL CALLING

RUGGIERO A "PROVOCATEUR, REPRESENTING BUREAUCRATS AND

TYRANTS, WITH A FATAL ATTRACTION FOR THE LEFT."

3. (SBU) FOR THE TIME BEING, BERLUSCONI POURED OIL ON THE

TROUBLED WATERS AND DECLINED TO CRITICIZE HIS FOREIGN

MINISTER. HIS SPOKESPERSON NOTED THAT DISSENT AND

CONTROVERSY ARE HEALTHY IN A GOVERNMENT. THE PRIME MINISTER

STATED, HOWEVER, THAT HE CONTROLS ITALY\'S FOREIGN POLICY,

THAT HE IS THE GUARANTOR OF THE COUNTRY\'S POLITICAL

DIRECTION, AND THAT AS SUCH HE ASSURES THERE IS CONTINUITY IN

THE GOI TOWARD EUROPE. BERLUSCONI DENIED THE POSSIBILITY

THAT SOMETHING SAID BY RUGGIERO, WHOM HE DEFINED AS A

"TECHNOCRATIC MINISTER," COULD HAVE POLITICAL REPERCUSSIONS

AND DECLINED TO SPECULATE ON THE POSSIBILITY OF CHANGES IN

HIS CABINET. FINALLY, BERLUSCONI ANNOUNCED THAT HE AND

RUGGIERO WOULD MEET ON JANUARY 7, WHEN THEY WOULD "CLARIFY

EVERYTHING."

4. (C) NATURALLY, SPECULATION ON RUGGIERO\'S INTENT IS

RAMPANT. SOME SPECULATE THAT HE IS ANGLING TO GET OUT OF HIS

18-MONTH COMMITMENT AS MINISTER AND MOVE ON TO SOMETHING

ELSE. (ONE JOURNALIST EVEN SUGGESTED RUGGIERO NOW WANTS TO

BECOME A UN SPECIAL REPRESENTATIVE IN AFGHANISTAN.) OTHERS

SUGGEST THE FOREIGN MINISTER IS SIMPLY TRYING TO ENSURE THAT

THE GOI STAYS FIRMLY PLACED ON THE EU TRACK, READYING IT TO

FULFILL ITS ROLE IN THE PRESIDENCY, SOMETHING THAT TRACKS

MORE CLOSELY WITH RUGGIERO\'S OWN COMMENTS. THERE ARE ALSO

THEORIES -- MAINLY AMONG RIGHT-WINGERS -- OF A LEFTIST PLOT

TO "CAPTURE" THE FOREIGN MINISTRY (OR MORE).

5. (C) COMMENT: WE AREN\'T YET READY TO PULL OUT OUR

CRYSTAL BALL ON RUGGIERO\'S FUTURE. HIS STATEMENTS DON\'T

STRIKE US AS ALL THAT EXTREME, BUT ANY PUBLIC CRITICISM OF

ONE\'S OWN GOVERNMENT, NO MATTER HOW MILD, IS CORROSIVE.

WE\'LL STAY TUNED FOR MONDAY\'S MEETING.

SEMBLER
