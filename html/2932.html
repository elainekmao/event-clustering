SUBJECT: BLUE LANTERN LEVEL 3: CONFIRMATION OF PRE-LICENSE

REF: SECSTATE 28693

1. MISSION HAS CONFIRMED WITH OFFICIALS OF THE UNIVERSITY OF

ROME\'S ("LA SAPIENZA") SAN MARCO PROJECT RESEARCH CENTER

(CRPSM) THAT THEY UNDERSTAND THE PARAMETERS OF THE TECHNICAL

ASSISTANCE AGREEMENT (TAA) AG-272-96E REGARDING THE

PROTECTION OF THE TECHNICAL DATA COLLECTED BY THEIR TRACKING

STATION IN KENYA ON BEHALF OF BOEING. IN ADDITION, CRPSM

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 02 ROME 02321 131326Z

TECHNICAL DIRECTOR PROFESSOR MAURIZIO DIRUSCIO EXPLAINED TO

ESTOFF THAT BOEING HAS SET UP A COMPLETELY AUTOMATED SYSTEM

THAT GUARANTEES THAT NO CRPSM EMPLOYEE, ITALIAN OR KENYAN,

HAS DIRECT ACCESS TO THE TELEMETRY DATA. THE DATA IS

COLLECTED BY A BOEING STAND-ALONE RECEIVER AND IS THEN

IMMEDIATELY RE-TRANSMITTED OVER CONVENTIONAL TELEPHONE LINES

DIRECTLY BACK TO A BOEING FACILITY IN THE UNITED STATES.

ROUTINE AND EMERGENCY MAINTENANCE OF THE BOEING DELTA LAUNCH

TELEMETRY TRACKING RECEIVER WILL BE CONDUCTED AS NEEDED BY

BOEING PERSONNEL ONLY. ALTHOUGH ITALIAN CRPSM STAFF WILL

HAVE PHYSICAL ACCESS TO THE EQUIPMENT, THEY WILL BE UNABLE TO

MANIPULATE THE EQUIPMENT WITHOUT ONSITE ASSISTANCE FROM

BOEING.
