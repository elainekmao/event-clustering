SUBJECT: TERRORIST FINANCE- TURKEY: MFA WELCOMES KADEK\'S

INCLUSION ON USG LIST OF FOREIGN TERRORIST ORGANIZATIONS

REF: SECSTATE 246714

Classified by Charge Robert S. Deutsch; reasons 1.5 (b) and

(d).

1. (C) On December 11, A/POL and poloff delivered reftel

talking points to Kemal Asya, MFA Deputy Director General for

Intelligence and Research. Asya thanked USG for including

KADEK as an alias to PKK and for asking the EU to do the

same. He cautioned however, that he was not optimistic about

either the European Union\'s inclusion of KADEK or some of its

members\' ability to freeze the assets of the members of

terrorist organization.

2. (U) The GOT MFA released a statement December 10 on the

inclusion KADEK on USG list of Foreign Terrorist

Organizations. A translation follows.

Begin text:

The U.S. government announced that terrorist organization

PKK\'s newly adopted name KADEK is also included on the

terrorist organizations list. The above mentioned decision

of the U.S. government was welcomed and there is no doubt

that from the aspect of determined struggle in the

international platform against terrorism, this decision is an

important development and will cast an example ("will cast an

example" may also be translated as "will be exemplary").

It is expected/awaited that the U.S. decision concerning the

name change by the terrorist organization PKK done solely

with the purpose of getting away from the responsibility of

the actions and eliminating its terrorist identity, will be

assessed by other countries with the same sensitivity and

that they would consider including the organization on the EU

terrorist organizations list with its new name KADEK.

End text.
