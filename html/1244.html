SUBJECT: POST-ELECTION VIOLENCE CONTINUES MOSTLY UNABATED

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 HARARE 04389 081603Z

REF: HARARE 04362

CLASSIFIED BY CDA EARL M. IRVING FOR REASONS 1.5 (B) AND (D)

1. (C) THE AMANI TRUST, A RESPECTED HUMAN RIGHTS MONITORING

ORGANIZATION IN ZIMBABWE, RECENTLY RELEASED A REPORT

DETAILING THE CONTINUED VIOLENCE FOLLOWING THE JUNE 24-25

PARLIAMENTARY ELECTIONS. THE TRUST PUTS THE NUMBER OF

PEOPLE ASSAULTED OR OTHERWISE VICTIMS OF RIGHTS VIOLATIONS

IN THE POST-ELECTION PERIOD AT ALMOST 900. THESE INCLUDE 5

DEATHS, 232 SERIOUS ASSAULTS, 8 RAPES (NOTE: IT IS NOTEWORTHY

THAT SO MANY RAPES HAVE BEEN REPORTED; RAPE IS

TRADITIONALLY A TABOO SUBJECT, A SOCIAL STIGMA EXAGGERATED

BY THE HIV/AIDS CRISIS. END NOTE.), AND 18 DETENTIONS OR

ABDUCTIONS. THE NUMBER OF DOCUMENTED CASES OF VIOLENT

INCIDENTS AND DEATHS RISES DAILY; INCLUDED ARE MANY VICTIMS

WHO WENT UNDERGROUND BEFORE THE VOTING, FEARING

RETRIBUTION AND WHO ARE ONLY NOW APPEARING AT HOSPITALS

FOR TREATMENT OR AT NGOS TO REPORT THEIR EXPERIENCES. WE

BELIEVE, HOWEVER, THAT THESE FIGURES ARE STILL LOW, AS

INTIMIDATION CONTINUES TO RUN HIGH -- ESPECIALLY WHERE IT

CONCERNS ATTACKS THAT HAVE OCCURRED IN THE RURAL AREAS.

2. (C) IN THIS INCREASINGLY MURKY POLITICAL ENVIRONMENT, IT IS

DIFFICULT TO PINPOINT A PARTICULAR REASON FOR THE CONTINUED

VIOLENCE. THERE ARE PROBABLY MULTIPLE FACTORS. CAUSES

VARY FROM ANGER OVER LOST SEATS IN PARLIAMENT BY THE

RULING PARTY, FEAR OF EVICTION FROM OCCUPIED LAND, DESIRE

FOR LAND, OR EVEN RANDOM VIOLENT EXPRESSIONS OF

FRUSTRATION AT ZIMBABWE\'S UNENDING ECONOMIC WOES (METED

CONFIDENTIAL

OUT AGAINST SUPPOSED SUPPORTERS OF "WESTERN-LED", I.E., MDC

MEMBERS). OFFICIALS AT THE AMANI TRUST BELIEVE THAT THE WAR

VETERANS GROUPS TERRORIZING FARMERS, WORKERS, AND ALLEGED

OPPOSITION MEMBER SUPPORTERS ARE INCREASINGLY

AUTONOMOUS, PRESSING THEIR CAMPAIGN IN AN EFFORT TO HOUND

THE GOVERNMENT TO RECALL THAT THEY ARE THE ONES WHO

SWUNG THE RURAL VOTE FOR ZANU-PF CANDIDATES. EVEN IF THE

GOVERNMENT FEELS THOSE OCCUPYING THE LAND HAVE OUTLIVED

THEIR USEFULNESS AND DISTANCES ITSELF FOR THEM, THEIR

CONTINUED CAMPAIGN OF TERROR NEVERTHELESS MAKES CLEAR

THEIR ABILITY TO MAINTAIN A STATE OF CHAOS IN THE

COUNTRYSIDE. THIS, IN TURN, ENHANCES THEIR ROLE AS

POWERBROKERS.

3. (C) THE POLICE AND THE ARMY APPEAR TO BE PLAYING DIFFERENT

ROLES, DEPENDING ON WHERE IN THE COUNTRY IN THEY ARE

LOCATED. IN SOME LOCALES, POLICE AND ARMY OFFICERS ARE THE

PERPETRATORS OF VIOLENCE (VARYING ESTIMATES PUT THEIR

PARTICIPATION AT 20-30%), WHILE IN OTHERS, THEY HAVE

ATTEMPTED TO UPHOLD THE RULE OF LAW ONLY TO BE RENDERED

HAPLESS BY MENACING GROUPS OF ZANU-PF YOUTHS, WAR

VETERANS, OR EVEN "HIGHER POWER" GOVERNMENT OFFICIALS WHO

THREATEN TRANSFER OR DEMOTION. PRESS REPORTS ALSO DEPICT

THE CENTRAL INTELLIGENCE ORGANIZATION (CIO), ZIMBABWE\'S

EXTERNAL AND INTERNAL SECURITY AGENCY, AS EXPERIENCING

INTERNAL DIVISIONS OVER THE BEST WAY TO HANDLE THE VIOLENCE

- THOUGH THEIR ROLE NEVERTHELESS AS A CENTRAL

ORCHESTRATING AND FIELD MANAGEMENT ASSET REMAINS HIGH.

4. (C) COMMENT: WE BELIEVE THAT THIS VIOLENCE COULD BE

HALTED IF PRESIDENT MUGABE WISHED IT AND DECREED SO. HIS

DESIRE TO PUSH THE LAND ISSUE AS A FOCAL POINT FOR THE 2002

PRESIDENTIAL CAMPAIGN (REFTEL) ADDS FUEL TO THE FIRE, MAKES A

CREDENCE TO THE BELIEF BY MANY THAT THIS UGLY ISSUE WILL

DRAG ON FOR A LONG TIME TO COME. MEANWHILE, THE LIST OF

VICTIMS GROWS, AS DOES THE PUBLIC\'S SENSE OF FRUSTRATION AND

HELPLESSNESS. END COMMENT.
