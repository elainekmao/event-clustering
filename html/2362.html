SUBJECT: ZIMBABWE: PROBLEMS ON LAND DISTRIBUTION AND

DEEPER PROBLEMS ON HUMAN RIGHTS AND DEMOCRATIC PRACTICE

REF: ABUJA 005(NOTAL)

CLASSIFIED BY AMBASSADOR SULLIVAN FOR REASONS 1.5B/D

1. (C) WITH RESPECT TO PRESIDENT OBASANJO\'S COMMENTS ON

DEVELOPMENTS IN ZIMBABWE(REFTEL), WE OFFER THE FOLLOWING

COMMENTS ON THE LAND DISTRIBUTION PROGRAM AND CALL

ATTENTION TO THE INCREASED VIOLENCE, INTIMIDATION AND

VIOLATION OF THE RULE OF LAW WHICH THREATEN HUMAN RIGHTS

AND THE DEMOCRATIC PROCESS. WHILE THE ABUJA AGREEMENT

GAVE PRIORITY TO RESTORING THE RULE OF LAW IN THE LAND

REFORM PROCESS, IT ALSO CALLED FOR RESPECT FOR HUMAN

RIGHTS AND DEMOCRATIC PRINCIPLES. ORGANIZATION OF AN

ELECTION, WHICH IS PERCEIVED DOMESTICALLY AND

INTERNATIONALLY AS FREE AND FAIR, AND WHOSE RESULTS WILL

BE ACCEPTED AS LEGITIMATE, IS CRITICAL FOR ZIMBABWE\'S

FUTURE. THUS FAR, THE OPPOSITE HAS BEEN THE TREND AND

THE ACTIVE ENGAGEMENT OF ALL OF AFRICA\'S DEMOCRATICALLY

ELECTED LEADERS IN PRESSING FOR RESPECT FOR HUMAN AND

CIVIC RIGHTS AND A FREE AND FAIR ELECTIONS IS EXTREMELY

IMPORTANT.

ON LAND

-------

2.(C) SINCE THE ABUJA AGREEMENT, THE GOZ HAS STATED

REPEATEDLY THAT IT HAS COMPLIED FULLY WITH ITS ABUJA

COMMITMENTS, WHILE FAULTING THE UK AND OTHER POTENTIAL

DONORS FOR NOT PROVIDING "PROMISED" SUPPORT. THE REPORT

OF THE UNDP-LED MISSION FOCUSED ON LAND ASPECTS OF THE

ABUJA AGREEMENT IS EXPECTED THIS WEEK. WHILE IN

ZIMBABWE, THE TEAM, LED BY UNDP AFRICA DIRECTOR ABDOULIE

JANNEH, FOUND MANY FLAWS IN THE LAND DISTRIBUTION

PROCESS AND DOCUMENTED AN ENORMOUS DECLINE IN ACREAGE

BEING TILLED DUE TO THE CHAOTIC NATURE OF THE LAND

DISTRIBUTION PROCESS. SUBJECT TO ENORMOUS GOZ PRESSURE,

THE UNDP-LED TEAM REFRAINED FROM RELEASING A FORMAL

REPORT DURING THEIR VISIT, BUT WE WOULD EXPECT THE FINAL

REPORT TO SPEAK POLITELY, BUT FRANKLY, ABOUT THE MANY

FLAWS IN IMPLEMENTATION OF THE LAND DISTRIBUTION

PROGRAM.

3.(C) THE GOZ\'S BOAST THAT ITS LAND DISTRIBUTION PROGRAM

IS NOW TOTALLY IN COMPLIANCE WITH THE RULE OF LAW IS

BASED PRINCIPALLY ON THE CONTROVERSIAL JUDGMENT OF THE

RECONFIGURED SUPREME COURT REVERSING AN EARLIER SUPREME

COURT DECISION. WHETHER LEGAL UNDER NEW GOZ LEGISLATION

AND COURT RULINGS OR NOT, LAND DISTRIBUTION IS BEING

CARRIED OUT IN A LARGELY OPAQUE MANNER AND, IN MANY

CASES, TO THE BENEFIT OF ZANU-PF INSIDERS. THE ZIMBABWE

HUMAN RIGHTS NGO FORUM, A GROUP OF TEN PROMINENT NGO\'S

PUBLISHED ON DEC 17 A REPORT ON THE ABUJA AGREEMENT

(FOUND IN WWW:ZWNEWS.COM/ABUJA3.COM ). THE REPORT

CONCLUDED THAT "INVASIONS OF THE FARMS HAVE NOT CEASED

SINCE THE ABUJA AGREEMENT AND . IN SOME AREAS VIOLENCE

MAY EVEN HAVE WORSENED." THE REPORT OFFERS SUBSTANTIAL

DOCUMENTATION OF ITS ASSERTIONS.

ON HUMAN RIGHTS AND DEMOCRATIC PRINCIPLES

-----------------------------------------

4.(C) REGARDING THE GOZ\'S COMMITMENTS TO RESPECT HUMAN

RIGHTS AND DEMOCRATIC PRINCIPLES, THE ZIMBABWE HUMAN

RIGHTS NGO FORUM (OP. CIT.) CONCLUDED THAT, BEGIN QUOTE:

THE GOZ IS TAKING STEPS INIMICAL TO ITS ABUJA

COMMITMENTS. THE GOZ ACTIONS SUGGEST THAT IT INTENDS TO

HOLD ELECTIONS AGAINST A BACKGROUND OF ORGANIZED

VIOLENCE AND TORTURE AND WITHIN A FRAMEWORK INIMICAL TO

COMMON STANDARDS FOR FREE AND FAIR ELECTIONS. END QUOTE

5.(C) THESE ISSUES GO BEYOND DIFFERENCES OVER ELECTION

RULES OR ELECTION OBSERVATION. THE EMBASSY HAS SOLID

INFORMATION THAT THE GOZ AND ZANU-PF ARE MAKING

DELIBERATE USE OF WAR VETERANS AND GOVERNMENT-TRAINED

YOUTH BRIGADES TO INTIMIDATE, BEAT AND TORTURE PEOPLE

WHO ARE LINKED TO THE OPPOSITION OR WHO CANNOT PRODUCE

EVIDENCE OF ZANU-PF AFFILIATION. IN THE LAST SEVERAL

WEEKS, AT LEAST FIVE OPPOSITION MDC ACTIVISTS HAVE BEEN

KILLED BY SUCH GANGS. IN ADDITION, THE GOZ HAS

INTRODUCED NEW LEGISLATION WHICH WILL PROVIDE FOR HEAVY

STATE LIMITS ON FREEDOM OF INFORMATION, INTRODUCE NEW

PENALTIES IN THE NAME OF PUBLIC SECURITY AND MAKE THE

ELECTORAL PROCESS INCREASINGLY SUBJECT TO GOVERNMENT

CONTROL BY LIMITING INDEPENDENT OVERSIGHT.
