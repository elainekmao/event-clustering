Classified by Ambassador W. Robert Pearson. Reasons 1.5 b

and d.

1. (U) This is an urgent action request. See Para 3.

2. (S) During mil-mil discussions Jan. 24 on SOF planning for

northern Iraq, the TGS proposed to SOCEUR BG Jones a need for

Turkish, US, KDP, PUK and Turkmen military reps together to

discuss military coordination in northern Iraq. Turkish SOF

suggested a common understanding of each party\'s role would

be necessary to execute successfully.

3. (S) Action Request: We urgently request points by OOB

Ankara time Monday, Jan. 27, for the Ambassador to use with

the Turks explaining the role of each group involved in

northern Iraq during and after hostilities, and affirming

that we are ready now to begin discussions between Turkey and

the US to prepare for five-way (US-TU-KDP-PUK-Iraqi Turkmen

Front) talks the first week of February (during Amb.

Khalilzad\'s visit to Ankara). We would propose the first

agenda item of those talks be issuing political guidance to

military commanders of US and Turkish SOF and of KDP and PUK

forces to sit down together and coordinate their efforts in

northern Iraq. End Action Request.

PEARSON
