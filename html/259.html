SUBJECT: QADDHAFI MESSAGE TO KING HUSSEIN

REFS: (A) FBIS JN2001110089; (B) AMMAN 210;

(C) AMMAN 198

1. SAWT AL-SHA\'B (SEMI-OFFICIAL ARABIC LANGUAGE

DAILY) ON JANUARY 20 PRINTED WITHOUT COMMENT THE TEXT

OF A MESSAGE RECEIVED BY KING HUSSEIN BY LIBYAN

LEADER MU\'AMMAR QADHAFI (REF A). THE ITEM APPEARED

ON PAGE 16 OF THE PAPER, AND WAS NOT REPORTED IN THE

ENGLISH LANGUAGE PRESS. THE GOJ HAS MADE NO PUBLIC

COMMENT ON THE MESSAGE. BEGIN TEXT:

2. BROTHER KING HUSSEIN IBN TALAL, KING OF THE

FRATERNAL HASHEMITE KINGDOM OF JORDAN: ON BEHALF OF

YOUR LIBYAN BROTHERS AND SISTERS WHO ARE STRUGGLING

AGAINST THE UNJUST BIG POWERS, AND IN MY OWN NAME, I

GREET YOU AND THE MINISTERS OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS,

HEALTH, AND INFORMATION IN THE FRATERNAL HASHEMITE

KINGDOM OF JORDAN FOR YOUR FIRM STAND AGAINST THE

STATE TERRORISM AND REPEATED AGGRESSION AGAINST

AL-RABITAH VILLAGE AND KHALIJ AL-BUMBAH AFTER THE

GULF OF SIDRA. THE AGGRESSION WAS DEFEATED THANKS TO

THE ARAB NATION\'S UNIFIED STAND. THE FLEETS

RETREATED NOT BECAUSE OF AN EQUIVALENT LIBYAN

MILITARY FORCE, BUT BECAUSE OF AN INTERNATIONAL STAND

STEMMING FROM THE UNITY OF THE ARAB STAND. YOU HAVE

ADDED AN IMMORTAL AND SHINING EXAMPLE TO OUR ARAB

NATION\'S CONTEMPORARY HISTORY. THIS EXAMPLE WOULD

NOT HAVE BEEN SET BY ONE HAND ONLY, BUT WAS SET BY

THE HANDS OF ALL OF YOU, BECAUSE THIS IS AN HONOR TO

ALL OF YOU. YOU HAVE RETURNED TO THE ARAB NATION ITS

PRESTIGE AND SOVEREIGNTY BEFORE THE WORLD, THANKS TO

THIS UNFORGETTABLE UNIFIED STAND. I HOPE WE WILL

PLEDGE AS FROM NOW TO ACHIEVE THE ARAB NATION\'S UNITY

IN ALL FIELDS, ON ALL FRONTS, AND AT ALL BATTLES IN

THE ENTIRE ARAB HOMELAND. SIGNED: YOUR BROTHER,

COLONEL MU\'AMMAR AL-QADHAFI. END TEXT.

SUDDARTH
