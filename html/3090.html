SUBJECT: MFA VIEW ON POSSIBLE INDICTMENT OF DJUKANOVIC

REF: A) ROME 2660 B) BELGRADE 1399 C) BELGRADE 1371

CLASSIFIED BY: POL MC TOM COUNTRYMAN. REASON:1.5 (B)(D)

1. (C) AS PROMISED REF A, POLOFF FOLLOWED UP WITH THE

DIRECTOR OF THE MFA\'S BALKAN OFFICE, MARIZIO MASSARI, TO

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 ROME 02729 061321Z

REGISTER THE MINISTRY\'S VIEWS ON THE NEW INVESTIGATION AND

POSSIBLE INDICTMENT OF MONTENEGRIN PRESIDENT DJUKANOVIC BY

THE ANTI-MAFIA PUBLIC PROSECUTOR IN BARI, GIUSEPPE SCELSI, ON

CHARGES OF MAFIA CONSPIRACY FOR THE PURPOSE OF INTERNATIONAL

TRAFFICKING IN CONTRABAND CIGARETTES.

2. (C) MASSARI CONFIRMED REPORTS IN REFTELS THAT THE MFA

HAD NO PRIOR WARNING OF THE INVESTIGATION, NOR HAD IT BEEN

CONSULTED ON THE QUESTION OF IMMUNITY FROM PROSECUTION FOR

DJUKANOVIC. HE SURMISED THAT IF THERE WERE ANY POLITICAL

ANGLE TO THE LATEST DEVELOPMENTS IN THIS LONG RUNNING STORY,

THEY WOULD BE TIED TO INTERNAL ITALIAN POLITICS, NOT TO ANY

ATTEMPT BY THE GOI TO UNDERMINE DJUKANOVIC. THE GOI IS A

STRONG SUPPORTER OF THE EU BROKERED AGREEMENT BETWEEN

PODGORICA AND BELGRADE TO KEEP MONTENEGRO IN THE FEDERATION,

CONTINUED MASSARI, AND UNDERMINING DJUKANOVIC\'S CREDIBILITY

WITH A CRIMINAL INDICTMENT WOULD NOT, OBVIOUSLY, SERVE THAT

END.

3. (C) WHEN PRESSED ON THE INTERNAL POLITICAL FACTORS THAT

COULD BE DRIVING THE TIMING OF THE INVESTIGATION, MASSARI

SPECULATED THAT IT COULD BE AN ATTEMPT BY THE "LEFT-LEANING"

SCELSI TO "CAUSE EMBARRASSMENT" TO THE CURRENT GOVERNMENT.

MASSARI SAID THAT IT WAS NOT A COINCIDENCE THAT DETAILS OF

THE INVESTIGATION WERE ANNOUNCED IMMEDIATELY AFTER DEPUTY

MINISTER FOR PRODUCTIVE ACTIVITIES (TRADE) ADOLFO URSO\'S

VISIT TO PODGORICA, DURING WHICH HE PRAISED MONTENEGRO\'S

EFFORTS TO COMBAT ILLEGAL TRAFFICKING.
