INTERNATIONAL AFFAIRS), TO DR. LUISA TIMPONE (INTERNATIONAL

AFFAIRS DIRECTOR FOR THE ITALIAN CIVIL AVIATION AUTHORITY

ENAC) AND CONS. ARNALDO ABETI (FOREIGN MINISTRY DIRECTOR

RESPONSIBLE FOR INTERNATIONAL AVIATION ISSUES). DR. TIMPONE

WILL BE AT THE EU MEETING IN ATHENS ON JANUARY 24. DR.

SANSONE WILL BE AT THE ATHENS MEETING AND ALSO IN BRUSSELS ON

JANUARY 28.

3. THE RESPONSE WAS NON-COMMITTAL. SANSONE, TIMPONE AND

ABETI ALL SAID THAT THE GOI WOULD CONSIDER THE PROPOSAL

INTER-AGENCY AND DISCUSS IT WITH OTHER MEMBER STATES AND THE

COMMISSION. TIMPONE CAUTIONED THAT THE COMMISSION LIKELY

WOULD ARGUE STRONGLY AGAINST THE PROPOSED AMENDMENTS.
