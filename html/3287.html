SUBJECT: JORDAN: MEDIA REACTION ON IRAQ, OCCUPIED

TERRITORIES

SUMMARY

NEWS REPORTS IN JORDAN\'S DAILIES CONCENTRATED ON THE

KING ABDULLAH-PRESIDENT PUTIN\'S AFFIRMATION THAT THEIR

COUNTRIES REJECT MILITARY ACTION AGAINST IRAQ,

PRESIDENT BUSH\'S CONFIRMATION THAT THE U.S. WILL WORK

BY ALL MEANS TO CHANGE THE IRAQI REGIME, THE JORDANIAN

GOVERNMENT\'S ATTEMPT TO CALM THE CONTROVERSY

SURROUNDING ITS DECISION TO FACILITATE THE PASSAGE OF

PALESTINIANS FROM THE WEST BANK TO JORDAN, AND AN

INTERVIEW WITH JORDAN\'S AMBASSADOR DESIGNATE TO THE

U.S.

EDITORIAL WRITERS COMMENTED ON THE PRESIDENT\'S REMARK

ON IRAQ AND THE SITUATION IN THE OCCUPIED TERRITORIES

NEWS REPORTS

JORDANIAN DAILIES ON JULY 10 ALL LED WITH REPORTS ON

THE STATEMENT BY KING ABDULLAH AND RUSSIAN PRESIDENT

PUTIN, IN WHICH THEY AFFIRMED THEIR COUNTRIES\'

REJECTION OF A MILITARY STRIKE AGAINST IRAQ.

DAILIES ALSO QUOTED PRESIDENT BUSH\'S ASSERTION THAT THE

U.S. IS WORKING BY ALL MEANS TO CHANGE THE IRAQI

REGIME.

CENTER-LEFT, INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR

CARRIED A FEATURE ON THE IRAQI PRODUCTS FAIR IN AMMAN

THAT ATTRACTED A LARGE NUMBER OF PEOPLE, ACCORDING TO

THE REPORT.

THE MINISTER OF INTERIOR COMMENTED FOR THE SECOND TIME

IN THREE DAYS ON THE JORDANIAN GOVERNMENT\'S DECISION TO

FACILITATE THE PASSAGE OF PALESTINIANS COMING FROM THE

WEST BANK. HE STRESSED THAT PRIORITY WILL BE GIVEN TO

HUMANITARIAN CASES AND THAT JORDAN WILL CONTINUE

WORKING TO SUPPORT THE PALESTINIAN PEOPLE.

CENTER-LEFT, INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR

INTERVIEWED JORDAN\'S AMBASSADOR DESIGNATE TO THE U.S.

KARIM QAWAR, WHO SAID THAT THE WOULD WORK TO EXPAND

JORDAN-U.S. RELATIONS IN ALL FIELDS.

EDITORIAL COMMENTARIES

-- BUSH RAISES THE RED CARD

PROMINENT DAILY COLUMNIST DR. FAHED AL-FANEK WROTE IN

SEMI-OFFICIAL INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-RAI ON JULY

10: "BUSH IS DOES NOT RAISE THE RED CARD AS A FAIR

INTERNATIONAL REFEREE, BUT AS THE LEADER OF THE MOST

POWERFUL NATION ON EARTH, WHICH GIVES HIM NON-

NEGLIGIBLE WEIGHT. AT THE SAME TIME, NO ARAB REGIMES

WILL DEFEND ARAFAT\'S LEADERSHIP OF THE PALESTINIANS

BECAUSE NO ONE WANTS TO UPSET THE AMERICAN PRESIDENT OR

BET ON A LOSING HORSE. ARAFAT SAID WHILE UNDER SIEGE

IN RAMALLAH THAT HE WOULD NOT BE AN EXILE OR A

PRISONER, BUT A MARTYR. BUT MARTYRDOM IS NOT LIMITED

TO LAYING DOWN ONE\'S LIFE. IT CAN INCLUDE FORSAKING

ONE\'S POSITION. FRIENDS OF THE PALESTINIAN PEOPLE ARE

BORED WITH THEIR OWN WARNINGS AGAINST THE MONOPOLY OF

POWER AND CORRUPTION THAT THREATENED THE FUTURE OF THE

PALESTINIAN ENTITY. THESE WARNINGS WERE IGNORED,

PERMITTING THE ENEMIES OF THE PALESTINIAN PEOPLE TO USE

THE SITUATION CREATED BY ARAFAT TO DESTROY THE

PALESTINIAN DREAM."

-- NORMALIZATION WITH THE ENEMY BARS THE ROAD TO PEACE

TAHER AL-ADWAN, CHIEF EDITOR OF INDEPENDENT, MASS-

APPEAL ARABIC DAILY AL-ARAB AL-YAWM WROTE ON JULY 10:

"WHAT PRESIDENT BUSH DESCRIBED AS PROGRESS IN

PALESTINIAN REFORMS DOES NOT MATCH THE REALITY OF THE

SITUATION BECAUSE TALK OF REFORMS UNDER OCCUPATION IS

LIKE HAVING HAPPY DREAMS IN THE MIDDLE OF A GRAVEYARD.

THE PALESTINIAN-ISRAELI MEETINGS ARE NOT STEPS TOWARDS

A PEACE PROCESS, BUT FORCED PALESTINIAN NORMALIZATION

WITH THE OCCUPATION. THIS PALESTINIAN AND ARAB

NORMALIZATION WITH THE PRESENT REALITY WILL ONLY TEMPT

SHARON TO CONTINUE DESTROYING THE PEACE PROCESS WITH

THE PALESTINIANS. THE RETURN TO THE PEACE PROCESS WILL

NOT START WITH PALESTINIAN REFORMS BUT WITH

INTERNATIONAL LEGITIMACY AS EXPRESSED IN SECURITY

COUNCIL RESOLUTIONS FROM 242 TO 1397. IGNORING ISRAELI

SAVAGERY THAT IS JUSTIFIED BY THE NEED TO KEEP ISRAEL

SAFE WILL ONLY SPELL DANGER TO THE WHOLE REGION."

-- AMERICAN THREATS TO STRIKE IRAQ

THE LEADER COMMENTARY IN CENTER-LEFT, INFLUENTIAL

ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR ON JULY 10 SAID: "PRESIDENT

BUSH HAS STARTED AGAIN TO ISSUE THREATS OF A MILITARY

STRIKE AGAINST IRAQ. THIS TIME HE DOES NOT BOTHER TO

GIVE ANY PRETEXTS, BUT STATES OPENLY THE OBJECTIVE THAT

THE U.S. KEPT HIDING AND DENYING FOR 12 YEARS, WHICH IS

TO REPLACE THE IRAQI REGIME BY ONE MADE IN AMERICA. WE

REALIZE THAT OUR VOICES WILL BE DROWNED BY THE NOISE OF

AMERICAN WEAPONRY, BUT STILL WE WARN THAT THE RESULT OF

THE NEW AGGRESSION WILL HURT NOT ONLY 20 MILLION

IRAQIS, BUT ALL ARABS. IT WILL NOT SERVE EVEN THE

INTERESTS OF THE U.S. NOR ANYONE EXCEPT PERHAPS ISRAEL,

WHICH CONTINUES TO URGE WASHINGTON TO DESTROY IRAQ AND

WEAKEN THEN ARABS." GNEHM
