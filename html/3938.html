DAR MS. HEILEMAN,

REFERENCE IS MADE TO YOUR LETTER OF OCTOBER 1, 2002

REGARDING THE U.S. ASSESSED ONTRIBUTION TO ICCROM FOR

THE YEAR 2003. I AM PEASE TO PROVIDE THE INFORMATION

REQUESTED, AS FOLLOWS:

1. ICCROM DOES NOT HAVE ANY PROJECTS THAT PROVIDE

BENEFITS TO THE PALESTINIAN LIBERATION ORGANIZATION OR

ANY ENTITIES ASSOCIATED WITH IT, OR TO THE SOUTH WEST

AFRICA PEOPLE\'S ORGANIZATION. THUS, NO PORTION OF THE

ICCROM BUDGET FOR CALENDAR YEAR 2003 IS ALLOCATED FOR

SUCH PURPOSES.

THE ICCROM INVOICE FOR THE UNITED STATES CONTRIBUTION FOR

CALENDAR YEAR 2003 WILL BE SENT BEFORE THE END OF THE

CURRENT YEAR. IN THE MEANTIME, PLEASE NOTE THAT THE

ASSESSED CONTRIBUTION FOR 2003 IS USD 691,998. PLEASE

NOTE ALSO THAT OUR ACCOUNTS SHOW THAT THE UNITED STATES

HAS ARREARS IN THE AMOUNT OF USD 48,588.

I TRUST THAT THIS IS THE INFORMATION YOU REQUIRED.

SINCERELY,

P. RICHARD LINDO

CHIEF OPERATING OFFICER

ICCROM
