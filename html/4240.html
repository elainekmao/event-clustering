Sri Lanka. He is an advocate for a peaceful resolution

Ramadious with an exposure to the American system of

government and the role it plays in protecting human

rights.

NOMINATING-OFFICER: Ambassador Ashley Wills

START MEMO NOMINATING-JUSTIFICATION:

Dr. Ambalavanar is a civil activist who sincerely wants

peace and strikes as a reasonable person able to

entertain different points of view. Frankly his

sympathies lie with the Tigers but not because of their

tactics but because of their goal of defending Tamil

rights. Dr. Ambalavanar has been "discovered" by

various peace groups and recently traveled to

Switzerland to participate in a conference on Sri

Lanka\'s future. He thus has had exposure

internationally, but has never been to the United

States. Post certainly thinks he will be a very

prominent person in Jaffna post-peace. For the latter

reason, we believe it would be wise for us to invest in

him by sending him to the United States and exposing

him to our open society.

END MEMO NOMINATING-JUSTIFICATION:
