SUBJECT: FORMER PRESIDENT CANAAN BANANA SENTENCED TO

ONE-YEAR IMPRISONMENT FOR SODOMY

1. ON MAY 29, THE ZIMBABWE SUPREME COURT UPHELD A HIGH

COURT CONVICTION OF ZIMBABWE\'S FORMER NON-EXECUTIVE

PRESIDENT CANAAN BANANA (1980-1987), SENTENCING HIM TO

ONE-YEAR IMPRISONMENT FOR 11 COUNTS OF SEXUAL OFFENSES.

BANANA WAS CONVICTED IN THE HIGH COURT IN JANUARY 1999 FOR

11 COUNTS OF SODOMY, ARISING OUT OF CHARGES BROUGHT AGAINST

HIM IN 1997 BY HIS FORMER AIDE-DE- CAMP, JEFTA DUBE. THE

SODOMY CHARGES AGAINST BANANA FIRST SURFACE IN DURING

DURING DUBE\'S TRAIL FOR MURDERING A POLICEMAN IN 1997 WHO

HAD CALLED HIM BANANA\'S "WIFE." DUBE PLEADED MENTAL

INCAPACITY DUE TO THE YEARS OF SEXUAL ABUSE BY BANANA AND

WAS SENTENCED TO 10 YEARS IMPRISONMENT. FOLLOWING BANANA\'S

HIGH COURT CONVICTION, DUBE APPLIED FOR A PRESIDENTIAL

PARDON.

2. THE SUPREME COURT RULED THAT ALTHOUGH THE ZIMBABWE

CONSTITUTION DID NOT MAKE SODOMY A CRIME, THE PRACTICE WAS

ILLEGAL UNDER THE COMMON LAW. THE COURT, HOWEVER, REDUCED

THE HIGH COURT SENTENCES IMPOSED ON EACH OF THE 11 COUNTS,

SUSPENDING THE JAIL SENTENCES ON SEVERAL CHARGES OF SODOMY

AND INDECENT ASSAULT, ON THE CONDITION THAT BANANA NOT

COMMIT THE SAME CRIME IN THE NEXT THREE YEARS. BANANA WAS,

HOWEVER, SENTENCED TO FIVE YEARS FOR THE NUMEROUS

"UNNATURAL OFFENSES" AGAINST DUBE, WITH TWO YEARS SUSPENDED

FOR THREE YEARS ON THE CONDITION THAT HE PAID APPROXIMATELY

USD 89 RESTITUTION TO DUBE BY AUGUST 31. THE SUPREME COURT

EFFECTIVELY ONLY ORDERED THAT THE FORMER PRESIDENT SERVE

ONE-YEAR INCARCERATED.

3: BANANA\'S CONVICTION IS THE FIRST TIME A SENIOR

GOVERNMENT OFFICIAL HAS BEEN PROSECUTED FOR A CRIME

INVOLVING HOMOSEXUALITY. BANANA CONTINUES TO MAINTAIN THAT

HIS CONVICTION WAS "POLITICALLY MOTIVATED" AND HE HAS

DECLINED TO REVEAL WHETHER HE WOULD APPLY FOR A

PRESIDENTIAL PARDON. DURING BANANA\'S APPEAL PERIOD,

HOWEVER, WE HAVE SEEN NO EVIDENCE OF ANY HARASSMENT

DIRECTED AT THE FORMER PRESIDENT. SINCE JANUARY 1997

BANANA HAS BEEN FREE TO TRAVEL ABROAD FOR MEDICAL TREATMENT

AND HAS NOT BEEN SUBJECTED TO ANY EXTREME BAIL RESTRICTIONS

OR OTHER HARDSHIPS. NEVERTHELESS, PRESIDENT MUGABE HAS

CONSISTENTLY ATTACKED HOMOSEXUALS AS "PIGS AND DOGS" AND

HAS DESCRIBED SUCH SEXUAL ORIENTATION AS AGAINST AFRICAN

CULTURE. THIS VEHEMENT ANTI-GAY STANCE WILL MAKE IT

DIFFICULT FOR HIM TO NOW PARDON HIS CLOSE ALLY. ZIMBABWEAN

SOCIETY REMAINS GENERALLY HOSTILE TOWARDS GAY PEOPLE,

ALTHOUGH NOT VIOLENT, AS EVIDENCED IN THE PUBLIC\'S

RELUCTANCE TO ADDRESS HOMOSEXUAL CONCERNS DURING THE

NATIONAL DEBATES DURING LAST YEAR\'S CONSTITUTIONAL

REFERENDUM OUTREACH MEETINGS. FOLLOWING THE HIGH COURT

CONVICTION, MANY PEOPLE INDICATED THAT BANANA\'S SENTENCE

WAS TOO LENIENT, BUT EXPRESSED SATISFACTION THAT AT LEAST

HE DID NOT TOTALLY ESCAPE PUNISHMENT FOR HIS CRIMES.
