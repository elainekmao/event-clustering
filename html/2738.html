SUBJECT: (U) IIR 7 300 0410: "ZAHIR SHAH HOPES FOR A NEW

CLASSIFIED BY: POL MIN COUNS THOMAS COUNTRYMAN, REASON 1.5 (D)

1. (SBU) SUMMARY: POST WOULD LIKE TO CORRECT INFORMATION

PRESENTED IN REF IIR. THE SOURCE MAY HAVE REPORTED RELIABLY

IN THE PAST, AND MAY HAVE CONSIDERABLE KNOWLEDGE ABOUT SOME

ASPECTS OF AFGHANISTAN, BUT S/HE IS UNINFORMED ON THE FORMER

KING\'S PLANS AND THINKING. MUCH OF THE INFORMATION IS

SERIOUSLY FLAWED. END SUMMARY.

2. (C) FORMER KING ZAHIR SHAH POSTPONED (VICE CANCELED) HIS

SCHEDULED RETURN TO KABUL AT THE REQUEST AND RECOMMENDATION

OF THE GOVERNMENTS OF ITALY AND THE UNITED STATES. HE WAS

RELUCTANT TO DO SO AND INITIALLY RESISTED. THERE WERE NO

PRE-CONDITIONS TO HIS RETURN SET BY THE AFGHAN INTERIM

ADMINISTRATION (AIA). HE HAS NOT SOUGHT THE RESTORATION OF

THE MONARCHY AT ANY POINT IN HIS PREPARATIONS TO RETURN TO

AFGHANISTAN, AND HE HAS NEVER/NEVER SOUGHT "HEREDITARY

SUCCESSION...GUARANTEED BY THE CONSTITUTION."

3. (C) WHILE THE KING HAS PERHAPS EXPRESSED SOME VAGUE

NOSTALGIA FOR HIS ERSTWHILE PALACE, HE HAS NEVER INTIMATED TO

US THAT THE SMALL PALACE PROVIDED FOR HIM BY THE AIA IS AN

"INSULT TO HIS DIGNITY." TO THE CONTRARY, HE HAS SAID

SEVERAL TIMES AND IN DIFFERENT SETTINGS THAT FOR A FORMER

MONARCH TO RETURN TO THE PALACE IS UNDERSTANDABLY VERBOTEN.

ZAHIR SHAH KNOWS QUITE WELL WHO SUGGESTED GRANDSON MUSTAFA

NOT RETURN WITH HIM; THE MESSAGE WAS PERSONALLY DELIVERED TO

THE KING BY A SENIOR USG REPRESENTATIVE.

4. (C) THE KING\'S SON-IN-LAW AND SPOKESMAN TOLD US ONLY

YESTERDAY THAT THE KING IS NOT ILL-AT-EASE WITH PROVISIONS

FOR THE AIA TO PROVIDE FOR HIS SECURITY, AND THE KING HAS

TOLD US THIS HIMSELF SEVERAL TIMES. WHILE IT IS INDEED TRUE

THAT THE KING HAS BEEN IN CONTACT WITH THE ITALIAN GOVERNMENT

DURING THE LAST TWO WEEKS OF MARCH TO DISCUSS THE POSSIBILITY

OF THE GOI\'S PROVIDING SECURITY, THESE DISCUSSIONS BEGAN AT

THE REQUEST OF THE USG.

5. (S) FINALLY, WE DISAGREE COMPLETELY WITH FIELD COMMENTS

WHICH SUGGEST "THE KING WILL NEVER BE SATISFIED BY THE

SITUATION ON THE GROUND UNLESS HE SEES THE POSSIBILITY OF THE

RETURN OF THE MONARCHY." THIS IS PREPOSTEROUS WHEN VIEWED IN

THE CONTEXT OF THE KING\'S OWN REMARKS. HE HAS OFTEN STATED

HIS DEEP WISH TO RETURN TO AFGHANISTAN IN ORDER TO DIE THERE,

BUT PRIOR TO THAT TO SERVE THE PEOPLE OF AFGHANISTAN IN SOME

WAY IF SUCH IS THEIR WILL AS INDICATED THROUGH THE LOYA

JIRGA. EQUALLY, HE HAS REITERATED HIS UNDERSTANDING THAT THE

MONARCHY IS A DEFUNCT INSTITUTION. HE HAS GIVEN NO

INDICATION OF SEEKING TO REVIVE IT.
