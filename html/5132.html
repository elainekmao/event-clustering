SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION: OCCUPIED TERRITORIES, LONDON SUMMIT

1. SUMMARY: UAE AND REGIONAL MEDIA FOCUSED PRIMARILY ON IRAQ.

HOWEVER, EDITORIALS COMMENTED ON THE PALESTINIANS AND THE LONDON

CONFERENCE TO DISCUSS PALESTINIAN REFORMS. MEDIA CRITICIZED

ISRAEL\'S DECISION TO BAR SEVERAL PALESTINIANS FROM ATTENDING THE

CONFERENCE WHILE ALLOWING THEIR PARTICIPATION VIA DIGITAL VIDEO

CONFERENCE. AN "AL ITTIHAD" EDITORIAL ARGUED THAT ISRAELI

SOCIETY BELIEVES THE ELIMINATION OF THE PALESTINIAN PEOPLE IS THE

ONLY SOLUTION TO THE PALESTINIAN ISSUE. LIKEWISE, AN "AKHBAR AL

ARAB" EDITORIAL DESCRIBED THE LONDON CONFERENCE AS A CHEAP

ATTEMPT TO BRIBE ARABS BEFORE THE WAR ON IRAQ.

2. COMMENTING ON THE LABOR PARTY\'S SURPRISE FOR THE POPULARITY

OF THE LIKUD PARTY, ABU DHABI-BASED SEMI OFFICIAL "AL ITTIHAD"

EDITORIALIZED ON 1/15:

"... THEY HAVE NO RIGHT TO WONDER WHY SHARON\'S POPULARITY

INCREASES, SIMPLY BECAUSE THIS REFLECTS THE GENERAL MOOD OF AN

ISRAELI SOCIETY BENT ON WAR. THIS SOCIETY BELIEVES POWER AND

BLOODSHED ARE THE ONLY SOLUTION FOR THE PALESTINIAN ISSUE,

INCLUDING THE ELIMINATION OF THE PALESTINIAN PEOPLE."

3. COMMENTING ON THE LONDON CONFERENCE TO REFORM THE PALESTINIAN

AUTHORITY, ABU DHABI-BASED PAN-ARAB "AKHBAR AL ARAB"

EDITORIALIZED ON 1/15:

"... WHAT WE ARE AFRAID OF IS THAT THE LONDON CONFERENCE IS A

CHEAP ATTEMPT TO BRIBE ARABS (TO SUPPORT) THE U.S.- U.K. MILITARY

BUILD-UP BEFORE THE WAR ON IRAQ. IT IS MORE PRACTICAL FOR THE

LONDON CONFERENCE PARTICIPANTS AND THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY TO

FIND A NEW FORMULA TO REBUILD THE INSTITUTIONS DESTROYED BY

OCCUPATION BEFORE STARTING A SURREAL DIALOGUE ON WHO IS

RESPONSIBLE FOR REFORMING THE PALESTINIAN AUTHORITY."
