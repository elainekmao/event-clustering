SUBJECT: UAE SUCCESSFULLY COMMEMORATES INTERNATIONAL

EDUCATION WEEK

1. SUMMARY: AT A TIME WHEN THE U.S.-ARAB EDUCATIONAL EXCHANGE

IS THREATENED, PUBLIC AFFAIRS OFFICE ORGANIZED THREE MAIN

ACTIVITIES TO PROMOTE U.S. EDUCATION AND CULTURAL EXCHANGES

DURING INTERNATIONAL EDUCATION WEEK. PROGRAMS ADDRESSED THE

CONCERNS OF UAE STUDENTS, PARENTS, AND ACADEMICS WITH REGARD TO

TRAVELING TO THE U.S. PROGRAMS ALSO OPENED NEW CHANNELS OF

DISCUSSIONS AND COOPERATION WITH LEADERS IN EDUCATION AND CULTURE

WHO BELIEVE IN THE QUALITIES OF U.S. EDUCATION AND WHO ENCOURAGE

INTERNATIONAL UNDERSTANDING OF U.S. SOCIETY AND VALUES. END

SUMMARY.

2. PAO TOOK THE OPPORTUNITY OF INTERNATIONAL EDUCATION WEEK TO

HONOR DR. ALI RASHED AL NOAIMI, DEPUTY VICE CHANCELLOR FOR

ACADEMIC AFFAIRS, UAE UNIVERSITY. ON NOVEMBER 20TH, AMBASSADOR

WAHBA PRESENTED DR. AL NOAIMI WITH A CERTIFICATE OF APPRECIATION

AT HER RESIDENCE FOR HIS SUSTAINED EFFORTS AND CONTINUOUS

COOPERATION WITH THE US EMBASSY IN ABU DHABI AND THE CONSULATE

GENERAL IN DUBAI, ON EDUCATIONAL AND CULTURAL AFFAIRS AND FOR HIS

SUPPORT FOR INTERNATIONAL UNDERSTANDING THROUGH US-UAE

EDUCATIONAL EXCHANGES, PARTICULARLY THE FULBRIGHT PROGRAM. DR.

NOAIMI APPRECIATED THE RECOGNITION, NOTING THAT THE UAE

UNIVERSITY VIEWS THE U.S. AS THE PREMIER DESTINATION FOR ITS

STUDENTS AND GRADUATES, WITH BETWEEN 60 TO 75 PERCENT OF UAE

NATIONAL STUDENTS BENEFITING FROM THE U.S. EDUCATIONAL SYSTEM.

THE PRESENTATION RECEIVED PROMINENT COVERAGE IN ALL MAJOR LOCAL

ENGLISH AND ARABIC NEWSPAPERS. HEADLINES INCLUDED: "U.S. EMBASSY

HONOURS UAE EDUCATOR" (GULF NEWS), "IN APPRECIATION OF HIS

EFFORTS TO IMPROVE CULTURAL RELATIONS, THE U.S. EMBASSY HONOURS

DR. ALI AL NOAIMI" (AL KHALEEJ), AND "THE U.S. AMBASSADOR

EMPHASIZES THE IMPORTANCE OF EDUCATION TO ENHANCE COMMUNICATIONS

BETWEEN CULTURES" (AL ITTIHAD).

3. PAO EDUCATIONAL ADVISORS CONDUCTED AN OUTREACH VISIT TO RAS

AL KHAIMAH (RAK) EMIRATE, ONE OF THE POOREST AND LEAST PRIVILEGED

EMIRATES IN THE UAE. ADVISORS MET WITH THE PRESIDENT OF AL-

ITTIHAD UNIVERSITY, THE CULTURAL ADVISOR TO RAK CROWN PRINCE, THE

PRESIDENT OF RAK TEACHERS\' ASSOCIATION, AND THE DIRECTOR OF RAK

EDUCATION ZONE. ADVISORS DISCUSSED SERVICES AND ACTIVITIES OF

PAO AND IDENTIFIED AREAS OF COOPERATION WITH THE EMIRATE IN THE

EDUCATIONAL, CULTURAL AND INFORMATION FIELDS. THE ACADEMICS

EXPRESSED A DESIRE TO COOPERATE WITH US ON ALL LEVELS BUT FOUND

DIFFICULTY SEPARATING PEOPLE\'S DEMAND FOR U.S. EDUCATION FROM THE

CURRENT TENSE POLITICAL SITUATION IN THE REGION. THEY WERE VERY

INTERESTED IN OUR CULTURAL PROGRAMS AND IN ENCOURAGING STUDENTS

TO TRAVEL TO THE U.S. BUT ALSO EXPRESSED CONCERN FOR CURRENT U.S.

FOREIGN POLICY IN THE REGION, U.S. SECURITY MEASURES, AND NEW

VISA REGULATIONS NEGATIVELY INFLUENCING STUDENTS AND PARENTS

DESIRE TO STUDY OR TRAVEL IN THE U.S. ALTHOUGH THESE MUSLIM

ACADEMICS WERE RELIGIOUSLY CONSERVATIVE, THEY DID NOT HESITATE TO

EXPRESS THEIR RESPECT AND SUPPORT FOR U.S. EDUCATION AND CULTURE.

4. PAO EDUCATIONAL ADVISORS, AS PART OF THEIR OUTREACH IN RAK,

CONDUCTED A POWERPOINT PRESENTATION ON UNDERGRADUATE EDUCATION IN

THE U.S. FOR 30 HIGH SCHOOL STUDENTS AT RAK ENGLISH SPEAKING

SCHOOL. PRIOR TO THE PRESENTATION, ADVISORS VISITED THE OFFICE

OF THE CROWN PRINCE AND SPONSOR OF THE SCHOOL WHO WELCOMED OUR

VISIT TO THE SCHOOL AND STRESSED THE IMPORTANCE OF EDUCATION IN

BUILDING BRIDGES AND CONNECTING PEOPLE. THE SCHOOL IS VERY WELL-

ESTABLISHED IN THE EMIRATE, OFFERS THE BRITISH CURRICULUM AND IS

CURRENTLY SEEKING U.S. ACCREDITATION FROM NEW ENGLAND ASSOCIATION

OF SCHOOLS AND COLLEGES.

5. ABU DHABI-BASED SEMI GOVERNMENT ARABIC DAILY, "AL-ITTIHAD",

PUBLISHED FOR THREE CONSECUTIVE DAYS ARTICLES ON U.S. EDUCATION,

APPLICATION PROCEDURES, AND EDUCATIONAL WEBSITES. THESE ARTICLES

WERE PROVIDED BY PAO EDUCATIONAL ADVISORS IN AN EFFORT TO UTILIZE

THE MEDIA TO PROMOTE U.S. EDUCATION AMONG A WIDER AUDIENCE.

FOLLOWING THESE INSERTIONS, THE NEWSPAPER AND THE PAO ADVISORS

RECEIVED INQUIRIES AND REQUESTS TO PLACE OTHER ARTICLES ON U.S.

EDUCATION.

6. RESULTS: EXCELLENT. THROUGH SUSTAINED EFFORTS AND OUTREACH

TO PROMOTE U.S. EDUCATION AND CULTURE, WE WERE ABLE TO REACH OUT

TO A LARGE AUDIENCE WHO ARE VERY RECEPTIVE WHEN IT COMES TO

EDUCATION DESPITE THEIR ATTITUDE TOWARDS U.S. FOREIGN POLICY.

EDUCATIONAL OUTREACH HAS PROVEN, ONCE AGAIN, TO BE A VERY

EFFECTIVE TOOL IN BRIDGING THE WIDENING GAP THAT SEPTEMBER 11

CREATED BETWEEN THE U.S. AND MANY ARAB NATIONS.

WAHBA
