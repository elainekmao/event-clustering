1. (C) GUIDANCE REQUEST. SEE PARA 3.

2. (C) THE MFA\'S BALKANS OFFICE DIRECTOR, MAURIZIO MASSARI,

TOLD POLOFF ON MARCH 27 THAT THE GOI WILL MAKE A FINANCIAL

CONTRIBUTION TO THE INTERNATIONAL ORGANIZATION FOR

MIGRATION\'S PROGRAM TO HELP REINTEGRATE THE 10,000 BIH

SOLDIERS TO BE DISCHARGED BY 1 APRIL. MASSARI WAS AWARE OF

THE IOM\'S PROGRAM (AS DESCRIBED IN REFTEL), AND THE GOI IS

VERY SUPPORTIVE OF THE INITIATIVE. MASSARI DID NOT HAVE AN

AMOUNT OR TIMING ON THE CONTRIBUTION, BUT ASSURED POLOFF THAT

THE ISSUE WAS UNDER DISCUSSION IN THE MFA\'S ECONOMIC

COOPERATION DEPARTMENT. THE REINTEGRATION FUNDS, ALONG WITH

OTHER PROJECTS AIMED AT BOSNIAN YOUTHS AND ESTABLISHING

COMMUNITY CENTERS, WERE HIGH ON THE LIST OF THE GOI\'S

PRIORITIES FOR ASSISTANCE TO BOSNIA.

3. (C) TURNING TO ALBANIA, MASSARI THOUGHT THAT IT WOULD BE

USEFUL TO THE NEW MAJKO GOVERNMENT TO GET A "SHOW OF SUPPORT"

FROM THE WEST. MASSARI WILL ATTEND THE APRIL 17 FRIENDS OF

ALBANIA MEETING IN VIENNA, AND WILL BE CRAFTING A GOI

STATEMENT WHICH, WHILE ACKNOWLEDGING ALBANIA\'S PROBLEMS, WILL

CONTAIN POSITIVE ELEMENTS TO BOLSTER MAJKO. MASSARI

ACKNOWLEDGED POLOFF\'S POINT THAT THERE WAS NOT MUCH NEWS

COMING OUT OF TIRANA TO CHEER ABOUT, PARTICULARLY IN THE AREA

OF ECONOMIC REFORM, BUT INSISTED THAT IT WAS IMPORTANT TO

GIVE A POSITIVE SIGNAL, PERHAPS JUST AN ACKNOWLEDGMENT OF

ALBANIA\'S RELATIVE POLITICAL STABILITY. MASSARI ASKED

WHETHER THE U.S. COULD ALSO DELIVER A STATEMENT WITH A

"POSITIVE TONE" AT THE FOA. POLOFF PROMISED TO CHECK WITH

WASHINGTON AND GET BACK TO MASSARI.

4. (C) MASSARI SAID THAT ANOTHER WAY TO GIVE SUPPORT TO THE

MAJKO GOVERNMENT WOULD BE THE MAINTENANCE OF AN INTERNATIONAL

MILITARY PRESENCE IN ALBANIA, SPECIFICALLY THE APPROXIMATELY

1350 NATO FORCES (1200 OF WHOM ARE ITALIAN) CURRENTLY

DEPLOYED TO ALBANIA IN COMMZ WEST . MASSARI WAS AWARE OF

DISCUSSIONS IN BRUSSELS TO RESTRUCTURE FORCES DEPLOYED IN THE

BALKANS, BUT HOPED THAT THERE WOULD BE NO SIGNIFICANT

REDUCTION IN ALBANIA. MASSARI SAID THAT THE GOI WOULD BE

MAKING A FORMAL DEMARCHE ON THIS ISSUE IN BRUSSELS AND NATO

CAPITALS SOMETIME SOON.
