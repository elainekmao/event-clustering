1. (C) SUMMARY. THE GOZ HAS TO A LARGE DEGREE

SUCCEEDED IN ITS EFFORTS TO KEEP PRYING OUTSIDE EYES

AWAY FROM THE PRE-ELECTION CAMPAIGN FIELD AND, IT NOW

APPEARS, OUT OF THE POLLING STATIONS ON VOTING DAY. BY

DERAILING THE EFFORTS OF THE EUROPEAN UNION MISSION AND

THE ZIMBABWE ELECTIONS SUPPORT NETWORK (ZESN) DOMESTIC

OBSERVERS, THE GOZ HAS ASSURED SPOTTY COVERAGE BUT HAS

ALSO, INADVERTENTLY OR NOT, STRIPPED ITSELF OF ANY FIG

LEAF AGAINST POST-ELECTION CHARGES THAT THE ELECTION WAS

NOT PROPERLY OBSERVED. THOSE OBSERVERS OUT IN THE

FIELD, AS OPPOSED TO HARARE\'S LUXURY HOTELS, RANGE FROM

CRITICS TO OBJECTIVISTS TO APOLOGISTS. A NUMBER OF SADC

BILATERAL DELEGATIONS HAVE ALREADY SHOWN THEIR PRO-ZANU-

PF COLORS, AND THE FINAL STANCES OF LARGER AFRICAN

GROUPS SUCH AS NIGERIA AND SOUTH AFRICA REMAIN

UNCERTAIN.

2. (C) THE EMBASSY\'S OWN MODEST IN-HOUSE EFFORT HAS

BEEN AS OBJECTIVE AS POSSIBLE IN COVERAGE. WE EXPECT

THAT ALL OF THE CREDIBLE REPORTS THAT EMERGE IN THE WAKE

OF THE ELECTIONS WILL ADEQUATELY DOCUMENT INSTANCES OF

CONFIDENTIAL

INTIMIDATION, VIOLENCE, AND ELECTORAL CHICANERY. THE

STATEMENT BY THE USG ON THE OUTCOME WILL BE SUFFICIENTLY

SUPPORTED BY FACT THAT WE CAN FEEL CONFIDENT THAT OUR

STATEMENT WILL NOT BE UNDERCUT BY WHITEWASHING OR LACK

OF DETAIL IN REPORTS FROM OTHER, LESS RELIABLE

DELEGATIONS. END SUMMARY.

THE EARLY CASUALTIES

--------------------

3. (C) THE GOZ HAS PURSUED A DUAL STRATEGY OF BLOCKAGE

AND "DIVIDE AND CONQUER" IN ITS EFFORTS TO KEEP

POTENTIALLY UNSYMPATHETIC ELECTION OBSERVATION MISSIONS

OUT OF THE FRAY. THE FIRST CASUALTY WAS THE

INTERNATIONAL FOUNDATION FOR ELECTION SYSTEMS (IFES),

WHICH WAS UNCEREMONIOUSLY EXPELLED LAST SEPTEMBER IN THE

MIDDLE OF ITS ASSESSMENT MISSION. SUBSEQUENT CAT-AND-

MOUSING WITH THE ZIMBABWEAN EMBASSY IN WASHINGTON LED

NOWHERE.

4. (C) THE EU AND ITS PROPOSED 150-STRONG TEAM OF

OBSERVERS WERE NEXT. THE GOZ SOUGHT TO CREATE A

NORDIC/MEDITERRANEAN RIFT (AND SUCCEEDED TO SOME DEGREE)

BY ALLOWING ONLY NINE MEMBER STATES TO SEND BILATERAL

REPRESENTATIVES UNDER THE TITULAR AUTHORITY OF THE ACP.

WHEN THE EU ACQUIESCED TO SOME DEGREE AND SOUGHT A

COMPROMISE SOLUTION, THE GOZ RESPONDED BY EXPELLING EU

MISSION HEAD PIERRE SCHORRI. THE REMAINDER OF THE

DELEGATION SUBSEQUENTLY WITHDREW, AND THE EU MISSIONS

LOCATED HERE, IN A FINAL IRONIC GESTURE OF SOLIDARITY,

AGREED THAT NONE OF THEIR NUMBER WOULD SEEK

ACCREDITATION TO OBSERVE.

AGREED TWO DAYS BEFORE THE ELECTIONS TO ACCREDIT A TOTAL

OF 420 ZESN OBSERVERS, ONE OBSERVER PER EACH TEN POLLING

STATIONS.

WHAT\'S LEFT

-----------

7. (C) BY MAINTAINING FOREIGN MINISTRY CONTROL OVER

WHAT ORGANIZATIONS/COUNTRIES WERE INVITED, THE GOZ HAS

MANAGED TO BRING IN SEVERAL HUNDRED MONITORS FROM TWO

DOZEN DIFFERENT POINTS. THE GOZ\'S INSISTENCE THAT UNDP

SHOULD NOT PLAY A COORDINATING ROLE HAS ENSURED THAT

THERE WOULD BE NO CENTRAL COORDINATION, ALTHOUGH THERE

HAS BEEN AD HOC COORDINATION AMONG THE MORE SERIOUS

GROUPS. DELAYS WITH INVITATIONS AND A CUMBERSOME

CREDENTIALING PROCEDURE HAVE FURTHER SLOWED THE ARRIVAL

AND DEPLOYMENT OF OBSERVERS TO THE FIELD. LATECOMERS,

MANY OF THEM WITH QUESTIONABLE OBSERVER CREDENTIALS,

HAVE ARRIVED TOO LATE TO ENGAGE IN MUCH MORE THAN

ELECTORAL TOURISM. WE HAVE ALSO OBSERVED THAT 24 HOURS

BEFORE THE POLLS ARE SLATED TO OPEN, THE LUXURY HOTELS

OF HARARE REMAIN CRAMMED WITH UNDEPLOYED INTERNATIONAL

OBSERVERS OF VARIOUS STRIPE.

8. (C) THE VAST MAJORITY OF INTERNATIONAL OBSERVERS ARE

AFRICAN, AN INTEGRAL PART OF MUGABE\'S STRATEGY TO

PORTRAY THE CENTRAL ELECTORAL ISSUE AS WHITE COLONIALIST

LAND HOLDERS VERSUS INDIGENOUS BLACK AFRICAN PEASANTS

SEEKING TO REGAIN THEIR AFRICAN BIRTHRIGHT. MUGABE HAS

SOUGHT TO DIVIDE AND CONQUER ON ANOTHER LEVEL BY

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 HARARE 00620 02 OF 05 081222Z

PORTRAYING WESTERN CRITICISM OF ELECTORAL VIOLENCE AND

THE BREAKDOWN OF THE RULE OF LAW AS A MANIFESTATION OF

SUPPORT BY WHITES FOR WHITES. THIS HAS RESONATED TO

SOME DEGREE WITHIN AFRICA. TO DATE, MOST COUNTRIES AND

AFRICAN MULTILATERAL GROUPS HAVE PUBLICLY TAKEN A SOFT

LINE. THE GOZ ALSO MANAGED TO DRIVE A WEDGE INTO CHOGM,

WITH AFRICAN AND WESTERN STATES DIVIDED AND FORCED INTO

A COMPROMISE TO ACT AFTER THE ELECTIONS.

9. (C) THERE WILL BE AS MANY POINTS OF VIEW AS THERE

ARE GROUPS IN THE AFTERMATH OF THE VOTE. SEVERAL OF THE

SADC STATE DELEGATIONS WILL SERVE AS APOLOGISTS FOR THE

ZANU-PF VICTORY THAT THEY CLEARLY FAVOR. OTHERS, SUCH

AS THE NORWEGIANS, WILL HEW TO A HARDER, MORE OBJECTIVE

LINE. IN BETWEEN REMAIN THE OTHERS, MANY OF WHICH HAVE

NOT GIVEN A CLEAR INDICATION OF WHERE THEY WILL COME

DOWN. OUR PREDICTIONS, ADMITTEDLY INTUITIVE, FOLLOW.

SOUTH AFRICA OBSERVATION MISSION (SAOM)_

---------------------------------------

10. (C) THE LARGEST OBSERVER MISSION BY FAR, SAOM IS

HARD TO SUM UP DUE TO ITS DISPARATE NATURE. UNIONISTS,

ANC REPRESENTATIVES, OPPOSITION FIGURES, AND

PARLIAMENTARIANS ALL FIGURE INTO THIS. SEVERAL CHURCH

AND CIVIL SOCIETY GROUPS WERE REFUSED CREDENTIALS AND

FORCED TO RETURN TO SOUTH AFRICA, IN YET ANOTHER EXAMPLE

OF GOZ DIVIDE AND CONQUER. SOME SOUTH AFRICAN DELEGATES

HAVE TAKEN A HARD LINE WITH US IN PRIVATE ON ZANU-PF.

OTHERS HAVE EVINCED PUBLIC SYMPATHY TOWARD MUGABE AND

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 04 HARARE 00620 02 OF 05 081222Z

SUGGESTED THAT IT IS THE DUTY OF THE OPPOSITION MOVEMENT

FOR DEMOCRATIC CHANGE (MDC) TO ACCEPT WHATEVER OUTCOME

IS ANNOUNCED, NO MATTER HOW TILTED THE ELECTION FIELD

MIGHT HAVE BEEN.

11. (C) WE UNDERSTAND THAT THE SAOM HEAD RETURNED HOME

FOR GUIDANCE ON HOW TO DEAL WITH THE WIDELY DIVERGENT

VIEWS OF HIS GROUP; HE WAS SENT BACK WITH THE

INSTRUCTION TO FIND A CONSENSUS. A FURTHER COMPLICATION

IS THE HEAVY PRESENCE OF SOUTH AFRICAN REPRESENTATIVES

ON THE SADC-PF, COMMONWEALTH, AND OAU DELEGATIONS.

GIVEN THIS COMPLEX LINE-UP, IT IS DIFFICULT TO JUDGE

WHERE THE SOUTH AFRICANS WILL COME OUT, EITHER BY GROUP

OR COLLECTIVELY. HOWEVER, BASED ON STATEMENTS

QUESTIONING THE MDC\'S ABILITY TO GOVERN ZIMBABWE, WE DO

NOT EXPECT THE SOUTH AFRICANS TO TAKE A HARD-LINE IF

ZANU-PF IS ANNOUNCED THE VICTOR, ALTHOUGH THEY WILL

UNDOUBTEDLY CRITICIZE VIOLENCE, INTIMIDATION, AND

PROCEDURAL FLAWS.

12. (C) CHIEF ERNEST SHONEKAN LED APPROXIMATELY 20

NIGERIAN OBSERVERS TO THE FIELD LESS THAN A WEEK BEFORE

THE ELECTION WAS TO TAKE PLACE. THEY HAVE DEPLOYED TO

THE FIELD AND HAVE WITNESSED SOME OF THE VIOLENCE AND

PROCEDURAL IRREGULARITIES FIRST-HAND, ACCORDING TO

REPORTS FROM ACCOMPANYING NIGERIAN DIPLOMATS. NIGERIA

IS ALSO WELL REPRESENTED ON THE COMMONWEALTH DELEGATION.

THE AMBASSADOR\'S MEETING WITH SHONEKAN WAS NOT

PARTICULARLY REASSURING (SEPTEL), AND WE CANNOT AT THIS

JUNCTURE TELL WHERE THE NIGERIANS WILL COME OUT ON THE

COMPORTMENT OF THE ELECTIONS, BUT IT IS LIKELY TO

REPRESENT A NIGERIAN GOVERNMENT DECISION OF WHAT POSTURE

TO TAKE. PRESIDENT OBASANJO\'S PRESENCE ON THE THREE-

PERSON COMMONWEALTH REVIEW PANEL WILL ENSURE THAT

WHATEVER THE OUTCOME, THE NIGERIANS WILL REMAIN CLOSELY

ENGAGED.

COMMENT

-------

23. (C) IT IS OUR CONCLUSION THAT DUE TO GOZ

SHENANIGANS, THE OVERALL ASSESSMENT OF THE PRE-ELECTION

ENVIRONMENT HAS BEEN INADEQUATE. ONLY FOUR OF THE

GROUPS INVOLVED -- COMMONWEALTH, SAOM, US, AND SADC-PF --

HAVE GOTTEN ENOUGH PEOPLE INTO THE FIELD FOR A

SUFFICIENT TIME TO BE ABLE TO DRAW ACCURATE CONCLUSIONS,

IF THESE GROUPS SO CHOOSE. ELECTION DAY WILL BE EQUALLY

HIT AND MISS LARGELY DUE TO THE BLOCKAGE OF THE ZESN

DOMESTIC OBSERVERS. THE GOZ MAY HAVE BEEN SUCCESSFUL IN

OBSCURING TO SOME DEGREE WHAT THEY HAVE DONE IN THE

LEAD-UP TO THE ELECTION, AND PROBABLY ON ELECTION DAY AS

WELL. HOWEVER, IN THE PROCESS THEY HAVE ALSO OPENED

THEMSELVES TO POST-ELECTION ACCUSATIONS THAT NEITHER THE

PRE-ELECTION ENVIRONMENT NOR ELECTION DAY ACTIVITIES ADD

UP TO A VOTE, AND AN ANNOUNCED RESULT, THAT CAN BE

JUDGED AS FREE AND FAIR. WE BELIEVE THAT THERE WILL BE

MORE THAN SUFFICIENT DOCUMENTARY EVIDENCE ON HAND TO
