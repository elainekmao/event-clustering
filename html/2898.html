1. (C) SUMMARY: VATICAN MFA RUSSIA DIRECTOR TRICARICO IS

UNHAPPY AND AGITATED ABOUT THE GOR REFUSAL OF VISAS TO

CATHOLIC CLERGY, ESPECIALLY TO BISHOP MAZUR. HE BELIEVES THE

HIGHEST LEVEL OF THE GOR MUST HAVE BEEN AWARE OF THE NEW

POLICY TO REJECT CATHOLIC CLERGY VISAS. HE SAID THE POPE AND

HIS CHURCH WANT RECONCILIATION WITH THE RUSSIAN ORTHODOX, BUT

THAT AFTER 80 YEARS OF PERSECUTION AND A FURTHER DECADE OF

ONE-SIDED ATTEMPTS AT RAPPROCHEMENT, THEY HAVE YET TO SEE

PROGRESS. NEVERTHELESS, THE CHURCH WILL CONTINUE ITS

MINISTRY IN RUSSIA, AND CONTINUE TO PURSUE A RAPPROCHEMENT

WITH THE RUSSIAN ORTHODOX.

---------------------------

AGITATED OVER VISA REFUSALS

---------------------------

2. (C) POLOFF MET 05/02 WITH VATICAN MFA RUSSIA/NIS DIRECTOR

ARCHBISHOP ALBERTO TRICARICO TO DISCUSS VATICAN/RUSSIAN

RELATIONS IN WAKE OF EXPULSION OF TWO CLERGY MEMBERS IN

RECENT DAYS. TRICARICO, ONE OF THE MOST SENIOR OF VATICAN

MFA "COUNTRY DIRECTORS," WAS AGITATED, SAYING THE HOLY SEE

HAD BENT OVER BACKWARDS TO ACCOMMODATE RUSSIAN ORTHODOX

PATRIARCH ALEXIY, BUT THERE HAD BEEN NO POSITIVE RESPONSE.

HE SAID THE GOR AVERRED CANCELLATION OF VISAS FOR THE TWO

CLERGY WAS AN ACT OF "SOVEREIGNTY," BUT THE VATICAN WAS STILL

WAITING FOR AN EXPLANATION OF THE LEGAL BASIS FOR IT. HE WAS

PARTICULARLY UPSET THAT THE GOR HAD DENIED BISHOP MAZUR THE

RIGHT TO RETURN TO HIS FLOCK. TRICARICO UNDERSCORED THAT THE

REFUSAL OF A VISA TO A BISHOP WAS TAKEN VERY SERIOUSLY BY THE

VATICAN -- IN THIS CASE IT WAS A "HISTORICAL ACT" WHICH

DEFIED THE HOLY SEE\'S 1989 AGREEMENT WITH THE SOVIET UNION ON

RELIGIOUS FREEDOM. HE BELIEVED THAT THE GOR WOULDN\'T REFUSE

A VISA TO A BISHOP WITHOUT HIGHEST LEVEL OF GOVERNMENT

KNOWING.

3. (C) TRICARICO SAID THE HOLY SEE HAD PROTESTED THE

EXPULSION OF ITS CLERGY AT THE OSCE. WE ASKED HIM ABOUT THE

RUSSIAN OSCE AMBASSADOR\'S COMMENT THAT MAZUR\'S VISA REFUSAL

WAS LINKED TO THE USE OF THE OLD JAPANESE NAME FOR SAKHALIN

DIOCESE\'S SOUTHERN DISTRICT (REF B). TRICARICO SAID THAT

WAS SPECIOUS. HE EXPLAINED THAT THE CHURCH HAD CALLED THAT

DISTRICT KARAFUTO SINCE 1938, AND THAT IT WAS ONLY AFTER THE

DECISION BY THE POPE TO MAKE THE FOUR DIOCESES OFFICIAL THAT

THEY HAD HEARD ANY COMPLAINT ABOUT IT.

------------------------------------------

TRICARICO ON THE CATHOLIC CHURCH IN RUSSIA

------------------------------------------

4. (C) TO PUT THE CHURCH\'S SITUATION IN RUSSIA INTO

PERSPECTIVE, TRICARICO SAID IT NEEDS 100\'S OF BISHOPS, BUT

ONLY HAS FOUR. HE AGREED WITH THE GENERAL PRINCIPAL THAT

CLERGY SHOULD BE NATIONALS OF THE COUNTRY WHERE THEY

MINISTER, AND NOTED THAT THERE ARE NOW 74 SEMINARIANS IN THE

ST. PETERSBURG SEMINARY. OVER TIME, HE FORECAST, FOREIGN

CLERGY WON\'T BE NEEDED. IN THE MEANTIME, THE CATHOLIC CHURCH

WAS DOING LOTS OF SOCIAL WORK IN RUSSIA, WITH HOSPITALS,

SCHOOLS AND SOCIAL CENTERS FOR PENSIONERS. GIVEN THIS, MANY

IN THE VATICAN FEEL PARTICULARLY BETRAYED BY THE GOR\'S

ACTIONS AFTER THE CREATION OF THE FOUR RUSSIAN DIOCESES.

TRICARICO REPEATEDLY ASSERTED THAT CATHOLICS HAD BEEN

PERSECUTED IN RUSSIA FOR 80 YEARS, THROWN INTO THE GULAG, AND

MANY MARTYRED, AND "THIS IS OUR REWARD?"

5. (C) TRICARICO SAID THAT THE POPE SINCERELY WISHED FOR A

RAPPROCHEMENT WITH THE RUSSIAN ORTHODOX; HE WOULD GIVE UP

ANYTHING "SAVE THE FREEDOM" OF HIS CATHOLIC FLOCK TO PRACTICE

THEIR FAITH. BUT DESPITE ALL THE APOLOGIES FOR HISTORICAL

TRANSGRESSIONS, THE OUTREACH TO ORTHODOX CHURCHES IN OTHER

COUNTRIES, THE DISCRETION THAT CATHOLICS PRACTICED IN RUSSIA

(I.E. MAKING RUSSIANS WHO WISH TO CONVERT TAKE SEVERAL WEEKS

OF REFLECTION BEFORE ACCEPTING THEM TO THE CHURCH), PATRIARCH

ALEXIY AND THE ORTHODOX OFFER NAUGHT BUT A STONEWALL.

TRICARICO AVERRED THAT THE RUSSIAN ORTHODOX CLERGY SUFFERED

FROM "SOCIALIST WAYS OF THINKING; THEY WERE ALL COLLABORATORS

IN PAST AND CAN\'T SHAKE OLD WAYS" (PLEASE STRICTLY PROTECT).

WHAT NEXT?

----------

WILL BREAK, AND MAZUR WILL BE PERMITTED TO RETURN. TRICARICO

COUNTRY DIRECTORS. PERHAPS BECAUSE OF HIS AGE (HE WILL TURN
