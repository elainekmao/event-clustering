SUBJECT: CODEL HASTERT TO THE NETHERLANDS (JUL 30 - JUL 31)

REF: STATE 184642

As requested reftel, Post provides in-country schedule for

visit of CODEL Hastert to the Netherlands July 30-31:

July 30

1045 Arrive Valkenburg Naval Air Station from Copenhagen,

Denmark, via buses to Amsterdam. Embassy briefings en route.

1200 Luncheon for Official Delegation hosted by Acting Prime

Minister Gerrit Zalm; luncheon and tour of Anne Frank House

in Amsterdam for Spouse and Staff Delegation

1400 Meeting with Director and Tour of van Gogh Museum for

full Delegation, Amsterdam

1500 Check in to Hilton Hotel, Amsterdam

1830 Reception for Delegation and U.S. and Dutch business

representatives hosted by Acting Mayor of Amsterdam, Hester

Maij

1930 Boat tour for Delegation of Amsterdam canals hosted by

Amsterdam Promotion Board with U.S. business representatives.

2100 No-host dinner options in Amsterdam

July 31

0700 Optional tour of Aalsmeer Flower Auction

0800 Optional walking tour of Amsterdam

1100 Depart Hilton hotel en route to Valkenburg NAS

1200 Depart Valkenburg NAS en route to Madrid, Spain.
