SUMMARY

-------

1. (C) THE MEANING OF DEMOCRACY IS BEING VITIATED IN

ZIMBABWE. IN ORDER TO WIN THE PARLIAMENTARY ELECTION,

THE DATE OF WHICH IS STILL NOT FIXED, PRESIDENT MUGABE

HAS UNLEASHED A CAMPAIGN OF TERROR IN THE COUNTRYSIDE,

COMPARABLE IN PURPOSE TO CHINA\'S CULTURAL REVOLUTION.

HIS TRUSTED AGENTS ARE THE "WAR VETERANS," MISFITS WHO

MISSED THE BOAT ON THE BENEFITS OF ZANU-PF\'S ASCENT TO

POWER IN 1980, WHO WE PREDICT WILL REMAIN ON THE FARMS

UNTIL AFTER THE ELECTION AT THE VERY LEAST TO SETTLE

SCORES WITH OPPOSITION SUPPORTERS. MUGABE HAS FORCED

WHITE COMMERCIAL FARMERS (WHOSE WEALTH, AND NOT RACIAL

PREJUDICE, HAS BROUGHT THEM INTO THE RULING PARTY\'S

SIGHTS -- A TRIUMPH OF GREED) TO NEGOTIATE WITH THE WAR

VETS, THUS GRANTING THE VETS SOME DEGREE OF LEGITIMACY.

THIS STEP ENSURES THAT THEY WILL RETAIN A SEAT AT ALL

FUTURE DISCUSSIONS ON LAND REFORM. IN THE RURAL AREAS

WAR VETERANS AND YOUNG ZANU-PF THUGS ARE CONDUCTING MAO-

STYLE POLITICAL "RE-EDUCATION" CAMPS TO FORCE FARMERS

AND THEIR WORKERS TO RECANT AND VOTE FOR THE RULING

PARTY. IN THE CITIES WE EXPECT ZANU-PF TO INUNDATE THE

POLLING PLACES WITH PERI-URBAN SQUATTERS EAGER FOR A

PETTY PAYOFF, DILUTING CHANCES OF AN OPPOSITION WIN IN

THESE CONSTITUENCIES. THE OPPOSITION MOVEMENT FOR

DEMOCRATIC CHANGE (MDC) HAS BEEN UNABLE TO COUNTERACT

EFFECTIVELY THE RULING PARTY\'S VIOLENCE AND TACTICS,

BEING NEARLY FORCED TO GO AND ACT UNDERGROUND. THE

SHEER BRUTALITY OF THE CAMPAIGN, EXPECTED TO INTENSIFY

AS ELECTION DAY APPROACHES, COUPLED WITH THE

GOVERNMENT\'S CONTINUED INATTENTION TO THE ECONOMY, WILL

HAVE NEGATIVE IMPLICATIONS FOR THE REGION THAT SHOULD BE

DISCUSSED DURING SOUTH AFRICAN PRESIDENT MBEKI\'S STATE

VISIT TO WASHINGTON. END SUMMARY.

RECANT AND CONVERT...OR ELSE

2. (C) ALTHOUGH ZIMBABWE IS SCHEDULED TO HOLD A

PARLIAMENTARY ELECTION WITHIN THE NEXT TWO MONTHS OR SO

(WE HAVE HEARD JUNE 17 AND JULY 11 AS POSSIBLE DATES),

WHAT IS HAPPENING IN THE RUN-UP TO THEM HARDLY FALLS

UNDER THE RUBRIC OF DEMOCRACY, EVEN MAKING ALLOWANCES

FOR A YOUNG STATE IN THE DEVELOPING WORLD. FREEDOM OF

EXPRESSION AND ASSEMBLY ARE VIRTUALLY NON-EXISTENT. THE

POLICE COMMISSIONER HAS SAID HE WILL APPLY THE LAW AND

ORDER MAINTENANCE ACT TO PREVENT OPPOSITION PARTIES FROM

BUSING SUPPORTERS TO RALLIES. A BOMB EXPLODED IN FRONT

OF THE OFFICES OF AN INDEPENDENT NEWSPAPER THAT SUPPORTS

THE OPPOSITION MDC. THE GOVERNMENT-INFLUENCED "THE

THE NEWS. IN THE COUNTRYSIDE "WAR VETERANS" OCCUPY

FARMS, BEAT, KILL AND DESTROY THE PROPERTY OF ANYONE

SUSPECTED OF EVEN THE REMOTEST SYMPATHY OR CONNECTION TO

4. (C) THE WAR VETERANS, SUPPORTED BY THE POLICE, SOME

PLAINCLOTHES MILITARY AND CENTRAL INTELLIGENCE

ORGANIZATION OFFICERS, ARE NOW MUGABE\'S PROTEAN GUARD,

THE PEOPLE HE RELIES UPON, WITH OR WITHOUT THE ADDED

BENEFIT OF TRUST OF ITS LEADER, CHENJERAI "HITLER"

HUNZVI. THE PARTY AND EVEN THE SECURITY FORCES HAVE

BECOME LESS RELIABLE, WITH MEMBERS OF BOTH OPPOSED TO

MUGABE\'S USE OF VIOLENCE AND INTIMIDATION AS BASIC

CAMPAIGN TOOLS. THE STILL-DETERIORATING ECONOMY ALSO

LENDS THEM NO SUCCOR. BUT THE LUMPEN, KNOW-NOTHING "WAR

VETS," MANY BLATANTLY TOO YOUNG TO HAVE PARTICIPATED IN

THE CHIMURENGA (WAR OF NATIONAL LIBERATION), ARE LOYAL

IN A CANINE WAY AND ARE, IN OUR JUDGMENT, UNDER MUGABE\'S

FIRM DIRECTION THOUGH THE CONTROL FACTOR IS LESS

CERTAIN.

5. (C) AT THE BEGINNING OF THE FARM OCCUPATION EXERCISE

THE NLWVA SUFFERED FROM A PROBLEM: LACK OF LEGITIMACY.

THOUGH EVERYONE UNDERSTOOD THAT THEIR LAWLESS BEHAVIOR

WAS SANCTIONED BY THE PRESIDENT\'S OFFICE, THERE WAS NO

PUBLIC DISCLOSURE OF THE NEW SYMBIOSIS. IN ORDER TO

OVERCOME THE LEGITIMACY HURDLE AND TO BE SEEN AS A

REASONABLE AND ENLIGHTENED LEADER, MUGABE AUTHORIZED THE

WAR VETERANS TO NEGOTIATE DIRECTLY WITH THE COMMERCIAL

FARMERS, AIDED BY THE MEDIATION EFFORTS OF FATHER

FIDELIS MUKONORI, S.J., A MEMBER OF THE CLERGY TRUSTED

BY MUGABE. THE FARMERS, DESPERATE FOR RELIEF FROM THE

OCCUPATIONS AND WISHING TO REAP THEIR HARVEST, SAT DOWN,

TALKED AND CONCEDED. IT\'S HARD NOT TO DO SO WITH A GUN

AT YOUR HEAD. THE WAR VETS, MEANWHILE, HAVE NOT

FULFILLED THE TERMS OF THE AGREEMENT, PEACE FOR LAND,

BUT NOW WILL HAVE A SEAT AT THE TABLE IN ANY FUTURE

DISCUSSIONS ON LAND REFORMS. THEY HAVE PROVEN, AT A

MINIMUM, TO BE PERFIDIOUS INTERLOCUTORS.

THE URBAN CENTERS ARE NOT FORGOTTEN

-----------------------------------

6. (C) THE RULING PARTY APPEARS TO HAVE ABANDONED

COMPLETELY ITS ANTI-CORRUPTION STRATEGY, ONCE THOUGHT TO

BE A STEP TO COURT THE URBAN VOTE AND EVEN INTERNATIONAL

GOOD WILL. THE ATTORNEY GENERAL IS NEITHER TARGETING

ANY OTHER SENIOR GOVERNMENT FIGURE FOR ARREST NOR EVEN

GOING THROUGH THE MOTIONS OF PUSHING FORWARD THE CASES

OF THOSE WHO HAVE BEEN ARRESTED. INDEED, THE ZANU-PF

POLITBURO, WITH THE COMPLICITY OF THE COURTS WHICH

SUSPENDED ALL THEIR BAIL RESTRICTIONS, HAS ALLOWED

FORMER MINISTER OF LANDS AND AGRICULTURE KUMBIRAI KANGAI

TO RUN FOR AND WIN THE ZANU-PF PRIMARY ELECTION IN

BUHERA SOUTH, A CRYSTAL-CLEAR SIGN THAT ALL IS FORGIVEN.

7. (C) FOR THE CITIES WIDESPREAD TERROR IS BAD

BUSINESS, BUT THE RULING PARTY HAS NOT CONCEDED THESE

CONSTITUENCIES ENTIRELY TO THE MDC. INSTEAD, IT APPEARS

THAT ITS TACTIC WILL BE TO FLOOD THE POLLING PLACES ON

MAY 10, ELECTION REGISTRATION CONTINUES, IN SPITE OF THE

OSTENSIBLY OFFICIAL CLOSING DATE OF MARCH 31.

WHEN THE ROLLS WERE TURNED OVER TO THE DELIMITATION

COMMISSION, COULD VOTE BY BEING PLACED ON A

SUPPLEMENTARY REGISTER.

THE REGIONAL IMPACT

10. (C) THE IMPACT THAT SUCH DIRE ACTIONS WILL HAVE ON

THE REGION ARE SERIOUS. AT THAT STAGE ZIMBABWE\'S

ECONOMY WILL BE IN THE FIRST STAGES OF AN EFFECTIVE

MELTDOWN, WITH CONSEQUENT REGIONAL NEGATIVE EFFECTS.

VIRTUALLY ALL THE SADC MEMBER COUNTRIES WILL SEE THE

STRENGTH OF THEIR COMMERCIAL CREDIT RATINGS UNDER

THREAT. THE RAND WILL FACE AN EVEN MORE SERIOUS LOSS OF

CONFIDENCE THAN THAT BEHIND ITS RECENT, NEARLY 20

PERCENT DROP VIS-A-VIS THE U.S. DOLLAR. HOW DEEP THE

HOLE GETS IN ZIMBABWE IS AT THIS TIME ANYONE\'S GUESS.

11. (C) NOW IS THE TIME TO CONTINUE OUR BEST EFFORTS TO

ALTER THIS PROJECTED COURSE WHICH IS GATHERING MOMENTUM.
