SUBJECT: ADNAN PACHACHI IS BAGHDAD BOUND

1. (U) Classified by Ambassador Marcelle M. Wahba

for reasons 1.5 (B) and (D).

2. (S) Former Iraqi foreign minister Adnan Pachachi

contacted the Ambassador over the local weekend

with the news that he will be flying into Baghdad

on Tuesday, May 6 aboard a UAE Air Force C-130 with

a small group of his supporters. MFA Minstate

Hamdan bin Zayid and Armed Forces Chief of Staff

Shaykh Muhammad bin Zayid separately told the

Ambassador that the UAEG is supporting the

establishment of an office for Pachachi in Baghdad

and providing him with security, until he has set-

up his own Iraqi security detail.

3. (S) Comment: Pachachi believes he has much to

contribute in the evolving dialogue on the future

Iraqi government. The octogenarian statesman has

spent the last several weeks touring various Arab

capitals briefing leaders, including Hosni Mubarak

and King Abdullah, on his vision, and soliciting

their advice. Here in Abu Dhabi, where he has

resided with his wife since the early 1970s,

Pachachi has been hosting informal diwans where

Iraqi expats of all stripes have been airing their

concerns about the current state of affairs and

presenting suggestions on how to shape the new

government. For the Emiratis, Pachachi\'s

moderation, his message of inclusion and his strong

pan-Arab credentials are an important balance to

what they view as the alarming emergence of Shi\'a

sectarianism coupled with signs of Iranian meddling

in Iraqi affairs. End Comment.
