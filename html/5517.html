SUBJECT: SUPPORTING THE SECRETARY\'S FEBRUARY 5 SECURITY

COUNCIL PRESENTATION

REF: STATE 31246

Classified By: Charge d\'Affaires Frank C. Urbancic for reasons 1.5 (B)

and (D).

(C) With Kuwaiti MinState for Foreign Affairs Dr. Mohamad

al-Sabah in Riyadh for the GCC Ministerial, Charge called MFA

Undersecretary Khaled Al-Jarallah February 5 to remind him

that the Secretary would be making a major presentation on

Iraq at the UN later that day. Drawing from points in reftel,

Charge urged the Kuwaitis to have a statement in support of

the US position ready for release soon after the Secretary

ended his speech. Jarallah thanked the US for the

suggestion, and said he was sure the Kuwaitis would in fact

take the advice. Since he would have to clear this text with

Dr. Mohamad in Riyadh in any case, Jarallah said he would

also suggest that the GCC make a statement of support as

well. Jarallah added that regardless of the decision in

Riyadh, the US could count on a statement from Kuwait.
