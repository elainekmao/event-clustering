SUBJECT: JORDAN: MEDIA REACTION ON IRAQ AND T OCCUPIED

TERRITORIES

SUMMARY

JORDANIAN DAILIES ON JULY 30 WERE DOMINATED BY KING

ABDULLAH\'S INTERVIEW WITH THE TIMES AND HIS MEETING

WITH PRIME MINSTER TONY BLAIR, IN WHICH HE COMMENTED ON

IRAQ AND THE OCCUPIED TERRITORIES.

EDITORIAL WRITERS COMMENTED ON A NUMBER OF DOMESTIC

ISSUES. THE MOST IMPORTANT THEME WAS PRAISE FOR THE

KING\'S STATEMENTS ON IRAQ.

NEWS REPORTS

JORDANIAN DAILIES ON JULY 30 WERE DOMINATED BY KING

ABDULLAH\'S MEETING WITH THE PRIME MINSTER OF BRITAIN

AND HIS INTERVIEW WITH THE TIMES, IN WHICH HE ADDRESSED

THE SITUATION IN IRAQ AND THE OCCUPIED TERRITORIES.

DAILIES HIGHLIGHTED THE FOLLOWING QUOTES:

PRINCE HASSAN\'S PARTICIPATION IN THE IRAQI OPPOSITION

MEETING WAS A GROSS ERROR, AND WE ARE WORKING TO

RECTIFY THE SITUATION.

JORDAN WOULD NOT BE THE LAUNCHING GROUND FOR ANY

AMERICAN OPERATIONS.

IT IS INAPPROPRIATE TO TARGET THE LEADERSHIP PF ANY

COUNTRY.

JORDAN WARNS AGAINST A HUMANITARIAN CATASTROPHE IN THE

OCCUPIED TERRITORIES, AND AGAINST ATTACKING IRAQ.

SECRETARY POWELL UNDERSTANDS THE PALESTINIAN PROBLEM

SIPDIS

AND HIS DEPARTURE FROM THE ADMINISTRATION WOULD BE A

MAJOR LOSS.

[AHMAD] JALABI IS WANTED IN JORDAN FOR EMBEZZLING FUNDS

FROM A JORDANIAN BANK.

DAILIES ALSO REPORTED THE NEW YORK TIMES ARTICLE THAT

SPOKE OF AN AMERICAN PLAN TO STRIKE BAGHDAD FIRST.

EDITORIAL COMMENTARIES

-- THE KING\'S ANSWERS

DAILY COLUMNIST BASSEM SAKEJHA WROTE IN CENTER-LEFT,

INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR ON JULY 30:

"JORDAN\'S POSITION REGARDING REGIONAL ISSUES NEEDS TO

BE THE CLEAREST, AND IT APPEARS THAT THE KING, IN HIS

CNN INTERVIEW, ADDRESSED EACH OF THE TRIAL BALLOONS

RECENTLY LAUNCHED REGARDING A JORDANIAN ROLE IN THE

PALESTINIAN PROBLEM AND ANOTHER IN A U.S. AGGRESSION

AGAINST IRAQ. DESPITE ENORMOUS PRESSURE THAT STARTED

WITH LEAKS TOP THE MEDIA AND REACHED THE LEVEL OF

HINTING AT A NEW SIEGE OF AQABA, THE ROYAL POSITION IS

CLEAR AND FORTHRIGHT. ALL JORDANIANS FEEL PROUD OF THE

KING WHO SPOKE IN THEIR NAMES, AND THAT THEIR COUNTRY

WOULD NOT BE DRAGGED INTO A GAME THAT THEY WOULD NOT

ACCEPT, AND THAT IS OPPOSED TO THEIR NATIONALIST

OUTLOOKS."

-- THE KING\'S INTERVIEW WITH CNN

DAILY COLUMNIST MAZEN AL-SAKET WROTE IN CENTER-LEFT,

INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR ON JULY 30: "IT IS

OUR DUTY TO TAKE NOTE OF THE FORTHRIGHTNESS AND CLARITY

WITH WHICH THE KING EXPRESSED HIMSELF ON THE TWO MOST

IMPORTANT ISSUES THAT OCCUPY JORDANIAN PUBLIC OPINION.

THESE ISSUES REPRESENT JORDAN\'S NATIONALIST OUTLOOK

REGARDING THE CHALLENGES THAT FACE OUR NATION AND THAT

THREATEN THE REGION\'S SECURITY AND STABILITY. ONLY

THIS POSITION THAT EXPRESSES JORDAN\'S POPULAR AS WELL

AS OFFICIAL VIEWPOINTS CAN MAKE US FACE THESE

CHALLENGES AND DANGERS SUCCESSFULLY."

-- HAS JORDANIAN DIPLOMACY MENDED THE DAMAGE DONE BY

THE PRINCE\'S PARTICIPATION [IN ET IRAQI OPPOSITION

MEETING]?

IMPORTANT COLUMNIST SAMIH AL-MA\'AYTAH WROTE IN SEMI-

OFFICIAL INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-RAI ON JULY 30:

"HIS MAJESTY THE KING SPOKE ACCURATELY WHEN HE

DESCRIBED THE PRINCE\'S MOVE AS A GROSS ERROR BECAUSE

THAT STEP CAUSED CONSIDERABLE POLITICAL DAMAGE TO

JORDAN AND ITS LEADERSHIP FAR BEYOND THE PRINCE\'S

JUSTIFICATION THAT IT WAS A PERSONAL DECISION. THE

PRINCE FORGOT THAT HIS MOVE WAS CONSIDERED AS COMING

FORM THE JORDANIAN LEADERSHIP, WHICH NEEDED TO EXERT

CONSIDERABLE EFFORTS TO PERSUADE FRIENDS AND BROTHERS

THAT IT WAS NOT A PARTNER IN THIS POLITICAL STEP. ALL

ARABS SHOULD REALIZE THAT THEY WILL BE SUBJECTED TO

CONSIDERABLE PRESSURE TO TAKE PART IN THE AGGRESSION

AGAINST IRAQ, AND THAT THE PROPER NATIONALIST POSITION

WOULD BE IN A COLLECTIVE STAND TO RESIST THESE

PRESSURES. WE HOPE THAT WE SHALL SEE SUCH A COLLECTIVE

STAND INSTEAD OF THE USUAL ARAB BEHAVIOR OF SNEAKING TO

THE AMERICANS INDIVIDUALLY AND IN SECRET." GNEHM
