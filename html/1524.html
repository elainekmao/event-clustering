SUBJECT: ZIMBABWE\'S PRIVATE SECTOR INITIATIVE; A

COMPLIMENTARY LAND RESETTLEMENT PLAN

1. (C) SUMMARY: THE PRIVATE SECTOR INITIATIVE (PSI) IS AN

ALTERNATE LAND ACQUISITION, RESETTLEMENT AND DEVELOPMENT

PLAN THAT IS BEING QUIETLY PROMOTED AS A COMPLEMENTARY

APPROACH TO THE GOZ\'S "FAST TRACK" PROGRAM. IT CALLS FOR

THE CREATION OF AN AGRICULTURAL CREDIT GUARANTEE BANK

FUNDED BY COMMERCIAL BANKS, THE GOZ AND DONOR COUNTRIES.

ITS AIM IS TO PURCHASE COMMERCIAL FARMS FROM WILLING

SELLERS AT MARKET RATES (THE PROGRAM TARGET IS 200 FARMS),

TO PAY FOR BOTH THE LAND AND IMPROVEMENTS, AND TO RESETTLE

THE LAND WITH ABLE AND QUALIFIED INDIGENOUS FARMERS WHO

WILL PURSUE COMMERCIAL AGRICULTURE ON A SMALL OR MEDIUM

SCALE. IT IS IMPORTANT TO NOTE THAT THE NEW OWNERS WOULD

GET A TRANSFERABLE OWNERSHIP OR LEASEHOLD INTEREST IN THE

PROPERTY ALONG WITH SUBSIDIZED FINANCING (A 20 YEAR, 15

PERCENT FIXED MORTGAGE), AND WOULD HAVE TO PRODUCE AND

MARKET A SALEABLE CROP. THE TOTAL SIZE OF THE PROGRAM IN

FINANCIAL TERMS IS ABOUT U.S. $40 MILLION DOLLARS (OR Z

$2.25 BILLION) SPLIT AS FOLLOWS: U.S. $2 MILLION EQUIVALENT

FROM LOCAL FINANCIAL INSTITUTIONS; U.S. $11 MILLION FROM A

LONG-TERM GOZ BOND ISSUE; U.S. $7.3 MILLION FROM DONORS TO

PAY FOR THE LAND (PERHAPS THE BRITISH?); AND ADDITIONAL

DONOR CONTRIBUTIONS TOTALLING U.S. $21 MILLION. WHILE THE

PLAN IS WORTHWHILE AND BASED ON FREE-MARKET PRINCIPLES AND

GEARED TO SUCCESSFUL LONG-TERM DEVELOPMENT, IT FACES

NUMEROUS OBSTACLES, BOTH POLITICAL AND FINANCIAL. FOR

EXAMPLE: THE RULING PARTY NEEDS TO REORIENT ITS SUPPORT

FROM SOCIALIST TO FREE-MARKET PRINCIPLES; THE DONORS HAVE

TO COME ON BOARD IN A VERY BIG WAY; AND A DIVERSE GROUP OF

STAKEHOLDERS NEEDS TO ADMINISTER THE PROGRAM WITH A FREE

HAND AND WITHOUT GOZ INTERFERENCE AND CORRUPTION. IN THE

CURRENT AND FORESEEABLE NEAR TERM ENVIRONMENT, THE ODDS OF

SUCH A CONFLUENCE ARE, IN OUR OPINION, QUITE SMALL. END

SUMMARY.

----------------------------

WHO IS BEHIND THE INITIATIVE

----------------------------

2. (C) THE INITIATIVE WAS CONCEIVED AND IS BEING PROMOTED

BY A COMMERCIAL BANKER (GREG BRACKENRIDGE, MANAGING

DIRECTOR, STANBIC BANK ZIMBABWE) AND A COMMERCIAL FARMER

(NICK SWANEPOEL, THE PRIOR COMMERCIAL FARMERS UNION (CFU)

PRESIDENT WHO HAS BEEN DEEPLY INVOLVED IN THE LAND ISSUE

SINCE BEFORE THE SEPTEMBER 1998 DONOR\'S LAND CONFERENCE).

IT AIMS TO PURCHASE AND RESETTLE A RELATIVELY SMALL AMOUNT

OF LAND (200 FARMS V.S. THE GOVERNMENT\'S CURRENT GOAL OF

3,000, OR ABOUT 350,000 HECTARES V.S. THE GOZ GOAL OF 5

MILLION) USING A SUBSIDIZED, COMMERCIALLY-DRIVEN FINANCE

STRUCTURE. THE PROGRAM INTENDS TO UTILIZE EXISTING

AGRICULTURAL DEVELOPMENT AND SUPPORT TECHNIQUES THAT THE

CFU HAS ALREADY SUCCESSFULLY APPLIED IN ITS OWN SMALL

PROGRAMS AIMED AT HELPING INDIGENOUS FARMERS WORK THE LAND

ON A COMMERCIAL BASIS (SUCH AS THE FARMER\'S DEVELOPMENT

TRUST).

3. (C) THE PROGRAM GOES AGAINST THE GOVERNMENT\'S GRAIN IN

THAT IT GIVES TITLE TO THE RESETTLED FARMERS (AND IN TURN

GREATER POLITICAL AND ECONOMIC INDEPENDENCE), APPLIES THE

SAME STANDARDS AND RULES TO ALL (I.E. THAT OF THE

MARKETPLACE), REQUIRES PAYMENT FOR THE LAND BY THOSE WHO

ACQUIRE IT, AND DEMANDS PERFORMANCE, RESULTS AND

ACCOUNTABILITY OF THE NEW OCCUPANTS, WITH BANKRUPTCY AS THE

ALTERNATIVE. IN ESSENCE, IT ROLLS OUT ON A SMALL SCALE

WHAT THE WHITE, ZIMBABWEAN COMMERCIAL FARMERS DO NOW.

GIVEN THE GOVERNMENT\'S USE OF THE LAND ISSUE TO ACHIEVE ITS

POLITICAL AND POWER RETENTION GOALS, WE BELIEVE IT WILL BE

DIFFICULT TO GET THE GOZ BACKING NEEDED FOR THE INITIATIVE

TO BE SUCCESSFUL.

5. (SBU) THE OBJECTIVES, IN GENERAL TERMS, ARE TO ENSURE

THAT AT A LEAST A PORTION OF THE CURRENT LAND RESETTLEMENT

EXERCISE IS CONDUCTED ALONG CAPITALIST, FREE-MARKET

PRINCIPLES, AND PROPERTY RIGHTS, INDIVIDUAL EMPOWERMENT AND

RESPONSIBILITY, AND PROFIT AND LOSS FROM AGRICULTURAL

OPERATIONS ALL APPLY. THE INITIATIVES BACKER\'S STATED

GOALS ARE:

= TO PROVIDE AND EFFICIENT CONDUIT FOR FUNDING LAND

RESETTLEMENT AND DEVELOPMENT;

= TO FUND THE RESETTLEMENT PROGRAMS IN AN EFFECTIVE,

FISCALLY SOUND AND MACRO-ECONOMICALLY STABILIZING MANNER;

= TO ENCOURAGE AND PROMOTE GOVERNMENT AND PRIVATE SECTOR

SCHEMES IN ASSOCIATION WITH THE RESOURCES OF MULTILATERAL,

BILATERAL AND NGO ORGANIZATIONS;

= TO SUPPORT AGRICULTURAL RESETTLEMENT AND DEVELOPMENT AND

PROVIDE A REAL ECONOMIC RETURN TO THE INVESTMENT.

COMMENT

-------

6. (C) IN THE CURRENT POLITICALLY-DRIVEN LAND-ISSUE

ENVIRONMENT, WHERE DARK FORCES AND BEHIND-THE-SCENES

MANEUVERINGS AND DEALS ARE COMMONPLACE, THE INITIATIVE IS

AN ANOMALOUS ORPHAN. ALTHOUGH MANY OF THE CFU\'S MEMBERSHIP

ARE STRONGLY AGAINST ANY NEGOTIATIONS WITH THE GOVERNMENT

ON THE LAND ISSUE, A FORMER CFU PRESIDENT IS PROMOTING IT.

THE OFFICIAL LINE ON THE GOVERNMENT SIDE, TOO, IS THAT

NEGOTIATING WITH THE WHITE FARMERS IS TABOO. THEREFORE,

ANY REAL MOVEMENT ON THE INITIATIVE WILL TAKE PLACE BEHIND

CLOSED DOORS. IN ADDITION, THE PROPOSAL ENVISIONS THAT A

BULK OF THE PLANNED FUNDING COME FROM THE DONORS (ABOUT 70

PERCENT OF U.S. $41 MILLION). GIVEN THE CURRENT

ENVIRONMENT (CONTINUED VIOLENCE, ATTACKS ON THE JUDICIARY,

SUSPENSION OF THE RULE OF LAW AND THE LIKE), AND THE STATE

OF RELATIONS BETWEEN THE DONOR COMMUNITY AND ZIMBABWE, IT

IS DIFFICULT TO SEE HOW SUCH FUNDING CAN OCCUR. WE ALSO

POINT OUT THAT THE ACREAGE OF THE PROGRAM IS LESS THAN 10

PERCENT OF THE GOZ\'S STATED ACQUISITION GOALS, AND FRANKLY

ASK IF SUCH EFFORTS AT THE MARGIN ARE WORTH THE REQUIRED

EFFORT? WE HAVE BEEN TOLD, HOWEVER, THAT MOVEMENT CHANGING

THE THRUST AND METHODOLOGY OF THE CURRENT FAST-TRACK LAND

RESETTLEMENT PROGRAM MAY BE AFOOT AT THE HIGHEST LEVELS

HERE, AND WE CAN ONLY HOPE THAT SOME MEASURES OF JUSTICE

AND SANITY ARE REINTRODUCED. IF THAT BECOMES THE CASE,

PERHAPS THEN THE PRIVATE SECTOR INITIATIVE WILL BECOME MORE

ATTRACTIVE AND DOABLE. END COMMENT.
