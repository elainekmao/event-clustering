SUBJECT: ESTABLISHMENT OF HOMELAND SECURITY WORKING GROUP

REF: A. STATE 263389 B. AMMAN 5239

Classified By: Ambassador Edward W. Gnehm for reasons 1.5 (B), (C),

and (D)

1. (S/NF) Ref A, of 20 Dec. 2002, requests that posts

establish Homeland Security Working Groups (HSWG) as either

standalone committees or as subsets of existing security

entities. Ref B is Post\'s response to an earlier request

along the same lines,reporting that AmEmbassy Amman had

established a HSWG at post on September 11, 2002.

2. (S/NF) The Post\'s HSWG is chaired by the DCM and

members include the following agency/section heads: DAO,

LEGATT, ORCA, RSO, POL, ECON, CONS, and ADMIN.

3. (S/NF) AmEmbassy Amman HSWG stands ready to provide

any required support for Homeland Security initiatives.

Regards.

GNEHM
