SUBJECT: MINISTER MARZANO VISITS IRAN

CLASSIFIED BY: CDA POPE FOR REASON 1.5(B)

1. (C) SUMMARY: AMBASSADOR SEMBLER ENGAGED THE MINISTER

OF PRODUCTIVE ACTIVITIES ON IRAN, UNDERSCORING OUR CONCERNS

WITH IRANIAN BEHAVIOR. MARZANO\'S RELATIVE UNFAMILIARITY

WITH OUR OBJECTIONS OR WITH THE DIVISION BETWEEN THE

OFFICIAL AND SHADOW GOVERNMENTS IN CONTROLLING IRAN SHOWS

THE NECESSITY OF REPEATING OUR MESSAGE FREQUENTLY AND

CLEARLY. END SUMMARY.

2. (U) TUESDAY, MARCH 5, THE AMBASSADOR PAID A COURTESY

CALL ON MINISTER FOR PRODUCTIVE ACTIVITIES, ANTONIO

MARZANO. ALSO PRESENT WERE DCM POPE, ECMIN DEAN, AND MPA

DIPLOMATIC ADVISOR GABRIELE CHECCHIA. THE MEETING

PRINCIPALLY ADDRESSED TWO TOPICS: MARZANO\'S RECENT VISIT

TO IRAN AND OUR ADVOCACY FOR THE JOINT STRIKE FIGHTER. THE

JSF WILL BE COVERED SEPTEL.

3. (SBU) RESPONDING TO THE AMBASSADOR\'S INVITATION TO HEAR

ABOUT HIS TRIP TO IRAN, MARZANO SAID THAT HE HAD GONE FOR

TWO REASONS. THE FIRST, MORE OBVIOUS ONE, WAS ECONOMIC.

ITALY DOES A LOT OF BUSINESS IN IRAN AND MARZANO FOUND IT

USEFUL TO VISIT. IN THIS INSTANCE THERE WERE TWO LARGE

CONTRACTS IN DIFFICULTY, ONE FOR ELECTRICITY GENERATION AND

ANOTHER FOR ALUMINUM PRODUCTION. MARZANO FELT THAT HIS

INTERVENTION HAD STRAIGHTENED OUT THE PROBLEMS.

4. (C) THE SECOND REASON FOR THE VISIT WAS POLITICAL, BUT

MARZANO SAID THIS WAS "ALMOST BY ACCIDENT". THE MEETING

WITH PRESIDENT KHATAMI WAS UNSCHEDULED. MARZANO THOUGHT

THAT HE WAS A REFORMIST, WHO WAS MAKING THE SOCIETY FREER.

THE PROBLEM WAS HIS ENEMIES, THE FANATIC MULLAHS.

UNFORTUNATELY PRESIDENT BUSH\'S STATEMENT ABOUT THE \'AXIS

OF EVIL\' ONLY SERVED TO STRENGTHEN THOSE EXTREMISTS.

MARZANO TOLD US THAT, IN AN IRANIAN PRESS CONFERENCE, IN

RESPONSE TO A QUESTION HE HAD EXPLAINED THE \'AXIS OF EVIL\'

AS A WARNING, NOT AS A U.S. DECLARATION OF WAR, THAT IT

SHOULD BE TAKEN AS A CAUTION NOT AS AN ACTION. MARZANO

CONCLUDED BY ENDORSING THE ITALIAN POLICY LINE THAT IT

WOULD BE USEFUL TO REINFORCE THE MODERATE FORCES HEADED

BY KHATAMI.

5. (C) SWITCHING TO AFGHANISTAN AND IRAN, MARZANO

REPORTED THAT IRAN SEEKS PEACE IN AFGHANISTAN AND FEELS FOR

ITS PEOPLE. IRAN IS READY TO BE A PLATFORM FOR

HUMANITARIAN AIR SUPPORT TO AFGHANISTAN. MARZANO ADMITTED

THAT HE DID NOT KNOW WHAT HAPPENED IN PRIVATE COUNCILS, BUT

THAT ITALY NONETHELESS THOUGHT IT WAS IMPORTANT TO

REINFORCE KHATAMI AND THOSE WHO WOULD ENDORSE CHANGE. THE

DCM ADDED THAT THE IRANIANS HAD PLAYED A CONSTRUCTIVE ROLE

IN BONN, BUT THAT THEY HAVE NOT FOLLOWED UP. RATHER THEY

ARE STARTING TO PLAY A DESTABILIZING ROLE, AND THIS

RE-IGNITES U.S. CONCERNS ABOUT IRAN\'S REAL INTENTIONS.

6. (C) MARZANO NOTED THAT ITALY IS ONE OF IRAN\'S MAJOR

EUROPEAN IMPORTERS, PARTICULARLY OF ENERGY. ENI AND ENEL

PLUS MANY OTHERS WORK DIRECTLY WITH IRAN AND THE

RELATIONSHIP IS TOO IMPORTANT TO BREAK. ON IMPORTS,

MARZANO TOLD KHATAMI THAT IRAN SHOULD NOT EVEN SEEK TO

IMPORT DUAL USE ITEMS. ASKED IF THERE WAS A SPECIFIC

REQUEST WHICH HAD TRIGGERED THIS STATEMENT, MARZANO

REPORTED THAT APPARENTLY THERE IS CONSIDERABLE HEPATITIS

B IN IRAN AND THE IRANIANS WERE SEEKING TO IMPORT

EQUIPMENT THAT COULD PRODUCE VACCINES, BUT WHICH COULD

ALSO BE USED FOR BIO WARFARE. CONSEQUENTLY HE WAS ADAMANT

IN REFUSING THE APPROVAL.

7. (C) THE AMBASSADOR MADE IT CLEAR THAT HOWEVER

SYMPATHETIC ITALY WAS TO IRAN, IRANIAN INTERNATIONAL

BEHAVIOR WAS UNACCEPTABLE. IN IRAN THERE ARE BOTH ELECTED

POWERS AND UNELECTED POWERS. THE MULLAHS CONTROL THE

MILITARY, THE COURTS AND THE SECURITY SERVICES, SO THE

ELECTED GOVERNMENT IS NOT IN CHARGE. FOR EXAMPLE, EVEN

THOUGH THE ELECTED GOVERNMENT MAY GIVE ASSURANCES THEY ARE

NOT DESTABILIZING THE MIDDLE EAST, THE CAPTURE OF THE

KARIN A LOADED WITH IRANIAN WEAPONS BELIES THEM. IRAN HAS

THE CAPABILITY TO PRODUCE WEAPONS OF MASS DESTRUCTION AND

TO EXPORT THEM, DESTABILIZING THE MIDDLE EAST. THE

AMBASSADOR ADDED THAT WE ARE READY TO SIT DOWN WITH IRAN

ANYTIME; THE PROBLEM IS IN TEHERAN. IRAN\'S ACTIONS MUST BE

IN CONFORMITY WITH ITS WORDS. COMMENT: MARZANO APPEARED

NOT TO HAVE HEARD THIS LINE OF DISCUSSION BEFORE AND WAS

SURPRISED THAT WE WOULD SAY THE U.S. WAS WILLING TO TALK TO

IRAN. HE DID AGREE THAT KHATAMI DID NOT CONTROL THE

GOVERNMENT. IT APPEARS, MARZANO HAS NOT SERIOUSLY ENGAGED

U.S. THINKING ON IRAN BEFORE AND THAT THE DISCONNECT

BETWEEN IRAN\'S PUBLIC STATEMENTS AND PRIVATE ACTS, BETWEEN

OFFICIALS AND THE SHADOW GOVERNMENT WERE NEW CONCEPTS. END

COMMENT.
