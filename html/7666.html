SUBJECT: FLEEING IRAQI OFFICIALS LIST: TURKS SAY NO

SAFEHAVEN HERE

REF: STATE 102827

1. (U) Classified by Political-Military Counselor Stuart

Brown. Reasons 1.5 (b) and (d).

2. (S) On April 24, Polmiloff delivered ref demarche to

MFA/Middle East Department Ferhat Alkan. Alkan promised to

deliver it at once to MFA DG for Middle East Affairs Tahsin

Burcuoglu, who had been notified by DCM that the list would

be forthcoming. Alkan also said that the list would be

passed to FM Gul as well as other appropriate GOT Ministries.

Alkan said that he would convey USG urgency over this matter

and added that his government shares our concern that such

individuals not be provided safehaven, asylum or assistance

of any kind.
