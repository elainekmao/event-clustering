SUBJECT: SUDAN CEASEFIRE: ITALY CANNOT CONTRIBUTE TO

1. (C) POLOFF MET WITH PAOLA IMPERIALE, MFA OFFICE DIRECTOR

FOR EAST AFRICAN AFFAIRS, ON MAY 2 AT THE MFA. IMPERIALE HAD

REQUESTED THE MEETING BECAUSE SHE DID NOT WANT TO DISCUSS BY

TELEPHONE THE GOI\'S ISSUES WITH FUNDING THE INTERNATIONAL

MONITORING UNIT FOR THE NUBA MOUNTAINS CEASE-FIRE.

2. (C) AT THE PRESENT TIME, IMPERIALE SAID, THE GOI IS

UNABLE TO CONTRIBUTE FINANCIALLY TO THE IMU. SHE HAD MADE

EVERY EFFORT TO SECURE FUNDS, BUT THE AUDITING BOARD (GAO

EQUIVALENT) HAD CALLED IT IMPOSSIBLE. APPARENTLY, ITALIAN

LAW DOES NOT ALLOW FUNDS FOR PEACEKEEPING TO BE DISBURSED

EXCEPT TO AN ORGAN OF THE UNITED NATIONS.

3. (C) IMPERIALE HAD DISCUSSED THIS PROBLEM WITH GEN.

WILHELMSEN AT THE IGAD PARTNERS\' FORUM MEETINGS IN ROME APRIL

11-12. HE SAID THAT GERMANY FACED A SIMILAR CONSTRAINT.

THEY DISCUSSED THE POSSIBILITY OF UNDP\'S SETTING UP A FUND TO

RECEIVE THE MONIES.

4. (C) WILHELMSEN HAD REQUESTED THAT THE GOI PROVIDE A

POLICEMAN FOR THE MISSION. IMPERIALE HAD PURSUED THIS AND IS

WAITING FOR THE CHIEF OF POLICE\'S RESPONSE. THE FUNDING

PROHIBITION DOES NOT APPLY TO PERSONNEL.

5. (C) IMPERIALE WAS UNABLE TO QUANTIFY THE SUPPORT THAT THE

GOI MIGHT PROVIDE IF A UN FUND WERE ESTABLISHED BUT INDICATED

A WILLINGNESS TO PRODUCE AN ESTIMATE. POLOFF WILL FOLLOW UP.
