SUBJECT: 1994 ANNUAL TERRORISM REPORT - SPAIN

REF: STATE 286744

1. THE TERRORIST SITUATION IN SPAIN IS NOT MARKEDLY

DIFFERENT FROM THAT DESCRIBED IN "PATTERNS OF GLOBAL

TERRORISM - 1993". EMBASSY PROPOSES THE FOLLOWING FOR

INCLUSION IN THE 1994 TERRORISM REPORT, REQUESTED REFTEL:

2. DURING 1994 SPANISH AND FRENCH AUTHORITIES ENJOYED

CONSIDERABLE SUCCESS AGAINST THE BASQUE FATHERLAND AND

LIBERTY (ETA) TERRORIST GROUP. SEVERAL IMPORTANT ARRESTS

WERE MADE, INCLUDING THAT OF THE SECOND IN COMMAND OF ETA\'S

MILITARY WING. SIGNIFICANT CACHES OF WEAPONS AND RECORDS

IN FRANCE, INCLUDING A COMPUTER HARD-DISK OF BANK ACCOUNTS

AND PHONE NUMBERS, WERE ALSO SEIZED. NEVERTHELESS, ETA

REMAINS A SIGNIFICANT TERRORIST THREAT IN SPAIN. ON JULY

29 THE THIRD RANKING OFFICIAL IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENSE,

LIEUTENANT GENERAL FRANCISCO VEGUILLAS, WAS KILLED BY A CAR

BOMB IN DOWNTOWN MADRID. DESPITE ITS PROGRESS IN

NEUTRALIZING ETA, THE MINISTER OF INTERIOR RECOGNIZES THAT

A SIGNIFICANT COUNTERTERRORIST EFFORT MUST BE SUSTAINED.

THE LEFTIST GRAPO TERRORIST ORGANIZATION HAS BECOME

INCREASINGLY INSIGNIFICANT. IN 1994 GRAPO\'S OPERATIONS

WERE LIMITED TO OCCASIONAL ROBBERIES AND SMALL SCALE

BOMBINGS.
