DEAR MR. CHIYANGWA,

I AM WRITING TO YOU AS THE CATHOLIC PRIEST OF BANKET, A

PLACE IN YOUR FUTURE CONSTITUENCY. I AM WRITING BECAUSE I AM

VERY CONCERNED ABOUT YOUR MEETING WHICH YOU HELD IN

KUWADZANA TOWNSHIP ON FRIDAY 19TH MAY, 2000 IN THE KUWADZANA

HALL. A NUMBER OF OUR PARISHIONERS AND CHRISTIANS FROM OTHER

DENOMINATIONS ARE VERY WORRIED ABOUT YOUR SPEECH AND THE WAY

YOU ADVOCATED VIOLENCE, DESTRUCTION AND HATRED AMONG OUR

PEOPLE. YOU HAD BEEN SAYING THAT IF ANYONE KNOWS OF A MEMBER

OF ANOTHER PARTY (YOU MENTIONED THE MDC) "KILL HIM". YOU

ENCOURAGED THE DESTRUCTION OF PUBLIC PROPERTY (PTC) WHICH IS

THERE FOR THE GOOD OF ALL PEOPLE.

THE PEOPLE OF BANKET ARE PEACEFUL PEOPLE IRRESPECTIVE OF

COLOR, CREEO OR POLITICAL ALLEGIANCE, WHO WANT TO WORK

TOGETHER ANO BUILO UP THE COUNTRY. YOUR AIM SEEMS TO BE THE

OPPOSITE. YOUR SPEECH HAS MAOE MANY PEOPLE VERY SAO, BECAUSE

YOU SEEM TO ENCOURAGE PEOPLE TO FIGHT A CIVIL WAR, A WAR OF

MURDER ANO OESTRUCTION.

MOST OF YOUR LISTENERS ARE CHRISTIANS. REMEMBER, THEY KNOW

THE COMMANDMENT "THOU SHALT NOT KILL". MOST OF YOUR

LISTENERS RESPECT THE TRADITIONAL VALUES OF THEIR

FOREFATHERS, FOR WHICH MURDER WAS A CRIME. THEY STILL CLING

TO THE IDEA OF HUMAN INTEGRITY (IN SHONA "UNHU"). WE ARE

VERY WORRIED THAT THESE VALUES ARE BEING DESTROYED THESE

DAYS AND ASK YOU TO THINK ABOUT YOUR ATTACK ON HUMAN DIGNITY

ON FRIDAY 19TH MAY, IN THE KUWADZANA HALL.

I WOULD LIKE TO ASK THE FOLLOWING QUESTION: WHEN YOU ARE

ELECTED WILL YOU FEEL RESPONSIBLE FOR ALL PEOPLE IN THE

CONSTITUENCY OR ONLY TO THE MEMBERS OF YOUR PARTY? THIS IS

IMPORTANT FOR ALL WHO VOTE, BECAUSE WE WANT MEMBERS IN

PARLIAMENT WHO INTEGRATE A COMMUNITY AND NOT THOSE WHO

DIVIDE, INTIMIDATE AND EVEN ENCOURAGE LAWLESSNESS.

WE ARE PRAYING DAILY IN OUR CONGREGATION FOR PEACE AND THE

ADVANCEMENT OF ZIMBABWE AND OR MUTUAL UNDERSTANDING OF ALL

PEOPLE IN THIS COUNTRY. WE PRAY ALSO FOR YOU, THAT YOU MAY

REALIZE WHAT THE TRUE VALUES ARE. I AM NOT WRITING AS A

POLITICIAN TO YOU BUT AS A PRIEST, WHO HAS THE GOOD OF ALL

PEOPLE IN THIS COUNTRY AT HEART.

YOURS SINCERELY,

W. THAMM SJ
