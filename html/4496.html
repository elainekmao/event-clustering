SUBJECT: COMMUNICATIONS BILATERAL: UPDATE AND GOI REQUEST

REF: A. ROME 5535

B. DOHERTY/FINTON COMMUNICATIONS

1. THIS IS AN ACTION REQUEST, PLEASE SEE PARAGRAPH 3.

2. ECONCOUNS MET WITH COMMUNICATIONS MINISTRY SENIOR

OFFICIALS NOVEMBER 20 AS A FOLLOW ON TO OUR OCTOBER 30

MEETING (REFTEL). PENDING RUBBERSTAMP BY COMMUNICATIONS

MINISTER GASPARRI, SECRETARY GENERAL VITTORIO STELO HAS NO

OBJECTIONS TO POSTPONING BILATERAL MEETING DATES TO MARCH 6

AND 7. (EMBASSY COMMENT: IN VIEW OF UPCOMING ITALIAN EU

PRESIDENCY, IT WOULD BE DIFFICULT, IF NOT IMPOSSIBLE, TO

RESCHEDULE DATES FOR BILATERAL MEETINGS AGAIN.)

3. MINISTRY OFFICIALS REQUESTED THAT WE PROVIDE THEM NO

LATER THE SECOND WEEK OF DECEMBER A PROPOSED DETAILED AGENDA

(I.E., SPECIFIC DISCUSSION ISSUES OF INTEREST TO THE USG

RATHER THAN GENERIC THEMES SUCH AS &E-COMMERCE8 OR

&AUDIOVISUAL8). THEY WILL PROVIDE EMBASSY WITH THEIR OWN

&WISH-LIST8 WITHIN THE SAME TIMEFRAME. MINISTRY OFFICIALS

ANTICIPATED THAT TWO AREAS THEY ARE CURRENTLY PARTICULARLY

FOCUSED ON ARE COMMUNICATIONS INFRASTRUCTURE SECURITY AND

WRC. THEY WOULD LIKE TO DEVELOP EXTENSIVELY THE SECURITY

ISSUES (AS PER REFTEL, THEY PROPOSE SECURITY ISSUES TO OCCUPY

THE ENTIRE FIRST AFTERNOON), WHILE THEY WOULD LIKE TO DISCUSS

CERTAIN KEY WRC QUESTIONS AS PART OF ONE OF THE THREE/FOUR

MARCH 7 SESSIONS.

4. REGARDING FUNDING, WE ENVISION EB WILL NEED TO BUDGET FOR

THE FOLLOWING:

TRANSPORTATION. IN PRINCIPLE, SINCE THE BILATERAL WILL BE

HELD ENTIRELY ON SITE AT THE COMMUNICATIONS MINISTRY, THE

ONLY TRANSPORTATION NEEDED WILL BE TO AND FROM THE MINISTRY.

THIS CAN BE ARRANGED AT NO COST THROUGH REGULAR MOTOR POOL

SERVICES. HOWEVER, SINCE MEETINGS ARE LIKELY TO RUN PAST

REGULAR OFFICE HOURS AND ALSO SINCE TRANSPORTATION WILL

LIKELY BE NEEDED TO TRAVEL TO AND FROM REPRESENTATIONAL

EVENTS, WE ESTIMATE THAT APPROXIMATELY 300-350 USD SHOULD BE

BUDGETED TO COVER DRIVER OVERTIME COSTS FOR THE DAYS OF MARCH

6 AND 7.

INTERPRETATION COSTS. COMMUNICATIONS MINISTRY OFFICIALS HAVE

INDICATED THAT THEY ARE WILLING TAKE ON ALL COSTS FOR

TECHNICAL/EQUIPMENT SUPPORT, BUT HAVE ASKED THAT WE SHARE THE

COST OF SIMULTANEOUS INTERPRETATION (I.E., USG AND GOI WOULD

COVER ONE DAY EACH). WE ESTIMATE THAT ONE INTERPRETATION DAY

WILL COST APPROXIMATELY 1000 USD.

REPRESENTATIONAL EVENT. THE MINISTRY IS PLANNING TO HOST ONE

DINNER AND TWO BUSINESS LUNCHES (IN ADDITION TO A SERIES OF

COFFEE BREAKS). WE BELIEVE IT WOULD BE MOST APPROPRIATE FOR

THE USG TO HOST A SEPARATE REPRESENTATIONAL EVENT. COST

WOULD DEPEND VERY MUCH ON THE TYPE OF EVENT. WE WOULD

ESTIMATE THAT A DINNER EVENT AT THE AMBASSADOR,S RESIDENCE

TO INCLUDE ALL BILATERAL PARTICIPANTS (USG AND GOI) PLUS

SELECTED SENIOR TELECOMMUNICATIONS SECTOR CONTACTS WOULD

RANGE BETWEEN 1600 AND 2000 USD.

5. SEPARATELY, BOTH THE AMERICAN CHAMBER OF COMMERCE

(AMCHAM) IN ITALY AND ITALIAN PRIVATE SECTOR

TELECOMMUNICATIONS OPERATORS HAVE EXPRESSED INTEREST IN

MEETING WITH THE U.S. DELEGATION OUTSIDE THE CONTEXT OF THE

OFFICIAL GOVERNMENT TO GOVERNMENT BILATERALS. IT HAS BEEN

TRADITION FOR THE AMCHAM TO HOST HIGH LEVEL U.S. VISITORS

(MOST RECENTLY AMCHAM HOSTED A DINNER RECEPTION IN HONOR OF

COMMERCE UNDERSECRETARY JAMES ROGAN). SUCH EVENT WOULD

DEPEND ON AVAILABILITY AND ARRIVAL DATES OF DELEGATION.

EMBASSY WOULD ALSO RECOMMEND THAT THE DELEGATION MEET WITH

ITALIAN OPERATORS, PRINCIPALLY TO OBTAIN INDUSTRY VIEWS

REGARDING THE ITALIAN COMMUNICATIONS MARKETS AND TO SOUND

THEM OUT ON POSSIBLE BUSINESS OPPORTUNITIES FOR U.S.

OPERATORS.
