SUBJECT: DEMARCHING THE ARAB MULTILATERAL AID

AGENCIES

REF: A) STATE 100455, B) KUWAIT 1468

1. (U) CLASSIFIED BY DCM RICHARD A. ALBRIGHT FOR

REASONS 1.5 (B,D).

2. (C) ECONCHIEF RAISED REF A DEMARCHE APRIL 19

WITH FINANCE MINISTRY UNDERSECRETARY KHALID AL-

BUSTANI. AL-BUSTANI SAID THAT THE UAE WOULD BE

FAVORABLY DISPOSED TOWARDS CONSIDERATION OF WAYS

IN WHICH THE ARAB FUND FOR ECONOMIC AND SOCIAL

DEVELOPMENT (AFESD) COULD ENGAGE POSTIVELY WITH

IRAQ. THAT SAID, HE NOTED THAT THE UAE AS HOST OF

THE UPCOMING BOARD OF GOVERNORS MEETING -- AND AS

AN ONLY MODEST CONTRIBUTOR TO AFESD -- WOULD NOT

BE PREPARED TO TAKE A LEADERSHIP ROLE ON THIS

ISSUE. HOWEVER, AS LONG AS AFSED DG ABDULLATIF

AL-HAMAD WAS FULLY ON BOARD, THE UAEG OFFICIAL

PREDICTED THAT THE MATTER WOULD BE WELL IN HAND.

3. (C) AL-BUSTANI NOTED THAT THE APRIL 22-23

GATHERING IN ABU DHABI WOULD BE BROADER THAN JUST

THE AFESD SESSION. THE ARAB MONETARY FUND BOARD

WOULD ALSO BE MEETING, AS WOULD A NUMBER OF

SMALLER ARAB MULTILATERAL LENDING INSTITUTIONS,

SUCH AS THE ARAB AUTHORITY FOR AGRICULTURAL

DEVELOPMENT AND THE ARAB ORGANIZATION FOR

INVESTMENT PROTECTION. IF THE USG\'S INTEREST IS

IN SEEKING IRAQ\'S REHABILITATION, WE WOULD BE WELL

ADVISED TO CAST OUR NET MORE BROADLY THAN JUST

AFESD, THE EMIRATI REMARKED.

4. (C) COMMENT: AL-BUSTANI\'S OBSERVATION

STRIKES US AS ON TARGET. WE WOULD NOTE THAT THE

ARAB MONETARY FUND -- WHICH IS ACTUALLY

RESPONSIBLE FOR ORGANIZING THE APRIL 22-23

MEETINGS -- IS CURRENTLY OWED MORE THAN 369

MILLION USD BY IRAQ (189 MILLION IN PRINCIPAL, THE

REST IN INTEREST). IRAQ HAS MADE NO PAYMENTS TO

THE AMF IN MORE THAN A DECADE, THE NUMBER TWO

OFFICIAL TOLD ECONOFF IN LATE 2002. POST

THEREFORE SUGGESTS THAT IN ADDITION TO THE AFESD-

SPECIFIC POINTS OUTLINED IN REF. A, CONSIDERATION

BE GIVEN TO BROADENING THE DEMARCHE TO ASK THAT

ALL THE RELEVANT ARAB FINANCIAL INSTITUTIONS

MEETING IN ABU DHABI LATER THIS MONTH TAKE THE

NECESSARY STEPS TO ENGAGE POSITIVELY WITH IRAQ AND

PARTICIPATE FULLY IN THE PROCESS OF

RECONSTRUCTION.

WAHBA
