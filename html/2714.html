SUBJECT: ITALY\'S MFA REQUESTS GEN. ZINNI TO SERVE AS

MEDIATOR IN BETHLEHEM

CLASSIFIED BY: DCM WILLIAM POPE FOR REASONS 1.5 (B) AND (D)

CONFIDENTIAL

1. (C) ON APRIL 4, LUCA FERRARI, MFA\'S OFFICE DIRECTOR FOR

THE MIDDLE EAST, CONTACTED NEA WATCHER TO EXPRESS THE GOI\'S

GRAVE CONCERN OVER THE SITUATION IN BETHLEHEM. ACCORDING TO

FERRARI, TANZIM MILITANTS, PALESTINIAN CIVILIANS, AND THE

GOVERNOR OF BETHLEHEM (WHOM HE DESCRIBED AS A "MODERATE

CHRISTIAN") HAVE TAKEN REFUGE INSIDE THE HOLY COMPLEX IN

BETHLEHEM (WHICH INCLUDES THE CHURCH OF NATIVITY) AND ARE

SURROUNDED BY ISRAELI TANKS.

2. (C) THE GOI WISHES TO FIND A MEDIATOR BETWEEN THE TWO

SIDES. THE SUGGESTION OF HAVING THE FRENCH, ITALIAN,

SPANISH, OR BELGIAN CG IN JERUSALEM SERVE AS THE MEDIATOR HAS

BEEN REFUSED BY BOTH SIDES. THE SUGGESTION OF HAVING EU

SECRETARY GENERAL JAVIER SOLANA SERVE AS MEDIATOR WAS REFUSED

SIPDIS

BY THE ISRAELIS, SAID FERRARI.

3. (C) FERRARI ADDED THAT THE GOI NOW WISHES TO HAVE GEN.

ZINNI SERVE AS THE MEDIATOR BETWEEN THE TWO SIDES. WHEN

ASKED WHY GEN. ZINNI, FERRARI ANSWERED: "GEN. ZINNI IS

AMERICAN, CATHOLIC, AND A MILITARY MAN".

4. (C) ACCORDING TO FERRARI, THE SAME REQUEST OF HAVING GEN.

ZINNI SERVE AS MEDIATOR IN BETHLEHEM WILL BE MADE APRIL 4 BY

THE ITALIAN CG AND ITALIAN AMBASSADOR TO OUR CG AND OUR

AMBASSADOR IN JERUSALEM AND TEL AVIV, RESPECTIVELY. THE

ITALIAN EMBASSY IN WASHINGTON IS ALSO EXPECTED TO DELIVER THE

SAME REQUEST TO THE DEPARTMENT APRIL 4.

5. (C) IN A MEETING APRIL 4 BETWEEN AMBASSADOR SEMBLER AND PM

BERLUSCONI, THE PM DID NOT RAISE THE SITUATION IN BETHLEHEM

BUT SPOKE IN MORE GENERAL TERMS ABOUT THE CONFLICT IN THE

MIDDLE EAST AND THE NEED FOR THE US TO INVOLVE THE EU,

RUSSIA, AND FRIENDLY ARAB COUNTRIES AND HAVE THEM SHARE IN

THE RESPONSIBILITY OF SEEKING A PEACEFUL SOLUTION TO THE

CONFLICT IN THE MIDDLE EAST.

6. (C) COMMENT: ITALY\'S SENSE OF URGENCY COULD PARTLY BE

EXPLAINED BY THE FACT THAT, UP UNTIL APRIL 4, THERE WERE FIVE

ITALIAN JOURNALISTS TRAPPED IN THE CHURCH OF NATIVITY. AN

ITALIAN JOURNALIST WAS ALSO KILLED RECENTLY IN THE MIDDLE

EAST VIOLENCE. END COMMENT.
