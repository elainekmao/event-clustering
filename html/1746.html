1. (C) BEGIN TEXT. THE VATICAN CONDEMNED THE WEST\'S

PROSECUTION OF THE GULF WAR, AND IT HAS CONSISTENTLY

OPPOSED U.S. SANCTIONS POLICY TOWARD IRAQ. WE SHOULD

EXPECT IT TO CONTINUE TO DO SO. MANY FACTORS ARE

INVOLVED IN THE VATICAN\'S TOLERATION OF THE IRAQI

REGIME. THE MOST IMPORTANT ARE:

-- RELIGIOUS PRINCIPLES/HUMANITARIAN CONCERNS: THE

VATICAN SEEKS TO TRANSLATE ITS RELIGIOUS BELIEFS, AS

WELL AS ITS SINCERE HUMANITARIAN CONCERNS, INTO

CONCRETE POLICY. ITS NON-CONFRONTATIONAL POLICY TOWARD

IRAQ IS PARTIALLY BASED ON ITS CONVICTION THAT THE

ECONOMIC EMBARGO IS SEVERELY IMPACTING THE PEOPLE OF

IRAQ. TO COUNTER THIS BELIEF, CREDIBLE ARGUMENTS MUST

BE MADE THAT THE EMBARGO IS NOT RESPONSIBLE FOR THE

HARDSHIP BEING EXPERIENCED BY THE IRAQI PEOPLE;

-- NATIONAL SELF-INTEREST: THE VATICAN LABORS TO

PROTECT CATHOLICS AROUND THE WORLD. ITS CONSISTENT

SUPPORT OF SADDAM HUSSEIN PARTIALLY RESULTS FROM ITS

DESIRE TO PROTECT THE 600,000 IRAQI CATHOLICS FROM

ISLAMIC FUNDAMENTALISM. THE VATICAN HAS CONSISTENTLY

TOLD US THAT, IN ITS VIEW, THE STATE OF RELIGIOUS

FREEDOM IN IRAQ IS BETTER THAN IN MANY OTHER ARAB

COUNTRIES. IN THE VATICAN\'S VIEW, THE LIKELY

ALTERNATIVE TO SADDAM WOULD BE AN ISLAMIC GOVERNMENT.

FOR THE VATICAN, A SECULAR DICTATORSHIP IS MUCH

PREFERABLE TO AN ISLAMIC ONE. (NOTE: 600,00 IRAQI\'S

BELONG TO THE CHALDEAN CATHOLIC CHURCH. THIS CHURCH

IS IN FULL COMMUNION WITH ROME, ALTHOUGH IT RETAINS

RITES, TRADITIONS, AND DISCIPLINE WHICH ARE

SUBSTANTIALLY DIFFERENT FROM ROMAN CATHOLICISM).

--RELIGIOUS MANEUVERING: THE ROMAN CATHOLIC CHURCH IS

CLOSE TO SIGNING AN AGREEMENT WITH THE ASSYRIAN CHURCH

OF THE EAST ON REUNIFICATION WITH ROME. (NOTE: THE

ASSYRIAN CHURCH OF THE EAST IS AN ANCIENT CHURCH WHICH

BROKE WITH THE REST OF WORLD CHRISTENDOM IN THE 4TH

CENTURY). THE ASSYRIAN CHURCH OF THE EAST IS CENTERED

IN IRAQ, AND AS A CHRISTIAN DENOMINATION PREFERS

SADDAM HUSSEIN TO AN ISLAMICIST LEADER. THE VATICAN

IS VERY SENSITIVE AT THIS POINT TO AVOID TAKING

POSITIONS WHICH WOULD ALIENATE THE ASSYRIAN CHURCH.

-- PERSONAL DESIRES OF JOHN PAUL II: THERE ARE THREE

MAJOR TRIPS WHICH THE POPE HAS EXPRESSED A DESIRE TO

COMPLETE BEFORE HIS HEALTH FURTHER DETERIORATES--

RUSSIA, CHINA, IRAQ. PLANS FOR THE POPE TO VISIT THE

SITE OF ABRAHAM\'S BIRTH IN UR (IRAQ) WERE CALLED OFF

IN 2000. THE POPE STILL HARBORS A DESIRE TO PRAY AT

THIS SITE. THIS PAPAL DESIRE IS ANOTHER REASON WHY

THE VATICAN WISHES TO MAINTAIN GOOD RELATIONS WITH

IRAQ.

2. (C) CONCLUSION: UNLESS SADDAM\'S HUMAN RIGHTS

ABUSES INCREASE SUBSTANTIALLY, AND DIRECTLY IMPACT THE

IRAQI CATHOLIC COMMUNITY, IT IS UNLIKELY THAT THE

VATICAN WILL CHANGE ITS POSITION ON IRAQ. IT IS

IMPORTANT THAT WE CONTINUE TO BRIEF THE VATICAN ON

CONTINUED IRAQI ABUSES, AND TO PROVIDE CREDIBLE

EVIDENCE OF SADDAM HUSSEIN\'S MALEVOLENT INTENT AGAINST

NEIGHBORING COUNTRIES.

MERANTE
