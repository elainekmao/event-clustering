1. SUMMARY: AS REPORTED IN SEPTEL, THE FOLLOWING

IS AN ENGLISH TRANSLATION OF THE SIXTEEN RESOLUTIONS

PASSED BY THE CROATIAN PARLIAMENT REGARDING THE

POLITICAL CONDITIONS IN CROATIA AND THE

IMPLEMENTATION OF THE CONSTITUTIONAL RESOLUTION ON

THE SOVEREIGNTY AND INDEPENDENCE OF CROATIA. END

SUMMARY.

FOR THESE REASONS, THE REPUBLIC OF CROATIA IS

SUSPENDING ALL RELATIONS WITH THE REPUBLIC OF SERBIA

AS LONG AS OFFICIAL SERBIA SUPPORTS SUCH A POLICY,

AND THE GOVERNMENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF CROATIA WILL

2. AN UNDECLARED AND UNPROVOKED AGGRESSION HAS BEEN

CARRIED OUT AGAINST CROATIA FOR SOME TIME NOW.

IT IS A CAMOUFLAGED, UNDERHANDED AND DIRTY WAR

IMPOSED ON CROATIA WHICH HAS SO FAR TAKEN HUNDREDS

OF HUMAN LIVES, AND CAUSED HUGE MATERIAL

DESTRUCTION, EVEN DESTROYED ENTIRE SUBURBS.

THE YUGOSLAV PEOPLES\' ARMY IS DIRECTLY AND

INDIRECTLY PARTICIPATING IN THE AGGRESSION AGAINST

CROATIA ORGANIZED BY THE REPUBLIC OF SERBIA WITH THE

ASSISTANCE OF THE TERRORIST REBELLIOUS GROUPS.

GROUPS OF TERRORISTS WERE RECRUITED FROM WITHIN

SERBIAN RANKS IN CROATIA WHO ARE INDOCTRINATED WITH

THE GREATER SERBIAN AND CHETNIK IDEOLOGY.

BASED ON THE UNTRUTHS AND HISTORICAL FABRICATIONS,

THE MILITARISM AND GREATER SERBIAN HEGEMONY, THE

PRESENT CONDUCT OF THE STATE BODIES OF THE REPUBLIC

OF SERBIA CANNOT REPRESENT A BASIS FOR THE

CONSTRUCTIVE, PEACEFUL RESOLUTION OF THE YUGOSLAV

CRISIS.

3. FROM THE ESTABLISHMENT OF THE POSTWAR BOLSHEVIK

YUGOSLAVIA, THE YUGOSLAV PEOPLES\' ARMY WAS A

CONTINUOUS IMPORTANT INSTRUMENT OF THE POLITICIAL

AUTHORITIES, AND TODAY IT IS A PART OF THE COMMUNIST

MILITARY COMMAND, RESISTING THE INEVITABLE

DEMOCRATIC REFORM OF SOCIETY, ESCAPING EVERY CONTROL

AND THREATENING THE DEMOCRATICALLY ELECTED

AUTHORITIES IN THE REPUBLICS.

A PART OF THE ARMY IS DIRECTLY SIDING WITH THE

TERRORISTS.

4. THESE DAYS THE AGGRESSIVE ATTACKS ON CROAITAN

TERRITIORIES CAUSED THE DESTRUCTION OF ENTIRE

SUBURBS AND THE EXODUS OF THOUSANDS OF FAMILIES FROM

THEIR HOMES, IN KEEPING WITH THE TERRORISTS\'

GENOCIDAL PLANS FOR THE CREATION OF ETHNICALLY PURE

SERBIAN TERRITIORIES ON THE TERRITORY OF THE

REPUBLIC OF CROATIA.

THE PARLIAMENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF CROATIA ON BEHALF

OF ALL THE CITIZENS WHO HAVE RECEIVED ASSISTANCE IS

PUBLICLY THANKING THOSE INDIVIDUALS, FAMILIES,

5. THE PARLIAMENT REQUESTS THAT THE GOVERNMENT OF

THE REPUBLIC OF CROATIA TAKE IMMEDIATE ACTION FOR

THE NECESSARY MOBILIZATION OF ALL DEFENSE FORCES.

COMPANIES, HEALTH, SOCIAL AND OTHER SERVICES SHOULD

IMMEDIATELY ADAPT THEIR STRUCTURES, STAFF AND

ACTIVITIES FOR SPECIAL DEFENSE CONDITIONS AND FOR

THE REAL NEEDS OF THE POPULATION, SUBORDINATING

EVERYTHING TO THE DEFENSE OF DEMOCRACY AND THE

TERRITORIAL INTEGRITY OF THE REPUBLIC.

ALL STATE SERVICES AND THE ENTIRE NATION SHOULD

ADAPT THEIR ACTIVITIES TO THE CONDITIONS IN WHICH

THE REPUBLIC OF CROATIA FINDS ITSELF.

-

6. IN THE SITUATION WHEN THE REPUBLIC OF CROATIA IS

EXPOSED TO OPEN AGGRESSION ON ITS TERRITORY, THE

MOST IMPORTANT TASK OF ALL ITS CITIZENS INCLUDING

ALL STATE AND POLITICAL STRUCTURES AS WELL AS

REPRESENTATIVES IN THE PARLIAMENT IS THE DEFENSE OF

THE HOMELAND ACCORDING TO THEIR STATUS AND

RESPONSIBILITIES.

7. THE PARLIAMENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF CROATIA

CONSIDERS THAT THE MEDIA IN THE REPUBLIC SHOULD BE

MORE EFFICIENT IN CARRYING OUT THEIR DUTIES OF

INFORMING TRUTHFULLY AND OBJECTIVELY THE DOMESTIC

AND INTERNATIONAL PUBLICS ABOUT EVENTS IN CROATIA.

8. THE PARLIAMENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF CROATIA IS

EXPRESSING ITS GRATITUDE TO ALL CROATS OUTSIDE OF

THE HOMELAND FOR THEIR PAST AND FUTURE SUPPORT FOR

DEMOCRATIC CROATIA IN ITS EFFORTS FOR DEFENSE AND

DEVELOPMENT.

9. THE PARLIAMENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF CROATIA IS

GRATEFUL TO THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY FOR ITS HELP

SO FAR TO CROATIAN DEMOCRACY AND IS CALLING ALL

PARLIAMENTS OF DEMOCRATIC COUNTRIES ALL OVER THE

WORLD TO GRANT RECOGNITION TO THE REPUBLIC OF

CROATIA WHICH IS CONSTITUTIONALLY ESTABLISHED AS A

DEMOCRATIC AND SOCIAL STATE BASED ON LAW.

IN REGARD TO THIS, THE PARLIAMENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF

CROATIA ENDORSES THE LETTER WHICH THE PRESIDENT OF

THE PARLIAMENT SENT TO THE PARLIAMENTS OF EUROPEAN

COUNTRIES AND INTERNATIONAL ORGANIZATIONS.

CROATIA.

FOR THE REASONS BROUGHT FORTH, THE CITIZENS OF THE

REPUBLIC OF CROATIA CONSIDER THE YUGOSLAV PEOPLES\'

ARMY AS AN OCCUPYING FORCE.

CONSEQUENTLY, THE PARLIAMENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF

CROATIA, AS THE REPRESENTATIVE BODY OF ITS CITIZENS,

DEMANDS THAT THE YUGOSLAV PEOPLES\' ARMY WITHOUT

DELAY RETREAT TO ITS BASES AND THAT, IN ADEQUATE

TIME, IT RETREAT FROM THE TERRITORY OF THE REPUBLIC

OF CROATIA DURING THE PROCESS OF DISASSOCIATION.

THE PARLIAMENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF CROATIA EXPECTS

THAT THE PRESIDENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF CROATIA AND

THE GOVERNMENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF CROATIA WILL TAKE

NECESSARY STEPS TO IMPLEMENT THIS PARLIAMENTARY

RESOLUTION.

11. THE PARLIAMENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF CROATIA IS

CALLING ALL INTERNATIONAL ORGANIZATIONS WHICH DEAL

WITH THE ISSUE OF HUMAN RIGHTS AND THE RIGHTS OF

NATIONAL MINORITIES TO SEND THEIR REPRESENTATVIES TO

CROATIA SO THAT THEY MAY SEE FOR THEMSELVES THE REAL

UNCLASSIFIED

UNCLASSIFIED

SITUATION OF CIVIL, HUMAN AND NATIONAL RIGHTS OF

SERBS AND ALL OTHER NATIONS THAT LIVE IN CROATIA.

12. THE MEMBER OF THE PRESIDENCY FROM MONTENEGRO,

BRANKO KOSTIC, THROUGH HIS ACTIVITIES SO FAR, AND

ESPECIALLY HAVING PUBLICLY SUPPORTED GREATER SERBIAN

HEGEMONY, AND ESSENTIALLY ENTICING DISOBEDIENCE AND

VIOLENCE TOWARDS THE LEGAL ORGANS OF THE REPUBLIC OF

CROATIA, HAS DISCREDITED HIMSELF FROM CARRYING OUT

ANY ACTIVITY WHATSOEVER IN THE ORGANS OF THE FEDERAL

GOVERNMENT OF THE SFRJ THAT IS RELATED TO THE

PRESENT CONDITIONS IN CROATIA.

13. THE PARLIAMENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF CROATIA

ACCEPTS THE DECISION OF THE PRESIDENCY OF THE SFRJ

OF AUGUST 3, 1991 ABOUT AN URGENT AND ABSOLUTE

CEASE-FIRE IN THE REPUBLIC OF CROATIA AND CONSIDERS

NECESSARY EVERY INITIATIVE AND EVERY EFFORT,

ESPECIALLY FROM THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITY, THAT AN

IMMEDIATE CEASE-FIRE BE IMPLEMENTED IN CROATIA.

CONSEQUENTLY, THE MISSION OF THE EUROPEAN OBSERVERS

IS INEVITABLE AND WELCOME AS WELL AS ALL OTHER FORM

OF DIRECT PARTICIPATION OF EUROPE IN THE RESOLUTION

OF THE CRISIS. AT THE SAME TIME, THE PARLIAMENT

FIRMLY DECIDED, BECAUSE OF THE ABOVE-MENTIONED

REASONS, TO PROTEST AGAINST THE APPOINTMENT OF

BRANKO KOSTIC IN THE SFRJ PRESIDENCY AS CHAIRMAN OF

THE COMMISSION TO MONITOR THE CEASE-FIRE.

14. THE PARLIAMENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF CROATIA IS

15. GENERAL ZIVOTA AVRAMOVIC UNTIL THE BEGINNING OF

JULY THIS YEAR WAS THE COMMANDER OF THE III. ARMY

DISTRICT UNDER WHOSE JURISDICTION KOSOVO FALLS.

GENERAL ZIVOTA AVRAMOVIC, THROUGH HIS FLAGRANT

VIOLATION OF HUMAN RIGHTS, PLAYED THE ROLE OF THE

PACIFIER OF KOSOVO, FOR WHICH THE REPUBLIC OF SERBIA

WAS COMDEMNED BY THE WORLD COMMUNITY AND MANY

PARLIAMENTS OF DEMOCRATIC COUNTRIES, INCLUDING THE

EUROPEAN PARLIAMENT.

THE FACT IS THAT THIS SAME GENERAL, SINCE JULY 1991,

HAS BEEN THE COMMANDER OF THE V. ARMY DISTRICT UNDER

WHOSE JURISDICTION MOST OF THE TERRITORY OF THE

REPUBLIC OF CROATIA FALLS.

THE PARLIAMENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF CROATIA CONSIDERS

HIS APPOINTMENT TO BE A RUDE PROVOCATION.
