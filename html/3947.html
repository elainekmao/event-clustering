SUBJECT: PROPOSED DEMOCRACY CONFERENCE

1. POST CONCURS WITH THE IDEA OF A REGIONAL DEMOCRACY

CONFERENCE TO DISCUSS HOW BEST TO PROMOTE THE ISSUES RAISED

IN REFTEL, WITH THE GOAL OF BETTER FOCUSING OUR REGIONAL

EFFORTS AND OF ACHIEVING GREATER REGIONAL COORDINATION

BETWEEN EMBASSIES AND WITH THE VARIOUS WASHINGTON AGENCIES.

WHILE THE SITUATION IN EACH COUNTRY IS UNIQUE, NONE OPERATES

IN TOTAL ISOLATION FROM ITS NEIGHBORS, AND THERE ARE

CONSTANTS WHERE A REGIONAL APPROACH CAN BE BENEFICIAL.

2. POST IS INTERESTED IN EXPLORING BEST PRACTICES, AS WELL

AS COORDINATING APPROACHES TO THE MEPI INITIATIVE. ISSUES

OF MOST RELEVANCE HERE WOULD BE JUDICIAL REFORM, PRESS

FREEDOMS, THE ROLE OF NGOS, CIVIL SOCIETY AND EDUCATION,

INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY AS A CATALYST FOR DEMOCRACY, AND

ELECTIONS TRAINING.

GNEHM
