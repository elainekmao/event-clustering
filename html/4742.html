SUBJECT: JAZZ AMBASSADORS 2003/AMMAN

REF: STATE 250221

1. PA AMMAN IS DELIGHTED TO REQUEST INCLUSION IN THE 2003

JAZZ AMBASSADORS TOUR. POST PREFERS THE MAY TOUR TIMEFRAME

IN ORDER TO COINCIDE WITH POSSIBLE PA PLANS TO ORGANIZE AN

AMERICAN ARTS FESTIVAL DURING THAT MONTH. IF MAY IS NOT

FEASIBLE, POST ALSO WOULD BE INTERESTED IN HOSTING THE TOUR

IN SEPTEMBER. THE IDEAL NUMBER OF DAYS IS THREE FULL

PROGRAM DAYS, PLUS ADDITIONAL TRAVEL DAYS AS REQUIRED. IN

ADDITION TO POST\'S WEEKEND OF FRIDAY AND SATURDAY, MAY 14

AND MAY 25 ARE EXPECTED IN-COUNTRY HOLIDAYS AND THUS ARE NOT

PREFERABLE PROGRAMMING DAYS.

2. PA AMMAN IS VERY PLEASED WITH SELECTION OF THE 2003

TOUR\'S THEME OF THE INFLUENCE OF LATIN RHYTHMS ON AMERICAN

JAZZ. THROUGHOUT JORDAN, YOUNG AUDIENCES IN PARTICULAR HAVE

A REAL THIRST FOR ALL TYPES OF LATIN-INSPIRED MUSIC. THIS

LATIN THEME WILL ENABLE US TO DEVELOP A PROGRAM WITH MAXIMUM

EXPOSURE AMONG THE YOUTH AUDIENCE. PRESENTATION OF THIS

YEAR\'S JAZZ AMBASSADORS TOUR THUS WILL HELP US MEET COUNTRY

PLAN OBJECTIVES OF PROMOTING MUTUAL UNDERSTANDING AMONG THE

YOUTH. BOTH THE SHORT- AND LONG-TERM RESULTS OF THIS

PROGRAM WILL BE A CONTINUED INCREASE IN CULTURAL DIALOGUE

WITH WIDER, PARTICULARLY YOUTH AUDIENCES IN MUSLIM-MAJORITY

COUNTRIES LIKE JORDAN.

3. SECURITY: EMBASSY AMMAN IS CURRENTLY UNDER A TRAVEL

WARNING, BUT ALL SERVICES AND PROGRAMS, INCLUDING PUBLIC

AFFAIRS EXCHANGE PROGRAMS, ARE OPERATING NORMALLY. PA

COORDINATES WITH POST RSO TO ENSURE THAT SECURITY

CONSIDERATIONS ARE MET WHEN PLANNING EVENTS FOR VISITING

PERFORMERS AND SPEAKERS.

GNEHM
