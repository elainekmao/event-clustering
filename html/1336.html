SUBJECT: PROMINENT ZIMBABWEAN BUSINESS LEADERS TRYING

TO ENCOURAGE A SOLUTION TO THE LAND PROBLEM AND URGE

POLITICAL PARTIES TO WORK OUT THEIR DIFFERENCES

REFTEL: HARARE 5983

CLASSIFIED BY AMBASSADOR TOM MCDONALD FOR REASONS 1.5

(B) AND (D).

1. (C) SUMMARY: THE CEO OF A MAJOR ZIMBABWEAN

CORPORATION TOLD AMBASSADOR NOVEMBER 2 THAT HE AND OLD

MUTUAL INSURANCE COMPANY ARE ARRANGING A PRIVATE CREDIT

FACILITY TO PURCHASE 300 TO 400 COMMERCIAL FARMS TO HELP

BREAK THE LOGJAM ON LAND REFORM. THE CEO ALSO STATED

THAT HE AND OTHER BUSINESS LEADERS ARE ENCOURAGING THE

RULING ZANU-PF AND OPPOSITION MDC TO IRON OUT THEIR

DIFFERENCES THROUGH THIRD-PARTY MEDIATION. HE IS HAVING

AN INTERMEDIARY APPROACH FORMER ZAMBIAN PRESIDENT

KENNETH KAUNDA TO DETERMINE IF HE WOULD SERVE AS

MEDIATOR. END SUMMARY.

2. (C) THE AMBASSADOR SPOKE NOVEMBER 2 WITH PATRICK

ROONEY (PROTECT), THE CEO OF DELTA CORPORATION, A

ZIMBABWEAN CORPORATION THAT IS CONTROLLED BY OLD MUTUAL

AND SOUTH AFRICAN BREWERIES, AND IS IN THE BEVERAGE AND

TOURISM BUSINESSES IN ZIMBABWE. ROONEY IS A WELL-

CONNECTED WHITE ZIMBABWEAN WHO IS POLITICALLY ASTUTE AND

MOVES EASILY IN A VARIETY OF BUSINESS AND GOVERNMENT

CIRCLES. ROONEY TOLD THE AMBASSADOR THAT HE AND OLD

MUTUAL ARE ARRANGING A CREDIT FACILITY FOR THE GOZ TO

PURCHASE 300 TO 400 COMMERCIAL FARMS CURRENTLY

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 HARARE 06191 01 OF 02 021653Z

DESIGNATED FOR ACQUISITION. PART OF THE PROPOSAL WOULD

ALLOW WAR VETERANS OCCUPYING FARMS TO BE RELOCATED TO

THE NEWLY PURCHASED PROPERTIES. ROONEY STATED THAT HE

HOPES THIS MOVE WILL HELP BREAK THE LOGJAM ON LAND

REFORM AND REMOVE THE LAWLESS ELEMENTS, NAMELY THE WAR

VETERANS, FROM THE LAND RESETTLEMENT PICTURE, THEREBY

DEMONSTRATING TO THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY THAT LAW

AND ORDER IS RETURNING TO ZIMBABWE.

3. (C) ROONEY CONTINUED THAT THIS PROPOSAL WOULD SOON

BE PUT TO PRESIDENT MUGABE, BUT IT WAS UNCLEAR TO HIM

WHETHER THE PRESIDENT WOULD ACCEPT IT. THE CEO SAID

THAT VICE-PRESIDENT MSIKA HAD ENDORSED THE PROGRAM, BUT

BELIEVED THAT MINISTER OF STATE FOR INFORMATION AND

PUBLICITY JONATHAN MOYO, MINISTER FOR LOCAL GOVERNMENT

IGNATIUS CHOMBO, AND MINISTER FOR LANDS, AGRICULTURE AND

RESETTLEMENT JOSEPH MADE COULD STILL SCUTTLE THE EFFORT.

ROONEY STATED THAT THE COMMERCIAL FARMER\'S UNION (CFU)

HAS NOT BEEN ABLE TO GET ITS ACT TOGETHER IN NEGOTIATING

WITH THE GOZ. HE SAID THAT THE CFU IS NOT INVOLVED IN

THE DEAL, NOTING THAT IT WAS STRICTLY A PRIVATE BUSINESS

PROPOSAL.

4. (C) ON A SEPARATE SUBJECT, ROONEY INFORMED THE

AMBASSADOR THAT HE AND OTHER BUSINESS LEADERS ARE

ENCOURAGING THE RULING ZANU-PF AND OPPOSITION MDC TO

WORK OUT THEIR DIFFERENCES AND ARE SEEKING A MEDIATOR TO

HELP THEM. THERE IS CURRENTLY A SHORT LIST OF POTENTIAL

MEDIATORS, AT THE TOP OF WHICH IS FORMER ZAMBIAN

PRESIDENT KENNETH KAUNDA. KAUNDA IS A GOOD POSSIBILITY

BUT MAY BE A PROBLEM WITH MUGABE BECAUSE OF HIS PRIOR

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 04 HARARE 06191 01 OF 02 021653Z

SUPPORT OF JOSHUA NKOMO. THERE ARE NO SOUTH AFRICANS ON

THE SHORT LIST, ROONEY CONTINUED. THE AMBASSADOR TOLD

ROONEY THAT THE USG WELCOMES BOTH THESE INITIATIVES AND

ENCOURAGES ALL THE PARTIES INVOLVED TO KEEP THE CHANNELS

OF DIALOGUE OPEN. THE AMBASSADOR ASKED ROONEY TO KEEP

US APPRISED OF ANY DEVELOPMENTS IN THESE NEGOTIATIONS.

5. (C) COMMENT: WE BELIEVE ROONEY TO BE A VERY CREDIBLE

SOURCE AND WHAT HE TOLD US REPRESENTS A PROLIFERATION OF

BEHIND-THE-SCENES ATTEMPTS TO GET THE GOVERNMENT AND

OPPOSITION TALKING. IT TRACKS PARTICULARLY WELL WITH

WHAT BUSINESSMAN AND MDC SUPPORTER CLIVE PUZEY TOLD US,

IN THAT THE PRESIDENT\'S OFFICE HAD BEEN TALKING TO THE

MDC ABOUT A PRIVATELY-FUNDED LAND DEAL AS PART OF A

RETIREMENT PACKAGE FOR MUGABE (REFTEL). ROONEY\'S

REMARKS ABOUT ENCOURAGING THE PARTIES TO SEEK MEDIATION

COULD ALSO BE AN INDICATION THAT ATTEMPTS BY ZANU-PF AND

MDC TO NEGOTIATE HAVE STALLED. IT IS DIFFICULT TO

PREDICT WHETHER ANY OF THESE DISCUSSIONS WILL AMOUNT TO

ANYTHING AND HEAD OFF A MORE PUBLIC CONFRONTATION

BETWEEN OPPOSITION SUPPORTERS AND THE POWERS-THAT-BE.

WE ARE ENCOURAGED NEVERTHELESS THAT THE STAKEHOLDERS DO

SEEM TO BE TALKING OFF-STAGE EVEN AS THEIR ON-STAGE

THEATRICS BECOME MORE VITRIOLIC. THE AMBASSADOR WILL

CONTINUE TO ENCOURAGE ROONEY\'S EFFORTS. END COMMENT.
