SUBJECT: PRESIDENT BUSH\'S RAMADAN GREETING:

DISTRIBUTION AND MEDIA COVERAGE

REF: STATE 220289

1. PRESIDENT BUSH\'S NOVEMBER 5 RAMADAN GREETING

RECEIVED WIDE MEDIA PLACEMENT IN JORDAN. PA AMMAN

DISTRIBUTED THE STATEMENT TO EACH OF THE LOCAL

DAILIES, FOLLOWED BY PERSONAL PHONE CALLS TO EDITORS-

IN-CHIEF, RESULTING IN PLACEMENT IN EACH OF JORDAN\'S

ARABIC AND ENGLISH DAILIES TODAY, NOVEMBER 6.

HEADLINES READ: "BUSH CONGRATULATES MUSLIMS ON THE

BEGINNING OF THE HOLY MONTH OF RAMADAN" (AL-DUSTOUR

AND AL-RA\'I); "BUSH OFFERS HIS BEST WISHES TO MUSLIMS

ON THE OCCASION OF THE BEGINNING OF THE HOLY MONTH"

(AL-ARAB AL-YAWM); "U.S. PRESIDENT GREETS MUSLIMS ON

RAMADAN" (JORDAN TIMES). ADDITIONALLY, THE PETRA NEWS

AGENCY, JORDAN\'S OFFICIAL WIRE SERVICE, CARRIED A

REPORT ABOUT THE PRESIDENT\'S STATEMENT, QUOTING

EXCERPTS FROM THE MESSAGE.

3. ELECTRONIC MEDIA COVERAGE: RADIO SAWA, JORDAN\'S

MOST POPULAR RADIO STATION AMONG YOUTH AUDIENCES,

REPORTED THE PRESIDENT\'S STATEMENT THROUGOUT THE DAY

AT EACH OF ITS QUARTER HOUR NEWS BULLETINS. PA AMMAN

ALSO MADE THE STATEMENT AVAILABLE TO THE JORDAN

TELEVISION AND RADIO SERVICES AND WILL MONITOR THOSE

OUTLETS FOR PLACEMENT.

GNEHM
