SUBJECT: KUWAITIS DENOUNCE BIN LADEN

1. KUWAITI GOVERNMENT OFFICIALS AND OPINION LEADERS

DENOUNCED THE BIN LADEN TAPE RELEASED BY THE PENTAGON

DECEMBER 13.

2. ASKED HIS VIEW OF THE TAPE, KUWAIT\'S ACTING PRIME

MINISTER AND FOREIGN MINISTER SHAYKH SABAH SAID: "EVERYONE

WHO HEARS SUCH THINGS WILL DEFINITELY BE SHOCKED WHEN HE

HEARS THE ONE WHO MURDERS PEOPLE LAUGHING AND HAPPY.

THEREFORE, WE REJECT SUCH THINGS." SHAYKH SABAH MADE THIS

STATEMENT TO LOCAL REPORTERS UPON HIS RETURN FROM LONDON

DECEMBER 14.

3. KUWAIT\'S ARABIC DAILY "AL-ANBA" QUOTED THE VIEWS OF AN

UNNAMED OFFICIAL SOURCE AT THE FOREIGN MINISTRY IN AN

ARTICLE PUBLISHED DECEMBER 15. ACCORDING TO THE NEWSPAPER,

THE OFFICIAL SAID THE TAPE REPRESENTS A DEFINITE CONVICTION

AGAINST BIN LADEN AND DECISIVE EVIDENCE ABOUT HIS

INVOLVEMENT AND THAT OF HIS ASSOCIATES IN THE SEPTEMBER 11

ATTACKS.

4. A LIBERAL COLUMNIST, FOUAD AL-HASHEM, PUBLISHED A PIECE

DECEMBER 15 IN THE KUWAITI ARABIC DAILY "AL-WATAN" ABOUT THE

BIN LADEN TAPE. AL-HASHEM ARGUED: "NO HUMAN BEING HAS EVER

SEEN THE DEVIL.... (BUT) THE SEPTEMBER 11 ATTACKS SHOWED

THE WHOLE WORLD WHAT THE REAL DEVIL LOOKED LIKE WHEN HE

APPEARED ON THE TAPE FOUND BY THE AMERICAN FORCES. WE SAW

HIM AS HE LAUGHED...AND AS HE EXPLAINED HOW HE HAD EXPECTED

LESS DESTRUCTION AND FEWER DEATHS FROM THE ATTACK ON THE

WORLD TRADE CENTER. DO YOU NOW KNOW WHAT THE DEVIL LOOKS

LIKE?"
