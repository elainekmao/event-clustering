SUBJECT: URGENT ACTION REQUEST ON FRY ANTI-TERRORISM LAW

REF: SECSTATE 125049

Classified by A/Polcouns Eric Lundberg, Reasons 1.5 b/d

1.(C) In response to reftel message, Dutch MFA Balkans expert

Joop Wijnands told A/Polcouns that EU action on the proposed

FRY anti-terrorism law was not likely today or this weekend.

However, Wijnands said that the Dutch plan to propose the

adoption of a tough statement at the July 4 POCO in Brussels.
