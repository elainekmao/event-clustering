SUBJECT: INVITATION TO ORDER SEPTEMBER 11 COMMEMORATION

BOOK PACKAGE.

1. THE AMMAN ARABIC BOOK PROGRAM IS PREPARING A

SEPTEMBER 11 COMMEMORATION BOOK SET INTENDED FOR

DISTRIBUTION TO OFFICIAL CONTACTS TO MARK THE FIRST

ANNIVERSARY OF THE 9/11 ATTACKS. THE PACKAGE CONSISTS

OF THREE BOOKS TRANSLATED INTO ARABIC: "ANTHOLOGY OF

AMERICAN LITERATURE," "THE AMERICAN READER: WORDS THAT

MOVED A NATION," AND "READINGS IN AMERICAN DEMOCRACY."

THE THREE BOOKS WILL BE HARD BOUND AND CONTAINED IN A

HARD FOLDER BOX. AN INSCRIPTION OF A QUOTATION FROM

WILLIAM FAULKNER\'S NOBEL PRIZE SPEECH WILL BE INSCRIBED

ON THE COVER.

2. AMMAN PLANS TO PREPARE BETWEEN 500 AND 1000 SETS

WHICH WILL BE READY FOR SHIPPING AROUND AUGUST 15.

POSTS ARE ENCOURAGED TO PLACE AN ORDER FOR THE NUMBER

OF SETS THEY WOULD LIKE TO RECEIVE. NUMBERS REQUESTED

WILL BE SENT ON PRO-RATED BASIS. THE COST OF

PREPARATION AND SHIPPING WILL BE COVERED BY THE AMMAN

ARABIC BOOK PROGRAM AND WILL BE AT NO CHARGE TO POSTS.

3. PLEASE ADVISE BY CLOSE OF BUSINESS TUESDAY, JULY 3

IF AND HOW MANY BOOK SETS POST WOULD LIKE TO RECEIVE.

POSTS SHOULD SUBMIT ORDERS BY EMAIL TO BOTH ARABIC BOOK

PROGRAM SPECIALIST AZMI TUBBEH AT atubbeh@usembassy-

amman.org.jo AND A/CAO CYNTHIA EBEID AT

cebeid@pd.state.gov. REGARDS.

GNEHM#
