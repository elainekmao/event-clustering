1. (C) SUMMARY: WHILE PRIME MINISTER BERLUSCONI IS LIKELY

TO SIGN ITALY UP FOR THE JOINT STRIKE FIGHTER (JSF) PROGRAM,

THE PERCEPTION IN NON-DEFENSE MINISTRIES IS OF AN UNEQUAL

AGREEMENT, WITH INSUFFICIENT ECONOMIC/INDUSTRIAL BENEFITS FOR

ITALY. ACCORDING TO MINISTRY OF PRODUCTIVE ACTIVITIES

AEROSPACE DIRECTOR (PROTECT) CUZZONI, NO ATTEMPT HAS BEEN

MADE BY THE MINISTRY OF DEFENSE TO DEMONSTRATE THE ECONOMIC

OR INDUSTRIAL BENEFITS OF JSF FOR ITALY, LEAVING A PERCEPTION

THAT THE CASE CANNOT BE MADE. ALENIA\'S ZAPPA HAS REPORTEDLY

GAINED SUPPORT FROM PRESIDENT CIAMPI AND NATIONAL ARMAMENTS

DIRECTOR ADMIRAL DI PAOLA TO PRESS THE U.S. FOR A BETTER DEAL

(DURING DI PAOLA\'S VISIT TO WASHINGTON FEB. 12-13). CUZZONI

DOUBTS THAT THE PENDING BILATERAL MOU WILL BE DERAILED, BUT

FEARED POLITICAL FALLOUT FOR BERLUSCONI IF THE CENTER-LEFT

PAINTS HIM AS HAVING SIGNED AN UNEQUAL ACCORD AGAINST ITALY\'S

ECONOMIC INTERESTS. END SUMMARY.

2. (C) AT LUNCH FEBRUARY 6 WITH ECON COUNSELOR, ALFREDO

CUZZONI (PROTECT), AEROSPACE DIRECTOR FOR THE MINISTRY OF

PRODUCTIVE ACTIVITIES, SAID THAT PRESIDENT CIAMPI HAD AGREED

TO TAKE ON THE PRIME MINISTER REGARDING JSF ON BEHALF OF

ITALIAN INDUSTRY (AND ALENIA IN PARTICULAR). THIS WOULD BE

PART OF A COMBINED STRATEGY TO PRESS FOR A BETTER DEAL --

CIAMPI AT THE HIGHEST POLITICAL LEVEL, NATIONAL ARMAMENTS

DIRECTOR ADMIRAL DI PAOLA RAISING THE ISSUE WITH EMBASSY AND

IN WASHINGTON MEETINGS ON FEBRUARY 12-13, AND ALENIA CEO

ZAPPA CONTINUING TO PRESS LOCKHEED MARTIN. ZAPPA HAS

REPORTEDLY CONVINCED DI PAOLA AND CIAMPI THAT -- IF THE GOI

REFUSES TO SIGN A BILATERAL JSF MOU UNTIL ALENIA IS SATISFIED

WITH ITS DEAL -- THE USG WILL HAVE NO CHOICE BUT TO LEAN ON

LOCKHEED TO BETTER ITS TERMS.

3. (C) ECON COUNSELOR POINTED OUT A NUMBER OF ADVANTAGES TO

ITALY THOUGH JSF PARTICIPATION, INCLUDING EXPOSURE TO NEW

MANUFACTURING, DESIGN AND MANAGEMENT TECHNOLGY, LONG TERM

ITALIAN JOBS AND REVENUES BASED ON THE GLOBAL PRODUCTION RUN

RATHER THAN JUST THE ITALIAN RUN, BENEFITS TO ITALIAN SMES AS

WELL AS ALENIA AND FIATAVIO. SHE ALSO DISCUSSED THE

IMPORTANCE OF PUTTING ASIDE THE OFFSETS/WORKSHARE MENTALITY,

AND LOOKING TOWARD THE BENEFITS OF MARKET/PERFORMANCE BASED

CONTRACTS. CUZZONI RESPONDED THAT ITALY COULD ONLY COUNT ON

WRITTEN GUARANTEES, THAT STATEMENTS OF POSSIBLE FUTURE

BENEFITS HAD NO VALUE WITHOUT GUARANTEES AND THAT, IN HIS

MANY YEARS IN THIS AREA HE HAD SEEN U.S. PROMISES FADE ON

MANY OTHER DEALS.

4. (C) WHEN ECON COUNSELOR EXPLAINED THAT THE BILATERAL MOU

MUST BE SIGNED BEFORE THE INDUSTRY CONTRACTS ARE SIGNED,

CUZZONI WAS SURPRISED. HE NOTED THAT NEITHER DI PAOLO NOR

DEFENSE NOR ZAPPA HAD TOLD HIM THIS. "IN THAT CASE, WE HAVE

NO LEVERAGE. WE HAVE TO ACCEPT WHAT IS ON THE TABLE," HE

STATED.

5. (C) CUZZONI NOTED THAT HE, HIS MINISTER (MARZANO) AND

MINISTER TREMONTI OF FINANCE ARE STILL SEEKING SOLID

SUPPORTING DATA ON ECONOMIC BENEFITS FROM JSF FOR ITALY

(REFTEL A). THE ITALIAN DEFENSE MINISTRY IS SOLD ON THE

MILITARY BENEFITS, BUT NO ONE HAS CRITICALLY EXAMINED THE

INDUSTRIAL/ECONOMIC BENEFITS FROM THIS ONE BILLION DOLLAR GOI

INVESTMENT. ABSENT ANY SUCH CASE BEING MADE, THE PRESUMPTION

IN THE MINISTRY OF PRODUCTIVE ACTIVITIES IS THAT THE CASE

CANNOT BE MADE. AT THE SAME TIME, ALENIA AND FIAT AVIO

OFFICIALS ARE TELLING THE GOI THAT THE DEAL IS NOT

SUFFICIENTLY ATTRACTIVE. GOI OFFICIALS BELIEVE THAT THE UK

SIDE LETTER OFFERED SUBSTANTIALLY BETTER TERMS FOR UK

INDUSTRY.

6. (C) NONETHELESS, CUZZONI NOW THINKS THAT A POLITICAL

DECISION IN FAVOR OF JSF IS LIKELY. HE BELIEVES THAT CIAMPI

AND ZAPPA WILL TRY TO DERAIL IT, BUT THAT BERLUSCONI WILL

DECIDE FOR JSF IN THE END. HE IS CONCERNED, HOWEVER, THAT

CIAMPI AND ZAPPA MIGHT CREATE A PARLIAMENTARY AND PUBLIC

PERCEPTION THAT THE PRIME MINISTER HAS SIGNED AN UNEQUAL

AGREEMENT WITH LITTLE ECONOMIC BENEFIT FOR ITALY IN TERMS OF

TECHNOLOGY, JOBS OR MONEY.

7. (C) COMMENT: ABSENT ANY INTERAGENCY PROCESS IN THE

INTERNAL ITALIAN DISCUSSIONS, THE MINISTRY OF DEFENSE HAS NOT

EXPLAINED, AND APPARENTLY CANNOT EXPLAIN, THE COST/BENEFIT TO

ITALY OF JSF PARTICIPATION. ZAPPA (AND POSSIBLY ALSO FIAT)

HAS ARGUED TIRELESSLY THAT THE DEAL IS BAD FOR ITALY.

DEFENSE MINISTRY OFFICIALS SIMPLY CONVEY THEIR ENTHUSIASM FOR

THE JSF. THE EMBASSY HAS TALKED TO OTHER MINISTRIES,

BENEFITS, INCLUDING THOSE BEYONE ZAPPA\'S ALENIA. CUZZONI
