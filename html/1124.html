SUBJECT: DUTCH WILL PROVIDE ALBANIA ELECTION SUPPORT

1. The Dutch have decided to grant USD 500,000 to the UNDP

for the OSCE-coordinated election support effort for the

October Albania elections, with the proviso that it be used

for voter registry preparations and data verification.

2. The Dutch plan to open their embassy in Tirana by late

June. Ambassador J.F.L. (Hans) Blankenberg will arrive in

Albania around June 27.
