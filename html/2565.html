SUBJECT: REPORTED UNSYG CONSIDERATIONS RE ZIMBABWE

CLASSIFIED BY JOSEPH SULLIVAN FOR REASONS 1.5 B/D.

1.(C) UN RESREP VICTOR ANGELO CALLED ON THE AMBASSADOR

MARCH 6 TO RELATE CONSIDERATIONS OF UNSYG ANNAN RE

ZIMBABWE AND TO DISCUSS POSSIBLE FUTURE SYG ACTIONS. HE

NOTED THAT SYG HAD ISSUED FEB 21 APPEAL THAT "PEOPLE OF

ZIMBABWE BE ALLOWED TO CAST THEIR BALLOTS FREE FROM

VIOLENCE, INTIMIDATION AND HINDRANCE," THAT "PRESIDENT

MUGABE AND THE OPPOSITION TO WORK TOGETHER TO ENSURE

THAT ELECTIONS ARE CONDUCTED IN A FREE AND FAIR MANNER"

AND THAT "THE GOVERNMENT ... LET THE PEOPLE MAKE THEIR

CHOICE AND TO LIVE BY IT." HE SAID THAT HE HAD SPOKEN

TO THE SYG FOR AN HOUR LAST FRIDAY AS THE SYG

CONTEMPLATED WHAT MORE COULD BE DONE IN ADVANCE OF

ELECTIONS. ANGELO AND THE AMBASSADOR AGREED THAT MORE

APPEALS TO MUGABE IN ADVANCE OF ELECTIONS WOULD NOT BE

WORTHWHILE, SINCE MUGABE HAD TURNED A DEAF EAR TO ALL

APPEALS TO DATE AND WAS FIXED ON THE COURSE HE HAD SET.

WE AGREED THAT THE DAYS IMMEDIATELY FOLLOWING ELECTIONS

WOULD BE THE NEXT CRITICAL MOMENT FOR PHONE CALLS,

MESSAGES AND PUBLIC STATEMENTS.

2. (C) ANGELO SAID THAT THE SYG HAD BEEN IN FREQUENT

CONTACT WITH PRESIDENTS MBEKI AND OBASSANJO REGARDING

ZIMBABWE AND TENDED TO HAVE A TOUGHER ATTITUDE THAN THEY

DID. WE AGREED THAT IT WOULD BE IMPORTANT FOR THE SYG

TO BE IN CLOSE CONTACT WITH THOSE TWO PRESIDENTS AND

WITH THE USG IN THE LEADUP TO AND DAYS IMMEDIATELY

FOLLOWING ELECTIONS. IN THE EVENT OF A LIKELY MDC

VICTORY, WE AGREED THAT IT WOULD BE IMPORTANT TO

PUBLICLY RECOGNIZE SUCH A VICTORY EARLY AND GET CALLS

AND MESSAGES TO PRESIDENT MUGABE AND MILITARY CHIEFS

URGING THAT THEY DO THE RIGHT THING BY WORKING FOR A

PEACEFUL DEMOCRATIC TRANSITION. IN THE EVENT OF A

BLATANT ZANU-PF FRAUD OR SEIZURE OF POWER, WE AGREED

THAT ALL THOSE WITH INFLUENCE NEEDED TO SEND IMMEDIATE

PRIVATE MESSAGES AND TO MAKE PUBLIC STATEMENTS TO INSIST

THAT SUCH A RESULT COULD NOT STAND AND MUST BE ANNULLED.

IN THE EVENT OF AN APPARENT ZANU VICTORY ACHIEVED BY

DINT OF THE VIOLENCE, INTIMIDATION AND ELECTORAL

SHENANIGANS CURRENTLY UNDERWAY, ANGELO JUDGED THAT THE

SOUTH AFRICANS WOULD BE THE KEY DETERMINANTS OF WHETHER

SUCH A RESULT WOULD STAND OR COULD BE ROLLED BACK.

3.(C) WE WILL MAINTAIN CLOSE CONTACT WITH UN RESREP IN

THE DAYS FOLLOWING ELECTIONS AND URGE THAT WE COORDINATE

CLOSELY WITH THE UN SYG IN OUR EFFORTS TO INFLUENCE

EVENTS IN ZIMBABWE AND THE POSITIONS OF PRESIDENTS MBEKI

AND OBASSANJO.

SULLIVAN
