INTRODUCTION AND SUMMARY

------------------------

1. (U) THIS IS ONE IN A SERIES OF REPORTS OF

SPECIFIC AND GROSS HUMAN RIGHTS ABUSES IN BOSNIA AND

HERCEGOVINA (B-H) BASED ON PERSONAL INTERVIEWS WITH

THE SUBJECTS NAMED. THE SUBJECTS IN THIS REPORT

AGREED TO SPEAK ONLY AFTER RECEIVING ASSURANCES THAT

THEIR NAMES WOULD NOT BE USED PUBLICLY FOR FEAR THAT

THAT WOULD ENDANGER THE LIVES OF FAMILY MEMBERS STILL

IN SERBIAN-CONTROLLED AREAS IN B-H.

2. (LOU) ZDENKA RAJKOVIC, IDENTIFIED BELOW AS "X.",

FAROUK BURAZOROVIC, IDENTIFIED BELOW AS "F.B.", AND

ABID VELIC, IDENTIFIED BELOW AS "A.V.", ARE BOSNIANS

WOMAN FROM KOZARAC, BOSNIA, WHERE SHE WORKED AS AN

40-YEAR-OLD MUSLIM MALE FROM PRIJEDOR, WHERE HE

INTERNED IN OMARSKA FROM MAY 30 TO AUGUST 3. VELIC IS

A 39-YEAR-OLD MOSLEM MALE FROM KOZARUSIC. HE WORKED

IN THE OMARSKA MINE AS A STEEL FRAMEWORK BUILDER

BEFORE HIS THREE-MONTH INTERNMENT IN OMARSKA CAMP.

3. (LOU) RAJKOVIC SAID SHE WAS AMONG THE FIRST

DETAINEES TO ARRIVE AT OMARSKA AND AMONG THE LAST

FIVE WOMEN TO LEAVE THE CAMP -- SEVERAL DAYS AFTER

OMARSKA WAS EMPTIED OF ITS LAST MALE PRISONERS.

MOREOVER, RAJKOVIC AND SEVERAL OTHER WOMEN HAD

SPECIAL ACCESS TO THE CAMP COMMANDERS AND

ADMINISTRATORS BECAUSE THEY WERE CONFINED NEAR THE

CAMP OFFICES, NEXT TO THE INTERROGATION ROOMS.

4. (U) ALL THREE SUBJECTS CLAIM TO HAVE WITNESSED

SEVERE BEATINGS, SEXUAL TORTURE, MUTILATION, AND

KILLINGS. IN PART BECAUSE THEY HAD SPENT SUCH LONG

PERIODS IN THE CAMP, THEY WERE ABLE TO IDENTIFY WHAT

STRUCTURE OF OMARSKA CAMP. THESE SUBJECTS WERE

MAJOR PRIJEDOR AREA CAMPS

-------------------------

7. (U) OMARSKA IS ONE OF FOUR VERY LARGE CAMPS IN

THE PRIJEDOR AREA. IT WAS AN ALUMINUM MINE BEFORE

THE WAR. THE OTHER THREE CAMPS ARE KERATERM,

TRNOPOLJE, AND MANJACA. CIVILIANS WERE INTERNED AT

ALL FOUR CAMPS, WHILE MOST ALLEGED POWS WERE SENT TO

MANJACA. MANY PRISONERS DESCRIBED OMARSKA AS THE

WORST OF THE FOUR. MANY PRISONERS WERE MOVED AMONG

THE CAMPS DURING THEIR INTERNMENT.
