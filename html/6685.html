SUBJECT: DENYING PETROFAC\'S ADVOCACY REQUEST

REF: A. STATE 66481

B. AMMAN 1252

1. (u) On March 13, Embassy informed Petrofac Business

Development Manager Brock Tuppeny of the USG\'s determination

that Petrofac\'s bid on the Iraq-Zarqa pipeline project would

not qualify for advocacy support (ref a). We cited USG

concerns over the Iraq portion of the project and possible US

and UN sanctions violations inherent in the project as key

reasons for denying advocacy. We offered to re-examine the

request in the event of changes to the RFP or to the

sanctions regimes on Iraq.
