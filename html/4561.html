SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED TO GOJ ON POSSIBLE TERRORIST

ATTACKS

REF: SECSTATE 244117

1. (SBU) On December 1, PolCouns and PolOff delivered the

message in reftel concerning the possibility of forthcoming

terrorist attacks in Kenya, Yemen and/or Djibouti to Ali

Ayad, personal assistant to Foreign Minister Marwan Muasher.

Ayad noted that Royal Jordanian flies to Yemen and said the

GOJ is concerned about strikes against commercial airliners,

and would pass the information to the appropriate

authorities.

2. (SBU) We also delivered the message to the Civil

Aviation Authority.
