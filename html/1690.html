SUBJECT: VATICAN NON-REACTIONS ON CHINA AND DEMARCHE

ON TAIWAN ARMS TALKS.

REF A: STATE 72589 REF B: VATICAN 00747

REF C: BEIJING 3977

(U) CLASSIFIED BY CHARGE D\'AFFAIRES A.I. JOSEPH

MERANTE. REASONS 1.5 (B) AND (D).

1. (S) POLOFF DELIVERED REFTEL DEMARCHE POINTS ON

APRIL 27 TO MONSIGNOR ROTA-GRAZIOSI, VATICAN CHINA

DESK OFFICER. ROTA-GRAZIOSI WELCOMED USG APPROACH BUT

RESPONDED THAT THE VATICAN MAINTAINED CAREFUL

NEUTRALITY ON SECURITY QUESTIONS RELATING TO THE PRC

AND TAIWAN AND DECLINED TO OFFER COMMENT.

2. (C) POLOFF USED OCCASION TO DISCUSS PRC DETENTION

OF UNDERGROUND CATHOLIC CLERGY, RAISING THE CASE OF

BISHOP SHI ENXIANG. ROTA-GRAZIOSI ADMITTED THE VATICAN

WAS CONVINCED BISHOP SHI HAD BEEN DETAINED BUT HE

DECLINED TO STATE WHETHER THE VATICAN HAD RECEIVED

INDEPENDENT CONFIRMATION. HE SHOWED ONLY SUBDUED

CONCERN, NOTING THAT DETENTIONS OF THIS NATURE WERE

ROUTINE FOR THE EASTER AND CHRISTMAS SEASON AND

CONSTITUTED A FORM OF HABITUAL STATE HARASSMENT. ROTA-

GRAZIOSI APPEARED OBLIVIOUS TO USG AND OTHER

DIPLOMATIC INITIATIVES ON BEHALF OF BISHOP SHI. WHILE

HE WELCOMED USG INTEREST IN BISHOP SHI AND OTHER

DETAINED CLERGY, ROTA-GRAZIOSI NEITHER REQUESTED NOR

PROVIDED ADDITIONAL INFORMATION. HE STRESSED THAT THE

VATICAN WAS NOT SEEKING SPECIAL TREATMENT FROM THE USG

ON BEHALF OF THE UNDERGROUND CLERGY IN CHINA, YET HE

ENJOINED THE USG TO CONTINUE TO PROMOTE RELIGIOUS

FREEDOM FOR ALL RELIGIONS IN THE PRC.

3. (C) ROTA-GRAZIOSI ACKNOWLEDGED VATICAN-PRC

RAPPROCHEMENT WAS AT AN IMPASSE. HE LAMENTED THE

DISASTROUS CHOICE OF DATE -- OCTOBER 1, CHINESE

NATIONAL DAY - FOR THE MASS BEATIFICATION OF CHINESE

MARTYRS IN A VATICAN CEREMONY. HE INSISTED THIS WAS A

BUREAUCRATIC BLUNDER CAUSED BY THE COMPLICATED

CALENDAR OF EVENTS IN THE JUBILEE YEAR, A REMARK

MISSION HAS HEARD REPEATEDLY WITHIN VATICAN CIRCLES.

HE REGRETTED THAT THE PRC CHOSE TO EXPLOIT THE

VATICAN\'S CHOICE OF DATE AS A PRETEXT TO ASSAIL THE

BEATIFICATIONS AND HEIGHTEN TENSIONS. HE MENTIONED

THAT THE PRC HAD NOT MADE ANY PUBLIC STATEMENT WHEN

THE RUSSIAN ORTHODOX CHURCH SIMILARLY BEATIFIED

CHRISTIAN MARTYRS OF THE CHINESE REVOLUTION A MONTH

EARLIER.

4. (C) ROTA-GRAZIOSI BRUSHED ASIDE REPORTS THAT THE

VATICAN AND THE PRC HAD BEEN ON THE VERGE OF A

BREAKTHROUGH IN RELATIONS PRIOR TO THE CONTROVERSIAL

BEATIFICATION CEREMONY ON OCTOBER 1. ROTA-GRAZIOI

ATTRIBUTED THE RUMORS DIRECTLY TO THE PRC, WHICH HE

SAID HOPED THEREBY TO EMBELLISH ITS CANDIDACY FOR THE

2008 OLYMPIC GAMES.

5. (C) COMMENT: ROTA-GRAZIOSI EXHIBITS A DEGREE OF

CAUTION WITH USG INTERLOCUTORS THAT IS UNUSUAL EVEN BY

THE VATICAN\'S USUAL RESERVED STANDARDS. HE AVOIDS

MEETINGS WHEN POSSIBLE. ROTA-GRAZIOSI HAS BEEN
