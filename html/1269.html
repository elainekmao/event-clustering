SUBJECT: MUGABE DISCUSSES FUEL AND PAN-AFRICAN UNITY IN

TRIPOLI

CLASSIFIED BY AMBASSADOR MCDONALD. REASONS: 1.5 (B) AND

(D).

1. (C) IN A SEPTEMBER 5 CONVERSATION WITH THE

AMBASSADOR, FORMER COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY MINISTER -- AND

MUGABE CONFIDANT -- NATHAN SHAMUYARIRA PROVIDED A SHORT

READOUT OF PRESIDENT MUGABE\'S VISIT TO TRIPOLI THE WEEK

OF AUGUST 28. ACCORDING TO SHAMUYARIRA (WHO WAS PART OF

THE ZIMBABWEAN DELEGATION), MUGABE ASKED LIBYAN LEADER

KHADAFY IN A PRIVATE MEETING TO HELP MEET ZIMBABWE\'S

CRITICAL FUEL NEEDS. KHADAFY REPORTEDLY AGREED TO SEND A

TECHNICAL TEAM IN THE NEXT SEVERAL WEEKS TO DISCUSS WITH

NOCZIM, ZIMBABWE\'S NATIONAL OIL COMPANY, POSSIBLE

PROVISION OF ASSISTANCE. ASKED WHETHER KHADAFY HAD

AGREED TO EXTEND A LINE OF CREDIT TO THE GOZ FOR FUEL

PURCHASES, SHAMUYARIRA ANSWERED YES, BUT ADDED THAT NO

SPECIFIC TERMS HAD BEEN DISCUSSED.

2. (C) ACCORDING TO SHAMUYARIRA, MUGABE HAD TRAVELLED TO

TRIPOLI ALONG WITH 12 OTHER HEADS OF STATE (AND OTHER

LOWER-LEVEL OFFICIALS FROM VARIOUS COUNTRIES,

PREDOMINANTLY AFRICAN), TO PARTICIPATE AUGUST 30-

SEPTEMBER 1 IN THE WORLD MATHABA, AN ORGANIZATION OF

DEVELOPING NATIONS FORMED SEVERAL YEARS AGO BY KHADAFY.

DISCUSSIONS, SHAMUYARIRA SAID, FOCUSED ON HOW BEST TO

STRENGTHEN AFRICAN UNITY, INCLUDING FORMATION OF A PAN-

AFRICAN PARLIAMENT TO REPLACE THE OAU. HE SAID THE DROC

CONFLICT WAS NOT DISCUSSED IN ANY FORMAL SESSIONS, AS

NEITHER PRESIDENT KABILA NOR RWANDAN PRESIDENT KAGAME

WERE IN TRIPOLI.

3. (C) COMMENT: WE ARE VERY SKEPTICAL THAT KHADAFY

COMMITTED LIBYA TO EXTEND A LINE OF CREDIT TO THE GOZ FOR

FUEL IMPORTS. THIS IS ONLY THE MOST RECENT OF REPEATED

GOZ REQUESTS FOR HELP ON ENERGY NEEDS AND OTHER MATTERS

FROM LIBYA. AS IN THE PAST, WE SUSPECT THAT LIBYA IS

WILLING TO PROVIDE FUEL TO ZIMBABWE ON COMMERCIAL, NOT

PHILANTHROPIC, TERMS. WE DOUBT, HOWEVER, THAT THE GOZ

WILL BE ABLE TO COME UP WITH SUFFICIENT PAYMENT

GUARANTEES TO SATISFY THE VISITING LIBYAN TEAM.

MCDONALD
