SUBJECT: Colombo Plan: Amendment for USD 500,000 for

Afghan counter-narcotics program signed

Refs: (A) State 67262; (B) Colombo 306

1. Per Ref A, Ambassador Wills and Secretary General of

the Colombo Plan U. Sarat Chandran signed an amendment

to the Colombo Plan funding agreement for the allotment

of USD 500,000 to the Drug Advisory Program on March 31.

The amendment provides funding for counter-narcotics

projects in Afghanistan (detailed in Ref B). The signed

amendment has been forwarded to INL/RM by registered

pouch number 8026419.

2. Minimize considered.
