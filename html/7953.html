SUBJECT: TERRORIST FINANCE: TURKISH REACTION TO BATASUNA

DESIGNATION

REF: STATE 118179

Sensitive but unclassified. Not for internet distribution.

1. (SBU) On May 9, MFA Deputy DG for intelligence and

research Hakan Okcal told us the MFA had circulated

interagency reftel information about additional names

affiliated with ETA (Batasuna, Euskal Herritarrock and Herri

Batasuna). If GOT agencies found assets owned or controlled

by these additional entities, they would blocked such assets

using administrative means, he said. The GOT would not be

able to formally designate these names on its terrorist

finance freeze list, Okcal explained, because the GOT only

designates names that are on the consolidated list of the UN

sanctions committee.

2. (SBU) Okcal said Batusuna was well known in Turkey

because Turks see parallels between ETA/Batasuna and

KADEK/HADEP - both are political wings of terrorist groups,

he claimed. He hastened to add that the GOT doesn\'t expect

the designation of Batasuna to lead to similiar action on

HADEP; rather he said he was characterizing private views of

some Turks on the KADEK/HADEP connection.
