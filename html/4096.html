SUBJECT: ORGANIZING THE MASSES: WOMEN AND YOUTH

REF: A. 01 HANOI 2402 B. HANOI 1199

1. (U) SUMMARY. VIETNAMESE "MASS ORGANIZATIONS" FOR WOMEN

AND YOUNG PEOPLE ARE INCREASINGLY CHANGING THEIR FOCUS AWAY

FROM BEING ONE-WAY COMMUNIST PROPAGANDA TOOLS TO BECOMING

MORE DYNAMIC INSTITUTIONS REPRESENTING THE INTERESTS OF

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 02 HANOI 02598 01 OF 03 281014Z

THOSE POPULATIONS AND ENGAGING IN IMPORTANT DEVELOPMENTAL

WORK, OFTEN WITH OUTSIDE DONOR ASSISTANCE. THEIR CONTINUED

EXISTENCE DEMONSTRATES BOTH THE COMMUNIST PARTY\'S COMPULSION

TO MONITOR AND CONTROL THE PEOPLE OF VIETNAM AND, MORE

INTERESTINGLY, INSTITUTIONAL RESILIENCE IN ADAPTING TO NEW

REALITIES. END SUMMARY.

2. (U) UNDER THE UMBRELLA OF THE VIETNAM FATHERLAND FRONT

(VFF), THE COMMUNIST PARTY OF VIETNAM (CPV) HAS A NETWORK OF

ABOUT 30 "MASS ORGANIZATIONS" THAT RUN FROM THE NATIONAL

LEVEL DOWN TO VIRTUALLY EACH VILLAGE THROUGHOUT THE COUNTRY

(REF A). VETERANS, FARMERS, SENIOR CITIZENS, LABORERS, ETC.

ALL HAVE THEIR OWN ORGANIZATIONS, WHICH SERVE NOT ONLY TO

CONVEY CPV POLICY DOWNWARD BUT ALSO IN PRINCIPLE TO PROTECT

THE RIGHTS AND INTERESTS OF THOSE GROUPS AND GIVE THOSE

POPULATIONS AN OPPORTUNITY TO CHANNEL THEIR CONCERNS

UPWARDS. THESE ORGANIZATIONS ALSO PLAYED AN IMPORTANT ROLE

IN THE SELECTION OF CANDIDATES FOR THE 2002 NATIONAL

ASSEMBLY ELECTION (REF B).

BIG NUMBERS

-----------

3. (U) WITH WOMEN REPRESENTING 52% OF THE TOTAL POPULATION

AND PEOPLE UNDER 25 ALSO MORE THAN HALF OF THE TOTAL

POPULATION, THE VIETNAM WOMEN\'S UNION (VWU) AND VIETNAM

YOUTH FEDERATION (VYF) REPRESENT TWO "POWERHOUSE" MASS

ORGANIZATIONS. ACCORDING TO VICE PRESIDENT NGUYEN PHUONG

MINH, TOTAL VWU MEMBERSHIP IS 11 MILLION WOMEN. (BY

CONTRAST, TOTAL CPV MEMBERSHIP IS UNDER 3 MILLION.) (NOTE:

EXCLUDING GIRLS UNDER 18 --THE AGE AT WHICH WOMEN CAN JOIN -

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 03 HANOI 02598 01 OF 03 281014Z

- THIS WOULD MEAN THAT ALMOST HALF OF ALL ELIGIBLE WOMEN ARE

AT LEAST NOMINAL VWU MEMBERS. END NOTE) MEMBERSHIP FEES

ARE NOMINAL -- ABOUT 500 DONG (3 US CENTS) A MONTH.

SUBSCRIPTION TO THE WEEKLY "VIETNAMESE WOMEN\'S NEWSPAPER" IS

AN ADDITIONAL 80,000 DONG (UNDER US $6) PER YEAR. THERE IS

ALSO A GLOSSY "WOMEN OF VIETNAM IN REVIEW" MAGAZINE IN

ENGLISH. (MS. MINH IS EDITOR-IN-CHIEF OF BOTH

PUBLICATIONS.)

4. (U) WITHIN THE FRAMEWORK OF THE VYFU -- WHICH INCLUDES

THE HO CHI MINH COMMUNIST YOUTH UNION, THE YOUTH PIONEERS,

THE VIETNAM ASSOCIATION OF STUDENTS, AND, MOST RECENTLY, THE

VIETNAMESE COUNCIL OF YOUNG ENTREPRENEURS -- ARE A TOTAL OF

9 MILLION MEMBERS, VYF VICE PRESIDENT TRAN DAC LOI TOLD

POL/C. THEIR AGES RANGE FROM 9 (WHEN THEY MAY JOIN THE

PIONEERS; VIRTUALLY 100 PCT OF STUDENTS DO) TO ABOUT 35,

WHEN THEY MUST STEP DOWN FROM VYF POSITIONS AND MEMBERSHIPS.

THE 4 MILLION MEMBER-STRONG HO CHI MINH COMMUNIST YOUTH

UNION IS USUALLY SEEN AS THE TESTING GROUND FOR WOULD-BE CPV

MEMBERS; 67,926 YOUTH UNION MEMBERS JOINED THE CPV IN 2001,

COMPARED TO ONLY 46,000 IN 1996, ACCORDING TO THE OFFICIAL

MEDIA. MEMBERSHIP IN THE HCM YOUTH UNION IS BY WRITTEN

APPLICATION ONLY, ALONG WITH RECOMMENDATION BY A CURRENT

MEMBER.

-------------------

5. (U) BEFORE THE "DOI MOI" ERA BEGAN IN 1986, THE VWU WAS

PRIMARILY A MOUTHPIECE TO EXPLAIN CPV DECISIONS AND

PROPAGANDA LINES TO WOMEN, MS. MINH ADMITTED. SINCE THEN,

HOWEVER, VWU HAS ALSO MOVED TO UNDERTAKE A VARIETY OF MORE

ACTIVIST PROGRAMS, SHE CLAIMED. FOR EXAMPLE, BEGINNING IN

1997, THE VWU HAS JOINED WITH THE GVN IN ADDRESSING

TRAFFICKING IN PERSONS. UNDER THE GVN\'S ACTION PLAN FOR

TRAFFICKING PREVENTION, THE VWU HAS USED ITS WIDESPREAD

COMMUNITY PRESENCE TO RAISE AWARENESS OF TRAFFICKING IN

PERSONS AND TO HELP IMPLEMENT PROGRAMS TO REDUCE THE

SUSCEPTIBILITY OF VULNERABLE GROUPS TO TRAFFICKING, AS WELL

AS TO COOPERATE WITH GVN AGENCIES IN ASSISTING VICTIMS AND

IN INVESTIGATING AND PROSECUTING TRAFFICKERS. MS. MINH

NOTED VWU RECEIVES SOME FOREIGN NGO FUNDING; ONE PROJECT IN

QUANG NINH PROVINCE PROVIDES HOUSING AND EMPLOYMENT FOR

WOMEN WHO HAVE RETURNED AFTER HAVING BEEN TRAFFICKED AND/OR

TRICKED INTO FAKE MARRIAGES IN NEIGHBORING CHINA. (THE USG,

WORKING THROUGH THE INTERNATIONAL ORGANIZATION FOR MIGRATION

AND THE ASIA FOUNDATION, WILL ALSO BE PROVIDING FUNDING FOR

TRAFFICKING PROJECTS IMPLEMENTED BY PROVINCIAL AND LOCAL

WOMEN\'S UNIONS.)

OFFERS MODEST CAPITAL FUNDS AS WELL AS SAVINGS PROGRAMS TO

ENCOURAGE RURAL ENTREPRENEURSHIP, SHE EXPLAINED. HEALTH IS

7. (U) THE VWU IS ALSO EXPANDING INTERNATIONALLY, WITH

RELATIONS WITH 880 ORGANIZATIONS IN 66 COUNTRIES AROUND THE

WORLD, MS. MINH BOASTED. THE VWU ASSISTS IN RECEIVING

"HUNDREDS" OF WOMEN\'S DELEGATIONS EACH YEAR, SHE ADDED.

WITH MODEST FUNDING (ABOUT USD 8,000 EACH) FROM THE NEW

ZEALAND GOVERNMENT, VWU RUNS INCOME GENERATION PROJECTS IN

HAI DUONG PROVINCE (POTTERY) AND HUNG YEN PROVINCE (MUSHROOM

CULTIVATION). SWEDEN\'S SIDA HAS GIVEN VWU USD 450,000 FOR A

PROJECT ON "ENHANCING GENDER EQUALITY AND ECONOMIC POWER FOR

WOMEN," INCLUDING A FOCUS ON IMPROVING VWU\'S ADMINISTRATIVE

CAPACITY AT THE GRASSROOTS LEVEL.

PROMOTING YOUNG TALENT -- AND GETTING INTO BUSINESS

--------------------------------------------- ------

8. (U) VYF\'S LOI DESCRIBED ITS MAIN OBJECTIVES AS

PROMOTING THE INTERESTS OF THE YOUTH OF VIETNAM, EDUCATING

THEM ON "POSITIVE, PRO-ACTIVE CITIZENSHIP," MOBILIZING YOUTH

IN POLITICAL, SOCIAL, ECONOMIC, AND DEVELOPMENTAL

ACTIVITIES, AND CONTRIBUTING TO THE "DEFENSE OF THE

HOMELAND." LOI NOTED THAT THE VYF -- WHICH, LIKE THE VWU,

OPERATES AT THE NATIONAL, PROVINCIAL, DISTRICT, AND VILLAGE

LEVEL -- ENCOURAGES EDUCATION BOTH IN AND OUT OF SCHOOL, AND

PROVIDES SOME SCHOLARSHIPS FOR ESPECIALLY POOR YOUNG PEOPLE.

THE VYF ATTEMPTS TO RECOGNIZE "YOUNG TALENT" BY OFFERING

AWARDS AND ASSISTANCE IN FINDING JOBS, HE EXPLAINED. AS A

COMPLEMENT TO REGULAR CLASSROOM EDUCATION, VYF ACTIVITIES

INCLUDE LITERACY PROGRAMS, LIFE-LONG LEARNING MODELS, AND

VOCATIONAL SKILLS TRAINING, HE ADDED.

9. (U) A GROWING PRIORITY FOR VYF OVER THE PAST DECADE HAS

BEEN PROVIDING SUPPORT FOR SELF-EMPLOYMENT BY SCHOOL

LEAVERS, INCLUDING SMALL-SCALE CAPITAL LOANS AND KNOWLEDGE

TRANSFERS. (NOTE: THIS IS A CLEAR RECOGNITION THAT THE

PRIVATE SECTOR WILL NEED TO CREATE VIRTUALLY ALL OF THE NEW

JOBS NECESSARY FOR THE GROWING WORKFORCE OVER THE COMING

DECADE AS WELL. END NOTE) THE FOUNDING OF THE COUNCIL OF

YOUNG ENTREPRENEURS -- NOW WITH 1,650 MEMBERS -- UNDER THE

VYF IN 2002 WAS A SIGNAL OF THE GROWING IMPORTANCE OF THIS

SECTOR. IT NOW OPERATES IN 30 CITIES AND PROVINCES; LOI

CLAIMED THAT THE "COLLECTIVE VALUE" OF THE PRODUCTS OF THESE

ENTERPRISES WAS USD 4 BILLION. VICE CHAIRMAN PHUONG HUU

VIET OF THE COUNCIL, WHO IS ALSO A NEW DELEGATE TO THE

NATIONAL ASSEMBLY, SEPARATELY TOLD POL/C THAT THESE

ENTERPRISES RUN THE GAMUT FROM BICYCLE REPAIR BOOTHS TO BEER

HALLS TO INVESTMENT BROKERAGES (LIKE HIS OWN).

10. (U) APART FROM ENCOURAGING THESE ENTREPRENEURS, THE

VYF ALSO RUNS A YOUTH VOLUNTEER PROGRAM, LOI ADDED. MORE

THAN 3 MILLION YOUTHS TOOK PART IN ACTIVITIES UNDER THIS

PROGRAM DURING THE SCHOOL HOLIDAYS IN 2002, HE CLAIMED,

INCLUDING EFFORTS AIMED AT POVERTY ALLEVIATION, ILLITERACY

ERADICATION, ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION, BLOOD DONATIONS, AND

HELP FOR NATURAL DISASTER VICTIMS. HE ALSO NOTED THAT THIS

PROGRAM ALSO HIRES YOUNGSTERS TO TAKE PART IN RE-BUILDING OF

"MONKEY BRIDGES" IN THE MEKONG RIVER DELTA AND IN ENGAGING

IN NATIONAL ROAD CONSTRUCTION PROJECTS, INCLUDING THE NEW

NATIONAL HO CHI MINH HIGHWAY. HE STRESSED THAT THE LATTER

11. (U) VYF IS ALSO ENGAGED IN A VARIETY OF HEALTH

PROJECTS, RANGING FROM COMBATING DRUG ABUSE, EDUCATING ABOUT

HIV/AIDS, FAMILY PLANNING, AND ENCOURAGING PHYSICAL FITNESS,

ACCORDING TO LOI. THE VYF RUNS A NETWORK OF "YOUTH PALACES"

(MANY OF WHICH SEEM TO HAVE BEEN LEASED OUT TO DISCOS AND

KARAOKES), AS WELL AS TWO PUBLISHING HOUSES, "THANH NIEN"

(YOUTH) NEWSPAPER, YOUTH RADIO, YOUTH TV PROGRAMS, AND YOUTH

INSTITUTES (FOR RESEARCH AND TRAINING) IN HANOI AND HO CHI

MINH CITY. THEIR REVENUES HELP SUSTAIN THE VYF, ALTHOUGH

LOI ADMITTED THAT THE BIGGEST CHUNK OF ITS BUDGET COMES FROM

THE STATE.
