1. THE ITALIAN MINISTRY OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS\' AUTHORIZATION

UNIT FOR ARMAMENTS (UAMA) REGULATES EXPORTS OF ARMAMENTS,

INCLUDING AUTOMATIC FIREARMS AND ASSOCIATED MUNITIONS. THE

ITALIAN MINISTRY OF INTERIOR, DIRECTORATE OF ARMS AND

EXPLOSIVES REGULATE THE EXPORT OF OTHER HANDGUNS.

2. UAMA DIRECTOR FERNANDO ZEZZA HAS PROVIDED THE EMBASSY

WITH A COPY OF DECREE 448 OF SEPTEMBER 25, 1999, WHICH

INCORPORATES THE MOST RECENT REGULATIONS FOR EXPORT, IMPORT

AND TRANSIT OF ARMAMENTS, AND OF LAW 185 OF JULY 9, 1990. AS

THESE ARE IN ITALIAN, THE ANSWERS TO REFTEL QUESTIONS ARE

BRIEFLY SUMMARIZED BELOW. A COPY OF THE LAW AND DECREE WILL

BE SENT TO PM/DTC.

A. THE POLICY OF THE GOVERNMENT OF ITALY IS TO RESTRICT

IMPORT, OWNERSHIP, RE-SALE AND RE-EXPORT OF AUTOMATIC

FIREARMS. PERMITS FOR AUTOMATIC WEAPON EXPORT AND RE-EXPORT

MUST BE OBTAINED FROM THE MFA\'S UAMA. ALL OTHER PERMITS MUST

BE OBTAINED FROM THE MINISTRY OF INTERIOR\'S DIRECTORATE OF

ARMS AND EXPLOSIVES. DECREE 448 EXPLICITLY REQUIRES THAT

ARMAMENTS EXPORTS BE APPROVED BY THE MFA, AND THAT NO

RE-EXPORT OF ARMAMENTS ACQUIRED FROM ANOTHER COUNTRY BE

APPROVED WITHOUT ADVANCE APPROVAL FROM THE GOVERNMENT OF THE

COUNTRY OF ORIGIN.

THE LAW SPECIFICALLY FORBIDS EXPORTS TO COUNTRIES IN A STATE

OF ARMED CONFLICT, TO COUNTRIES FOR WHICH THE UNITED NATIONS

HAS DECLARED AN EMBARGO AND TO COUNTRIES WHOSE GOVERNMENTS

ARE IN VIOLATION OF HUMAN RIGHTS CONVENTIONS.

IMPORTS ARE PERMITTED ONLY WHEN CARRIED OUT BY: (A) THE

ITALIAN MILITARY AND POLICE AS AUTHORIZED; (B) FIRMS

AUTHORIZED AND REGISTERED IN THE NATIONAL REGISTRY IN ACCORD

WITH ITALIAN LAW; (C) FIRMS IMPORTING FOR THE PURPOSE OF

TEMPORARY INSPECTION; (D) STATE ENTITIES, WHEN AUTHORIZED BY

THE POLICE; (E) FOREIGN FIRMS, WHEN TEMPORARILY IMPORTING

SAMPLES FOR DEMONSTRATION PURPOSES, AUTHORIZED BY THE

MINISTRY OF INTERIOR WITHOUT OBJECTION FROM THE MINISTRY OF

DEFENSE. ALSO PERMITTED ARE (A) IMPORT BY THE GOVERNMENT OF

ITALY TO EQUIP THE ARMED FORCES AND THE POLICE, (B) STATE TO

STATE TRANSFERS IN ACCORD WITH INTERNATIONAL TREATY AND (C)

TRANSIT OF MATERIAL NEEDED BY NATO ALLIES.

THE MINISTRY OF DEFENSE, OFFICE OF THE SECRETARY GENERAL,

MAINTAINS A REGISTRY OF FIRMS AND CONSORTIA AUTHORIZED TO

DESIGN, PRODUCE, IMPORT, EXPORT, MAINTAIN OR WORK ON

ARMAMENTS, SUBDIVIDED BY CATEGORY. THESE ARE THE ONLY PRIVATE

ENTITIES PERMITTED TO EXPORT, IMPORT OR ENGAGE IN TRANSIT OF

ARMAMENTS.

B. N/A.

C. EXPORT REQUESTS MUST INCLUDE A CERTIFICATION BY THE

DESTINATION OR TRANSIT COUNTRY THAT THE RECIPIENT IS

AUTHORIZED LEGALLY BY THAT GOVERNMENT TO PRODUCE AND SELL

SUCH MATERIAL. THIS CERTIFICATION AND AN AUTHORIZED

TRANSLATION MUST BE NOTARIZED IN THE COMPETENT ITALIAN

EMBASSY OR CONSULATE.

D. THE LAW ON ARMAMENTS DOES NOT ENVISION RESALE OF FIREARMS

WITHIN ITALY.
