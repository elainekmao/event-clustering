SUMMARY

-------

1. (C) BASED ON MEETINGS WITH KEY GOC AND GOBIH OFFICIALS IN

CROATIA IN RECENT WEEKS, WE HAVE BEEN ABLE TO DETERMINE WHAT

TYPES AND LEVELS OF FINANCIAL SUPPORT THE GOC IS PROVIDING TO

ETHNIC CROATS IN BOSNIA -- TARGETED LARGELY TO THREE AREAS:

PENSIONS FOR FORMER HVO SOLDIERS, REFUGEE RETURNS, AND

CULTURAL COOPERATION. MOST OF THE BENEFICIARIES ARE CROATIAN

CITIZENS. THE GOC IS SEEKING TO ENSURE THAT ALL AID IS

COORDINATED WITH THE RELEVANT GOBIH MINISTRIES AND IS

PROVIDED IN A TRANSPARENT WAY. THE GOC MAINTAINS CONTROL

OVER THE PROCESS BY WHICH THE ASSISTANCE IS CHANNELED TO BIH,

THUS ELIMINATING THE POSSIBILITY THAT EXTREMIST BOSNIAN CROAT

LEADERS WILL MISUSE THE FUNDING AS WAS THE CASE -- AND

PROBABLY THE INTENT -- WITH THE TUDJMAN GOVERNMENT.

2. (C) WE ARE REASONABLY CONFIDENT THAT THERE IS A

TRANSPARENT PROCESS AT WORK AND THAT THE GOC, UNLIKE ITS

PREDECESSOR, IS SEEKING TO PLAY A CONSTRUCTIVE ROLE IN BIH.

WHILE THERE MAY BE SOME CONCERNS OVER CONTINUED PENSION

PAYMENTS, THESE PAYMENTS WERE APPROVED AS PART OF THE DAYTON

PROCESS. THERE HAVE BEEN NO COMPLAINTS BY IC OFFICIALS IN

BOSNIA CONCERNING SUCH ASSISTANCE, WHICH IS RELATIVELY MODEST

COMPARED TO FIVE YEARS AGO. OFFICIALS WE MET TO DISCUSS THESE

MATTERS INCLUDE THE HEAD OF THE MFA,S NEIGHBORING RELATIONS

DEPARTMENT, FILIP VUCAK; ASSISTANT MINISTER FOR THE OFFICE OF

DISPLACED PERSONS AND REFUGEES, LOVRE PEJKOVIC; AND THE BIH

PENSIONS/SOCIAL WELFARE PAYMENTS

--------------------------------

3. (C) AT THE TOP OF THE GOC ASSISTANCE PROGRAMS TO CROATS IN

BOSNIA ARE PENSION PAYMENTS TO FORMER HVO SOLDIERS AND THEIR

BENEFICIARIES. THE SCOPE OF THE PROGRAM IS BETWEEN 3 AND 5

MILLION USD MONTHLY, WHICH COVERS A TOTAL OF ABOUT 10,000

BENEFICIARIES. THE DISTRIBUTION OF THE PAYMENTS IS OVERSEEN

BY THE MINISTRY OF LABOR AND SOCIAL WELFARE IN CROATIA IN

CONJUNCTION WITH ITS COUNTERPART IN BIH.

4. (C) THE GOC DOES NOT PROVIDE ANY ASSISTANCE FOR MILITARY

EQUIPMENT/TRAINING OR SALARIES FOR BOSNIAN CROATS SERVING IN

THE FEDERATION ARMY. UNDER THE STANDING COMMITTEE ON

MILITARY MATTERS (SCMM) ARRANGEMENT, AND PURSUANT TO A

BILATERAL AGREEMENT BETWEEN THE GOC AND GOBIH, SUCH DIRECT

SUPPORT WOULD BE CHANNELED THROUGH THE RELEVANT MINISTRIES IN

THE BIH GOVERNMENT IN A CLEAR AND TRANSPARENT WAY. ABOUT

THREE MONTHS AGO, THERE WAS A DEBATE WITHIN CROATIA BASED ON

COMPLAINTS THAT FORMER HVO SOLDIERS WERE BEING PAID PENSIONS

AT LOWER LEVELS THAN SOLDIERS IN CROATIA. THE GOC IS

REVIEWING THE ISSUE AND IS CONSIDERING INCREASING THE

PENSIONS PAID TO FORMERS HVO SOLDIERS TO BRING THEM ON PAR

WITH THEIR HV COUNTERPARTS. THIS WOULD MEAN ADDING TO THE

EXISTING BUDGET FOR THIS ITEM BETWEEN $200,000 AND $600,000 A

YEAR FOR THE INCREASED PAYMENTS.

5. (C) SINCE 2001, THE GOC HAS BUDGETED ABOUT 3 MILLION USD

PER YEAR AS PART OF A PROGRAM DESIGNED TO HELP BOSNIAN CROATS

CURRENTLY LIVING IN CROATIA MOVE BACK TO BOSNIA. THE GOC

PROVIDES HOUSING MATERIALS TO BOSNIAN CROATS WHO, IN RETURN,

SIGN CONTRACTS STIPULATING THEY WILL VACATE THE HOUSES THEY

OCCUPY IN CROATIA ONCE THEIR HOMES IN BIH HAVE BEEN

RECONSTRUCTED. ASSISTANT MINISTER PEJKOVIC DIRECTLY OVERSEES

THIS PROGRAM AND WORKS WITH HIS BIH COUNTERPART TO ENSURE

THAT THE APPLICANTS HAVE NOT ALREADY RECEIVED ASSISTANCE FROM

THE IC OR GOBIH. IT IS A "SELF HELP" PROGRAM WHEREBY OWNERS

ARE ACTUALLY RESPONSIBLE FOR THE CONSTRUCTION THEMSELVES.

THE PROGRAM HAS NOT BEEN WELL MANAGED. COMPLAINTS BY

BENEFICIARIES ARE RAMPANT OVER THE PROCESS IN WHICH THE

MATERIALS ARE DELIVERED (IN THREE STAGES), HIGH EXPENSES

PLACED ON THE OWNERS TO COMPLETE RECONSTRUCTION, AND LACK OF

GOC INVOLVEMENT IN THE PROCESS. WHILE FUNDS FOR MATERIALS

FOR OVER 300 HOUSES HAVE BEEN ALLOCATED, MANY OF THESE HOUSES

HAVE NOT BEEN COMPLETED (SEE REFTELS).

COMMENT.)

HEALTH INSURANCE

----------------

8. (C) BIH AMBASSADOR TO CROATIA DIZDAREVIC TOLD US THAT THE

GOC DOES NOT PROVIDE DIRECT PAYMENTS TO THE GOBIH FOR HEALTH

INSURANCE FOR ETHNIC CROATS IN BOSNIA. RATHER, THE GOC

ALLOWS THEM TO RECEIVE HEALTH CARE IN CROATIA AT COSTS

SIMILAR TO WHAT CROATIAN CITIZENS PAY. THEY ARE NOT TREATED

AS FOREIGNERS, WHO PAY HIGHER COSTS. CROATS IN BOSNIA THAT

USE HEALTHCARE FACILITIES IN BOSNIA RECEIVE COVERAGE UNDER

BOSNIA,S HEALTHCARE SYSTEM. THE GOC IS SEEKING TO

DISCOURAGE CROATS IN BOSNIA FROM TRAVELING TO CROATIA BY

COMMENT

-------

9. (C) WE ARE CONFIDENT THAT GOC-PROVIDED ASSISTANCE TO

CROATS IN BOSNIA IS TRANSPARENT AND DONE IN COOPERATION WITH

GOBIH OFFICIALS. THE TYPES OF ASSISTANCE BEING PROVIDED

OUGHT TO HAVE A STABILIZING EFFECT IN BIH. IT ALSO ALLOWS

THE GOC TO CLAIM CREDIT FOR SEEKING TO MAINTAIN GOOD

RELATIONS WITH ITS NEIGHBOR. AMBASSADOR DIZDAREVIC TOLD US

THAT THE GOBIH DOES NOT HAVE ANY MISGIVINGS ABOUT GOC

INTENTIONS WITHIN BOSNIA AND IS PLEASED WITH THE ROLE THE GOC

IS PLAYING. THE GOC WOULD LIKE TO REDUCE THE AMOUNT OF

SUPPORT IT IS PROVIDING TO CROATS IN BOSNIA, PARTICULARLY

PENSION PAYMENTS AND ACCESS TO SUBSIDIZED HEALTH CARE.

RESOLVING THE ISSUE OF DUAL CITIZENSHIP WILL BE THE ONLY WAY

FOR THE GOC TO REMEDY THIS ISSUE.
