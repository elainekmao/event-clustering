SUBJECT: MAOISTS DISRUPT BUSY INTERSECTION WITH FOUR

IMPROVISED DEVICES

1. At approximately 1508 (local) area residents in the

Gyaneshwor section of Kathmandu reported seeing several

individuals leave improvised explosive devices (IEDs) in one

of the city\'s busiest intersections. Police detailed to the

American Center, located about 400 meters from this junction,

responded to the scene and reported the pipe bombs to their

operations center. The intersection links three of the

city\'s major roads and the timing coincided with the early

start of the rush hour for many Nepalese. Police sources

report the Maoists\' goal appears to have been to disrupt

traffic in the area. Security forces blocked traffic until

an army bomb squad defused the devices. The entire incident

lasted just under 2 hours. No injuries resulted and no

arrests have been made.
