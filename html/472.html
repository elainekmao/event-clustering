SECURITY COUNCIL OF THE UN

NEW YORK

NOVEMBER 11, 1991

GENTLEMEN,

ACCORDING TO LATEST NEWS DUBROVNIK - THE TOWN UNDER

UNESCO PROTECTION AS A CULTURAL MONUMENT OF WORLDWIDE

SIGNIFICANCE - IS BEING SHELLED WITH A VENGEANCE. THE

OLD PART OF THE CITY AND ITS HISTORICAL CORE

CONTAINING MOST VALUABLE MONUMENTS OF THE CROATIAN AND

WORLD CULTURAL HERITAGE HAS NOW COME UNDER HEAVIEST

ATTACKS, WHILE THERE IS ALREADY STREET FIGHTING IN ITS

IMMEDIATE VICINITY.

SIMULTANEOUSLY, THE TOWN OF VUKOVAR THAT HAS BEEN

UNDER SIEGE SINCE SEPTEMBER IS SUBJECT TO FEROCIOUS

ATTACKS. ITS REMAINING 15,000 INHABITANTS LIVE IN

CELLARS UNDER RUINS, WITHOUT WATER, ELECTRICITY AND

THE BASIC NECESSITIES, WHILE THEIR VERY LIVES ARE

UNDER CONSTANT THREAT. AS WAS DECLARED IN THE

STATEMENT BY THE HEADS OF STATES AND GOVERNMENTS AT

THE NATO COUNCIL IN ROME, ON NOVEMBER 8, THE

CONTINUING ATTACKS ON DUBROVNIK AND OTHER CROATIAN

TOWNS ARE OUT OF ALL PROPORTION.

WE WANT TO CALL THE ATTENTION OF THE WORLD PUBLIC TO

THE FACT THAT ASIDE FROM THE YUGOSLAV FEDERAL ARMY

THERE ARE ALSO UNITS MADE UP OF SERBIAN AND

MONTENEGRIN RESERVISTS TAKING PART IN THESE BRUTAL

ATTACKS.

THE ABSENCE OF AN EFFICIENT AND RAPID INTERVENTION OF

THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY WE WERE CALLING FOR SINCE

THE BEGINNING OF THE AGGRESSION AGAINST CROATIA, MADE

POSSIBLE THE COMMITMENT OF NUMEROUS WAR CRIMES AGAINST

UNARMED CIVILIANS, UNPROTECTED URBAN CENTRES AND

CULTURAL MONUMENTS OF NATIONAL AND WORLDWIDE

SIGNIFICANCE BY THE YUGOSLAV ARMY. DESTRUCTION ON

THIS SCALE HAS NO PRECEDENT IN OUR MODERN HISTORY.

YUGOSLAV FEDERAL ARMY AND SERBIA ARE THE AGGRESSORS,

WE ARE HEREWITH URGING YOU TO TAKE IMMEDIATE ACTION IN

ORDER TO SAVE DUBROVNIK, VUKOVAR AND THE OTHER
