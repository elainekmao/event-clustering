SUBJECT: HOLY SEE NOMINATES NEW DEPUTY FOREIGN MINISTER:

BIOGRAPHICAL INFORMATION ON PIETRO PAROLIN

1. (U) POPE JOHN PAUL II HAS APPOINTED MONSIGNOR PIETRO

PAROLIN TO BE THE HOLY SEE\'S DEPUTY FOREIGN MINISTER (UNDER-

SECRETARY FOR THE SECTION FOR RELATIONS WITH STATES OF THE

SIPDIS

SECRETARIAT OF STATE). PAROLIN REPLACES ARCHBISHOP-

SIPDIS

DESIGNATE CELESTINO MIGLIORE WHO HAS BEEN APPOINTED THE HOLY

SEE\'S PERMANENT OBSERVER AT THE U.N. IN NEW YORK.

2. (U) PIETRO PAROLIN WAS BORN IN SCHIAVON, IN VICENZA

PROVINCE, IN THE NORTH OF ITALY IN 1955. AFTER HIS

ORDINATION AS A CATHOLIC PRIEST HE TOOK UP GRADUATE STUDIES

UNCLASSIFIED

IN CANON LAW AND DIPLOMACY AND ENTERED THE HOLY SEE\'S

DIPLOMATIC SERVICE IN 1986. PAROLIN SERVED AT HOLY SEE

NUNCIATURES IN NIGERIA AND MEXICO, AND MOST RECENTLY AS

DIRECTOR FOR SPAIN, ANDORRA, ITALY, SAN MARINO AND CONCORDAT

ISSUES AT THE VATICAN\'S FOREIGN MINISTRY. HE SPEAKS

ITALIAN, ENGLISH, FRENCH, AND SPANISH.

3. (SBU) PAROLIN WAS A 2001 INTERNATIONAL VISITOR TO THE

UNITED STATES FROM THE VATICAN, AND HAS BEEN A GOOD CONTACT

FOR POST. WE WORKED VERY CLOSELY WITH HIM IN HIS CAPACITY

AS COUNTRY DIRECTOR FOR INDONESIA DURING THE EAST TIMOR

CRISIS. HE HAS CONSISTENTLY PROVEN HIMSELF AN OPEN-MINDED

"VATICANISTA" WHO HAS BEEN GROOMED FOR GREATER

RESPONSIBILITY IN THE HOLY SEE. HE WOULD HAVE BEEN AN

OBVIOUS CHOICE FOR A PAPAL NUNCIO -- A COMMON ONWARD

ASSIGNMENT FOR VATICAN MFA COUNTRY DIRECTORS

-- BUT WE SUSPECT HIS SUPERIOR ABILITIES, AS WELL AS PERHAPS

FAMILY REASONS (WE KNOW HIS ELDERLY MOTHER\'S ILLNESS

SLIGHTLY ABBREVIATED HIS IV PROGRAM), HAVE KEPT HIM AT THE

VATICAN WHERE HE HAS NOW LEAP-FROGGED INTO A MORE SENIOR

POSITION. PAROLIN WILL LIKELY CARRY ON THE TRADITION OF

FRIENDLINESS, OPERATIONAL EFFECTIVENESS, AND OPEN-MINDEDNESS

ESTABLISHED BY HIS PREDECESSOR, AND WILL BE AN EFFECTIVE

HIGH-LEVEL INTERLOCUTOR FOR THE U.S.

NICHOLSON
