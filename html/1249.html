SUBJECT: WINDOW DRESSING OR REFORM? FOR NOW AT LEAST,

CLASSIFIED BY AMBASSADOR TOM MCDONALD. REASONS: 1.5 (B)

AND (D).

1. (C) AFTER ONLY FOUR MEETINGS, ZIMBABWE\'S CABINET

APPEARS TO BE SPLIT BETWEEN FACTIONS: ONE LED BY

INFORMATION AND PUBLICITY MINISTER JONATHAN MOYO AND THE

OTHER LED BY FINANCE AND ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT MINISTER

SIMBA MAKONI AND INDUSTRY AND INTERNATIONAL TRADE

MINISTER NKOSANA MOYO. THESE LATTER INDIVIDUALS HAVE

EMERGED AS THE CABINET\'S MORE MODERATE, OBJECTIVE AND

PERHAPS MORE INTELLECTUAL OR AT LEAST PRAGMATIC LEADERS,

WHILE ITS OTHER MEMBERS HAVE NOT DEMONSTRATED THE SAME

ABILITY TO TAKE STOCK OF THE FUTURE OR ARTICULATE

POSITIONS. JONATHAN MOYO LEADS THE ANTI-WEST GROUP WHO

ARE PROPONENTS OF THE ACCELERATED LAND REFORM PROGRAM,

THE DONORS AND CONSEQUENCES BE DAMNED. IN THAT GROUP WE

WOULD PLACE FOREIGN MINISTER STANISLAUS MUDENGE, AND

LOCAL GOVERNMENT, PUBLIC WORKS AND NATIONAL HOUSING

MINISTER IGNATIUS CHOMBO, WHO IS ALSO THE LEADER OF THE

INTER-MINISTERIAL GROUP RESPONSIBLE FOR IMPLEMENTING THE

FAST TRACK LAND RESETTLEMENT EXERCISE. THE OTHER

FACTION IS WHAT ONE MIGHT LOOSELY TERM THE PRAGMATISTS,

WHO WANT TO TURN THE ECONOMY AROUND AND WOULD BE WILLING

TO COMPROMISE WITH THE OPPOSITION MOVEMENT FOR

DEMOCRATIC CHANGE ON CERTAIN ISSUES OF NATIONAL

SIGNIFICANCE. WHILE IN THE MINORITY, WE WOULD PLACE IN

THE GROUP HIGHER EDUCATION AND TECHNOLOGY MINISTER

HERBERT MURERWA.

2. (C) OF GREATER INTEREST TO US IS HOW THE MAKONI

SCHOOL OF THOUGHT WILL FARE. AT THIS EARLY STAGE,

PRESIDENT MUGABE APPEARS TO HAVE ALLOWED THE CABINET

RIFT TO EMERGE, WHILE GIVING NO OVERT SIGN WITH RESPECT

TO WHICH SIDE HE ALIGNS HIMSELF. HE SEEMS HAPPY TO PLAY

THE ONE OFF AGAINST THE OTHER, IN THE TRADITION OF AN

AFRICAN CHIEF. MAKONI, FOR HIS PART, IS ALSO MOVING

QUICKLY. ZIMBABWE\'S MINISTRIES ARE ALREADY FEELING HIS

REDUCED AND PENNY PINCHING DISBURSEMENTS. THE FINANCE

MINISTER IS OVERWHELMING THE CABINET WITH THE NUMBER OF

PROPOSALS HE PUTS TO IT EVERY SESSION. AT THE AUGUST 15

MEETING, MAKONI WAS SCHEDULED TO PRESENT A NUMBER OF

PROPOSALS FOR SIGNIFICANTLY REDUCING EXPENDITURES BY

MINISTERS ON PERKS. OBVIOUSLY, THIS WILL BE MET WITH

SOME RESISTANCE. ADDITIONALLY, HE IS ALSO SAID TO BE

LOOKING FOR AN ENTRY POINT ON THE LAND QUESTION TO WREST

ITS OWNERSHIP AWAY FROM THE HARDLINERS.

3. (C) MUGABE, AT LEAST INITIALLY, SEEMS TO HAVE GIVEN

MAKONI SOME LEASH TO PROPOSE PROGRAMS TO REVERSE

ZIMBABWE\'S ECONOMIC SLIDE. RUMORS CONTINUE TO MULTIPLY

THAT MUGABE WILL ELEVATE HIS FINANCE MINISTER TO THE

POLITBURO. THE PRESIDENT IS PLAYING AN IMPORTANT CARD

BY INCREASING THE AUTHORITY AND CLOUT OF THIS APPEALING

AND CAPABLE CABINET MEMBER. AGAINST WHOM AND FOR WHAT

REASON REMAINS UNCLEAR, BUT IN SO DOING, HE WILL

EVENTUALLY CONSOLIDATE HIS CONTROL OVER HIS FINANCE

MINISTER. AMONG POSSIBLE SCENARIOS, MUGABE MAY BE

CONSIDERING MAKONI AS HIS SUCCESSOR.

4. (C) THE ZIMBABWEAN PRESIDENT REMAINS AN ENIGMATIC

CONFIDENTIAL

IF NOT MERCURIAL FIGURE. HE HAS NOT LOST HIS EDGE AS A

STRATEGIC THINKER AND IS PROMOTING MAKONI FOR A REASON,

PROBABLY AS A TRUMP CARD TO ENSURE THAT ZANU-PF WINS THE

PRESIDENCY IN 2002. MUGABE WOULD NEED THAT OUTCOME FOR

HIS OWN PROTECTION, IF HE CHOOSES NOT TO RUN. AT THE

SAME TIME, THE PRESIDENT REMAINS CONSISTENT: HE

CONTINUES TO HAVE NEAR TOTAL DISREGARD FOR THE ECONOMY.

HIS DISCOURSE FOLLOWS A POLITICAL/MILITARY LOGIC, AND IN

HIS MIND, IT IS CORRECT FOR WHAT HE WANTS: POWER AND THE

ASSETS OF THE WHITE COMMERCIAL FARMING COMMUNITY. THAT

THE DESTRUCTION OF COMMERCIAL AGRICULTURE SOUNDS

MONSTROUS IN LIGHT OF THE POPULAR FRUSTRATION WITH THE

COUNTRY\'S ECONOMIC DOWNFALL IS OF LITTLE CONCERN TO HIM.

HE DOES UNDERSTAND POLITICAL ECONOMY: A ZANU-PF

ELECTORAL VICTORY IN 2002 WOULD BE CLINCHED IF THE

COUNTRY\'S ECONOMY WERE TO START EXHIBITING SIGNS OF A

POSITIVE GROWTH RATE SOMETIME LATER THIS YEAR AND INTO

NEXT. THAT WOULD BE AN ASTOUNDING PERFORMANCE FOR SIMBA

MAKONI, POSSIBLE IF THE PRESIDENT WERE TO STOP TALKING

AND ABANDON HIS CURRENT TACK ON LAND REFORM. HAVE WE

SEEN ANY SIGNS OF THIS? NO. IS IT POSSIBLE? WE DOUBT

IT. WHILE WE WISH MAKONI WELL, AND HAVE HIGH REGARD FOR

HIM, HIS EFFORTS MAY AMOUNT TO TOO LITTLE, TOO LATE, AND

HE MAY NOT LAST LONG. IN FACT, THE FAILURE OF SUCH AN

INDIVIDUAL WOULD, WITHOUT DOUBT, BE POINTED TO BY MUGABE
