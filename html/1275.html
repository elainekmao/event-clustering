SUBJECT: CIVIL SUIT AGAINST PRESIDENT MUGABE

CLASSIFIED BY AMBASSADOR MCDONALD. REASONS: 1.5 (B) AND

(D).

1. (U) THE FILING OF A CIVIL SUIT AGAINST PRESIDENT MUGABE

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 HARARE 05052 111737Z

LAST WEEK IN U.S. DISTRICT COURT RECEIVED PROMINENT

COVERAGE IN ZIMBABWE\'S INDEPENDENT PRESS. THE MEDIA

FOCUSED PRIMARILY ON THE BASIS FOR THE LAWSUIT -- HUMAN

RIGHTS ABUSES AGAINST POLITICAL ENEMIES AND THE COLLAPSE OF

LAW AND ORDER -- AND THE BACKGROUNDS OF THE FOUR

PLAINTIFFS. THEIR SPOKESMAN, ROLAND WHITEHEAD, TOLD THE

INDEPENDENT "DAILY NEWS" THAT "WE HAVE HAD TO RESORT TO

THIS ACTION BECAUSE OF THE BREAKDOWN OF LAW AND ORDER...AND

THE INACTION OF THE POLICE IN BRINGING...CRIMINALS AND

MURDERERS TO BOOK BECAUSE OF THE REAL THREAT OF VIOLENT

INTIMIDATION BY MUGABE\'S CRONIES..."

2. (C) THE LAWSUIT WAS FILED BY FOUR ZIMBABWEANS -- EVELYN

MASAITI, ELLIOT PFEBVE, MARIA STEPHENS, AND ADELLA CHIMINYA

-- WHO TOLD JOURNALISTS HERE THEY HAD FILED THE SUIT

BECAUSE THE POLICE HAD MADE NO ARRESTS IN THE MURDERS OF

THEIR RELATIVES IN THE RUNUP TO THE PARLIAMENTARY ELECTIONS

IN JUNE. MASAITI IS AN MDC MEMBER OF PARLIAMENT WHO WAS

BEATEN AND FORCED FROM HER HOUSE DURING THE CAMPAIGN. HER

CONSTITUENCY IN MANICALAND PROVINCE WAS ONE OF THE MOST

VIOLENT DURING THE PRE-ELECTION PERIOD. ELLIOT PFEBVE, A

MEMBER OF THE MDC\'S EXECUTIVE COUNCIL, RAN UNSUCCESSFULLY

FOR PARLIAMENT IN MASHONALAND CENTRAL. HIS BROTHER

MATTHEW, AN MDC CANDIDATE IN THE SAME PROVINCE, WAS

MURDERED DURING THE CAMPAIGN. MARIA STEPHENS\' HUSBAND

DAVID, A WHITE COMMERCIAL FARMER FROM MACHEKE, ABOUT 75

MILES SOUTHEAST OF HARARE, WAS KILLED NEAR A POLICE

STATION. CHIMINYA\'S HUSBAND, TICHAONA, WAS BURNED TO DEATH

IN A PETROL BOMB ATTACK. TICHAONA WAS A KEY MDC STRATEGIST

AND ORGANIZER.
