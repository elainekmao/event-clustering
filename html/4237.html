START MEMO NOMINATING-JUSTIFICATION:

Asha Dhanasiri is the Senior Legal Officer and head of

the legal division of the Environmental Foundation Ltd.

(EFL), a leading environmental NGO in Sri Lanka. Asha,

as head of the legal division, is the second most

senior officer at EFL and plays a key role in

administration and management of the foundation. The

section heads are involved in day to day

administration, fund raising, policy formulation,

litigation decisions and campaign issues. Asha is

responsible for environmental litigation and public

interest cases of the foundation.

EFL was founded in 1981. It is currently supported by

the Ford Foundation and Helvos. EFL is a member of the

"Friends of the Earth International," a leading

international NGO similar to Green Peace. EFL has 26

full time employees, including six lawyers, four

environmental scientists, and several science and

management graduates. It takes a visible and an active

role in all major environment related issues in Sri

Lanka such as bio-technology, environmental pollution

and protection, forestry, elephant conservation etc.

Most recently, EFL has been advocating against GM foods

and the U.S. tropical Forest Conservation Act as well

as the ADB funded wildlife and nature protection

project. EFL protests were in direct conflict with

U.S. commercial and diplomatic interests. For example,

a GM food ban will have an adverse effect on US food

exports to Sri Lanka and set a bad precedence on trade

policy. Similarly, a US company has recently won a

contract under the ADB funded wildlife project and

smooth implementation of the project is vital for the

US Company. EFL is likely to continue to play a key

role in environment policy formulation and litigation

in Sri Lanka.

Asha is fluent in English and has a strong legal

background. We expect her to play a key role in the

EFL management in the future. Asha has never traveled

to the U.S.
