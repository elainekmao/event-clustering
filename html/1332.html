SUBJECT: PROGRESS TOWARD PROTESTANT RECOGNITION

1. THIS IS AN ACTION MESSAGE FOR DRL/CRT.

2. THE GOVERNMENT COMMITTEE ON RELIGION ON OCTOBER 21

RELEASED A COMMUNIQUE ANNOUNCING THE ESTABLISHMENT OF A

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 02 HANOI 02421 020714Z

"MOBILIZATION BOARD" OF 25 PROTESTANT MINISTERS TO PREPARE

FOR A CONGRESS THAT WOULD OFFICIALLY RECOGNIZE SELECTED

PROTESTANT ("TIN LANH") CHURCHES IN THE SOUTH AND CENTER OF

THE COUNTRY. THIS STEP HAS BEEN DISCUSSED FOR SOME TIME,

AND WE BELIEVE THIS IS A PRECURSOR TO THE GVN\'S RECOGNIZING

THE "TIN LANH" (GOOD NEWS) CHURCHES IN THE SOUTH. (NOTE:

CURRENTLY THE GVN RECOGNIZES "PROTESTANTISM" AS ONE OF ITS

SIX OFFICIALLY APPROVED RELIGIONS, BUT ONLY ABOUT A DOZEN

"TIN LANH" PROTESTANT CONGREGATIONS HAVE OFFICIAL STATUS.

END NOTE.)

3. A LEADER OF ONE OF THE LARGE "TIN LANH" CHURCHES IN HO

CHI MINH CITY TOLD A VISITING UK PARLIAMENTARY DELEGATION

RECENTLY THAT HE EXPECTED THAT RECOGNITION COULD COME

"BEFORE THE END OF THE YEAR." WHILE WE ARE UNCERTAIN AS TO

WHEN THIS WILL TAKE PLACE, THE RELEASE OF THE COMMUNIQUE IS

THE FIRST OFFICIAL CONFIRMATION THIS IS COMING CLOSER TO

REALITY.

4. ACTION REQUEST FOR DRL/CRT: WE WOULD RECOMMEND INCLUDING

A MENTION OF THE COMMUNIQUE IN THE HUMAN RIGHTS REPORT, FOR

EXAMPLE: "IN OCTOBER THE VIETNAMESE GOVERNMENT COMMITTEE ON

RELIGION RELEASED A COMMUNIQUE ANNOUNCING THE ESTABLISHMENT

OF A "MOBILIZATION BOARD" OF 25 PROTESTANT MINISTERS TO

PREPARE FOR A CONGRESS THAT WOULD OFFICIALLY RECOGNIZE

SELECTED "TIN LANH" PROTESTANT CHURCHES IN THE SOUTH AND

CENTER OF THE COUNTRY. ONCE FINALIZED, THIS STEP IS

EXPECTED TO GRANT TO THESE "TIN LANH" CHURCHES THE SAME

LEGAL STANDING CURRENTLY HELD BY THE ROUGHLY DOZEN

RECOGNIZED "TIN LANH" CONGREGATIONS IN THE NORTH."

PETERSON
