CLASSIFIED BY: POLMC T. COUNTRYMAN FOR REASONS 1.5 (B) AND (D)

1. (C) ON NOVEMBER 14, POLMINCOUNS DELIVERED REFTEL POINTS

TO ROCCO ANTONIO CANGELOSI, MFA DIRECTOR GENERAL FOR EUROPEAN

INTEGRATION. ON THE DPRK, CANGELOSI\'S WANTED TO KNOW, "WHAT

DO YOU EXPECT FROM THEM IN ORDER TO REOPEN THE DIALOGUE?"

POLMINCOUNS REPLIED THAT WE HAD LOST CONFIDENCE IN ANYTHING

THE DPRK HAD TO SAY; WHAT WE EXPECTED WAS AN END TO THE

NUCLEAR WEAPONS PROGRAM. CANGELOSI ASKED IF WE WERE SURE

THAT THE PROGRAM HAD RESUMED. POLMINCOUNS ASSURED HIM THAT

THE EVIDENCE WAS CLEAR, AS WAS THEIR ADMISSION.

2. (C) ON TURKEY, POLMINCOUNS STRESSED THAT NOT ONLY WAS

TAYYIP ERDOGAN SAYING "ALL THE RIGHT THINGS," BUT WE BELIEVED

THAT THE ELECTION RESULTS PRESENTED A VERY GOOD OPPORTUNITY

FOR THE EU AND FOR TURKEY, SINCE THE TURKISH PARLIAMENT WAS

NOW BOTH PRO-EU AND PRO-REFORM. CANGELOSI SAID THE GOI

SHARED THIS ANALYSIS AND FURTHERMORE BELIEVED THAT ERDOGAN

WOULD NOT JEOPARDIZE TURKISH SECULARISM. IN RESPONSE TO

POLMINCOUNS\' REMARK THAT THE EU NEEDED TO SET A DATE FOR

TURKISH ACCESSION TALKS, AS PRIME MINISTER AND ACTING FOREIGN

MINISTER SILVIO BERLUSCONI HAD REQUESTED, CANGELOSI SAID THAT

BERLUSCONI WAS HAVING A DIFFICULT TIME FINDING SUPPORT AMONG

HIS EU COUNTERPARTS FOR THIS SUGGESTION. ACCESSION TALKS

WOULD MOST LIKELY BE A CLOSED MEETING, WHICH THE TURKS DID

NOT FAVOR. IF THE BRITISH GOVERNMENT AGREED, HOWEVER, THE EU

COULD DO IT PUBLICLY.

3. (C) WITH REGARD TO CYPRUS, CANGELOSI REMARKED THAT THE

EU VIEWED TURKISH ACCESSION, THE EU-NATO RELATIONSHIP IN

ESDP, AND CYPRUS AS "ALL A PACKAGE." THEY WOULD ALL NEED TO

4. (SBU) FINALLY, POLMINCOUNS ASKED WHETHER AN EU MISSION
