SUBJECT: PLACEMENT OF PRESIDENT\'S EMERGENCY HIV/AIDS

INITIATIVE

REF: STATE 142342

1. Reftel requested posts to use IIP materials to publicize

the president\'s new HIV/AIDS initiative.

2. Colombo used two IIP products to splash the rollout

across the Sri Lankan press.

- News File. During noon and evening newscasts "News File"

clips describing the initiative were used by MTV

(independent English telecaster) and its Sinhala- and

Tamil-medium affiliates, as well as by SWARNAVAHINI

(independent Sinhala telecaster).

- Washington file. The May 27 Washington File piece, "Bush

Signs Initiative to Fight AIDS Abroad," was placed in the

DAILY NEWS (government-owned English daily), the ISLAND

(independent English daily), and THINAKARAN (government-

owned Tamil daily).

3. We await receipt of the generic op-eds described in

reftel, and will use them as appropriate in the Sri Lankan

context.
