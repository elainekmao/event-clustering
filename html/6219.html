SUBJECT: RESPONSE TO SECRETARY POWELL\'S EID

GREETINGS

THE EMBASSY RECEIVED A LETTER FROM HIS HIGHNESS

SHEIKH KHALIFA BIN ZAYED AL NAHYAN, DEPUTY SUPREME

COMMANDER OF THE UNITED ARAB EMIRATES ARMED FORCES

AND CROWN PRINCE OF ABU DHABI TO SECRETARY OF STATE

COLIN POWELL, IN RESPONSE TO EID AL ADHA GREETINGS.

BEGIN TEXT:

"AFTER COMPLIMENTS

I RECEIVED WITH GREAT PLEASURE YOUR NOTE

CONGRATULATING ME ON THE OCCASION OF EID AL ADHA,

WITH ALL THE GOOD FEELINGS THAT THE MESSAGE CARRIED

TOWARDS US AND THE PEOPLE OF THE UAE.

I WISH YOU MANY HAPPY RETURNS OF THE DAY TO YOU AND

THE AMERICAN PEOPLE, WISHING YOU PROSPERITY AND FOR

THE RELATIONS BETWEEN THE TWO FRIENDLY COUNTRIES

PROSPERITY IN ALL THE VARIOUS FIELDS, ENABLING US

TO ACHIEVE OUR MUTUAL INTERESTS." END TEXT.
