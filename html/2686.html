SUBJECT: ITALIAN VIEWS ON CHINA RESOLUTION AT UNCHR

REF: STATE 53847

CLASSIFIED BY: POLMINCOUNSELOR TOM COUNTRYMAN. REASON 1.5 (B) (D).

1. (SBU) POLOFF DELIVERED REFTEL TALKING POINTS MARCH 21 TO

MFA OFFICE OF HUMAN RIGHTS COUNSELOR ALBERTO CECCARELLI.

DRAWING ON POINTS REFTEL, THE AMBASSADOR WROTE MFA SECRETARY

GENERAL GIUSEPPE BALDOCCI MARCH 22, FOLLOWING-UP WITH A

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 ROME 01557 271601Z

PERSONAL CALL ON BALDOCCI MARCH 26. WE TOOK ADVANTAGE OF

EACH OF THESE OCCASIONS TO SAY "THANK YOU" TO ITALY FOR THE

GOI\'S DECISION TO POSTPONE ITS CANDIDACY FOR A SEAT ON THE UN

COMMISSION ON HUMAN RIGHTS (UNCHR). AMBASSADOR BALDOCCI

RESPONDED BY TELLING THE AMBASSADOR HE PERSONALLY CONSIDERED

IT IMPORTANT FOR THE U.S. TO BE A MEMBER OF THE UNCHR.

2. (C) BALDOCCI SAID THERE ARE "NO EASY ANSWERS" REGARDING

THE EU\'S APPROACH TO A CHINA RESOLUTION. IN THE PAST, HE

SAID, THE EU HAS NEITHER PRESENTED NOR CO-SPONSORED A

RESOLUTION. WHAT IF, HE ASKED, THERE WERE NO CHINA

RESOLUTION THIS YEAR? WOULD THAT NECESSARILY BE A BAD THING?

WOULD THE ABSENCE OF A RESOLUTION HELP FURTHER OUR HUMAN

RIGHTS DIALOGUE WITH THE CHINESE? WE COULD HAVE SERIOUS

DISCUSSIONS WITH THEM, HE SAID, THEN ASSESS MATTERS AT YEAR\'S

END. BALDOCCI SAID THESE QUESTIONS WERE HIS OWN THINKING,

NOT THOSE OF THE GOI -- WHICH HAS YET TO TAKE A POSITION.

3. (C) WE WELCOMED BALDOCCI\'S INTEREST IN RETHINKING

"TRADITIONAL" EU POSITIONS AND OFFERED SOME QUESTIONS OF OUR

OWN. WERE THERE NO CHINA RESOLUTION PRESENTED AT THE 58TH

UNCHR, OF WHICH THE U.S. IS NOT A MEMBER, WOULD THE CHINESE

CONCLUDE THE ONE EVENT (NO RESOLUTION) WAS THE CONSEQUENCE OF

THE OTHER (U.S. OFF THE COMMISSION)? WHAT WOULD BE THE

IMPACT INSIDE CHINA? HOW WOULD CHINESE HUMAN RIGHTS GROUPS

INTERPRET THE ABSENCE OF A RESOLUTION? MIGHT THEY SEE THIS

AS THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY "ABANDONING" THEM?

4. (C) COMMENT: THIS IS THE FIRST OCCASION ON WHICH THE

CENTER-RIGHT BERLUSCONI GOVERNMENT HAS AN OPPORTUNITY (OR IS

BEING REQUIRED) TO ADDRESS THIS ISSUE. MISSION WILL SEEK
