AIRTIME ON DUTCH RADIO AND TELEVISION IS SUBJECT TO

LICENSING BY THE INDEPENDENT COMMISSION FOR THE MEDIA.

IT IS GRANTED ON THE BASIS OF STRICT CRITERIA TO

LEGITIMATE ORGANIZATIONS THAT HAVE A SUFFICIENT

FOLLOWING. THESE CRITERIA ARE STRICTLY NON-

DISCRIMINATORY. THERE IS NO GOVERNMENT INTERFERENCE

WITH THE LICENSING SYSTEM. ALL THE MAJOR RELIGIOUS

DENOMITATIONS HAVE AIRTIME. THERE IS NO CENSORSHIP, AS

THE DUTCH FULLY RESPECT THE FREEDOM OF RELIGION AND OF

SPEECH. FENDRICK
