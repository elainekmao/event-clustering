SUBJECT: WHY PM BERLUSCONI WON\'T MOVE FORWARD ON BIOTECH

CONFIDENTIAL

CLASSIFIED BY: AMBASSADOR MEL SEMBLER FOR REASONS 1.5 (B) AND (D).

1. (C) SUMMARY. AFTER MORE THAN A YEAR OF HIGH-LEVEL AND

INTENSIVE EMBASSY WORK WITH THE PRIME MINISTER AND HIS

GOVERNMENT, A COORDINATED, ITALIAN NATIONAL POLICY ON

BIOTECHNOLOGY REMAINS ELUSIVE. MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE

ALEMANNO CONTINUES TO PRESS THE ANTI-GMO CASE BY ARGUING FOR

A "ZERO-TOLERANCE" POLICY ON SEEDS FOR PLANTING, AND BY

ARGUING THAT ITALIAN QUALITY PRODUCTS AND ALIMENTARY

PATRIMONY MAY BE SUBJECT TO "IRREVERSIBLE LOSS" IF

AGRICULTURAL BIOTECHNOLOGY WERE INTRODUCED IN ITALY. WITH NO

DOMESTIC POLITICAL UPSIDE FOR THE PM TO CHANGE POLICY,

RENEWED AND CONCERTED U.S. EFFORTS AT THE CABINET LEVEL WILL

BE NEEDED TO CONVINCE BERLUSCONI THAT BIOTECHNOLOGY POLICY

OUGHT TO BE ON HIS RADAR SCREEN. ABSENT SUCH PRESSURE FROM

WASHINGTON, THE PM IS LIKELY TO CONTINUE TO DRIFT ALONG,

ALLOWING HIS OUTSPOKEN AGRICULTURE MINISTER TO STEAL THE SHOW

AND PUSH ITALIAN POLICY BEYOND THE POINT WHERE WE CAN ENGAGE

CONSTRUCTIVELY. WE ENCOURAGE DEPARTMENT AND WASHINGTON

AGENCIES TO USE THE FIRST SIX MONTHS OF 2003 TO PUSH THE GOI

TO PUT ITS NATIONAL HOUSE IN ORDER, AND TO USE THE GOI EU

PRESIDENCY TO CLOSE FURTHER REMAINING POLICY DIFFERENCES. SEE

EMBASSY RECOMMENDATION PARA 14.

BACKGROUND AND CURRENT SITUATION

2. (C) THE PRINCIPAL GOALS OF THE TRI-MISSION BIOTECHNOLOGY

WORKING GROUP (SEE ACTION AND FINANCIAL PLAN SEPTEL) ARE TO

REMOVE BARRIERS TO INTERNATIONAL TRADE IN BIOTECHNOLOGY

PRODUCTS AND TO PROMOTE THE TECHNOLOGY AS A USEFUL TOOL IN

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 ROME 06137 01 OF 03 091117Z

ACHIEVING SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT IN DEVELOPED AND DEVELOPING

COUNTRIES. IN ITALY, THE FIRST PRIORITY IS TO GET THE PRIME

MINISTER TO PULL HIS GOVERNMENT AROUND HIS POSITIVE PERSONAL

VIEWS OF BIOTECHNOLOGY, AS EXPRESSED TO THE PRESIDENT AND

MOST FULLY AT THE FAO SUMMIT IN JUNE 2002. IN PARTICULAR,

THE PM NEEDS TO REIN IN THE ONLY PROMINENT OUTLIER IN HIS

GOVERNMENT, MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE ALEMANNO. OTHER

MINISTERS--ENVIRONMENT, HEALTH, RESEARCH, PRODUCTIVE

ACTIVITIES-- ARE IN THE PRO CAMP, BUT THEIR VOICES WILL

REMAIN FRAGMENTED AND UNHEEDED UNTIL THE PM GIVES THE RIGHT

SIGNAL.

3. (C) IN THE ABSENCE OF CLEAR DIRECTION FROM THE PM, THE

GOI FOUNDERS AT IMPORTANT MEETINGS LIKE THE OCTOBER AG AND

ENVIRONMENT COUNCIL MEETINGS, OR WORSE, AS HAPPENED IN

DECEMBER, TAKES UNHELPFUL POSITIONS OR HIDES BEHIND EU

CONSENSUS. FOR EXAMPLE, MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT MATTEOLI,

WITH WHOM WE HAVE WORKED HARD TO CREATE OPENINGS FOR

BILATERAL SCIENTIFIC DISCUSSIONS AND PROGRAMS, WAS UNABLE TO

GET MINISTER OF EU AFFAIRS BUTTIGLIONE TO CONVENE AN

INTERMINISTERIAL MEETING ON THE EU TRACEABILITY AND LABELING

AND FOOD AND FEED DIRECTIVES. THE RESULT IS THAT ITALY STAYS

IN THE BACKGROUND, AND CONTINUES ITS INERTIAL ALLIANCE WITH

MORE RESTRICTIVE MEMBER STATES.

4. (SBU) PREVIOUS EMBASSY REPORTING HAS INCLUDED EXTENSIVE

DISCUSSION OF THE "AMATO DECREE", WHICH BANNED FOUR CORN

VARIETIES FOR PLANTING IN ITALY ON PRECAUTIONARY GROUNDS,

EVEN THOUGH THE VARIETIES HAD BEEN APPROVED ALREADY BY THE

EU. ITALIAN EXPERT OPINION ARGUING THAT A REVIEW OF SAFETY

CONCERNS LEFT NO FURTHER BASIS FOR THE BAN HAS BEEN STYMIED

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 04 ROME 06137 01 OF 03 091117Z

AT THE POLITICAL LEVEL IN THE PM\'S OFFICE FOR MONTHS. THE

ARBITRARINESS OF THE DECREE, IF LEFT UNCHALLENGED, IS LIKELY

TO ENCOURAGE SIMILAR MEASURES AS AG MINISTER ALEMANNO SHIFTS

HIS ATTENTION FROM FOOD AND FEED TO SEEDS IN COMING MONTHS.

WE UNDERSTAND THAT INDUSTRY FILED MONTHS AGO AN APPEAL TO THE

"AMATO DECREE" WITH THE LAZIO REGION ADMINISTRATIVE COURT

(TAR), AND THAT THE TAR HAS SOUGHT AN OPINION FROM THE

EUROPEAN COURT OF JUSTICE, WHICH IS EXPECTED IN FEBRUARY 2003.

5. (SBU) THE MOST IMPORTANT OF POTENTIAL NEW (OR MORE

PROPERLY SPEAKING "RENEWED") RESTRICTIVE MEASURES RELATES TO

THE IMPORT OF SEEDS FOR PLANTING. LATE IN DECEMBER 2001, THE

MINISTRY OF AGRICULTURE ANNOUNCED A GOAL OF A "ZERO

TOLERANCE" FOR GM ADVENTITIOUS PRESENCE IN SEEDS. WHEN THIS

PROVED IMPOSSIBLE, AN INTERIM PROCEDURE TO ALLOW AN AP OF

0.1-0.5 % IN SEEDS WAS FOUND, BUT THE GOAL REMAINS ZERO

TOLERANCE. IN EARLY NOVEMBER THE MINISTRY OF AGRICULTURE

GROUP ON GMOS PROPOSED A 0.01 % AP LIMIT FOR DESIGNATION OF

NON-GMO SEEDS, THE CURRENT TESTING LIMIT WITH AVAILABLE

ANALYTICAL TOOLS. ENVISIONED, HOWEVER, IS A DESIGNATION OF

GMO FREE SEEDS, WHICH WOULD BE GUARANTEED BY THE PRODUCER,

AND WOULD HAVE TO PASS TESTS OF GREATER SENSITIVITY. SUCH

SEEDS WOULD BE PRODUCED IN "UNCONTAMINATED ENCLAVES" IN ITALY

AND ELSEWHERE. A DECEMBER 4 CIRCULAR FROM THE MINISTRY TO

SEED SUPPLIERS ASKS COMPANIES TO IDENTIFY QUANTITIES OF SOY

AND CORN SEED WHICH CAN MEET THE GMO-FREE "ZERO TOLERANCE"

WHY DOESN\'T BERLUSCONI TAKE CONTROL?

7. (C) IF OUR INFORMATION IS CORRECT, AND THE MAJORITY OF THE

BERLUSCONI CABINET AND THE PM HIMSELF FAVOR A CHANGE IN

ITALIAN POLICY, WHY HASN\'T IT HAPPENED? FOUR INTERLOCKING

FACTORS ARE HAMPERING CHANGE: (1) COALITION POLITICS; (2) THE

BROADER BERLUSCONI DOMESTIC AND INTERNATIONAL AGENDA; (3) THE

PERCEIVED LOWER PRIORITY OF BIOTECHNOLOGY IN THE OVERALL

U.S-ITALY AGENDA AS COMPARED TO A YEAR AGO; AND (4) THE LACK

OF A PERCEIVED POLITICAL "UPSIDE" FOR BERLUSCONI IN

SUPPORTING A POLICY CHANGE IN ITALY AND THE EU.

8. (C) FOR THE BERLUSCONI GOVERNMENT, AGRICULTURE MINISTER

ALEMANNO IS AN IRRITANT ON BIOTECHNOLOGY, BUT HE BRINGS TO

HIS PARTY (AN) AND THE COALITION A SUBSTANTIAL CONSTITUENCY:

ROUGHLY 40 PERCENT OF THE NATIONAL ALLIANCE PARTY. LARGELY

COMPRISED OF WORKING-CLASS POPULISTS, ALEMANNO\'S CONSTITUENCY

IS ALREADY RILED UP OVER LAYOFFS AND PLANT CLOSURES BY FIAT.

BERLUSCONI CAN ILL-AFFORD TO FURTHER ANTAGONIZE THEM; ABSENT

SOME COMPELLING INCENTIVE (OR PENALTY FOR CONTINUED

INACTION), HE WON\'T. ALEMANNO\'S MESSAGE--THAT BIOTECHNOLOGY

IS A THREAT TO QUALITY ITALIAN AGRICULTURE--APPEALS

ESPECIALLY TO MANY UNSUSPECTING CONSUMERS AND SMALL RURAL

FARMERS WHO DEPEND ON THE MINISTRY FOR SUPPORT AT HOME AND AT

THE EU LEVEL. ALEMANNO HAS ALSO TAPPED INTO THE GREAT

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 ROME 06137 02 OF 03 270952Z

CULTURAL AORTA OF THE ITALIAN HEART WITH HIS BASELESS CLAIMS

THAT AGRICULTURAL BIOTECHNOLOGY PRODUCTS ARE "LOW QUALITY",

"TASTE BAD", AND MIGHT IF INTRODUCED ALONGSIDE PURE ITALIAN

PRODUCTS RESULT IN "IRREVERSIBLE GENETIC CONTAMINATION." AS

LONG AS ALEMANNO\'S PREACHING TO THE FAITHFUL KEEPS THEM LOYAL

TO THE COALITION, BERLUSCONI IS LIKELY TO OVERLOOK THE

BROADER POLICY CONSEQUENCES OF HIS MINISTER\'S TACTICS.

9. (C) THE BERLUSCONI GOVERNMENT NEEDS STABILITY IN THE

COALITION TO ACHIEVE ITS AMBITIOUS AND DIFFICULT DOMESTIC

POLITICAL OBJECTIVES. RESOURCES FOR NATIONAL SECURITY AND

DEFENSE MODERNIZATION ARE COMPETING WITH PUBLIC EDUCATION

AND RESEARCH, PENSION COSTS, AND HEALTH CARE COSTS IN A

BUDGET ENVIRONMENT THAT AFFORDS LITTLE ROOM FOR MANEUVER.

PROMISES TO REDUCE TAXES, MANAGE IMMIGRATION, DEVOLVE

AUTHORITIES TO REGIONAL GOVERNMENTS, AND INCREASE PUBLIC

INFRASTRUCTURES ARE OTHER COMPETING CHALLENGES. ACTION ON ANY

OF THESE ISSUES IS SURE TO RAISE THE HACKLES OF ONE OR MORE

PARTICULAR CONSTITUENCIES; BIOTECHNOLOGY, IN CONTRAST, HAS NO
