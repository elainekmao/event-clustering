SUBJECT: DOPCAT DEMARCHE DELIVERED: JORDAN WILL ALLY ITSELF

WITH U.S. POSITION

REF: STATE 206414

1. (SBU) On October 24, PolCouns and Poloff delivered reftel

demarche on the Draft Optional Protocol to the Convention

Against Torture (DOPCAT) to Samer Naber, the MFA\'s Legal

Advisor. Naber welcomed the points saying that Jordan had

its own reservations about the DOPCAT and how it had been

handled in the working group. The MFA, Naber noted,

following his own recommendation, had already sent

instructions to its Mission in New York to coordinate with

USUN and "ally itself with the U.S. position."
