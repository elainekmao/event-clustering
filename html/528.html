MINISTER SKRABALO

BY TAKING ADVANTAGE OF THE FACT THAT THE ATTENTION

OF THE WORLD IS CONCENTRATED ON SARAJEVO AND THE

ATTEMPTS TO OPEN THE HUMANITARIAN CORRIDOR AND

TRYING TO FORESTALL DECISIVE ACTION BY

INTERNATIONAL FORCES, WHICH WILL HAVE TO BE TAKEN

SOONER OR LATER ANYWAY, THE SERBIAN-MONTENEGRIN

UNCLASSIFIED

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 03 ZAGREB 01169 02 OF 03 141311Z

AGGRESSORS ARE INTENT ON ACHIEVING TWO BASIC

OBJECTIVES AS SOON AS POSSIBLE AND THROUGH A

LARGE-SCALE MILITARY OPERATION:

1) ROUNDING UP THE BORDERS OF THE SO-CALLED

GREATER SERBIAN THROUGH DESTRUCTION OF ALL MOSLEM

AND CROATIAN ENTITIES IN CONTACT ZONES;

2) ACCOMPLISHING COMPLETE ETHNIC CLEANSING OF

TERRITORIES WITHIN THE SO-CALLED SERBIAN KRAINAS.

IN ORDER TO ACHIEVE THE SECOND OBJECTIVE, THEY ARE

COMMITTING CRIMES UNHEARD-OF SINCE THE HOLOCAUST

AND THE END OF THE WORLD WAR II. ON A MASSIVE

SCALE MOSLEMS AND CROATS ARE BEING EXECUTED AND

HERDED INTO PRISON CAMPS, ENTIRE PLACES RAZED TO

THE GROUND, EVEN GRAVEYARD DESTROYED (E.G. THE

AREA OF PRIJEDOR-KOZARAC). THE PURPOSE OF ALL

THIS IS OBVIOUS: TO DRIVE OUT THE NON-SERBIAN

POPULATION FROM THESE AREAS FOR GOOD.

THE SITUATION IS PARTICULARLY DRAMATIC IN THE

AREAS OF NORTHERN BOSIA (POSAVINA) WHERE THE

SERBIAN ATROCITIES HAVE FORCED THE MAJORITY MOSLEM

POPULATION THERE INTO A PANIC FLIGHT TO CROATIA.

ONLY TODAY MORE THAN 20,000 PEOPLE HAVE FLED

ACROSS THE RIVER SAVA TO CROATIA TO SEEK SHELTER

IN THE AREA BETWEEN SLAVONSKI BROD AND ZUPANJA.

AS THE SAVA BRIDGES ARE DEMOLISHED, PEOPLE ARE

USING ALL KINDS OF MEANS TO RUN TO SAFETY AND MANY

THE AFORESAID IS AN ELEVENTH-HOUR WARNING THAT

UNLESS AN INTERNATIONAL MILITARY INTERVENTION IS

UNDERTAKEN SHORTLY, THE CONSEQUENCES IN TERMS OF

THE EXTENT OF HUMAN TRAGEDY, MOSTLY AFFECTING

CHILDREN, WOMEN AND OLD PEOPLE, WILL BE

UNCLASSIFIED

UNCLASSIFIED

IMMEASURABLE.

CROATIA IS DOING ITS UTMOST TO GIVE EVERY POSSIBLE

HELP TO THIS VAST NUMBER OF EXPELLED PEOPLE, BUT

UTTERLY EXHAUSTED AS IT IS AS A RESULT OF THE WAR

AND THE HUNDREDS OF THOUSANDS OF REFUGEES IN NEED

OF ACCOMMODATION, CLOTHES AND FOOD, IT HAS FOUND

ITSELF IN A POSITION WHERE IT WILL BE FORCED TO

REDIRECT THE FLOW OF REFUGEES TOWARDS WESTERN

EUROPE.

THEREFORE, I APPEAL TO YOU TO DO ALL YOU CAN TO

PREVENT THE TRAGEDY WHICH IS LOOMING LARGER AND

LARGER EVERY MINUTE. WE BELIEVE THAT THE ONLY

THING LEFT IS TO CALL AN EMERGENCY MEETING OF THE

SECURITY COUNCIL AND TO APPROVE AN INTERNATIONAL

MILITARY ACTION.

PROF. ZDENKO SKRABALO, M.D. PH.D.
