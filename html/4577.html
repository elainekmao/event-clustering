SUBJECT: PROGRESS ON UNHCR-WFP MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING

SIGNED ON JULY 9 - SELECTION OF THREE PILOT COUNTRIES FOR

DIRECT WFP DELIVERY OF FOOD TO THE END USER

REF: (A) ROME 3566

-------

SUMMARY

-------

1. UNHCR AND WFP SIGNED A REVISED MEMORANDUM OF

UNDERSTANDING (MOU) ON JULY 9 WHERE IT WAS AGREED THAT, ON A

PILOT ONE-YEAR TRIAL BASIS, WFP WILL ASSUME FULL

RESPONSIBILITY FOR THE FINAL DISTRIBUTION OF THE BASIC FOOD

RATION IN FIVE REFUGEE PROGRAMS. USMISSION HAS NOW BEEN

INFORMED BY WFP THAT BOTH UN AGENCIES HAVE AGREED ON

PAKISTAN, UGANDA AND SIERRA LEONE. TANZANIA, GUINEA AND

BANGLADESH - REMAIN UNDER DISCUSSION FOR THE REMAINING TWO

PILOT TESTS. END SUMMARY.

----------

BACKGROUND

----------

2. RUUD LUBBERS, UN HIGH COMMISSIONER FOR REFUGEES (UNHCR)

AND JIM MORRIS, EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR OF WORLD FOOD PROGRAM

(WFP) SIGNED ON JULY 9 A REVISED MOU AIMED AT STRENGTHERNING

COPPERATION AT THE FIELD LEVEL IN DELIVERING FOOD AID TO

REFUGEES, RETURNEES AND INTERNALLY DISPLACED PERSONS (IDPS)

WORLDWIDE. CLAUSE 5.8 OF THE REVISION STATES: "ON A PILOT

BASIS AND FOR AN INITIAL DURATION OF 12 MONTHS (PER

COUNTRY), WFP WILL ASSUME, AT ITS OWN COST, RESPONSIBILITY

FOR THE FINAL DISTRIBUTION OF THE BASIC FOOD RATION IN FIVE

REFUGEE PROGRAMMES. THE PILOT COUNTRY PROGRAMMES WILL BE

SELECTED JOINTLY BY UNHCR AND WFP BASED UPON JOINTLY AGREED

CRITERIA AND IN CONSULTATION WITH THE CONCERNED WFP/UNHCR

COUNTRY TEAMS."

3. WFP HEADQUARTERS HAS ADVISED USMISSION THAT BOTH UN

AGENCIES HAVE NOW AGREED ON TESTING WFP FINAL DISTRIBUTION

OF BASIC FOOD COMMODITIES IN REFUGEE OPERATIONS IN PAKISTAN,

UGANDA AND SIERRA LEONE. TANZANIA, GUINEA AND BANGLADESH -

REMAIN UNDER ACTIVE DISCUSSION FOR THE REMAINING TWO PILOT

TESTS.

4. IN THE AGREED UPON COUNTRY OPERATIONS, WFP WOULD ASSUME

THE LEAD IN NEGOTIATING THE TRIPARTITE AGREEMENT AND

DIRECTLY CONTRACT WITH IMPLEMENTING PARTNER NON-GOVERNMENTAL

ORGANIZATIONS (NGOS) FOR FINAL FOOD ASSISTANCE DISTRIBUTION

TO BENEFICIARIES. NOTE: THIS IS STANDARD PRACTICE FOR WFP IN

NON-REFUGEE PROGRAMS, AND WFP BELIEVES THAT THIS PILOT

APPROACH COULD RESULT IN AN OVERALL COST-SAVINGS TO DONORS.

WFP IS ALSO OF THE VIEW THAT ACCOUNTABILITY FOR FOOD

DISTRIBUTION WOULD ALSO IMPROVE SINCE ONLY ONE AGENCY (I.E.,

WFP) WOULD RETAIN OVERSIGHT RESPONSIBILITY FROM START TO

FINISH. END NOTE.

5. FOR THE FIVE PILOT COUNTRIES IN WHICH WFP WILL TAKE

RESPONSIBILITY FOR FOOD DISTRIBUTION, WFP AND UNHCR WILL

ENACT TRANSITIONAL PROVISIONS SO AS TO ENSURE A SMOOTH HAND-

OVER OF RELATED RESPONSIBILITIES. AT THE END OF A YEAR\'S

TRIAL PER COUNTRY, THE PILOT ACTIVITIES WILL BE JOINTLY

EVALUATED. THE FINDINGS OF THAT EVALUATION AND ITS

IMPLICATIONS WILL THEN BE THE SUBJECT OF FURTHER DISCUSSIONS

BETWEEN UNHCR AND WFP.

-------

-------
