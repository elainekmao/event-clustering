(U) Classified by Lewis Amselem, DCM. Reasons 1.5

(b, d).

1. (U) This is an Action Request. Please see

paragraph four.

2. (C) A Joint/Combined Exercise Training (JCET) has

been proposed for Sri Lanka for the period of January 13

to March 3, 2003, involving the First, Second and Third

Special Forces Regiments, the First and Second Commando

Regiments and the Psyops Directorate of the 53rd

Division of the Sri Lankan Army. The training will also

include the Special Boat Squadron and the Fast Attack

Flotilla of the Sri Lankan Navy.

3. (C) In accordance with Reftel A, Mission has

reviewed available human rights reports and files and

has found no credible information linking any member of

these units with allegations of human rights violations.

4. (U) Action Request: Does the Department possess

relevant information that would affect this finding? If

so, please advise. If not, Mission requests

Department\'s authorization to provide CINCPAC/SOCPAC

with certification per para 8 reftel A. End Action

Request.
