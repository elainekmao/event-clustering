FOR REASONS 1.5 (B) AND (D)

1. (C) SUMMARY: MFA REPORTS THAT THE ITALIAN WATER EXPERT

CURRENTLY WORKING THE ISRAELI-LEBANESE WATER DISPUTE MAY SEEK

TO HELP BROKER A LONGER-TERM, BROADER SHARING AGREEMENT, BUT

AS AN EU INITIATIVE, NOT AN ITALIAN EFFORT. ROME RECEIVED

THE PITCH FROM LEBANESE FINANCE MINISTER SINIORA ON THE EU

GUARANTEEING BONDS FOR LEBANON. SYRIAN FM AL-SHAR\'A LEFT

OPEN THE POSSIBILITY OF SYRIA FIGHTING IRAQ IF SADDAM STRIKES

ANOTHER ARAB STATE. ITALY IS PREPARED TO PROPOSE A CANDIDATE

FOR EU SPECIAL ENVOY IF MORATINOS STEPS DOWN. THE GOI TURNED

DOWN A SAUDI DEMARCHE ASKING FOR A STATEMENT CONDEMNING

RECENT US LEGISLATION ON JERUSALEM. END SUMMARY

-------------------------------------

ON THE ISRAEL/PALESTINE WATER DISPUTE

-------------------------------------

2. (C) LUCA FERRARI, EASTERN MED OFFICE DIRECTOR, TOLD POLMC

AND NEA WATCHER THAT MAZZITTI, THE EU WATER EXPERT, AN

ITALIAN, WAS SURPRISED TO LEARN IN RECENT MEETINGS IN THE

REGION THAT THE ISRAELIS WERE NOT AUTOMATICALLY NEGATIVE ON

THE IDEA OF AN EU ROLE IN HELPING TO MONITOR AND POSSIBLY

NEGOTIATE, IN THE LONGER TERM, AN ACCORD ON WATER SHARING.

THE EXPERT\'S EFFORTS ARE AND WILL REMAIN AN EU AFFAIR. THE

GOI HAS NO INTENTIONS TO BECOME INVOLVED BILATERALLY, NOR TO

PAY FOR PUMPS OR METERS, ADDED FERRARI.

3. (C) ROME HOSTED OCTOBER 7 THE LEBANESE FINANCE MINISTER

FUAD SINIORA, WHO PRESENTED PM HARIRI\'S PLAN FOR DEBT

RESTRUCTURING, INCLUDING THE CONCEPT OF THE EUROPEAN UNION,

OR SOME OF ITS MEMBER STATES, GUARANTEEING BONDS FOR LEBANON.

WHILE THE CONCEPT WAS CREATIVE, THE MFA HAD GIVEN NO

ENCOURAGEMENT. FERRARI COULD NOT PICTURE THAT THE ITALIAN

MINISTRY OF FINANCE WOULD ACCEPT THE IDEA.

--------------------------------

SYRIA ON A POSSIBLE WAR WITH IRAQ

---------------------------------

4. (C) FERRARI GAVE A READ-OUT OF THE SEPT. 26 VISIT OF MFA

UNDERSECRETARY MANTICA TO DAMASCUS, ONE DAY AFTER PM

BERLUSCONI HAD STRONGLY SUPPORTED U.S. POLICY DURING A

PARLIAMENTARY DEBATE ON IRAQ. SYRIAN FM FAROUK AL-SHAR\'A HAD

HARANGUED THE ITALIANS FOR TWENTY MINUTES, CRITICIZING

BERLUSCONI\'S SPEECH IN DETAIL. SYRIA COULD NOT JUSTIFY TO

ITS PEOPLE A PRO-AMERICAN POSITION AGAINST IRAQ. AL-SHAR\'A

THEN TOOK A BREATH, RELAXED AND EXPLAINED THAT THE MAIN

DIFFERENCE BETWEEN THE PERSIAN GULF WAR AND A POSSIBLE ATTACK

ON IRAQ TODAY IS THAT IRAQ THIS TIME DID NOT ATTACK AN ARAB

COUNTRY. SYRIA IS NOT ANTI-AMERICAN, STRESSED AL-SHAR\'A, AND

WOULD AGAIN FIGHT ALONGSIDE THE U.S. AGAINST IRAQ IF IRAQ

WERE TO ATTACK A "FELLOW ARAB COUNTRY." THE ITALIANS ASKED

IF AL-SHAR\'A MEANT THAT DAMASCUS WOULD FIGHT AGAINST IRAQ IF

SADDAM RESPONDED TO A U.S. MILITARY ATTACK BY ATTACKING SAUDI

ARABIA OR ANOTHER ARAB NEIGHBOR, TO WHICH AL-SHAR\'A REPLIED

AFFIRMATIVELY. FERRARI COMMENTED THAT THE SYRIANS ARE STILL

QUITE NERVOUS ABOUT THE PROSPECT OF BEING THE NEXT U.S.

TARGET AFTER IRAQ.

ITALIAN CANDIDATE FOR EU MIDDLE EAST ENVOY

------------------------------------------

5. (C) FERRARI RECOUNTED SOME OF THE GOSSIP AND MANEUVERING

IN BRUSSELS AND MADRID OVER MORATINOS\' EXTENSION AS THE EU

REP FOR THE PEACE PROCESS. WHILE FERRARI

(UNCHARACTERISTICALLY) HAD SOME GOOD WORDS ABOUT MORATINOS\'

RECENT EFFORTS WITHIN THE QUARTET FRAMEWORK, HE STATED

CLEARLY THAT THE ITALIAN MFA WOULD NOT BE SAD TO SEE HIM

RETIRE. THE GOI WAS QUIETLY PREPARING TO PROMOTE THE

CANDIDACY OF ITS FORMER AMBASSADOR TO TEL AVIV, GIANPAOLO

CARAVAI, TO REPLACE MORATINOS. WHILE EMPHASIZING THAT THIS

WOULD ONLY GO FORWARD IF MORATINOS CHOSE NOT TO CONTINUE,

FERRARI SAID THAT CARAVAI WOULD BE LOOKING TO GET TO KNOW USG

PEACE PROCESS SPECIALISTS BETTER. (NOTE: WE UNDERSTAND THAT

THE ITALIAN EMBASSY IN WASHINGTON HAS ALREADY APPROACHED NEA

INFORMALLY).

6. (C) FERRARI SAID THAT THE SAUDI AMBASSADOR HAD

"INFORMALLY, BUT FIRMLY" DEMARCHED HIS BOSS, DIRECTOR-GENERAL

FOR MID-EAST BADINI, TO URGE THAT THE GOI ISSUE A STATEMENT
