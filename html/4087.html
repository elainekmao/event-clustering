SUBJECT: BOSNIA TRAIN AND EQUIP PROGRAM: POTUS MESSAGE TO

KING ABDULLAH DELIVERED

REF: WHITE HOUSE 270152Z OCT 02

1. (U) On October 31, Embassy conveyed reftel message from

the President to King Abdullah. On November 4, Chief of the

Royal Court Fayez Tarawneh called the Ambassador to say that

the King had received the letter and wished to convey that

Jordan was pleased to be a participant in Bosnia and, if

there were further needs, the U.S. should raise them with the

GOJ.
