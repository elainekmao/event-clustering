SUBJECT: DEMARCHE ON CANDIDACY OF PROFESSOR RUTH WEDGWOOD

DELIVERED TO JORDAN

REF: STATE 150004

Classified By: Political Counselor Douglas Silliman. Reasons 1.5 (b,d)

.

1. (U) On August 7, Poloff delivered reftel information on

the candidacy of Professor Ruth Wedgwood to the Jordanian

MFA. On August 12, Ayman Aamiri, Deputy Head of the MFA\'s

International Organizations Department, and Samer Naber,

MFA\'s Legal Advisor, told us that MFA has since conveyed all

the information on Professor Wedgwood to its Mission in New

York, where it will be further evaluated. Naber said that

Professor Wedgwood is clearly qualified to serve on the

UNHRC, but noted that Jordan will factor in other political

considerations in deciding for whom to vote. We encourage

USUN to contact the Jordanian Mission directly to underline

the importance the USG attaches to the Wedgwood candidacy and

encourage GOJ support.
