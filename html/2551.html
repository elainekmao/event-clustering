REF: A) HARARE 411 B) HARARE 501

CLASSIFIED BY DCM REWHITEHEAD DUE TO 1.5 (B) AND (D).

1. (SBU) SUMMARY. THE GOZ FIRED ANOTHER OFF-TARGET

SALVO IN ITS CONTINUING ATTEMPT TO DISCREDIT MDC

PRESIDENTIAL CANDIDATE MORGAN TSVANGIRAI THROUGH

ACCUSATIONS THAT HE APPROACHED THE CANADIAN DICKENS AND

CONFIDENTIAL

MADSON LOBBYING FIRM, HEADED BY ONE ARI BEN MENASHE, TO

ARRANGE THE "ASSASSINATION" OF PRESIDENT MUGABE (REFS A

AND B). A MARCH 3 ZBC TELEVISION BROADCAST AGAIN SHOWED

FILM CLIPS OF TSVANGIRAI TALKING WITH BEN MENASHE AND

OTHER UNIDENTIFIED OBSERVERS. A SECOND SEGMENT OF THE

ZBC PROGRAM FEATURED AIR FORCE COMMANDER PERENCE SHIRI

DESCRIBING A SECOND MEETING WITH MDC MPS JOB SIKHALA AND

TAFADZWA MUSEKIWA, WHOM SHIRI CLAIMED HAD OFFERED HIM A

HEFTY FEE TO ASSIST THE MDC WITH A SMOOTH TRANSITION TO

POWER AFTER THE PRESIDENTIAL ELECTIONS. THE ENTIRE ZBC

PROGRAM WAS AMATEURISH TO THE EXTREME AND OFFERED LITTLE

CONVINCING EVIDENCE TO BUTTRESS THE GOZ\'S CASE (OR

ELECTORAL STRENGTH). ADDITIONAL INFORMATION GLEANED

FROM CANADIAN DIPLOMATS SHEDS LIGHT ON TSVANGIRAI\'S

MOTIVE FOR MEETING BEN MENASHE AND COMPANY, BUT BRINGS

INTO QUESTION HIS JUDGMENT IN THIS AFFAIR. END SUMMARY.

COMMENT

-------

6. (C) UNLESS MUGABE AND HIS BACKERS HAVE MORE

COMPELLING EVIDENCE THAN THAT PRESENTED IN THE

BROADCAST, IT IS DIFFICULT TO IMAGINE THAT TSVANGIRAI

AND HIS ASSOCIATE ARE IN MUCH DANGER OF BEING CONVICTED

IN AN IMPARTIAL COURT OF LAW. WE OBVIOUSLY DO NOT

DISCOUNT THE ABILITY OF THE GOZ TO STRING OUT THE CASE

AND HARASS TSVANGIRAI AT THE TIME OF ITS OWN CHOOSING.

THE MONTY-PYTHONESQUE NATURE OF THE ZBC BROADCAST OFFERS

LITTLE TO CONVINCE THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY THAT THE

ENTIRE AFFAIR IS MORE THAN JUST ANOTHER ITEM PULLED OUT

OF THE GOZ\'S BAG OF DIRTY TRICKS. IN TERMS OF

ELECTIONS, WE THINK THAT THE ENTIRE AFFAIR IS A NON-

EVENT. THOSE INCLINED TO BELIEVE THE TAPE PROBABLY WILL

DO SO, AND THOSE NOT SO INCLINED WILL NOT. WE NOTE,

HOWEVER, THAT IN TERMS OF GOOD JUDGMENT AND ADEQUATE

STAFF WORK, THE MONTREAL MEETING WAS NOT MORGAN

TSVANGIRAI\'S FINEST HOUR.
