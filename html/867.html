SUBJECT: LTTE USE OF LANDMINES TAKES ITS TOLL

REF: 96 COLOMBO 3209

1. SUMMARY: DURING THE PAST EIGHT MONTHS, OVER 225

PERSONS--SOME MILITARY, MANY CIVILIAN--HAVE

REPORTEDLY FALLEN VICTIM TO LANDMINES LAID BY THE

INSURGENT LIBERATION TIGERS OF TAMIL EELAM (LTTE) IN

THE WAR-AFFLICTED NORTH AND EAST. INCIDENTS THIS

YEAR ALONE HAVE KILLED AT LEAST 16 CIVILIANS AND

WOUNDED 12. THE LAYING OF LANDMINES--BY BOTH THE

LTTE AND THE GOVERNMENT--HAS BEEN COMMON IN THE

FOURTEEN-YEAR-OLD INSURGENCY. UNTIL THE INSURGENCY

IS RESOLVED, RESTRAINT IN THEIR USE IS UNLIKELY.

END COMMENT.

2. RECENT PRESS REPORTS STATE THAT DURING THE PAST

EIGHT MONTHS, OVER 225 PERSONS--SOME MILITARY, MANY

CIVILIAN--HAVE BEEN KILLED BY LANDMINES LAID BY THE

INSURGENT LIBERATION TIGERS OF TAMIL EELAM (LTTE) IN

JAFFNA AND OTHER PARTS OF THE WAR-TORN NORTH AND

EAST. IN A RECENT INCIDENT IN LATE JANUARY, THE

LTTE ON THE JAFFNA PENINSULA BLASTED CLAYMORE MINES

ON TWO OCCASIONS WITHIN CLOSE PROXIMITY OF EACH

OTHER, KILLING NINE CIVILIANS AND ONE SOLDIER. FIVE

OF THE CIVILIANS WERE WOMEN. SIX CIVILIANS AND TWO

SOLDIERS WERE ALSO WOUNDED. THE BLASTS OCCURRED ON

THE PONNALAI CAUSEWAY CONNECTING JAFFNA AND

KARAITHIVU ISLAND AS TROOPS CONDUCTED A ROUTINE

ROUTE-CLEARING OPERATION.

3. ON JANUARY 28, SIX CIVILIANS WERE KILLED AND

FIVE INJURED WHEN A VAN IN WHICH THEY WERE TRAVELING

RAN OVER A LANDMINE AT KUDAPOKUNA IN WELIKANDA IN

EASTERN PROVINCE. THE ARMY HAS FORCED TO CARRY OUT

ROUTE-CLEARING OPERATIONS BEFORE REACHING THE AREA

AS THEY FEARED THE LTTE MIGHT HAVE PLANTED

ADDITIONAL EXPLOSIVES. MEANWHILE ONE SOLDIER HAS

KILLED AND THREE INJURED WHEN THEIR TRUCK HIT A

LANDMINE ON THE SAME DAY. ON JANUARY 26, A DEFENSE

MINISTRY PRESS RELEASE SAID THAT A CIVILIAN HAS

KILLED AND ANOTHER INJURED WHEN A TRACTOR HAS

DESTROYED IN A LANDMINE BLAST AT PETHALAI IN

BATTICALOA DISTRICT OF THE EAST.

4. ON JANUARY 18, THE TIGERS SPREAD MINES AFTER A

PRE-DAWN ATTACK ON KANUGAHAWEWA POLICE POST AT

KEBITHIGOLLEWA IN ANURADHAPURA DISTRICT. TWENTY

THREE POLICE PERSONNEL WERE KILLED AND 20 OTHERS

WERE WOUNDED IN THE ATTACK, SOME REPORTEDLY BY THE

MINES. ONE AMBULANCE HAS DESTROYED WHEN A MINE

EXPLODED. ON FEBRUARY 10, TWO SOLDIERS AND THREE

TERRORISTS WERE KILLED IN A CONFRONTATION AT PUTTUR

IN THE JAFFNA DISTRICT. MILITARY SOURCES SAID

TERRORISTS HAD ACTIVATED A CLAYMORE MINE WHEN TROOPS

WERE RETURNING AFTER CONDUCTING AN OPERATION. ON

JANUARY 12, EIGHT SOLDIERS WERE KILLED AND ANOTHER

TEN WERE WOUNDED IN A LANDMINE EXPLOSION AT KACHCHAI

IN JAFFNA. THE ARMY TRACTOR ON WHICH THE TROOPS

WERE TRAVELING WAS CAUGHT IN THE LANDMINE BLAST.

5. COMMENT: THE LAYING OF LANDMINES--BY BOTH THE

LTTE AND THE GOVERNMENT--HAS BEEN COMMON IN THE

FOURTEEN-YEAR-OLD CIVIL WAR. CIVILIAN CASUALTIES,

THOUGH NOT REGULARLY NOTED IN THE PRESS, MOUNT.

UNTIL THE INSURGENCY IS RESOLVED, RESTRAINT IN

LANDMINE USE IS UNLIKELY. END COMMENT.
