SUBJECT: THE UNHCR EFFORT TO FREE THE CUBAN DOCTORS

DRIFTS

REF: HARARE 3344

CLASSIFIED BY AMBASSADOR TOM MCDONALD FOR REASONS 1.5

B/D.

1. (C) UNHCR ATTORNEY MICHIEL SALOMONS INFORMED CONSUL

ON JUNE 21 THAT DOCTORS LEONEL CORDOVA RODRIGUEZ AND

NORIS DEL CARMEN PENA MARTINEZ NOW HAVE VISAS STAMPED IN

THEIR CUBAN PASSPORTS ALLOWING TRAVEL TO THE UNSPECIFIED

COUNTRY WILLING TO RECEIVE THEM. HE SAID THAT

IMMIGRATION OFFICIALS ATTEMPTED TO TRANSFER THE DOCTORS

TO HARARE CENTRAL PRISON, BUT THE PAIR BALKED. THE

UNHCR HAD PREVIOUSLY BROUGHT THE DOCTORS TO HARARE

CENTRAL PRISON, WHERE SEVERAL INMATES THREATENED TO RAPE

DOCTOR CORDOVA IF HE WERE PLACED AMONG THEM. SALOMONS

SUGGESTED THAT THEY BE RETURNED TO GOROMONZI DETENTION

CENTER, BUT THE OFFICER IN CHARGE WOULD NOT HAVE THEM

BACK. THE TWO REMAIN IN A REMAND FACILITY WITHOUT

RUNNING WATER; THE DOCTORS HAVE NOT SHOWERED FOR FOUR

DAYS AND ARE BRUSHING THEIR TEETH WITH BOTTLED WATER

BEING BROUGHT BY UNHCR AT THEIR DAILY VISITS.

2. (C) THE UNHCR ATTORNEY REPORTED THAT A DECISION HAD
