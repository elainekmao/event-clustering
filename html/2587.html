SUBJECT: GOI\'S REACTION TO THE NEW ZINNI MISSION AND

1. (C) ON MARCH 8, POL MC AND NEA WATCHER DELIVERED REFTEL

POINTS AND THE PRESIDENT\'S ANNOUNCEMENT TO LUCA FERRARI, THE

MFA\'S OFFICE DIRECTOR FOR ISRAEL AND PALESTINIAN AFFAIRS.

POL MC STRESSED THE IMPORTANCE OF HAVING THE EU AND THE US

SPEAK THE SAME MESSAGE ON THE MEPP: IT\'S IMPERATIVE FOR

ARAFAT TO END THE VIOLENCE AND FOR ISRAEL TO ACT IN A MANNER

THAT HELPS RESTORE CALM. POL MC ALSO UNDERLINED THE

IMPORTANCE OF THE EU PUBLICLY SUPPORTING BOTH PARTIES ENDING

THE VIOLENCE AND GOING BACK TO NEGOCIATIONS.

2. (C) FERRARI DESCRIBED GENERAL ZINNI\'S RETURN TO THE REGION

AS A "VERY WISE MOVE" ON THE PART OF THE PRESIDENT,

ESPECIALLY BEFORE THE BEIRUT ARAB SUMMIT. THE GOI WOULD BE

FULLY SUPPORTIVE OF THE THE KIND OF MESSAGE DESCRIBED IN

REFTEL. HE EXPECTED SOLANA TO MAKE A PUBLIC STATEMENT FOR

BOTH ISRAELIS AND PALESTINIANS TO END THE VIOLENCE.

3. (C) ON PM AND ACTING FM BERLUSCONI\'S MARCH 12 TRIP TO

SAUDI ARABIA, FERRARI SAID THAT BERLUSCONI WAS GOING TO

EXPRESS HIS SUPPORT FOR THE CROWN PRINCE\'S PEACE VISION.

HOWEVER, THE PM IS PLANNING TO ASK THE SAUDIS FOR CONCRETE

DETAILS ABOUT THEIR PLAN, ESPECIALLY ON SENSITIVE ISSUES SUCH

AS JERUSALEM, THE REFUGEES, THE BORDER, AND SECURITY

GUARANTEES TO THE ISRAELIS.

4. (C) COMMENT: ITALY REACTED VERY POSITIVELY TO THE RETURN

OF GENERAL ZINNI TO THE MIDDLE EAST, AND WE EXPECT OTHER

EUROPEANS TO HAVE A SIMILAR REACTION. EVEN THOUGH THE

ITALIANS SUPPORT THE SAUDI PEACE VISION, MFA OFFICIALS DO NOT

EXPECT BERLUSCONI TO GET ANY PRECISE AND DETAILED ANSWERS ON

HIS TRIP NEXT WEEK TO SAUDI ARABIA. END COMMENT.
