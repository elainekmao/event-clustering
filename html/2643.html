SUBJECT: ALBANIAN PM MAJKO\'S MEETING WITH PM BERLUSCONI:

ENERGY; TOURISM MAIN POINTS OF DISCUSSION

CLASSIFIED BY: POLITICAL MINISTER-COUNSELOR TOM COUNTRYMAN.

REASON: 1.5 (B)(D).

1. (C) SUMMARY: ON MARCH 12, ALBANIAN AMBASSADOR TO ITALY

PELLUMB XHUFI GAVE POLITICAL MINISTER-COUNSELOR TOM

COUNTRYMAN AND POLOFF JIM DONEGAN A READOUT ON ALBANIAN PRIME

MINISTER PANDELI MAJKO\'S MARCH 6 MEETING IN ROME WITH PM

BERLUSCONI. ACCORDING TO XHUFI, THE MEETINGS FOCUSED ON TWO

ISSUES: ITALY\'S COMMITMENT TO HELP ALBANIA TO SOLVE ITS

CONTINUING ENERGY CRISIS, AND DEVELOPMENT OF THE ALBANIAN

TOURISM SECTOR. THE ITALIAN MFA CONFIRMED THAT A TEAM FROM

THE ITALIAN ELECTRICITY COMPANY ENEL WOULD VISIT ALBANIA

DURING THE WEEK OF MARCH 25. END SUMMARY.

2. (C) ACCORDING TO XHUFI, AN INDICATION OF HOW IMPORTANT

ITALY IS TO ALBANIA\'S FOREIGN RELATIONS IS THE FACT THAT THIS

WAS MAJKO\'S FIRST OFFICIAL FOREIGN VISIT SINCE TAKING OFFICE.

THE PMS DISCUSSED ALBANIA\'S CONTINUING SERIOUS ELECTRICITY

GENERATION AND DISTRIBUTION PROBLEMS. BERLUSCONI PROMISED

ITALIAN ASSISTANCE FOR THE SHORT AND LONG TERM. FIRST, A

TEAM FROM ENEL (ENTE NAZIONALE PER L\'ENERGIA ELETTRICA) WOULD

VISIT ALBANIA DURING THE WEEK OF MARCH 25 TO DETERMINE HOW TO

PROVIDE RELIEF FOR THE COMING MONTHS. THIS WOULD BE FOLLOWED

UP WITH MORE ASSISTANCE DESIGNED TO SOLVE THE ENERGY

INFRASTRUCTURE PROBLEM IN ALBANIA FOR THE LONG TERM.

3. (C) XHUFI SAID THAT BERLUSCONI HAD ALSO PROMISED ITALIAN

ASSISTANCE FOR ALBANIA\'S NASCENT TOURISM INDUSTRY. FOR

INSTANCE, BERLUSCONI PROMISED TO TALK TO HIS CONTACTS IN THE

MEDIA INDUSTRY REGARDING TELEVISION AND PRINT ADVERTISING

CAMPAIGNS TO MAKE ITALIANS MORE AWARE OF THE TOURISM

OPPORTUNITIES IN ALBANIA. BERLUSCONI EVEN PROMISED TO VISIT

(NON-OFFICIALLY) ALBANIA HIMSELF TO HELP PROVIDE SOME

PUBLICITY FOR THE CAMPAIGN.

4. (C) XHUFI COMMENTED THAT MORE INVESTMENT, IN GENERAL,

WAS NEEDED FROM EUROPEAN COUNTRIES LIKE ITALY IN ORDER TO

HELP STABILIZE THE ECONOMIC AND POLITICAL SITUATION IN

ALBANIA. FOR INSTANCE THERE IS ONLY "ONE-HALF OF AN ITALIAN

BANK" (THE ITALO-ALBANIAN BANK) IN TIRANA, WHICH CANNOT

SUPPORT MAJOR ITALIAN INVESTMENT. IN COMPARISON, THERE ARE 5

MAJOR GREEK BANKS PROVIDING SUPPORT TO INVESTORS FROM GREECE.

ITALY ALSO NEEDS TO BECOME MORE INVOLVED IN THE

PRIVATIZATION PROCESS IN ALBANIA. HE SAID THAT BERLUSCONI

HAD PROMISED MAJKO THAT HE WOULD URGE SOME OF HIS CONTACTS IN

THE TELECOM SECTOR TO GIVE A CLOSE LOOK TO ALBANIA, BUT MAJKO

REALIZES THAT ITALIANS WILL BE CAUTIOUS, GIVEN THAT SOME

OTHER INVESTMENTS BY ITALIAN COMPANIES IN THE TELECOM SECTOR

IN OTHER REGIONS HAD FAILED.

5. (C) XHUFI ACKNOWLEDGED THAT THE MOST IMPORTANT OBSTACLE

TO INVESTMENT IN ALBANIA WAS ENERGY, FOLLOWED CLOSELY BY

CONCERN AMONG INTERNATIONAL INVESTORS OVER TAXATION AND

CORRUPTION ISSUES. ALBANIA WAS WORKING HARD TO IMPROVE IN

THESE AREAS. HE CITED THE EU ACCESSION AND WTO PROCESSES AS

DRIVERS FOR THIS IMPROVEMENT, AND NOTED THE FREE TRADE ZONES

WITH MACEDONIA AND CROATIA AS SIGNS OF PROGRESS.

6. (C) XHUFI SAID THAT HE WAS PLEASED WITH THE INCREASED

COOPERATION BETWEEN ITALY AND ALBANIA ON TRAFFICKING ISSUES,

NOTING THAT ALBANIAN ORIGIN HUMAN TRAFFICKING INTO ITALY WAS

ONE-QUARTER OF THE PREVIOUS YEAR\'S LEVEL. HE EMPHASIZED THE

NEED FOR EVEN CLOSER COOPERATION AMONG LAW ENFORCEMENT

AUTHORITIES THROUGHOUT THE REGION IN DEALING WITH

TRAFFICKING/SMUGGLING ISSUES, PARTICULARLY SINCE MUCH OF THE

ALBANIA-ITALY ILLEGAL IMMIGRATION PROBLEM INVOLVED THIRD

COUNTRY NATIONALS. THERE MUST BE A "REGIONAL LEGAL

FRAMEWORK," AND OTHER COUNTRIES OF ORIGIN FOR TRAFFICKING

WOULD HAVE TO "ACT RESPONSIBLY." NOTE: OUR CONVERSATION WITH

XHUFI TOOK PLACE A FEW DAYS AFTER SIX ALBANIANS HAD DROWNED

ATTEMPTING TO MAKE A CLANDESTINE CROSSING OF THE ADRIATIC.

WE WILL REPORT MORE ON THAT TOPIC SEPTEL. END NOTE.

7. (C) DURING A FOLLOW UP TO THIS READOUT, MFA BALKAN

OFFICE DIRECTOR MAURIZIO MASSARI CONFIRMED THE ENEL VISIT,

AND SAID THAT MFA UNDER SECRETARY OF STATE ROBERTO ANTONIONE

WOULD VISIT ALBANIA APRIL 8-9, WHERE HE WOULD ANNOUNCE A GOI

ASSISTANCE PACKAGE TO ALBANIA CONSISTING OF 150 MILLION EURO

FOR DEVELOPMENT SPREAD OVER NEXT 3 YEARS. MASSARI ALSO

CONFIRMED THAT BERLUSCONI HAD PROMISED ASSISTANCE WITH THE

TOURISM SECTOR. FOR INSTANCE, THE GOI IS INTERESTED IN

HELPING ALBANIA DEVELOP AS TOURIST SITES A NUMBER OF ROMAN

RUINS SCATTERED THROUGHOUT THE COUNTRY. MASSARI DOUBTED,

8. (C) COMMENT: ALBANIA\'S ENERGY PROBLEMS ARE

LONG-STANDING AND HAVE BECOME A MAJOR FOCUS OF STABILITY PACT

EFFORTS. ALTHOUGH THE GOI, STILL THE MAJORITY OWNER OF THE

SLOWLY PRIVATIZING ENEL, CAN ARRANGE A VISIT TO ALBANIA BY AN

ENEL TEAM, ENEL IS NOT IN THE FOREIGN AID BUSINESS AND IS

UNLIKELY TO MAKE A MAJOR INVESTMENT IN IMPOVERISHED ALBANIA.

THE ASSISTANCE THE ALBANIANS THINK BERLUSCONI PROMISED WILL

NOT COME FROM ENEL\'S COFFERS. WHILE THERE MAY BE NOTHING ON

BERLUSCONI\'S CALENDAR INDICATING A VISIT TO ALBANIA, WE WOULD

NOT BE SURPRISED TO SEE HIM MAKE A QUICK TRIP, PARTICULARLY

IF ONE OF HIS MANY MEDIA CONTACTS ASKED HIM TO LEND HIS

ASSISTANCE TO A PUBLICITY CAMPAIGN TO SELL ALBANIA AS A

TOURIST DESTINATION TO ITALIANS. END COMMENT.

9. (U) MINIMIZE CONSIDERED.
