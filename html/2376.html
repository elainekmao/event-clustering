SUBJECT: AMBASSADOR\'S MEETING WITH PRESIDENTIAL

COUNSELOR UTETE

CLASSIFIED BY DCM REWHITEHEAD DUE TO 1.5 (B) AND (D).

REF: 01 HARARE 3229

1. (C) SUMMARY. AMBASSADOR AND DCM MET WITH

PRESIDENTIAL COUNSELOR CHARLES UTETE AND HIS CHIEF OF

STAFF ON JANUARY 9. THE AMBASSADOR CITED CONTINUED U.S.

ENGAGEMENT IN ZIMBABWE -- COUNTER-TERRORISM, HIV/AIDS

ASSISTANCE, FOOD ASSISTANCE -- BUT NOTED THAT PREVIOUSLY

EXPRESSED USG CONCERNS OVER NECESSARY CHANGES TO ENSURE

FREE AND FAIR PRESIDENTIAL ELECTIONS HAD IN FACT

DEEPENED. THE AMBASSADOR CAUTIONED THAT WHILE WE HOPE

TO BE ABLE TO IMPROVE RELATIONS WITH THE GOZ, GOZ

ACTIONS WILL BE THE DETERMINANT. UTETE REPLIED WITH THE

STANDARD GOZ LINE ON ALL POINTS SAVE THE LATTER, ON

WHICH HE DISSEMBLED. THE MEETING REVERTED TO CORDIAL

VERBAL SPARRING, WITH UTETE SPEAKING MOST POINTEDLY ON

THE ISSUES OF FOREIGN INTERFERENCE IN ELECTIONS AND THE

PASSAGE OF ZDERA. IT WAS OUR SENSE THAT UTETE BOTH

LISTENED AND HEARD, BUT NOT THAT HE -- LIKE MOST ZANU-PF

INSIDERS -- IS PREPARED TO EMBRACE A CHANGE OF POLICY.

END SUMMARY.

THE GOOD, THE BAD,

------------------

2. (C) THE AMBASSADOR BROKE THE ICE WITH AN EXCHANGE ON

JAZZ (UTETE IS SOMETHING OF A COGNOSCENTE), THEN SEGUED

CONFIDENTIAL

INTO THE USG\'S CONTINUED POSITIVE ENGAGEMENT WITH

ZIMBABWE ON NUMEROUS FRONTS. HE CITED COOPERATION ON

COUNTER-TERRORISM AND NOTED THE POSITIVE DEVELOPMENTS IN

AFGHANISTAN. OBSERVING THAT A LONG STRUGGLE LIES AHEAD,

HE STRESSED THAT CONTINUED COOPERATION IS ESSENTIAL. IN

THE DIRECT BILATERAL RELATIONSHIP, USAID AND CDC REMAIN

FULLY ENGAGED WITH THE GOZ AND NGOS IN THE STRUGGLE

AGAINST HIV/AIDS. THE USG HAS CONSECRATED AN ANNUAL WAR

CHEST OF USD 13 MILLION FOR ITS ZIMBABWE HIV/AIDS

INITIATIVES. IN ADDITION, WE ARE AWARE OF IMPENDING

FOOD SHORTAGES IN ZIMBABWE AND HAVE BEEN WORKING SINCE

LAST SUMMER TO HELP PROVIDE NECESSARY ASSISTANCE. OUR

ATTEMPTS TO BROKER A BILATERAL ASSISTANCE PROGRAM WITH

WORLD VISION INTERNATIONAL HAD BEEN SCUTTLED BY THE GOZ

OVER TECHNICAL CONCERNS, INCLUDING OBJECTIONS TO

GENETICALLY MODIFIED ORGANISMS, BUT WE WERE WORKING

CLOSELY WITH THE WORLD FOOD PROGRAM AND BELIEVED THAT WE

WERE NEAR A SOLUTION THAT WOULD PERMIT US TO ASSIST IN A

TIMELY FASHION.

...AND THE UGLY

---------------

3. (C) THE AMBASSADOR CONTINUED THAT THE UPCOMING

PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION POSES SERIOUS CHALLENGES FOR

ZIMBABWE AND ITS RELATIONS WITH THE U.S. A FREE AND

FAIR ELECTION ACCEPTED INTERNATIONALLY WOULD LEND NEW

LEGITIMACY TO ZIMBABWE AND ITS GOVERNMENT; TO THE

CONTRARY, A BOTCHED ELECTION WOULD SPAWN ADDITIONAL

PROBLEMS FOR THE COUNTRY. THE AMBASSADOR RECALLED THAT

PDAS BELLAMY HAD MADE SIMILAR OBSERVATIONS DURING HIS

VISIT LAST OCTOBER (REFTEL), BUT SINCE THAT TIME IT WAS

OUR VIEW THAT THE PROBLEMS IN ZIMBABWE HAVE WORSENED,

SETTING THE STAGE FOR THE PASSAGE OF ZDERA. DURING HIS

DECEMBER VISIT, ASSISTANT SECRETARY KANSTEINER STATED

PUBLICLY AND PRIVATELY TO GOZ OFFICIALS THAT ZDERA WAS

AN INCENTIVES BILL WITH THE POTENTIAL TO IMPOSE

RESTRICTIONS IF CERTAIN CONDITIONS WERE NOT MET. HE HAD

EMPHASIZED THAT WHILE CONGRESS HAD PASSED THE BILL AND

ASKED THE ADMINISTRATION TO CONSIDER THESE OPTIONS,

THERE WAS STILL TIME FOR THE GOZ TO TAKE THE NECESSARY

STEPS TO ENSURE FREE/FAIR ELECTIONS CONDUCTED IN AN OPEN

MANNER.

4. (C) THE AMBASSADOR STRESSED THAT THE BENCHMARKS FOR

FREE AND FAIR ELECTIONS ADOPTED BY THE U.S. WERE IN FACT

STANDARDS THAT THE GOZ HAD ALREADY ACCEPTED -- THE SADC-

PF NORMS AND STANDARDS. DESPITE EARLY STATEMENTS BY

PARLIAMENT SPEAKER MNANGAGWA THAT HE WOULD SEEK TO

REMOVE SOME OF THE MORE CONTENTIOUS AMENDMENTS TO THE

ELECTORAL ACT, THERE NOW APPEARS TO BE A MOVEMENT TOWARD

A LESS INCLUSIVE ELECTORAL PROCESS ABSENT CLEAR,

5. (C) UTETE REPLIED THAT ON THE COUNTER-TERRORISM

FRONT, AS DISCUSSED WITH PDAS BELLAMY, THERE IS NO

SIGNIFICANT DIFFERENCE OF VIEW. THE GOZ NOTES WE HAVE

TURNED AROUND CIRCUMSTANCES IN AFGHANISTAN. THERE IS

THE STILL UNRESOLVED ISSUE OF THE DEFINITION OF

TERRORISM, BUT NO ROOT DIFFERENCES. HE EXPRESSED

ZIMBABWEAN APPRECIATION OF USG SUPPORT FOR VARIOUS

HIV/AIDS INITIATIVES, AND NOTED THAT THE STRUGGLE SHOULD

BE MORE SHARPLY DEFINED, WITH THE GOZ USING ITS OWN

RESOURCES IN ADDITION TO THOSE OF OTHERS TO CHECK THE

SPREAD OF THE PANDEMIC.

-------------

BUT NO THANKS

-------------

CONFIDENTIAL

6. (C) UTETE OBSERVED THAT HE DID NOT KNOW WHICH ASPECT

TO DEAL WITH ON THE POLITICAL FRONT, BUT WOULD START

WITH VIOLENCE. HE SAID THAT THERE WAS A POSSIBILITY

THAT THE AMBASSADOR RELIED ON NEWSPAPERS FOR

INFORMATION, WHICH WOULD INDEED INDICATE INCREASED

VIOLENCE. HE FELT THAT EFFORTS NOW UNDERWAY BY THE GOZ

WOULD HELP CHECK THIS, AND ADDED THAT IT IS NOT FAIR TO

STATE THAT GOZ SUPPORTERS PERPETRATE ALL VIOLENCE. THIS

PHENOMENON STRETCHES ACROSS THE POLITICAL SPECTRUM.

WHAT MUST BE UNDERSTOOD IS THAT THERE IS NO MECHANISM

WITHIN GOVERNMENT DESIGNED TO HAVE PEOPLE BEATEN. TO

THE CONTRARY, THE GOVERNMENT UNDERSTANDS THAT WHATEVER

THE SOURCE OF VIOLENCE, IT IS THE GOVERNMENT\'S DUTY TO

PREVENT SUCH BEHAVIOR.

7. (C) UTETE RECALLED THAT THE POLICE COMMISSIONER HAS

REPEATEDLY STATED THAT VIOLENCE WOULD NOT BE TOLERATED.

UNFORTUNATELY, THE GOZ HAS NEITHER THE PRESCIENCE TO

PREDICT WHERE VIOLENCE MAY FLARE NOR THE MANPOWER TO

COVER ALL CONTINGENCIES. IN THE PRESENT "CHARGED

ATMOSPHERE," SOME CRIMINAL ELEMENTS WERE INTRUDING UNDER

THE GUISE OF ONE POLITICAL PARTY OR THE OTHER.

JOURNALISTS HAD SENSATIONALIZED THIS ISSUE, BUT UTETE

REASSURED THE AMBASSADOR THAT IT IS NOT GOZ POLICY TO

USE VIOLENCE AS A POLITICAL INSTRUMENT. HE NOTED THAT

THIS WAS IN FACT NOT A VERY GOOD WAY TO ATTRACT SUPPORT,

SINCE VOTERS COULD VOTE FOR WHOMEVER THEY WISHED IN

ANONYMITY ONCE IN THE VOTING BOOTH. IT WAS UNLIKELY

THAT THEY WOULD VOTE FOR SOMEONE WHO HAD ABUSED THEM.

8. (C) UTETE TURNED TO PROPOSED ELECTORAL AMENDMENTS,

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 04 HARARE 00096 02 OF 04 101219Z

STRESSING THAT THERE WERE PROVISIONS FOR AN INDEPENDENT

ELECTORAL COMMISSION IN THE DRAFT CONSTITUTION REJECTED

DURING THE 2000 REFERENDUM. HE DESCRIBED THE

OPPOSITION\'S CAMPAIGN AGAINST THE DRAFT AS "VERY

FOOLISH" -- WHEN IT FAILED TO PASS, THE GOZ HAD NO

CHOICE BUT TO REVERT TO THE PREVAILING LANCASTER HOUSE

CONSTITUTION. HE ADDED THAT ZANU-PF NO LONGER HAS A

LARGE ENOUGH PARLIAMENTARY MAJORITY TO CHANGE THE

CONSTITUTION, AND MDC WOULD CERTAINLY REFUSE TO SUPPORT

ANY ZANU-PF INITIATIVE. AS FOR CIVIL SERVANTS

MONITORING (IN LIEU OF INDEPENDENT MONITORS), HE ARGUED

THAT CIVIL SERVANTS ARE NOT NECESSARILY SUPPORTERS OF

THE INCUMBENT GOVERNMENT. THEY HAVE THE RIGHT TO

SUPPORT THE POLITICAL PARTY OF THEIR CHOICE, AND AS SUCH

ARE REPRESENTATIVE OF THE BODY POLITIC. POLITICAL

PARTIES CAN CHOOSE THEIR OWN REPRESENTATIVES AT THE

POLLS; BY USING CIVIL SERVANTS, THE GOZ HAD SIMPLY HOPED

TO IMPROVE THE EXISTING ELECTORAL SYSTEM.

9. (C) THE AMBASSADOR SAID THAT WHILE HE DID NOT THINK

IT NECESSARY TO REPLY POINT BY POINT, IT IS A FACT THAT

SADC-PF RECOMMENDS ESTABLISHMENT OF AN INDEPENDENT

ELECTORAL COMMISSION IN EACH MEMBER STATE. WE REALIZE

THAT VIOLENCE IN NOT UNILATERAL AND DON\'T\' BELIEVE

EVERYTHING WE READ; HOWEVER, WE HAVE NUMEROUS RELIABLE

SOURCES UPON WHICH TO RELY. HE CITED THE RECENT

PRONOUNCEMENT BY A WAR VET THAT CHINHOYI AND BINDURA ARE

"NO GO" ZONES FOR MDC, A STATEMENT NEVER REPUDIATED BY

THE GOVERNMENT. UTETE REPLIED THAT HE HAD LIVED IN THE

U.S. AND UNDERSTOOD THE PRINCIPLES OF FREE SPEECH -- NO

ONE EXPECTS THE USG TO SPEAK OUT ON EVERY STATEMENT ANY

AMERICAN CITIZEN MAKES. ONLY WHEN SOMEONE ACTS OUTSIDE

THE LAW IS IT TIME FOR THE GOVERNMENT TO STEP IN. THE

GOZ COULD NOT SPEND ALL OF ITS TIME REBUTTING EVERY

CRAZY STATEMENT MADE BY SOME "SO CALLED" WAR VET.

10. (C) THE AMBASSADOR SAID THAT THE ROOT PROBLEM IS A

LACK OF TRUST IN THE ZIMBABWEAN POLITICAL SITUATION, AND

THE LACK OF A MECHANISM TO CREATE THIS. THE ACTUAL FORM

OF AN ELECTORAL COMMISSION IS NOT AS IMPORTANT AS THE

ROLE THAT SUCH A BODY PLAYS IN CREATING TRUST. IT IS IN

THE INTEREST OF THE GOZ TO SEEK SUCH A MECHANISM TO

BUILD CONFIDENCE AND CREATE FAIR ELECTORAL RULES, AND

RESPECT FOR THOSE RULES. UTETE AGREED THAT TRUST IS

IMPORTANT FOR THE HEALTH OF THE COUNTRY BUT CITED THE

MDC\'S "BETRAYAL" OF ZANU-PF BY RENEGING ON AGREED

SUPPORT FOR THE AMENDED ELECTORAL LAWS (WHICH FAILED TO

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 HARARE 00096 03 OF 04 101220Z

PASS A VOTE IN PARLIAMENT).

11. (C) UTETE ADDED THAT HE WANTED TO BE FRANK -- THE

SITUATION IN ZIMBABWE WAS PUMPED UP FROM OUTSIDE.

OPPOSITION IDEAS AND TACTICS WERE IMPORTED FROM ABROAD;

THE OPPOSITION SEEMED TO BE A CREATURE FROM THE OUTSIDE.

THE GOZ WAS AWARE THAT FOREIGNERS FROM SOUTH AFRICA

STAFFED OPPOSITION OFFICES. IT APPEARED THAT OUTSIDERS

WERE SEEKING TO INFLUENCE ZIMBABWE\'S INTERNAL POLITICAL

PROCESS. IN THE U.S., HE OBSERVED, IT IS A CRIME FOR

OUTSIDERS TO CONTRIBUTE TO POLITICAL CAMPAIGNS. HOW CAN

THE GOZ HAVE TRUST/CONFIDENCE WITH UNDER SUCH

CIRCUMSTANCES, WHICH ARE GROUNDED IN VERIFIABLE FACT?

HE DERIDED "YOUR EU FRIENDS" AND SAID THE OPPOSITION

SHOULD GARNER VOTES IN AN HONEST MANNER, NOT BECAUSE OF

OUTSIDE WEALTH. THE AMBASSADOR REPEATED OUR VIEW THAT

ELECTIONS SHOULD BE FREE AND FAIR, SO THAT WE WILL BE

ABLE TO RESPECT THE RESULTS. THE GOZ HAS THE POWER TO

TAKE THE NECESSARY STEPS TO CREATE THESE CONDITIONS.

ZDERA

-----

12. (C) UTETE ASKED WHY ZDERA? DID THE USG INTEND TO

IMPOSE SUCH ACTS AROUND THE WORLD ON EVERY GOVERNMENT

WITH WHICH IT DISAGREED? UTETE SAID THAT HE UNDERSTOOD

WHY WE MIGHT DISAGREE WITH HOW THE GOZ IS HANDLING

THINGS. HOWEVER, THERE ARE MANY REGIMES AROUND THE

WORLD THAT ARE DOING MUCH WORSE. HOW HAS "LITTLE

ZIMBABWE" SO PROVOKED CONGRESS AND THE ADMINISTRATION

PAGE 04 HARARE 00096 03 OF 04 101220Z

THAT THEY HAVE RECEIVED THIS EXCEPTIONAL TREATMENT. THE

AMBASSADOR CITED SEVERAL SPECIFIC INCIDENTS, SUCH AS

JAILING THE DAILY NEWS EDITOR ON FLIMSY CHARGES SHORTLY

BEFORE HIS SCHEDULED VISIT TO THE U.S. SUCH BEHAVIOR

HAD PROVOKED STRONG FEELINGS. THE AMBASSADOR ADDED THAT

ZIMBABWE IS IMPORTANT. IT HAS HAD STRONG INSTITUTIONS

AND IS TAKEN SERIOUSLY. MANY FRIENDS OF ZIMBABWE ARE

DISAPPOINTED. HE CONCLUDED THAT ZDERA IS TWO-EDGED --

INCENTIVES AND RESTRICTIONS -- AND THAT WE HOPE THAT

EVENTS HERE EVOLVE IN SUCH A FASHION THAT INCENTIVES CAN

BE INVOKED.
