SUBJECT: AJELLO TRYING TO SELL NON-CONGOLESE

BELLIGERENTS\' SUMMIT TO ZIMBABWEANS

REF: (A) LUSAKA 3300 (NOTAL)

(B) MAPUTO 2843 (NOTAL)

CLASSIFIED BY CHARGE D\'AFFAIRES EARL M. IRVING FOR

REASONS 1.5 (B) AND (D).

1. (C) EUROPEAN UNION SPECIAL ENVOY ALDO AJELLO IS IN

HARARE FOR A TWO-DAY VISIT DURING WHICH HE HOPES TO SELL

THE ZIMBABWEAN GOVERNMENT ON A SUMMIT OF NON-CONGOLESE

BELLIGERENTS, AS DESCRIBED IN REFTELS. AJELLO, WHO HAD

BEEN IN HARARE FOR 24 HOURS AND HAD MET ONLY WITH

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 HARARE 04064 250725Z

THE CONGOLESE AMBASSADOR, DR. KIKAYA BIN KARUBI, WAS

HOPING TO SEE FOREIGN MINISTER MUDENGE, NATIONAL SECURITY

MINISTER GOCHE AND PRESIDENT MUGABE, BUT HAD RECEIVED NO

CONFIRMATION OF ANY OF THE REQUESTED MEETINGS BY LATE

JULY 24.

2. (C) AJELLO EXPRESSED THE VIEW TO CHARGE THAT NEITHER

SIDE OF THE CONGOLESE CIVIL WAR WANTED PEACE, AND THAT

THE CURRENT SITUATION FOLLOWED AN ALREADY ESTABLISHED

PATTERN. ONCE ONE OF THE SIDES ENJOYED A SLIGHT

ADVANTAGE IN THE FIELD, IT BEGAN TO PLAY GAMES IN TERMS

OF THE PEACE PROCESS. AT THE MOMENT IT WAS KABILA, WHO

WAS MOUNTING SUNDRY PRETEXTS TO DUMP SIR KETUMILE MASIRE

AS FACILITATOR, AND PURPOSELY MISINTERPRETING THE LIMITS

OF THE UNITED NATIONS SECURITY COUNCIL RESOLUTIONS AND

THE MANDATE OF A PEACEKEEPING OPERATION THAT WOULD BE

DEPLOYED IN PHASES. WITHOUT ALLIES NEITHER SIDE WOULD

LAST LONG, AJELLO REASONED, PARTICULARLY KABILA. THE

EUROPEAN UNION ENVOY ALSO ESTIMATED THAT THE POLITICAL

AND HUMAN COST TO THE EXTERNAL BELLIGERENTS HAD BEEN

SUFFICIENTLY HIGH THAT THEY WERE READY TO WITHDRAW.

3. (C) THE GOAL OF A SUMMIT OF THE NON-CONGOLESE

BELLIGERENTS WOULD BE TO PUT THEIR CONGOLESE PROXIES ON

NOTICE THAT EITHER THEY ADHERED TO THE LUSAKA PEACE

PLAN, HOWEVER IMPERFECT, OR FACED REVOCATION OF SUPPORT.

AJELLO WAS ESPECIALLY CURIOUS TO KNOW THE ZIMBABWEAN

REACTION TO SUCH AN IDEA, AS HE WAS CONFIDENT THE OTHER

PARTIES (INCLUDING RWANDA AND UGANDA) WOULD AGREE TO IT.

A MEETING OF THE CONGOLESE BELLIGERENTS WOULD FOLLOW THE

SUMMIT, HE CONTINUED. CALLING CONGOLESE PRESIDENT

KABILA A MAN WITH NO VISION BUT A MASTER MANIPULATOR,

THE EUROPEAN UNION ENVOY AFFIRMED THAT THE CONGOLESE HAD

TO BE PERSUADED THAT CONTINUING TO PROSECUTE THE WAR WAS

NOT AN OPTION.

4. (C) AJELLO THEN EXPLAINED THAT MOZAMBICAN PRESIDENT

CHISSANO ("A MAN WITH NO CHARISMA, GREY AND PALE") WOULD

BE THE ONE TO ORGANIZE AND HOST THE SUMMIT. ALTHOUGH

CHISSANO REPORTEDLY CONFIDED TO AJELLO THAT HE WAS IN

KABILA\'S BAD BOOKS, AS THE CONGOLESE PRESIDENT THOUGHT

HE WAS IN LEAGUE WITH THE SOUTH AFRICANS, THE ANGOLANS

RULED OUT ZAMBIAN MEDIATOR CHILUBA CONVENING THE SUMMIT.
