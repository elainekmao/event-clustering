3. CROATIA IS NOW HOST TO AT LEAST 750,000 REFUGEES

AND DISPLACED PERSONS. THE DIRECT DAILY COST TO THE

CROATIAN GOVERNMENT OF SUSTAINING THESE PEOPLE IS

SOME THREE MILLION USD -- AND RISING. THE INDIRECT

COSTS TO THE CROATIAN GOVERNMENT, INCLUDING SOCIAL

AND HEALTH SERVICES, UTILITY BILLS UNPAID BY FAMILIES

HOSTING REFUGEES, AND VISIBLE DESTRUCTION OF HOTELS

AND OTHER INFRASTRUCTURE BY REFUGEES, ARE INCALCULABLY

GREATER. CROATIA RECEIVES FOOD, CLOTHING, AND OTHER

IN-KIND ASSISTANCE FROM UNHCR, OTHER INTERNATIONAL

BODIES, OTHER GOVERNMENTS, AND PVO\'S, BUT IT RECEIVES

NO/NO FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE TO HELP OFFSET EVEN THE

DIRECT, THREE MILLION USD PER DAY BURDEN, EXCEPT FOR

SOME VERY RECENT, COMPARATIVELY TINY SUMS FROM A

COUPLE WESTERN GOVERNMENTS AND UNHCR. CARING FOR THE

REFUGEES AND DISPLACED ABSORBS SOME TWENTY PERCENT OF

CROATIA\'S BUDGET AND CONTRIBUTES HEAVILY TO INFLATION

NOW RUNNING AT AN ANNUALIZED RATE OF OVER 2,500

PERCE5. THE SOCIAL COST OF THE REFUGEE/DISPLACED

BURDEN -- MEASURED IN LOCAL TENSION LEVELS AND

PALPABLE PUBLIC RESENTMENT OF REFUGEES AS THE CROATS\'

LIVING STANDARD PLUMMETS -- IS LARGE AND GROWING.

7. COMMENT: ONE DISCUSSES THESE ISSUES WITH

KNOWLEDGEABLE CROATS AGAINST THE BACKDROP OF SOME

MUTUALLY-UNDERSTOOD PLAIN TRUTHS: THE REFUGEE

PROBLEM IN B-H IS NOT BEING BROUGHT UNDER CONTROL; IT

IS GROWING. WE ARE NOT GAINING GROUND ON THE

WINTERIZATION PROBLEM IN B-H; WE ARE LOSING GROUND,

AS MORE HOMES ARE DESTROYED EACH DAY. THE REASON

CONFIDENTIAL

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 ZAGREB 02392 03 OF 09 131610Z

THESE PROBLEMS ARE ESCALATING IS NEITHER THE

OCCASIONAL LOCAL FIREFIGHT BETWEEN CROATIAN AND

MUSLIM FORCES NOR BALLYHOOED CROATIAN/SERBIAN

COLLUSION TO "CARVE UP" BOSNIA. REFUGEES ARE BEING

GENERATED, OVERWHELMINGLY, BY THE STILL-UNCHECKED

ETHNIC CLEANSING OF A CONTEMPTUOUSLY ARROGANT BOSNIAN

SERB LEADERSHIP. THE MOST LIKELY CONSEQUENCE OF

CROATIA\'S DOING WHAT THE WEST SAYS IT WANTS CROATIA

TO DO IN B-H, THAT IS, WITHDRAW MILITARILY, WOULD BE

THE BOSNIAN SERBS\' ROLLING UP ANOTHER 20-30 PERCENT

OF B-H TERRITORY AND CROATIA\'S BECOMING SWAMPED

OVERNIGHT BY ANOTHER HALF MILLION REFUGEES, OR MORE.

A SIMILAR FATE MIGHT ALREADY HAVE BEFALLEN CROATIA

WERE IT IN FACT GUILTY OF ETHNIC CLEANSING IN B-H.

DESPITE THE CLAIMS OF VARIOUS WESTERN EDITORIALISTS,

HOWEVER, CROATIA HAS NOT/NOT CLEANSED IN B-H --

EITHER AS A MATTER OF POLICY OR AS A SIGNIFICANTLY

WIDESPREAD, RECURRING PRACTICE ON THE GROUND.

ENORMOUS NUMBERS OF BOSNIAN MUSLIMS, AND EVEN SOME

BOSNIAN SERBS, CONTINUE TO LIVE IN RELATIVE PEACE

WITH THEIR CROATIAN NEIGHBORS IN PARTS OF B-H

DEFENDED BY CROATIAN OR CROATIAN/B-H GOV\'T FORCES.

AND LASTLY, CROATIA\'S BORDER WITH B-H, DESPITE THE

CURRENT CONTROLS, IS INCOMPARABLY MORE OPEN TO

BOSNIAN REFUGEES THAN THE BORDERS OF ANY OF CROATIA\'S

CONCERNED WESTERN NEIGHBORS.
