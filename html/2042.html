1. EDITORIALS IN THE OCTOBER 9 EDITIONS OF THE

GOVERNMENT-CONTROLLED DAILY "THE HERALD" AND THE

INDEPENDENT DAILY "THE DAILY NEWS" FOCUS ON THE

UNITED STATES-LED STRIKES IN AFGHANISTAN. FULL

EDITORIAL TEXTS FOLLOW:

2. UNDER HEADLINE "U. S. GOVERNMENT MUST ACT

CORRECTLY," THE "HERALD" COMMENTS:

"THE U. S. FACES BOTH OPPORTUNITIES AND DREADFUL RISKS

NOW THAT IT HAS STARTED ITS BOMBING CAMPAIGN IN

AFGHANISTAN. UNDER INTERNATIONAL LAW ANY STATE IS

ENTITLED TO DEFEND ITSELF AND IS ENTITLED TO RETALIATE

AGAINST THOSE WHO ATTACK IT AND THOSE WHO ACTIVELY

SUPPORT ITS ATTACKERS. THE U. S. WAS BRUTALLY ATTACKED

ON SEPTEMBER 11. THE MAIN ATTACKS IN NEW YORK WERE

CARRIED OUT AGAINST CIVILIAN TARGETS WITH THE

DELIBERATE INTENTION OF KILLING CIVILIANS. SO ASSUMING

THAT THE EVIDENCE AGAINST AL-QUAEDS, LED BY OSAMA BIN

LADEN, IS AS GOOD AS THE AMERICANS CLAIM, THEN THE

ATTACKS ON AL-QUAEDS AND THE TALIBAN RULERS OF

AFGHANISTAN ARE JUSTIFIED. THE TALIBAN HAVE SUPPORTED

AND PROTECTED AL-QUAEDS. BUT THIS RIGHT OF SELF-

DEFENSE IS NOT A BLANK CHECK. THE U. S. WOULD HAVE

BEEN BETTER ADVISED TO HAVE MADE PUBLIC THE EVIDENCE IT

HAS BEEN PRESENTING TO MANY OF ITS ALLIES, BOTH OLD AND

NEW, AS TO THE BLAMEWORTHINESS OF BOT AL-QUAEDS AND THE

TALIBAN. THE EVIDENCE IS SAID TO BE IMPRESSIVE. YET

MOST PEOPLE AROUND THE WORLD WOULD LIKE TO SEE IT FOR

THEMSELVES AND MAKE UP THEIR OWN MINDS. IT IS

UNFORTUNATE THAT THEY HAVE NOT BEEN ALLOWED TO DO SO.

CARPET BOMBING OF AFGHANISTAN, FOR EXAMPLE, WOULD PUT

THE U. S. IN THE SAME CAMP AS THOSE WHO FLEW THE PLANES

INTO THE WORLD TRADE CENTER. KILLING CIVILIANS FOR

IGNOBLE MOTIVES OF REVENGE IS TERRORISM. THE INITIAL

ATTACKS APPEAR TO HAVE BEEN AGAINST LEGITIMATE TARGETS

AND IT ALSO APPEARS, FROM THE CASUALTY TOLL, THAT THE

BOMBING EXHIBITED A HIGH DEGREE OF PRECISION. IT IS

ESSENTIAL THAT FUTURE ATTACKS FOLLOW THE SAME COURSE.

IT IS ALSO ESSENTIAL THAT THE U. S. RESTRICTS ITS WAR

AIMS. THE U. S. MUST RESISTS THE TEMPTATION TO TRY AND

IMPOSE SOME SORT OF GOVERNMENT ON AFGHANISTAN. THAT

MUST BE LEFT TO THE AFGHAN PEOPLE WITH THE ADVICE AND

HELP OF NEIGHBORING COUNTRIES. IT IS HIGHLY LIKELY

THAT ANY FUTURE GOVERNMENT OF AFGHANISTAN WILL BE BOTH

NATIONALISTIC AND ISLAMIC. AFTER ALL ALMOST ALL

AFGHANS ARE MUSLIMS AND, AS FOREIGN INVADERS HAVE

DISCOVERED, ARE INTENSELY NATIONALISTIC. IT IS INDEED

HIGHLY LIKELY THAT ANY FUTURE AFGHAN GOVERNMENT WILL BE

ALLIED TO BOTH IRAN AND PAKISTAN, ITS MOST IMPORTANT

NEIGHBORS, AND WILL BE CLOSER TO RUSSIA AND CHINA AMONG

THE GREAT POWERS THAN TO THE U. S. SUCH A GOVERNMENT

IS UNLIKELY TO REGARD THE U. S. AS ITS BEST FRIEND AND

IT WOULD BE UNREASONABLE OF THE AMERICANS TO EXPECT

THIS. ALL THAT THE U. S. CAN EXPECT, AND IS ENTITLED

TO EXPECT, IS A GOVERNMENT THAT ACCEPTS INTERNATIONAL

LAW. THE U. S. IS REGARDED, JUSTIFIABLY, WITH GREAT

SUSPICION IN MUCH OF THE ISLAMIC WORLD. ANY MEDDLING

IN AFGHAN POLITICS WOULD DOOM A RESULTANT GOVERNMENT.

THE ISLAMIC SUSPICION OF THE U. S. HAS THREE STRANDS.

FIRST, AND MOST IMPORTANT, IS THE U. S. BACKING IN

ALMOST ALL CIRCUMSTANCES OF ISRAEL. THERE HAVE BEEN

INDICATIONS IN THE LAST WEEK THAT THE U. S. DOES

BELIEVE THAT THE PALESTINIANS TOO HAVE RIGHTS, BUT MANY

WILL WANT TO SEE ACTION BEFORE THEY BELIEVE THIS.

SECONDLY, THERE IS THE GENERAL THIRD WORLD IRRITATION

WITH THE U. S.\'S ASSUMPTION OF GLOBAL DOMINANCE. THE

U. S. NEEDS TO BE LESS DOMINEERING. AND THIRDLY THERE

IS A DISTASTE OF THE SECULAR CONSUMER CULTURE OF THE U.

S., A GAP THAT IS NOT GOING TO BE BRIDGED BUT WHICH

NEEDS TO BE UNDERSTOOD. SO THE U. S. HAS THE

OPPORTUNITY OF CRIPPLING AL-QUAEDS IN THE WAR ON

TERRORISM AND GIVING THE AFGHAN PEOPLE A CHANCE TO HAVE

A GOVERNMENT THEY DESERVE. IT RUNS THE RISK OF BEING

SEEN AS A BIG ANTI-ISLAMIC BULLY. THE CHOICE IS UP TO

THE U. S. GOVERNMENT AND IT MUST ACT AS CORRECTLY AS IT

HAS STARTED."

TO THE 11 SEPTEMBER 2001 TERRORIST ATTACKS IN NEW YORK

AND WASHINGTON, BUT THE REAL DANGER IS THAT THE U.S.

COULD BE SUCKED INTO A LONG-DRAWN CONFLICT. BUSH HAS

IGNORED WORLD OPINION NOT TO ACT HASTILY. IT WAS

ESSENTIAL TO FURNISH THE REST OF THE WORLD WITH

SPECIFIC EVIDENCE LINKING THE CHIEF SUSPECT IN LAST

MONTH\'S ATTACK ON THE U. S., OSAMA BIN LADEN, TO THE

DASTARDLY ACTS. THAT EVIDENCE REMAINS PRIVY TO THE

AMERICAN ADMINISTRATION AND THEIR BRITISH ALLIES. IT

WOULD HAVE BEEN FAR MORE PREFERABLE TO LET THE WHOLE OF

THE WORLD KNOW OF THIS EVIDENCE FOR THEM TO MAKE AN

INFORMED DECISION, WHETHER OR NOT IT WAS GOING TO BE IN

CONCERT WITH THAT OF THE AMERICANS. BUT SUCH AN

ARGUMENT HAS ALREADY BEEN OVERTAKEN BY EVENTS. AMERICA

IS ENTITLED TO PURSUE ITS INTERESTS AND SECURITY, BUT

IN SO DOING IT IS ERODING THE ENORMOUS SYMPATHY AND

SUPPORT IT GARNERED SINCE THE ATTACKS ALMOST A MONTH

AGO. AMERICAN INTELLIGENCE IS SUPPOSED TO BE SO

SOPHISTICATED THAT THEY SHOULD NOT BE STRIKING TARGETS

OTHER THAN EXACT LOCATIONS OF BIN LADEN, IF IT IS

ACCEPTED THAT HE BE CONVICTED BEFORE A TRIAL. BUT THAT

THEY SHOULD BE DROPPING BOMBS ALL OVER AFGHANISTAN

RAISES FAR MORE SERIOUS CONCERNS: THE CASUALTIES ARE

GOING TO BE MORE THAN THE ALLEGED PERPETRATORS OF THE

11 SEPTEMBER ATTACKS. AFGHANISTAN IS A NATION OF

DISPLACED PEOPLE. SUNDAY\'S BOMBINGS WILL HAVE HASTENED

THE WAVE OF AFGHANS FLEEING THEIR COUNTRY. WHILE THEY

SEEK REFUGE IN NEIGHBORING COUNTRIES, THERE IS

POTENTIAL FOR DESTABILIZATION OF THE REGION. THERE

COULD BE MORE CASUALTIES AMONG LEADERS IN THAT REGION

AS RESENTMENT AGAINST DISPLACEMENT AND AMERICA BUILDS

UP. IT IS IMPORTANT TO UNDERSTAND THE PSYCHE THAT

DRIVES PEOPLE WHO ARE PREPARED TO SACRIFICE THEIR LIVES

FOR A CAUSE. AMONG THOSE FLEEING AFGHANISTAN INTO

NEIGHBORING STATES ARE PEOPLE COMMITTED TO A CAUSE, ONE

OF THEM BEING TO FIGHT AMERICA. THEY WILL BE DIFFICULT

TO DETECT BECAUSE THEY ARE NOT AN ARMY, BUT THE EFFECTS

OF THEIR ACTIONS ARE DEADLY. THE FORMER SOVIET UNION

KNOWS THE LESSON TOO WELL. IT MET ITS WATERLOO AGAINST

THE TALIBAN. MANY OF THOSE BACKING THE AMERICAN

STRIKES AGAINST AFGHANISTAN DO SO BECAUSE THEY BELIEVE

THE OUTCOME WILL CONVENIENTLY SOLVE THEIR OWN LONG-

STANDING PROBLEMS, WHOSE ORIGINS ARE SEEN AS EMANATING

FROM AFGHANISTAN OR THAT REGION OF THE WORLD. BY

BOMBING AFGHANISTAN, THE U. S. AND ITS ALLIES ARE

MERELY VICTIMIZING THE ORDINARY AFGHANS WHO HAVE NO

CAPACITY TO ESCAPE OR HIDE FROM THE ATTACKS. THE

QUESTION THEY WILL ASK IS: WHAT DO WE HAVE TO DO WITH

BIN LADEN\'S AGENDA? INSTEAD OF SYMPATHY MORE HATRED IS

ENGENDERED. THE AMERICANS SHOULD HAVE LEARNT FROM THE

NORTH ATLANTIC ORGANIZATION\'S EXPERIENCE IN YUGOSLAVIA.

THE AMERICANS SHOULD HAVE USED THEIR SOPHISTICATED

INTELLIGENCE TO TRACK DOWN BIN LADEN AND BRING HIM TO

JUSTICE WITHOUT RISKING UNNECESSARY CIVILIAN

CASUALTIES. IT IS POSSIBLE THE CHIEF SUSPECT IS

NOWHERE NEAR THE PLACES BEING BOMBED AS HE WOULD HAVE

LONG ANTICIPATED THIS ACTION. THE LIVES AND SECURITY

OF NATIONALS BELONGING TO THE U. S. AND ITS ALLIES

THROUGHOUT THE WORLD ARE NOW IN SERIOUS JEOPARDY. NO

ONE WILL EVER KNOW WHERE AND WHEN THE NEXT ATTACK WILL
