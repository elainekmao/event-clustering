YOUR EXCELLENCY:

THE NUMBER OF CEASE-FIRE VIOLATIONS AND ESCALATING

ATTACKS ON CROATIAN CITIES AND VILLAGES, AS WELL AS

EXPULSIONS OF REMAINING CROATIAN AND NON-SERBIAN

POPULATIONS FROM THE OCCUPIED AREAS OF THE REPUBLIC OF

CROATIA, IS ASSUMING ALARMING PROPORTIONS.

UNFORTUNATELY, OUR FEARS ABOUT THE WAR SPREADING TO

NEIGHBOURING BOSNIA-HERZEGOVINA HAVE BEEN REALIZED.

THERE, THE SAME SCENARIO AND THE SAME TERRITORIAL

PRETENSIONS ARE BEING INFLICTED UPON THE CROATIAN AND

MOSLEM POPULATIONS. HORRIBLE CRIMES ARE BEING

PERPETRATED AGAINST THEM AND THEY ARE BEING EXPELLED

FROM THEIR HOMES IN ORDER TO ETHNICALLY "PURIFY" THE

TERRITORY FOR A GREATER SERBIA. THESE ACTIONS

BEEN JOINED IN THE PAST SEVERAL DAYS BY 65,000 MORE

CROATIA IS DOING ALL POSSIBLE TO ASSIST THOSE AFFECTED

WE APPEAL TO YOU TO USE YOUR PERSONAL INFLUENCE AND

THE INTERNATIONAL REPUTATION YOUR COUNTRY ENJOYS TO

MECHANISM FOR HALTING THE AGGRESSION AGAINST THE

MORE CONCRETELY, IT IS NECESSARY TO SPEED UP THE

DEPLOYMENT DATE ENVISIONED FOR THE PEACEKEEPING TROOPS

AND THE RETREAT OF THE YUGOSLAV NATIONAL ARMY FROM THE

ENTIRE TERRITORY OF THE REPUBLIC OF CROATIA; TO LIFT

ALL AIR, SEA AND LAND BLOCKADES; AND TO GUARANTEE THE

RETURN OF ALL REFUGEES. THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY

SHOULD IMMEDIATELY CONSIDER A PEACE-KEEPING PLAN AND

OPERATION IN BOSNIA-HERZEGOVINA. IT IS ALSO

IMPERATIVE TO CONCLUDE THE PROCESS OF INTERNATIONALLY

DE-RECOGNIZING YUGOSLAVIA.
