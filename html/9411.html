SUBJECT: FOLEY TRIAL: COURT HEARS PROSECUTION TESTIMONY,

THEN ADJOURNS UNTIL SEPTEMBER

REF: AMMAN 3987

1. (U) The State Security Court on July 15 adjourned the

trial of those accused of assassinating USAID diplomat

Laurence Foley until September 2 for a judicial holiday,

according to local press reports. The court--which had

resumed hearings on July 8 after previous postponements (see

Ref)--heard opening prosecution arguements and the testimony

of several security officers involved in the investigation of

the defendents. When the trial resumes in September, the

prosecution will continue to present its case.
