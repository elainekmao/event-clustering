1. (C) THE VATICAN ANNOUNCED ON NOVEMBER 28 THAT IT HAS

INVITED THE LEADERS OF THE DIFFERENT CHRISTIAN COMMUNITIES

IN THE HOLY LAND TO A MEETING ON DECEMBER 13. THE VATICAN

PRESS ANNOUNCEMENT CATEGORIZES THE MEETING AS PURELY

"PASTORAL" IN NATURE.

2. (C) DCM SPOKE WITH HIS ISRAELI COUNTERPART NOVEMBER 29.

ISRAELI DCM SAID THAT HIS EMBASSY HAS DISCOVERED THAT THE

MEETING IS BEING ORGANIZED BY THE VATICAN FOREIGN MINISTRY,

AND NOT ONE OF THE VATICAN COUNCILS (I.E. MINISTRIES) WHICH

WOULD NORMALLY BE INVOLVED IN RELIGIOUS/PASTORAL AFFAIRS.

IN ADDITION, THE ISRAELIS BELIEVE THAT THE ANNOUNCEMENT OF

THE MEETING WAS MEANT TO COINCIDE WITH A DOCUMENT ISSUED BY

THE CHRISTIAN LEADERS IN ISRAEL AND THE TERRITORIES

CONDEMNING THE CONSTRUCTION OF THE NAZARETH MOSQUE.

3. (C) COMMENT: THE TIMING OF THIS MEETING, COUPLED WITH

THE VATICAN\'S REQUEST FOR A MEETING WITH SECRETARY POWELL

IS A STRONG INDICATION THAT THE VATICAN IS PREPARED TO

BEGIN ACTIVELY PUSHING WHAT IT CONSIDERS ITS INTERESTS IN

THE MEPP. IT IS LIKELY THAT IT SEES AN OPENING CREATED BY

THE WAR ON TERRORISM TO PRESSURE THE ISRAELIS ON THE ISSUE

OF INTERNATIONAL GUARANTEES FOR THE RELIGIOUS SHRINES. IT

IS ALSO POSSIBLE THAT THAT FOREIGN MINISTER WILL RAISE THE

ISSUE OF THE NAZARETH MOSQUE WHEN HE MEETS THE SECRETARY ON

DECEMBER 4. NICHOLSON
