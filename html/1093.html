1. (C) SUMMARY: ZANU-PF\'S LEGAL SECRETARY, EDDISON

ZVOGBO TOLD AMBASSADOR MCDONALD MAY 31 THAT THE "WAR

VETERANS" WHO ARE OCCUPYING COMMERCIAL FARMS WILL BE

REMOVED SHORTLY AFTER THE PARLIAMENTARY ELECTION AT THE

END OF JUNE. IN RESPONSE TO THE AMBASSADOR\'S EXPRESSION

OF SERIOUS CONCERN, ZVOGBO DISMISSED THE LEVEL OF

POLITICAL VIOLENCE ACROSS THE COUNTRY AS LESS THAN WHAT

HAS OCCURRED IN OTHER COUNTRIES, NOTABLY SOUTH AFRICA,

DURING RECENT ELECTIONS. THE MINISTER IS CONFIDENT THAT

THE MOVEMENT FOR DEMOCRATIC CHANGE (MDC) WILL GET ONLY A

HANDFUL OF THE PARLIAMENTARY SEATS AND ASSERTED THAT THE

OPPOSITION PARTY WAS LOSING SUPPORT EVEN IN ITS URBAN

BASE. THE MINISTER REITERATED THAT INTERNATIONAL

ELECTION OBSERVERS ARE WELCOME AND THAT THE RULING PARTY

WOULD ENSURE A PEACEFUL PROCESS. END SUMMARY.

2. (C) AMBASSADOR MCDONALD, ACCOMPANIED BY DCM IRVING

AND POLOFF, CALLED ON MINISTER WITHOUT PORTFOLIO AND

ZANU-PF LEGAL SECRETARY EDDISON ZVOGBO ON MAY 31 FOR AN

UPDATE ON THE RULING PARTY\'S VIEWS ON THE LAND

OCCUPATIONS AND ELECTION CHANCES, AND TO EXPRESS THE

USG\'S SERIOUS CONCERN ABOUT THE LEVEL OF POLITICAL

VIOLENCE AND INTIMIDATION OCCURRING IN THE COUNTRY.

ZVOGBO, A HARVARD-TRAINED LAWYER, WAS IN A COMBATIVE

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 HARARE 02894 01 OF 02 311712Z

MOOD AS HE EXPOUNDED ON THE PROBLEMS HE EXPERIENCED IN

CONVINCING THE COMMERCIAL FARM OCCUPIERS TO PREPARE TO

VACATE THOSE PROPERTIES. ZVOGBO SAID THAT PRESIDENT

MUGABE HAD DIRECTED HIM AND MINISTER OF HOME AFFAIRS,

DUMISO DABENGWA, TO TALK TO THE OCCUPIERS ON FARMS IN

MASVINGO PROVINCE WHERE THERE HAD BEEN PROBLEMS AMONG

THE "WAR VETS" AND THE WHITE FARMERS, AND THEIR BLACK

FARM WORKERS. THE PRESIDENT HAD INSTRUCTED THE

MINISTERS TO INFORM THE OCCUPIERS THAT THEY MUST VACATE

THE FARMS BEGINNING ON MAY 29 NOW THAT THE GOVERNMENT

HAD ENACTED LEGISLATION THAT WOULD ALLOW FOR AN

EXPEDITED RESETTLEMENT PLAN (REFTEL).

3. (C) ZVOGBO SAID THE TWO MINISTERS, ACCOMPANIED BY

WAR VETS LEADER, CHENJERAI "HITLER" HUNZVI, WERE

UNSUCCESSFUL IN GETTING THE OCCUPIERS TO LISTEN TO THEIR

MESSAGE TO VACATE THE PROPERTIES. THE MINISTER SAID HE

"DETESTED" HUNZVI, THAT THE MAN WAS "CLEARLY CERTIFIABLY

UNSTABLE," AND THAT HE HAD DISRUPTED THE MINISTERS\'

SPEECHES AND FRUSTRATED THEIR MISSION BY INSTRUCTING THE

OCCUPIERS TO REMAIN ON THE LAND UNTIL THE PRESIDENT TOLD

THEM TO LEAVE. HUNZVI WILL, HOWEVER, BE DISPOSED OF BY

THE GOVERNMENT AS SOON AS THE JUNE 24-25 PARLIAMENTARY

ELECTION IS COMPLETED, THE MINISTER STATED AND WILL NOT

BE THE ZANU-PF CANDIDATE FOR THE CHIKOMBA CONSTITUENCY.

REPRESENTATIVES OF THE COMMERCIAL FARMERS UNION (CFU)

WITH WHOM THE AMBASSADOR MET ON MAY 30 ALSO OPINED THAT

HUNZVI WOULD LIKELY BE ARRESTED FOR ANY ONE OF THE

SEVERAL LEGAL CHARGES PENDING AGAINST HIM AS SOON AS HE

FINISHED SERVING THE RULING PARTY\'S REELECTION PURPOSES.

4. (C) DESPITE THESE DIFFICULTIES, ZVOGBO SAID THE WAR

VETS AND THEIR SUPPORTERS COULD BE REMOVED IN ABOUT FIVE

HOURS, AS SOON AS PRESIDENT MUGABE GAVE THE GO AHEAD TO

DO SO. THIS COULD BE DONE WITHOUT ANY MILITARY

INVOLVEMENT, MERELY EFFECTIVE POLICE ACTION, HE ADDED.

THE MINISTER ASSERTED THAT THE LAND ISSUE NEEDS TO BE

FINALLY RESOLVED AND THAT THE WAR VETS HAD ONLY FORCED

THE GOVERNMENT TO ACCELERATE ITS EFFORTS TO ACQUIRE

COMMERCIAL FARMS AND BEGIN TO RESETTLE PEOPLE. ONLY THE

GOVERNMENT, HOWEVER, IS EMPOWERED TO RESETTLE PEOPLE, HE

EMPHASIZED. THE MINISTER THEN STATED THAT THE PROBLEM

IS LESS COMPLEX THAN IMAGINED, AS THE GOVERNMENT DOES

NOT EVEN NEED ALL THE LAND IT HAS DESIGNATED FOR

RESETTLEMENT (841 FARMS) TO MEET THE DEMAND THAT EXISTS.

SOUTH AFRICAN PRESIDENT MBEKI IS FACING A SIMILAR LAND

DISTRIBUTION CRISIS AND SHOULD TAKE A LESSON FROM

ZIMBABWE AND NOT WAIT TOO LONG TO ADDRESS IT, ZVOGBO

STATED. THE MINISTER ALSO COMMENDED MBEKI FOR

REPORTEDLY WORKING TO HELP IDENTIFY POSSIBLE FINANCING

FOR ZIMBABWE\'S LAND REFORM PROGRAM.

5. (C) AMBASSADOR MCDONALD THEN PROBED THE GOVERNMENT\'S

ABILITY TO EFFECT AN ORDERLY LAND REFORM PROGRAM AFTER

HAVING ENCOURAGED, AND IN MANY CASES, FACILITATED THE

ILLEGAL LAND OCCUPATIONS OF THESE COMMERCIAL FARMS.

6. (C) THE MDC IS NO SERIOUS THREAT TO ZANU-PF, BUT

MERELY "BOYS" PLAYING POLITICS IN A MAN\'S WORLD,

ACCORDING TO ZVOGBO. THE MINISTER ESTIMATED THAT THE

MDC WOULD GET NO MORE THAN TEN OF THE 120 CONTESTED

PARLIAMENTARY SEATS. THE MDC WAS MOST LIKELY TO TAKE

SEATS IN BULAWAYO AND HARARE, ALTHOUGH ZVOGBO ASSERTED

THAT THE OPPOSITION PARTY WAS LOSING SUPPORT IN THE

LATTER AREA. ZVOGBO DOWNPLAYED THE WIDESPREAD POLITICAL

VIOLENCE THAT IS OCCURRING AND ARGUED THAT ZIMBABWE\'S

PRE-ELECTION VIOLENCE WAS MUCH LESS THAN WHAT TOOK PLACE

IN SOUTH AFRICA AND OTHER PLACES.

7. (C) AMBASSADOR MCDONALD STRESSED THAT THERE WERE

CLEARLY GOZ AND ZANU-PF ORGANIZED AND SYSTEMATIC HUMAN

RIGHTS VIOLATIONS TAKING PLACE IN THE COUNTRYSIDE,

TARGETING FARM WORKERS AND TEACHERS, AS ALLEGED

SUPPORTERS OF THE MDC. THE HIGHLY SUCCESSFUL PEACE

CORPS PROGRAM, WHICH PRIMARILY ASSISTED RURAL ZIMBABWEAN

SCHOOLS, WAS RECENTLY REDUCED BY TWO-THIRDS ITS

VOLUNTEER STAFF BECAUSE OF THE INSECURITY CREATED BY

THIS GOVERNMENT-CONDONED VIOLENCE, THE AMBASSADOR

INFORMED. THE MINISTER JUSTIFIED THIS VIOLENCE BY

NOTING THAT IN SOME COMMUNITIES PARENTS HAD COMPLAINED

THAT TEACHERS WERE OPENLY SUPPORTING MDC AND TEACHING

OPPOSITION SLOGANS IN THE CLASSROOMS. THE AMBASSADOR

RESPONDED THAT THE RURAL SCHOOLS AND ZIMBABWEAN CHILDREN

ARE MOST ADVERSELY AFFECTED BY THE THREATS AGAINST

TEACHERS AND OTHER CIVIL SERVANTS IN THE COUNTRYSIDE AND

THE GOVERNMENT HAD TO ASSUME RESPONSIBILITY FOR THIS

SITUATION AND ITS CONSEQUENCES. THE MINISTER WHILE

DEPLORING VIOLENCE THEN REITERATED HIS POSITION THAT

EVERYONE ELSE IN THE REGION HAS HAD WORSE ELECTIONS.

ZVOGBO CONCEDED, HOWEVER, THAT WHILE ZIMBABWE WOULD HAVE

A "FREE" ELECTION, IT WOULD NOT BE A "FAIR" ONE, WHICH

HE ATTRIBUTED TO THE RULING PARTY\'S NUMERICAL AND

INCUMBENCY ADVANTAGES.

8. (C) THE MINISTER FEIGNED OFFENSE THAT WE WOULD

QUESTION THE TRANSPARENCY AND INTEGRITY OF ZIMBABWE\'S

ELECTORAL PROCESS. HE NOTED THAT THE PRESIDENT HAD TOLD

SECRETARY ALBRIGHT THAT INTERNATIONAL ELECTION OBSERVERS

POSITION AS IT HAD NOTHING TO HIDE FROM THE WORLD.

-------

9. (C) ZVOGBO IS A FEISTY, INTELLIGENT, AND FRANK

SPEAKING INTERLOCUTOR WHO MAY NOT ALWAYS BELIEVE HIS OWN
