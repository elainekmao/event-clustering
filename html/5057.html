SUBJECT: EXTRANCHECK: PRELICENSE CHECK AL RAJIR GLASS

INDUSTRIES.

REF USDOC 06372 APPLICATION NUMBER D302980.

1. REFTEL REQUESTED A PRE-LICENSE CHECK TO DETERMINE THE

SUITABILITY OF AL RAJIR GLASS INDUSTRIES AS A RECIPIENT OF

US ORIGIN COMMODITIES. PLEASE NOTE THAT THE NAME OF THE

COMPANY MENTIONED IN THE ACTION CABLE IS NOT CORRECT AND THE

CORRECT NAME OF THE COMPANY IS AL TAJIR GLASS INDUSTRIES

LOCATED OFF HATTA ROAD, JEBEL ALI, UNITED ARAB EMIRATES.

2.COMMERCIAL OFFICER MARY O\'BRIEN AND COMMERCIAL RESEARCHER

SHEREEN DEGHEDY VISITED AL TAJIR GLASS INDUSTRIES ON

DECEMBER 30TH. COMMOFF MARY O\'BRIEN MET WITH MR. B.K.

CHETAL, DEPUTY GENERAL MANAGER (TECHNICAL). MR. CHETAL

EXPLAINED THAT THE CAMERA WOULD BE USED TO MEASURE THE

TEMPERATURE OF BOTTLES PRODUCED ON AL TAJIR\'S PRODUCTIN

LINE. THE INFORMATION PROVIDED BY THE CAMERA WILL ENHANCE

THE QUALITY OF THEIR PRODUCT BY EXAMINING THE TEMPERATURE

INSIDE THE BOTTLES DURING THE MANUFACTURING PROCESS.

3. MR. B.K. CHETAL, DEPUTY GENERAL MANAGER (TECHNICAL), GAVE

MARY O\'BRIEN A COPY OF:

a. INDUSTRIAL LICENSE FOR AL TAJIR GLASS INDUSTRIES ISSUED

FROM THE DEPARTMENT OF ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT, GOVERNMENT OF

DUBAI, UNITED ARAB EMIRATES.

b. STATEMENT OF THE ULTIMATE CONSIGNEE FORM SIGNED BY THE

MR. YOGESH BAKHSHI, GENERAL MANAGER OF AL TAJIR GLASS

INDUSTRIES.

c. RECENT CORRESPONDENCE BETWEEN OWENS-ILLINOIS INC. AND

YOGESH BAKHSHI, GENERAL MANAGER, OWENS-ILLINOIS INC.

REGARDING THE US EXPORT REGULATIONS.

d. THE ORDER PROPOSAL FROM MR. LYNN DEFFENDALL, GLOBAL

EQUIPMENT SALES, OWENS GLASS CONTAINER INC. ILLUSTRATING THE

PICTORIAL DEMONSTRATION FOR THE THERMAL IMAGING CAMERA.

THESE DOCUMENTS CAN BE IMAGED AND FORWARDED BY E-MAIL UPON

REQUEST.

4. MR. CHETAL SHOWED COMMOFF THE SITE PLANT, EXPLAINING THE

VARIOUS STAGES OF PRODUCTION, THE FACILITY IS LARGE WITH

SPACIOUS STORAGE AND PACKAGING AREA. THE TOUR TOOK AROUND 30

MINUTES. DURING THE TOUR, MR. CHETAL ILLUSTRATED THAT THE AL

TAJIR GLASS INDUSTRIES HAS THE CAPABILITIES TO PRODUCE OVER

400 BOTTLES PER MINUTE.

5. MR. B.K.CHETAL SAID THAT AL TAJIR GLASS INDUSTRIES IS

PLANNING TO EXPAND THE FACTORY. AL TAJIR GLASS INDUSTRIES

HAS TWO PRODUCTION LINES AND THEY WILL BUILD A THIRD

PRODUCTION LINE SOON. THEY HAVE CLIENTS IN MORE THAN 40

COUNTRIES AND PEPSI, COCA-COLA AND SEVEN-UP ARE THEIR

PRIMARY CUSTOMERS. MR. CHETAL HAS ALSO STATED THAT THERE ARE

TWO HUNDRED EMPLOYEES IN THE FACTORY.

6. MR. B.K. CHETAL IS FULLY AWARE OF US EXPORT REGULATIONS

AND HE UNDERSTANDS HIS OBLIGATIONS UNDER THE US EXPORT

REGULATIONS.

7. RECOMMENDATION: COMMOFF RECOMMENDS AL TAJIR GLASS

INDUSTRIES AS A RELIABLE RECIPIENT OF US-ORIGIN COMMODITIES

AND TECHNICAL DATA.
