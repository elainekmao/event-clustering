1. (S) IN RESPONSE TO QUESTIONS POSED IN REF A, ROLES AND

RESPONSIBILITIES OF THE U.N. WORLD FOOD PROGRAM (WFP) AND THE

SECRET

GOVERNMENT OF IRAQ\'S (GOI) MINISTRY OF TRANSPORT (MOT) ARE

FURTHER DEFINED AS THEY RELATE TO THE CURRENT FOOD

DISTRIBUTION SYSTEM. THERE IS NO/NO WFP FOOD DELIVERY AND

DISTRIBUTION NETWORK IN IRAQ. WFP PLAYS AN INTEGRAL ROLE IN

THE SYSTEM IN THE NORTH AND PROVIDES OVERSIGHT IN

SOUTH/CENTRAL, BUT IT IS A GOVERNMENT SYSTEM. ESTIMATES ARE

GIVEN FOR STOCKPILES INSIDE AND OUTSIDE THE COUNTRY, ALBEIT

SHARES VIEWS ON RE-ESTABLISHING THE CURRENT SYSTEM, IF IT

WERE DERAILED. SEE PARAS 12-16. END SUMMARY.

WFP RESPONSIBILITIES

---------------------

2. (S) AS REPORTED IN REF C, WFP PLAYS A MUCH GREATER ROLE

IN THE NORTH THAN IN THE SOUTH/CENTRAL REGIONS, BUT EVEN IN

THE NORTH, WFP IS NOT IN CONTROL OF THE ENTIRE OPERATION. IN

THE NORTH, WFP MANAGES THE EXTENDED DELIVERY POINT (EDP)

WAREHOUSES IN DAHUK, ARBIL, AND SULAYMANIAH, CONTRACTS FOR

THE TRUCKING FROM THE TRANSIT WAREHOUSES IN MOSUL AND KIRKUK

TO THE NORTH AND THE TRUCKS FROM THE EDPS TO THE FOOD/FLOUR

AGENTS, AND MAINTAINS THE DATABASE OF BENEFICIARIES. AS IT

DOES IN SOUTH/CENTRAL, WFP ALSO OBSERVES ALL LOADING OF

COMMODITIES, CHECKS THE BOOKS OF THE FOOD/FLOUR AGENTS,

OVERSEES THE DISTRIBUTIONS, AND CONDUCTS POST-DISTRIBUTION

MONITORING IN THE HOUSEHOLDS. IN NONE/NONE OF THE COUNTRY,

DOES WFP DECIDE THE DATES OF THE DISTRIBUTIONS, MAKE TENDERS

FOR FOOD PURCHASES, CONTRACT OUTSIDE TRANSPORT, PLAY ANY ROLE

SECRET

PAGE 04 ROME 06153 01 OF 05 301327Z

IN THE MILLING OPERATION (OTHER THAN OVERSEEING IT), WORK

WITH ANY NON-GOVERNMENTAL ORGANIZATIONS (NGOS) FOR THE

GENERAL DISTRIBUTION, DO ANY DISTRIBUTIONS--EVEN IN THE

NORTH, OR MANAGE THE FOOD PIPELINE. THE MOT PERFORMS ALL THE

TASKS THAT WFP DOES NOT.

3. (C) THE MOT SERVES AS THE INTERNATIONAL PROCUREMENT AGENT

FOR THE ENTIRE COUNTRY AND ARRANGES FOR THE INTERNATIONAL

TRANSPORT OF COMMODITIES. IT ARRANGES FOR THE TRANSPORT AND

MAINTAINS THE DATABASE OF BENEFICIARIES IN THE CENTRAL/SOUTH,

AND KEEPS TABS ON THE BENEFICIARIES IN THE NORTH AS IT

CALCULATES HOW MUCH FOOD IS SENT TO THE NORTH MONTHLY BASED

ON THE BENEFICIARY FIGURES. IT ALSO MAINTAINS THE

WAREHOUSES, FLOUR MILLS, AND SILOS, AND SERVES AS THE OVERALL

PLANNING CENTER, INCLUDING THE MANAGEMENT OF THE PIPELINE.

LOGISTICS

-----------

6. (S) QUESTION 3.3. PART ONE. WHAT ASSESTS/FACILITIES

DOES THE UN OWN IN IRAQ THAT ARE USED BY WFP? WHAT

IRAQI-OWNED FACILITIES/ASSETS ARE USED FOR WFP? COULD

REPLACEMENTS BE FOUND? THE LOCAL AUTHORITIES OR THE MOT OWN

EVERYTHING THAT WFP USES EXCEPT FOR WFP\'S STAFF VEHICLES AND

COMPUTERS AND OTHER GENERAL OFFICE EQUIPMENT. WFP REPORTS

THAT THE GOI IS THE ONLY INSTITUTION THAT HAS WAREHOUSES.

SECRET

PAGE 03 ROME 06153 03 OF 05 301329Z

WFP COULD, OF COURSE, CONSTRUCT ITS OWN--IN A DIFFERENT

ENVIRONMENT OR UNDER A DIFFERENT SYSTEM. THE TRUCKS FOR FOOD

TRANSPORT ARE RENTED COMMERCIALLY IN THE NORTH.

7. QUESTION 3.3. PART TWO. IS ACCESS TO THE WFP DELIVERY

AND DISTRIBUTION NETWORK, WAREHOUSES, AND FOOD/FLOUR AGENTS

LIKELY TO BE AVAILABLE TO INTERNATIONAL HUMANITARIAN AGENCIES

IN A POST-CONFLICT ENVIRONMENT? AS STATED IN REF TELS,

THERE IS NO/NO WFP DELIVERY AND DISTRIBUTION NETWORK. THERE

IS ONLY THE MOT DISTRIBUTION SYSTEM, TO WHICH WFP PROVIDES

OVERSIGHT IN SOUTH/CENTRAL, AND PLAYS A MORE INTEGRAL ROLE IN

THE NORTH. PLEASE SEE PARA 2 ABOVE. THE WAREHOUSE SPACE

BEING USED BY WFP IN THE NORTH IS MINIMAL, SO THERE IS NO

UNUSED SPACE AVAILABLE TO OTHER INTERNATIONAL HUMANITARIAN

AGENCIES. WFP, HOWEVER, COULD CONSTRUCT TEMPORARY

WAREHOUSES, I.E. RUBB HALLS, AS IT DOES IN PLACES AROUND THE

WORLD THAT COULD BE SHARED WITH OTHERS. PERHAPS, OTHERS

COULD USE THE FOOD/FLOUR AGENTS TO DISTRIBUTE NON-FOOD ITEMS,

HOWEVER IT SHOULD BE REMEMBERED THAT THE AGENTS ARE SMALL

SHOP OWNERS, SO THEY DO NOT HAVE A LOT OF STORAGE SPACE.

PORT UMM QASR

8. (S) QUESTION 4.1. APPROXIMATELY HOW MANY WFP PERSONNEL

WORK AT THE UMM QASR FACILITY? WHAT ARE THE NUMBERS AND

DISTRIBTION OF PERSONNEL, INTERNATIONAL VERSUS NATIONAL,

CROSS-REFERENCED BY ROLE. COULD OTHER PERSONNEL FILL IN FOR

THE UN IF NEEDED? WFP REPORTS THAT NO/NO WFP PERSONNEL WORK

IN THE PORT UMM QASR. TOTECNA, THE AGENCY THAT OVERSEES THE

IMPORTS UNDER THE OIL FOR IRAQ PROGRAM (OIP), HAS A COMPOUND

AT THE PORT WHERE ABOUT 15 STAFF ARE POSTED. ALL OTHER

PERSONNEL AT THE PORT THAT ARE INVOLVED IN HE OIL FOR FOOD

(OFF) ARE EMPLOYEES OF THE MOT.

9. (S) QUESTION 4.2 HOW DO THE BULK OF THE IRAQ NATIONAL

WORKING WITH WFP GET PAID? WHAT IS TH WAGE FOR A PORT

WORKER, TRUCK DRIVER, CRANE OPEATOR, ETC.? WFP\'S NATIONAL

STAFF GET PAID IN USDOLLARS, IN CASH, ON A MONTHLY BASIS.

WFP\'S BAGHAD OFFICE SENDS THE STAFF HOURS TO WFP\'S REGIONALOFFICE IN CAIR

O. WFP/CAIRO SENDS THE REQUEST FOR UNDS TO

OIP. OIP DISBURSES THE MONEY TO U.N. DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM

(UNDP) IN BAGHDAD, WHO IN TURN GIVES THE DOLLARS TO THE

WFP/BAGHDAD. SINCE WFP HAS NO NATIONAL STAFF AT THE PORT,

NO AVERAGE WAGES CAN BE PROVIDED FOR PORT STAFF. THE

FUNCTIONS MENTIONED ABOVE ARE FILLED BY THE MOT.

10. (S)QUESTION 4.3. WOULD IRAQIS BE LIKELY TO CONTINUE TO

WORK IN THE ABSENCE OF THE UN IF THEY CONTINUED TO GET PAID?

YES, WFP THINKS THEY WOULD, TO THE EXTENT THEY WERE ABLE TO

DO SO.

VIEWS ON RE-ESTABLISHING THE CURRENT SYSTEM

---------------------------------------------

13. (S) THE KEY TO RE-STARTING THE CURRENT FOOD DISTRIBUTION

SYSTEM, IF THE ENTIRE OR PARTS OF THE SYSTEM ARE DERAILED, IS

THE MOT AND THE WFP NATIONAL STAFF. AS STATED IN REF B AND

REITERATED IN PARA 3 ABOVE, THE MOT PLAYS A TREMENDOUS ROLE

IN THE SYSTEM, FROM START TO FINISH. IF POSSIBLE AT THIS

STAGE, U.S. MISSION RECOMMENDS THE USG CONTEMPLATE HOW BEST

TO CONTACT THESE INDIVIDUALS IF THERE IS A REGIME CHANGE, IN

ORDER TO ASK/ENCOURAGE THEM TO RESUME THEIR PRIOR DUTIES.

THESE STAFF KNOW THEIR JOBS WELL, AND AS IT IS DESCRIBED, THE

CURRENT SYSTEM IS A WELL OILED-MACHINE THAT REQUIRES MINIMUM

DAILY MAINTENANCE TO KEEP IT GOING, BUT THE MOT STAFF ARE

KEY.

14. (S) WE WOULD RECOMMEND THAT WFP ADAPT THE SAME DUTIES FOR

THE SOUTH/CENTRAL REGION THAT IT CURRENTLY ASSUMES IN THE

NORTH, WITH SOME ADDITIONAL ONES. THE MOT STAFF COULD

CONTINUE THEIR SAME DUTIES BUT WITH CLOSER OVERISIGHT AND
