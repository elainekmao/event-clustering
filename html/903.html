SUBJECT: MORE ON THE LTTE ATTACK ON

US-CHARTERED MV CORDIALITY - A NEW LTTE

TACTIC?

REF: COLOMBO 03477

1. (U) CLASSIFIED BY POLITICAL COUNSELOR

ANDREW MANN. REASON: 1.5 (D).

2. (C) THE OWNER OF THE COMPANY HIRED TO

SALVAGE MV CORDIALITY WHICH WAS ATTACKED IN

THE EARLY MORNING HOURS ON SEPTEMBER 9 (SEE

REFTEL) HAS PROVIDED ADDITIONAL DETAILS OF

THE ATTACK. THE SHIP HAD BEEN LOADING

ILMENITE FOR OVER A WEEK, USING LARGE

NUMBERS OF LOCAL WORKERS. IT HELD ALMOST

29,000 TONS OF AN ESTIMATED 30,000 TON LOAD

AT THE TIME OF THE ATTACK. THE SHIP WAS

SURROUNDED BY A NUMBER OF SRI LANKA NAVAL

CRAFT.

3. (C) EVIDENTLY, THE LTTE TOOK ADVANTAGE

OF AN UNSECURED GANGPLANK TO GAIN ENTRY

ONTO THE SHIP QUICKLY. THEY THREW GRENADES

INTO THE ENGINE ROOM CAUSING EXTENSIVE

DAMAGE. THE SALVAGE COMPANY OWNER SAID THE

LTTE TRIED TO TAKE SOME OF THE CHINESE CREW

MEMBERS HOSTAGE. IT IS NOT KNOWN IF IT WAS

SUCCESSFUL. FIVE CREW MEMBERS ARE STILL

MISSING.

4. (C) FIRE FROM THE GRENADE ATTACK

SPREAD TO SOME OF THE LIVING QUARTERS. THE

FIRES WERE DOUSED BY SLN FORCES RESPONDING

TO THE ATTACK, BUT THE ENGINE ROOM WAS

EFFECTIVELY DESTROYED. DUE TO THE AMOUNT

OF WATER USED TO COMBAT THE FLAMES, THE

STERN NOW SITS LOWER IN THE WATER THAN THE

BOW. DURING THE ATTACK, RPG\'S HAD RIPPED

THROUGH THE SIDE OF THE SHIP BUT ABOVE THE

WATER LINE. THE SHIP WAS/IS NOT IN DANGER

OF SINKING. THE SHIP IS TOWABLE TO EITHER

TRINCOMALEE OR COLOMBO DEPENDING UPON THE

DETERMINATION OF THE INSURANCE COMPANY.

5. (U) IN A FAX FROM ITS LONDON OFFICE,

THE LTTE JUSTIFIED ITS ATTACK ON MV

CORDIALITY: "THE ROBBERY OF OUR NATURAL

RESOURCES BY THE RELENTLESS ENEMY CANNOT BE

PERMITTED." IT ARGUED THE ILMENITE WAS A

NATURAL RESOURSE OF TAMIL EELAM, BEING

"SYSTEMATICALLY PLUNDERED" BY THE SINHALA

GOVERNMENT TO BE SOLD ABROAD WITH THE

PROCEEDS USED TO PURCHASE ARMS AGAINST THE

TAMIL PEOPLE.

6. (U) PERHAPS IN RESPONSE TO THE USG

JULY 25 STATEMENT CONDEMNING LTTE

INTERFERENCE WITH INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING,

THE LTTE SAID ITS ATTACK "SHOULD NOT BE

CONSTRUED AS AN ACT OF HOSTILITY DIRECTED

TOWARDS ANY PARTICULAR TRADE OR SHIPPING

ORGANIZATION. NEITHER SHOULD IT BE

INFERRED AS AN ACT PREJUDICIAL TO

INTERNATIONAL TRADE OR SHIPPING." THE LTTE

STATEMENT EXPRESSED REGRET FOR THE LOSS OF

LIFE, BUT THE ORGANIZATION, IT CLAIMED, HAS

COMPELLED TO ACT IN ORDER TO PREVENT THE

REMOVAL AND SALE OF EELAM RESOURCES AND TO

"BEAT THE WAR STRATEGY" OF THE GSL.

7. (U) COMMENT. THE LTTE HAS PREVIOUSLY

ATTACKED FOREIGN-FLAGGED COMMERCIAL VESSELS

(MAY 97 ATTACK ON GREEK CARGO SHIP OUTSIDE

TRINCOMALEE; AUGUST 96 ATTACK OF

JAPANESE-OWNED, PHILIPPINE-FLAGGED CARGO

SHIP IN PULMODDAI - NEAR THE SITE OF THE

RECENT ATTACK (THE LTTE REFERRED TO THIS

ATTACK IN ITS FAX); APRIL 96 ATTACK ON

COLOMBO HARBOR LEAVING THREE CARGO SHIPS

SLIGHTLY DAMAGED) IN ADDITION TO ITS JULY

ATTACKS ON AN INDONESIA-FLAGGED PASSENGER

FERRY AND A NORTH KOREAN CARGO SHIP. THE

LTTE HAD ALSO SAID IN THE PAST ANY VESSEL

(FOREIGN-OWNED OR SRI LANKAN) IN "TAMIL

TERRITORIAL WATERS" ENGAGED IN "ACTIVITIES

ASSOCIATED WITH THE SRI LANKAN MILITARY"

WILL BE CONSIDERED A LEGITIMATE TARGET.

8. (C) THIS ATTACK IS WORRISOME FOR TWO

REASONS. FIRST, THE LTTE APPEARS WILLING

TO ENGAGE A MAJOR POWER. OUR SOURCE SAID

IT WAS NOT POSSIBLE NOT TO HAVE KNOWN THE

SHIP WAS CHINESE-OWNED AFTER A WEEK AT

ANCHOR. SECONDLY, THE ECONOMIC

JUSTIFICATION USED BY THE LTTE COULD PUT

ALL ECONOMIC ACTIVITY IN THE NORTH EAST AT

RISK. THIS ATTACK MAY SIGNAL THE GSL\'S

EFFORTS TO TAKE THE WAR TO THE LTTE IS

HAVING SOME SUCCESS IN SQUEEZING IT. THE

LTTE MAY BE LASHING OUT TO CAUSE THE

GOVERNMENT TO DIVERT RESOURCES TO PROTECT

ECONOMIC ACTIVITY, THUS GIVING THE LTTE A

BREAK ON THE GROUND AND SEA. END COMMENT.
