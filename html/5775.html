SUBJECT: Turkey Officially Suspends New Quarantine Regs

Ref:(A) 2002 Ankara 9192 (B) 2003 Ankara 441 (C)Ankara 1035

1. Turkey officially announced the temporary suspension

until July 7, 2003 of new phytosanitary regulations that had

been implemented on January 3, 2003.(Refs A&B) The

announcement was published in the "Official Gazette" on

Tuesday, February 18. The suspension will be retroactive to

January 3, 2003. USDA Quarantine officials had been informed

of the proposed suspension during discussions in Ankara at

the end of January (Ref C).
