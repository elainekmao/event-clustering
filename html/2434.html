SUMMARY

-------

1. (SBU) WHEN THE AMBASSADOR CALLED ON FINANCE MINISTER

GIULIO TREMONTI, HE PRAISED ITALIAN ACTION IN FREEZING

TERRORIST ASSETS, INQUIRED HOW ECONOMIC REFORM WAS

PROGRESSING AND MADE A STRONG ARGUMENT THAT THE JOINT STRIKE

FIGHTER WAS IN ITALY\'S TECHNOLOGICAL AND COMMERCIAL

INTERESTS. TREMONTI WAS PLEASED WITH THE WAY THE

INTER-MINISTERIAL FINANCIAL SECURITY COMMITTEE WAS WORKING TO

IDENTIFY AND FREEZE TERRORIST ASSETS, DESCRIBED HIS PROGRESS

AND PROBLEMS WITH IMPLEMENTING ECONOMIC REFORM, PARTICULARLY

IN THE AREAS OF TAX AND PENSION REFORM, FRETTED ABOUT

FINANCING PUBLIC DEBT IN A EURO CAPITAL MARKET, AND WAS

INTERESTED IN OUR ARGUMENT SUPPORTING THE JSF. END SUMMARY.

----------------

WAR ON TERRORISM

----------------

2. (C) AMBASSADOR SEMBLER PAID A COURTESY CALL ON FINANCE

MINISTER GIULIO TREMONTI WEDNESDAY, JANUARY 23. ALSO PRESENT

WERE U/S DOMINICO SINISCALCO AND DIPLOMATIC REPRESENTATIVE

GUGLIERMO ARDIZZONI. IN RESPONSE TO THE AMBASSADOR\'S

EXPRESSED APPRECIATION FOR THE HIGH LEVEL OF ITALIAN

COOPERATION AND ENFORCEMENT OF THE FINANCIAL WAR ON

TERRORISM, TREMONTI SAID THAT HE WAS PLEASED WITH THE

OPERATION OF THE NEWLY ESTABLISHED FINANCIAL SECURITY

COMMITTEE. HE PRAISED IT AND ITS BI-MONTHLY MEETINGS, AS

FRUITFUL, EFFICIENT, WORKING IN CLOSE COOPERATION, AND

CAPABLE OF EFFECTIVELY MONITORING LISTS FROM US AND THE E.U.,

BUT ALSO CAPABLE OF GENERATING ITS OWN LISTS. (COMMENT: WE

FIND THIS IS A RARE EXAMPLE OF A STRONG, FUNCTIONING

INTER-MINISTERIAL COMMITTEE. MUCH OF ITS OPERATIONAL SUCCESS

BELONGS TO TREMONTI AND SINISCALCO. END COMMENT). TREMONTI

AND SINISCALCO NOTED THAT ACTUAL PERFORMANCE IS EVEN BETTER

THAN IT APPEARS BECAUSE THERE ARE ACTUALLY TWO LISTS. ONE IS

THE LEGAL LIST, WHICH HAS JURIDICAL DIMENSIONS, AND ONE IS AN

OPERATIONAL LIST FOR THE LAW ENFORCEMENT SERVICES WHICH

ALLOWS A WIDER HORIZON OF SURVEILLANCE AND INVESTIGATIVE

ACTIVITY.

THE ECONOMY

-----------

3. (SBU) IN RESPONSE TO A QUESTION ON THE ECONOMY, TREMONTI

THEN PRESENTED A LIST, SHOWING THE STATUS OF ACTION ON ALL OF

THE PRIME MINISTER\'S FINANCIAL CAMPAIGN PROMISES. EVERY ONE

HAD BEEN ENACTED INTO LAW (E.G., REFTEL) EXCEPT THE REFORM OF

THE TAX LAW AND OF THE PENSION SYSTEM. THESE ARE TWO OF THE

MOST DIFFICULT AREAS, AND ONES THAT LIE OUTSIDE OF THE SOLE

CONTROL OF THE MINISTRY. THEY EXPECT TO MAKE PROGRESS IN

THESE TWO AREAS, ALTHOUGH THE UNEXPECTEDLY LARGE DEFICIT THAT

THE TREMONTI GROUP HAD FOUND WHEN THEY ARRIVED IN OFFICE HAS

MADE TAX REFORM MUCH MORE DIFFICULT. (COMMENT: PREVAILING

PRIMARILY FROM CAPITAL GAINS, ALSO HAS PUT PRESSURE ON THE

GOI\'S COFFERS).

4. (SBU) TAX REFORM, HOWEVER, IS A HIGH PRIORITY BECAUSE IT

WAS CENTRAL TO THE BERLUSCONI CAMPAIGN. TAX REFORM IS A

PROMISE THAT BERLUSCONI IS COMMITTED TO KEEPING. MANAGING

THE BUDGET AND THE DEFICIT (AND INDIRECTLY THE TIMING OF TAX

REFORM) DEPENDS ON THREE THINGS: THE COMMISSION, THE ITALIAN

ECONOMY AND ITALY\'S OWN CONTROL (I.E., LEGISLATION,

DEVOLUTION OF EXPENSES TO THE REGIONS, ETC.)). EXPENSES FOR

AFGHANISTAN WERE NOT OVERWHELMING AND COULD BE MANAGED.

LEGALLY, TAX CUTS MUST BE COVERED BY OTHER MEANS. (NOTE:

RECOVERY CANNOT TECHNICALLY BE SPREAD OUT OVER TIME, BUT MUST

OCCUR IN THE SAME TAX YEAR.) THIS HAS BLOCKED A CERTAIN

AMOUNT OF PUBLIC SPENDING. TREMONTI REMAINED CONFIDENT THAT

IT COULD BE DONE HOWEVER, SAYING THAT "SINCE 1992, WE HAVE

DROPPED MANY POINTS IN THE DEFICIT/GDP RATIO; WE ARE ONLY

TALKING ONE OR PERHAPS 1.5 PERCENTAGE POINTS NOW". (COMMENT:

THE GOI OFFICIALLY MAINTAINS THAT THE DEFICIT WILL BE 1.1

PERCENT IN 2001. WE BELIEVE THAT WHEN FOURTH QUARTER RESULTS

ARE IN, THE DEFICIT WILL BE CLOSER TO 1.3 PERCENT.)

5. (SBU) NONETHELESS, TREMONTI IS LOOKING FORWARD TO

STARTING THE TAX REFORM DISCUSSION. HE ANTICIPATES A NEW

FISCAL FEDERALISM, ALTHOUGH HE WAS NOT SURE WHEN THAT WOULD

BE. THE FIRST STEP WILL BE A SIMPLIFICATION OF THE MULTIPLE,

THE EURO

--------

6. (SBU) ON THE INSTITUTION OF THE EURO, TREMONTI SAID THERE

WAS NO IMPACT OPERATIONALLY. THINGS WERE GOING WELL.

HOWEVER, THE PUBLIC DEBT IS HIGH AND NOW FOR THE FIRST TIME

THE COST OF FINANCING THE PUBLIC DEBT BECOMES MUCH MORE

TRANSPARENT. THE FINANCING RATE BECOMES CRITICAL, BUT HAVING

A SINGLE CURRENCY SHOULD MAKE THE PROCESS MORE COMPETITIVE

AND MORE EFFECTIVE. TREMONTI\'S CONCERN IS THAT ITALY STAY

WITHIN THE MAASTRICHT TREATY GUIDELINES. STATING THAT THE

ITALIAN GDP WAS DIRECTLY LINKED TO BOTH THE GLOBAL AND U.S.

ECONOMIES, TREMONTI WAS MUCH MORE INTERESTED IN A POTENTIAL

US ECONOMIC RECOVERY.

--------------------

JOINT STRIKE FIGHTER

--------------------

7. (C) THE AMBASSADOR MADE A CLEAR EXPLANATION OF THE

BENEFITS TO ITALY ON JSF PARTICIPATION, PARTICULARLY AT A

FULL LEVEL TWO COMMITMENT. HE EXPLAINED THE INDUSTRIAL

PARTICIPATION AS WELL AS THE RETURN ON JSF SALES TO ITALY AS

A PARTNER. THE AMBASSADOR EMPHASIZED THAT THE INDUSTRIAL

PARTICIPATION WAS NOT JUST FOR ONE COMPANY BUT COULD INCLUDE

COMPANIES ACROSS ITALY. HE MADE CLEAR THAT ITALIAN

PARTICIPATION WOULD BE WELCOME, PARTICULARLY IN THE DESIGN

STAGE, AS AN AREA OF ITALIAN STRENGTH AND THAT LOCKHEED IS

AWARE OF MANY SMALL AND MEDIUM SIZE COMPANIES THAT WOULD BE

COMPETITIVE FOR CONTRACTS. HE ALSO MENTIONED THE LOOMING

DECISION DEADLINE AS REAL BECAUSE LOCKHEED IS CONTRACTUALLY

OBLIGATED TO THE USG TO ASSIGN CONTRACTS (THE POINT OF

INTENSE ITALIAN INTEREST) IN THE NEAR FUTURE. (COMMENT. THE

AMBASSADOR MADE THE SMALL AND MEDIUM SIZE INDUSTRY POINTS

BECAUSE WE HAVE FOR SOME TIME BELIEVED THAT ALENIA\'S GIORGIO

ZAPPA, WHO IS NEGOTIATING FOR ALL OF ITALIAN INDUSTRY, IS NOT

REPRESENTING ANYONE\'S INTERESTS BUT ALENIA\'S. THUS, WE

BELIEVE THAT THE GOVERNMENT OVERALL IS LESS THAN FULLY

KNOWLEDGEABLE OF ALL THE BENEFITS THAT MIGHT ACCRUE TO ITALY

FROM THE JSF.)

8. (C) TREMONTI SAID THAT THE DECISION WAS GOING TO BE MADE

BY BERLUSCONI. FINANCE\'S ONLY RESPONSIBILITY IS "WE WILL

PAY". HE DID NOT PROFFER ANY VIEWS ON THE DIFFERENCES

BETWEEN A DECISION TO BUY-IN AT ONE BILLION VERSUS EIGHT

HUNDRED MILLION DOLLARS. NOR DID THEY HAVE THE DETAILS.

DESPITE SAYING THAT FINANCE WILL PAY, TREMONTI WAS EXTREMELY

INTERESTED IN THE MECHANICS: WOULD THE GOVERNMENT BE ABLE TO

WRITE THE MONEY FOR JSF INTO AN INVESTMENT CATEGORY, AS AN

ACQUISITION OF EQUITY, OR WOULD IT HAVE TO BE LISTED AS AN

EXPENDITURE? WOULD THE MONEY GO TO ITALIAN FIRMS, THE USG OR

US CORPORATIONS? (COMMENT: WE WILL APPROACH MOD ABOUT

PROVIDING INFORMATION TO MOF OR TO INCLUDE THEM (AS WELL AS

THE MINISTRY OF PRODUCTIVE ACTIVITIES, AND PERHAPS THE
