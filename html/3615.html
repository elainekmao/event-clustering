1. (C) CHARGE FOLLOWED UP ARTICLE 98 DEMARCHE (REFS) IN HIS

INITIAL CALL ON PRIME (AND FOREIGN) MINISTER BERLUSCONI\'S

DIPLOMATIC ADVISER, GIANNI CASTELLANETA, AUGUST 30.

CASTELLANETA EXPRESSED APPRECIATION FOR THE INFORMATION

ALREADY PROVIDED, FROM WHICH HE HAD DRAWN IN WORKING OUT THE

GOI\'S APPROACH WITH THE MFA\'S LEGAL ADVISER. HE REAFFIRMED

THE GOI VIEW THAT THERE SHOULD BE NO ACTION TAKEN AT THE AUG.

30-31 GYMNICH MEETING THAT WOULD PREJUDGE THE EU\'S DECISION.

HE DESCRIBED IN DETAIL THE PERSONAL EFFORT AT COORDINATION

WITH THE UK THAT HE HAD MADE WITH THE UK EMBASSY IN ROME.

2. (C) CASTELLANETA NOTED THAT THE MFA\'S LEGAL ADVISERS HAD

IDENTIFIED ONE POTENTIAL PROBLEM WITH THE DRAFT BILATERAL

AGREEMENT PROVIDED TO THE GOI: THE APPLICATION OF THE

AGREEMENT TO AMERICAN CITIZENS NOT ACTING IN AN OFFICIAL

CAPACITY. CHARGE NOTED THAT THE USG DID NOT BELIEVE THERE

WAS ANY OBSTACLE IN THE STATUTE OF ROME TO INCLUDING ALL

CITIZENS OF A COUNTRY IN SUCH AN AGREEMENT, AND WASHINGTON

WOULD STRONGLY PREFER THAT ANY AGREEMENT NOT BE SO LIMITED.

CASTELLANETA SAID THE GOI WAS CONCERNED THAT THERE BE NO

BARRIER FOR EXTRADITION IN A CASE SUCH AS THAT OF JOHN WALKER

LINDH, THE US CITIZEN WHO FOUGHT WITH THE TALIBAN.

3. (C) CASTELLANETA POINTED US TO THAT MORNING\'S ISSUE OF IL

FOGLIO, THE INFLUENTIAL DAILY. HE SAID THAT HE HAD BRIEFED

EDITOR GIULIO FERRARA EXTENSIVELY ON THE GOI\'S APPROACH TO

ARTICLE 98, AND THAT THE FRONT-PAGE ARTICLE "IS OUR

PRESENTATION OF THE ARTICLE 98 ISSUE WE HAVE YET SEEN IN THE

FIRST TO EXPLORE THE POSSIBILITY OF A COOPERATIVE EU-US

APPROACH TO THE SUBJECT, AND IS ACTIVELY COORDINATING WITH

LIKE-MINDED MEMBERS, WHICH ARE LISTED AS THE UK, PORTUGAL,

SPAIN AND THE NETHERLANDS. EMBASSY WILL FORWARD TRANSLATION

OF ARTICLE SHORTLY.)

OPERATION ENDURING FREEDOM

4. (C) THE CHARGE NOTED THE DEEP RESPECT THE US HAD FOR THE

COMBAT CAPABILITY OF THE ITALIAN ARMY\'S ALPINI UNIT, AND

ALERTED CASTELLANETA THAT A FORMAL REQUEST WOULD SOON BE

FORTHCOMING FOR A SIGNIFICANT NUMBER OF "ALPINI-LIKE" TROOPS

TO BE DEPLOYED TO OEF. AS CASTELLANETA WAS AWARE, CENTCOM

AND ITALIAN MILITARY OFFICIALS HAD WORKED OUT A MUTUALLY

ACCEPTABLE DEFINITION OF THE MISSION AND DEPLOYMENT

TIMEFRAME. CASTELLANETA AFFIRMED THAT PM BERLUSCONI WOULD

INFORM THE PARLIAMENT WHEN IT RESUMES WORK IN SEPTEMBER OF

THE INTENTION TO DEPLOY, BUT WOULD NOT NEED TO SEEK THE

PARLIAMENT\'S AUTHORIZATION.
