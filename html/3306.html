SUBJECT: JORDAN: MEDIA REACTION ON IRAQ, THE

QUADRIPARTITE MEETING

SUMMARY

NEWS REPORTS IN JORDAN\'S DAILIES ON JULY 15

CONCENTRATED ON THE IRAQI SITUATION, QUOTING THE PRIME

MINISTER\'S REAFFIRMATION THAT JORDAN REJECTS MILITARY

ACTION AGAINST IRAQ, AND DENIAL OF THE EXISTENCE OF

U.S. BASES IN JORDAN. REPORTS ALSO COVERED THE IRAQI

OFFICERS\' MEETING IN LONDON, THE SUNDAY TELEGRAPH\'S

REPORT FORESEEING THAT BRITISH FORCES WOULD OCCUPY IRAQ

FOR FIVE YEARS, AND A/S WOLFOWITZ\'S STATEMENT THAT

THERE WOULD NOT BE A KURDISH STATE IN NORTHERN IRAQ.

ON THE QUADRIPARTITE MEETING, SEMI-OFFICIAL INFLUENTIAL

ARABIC DAILY AL-RAI REPORTED THAT THE U.S. WOULD SUBMIT

A PLAN TO LIMIT THE POWERS OF ARAFAT.

EDITORIAL WRITERS COMMENTED ON THE QUADRIPARTITE

MEETING AND WHAT THEY CONCLUDED ID THE U.S. WAR ON

ISLAM.

NEWS REPORTS

ALL JORDANIAN DAILIES ON JULY 15 REPORTED PRIME

MINISTER ALI ABUL RAGHEB\'S STATEMENT MADE AFTER HIS

MEETING WITH ARAB LEAGUE SECRETARY-GENERAL AMR MUSSA,

IN WHICH HE REAFFIRMED THAT JORDAN REJECTS A MILITARY

STRIKE AGAINST IRAQ, AND THAT THERE ARE NO U.S. BASES

IN JORDAN.

JORDANIAN DAILIES ALSO REPORTED THE MEETING OF IRAQI

OFFICERS IN LONDON, WHICH FORMED A MILITARY COUNCIL TO

LEAD CHANGE IN IRAQ. CENTER-LEFT, INFLUENTIAL ARABIC

DAILY AL-DUSTOUR LED WITH THE REPORT FROM THE SUNDAY

TELEGRAPH QUOTING "LEAKS" THAT BRITISH FORCES WOULD

OCCUPY IRAQ FOR FIVE YEARS. INDEPENDENT, MASS-APPEAL

ARABIC DAILY AL-ARAB AL-YAWM REPORTED ON THE FRONT PAGE

AS WOLFOWITZ\'S STATEMENT THAT THERE WOULD NOT BE A

KURDISH STATE IN THE NORTH OF IRAQ.

SEMI-OFFICIAL INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-RAI REPORTED

AN AMERICAN PLAN TO BE SUBMITTED TO THE QUADRIPARTITE

MEETING IN WASHINGTON, WHICH AIMS TO LIMIT THE POWERS

OF ARAFAT.

EDITORIAL COMMENTARIES

-- WILL THE QUADRIPARTITE MEETING BECOME A QUINTET?

DAILY COLUMNIST MAHMOUD AL-RIMAWI WROTE IN SEMI-

OFFICIAL INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-RAI ON JULY 15:

"INTERNATIONAL PARTIES, INCLUDING RUSSIA, ARE WORRIED

THAT THE QUADRIPARTITE COMMITTEE MAY COLLAPSE BECAUSE

OF THE ENORMOUS DIFFERENCES BETWEEN THE U.S. AND THE

OTHER MEMBERS. HOWEVER, THESE DIFFERENCES, NO MATTER

HOW SHARP, ARE BETTER THAT WASHINGTON SUCCEEDING IN

FORCING ITS WILL ON RUSSIA AND/ OR EUROPE. THIS

HIGHLIGHTS THE IMPORTANCE OF THE ARAB PARTICIPATION IN

THE COMMITTEE FOR TWO REASONS: THE FIRST IS TO AFFIRM

THE IMPORTANCE OF INTERNATIONAL POSITIONS ON THE

CONFLICT, AND THE SECOND TO RESTORE SOME DIALOGUE

BETWEEN THE U.S. STATE DEPARTMENT AND THE REST OF THE

U.S. ADMINISTRATION, WHICH HAS COMMITTED ITSELF TO THE

LETTER OF THE ISRAELI AGENDA, THUS MAKING THE U.S. A

PARTNER IN THE OCCUPATION."

-- AMERICAN REFORMS BETWEEN DICTATORSHIPS, TERRORISM,

AND CORRUPTION

COLUMNIST ARAFAT HIJAZI WROTE A LENGTHY COMMENTARY IN

CENTER-LEFT, INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR ON

JULY 15 IN WHICH HE SAID: "THE U.S. POSES AS THE SELF-

APPOINTED GUARDIAN OF HUMAN RIGHTS IN THE WORLD, WHICH

IS PROMPTED BY HUMANITARIAN REASONS TO LIBERATE THE

DOWNTRODDEN PEOPLE FROM THEIR CORRUPT AND VILE

DICTATORS, ALL OF WHOM HAPPEN TO BE ARABS OR MUSLIMS.

BUSH THE FATHER COULD NOT FIND A SINGLE DICTATOR IN THE

WORLD EXCEPT IRAQ, SO HE DESTROYED THE IRAQI POWDERED

MILK FACTORY, THE AMIRIYAH SHELTER WHERE WOMEN AND

CHILDREN WERE HIDING FROM THE AMERICAN BOMBING, AND

PLACED IRAQ UNDER A SIEGE THAT KILLED OVER A MILLION

CIVILIANS. THEN THE U.S. MOVED TO FIGHT TERRORISM, SO

IT DESTROYED A PHARMACEUTICAL FACTORY IN SUDAN AND

BOMBED THE HOVELS OF THE POOREST COUNTRY ON EARTH,

AFGHANISTAN. FINALLY, BUSH THE SON DECLARED HIS WAR ON

CORRUPTION, AS DEFINED BY THE PALESTINIAN LEADERSHIP,

AND GAVE A MANDATE TO THE WAR CRIMINAL AND MASS

MURDERER SHARON TO USE AMERICAN WEAPONS TO DESTROY IT.

THE ISRAELI ARMY DID NOT ASSASSINATE OR ARREST A SINGLE

CORRUPT PALESTINIAN. INSTEAD THEY PROCEEDED TO

MASSACRE WOMEN AND CHILDREN AND DESTROY THEIR HOUSES,

AS THE AMERICANS DID IN AFGHANISTAN. THESE WARS ARE

PRETEXTS FOR THE REAL WAR THAT BUSH THE SON DECLARED

SOON AFTER TAKING POWER, AND WHICH HE LATER DESCRIBED

AS A SLIP OF THE TONGUE, WHEN HE DECLARED THAT HE WOULD

LEAD A CRUSADE TO BRING PEACE TO THE WORLD, AS IF

POINTING TO MUSLIMS AS THE ROOT OF ALL EVIL IN THE

WORLD." GNEHM
