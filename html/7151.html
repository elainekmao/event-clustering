SUBJECT: UNESCO EXECUTIVE BOARD: DEMARCHE ON IRAQ DELIVERED

TO JORDAN

REF: A. STATE 84179

B. STATE 88308

Classified By: PolCouns Doug Silliman for reasons 1.5 (B)(D)

1. (C) On April 3 and again on April 6, PolCouns raised

with Ali al-Ayed, Personal Assistant to Foreign Minister

Marwan Muasher, points from reftels on the possibility of a

Cuban proposal to discuss Iraq in the UNESCO Executive Board.

PolCouns stressed the inappropriateness of a politicized

discussion of Iraq at UNESCO. Discussion of this matter

belongs more appropriately in the Security Council. Al-Ayed

took the points and promised to convey them to the Minister

and others dealing with UN affairs.
