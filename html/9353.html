SUBJECT: UAE READY FOR MLAT VIDEO CONFERENCE JULY 15

REF 7/9/03 Deeks- Menard e-mail

1. (U) Classified by A/DCM Katherine Van De Vate, for

reasons 1.5 (B) and (D).

2. (U) This is an Action request. Please see para 5.

3. (C) The Ambassador Met with UAE Minister of Justice,

Islamic Affairs, and Awqaf Mohamed Nakhera Al-Daheri on

July 13, 2003. The Minister said that of the 3 dates

proposed (July 15 and 16, August 11) for a digital

videoconference on the Mutual Legal Assistance Treaty

(MLAT), the UAE preferred July 15. Due to summer travel

schedules, the UAE will not be able to make an August date.

4. (U) The UAEG is proposing a 1945 (local) start time -

1145 Washington time. Note: We have talked to the

technicians responsible for setting up the conference and

they are looking to connect at 1930 (local).

5. (U) Comment and Action Request: The UAEG is eager to

talk, but given the summer schedule here, there is a small

window of opportunity before the fall that we want to take

advantage of. Please arrange for a translator in

Washington. Many of the UAEG participants do not have a

good command of English. The UAEG will try to provide a

translator here as well. End Comment and Action Request
