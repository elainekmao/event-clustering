IN 1984 THE UNITED STATES JOINED 107 OTHER COUNTRIES,

INCLUDING ALL OF OUR WESTERN ALLIES, WHEN WE ESTABLISHED

NORMAL DIPLOMATIC RELATIONS WITH THE HOLY SEE. THE HOLY SEE

IS AN ENTITY THAT, UNDER INTERNATIONAL LAW, HAS AN

INTERNATIONAL "PERSONALITY" WHICH GIVES THE JURIDICAL

EQUIVALENT OF A STATE AND THAT HAS ITS TERRITORIAL

MANIFESTATION IN THE VATICAN CITY STATE. IN THE CONTEXT OF

THE DAY, THIS NEW RELATIONSHIP MADE A LOT OF SENSE: IT

HELPED US MAKE COMMON CAUSE WITH HOLY SEE AGAINST COMMUNISM

AND THREATS TO WORLD SECURITY IN WANING DAYS OF COLD WAR.

AT THAT TIME THERE WERE CONCERNS THAT ESTABLISHING THIS

RELATIONSHIP WOULD INFRINGE ON THE CONSTITUTIONAL SEPARATION

OF CHURCH AND STATE. I THINK THAT THE WORK OF OUR EMBASSY

DURING THE INTERVENING YEARS HAS BORNE OUT THE WISDOM OF

ESTABLISHING THIS RELATIONSHIP. THAT WORK HAS FOCUSED ON

THE MOST IMPORTANT INTERNATIONAL ISSUES OF THE DAY.

DURING THOSE YEARS THE VERY SMALL STAFF OF OUR EMBASSY TO

THE HOLY SEE HAS PROVIDED INVALUABLE REPORTING ON VATICAN

EFFORTS TO PROMOTE HUMAN RIGHTS AND RESOLVE CONFLICTS AROUND

THE WORLD. IT HAS HELPED TO SUSTAIN A COMMON FRONT AGAINST

AGGRESSION IN PLACES AS DIVERSE AS KOSOVO AND IRAQ, AND IT

HAS WORKED WITH VATICAN COUNTERPARTS TO RESOLVE CONFLICT IN

AFRICA -- MOST NOTABLY IN MOZAMBIQUE.

(NOTE: EMBASSY STAFF CONSISTS OF THREE FOREIGN SERVICE

OFFICERS AND ABOUT A DOZEN LOCALLY HIRED STAFF).

THE TIMES ARE CHANGING. IN THIS NEW MILLENIUM MANY THREATS

TO OUR SECURITY AND PROSPERITY ARE GLOBAL IN NATURE -- AND

HOLY SEE CAN BE A USEFUL PARTNER ON MANY ISSUES AND IN MANY

AREAS OF THE WORLD:

EXAMPLES OF WHERE WE ARE COLLABORATING OR CAN COLLABORATE

FRUITFULLY WITH THE HOLY SEE:

DEVELOPMENT ISSUES:

-- FIGHTING AIDS, ESPECIALLY IN AFRICA; PROMOTING

RESPONSIBLE USE OF BIOTECHNOLOGY IN AGRICULTURE; DEBT

RELIEF.

SECURITY ISSUES:

-- ENDING REGIONAL CONFLICTS (CONGO, BURUNDI, SUDAN,

NAGORNO-KARABAKH),

-- INTER-COMMUNAL RECONCILIATION (OUR EMBASSY HAS DEVELOPED

PROJECTS IN CONJUNCTION WITH THE VATICAN AFFILIATED LAY

COMMUNITY OF SANT\'EGIDIO IN THE BALKANS, AND IN MOZAMBIQUE),

-- EUROPEAN SECURITY (THE POPE\'S OUTREACH TO ORTHODOX FITS

WITH OUR OWN INTEREST IN PROMOTING "A EUROPE WHOLE AND

FREE").

DEMOCRACY AND HUMAN RIGHTS ISSUES:

-- HOLY SEE IS A VALUABLE PARTNER IN PROMOTING DEMOCRACY

ABROAD (CUBA, CHINA, MIDDLE EAST, ETC.)

-- VERY IMPORTANT PARTNERSHIP TO PROMOTE INTERNATIONAL

RELIGIOUS FREEDOM; OUR EMBASSY TO THE HOLY SEE PLAYS UNIQUE

ROLE IN SUPPORT OF THE INTERNATIONAL RELIGIOUS FREEDOM ACT

OF 1998 BY SHARING INFORMATION ON PROBLEM AREAS LITERALLY

AROUND THE ENTIRE WORLD, WHERE THE HOLY SEE, UNIQUELY AMONG

SMALL "COUNTRIES" HAS IMPORTANT PRESENCE THROUGH THE ROMAN

CATHOLIC CHURCH AND ITS MISSIONARY AND SPECIALIZED COUNCILS.

TO ELABORATE,

EMBASSY VATICAN IS KEEPING CLOSE CONTACT WITH THE HOLY SEE

IN REGARD TO QUESTIONS OF RELIGIOUS FREEDOM IN CHINA. THE

VATICAN SHARES U.S. CONCERNS ON THE NEED TO PROMOTE

RELIGIOUS FREEDOM IN CHINA. THE VATICAN DOES NOT HAVE

OFFICIAL TIES WITH CHINA. IT DOES HOWEVER MAINTAIN FORMAL

DIPLOMATIC TIES WITH TAIWAN. THIS IS A UNIQUE DIPLOMATIC

SITUATION ON THE EUROPEAN CONTINENT.

WE FOLLOW WITH INTEREST THE EFFORTS OF THE VATICAN AND

CARITAS, THE VATICAN\'S ARM FOR HUMANITARIAN ASSISTANCE, TO

ALLEVIATE THE SUFFERING OF THE POPULATION OF NORTH KOREA.

CARITAS HAS SUPPLIED AID ESTIMATED AT $25 MILLION IN VALUE

TO NORTH KOREA OVER THE LAST FIVE YEARS. WE RECOGNIZE THAT

THE VATICAN, WHICH DOES NOT HAVE FORMAL DIPLOMATIC RELATIONS

WITH NORTH KOREA, HAS LEGITIMATE INTERESTS IN THE WELFARE OF

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 02 VATICA 03756 02 OF 02 131452Z

THE ESTIMATED 2,000-4,000 CATHOLICS IN NORTH KOREA.

WE ARE KEEPING TRACK OF VATICAN DISCUSSIONS WITH VIETNAM ON

THE STATUS OF THE CATHOLIC CHURCH IN VIETNAM AND THE

EVENTUAL ESTABLISHMENT OF OFFICIAL DIPLOMATIC RELATIONS

BETWEEN VIETNAM AND THE HOLY SEE.

NOT TO SAY WE AGREE ON EVERYTHING -- WE HAVE DIFFERENCES ON

MEPP, ON THE EFFECTIVENESS OF INTERNATIONAL SANCTIONS, AND

ON SOCIAL ISSUES LIKE THE DEATH PENALTY. BUT THESE

DIFFERENCES ARE A NATURAL PART OF ANY HEALTHY RELATIONSHIP.

ON GLOBALIZATION, FOR EXAMPLE, WE MIGHT WANT TO LISTEN TO

THE POPE\'S CONSTRUCTIVE CRITICISM, WHICH HE SEEKS TO

COMMUNICATE OVER THE DIN OF ANARCHIC BABBLE WE SEE IN GENOA

THIS WEEK. THE VATICAN URGES THE CREATION OF AN

INTERNATIONAL ECONOMY THAT IS MORE SUPPORTIVE OF POOR, MORE

SHARING OF TECHNOLOGY AND KNOWLEDGE AND LESS UNEQUAL IN

DISTRIBUTION OF WEALTH -- PRECISELY TO MAKE THE WORD A MORE

STABLE AND PROSPEROUS PLACE FOR ALL OF US.

I WILL MAKE SURE THAT OUR EMBASSY TO HOLY SEE WILL BE A

VALUABLE TOOL TO PROMOTE U.S. VIEWS ACROSS ALL THESE AREAS

AND MORE -- AND TO MAKE IT A VALUABLE "LISTENING POST" TO

BENEFIT FROM THE KNOWLEDGE AND VIEWS OF ONE OF THE WORLD\'S

MOST SOPHISTICATED AND GLOBALLY ACTIVE DIPLOMATIC CORPS.

END TEXT.
