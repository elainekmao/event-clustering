SUBJECT: JUNE 24 UN-HOSTED TECHNICAL CONSULTATIONS ON

RECONSTRUCTION NEEDS FOR IRAQ: JORDAN MAY SEND PLANNING

MINISTER AWADALLAH

REF: STATE 163448

1. (U) On June 15, poloff conveyed reftel points on the

upcoming June 24 UN-hosted meeting on Iraq reconstruction to

Ali al-Ayed, Personal Assistant to Foreign Minister Muasher,

stressing the USG\'s desire to see Jordan represented at a

senior level. On June 16, in a follow-up discussion, al-Ayed

confirmed that Jordan had received an invitation to the

meeting from the UN, that FM Muasher would not be able to

attend, but that Planning Minister Bassem Awadallah may. He

stressed that no final decision regarding Minister

Awadallah\'s participation had been made, but promised to

inform the Embassy as soon as one was. We note that Minister

Awadallah is the chief GOJ organizer for the June 21-23

Extraordinary Session of the World Economic Forum (WEF) that

will be held at the Dead Sea.
