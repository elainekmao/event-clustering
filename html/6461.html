SUBJECT: IRAQ: TURKEY WILL CONSIDER OPPOSING UNGA SPECIAL

REF: A. STATE 70678

B. USUN 710

(U) Classified by DCM Robert Deutsch. Reasons 1.5 (b) and

(d).

(C) On March 18 polmiloff met with MFA Iraq Desk officer

Ferhat Alkan and delivered Ref A points. Alkan readily

agreed that a UNGA emergency special session on Iraq would

serve only to intensify and highlight the divisions within

the UN. He thought that it had the potential to damage the

institution further. Though he was unable to answer what

Turkey\'s position would be, he committed to passing our

request up his chain of command expeditiously.
