CLASSIFIED BY: ACTING POL MC GARY ROBBINS FOR REASONS 1.5 (B) AND (D)

1. (C) SUMMARY: ACCORDING TO HIS DEPUTY DIPLOMATIC ADVISOR,

PM AND ACTING FM BERLUSCONI REACTED VERY POSITIVELY TO THE

PRESIDENT\'S JUNE 24 SPEECH ON THE MIDDLE EAST. BERLUSCONI

AGREED THAT THE PA NEEDED A NEW LEADERSHIP, AND STRESSED THE

IMPORTANCE OF INSTITUTIONAL AND ECONOMIC REFORM FOR

PALESTINIANS. ON THE INTERNATIONAL PEACE CONFERENCE,

BERLUSCONI, WHO HAD EXPRESSED SERIOUS INTEREST IN HOSTING THE

CONFERENCE THIS SUMMER, HAS CONCLUDED THAT THE CURRENT

CLIMATE WAS NOT APPROPRIATE FOR HOLDING A SUCCESSFUL PEACE

CONFERENCE. END SUMMARY.

------------------------------------

ON THE PRESIDENT\'S SPEECH AND ARAFAT

------------------------------------

2. (C) ON JULY 2, POLOFF MET PAOLO DIONISI, BERLUSCONI\'S

DEPUTY DIPLOMATIC ADVISOR, WHO ACCOMPANIED THE ITALIAN PM ON

THE JUNE 26-27 G8 SUMMIT. DIONISI SAID THAT BERLUSCONI

PRAISED THE PRESIDENT\'S SPEECH AS "STRONG AND ENGAGED" AND AS

THE FIRST PUBLIC CALL FOR A CHANGE IN PA LEADERSHIP. THE

METAPHOR BERLUSCONI USED IS ONE HE BORROWED FROM AVRAHAM

BURG, PRESIDENT OF THE ISRAELI KNESSET, WHOM HE HAD MET IN

ROME ON JUNE 19: "THE ISRAELIS ASK THEMSELVES WHETHER THEY

DON\'T RUN THE RISK OF FALLING INTO A SITUATION WHERE THEY

NEGOTIATE THE SALE OF GOODS WITH THE OWNER OF THE SHOP, REACH

AN AGREEMENT ON THE COST WITH HIM, AND THEN HIS EMPLOYEES

REFUSE TO DELIVER THESE GOODS." BERLUSCONI STRESSED THAT THE

PALESTINIANS DESPERATELY NEEDED INSTITUTIONAL AND ECONOMIC

REFORMS. HE CALLED FOR A NEW PALESTINIAN LEADERSHIP AND FOR

ELECTIONS TO BE HELD AS SOON AS POSSIBLE. MEANWHILE HOWEVER,

UNTIL A NEW PALESTINIAN LEADERSHIP WAS ELECTED BY THE

PALESTINIANS, THERE WAS NO ALTERNATIVE TO DEALING WITH ARAFAT

IN HIS CAPACITY AS THE ELECTED LEADER OF THE PA, STRESSED

BERLUSCONI.

-------------------------------------

ON THE INTERNATIONAL PEACE CONFERENCE

-------------------------------------

3. (C) BERLUSCONI STILL BELIEVED IN THE IMPORTANCE OF AN

INTERNATIONAL PEACE CONFERENCE, AND WAS STILL WILLING TO HOST

IT AND PAY FOR IT. HOWEVER, BERLUSCONI NO LONGER FELT THAT

THE CURRENT CLIMATE WAS THE RIGHT ONE TO HAVE A SUCCESSFUL

CONFERENCE. HE FELT THAT THE PA DID NOT CURRENTLY HAVE THE

KIND OF RESPONSIBLE LEADERSHIP NEEDED FOR A SUCCESSFUL

CONFERENCE, AND REEMPHASIZED THE NEED FOR THE PALESTINIANS TO

ELECT A NEW LEADERSHIP AS SOON AS POSSIBLE.

4. (C) COMMENT: THE JUNE 26-27 G8 SUMMIT IN KANANASKIS MARKED

A METAMORPHOSIS IN BERLUSCONI\'S POSITION ON ARAFAT (REF) AND

ON THE INTERNATIONAL PEACE CONFERENCE, WHICH HE HAD

PREVIOUSLY STAUNCHLY SUPPORTED AND OFFERED TO HOST. AMONG

THE LEADERS PRESENT AT THE G8 SUMMIT, BERLUSCONI APPEARS TO

HAVE EXPRESSED SOME OF THE STRONGEST SUPPORT FOR THE

PRESIDENT\'S PLAN ON THE MIDDLE EAST. ALTHOUGH HE IS NOW

STEPPING BACK FROM HIS NEAR-TERM PLAN FOR AN ERICE

CONFERENCE, BERLUSCONI WILL CONTINUE TO SEEK OPPORTUNITIES TO

PLAY A HELPFUL, HIGH-PROFILE, ROLE. END COMMENT.
