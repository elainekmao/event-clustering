REFCOORD ERIC WHITAKER -- (0) 385-41-444-800, EXT. 63;

3. THE DATES SCHEDULED FOR THE TEAM\'S BRIEF STOP IN

CROATIA INCLUDE EASTER SUNDAY, APRIL 3, AND EASTER

MONDAY, APRIL 4, A NATIONAL HOLIDAY. IF IT IS

IMPOSSIBLE TO CHANGE THE TEAM\'S ITINERARY, WE

WILL TRY TO WORK AROUND THIS POTENTIALLY

SIGNIFICANT OBSTACLE. WE NOTE THAT THE CROATIAN

AUTHORITIES ARE PREPARED TO COOPERATE IN ANY WAY

THEY CAN. THEY HAVE AGREED, FOR EXAMPLE, TO

PROVIDE AN INTERPRETER.

CONTACT OF HIS THAT HE IS NOW IN SWEDEN. HIS

NAME (STRICTLY PROTECT - HE IS THOUGHT TO STILL

93 ZAGREB 731), WHO REPORTEDLY UNDER COERCION

HELPED "PREPARE" YOUNG WOMEN FOR MASS RAPE

SESSIONS WAS REPORTED TO HAVE LEFT CROATIA

ALMOST ONE YEAR AGO. OUR CONTACTS DID NOT KNOW

WHERE SHE WAS NOW.

6. OUR INITIAL REQUEST TO THE GOC OFFICE OF

DISPLACED PERSONS AND REFUGEES ELICITED A

RESPONSE THAT THERE ARE THOUGHT TO BE SOME 8,000

REFUGEES IN GOVERNMENT-CONTROLLED PARTS OF

CROATIA WHO CAME ORIGINALLY FROM THE BRCKO AREA.

THE NUMBER OF REFUGEES HERE WHO WOULD HAVE COME

FROM (AND CONTINUE TO COME FROM) THE PRIJEDOR

AREA WOULD, WE SUSPECT, BE FAR LARGER.
