SUBJECT: (U) ONE AMCIT KILLED, ONE WOUNDED IN KUWAIT

SHOOTING

1. (SBU/NF) At approximately 9:20 a.m. local time, two U.S.

citizen civilian DoD contract employees working at Camp Doha,

Kuwait were shot in their vehicle, a Toyota Prado, while it

sat at a traffic light at the intersection of Route 85 and

Route 7, heading from Camp Doha. They were wearing civilian

clothing and their vehicle was unmarked. According to U.S.

Army CID, at least one gunman behind bushes approximately 20

yards from the vehicle on the passenger side fired over 20

rounds of 7.62 mm into the vehicle. The Embassy first heard

of this incident shortly before 10:00 a.m., and the DCM

phoned an initial report to the Operations Center.

2. (SBU/NF) According to U.S. Army CID, the passenger,

identified as Michael Pouliote, was killed and the driver,

identified as David Caraway, was wounded by multiple bullets;

at last report, Caraway had been taken to hospital and was in

critical but stable condition. Embassy personnel are

intensively engaged in following up the various aspects of

this matter.

3. (U) Proposed press guidance follows:

"Two U.S. civilian contractors working for the U.S. military

in Kuwait were shot this morning in a terrorist attack at

approximately 9:20 a.m. (1:20 a.m. EST) at the intersection

of Route 7 and Route 85 in Kuwait. Both men sustained

multiple gunshot wounds. One individual was killed and the

other was taken to hospital in critical but stable condition.

The incident is currently under investigation by Kuwaiti and

U.S. Army CID authorities.

U.S. Ambassador Richard Jones said, "We condemn this

terrorist incident which has tragically cost the life of an

innocent American citizen. We have full confidence that the

Kuwaiti authorities will pursue their investigation of the

incident vigorously and professionally. We stand ready to

assist them in any way possible as they work to bring the

assailant to justice."

- (If asked): names of the victims are being withheld in

keeping with the Privacy Act and pending notification of next

of kin.

4. (U) Proposed warden message follows: "Two U.S. civilian

contractors working for the U.S. military in Kuwait were shot

this morning in a terrorist attack at approximately 9:20 a.m.

(1:20 a.m. EST) at the intersection of Route 7 and Route 85

in Kuwait. One individual was killed and the other is in

critical condition. Both men sustained multiple gunshot

wounds. The incident is currently under investigation by

Kuwaiti and U.S. authorities. The Embassy reminds all

American citizens in Kuwait to be alert to their surroundings

and continually assess their security, to include varying the

times and routes of their movements."
