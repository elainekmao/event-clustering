SUBJECT: CARDINAL PROTESTS G8, SEEKS

GLOBALIZATION WITH A HUMAN FACE

1. (U) THIS IS A REQUEST FOR GUIDANCE, PLEASE

SEE PARA FOUR.

2. (U) CARDINAL DIONIGI TETTAMANZI OF THE GENOA

ARCHDIOCESE IS PLANNING TO TAKE ADVANTAGE OF

THE G8 SUMMIT IN GENOA TO PROMOTE THE VATICAN\'S

VISION OF A GLOBALIZATION THAT LEAVES NO

INDIVIDUAL BEHIND. THE CARDINAL HAS INITIATED

A SERIES OF PRE-G8 CONFERENCES TO REFLECT ON

CHRISTIAN HUMANISM AND THE ECONOMY IN THE FACE

OF GLOBALIZATION. THE LATEST OF THESE

CONFERENCES, ATTENDED BY OVER 1000 PEOPLE, WAS

SPONSORED BY A CATHOLIC NGO (NEW HUMANITY) AND

WAS SUPPORTED BY ARCHDIOCESE OF GENOA. THE

WEEKEND CONFERENCE DEVELOPED A PLAN TO PRESENT

TO G8 SUMMITEERS A "GENOA DOCUMENT" INTENDED TO

PROMOTE "AN ECONOMY OF COMMUNION" (I.E. AN

ECONOMY WHICH VALUES THE PARTICIPATION OF ALL

MEMBERS OF A SOCIETY AND WHICH EXPLICITLY

PROVIDES SUPPORT FOR THE POOR).

3. (U) TETTAMANZI UNDERLINES THAT THE CHURCH,

UNLIKE MOST SECULAR PROTESTERS (E.G. THE

"SEATTLE GROUP") IS NOT AGAINST GLOBALIZATION

PER SE, WHICH IT VIEWS AS AN HISTORICAL

INEVITABILITY. VATICAN AND CHURCH RELATED NGOS

WILL SEEK TO CONFRONT IN GENOA ONLY THE

NEGATIVE ASPECTS OF GLOBALIZATION. DURING THE

G8 TETTAMANZI WILL FOCUS PROTEST ON AREAS WHERE

GLOBALIZATION RESULTS IN BASIC INJUSTICES (SUCH

AS INCREASING POVERTY) WHICH HE ASSERTS ARE

REAL AND DRAMATIC IN MANY PARTS OF THE WORLD.

HE WILL ALSO OFFER SOLUTIONS BASED ON THE

4. (SBU) CHARGE PLANS TO VISIT THE CARDINAL AND

PROBLEMS AND REAL SOLUTIONS. POST WOULD
