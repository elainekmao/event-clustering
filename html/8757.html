SUBJECT: UAEG UNDECIDED ABOUT THE U.S. CANDIDATE FOR

CODEX CHAIRPERSON

REF: STATE 145802

1. (SBU) Econoff delivered reftel demarche June 16 to

UAE Authority for Standardization & Metrology Deputy

General Director Dr. Rashid Ahmad Bin Fahad. Dr. Ahmad

said that his office is responsible for CODEX issues

but is unlikely to attend the CODEX Alimentarius

Commission (CAC) session in Rome. The UAE delegation

will likely be led by Halid Sharif, Food Control

Division Director for Dubai Municipality.

2. (SBU) The UAE has not yet decided who it will

support for CODEX Chairperson. If Sharif goes to the

CAC meeting, Dr. Ahmad will consult with him about our

request to support the candidacy of Mr. Gonzalo Rios

Kantorowitz. However, according to Dr. Ahmad, the GCC

countries usually confer with each other on CODEX

matters and probably will back the same candidate. He

believes members of the GCC Food Control Committee

will attempt to interview the candidates and will make

a decision on whom to support just before the CAC

session.
