SUBJECT: BANJA LUKA MUSLIM LEADERS ARRESTED FOR

"ESPIONAGE" FACE POSSIBLE DEATH SENTENCE

1. THIS IS AN ACTION REQUEST. SEE PARA 7.

2. (U) IN THE PAST TWO WEEKS, OVER TEN OF THE MOST

PROMINENT MUSLIM COMMUNITY LEADERS IN THE BANJA LUKA

REGION HAVE BEEN ARRESTED AT THEIR MERHAMET OFFICES AND IN

THEIR HOMES BY SERB MILITARY AUTHORITIES. THE DETAINED

INCLUDED NIJAZ KARASELIMOVIC, THE 73-YEAR-OLD PRESIDENT OF

MERHAMET BANJA LUKA. POLICE ALSO CARRIED AWAY COMPUTERS

AND DISKS CONTAINING THE ORGANIZATION\'S RECORDS. THERE

ARE UNCONFIRMED REPORTS OF SIMILAR ARRESTS IN NOVI GRAD

(FORMERLY BOSANSKI NOVI) AND PRNJAVOR. AFTER THESE

ARRESTS AND THOSE OF THE SDA LEADERSHIP FIVE MONTHS AGO,

THE ONLY REMAINING MUSLIM COMMUNITY LEADER IN BANJA LUKA

IS THE RELIGIOUS MUFTI, IBRAHIM HALILOVIC.

3. (LOU) ACCORDING TO AP/HINA, MERHAMET ZAGREB SOURCES,

AND KARASELIMOVIC\'S SON EMIR (PROTECT), THESE PRISONERS

ARE CURRENTLY UNDER DETENTION IN A MILITARY PRISON, HAVE

BEEN INDICTED BY THE MILITARY PROSECUTOR IN BANJA LUKA FOR

EFFORTS BY INTERNATIONAL AGENCIES TO VISIT THE PRISONERS

SRPSKI" LINKED THESE ALLEGED ESPIONAGE ACTIVITIES WITH

THOSE OF THE SDA LEADERSHIP "VALTER GROUP," SIMILARLY

CHARGED, AND CURRENTLY ON TRIAL BY THE MILITARY

PROSECUTOR.

THEIR LIVES.
