VATICAN CRITICIZES AIR STRIKES ON IRAQ

--------------------------------------

2. VATICAN SECRETARY OF STATE (PM EQUIVALENT) CARDINAL

ANGELO SODANO HAS CONDEMNED FORCE AS A MEANS OF BRINGING

STABILITY TO THE GULF REGION. THE CARDINAL SAID THE

VATICAN HOPES THAT "VIOLENT METHODS DO NOT CONTINUE. IF

YOU WANT PEACE, YOU HAVE TO PREPARE FOR PEACE." HE SAID

SOME PEOPLE THINK THAT PROBLEMS CAN BE RESOLVED WITH

FORCE, "BUT THE HOLY SEE THINKS DIFFERENTLY." SODANO ALSO

RENEWED THE VATICAN\'S CONDEMNATION OF THE UN ECONOMIC

EMBARGO DIRECTED AGAINST IRAQ SINCE THE END OF THE GULF

WAR. HE NOTED THAT MANY COUNTRIES SHARED THE VATICAN\'S

POSITION, AND SAID, "WE HOPE WISDOM WILL PREVAIL."

(COMMENT: THE VATICAN IS UNLIKELY TO TAKE UP ANY POSITION

THAT MIGHT THREATEN THE RELATIVE RELIGIOUS FREEDOM

ENJOYED BY THE SMALL CHRISTIAN COMMUNITIES IN THE GULF

REGION. THE VATICAN ALSO SEEKS TO ENSURE A SMOOTH VISIT

FOR THE POPE WHEN HE VISITS SYRIA IN MAY AND TO MAINTAIN

THE GOOD WILL NECESSARY FOR AN EVENTUAL PAPAL VISIT TO

IRAQ PRESENTLY ON THE BACKBURNER. END COMMENT).

POPE AND EGYPTIAN PRESIDENT DISCUSS MEPP

----------------------------------------

3. DURING A FEBRUARY 20 MEETING WITH EGYPT\'S PRESIDENT

MUBARAK, POPE JOHN PAUL II CALLED FOR AN END TO THE

VIOLENCE IN THE MIDDLE EAST AND A RESUMPTION OF PEACE

TALKS. THE POPE SPOKE PRIVATELY TO MUBARAK FOR ABOUT 30

MINUTES. THE VATICAN LATER DISCLOSED THAT THE MAIN TOPIC

OF CONVERSATION WAS THE PEACE PROCESS AND THE NEED FOR "A

CLIMATE OF DQTENTE AND DIALOGUE." (SEPTEL TO FOLLOW). THE

VATICAN STATEMENT SAID PEACE IN THE MIDDLE EAST IS LINKED

TO "RESPECT FOR INTERNATIONAL LAW ON THE PART OF ALL AND

IN THE CONVICTION THAT THE PEOPLES HAVE THE SAME RIGHTS

AND DUTIES." VATICAN FM, ARCHBISHOP JEAN-LOUIS TAURAN,

ALSO HELD DISCUSSIONS WITH HIS EGYPTIAN COUNTERPART, AMR

MOUSSA. ON FEBRUARY 18, POPE JOHN PAUL LAMENTED THE

INCREASE IN PALESTINIAN-ISRAELI VIOLENCE AND SAID THE

PROSPECTS FOR PEACE WERE GROWING DIM. MEANWHILE, THE

VATICAN AMBASSADOR TO ISRAEL, ARCHBISHOP PIETRO SAMBI,

CALLED ON TOURISTS AND PILGRIMS NOT TO ABANDON JERUSALEM

AND THE HOLY LAND. HE SAID VISITORS SHOULD NOT HEED

"ALARMING MEDIA REPORTS ACCORDING TO WHICH THE HOLY LAND

IS DROWNED IN TERROR." SAMBI SAID PRECAUTIONS HAD TO BE

TAKEN, BUT THAT PILGRIMS SHOULD NOT BE AFFECTED BY THE

PRESENT VIOLENCE. (COMMENT: SAMBI EARLIER EXPRESSED

CONCERN ABOUT THE FALL OFF IN PILGRIMS AND TOURISTS

IMPACTING ECONOMICALLY ON PALESTINIANS, BOTH MOSLEM AND

CHRISTIAN. HIS ASSURANCES ABOUT PILGRIM SAFETY SUGGEST

THE ISSUE CONTINUES TO BE SIGNIFICANT FOR THE VATICAN

BLURRING OF CHURCH-STATE SEPARATION

5. THE VATICAN HAS DEFENDED STEPPING INTO THE ITALIAN

POLITICAL ARENA BY SAYING ITALIAN POLITICIANS REQUESTED

CONSULTATIONS WITH ITS OFFICIALS. VATICAN SECRETARY OF

STATE, CARDINAL ANGELO SODANO, HAS RECEIVED LEADERS FROM

LEFT AND RIGHT WING POLITICAL PARTIES. HE CLAIMED THE

VATICAN "LIMITED ITSELF" TO EXPLAINING ITS POSITION ON

MORAL AND SOCIAL ISSUES.

VATICAN SUPPORTS BIOMEDICAL RESEARCH "WITHIN LIMITS"

--------------------------------------------- -------

6. THE VATICAN\'S TOP BIOMEDICAL EXPERT, BISHOP ELIO

SGRECCIA, SAYS HE SUPPORTS BIOMEDICAL RESEARCH. IN AN

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 03 VATICA 01286 02 OF 02 072228Z

INTERVIEW WITH VATICAN RADIO, SGRECCIA SAID THE VATICAN

APPRECIATES AND ENCOURAGES SCIENTIFIC RESEARCH WHEN IT IS

GEARED TOWARDS "THE PREVENTION AND HEALING OF ILLNESSES

AND THE RELIEF OF HUMAN SUFFERING." THE BISHOPS STRESSED

THAT RESEARCH AND EXPERIMENTATION MUST SHOW RESPECT FOR

ALL HUMAN LIFE FROM CONCEPTION TO NATURAL DEATH, AND

ESPECIALLY WHEN SUBJECTS ARE UNABLE TO CONSENT TO

PROCEDURES. BISHOP SGRECCIA REAFFIRMED THE VATICAN\'S

OPPOSITION TO THE USE OF FETAL STEM CELLS FOR RESEARCH

UNLESS THE CELLS CAME FROM SPONTANEOUSLY ABORTED FETUSES.

THE VATICAN ACCEPTS RESEARCH USING STEM CELLS TAKEN FROM

THE UMBILICAL CORD OF NEWBORN INFANTS AND FROM ADULTS.

SGRECCIA SAID THE VATICAN ALSO SUPPORTS BIOTECHNOLOGY AS

LONG AS PROPER SAFEGUARDS ARE MAINTAINED, CONSUMERS

INFORMED OF MODIFIED ORGANISMS IN THEIR FOOD AND ECONOMIC

ADVANTAGES SHARED.

NO RED HATS FOR EASTERN-RITE CARDINALS

--------------------------------------

7. IN A SIGN OF RESPECT FOR EASTERN-RITE CHURCHES

UNITED TO ROME, THREE NEW CARDINALS DID NOT RECEIVE THE

TRADITIONAL RED "BEANIES" AND BRIM-LESS "BIRETTAS" DURING

A FEBRUARY 21 VATICAN CITY CEREMONY. THE MODIFICATION OF

THE CEREMONY REFLECTS CURRENT VATICAN POLICY TOWARDS

ANCIENT NON-ROMAN CATHOLIC COMMUNITIES THAT HAVE UNIQUE

RITUALS, LANGUAGE AND TRADITIONS. ONE OF THE NEW

CARDINALS AND HEAD OF THE VATICAN OFFICE FOR EASTERN

CHURCHES, SYRIAN PATRIARCH MOUSSA DAOUD SAID NOT
