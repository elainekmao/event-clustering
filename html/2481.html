SUBJECT: VATICAN ON CLONING CONVENTION

REF: STATE 20863

CLASSIFIED BY: POLOFF GHFROWICK, REASON 1.5 (B), (D)

1. (C) POLOFF MET VATICAN UN ISSUES DIRECTOR MONS. DOMINIQUE

MAMBERTI 02/07 TO DISCUSS REFTEL QUESTIONS ON VATICAN

POSITION REGARDING UN ANTI-CLONING CONVENTION DEBATE.

MAMBERTI UNDERSCORED THE HOLY SEE\'S ABSOLUTE OPPOSITION TO

CONFIDENTIAL

HUMAN CLONING IN ANY FORM, AND REFERRED TO THE POPE\'S

CONTINUING PUBLIC STATEMENTS OPPOSING IT (MOST RECENTLY

DURING THE WEDNESDAY, 2/6 GENERAL AUDIENCE). HE SAID THAT

THE HOLY SEE TOOK ITS OBSERVER ROLE IN THE AD HOC COMMITTEE

OF THE SIXTH COMMITTEE VERY SERIOUSLY AND HOPED THAT A

CONSENSUS WOULD EMERGE IN SUPPORT OF BROADENING THE MANDATE

GIVEN IT BY UNGA RES. 56/93. HE SAID THERE WAS NOTHING IN

56/93 THAT "EXCLUDES THE POSSIBILITY TO GO BEYOND THE DEBATE

ON REPRODUCTIVE CLONING."

2. (C) ASKED WHETHER THE HOLY SEE WAS CONCERNED THAT

PUSHING TO BROADEN THE MANDATE MIGHT RISK MAKING IT MORE

DIFFICULT TO OBTAIN AN AGREEMENT BANNING REPRODUCTIVE

CLONING, MAMBERTI REPLIED THAT BROADENING IS A "TECHNICAL

CONCERN" AND DEPENDED ON THE AD HOC COMMITTEE CONSENSUS,

WHICH HE WOULDN\'T PREDICT. HE THOUGHT IT WOULD BE QUITE OK

FOR THE HOLY SEE WERE THE UN TO REACH AGREEMENT NOW ON A

CONVENTION TO BAN REPRODUCTIVE CLONING, AND TO KEEP WORKING

FOR A BROADER BAN LATER. (NOTE: WE WERE SURPRISED TO HEAR

THIS). CITING THE ANTI-TERRORISM CONVENTION, MAMBERTI WAS

CONCERNED THAT IF THE DEBATE GOT WRAPPED UP OVER BROADENING

THE MANDATE IT COULD PROLONG ITSELF FOR YEARS, WITH NO

RESULT.

3. (U) MAMBERTI SAID THAT THE HOLY SEE\'S TECHNICAL EXPERT

ON THE DELEGATION TO THE FIRST MEETING OF THE AD HOC

COMMITTEE WILL BE DON ROBERTO COLOMBO (NOTE: "DON" IS HIS

ECCLESIASTICAL TITLE). DON ROBERTO, A MILAN BASED PROFESSOR

OF MICROBIOLOGY AND BIOETHICS, WAS JUST APPOINTED TO THE

PONTIFICAL ACADEMY FOR LIFE. THE PONTIFICAL ACADEMY FOR LIFE

CONDUCTS RESEARCH ON BIOETHICS TO INFORM THE CHURCH\'S VIEWS.

4. (C) MAMBERTI DOES NOT COVER UNESCO, AND SO WAS NOT

BRIEFED ON THAT ASPECT OF THE CLONING DEBATE. AMBASSADOR

NICHOLSON WILL DINE WITH MAMBERTI\'S BOSS -- FM TAURAN -- NEXT

THURSDAY (02/14). THE PURPOSE OF THAT DINNER WAS TO DISCUSS

BIOTECHNOLOGY IN AGRICULTURE WITH VISITING EXPERT C.S.

PRAKASH. AMBASSADOR WILL RAISE THE ANTI-CLONING CONVENTION,

WHICH IS RELATED TO THE AG-BIOTECHNOLOGY DEBATE, WITH FM

TAURAN. MAMBERTI ASSURED US HE WILL BRIEF TAURAN.
