SUBJECT: JORDAN: MEDIA REACTION ON OCCUPIED TERRITORIES

SUMMARY

NEWS REPORTS IN JORDAN\'S DAILIES COVERED THE NEW

ISRAELI LAW BANNING THE SALE OF LAND TO NON-JEWS AND

THE ISRAELI ARMY\'S ATTACK ON JORDAN RADIO AND

TELEVISION\'S OFFICE IN RAMALLAH.

EDITORIAL WRITERS COMMENTED ON THE MUTINY BY THE

PALESTINIAN PREVENTIVE SECURITY AGENCY AGAINST THE

DISMISSAL OF ITS LEADER JIBRIL RJOUB.

NEWS REPORTS

ALL JORDANIAN DAILIES LED WITH REPORTS ON THE NEW

ISRAELI LAW THAT BANS THE SALE OF LAND TO NON-JEWS AND

FORBIDS ARABS FROM LIVING IN JEWISH QUARTERS, WHICH THE

DAILIES DESCRIBED AS RACIST.

REPORTS ALSO COVERED THE ISRAELI ARMY\'S ATTACK ON

JORDAN RADIO AND TELEVISION\'S OFFICES IN RAMALLAH.

EDITORIAL COMMENTARIES

-- TERRORISM OR WAR OF INDEPENDENCE?

PROMINENT DAILY COLUMNIST DR. FAHED AL-FANEK WROTE IN

SEMI-OFFICIAL INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-RAI ON JULY

9: "IF KILLING CIVILIANS CONSTITUTES TERRORISM, WHICH

IT DOES, THE KILLING COMMITTED BY THE FORCES OF

[ISRAELI] OCCUPATION IS FAR WORSE AND MUCH MORE WORTHY

OF CONDEMNATION THAN THE ACTS OF COMMANDOES WHO TRY TO

RAISE THE COST OF OCCUPATION TO A POINT THAT PERSUADES

ISRAEL OF THE NECESSITY OF WITHDRAWAL AND ACCEPTING

PEACE. BUT PRESIDENT BUSH, IN HIS MOST RECENT SPEECH

DID NOT ASK ISRAEL TO CEASE ITS TERRORIST ACTS. THE

PROBLEM IS NOT ONE OF TERRORISM BUT OF OCCUPATION, THE

CONTINUATION OF WHICH IS THE FATHER OF ALL VIOLENCE."

-- FIGHTING OVER NOTHING

PROMINENT DAILY COLUMNIST TARIQ MASARWAH WROTE IN SEMI-

OFFICIAL INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-RAI ON JULY 9: "IT

APPEARS THAT THOSE WHO FIGHT AND MUTINY AND NEGOTIATE

OVER POSITIONS IN THE PNA ARE NOT AWARE OF WHAT IS

HAPPENING IN PALESTINE. THERE IS NO MORE AUTHORITY

EXCEPT THOSE OF THE OCCUPATION, AND THE RESISTANCE TO

IT. THE PROMISED PALESTINIAN STATE WILL NOT BE THAT

HOPED FOR BY ARAFAT AND [JIBRIL] RJOUB, BUT ONE MADE TO

AMERICAN-ISRAELI MEASURES. THE PALESTINIAN STATE IS

THERE TO BE MADE BY THOSE WHO LOVE PALESTINE, THOSE WHO

FIGHT FOR IT, THOSE WHO ARE MARTYRED FOR ITS CAUSE.

THE LIBERATION OF THE HOMELAND AND ITS CITIZENS,

ESPECIALLY IN PALESTINE WILL NOT BE ACHIEVED THROUGH

FIGHTING OVER NOTHING."

-- AT THE CROSSROADS WITH OBSCURE CHOICES

TAHER AL-ADWAN, CHIEF EDITOR OF INDEPENDENT, MASS-

APPEAL ARABIC DAILY AL-ARAB AL-YAWM WROTE ON JULY 9:

"THE STATE OF THE PNA WOULD BE LAUGHABLE WERE IT NOT SO

TRAGIC. THE MEN OF THE PREVENTIVE SECURITY AGENCY NOW

DEMONSTRATE AGAINST THE DISMISSAL OF THEIR LEADER IN

THE SHADOW OF THE TANKS AND ARTILLERY OF THE OCCUPATION

FORCES. ONCE AGAIN THE PALESTINIANS ARE AT A

CROSSROADS AND THE ONLY WAY OUT OF THE DILEMMA FOR THEM

IS TO STAND UNITED BEHIND THEIR GOAL OF LIBERATION.

THEY SHOULD ALWAYS REMEMBER THAT THEY STAND ALONE IN A

MILITARY AND POLITICAL BATTLE AGAINST ISRAEL/U.S. AND

THAT THEY CANNOT AFFORD THE LEISURE OF SLOGANEERING,

HIDING BEHIND OBSCURITY."
