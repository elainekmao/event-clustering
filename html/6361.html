1. (C) SUMMARY: ON MARCH 11, MFA BALKAN AFFAIRS OFFICE

DIRECTOR FERNANDO GENTILINI TOLD POLOFF THAT THE GOI AGREES

WITH USG POINTS (REFTELS) ON THE NEED FOR KOSOVO PROTECTION

CORPS (KPC) REFORM, FORMATION OF THE UNION OF SERB

MUNICIPALITIES, AND, WITH THE EXCEPTION OF CARDS ASSISTANCE,

TYING IC AID TO GOSM COOPERATION WITH THE ICTY. ITALY LOOKS

FORWARD TO DISCUSSING THESE ISSUES AT THE MARCH 20 QUINT IN

LONDON. END SUMMARY.

KPC REFORM

----------

2. (C) GENTILINI TOLD POLOFF THAT THE GOI CONCURS WITH USG

VIEW (REF A) THAT COORDINATED QUINT ACTION IS NECESSARY TO

ENERGIZE THE KOSOVO PROTECTION CORPS REFORM PROCESS. HE HAD

ALREADY INSTRUCTED ITALIAN MISSIONS IN BELGRADE AND PRISTINA

TO PRESS LOCAL AUTHORITIES FOR A QUICK START-UP OF THE CIVIL

PROTECTION DEVELOPMENT GROUP.

3. (C) GENTILINI SAID THAT AT THE MARCH 20 QUINT MEETING IN

LONDON THE GOI WOULD UNDERLINE THE NEED FOR A COORDINATED,

CLEAR STRATEGY IN KOSOVO TO TURN "STANDARDS BEFORE STATUS"

FROM MERELY A SLOGAN TO A DEVELOPMENT ACTION PLAN. EU HIREP

SOLANA\'S STAFF WAS DRAFTING A NON-PAPER TO HELP THE IC MOVE

IN THAT DIRECTION; IT WAS GENTILINI\'S HOPE THAT THE QUINT

COULD DISCUSS THE PAPER IN LONDON. HE ALSO SAID THAT THERE

IN KOSOVO, AND HE WAS NOT SURE HOW MUCH LOCAL QUINT

REPRESENTATIVES COULD DO TO CHANGE THAT SITUATION. "IT MAY

BE TIME FOR ANOTHER HIGH-LEVEL (SOLANA) VISIT."

UNION OF SERB MUNICIPALITIES

----------------------------

4. (C) THE GOI ALSO SHARES USG CONCERNS OVER THE FORMATION

OF THE UNION OF SERB MUNICIPALITIES (REF B). SUCH

ORGANIZATIONS ARE NOT TO BE ENCOURAGED, SAID GENTILINI, AND

THE TENDENCY OF LOCALS TO FORM SUCH GROUPS OUTSIDE OF THE

UNSC 1244 FRAMEWORK IS YET ANOTHER MANIFESTATION OF THE LACK

OF STRONG LEADERSHIP IN THE REGION. IT WAS OBVIOUS, HE SAID,

THAT THIS WAS BEING DONE WITH A WINK AND PERHAPS ACTIVE

ENCOURAGEMENT AND ASSISTANCE FROM BELGRADE. GENTILINI

CONCURRED WITH, AND WOULD INSTRUCT GOI MISSIONS TO

PARTICIPATE IN ACTIVITIES DESCRIBED REF B TO DISCOURAGE

CONTACT WITH SUCH ORGANIZATIONS. BUT HE EMPHASIZED THAT THIS

EFFORT NEEDED TO BE PART OF A COORDINATED STRATEGY FOR THE

QUINT TO DISCUSS IN LONDON.

GOSM COOPERATION WITH ICTY

--------------------------

5. (C) GENTILINI SAID THAT THE GOI AGREED THAT THE IC MUST

USE ALL TOOLS AT ITS DISPOSAL TO PRESS THE GOSM TO IMPROVE

COOPERATION WITH THE ICTY (REF C). FOR EUROPEAN COUNTRIES,

THAT WOULD INCLUDE CONDITIONALITY ON BELGRADE\'S REQUEST TO

JOIN THE COE AND DEVELOPMENT OF A STABILIZATION AND

ASSOCIATION AGREEMENT. HOWEVER, HE CONTINUED, THAT

CONDITIONALITY SHOULD NOT EXTEND TO CARDS ASSISTANCE, WHICH

IS AIMED AT SUPPORTING THE VERY INSTITUTIONAL CAPACITY THAT

THE IC IS TRYING TO PROMOTE, AND INCREASING CONFIDENCE IN THE

RULE OF LAW. GENTILINI ARGUED THAT IT WOULD BE DIFFICULT TO

UNDERSTAND IF ONE WAS A SERB WHY, AT THE MOMENT THE COUNTRY

WAS BEGINNING TO MAKE PROGRESS ON RECONSTRUCTING THE JUDICIAL

SYSTEM AND OTHER SEGMENTS OF THE LEGAL INFRASTRUCTURE, THE IC

HALTED SUCH AID. IT WOULD BE COUNTERPRODUCTIVE TO THE IC\'S

GOALS.

6. (C) NOTE: THIS CONVERSATION TOOK PLACE PRIOR TO THE

DJINDJIC ASSASSINATION. POLOFF WILL FOLLOW UP WITH GENTILINI

ON POINTS RELATED TO THE LATEST DEVELOPMENTS IN BELGRADE THAT

GOI WILL RAISE AT THE QUINT AND EU GAC AND COUNCIL MEETINGS

DURING THE WEEK OF MARCH 17.
