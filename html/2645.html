MILITARY ASSISTANCE

-------------------

2. PER REF C, ITALY IS ALBANIAS MOST IMPORTANT TRADING

PARTNER, LARGEST INVESTOR, AND LARGEST ASSISTANCE

DONOR. IT HAS TAKEN THE LEAD IN PROVIDING ALBANIA WITH

EQUIPMENT AND VEHICLES TO BRING THE COUNTRY UP CLOSE TO

APPROXIMATELY USD 28 MILLION IN VEHICLES AND MATERIAL

TO SUPPORT THE ALBANIAN ARMED FORCES (AAF). THIS

FIGURE DOES NOT INCLUDE DOZENS OF TRAINING COURSES FOR

HUNDREDS OF OFFICERS/NCOS AND MILITARY STUDENTS TO

-- HELICOPTER CAPABILITY ASSISTANCE.

B. MACEDONIA

------------

9. SINCE THE 1992 EDINBURG MEETING, THE GOI, IN LINE

WITH THE EU AND THE MEMBER COUNTRIES, TOOK THE

COMMITMENT TO SUSTAIN THE PROCESS OF TRANSITION OF

FYROM, FOLLOWED BY HUMANITARIAN AND EMERGENCY PROGRAMS.

AFTER THE KOSOVO CRISIS OF 1999, THE PARTICULAR ITALIAN

INTEREST FOR THE SOCIAL AND POLITICAL STABILITY IN THE

BALKAN REGION WAS CONFIRMED DURING A VISIT TO SKOPJE BY

THE PRESIDENT OF REPUBLIC SCALFARO AND LATER

RECONFIRMED DURING A VISIT BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE

COUNCIL AND MINISTER OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS. AS A RESULT

OF THE CIVIL WAR OF 2001, WHICH PRODUCED THE WELL-KNOWN

NATO OPERATION ESSENTIAL HARVEST, THE INTERNATIONAL

COMMUNITY TOOK THE COMMITMENT TO SUSTAIN THE PROCESS OF

PACIFICATION AND STABILIZATION OF THE COUNTRY.

THE AGREEMENT BETWEEN THE GOVERNMENT OF SKOPJE AND THE

ODA DEPARTMENT OF MFA CONSIST OF THE FOLLOWING

INITIATIVES:

A. RATIONALIZATION OF THE MANAGING SECTOR AND

MODERNIZATION OF BIOMEDICAL EQUIPMENT: A GIFT OF 1.14

MILLION EURO AND A CREDIT AID OF 2.58 MILLION EURO. THE

PROGRAM STARTED IN FEBRUARY 2000. AT PRESENT IT IS

UNDER IMPLEMENTATION FOR THE GIFT PORTION OF THE

INTERVENTION WITH THE TECHNICAL ASSISTANCE PROVIDED BY

ITALIAN EXPERTS TO THE LOCAL AUTHORITIES FOR THE

MODERNIZATION OF EXISTING MEDICAL EQUIPMENT.

B. LINE OF SOFT CREDIT FOR 12.7 MILLION EURO, AIMED TO

SUPPLY ITALIAN GOODS AND SERVICES FOR THE SAME VALUE,

WITH THE PURPOSE OF SUPPORTING THE ACCESS OF MACEDONIAN

SMALL AND MEDIUM SIZED COMPANIES TO CREDITS AND OTHER

FINANCIAL INSTRUMENTS. THE ADVISORY ITALIAN COMPANY

CHOSEN IS FORTRADE FINANCING SPA BASED IN MILAN.

C. PROGRAM FOR THE CONSTRUCTION OF INFRASTRUCTURE FOR
