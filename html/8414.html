SUBJECT: Invitations to Foreign/Trade Ministers Event

during World Economic Forum

1. Post received the following two letters from Foreign

Minister Marwan Muasher for transmission to Secretary

Powell and Ambassador Zoellick. The letters are

identical with the exception of portions marked with

parentheses. Originals have been faxed to NEA/ARN.

2. Begin text:

Dear Mr. Secretary (Dear Mr. Ambassador),

As you are aware, an Extraordinary Annual Meeting of the

World Economic Forum will be held in Jordan under the

patronage of His Majesty King Abdullah II during the

period June 21-23, 2003. The participants of this unique

gathering will constitute a broad range of political,

economic, business and media leaders who will deliberate

on issues ranging from strengthening international

collaboration to global economic performance, as well as

mechanisms to achieve regional and global peace and

sustainable socio-economic development.

Capitalizing on such an event, Jordan would like to

provide Arab countries with the opportunity to discuss

during a side meeting, the initiative of the Honorable

President of the United States for establishing a

partnership of peace and a Free Trade Area Zone with Arab

countries. Accordingly, I have extended invitations to

representatives of the Governments of Morocco, Tunisia,

Algeria, Egypt, Yemen, Oman, Qatar, Bahrain, Saudi

Arabia, Kuwait, UAE and the Palestinian Authority to

attend a working lunch on the sidelines of the

Extraordinary Annual Meeting of the World Economic Forum,

to be held under the Patronage of His Majesty King

Abdullah II, at 1:30pm, on Monday, June 23, 2003, at the

Dead Sea Marriott Hotel.

I have therefore the privilege of inviting your Honorable

and the US Trade Representative, His Excellency Robert

Zoellick (your Excellency and the US Secretary of State,

the Honorable Colin Powell) to attend the said meeting in

an effort to initiate a continued dialogue and a

productive exchange of ideas on how best to realize this

important initiative.

I would greatly appreciate your confirmation in joining

us for this important gathering. In the meantime, please

accept my high esteem and consideration.

S/

Dr. Marwan Muasher

End text.

GNEHM
