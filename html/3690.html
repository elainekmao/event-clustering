SUBJECT: JORDAN: MEDIA REACTION ON SEPTEMBER 11

ANNIVERSARY, IRAQ, OCCUPIED TERRITORIES

SUMMARY

JORDANIAN DAILIES ON SEPTEMBER 11 WERE OVERWHELMINGLY

DOMINATED BY THE SEPTEMBER 11 ANNIVERSARY, THE

SITUATION IN IRAQ, AND THE U.S. REACTION TO ARAFAT\'S

SPEECH.

MOST EDITORIAL WRITERS COMMENTED ON THE SEPTEMBER 11

ANNIVERSARY. COMMENTARIES REFLECTED A MIXTURE OF

SYMPATHY WITH THE BEREAVED FAMILIES, COUPLED WITH FEAR

OF, AND ANGER AGAINST U.S. POLICY, PARTICULARLY IN

PALESTINE AND IRAQ.

NEWS REPORTS

THE ANNIVERSARY OF SEPTEMBER 11 DOMINATED NEWS REPORTS

ON SEPTEMBER 11, AND TWO OF THE THREE MAIN ARABIC

DAILIES PRODUCED SPECIAL EDITIONS MARKING THE OCCASION.

NEWS COVERAGE CONCENTRATED ON THE SECURITY PRECAUTIONS

BEING TAKEN TO AVOID FURTHER ATTACKS ON THE DAY.

COVERAGE OF THE IRAQI CRISIS COMPRISED TWO ELEMENTS:

THE MESSAGE FROM SADDAM HUSSEIN TO KING ABDULLAH, WHICH

WAS DELIVERED BY IRAQI VICE-PRESIDENT TAHA YASSIN

RAMADAN. NEWSPAPERS REPORTED THAT THE KING ASSURED

RAMADAN OF JORDAN\'S EFFORTS TO PREVENT AN ATTACK ON HIS

COUNTRY.

ALL DAILIES REPORTED THE U.S. DISMISSAL OF ARAFAT\'S

SPEECH IN WHICH HE CALLED FOR A HALT ON OPERATIONS

AGAINST ISRAELI CIVILIANS.

EDITORIAL COMMENTARIES

-- WHO WILL LEARN THE LESSON AND STAND UP FOR HUMANITY?

THE LEADER COMMENTARY IN SEMI-OFFICIAL INFLUENTIAL

ARABIC DAILY AL-RAI ON SEPTEMBER 11 READ: "THE PEOPLES

AND NATIONS OF THE WORLD STOOD IN CLEAR SYMPATHY WITH

THE VICTIMS AND THEIR FAMILIES, THEY SUPPORTED THE

AMERICAN CAMPAIGN AGAINST TERROR, AND THEY SEEMED

UNITED IN REALIZING THE UGLINESS OF THE ATTACKS AND THE

SAVAGERY OF THE POLITICAL MOTIVES BEHIND THEM. BUT THE

CHANGES IN PRIORITIES OF AMERICA\'S WAR ON TERROR, AND

THE U.S. REFUSAL TO DEAL WITH THE CAUSES OF TERRORISM

HAVE CREATED DISILLUSIONMENT AMONG PEOPLES AND

DEMOCRATIC REGIMES IN THE WORLD. IN THE DAYS THAT

FOLLOWED SEPTEMBER 11 THERE EMERGED A STATE OF HATRED

AND RACIST INCITEMENT AGAINST ALL THAT IS ARAB AND

MUSLIM, WHICH RESULTED IN SUMMARY ARRESTS AND ATTACKS,

DESPITE SOME SANE VOICES THAT SAW THE WAR AS BEING

AGAINST TERROR NOT ISLAM. THE AMERICAN MOVES AGAINST

TERROR WOULD GAIN CREDIBILITY AND RESPECT BY ADOPTING A

SINGLE STANDARD IN DEFINING TERRORISM, AND IN

ACKNOWLEDGING THAT OCCUPATION IS THE WORST FORM OF

TERRORISM; SO IS PREVENTING PEOPLES FROM SELF-

DETERMINATION AND ELECTING THE LEADERSHIPS THAT THEY

CHOSE DEMOCRATICALLY. THIS FORM OF TERRORISM IS NOT

ANNULLED BY POWER OR UNILATERALISM."

-- THE EMBASSY\'S PAMPHLETS AND PEOPLE\'S CONVICTIONS

PROMINENT DAILY COLUMNIST TARIQ MASARWAH WROTE IN SEMI-

OFFICIAL INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-RAI ON SEPTEMBER

11: "THE U.S. EMBASSY DROWNED US IN ITS KINDNESS BY

ADDRESSING US AS `DEAR FRIEND\' IN A LETTER THAT

ACCOMPANIED AN INFORMATION KIT MARKING SEPTEMBER 11.

BUT THE AMERICAN POLITICAL DISCOURSE IS NOT INTERESTED

IN ADDRESSING PEOPLE AND GAINING THEIR SUPPORT. IT

SAYS: `YOU ARE EITHER WITH ME OR WITH THE TERRORISTS,"

AND: `WE SHALL GO TO WAR WHETHER THE UN, OUR ALLIES,

AND OUR FRIENDS AGREE OR NOT." THIS IS NOT THE

ATTITUDE OF SOMEONE WHO CARES ABOUT THE OPINION OF A

HUMBLE JORDANIAN JOURNALIST NOR THAT OF ALL JORDANIANS.

WE HOPE THAT THE U.S. EMBASSY IN THE FUTURE WILL

PROVIDE US WITH BOOKS ABOUT `ARIEL SHARON THE MAN OF

PEACE,\' ABOUT THE USE OF F16S AND APACHES, WHICH ARE

WEAPONS OF MASS DESTRUCTION, AGAINST THE TOWNS OF

PALESTINE, ABOUT THE NOBILITY OF THE ISRAELI

OCCUPATION, AND ABOUT THE SIGNIFICANCE OF THE U.S.

PRESIDENT\'S SIGNATURE ON THE OSLO ACCORD."

-- WHY WASHINGTON DOES NOT ALLOW US TO FEEL SYMPATHY OR

COMPASSION

FORMER PRESS ADVISOR TO KING HUSSEIN KHALED MAHADIN

WROTE IN SEMI-OFFICIAL INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-RAI

ON SEPTEMBER 11: "NO SANE PERSON WOULD DENY THAT

KILLING CIVILIANS IS A TRAVESTY AGAINST DIVINE

RELIGIONS, HUMAN VALUES, AND ALL CODES OF MORALITY.

BUT THE AMERICAN REACTION TO THIS EVENT WAS AN OPEN

CALL FOR REVENGE AGAINST ARAB AND MUSLIM CIVILIANS, AND

THAT CALL WAS PUT INTO ACTION. WHAT THE COUNTRIES THAT

OPPOSE RELIGIONS, HUMAN RIGHTS, AND MORAL VALUES SEEK

IS REVENGE FOR THE INNOCENT VICTIMS OF NEW YORK AND

WASHINGTON. BUT IT IS DIFFICULT TO ACCEPT THE LOGIC OF

FACING TERRORISM BY INDIVIDUALS WITH STATE TERRORISM.

IF WE ACCEPT THAT A KILLER SHOULD BE KILLED, THEN WE

SHOULD ALL AGREE ON A BIGGER PRINCIPLE, THAT KILLING

TENS OF THOUSANDS OF INNOCENT PEOPLE TO AVENGE A FEW

HUNDRED OR EVEN A THOUSAND EQUALLY INNOCENT PEOPLE IS

REAL TERRORISM, WHICH ROBS AN OBSERVER OF ANY CAPACITY

FOR COMPASSION. WHAT SEPTEMBER 11 CONFIRMED IS THAT

THE WEST HAS BORNE AN OBJECTIVE FOR DECADES, WHICH IS

TO DESTROY ISLAM AND TO CONVERT TRUE CHRISTIANITY INTO

A WESTERN STYLE CHRISTIANITY THAT GIVES INDULGENCES TO

THE AMERICAN WAR MACHINE."

-- WHAT HAPPENED?

THE LEADER COMMENTARY IN CENTRIST, INFLUENTIAL AMONG

THE ELITE ENGLISH DAILY JORDAN TIMES ON SEPTEMBER 11

READ: "AS THE UNITED STATES MOURNS THE INNOCENT VICTIMS

OF THE PREMEDITATED ASSAULTS ON MAJOR AMERICAN

LANDMARKS ONE YEAR AGO, ARAB COUNTRIES CAN ONLY WONDER:

WHAT HAPPENED? IN THE AFTERMATH OF A RAGTAG BOMB-

DROPPING CAMPAIGN, AFGHANISTAN LOOKS MORE CHAOTIC.WITH

AN INTERIM PRESIDENT DESPERATE FOR FUNDS TO REBUILD, IF

HE CAN STAY ALIVE LONG ENOUGH TO DO SO. USAMA BIN

LADEN HAS FADED FROM THE HEADLINES, AFTER PENTAGON

OFFICIALS PREDICTED CAPTURING HIM BY LAST CHRISTMAS.

SADDAM HUSSEIN HAS REAPPEARED ON THE SCENE AS AMERICA\'S

MOST WANTED MAN. MEANWHILE, AS THE PALESTINIANS COPE

WITH A BARRAGE OF BULLETS, BOMBS AND BULLDOZERS,

PRESIDENT BUSH LECTURES THEIR LEADERSHIP ON THE

NECESSITY TO ADOPT MORE DEMOCRATIC PROCEDURES AND

PRODUCE A CONSTITUTION - A DOCUMENT THAT ISRAEL ITSELF

LACKS. IT IS THE ALIENATION OF AMERICA\'S FRIENDS IN THE

AREA THAT FURTHERS THE GOALS OF EXTREMISTS EVERYWHERE.

WITH A TACIT GREEN LIGHT OF ISRAEL\'S DAILY OPPRESSION

AND A SO-CALLED AXIS OF EVIL THAT INCLUDES TWO OF THREE

ARAB STATES, IT IS NOT DIFFICULT TO UNDERSTAND WHY

PUBLIC SYMPATHY FOR THE U.S. HAS DRIED UP IN THE PAST

YEAR."

-- WILL IT LEARN THE LESSON?

FORMER DIRECTOR OF PRESS AND PUBLICATIONS BILAL AL-TAL

WROTE IN INDEPENDENT WEEKLY AL-LIWA\' (THE BANNER) ON

SEPTEMBER 11: "ONE OF THE MOST REMARKABLE CONSEQUENCES

OF THE SEPTEMBER 11 ATTACKS IS THAT THEY MADE USAMA BIN

LADEN THE MOST FAMOUS MAN IN THE WORLD. WHETHER WE

LIKE IT OR NOT, HE HAS BECOME A ROLE MODEL FOR A LARGE

NUMBER OF PEOPLE IN MANY PARTS OF THE WORLD. THE REASON

IS NOT ADMIRATION FOR THE PERSON OR HIS IDEOLOGICAL OR

SOCIAL THOUGHT, FOR HE DOES NOT PRESENT HIMSELF AS A

MAN OF THOUGHT IN THE FIRST PLACE. HE MERELY DESCRIBES

THE ROLE THAT HE DREW FOR HIMSELF, AS INCITING PEOPLE

TO REBEL AGAINST THEIR CHAINS AND SEEK FREEDOM. THIS

IS WHY SO MANY PEOPLE ADMIRE HIM. IN ADDITION, HE DID

NOT STOP AT CURSING IMPERIALISM, BUT TARGETED AMERICA

ITSELF, WHICH INSPIRED ALL THE PEOPLES THAT HAVE

SUFFERED FROM AMERICA\'S POLICIES. USAMA BIN LADEN IS A

CASE THAT MERITS STUDY AND REFLECTION. IT WOULD NOT BE

SUFFICIENT TO CURSE HIM ON THE MEDIA AND LAY PLANS FOR

HIS DEATH IN THE DARK CORRIDORS OF SECURITY AGENCIES,

BECAUSE IDEAS DO NOT DIE WITH THE DEATH OF THOSE WHO

BELIEVE IN THEM. THEY CAN ONLY BE DEFEATED BY MORE

POWERFUL THOUGHTS AND PRACTICES. UNLESS THE U.S.

REVISES ITS POLICIES, AND UNLESS THE PEOPLES OF THE

WORLD GAIN THEIR FREEDOM, THEN THE WORLD WILL REMAIN A

FERTILE GROUND THAT PRODUCES MANY LIKE USAMA BIN LADEN.

SO, A YEAR AFTER THE SEPTEMBER 11 ATTACKS, WE SAY THAT

THE U.S. HAS NOT SUCCEEDED IN CAPTURING BIN LADEN

BECAUSE THERE ARE PEOPLE READY TO HARBOR HIM, BECAUSE

THEY BELIEVE THAT HE IS DEFENDING THE RIGHTS OF A

NATION THAT THE U.S. SEEKS TO USURP." GNEHM
