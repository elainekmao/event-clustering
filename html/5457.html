SUBJECT: FOLLOW UP ON WTO NON-AGRICULTURE MARKET ACCESS

PROCESS

REF: A. KUWAIT 00315

B. STATE 19378

On February 1, 2003, the Ambassador met with Salah Abdul

Redha A. Khourshid, Kuwait\'s Minister for Commerce and

Industry (MOCI), to discuss reftel information and other

trade-related issues (septels). Khourshid was joined by four

MOCI officials, including three officials from the Public

Authority for Industry (PAI); the Ambassador was joined by

EconOff (note taker) and a senior local staff member. The

Ambassador explained the USG proposal on WTO NAMA

negotiations, stressed its significance, and asked for GOK

support at the February 19-21 Geneva meeting of the NAMA

Negotiating Group. Khourshid replied that he would make

certain Kuwait\'s Geneva representative received the proposal

and be prepared to speak on it ahead of the meeting. Post

will report back any additional responses from these

officials.
