SUBJECT: PRESIDENT MUGABE\'S HEALTH

CLASSIFIED BY AMBASSADOR MCDONALD. REASONS: 1.5 (B) AND

(D).

REF: STATE 171074

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 HARARE 04987 080946Z

1. (U) WE HAVE HEARD RECENT RUMORS CIRCULATING IN HARARE

THAT PRESIDENT MUGABE COLLAPSED -- IN PRIVATE -- THREE

TIMES OVER THE PAST SEVERAL WEEKS -- ONCE IN MAPUTO AND

TWICE MORE IN TRIPOLI WHILE EN ROUTE TO NEW YORK FOR THE

MILLENIUM SUMMIT. PRESS REPORTS, CITING ANONYMOUS SOURCES

IN THE PRESIDENT\'S OFFICE, HAVE SPECULATED THAT MUGABE

SUFFERED A HEART ATTACK, A STROKE, OR A SUICIDE ATTEMPT.

PRESIDENTIAL SPOKESMAN GEORGE CHARAMBA DISMISSED THE

SPECULATION, ATTRIBUTING IT TO "APRIL FOOLS," AND

PRONOUNCED MUGABE "FIT AS A FIDDLE."

2. (C) WE CANNOT CONFIRM WHETHER MUGABE DID IN FACT

COLLAPSE, BUT WE DO NOT RULE OUT THE POSSIBILITY THAT HE

COULD BE SUFFERING FROM FATIGUE OR EXHAUSTION. HE HAS HAD

A FULL TRAVEL SCHEDULE DURING THE PAST SEVERAL WEEKS, WITH

VISITS TO MAPUTO, TRIPOLI, PARIS, AND NEW YORK. WE NOTE

THAT, FROM OUR DISTANT PERSPECTIVE, THE PRESIDENT DID NOT

APPEAR TO HAVE ANY SERIOUS PHYSICAL AILMENT DURING HIS BUSY

SCHEDULE OF PUBLIC APPEARANCES WHILE ON HIS CURRENT VISIT

TO NEW YORK.

3. (C) RUMORS ABOUT MUGABE\'S HEALTH SURFACE REGULARLY

HERE. WE, HOWEVER, HAVE SEEN OR HEARD NOTHING TO SUGGEST

THAT THE 76 YEAR-OLD PRESIDENT IS SUFFERING FROM

DEBILITATING HEALTH PROBLEMS OR A DETERIORATION IN MENTAL

ACUITY (HE DID HAVE SURGERY FOR THROAT PROBLEMS IN 1996 AND

RECEIVED TREATMENT FOR A URINARY TRACT DISORDER IN 1994).

HE REMAINS PHYSICALLY ACTIVE, DOING DAILY EARLY MORNING

SIT-UPS, AND APPEARS AS PHYSICALLY VIGOROUS AND AS MENTALLY

SHARP AS EVER IN HIS PUBLIC APPEARANCES HERE.
