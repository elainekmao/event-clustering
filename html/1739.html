SUBJECT: EMBASSY REPRESENTATIONS ON ACTIONS AGAINST DISSIDENTS; WHAT DRIVES THE GVN?

REF: HCMC 0684 AND PREVIOUS

1. (SBU) REFTEL PROVIDES LATEST AVAILABLE INFORMATION ON

GVN ACTIONS AGAINST SENIOR MONKS OF THE UNIFIED BUDDHIST

CHURCH OF VIETNAM (UBCV). THE AMBASSADOR AND OTHER

EMBASSY/CONGEN OFFICIALS HAVE WEIGHED IN AT VARIOUS

LEVELS, URGING THAT THE ADMINISTRATIVE DETENTION ORDER BE

LIFTED FROM THICH QUANG DO, THAT THICH HUYEN QUANG BE

ALLOWED TO TRAVEL FREELY TO HCMC, AND THAT OTHER OUTSPOKEN

FIGURES SUCH AS FATHER NGUYEN VAN LY BE ALLOWED TO VOICE

THEIR NON-VIOLENT OPINIONS WITHOUT GOVERNMENT

INTERFERENCE. DCM SPOKE WITH MFA AND OFFICE OF THE

GOVERNMENT REPRESENTATIVES LAST WEEK, URGING THE

GOVERNMENT TO FIND A WAY TO DEMONSTRATE ITS "HUMANITARIAN"

CONCERNS OVER QUANG\'S HEALTH SITUATION AND TO AVOID ANY

PUBLIC CONFRONTATION WITH UBCV SUPPORTERS. IN THE PAST TWO

DAYS, POLCOUNS, SENIOR ADVISOR AND POLOFF MET WITH

MINISTRY OF PUBLIC SECURITY (MPS) DIRECTOR OF EXTERNAL

RELATIONS TO PRESS THESE POINTS. LIKEWISE, THE AMBASSADOR

HAS RAISED OUR CONCERNS WITH MINISTER OF PLANNING AND

INVESTMENT GIA AND OTHER SENIOR OFFICIALS. WE ALSO

CONTINUE TO PRESS OUR INTERESTS WITH MFA AND THE

GOVERNMENT COMMITTEE ON RELIGION.

2. (SBU) CLOSE POLICE SURVEILLANCE OF UBCV MONKS AT THEIR

VARIOUS PAGODAS LIKELY WILL PREVENT ANY OF THEM FROM

MEETING WITH THICH HUYEN QUANG OR THICH QUANG DO FOR THE

TIME BEING. THE AUTHORITIES\' SUCCESS IN BLOCKING THE

VISIT TO THICH HUYEN QUANG NO DOUBT WILL ONLY ADD TO THE

FRUSTRATION AND RESENTMENT OF HIS SUPPORTERS. SHOULD

QUANG\'S HEALTH EVENTUALLY FAIL WHILE STILL IN ISOLATION IN

QUANG NGAI, THE PUBLIC OUTCRY WILL GET CONSIDERABLY

STRONGER AND GO BEYOND THE UBCV AND ITS SUPPORTERS.

VIETNAM\'S DECISION TO PLACE THE ACTIVIST MONK THICH QUANG

DO UNDER ADMINISTRATIVE DETENTION, WILL CONTINUE TO HURT

VIETNAM\'S INTERNATIONAL IMAGE.

3. (SBU) THE TOUGH ACTION AGAINST DISSIDENTS BEGAN DURING

EX-PARTY CHIEF LE KHA PHIEU\'S TENURE AND IS LIKELY ON

"AUTOMATIC PILOT." THE LAST SEVERAL MONTHS HAVE SEEN A

STEADY ESCALATION OF ACTIVISM ON THE PART OF RELIGIOUS

FIGURES SEEKING LARGELY TO PROMOTE A POLITICAL AGENDA IN

VIETNAM. FOR A TIME, THEY SEEMED TO BE TRYING TO TAKE

ADVANTAGE OF THE WEAKENED POSITION OF PARTY-BOSS LE KHA

PHIEU WHOSE LEADERSHIP WAS UNDER CHALLENGE. PHIEU,

HOWEVER, SEEMED ABLE TO UTILIZE THESE ACTIVITIES AND THE

DISTURBANCES IN THE CENTRAL HIGHLANDS TO ADVANCE HIS OWN

CLAIMS FOR MORE ATTENTION TO "STABILITY," RESULTING IN THE

"GET TOUGH" POLICY AGAINST SUCH FIGURES AS FATHER LY,

THICH QUANG DO, AND HOA HAO LEADER LIEM.

4. (SBU) PHIEU\'S EFFORTS TO HIGHLIGHT THE NEED FOR

"STABILITY" WERE UNABLE TO REVERSE HIS DECLINING POLITICAL

STATURE AND HE WAS REPLACED BY NONG DUC MANH WHO CAME

ABOARD WITH A "REFORMIST" IMAGE. BUT HIS FEW SHORT WEEKS

OF TENURE SEEM TO HAVE INTENSIFIED THE "TESTING PERIOD"

BETWEEN THE TWO GROUPS. THE ACTIVISTS HAVE BEEN PUSHING

THEIR POLITICAL AGENDA TO SEE WHAT THEY CAN GET AWAY WITH

AND THE SECURITY FORCES HAVE BEEN ACTING MORE FORCEFULLY

TO DEMONSTRATE THE NEED FOR THE LEADERSHIP TO RESTRICT

THIS ACTIVISM. THE SECURITY FORCES -- ALREADY SOMEWHAT

DIMINISHED IN PRESTIGE BY PUBLIC CRITICISM AT THE PARTY

CONGRESS -- ARE PROBABLY ALSO "TESTING" TO SEE HOW THEY

MAY FARE UNDER MANH\'S LEADERSHIP. FOR HIS OWN PART,

GENERAL SECRETARY MANH STILL NEEDS TIME TO FEEL OUT THESE

COMPETING INTERESTS WITHIN THE PARTY, GOVERNMENT AND

SOCIETY BEFORE HE AND THE OTHER NEW LEADERS CAN PUT THEIR

IMPRINT ON NATIONAL POLICY. FOR THE MISSION AND THE USG,

THIS IS A TIME TO COUNSEL "MODERATION" AND TO AVOID A

PUBLIC CONFRONTATION ON THE ISSUES. WE WILL CONTINUE TO

WORK WITH ALL SIDES TO SEEK NEW AVENUES OF CONSTRUCTIVE

ENGAGEMENT.
