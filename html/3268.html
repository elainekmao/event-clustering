SUBJECT: JORDAN: MEDIA REACTION ON IRAQ, OCCUPIED

TERRITORIES

SUMMARY

NEWS REPORTS TODAY CONCENTRATED ON TWO ITEMS: JORDAN\'S

DENIAL OF PRESS REPORTS THAT JORDANIAN TERRITORY WOULD

BE USED TO LAUNCH AN ATTACK ON IRAQ, AND THE SACKING OF

THE PNA\'S CHIEF OF INTELLIGENCE IN THE WEST BANK.

EDITORIAL WRITERS COMMENTED ON THE FAILED TALKS BETWEEN

IRAQ AND THE UN, AND THE JORDANIAN GOVERNMENT\'S

DECISION TO FACILITATE THE PASSAGE OF PALESTINIANS

COMING FROM THE WEST BANK. SEMI-OFFICIAL INFLUENTIAL

ARABIC DAILY AL-RAI\'S COLUMNISTS ASKED IF THIS IS THE

START OF THE TRANSFER, WHILE CENTER-LEFT, INFLUENTIAL

ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR\'S COLUMNISTS HIGHLIGHTED THE

POSITIVE HUMANITARIAN CONSIDERATIONS INVOLVED.

NEWS REPORTS

JORDANIAN DAILIES ON JULY 8 WERE DOMINATED BY THE

JORDANIAN MINISTER OF INFORMATION\'S DENIAL OF PRESS

REPORTS THAT JORDANIAN TERRITORIES WOULD BE USED AS A

LAUNCHING GROUND FOR AN ATTACK AGAINST IRAQ, AND HIS

ACCUSATION THAT THESE REPORTS ARE PART OF AN INSIDIOUS

CAMPAIGN TO CAST DOUBT ON JORDAN\'S NATIONALIST

CREDENTIALS. THE MINISTER OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS ALSO

ADDED HIS ASSERTION THAT JORDAN RESPECTS IRAQ\'S UNITY

AND REFUSES THE USE OF VIOLENCE AGAINST THAT COUNTRY.

REPORTS ALSO ADDRESSED THE SACKING OF THE DIRECTOR OF

THE PALESTINIAN INTELLIGENCE SERVICE IN THE WEST BANK.

SEMI-OFFICIAL INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-RAI REFERRED

TO "CONFLICTING REPORTS ON THE DISMISSAL, WHILE OTHER

DAILIES REPORTED THAT THE DISMISSAL HAS TAKEN PLACE,

EDITORIAL COMMENTARIES

-- THE TALKS AND THE POLITICAL FANTASIA

PROMINENT JORDANIAN COLUMNIST TARIQ MASARWAH WROTE IN

SEMI-OFFICIAL INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-RAI ON JULY

8: "NO ONE SHOULD HAVE DELUDED HIMSELF INTO THINKING

THAT A SUCCESSFUL CONCLUSION TO THE SABRI-ANAN TALKS

WOULD HAVE OPENED THE ROAD TO A SETTLEMENT. ANA\'S TASK

WAS TO ASK IRAQ FOR THE RETURN OF THE WEAPONS

INSPECTORS, PERIOD. SHOULD HE GO BEYOND THIS SCRIPT,

HE REMEMBERS VERY WELL HIS PREDECESSOR WHO WAS SACKED

BY WASHINGTON BECAUSE HE DISOBEYED INSTRUCTIONS AND

PUBLISHED THE REPORT ON THE QANA MASSACRE IN KEEPING

WITH THE SECURITY COUNCIL RESOLUTION. IRAQ\'S PROBLEM IS

WITH THE U.S. ONLY. ARAB RELATIONS OR RELATIONS WITH

THE GULF WILL NOT PLAY ANY ROLE IN LIFTING THE

SANCTIONS, NOT IN PREVENTING THE AMERICAN AGGRESSION

AGAINST THAT COUNTRY."

-- HAS THE TRANSFER STARTED?

PROMINENT COLUMNIST FAHED AL-FANEK WROTE IN SEMI-

OFFICIAL INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-RAI ON JULY 8: "IN

1948, JORDAN OPENED ITS BORDERS TO PALESTINIAN

IMMIGRATION IN PREPARATION FOR ANNEXING THE WEST BANK.

IN 1967, JORDAN DID THE SAME ON HUMANITARIAN GROUNDS,

AND EACH TIME THIS ONLY SERVED TO EMPTY PALESTINIAN

TERRITORIES OF THEIR POPULATION AND TEMPT ISRAEL TO

ANNEX THIS TERRITORY. EGYPT, BY CONTRAST, DID NOT ACT

ON THE SAME HUMANITARIAN GROUNDS IN GAZA, WHICH IS NOW

SO DENSELY POPULATED THAT ISRAEL CANNOT WAIT TO GET RID

OF IT. IT IS NOT TRUE THAT THE NUMBER INCOMERS AND

OUTGOERS ARE EQUAL, AN ASSERTION THAT ONLY AIMS TO DENY

THE PROBLEM. IF WE ARE KEEN TO KEEP THE WEST BANK

PALESTINIAN, WE SHOULD START BY KEEPING JORDAN

JORDANIAN."

-- REGARDING THE MINISTER OF INTERIOR\'S STATEMENT ON

PROCEDURES AT THE BRIDGE

DAILY COLUMNIST YASSER ZA\'ATREH WROTE IN CENTER-LEFT,

INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR ON JULY 8: "THIS IS

A NORMAL SEASON WHEN MANY PEOPLE COME TO SEE THEIR

RELATIONS, THEN RETURN HOME. AS FOR THOSE WHO WISH TO

ESCAPE, THEY PREFER A LESS BUSY MONTH TO GO TO ANY OF

THE FOREIGN CONSULATES IN JERUSALEM TO GET AN EASY

VISA. THE MEASURES TAKEN BY THE MINISTRY OF INTERIOR

WERE NECESSARY AND WE HOPE THAT THEY WILL RESOLVE THE

SEASONAL PROBLEM. WE ALSO ADD OUR ASSERTION THAT NO

ONE MUST THINK OF THE PALESTINIAN PEOPLE AS A HERD OF

SHEEP WHO CAN SIMPLY BE LOADED ONTO TRUCKS AND DUMPED

ON THE OTHER SIDE OF THE BORDER.; DO YOU NOT REMEMBER

THE HEROES OF MARJ AL-ZUHOUR?"
