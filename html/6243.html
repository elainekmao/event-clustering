SUBJECT: OFFER OF VACCINIA IMMUNE GLOBULIN (VIG) PASSED TO

JORDAN

REF: STATE 61508

1. (SBU) On March 9, Embassy passed reftel information on VIG

to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the Jordanian Armed

Forces (JAF). If/when we receive a letter from the GOJ

officially requesting FDA authorization for the export of VIG

to Jordan in the quantities and for the purposes outlined

reftel, we will promptly convey it to the Department for

action. We would also appreciate guidance on coverage of

civilian personnel under this program.
