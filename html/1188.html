SUBJECT: DUTCH CONFIRM EU PLANS FOR MINISTERIAL TROIKA

VISIT TO MINSK UNDERWAY

REF: SECSTATE 122886

CLASSIFIED BY A/POLCOUNS ERIC LUNDBERG; REASONS 1.5 (B,D).

1.(C) DUTCH MFA UKRAINE/BELARUS OFFICERS CONFIRMED THAT THE

FRENCH EU PRESIDENCY IS ASSUMING THE EARLIER EU TASKING TO

THE PORTUGESE FOR A MINISTERIAL TROIKA VISIT TO MINSK BEFORE

THE FALL ELECTIONS. THEY ATTRIBUTE THE IMPETUS FOR THIS HIGH

LEVEL VISIT TO OSCE AMG HEAD AMB WIECK\'S EARLIER APPEALS TO

THE EU FOR HIGH LEVEL POLITICAL SUPPORT. THEY NOTE THAT,

WHILE THE NETHERLANDS PREFERRED A POLITICAL DIRECTOR LEVEL

VISIT, MOST EU STATES OPTED FOR THE MINISTERIAL LEVEL BECAUSE

THEY CONSIDERED THE CURRENT ELECTION WINDOW SUCH A HIGH

PRIORITY. ADDITIONALLY, THEY COMMENTED THAT THE PREVAILING

SENTIMENT WITHIN THE EU IS THAT A VISIT TO MINSK MUST OCCUR

BEFORE LUKASHENKO PROMULGATES A NEW ELECTION FRAMEWORK,

OPERATIONALLY MEANING THE VISIT SHOULD OCCUR WITHIN THE NEXT

TWO WEEKS.

2.(C) AS FOR THE VISIT\'S GUIDELINES, THE DUTCH MFA OFFICIALS

SAID THE TROIKA MINISTERS WOULD HEW CLOSELY TO THE OSCE

TROIKA\'S FOUR CONDITIONS AND COMMENTED THAT THE NETHERLANDS

UNDERSTANDS THE NEED FOR THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY TO SPEAK

TO LUKASHENKO WITH ONE VOICE. THEY ADDED THAT THE

NETHERLANDS WAS DEEPLY CONCERNED BY LUKASHENKO\'S "BLATANT USE

OF JUDICIAL PROCESSES TO MARGINALIZE AND LIMIT HIS POLITICAL

OPPOSITION."

3.(C) ON A BILATERAL NOTE, MFA UKRAINE DESK OFFICER HANS

SCHUTTE REMARKED THAT THE NETHERLANDS HOPES THE NEAR-TERM

VISIT OF FOREIGN MINISTER VAN AARTSEN TO KIEV WILL CONTRAST

POIGNANTLY THE WESTERN COMMUNITY\'S EMBRACE OF UKRAINE WITH

ITS VERY CRITICAL ENGAGEMENT OF BELARUS. SCHUTTE EXPLAINED

THAT VAN AARTSEN PLANS TO PRAISE UKRAINE-NATO COOPERATION,

SUCH AS THE RECENT SOFA FINALIZATION AND UKRAINIAN KFOR

CONTRIBUTIONS, AS WELL AS DEMOCRATIC DEVELOPMENT IN UKRAINE.
