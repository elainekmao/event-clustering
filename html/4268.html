SUBJECT: TURKS LUKEWARM ABOUT LUKASHENKO\'S ATTENDANCE AT

PRAGUE

REF: STATE 224591

Classified by Pol-Mil Counselor Stuart Brown, reasons 1.5,

b/d.

(C) Pol-MilOff delivered reftel demarche Nov. 8 to MFA DDG

for NATO Affairs Sanivar Kizildeli. Kizildeli noted that

Turkey had voted with the consensus when the PermReps decided

to demarche Minsk on Lukashenko\'s non-attendance at the

summit. Turkey would act in accordance with that decision.

However, because the Turks have bilateral relations with

Minsk and prefer to keep the dialogue with Belarus open and

not push the country any farther away from NATO, Turkey will

discourage Lukashenko\'s attendance through oral, vice

written, messages. Kizildeli said it would "not be such a

bad idea" for Lukashenko to hear the truth about the manner

in which he is running Belarus at the EAPC, because he would

not hear the truth from his own people. But she reiterated

Turkey would honor its commitment to discourage him from

attending.
