SUBJECT: UAEG SUPPORT FOR IRAQ\'S MINISTRIES

REFS: A) STATE 130794 B) ABU DHABI 2256

1. (U) Classified by Charge D\'Affaires Richard A.

Albright, for reasons 1.5 (b) and (d).

2. (C) Post delivered ref A talking points to the

Office of MFA Assistant Undersecretary Abdullah Rashid

Al-Nuami, who is heading a delegation to Washington

this week to discuss Iraq reconstruction opportunities

for the UAE. The Consulate General also delivered the

talking points to the Offices of Executive Chairman of

Dubai Ports Authority, Customs, and Jebel Ali Free

Zone Authority Sultan bin Sulayem, and Chairman of the

Dubai Chamber of Commerce Obaid Al-Tayer -- both of

whom are accompanying Al-Nuami to Washington.

3. (C) Comment: The UAEG offered to the informal

working group on Iraq reconstruction 5/6 to send UAE

oil sector and banking experts to assist in the

rebuilding of Iraq\'s ministries (ref B). We will

follow-up with Al-Nuami, Sulayem, and Al-Tayer upon

their return from Washington, but suggest that the

Department and ORHA engage directly with the UAE

delegation on staffing gaps in Iraqi ministries this

week. End comment.

Albright
