1. SEPTEMBER 14 EDITORIALS IN THE ZIMBABWEAN PRINT

MEDIA FOCUS ON THE TERRORIST ATTACKS IN THE U. S. THE

NEWSPAPERS ALSO CARRY LENGTHY OPINION PIECES ABOUT THE

DISASTER. EXCERPTS FOLLOW:

2. UNDER HEADLINE "TERROR, VIOLENCE WON\'T END WORLD\'S

CONFLICTS," THE INDEPENDENT DAILY "THE DAILY NEWS"

COMMENTS:

"TUESDAY\'S ATTACKS ON THE WORLD TRADE CENTER IN

NEW YORK AND THE PENTAGON IN WASHINGTON MUST HAVE

INDUCED IN MOST ZIMBABWEANS A SENSE OF GENUINE

HORROR, OUTRAGE AND A CERTAIN HELPLESSNESS.

WHATEVER POLITICAL DIFFERENCES THE MASTERS OF THE

PERPETRATORS OF THE SAVAGE ATTACKS HAD WITH THE

UNITED STATES ADMINISTRATION COULD NEVER JUSTIFY

THE COLD-BLOODED MURDER OF INNOCENT CIVILIANS

GOING ABOUT THEIR DAILY BUSINESS IN A FREE AND

DEMOCRATIC COUNTRY. . . THE

U. S. GOVERNMENT HAS NOT ALWAYS ACQUITTED ITSELF

WELL AS WHAT ITS ENEMIES HAVE CALLED \'THE WORLD

POLICEMAN.\' THERE HAVE BEEN MISTAKES AND SOME OF

THE PEOPLE WHO HAVE RESORTED TO VIOLENCE AGAINST

UNCLE SAM HAVE NOT BEEN FOREIGNERS, LIKE THE SAUDI

DISSIDENT, OSAMA BIN LADEN, THE NUMBER ONE SUSPECT

IN THE LATEST OUTRAGE. THE OKLAHOMA BOMBING IN

1995 WAS INITIALLY BLAMED ON ARAB TERRORISTS,

UNTIL IT TRANSPIRED THAT THIS WAS A

HOME-GROWN ATROCITY. . . IN THE MIDDLE EAST,

SUCCESSIVE U. S. ADMINISTRATIONS HAVE GRAPPLED IN

VAIN WITH THE DIFFERENCES BETWEEN TWO COMBATANTS,

THE ARABS AND THE JEWS. . . SOME OF WASHINGTON\'S

PRESCRIPTIONS HAVE BEEN SO NAVE THEY HAVE NOT

HELPED TO COOL TEMPERS IN JERUSALEM OR THE WEST

BANK. BUT GEORGE W. BUSH\'S \'HANDS OFF\' POLICY HAS

PROBABLY MADE PEACE MORE DIFFICULT TO ACHIEVE.

YET THAT IS STILL NOT A GOOD REASON TO BLOW UP

BUILDINGS AND KILL THOUSANDS OF PEOPLE - IF IT IS

PROVED THIS OUTRAGE WAS SPURRED BY ARABS INCENSED

BY THE ADMINISTRATION\'S PERCEIVED

PRO-ISRAELI BIAS. FOR THE WORLD, THE LATEST

DISPLAY OF HUMANKIND\'S OWN INHUMANITY TO HUMANKIND

MUST REMIND PEOPLE THAT TERROR AND VIOLENCE MAY

HAVE ONLY

SHORT-TERM ADVANTAGES, BUT IN THE END THEY WILL

NOT SOLVE CONFLICTS. THE UNITED STATES WILL

SURVIVE TUESDAY\'S HORROR. . . THE PEOPLE WHO SENT

THE SUICIDE PILOTS HURTLING INTO THE TWIN TOWERS

OF THE WORLD TRADE CENTER AND THE PENTAGON MOST

CERTAINLY DESERVE SEVERE PUNISHMENT, AS DO THE

COUNTRIES WHICH AIDED AND ABETTED THEM. BUT IF

THE RETALIATION CAUSES THE SAME MASSIVE LOSS OF

CIVILIAN LIFE THAT WAS WITNESSED ON TUESDAY, THEN

THE WORLD WILL NOT HAVE LEARNT ANYTHING FROM THE

UNBRIDLED USE OF POWER. . . RESTRAINT IS CALLED

FOR AS ANY EXCESSIVE USE OF FORCE MIGHT TURN

SYMPATHY INTO THE SAME OUTRAGE THAT GREETED

TUESDAY\'S INSANITY."

AUTHORED BY TIM CHIGODO ON PAGE 11 UNDER HEADLINE

"U. S. TERRORIST ATTACKS: TRAGEDY FOR ENTIRE

INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY." EXCERPTS:

"THE TERRORIST ATTACKS ON THE U. S. ON TUESDAY. .

.HAVE DEVASTATED THE WHOLE WORLD AND SHOULD BE

CONDEMNED BY THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY. THE

UNPRECEDENTED ATTACKS HAVE WIDE IMPLICATIONS AND

ARE A DEVELOPMENT WHICH NEEDS QUICK ACTION. ALL

INTERNATIONAL ORGANIZATIONS SHOULD STAND UP AND

CONDEMN ALL FORMS OF TERRORISM. . . AS THE WORLD

MOURNS WITH RELATIVES OF PEOPLE WHO DIED IN THE

5. UNDER HEADLINE "A DAY IN INFAMY," IDEN WETHERELL,

EDITOR OF THE INDEPENDENT WEEKLY "ZIMBABWE

INDEPENDENT," WROTE THE FOLLOWING PIECE UNDER THE

"EDITOR\'S MEMO" COLUMN IN THE SEPTEMBER 14 EDITION:

". . .I WATCHED NUMBLY THE SHOCKING PICTURES

BEAMED FROM NEW YORK. . . IT WAS A TERRIBLE,

UNFORGETTABLE SIGHT MADE WORSE BY THE KNOWLEDGE

THAT THOUSANDS OF INNOCENT VICTIMS WERE CAUGHT

INSIDE. . . WE SHALL SOON KNOW WHICH FANATICS

SENT THOUSANDS OF INNOCENT PEOPLE TO THEIR DEATHS.

AND HOPEFULLY JUDGEMENT WILL BE QUICK IN COMING.

TERRORISTS EVERYWHERE DESERVE THE SAME ROBUST

RESPONSE BY SOCIETY. NO DOUBT THERE WILL BE THE

USUAL APOLOGISTS FOR EXTREMISM WHO ARGUE THAT

THESE EVENTS AND THE ATTACK ON THE PENTAGON WERE

RETRIBUTION FOR U. S. SUPPORT FOR ISRAEL - THE

SAME PEOPLE WHO BRANDED ISRAEL A RACIST STATE IN

DURBAN WHEN IT WAS THE ONLY COUNTRY TO TAKE IN

AFRICAN REFUGEES IN THE 1980S. BUT WHATEVER

HAPPENS, THE U. S. WILL BE SCARRED BY THE EVENTS

OF TUESDAY. SEPTEMBER 11, 2001 IS SURELY A DAY

THAT WILL LIVE IN INFAMY. AND THE WORLD WILL
