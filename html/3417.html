SUBJECT: JORDAN: MEDIA REACTION ON KING\'S U.S. VISIT,

EXPLOSION AT HEBREW UNIVERSITY

SUMMARY

TWO THEMES DOMINATED JORDANIAN DAILIES ON AUGUST 1: THE

FIRST WAS KING ABDULLAH\'S OPEN DISCUSSION WITH

PARTICIPANTS IN ET FORTUNE INSTITUTE, AND THE OTHER WAS

THE EXPLOSION AT THE HEBREW UNIVERSITY, WHICH DAILIES

DESCRIBED AS "THE FIRST PALESTINIAN RESPONSE TO THE

GAZA MASSACRE."

EDITORIAL WRITERS COMMENTED ON THE FORTHCOMING MEETING

BETWEEN KING ABDULLAH AND PRESIDENT BUSH, AND ON U.S.

POLICY IN THE REGION, WHICH IS NOW INCREASINGLY

REFERRED TO AS U.S.-ISRAELI POLICY.

NEWS REPORTS

ALL JORDANIAN DAILIES ON AUGUST 1 LED WITH REPORTS ON

KING ABDULLAH\'S OPEN DISCUSSION WITH PARTICIPANTS IN

FORTUNE INSTITUTE CONFERENCE. NEWSPAPERS HIGHLIGHTED

TWO ELEMENTS FROM THE DISCOURSE: THE NEED TO ESTABLISH

A PALESTINIAN STATE WITHIN THREE YEARS, THIS BEING THE

KEY TO STABILITY AND PROSPERITY IN THE REGION, AND THAT

THERE IS NO BALANCE IN THE U.S. VISION OF THE MIDDLE

EAST.

ALL JORDANIAN DAILIES REPORTED THE PALESTINIAN

"COMMANDO" OPERATION, WHICH THEY DESCRIBED AS "THE

FIRST PALESTINIAN RESPONSE TO THE MASSACRE OF GAZA."

EDITORIAL COMMENTARIES

-- THE JORDANIAN-AMERICAN SUMMIT: ANOTHER LINK IN A

CONTINUOUS EFFORT

DAILY COLUMNIST MAHMOUD AL-RIMAWI WROTE IN SEMI-

OFFICIAL INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-RAI ON AUGUST 1:

"THE JORDANIAN-AMERICAN SUMMIT SCHEDULED FOR TODAY,

THURSDAY, IS ANOTHER OPPORTUNITY TO REMIND [THE U.S.

ADMINISTRATION] OF THE CONSTANTS OF JORDAN\'S POSITION

REGARDING THE [ARAB-ISRAELI CONFLICT] AND THE

SETTLEMENT IN THE REGION. THE U.S. SHOULD BE REMINDED

OF ITS RESPONSIBILITY ON THIS ISSUE, SINCE THE AMERICAN

PERFORMANCE SO FAR HAS ONLY EXACERBATED THE SITUATION

INSTEAD OF CALMING IT."

-- BORN TO KILL

OCCASIONAL COLUMNIST KHALIL AL-SAWAHRI WROTE IN CENTER-

LEFT, INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR ON AUGUST 1:

"DURING SHARON\'S BLACK RULE, ISRAEL HAS SURPASSED

ITSELF IN COINING EUPHEMISMS FOR THE ITS CRIMES AGAINST

THE PALESTINIANS. ASSASSINATION IS NOW TERMED TARGETED

KILLING, MASSACRES ARE TERMED PREEMPTIVE SELF-DEFENSE,

AND KILLING ARAB CHILDREN IS PERMISSIBLE SINCE THEY ARE

TOMORROW\'S TERRORISTS. MEANWHILE, PALESTINIAN SELF-

DEFENSE IS TERMED TERRORISM. IT IS STRANGE AND

REGRETTABLE THAT THE INTERNATIONAL MEDIA QUICKLY LAP UP

THESE ZIONIST TERMS, AND ACCEPT THAT SELF-DEFENSE IS

TERRORISM, WHILE STATE TERRORISM IS A LEGITIMATE RIGHT.

MEANWHILE, ISRAELIS PRACTICE COLD-BLOODED MURDER AND

PROUDLY WRITE ON THEIR CLOTHES: `BORN TO KILL.\'"

-- SUBJUGATING THE ARAB MIND

TAHER AL-ADWAN, CHIEF EDITOR OF INDEPENDENT, MASS-

APPEAL ARABIC DAILY AL-ARAB AL-YAWM WROTE ON AUGUST 1:

"ARAFAT\'S DICTATORSHIP IS NOT THE MAIN PROBLEM FACING

THE PALESTINIANS. THEIR MAIN PROBLEMS ARE THE

OCCUPATION AND THE PRACTICES OF THE WAR CRIMINAL

SHARON. NOR IS SADDAM\'S DICTATORSHIP THE MAIN PROBLEM

OF THE IRAQIS. THEY SUFFER FROM SIEGE AND THE DAILY

AIR RAIDS. MOREOVER, WHAT ISRAEL AND THE U.S. PLAN IS

NOT FOR THE BENEFIT OF THE PALESTINIANS AND IRAQIS, BUT

TO CONVERT THEM INTO DEAD BODIES. THIS IS A VERY

DANGEROUS TURNING POINT BECAUSE IF THE PLAN IS CARRIED

OUT, THERE WILL BE A LONG PERIOD OF SUBJUGATION, NOT

ONLY OF OUR NATIONS, BUT ALSO OF OUR MINDS AND OUR

WILLS." GNEHM
