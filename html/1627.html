SUMMARY AND INTRODUCTION

------------------------

1. (C) THE IMPENDING ARRIVAL OF A NEW ASSISTANT SECRETARY OF STATE

FOR AFRICA PROVIDES AN EXCELLENT OPPORTUNITY TO TAKE STOCK OF OUR

RELATIONSHIP ITH ZIMBABWE. THAT RELATIONSHIP HAS DETERIORATED

BADLY AS THE CURRENT GOVERNMENT HAS INCREASINGLY ELIED ON VIOLENCE

AND INTIMIDATION TO PRESERVE IS HOLD ON POWER AND HAS DRIVEN A

ONCE-PROMISING CONOMY INTO THE GROUND. CAUGHT IN A PAN-

AFRICAIST TIME WARP, THE PRESENT LEADERSHIP SEEM CLUELES ABOUT

HOW THEY COME OFF TO US AND WHAT THEY MUS DO TO REVERSE ZIMBABWE\'S

INTERNATIONAL ISOLATION. UNDER THESE CIRCUMSTANCES, WE BELIEVE

THERE IS VALUE IN DEVELOPING A DIPLOMATIC "ROADMAP" WHICH SPELLS

OUT THE STEPS PRESIDENT MUGABE AND HIS GOVERNMENT MUST TAKE IN

ORDER TO EXTRICATE THEMSELVES FROM THE CURRENT MESS AND IMPROVE

THEIR RELATIONSHIP WITH THE UNITED STATES.

2. (C) TOWARD THAT END, WE HAVE ATTEMPTED IN THIS CABLE TO DISTILL

OUR CONCERNS ABOUT THE CURRENT STATE OF AFFAIRS IN ZIMBABWE INTO A

LIST OF SPECIFIC, ACHIEVABLE ACTIONS THE GOZ MUST TAKE IN THE SHORT

TERM IF IT WANTS TO IMPROVE ITS RELATIONSHIP WITH US. AS WE

ENVISION IT, THE ROADMAP COULD BE PRESENTED AS A NON-PAPER TO

PRESIDENT MUGABE, FOREIGN MINISTER MUDENGE, OR ANOTHER SENIOR

OFFICIAL, AND WOULD SUBSEQUENTLY GUIDE OUR DIPLOMATIC ENGAGEMENT

WITH THE GOVERNMENT OF ZIMBABWE. THE GOZ MAY BE DISMISSIVE OF SUCH

AN EFFORT ON OUR PART, BUT WE BELIEVE THERE IS VALUE NONETHELESS IN

MAKING ABSOLUTELY CLER THE SPECIFIC ACTIONS WE CONSIDER

PREREQUISITESTO AN IMPROVED RELATIONSHIP. WE REALIZE THAT

PRESENTING THE GOZ WITH A MERE LIST OF DEMANDS IS PROBABLY NOT THE

MOST CONSTRUCTIVE APPROACH. THE ROADMAP SHOULD ALSO LAY OUT

PARTICULAR PRESSURES AND INCENTIVES THAT THE UNITED STATES IS

PREPARED TO UTILIZE IN OUR EFFORT TO ELICIT A POSITIVE CHANGE IN

POLICY AND BEHAVIOR.

3. (C) WE HOPE THE TALKING POINTS RECOMMENDED BELOW MIGHT ALSO BE

HELPFUL TO A/S-DESIGNATE KANSTEINER IN HIS SENATE CONFIRMATION

HEARINGS, WHERE WE ASSUME HE WILL BE ASKED WHAT HE PLANS TO DO

ABOUT ZIMBABWE\'S RECALCITRANT LEADER. END SUMMARY AND

INTRODUCTION.

SUGGESTED SUBSTANCE OF ROAD MAP

-------------------------------

4. (C) WE PROVIDE BELOW RECOMMENDATIONS ON THE CATEGORIES (AND

SPECIFIC TALKING POINTS) WHICH MIGHT FORM THE SUBSTANCE OF A

ROADMAP, AS WELL AS SUGGESTIONS OF POSSIBLE STICKS AND CARROTS. WE

WOULD WELCOME THE DEPARTMENT\'S FEEDBACK, BOTH ON THE ROADMAP

CONCEPT AND OUR SUGGESTED SUBSTANCE. WE HAVE NOT ADDRESSED

TIMELINES OR THE QUESTION OF WHICH GOZ ACTIONS MIGHT ELICIT WHICH

REACTIONS FROM US, LEAVING SUCH REFINEMENT UP TO THE DEPARTMENT AND

THE INTERAGENCY DECISION-MAKING PROCESS.

-----------

BEING ALLOWED TO TERRORIZE ZIMBABWEAN CITIZENS -- FARMERS, FARM

WORKERS, TEACHERS, JUDGES, JOURNALISTS, BUSINESS OWNERS, AND

ANYONE SUSPECTED OF SUPPORTING THE OPPOSITION. ARREST AND

PROSECUTE THEIR LEADERS.

-- DISCONTINUE DEPLOYMENT OF SECURITY FORCES IN HARARE\'S HIGH-
