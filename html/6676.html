SUBJECT: FONMIN MUASHER WILL NOT ATTEND MARCH 24 CAIRO ARAB

LEAGUE MINISTERIAL

REF: STATE 75616

Classified By: A/DCM Doug Silliman for reasons 1.5 (B)(D)

1. (C) Ambassador spoke with Foreign Minister Marwan

Muasher March 23 to deliver ref points on the planned March

24 Arab League ministerial meeting in Cairo. The Ambassador

asked that Jordan work to avoid statements or actions that

would undermine current efforts to disarm the Iraqi regime

and liberate the Iraqi people, and to help the meeting focus

on support for a new Iraq.

2. (C) Muasher responded that he was sure the ministerial

would be "a bad meeting," especially "with Libya in the

chair." Consequently, he did not want to be associated with

it and would not attend. He said he did not expect "the Gulf

Foreign Ministers" to attend. Muasher said Jordan\'s

Ambassador in Cairo will represent Jordan, and that he has

instructions to avoid language that will undermine coalition

efforts, and to encourage discussion of the future of Iraq.
