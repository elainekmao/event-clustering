SUBJECT: PHU QUOC ISLAND - BULLISH ON DEVELOPMENT BUT. . .

REF: A. HANOI 2200 B. HANOI 2232

1. (U) SUMMARY. PHU QUOC ISLAND DISTRICT OFFICIALS ARE

OPTIMISTIC ABOUT DEVELOPMENT BEYOND AGRICULTURE AND FISHING,

ITS TRADITIONAL ECONOMIC MAINSTAYS. THEY HOPE THAT ITS

TROPICAL ISLAND SETTING AND IMPROVING (THOUGH STILL WEAK)

INFRASTRUCTURE WILL ENCOURAGE DEVELOPMENT, ATTRACT FOREIGN

INVESTMENT, AND BRING IN MORE DOMESTIC AND FOREIGN TOURISTS.

THE POTENTIAL FOR TOURISM IS REAL BUT WILL PROBABLY NEED

VASTLY MORE RESOURCES THAN NOW AVAILABLE. END SUMMARY.

2. (U) POLOFF AND POL FSN VISITED PHU QUOC ISLAND ON

SEPTEMBER 4. (REFTELS REPORTED ON COUNTERTERRORISM

DISCUSSIONS AND NARCOTICS ISSUES, RESPECTIVELY). THE ISLAND

IS LOCATED OFF THE SOUTHWEST COAST OF VIETNAM, 40 KILOMETERS

WEST OF THE VIETNAMESE MAINLAND AND ABOUT 15 KILOMETERS

SOUTH OF CAMBODIA. IT IS ADMINISTERED AS A DISTRICT OF KIEN

GIANG PROVINCE. THERE ARE GOOD BEACHES AROUND MOST OF THE

ISLAND, ESPECIALLY ON THE WEST AND SOUTHEAST SIDES. UNLIKE

THE DELTA MAINLAND, MUCH OF THE ISLAND IS HILLY AND HEAVILY

FORESTED.

3. (U) ACCORDING TO NGO RANG DONG, VICE CHAIRMAN OF THE

DISTRICT PEOPLE\'S COMMITTEE, THE ISLAND CONSISTS OF ABOUT

600 SQUARE KILOMETERS AND HAS A POPULATION OF ABOUT 80,000,

97 PERCENT OF WHOM ARE ETHNIC KINH. THE MAIN TOWN, DUONG

DONG, IS LOCATED ON THE WEST SIDE OF THE ISLAND, WITH A

POPULATION OF ABOUT 30,000. DONG NOTED THAT AGRICULTURE "IS

MORE DIFFICULT THAN ON THE MAINLAND" DUE TO THE POOR QUALITY

OF SOIL AND THE LACK OF FLAT LAND. FISHING HAS

TRADITIONALLY BEEN MORE IMPORTANT; PHU QUOC IS ALSO FAMOUS

IN VIETNAM FOR ITS HIGH QUALITY FISH SAUCE AS WELL AS BLACK

GETTING THERE IS HALF THE FUN

-----------------------------

4. (U) THE ISLAND IS REACHED EITHER VIA FLIGHTS FROM HO

CHI MINH CITY (ONE HOUR) OR THE PROVINCIAL CAPITAL OF RACH

GIA (ABOUT 25 MINUTES), OR VIA A DAILY FERRY FROM RACH GIA

(ABOUT SEVEN HOURS). ONE FOREIGN TOURIST DESCRIBED THE

FERRY TRIP SIMPLY AS "HELLISH" BUT CHEAP AT USD FOUR.

DONG CLAIMED THAT THE PROVINCE IS NOW IMPLEMENTING A PLAN

FOR A HIGH-SPEED FERRY THAT WILL REDUCE TRAVEL TIME IN HALF,

BUT WAS UNABLE TO SAY WHEN THE NEW FERRY SERVICE MIGHT

ACTUALLY BEGIN. ONCE ON LAND, THE ROAD SYSTEM IS QUITE

BASIC; ONLY ABOUT 27 KILOMETERS OF THE 60 KILOMETERS OF ROAD

ARE PAVED, DONG ADMITTED. HE NONETHELESS ASSURED POLOFFS

THAT "IF YOU COME BACK IN FIVE YEARS, IT WILL LOOK

COMPLETELY DIFFERENT."

--------------------

COUNTING ON TOURISM

--------------------

5. (U) TRAN QUOC KHANH, CHIEF OF THE DISTRICT INDUSTRIAL

AND TRADE AFFAIRS OFFICE, NOTED THAT TOURISM IS GROWING

STEADILY. WHILE HE WAS UNABLE TO PROVIDE PRECISE FIGURES,

KHANH ESTIMATED THAT TOURISM IS GROWING AT "FIVE TO 10

PERCENT" PER YEAR. HE CITED THE ISSUANCE OF GOVERNMENT

DECREE 97 IN AUGUST 2000 AS A KEY MILESTONE, CREATING IN

DUONG DONG TOWN AND THE SURROUNDING AREA AN "OPEN ECONOMIC

OFFICIALS TOLD AMBASSADOR IN JULY 2002 THAT THE PROVINCE WAS

6. (U) TOURISTS ARE NOW ABOUT EVENLY SPLIT BETWEEN FOREIGN

AND DOMESTIC, DEPENDING ON THE SEASON, ACCORDING TO KHANH.

DURING SUMMER AND FALL, THERE ARE RELATIVELY MORE VIETNAMESE

TOURISTS, MOST COMING IN ORGANIZED GROUPS OR THROUGH

SPONSORING ORGANIZATIONS (WORK UNITS, WOMEN\'S ASSOCIATIONS,

ETC.). FOREIGN TOURISTS TEND TO ARRIVE DURING THE WINTER

MONTHS. THE ASSISTANT MANAGER OF PHU QUOC\'S SOLE THREE STAR

HOTEL SEPARATELY CLAIMED TO HAVE HAD "ABOUT 80 PERCENT

OCCUPANCY" OVER THE PAST THREE MONTHS, WITH INCREASING

NUMBERS OF ORGANIZED JAPANESE GROUPS, AS WELL AS FRENCH AND

GERMAN TOURISTS.

OPEN FOR BUSINESS - SORT OF

---------------------------

8. (U) KHANH ALSO EXPRESSED A HOPE THAT INCREASED TOURISM

COULD FORM THE BASIS FOR MORE FOREIGN DIRECT INVESTMENT. HE

ASKED THAT POLOFFS TAKE BACK TO THE US EMBASSY THE MESSAGE

THAT PHU QUOC IS INTERESTED IN FOREIGN INVESTMENT,

ESPECIALLY IN THE TOURISM AND INDUSTRY SECTORS. IN THE

TOURIST SECTOR, KHANH SUGGESTED A JOINT VENTURE WORLD-CLASS

RESORT MIGHT BE FEASIBLE. IN THE INDUSTRIAL SECTOR, KHANH

SPECIFICALLY POINTED TOWARD SEAFOOD PROCESSING AS AN AREA

WITH "GREAT POTENTIAL." KHANH ADMITTED THAT, AS FAR AS HE

KNOWS, THERE IS NO US FOREIGN DIRECT INVESTMENT AT THIS

TIME. HOWEVER, PHU QUOC AUTHORITIES "WOULD VERY MUCH

WELCOME US INVESTMENT," HE DECLARED.

9. (U) DESPITE KHANH\'S APPARENT INTEREST IN ATTRACTING

FOREIGN INVESTMENT, PHU QUOC HAS NOT YET ESTABLISHED AN

INVESTMENT PROMOTION OFFICE, HE ADMITTED. KHANH EXPRESSED A

HOPE THAT THE PROVINCIAL AUTHORITIES WOULD ESTABLISH SUCH AN

OFFICE SOON, BUT HE WAS UNABLE TO PREDICT WHEN. INSTEAD,

THE THREE STAR HOTEL, FOR A FEE OF USD 50, PROMISES TO

-------

COMMENT

-------

10. (U) PHU QUOC ARGUABLY HAS THE NATURAL BEAUTY AND

TROPICAL CLIMATE TO COMPETE FOR INTERNATIONAL TOURISTS WITH

THAILAND\'S PHUKET OR INDONESIA\'S BALI. HOWEVER, WITHOUT

SIGNIFICANT COMMITMENT OF RESOURCES DEVOTED TO

INFRASTRUCTURE DEVELOPMENT, AND EVEN MORE FAVORABLE

INVESTMENT CONDITIONS THAN NOW EXIST, PHU QUOC\'S TOURISM

BOOM IS APT TO REMAIN LIMITED, AND ITS ECONOMIC PROSPECTS

MODEST.
