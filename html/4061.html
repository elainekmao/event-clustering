1. (C) SUMMARY. ON SEPTEMBER 30, ITALIAN MINDEF MARTINO

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 ROME 05184 01 OF 03 232138Z

REVIEWED OEF, ISAF/AFGHANISTAN DEVELOPMENTS, IRAQ, DEFENSE

PROCUREMENT, AND THE ICC WITH USD/P FEITH. MARTINO

PINPOINTED MARCH 1 AS THE TARGET DATE FOR DEPLOYING THE

ALPINI TO AFGHANISTAN (COMMENT: PER REFTEL, THE PARLIAMENT ON

OCTOBER 3 APPROVED THE MISSION.) ITALY\'S KFOR COMMAND ROLE

AND BUDGET CONSTRAINTS--WHICH MARTINO SAID WERE HAMPERING

DEFENSE PLANNING--WILL PREVENT THE GOI FROM TAKING ON ANY

ISAF OR ISAF-LIKE LEADERSHIP RESPONSIBILITIES AT THIS TIME.

THE THREAT OF MILITARY ACTION AGAINST IRAQ STRENGTHENS THE

UN, MARTINO SAID. HE SPECIFIED THAT A DECISION TO USE FORCE

WAS A SEPARATE MATTER, BUT THOUGHT THAT IF THE U.S. MOVES TO

ASSEMBLE A MILITARY COALITION, FRANCE, RUSSIA, AND EVEN

GERMANY WOULD PARTICIPATE. REGARDING ITALY\'S DESIRE TO SIGN

A DECLARATION OF PRINCIPLES WITH THE U.S., FEITH NOTED THAT

COMMERCIAL RELATIONS WITH THIRD COUNTRIES WAS STILL AN ISSUE,

BUT SAID IT WAS SOLUBLE. THE DEFENSE MINISTRY IS STUDYING

HOW TO MINIMIZE THE IMPACT OF THE ICC ON PEACEKEEPING

OPERATIONS. MARTINO CALLED AN ARTICLE 98 AGREEMENT A GOOD,

IF IMPERFECT, STOPGAP TO MEET U.S. NEEDS. END SUMMARY.

MARTINO, OF BEING WAR-MONGERS.

6. (C) FEITH DESCRIBED SOME OF HIS IMPRESSIONS FROM HIS

JUST-COMPLETED VISIT TO

AFGHANISTAN. THERE IS AN URGENT NEED TO SPEED THE TRAINING

OF THE AFGHAN ARMY

AND POLICE, SO THAT WE CAN GIVE THE GOA MORE RESPONSIBILITY

FOR THE COUNTRY AND

AVOID CREATING A CULTURE OF OVERDEPENDENCE ON OUTSIDE HELP.

MARTINO AGREED,

NOTING THAT OVER-RELIANCE ON THE WEST WAS STILL A MAJOR

PROBLEM IN THE BALKANS.

FEITH SAID WE HAVE TO BUILD UP THE GOA\'S TOOLS, PARTICULARLY

THE POLICE AND A

MEANS FOR THE CENTRAL GOVERNMENT TO BECOME THE CHIEF CONDUIT

FOR INTERNATIONAL AID.

WHEN HE MENTIONED HIS MEETING WITH MINDEF FAHIM KHAN, MARTINO

INTERJECTED THAT

FAHIM REMINDED HIM OF A REAL SICILIAN GODFATHER. FEITH SAID

THAT OUR TASK WITH

FAHIM IS TO CONVINCE HIM THAT POLITICAL MANEUVERING WILL GIVE

HIM MORE OF A PAYBACK

THAN THE BRUTE FORCE HE IS USED TO WIELDING.

EAL OF THOUGHT TO

THE AFTERMATH OF SADDAM\'S REMOVL FROM POWER. THERE WILL BE

A NEED FOR INTERNATONAL

10. (C) FOR THE THIRD TIME, MARTINO LAMENTED THE EFFECT THE

JUST-SUBMITTED BUDGET

WOULD HAVE ON THE MINISTRY\'S PLANS. ITALY WAS STILL SPENDING

JUST 1.05% OF ITS GDP

ON DEFENSE, ALTHOUGH THE GOI FULLY APPRECIATED THE NEED FOR

ITALY AND ALL OF EUROPE

TO SPEND MORE ON CAPABILITY-BUILDING. THE GOVERNMENT HAD

MADE A COMMITMENT TO

IMPROVE THIS RATIO TO 1.5% BY 2006. MARTINO HOPED THIS GOAL

WOULD BE ACHIEVED,

EVEN THOUGH THIS YEAR\'S BUDGET CONSTRAINTS MEANT NO PROGRESS

IN THAT DIRECTION.

HE QUOTED WHAT LORD ROBERTSON HAD SAID ABOUT ITALY\'S DEFENSE

CAPABILITIES IN A

RECENT MEETING WITH BERLUSCONI: "YOUR OUTPUT IS ADMIRABLE;

YOUR INPUT IS INADEQUATE."
