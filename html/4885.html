SUBJECT: TERRORIST FINANCE: KUWAIT - STATUS OF GRF FREEZE

REF: A. KUWAIT 5172

B. STATE 216024 AND PREVIOUS

Classified By: Charge\' d\'Affaires a.i. Frank C. Urbancic for reasons 1.

5 (B) and (D)

(C) On 29 December EconChief spoke with Ayham Al-Omar, Deputy

Director for Americas Affairs in the Ministry of Foreign

Affairs, and requested an update on the status of the GRF

freeze order. Al-Omar reported that he had been informed by

the Central Bank of Kuwait that it had ordered all

institutions under its supervision to search for and freeze

any GRF assets, and that all institutions reported back that

they had found no such assets. Al-Omar told EconChief that

he was still waiting for written confirmation of this from

the Central Bank, and that when he received it, he would pass

it to the Embassy.
