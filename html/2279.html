SUBJECT: VATICAN REMEMBERS SEPTEMBER 11

1. THE VATICAN ENTHUSIASTICALLY EMBRACED THE WHITE HOUSE\'S

REQUEST FOR A COMMEMORATION ON THE THIRD MONTH ANNIVERSARY

OF THE SEPTEMBER 11 ATTACKS. THE GOVERNOR OF VATICAN CITY

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 02 VATICA 06414 111610Z

STATE, AMERICAN CARDINAL EDMUND SZOKA CELEBRATED A MEMORIAL

MASS IN VATICAN CITY DECEMBER 11 FOR THE VICTIMS OF THE

TERRORIST ATTACKS ON THE UNITED STATES. THE MASS BEGAN AT

PRECISELY 1446 LOCAL TIME (0846 EST) WITH THE SINGING OF THE

STAR SPANGLED BANNER AND THE PLAYING OF THE VATICAN NATIONAL

ANTHEM. AMBASSADOR NICHOLSON DID AN OLD TESTAMENT READING

DURING THE MASS, AND THE ENTIRE U.S. EMBASSY STAFF ATTENDED

THE COMMEMORATION. IN ADDITION OVER 150 OTHERS CAME TO THE

EVENT INCLUDING MANY AMBASSADORS AND MEMBERS OF THE

DIPLOMATIC CORPS ACCREDITED TO THE HOLY SEE. THIS TURNOUT

SHOWED THE DEPTH OF SOLIDARITY AND SUPPORT AMONG OUR FRIENDS

AND ALLIES FOR THE U.S. AND ITS PEOPLE. THE EVENT WAS WELL

COVERED BY VATICAN AND U.S. MEDIA. IN HIS ADDRESS, CARDINAL

SZOKA SAID POPE JOHN PAUL II IS NOT A PACIFIST AND THAT THE

PONTIFF, IN ACCORDANCE WITH OFFICIAL CATHOLIC DOCTRINE,

ACKNOWLEDGES THE RIGHT, AND INDEED, THE RESPONSIBILITY OF A

COUNTRY TO PROTECT ITS PEOPLE FROM ATTACK. CARDINAL SZOKA

LED THE CONGREGATION IN SINGING GOD BLESS AMERICA AT THE

CONCLUSION OF THE MASS.

2. AFTER THE COMMEMORATION AMBASSADOR NICHOLSON WAS

INTERVIEWED BY VATICAN RADIO. A FEATURE ON THE

COMMEMORATION WILL BE INTERNATIONALLY BROADCAST ON DECEMBER

12. NICHOLSON
