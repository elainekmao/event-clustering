SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION SECRETARY POWELL\'S OP-ED ON ZIM

UNDER FIRE; HARARE

1. The government-controlled daily "The Herald" continues

to publish discourteous articles against Secretary of State

Colin Powell. Excerpts of an

op-ed carried in the July 2 edition follow:

2. Under headline "U. S. Secretary of State ignorant of

Zimbabwean situation" the "Herald" (07/02) carried

the following op-ed by Willmore Kanyongo - based in

the United States - on page 4:

"It is clear the U. S. Secretary of State - Colin

Powell - is totally ignorant of the Zimbabwean

situation. The article he wrote. . .has further

shown him as a shameless liar, who habitually sought

to build his foreign policy on utter fabrication. .

.American foreign policy has always been underpinned

by double talk, double standards, deception and

false promises of money if certain leaders are

removed. . .Zimbabweans should be wary and not

succumb to these American machinations."

SULLIVAN
