1. (S/NF) Ref A requested a summary of AmEmbassy Colombo\'s

activities concerning Iraqi national Mahmud Shakir Mahmud AL

HASHIMI. RSO provides the following chronology:

2. (S/NF) On March 25, 2003, at approximately 1445 hours,

RSO/SD team, while on routine surveillance of DATT residence,

reported that it observed Subject depart the Iraq Embassy and

walk several hundred feet down Horton Place to a Chinese

Medical Clinic that faces the DATT residential street which

intersects Horton. The Subject did not enter the clinic, but

focused his attention on the street leading down to the DATT

residence. U.S. and Sri Lankan-provided security guards at

the DATT residence were visible from the Chinese Clinic.

After about five minutes, Subject left the clinic and

continued walking approximately 500 meters around a corner

and entered a business named AL Amana or ALAMANA. Post

passed this information to ORCA New Delhi and Sri Lankan

Directorate of Internal Intelligence (DII). Incident was

reported in Ref B on April 3.

3. (S/NF) On April 4, Ref C reported that ORCA New Delhi had

identified Subject as Mahmud Shakir Mahmud AL HASHIMI, Iraqi

passport number M0847934, owner of Al Amana Steel Buildings,

Ltd. Sri Lankan DII advised Al Hashimi maintained the U.S.

address 5601 Seminary Road, Apt 2204N, Falls Church, VA

22041. Reftel also requested DS conduct record checks on

Subject, results were negative.

4. (S/NF) During this same period, Sri Lankan DII advised Al

Amana (ALAMANA) Steel Buildings, located at No.6/3, Horton

Place, Colombo 7, was a building construction and engineering

company. In addition to Al HASHIMI, Al Amana\'s Board of

Directors was listed as:

Chebel Joseph Basibes (Lebanese), PO Box 5643, Abu Dhabi, UAE

M.W. Perera (Sri Lankan), No.134 Kohalwila Road, Gonawala,

Kelaniya

Jayantha Darmadasa (Sri Lankan), No.7, Longdon Terrace,

Colombo 7

5. (S/NF) On June 10, at approximately 1510 hours, RSO/SD

observed AL HASHIMI walking in front of the U.S. Embassy. He

used a cell phone, waited for a period of time around the

corner from the Embassy, then got into a white Toyota Carina

(WP GH-4874) with two unidentified females.

6. (S/NF) On July 23, ConOff reported that AL HASHIMI

appeared at the visa window requesting a certified copy of

his greencard (file number A43821387, DOB 11/17/1952).

Subject explained that he was traveling to the U.S. on

Saturday, July 26, and needed the certified copy as required

by a letter from a Merrill Lynch office in Bahrain (Acct

#1BY-03A64), signed by Mohamed Ahmed, Assistant Vice

President. ConOff requested RSO conduct record checks on

Subject, and aside from the aforementioned SD reports, the

results were negative. On July 26, Subject returned to the

Embassy and presented Iraqi passport number M0197107 issued

08/07/2001 in Sri Lanka. (This PPT number is different from

that provided by ORCA New Delhi.) Also presented was a

Virginia Drivers License #558-59-5695, DOB 11/17/1952. The

driver\'s license listed an address of 6300 Stoneham Lane,

McLean, VA 22101-2345. Subject explained that was the

address of his estranged wife, who is an AmCit. After

considering the negative responses to record checks, ConOff

provided Subject a certified copy of his greencard and he

departed the Embassy. Subject\'s photograph was emailed to

DS/ICI/PII on July 30. No further action has been taken.
