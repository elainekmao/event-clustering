START MEMO NOMINATING-JUSTIFICATION:

Mr. Muhsin who has previously worked as an acccountant

for the government now works as the Director, Finance

at the largest public company in the Maldives, the

Maldives Transport and Contracting Company (PLC) Ltd.

(MTCC). MTCC, which has shares owned by the

government, banks, other companies and individuals, is

involved in general trading, sea transporting, building

construction, island harbor dredging, equipment rental

and real estate. They are also agents for a number of

products with reputed brand names.

Mr. Muhsin, as the Director Finance, plays a vital role

in keeping financial discipline in the biggest from

Maldivian standards. Although he has had exposure to

financial field in countries like Singapore and Sri

Lanka he has not had the opportunity to get any

exposure to the U.S. or any other western country. Mr.

Muhsin will be a strong private sector candidate from

the Maldives for an IV program. This will provide him

the opportunity to see for himself and understand how

ethical standards are defined, monitored and enforces

in the government and the private sector in the U.S.

He will be able to bring in financial decipline and

accountability in private sector in the Maldives.

END MEMO NOMINATING-JUSTIFICATION:
