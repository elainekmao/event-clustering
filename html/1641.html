SUBJECT: MUGABE ANNOUNCES RE-ELECTION BID AND LASHES OUT

CONFIDENTIAL

AT THE BRITISH AND WHITE ZIMBABWEANS WHILE MARKING

ZIMBABWE\'S 21ST INDEPENDENCE ANNIVERSARY

CLASSIFIED BY CHARGE D\' AFFAIRES EARL M. IRVING FOR

REASONS 1.5 (B/D).

-------

SUMMARY

-------

1. (C) PRESIDENT MUGABE, IN A ZIMBABWE BROADCASTING

CORPORATION (ZBC) TELEVISION INTERVIEW THE NIGHT OF

APRIL 17, AND THEN AGAIN IN HIS SPEECH AT THE OFFICIAL

INDEPENDENCE DAY CELEBRATIONS IN HARARE ON APRIL 18,

REPEATEDLY CRITICIZED BRITAIN AND WHITE ZIMBABWEANS FOR

BEING THE PRINCIPLE CAUSES OF ZIMBABWE\'S CURRENT

ECONOMIC PROBLEMS. MOST OF THIS CRITICISM OF THE

BRITISH AND WHITES CENTERED ON THE LAND ISSUE, BUT HE

ALSO AGAIN ATTACKED THE OPPOSITION MOVEMENT FOR

DEMOCRATIC CHANGE (MDC) FOR MERELY BEING A FRONT FOR

WHITES. HE CONFIRMED THAT HE WILL BE THE ZIMBABWE

AFRICAN NATIONAL UNION - PATRIOTIC FRONT (ZANU-PF)

CANDIDATE IN THE UPCOMING PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION, AND

WILL ONLY RETIRE WHEN HE IS SURE THAT ZANU-PF HAS A

SECURE HOLD ON POWER. MUGABE ALSO THREATENED THAT THE

GOVERNMENT OF ZIMBABWE (GOZ) WILL TAKE OVER COMPANIES

THAT ATTEMPT TO SHUTDOWN OPERATIONS WITHOUT "GENUINE

REASONS", ALLEGING THESE COMPANIES COMPLICITY IN

FRUSTRATING THE "FAST-TRACK" LAND RESETTLEMENT PROGRAM.

WE SEE THIS THREAT AS A HARBINGER OF MORE URBAN VIOLENCE

TO COME. END SUMMARY.

------------------------

THE BRITISH ARE TO BLAME

------------------------

2. (SBU) ON A NUMBER OF DIFFERENT OCCASIONS IN BOTH THE

TELEVISION INTERVIEW AND DURING HIS SPEECH PRESIDENT

MUGABE CASTIGATED THE BRITISH FOR INTERFERING IN

ZIMBABWE\'S INTERNAL AFFAIRS AND SEEKING TO TURN THE

INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY AGAINST ZIMBABWE. THE FOCUS OF

HIS ATTACK AGAINST THE BRITISH GOVERNMENT REVOLVED

AROUND THE LAND ISSUE, AND HIS BELIEF THAT THE BRITISH

GOVERNMENT RENEGED ON ITS COMMITMENT TO PAY FOR LAND

REFORM IN ZIMBABWE. HE CRITICIZED THE BRITISH LABOR

GOVERNMENT, AND PRIME MINISTER BLAIR, BY NAME ON

NUMEROUS OCCASIONS, AND ALLEGED THAT "BRITAIN WAS

BEHAVING LIKE AN INTERNATIONAL DELINQUENT ON THE

MATTER." HE WENT ON TO ARGUE THAT THOSE COUNTRIES THAT

SUPPORT THE BRITISH POSITION NEED TO RECOGNIZE THAT

ZIMBABWE IS A SOVEREIGN STATE AND THAT IT SHOULD BE

ASSISTED WITH THE PROGRAMS IT DEVELOPS WITHOUT

INTERFERENCE FROM ANY SOURCE. REVEALING HIS OBSESSION

WITH THE INDEPENDENCE STRUGGLE, DURING HIS INDEPENDENCE

DAY SPEECH HE SAID, "THIS LAND, THIS ZIMBABWE, IS A

SACRED INHERITANCE FROM OUR FOREFATHERS. IT WAS THE

\'CASUS BELLI\' OF OUR ARMED NATIONAL LIBERATION STRUGGLE.

IT CANNOT, THEREFORE, BE THAT WHICH WE HAVE TO BEG A

FOREIGN POWER FOR AND SO WE SAY HANDS OFF, BRITAIN,

HANDS OFF, BRITAIN!" HE WENT ON TO SHOUT, "WE ARE NOT

AN EXTENSION OF BRITAIN. SO MR. BLAIR HANDS OFF,

PLEASE!" TOWARDS THE END OF THE TELEVISION INTERVIEW,

MUGABE STATED THAT "LOOKING BACK 21 YEARS THE GREATEST

MOMENT IN MY LIFE WAS SEEING THE BRITISH FLAG GO DOWN

AND REPLACED BY THE FLAG OF THE NEW NATION OF ZIMBABWE."

---------------------

AND SO ARE THE WHITES

---------------------

3. (U) MUGABE CONTINUED HIS VITRIOLIC ATTACKS ON WHITE

ZIMBABWEANS IN BOTH THE TELEVISION INTERVIEW AND

INDEPENDENCE DAY SPEECH. HE CRITICIZED THE WHITES FOR

HAVING SPURNED THE HAND OF RECONCILIATION HE HAD

EXTENDED AT INDEPENDENCE, SAYING "RECONCILIATION WAS NOT

A ONE-SIDED AFFAIR. THE WHITES HAD NOT BEEN FORTHCOMING

AND BEHAVED AS IF THEY BELONGED TO BRITAIN AND WERE IN

ZIMBABWE ONLY TO MAKE MONEY." HE CONTINUED THAT, "THE

WHITES WANTED TO REVERSE THE GAINS ATTAINED AT

INDEPENDENCE BY OPPOSING THE \'FAST-TRACK\' LAND REFORM

PROGRAM." AS HE OFTEN DOES, HE REFERRED NUMEROUS TIMES

TO WHITE ZIMBABWEANS, THE MAJORITY OF WHOM WERE BORN IN

THIS COUNTRY, AS "SETTLERS." ACCORDING TO MUGABE, "THE

SETTLERS ARE USING EVERY TRICK, INCLUDING THAT OF USING

SOME AMONG US, TO DERAIL THE EFFORT BY THE GOVERNMENT TO

ALLOW AN EQUITABLE REDISTRIBUTION OF LAND."

THE WHITES BEHIND THE MDC

-------------------------

4. (U) IN THE TELEVISION INTERVIEW MUGABE CRITICIZED THE

MDC AS BEING JUST A FRONT FOR WHITES, SAYING THAT THE

"MDC HAD MADE A MISTAKE BY PUTTING WHITES IN THE

FOREFRONT." HE LINKED THIS ALLEGED DOMINATION OF THE

MDC BY WHITES AS EVIDENCE OF THE CONSPIRACY TO RETURN

ZIMBABWE TO WHITE MINORITY RULE, STATING "IT INVOKES IN

ALL OF US A BELIEF THAT THE WHITE MAN DOES NOT CHANGE.

AS LONG AS THE WHITES DID NOT CHANGE, PEOPLE SHOULD

STRENGTHEN THEMSELVES TO FIGHT EVEN HARDER." IN HIS

INDEPENDENCE DAY SPEECH HE STATED "LET US WATCH OUT FOR

THESE VICIOUS ENEMIES AMONG US AND TRY TO OVERCOME

THEM", CLEARLY A REFERENCE TO BOTH THE MDC AND

ZIMBABWE\'S WHITE MINORITY AND THEIR OSTENSIBLY VIOLENT

AGENDA.

---------------------

MUGABE WILL RUN AGAIN

---------------------

5. (U) MUGABE PUBLICLY CONFIRMED FOR THE FIRST TIME

SINCE THE ZANU-PF PARTY CONGRESS LAST FEBRUARY THAT HE

WILL BE THE PARTY\'S PRESIDENTIAL CANDIDATE. HE ASSERTED

THAT HE IS IN GOOD HEALTH, AND THAT HE WILL ONLY

CONSIDER RETIRING AFTER HE AGAIN LEADS ZANU-PF TO

VICTORY IN THE UPCOMING ELECTION.

NO COMPANY CLOSURES WITHOUT GOZ APPROVAL

----------------------------------------

6. (U) IN BOTH THE TELEVISON INTERVIEW AND THE

INDEPENDENCE DAY SPEECH MUGABE THREATENED THAT THE GOZ

WILL TAKE OVER THE OPERATIONS OF ANY COMPANIES THAT TRY

TO SHUTDOWN OPERATIONS WITHOUT GOZ APPROVAL. HE

ASSERTED THAT MANY OF THESE COMPANY CLOSURES DID NOT

RESULT FROM THE ECONOMIC CLIMATE IN ZIMBABWE, BUT WERE

BEING DONE AS PART OF THE BRITISH AND WHITE ZIMBABWEANS\'

CAMPAIGN TO FRUSTRATE THE "FAST-TRACK" LAND PROGRAM. HE

STATED THAT "GOVERNMENT WILL IN THE FUTURE NOT ALLOW ANY

VIABLE MINING OR MANUFACTURING ENTERPRISE TO CLOSE FOR

UNCLEAR REASONS. ANY CLOSURES WILL HAVE TO HAVE THE

ASSENT OF THE GOVERNMENT."

-------

COMMENT

-------

7. (C) PRESIDENT MUGABE\'S TELEVISION INTERVIEW, AND

INDEPENDENCE DAY SPEECH AT THE HALF-FILLED NATIONAL

STADIUM (WHERE A SOCCER MATCH BETWEEN RIVAL TEAMS

HIGHLANDERS AND DYNAMOS THAT AFTERNOON WAS LIKELY THE

BIGGER DRAW), CONFIRMED THAT HE IS A LEADER TRAPPED IN

THE TIME WARP OF ZIMBABWE\'S INDEPENDENCE STRUGGLE AND

HIS PERCEIVED GLORY DAYS. THE FACT THAT HIS "FAST-

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 04 HARARE 01354 02 OF 03 191450Z

TRACK" LAND REFORM PROGRAM WILL BE THE ISSUE ON WHICH HE

WILL RUN HIS 2002 PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION CAMPAIGN WAS

EVIDENCED BY THE CENTRAL ROLE IT PLAYED IN THE INTERVIEW

AND THE SPEECH. IN REALITY, THIS IS THE ONLY ISSUE

MUGABE AND ZANU-PF CAN USE TO TRY AND GARNER SUPPORT

FROM THE ZIMBABWE PEOPLE. HE MENTIONED THE TREMENDOUS

STRIDES THAT ZIMBABWE HAD MADE SINCE INDEPENDENCE IN THE

FIELDS OF EDUCATION AND HEALTH CARE, BUT THE COLD-HARD

TRUTH IS THAT BOTH THE PUBLIC EDUCATION AND PUBLIC

HEALTH CARE SYSTEMS ARE ON THE VERGE OF COLLAPSE.

INDEED, A TWENTY-ONE MINUTE POWER FAILURE AT THE

BEGINNING OF THE INDEPENDENCE DAY CEREMONIES WAS ALMOST

A LITERARY TROPE: A PATHETIC FALLACY TO ZIMBABWE\'S

REACHING THE AGE OF MAJORITY UNDER MUGABE\'S LEADERSHIP.

LASTLY, MUGABE\'S BIZARRE AND PROBABLY UNENFORCEABLE

THREAT TO PREVENT COMPANIES FROM SHUTTING DOWN, ALLEGING

THEY ARE DOING SO JUST TO FRUSTRATE LAND REFORM,

PORTENDS MORE URBAN VIOLENCE TO US. WE SEE ZANU-PF

DEPLOYING WAR VETERANS IN EVEN GREATER NUMBERS TO FORCE

OWNERS OF FALTERING FACTORIES AND OTHER BUSINESSES TO

REMAIN OPEN THROUGH PSYCHOLOGICAL INTIMIDATION AND THE

OCCASIONAL VICIOUS BEATING. NEEDLESS TO SAY, THIS WILL

NOT HELP THE ZIMBABWE ECONOMY TO IMPROVE NOR RESTORE

INVESTOR CONFIDENCE. END COMMENT.
