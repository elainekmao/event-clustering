SUBJECT: CMAG DECISION A SHOCK FOR ZIMBABWE

REF: FBIS AFP20011221000024

CLASSIFIED BY JOSEPH G. SULLIVAN FOR REASONS 1.5B/D

1. (C) THE DEC 20 DECISION BY THE COMMONWEALTH

MINISTERIAL ACTION GROUP(CMAG) "TO EXPRESS ITS DEEP

CONCERN ABOUT THE SITUATION IN ZIMBABWE ESPECIALLY THE

CONTINUED VIOLENCE, OCCUPATION OF PROPERTY, ACTIONS

AGAINST THE FREEDOM AND INDEPENDENCE OF THE MEDIA AND

POLITICAL INTIMIDATION" . (WHICH) "CONSTITUTES A SERIOUS

AND PERSISTENT VIOLATION OF THE COMMONWEALTH\'S

FUNDAMENTAL POLTICAL VALUES AND THE RULE OF LAW AS

ENSHRINED IN THE HARARE COMMONWEALTH DECLARATION" COMES

AS A SHOCK TO THE GOZ. THE CMAG\'S DRAWING ATTENTION OF

THE GOZ TO ITS ABUJA AGREEMENT COMMITMENTS AND STRONG

URGING TO IMPLEMENT THESE COMMITMENTS COMES AS A

PARTICULAR BLOW, SINCE IT CONTRADICTS THE GOZ CLAIM THAT

IT HAS IMPLEMENTED ABUJA AND ONLY THE UK HAS FAILED TO

FULFILL ITS FUNDING COMMITMENTS. THE PRESENCE OF

NIGERIAN FOREIGN MINISTER LAMIDO ON THE CMAG AND HIS

SUBSEQUENT INTERVIEWS ON BBC, IN PARTICULAR UNDERMINE

THE GOZ PRETENSE TO BE ABIDING BY ABUJA. THE PRESENCE

OF NIGERIA AND BOTSWANA IN THE CMAG, NOT TO MENTION

MALAYSIA, ALSO UNDERMINES THE GOZ ARGUMENT THAT ANY

CRITICISM OF ZIMBABWE IS A UK, EUROPEAN OR NORTH

2. (C) THE CMAG STATEMENT LEFT THE STATE MEDIA

SPEECHLESS; THE BEST RESPONSE FOREIGN MINISTER MUDENGE

COULD MUSTER WAS A CLAIM THAT CMAG ACTIONWITHOUT A

CHOGOM DIRECTIVE WAS ILLEGAL (A POINT ON WHICH OUR UK

AND CANADIAN COLLEAGUES CONCED HE HAS HALF A POINT.)

OTHERWISE, HE FELL BACK ON UNSUSTAINABLE CLAIMS OF A

BRITISH CONSPIRACY.

3. (C) COMMENT: COMING SO SHORTLY AFTER THE SADC

MINISTERIAL MISSION HAD AGREED WITH THE GOZ VIEW THAT

THERE HAD BEEN SUBSTANTIAL PROGRESS ON THE LAND ISSUE

AND THAT VIOLENCE HAD DIMINISHED, THE CMAG REPORT IS A

MAJOR SHOCK FOR THE GOZ. PRESIDENT MUGABE, CURRENTLY

VISITING LIBYA FOR ANOTHER INJECTION OF PETROL, WOULD

SEEM LIKELY TO REACT AS HE HAS TO ALL OTHER CRITICISMS,

WITH A " THEY CAN GO TO HELL." BUT NIGERIA\'S JOINING IN

THE CRITICISM OF ZIMBABWE\'S ABUJA AGREEMENT PERFORMANCE

STRIPS BARE A MAJOR DEFENCE PLANK OF THE GOZ. COUPLED

WITH RISING FOOD SHORTAGES AND OTHER PROBLEMS, MORE

VOICES IN THE GOZ AND ZUNU-PF ARE LIKELY TO QUESTION

WHETHER MUGABE AND ZANU-PF CAN TOUGH IT OUT IN ISOLATION

FROM THE NON-QADDAFI WORLD.
