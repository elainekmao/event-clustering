SUBJECT: VATICAN TELLS AMBASSADOR: POWELL IS NEEDED

IMMEDIATELY

CLASSIFIED BY: AMBASSADOR JIM NICHOLSON FOR REASONS 1.5 (A) AND (D).

1. (C) FOREIGN MINISTER TAURAN CALLED AMBASSADOR THIS MORNING

(4/08). TAURAN SAID THAT HE HAD JUST SPOKEN TO THE PAPAL

NUNCIO IN JERUSALEM WHO WAS CONTACTED BY SENIOR ARAFAT AIDE

MUSTAFI. MUSTAFI ASKED THAT THE VATICAN CONVEY TO US THE

NEED FOR SECRETARY POWELL TO GO IMMEDIATELY TO JERUSALEM. HE

SAID IF THE SECRETARY WAITS UNTIL THURSDAY OR FRIDAY "HE WILL

ONLY FIND RUINS AND DEAD". TAURAN URGED THE SECRETARY TO

TAKE THIS MESSAGE SERIOUSLY.

2. (C) THE ISRAELIS TOLD US EARLIER IN THE DAY THAT TAURAN

CALLED AMBASSADOR LAMDAN ALSO THIS MORNING. ACCORDING TO

ISRAELI EMBASSY SOURCE, IT WAS A "DIFFICULT CONVERSATION".

ISRAELIS CLAIM THAT TAURAN SAID IF REPORTS ARE TRUE THAT

ISRAELI SOLDIERS HAVE FIRED INTO THE CHURCH OF THE NATIVITY,

THE VATICAN WILL REACT "VERY STRONGLY". NICHOLSON
