SUBJECT: IRAQ: GOT PROVIDES DIP NOTE PUTTING SITE PREP MOU

INTO EFFECT

REF: A) ANKARA 1005 B) STATE 36584 C)ANKARA 995

(u) Classified by Acting Deputy Chief of Mission Scott

Marciel. Reasons: 1.5 (B and D)

1. (C) Late afternoon February 10, the MFA informed the

Embassy via diplomatic note that the GOT had fulfilled all of

the internal procedures necessary for the Memorandum of

Understanding (MOU) for Site Preparations that was signed

late February 8 (ref a) to enter into force. Paragraph 2

contains the text of the MFA\'s diplomatic note. Para 3

contains the text of the diplomatic note the Embassy

delivered to the MFA late February 8 following the signature

of the MOU as authorized by the Department (ref b).

2. (U) Begin text of the MFA\'s diplomatic note (number

2003/ALGY/56737):

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Turkey

presents its compliments to the Embassy of the United States

of America and with reference to Article VII of the

"Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) between the Government of

the Republic of Turkey and the Government of the United

States of America, on the Establishment and Implementation of

Basic Policy, Principles, Procedures and to Determine the

Status of the Site Preparation Personnel of the United States

of America in Turkey for the Purposes of Site Preparation

Activities," signed in Ankara on February 8, 2003, has the

honor to inform the Embassy of the fulfillment of all

internal procedures necessary for the aforesaid MOU\'s entry

into force.

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Turkey

avails itself of this opportunity to renew to the Embassy of

the United States of America the assurances of its highest

consideration.

End text.

3. (U) Below is the text of the diplomatic note (number 249)

the Embassy sent the MFA on February 8:

The Embassy of the United States of America presents its

compliments to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the

Republic of Turkey and has the honor to inform the Ministry

that the Government of the United States of America has

completed all of its internal procedures necessary for entry

into force of the Memorandum of Understanding between the

Government of the United States of America and the Government

of the Republic of Turkey on the Establishment and

Implementation of Basic Policy, Principles, Procedures and to

Determine the Status of the Site Preparation Personnel of the

United States of America in Turkey for the Purposes of Site

Preparation Activities, signed in Ankara on February 8, 2003.

The Embassy of the United States of America avails itself of

this opportunity to renew to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs

the assurances of its highest consideration.

End text.
