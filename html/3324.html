SUBJECT: JORDAN: MEDIA REACTION ON IRAQ, QUADRIPARTITE

MEETING

SUMMARY

A LEAD STORY IN ALL JORDANIAN DAILIES TODAY, JULY 17,

IS ON AN AMBUSH BY PALESTINIAN RESISTANCE FIGHTERS ON A

BUS NEAR A JEWISH SETTLEMENT YESTERDAY, KILLING SEVEN

SETTLERS. PRESIDENT BUSH IS REPORTED AS HAVING

CONDEMNED THE OPERATION AND WHITEHOUSE SPOKESMAN ARI

FLEISCHER IS QUOTED AS SAYING THAT THIS ATTACK

UNDERSCORES THE IMPORTANCE OF FOCUSING ON PEACE. WITHIN

THAT SAME LEAD STORY, THE DAILIES REPORT THAT THE

QUADRIPARTITE COMMITTEE IS DIVIDED OVER THE U.S. STAND

ON ARAFAT. ANOTHER FRONTPAGE REPORT SAYS THAT

U.S.DEFENSE SECRTARY RUMSFELD HAS ORDERED AN UPDATE OF

ALL U.S. CONTINGENCY PLANS FOR A POSSIBLE WAR AGAINST

IRAQ. INDEPENDENT, MASS-APPEAL ARABIC DAILY AL-ARAB AL-

YAWM CARRIES A REUTERS REPORT FROM WASHINGTON, QUOTING

FLEISCHER AS SAYING THAT PRESIDENT BUSH WILL MEET KING

ABDULLAH IN WASHINGTON ON AUGUST 1.

EDITORIAL COMMENTARIES DEAL WITH THE ANTICIPATED

MIILITARY STRIKE AGAINST IRAQ, AND COMMENT ON THE ARAB

MINISTERS\' MEETINGS WITH THE QUADRIPARTITE COMMITTEE IN

NEW YORK, CALLING FOR AN ARAB STAND THAT DEFENDS THE

ARAB STATUS QUO.

EDITORIAL COMMENTARIES ON IRAQ

-- "THE AFGHANIZATION OF PALESTINE AND IRAQ"

DAILY COLUMNIST MOUSA HAWAMDAH WRITES ON THE OP-ED PAGE

OF CENTER-LEFT, INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY

AL-DUSTOUR (07/17): "THE AFGHANI EXPERIENCE WILL BE THE

BEST METHOD THAT CAN BE IMPLEMENTED BY U.S. POLITICIANS

IN MANY PARTS OF THE WORLD. THE AMERICANS\' SUCCESS IN

BRINGING ZAHER SHAH, WHO DOES NOT RULE NOW, BACK TO

KABUL AMD PUTTING HAMID KARZAI AS A TEMPORARY PRESIDENT

AND THEN ELECTED BY THE LOUGERGA WILL BE THE MODEL

FOLLOWED BY THE U.S. ADMINISTRATION IN THE YEARS TO

COME UNTIL THIS MODEL FAILS A DISMAL FAILURE OR THE

UNITED STATES SUFFERS GREATER HARM FROM IT."

MAKING IT A POINT THAT IN ADDITION TO THE FACT THE

UNITED STATES IS IMPLEMENTING THE AFGHANI MODEL IN

PALESTINE, IT IS ALSO IMPLEMENTING IT IN IRAQ, HAWAMDAH

CONCLUDES: "THE UNITED STATES WANTS, BY ALL MEANS, TO

AFGHANIZE IRAQ IN SUCH A BLIND MANNER AS IF IT IS

MEDICAL PERSCRIPTION OF A RECIPE. HOWEVER, PALESTINE

AND IRAQ ARE DIFFERENT. THE U.S. LOGIC DOES NOT KNOW

THE GAME OF DIFFERENCES. IT WANTS TO IMPLEMENT WHAT IT

THINKS IS SUCCESSFUL IN ANY PART OF THE WORLD. IT MIGHT

EVEN GENERALIZE SUCH A MODEL AND IMPLEMENT IT IN

EUROPEAN, AMERICAN, AND ASIAN COUNTRIES, WHICH WILL

BRING BACK THE HANDS OF TIME WITHOUT TAKING INTO

CONSIDERATION THE OPINION OF THE WORLD AND THE PEOPLES,

WHOM THE UNITED STATES WANTS TO HELP."

-- "A FALSE U.S. PRETEXT TO TOPPLE SADDAM !!"

DAILY COLUMNIST IBRAHIM ABSI WRITES ON THE OP-ED PAGE

OF CENTER-LEFT, INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY

AL-DUSTOUR (07/17): "THE UNITED STATES IS PREPARING ALL

ITS INTELLIGENCE, MILITARY,POLITICAL, AND FINANCIAL

CAPABILITIES TO TOPPLE SADDAM NOT BECAUSE HE IS A SOLO

RULER OR DESPOTIC DICTATOR AS PROPAGATED BY U.S. MEDIA

ORGANS AS WELL AS DIPLOMATIC AND POLITICAL

INSTITUTIONS. THIS DOES NOT CONCERN THE UNITED STATES

WHATSOEVER. HOWEVER, WHAT CONCERNS IT IS THE FACT THAT

THE PANARAB IRAQI LEADERSHIP - NOT JUST SADDAM ALONE -

REFUSES TO SUBMIT TO DOMINANT U.S. POWER AND ALSO

REFUSES TO ORBIT IN THE U.S. CELESTIAL SPHERE AND TO

SUCCUMB TO U.S. HEGEMONY, INFLUENCE, AND THE DICTATIONS

OF U.S. POLICY. ON THE OTHER HAND, THERE IS SOME SORT

OF A PERSONAL VENDETTA THAT PROMPTS THE U.S. PRESIDENT

TO TAKE REVENGE FROM SADDAM HUSSEIN AFTER GEORGE BUSH

SR. HAD FAILED TO OUST THE IRAQI PRSIDENT."

AFTER NOTING THAT THE UNITED STATES IS ACCUSING IRAQ OF

TERRORISM AND OF POSSESSING WEAPONS OF MASS

DESTRUCTION, ABSI CONCLUDES: "THE RACIST ZIONIST ENTITY

IN OCCUPIED PALESTINE IS THE UNDECLARED PRETEXT, BEHIND

WHICH U.S. CALLS FOR EXPEDITING THE TOPPLING OF THE

IRAQI LEADERSHIP HIDE. WASHINGTON CONSIDERS THE IRAQI

REGIME AND BAGHDAD\'S REJECTION OF U.S.-ZIONIST PROJECTS

TO SETTLE THE PALESTINIAN PEOPLE\'S ISSUE AS A THREAT TO

THIS ENTITY. THIS IS ESPECIALLY SO SINCE IRAQ - DESPITE

THE UNJUST SIEGE THAT HAS BEEN IMPOSED ON IT FOR OVER

TEN YEARS - IS STILL CHAMPIONING AND WILL CONTINUE TO

CHAMPION THE ARAB DREAM AND STAND AGAINST ALL

IMPERIALIST AND ZIONIST SCHEMES, WHICH SEEK TO MAKE THE

ARAB NATION DROP TO ITS KNEES."

EDITORIAL COMMENTARIES ON THE QUADRIPARTITE MEETING

-- "THE NEW YORK MEETING AND U.S. SELECTIVITY"

COMMENTING ON THE QUADRIPARTITE COMMITTEE\'S MEETING IN

NEW YORK, TAHER AL-ADWAN, CHIEF EDITOR OF INDEPENDENT,

MASS-APPEAL ARABIC DAILY AL-ARAB AL-YAWM WRITES ON JULY

17: "THE FOREIGN MINISTERS OF JORDAN, EGYPT, AND SAUDI

ARABIA WENT TO NEW YORK TO MEET WITH THE INTERNATIONAL

QUADRIPARTITE COMMITTEE WHILE HOLDING A WEAK STAND . .

. IT IS REGRETTABLE THAT INTER-ARAB DIFFERENCES OVER

PARTICIPATION AND REPRESENTATION AT THE QUADRIPATITE

COMMITTEE\'S MEETING SHOULD RENDER ANY DECISION MADE A

MERE CONSECRATION OF THE STATE OF ARAB WEAKNESS."

ADWAN CONCLUDES: "WE DEMAND THAT THE THREE FOREIGN

MINISTERS DEFEND THE ARAB STAND AS A WHOLE. THIS WILL

REQUIRE THEM TO CONVINCE THE QUADRIPARTITE COMMITTEE TO

AGREE TO MEET WITH ALL MEMBERS OF THE FOLLOW-UP

COMMITTEE. THE FACT IS THAT U.S. SELECTIVITY AT THIS

QUADRIPARTITE COMMITTEE\'S MEETING MAY PAVE THE WAY FOR

FUTURE SELECTIVITY WHEN THE INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE IS

CONVENES. INDEED, THE UNITED STATES AND ISRAEL, WHICH

NOWADAYS REFUSE TO MEET OR DEAL WITH ARAFAT, MAY DEMAND

THE EXCLUSION OF SYRIA AND LEBANON FROM THE

INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE. THIS IN ITSELF IS A

DISASTEROUS POLICY THAT SERVES THE U.S. POLICY OF

SUPERIORITY, WHICH DOES NOT SEE BEYOND SHARON\'S NOSE."

-- "THE NEW YORK MEETINGS"

CENTER-LEFT, INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR

(07/17) EDITORIALIZES: "THERE IS NO DOUBT THAT THIS

IMPORTANT INTERNATIONAL MEETING CAN OFFER A NEW PUSH

FOR THE PEACE PROCESS IF IT TAKES THE RIGHT START BY

BASICALLY CONSECRATING THE ISSUE OF WITHDRAWAL OF

OCCUPATION FORCES FROM PALESTINIAN CITIES AND TOWNS TO

PAVE THE WAY FOR REQUIRED REFORMS ON THE PART OF THE

PALESTINIAN AUTHORITY."

AL-DUSTOUR ADDS: "WE ALREADY REALIZE THAT THE UNITED

STATES HAS THE FIRST SAY IN THE QUADRIPARTITE

COMMITTEE\'S MEETINGS. HOWEVER, OPPOSITION FROM THE REST

OF THE PARTIES OF THE U.S. VIEW, WHICH IS TOTALLY

IDENTICAL TO THAT OF THE ISRAELI VIEW, SHOULD LEAVE A

RAY OF HOPE OF PUTTING THE NEW YORK MEETINGS ON THE

RIGHT TRACK. THIS SHOULD YIELD PARCTICAL AND TANGIBLE

RESULTS THAT COULD OPEN THE ROAD FOR A RETURN TO

NEGOTIATIONS."

AL-DUSTOUR CONCLUDES: "WE HOPE THAT INTERNATIONAL

EFFORT WILL SUCCEED IN GIVING THE PEACE PROCESS A REAL

PUSH FORWARD AND THAT THE PARTIES WILL BE ABLE TO

ACHIEVE PRACTICAL AND TANGIBLE RESULTS ON THE GROUND."
