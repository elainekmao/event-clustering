SUBJECT: DUTCH ON HALTING ASSISTANCE TO HAMAS

REF: SECSTATE 157973

Classified By: CDA Daniel Russel Reasons 1.5 (b) and (d)

(S) Ambassador conveyed Secretary\'s oral message on Hamas in

a June 12 meeting with Dutch MFA Political Director Hugo

Siblesz. Siblesz said that the Netherlands has argued in

Brussels for including Hamas on the EU\'s list of Foreign

Terrorist Organizations and for freezing its assets. Siblesz

further stated the Netherlands has made the point that it is

time to do this given the negative role Hamas is playing in

the peace process.
