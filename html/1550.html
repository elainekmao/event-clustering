SUBJECT: ZIMBABWE FULLY BACKS JMC AND IS EAGER FOR ITS

MOVE TO KINSHASA

REFTEL: STATE 35571

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 HARARE 00870 281442Z

CLASSIFIED BY CHARGE D\'AFFAIRES EARL M. IRVING FOR

REASONS: 1.5 (B) AND (D).

1. (C) ON FEBRUARY 28, POLOFF CONVEYED REFTEL TALKING

POINTS TO MAXWELL CHIKOROWONDO, THE ZIMBABWEAN MFA\'S

PRINCIPAL ADMINISTRATIVE OFFICER (PAO) FOR THE DRC.

CHIKOROWONDO RESPONDED THAT THE GOZ IS FULLY COMMITTED

TO THE JOINT MILITARY COMMISSION (JMC) AND THE IDEA OF

RELOCATING ITS HEADQUARTERS TO KINSHASA, AND THINKS IT

SHOULD HAVE HAPPENED "YESTERDAY." THE PAO STATED THAT

RWANDA, UGANDA AND THEIR REBEL ALLIES WERE THE ONLY

OBSTACLES TO MOVING THE JMC. THEIR COMPLAINTS ABOUT

INADEQUATE SECURITY ARE UNJUSTIFIED BECAUSE THEY ALREADY

HAVE JUNIOR REGIONAL JMC REPRESENTATIVES STATIONED IN

THE COUNTRYSIDE, WHICH IS MUCH MORE INSECURE THAN

KINSHASA, CHIKOROWONDO ARGUED.

2. (C) CHIKOROWONDO ASKED IF THE U.S. WILL SUPPLY NEW

FUNDING TO THE JMC, TO WHICH POLOFF RESPONDED THAT

BEFORE THE USG CAN SUPPLY MORE FUNDS, FISCAL CONTROLS

FOR ACCOUNTABILITY MUST BE IN PLACE. THE MFA OFFICIAL

STATED HE BELIEVED JMC HEAD GENERAL MWANIKI WAS WORKING

ON A PLAN TO TRACK AND CONTROL JMC REPRESENTATIVES\'

EXPENDITURES. THE GOZ IS ENCOURAGED BY THE NEW MOMENTUM

IN THE PEACE PROCESS, CHIKOROWONDO STATED. HE SAID A

JMC MEETING IN LUSAKA ON FEBRUARY 27 PROBABLY GAVE NEW

IMPETUS TO RESOLVING THE JMC\'S PROBLEMS.
