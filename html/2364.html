SUBJECT: ANGELO SCOLA: NEW PATRIARCH AND NOW PAPABILE?

1. (SBU) POPE JOHN PAUL II HAS APPOINTED A NEW PATRIARCH

TO LEAD THE CATHOLIC ECCLESIASTICAL JURISDICTION OF VENICE,

ITALY. 60 YEAR OLD BISHOP ANGELO SCOLA IS ALREADY BEING

TOUTED AS A CANDIDATE FOR THE NEXT PAPACY. VENICE HAS

PRODUCED THREE RECENT POPES WHO WERE PATRIARCHS PRIOR TO

ELECTION - PIUS X, JOHN XXIII, AND JOHN PAUL I. SCOLA\'S

APPOINTMENT TO VENICE IS A SIGN OF THE PRESENT PONTIFF\'S

GREAT ESTEEM FOR THE NEW PATRIARCH. IT REPRESENTS A

CONSOLIDATION OF THE CONSERVATIVE BLOC WITHIN THE ITALIAN

BISHOPS CONFERENCE AND ENHANCES THE CHANCE OF AN ITALIAN

EVENTUALLY REPLACING JOHN PAUL II.

2. (SBU) BIOGRAPHICAL INFORMATION: ANGELO SCOLA WAS BORN

NOVEMBER 7, 1941 IN MALGRATE, ITALY. AFTER OBTAINING A

DEGREE IN PHILOSOPHY FROM THE CATHOLIC UNIVERSITY OF THE

SACRED HEART IN MILAN, HE BEGAN HIS THEOLOGICAL STUDIES AT

SEMINARIES IN SARONNO AND VENEGONO, EVENTUALLY EARNING A

DOCTORAL DEGREE IN THEOLOGY FROM THE UNIVERSITY OF FRIBOURG

IN SWITZERLAND. ACADEMICALLY BRILLIANT, SCOLA WAS ORDAINED A

PRIEST ON JULY 18, 1970. HIS PRIESTLY CAREER INCLUDED

UNIVERSITY PROFESSORSHIPS IN SWITZERLAND AND ITALY,

CULMINATING WITH HIS APPOINTMENT IN 1995 AS RECTOR OF THE

PONTIFICAL LATERAN UNIVERSITY IN ROME. SCOLA WAS APPOINTED

BISHOP OF THE ITALIAN DIOCESE OF GROSETTO IN 1991, BUT

REMAINED THERE A RELATIVELY SHORT TIME BEFORE MOVING ON TO

THE LATERAN. HE HAS SERVED AS AN ADVISOR TO CARDINAL JOSEPH

RATZINGER AT THE VATICAN OFFICE FOR DOCTRINAL ORTHODOXY.

SCOLA IS COSMOPOLITAN, AFFABLE, INTELLIGENT, AND HAS A WARM

AND FRIENDLY PERSONALITY. HE IS FIERCELY LOYAL TO POPE JOHN

PAUL II AND THE TENETS OF CATHOLICISM BUT WITHOUT THE

HANDICAP OF BEING A DEMAGOGUE - PREFERRING TO ENGAGE OTHERS

BY REASON AND DIALOGUE

3. (SBU) COMMENT: SCOLA\'S APPOINTMENT TO VENICE IS ANOTHER

STEP ON WHAT APPEARS TO BE A CAREFULLY ORCHESTRATED CAREER.
