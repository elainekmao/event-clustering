SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION IRAQ; HARARE

1. Under headline "NAM opposes war on Iraq" the

pro-government weekly "The Sunday Mirror"

dedicated its February 23 editorial to

embracing the Non Aligned Movement\'s (NAM) anti-

war position against Iraq. Excerpts:

2. ". . .The Non Aligned Movement. . .has already

stated that it will oppose the American and British-

led war on Iraq. . . The attitude by NAM gives the

world an additional ray of hope, coming as it is at a

time world mass opinion, a superpower in itself, is

gaining greater momentum against the its verbal

nobility into a pro-active crusade against the

murderers of peace."
