1. (C) SUMMARY. MFA DIRECTOR FOR EU EXTERNAL RELATIONS

MASSIMO GAIANI TOLD EMBOFFS ON APRIL 2 THAT IRAQ AND

RESTORING INTRA-EU AND TRANSATLANTIC RELATIONS WILL LIKELY BE

THE POLITICAL PRIORITIES WHEN ITALY TAKES OVER THE EU

PRESIDENCY JULY 1. (SEPTEL WILL REPORT ECONOMIC (FIRST

PILLAR) PRIORITIES.) HE ALSO ACKNOWLEDGED THAT THERE WAS A

GOOD CHANCE THAT THE CONVENTION/INTER-GOVERNMENTAL CONFERENCE

(IGC) PROCESS WOULD NOT BE COMPLETED IN TIME TO SIGN A SECOND

TREATY OF ROME. THE BALKANS, ILLEGAL IMMIGRATION CONTROL,

EU-RUSSIA, AND ENLARGEMENT ALL REMAIN IMPORTANT TO THE GOI,

AND WILL BE GIVEN APPROPRIATE ATTENTION DURING ITS EU

PRESIDENCY. END SUMMARY.

2. (C) IN A NOD TO THE NEW PARAMETERS DEFINING EU INTERNAL

AND EXTERNAL RELATIONS, GAIANI SAID THAT "IRAQ IS THE

PRIORITY" FOR THE EU NOW, AND THE SITUATION IS LIKELY TO BE

THE SAME WHEN ITALY TAKES OVER THE PRESIDENCY FROM GREECE ON

JULY 1. PIECING BACK TOGETHER THE TATTERED RELATIONS AMONG

EU MEMBERS AND BETWEEN EUROPE AND THE U.S. WILL CONSUME MOST

POLITICAL ENERGY FOR THE FORESEEABLE FUTURE.

3. (C) ACCORDING TO GAIANI, THE GOI HAD ALL BUT WRITTEN OFF

HOPES FOR A SECOND "TREATY OF ROME," AS REPORTED REFTEL. HE

SAID THAT THE CONVENTION PROCESS WAS NOT FOCUSED SUFFICIENTLY

ON THE "CONSTITUTION" GIVEN CURRENT EVENTS; THERE WAS A GOOD

CHANCE THAT CONVENTION PRESIDENT GISCARD D\'ESTAING WOULD NOT

BE ABLE TO KEEP TO HIS SCHEDULE OF A JUNE CLOSE FOR THE

CONVENTION AND PRODUCTION OF THE FINAL DRAFT OF THE DOCUMENT.

IF THIS SLIPPED TO JULY OR LATER, A TREATY SIGNING CEREMONY

DURING THE ITALIAN PRESIDENCY WAS HIGHLY UNLIKELY. COUPLE

THIS WITH THE PROPOSALS OF CERTAIN MEMBERS, SUCH AS THE UK,

FOR A COOLING OFF PERIOD OF A NUMBER OF MONTHS BETWEEN THE

CLOSE OF THE CONVENTION AND THE START OF THE IGC, AND "WE

WILL BE LUCKY TO EVEN START, LET ALONE FINISH," THE IGC

DURING THE ITALIAN PRESIDENCY, LAMENTED GAIANI. HE HOPED THE

WAY AHEAD WOULD BE CLEARER AFTER THE EXTRAORDINARY SUMMIT IN

ATHENS ON APRIL 16/17, TO COINCIDE WITH THE ENLARGEMENT

TREATY SIGNING CEREMONY.

4. (C) THE PRIORITIES REPORTED REFTEL ARE STILL THERE,

GAIANI SAID, THEY WILL JUST HAVE TO TAKE THEIR PLACE IN THE

NEW PECKING ORDER. THE BALKANS, ILLEGAL IMMIGRATION CONTROL,

EU-RUSSIA, ENLARGEMENT, ARE ALL IMPORTANT TO THE GOI, AND

WILL BE GIVEN APPROPRIATE ATTENTION. ITALY WAS PARTICULARLY

CONCERNED THAT THE GRECO-ITALIAN PLAN TO RE-FOCUS EU

ATTENTION ON BALKANS DEVELOPMENT AND INTEGRATION REMAIN ON

TRACK. THE GOI WOULD DO WHAT IT COULD TO MAKE SURE IT DID.

THE EU WILL PROMOTE THE STABILIZATION AND ASSOCIATION PROCESS

(SAP) AS A "PRE-ACCESSION" MODEL TO HELP INTEGRATE SOUTHEAST

EUROPE INTO THE EU.

5. (C) OTHER POLITICAL ISSUES ON WHICH THE GOI WOULD FOCUS

INCLUDE:

- THE NEXT ROUND OF ENLARGEMENT: ITALY WOULD MAKE CERTAIN

THAT BULGARIA AND ROMANIA\'S SUPPORT FOR THE US ON IRAQ WOULD

NOT BE USED BY CERTAIN EU MEMBERS TO DERAIL THEIR ACCESSION.

ITALY WILL ALSO ENSURE THAT TURKISH ACCESSION REMAINS ON THE

AGENDA.

- EU/RUSSIA: ITALY WOULD USE THE STILL STRONG FRIENDSHIP

BETWEEN PUTIN AND BERLUSCONI TO MEND ANY DIFFERENCES FROM THE

WAR, AND TO BEGIN A MORE "STRUCTURED, FORMAL" RELATIONSHIP

BETWEEN MOSCOW AND BRUSSELS. THIS WOULD INCLUDE, FOR

EXAMPLE, FREQUENT MEETINGS BETWEEN THE COREPER AND RUSSIAN

OFFICIALS.

- EU AND "NEIGHBORING STATES": ITALY WILL USE ITS

PRESIDENCY TO PROMOTE THE INTEGRATION, BUT NOT THE ACCESSION,

OF STATES SUCH AS UKRAINE AND MOLDOVA.

- THE "MED" WOULD ALSO REMAIN A HIGH PRIORITY. ITALY HAD

HOPES THAT THE EURO-MED MINISTERIAL IN NAPLES IN NOVEMBER

WOULD ACT AS A CATALYST FOR PROCESSES RANGING FROM THE MEPP

TO MED IMMIGRATION CONTROL TO AN EU-MERCORSUR TREATY

AGREEMENT BY EARLY 2004.

THE ITALIAN PRESIDENCY WAS INCREASINGLY UNLIKELY. HE DID

7. (C) COMMENT: THAT THE WAR HAS BLOCKED PROGRESS ON

CONVENTION DISCUSSIONS, THEREBY JEOPARDIZING GOI PLANS FOR

SIGNING THE "CONSTITUTION" DURING ITS PRESIDENCY AND FORCING

IT TO ADJUST PRIORITIES, MUST BE A BLOW TO BERLUSCONI, GIVEN

THAT HE HAS MENTIONED NUMEROUS TIMES IN PUBLIC AND OFFICIAL

SETTINGS THAT IT WOULD BE THE CROWNING JEWEL OF THE ITALIAN

PRESIDENCY. HOWEVER, IT ALSO SIGNALS THAT ITALY IS READY TO

TAKE A PRAGMATIC APPROACH TO ITS PRESIDENCY. AS WE HAVE

REPORTED, AND AS EXEMPLIFIED IN HIS SELF-PERCEIVED ROLE AS

BROKER BETWEEN PUTIN AND THE WEST, BERLUSCONI LIKES NOTHING

MORE THAN TO ACT AS A GO-BETWEEN AND DEAL MAKER. IF HE CAN

INDEED PULL OFF A POLITICAL REUNITING OF THE EU AND A

RAPPROCHEMENT (PARDON THE FRENCH) BETWEEN EUROPE AND THE US,

THE SATISFACTION MAY BE SOME COMPENSATION FOR LOSING THE

CHANCE OF A SECOND "TREATY OF ROME."
