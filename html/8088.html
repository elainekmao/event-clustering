1. (C) SUMMARY: EMBASSY OFFICERS MET ON MAY 13 WITH

AMBASSADOR ANTONIO ARMELLINI, WHO WILL BE IN CHARGE OF

ITALY\'S CIVILIAN AND MILITARY PERSONNEL AS SPECIAL ENVOY IN

IRAQ AND REPRESENTATIVE TO THE INTERIM AUTHORITY (REFS A, B).

ARMELLINI RETURNED FROM BAGHDAD LAST WEEK HAVING RECEIVED,

HE SAID, A CLEAR APPEAL FROM ORHA FOR SWIFT ITALIAN

MOBILIZATION OF A TEAM OF TECHNICAL AND MANAGERIAL EXPERTS TO

INTEGRATE WITH ORHA AND IRAQ\'S EMERGING MINISTRIES.

ARMELLINI PLANS TO TRAVEL ALONE TO WASHINGTON ON MAY 18 TO

SECURE CLEAR ASSURANCES THAT THE TEAM THAT THE GOI IS

MOBILIZING MEETS USG NEEDS. ARMELLINI SAID THAT HIS TEAM OF

ABOUT 40 PEOPLE COULD BE DISPATCHED WITHIN THE NEXT TWO

WEEKS. HE SHARES OUR CONCERN THAT ITALIAN COORDINATION WITH

CENTCOM IMPROVE. END SUMMARY.

2. (C) ECMIN BEGAN THE MEETING WITH AMBASSADOR ARMELLINI BY

STRESSING THE FACT THAT IRAQ REMAINS A MILITARY ZONE, AND, AS

SUCH, COORDINATION WITH CENTCOM THROUGH THE GOI\'S LNO,

GENERAL SANTINI, REMAINS CRITICALLY IMPORTANT. ECMIN

DESCRIBED HOW THE EMBASSY\'S ECONOMIC AND POLITICAL SECTIONS

ARE ARRAYED ON IRAQ ISSUES, AND REVIEWED FOR ARMELLINI

VARIOUS CONDUITS FOR INFORMATION, INCLUDING WASHINGTON

BRIEFINGS, THE IMMINENT FORMATION OF THE HIGH LEVEL IRAQ

RECONSTRUCTION VIRTUAL WORKING GROUP, AND THE PUBLIC

INFORMATION PACKETS THAT THE EMBASSY HAS PLACED ON ITS WEB

SITE FOR PROSPECTIVE ITALIAN SUBCONTRACTORS AND DONORS OF

HUMANITARIAN ASSISTANCE. ON THE POLICY SIDE, ECMIN NOTED

THAT WE COUNTED ON ITALIAN SUPPORT FOR THE LIFTING OF

SANCTIONS AGAINST IRAQ AS A PREREQUISITE FOR THE MEDIUM AND

LONGER-TERM DEVELOPMENT OF THE IRAQI ECONOMY.

3. (C) ARMELLINI RESPONDED WITH THANKS FOR THIS INFORMATION,

ADDING THAT THE FLOW OF INFORMATION BETWEEN THE MFA AND THE

ITALIAN EMBASSY IN WASHINGTON WAS EXCELLENT. HE WENT ON TO

DESCRIBE HIS ROLE AS BEING THE REPRESENTATIVE OF THE MFA IRAQ

TASK FORCE ON THE GROUND IN IRAQ, COMMUTING BACK AND FORTH TO

THE REGION AS NEEDED. ARMELLINI DESCRIBED AS "UNPRECEDENTED"

THE IMPORTANCE THAT PM BERLUSCONI HAS GIVEN TO ITALY\'S "FULL

PARTICIPATION" ACROSS THE RANGE OF TECHNICAL AND MANAGERIAL

REQUIREMENTS THAT ORHA HAS IDENTIFIED. HE REPORTED THAT THE

GOI WAS FINALIZING THE IDENTIFICATION OF A 30-40 PERSON TEAM

TO SEND TO THE REGION IN THE COMING WEEKS. HE NOTED THAT THE

GOI WAS EAGER TO BE PART OF A DEMONSTRABLY SUCCESSFUL,

COLLECTIVE EFFORT IN IRAQ AND WANTS TO LEVERAGE ITALY\'S

STRONG HISTORY OF INVOLVEMENT IN THE REGION. THE GOI,

ARMELLINI ASSURED, WOULD PROVIDE FOR THE LOGISTICAL NEEDS OF

ITS TEAM.

4. (C) HAVING WITNESSED OUR SHORT-LIVED NEED FOR ITALY\'S

FIELD HOSPITAL AND OUR RECENT ANNOUNCEMENT OF NEW COALITION

LEADERSHIP IN IRAQ, ARMELLINI WAS CONCERNED THAT, JUST AS THE

GOI BEGINS AN UNPRECEDENTED MOBILIZATION, USG PRIORITIES MAY

SHIFT. HE WAS CURIOUS ABOUT THE PRESIDENT\'S APPOINTMENT OF

AMBASSADOR BREMER AS THE SENIOR COALITION OFFICIAL IN IRAQ

AND SPECIFICALLY WHETHER HE WOULD BE ARMELLINI\'S NEW

INTERLOCUTOR. ECMIN UNDERSCORED THAT THE PRESIDENT HAD

CLEARLY PRONOUNCED AMBASSADOR BREMER\'S AUTHORITY IN IRAQ. HE

ALSO AFFIRMED THAT ARMELLINI COULD EXPECT TO ENGAGE WITH

BREMER AT A POLITICAL LEVEL.

5. (C) ECMIN ASKED HOW ARMELLINI PLANNED TO ORGANIZE ON THE

GROUND. ARMELLINI REPLIED THAT HE WOULD HAVE OVERALL

RESPONSIBILITY FOR THE WHOLE ITALIAN MISSION IN IRAQ,

INCLUDING CIVILIAN AND MILITARY PERSONNEL. GENERAL CABIGIOSU

(PENDING CONFIRMATION) IS EXPECTED TO ACT AS HIS MILITARY

ADVISOR. UNDER ARMELLINI\'S LEADERSHIP, A COMMANDER WOULD

HAVE OPERATIONAL RESPONSIBILITY FOR ITALIAN TROOPS IN IRAQ AS

WELL AS MILITARY-TO-MILITARY LIAISON WITH THE BRITISH IN

BASRA.

6. (C) ECMIN RETURNED TO THE NEED TO IMPROVE COMMUNICATION

WITH CENTCOM, AND ARMELLINI NOTED THAT HE SHARED THE SAME

CONCERN. HE POINTED OUT THAT, DESPITE GETTING CLEARANCE FOR

HIS LAST TRIP TO BAGHDAD, WORD HAD NOT YET REACHED BAGHDAD BY

THE TIME HE ARRIVED. ARMELLINI ASKED WHETHER THE PROBLEM WAS

THE COMPLEX ROUTING OF INFORMATION OR WHETHER ADEQUATE

COMMUNICATIONS HAD YET TO BE SET UP. HE NOTED THAT GETTING

COORDINATION RIGHT WITH CENTCOM WAS A PRIORITY FOR GENERAL

CABIGIOSU.
