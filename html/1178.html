SUBJECT: ICTY: SFOR APPREHENSION OF DUSKO SIKIRICA

REF: A. 99 THE HAGUE 1976

B. 99 SARAJEVO 10160

1. EARLY IN THE MORNING OF JUNE 25, SFOR FORCES IN BOSNIA

DETAINED DUSKO SIKIRICA, A PUBLIC INDICTEE OF THE

INTERNATIONAL CRIMINAL TRIBUNAL FOR THE FORMER YUGOSLAVIA

(ICTY). SIKIRICA ARRIVED IN THE HAGUE AT APPROXIMATELY 14:15

ON JUNE 25 AND WAS TRANSFERRED TO THE ICTY\'S DETENTION

FACILITY IN THE HAGUE.

2. SIKIRICA, A BOSNIAN SERB, WAS THE LEAD INDICTEE IN THE

1995 "SIKIRICA AND OTHERS" INDICTMENT, WHICH FOCUSES ON

ABUSES OF DETAINEES AT THE KERATERM CAMP NEAR THE TOWN OF

PRIJEDOR. SIKIRICA IS ALLEGED TO HAVE BEEN THE COMMANDER OF

THE CAMP. UNDER THE INDICTMENT AS AMENDED IN 1998, HE IS

CHARGED WITH COMPLICITY IN GENOCIDE, AS WELL AS WELL AS

THIRTEEN ADDITIONAL COUNTS OF CRIMES AGAINST HUMANITY, GRAVE

BREACHES OF THE 1949 GENEVA CONVENTIONS, AND VIOLATIONS OF

THE LAWS OR CUSTOMS OF WAR, FOR KILLINGS, TORTURE, SEXUAL

ASSAULTS, AND THE UNLAWFUL CONFINEMENT OF CIVILIANS COMMITTED

BY HIS SUBORDINATES. FOR MOST CHARGES, SIKIRICA IS CHARGED

ON THE BASIS OF HIS SUPERIOR AUTHORITY OVER OTHERS IN THE

CAMP. WITH RESPECT TO ONE INCIDENT, HOWEVER, SIKIRICA IS

CHARGED BOTH ON THE BASIS OF HIS SUPERIOR AUTHORITY AND AS A

DIRECT PARTICIPANT IN THE SUMMARY EXECUTION OF TWENTY

KERATERM DETAINEES AS PUNISHMENT FOR THE ESCAPE OF SIX OTHER

DETAINEES.

3. SIKIRICA WAS ONE OF EIGHT INDIVIDUALS NAMED IN THE

AMENDED "SIKIRICA AND OTHERS" INDICTMENT. ONE OF SIKIRICA\'S

CO-INDICTEES UNDER THAT INDICTMENT, ZORAN ZIGIC, IS BEING

TRIED IN THE "KVOCKA AND OTHERS" CASE WITH FOUR OTHER

INDICTEES (MIROSLAV KVOCKA, MLADO RADIC, MILOJICA KOS, AND

DARGOLJUB PRCAC). THAT TRIAL CENTERS MAINLY ON EVENTS AT THE

OMARSKA DETENTION CAMP. TWO OF SIKIRICA\'S OTHER CO-INDICTEES

- DAMIR DOSEN AND DRAGAN KULUNDZIJA - ARE ALREADY IN CUSTODY

IN THE HAGUE (REFTELS) AND ARE AWAITING TRIAL UNDER A FURTHER

AMENDED INDICTMENT. THE DOSEN/KULUNDZIJA CASE IS LIKELY TO

COMMENCE SOMETIME THIS FALL. THE OFFICE OF THE PROSECUTOR

(OTP) EXPECTS TO FILE A MOTION SEEKING TO JOIN SIKIRICA TO

THE DOSEN/KULUNDZIJA CASE. THE FOUR OTHER INDICTEES UNDER

THE "SIKIRICA AND OTHERS" INDICTMENT AS AMENDED IN 1998 -

DRAGAN FUSTAR, NENAD BANOVIC, PREDRAG BANOVIC, AND DUSAN

KNEZEVIC - REMAIN AT LARGE.
