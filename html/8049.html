SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED TO JORDAN TO SUBMIT

AUTHORIZATION REQUEST TO 661 COMMITTEE FOR AIR SERV NGO

FLIGHT TO IRAQ

REF: STATE 126770

1. (SBU) On May 14, A/PolCouns delivered reftel points to

Ayman Aimiri, Deputy Director of the MFA\'s IO Department.

Aamiri said that he was unfamiliar with AIR SERV\'s request

and had not received any message asking for instructions from

Jordan\'s Mission in New York on this case. A/PolCouns

underlined the urgency and importance of AIR SERV\'s

humanitarian flights to the USG and requested Aamiri to

instruct Jordan\'s Mission in New York to submit the request

for authorization today, May 14. Aamiri promised he would do

that and did not expect there to be a problem. Aamiri also

provided his home and cell phone numbers to A/PolCouns to

convey to AIR SERV\'s Amman rep, Ms. Susan MacGregor (who had

also contacted the Embassy May 14 on the same issue).

A/PolCouns later spoke with Ms. MacGregor again and verified

that she had provided Aamiri with details relating to AIR

SERV\'s flights from Amman.
