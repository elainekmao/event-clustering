SUBJECT: NEA REGIONAL ENVIRONMENT OFFICE HOSTS NGO GRANT-WRITING

WORKSHOP

1. (U) The NEA Regional Environment Office sponsored a one-day

grant-writing workshop for the Jordanian environmental NGO

community on July 22. The workshop trained 22 participants in

the concrete skills of writing proposals for improved fundraising

and NGO sustainability. The Regional Environment Office summer

intern, Elissa Slotkin, developed, organized, and facilitated the

workshop.

2. (U) The grant-writing workshop targeted representatives from

Jordanian environmental NGO\'s, most of whom had no formal

training in grant or proposal writing. The goal of the workshop

was both to strengthen future proposals that the organizations

hope to submit under the OES Initiative and to other donors, and

to build the capacity of the NGO community in Jordan. The

training focused on practical skill building, and participants

spent the majority of the day practicing the mechanics of grant-

writing in pairs. The workshop also facilitated first-time

networking among participants and provided an opportunity for the

Regional Environment Office to show its commitment to Jordanian

civil society.

3. (U) The grant-writing handbook, with samples and interactive

exercises, that our summer intern developed can be shared with

other posts wishing to conduct similar training. Contact the NEA

Regional Environment Office, Paul Malik or Rana Safadi, via

unclassified Department email for copies of the materials.
