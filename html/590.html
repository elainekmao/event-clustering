2. HAVING DISCUSSED WITH RUBINE THE QUESTIONS TO BE

ASKED, CHARGE, WITH THE DEPARTMENT\'S CONCURRENCE, IS

WILLING TO DO THE INTERVIEW. IN OUR DISCUSSIONS WITH

RUBINE, SHE HAS VOLUNTEERED THAT SHE IS IMPRESSED

WITH THE USG\'S AND THE DEPARTMENT\'S RECORD ON THE

ATROCITIES/WAR CRIMES ANGLE OF THE EX-YUGO TRAGEDY.

SHE WOULD ASK THE CHARGE HOW ONE PERSONALLY ARRIVES

AT THE CONCLUSION THAT THE ALLEGATIONS OF MASS

ATROCITIES AND RAPES ARE CREDIBLE AND ABOUT THE

DEGREE TO WHICH THE ATROCITIES THUS FAR REPORTED

APPEAR TO HAVE BEEN SYSTEMATIC, AS OPPOSED TO THE

RANDOM ACTS OF UNDISCIPLINED SOLDIERS AT WAR. AT

CHARGE\'S INSISTENCE, THE QUESTIONS WOULD HAVE TO BE

FOCUSSED ON HIS PERSONAL IMPRESSIONS OF THE ATROCITY

PHENOMENON, NOT/NOT ON LEGAL OPINIONS REGARDING WAR

CRIMES, OR CURRENT, BROADER USG EX-YUGO POLICY.

3. RUBINE WOULD LIKE TO DO THE INTERVIEW IN THE NEXT

TWO DAYS. THEREFORE, WE WOULD APPRECIATE CONCURRENCE
