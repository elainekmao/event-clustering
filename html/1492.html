SUBJECT: NO FORMAL MEETINGS YET BETWEEN DRC ALLIES AND

DRC GOVERNMENT

REF: HARARE 450

CLASSIFIED BY CHARGE D\'AFFAIRES EARL M. IRVING FOR

REASONS: 1.5 (B) AND (D).

1. (C) POLOFF SPOKE TO MAXWELL CHIKOROWONDO, THE

ZIMBABWE MFA\'S DRC DESK OFFICER, ON JANUARY 24.

CHIKOROWONDO HAD JUST RETURNED FROM KINSHASA AND LUANDA,

CONFIDENTIAL

WHERE HE ACCOMPANIED PRESIDENT MUGABE TO KABILA\'S

FUNERAL AND THE DRC ALLIES\' MEETING. CHIKOROWONDO TOLD

POLOFF THAT PRESIDENT MUGABE AND THE OTHER ALLIED HEADS

OF STATE DID NOT/NOT HOLD A FORMAL MEETING WITH JOSEPH

KABILA. THEY MERELY "CHATTED" WITH HIM DURING THE

FUNERAL. THE ZIMBABWE MFA IS CURRENTLY WAITING FOR

INSTRUCTIONS TO ARRANGE A LUSAKA SIGNATORIES MEETING,

ACCORDING TO THE DESK OFFICER. HE ALSO REPORTED THAT

THE YOUNGER KABILA WAS NOT SWORN IN AS PRESIDENT, BUT

THAT HE PROBABLY WILL BE IN THE NEXT FEW DAYS. IN

LUANDA ON JANUARY 21, THE HEADS OF STATE AGREED THAT

THERE IS "NO GOING BACK" ON THE PEACE PROCESS, AND THAT

THEY WILL DO ALL THEY CAN TO ENSURE THAT IT GOES

FORWARD, CHIKOROWONDO REPORTED.

2. (C) ON THE SUBJECT OF THE INVESTIGATION INTO

KABILA\'S MURDER, THE DRC DESK OFFICER SAID IT IS

DIFFICULT TO JUDGE HOW LONG THE INVESTIGATION WILL TAKE.

HE BELIEVES THAT ANGOLA HAD NOTHING TO WITH THE

ASSASSINATION BECAUSE, AS HE PUT IT, "ALL THE ALLIES

WORKED WELL WITH KABILA." CHIKOROWONDO WAS LESS

SANGUINE ABOUT UGANDA AND RWANDA. WHEN ASKED IF THEIR

GOVERNMENTS HAD TRIED TO CONTACT THE GOZ, HE SAID THERE

HAD BEEN NO OFFICIAL CONTACT AS OF YET. CHIKOROWONDO

ADDED THAT HE DOES NOT THINK UGANDA AND RWANDA WERE

BEHIND THE KILLING, BUT NOTED THEY ARE "ENEMIES OF THE

CONGO," AND SAID HE WAS SURPRISED AT HOW QUICKLY KIGALI

HAD REPORTED THE SHOOTING OF KABILA (WITHIN AN HOUR OF

ITS OCCURRENCE). NONETHELESS, HE HAS NOT HEARD OF THEIR

REBEL ALLIES PREPARING ANY OFFENSIVE ACTION.

3. (C) COMMENT: CHIKOROWONDO BASICALLY STUCK TO THE GOZ

LINE ON THE DRC. HOWEVER, HIS REPORT THAT THE HEADS OF

STATE PUT OFF A FORMAL MEETING WITH JOSEPH KABILA AT AN

OPPORTUNE TIME SUGGESTS THEY ARE RELUCTANT TO MOVE

FORWARD ON THE PEACE PROCESS UNTIL THEY HAVE A FIRMER

IDEA OF WHO IN THE DRC SUPPORTS HIM. END COMMENT.
