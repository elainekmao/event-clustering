SUBJECT: JORDAN: SPECIAL MEDIA REACTION ON MEPI

SUMMARY

-- THE ANNOUNCEMENT OF THE MIDDLE EAST PARTNERSHIP

INITIATIVE (MEPI) RECEIVED EXTENSIVE COVERAGE IN THE

JORDANIAN PRESS OVER THE WEEKEND, DECEMBER 13-16. THE

SPEECH RECEIVED FRONT-PAGE COVERAGE IN ALL OF JORDAN\'S

ARABIC DAILIES ON THE 13TH, WITH REPORTING BASED ON

WIRE SERVICE REPORTS. SUBSEQUENTLY, THE INITIATIVE

HAS BEEN A MAIN SUBJECT FOR JORDANIAN COMMENTATORS.

EARLY REPORTING CARRIED STATEMENTS BY JORDAN\'S KING

ABDULLAH II WELCOMING THE INITIATIVE, AND INDICATING

THAT THE GOVERNMENT OF JORDAN SUPPORTS THE MAIN AIMS OF

THE INITIATIVE AS A MEANS TO IMPROVE CONDITIONS IN THE

MIDDLE EAST. COMMENTARY HAS BEEN MIXED, WITH MANY

WRITERS WELCOMING THE INTRODUCTION OF THE IDEAS

INFORMING THE INITIATIVE INTO PUBLIC DEBATE, WHILE

REMAINING SUSPICIOUS OF ULTIMATE U.S. MOTIVES AND

CALLING FOR GREATER "BALANCE" IN U.S. POLICIES TOWARD

THE ARAB-ISRAELI CONFLICT.

PRESSURE ON ARAB REGIMES WILL FAIL BECAUSE IT WILL BE

-- "ONE MORE ABOUT MEPI"

DAILY COLUMNIST URAYB RINTAWI IN CENTER-LEFT

INFLUENTIAL AL-DUSTOUR ARABIC DAILY COMMENTED FOR A

SECOND CONSECUTIVE DAY ON MEPI (12/15). HE VIEWS THE

THREE "PILLARS" OF THE INITIATIVE, ECONOMIC REFORM,

POLITICAL REFORM AND EDUCATIONAL REFORM, AND SAYS THAT

THAT THE AMERICAN VIEWPOINT OF THE ARAB WORLD IN THESE

FIELDS IS JUSTIFIED. ON THE ECONOMIC FRONT, RINTAWI

SAYS THAT THE "ARAB IMAGE THAT SECRETARY POWELL

PORTRAYED IS AS SHAMEFUL AS IT CAN BE" AND GOES ON TO

REVIEW THE SECRETARY\'S STATISTICS ABOUT THE

DETERIORATION OF ARAB ECONOMIES. ON THE POLITICAL

FRONT, RINTAWI SAYS THERE IS MUCH TO BE DONE. ON THE

EDUCATION FRONT, RINTAWI SAYS THE SAME THING. RINTAWI

MOVES ON TO JORDAN AND SAYS THAT THE SECRETARY POWELL

DID NOT MENTION JORDAN WHEN HE TALKED ABOUT PROMISING

COUNTRIES IN THE FIELD OF DEMOCRACY AND FREEDOM AND

WONDERS WHETHER "THE PROBLEM IS WITH POWELL OR WITH

US".

-- "POWELL AND THE ARABS: INCREASING DEMOCRATIC

IMMUNITY!"

DAILY COLUMNIST YASER ZAATREH WRITES IN AL-DUSTOUR

ABOUT MEPI (12/15). HE BEGINS BY VIEWING THE ECONOMIC

STATISTICS QUOTED BY SECRETARY POWELL AND THE CLAIM

THAT THE DETERIORATION IN THE ARAB WORLD PRODUCED THE

9/11 ATTACKERS. ZAATREH ARGUES THAT THE 9/11 ATTACKERS

DID NOT SUFFER FROM UNEMPLOYMENT OR POVERTY, AND THAT

THE FACT THAT THEY WERE "LOW ON DEMOCRACY" SHOULD HAVE

COMPELLED THEM TO ATTACK THEIR OWN GOVERNMENTS RATHER

THAN THE U.S. HE ASKS SECRETARY POWELL: "DIDN\'T YOUR

ADVISORS TELL YOU THAT SUCH A DEMOCRACY WILL BRING MORE

CONTEMPT FOR THE UNITED STATES?" HE GOES ON TO SAY

THAT THE US ADMINISTRATION REFUSES TO DEAL WITH FACTS

ON THE GROUND THAT LED TO THE 9/11 ATTACKS, NAMELY THE

FACT THAT "THE CONTEMPT FOR THE UNITED STATES STEMS

ONLY FROM THE UNITED STATES\' HUMILIATION OF ARABS AND

MUSLIMS, FROM STEALING THEIR WEALTH AND FORTUNE AND

FROM SUPPORTING THE KILLING OF THEIR CHILDREN IN

PALESTINE, THE MOST SACRED AND MOST IMPORTANT CAUSE FOR

THE MUSLIMS."

-- "A FAILING RECIPE FOR A DISTORTED SITUATION"

DAILY COLUMNIST MUSA HAWAMDEH IN AL-DUSTOUR (12/15)

ARGUES THAT THE U.S. IS ONLY INTERESTED IN DEMOCRACY IN

AS FAR AS IT SERVES ITS OWN INTERESTS, THAT THE

"DEMOCRACY THAT WASHINGTON WANTS IS A DISTORTED KIND OF

DEMOCRACY. THE UNITED STATES WANTS TO OPEN THE ARAB

LANDS FOR AMERICA FIRST, THEN IT WANTS TO GIVE ORDERS

AND TO GET RID OF ANYTHING THAT COULD CREATE ANIMOSITY

FOR THE UNITED STATES." HAWAMDEH CONCLUDES THAT THE

UNITED STATES "WOULD SAVE MILLIONS OF DOLLARS IF IT

WOULD DEAL WITH THE ISRAELI OCCUPATION AS A SOURCE OF

TERROR AND WORRY, AND IF IT WOULD CONSIDER THE IDEA

THAT ITS INTERESTS MAY NOT NECESSARILY COME INTO

CONFLICT WITH ARAB INTERESTS IF IT WOULD BECOME AN

HONEST COUNTRY AND APPLY UN RESOLUTIONS" TO ISRAEL.

-- "AN IMPOSED PARTNERSHIP?"

DAILY COLUMNIST HILMI AL-ASMAR IN AL-DUSTOUR WONDERS

(12/15) WHETHER MEPI IS AN "IMPOSED" PARTNERSHIP AND

WHETHER THE "OTHER PARTNER", THE ARABS, WANTS THAT

PARTNERSHIP. AL-ASMAR SAYS THAT HE DOES "NOT HOLD ANY

PERSONAL GRUDGE AGAINST AMERICA AND ITS CITIZENS. IN

FACT I AM JEALOUS OF THEIR GREAT ACHIEVEMENTS AND THEIR

CONTRIBUTION TO THE PROGRESS OF HUMANITY." HE ADDS

THAT "AT THE SAME TIME, I BELONG TO A PEOPLE WHO SEE

THEMSELVES AS VICTIMS OF AMERICA, JUST AS MUCH AS THEY

SEE THEMSELVES VICTIMS OF DILAPIDATING ARAB AND ISLAMIC

REGIMES WHO ACQUIRED THEIR OPPRESSIVE AND DESTRUCTIVE

\'LEGITIMACY\' FROM AMERICA\'S UNLIMITED SUPPORT".

-- "WHAT HINDERS DEMOCRACY IN THIS REGION?"

DAILY COLUMNIST JAMIL NIMRI WRITES (12/15) IN AL-ARAB

AL-YAWM ABOUT THE OBSTACLES OF DEMOCRACY IN THE ARAB

WORLD. HE SAYS THAT "IF THE REGIMES IN THIS REGION

WERE TO ABIDE BY WHAT POLLS AND BALLOTS TELL THEM, THEY

WOULD BE FACING A REAL DISASTER, BECAUSE THE PUBLIC

WANTS WHAT THESE REGIMES CANNOT ACCEPT, SUCH AS

FIGHTING ISRAEL AND THE AMERICAN ANTICIPATED WAR ON

IRAQ." NIMRI MAINTAINS THAT "THE REGIMES HAVE

DEVELOPED AN IRON GRIP ON AUTHORITY BECAUSE THEY DO NOT

TRUST, IN VIEW OF THEIR CURRENT POLICIES THAT ARE

\'REQUIRED BY THE WEST\', THAT THEY WOULD BE ABLE TO STAY

IN POWER FOR A DAY IF THE FINAL WORD IS GIVEN BACK TO

THE PEOPLE." NIMRI CONTINUES TO ARGUE THAT "NATIONAL

AND RELIGIOUS EXTREMISM FEEDS OFF THE CONFLICT IN

PALESTINE" AND THAT "SINCE THE CALL [FOR DEMOCRACY] IS

COMING FROM THE UNITED STATES, ISRAEL\'S ALLY WHO

ACCEPTS EVEN SHARON\'S BLOODY POLICIES, IT IS DIFFICULT

TO UNDERSTAND HOW THE UNITED STATES IS GOING TO

CONTRIBUTE TO THE SUPPORT OF DEMOCRACY WITHOUT

CONTRIBUTING TO RESOLVING THE PALESTINIAN ISSUE."
