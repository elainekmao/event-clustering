SUBJECT: NORTHERN IRAQ: TURKS GIVE CONDITIONAL "GREEN

LIGHT" TO CODEL BIDEN\'S REQUEST

REF: A) ANKARA 8638 B) STATE 241093 C) ANKARA 7563

(U) Classified by Deputy Chief of Mission Robert Deutsch.

Reasons: 1.5 (B and D).

1. (U) This is an action request. See para 4.

2. (S) MFA DG for Middle Eastern Affairs Burcuoglu notified

DCM November 26 that the GOT had approved Codel Biden\'s

request to travel to northern Iraq via Turkey December 4-5

(refs a-b). However, given the Ramadan holiday (December 4

at 1300 through December 7), Burcuoglu said that appropriate

GOT officials would not be available to meet with the Codel

December 6 upon its return from northern Iraq. Burcuoglu

stressed the importance the GOT attaches to meeting with

visiting USG officials and Codels, and expressed hope the

Codel could adjust its schedule to arrive in Ankara in time

to meet with GOT officials December 4 in the morning (before

the start of the holiday.) Despite the busy schedule that

day (UK FM Straw, Greek FM Papendreau, other USD

distinguished visitors), Burcuoglu said they would arrange

appropriate meetings.

3. (S) Burcuoglu reminded the DCM of the preconditions

attached to the GOT\'s approval for Staffdel Talwar to travel

to northern Iraq via Turkey in October -- one-time deal and

no publicity (ref b) -- and said these conditions still

applied. Recognizing that it would be difficult to avoid

publicity given the Codel\'s visibility, Burcuoglu expressed

hope that the visit could be handled in such as a way as to

avoid negative reactions in the Turkish press and additional

requests to travel to northern Iraq via Turkey by other US

and European VIPs or interested individuals.

4. (S) Action request: Embassy requests that the Department

advise Codel Biden of the "conditions" that the MFA attached

to its approval for the visit (i.e. one-time deal and no

publicity). Embassy also asks that the Department inform the

Codel that GOT officials would only be available December 4

in the morning. We believe it is important for the Senators

to hold these meetings. If the Codel concurs, the Embassy

will officially request appointments with appropriate GOT

officials.

PEARSON
