WITHOUT THE COMPLETE MILITARY DEFEAT OF THE ILL-FAMED SAO-PRESIDENT, TRUCK DRIVER BOZIDAR VUCUREVIC, THE ENDING OF THE BESTIAL DESTRUCTION OF DUBROVNIK IS OUT OF THE QUESTION, EVEN LESS THE LIBERATION OF KONAVLI IN THE EASTERN PART OF THE DUBROVNIK COMMUNITY WHICH IS UNDER JNA OCCUPATION. THIS HAS BEEN CONFIRMED BY THE NEWEST ATTACK, WHEN VUCUREVIC\'S ARTILLERY OF THE UNCLASSIFIED UNCLASSIFIED PAGE 03 ZAGREB 01002 01 OF 02 171305Z "HERCEGOVIAN CORPS" FROM THE DIRECTION OF TREBINJE, TOGETHER WITH THE JNA MARITIME SECTOR FOR BOKA KOTORSKA, FROM OCCUPIED CILIPI, HAVE DESTROYED DUBROVNIK. SINCE THE OCCUPYING FORCES HAVE SUFFERED HEAVY DEFEAT IN THE WESTERN PART OF THE DUBROVNIK COMMUNITY AND HAVE WITHDRAWN FROM STRATEGIC POSITIONS NEAR DUBROVNIK, FAVORABLE CONDITIONS HAVE BEEN CREATED FOR SUCH A RETALIATION. ON THE OTHER HAND, CROATIA HAS RECOGNIZED B-H AS A SOVEREIGN AND INDEPENDENT STATE AND ON THAT ACCOUNT CANNOT DIRECT ITS ARMY ON THAT TERRITORY IN SPITE OF THE FACT THAT DUBROVNIK IS CONSTANTLY THREATENED FROM THIS TERRITORY. SERBIA AND MONTENEGRO, WHICH DID NOT RECOGNIZE B-H, WOULD BARELY WAIT TO INFORM THE WORLD ABOUT CROATIAN INVOLVEMENT IN THE WAR ON THE B-H TERRITORY.
