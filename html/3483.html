1. (U) THIS IS AN ACTION REQUEST. PLEASE SEE PARA 3.

2. (C) WE UNDERSTAND FROM E-MAIL TRAFFIC THAT THE USG

PREFERS TO ACCEPT ITALY\'S OFFER OF TROOPS FOR OPERATION

ENDURING FREEDOM (OEF) RATHER THAN THE GOI\'S OFFER TO

BACKFILL IN THE BALKANS (REF A). IT WILL TAKE CAREFUL

HANDLING BY PRIME MINISTER BERLUSCONI AND DEFENSE MINISTER

MARTINO TO SHEPHERD THIS THROUGH PARLIAMENTARY CONSULTATIONS.

WITH BERLUSCONI AND MANY SENIOR DECISION-MAKERS OUT OF ROME

ON HOLIDAY, A GOI "GAME-PLAN" MAY TAKE SOME TIME TO

ORCHESTRATE. THE NEED TO PLAN -- AND IMPLEMENT -- A

POLITICAL STRATEGY MAY EXPLAIN WHY NO SPECIFIC INSTRUCTIONS

HAVE BEEN ISSUED THROUGH THE MOD CHAIN OF COMMAND REGARDING

BERLUSCONI\'S CONDITIONAL DECISION TO CONTRIBUTE TROOPS. THE

OPPOSITION, PRESS AND PUBLIC COULD EASILY MISINTERPRET

USG-GOI PRELIMINARY STEPS TO COORDINATE ITALIAN PARTICIPATION

AS AN ATTEMPT BY THE GOI TO CIRCUMVENT PARLIAMENTARY REVIEW

-- AND ITALY\'S OPPOSITION, WHICH LACKS MANY ISSUES IT CAN USE

AGAINST THE POPULAR PRIME MINISTER, WOULD BE ONLY TOO EAGER

TO TAKE ADVANTAGE OF SUCH AN OPPORTUNITY. THIS COULD CREATE

SIGNIFICANT POLITICAL FALL-OUT FOR BERLUSCONI, EVEN

ULTIMATELY DERAILING THE OFFER TO CONTRIBUTE. DUE TO THE

EXTREMELY SENSITIVE NATURE OF THIS ISSUE POLITICALLY WITHIN

ITALY, WE URGE CAREFUL, COMPREHENSIVE AND QUIET USG-GOI

COORDINATION, AND EMPHASIZE THAT THE GOI OFFER REMAINS

SUBJECT TO REVIEW BY PARLIAMENT WHEN IT RECONVENES IN

MID-SEPTEMBER. IN ADDITION, WE SHOULD NOT ASSUME AT THIS

POINT THAT THE GREEN LIGHT WE RECEIVED FROM BERLUSCONI

EXTENDS BEYOND THE REQUEST FOR GROUND TROOPS OR SPECIAL OPS

FORCES SPECIFIED IN REF B. IN PARTICULAR, ALL USG AGENCIES

SHOULD AVOID PUBLIC COMMENT ON THE LIKELIHOOD OF ITALIAN

PARTICIPATION IN ORDER TO AVOID COMPLICATING THE GOI\'S

MANAGEMENT OF PUBLIC REACTION. WE SHOULD NOT BE THE ONES TO

ANNOUNCE ITALY\'S COOPERATION.

3. (C) EMBASSY IS INFORMED THAT A REQUEST FOR FORCES IS BEING

PREPARED IN COORDINATION WITH THE ITALIAN LNO CELL AT CENTCOM

THAT INCLUDES NOT ONLY THE FORCES REQUESTED IN REF B, WHICH

BERLUSCONI HAS APPROVED IN PRINCIPLE, BUT ALSO ENGINEERS AND

DEMINERS. IN REF A, GOI OFFICIALS ASKED FOR A GENERAL

STATEMENT OF WHICH FORCES, AND FOR WHAT MISSION, THE USG IS

SEEKING FROM ITALY. WE PROPOSE THE FOLLOWING MESSAGE FOR

DELIVERY TO OFFICIALS AT MOD, MFA AND IN THE PRIME MINISTER\'S

OFFICE:

-- OUR REQUEST: THE USG IS REQUESTING THAT ITALY CONTRIBUTE A

LIGHT INFANTRY MANEUVER BATTALION, EQUIPPED WITH LIGHT

RECONNAISSANCE/SECURITY VEHICLES, TO REPLACE A COALITION

BATTALION DUE TO ROTATE OUT OF AFGHANISTAN.

-- THE MISSION: THE PROPOSED MISSION FOR THE ITALIAN

BATTALION IS SENSITIVE SITE EXPLORATION, TIME-SENSITIVE

TARGET POST-STRIKE EXPLOITATION, SUPPORT TO HUMANITARIAN

OPERATIONS, AIRFIELD SECURITY, AREA RECONNAISSANCE AND COMBAT

OPERATIONS TO DESTROY AL-QAIDA/TALIBAN FORCES. SPECIFIC

BASING LOCATION FOR ITALIAN FORCES IS TO BE DETERMINED.
