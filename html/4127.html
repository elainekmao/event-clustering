SUBJECT: Request for a DVC Program in Honor of Black History

Month.

1. Summary: In honor of Black History Month, post would like

to request IIP\'s support in organizing a 90 minute DVC

program discussing African-American Literature.

2. We expect an audience of 40 - 50 people

for this DVC program. The audience will consist of American

Studies students from Jordanian universities, writers,

university faculty members and scholars.

3. Our recommendation of a theme and timing for the program

is as follows:

- African-American Literature - Readings and discussions

featuring author Alice Walker: Wednesday, February 26, 1500

GMT

4. The control officer for this program will be CAO Donna

Winton, Tel: 962-6-592-0101, Ext. 2115; Fax: 962-6-592-0121.

GNEHM
