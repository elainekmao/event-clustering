SUBJECT: ILO/PALESTINIAN RESOLUTION: DEMARCHE TO JORDAN

REF: SECSTATE 107848

1. Per reftel, on June 5 PolCouns delivered ref points to

Ali al-Ayed, Personal Assistant to the Foreign Minister.

PolCouns emphasized that the draft resolution on the

situation of workers in the Occupied Territories contains

subjects outside the mandate of the ILO and is an attempt to

politicize the body. He urged Jordan to vote against the

resolution on June 7. Ayed promised to pass our points to

the Foreign Minister and the Ministry of Labor. In addition,

on June 6 POLOFF also contacted the Ministry of Labor.
