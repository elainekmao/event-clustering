SUBJECT: PROGRAM EVALUATION FOR U.S. SPEAKER MICHAEL

BARONE, SENIOR WRITER, "U.S. NEWS AND WORLD REPORT;" ROME,

MARCH 18-19, 2002

1. DESCRIPTION OF ACTIVITY: PA ROME ORGANIZED A TWO DAY

SPEAKER PROGRAM FOR U.S. SPEAKER MICHAEL BARONE, AUTHOR,

SENIOR WRITER, U.S. NEWS AND WORLD REPORT, AND CO-AUTHOR OF

THE "ALMANAC OF AMERICAN POLITICS", MARCH 18-19, 2002 IN

ROME. ON TUESDAY, MARCH 18, BARONE SPOKE ABOUT HIS BOOK,

"THE NEW AMERICANS: HOW THE MELTING POT CAN WORK AGAIN" AT

A CONFERENCE ON "MULTICULTURALISM IN THE U.S. AND EUROPE

AFTER SEPTEMBER 11" AT THE UNIVERSITY OF ROME "LA SAPIENZA."

ON WEDNESDAY, MARCH 19, HE SPOKE ON THE "U.S. CONGRESS" AT A

ROUNDTABLE DISCUSSION WITH ITALIAN PARLIAMENT MEMBERS AND

STAFF AND JOURNALISTS AT THE AMERICAN STUDIES CENTER IN

ROME. PA ROME ALSO FACILITATED A NUMBER OF APPOINTMENTS FOR

BARONE WITH ITALIAN MINISTERS AND AMBASSADOR SEMBLER.

BARONE WAS INTERVIEWED BY SEVERAL MEDIA OUTLETS, INCLUDING

RADIO RAI (NATIONAL ITALIAN RADIO AND TELEVISION NETWORK)

AND ADN KRONOS (NEWS AGENCY).

2. DATES: APRIL 18-19; 3RD QUARTER; FISCAL YEAR:

FY2002.

3. JUSTIFICATION/OBJECTIVE: AS A FOLLOW-UP TO PA ITALY\'S

IMMIGRATION CONFERENCE IN MAY 2001, THE UNIVERSITY OF ROME

ORGANIZED A MAJOR TWO-DAY EVENT INVOLVING OVER 25 SPEAKERS,

ON IMMIGRATION IN ITALY. TO CONTRIBUTE AN AMERICAN VOICE,

POST SUGGESTED AUTHOR MICHAEL BARONE, AS HIS NEW BOOK TAKES

A NOVEL APPROACH TO THE AMERICAN "MELTING POT" AND

MULTICULTURALISM. BECAUSE INTEGRATION IS AN HIGHLY DEBATED

ISSUE HERE IN ITALY, POST WANTED TO PRESENT BARONE\'S STRESS

ON THE IMPORTANCE OF WEAVING NEW IMMIGRANTS INTO "THE FABRIC

OF AMERICAN LIFE".

WE ALSO WANTED TO TAKE ADVANTAGE OF BARONE\'S EXPERTISE ON

AMERICAN POLITICS TO ENGAGE KEY ITALIAN PARLIAMENT MEMBERS

AND STAFF IN A DISCUSSION ON CURRENT ISSUES IN THE U.S.

CONGRESS.

4. MPP UMBRELLA THEMES: COOPERATION ON GLOBAL ISSUES,

PUBLIC DIPLOMACY

5. AUDIENCE REACHED: ITALIAN ACADEMICS, SOCIOLOGISTS,

PARLIAMENT MEMBERS AND STAFFERS, GOI OFFICIALS, JOURNALISTS.

6. RESULT/IMPACT: BARONE\'S PROGRAMS IN ROME PROVIDED AN

OPPORTUNITY TO CONNECT WITH KEY ITALIAN AUDIENCES ON A

VARIETY OF TIMELY SUBJECTS. IN PARTICULAR, ON IMMIGRATION

ISSUES WHICH ARE OF CRUCIAL IMPORTANCE FOR OUR COUNTRIES

ESPECIALLY AFTER SEPTEMBER 11, WHEN, DUE TO GROWING SECURITY

CONCERNS, THERE HAS BEEN A MORE RESTRICTIVE IMMIGRATION

POLICY. ITALIAN SOCIOLOGISTS, JOURNALISTS WERE ALL EAGER

TO HEAR ABOUT BARONE\'S NEW LOOK AT WHAT THE RICH AND VARIED

HISTORY OF IMMIGRANT GROUPS IN AMERICA CAN TEACH US ABOUT

THE FUTURE. BARONE ENCOURAGED ITALY TO A MAJOR OPENNESS

TOWARD IMMIGRATION: AMERICA HAS ALWAYS BEEN A NATION OF

IMMIGRANTS WHO BRING WITH THEM DIFFERENT GROUPS OF THINKING

AND VALUES, AND IT HAS ALWAYS SUCCEEDED IN BRINGING THEM

TOGETHER IN A COMMON CIVIC CULTURE. BARONE\'S CHALLENGING

MESSAGE WAS THAT: THE MELTING POT STILL HOLDS ITS POWER:

THE PROMISE THAT ALL IMMIGRANTS CAN BE TRANSFORMED INTO "ONE

AMERICA" WHERE ALL CITIZENS ARE UNITED IN DEMOCRACY, FREEDOM

AND CIVIC RESPONSIBILITY.

BARONE WAS EQUALLY IMPRESSIVE AND ENGAGING IN HIS

PRESENTATION ON CURRENT ISSUES IN THE U.S. CONGRESS,

ADDRESSED TO ITALIAN PARLIAMENT OFFICIALS AND JOURNALISTS.

(IN A FOLLOW UP ARTICLE FOR THE "U.S. NEWS AND WORLD REPORT"

ENTITLED "EUROPEANS FOR THE U.S.," BARONE REITERATES THAT

ITALY, AN IMPORTANT MEMBER OF THE EUROPEAN UNION, REMAINS A

STRONG SUPPORTER OF THE UNITED STATES AND GEORGE BUSH ON

SEVERAL FOREIGN POLICY ISSUES.)
