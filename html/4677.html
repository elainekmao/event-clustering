SUBJECT: COOPERATIVE AIRSPACE INITIATIVE AND BALKANS

DEMARCHES DELIVERED

REF: A. STATE 225669

B. STATE 252492

(U) Classified by Pol-Mil Counselor Stuart Brown, reasons

1.5, b/d.

1. (C) On December 11, Pol-MilOff delivered reftel A demarche

on the Cooperative Airspace Initiative (CAI) to MFA/NATO

officer Oya Ergin emphasizing the importance to the NRC of

succeeding in this effort collectively. Ergin noted the MFA

had been discussing this issue with the Turkish General Staff

and understood the importance of the initiative. However,

the GOT was waiting to see how other Allies supported the CAI

before determining Turkish support.

2. (U) Ergin also received reftel B demarche on NATO\'s

long-term role in the Balkans promising to share the

information with all appropriate officials in the GOT.
