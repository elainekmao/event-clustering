2. WE ARE FRANKLY APPALLED BY THE THRICE-REPEATED

CLAIM IN REF A THAT THE EVENTS IN AND AROUND PROZOR

OVER 6 WEEKS AGO CONSTITUTE HARD EVIDENCE OF PAST AND

LIKELY FUTURE CROATIAN ETHNIC CLEANSING IN BOSNIA.

FROM A POLICY STANDPOINT, THIS PERSISTENT ALLEGATION

IS DANGEROUSLY DISTRACTING. AND PLAINLY INACCURATE

CONFIDENTIAL

IN FACT. END SUMMARY COMMENT.

3. WE ARE TROUBLED BY THE IMPLICATION THROUGHOUT

REF A THAT THE FLIGHT OF ANY NUMBER OF PERSONS OF ANY

ETHNIC GROUP IN B-H UNDER ANY ACTUAL OR FEARED THREAT

FROM MEMBERS OF ANY OTHER ETHNIC GROUP CONSTITUTES,

IPSO FACTO, "ETHNIC CLEANSING." TO APPLY THIS VERY

EVOCATIVE TERM IN SUCH A MANNER IN OUR VIEW DEBASES

ITS CONSIDERABLE USEFULNESS. THROUGHOUT THE LONG,

WRENCHING SAGA OF YUGOSLAVIA\'S BREAKUP, THE LEAP FROM

SIMPLE RECOGNITION THAT NO SIDE IS WITHOUT BLAME TO

THE POLICY-STULTIFYING CONCLUSION OF VIRTUAL MORAL

EQUIVALENCE HAS BEEN MADE FAR TOO OFTEN AND TOO

QUICKLY. AGAINST THAT BACKGROUND, WE LAUD THE FACT

THAT USG SPOKESMEN HAVE BECOME INCREASINGLY CLEAR

ABOUT WHO IS RESPONSIBLE FOR "ETHNIC CLEANSING" IN

BOSNIA. AND WE THINK THE USG CAN TAKE PRIDE IN THE

LEADING ROLE IT HAS PLAYED BOTH IN PUSHING FOR A WAR

CRIMES TRIBUNAL TO IDENTIFY THE GUILTY (ON ANY SIDE)

AND IN ASSEMBLING EVIDENCE TO ASSIST IN THAT PROCESS.

CONTINUATION OF THIS USG CLEAR-HEADEDNESS WILL BE

VITAL, WE BELIEVE, IF WE ARE TO COUNTER REPORTED

SIGNALS FROM LORD OWEN, AMONG OTHERS, THAT TALK OF

WAR CRIMES TRIBUNALS MAY NEED TO BE MUTED IN THE

INTEREST OF FOSTERING POLITICAL COMPROMISE IN B-H

INVOLVING POTENTIALLY GUILTY PARTIES.

4. BASED ON THE INFORMATION AVAILABLE TO US, IT IS

THE CONCLUSION OF ALL INTERESTED ELEMENTS OF THIS

EMBASSY THAT CROATIAN FORCES IN BOSNIA HAVE NOT

ENGAGED IN ETHNIC CLEANSING -- EITHER AS A MATTER OF

5. CULPABILITY FOR THE EVENTS IN NOVI TRAVNIK AND

PROZOR IN MID-OCTOBER IS DISPUTED. THE CROATS CLAIM

TO HAVE RADIO INTERCEPT "PROOF" OF ORDERS GIVEN BY

RADICAL MUSLIM ELEMENTS TO KILL CROATS. THERE ARE

OTHER REPORTS OF BATTLE CRIES FROM CROATIAN ELEMENTS

TO KILL MUSLIMS. AND THE AFTERMATH INDICATES THAT

MUSLIM CIVILIANS DID SUFFER DISPROPORTIONATELY.

NOTWITHSTANDING WHO IS MORE GUILTY FOR THIS BOUT OF

CROATIAN/MUSLIM MADNESS, BOTH SIDES ARE PAINFULLY

AWARE THAT IT CONTRIBUTED DIRECTLY TO THE FALL OF

JAJCE. IN THE WAKE OF THAT TRAGEDY, IZETBEGOVIC AND

TUDJMAN SAY THEY HAVE CLEARED THE AIR, AND CROATIAN

AND MUSLIM FORCES APPEAR TO HAVE REESTABLISHED A

MODICUM OF CIVILITY AND COOPERATION IN MILITARY

ACTIONS. ALTHOUGH WE ARE NOT AWARE OF SUBSEQUENT

"PROZORS," NONE OF THIS IS TO SAY THAT THERE WON\'T BE

OTHER CROATIAN/MUSLIM SKIRMISHES FROM WHICH MUSLIMS

AND/OR CROATS MAY FLEE. IT IS TO SAY, HOWEVER, THAT

BY NO REASONABLE STRETCH OF MIND OR LANGUAGE DOES

"PROZOR" CONSTITUTE EVIDENCE OF CROATIAN ETHNIC

CLEANSING, LET ALONE A LIKELIHOOD OF "CONTINUED"

CROATIAN ETHNIC CLEANSING.
