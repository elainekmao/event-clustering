SUBJECT: JORDAN: PETROLEUM RE-EXPORT DEMARCHE DELIVERED

REF: STATE 130301

Classified By: DCM Greg Berry, reasons 1.5 (b,d)

1. (s) DCM met Jordanian Energy Minister Mohammed Bataineh

July 7 to deliver reftel demarche points. Bataineh was

clearly disappointed, but noted that the GOJ would look to

other methods to solve their summer refining problems. For

the current year this would mean continuing to import

gasoline for local consumption from Iraq under the

long-standing oil and trade protocols. For the future,

Bataineh said the GOJ would look again at increasing storage

capacity for fuel oil and gas oil, a project he estimated

would take one year and $45-50 million to complete.
