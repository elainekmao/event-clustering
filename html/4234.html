RESPONDS WITH A LIST OF CLARIFICATIONS TO SUGGESTED TEXT

REF: STATE 183208

1. (U) This message contains a request for Department

guidance. Please see para 3.

2. (U) On October 24, Embassy conveyed reftel draft MOI to

the MFA\'s Legal Advisor Samer Naber. On November 6, we

received the following diplomatic note (also faxed to DS/ATA)

in return. Begin text:

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Hashemite Kingdom

of Jordan presents its compliments to the Embassy of the

United States of America in Amman; and with reference to the

latter\'s note No. (1647) dated 24 October 2002 regarding the

desire to sign a memorandum of intent concerning the

provision of assistance to the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan

under the Department of State\'s anti-Terrorism Assistance

Program (ATA), has the honor to enclose herewith a list of

minor clarifications to the suggested text for consideration

of the esteemed Department of State.

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Hashemite Kingdom

of Jordan seizes this opportunity to renew to the Embassy of

the United States of America the assurances of its highest

consideration.

-- In the Title add after the word "assistance": "Under the

Department of State\'s Anti Terrorism Assistance Program

(ATA)."

-- In the Seventh line of the preamble add after the word

"Jordan": "through the Department of State\'s Anti Terrorism

Assistance Program (ATA)"

-- In the fifth line, paragraph one, under the subtitle "The

Needs Assessment" add the word "prevent" before the word

"deter."

-- In the sixth line under the subtitle "Participant

Selection" add after the word "assign":",to the maximum

extent possible,"

-- In the fifth line under the subtitle "Program constraints"

add after the word "assistance": "through the Department of

State\'s Anti-Terrorism Program"

-- It would be helpful to clarify what are the "other

requirements" applicable in the last line under the subtitle

"Participant Selection."

End Text of Diplomatic Note.

3. (U) As per reftel instructions, we will await Department\'s

assessment of the above listed proposed textual changes

before taking further action.

GNEHM
