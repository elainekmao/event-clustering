SUBJECT: THANKS TO THE REMOTE SENSING

INTERAGENCY TEAM

Assistant Secretary Wolf: I\'d like to extend my heartfelt

thanks to Delegation head NP/ECNP Director Chris Kessler,

NP/ECNP FAO Lisa Beames, NIMA Director of International

Plans and Policy Robert Weber, Acting Commerce/NOAA Chief

of Satellite Activities Branch Tim Stryker and JCS Space

Policy Branch Chief LTC Max Clayton for their visit last

week to the UAE to discuss remote sensing issues with the

UAE military. The team braved a Washington blizzard,

missed onward European connections, and went two days

without luggage in order to make it to Abu Dhabi for these

meetings. They arrived and went directly into a marathon

session -- heroic measures indeed! The Emiratis, from

Armed Forces Chief of Staff LTG Muhammad bin Zayid Al-

Nahyan to Director of Military Intelligence Brigadier

Muhammad Al-Neyyadi and Space Reconnaissance Center

Director LTC Mahash Al-Hameli, were all extremely

appreciative of the visit. I understand that the meetings

were very illuminating and that the interagency will have

much to discuss before the next session with the Emiratis.

Please rest assured that my staff and I stand ready to

assist the Department and the interagency team on follow-up

steps as needed. Regards from the UAE.

WAHBA
