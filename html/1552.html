SUBJECT: MUGABE OFF TO BELGIUM

REF: HARARE 522

CLASSIFIED BY CHARGE D\'AFFAIRES EARL M. IRVING FOR

REASONS 1.5 B/D.

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 HARARE 00873 010810Z

1. (C) ACCORDING TO BELGIUM EMBASSY FIRST SECRETARY

ALEXIS DE CROMBRUGGHE, PRESIDENT MUGABE W7LL TRAVEL TO

BELGIUM ON OR ABOUT MARCH 5. THE TRIP HAS ITS GENESIS

IN BELGIAN FOREIGN MINISTER LOUIS MICHEL\'S JANUARY

INVITATION TO FOREIGN MINISTER MUDENGE TO COME TO

BELGIUM TO DISCUSS THE CONGO. MUDENGE\'S RESPONSE,

CROMBRUGGHE JOKED, WAS, "COULDN\'T I BRING THE

PRESIDENT WITH ME?" MICHEL COULD HARDLY REFUSE, THE

BELGIUM DIPLOMAT CONCLUDED.

2. (C) COMMENT: WE SEE MUGABE\'S TRIP AS AN EFFORT TO

TAKE ADVANTAGE OF THE OPENING LEFT BY THE

ASSASSINATION OF LAURENT KABILA TO ACHIEVE PEACE IN

THE CONGO AND A RECOGNITION OF BELGIUM\'S INCREASING

IMPORTANCE AS A PLAYER IN THE PEACE PROCESS. WE ALSO

SUSPECT THAT MUGABE MAY TRY TO USE HIS VISIT TO

BRUSSELS TO BURNISH HIS IMAGE AS A STATESMAN AND

PEACEMAKER AND APPEAL TO THE BELGIAN GOVERNMENT FOR

ASSISTANCE IN SECURING WESTERN GOODWILL FOR HIS

DOMESTIC AGENDA. END COMMENT.

IRVING
