SUBJECT: ZIMBABWE\'S COMMERCIAL FARMERS UNION SEEKS

U.S. SUPPORT FOR MULTILATERAL LENDING RESUMPTION

CLASSIFIED BY AMBASSADOR MCDONALD, REASON 1.5 (B),

(D)

REFS: A) HARARE 2461, B)HARARE 2820

1. (C) ON MAY 30 AMBASSADOR, DCM, POL AND ECON

OFFICERS MET WITH REPRESENTATIVES (FORMER CFU

PRESIDENT NICK SWANEPOEL, AND FARMERS JOHN HAINES AND

PETER HENDERSON) OF THE COMMERCIAL FARMERS UNION

(CFU) WHO GAVE A READOUT ON RECENT CONFIDENTIAL

CFU/GOZ DISCUSSIONS AND NEGOTIATIONS ON LAND

INVASIONS AND LAND REDISTRIBUTION. THOUGH THEIR

EFFORTS ARE STILL VERY MUCH A WORK-IN-PROGRESS, GIVEN

THE CURRENT VOLATILE POLITICAL CLIMATE, THE THREE-MAN

TEAM LED BY SWANEPOEL (PRESIDENT MUGABE PERSONALLY

DIRECTED THAT SWANEPOEL SPEARHEAD THE NEGOTIATIONS,

SEE REF C) BELIEVES THAT THEY HAVE MADE PROGRESS AND

GOTTEN PROMISES FROM NATIONAL LEADERSHIP WHICH SERVE

TO INTRODUCE REASON, COMMON SENSE AND LONG-TERM

VIABILITY INTO THE PROCESS. THE SALIENT POINTS MADE

TO US BY SWANEPOEL AND COMPANY ARE:

== THE NEGOTIATIONS HAVE BEEN SUCCESSFUL IN LOWERING

THE INCIDENCE AND LEVEL OF VIOLENCE DIRECTED AT WHITE

FARMERS AND THEIR WORKERS (COMMENT: INFORMATION

COMING INTO THE EMBASSY IS THAT WHILE THERE HAS BEEN

A SLIGHT DROPOFF IN VIOLENCE ON THE FARMS IT IS STILL

CONTINUING AND DEMANDS FOR ATTENDANCE AT

"REEDUCATION" RALLIES HAVE INCREASED. WE ALSO POINT

== THE GOVERNMENT\'S CURRENT PLAN IS THAT, SOON AFTER

THE ELECTIONS, ALL INVADERS WILL BE WITHDRAWN,

ALLOWING THE START OF A MORE ORGANIZED RESETTLEMENT

PROGRAM DIRECTED OUT OF THE PRESIDENT\'S OFFICE;

== THOUGH VICE PRESIDENT MSIKA WILL LIKELY BE

TITULAR HEAD OF THE RESETTLEMENT PROGRAM, ITS EFFORTS

WILL BE PERSONALLY OVERSEEN BY PRESIDENT MUGABE WHO

REALIZES THAT THE COUNTRY MUST RETAIN SOME VESTIGE OF

AGRICULTURAL VIABILITY;

== THOUGH THE 841 PREVIOUSLY-DESIGNATED FARMS MAY

ALL BE GAZZETTED FOR ACQUISITION UNDER THE NEW

LEGISLATION (REF A), THE GOZ HAS AGREED THAT IN THE

INTERESTS OF NATIONAL SECURITY THE MOST PRODUCTIVE

PROPERTIES WILL NOT BE SEIZED (OTHER FARMS BEING PUT

ON THE MARKET BY BANKRUPT OR DEPARTING FARMERS WILL

BE OFFERED TO COVER THE GAP SO THAT 2.2 MILLION

HECTARES ARE GIVEN OVER TO STATE CONTROL);

== THE WAR VETERANS ARE NOT ALL UNIFORMLY UNDER THE

2. (C) THE CFU TEAM\'S INDIRECT MESSAGE WAS A PLEA

FOR INTERNATIONAL FUNDING TO ALLOW THE GOZ TO PROCEED

WITH A RATIONAL PLAN WHICH WOULD NOT CONTAIN THE

SEEDS OF THE DESTRUCTION OF ZIMBABWE\'S ECONOMY. HE

ALSO CLAIMED THAT TO DATE THE INVASIONS HAVE HAD ONLY

A NEGLIBLE EFFECT ON CURRENT PRODUCTION, THOUGH

FUTURE PRODUCTION IS VERY MUCH UNDER THREAT.

(COMMENT: SWANEPOEL, WHO FULLY COMPREHENDS THE U.S.

POSITION AND LEGAL LIMITS ON FUNDING FOR LAND REFORM,

WAS AGAIN GENTLY REMINDED OF THE FACTS BY THE

AMBASSADOR. THE AMBASSADOR ALSO POINTED OUT THAT THE

ONGOING, EVEN IF DIMISHED, VIOLENCE RELATED TO ZANU-

PF POLITICKING WAS VERY TROUBLING TO THE USG AND THUS

"BUSINESS AS USUAL" COULD NOT QUICKLY OCCUR AFTER THE

ELECTIONS. END COMMENT.) SWANEPOEL POINTED OUT THAT

BANKS WOULD LIKELY FAIL IF THE DISPOSSESSED FARMERS

WERE ONLY GIVEN PROMISSORY NOTES OR OTHER GOVERNMENT

PAPER OF QUESTIONABLE VALUE, AND WOULD THEMSELVES

HAVE NO LIQUID ASSETS TO PURSUE FARMING ANYWHERE

ELSE, IF THEY DESIRED TO DO SO.

3. (C) COMMENT: SWANEPOEL, WHILE PERHAPS NOT 100

PERCENT OPEN AND SOMEWHAT OVERLY ROSEY, WAS FRANK AND

CANDID IN HIS DESCRIPTION OF WHERE MATTERS STAND, THE

DIRECTION THE GOVERNMENT APPEARED HEADED AND WHAT THE

CFU DESIRED. AS MUGABE\'S HAND-PICKED POINT MAN AND

ARBITER FOR THE WHITE COMMERCIAL FARMING COMMUNITY HE

HAS A TOUGH ROW TO HOE. THE CFU CONTINUES TO

CONSISTENTLY MAINTAIN BOTH PUBLIC AND PRIVATE LINES

OF SUPPORT, WORKING CLOSELY WITH THE GOZ ON ITS LAND

REFORM PROGRAM AND TRYING TO PUSH IT INTO THE REALM

OF DOABLE AND REASONABLE. WHETHER THE RULE OF REASON

OR OF PRACTICALITY WILL SHINE DOWN ON THE PROCESS

ONCE THE ELECTIONS ARE COMPLETE LOOKS LIKE A COIN

TOSS AT THE MOMENT, THOUGH FOR THE FUTURE OF ALL

ZIMBABWEANS WE HOPE IT IS SO. FOR THE MOMENT,

THOUGH, WE WOULDN\'T BET THE FAMILY FARM ON IT. END

COMMENT.
