SUBJECT: AN UPBEAT HAMDAN ASSURES THAT UAEG

UNDERSTANDS USG WOULD NOT INTENTIONALLY

STRIKE BAGHDAD EMBASSY

REF: State 89928

1. (U) Classified by Ambassador Marcelle M. Wahba

for reasons 1.5 (B) and (D).

2. (S) The Ambassador contacted an extremely upbeat

MFA Minstate Shaykh Hamdan bin Zayid Al-Nahyan on

4/9 to convey reftel talking points. Hamdan was not

at all worried, noting that the UAE diplomatic staff

in Baghdad exited the country. Were the embassy to

be inadvertently hit, Hamdan assured, the UAEG would

understand; he fully realizes the complexity of the

situation.

3. (S) Hamdan said he had been following the

dramatic television coverage of the day\'s events in

Baghdad and was very pleased to see footage of

Iraqis celebrating in the streets and speaking out

about the regime. One image -- that of an Iraqi

hitting a picture of Saddam\'s face with the bottom

of shoe -- captured the day\'s emotions, in Hamdan\'s

view.

WAHBA
