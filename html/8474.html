SUBJECT: KUWAIT: SUPPORT FOR PRESIDENT\'S HIV/AIDS

INITIATIVE

REF: STATE 146483

On June 2, Ambassador sent a letter to Minister of Health

Dr. Mohammad Al-Jarallah based on information provided in

reftel about the new U.S. initiative and urging Kuwait to

do more domestically and internationally to help fight

HIV/AIDS. Post elements, including Public Affairs Section,

will explore other avenues to disseminate the information

contained in reftel.
