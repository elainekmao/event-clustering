SUBJECT: MEDIA REPORT U. S. ARMS BAN; HARARE

The recent U.S. arms export embargo on Zimbabwe has

received significant play in the Zimbabwe\'s independent

newspaper. The government-controlled print and electronic

media has, so far, ignored the story. However, the April

19 edition of the country\'s only independent daily "The

Daily News" carries a wire article by Reuters News Agency

on page one, under headline "U. S. widens sanctions by

slapping ban on sale of defense equipment." The April 18

edition of the independent financial weekly "The Financial

Gazette" also printed the Reuters story on page 54, under

headline "U. S. bans defense equipment sells to Zimbabwe."
