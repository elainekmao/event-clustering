Security/Military

1330 Duck and cover alarm was sounded, when the Consequent

Management Support Team (CMST) reported that 3 out of 5

biological/chemical detectors had detected a possible

biological/chemical agent in the area. Embassy personnel remained

in gas mask(s) for approximately 45 minutes, before the all clear

was announced. The CMST later determined that this was a false

alarm and no/no biological agent was detected.
