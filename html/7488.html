SUBJECT: MTAG 05-96: DEMARCHE ON IRAN\'S MISSILE RELATED

PROCUREMENT EFFORTS (S)

REF: STATE 96184

(U) Classified by Economic Counselor Scot Marciel for reasons

1.5 (B) and (D).

(S) Econoff delivered reftel information on Iranian entities

involved in missile-related procurement programs to Tevfik

Mengu, Director General for Agreements at the Turkish Foreign

Trade Undersecretariat, on April 15. Mengu thanked Econoff

for the information, but said, as he has in response to

previous nonproliferation demarches, that his agency could

take preventive action only if trade transactions were found

to be in violation of international sanctions or domestic

law. In other cases of apparently legitimate trade by

suspect companies, Foreign Trade would be limited to passing

information to us on the activities of those companies.
