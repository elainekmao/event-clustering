SUMMARY

-------

1. (U) VISITING DRL A/S SHATTUCK, AMBASSADOR GALBRAITH,

DRL/BA DIRECTOR COFFEY, AND EMBASSY PAO, POLOFF AND DART

TRAVELED TO WESTERN BOSNIA SEPTEMBER 29. THEY TALKED WITH

OFFICIALS AT THE UNPROFOR BIHAC CANTON HQ NEAR CAZIN, MET

THE NEW MUSLIM MAYOR OF BOSANSKI PETROVAC, VISITED THE

SITE OF AN ALLEGED MASS GRAVE NEAR KLJUC, AND FOUND A

POTENTIAL BORDER PROBLEM AT KULEN VAKUF. END SUMMARY.

--------------------------------------------- -

V CORPS STRETCHED THIN BUT SERBS NOT RESISTING

--------------------------------------------- -

2. (C) DANISH SECTOR COMMANDER COLONEL E.B. DAM,

AUSTRALIAN POLITICAL AND HUMANITARIAN AFFAIRS COORDINATOR

(PHAC) RUSS BALLANTYNE, AND THE BANGBAT UNMO LEADER

BRIEFED ON RECENT DEVELOPMENTS. DAM SAID THE BOSNIAN

FIFTH CORPS HAD ADVANCED ALMOST 200 KILOMETERS IN SIX

DAYS, AND NOW HAD TO COVER A CONFRONTATION LINE OF 103 KM

AND PATROL THE TERRITORY WITH ONLY 20,000 TROOPS. BOSNIAN

TROOPS WERE ALSO STATIONED ON THE CROATIAN-BOSNIAN BORDER.

THE VII CORPS HAD SECONDED ONE BRIGADE TO V CORPS

COMMANDER DUDAKOVIC, GIVING HIM 10 BRIGADES. DAM BELIEVED

DUDAKOVIC WOULD TAKE MRKONIC GRAD AND TRY TO TURN THE

EXPERT LIGHT INFANTRY CORPS ON PRIJEDOR UNLESS POLITICAL

PRESSURE FROM SARAJEVO AND NEW YORK STOPPED HIM. ONE

ARKAN BRIGADE HAD ARRIVED IN PRIJEDOR 10 DAYS EARLIER, DAM

SAID. THE RECENT V CORPS SUCCESSES, DESPITE INCREDIBLE

ODDS (THE SERB HAD OVER 60,000 TROOPS IN THE BANJA LUKA

AREA), AND THE LACK OF A COUNTER-OFFENSIVE BY THE BSA HAD

CONVINCED DAM THAT THE SERBS HAD PURPOSELY EVACUATED THE

AREA. THE SERBS EXPECTED TO GIVE UP MUCH OF THIS

TERRITORY IN POLITICAL NEGOTIATIONS ANYWAY, HE COMMENTED.

------------------

REFUGEES, REFUGEES

------------------

3. (U) THE UN ESTIMATED THAT 11,000 DISPLACED PERSONS

(DPS) WOULD REACH THE CANTON FROM OTHER PARTS OF BOSNIA,

MOSTLY BANJA LUKA. THE GOBH CLAIMED THE NORTHERN BIHAC

CANTON COULD NOT ACCOMMODATE THEM, AND WANTED TO PLACE

THEM IN THE NEWLY LIBERATED AREAS. 400-500 MUSLIM

REFUGEES FROM THE BANJA LUKA REGION HAD ALREADY RESETTLED

IN THE NORTH. UNHCR AND CANTON AUTHORITIES DID NOT WANT

TO SETTLE THESE REFUGEES IN THE EMPTY HOMES OF THE ABDIC

REFUGEES AND WERE SEARCHING FOR HOUSING. THE GOBH HAD

PUBLICLY WELCOMED BACK MOST OF THE ABDIC REFUGEES, BUT

REFUSED TO DECLARE ANOTHER AMNESTY. BALLANTYNE SAID THE

SARAJEVO AUTHORITIES HAD DECLARED THE 1994 AMNESTY STILL

VALID. THE BOSNIANS, CROATS, AND TURKS WERE DISCUSSING

ESTABLISHING A TRILATERAL POLICE FORCE IN THE AREA TO

4. (U) BALLANTYNE ESTIMATED THE CURRENT POPULATION OF

REFUSED TO TAKE THESE PEOPLE, AND HAD RECENTLY STOPPED

ALLOWING THE DPS TRANSIT THROUGH CROATIA AT DVOR, TO THE

BIHAC CANTON. THE GOC REFUGEE REPATRIATION ORDER, UN

OFFICIALS SAID, MIGHT SEND MANY MUSLIMS BACK TO THIS

TERRITORY BEFORE THE AREA IS SECURE.

THE CURRENT POPULATION WAS ABOUT 1500. BEFORE THE WAR,

PETROVAC WAS THE ELEVENTH LARGEST TOWN IN BOSNIA, WITH A

POPULATION OF 15,000. 3200 MUSLIMS HAD LIVED HERE, HE

SAID, AND ONLY A FEW CROATS. THE TOWN WAS DEEP IN ABH-

HELD TERRITORY AND WAS SAFE FROM SHELLING, HE REASSURED

US, ADDING THAT THE GOVERNMENT HAD ALREADY BEGUN TO MOVE

DPS INTO THE AREA, ALTHOUGH ELECTRICITY AND WATER SUPPLY

PROBLEMS EXISTED.

6. (U) THE TOWN WAS INTACT, AND HAD EXPERIENCED LITTLE

DAMAGE DURING THE FIGHTING. UNTENDED LIVESTOCK RAN

EVERYWHERE -- REMINISCENT OF AN OLD WESTERN FILM, AN

UNSADDLED HORSE BOLTED IN FEAR DOWN THE STREET, ALMOST RAN

INTO THE AMBASSADOR, TURNED THE CORNER AND DISAPPEARED.

SEVERAL PEOPLE WERE MILLING AROUND, SOLDIERS IN UNIFORM

AND REFUGEES CONFUSED ABOUT THEIR FATE. ONLY A FEW

VEHICLES WERE IN EVIDENCE. AT ONE APARTMENT BLOCK,

REFUGEES FROM PRNJAVOR AND BOSANSKI NOVI RECOUNTED THEIR

GRIM PAST. TWO EXPENSIVE GERMAN CARS WERE PARKED IN A

SHED NEARBY, REPORTEDLY BELONGING TO VISITORS WHO CAME

FROM MUNICH TO SEE THEIR ELDERLY PARENTS.

7. (U) THE VILLAGES SURROUNDING PETROVAC, HOWEVER, WERE

RUINED. A SPARSELY POPULATED AREA EVEN IN NORMAL TIMES,

THE BIHAC-PETROVAC-KLJUC ROAD WAS DESERTED. CLOTHING AND

BLANKETS LITTERED THE ROAD, MARKING THE PATH THE SERBS HAD

USED TO FLEE. MOST LARGE BUILDINGS AND RESTAURANTS HAD

BEEN RECENTLY BURNED. THERE WERE BROKEN-DOWN CARS AND

THREE BURNED-OUT TANKS. IN A FIELD PAST PETROVAC, AN OLD

STILL HID IN THE WOODS IN THE HILLS NORTH OF THE ROAD.

THEY WERE SUPPORTED BY POCKETS OF SERB CIVILIANS WHO HAD

BEEN UNABLE TO LEAVE BEFORE THE BOSNIAN ARMY SWEPT PAST

THEM.

----------------

KLJUC GRAVE SITE

----------------

8. (U) NEAR KLJUC, THE SHATTUCK/GALBRAITH PARTY TURNED

NORTH ONTO A GRAVEL ROAD NEAR CRVENLJA ZEMLJA. THE V

CORPS LIAISON OFFICER TO THE UN LED US TO THE NOW-INFAMOUS

SITE OF AN ALLEGED MASS GRAVE. THE ESCORT SOLDIERS

STRICTLY FORBADE THE DELEGATION AND REPORTERS FROM TAKING

ANY PICTURES. 200 METERS DOWN A MUDDY HILLSIDE, THE

AMBASSADOR AND A/S REACHED A DITCH WITH A HUMAN SKELETON.

THE SKULL AND RIB CAGE HAD BEEN EXCAVATED AND OLD,

TATTERED BITS OF CLOTHING WERE VISIBLE AROUND IT. THE

LIAISON OFFICER SAID OTHER BODIES HAD BEEN DISCOVERED IN

THIS DITCH AND IN A CAVE LOCATED TWENTY METERS TO THE

LEFT. AUTHORITIES HAD STOPPED THE EXCAVATION IMMEDIATELY

AFTER IT BECAME APPARENT WHAT LAY UNDERNEATH. AN EXPERT

KULEN VAKUF -- A LITTLE BORDER ADJUSTMENT AHEAD?

--------------------------------------------- ---

9. (U) FOR THE RETURN TRIP TO ZAGREB, THE PARTY HAD

PLANNED TO CROSS THE BRIDGE OVER THE UNA RIVER AT KULEN

VAKUF AND THEN CROSS INTO CROATIA. FOUR KILOMETERS

OUTSIDE THE TOWN, OUR UNMO GUIDE POINTED OUT WHERE HE HAD

FOUND 15 BODIES (CIVILIAN AND MILITARY, MALE AND FEMALE)

IN THE UNDERBRUSH NEAR THE ROAD THE PREVIOUS DAY. TWO

WERE DECAPITATED. THE DANISH UNMO SAID SOME CORPSES

APPEARED TO BE TWO WEEKS OLD, WHILE OTHERS APPEARED TO BE

MORE RECENT VICTIMS. THE CORPSES HAD BEEN REMOVED, BUT

ONLY RECENTLY; THE AREA STILL STANK OF DEATH.

10. (U) KULEN VAKUF WAS A HIGH MUSLIM MAJORITY TOWN IN A

PICTURESQUE RIVER VALLEY. THE BORDER BETWEEN BOSNIA AND

KULEN VAKUF AND THE MOUNTAIN BEYOND, ALTHOUGH THE RIVER

ITSELF FLOWS THROUGH THE TOWN. THE BRIDGE HAD BEEN

SHELLED NEAR THE MUSLIM SIDE AND WAS IMPASSABLE FOR THE

PARTY\'S VEHICLES. THE CROATIAN ARMY (HV) HAD CROSSED INTO

BOSNIA AND HAD OCCUPIED THE TOWN. THE CROATIAN FLAG FLEW

AT THE OTHER SIDE OF THE BRIDGE, ABOVE A MAZE OF RAZOR

WIRE AND SANDBAGS WHICH DISCOURAGED WOULD-BE VISITORS FROM

CROSSING.

11. (U) THE AMBASSADOR AND A/S WALKED TO THE OTHER SIDE,

TO GREET THE IRRITATED HV SOLDIERS. EVIDENCE OF WAR

DAMAGE WAS EVERYWHERE, ALTHOUGH THE RAILROAD BRIDGE

APPEARED WHOLE. CROATIA PLANS TO OPEN THIS RAIL LINE

CONNECTING ZAGREB AND SPLIT IN A FEW WEEKS. THE MUSLIM

MAYOR APPROACHED THE AMBASSADOR AND APPEALED FOR HELP TO

ALLOW THE ORIGINAL RESIDENTS TO RETURN. MOST OF THEM WERE

EAGER TO RETURN, HE CLAIMED, BUT THE CROATS WERE REFUSING

ENTRY TO ALL BOSNIANS. THE HV SOLDIERS WERE CLEARLY

RELIEVED WHEN THE U.S. PARTY CROSSED BACK TO THE ABH-HELD

SIDE OF TOWN. COMMENT: THE OCCUPATION OF KULEN VAKUF MAY

SOON BECOME ANOTHER BLEMISH ON FEDERATION RELATIONS IF THE

HV DOES NOT PULL BACK TO THE INTERNATIONALLY-RECOGNIZED

BORDER.

12. SARAJEVO MINIMIZE CONSIDERED.
