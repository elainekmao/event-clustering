SUBJECT: TWO CORRUPTION-TAINTED OFFICIALS SUSPENDED

REF: HANOI 1250

UNCLASSIFIED

1. (U) SUMMARY: TWO HIGH-RANKING GVN OFFICIALS, ONE THE

NUMBER TWO PROSECUTOR AND ANOTHER A CENTRAL COMMITTEE

MEMBER, HAVE BEEN SUSPENDED FROM THEIR GOVERNMENT AND

SOME PARTY POSITIONS FOR SUSPECTED COMPLICITY WITH AN

ALLEGED MAJOR CRIMINAL FIGURE. THESE MOVES ARE

EXPECTED TO FACILITATE ONGOING INVESTIGATIONS INTO THE

CASE, WHICH SO FAR HAS INVOLVED MORE THAN 100 ALLEGED

ACCOMPLICES, REPORTEDLY INCLUDING POLICEMEN AND

JOURNALISTS. THIS WELL-PUBLICIZED EFFORT NONETHELESS

WILL LIKELY BE INSUFFICIENT IN CONVINCING THE PUBLIC

THAT CPV AND GVN ACTIONS AGAINST WIDESPREAD CORRUPTION

AND GROWING CRIME ARE EFFECTIVE. END SUMMARY.

2. (U) ON MAY 31, STATE PRESIDENT TRAN DUC LUONG

ORDERED THE SUSPENSION OF PHAM SY CHIEN, DEPUTY HEAD OF

THE SUPREME PEOPLE\'S PROCURACY. ON THE SAME DAY, PRIME

MINISTER PHAN VAN KHAI AUTHORIZED THE SUSPENSION OF

TRAN MAI HANH FROM HIS POSITION AS GENERAL DIRECTOR OF

VIETNAM STATE RADIO. (NOTE: THE SUPREME PEOPLE\'S

PROCURACY REPORTS TO THE STATE PRESIDENT RATHER THAN

THE PRIME MINISTER. END NOTE.) THE TWO DECISIONS CAME

AFTER THE CPV POLITBURO AND SECRETARIAT HAD ENDORSED

RECOMMENDATIONS TO SUSPEND CHIEN AND HANH FROM THEIR

SEATS ON THE POLITBURO-APPOINTED SUPREME PEOPLE\'S

PROCURACY PARTY COMMITTEE AND THE POLITBURO-APPOINTED

VOICE OF VIETNAM PARTY COMMITTEE, RESPECTIVELY.

EARLIER, THE NATIONAL ASSEMBLY STANDING COMMITTEE AND

NATIONAL ELECTION COUNCIL HAD ORDERED THAT HANH\'S NAME

BE DROPPED FROM THE LIST OF FINAL CANDIDATES ONLY THREE

DAYS BEFORE THE MAY 19 NATIONAL ASSEMBLY ELECTION

(REFTEL). HANH HAS FOR NOW NOT LOST HIS SEAT ON THE

CPV CENTRAL COMMITTEE, HOWEVER.

3. (SBU) THESE CASES STEM FROM ALLEGED CONNECTIONS

WITH AND ASSISTANCE TO TRUONG VAN CAM, AKA "NAM CAM,"

ROUTINELY DESCRIBED AS A MAJOR HO CHI MINH CITY (HCMC)

CRIME SYNDICATE BOSS. IN MAY 1995, NAM CAM HAD

RECEIVED A THREE-YEAR ADMINISTRATIVE DETENTION TERM,

BUT WAS RELEASED IN OCTOBER 1997. CHIEN AND HANH

REPORTEDLY ISSUED A NUMBER OF OFFICIAL DOCUMENTS AT

THAT TIME ASKING NAM CAM\'S EARLY RELEASE, ALLEGEDLY

ONLY AFTER NAM CAM\'S WIFE SPENT "BILLIONS" OF

VIETNAMESE DONG TO SECURE HIS FREEDOM. ONE SENIOR

SUPREME PEOPLE\'S PROCURACY OFFICIAL COMMENTED PRIVATELY

TO EMBOFF THAT NAM CAM\'S RELEASE WOULD HAVE BEEN

"IMPOSSIBLE WITHOUT INTERVENTION OF PHAM SY CHIEN, AND

THOSE FROM OTHER CENTRAL OFFICES LIKE HANH."

4. (U) ACCORDING TO LOCAL OBSERVERS, THIS IS THE

FIRST TIME IN VIETNAM THAT HIGH-RANKING GVN OFFICIALS

HAVE BEEN TARGETED FOR THEIR INVOLVEMENT -- HOWEVER

INDIRECT -- IN VIOLENT CRIMINAL ACTIVITY, ALTHOUGH THE

GVN HAS IN THE PAST TAKEN ACTION AGAINST OFFICIALS

INVOLVED IN FRAUD AND CORRUPTION. "AN NINH THE GIOI,"

THE MINISTRY OF PUBLIC SECURITY\'S NEWSPAPER, TOUTED THE

SUSPENSIONS OF CHIEN AND HANH AS EXAMPLES OF THE CPV

AND GVN ACTING TO STEM THE "MORAL DEGRADATION OF STATE

OFFICIALS." THERE HAVE ALSO BEEN MEDIA EXPOSES OF

CHIEN\'S ALLEGED "HUGE" ASSETS; SOURCES IN THE CPV

INVESTIGATION COMMISSION HAVE LEAKED TO THE PRESS THAT

CHIEN HAD NOT DECLARED MANY OF THESE ASSETS.

5. (U) POLICE RE-ARRESTED NAM CAM IN DECEMBER 2001

BASED ON A TIP-OFF THAT HE HAD ORDERED THE MURDER OF

RIVAL ORGANIZED CRIME BOSS "DUNG HA." SO FAR, MORE

THAN 100 ALLEGED ACCOMPLICES HAVE ALSO BEEN ARRESTED; A

SENIOR MINISTRY OF PUBLIC SECURITY (MPS) OFFICIAL IN

LATE MAY TOLD THE PRESS THAT THE INVESTIGATION WOULD

LIKELY LAST UNTIL AT LEAST OCTOBER, WITH MORE ARRESTS

EXPECTED TO FOLLOW.

6. (SBU) COMMENT: THESE SUSPENSIONS AND WELL-

PUBLICIZED CAMPAIGNS AGAINST CHIEN, HANH, AS WELL AS

NAM CAM HIMSELF ARE LIKELY DESIGNED TO CONVINCE THE

PUBLIC THAT THE CPV AND GVN ARE RESOLUTE IN MOVING

AGAINST BOTH ORGANIZED CRIME AND OFFICIAL CORRUPTION.

THE MEDIA ATTENTION IS WITHOUT DOUBT THE RESULT OF AN

EXPLICIT CPV DECISION, NOT THE RESULT OF INDEPENDENT

INVESTIGATIVE JOURNALISM, WHICH IS VIRTUALLY NON-

EXISTENT IN VIETNAM. THE CPV IS TRYING TO CONVINCE THE

PUBLIC BY THESE ACTS THAT, AS A RECENTLY REPORTED CPV

GUIDELINE STATED, THERE IS "NO FORBIDDEN AREA" IN

INVESTIGATING AND PUNISHING CPV MEMBERS AND GVN

OFFICIALS WHO COMMIT MISTAKES AND ENGAGE IN WRONGDOING.
