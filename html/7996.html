SUBJECT: PRESENTING SANCTIONS LIFT UNSC RESOLUTION DEMARCHE

REF: STATE 122029

1. (U) Classified by Charge D\'Affaires Richard A.

Albright, for reasons 1.5 (B) and (D).

2. (C) Charge presented reftel points on lifting UNSC

sanctions on Iraq to MFA Assistant U/S Abdullah Rashid Al-

Nuaimi, May 12. Al-Nuaimi said that the UAE fully supports

the lifting of sanctions and remarked, "The regime is gone

so sanctions should be gone too." The most important thing

for the UAE, he commented, is to improve as quickly as

possible the condition of the Iraqi people -- something

that would best be served by an immediate lifting of

sanctions.
