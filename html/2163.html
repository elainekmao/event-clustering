1. (C) AT MEDIA THEMED LUNCH HOSTED BY AMBASSADOR

NICHOLSON, DIRECTOR OF MEDIA FOR THE CONGREGATION FOR THE

PROPAGATION OF THE FAITH (FIDES), FATHER BENARDO

CERVELLERA, SPOKE AT LENGTH ON HIS VIEWS OF RECENT

VATICAN/PRC DEVELOPMENTS. CERVELLERA SAID THAT ALTHOUGH

THE POPE\'S 10/24 STATEMENT APOLOGIZING TO THE CHINESE

PEOPLE FOR THE "MISTAKES\' MADE BY EARLY CHRISTIAN

MISSIONARIES AND FOR A RESUMPTION OF DIALOG BETWEEN THE

PRC AND THE HOLY SEE WAS WELL RECEIVED BY THE CHINESE

PEOPLE, IT "FELL ON DEAF EARS" WITHIN THE CHINESE

GOVERNMENT. HE WENT ON TO SAY THAT HE SEES THE

POSSIBILITY OF ANY RESUMPTION OF DIALOG BETWEEN CHINA AND

THE HOLY SEE AIMED AT NORMALIZING RELATIONS BEFORE

ELECTIONS THE MARCH ELECTIONS IN CHINA TO BE NIL. (NOTE:

WE HEARD THE SAME FROM OUR TAIWAN EMBASSY CONTACTS).

2. (C) CERVELLERA ALSO DISCUSSED THE ROLE OF THE

"OFFICIAL" CHURCH AND THE UNDERGROUND CHURCH IN CHINA.

HE BELIEVES 80 PERCENT OF THE "OFFICIAL" CHURCH IS LOYAL

TO THE POPE. HE ALSO SAID THAT THE REMAINING "OLD

LINERS" IN THE "OFFICIAL" CHURCH ARE DYING OFF. IN

EFFECT, THERE IS NO LONGER EVEN A DE FACTO SCHISM BETWEEN

THE VATICAN AND CHINESE CATHOLICISM. THE ONLY PROBLEM

IS THE "PATRIOTIC ASSOCIATION" WHICH BEIJING USES TO

CONTROL THE ACTIVITIES OF THE "OFFICIAL" CHURCH.

3. (C) COMMENT: CERVELLERA AND FIDES ARE STRONGLY

IDENTIFIED WITH THE FACTION IN THE VATICAN WHICH OPPOSES

NORMALIZATION OF RELATIONS WITH CHINA UNTIL THE COMMUNISM

ARE REMOVED. THAT SAID, HIS BELIEF THAT THERE WON\'T BE

ANY MOVEMENT UNTIL AFTER MARCH IS SHARED EVEN BY THOSE

WHO WOULD BE IN FAVOR OF QUICK RESUMPTION OF THE DIALOG

(REF). CERVELLERA\'S VIEWS ON THE "OFFICIAL" CHURCH ARE

QUITE PROGRESSIVE AND APPEAR TO REFLECT THE VATICAN\'S

ATTEMPT TO FULLY HEAL THE RIFT BETWEEN THE UNDERGROUND

CHURCH AND THE "OFFICIAL CHURCH" EVEN BEFORE THERE IS A

POLITICAL DIALOG WITH BEIJING.

NICHOLSON
