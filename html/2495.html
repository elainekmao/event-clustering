2. (U) AMBASSADOR DISCUSSES COMMERCIAL ADVOCACY WITH

ACOUSTIGUIDES: AMBASSADOR AND POLOFF MET 01/30 WITH

REPRESENTATIVES FROM THE U.S. COMPANY ACOUSTIGUIDES ABOUT

POSSIBLE COMMERCIAL ADVOCACY TO PLACE THEIR GUIDES IN THE

VATICAN MUSEUMS. WE REFERRED THEM TO DEPT OF COMMERCE FOR

FORMAL ADVOCACY REQUEST; POLOFF, WITH FCS ROME AUTHORIZATION,

ACCOMPANIED THEM TO THEIR PRESENTATION TO VATICAN MUSEUM

OFFICIALS.

3. (C) AMBASSADOR TALKS ZIMBABWE BACK-CHANNEL WITH JESUITS

FOR AF A/S: AMBASSADOR 02/01 AT BEHEST OF A/S KANSTEINER

EXPLORED INTEREST IN ESTABLISHING POSSIBLE BACK-CHANNEL

COMMUNICATION WITH ZIMBABWE PRESIDENT MUGABE ON ACCOUNT OF

MUGABE\'S JESUIT EDUCATION.

4. (U) EMBASSY MEETINGS ON OUR PROPOSED ANTI-TRAFFICKING

CONFERENCE: VATICAN 700 REPORTS OUR EFFORTS, UP TO FEBRUARY

11, TO ORGANIZE A MAY, 2002 CONFERENCE ON THE HUMAN RIGHTS

DIMENSIONS OF THE PROBLEM OF TRAFFICKING IN HUMAN BEINGS.

SINCE FEB. 11 WE HAVE SURVEYED THE SPACE OFFERED BY THE

PONTIFICAL GREGORIAN UNIVERSITY AND MADE PROGRESS SECURING

FUNDING FOR THE CONFERENCE FROM OTHER HOLY SEE ACCREDITED

EMBASSIES. DCM ALSO HOSTED LUNCH WITH KEY EUROPEAN DCMS, WHO

AGREED TO NOMINATE SPEAKER CANDIDATES ASAP.

5. (SBU) POLOFFS DISCUSS BIOTECH APPROACH TO VATICAN WITH

EMBASSY COUNTERPARTS: POLOFFS HOSTED 02/08 LUNCH WITH

COUNTERPARTS FROM ARGENTINA, TAIWAN, AND INDONESIA EMBASSIES

TO THE HOLY SEE TO DISCUSS OPENING DIALOGUE WITH VATICAN ON

BENEFITS OF BIOTECHNOLOGY IN AGRICULTURE. ALL WERE

ENTHUSIASTIC ABOUT THE IDEA, BELIEVING THAT THE VATICAN CAN

MAKE A POSITIVE CONTRIBUTION TO PUBLIC UNDERSTANDING OF

BIOTECH.

6. (U) AMBASSADOR AT HOSTS DINNER FOR BIOTECHNOLOGY EXPERT

PRAKASH: AMBASSADOR HOSTED DINNER EVENING OF 2/14 FOR

VISITING BIOTECH EXPERT DR. C.S. PRAKASH. DINNER EXPOSED

SENIOR VATICAN DECISION-MAKERS INCLUDING FM TAURAN AND

CARDINAL SCHOTTE (OF GENERAL SYNOD OF BISHOPS) TO THE "GOOD

NEWS" ON BIOTECH.

7. (C) EMBASSY REPORTING: DURING THE PERIOD EMBASSY REPORTED

PEACE AT ASSISI (VATICAN 521), U.S.-VATICAN BILATERAL ISSUES

(VATICAN 495), COLOMBIA PEACE (VATICAN 578), POPE\'S BULGARIA
