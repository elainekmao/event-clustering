1. (U) CLASSIFIED BY AMBASSADOR JOHN HAMILTON, REASONS 1.5

2. (S) SUMMARY: PRESIDENTIAL ASPIRANT ALEJANDRO TOLEDO\'S

CHARISMA, ABILITY TO APPEAL TO VARIOUS AUDIENCES, INCLUDING

PERU\'S INDIGENOUS AND POOREST COMMUNITIES, AND POSITIVE

HUMAN RIGHTS AND ECONOMIC MESSAGE HAVE HELPED TO CATAPULT

HIM INTO A RUN-OFF ELECTION WITH PRESIDENT FUJIMORI. HIS

NEGATIVES INCLUDE A HOT TEMPER, A TENDENCY TOWARD LECTURING,

SOME DOUBTS ABOUT HIS ECONOMIC CREDENTIALS, AND A REPUTATION

FOR HAVING BEEN A PHILANDERER AND OCCASIONALLY HEAVY

DRINKER. WHILE TOLEDO WON FAVOR WITH MIDDLE CLASS STUDENTS

FOR DONNING A RED HEAD BAND AND LEADING 50,000 PROTESTORS TO

THE PRESIDENTIAL PALACE AFTER THE POLLS CLOSED APRIL 9, PRO-

FUJIMORI SUPPORTERS HAVE ALREADY BRANDED HIM A RABBLE ROUSER

FOR THE SAME REASON. PERU 2000 OFFICIALS HAVE ALSO TRIED TO

LINK TOLEDO TO THE HIGHLY CONTROVERSIAL EX-PRESIDENT ALAN

GARCIA, AND TV TALK SHOWS REVISITED ALLEGATIONS THAT HE

REFUSED TO RECOGNIZE PATERNITY AND PROVIDE SUPPORT TO AN

ILLEGITIMATE DAUGHTER. OTHER PRO-GOVERNMENT PRESS REPORTED

HE AND HIS WIFE WERE DIVORCED AND HAVE NOT REMARRIED.

DOMESTIC VIOLENCE AND DRUG USE ARE AMONG OTHER, UNCONFIRMED

RUMORS THAT COULD SURFACE BETWEEN NOW AND THE NEXT ROUND.

END SUMMARY

DARLING OF THE INTERNATIONAL PRESS

3. (SBU) TOLEDO\'S EARLY YEARS READ LIKE (AND HE PORTRAYS

THEM AS) A HOLLYWOOD SCRIPT. (NO LESS THAN 10 U.S. DAILIES

AND 2 INTERNATIONAL WEEKLIES INCLUDED HIS RAGS-TO-RICHES

STORY IN THEIR COVERAGE OF PERU\'S ELECTIONS.) BORN MARCH

28, 1946, TO PARENTS OF 16 CHILDREN (SEVEN OF WHOM DIED

PREMATURELY) IN THE SMALL HIGHLAND HAMLET OF CABANA (ANCASH

DEPARTMENT), HE GREW UP IN THE SEAPORT TOWN OF CHIMBOTE. BY

AGE EIGHT, HE WAS WORKING PART TIME AS A SHOESHINE BOY TO

SUPPLEMENT THE FAMILY\'S INCOME. IN SECONDARY SCHOOL, HE WON

RECOGNITION FOR HIS WRITING SKILLS AND SOON AFTER LANDED A

JOB WORKING FOR A LOCAL PAPER. IN HIS LAST YEAR OF HIGH

SCHOOL (1962-63), HE WON (WITH THE HELP OF U.S. PEACE CORPS

VOLUNTEER JOEL MEISNER) A ONE-YEAR ROTARY SCHOLARSHIP TO SAN

FRANCISCO UNIVERSITY TO STUDY ECONOMICS.

4. (SBU) WORKING DURING HIS FIRST YEAR OF UNIVERSITY

STUDIES AS A PART-TIME GAS STATION ATTENDANT, HE PLAYED HIS

WAY ONTO THE UNIVERSITY\'S SOCCER TEAM AND WON A SCHOLARSHIP.

HE GRADUATED IN 1970 WITH A BA IN ECONOMICS AND BUSINESS

ADMINISTRATION AND THEN STUDIED AT STANFORD, WHERE HE

COMPLETED MASTERS DEGREES IN HUMAN RESOURCES (1971) AND

ECONOMICS (1972) AND MARRIED FELLOW STANFORD STUDENT ELIANE

KARP, A BELGIAN BORN AMCIT, IN 1972. STARTING IN 1973, HE

WORKED ON A PHD IN HUMAN RESOURCES AND COMPLETED HIS

COURSEWORK AND EXAMS IN 1976. (NOTE: TOLEDO RECEIVED

RESEARCH GRANTS FROM USAID AND THE FORD FOUNDATION DURING

1973-76. WE HAVE NO RECORDS ON HOW HE FINANCED HIS STUDIES

PHD IN ECONOMICS OF HUMAN RESOURCES UNTIL 1992.)

5. (SBU) BETWEEN 1976 AND 1991, HE WAS A LIMA-BASED

CONSULTANT TO THE UN, WORLD BANK, IBD, AND WORKED FOR THE

7. (C) TOLEDO RAN FOR PRESIDENT IN 1995, COMING OUT OF

NOWHERE TO PEAK AT 15 PERCENT BEFORE FADING TO THREE PERCENT

(AFTER BEING ACCUSED OF TIES TO THE CLAE PYRAMID SCHEME --

SEE BELOW) AND A FOURTH-PLACE FINISH. HIS CAMPAIGN CENTERED

ON JOB CREATION AND INSTITUTIONALIZING PERU\'S PRIVATIZATION

PROGRAM. ACCORDING TO EMBASSY FILES, TOLEDO MAINTAINED

CLOSE CONTACTS TO THE FUJIMORI GOVERNMENT AFTER THE ELECTION

AND WAS MANEUVERING TO LAND A HIGH-PROFILE MINISTRY OF TRADE

OR FINANCE JOB THAT ULTIMATELY NEVER MATERIALIZED. HE

RETURNED TO HIS JOB AT ESAN AND BECAME DIRECTOR OF THE

RESEARCH AND INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT DEPARTMENT IN 1998.

A RELIABLE AND REGULAR EMBASSY CONTACT, HE VOICED HIS

INTENTION TO RUN FOR PRESIDENT IN 2000 TO THE DCM IN

NOVEMBER 1996.

-------------------------------

BLACK MARK I: THE CLAE SCANDAL

-------------------------------

8. (SBU) TOLEDO\'S REPUTATION IN THE 1995 ELECTIONS SUFFERED

FROM ALLEGATIONS OF TIES TO THE 1993 COLLAPSE OF A MULTI-

MILLION DOLLAR PYRAMID SCHEME (CALLED CLAE -- THE LATIN

AMERICAN CENTER OF BUSINESS ADVICE) IN WHICH THOUSANDS OF

PERUVIANS LOST THEIR SAVINGS AND SEVERAL PEOPLE WERE

CONVICTED OF FRAUD. TOLEDO\'S DIRECT INVOLVEMENT WAS NEVER

ESTABLISHED, BUT HE HAD WELL KNOWN PERSONAL (AND ALLEGED

PROFESSIONAL) TIES TO CLAE\'S OWNERS, CARLOS MANRIQUE AND HIS

WIFE VIOLETA MORI, BOTH OF WHOM ARE IN JAIL AND HAVE

SUBSEQUENTLY STATED THAT TOLEDO HAD WORKED FOR CLAE (A

CHARGE HE DENIES). TOLEDO HAS BEEN ACCUSED OF HELPING HIS

BROTHER WITHDRAW MONEY BEFORE CLAE\'S COLLAPSE. THE CLAE

STORY RESURFACED IN MARCH 2000 AS TOLEDO\'S POLL NUMBERS

ILLEGITIMATE CHILD, DIVORCE

---------------------------

9. (C) ON MARCH 28, LUCRECIA OROZCO ZAPATA APPEARED ON A

POPULAR DAYTIME TV TALK SHOW AND ALLEGED THAT TOLEDO HAD

FATHERED HER SIX-YEAR OLD DAUGHTER AND REFUSED TO RECOGNIZE

AND SUPPORT HER. THOUGH TOLEDO HAD WON TWO PATERNITY COURT

JUDGMENTS SEVERAL YEARS AGO, THE SHOW\'S HOSTESS, LAURA

BOZZO, CLAIMED THE COURTS WERE CORRUPT AND HAD IGNORED A

POSITIVE BLOOD TYPE MATCH. BOZZO LED THE TV STUDIO AUDIENCE

IN CHANTS CALLING ON TOLEDO TO TAKE A DNA TEST FOR SEVERAL

DAYS THEREAFTER. TOLEDO REFUSED COMMENT ON THE CHARGES,

NOTED THE COURTS\' PREVIOUS RULINGS, AND SAID HE HAD ONLY ONE

WIFE AND CHILD. OUR FILES CONTAIN NO MENTION OF OROZCO, BUT

EMBASSY SOURCES TELL US THAT TOLEDO DATED HER OPENLY IN THE

EARLY 1990S AFTER HE AND HIS WIFE SEPARATED SOMETIME IN

1988.
