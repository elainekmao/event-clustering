START MEMO NOMINATING-JUSTIFICATION:

Mr. Jayasundera is the head of the project

management division of the Urban Development

Authority. He is an energetic public official

dedicated to his work. The Colombo Re-development

Project, which Mr. Jayasundera is in charge of, is

an ambitious project and he has a correct assessment

of this mammoth task. Although he has been to the

United States before, that was only for two weeks to

Harvard to attend a short training program five

years ago. An exposure in general to the American

culture and society and especially to the

achievements in urban development in the United

States, will be of immense value to this urban

developer. An in-depth look at civic planning,

economic development and the nexus of government and

private sector activities in community building

could prove inspirational to this idealistic civil

servant and prove of great value to Sri Lanka and

its democratic future.

END MEMO NOMINATING-JUSTIFICATION:
