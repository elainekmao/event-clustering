SREBRENICA UPDATE

-----------------

2. THE UN REPORTS THAT 26,000 REFUGEES FROM SREBRENICA

ARE STILL BEING TAKEN CARE OF BY THE DUTCH BATTALION IN

POTOCARI, HOWEVER, FOOD IS EXPECTED RUN OUT BEFORE THE END

OF THE DAY. SEVERAL THOUSAND DRAFT AGE MALES ARE REPORTED

TO HAVE BEEN TAKEN AWAY BY THE BSA FOR QUESTIONING

REGARDING WAR CRIMES CHARGES, BUT THE DESTINATION IS

UNKNOWN (POSSIBLY TO BRATUNAC WHERE UNCONFIRMED REPORTS

INDICATE THAT 48 DUTCH PEACEKEEPERS ARE STILL REING

"DETAINED.") SO FAR, 6,000 DISPLACED PERSONS (DP\'S) HAVE

BEEN TAKEN TO KLADANJ IN BOSNIAN GOVERNMENT TERRITORY.

THE BSA IS TAKING REFUGEES BY BUS AS FAR AS 1-2 KILOMETERS

FROM THE BOSNIAN GOVERNMENT LINES AND THEN TELLING THEM TO

WALK THE REST OF THE WAY. UNHCR WILL MOVE THE GROUP AT

KLADANJ TO TUZLA, WHERE 4,000 DP\'S ARE LOCATED AT THE

AIRPORT, IN AN AREA THAT IS STILL SURROUNDED BY MINES.

ONE GROUP OF 33 WOUNDED REFUGEES WHO WERE UNABLE TO WALK

TO GOBH LINES TRIED TO TURN BACK TO POTOCARI, BUT WERE

STOPPED AT A BSA CHECKPOINT AND ARE BEING DENIED ACCESS TO

MEDICAL CARE. A UNHCR CONVOY FROM BELGRADE CARRYING FOOD,

WATER, MEDECINE, AND SANITARY SUPPLIES TO POTOCARI IS

BEING DETAINED BY THE BSA.

3. SOURCES AT THE UN AND IOM TOLD US THAT BOSNIAN

GOVERNMENT OFFICIALS ARE ALSO IMPEDING ASSISTANCE TO

REFUGEES IN ORDER TO WORSEN THE SITUATION AND FOCUS

INTERNATIONAL ATTENTION. GOBH MINISTER WITHOUT PORTFOLIO

HASAN MURATOVIC REPORTEDLY TOOK A HARD LINE WITH UNHCR,

SAYING THAT RESPONSIBILITY FOR CARE OF THE REFUGEES LIES

SOLELY WITH THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY. WE WERE TOLD

THAT GOBH AUTHORITIES ARE BLOCKING UNHCR ATTEMPTS TO MOVE

DP\'S FROM THE TUZLA AIRPORT INTO 11,000 SPACES IN TOWN AND

IOM REPORTED THAT 40 INJURED DP\'S WERE TURNED AWAY FROM

TUZLA HOSPITAL. UN CONTACTS ALSO ACCUSED THE GOBH OF

DELAYING AID CONVOYS.

4. AS OF NOON ON JULY 13, GORAZDE AND ZEPA ARE REPORTED

TO BE CALM. HOWEVER, THE UN STATED THAT THERE IS LITTLE

THAT COULD BE DONE TO KEEP THE ZEPA ENCLAVE FROM BEING

OVERRUN IN CASE OF AN ATTACK. THERE ARE NO CURRENT PLANS

TO REINFORCE THE 70 UKRAINIAN SOLDIERS IN THE TOWN.

5. COMMENTING ON THE DRAFT UNSC RESOLUTION ON SREBRENICA,

UN DEPUTY PRESS SPOKESMAN CHRIS GUNNESS SAID THAT

REESTABLISHMENT OF THE SAFE AREA IS NOT LIKELY TO HAPPEN

AS IT WOULD REQUIRE RECONQUERING THE ENCLAVE. THE

SITUATION IN SREBRENICA, HE SAID, SHOWS THAT THE CONCEPT

OF SAFE AREAS IS UNREALISTIC. THIS CRISIS IS ANOTHER

EXAMPLE OF THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY PURSUING LOFTY

OBJECTIVES WITHOUT PROVIDING THE UN WITH SUFFICIENT

RESOURCES TO CARRY THEM OUT. WHEN ASKED IF UNPROFOR WILL

PROTECT THE OTHER SAFE AREAS IN CASE OF ATTACK, THE

SPOKESMAN\'S OFFICE REPLIED THAT UNPROFOR COULD DO THIS

ONLY IF MEMBER STATES HAVE THE POLITICAL WILL TO PROVIDE

THE MILITARY RESOURCES. REFERRING TO A POSSIBLE ROLE FOR

THE RAPID REACTION FORCE, GUNNESS SAID THAT TROOP

CONTRIBUTORS STILL HAVE NOT INDICATED HOW THIS FORCE IS TO

BE USED.

MALES; UNPROFOR SPOKESMAN CRITICIZES THE UNSC

BEEN INVITED TO ATTEND A BREIFING ON THE CROATIAN POSITION

ON THE SITUATION IN SREBRENICA BY FOREIGN MINISTER GRANIC

THE AFTERNOON OF JULY 13. WE EXPECT THE MESSAGE WILL BE

SIMILAR TO THAT EXPRESSED BY CROATIA\'S UN AMBASSADOR MARIO

NOBILO IN NEW YORK ON JULY 12, TO THE EFFECT THAT CROATIA

DEPLORES THE OUTCOME IN SREBRENICA AND IS CONCERNED ABOUT

ITS POTENTIAL TO CREATE NEW REFUGEE FLOWS, RUT WILL NOT

TAKE ANY CONCRETE ACTION IN RESPONSE. NOBILO STRESSED,

HOWEVER, THAT ANY SIMILAR LARGE-SCALE OFFENSIVE ACTION

AGAINST THE BIHAC POCKET MIGHT CREATE UP TO 60,000

REFUGEES AND THUS CONSTITUTE A THREAT TO CROATIA\'S WELL-

BEING. THEREFORE, THE GOC "MIGHT BE FORCED TO TAKE

MEASURES FOR ENSURING THE STATUS OF BIHAC AS A PROTECTED

AREA IF THAT STATUS WERE THREATENED."
