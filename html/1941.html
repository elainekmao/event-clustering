SUBJECT: AMBASSADOR NICHOLSON BEGINS CREDENTIALING PROCESS:

MEETING WITH SOSTITUTO SANDRI

CLASSIFIED BY AMBASSADOR FOR REASONS 1.5 (B) AND (D).

CONFIDENTIAL

1. (C) AMBASSADOR NICHOLSON BEGAN THE VATICAN\'S FORMAL

CREDENTIALING PROCESS MORNING OF SEPTEMBER 6 BY PRESENTING

COPIES OF HIS LETTERS OF CREDENTIAL TO ARCHBISHOP LEONARDO

SANDRI, "SUBSTITUTE" FOR VATICAN PM EQUIVALENT SODANO. THE

AMBASSADOR WILL HAVE THE FORMAL PRESENTATION TO POPE JOHN

PAUL II ON SEPTEMBER 13. SANDRI STRESSED THAT THE VATICAN

SEES THE UNITED STATES AS "A FRIENDLY NATION"; ONE IN WHICH

THE VALUES OF FREEDOM AND PEACE ARE APPRECIATED. IN

ADDITION, THE VATICAN IS NOW LOOKING TOWARD THE UNITED

STATES AS A PARTNER IN PROTECTING HUMAN LIFE, AS EUROPE IS

GOING IN A DIFFERENT DIRECTION ON THE CRUCIAL ISSUES OF

ABORTION AND EUTHANASIA. THE ARCHBISHOP SAID THE BILATERAL

RELATIONSHIP WITH THE UNITED STATES IS IMPORTANT. HE NOTED

THAT THE VISIT OF PRESIDENT BUSH TO THE POPE WAS VERY MUCH

APPRECIATED, AS ARE THE VIEWS OF THE PRESIDENT ON AN ISSUE

OF IMMENSE IMPORTANCE TO THE VATICAN, THE VALUE OF HUMAN

LIFE. AMBASSADOR NICHOLSON TOLD THE ARCHBISHOP THAT HE MET

WITH PRESIDENT BUSH BEFORE DEPARTING WASHINGTON, AND THAT

THE PRESIDENT SAID HE WAS VERY MOVED BY HIS MEETING WITH

THE POPE.

2. (C) SANDRI, A FORMER NUNCIO IN VENEZUELA AND MEXICO,

TOUCHED UPON CONDITIONS IN THOSE TWO COUNTRIES. HE SAID

THAT CHAVEZ IS FIRMLY IN POWER, BUT THAT WHEN THE PEOPLE

REALIZE THAT THERE HAS NOT BEEN MATERIAL IMPROVEMENT IN

THEIR LIVES, HIS POPULARITY SHOULD BEGIN TO WANE. SANDRI

ADDED THAT HE RECENTLY MET WITH 1992 FELLOW COUP PLOTTER

AND DEFEATED PRESIDENTIAL CANDIDATE FRANCISCO CARDENAS, WHO

SAID CHAVEZ\' POPULARITY IS ALREADY SLIPPING. IN MEXICO,

PRESIDENT FOX IS HAMPERED IN HIS REFORM EFFORTS BY THE LACK

OF A MAJORITY. THE SOSTITUTO CLOSED BY SAYING THE VATICAN
