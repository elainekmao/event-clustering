SUBJECT: AMBASSADOR SIGNS MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING WITH THE

NATIONAL DRILLING COMPANY

REF: STATE 60462

Per reftel, Ambassador Marcelle M. Wahba signed a Memorandum of

Understanding (MOU) with the National Drilling Company (NDC) of

Abu Dhabi on behalf of the U.S. Geological Survey (USGS) on March

10, 2003, at the Embassy. Attending on behalf of the NDC were

Abdul Munim Seif Al Kindy, General Manager of NDC, Suleiman Al

Maskri, Assistant General Manager, and Mohamed Ali Saeed Ali Al

Nuaimi, Ground Water Research Program Manager of NDC Al Ain.

Douglas A. Trudeau, USGS Program Advisor, attended on behalf of

the USGS.

Wahba
