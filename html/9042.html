1. (U) Classified by A/DCM Thomas E. Williams for

2. (C) Econoff accompanied the Dutch Ambassador and a

counterpart from the British Embassy in delivering

reftel demarche points June 28 to MFA Organizations

and Conferences Director Mohamed Jassem Samhan Al-

Nuaimi. The demarche was also presented on behalf of

the Canadian Embassy. Al-Nuaimi asked why the UN was

not funding the Court and repeatedly questioned why

the appeal was directed at individual states and did

not come through the OIC. While remaining

noncommittal, Al-Nuaimi said he would pass the

information on to his superiors. We will report the

UAEG response when we receive it.
