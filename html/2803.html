2. EMBOFF VISITED SELENIA 2000 AND SPOKE WITH

DR. PIETRO ROCCO, OWNER AND COMMERCIAL REPRESENTATIVE

OF THE FIRM. ROCCO CONFIRMED THAT THE MATERIAL

CONTAINED IN DTC CASE NUMBERS 05-843909 AND 05-843912

HAVE BEEN ORDERED BY THE ITALIAN ARMED FORCE FOR USE IN

OPERATION ENDURING FREEDOM.

3. ROCCO ALSO STATED THAT THE MATERIAL IN QUESTION

HAS NOT YET BEEN RECEIVED. HE ESTIMATED THAT THE FIRST

ORDER WOULD LEAVE THE U.S. BY APRIL 22 AND ARRIVE IN

ROME THE FIRST OR SECOND WEEK OF MAY. HE ESTIMATED

THAT THE SECOND WOULD LEAVE SOON AFTERWARD. THE CARGO

WILL BE PICKED UP BY HIS FIRM IMMEDIATELY UPON ITS

ARRIVAL AND WILL BE ESCORTED TO ITS FINAL DESTINATION

IN ITALY.

4. ROCCO STATED THAT HIS FIRM HAS WORKED IN THE

DEFENSE SECTOR SINCE 1992 AND DOES BUSINESS ONLY WITH

THE ITALIAN GOVERNMENT AND NOT WITH THE PRIVATE SECTOR.

SELENIA 2000 IS A MEMBER OF URNI (THE ITALIAN NATIONAL

REGISTRY OF ARMAMENTS) AND USES PRODUCTS FROM PRIMARILY

THE FOLLOWING THREE U.S. FIRMS: INSIDE TECHNOLOGY,

SUREFIRE, AND BUSHMASTER.

5. EMBASSY WILL CONFIRM RECEIPT OF EQUIPMENT BY END-

USER, ONCE THIS TAKES PLACE.
