SUBJECT: ISAF COMMANDER MG ZORLU\'S VISIT TO THE US -

RECOMMENDATIONS FOR MEETINGS WITH USG OFFICIALS

(U) Classified by Deputy Chief of Mission Robert Deutsch.

Reasons 1.5 (b) and (d).

1. (U) This is an action request. See para 5.

2. (C) MFA DDG for South Asian Affairs Aydin Evirgen recently

informed Deputy Pol-Mil Counselor that ISAF Commander MG

Zorlu would be traveling to the US November 20-26 to meet

with USG and UN officials and private organizations to

discuss Turkey\'s experience as ISAF lead nation and the

general security situation in Afghanistan. Zorlu is

scheduled to be in Washington November 21-22 and in New York

November 25-26. Evirgen said the visit was MG Zorlu\'s idea,

but stressed that it enjoyed widespread support within the

Turkish General Staff (TGS) and the MFA. (TGS Navy Captain

Sinani separately told Deputy Pol-Mil Counselor November 15

that the TGS leadership supported the visit as an opportunity

to increase awareness in the US of the role that Turkey is

playing in Afghanistan and in the larger global war on

terrorism.)

3. (C) Evirgen said the Turkish Embassy in Washington was

attempting to schedule meetings with senior USG officials and

asked that the Embassy support these efforts. According to

Evirgen, the Turkish Embassy initially requested appointments

with the Secretary of Defense and the Chairman of the Joint

Chiefs of Staff, but has since learned the two will be in

Prague for the NATO Summit. The Turkish Embassy reportedly

is now trying to schedule meetings with the Deputy Secretary

of Defense and VCJCS Gen Pace. Evirgen also noted that the

MFA believed it would be a good idea for MG Zorlu to meet

with a senior State Department official and said he would ask

the Turkish Embassy in Washington to request a meeting with

U/S Grossman.

4. Comment: Given that MG Zorlu has been stationed in Kabul

since June and focusing his attention on ISAF-related issues,

we do not expect him to initiate discussion with USG

officials on Iraq, nor do we expect him to be intimately

familiar with the details of the discussions between US and

Turkish officials on Iraq.

5. (C) Action request: The Embassy strongly supports the

visit of MG Zorlu to the US and recommends that the

Department of Defense (OSD and JCS) and the State Department

receive MG Zorlu at the highest possible level. By all

accounts, MG Zorlu -- who was hand-picked by TGS CHOD Ozkok

-- has done an excellent job as ISAF commander since taking

over in June. By receiving Zorlu at an appropriately high

level, the USG can reinforce the importance we attach to the

strategic partnership and our appreciation for Turkey\'s

performance as ISAF lead nation.

PEARSON
