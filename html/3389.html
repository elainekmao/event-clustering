1. (C) SUMMARY. AMBASSADOR SEMBLER MET WITH GIANNI LETTA,

U/S AT THE PRESIDENCY OF THE COUNCIL OF MINISTERS, ON JULY 26

TO PURSUE AT THE HIGHEST LEVEL REFTEL DEMARCHES ON THE

INTERNATIONAL CRIMINAL COURT (ICC) AND OPERATION ENDURING

FREEDOM (OEF) TROOP CONTRIBUTIONS. AMB. SEMBLER URGED THE GOI

TO NEGOTIATE AN ARTICLE 98 AGREEMENT WITH THE USG SOON TO

ENSURE PROTECTION FOR US TROOPS AGAINST IMPROPER APPLICATIONS

OF THE ICC\'S JURISDICTION. HE URGED THAT BERLUSCONI MAKE A

STRONG STATEMENT OF RECOMMENDATION TO PARLIAMENT THAT ITALY

CONTRIBUTE GROUND TROOPS OR SPECIAL OPERATIONS FORCES IN

SUPPORT OF OEF. LETTA, WHO IS BERLUSCONI\'S CLOSEST ADVISOR,

AGREED TO RAISE BOTH ISSUES WITH THE PM. WE EXPECT A REPLY

EARLY NEXT WEEK. END SUMMARY.

OEF TROOP CONTRIBUTIONS

-----------------------

2. (C) COMMENTS BY PM BERLUSCONI ON JULY 24 REGARDING

ITALY\'S TROOP DEPLOYMENT IN SUPPORT OF OEF (SEPTEL) OPENED

THE WAY FOR AMB. SEMBLER TO EMPHASIZE TO LETTA THE IMPORTANCE

OF A COMMITMENT OF GROUND TROOPS OR SPECIAL OPERATIONS FORCES

TO REPLACE UNITS ROTATING OUT OVER THE NEXT TWO YEARS. THE

AMBASSADOR NOTED THAT ONLY 379 ITALIAN TROOPS CURRENTLY WERE

DEPLOYED FOR OEF (307 ON A FRIGATE IN THE ARABIAN SEA),

RATHER THAN THE 1,000 TROOPS BERLUSCONI HAD MENTIONED DURING

HIS SPEECH TO ITALIAN AMBASSADORS. HE AND LETTA AGREED THAT

PAGE 03 ROME 03703 01 OF 02 261604Z

THE PM WAS LIKELY REFERRING TO ITALIAN TROOPS SUPPORTING BOTH

OEF AND ISAF, WHICH TOGETHER INCLUDE OVER 800 SOLDIERS AND

CARABINIERI (379 IN OEF PLUS 454 IN ISAF). THE AMBASSADOR

NOTED THAT THE ORIGINAL OFFER APPROVED BY PARLIAMENT LAST

NOVEMBER INCLUDED GROUND TROOPS AND SPECIAL OPERATIONS FORCES

THAT HAVE NOT BEEN CALLED UPON...UNTIL NOW. (IN HIS SPEECH,

BERLUSCONI, AND LATER DEFENSE MINISTER MARTINO, STATED THAT

PARLIAMENT WOULD NEED TO REVIEW A REQUEST TO DEPLOY TROOPS.)

3. (C) AMB. SEMBLER TOLD LETTA THAT HE WAS CONCERNED ABOUT

THE DELAY A PARLIAMENTARY REVIEW WOULD CAUSE, PARTICULARLY

GIVEN THE IMPENDING PARLIAMENTARY RECESS UNTIL MID-SEPTEMBER.

HE STRESSED THAT IT IS VERY IMPORTANT THAT ITALY INDICATE A

POSITIVE INTENT TO HELP, NOW THAT THE US IS ASKING FOR

SPECIFIC ASSISTANCE. A/DCM NOTED THAT WE WOULD HOPE A MAJOR

NATO ALLY WOULD RESPOND MORE QUICKLY THAN SOME OF THE SMALLER

COUNTRIES THAT ARE PREPARING TO STEP FORWARD. (NOTE:

FOLLOWING THE MEETING, THE PRESS REPORTED ALBANIA\'S PLANS TO

SEND A 30-MAN SPECIAL OPERATIONS TEAM -- TRAINED BY ITALIANS

-- TO AFGHANISTAN ON AUGUST 5. END NOTE.) THE AMBASSADOR

PROPOSED THAT THE PM RECOMMEND TO PARLIAMENT THAT GROUND

TROOPS OR SPECIAL OPERATION FORCES BE CONTRIBUTED TO OEF -- A

COMMITMENT THAT WILL CLEARLY SHOW THAT BERLUSCONI AND HIS

GOVERNMENT ARE WITH THE US IN THIS FIGHT. HE MENTIONED HIS

MEETING WITH THE PM LAST MARCH WHEN BERLUSCONI MADE AN

ON-THE-SPOT DECISION, AGAINST THE PREFERENCES OF HIS MILITARY

ADVISORS, TO PROVIDE SECURITY FOR THE FORMER AFGHAN KING --

THAT KIND OF IMMEDIATE POLITICAL COMMITMENT WAS CALLED FOR

HERE, THE AMBASSADOR SAID.

4. (C) LETTA PROMISED TO CONVEY TO THE PM OUR REQUEST THAT A

COMMITMENT BE MADE AS SOON AS POSSIBLE RATHER THAN WAITING

UNTIL MID-SEPTEMBER. HE EXPLAINED THAT PARLIAMENT\'S APPROVAL

OF ITALY\'S PARTICIPATION IN OEF LAST NOVEMBER INCLUDED THE

OFFER TO CONTRIBUTE. HE SAID HE WILL RELAY BERLUSCONI\'S

RESPONSE EARLY NEXT WEEK.

6. (C) COMMENT. LETTA CLEARLY GOT THE MESSAGE THAT THESE TWO

ISSUES ARE OF PARTICULAR IMPORTANCE TO THE USG RIGHT NOW. IT

WILL TAKE REAL LEADERSHIP BY BERLUSCONI TO RISK ROCKING THE

EU BOAT BY BEING THE FIRST TO START ARTICLE 98 AGREEMENT

NEGOTIATIONS. HE WILL ALSO LIKELY ENCOUNTER SOME DOMESTIC

RESISTANCE TO COMMITING TROOPS TO AFGHANISTAN, SOMETHING HE

AND HIS DEFENSE MINISTER HAVE BEEN HOPING TO AVOID. WE WOULD

NOT BE SURPRISED IF THE PM\'S SOLUTION IS TO MAKE A PERSONAL

BUT CONFIDENTIAL COMMITMENT TO PRESS PARLIAMENT FOR RAPID

APPROVAL IN SEPTEMBER, WITHOUT MAKING IT A MATTER OF PUBLIC

DISCUSSION BEFORE THEN. END COMMENT.
