SUBJECT: THE CESS POOL: QADHAFI ORGANIZES THE NEIGHBORS

REF: NDJAMENA 402 AND PREVIOUS (ALL NOTAL)

CLASSIFIED BY D. HALSTED. REASON 1.5 (B,D).

1. (U) PRESIDENT DEBY BROUGHT BACK FROM HIS FEBRUARY 3-

5 VISIT NEWS OF THE FORMATION OF THE COMMUNITY OF SAHEL-

SAHARA STATES (COMMUNAUTE DES ETATS SAHELO-SAHARIENS, OR

CESS). CESS, ACCORDING TO DEBY\'S ANNOUNCEMENT, WOULD

APPEAR TO BE FIVE STATES: LIBYA, CHAD, NIGER, MALI, AND

SUDAN. THE CENTRAL AFRICAN REPUBLIC, BURKINA FASO,

TUNISIA AND EGYPT WERE OTHERS REPORTED TO HAVE BEEN

REPRESENTED AT THE TRIPOLI MEETING, AND MAY ALSO BE PART

OF THE NEW ORGANIZATION.

2. (U) DEBY SAID THE IDEA DATED BACK TO 1972. CESS

WOULD BE FIRST AN ECONOMIC GROUPING, THEN LATER A

POLITICAL ASSOCIATION, GIVING A GROUP OF LANDLOCKED

STATES OCEAN FRONTAGE. UNITY THEMES FIGURED IN HIS

DESCRIPTION: "AT A TIME WHEN IMPORTANT GROUPS ARE BEING

FORMED IN EUROPE AND ASIA, WE HAVE A DUTY, AS AFRICANS,

TO DEVELOP THE REALITY OF TOTAL AFRICAN INTEGRATION."

CESS IS TO FOCUS ON ECONOMIC COOPERATION IN AGRICULTURE,

INDUSTRY, ENERGY, COMMERCE AND FINANCE. PROVISIONAL

HEADQUARTERS WILL BE IN TRIPOLI, WITH LIBYAN MAHDI

AZHARI AS EXECEUTIVE SECRETARY AND CHADIAN ADOUM TOGOI

AS HIS DEPUTY. QADHAFI WAS NAMED PROVISIONAL PRESIDENT

OF CESS.

3. (C) COMMENT. CESS IS NOT LIKELY TO AMOUNT TO MUCH,

AT LEAST WHERE CHAD IS CONCERNED. IT MAY PROMPT SOME

DEBATE AS ANY AGREEMENT WILL PROBABLY BE REVIEWED BY THE

NATIONAL ASSEMBLY. CHAD WOULD GO TO CONSIDERABLE

LENGTHS TO ACCOMODATE QADHAFI, WHO IS GENERALLY REGARDED

WITHIN CHAD\'S RULING GROUP AS A MENACE. UNDER BETTER

CONDITIONS, CHAD WOULD HAVE A GENUINE INTEREST IN A

ROUTE TO THE MEDITERRANEAN AS AN ALTERNATIVE TO CURRENT

TRANSPORTATION ARRANGEMENTS.

4. (C) THE ANNOUNCEMENT ABOUT ADOUM TOGOI SOLVES ANY

MYSTERY OF WHERE HE WENT AFTER HIS NAME SURFACED AS A

COUP PLOTTER AGAINST DEBY. ALREADY A LEGEND FOR HIS

PRO-LIBYAN SYMPATHIES, TOGOI LOST HIS JOB AS CHAD\'S

AMBASSADOR TO LIBYA WHEN HE GOT INTO A FIST FIGHT IN

NOVEMBER 1996 YEAR WITH THEN-FOREIGN MINISTER KEBZABO

OVER DIFFERENCES ABOUT LIBYAN POLICY, ALL RIGHT IN FRONT

OF A VISITING LIBYAN DELEGATION. DEBY GAVE HIM A JOB AT

THE PRESIDENCY SO HE COULD KEEP AN EYE ON HIM. TOGOI

WAS REMOVED FROM THAT JOB LAST NOVEMBER, AND THERE

ENSUED - FOR THIS REASON AND EVIDENTLY OTHERS - THE

CHALLENGE TO DEBY\'S LEADERSHIP FROM FELLOW ZAGHAWAS

(REFTELS).
