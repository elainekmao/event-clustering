SUBJECT: TURKEY WORKING ON CATCH-ALL CONTROLS

(C) PolMilOff delivered reftel points to MFA Disarmament

Head of Department Ibrahim Yagli May 27. On May 29, Yagli

told PolMilOff that he had forwarded the points to the

authorities within the GOT who were currently drafting

catch-all regulations, urging them to expedite their work.

He declined to offer an estimate of when the new catch-all

controls would be finalized.
