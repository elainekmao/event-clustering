2. (C) CELLI SAID THAT THE VATICAN ACCEPTS THAT THERE WILL BE

NO IMPROVEMENT IN CHINA/VATICAN RELATIONS UNTIL AT LEAST THE

COMMUNIST PARTY CONGRESS IN MARCH, 2002. THE CURRENT

DIRECTOR OF THE RELIGIOUS AFFAIRS BUREAU IN BEIJING IS AN

"OLD MAOIST". ACCORDING TO CELLI, THE POWER OF THE RELIGIOUS

AFFAIRS BUREAU IS A MAJOR REASON THAT BEIJING REFUSES TO

BEGIN DISCUSSIONS ON NORMALIZATION OF RELATIONS. CELLI

EXPLAINED THAT THE BUREAU IS A MAJOR BUREAUCRACY WHICH IS

FIGHTING HARD TO PROTECT ITS POWER, PERKS, AND PERSONNEL. THE

HEAD OF THE PATRIOTIC CATHOLIC CHURCH, THE BISHOP OF BEIJING,

IS COMPLACENT WITH THE CURRENT SITUATION AND HE TOO DEPENDS

ON THE BUREAU FOR HIS POWER AND POSITION.

3. (C) AMBASSADOR ASKED IF THE POPE\'S RECENT OVERTURE TO

BEIJING HAS HAD ANY EFFECT. CELLI REPLIED THAT THE VATICAN

IS NOW ACCEPTING THE FACT THAT IT HAS NOT. HE WENT ON TO SAY

THAT CHINA IS A "BIRD CAGE" AND THAT THE VATICAN\'S GOAL IS

NOT TO BREAK DOWN THE "CAGE" BUT TO WIDEN IT. THE VATICAN

DOES NOT EXPECT FULL FREEDOM OF RELIGION IN CHINA ANY TIME IN

THE NEAR FUTURE, AND IS THEREFORE WILLING TO ACCEPT A

"DECENT" LEVEL. THE AMBASSADOR INQUIRED IF VATICAN RELATIONS

WITH TAIWAN IS AN OBSTACLE TO NORMALIZATION. CELLI SAID THAT

BEIJING KNOWS "PERFECTLY WELL" THAT TAIWAN IS NOT AN ISSUE.

ACCORDING TO CELLI, BEJING USES THE TAIWAN ISSUE AS AN EXCUSE

TO PREVENT DISCUSSION ON THE REAL ISSUE-- THE RIGHT OF THE

CATHOLIC CHURCH TO NAME ITS OWN BISHOPS.

4. (C) CELLI SAID THAT THE VATICAN KNOWS PRESIDENT BUSH MADE

AN INTERVENTION ON ITS BEHALF IN SHANGHAI, AND THAT IT IS

VERY MUCH APPRECIATED. THIS TYPE OF HELP IS NEEDED AS THE

CATHOLIC COMMUNITY IN CHINA IS SUFFERING. CELLI POINTED TO

THE FACT THAT WITHIN FIVE OR SIX YEARS, THE MAJORITY OF THE

ELDERLY BISHOPS WILL PROBABLY DIE. AS THERE WAS A LONG

PERIOD IN WHICH THERE WERE NO ORDINATIONS OF PRIESTS AND

BISHOPS, THESE DYING BISHOPS WILL BE REPLACED BY VERY YOUNG,

INEXPERIENCED CHURCHMEN. THUS, THE VATICAN\'S GOAL IS TO

NORMALIZE ITS RELATIONSHIP WITH BEIJING BEFORE THIS OCCURS.

THIS WOULD ALLOW THE VATICAN TO GUIDE THE TRANSITION.

5. (C) AMBASSADOR ASKED CELLI FOR HIS VIEWS ON VIETNAM.

CELLI SAID THAT HE IS NO LONGER INVOLVED ON THE VIETNAM

ISSUE. THAT SAID, HE BELIEVED THAT THE CATHOLIC COMMUNITY

WAS SOMEWHAT IMPROVED THAN WHEN HE WAS LAST THERE FIVE YEARS

AGO. AT THAT TIME, HE WAS NOT ALLOWED TO VISIT ANY CATHOLIC

CHURCHES IN HANOI.

6. (C) COMMENT: THE VATICAN IS RESIGNED TO THE FACT THAT THE

POPE\'S OVERTURE IN OCTOBER FELL ON DEAF EARS. IT IS NOW

WAITING UNTIL AFTER MARCH TO RESUME ITS DIPLOMATIC PUSH. IT

IS LIKELY FOR THE VATICAN TO MAKE SOME CONCESSIONS TO BEIJING

TO REESTABLISH ITSELF BEFORE THE END OF THIS DECADE.
