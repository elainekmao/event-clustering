SUBJECT: JOURNALISTS\' COURT APPEARANCE POSTPONED

SENSITIVE BUT UNCLASSIFIED. NOT FOR INTERNET POSTING.

1. (U) A Harare magistrate has postponed the hearing

for two journalists arrested on May 1 for "publishing

falsehoods." "Guardian" correspondent Andrew Meldrum

(an American citizen) and "Daily News" reporter Lloyd

Mudiwa appeared in court on May 22, expecting their

case would be heard. Instead, the magistrate postponed

the hearing to May 30. Both reporters face charges

related to a now-disproven story about a woman beheaded

by ruling party supporters.

2. (SBU) Harare-based journalists have told us that

the presiding magistrate finds no merit in the charges

against Meldrum and Mudiwa and wants to dismiss the

case, but has been prevented from doing so by high-

level political pressure.
