SUBJECT: Mission conveys points on fleeing Iraqi regime

officials; GSL responds positively to points

Ref: State 98747

(U) Classified by Lewis Amselem, Deputy Chief of

Mission. Reasons 1.5 (b, d)

1. (C) Per Reftel, Mission has conveyed the points on

fleeing Iraqi regime officials to the Sri Lankan and

Maldivian Governments. Sri Lankan Foreign Secretary

Nihal Rodrigo assured the Ambassador that if the GSL had

any information that a former Saddam official was trying

to enter Sri Lanka the U.S. Embassy would be the first

to know. Rodrigo added that the GSL is now preventing

any monetary transfers into or out of Sri Lanka

involving the local Iraqi embassy. The GSL is

permitting internal monetary transactions, however, so

that the embassy can continue to operate.

2. (C) The Maldivian government has not yet responded to

our points, but we expect it will be cooperative on this

important matter.

3. (U) Minimize considered.
