SUBJECT: JORDAN MEDIA REACTION ON ARAB CONSULTATIONS,

IRAQ, U.S. REGIONAL POLICY.

SUMMARY

NEWS REPORTS IN JORDAN\'S DAILIES CONTAINED THREE

PRINCIPAL ELEMENTS: CONSULTATIONS BETWEEN THE SAUDI

FOREIGN MINISTER\'S MEETING WITH KING ABDULLAH, MEETINGS

BETWEEN JORDANIAN AND PALESTINIAN OFFICIALS OVER THE

NEW ARRANGEMENTS ON THE BRIDGE, AND THE JORDANIAN

GOVERNMENT\'S INVITATION TO THE IRAQI AMBASSADOR TO

VISIT ANY JORDANIAN MILITARY BASE TO ENSURE THAT JORDAN

WOULD NOT BE USED AS A LAUNCHING GROUND FOR AN ATTACK

AGAINST HIS COUNTRY.

EDITORIAL WRITERS COMMENTED ON THE AMERICAN THREATS TO

IRAQ, THE BOYCOTT OF AMERICAN PRODUCTS, AND U.S.

REGIONAL POLICY.

NEWS REPORTS

ALL JORDANIAN DAILIES LED WITH REPORTS ON THE MEETING

BETWEEN KING ABDULLAH AND THE SAUDI FOREIGN MINISTER,

IN WHICH THEY SOUGHT TO COORDINATE POSITIONS IN

PREPARATION FOR THE FORTHCOMING ARAB MINISTERS\' METING

IN CAIRO, ACCORDING TO THE REPORTS.

JORDANIAN DAILIES ALSO REPORTED THE MEETING BETWEEN

JORDAN\'S PRIME MINISTER AND SA\'EB EREQAT ON THE

SITUATION IN THE OCCUPIED TERRITORIES, AND ANOTHER

MEETING BETWEEN JORDAN\'S MINISTER OF INTERIOR AND HIS

PALESTINIAN COUNTERPART, WHICH DEALT WITH THE NEW

ARRANGEMENTS TO FACILITATE THE PASSAGE OF PALESTINIANS

FROM THE WEST BANK TO JORDAN.

CENTER-LEFT, INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR ON

JULY 11 REPORTED THAT THE JORDANIAN MINISTER OF FOREIGN

AFFAIRS HAS INVITED THE IRAQI AMBASSADOR TO INSPECT ANY

JORDANIAN MILITARY BASE TO ENSURE THAT JORDAN WOULD

HAVE NO ROLE IN ANY FORTHCOMING ATTACK AGAINST HIS

COUNTRY.

EDITORIAL COMMENTARIES

-- THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY REJECTS WASHINGTON\'S

PLANS TO STRIKE AGAINST IRAQ

DAILY COLUMNIST MOHAMMAD NAJI AMAYREH WROTE IN SEMI-

OFFICIAL INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-RAI ON JULY 11:

"AS INTERNATIONAL OPPOSITION TO U.S. PLANS IN IRAQ

RISES, PRESIDENT BUSH CONTINUES HIS THREATS AND UNVEILS

HIS DETERMINATION TO CHANGE THE IRAQI REGIME BY ALL

MEANS AT HIS DISPOSAL. IT IS NO LONGER A SECRET THAT

U.S. OBJECTIVES ARE NOT RELATED TO IRAQ\'S RESPECT OF

SECURITY COUNCIL RESOLUTIONS, ITS WEAPONS OF MASS

DESTRUCTION, OR WHAT AMERICANS CALL ITS THREATS TO ITS

NEIGHBORS. THE OBJECTIVE IS TO CONTROL ARAB OIL AND

SERVE ISRAEL\'S INTERESTS. IT IS ALSO CLEAR THAT

WASHINGTON CANNOT FIND A SINGLE ARAB OR NON-ARAB VOICE

THAT STANDS WITH IT IN ITS AGGRESSION. WILL THE U.S.

STAND ALONE AGAINST THE WILL OF THE WORLD, SUPPORTED

ONLY BY ISRAEL?"

-- THE BOYCOTT OF AMERICAN PRODUCTS IS NOT AS ITS

ENEMIES DESCRIBE IT

FORMER PRESS SECRETARY TO THE ROYAL COURT KHALED

MAHADIN WROTE IN SEMI-OFFICIAL INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY

AL-RAI ON JULY 11: "ATTEMPTS TO PORTRAY THE ARAB

BOYCOTT OF AMERICAN PRODUCTS IS NOT, AS ITS CRITICS TRY

TO PORTRAY IT, A NATIONAL STRUGGLE TO DESTROY THE

AMERICAN ECONOMY. THERE IS NOT A FRIEND OR ENEMY OF

THE U.S. WHO DOES NOT REALIZE THAT OUR NATIONAL

ECONOMIES ARE IN THE AMERICAN GRIP, WHICH USES IT TO

SERVE THE AMERICAN CONSUMER, WHO SPENDS LAVISHLY ON HIS

DIET AT THE EXPENSE OF ARABS WHO CANNOT FIND ENOUGH TO

EAT OR TO SEEK MEDICAL CARE. THE ISSUE IS NOT ECONOMIC

EVEN IN THE LAST DEGREE. IT IS A NATIONAL CAUSE THAT

BECOMES NECESSARY IN VIEW OF THE ARAB OFFICIAL MEDIA\'S

CONCERN ONLY WITH ISSUES THAT DO NOT CONCERN ARAB

CITIZENS. IT HAS TO DO WITH THE IDENTITY OF WHO IS WITH

US AND WHO IS AGAINST US."

-- A POLICY OF CONVULSIONS AND VENGEFULNESS

TAHER AL-ADWAN, CHIEF EDITOR OF INDEPENDENT, MASS-

APPEAL ARABIC DAILY AL-ARAB AL-YAWM WROTE ON JULY 11:

"THE AMERICAN PRESIDENT\'S AGENDA AFFIRMS HIS

PREOCCUPATION WITH TWO ITEMS: OUSTING YASSER ARAFAT

FROM THE LEADERSHIP OF THE PALESTINIANS AND ELIMINATING

PRESIDENT SADDAM HUSSEIN OF IRAQ. THIS REMINDS US OF

HIS MILITARY AGENDA IN AFGHANISTAN, TO KILL MULLA OMAR

AND BIN LADEN. THE WARS OF THE U.S. HAVE BECOME

PERSONAL AFFAIRS, AND ITS POLICIES ARE NO LONGER

BEFITTING OF A SUPERPOWER THAT IS SUPPOSED TO UPHOLD

INTERNATIONAL LEGITIMACY. WHAT THE U.S. PRESIDENT WANTS

IN IRAQ AND PALESTINE IS THE SAME AS WHAT SHARON WANTS,

AND ITS IS IMPOSSIBLE TO JUSTIFY, EXCEPT THROUGH SOME

WARPED EVANGELISM, SUCH AS THE CLAIM THAT IRAQ NEEDS TO

BE DESTROYED IN ORDER TO BUILD DEMOCRACY THERE, AND

THAT THE PNA SHOULD BE REFORMED THROUGH ISRAELI TANKS

AND ARTILLERY."
