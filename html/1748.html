THE VATICAN AND THE MIDDLE EAST PEACE PROCESS

---------------------------------------------

2. (C) THE VATICAN\'S POSITION ON THE MIDDLE EAST PEACE

PROCESS HAS EVOLVED SUBSTANTIALLY OVER THE LAST THREE

YEARS. FOR MANY YEARS, THE VATICAN CALLED FOR THE

"INTERNATIONALIZATION" OF JERUSALEM, BUT IN THE LATE \'90\'S

THE POSITION CHANGED TO THE "INTERNATIONALIZATION" OF

JERUSALEM "INTRAMURA" (WITHIN THE WALLS). MOST RECENTLY,

ON THE ISSUE OF JERUSALEM THE VATICAN MOVED SUBSTANTIALLY

IN A DIRECTION WHICH CAN BE SEEN AS CONSTRUCTIVE. IT HAS

DROPPED THE CALL FOR INTERNATIONALIZING ANY PART OF

JERUSALEM AND IS NOW ASKING THAT THE SHRINES OF THE THREE

RELIGIONS PRESERVE THEIR UNIQUE AND SACRED CHARACTER

THROUGH "INTERNATIONAL GUARANTEES", IN SUCH A WAY THAT IN

THE FUTURE "NONE OF THE PARTIES WILL BE ABLE TO CLAIM FOR

ITSELF EXCLUSIVE CONTROL OF THESE SACRED PARTS OF THE

CITY". SOVEREIGNTY OVER THE CITY WOULD BE A MATTER FOR THE

ISRAELIS AND THE PALESTINIANS TO NEGOTIATE.

3. (C) DESPITE A MORE CONSTRUCTIVE STAND ON THE FUTURE OF

JERUSALEM, THE VATICAN\'S TILT TOWARD THE PALESTINIAN SIDE

OF THE CONFLICT HAS BEEN MORE EVIDENT IN THE WAKE OF THE

MOST RECENT VIOLENCE. THERE ARE A NUMBER OF POSSIBLE

EXPLANATIONS TO THE INCREASE IN OVERT SYMPATHY FOR THE

PALESTINIAN CAUSE. IT REFLECTS IN LARGE PART THE VATICAN\'S

NEED TO MIND THE INTERESTS OF THE PALESTINIAN CHRISTIANS.

ONE OF THE VATICAN\'S WORST FEARS IS A JERUSALEM DEVOID OF

CHRISTIANS. THE VATICAN HAS BEEN ALSO TELLING EMBASSY
