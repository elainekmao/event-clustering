SUBJECT: TERRORISM FINANCE: ARABIC TRANSLITERATIONS

REF: STATE 190991

1. This is an action request: Post received the

following diplomatic note from the ministry of foreign

affairs requesting that embassy provide the Arabic

spellings of the names contained in the most recent

list of names added to E.O. 13224 (reftel). Post would

appreciate guidance on how to respond.

2. Begin text of diplomatic note.

THE HASHEMITE KINGDOM OF JORDAN

Ministry of Foreign Affairs

Ref. No.: Waw Meem/United States/1/2179

Date: October 3, 2002

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs presents its

compliments to the Embassy of the United States of

America in Amman. In reference to the Embassy

Diplomatic Note No. 1483 dated October 2, 2002

regarding the addition of four individuals to the

United States\' list that are subject to the American

President\'s decree No. 13224. The Ministry would like

to inform the Embassy that the concerned names were not

listed in the translation of the above mentioned

Embassy Diplomatic Note.

For the sake of accuracy and to avoid any problem, the

Ministry hopes that the Embassy supply it with the

concerned names in Arabic language to be able to take

the necessary procedures.

The Ministry takes this opportunity to renew to the

Embassy its highest consideration.

THE HASHEMITE KINGDOM OF JORDAN

Ministry of Foreign Affairs

End text.

Gnehm
