MALDIVES GLOBE REQUEST

INDIA AND OSLO CONFERENCE

-------------------------

1. (C) DCM met with Mohan Kumar, the Indian Deputy High

Commissioner, today. Out of the blue, Kumar said the GoI

was not planning to participate in the Oslo donors

conference. Kumar linked the Indian stance to their

reluctance to have anything to do with the LTTE. He

indicated that high-levels had reviewed the issue and

concluded that possible photos of high-level Indian

officials interacting with the LTTE would not be

acceptable back home. Kumar, who knew that D was

planning on attending, urged the U.S. to take a

low-key approach at the event, so as not to allow the

LTTE to benefit from it. We said that was our plan. He

added that he had met with the British DCM yesterday and

urged the UK not to attend, but the Brits have already

accepted. Kumar added that the GoI was not happy that the

GSL had agreed to funnel donor assistance to a Joint Task

Force made up of government and LTTE representatives.

2. (SBU) This is all news to us. Although India is not

technically a donor, this was the first we had heard of

any Indian reluctance to participate in Oslo. Kumar

seems like a pretty solid source. Had you heard any of

this? (POL)
