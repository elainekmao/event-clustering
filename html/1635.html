POPE REPLIES TO UKRAINIAN ORTHODOX PATRIARCH

--------------------------------------------

2. VATICAN SPOKESMAN JOAQUIN NAVARRO-VALLS ANNOUNCED APRIL

10 THAT POPE JOHN PAUL II HAS WRITTEN TO UKRAINIAN ORTHODOX

PATRIARCH VLADIMIR OF KIEV. THE SPOKESMAN DID NOT DIVULGE

THE CONTENTS OF THE PRIVATE LETTER. VATICAN SOURCES SAY IT

STRESSED THE ECUMENICAL NATURE OF THE PONTIFF\'S VISIT TO

UKRAINE THIS SUMMER. CARDINAL ROBERTO TUCCI, THE OUTGOING

DIRECTOR OF PAPAL TRAVEL, DELIVERED THE LETTER TO THE

ORTHODOX PATRIARCH. TUCCI ALSO DELIVERED AN INVITATION FOR

THE PATRIARCH TO ATTEND THE VATICAN\'S COMMEMORATION OF THE

LAST HOURS OF THE LIFE OF JESUS CHRIST (STATIONS OF THE

CROSS) AT ROME\'S COLISEUM ON APRIL 13. PATRIARCH VLADIMIR

WROTE TO THE POPE JANUARY 22 ON BEHALF OF THE 42 UKRAINIAN

ORTHODOX BISHOPS WHO ARE ALIGNED WITH THE PATRIARCHATE OF

MOSCOW. HE URGED THE POPE NOT TO GO THROUGH WITH HIS PLANNED

VISIT, SAYING IT WOULD COMPLICATE ECUMENICAL RELATIONS.

WHILE THE CONTENTS OF THE PATRIARCH\'S LETTER WERE MADE

VATICAN RADIO: REDUCTION IN MEDIUM WAVE TRANSMISSIONS

--------------------------------------------- --------

3. VATICAN RADIO ANNOUNCED APRIL 10 THAT IT WILL REDUCE

THE NUMBER OF ITS MEDIUM WAVE BROADCASTS AFTER A JOINT

VATICAN-ITALIAN COMMISSION DETERMINED THAT THE STATION

VIOLATED ITALY\'S STRICT LIMITS ON ELECTROMAGNETIC RADIATION.

PRELIMINARY MEASUREMENTS TAKEN BY A JOINT TEAM OF

TECHNICIANS FROM VATICAN RADIO AND THE ITALIAN MINISTRIES OF

HEALTH AND THE ENVIRONMENT CONFIRMED THAT SOME TRANSMISSION

LEVELS EXCEEDED BY AS MUCH AS THREE TIMES THE ITALIAN LIMITS

ON ELECTROMAGNETIC EMISSIONS. VATICAN SPOKESMAN JOAQUIN

NAVARRO-VALLS SAID THE RADIO REDUCED SIGNAL STRENGTH BY 50

PERCENT IN FEBRUARY 2001, AND THAT CUTS IN BROADCASTING

SCHEDULES WOULD BE MADE TO BRING LEVELS INTO LINE WITH

ITALIAN STANDARDS. BROADCASTS VIA FM AND SHORT WAVE (HOW

MOST OF THE WORLD RECEIVES THE RADIO\'S PROGRAMMING) WILL NOT

BE AFFECTED BY THE CHANGES. VATICAN RADIO POINTED OUT THAT

ITS TRANSMISSIONS CONFORM TO INTERNATIONAL STANDARDS ON

ELECTROMAGNETIC RADIATION, WHICH HAVE BEEN ADOPTED BY 14

OTHER EUROPEAN UNION COUNTRIES. ITALY PASSED MORE STRINGENT

TECHNOLOGY TO PROPAGATE ITS MESSAGE. END COMMENT).

CHANGES TO POPE\'S EASTER PROGRAM

--------------------------------

4. THE VATICAN ANNOUNCED APRIL 10 THAT POPE JOHN PAUL II

WILL NOT PARTICIPATE FULLY IN AN APRIL 13 COMMEMORATION OF

THE LAST HOURS OF THE LIFE OF JESUS CHRIST (STATIONS OF THE

CROSS) AT ROME\'S COLISEUM. THE INTERNATIONALLY TELEVISED

EVENT IS A HIGHLIGHT OF THE VATICAN\'S EASTER WEEKEND

PROGRAM. INSTEAD OF WALKING AROUND THE COLISEUM, THE POPE

WILL REMAIN KNEELING THROUGHOUT THE CEREMONY. HE WILL CARRY

A LIGHT WOODEN CROSS FOR THE FINAL FEW METERS OF THE

PROCESSION AND THEN DELIVER HIS CUSTOMARY MESSAGE AT THE

CONCLUSION OF THE SERVICE. THE CHANGE IN FORMAT IS A

CONCESSION TO THE POPE\'S PHYSICAL LIMITATIONS.

FROM AN ANNUAL WORLDWIDE COLLECTION IN CATHOLIC PARISHES AND

VATICAN PEEVED BY EUROPEAN PARLIAMENT CENSURE

---------------------------------------------

6. THE VATICAN HAS EXPRESSED "DISAPPOINTMENT" OVER A

EUROPEAN PARLIAMENT RESOLUTION CONDEMNING IT FOR ALLEGED

INACTION IN THE FACE OF REPORTS OF SEXUAL ABUSE OF NUNS BY

PRIESTS. (REFTEL). THE VATICAN\'S OBSERVER AT THE PARLIAMENT,

MONSIGNOR PAUL GALLAGHER, SAID THE VATICAN SHARED THE

EUROPEAN BODY\'S CONCERN ABOUT VIOLENCE AGAINST WOMEN, AND IN

PARTICULAR CATHOLIC NUNS. HE OPINED THE RESOLUTION REFLECTED

ANTI-VATICAN SENTIMENTS IN SOME QUARTERS OVER ABORTION AND

CONTRACEPTION POLICY CONFLICTS. GALLAGHER NOTED THAT LESS

THAN 20 PERCENT OF THE PARLIAMENT\'S MEMBERS TOOK PART IN THE

TAKEN SERIOUSLY."

VATICAN CABINET LEVEL APPOINTMENTS

----------------------------------

7. THE VATICAN HAS ANNOUNCED TWO SENIOR CABINET LEVEL

APPOINTMENTS. CARDINAL CRESCENZIO SEPE IS TO TAKE THE REINS

OF THE VATICAN\'S POWERFUL MISSIONARY OFFICE, THE

CONGREGATION FOR THE EVANGELIZATION OF PEOPLES, AND

ARCHBISHOP ANTONIO MARIA VEGLIO BECOMES SECRETARY (NUMBER 2

POSITION) OF THE CONGREGATION FOR THE ORIENTAL CHURCHES.

CARDINAL SEPE WAS THE BRAINS AND THE ENERGY BEHIND THE

VATICAN\'S SUCCESSFUL MILLENNIUM YEAR CELEBRATIONS. HE IS A

CHARISMATIC FIGURE AND MEDIA-SAVVY. HIS CONSIDERABLE

ORGANIZATIONAL AND FUND-RAISING ABILITY SHOULD RESULT IN THE

POWER CONGREGATION FOR THE EVANGELIZATION OF PEOPLES

ASSUMING AN EVEN HIGHER PROFILE IN THE VATICAN GOVERNMENT.

(NOTE: IT IS NO SECRET THAT SEPE IS A PAPAL FAVORITE AND HAS

BEEN REWARDED FOR HIS Y2K ACCOMPLISHMENTS. WHEN PAPAL

ADVISORS HEARD THAT SEPE WAS TO BE CREATED A CARDINAL

FEBRUARY LAST, THEY COMMENTED THAT HE WAS TOO YOUNG - HE

TURNS 58 THIS YEAR. POPE JOHN PAUL II REPLIED, "I BECAME

POPE AT THE SAME AGE!" END NOTE). ARCHBISHOP VEGLIO IS

PRESENTLY THE VATICAN\'S AMBASSADOR TO LEBANON AND KUWAIT,

AND THE POPE\'S REPRESENTATIVE TO THE CATHOLIC COMMUNITIES ON
