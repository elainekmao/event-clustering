SUBJECT: KICT: IRAN-U.S. CLAIMS TRIBUNAL: REQUEST FOR

FIRST QUARTER, 2003/2004, BUDGET CONTRIBUTION

1. THIS IS AN ACTION MESSAGE. PLEASE SEE PARAGRAPH TWO.

2. We have received from Secretary-General Pinto of the

Iran-U.S. Claims Tribunal the request for our First Quarter,

2003/2004, budget contribution of USDOLS 625,729.00. Iran

received in parallel an identical request. Please note that

the check must reach the Office of the Legal Counselor at

Embassy The Hague for delivery to the Tribunal by July 15,

2003.

3. TEXT OF LETTER AND ATTACHMENT FOLLOWS:

THE HAGUE

Dear Mr. Johnson

Budget 2003/2004 - Contribution for First Quarter of the

Financial Year 2003/2004

Please refer to the decision of the Tribunal at its 137th

meeting on 4 November 2002, adopting the Tribunal\'s Budget

for the Financial Year 1 July 2003 - 30 June 2004 in an

amount of US$ 5,005,832.

In accordance with the Tribunal\'s decision, I have the honour

to request the two Governments to contribute in equal shares

that amount of the total which would be required for the

First Quarter of the said Financial Year, i.e. US$ 1,251,458,

in equal shares, each share being US$ 625,729.00.

Accordingly I request that your Government remit the amount

of US$ 625,729.00 to reach the Tribunal no later than 15 July

2003.

Estimates for the First Quarter of Financial Year 2003/2004

are enclosed for your information.

Yours sincerely,

/s/

M.C.W. Pinto

cc. Agent of the Islamic Republic of Iran

//End text of letter//
