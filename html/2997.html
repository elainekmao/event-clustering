SUBJECT: DEMARCHE TO JORDAN ON MCLURG CANDIDACY FOR ACABQ

REF: SECSTATE 94548

Classified By: PolCouns Doug Silliman for reasons 1.5 (B) and (D)

1. (C) Ambassador covered ref points on the candidacy of

Susan McLurg for the UN Advisory Committee for Administrative

and Budgetary Questions (ACABQ) May 20 with Foreign Minister

Marwan Muasher, emphasizing the importance of continued U.S.

participation on the ACABQ to ensure Congressional support

for payment of UN assessments. PolCouns separately presented

FonMin Muasher\'s personal assistant Ali al-Ayed with copies

of ref talking points and Ms. McLurg\'s CV. Both Muasher and

Ayed indicated that Jordan will not make a final decision on

its vote until much closer to the UNGA, but said McLurg is

obviously a strong candidate for reelection.
