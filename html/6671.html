SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION: WAR IN IRAQ, IRAQI CIVILIAN

CASUALTIES, AND ABU DHABI TV WAR COVERAGE

1. SUMMARY: THE WAR IN IRAQ AND IRAQI CIVILIAN CASUALTIES IN

BASRA AND HALABJA DOMINATED COVERAGE IN UAE PRINT AND ELECTRONIC

MEDIA. AN "AL ITTIHAD" EDITORIAL BLAMED THE IRAQI REGIME FOR THE

WAR, AND A SAME-DAY ARTICLE REPORTED UAE FEDERAL NATIONAL COUNCIL

SPEAKER AL KINDI AS SAYING THE SAME. AN "AKHBAR AL ARAB"

EDITORIAL ASSERTED THAT THE DURATION AND INTENSITY OF THE WAR

SURPRISED AMERICAN OFFICIALS. A "GULF NEWS" EDITORIAL COMMENTED

ON U.S. PLANS FOR A POST-WAR IRAQI GOVERNMENT AND URGED UN

ASSISTANCE. AN "AL BAYAN" EDITORIAL CLAIMED THE WORLD IS OPPOSED

TO THE WAR. END SUMMARY.

2. HEADLINES FOR 3/23 INCLUDE:

--"AL KHALEEJ," "GULF TODAY," AND "AL BAYAN" CARRIED FRONT PAGE

COLOR PHOTOGRAPHS OF WOUNDED IRAQI CIVILIANS. "AL KHALEEJ" AND

"GULF TODAY" ALSO CARRIED A FULL INTERNAL PAGE OF SIMILAR

PHOTOGRAPHS.

--"HUNDREDS IRAQIS WOUNDED AND KILLED BY AMERICAN WEAPONS OF

DESTRUCTION," "DAY AND NIGHT BOMBING OF BAGHDAD AND ATTACKS ON

BASRA, TIKRIT, KHOURMAL, AND HALABJA" AND SUB HEADLINES:

"ISRAELI WEAPONS USED IN ATTACKS" AND "SHOCK AND AWE ORIGINALLY A

JEWISH IDEA WITH AN AMERICAN IMPLEMENTATION." (AL KHALEEJ)

--"BAGHDAD IS BURNING" AND "IMAGES OF IRAQ FUEL ARAB AND WORLD

FURY." (GULF TODAY)

--"BLOCKS ON ROAD TO BAGHDAD," "WORLD PROTESTS WAR ON IRAQ," AND

"IRAQIS WELCOMING TROOPS - FRANKS." (GULF NEWS)

--"ALLIES CLAIM BIG GAINS," AND "LIFE IN UAE NORMAL, SAY MOHAMMED

(BIN RASHID AL MAKTOUM, CROWN PRINCE OF DUBAI AND MINISTER OF

DEFENSE)." (KHALEEJ TIMES)

--"COALITION FORCES DRAWING CLOSER TO BAGHDAD" AND "FORCES

CONTROL NASIRIYYAH AND UM QASR AND ENCIRCLE BASRA." (AL ITTIHAD)

--"AMERICAN ATTACKS BURN BAGHDAD" AND "HUNDREDS OF CIVILIAN

CASUALTIES, INCLUDING 57 IN HALABJA." (AL BAYAN)

3. ABU DHABI TV\'S LIVE COVERAGE OF THE WAR CENTERS IN BAGHDAD

WITH ANALYSIS AND COMMENTARY FROM THE ABU DHABI TV NEWS CENTER.

ABU DHABI TV CONTACTS REPORT THAT ADTV\'S FOOTAGE FROM BAGHDAD AND

ELSEWHERE IS CURRENTLY USED IN OVER 160 COUNTRIES. ABU DHABI TV

DID NOT/NOT CARRY FOOTAGE OF THE CIVILIAN CASUALTIES IN BASRA AND

HALABJA AS ON AL JAZEERA. ALSO, AL JAZEERA AND ABU DHABI TV WERE

THE ONLY ARAB MEDIA TO ASK QUESTIONS TO GEN. TOMMY FRANKS DURING

HIS 3/22 PRESS BRIEFING IN DOHA.

4. ON 3/23, ABU DHABI-BASED SEMI-GOVERNMENT "AL ITTIHAD"

REPORTED UAE FEDERAL NATIONAL COUNCIL (FNC) SPEAKER SAEED AL

KINDI AS SAYING "THIS SITUATION OF WAR AND INSTABILITY IN THE

WORLD WAS CREATED BY THE IRAQI REGIME WHO DID NOT RESPOND TO

SHEIKH ZAYED\'S INITIATIVE."

5. NOTING THE BEST SOLUTION FOR THE CURRENT CRISIS, "AL ITTIHAD"

EDITORIALIZED ON 3/23:

"... BLIND, DEAF, AND PROUD, THE RULERS OF BAGHDAD JUST IGNORE

ALL ADVICE GIVEN TO THEM BY THE DEVOTED PEOPLE OF THE ARAB

NATION. THEY WERE WARNED THAT THE CONSEQUENCES OF THEIR ACTIONS

COULD LEAD TO A CRISIS. THEY WERE ALSO ASKED TO BE WISE IN ORDER

TO SOLVE THIS CRISIS, WHICH WAS A RESULT OF YEARS OF THEIR OWN

ACTIONS ... WE HOPE THEY LEAVE NOW WHILE THEY STILL HAVE THEIR

DIGNITY AND BEFORE THEY ARE FACED AND SURPRISED WITH THE

HUMILIATION OF IMPRISONMENT, WHICH AWAITS THE DEFEATED IN ANY

WAR. WE HOPE THEY LEAVE NOW AND ABANDON THE RUINS OF THEIR

PALACES AND THE HEADQUARTERS OF THEIR STUBBORNNESS AND CRUELTY."

6. COMMENTING ON THE SURPRISING DURATION AND INTENSITY OF THE

WAR, ABU DHABI-BASED PAN-ARAB DAILY "AKHBAR AL ARAB"

EDITORIALIZED ON 3/23:

"... AMERICAN OFFICIALS CONFESSED THAT THIS IS GOING TO BE A HARD

AND LONG WAR. THIS WAS ONLY AFTER THE THIRD DAY OF WAR, WHEN

AMERICANS WERE FACED WITH A NUMBER OF SURPRISES, SUCH AS

UNANTICIPATED RESISTANCE IN SOME AREAS. THIS INDICATES THAT THIS

WAR WILL BE LONGER AND LOSSES WILL EXCEED WASHINGTON\'S

EXPECTATIONS ..."

7. NOTING WORLDWIDE OPPOSITION TO THE WAR, DUBAI-BASED BUSINESS-

ORIENTED "AL BAYAN" EDITORIALIZED ON 3/23:

"... WAR IS ALWAYS GOING TO BE HATED, AND WE ARABS DO NOT PREFER

IT (WAR) AS A SOLUTION TO ANY CRISIS. WHILE WATCHING THIS

CHAPTER OF THE WAR, OUR HEARTS ARE WITH OUR PEOPLE IN IRAQ, WE

ARE ALL PRAYING TO GOD THAT THE CHILDREN, WOMEN AND MEN OF THE

LAND OF MESOPOTAMIA WILL BE SAFE. THIS IS THE LAND OF A PATIENT

PEOPLE WHO HAVE SUFFERED MORE THAN ANY NATION IN OUR REGION HAS

EVER SUFFERED ..."

8. DESCRIBING THE WAR AS ILLEGITIMATE, SHARJAH-BASED PAN-ARAB

DAILY "AL-KHALEEJ" EDITORIALIZED ON 3/23:

"... IF THE U.S. SUCCEEDS IN IRAQ, FRANCE AND RUSSIA EXPECT THAT

IT WILL CREATE A NEW INTERNATIONAL SYSTEM THAT HAS NOTHING TO DO

WITH INTERNATIONAL LEGITIMACY OR THAT WILL PLACE INTERNATIONAL

LEGITIMACY UNDER ITS (U.S.) CONTROL. U.S. WILL CARRY THIS (WAR)

OUT IN ORDER TO BECOME THE ONLY POWER IN THE WORLD THAT CONTROLS

ITS POLITICAL AND ECONOMIC DESTINY AND SECURITY. THIS WAR IS

ILLEGITIMATE AND WAS STARTED WITHOUT SECURITY COUNCIL SUPPORT AND

AGAINST THE WILL OF THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY, AND ITS

CONSEQUENCES WILL DEFINITELY BE THE SAME ..."

9. ARGUING THE U.S. ADMINISTRATION HAS NOT LAID OUT A CLEAR POST-

WAR STRATEGY FOR IRAQ, DUBAI-BASED ENGLISH-LANGUAGE DAILY "GULF

NEWS" EDITOIALIZED ON 3/23:

"... THE U.S. HAS NOT FORWARDED ANY DETAILS ON ITS PLANS ONCE IT

TAKES CONTROL OF ALL IRAQI CITIES. THIS BRINGS US TO QUESTION

THE FORM OF GOVERNMENT THAT WILL RULE IRAQ WHEN THIS TAKES PLACE.

IT IS NOT CLEAR WHETHER IT WOULD BE A GOVERNMENT COMPRISING THE

IRAQI OPPOSITION OR WHETHER WASHINGTON WILL ASSIGN AN AMERICAN

GENERAL OR COMMANDER TO GOVERN IRAQ. IF THE LATTER IS AMERICA\'S

INTENTION, THE EXECUTION OF THE ACTION IS WITHOUT A DOUBT AN

OCCUPATION OF THE COUNTRY RATHER THAN \'LIBERATION\' AS THE

AMERICANS HAVE CALLED IT ... THE UN IS THE ONLY BODY FULLY

CAPABLE OF OVERSEEING THE PROCESS OF REBUILDING A COUNTRY THAT

HAS BEEN TORN BY WAR AND CRIPPLED BY ECONOMIC SANCTIONS ... THE

TIME HAS COME AT LAST FOR THE ORGANIZATION TO BECOME ENGAGED IN

THE REBUILDING OF A NEW IRAQ - ONE THAT IS GOVERNED BY IRAQIS AND

CATERS TO THE PEOPLE OF IRAQ."
