SUBJECT: POPE TELLS ARAFAT TO CONTAIN VIOLENCE

CLASSIFIED BY AMBASSADOR R.J. NICHOLSON FOR REASONS 1.5

(A) AND (D).

1. (C) DCM AND POLOFF CALLED ON VATICAN DEPUTY FOREIGN

MINISTER CELESTINO MIGLIORE, OCTOBER 31. THE PURPOSE OF

THE CALL WAS TO DISCUSS ON GOING EMBASSY ANTI-TRAFFICKING

PROJECTS (SEPTEL) AND PALESTINIAN AUTHORITY CHAIR YASSAR

ARAFAT\'S OCTOBER 30 MEETING WITH THE POPE.

2. (C) MIGLIORE SAID THAT AT THIS 11TH VISIT WITH THE

POPE (HE JOKED ABOUT THE FREQUENCY WHICH ARAFAT COMES TO

THE VATICAN), NOTHING NEW WAS DISCUSSED. MIGLIORE DID

SAY THAT THE POPE TRIED TO IMPRESS UPON ARAFAT THE NEED

FOR HIM PERSONALLY TO CONTROL THE PALESTINIAN MILITANTS

AND TO END THE VIOLENCE. MIGLIORE ALSO VOLUNTEERED THE

VATICAN VIEW THAT DESPITE SUGGESTIONS ARAFAT IS LOSING

HIS GRIP AND CANNOT CONTROL THE MILITANTS, ARAFAT, IN

FACT, IS STILL "POWERFUL" AND CAN CONTROL THE MILITANTS

"WHEN HE WANTS TO".

3. (C) COMMENT: THIS IS THE FIRST TIME THAT THE VATICAN

HAS STRESSED TO THE MISSION ITS URGING OF THE

PALESTINIANS TO END THE VIOLENCE, AS OPPOSED TO ITS

SYMPATHY FOR THE SUFFERING OF THE PALESTINIANS IN FACE OF

ISRAELI ACTIONS. THIS COULD BE A SUBTLE SHIFT ON THE

PART OF THE VATICAN INDICATING A MORE BALANCED VIEW.

CORROBORATING THIS SUPPOSITION IS A REPORT GIVEN TO DCM

BY HIS ISRAELI COUNTERPART, ZVI TAL (PROTECT). ACCORDING

TO TAL, THE PAPAL NUNCIO IN ISRAEL WAS RECENTLY

REPRIMANDED BY THE VATICAN FOR TAKING PART IN A

PALESTINIAN ORGANIZED PEACE MARCH. NICHOLSON
