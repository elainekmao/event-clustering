SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION POWELL EVIDENCE AGAINST IRAQ;

HARARE

1. Secretary of State Colin Powell\'s February 5

presentation to the UN Security Council was barely

visible in the Zimbabwean media. An AFP report

published on page 4 of the government-owned "Herald"

and a wire service-based report read on the government-

owned Zimbabwean Broadcasting Corporation morning

television news program, are the sum total of local

mention of the Secretary\'s presentation. There is no

editorial or op-ed reaction to the presentation.

SULLIVAN
