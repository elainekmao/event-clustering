2. BEGINNING OF INFORMAL EMBASSY TRANSLATION:

THE MINISTRY OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS (MFA) PRESENTS ITS

COMPLIMENTS TO THE U.S. EMBASSY AND HAS THE HONOR TO REFER

TO THE ATTACHED MOU, SIGNED IN ROME BY THE ITALIAN SPACE

AGENCY (ASI) AND NASA ON OCTOBER 9, 1997, WHICH PROVIDES FOR

THE BILATERAL COOPERATION PROGRAM BETWEEN ASI AND NASA FOR

THE PROJECT, DEVELOPMENT, OPERATION, AND USE OF THREE MINI

PRESSURIZED LOGISTICS MODULES FOR THE INTERNATIONAL SPACE

STATION (ISS).

THE DRAFT OF THIS LETTER BECAME INDISPENSABLE CONSIDERING

THE TECHNICAL MODIFICATIONS MADE, DURING THE 90\'S, TO THE

ISS, IN WHICH ITALY PARTICIPATES BOTH AT BILATERAL LEVEL,

HAVING SIGNED AN MOU WITH THE U.S. ON DECEMBER 6, 1991, AND

AT MULTILATERAL LEVEL, BEING PART OF THE INTERGOVERNMENTAL

AGREEMENT, WHICH PROVIDES FOR THE ISS COOPERATION, SIGNED IN

WASHINGTON ON JANUARY 29, 1998.

TAKING INTO CONSIDERATION THE NEED TO UPDATE THE BEFORE-

MENTIONED 1991 MOU AND THE 1997 MOU, WHICH, ACCORDING TO

ARTICLE 2.1, IS JURIDICALLY UNDER THE 1998 INTERGOVERNMENTAL

AGREEMENT, RATIFIED BY ITALY BY LAW 418/2000, AND WHICH DOES

NOT DEROGATE IN ANY WAY ITS RULES, THE MFA HAS THE HONOR TO

PROPOSE THAT, IF THE U.S. GOVERNMENT AGREES, THIS NOTE, TO
