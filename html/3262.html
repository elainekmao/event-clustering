SUBJECT: MEDIA REPORT REPORTER ARRESTED UNDER AIPPA; HARARE

Independent "Daily News" reporter, Chris Gande, was on

July 4 charged under the Access to Information and

Protection of Privacy Act (AIPPA) for allegedly writing a

false story. Gande becomes the 13th reporter to be charged

under the Act, enacted in March this year. Last week the

"Daily News" carried an article by Gande in which he

reported that the government had "snubbed" the family of

the late vice-president Joshua Nkomo by "neither inviting

nor informing them" of the preparations for a state-funded

commemorative gala in his honor. Gande is standing by his

story as a true reflection of what Nkomo\'s eldest daughter,

Thandi, told him in an interview on the eve of the memorial

revelry held in Mutare. Johanna MaFuyana, Nkomo\'s widow,

attended the gala that was televised live on June 29. She

was, however, flown to Mutare on a military helicopter on

the day the "Daily News" story was published (June 28).
