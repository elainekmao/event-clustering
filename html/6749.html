SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION: WAR IN IRAQ POWS ABU DHABI TV

1. SUMMARY: THE WAR IN IRAQ, THE U.S. BOMBING OF THE AL

AATHAMIA AREA OF BAGHDAD, AND UAE HUMANITARIAN AID FOR THE IRAQI

PEOPLE DOMINATED COVERAGE IN UAE PRINT AND ELECTRONIC MEDIA.

UNDER THE HEADLINE, "A CONVENTION FOR ALL," A "GULF NEWS"

EDITORIAL CRITICIZED U.S. TREATMENT OF POWS. AN "AL KHALEEJ"

EDITORIAL ASSERTS THE U.S. VALUES OIL MORE THAN PEOPLE. IN AN

"AL KHALEEJ" OP-ED, DR. ABDULKHALEQ ABDULLA LISTS THE OPTIONS

AVAILABLE FOR THE IRAQI PEOPLE IN THEIR WAR AGAINST THE U.S. END

SUMMARY.

2. UNLIKE AL JAZEERA, ABU DHABI TV AND AL ARABIYYAH SATELLITE TV

DID NOT BROADCAST VIDEO FOOTAGE OF U.S. TROOPS HANDCUFFING

SURRENDERING IRAQI SOLDIERS. SIMILARLY, ABU DHABI TV BROADCAST

ONLY BLURRED IMAGES OF U.S. POWS WITHOUT SOUND. DURING A 3/24

INTERVIEW INITIATED WITH ABU DHABI TV, JASSEM AL SAQR, KUWAITI

PARLIAMENTARIAN AND FORMER JOURNALIST, COMPLAINED THAT ARAB

SATELLITE TV COVERAGE PERPETUATES THE PERCEPTION THAT ARABS BLAME

KUWAIT FOR THE WAR. AL SAQR THEN ACCUSED THE MAJORITY OF ARAB

SATELLITE TV STATIONS OF SUPPORTING ONLY IRAQ.

3. DUBAI TV AND RADIO WILL AIR A 12-HOUR TELETHON ENTITLED "OUR

PEOPLE IN IRAQ." TO RAISE FUNDS FOR HUMANITARIAN AID. THE

PROGRAM WILL ACCEPT TELEPHONE CALLS FROM CONCERNED LISTENERS TO

VOICE SUPPORT FOR THE IRAQI PEOPLE AND PLEDGE DONATIONS.

4. UNDER THE HEADLINE "A CONVENTION FOR ALL," DUBAI-BASED

ENGLISH-LANGUAGE DAILY "GULF NEWS" EDITORIALIZED 3/25:

"IT SEEMS THAT THE GEORGE W. BUSH CAMP SUFFERS FROM A MASSIVE

MEMORY LAPSE IN DEMANDING THAT ITS CAPTURED SOLDIERS BE TREATED

IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE GENEVA CONVENTION ... THE U.S. STAND IS

IRONIC IN THAT ITS PAST ACTIONS IN RELATION TO POWS DO NOT

MEASURE UP TO THOSE HIGH STANDARDS OF HUMAN RIGHTS AND DIGNITY

... AMONG A CATALOGUE OF ABUSES IN THIS REGARD IS THE TREATMENT

METED OUT IN 2001 TO PRISONERS HELD AT MAZAR-E-SHARIF IN

AFGHANISTAN ... ANOTHER EXAMPLE IS THAT OF THE MORE THAN 650

DETAINERS HELD AT GUANTANAMO BAY. THE FIRST PICTURES SHOWED THEM

BLINDFOLDED, SHACKLED, AND CHAINED IN SIX-BY-EIGHT-FOOT CAGES

EXPOSED TO THE ELEMENTS. RUMSFELD HAD SAID THEN THAT HE DID NOT

\'FEEL THE SLIGHTEST CONCERN\' FOR THE PRISONERS\' TREATMENT."

5. CLAIMING THE U.S. BELIEVES OIL IS MORE IMPORTANT THAN PEOPLE,

SHARJAH-BASED PAN-ARAB DAILY "AL-KHALEEJ" EDITORIALIZED 3/25:

"... (THE U.S. BELIEVES THAT) OIL IS MORE IMPORTANT THAN PEOPLE,

AND CHANGING THE WHOLE REGION WILL BE AT THE EXPENSE OF THE

PEOPLE AND THEIR RIGHTS. THIS IS AN OCCUPIER\'S POLICY, WHICH IS

RENEWED AUTOMATICALLY OVER THE DECADES. THIS POLICY USES KILLING

AND DESTRUCTION AS ITS TOOLS, IN ADDITION TO CHEATING, DECEIVING,

AND HYPOCRISY ... ROBBING THE IRAQI PEOPLE OF THE RESOURCES OF

IRAQ IS THE MAIN GOAL, WHICH IS THE FIRST OF THE PLANNED

OBJECTIVES FOR OCCUPYING IRAQ. AT THE SAME TIME, THE REGION IS

FACING THE CONTINUED OCCUPATION OF PALESTINE SINCE 1948 ..."

6. IN AN "AL-KHALEEJ" OP-ED ON IRAQ, UAE COLUMNIST DR.

ABDULKHALEQ ABDULA WROTE 3/25:

"... IT IS POSSIBLE THAT THE LARGE MAJORITY OF IRAQIS WANT SADDAM

TOPPLED BUT NOT BY GEORGE BUSH AND HIS SOLDIERS AND SURELY NOT

WITH WAR, DESTRUCTION, AND KILLING. MOREOVER, THEY DO NOT WANT

THIS THROUGH THE APPOINTMENT OF A MILITARY OR CIVIL GOVERNOR OF

BAGHDAD ... IF THE PEOPLE OF IRAQ DECIDE THAT THEY ARE DEFENDING

IRAQ AND ITS INDEPENDENCE AND CONSIDER THE U.S. TROOPS AS

OCCUPIERS, THEN THE BATTLE WILL BE VERY LONG, AND THE U.S. TROOPS

WILL BE DEFEATED AND FACE LOSSES EXCEEDING ALL EXPECTATIONS. BUT

IF THE IRAQI PEOPLE DECIDE THAT THIS IS A BATTLE FOR THE IRAQI

REGIME AND NOT FOR THE SAKE OF THE COUNTRY, THEN THE WAR WILL NOT

GO ON FOR LONG AND WILL LEAD TO THE FALL OF BAGHDAD IN LESS THAN

A MONTH."

7. CRITICIZING U.S. POLICY FOR THE WAR ON IRAQ AND THE REGION,

UAE COLUMNIST AYSHA SULTAN WROTE IN "AL BAYAN" 3/25:

"... THEY (AMERICANS) WANTED IT TO BE A FAST WAR, WHERE THEY

COULD TRY THEIR NEW WEAPONS OF DESTRUCTION AGAINST THE IRAQI

PEOPLE AND THE REGION. WHAT IS IMPORTANT (FOR THE U.S.) IS TO

TEST THEIR WEAPONS AND OCCUPY IRAQ AS A STARTING POINT FOR THE

REST OF THE NEIGHBORING COUNTRIES IN AN ATTEMPT TO FULFILL THE

DREAM OF PRODUCING COUNTRIES AND ADMINISTRATIONS SUITING AMERICAN

TASTES, SINCE THE ARAB-MUSLIM INCLINATION IS CONSIDERED RETARDED

AND NOT QUALIFIED TO DEAL WITH THE ZIONISTS AND AMERICANS IN A

CIVILIZED MANNER ..."
