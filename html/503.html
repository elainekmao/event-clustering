SUBJECT: GARCIA OPENS HIS PRESIDENTIAL

- CAMPAIGN VOWING NOT TO BE A

- CANDIDATE, DECLARES WAR ON FUJIMORI

1. (C) SUMMARY. AT A MARCH 21 PRESS

CONFERENCE, EX-PRESIDENT ALAN GARCIA CALLED FOR

A NATIONAL FRONT AGAINST FUJIMORI; HE ALSO RULED

CONFIDENTIAL

CONFIDENTIAL

OUT HIS RUNNING FOR PRESIDENT IN 1995. GARCIA

BLASTED ATTEMPTS BY THE FUJIMORI ADMINISTRATION

TO REOPEN CORRUPTION CHARGES AGAINST HIM. FEW

IN LIMA BELIEVE THAT GARCIA WILL NOT RUN FOR

PRESIDENT; IT REMAINS TO BE SEEN HOW MANY ARE

WILLING TO FOLLOW HIS LEAD INTO A NATIONAL

ANTI-FUJIMORI FRONT. END SUMMARY.

REOPENING THE GARCIA CORRUPTION CASE

------------------------------------

2. (U) THE CATALYST FOR GARCIA\'S PRESS

CONFERENCE WAS THE GOP\'S PUBLISHING OF A SUPREME

DECREE AUTHORIZING THE SOLICITOR GENERAL TO

REOPEN THE "ILLICIT ENRICHMENT" CHARGES AGAINST

GARCIA. GARCIA\'S LAWYERS IMMEDIATELY HAD A

LOW-LEVEL JUDGE ISSUE AN INJUNCTION AGAINST

REOPENING THE CASE ON THE GROUNDS OF DOUBLE

JEOPARDY. GARCIA TOLD THE PRESS CONFERENCE THAT

THE SUPREME DECREE WAS A "JUDICIAL MONSTROSITY"

AND THAT IT WAS THE ANSWER BY THE FUJIMORI

ADMINISTRATION TO GARCIA\'S CRITICISM OF GOP

POLICIES. GARCIA DECLARED IT WAS THE TIME TO

BREAK THE INFORMAL AGREEMENT BETWEEN THE

PRESIDENT AND APRA AGAINST CRITICIZING THE

ADMINISTRATION. HE BLAMED FUJIMORI AND SOME OF

HIS "MILITARY ADVISORS" FOR USING EVEN THE TAX

OFFICE TO PERSECUTE HIM.

GARCIA CALLS FOR A NATIONAL ANTI-FUJIMORI FRONT

--------------------------------------------- --

4. (U) GARCIA DECLARED THAT FOR THE LAST 18

MONTHS THE PERUVIAN PEOPLE HAD SUFFERED A PLAGUE

OF "VICIOUS CAPITALISM" THAT HAD NOT BORN

CONCRETE RESULTS. HE CHARACTERIZED FUJIMORI\'S

TRIP TO JAPAN AS A COMPLETE FAILURE SINCE THE

PRESIDENT RETURNED WITH "ONLY USDOLS 20 MILLION

IN DONATIONS." GARCIA CHARGED THAT BUSINESSMEN

WHO THAT TRAVEL WITH FUJIMORI DO SO ONLY TO TAKE

ADVANTAGE OF MONEY TO BE MADE OFF OF ASSISTANCE

CONTRACTS. GARCIA ADDED THAT THE GULF BETWEEN

FUJIMORI AND THE CONGRESS IS WIDENING AND THAT

EVEN THE PRESIDENT OF THE SENATE CANNOT GET AN

APPOINTMENT TO SEE THE PRESIDENT.

5. (U) REFERRING TO FAILURE OF MOTHERS FROM

THE "GLASS OF MILK" PROGRAM TO SEE A GOP

REPRESENTATIVE, GARCIA ASKED, "WHAT DANGER DO

THESE POOR PERUVIAN MOTHERS REPRESENT WHEN THEY

ONLY ASK FOR BREAD FOR THEIR CHILDREN?" HE

ALLEGED THAT THE PRESIDENT WAS CUT OFF IN THE

PALACE WITHOUT THE SUPPORT OF A STRONG ORGANIZED

PARTY. THE OPPOSITION WAS COMPOSED OF A RIGHT

WHOSE LEADER (MARIO VARGAS LLOSA) HAD LEFT THE

COUNTRY, APRA WHICH HAD JUST LEFT OFFICE, AND A

FRACTURED LEFT. GARCIA STATED THAT THE ONLY WAY

TO OPPOSE FUJIMORI WAS BY FORMING A UNITED FRONT

FROM THESE ELEMENTS TO SUPPORT ECONOMIC

REVITALIZATION AND PACIFICATION.

"I WILL NOT RUN FOR PRESIDENT"

6. (U) GARCIA CLAIMED THAT AN IMPEDIMENT TO A

SUCCESSFUL OPPOSITION TO FUJIMORI WAS THE

CONFLICTING AMBITIONS OF POSSIBLE PRESIDENTIAL

CANDIDATES. THE WAY TO SOLVE THIS

"CANDIDITITIS" WAS FOR POLITICAL LEADERS TO

RENOUNCE RUNNING FOR PRESIDENT. TO SET THE

EXAMPLE HE SAID, "I RENOUNCE BEING A CANDIDATE

FOR THE PRESIDENCY OF THE REPUBLIC IN 1995 TO

PAVE THE WAY FOR THE NATIONAL FRONT."

7. (C) FEW IN LIMA TAKE SERIOUSLY GARCIA\'S VOW

THAT HE WILL NOT BE A PRESIDENTIAL CANDIDATE.

LAST MONTH HE PROCLAIMED THAT HE WAS NOT A

CANDIDATE FOR SECRETARY GENERAL OF THE APRA

PARTY. WITHIN TWO WEEKS HE GRACIOUSLY ACCEPTED

THE JOB AFTER THE APRA CONVENTION INSISTED BY

ACCLAMATION THAT HE AGREE TO ACCEPT THE HONOR.

THE LIKELIHOOD OF HIS ORGANIZING A NATIONAL

FRONT AGAINST FUJIMORI IS SLIM. THE SECRETARY

THAT SHARES THE SUBSTANCE OF MANY OF GARCIA\'S

"HIS PARTY WOULD NEVER COOPERATE WITH APRA." HE

EXPLAINED THAT HE COULD DEAL WITH THE RIGHT

BECAUSE HE KNEW THEIR IDEOLOGICAL POSITION, BUT

COULD NOT COOPERATE WITH APRA BECAUSE THEY WERE

SIMPLE OPPORTUNISTS. THE OTHER PARTIES MAY BE

PLAYING THE SAME SONG, BUT THEY ARE HIGHLY

UNLIKELY TO ACCEPT ALAN GARCIA LEADING THE

BAND. QUAINTON
