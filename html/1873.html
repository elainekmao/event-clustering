1. (C) A/DCM FOLLOWED UP REF B WITH DON MATTEO AFTERNOON

OF AUGUST 9. DON MATTEO TOOK ON BOARD US VIEW THAT UNITA

NEEDED TO DEMONSTRATE COMMITMENT TO LUSAKA BEFORE MOVING

FARTHER. HE WAS CONFIDENT THAT UNITA WOULD BE ABLE TO MAKE

AN UNQUALIFIED STATEMENT IN SUPPORT OF LUSAKA, AND EVEN

THOUGHT THEY WOULD DO SOMETHING ABOUT KIDNAPPING (WHILE NOT

ADMITTING THAT THEY HAD EVER ENGAGED IN IT). DON MATTEO

CAUTIONED, HOWEVER, THAT UNITA WOULD NOT BE PUSHED TOO FAR

DOWN THE ROAD BEFORE IT WOULD INSIST ON SOME RECIPROCAL

MEASURES FROM THE GOA. THIS IS WHY MATTEO BELIEVES IT

WOULD BE USEFUL TO HAVE A "SECRET" MEETING, ONCE UNITA

MAKES THESE INITIAL DECLARATIONS, TO HASH OUT JUST HOW THE

GOA AND UNITA WOULD RECOMMENCE LUSAKA PROVISIONS.

2. (C) MATTEO SAID THAT HE IS NO FAN OF UNITA, BUT THAT HE

WANTED TO WORK WITH THEM ON THIS AND THAT GETTING SOMETHING

GOING MIGHT TAKE TIME. HE PLEDGED TO CONTACT HIS UNITA

INTERLOCUTORS (NOTE: HE SAID HE IS TALKING TO BOTH THE

UNITA REP IN ROME AND THE ONE IN PARIS), AND TO GET BACK TO

A/DCM WEEK OF 8/13. HE ALSO SAID HE WOULD WELCOME A VISIT

BY OUTGOING U.S. AMBASSADOR TO NAMIBIA TO DISCUSS FURTHER.

MERANTE
