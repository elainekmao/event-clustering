SUBJECT: OUR MESSAGE ON BIOTECH: GOI NEEDS TO HEAR IT DIRECT

REF: STATE 42136

CLASSIFIED BY: AMBASSADOR MEL SEMBLER FOR REASONS 1.5 (B AND D)

1. (U) THIS IS AN URGENT ACTION MESSAGE.

2. (C) SUMMARY AND ACTION REQUEST: I NEED YOUR HELP TO KEEP

THE GOI FROM SLIDING INTO THE ANTI-BIOTECH CAMP. SINCE THE

OCTOBER 2001 EXCHANGE BETWEEN THE PRESIDENT AND PRIME

MINISTER AND THE VISIT OF AGRICULTURE SECRETARY VENEMAN IN

NOVEMBER, THE PM HAS NOT MADE GOOD ON HIS PLEDGE TO OPEN

ITALY TO BIOTECHNOLOGY. IN THE MEANTIME, AN EVER BOLDER

MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE HAS DEVISED A CLEVER STRATEGY WHICH

LEADS IN THE DIRECTION OF CLOSING ITALY TO ALL AGRICULTURAL

BIOTECHNOLOGY. MINISTER ALEMANNO\'S WHITE PAPER, DESCRIBED

AND ANALYZED SEPARATELY, IS TO BE INTRODUCED INTO PARLIAMENT

FOR WHAT WILL SURELY BE A HIGHLY PREJUDICIAL DEBATE ABOUT THE

MERITS OF AGRICULTURAL BIOTECHNOLOGY FOR ITALY. IF THE PAPER

IS INTRODUCED IN ITS CURRENT FORM, I BELIEVE THE PRIME

MINISTER WILL BE POLITICALLY HARD-PRESSED TO MOVE ITALY

TOWARD A BIOTECH POLICY FRIENDLIER TO OUR INTERESTS.

ACTION REQUEST: I NEED YOU TO HELP ME SEND A WAKE-UP MESSAGE

TO THE PRIME MINISTER. WE NEED HIM TO PUT BIOTECHNOLOGY NEAR

THE TOP OF HIS AGENDA WITH US FOR A FEW CRITICAL DAYS. I\'M

ASKING EACH OF YOU TO CALL OR WRITE GOI MINISTERS AND SENIOR

ADVISORS AS OUTLINED IN THE GAME PLAN BELOW. OUR GOAL IS TO

BURY ALEMANNO\'S PAPER IN THE PM\'S NATIONAL COMMISSION ON

BIOTECHNOLOGY, AND TO ENSURE THAT FULL GOVERNMENT REVIEW

PRECEDES THE OPEN DEBATE IN PARLIAMENT. WE NEED TO GET OUR

MESSAGE THROUGH BEFORE THE WHITE PAPER\'S INTRODUCTION INTO

PARLIAMENT, WHICH COULD BE AS EARLY AS APRIL 15. END SUMMARY

AND ACTION REQUEST.

3. (C) PRIME MINISTER BERLUSCONI IS PREOCCUPIED WITH MESSY

FIGHTS OVER THE BUDGET AND LABOR REFORM, NOT TO MENTION

FOREIGN POLICY CONCERNS. (HE RETAINS THE FOREIGN MINISTER

PORTFOLIO.) ABSENT HIS DIRECT ENGAGEMENT, OR AT LEAST A

CLEAR SIGNAL FROM HIM, MOST OF THE GOVERNMENT FALLS BACK ON

EXCUSES AND DELAYING TACTICS. THERE\'S ONE EXCEPTION, THOUGH:

AGRICULTURE MINISTER ALEMANNO. HE IS TRYING TO USE HIS

POWER BASE AMONG GREENS AND THE WELL-ORGANIZED, HIGHLY

CHARGED ANTI-BIOTECH FARM ORGANIZATION COLDIRETTI TO LEAPFROG

OVER THE MAJORITY OF ITALIAN STAKEHOLDERS AND THE REST OF THE

GOI AND LOCK ITALY INTO AN ANTI-BIOTECH POSTURE.

4. (C) THERE ARE PLENTY OF PEOPLE WITHIN THE GOI OPPOSED TO

ALEMANNO\'S APPROACH; I BELIEVE THEY CONSTITUTE THE MAJORITY.

BUT THEIR POSITION IS UNCOORDINATED, AND THEY NEED MORE TIME

TO DEVELOP A COHERENT FRONT. WE NEED TO WORK HARD NOW TO BUY

THEM THAT TIME, VIA SOME INTERCESSION FROM THE TOP. TO GIVE

THEM EQUAL HEARING I URGENTLY ASK EACH OF YOU TO CONSIDER THE

FOLLOWING PROPOSED ACTIONS:

FOR SECRETARY VENEMAN: THAT YOU WRITE A LETTER TO MINISTER

ALEMANNO EXPLAINING U.S. CONCERNS WITH THE MINISTRY\'S BIASED

ASSESSMENT OF BIOTECHNOLOGY AND URGING HIM TO BASE HIS

ASSESSMENT OF BIOTECHNOLOGY ON SOUND SCIENTIFIC EVIDENCE. I

WILL PERSONALLY DELIVER YOUR LETTER TO THE MINISTER. EMBASSY

FAS MINISTER-COUNSELOR WILL WORK WITH YOUR OFFICE ON A DRAFT

AND TRANSLATION.

FOR EPA ADMINISTRATOR WHITMAN: THAT YOU MEET WITH MINISTER

OF ENVIRONMENT MATTEOLI AT BANFF ON APRIL 12, AND LET HIM

KNOW THAT BIOTECHNOLOGY IS NEAR THE TOP OF OUR PRESIDENT\'S

AGENDA, AND THAT WE LOOK TO ITALY FOR HELP. EMBASSY EST

COUNSELOR IS WORKING WITH YOUR OFFICE.

FOR U/S LARSON: THAT YOU TALK WITH AMBASSADOR SALLEO BEFORE

APRIL 15, AND SHARE WITH HIM YOUR VIEWS ON BIOTECHNOLOGY, AND

OUR CONCERN THAT ITALY IS ABOUT TO TAKE A SERIOUS WRONG TURN.

SUGGESTED TALKING POINTS TO FOLLOW SEPTEL.

FOR WHITE HOUSE NSC GARY EDSON: YOU KNOW THE PM\'S DIPLOMATIC

COUNSELOR, AMBASSADOR GIANNI CASTELLANETA, FROM G-8 MEETINGS.

I ASK THAT YOU CALL HIM, REMIND HIM OF THE OCTOBER EXCHANGE

BETWEEN THE PRESIDENT AND PM, AND EXPLAIN THAT THE PRIME

MINISTER\'S LEADERSHIP IS NEEDED NOW, MORE THAN EVER, TO

ENSURE THAT ITALIAN BIOTECH POLICY DOES NOT GO OFF THE RAILS.

MY DCM HAS ADDRESSED THIS ISSUE WITH CASTELLANETA, AND WE

KNOW THAT HE SHARED OUR PAPER WITH THE PM. NONETHELESS, IT

WOULD BE USEFUL FOR HIM TO HEAR IT AGAIN FROM YOU. HE\'LL GET

YOUR MESSAGE, AS WELL, TO THE PM.
