SUBJECT: UAEG ATTENDEES AT 58TH UNGA GENERAL DEBATE

On June 28, Poloff delivered reftel demarche to MFA

International Organizations Department Deputy Director

Ghanem Bin Mohammed Al-Mazroui, requesting information by

July 31 on VIP attendees at the 58th UNGA General Debate

head the UAE delegation. Post will forward confirmation of

the UAE delegation head and information on UAEG VIPs

attending the General Debate as soon as it is received.
