SUBJECT: TFIZ01: ITALY NEEDS GUIDANCE ON FIELD HOSPITAL

DEPLOYMENT

REF: A. ROME 1679

B. ROME 1660

CLASSIFIED BY: A/DCM TOM COUNTRYMAN FOR REASONS 1.5 (B) AND (D)

THIS IS AN ACTION REQUEST. PLEASE SEE PARA 4.

1. (C) THE ITALIAN PARLIAMENT, THE PUBLIC, AND THE GOI ARE

EAGER TO SUPPORT A HUMANITARIAN MISSION TO IRAQ. FOR THE

GOI, THE COMMITMENT OF HUMANITARIAN AID -- IMPORTANT IN AND

OF ITSELF -- ALSO PROVIDES THE JUSTIFICATION FOR PUTTING

BOOTS ON THE GROUND IN SUPPORT OF PHASE IV (REF A). WE

THEREFORE HAVE EVERY INTEREST IN HELPING THE ITALIANS SUCCEED

WITH BOTH EFFORTS. THE FOCUS OF THEIR EFFORTS TODAY IS ON

DEPLOYING TO IRAQ THE FIELD HOSPITAL THAT THE USG URGENTLY

REQUESTED OF ITALY.

2. (C) UNFORTUNATELY, THE GOI\'S IRAQ TASK FORCE HAS GOTTEN

AHEAD OF ITSELF IN MOVING AHEAD WITH PLANNING FOR DEPLOYING A

FIELD HOSPITAL TO A SPECIFIC SITE IN BAGHDAD (REF B) WITHOUT

PROPER COORDINATION IN TAMPA. A SIX MEMBER SITE SURVEILLANCE

TEAM LED BY THE DEPUTY HEAD OF THE MFA\'S DEVELOPMENT

ASSISTANCE OFFICE CROSSED INTO IRAQ FROM JORDAN ON APRIL 20.

THE TEAM INFORMED THE EMBASSY IN AMMAN OF ITS TRAVEL PLANS

JUST BEFORE ENTERING, BUT FAILED TO WORK THROUGH EITHER

CENTCOM OR THIS EMBASSY IN ARRANGING THE VISIT. ACCORDING TO

THE MFA, THE TEAM HAS BEEN IN CONTACT WITH (UNIDENTIFIED) USG

OFFICIALS IN BAGHDAD, WHO HAVE INDICATED THAT THE SITE IN

BAGHDAD IS APPROPRIATE IF HIGHER CENTCOM OFFICIALS CONCUR.

WE ARE PRESSING THE MFA TO PROVIDE MORE DETAIL ON THE TEAM\'S

ACTIVITIES AND CONTACTS.

3. (C) MOREOVER, THE TASK FORCE IS STILL HAVING PROBLEMS

WITH THE CONCEPT OF A "SELF-DEPLOYABLE/SELF-PROTECTING" FIELD

HOSPITAL. DEFMIN MARTINO TOLD THE AMBASSADOR TODAY (SEPTEL)

THAT WHILE A 30 MAN-CARABINIERI UNIT WOULD PROVIDE INTERNAL

SECURITY FOR THE HOSPITAL, THE GOI WILL ASK THE U.S. MILITARY

TO SECURE THE SURROUNDING PERIMETER. WE ARE FOLLOWING UP

WITH THE TASK FORCE TO REITERATE THAT 1) CENTCOM HAS THE CALL

ON THE HOSPITAL\'S LOCATION; 2) COORDINATION WITH CENTCOM

SHOULD BE WORKED THROUGH THE ITALIAN LNO, GENERAL SANTINI, IN

TAMPA; AND 3) ITALY SHOULD PROVIDE ALL NEEDED SECURITY, BOTH

INTERNAL AND PERIMETER.

4. (C) ACTION REQUEST: THE GOI, WE WERE TOLD ON APRIL 22

(SEPTEL), MUST STILL SEEK AUTHORIZATION FROM PARLIAMENT FOR

DEPLOYING A BRIGADE-SIZE FORCE TO ASSIST WITH PHASE IV

STABILIZATION EFFORTS. THIS WILL BE AN EASIER SELL FOR THE

GOI IF IT WILL HAVE ALREADY DEPLOYED BY THEN ITS FIELD

HOSPITAL IN A SITE ACCEPTABLE TO CENTCOM. EMBASSY REALIZES

HOW RAPIDLY THE HUMANITARIAN PICTURE ON THE GROUND IS

CHANGING, BUT WE STILL NEED, EITHER FROM SECSTATE OR DIRECTLY

FROM CENTCOM, CONFIRMATION THAT THE GOI-PROPOSED LOCATION

(REF B) IS ACCEPTABLE. IF IT IS NOT, WE REQUEST THAT CENTCOM

EXPLORE THE POSSIBILITY OF SITING THE HOSPITAL NEAR BASRA,

WHERE (WE UNDERSTAND) IT IS ENVISIONED THE BULK OF THE

ITALIAN MILITARY FORCES WILL EVENTUALLY DEPLOY. END ACTION

REQUEST.
