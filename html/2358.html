SUBJECT: UNIFIL MANDATE RENEWAL NOTICE: ITALIAN VIEWS

REF: 01 STATE 216231

CLASSIFIED BY: DCM WILLIAM P. POPE

1. (C) SUMMARY. THE GOI WILL VIEW WITH GREAT INTEREST, AND

SOME CONCERN, PRECISE PLANS FOR RESTRUCTURING/DOWNSIZING OF

UNIFIL. THIS STEMS IN LARGE PART FROM TODAY\'S HEIGHTENED

TENSIONS IN THE REGION, BUT IS ALSO LINKED TO ITALY\'S GENERAL

PREFERENCE FOR EMPLOYING MULTILATERAL PEACEKEEPING FORCES.

ABOVE ALL, THE GOI WISHES TO BE A PLAYER IN THE REGION WHILE

MAINTAINING A SPECIAL RELATIONSHIP WITH THE USG. EARLY AND

CLOSE CONSULTATION WITH ITALY WILL BE THE KEY FACTOR IN

SECURING SUPPORT FOR US VIEWS ON UNIFIL. END SUMMARY.

2. (C) REFTEL SEEKS POST\'S OPINION OF LIKELY ITALIAN VIEWS ON

UNIFIL DOWNSIZING. WHILE ITALY IS AWARE OF UNIFIL\'S

SHORTCOMINGS -- AND WILL THEREFORE LIKELY BACK CONTINUED

RESTRUCTURING -- IT WOULD PROBABLY VIEW WITH CONCERN ANY

CHANGES THAT MIGHT/MIGHT LEAD TO GREATER INSTABILITY.

ACCORDINGLY, THE PRECISE NATURE OF THE RESTRUCTURING WILL BE

KEY, AS WILL ADDRESSING ITS LIKELY IMPACT ON THE SITUATION ON

3. (C) EVEN IF PRESENT TENSIONS IN THE MIDDLE EAST WERE TO

EASE, HOWEVER, ITALY\'S GENERALLY FAVORABLE ATTITUDE TOWARD

MULTILATERAL PEACEKEEPING MISSIONS WOULD BE A FACTOR. SO

WOULD THE GOI\'S DESIRE TO EXERT INFLUENCE IN THE MIDDLE EAST

AND PM BERLUSCONI\'S COMMITMENT TO MAINTAINING WHAT HE VIEWS

AS A SPECIAL RELATIONSHIP WITH WASHINGTON.

4. (C) IN SUM, SECURING AND MAINTAINING ITALIAN SUPPORT FOR

US VIEWS ON UNIFIL WILL TURN IN LARGE PART ON EARLY AND

FREQUENT CONSULTATIONS AT SENIOR LEVELS. SHOULD THESE FAIL

TO BEAR FRUIT, INTERVENTIONS AT THE POLITICAL LEVEL MAY BE

NECESSARY.
