SUBJECT: JORDAN MEDIA REACTION ON PRESIDENT\'S MIDDLE

EAST SPEECH, AMERICAN PRODUCTS BOYCOTT RALLY.

SUMMARY. NEWS REPORTS ABOUT THE PRESIDENT\'S MIDDLE

EAST SPEECH VARIED WIDELY IN THE JORDANIAN PRESS.

WHILE SEMI-OFFICIAL, INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-RAI

AND ITS SISTER NEWSPAPER CENTRIST, INFLUENTIAL AMONG

THE ELITE ENGLISH DAILY JORDAN TIMES PRAISED IT AS

BEING COMPATIBLE WITH JORDAN\'S VISION, THE CENTER-LEFT,

INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR REPORTED

"INTERNATIONAL CRITICISM" AND LOCAL REJECTION OF THE

SPEECH. THE INDEPENDENT, MASS-APPEAL ARABIC DAILY AL-

ARAB AL-YAWM ONLY REPORTED BARE BONES COVERAGE OF THE

GOJ STATEMENT WELCOMING THE SPEECH. EDITORIAL

COMMENTARIES ALSO REFLECTED CONTRASTING VIEWS ABOUT THE

PRESIDENT\'S SPEECH, FROM COMMENTS LIKE "THE WAY

FORWARD" IN THE JORDAN TIMES AND "BUSH\'S VIEWS MEET THE

JORDANIAN VISION" IN AL-RAI, TO "BUSH ACTS AS

LIEUTENANT FOR SHARON" IN AL-DUSTOUR. INDIVIDUAL

COMMENTATORS WERE LARGELY CRITICAL OF THE SPEECH.

DAILIES ALSO VARIED IN THEIR COVERAGE OF THE

CANCELLATION OF A RALLY FOR THE BOYCOTT OF AMERICAN

PRODUCTS ORGANIZED BY THE PROFESSIONAL ASSOCIATIONS,

WHICH WAS PLANNED TO TAKE PLACE YESTERDAY. END

SUMMARY.

NEWS REPORTS:

JORDANIAN DAILIES ON JUNE 26 WERE DOMINATED BY

REACTIONS TO PRESIDENT BUSH\'S SPEECH ON THE MIDDLE

EAST. THERE WAS AN UNUSUALLY LARGE DIFFERENCE BETWEEN

THE TONE ADOPTED BY SEMI-OFFICIAL, INFLUENTIAL ARABIC

DAILY AL-RAI AND ITS SISTER NEWSPAPER CENTRIST,

INFLUENTIAL AMONG THE ELITE ENGLISH DAILY JORDAN TIMES

ON ONE HAND, AND THAT OF CENTER-LEFT, INFLUENTIAL

ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR AND INDEPENDENT, MASS-APPEAL

ARABIC DAILY AL-ARAB AL-YAWM ON THE OTHER.

AL-RAI AND THE JORDAN TIMES LED WITH THE HEADLINE:

"JORDAN WELCOMES BUSH\'S SPEECH AND CONSIDERS IT A

POSITIVE STEP TOWARDS CREATING A PALESTINIAN STATE." A

SECOND FRONT-PAGE STORY IN BOTH NEWSPAPERS ANNOUNCED:

"BUSH\'S SPEECH WELCOMED IN THE ARAB WORLD AND

INTERNATIONALLY, BUT CAUSED DISAPPOINTMENT IN THE

PALESTINIAN STREET." THE LEAD STORY IN BOTH NEWSPAPERS

QUOTED THE STATEMENT BY THE GOVERNMENT OF JORDAN, WHICH

SAID THAT: "THE SPEECH WAS COMPATIBLE WITH THE

JORDANIAN POSITION THAT CALLS FOR DEFINING THE OVERALL

FRAMEWORK FOR THE SOLUTION AND A TIME FRAME FOR

REVIVING THE PEACE PROCESS."

AL-DUSTOUR, ON THE OTHER HAND, LED WITH THE HEADLINE:

"INTERNATIONAL CRITICISM OF BUSH\'S CALL FOR REPLACING

ARAFAT" ON TOP OF A PHOTO OF A MUTILATED BODY OF A

"MARTYR" KILLED IN HEBRON YESTERDAY. IN A RARE MOVE BY

THE JORDANIAN MEDIA, AL-DUSTOUR RELEGATED THE

GOVERNMENT\'S STATEMENT TO A SECONDARY HEADLINE IN THE

THIRD STORY ON THE FRONT PAGE, WITH THE MAIN HEADLINE

OF THE STORY QUOTING THE FOREIGN MINISTER AS SAYING:

"BUSH\'S SPEECH GOES BEYOND ISRAEL\'S REQUEST FOR PHASED

SOLUTIONS."

THE CANCELLATION OF THE PROFESSIONAL ASSOCIATIONS\'

RALLY FOR BOYCOTTING AMERICAN PRODUCTS, PLANNED FOR

YESTERDAY, WAS TREATED WITH EQUALLY DISSIMILAR

COVERAGE. AL-RAI IGNORED THE EVENT COMPLETELY, AL-

DUSTOUR SAID IN A BRIEF REPORT THAT THE RALLY WAS

CANCELLED, AND AL-ARAB AL-YAWM REPORTED ON THE FRONT

PAGE THAT: "SECURITY AGENCIES PREVENTED THE

PROFESSIONAL ASSOCIATIONS FROM HOLDING THE RALLY FOR

THE BOYCOTT OF AMERICAN PRODUCTS."

EDITORIAL COMMENTARIES:

-- BUSH\'S IDEAS MEET WITH THE JORDANIAN VISION FOR A

SOLUTION TO THE ARAB-ISRAELI CONFLICT.

THE LEADING COMMENTARY IN SEMI-OFFICIAL, INFLUENTIAL

ARABIC DAILY AL-RAI ON JUNE 26 SAID: "THE JORDANIAN

WELCOME OF THE SPEECH CAME IN REACTION TO THE U.S.

PRESIDENT\'S COMMITMENT TO CONTINUE PLAYING A ROLE,

SOMETHING THAT HIS MAJESTY KING ABDULLAH STRESSED ON

MORE THAN ONE OCCASION IN THE U.S. THE PRESIDENT ALSO

AFFIRMED THE PALESTINIANS\' RIGHT TO AN INDEPENDENT

STATE IN WHICH THEY WOULD EXERCISE THEIR RIGHT TO SELF-

DETERMINATION. ONE SHOULD ALSO REMEMBER THAT JORDAN,

DURING THE PAST TWO YEARS, CALLED SETTING A TIME-FRAME

FOR ENDING THE OCCUPATION, AND HERE CAME THE

PRESIDENT\'S SPEECH TO SET A THREE-YEAR DEADLINE FOR THE

END OF THE OCCUPATION, THEREBY CONFIRMING THE

CORRECTNESS OF JORDAN\'S VISION."

-- BUSH\'S SPEECH, A STEP BACKWARDS

MOST WIDELY-READ DAILY COLUMNIST FAHED AL-FANEK WROTE

IN SEMI-OFFICIAL INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-RAI ON

JUNE 26: "PRESIDENT BUSH DID NOT UTTER WORDS SUCH AS

`OCCUPATION,\' `WITHDRAWAL,\' `THE 1967 BORDERS,\' OR

`ISRAELI RAIDS ON PALESTINIAN TOWNS AND REFUGEE CAMPS.\'

HE CONCENTRATED ON PALESTINIAN TERRORISM WHOLE IGNORING

ISRAELI TERRORISM, AND HE CALLED FOR REPLACING THE

PALESTINIAN PRESIDENT. IN SHORT, PRESIDENT BUSH GAVE A

SPEECH IN WHICH HE WENT FURTHER THAN SHARON WOULD HAVE

HAD HE WRITTEN THE SPEECH HIMSELF. AS FOR THE REFORMS

THAT BUSH DEMANDED, THEY ARE WORDS OF TRUTH INTENDED TO

COVER UP FOR MISCHIEF. THE TRUE INTENTION BEHIND THEM

IS TO OBFUSCATE AND BUY TIME FOR SHARON TO COMPLETE HIS

MISSION."

-- THE OCCUPATION GOVERNMENT WILL NOT RESPOND TO BUSH\'S

SPEECH.

DAILY COLUMNIST MAHMOUD AL-RIMAWI WROTE IN SEMI-

OFFICIAL, INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-RAI ON JUNE 26:

"THE PRESIDENT CALLED ON THE OCCUPATION GOVERNMENT TO

WITHDRAW TO ITS POSITIONS BEFORE SEPTEMBER 28, 2000.

SHARON\'S GOVERNMENT WILL NOT DO SO. INSTEAD, IT WILL

CONTINUE DESTROYING WHAT REMAINS OF THE PALESTINIAN

INFRASTRUCTURE. THE PRESIDENT ALSO ASKED FOR A STOP TO

SETTLEMENT ACTIVITY. SHARON\'S GOVERNMENT WILL NOT

ACCEPT TO DO SO EITHER. THE FEAR IS THAT THE

ADMINISTRATION WILL RESPOND TO THIS ISRAELI DISDAIN BY

LAUNCHING ANOTHER ATTACK AGAINST THE PALESTINIAN

AUTHORITY. THIS IS HOW IT HAS RESPONDED DURING THE

PAST YEAR."

-- BUSH ACTS AS SHARON\'S LIEUTENANT.

THE LEADING COMMENTARY OF CENTER-LEFT, INFLUENTIAL

ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR ON JUNE 26 SAID: "PRESIDENT

BUSH\'S SPEECH WAS NOT SURPRISING EXCEPT IN THE DEGREE

OF ITS BIAS FOR ISRAEL, THE FLAGRANCE OF ITS ENMITY TO

THE PALESTINIAN AUTHORITY, AND ITS TOTAL DISMISSAL OF

ARAB DIPLOMATIC EFFORTS. THIS HAS DASHED ALL THE HOPES

THAT WERE PINNED ON THE U.S. HAVING A RESIDUE OF

JUSTICE AND INTEGRITY. THIS SPEECH WILL ONLY

CONSOLIDATE SHARON\'S BELLIGERENCE, PROLONG THE

OCCUPATION, AND UNLEASH ANOTHER WAVE OF ZIONIST

EXTREMISM, WHICH WILL UNDOUBTEDLY BE MET WITH PARALLEL

PALESTINIAN REACTIONS. BY CONSEQUENCE, BUSH\'S SPEECH

WILL ONLY HAVE USHERED IN MORE BLOODSHED, DESTRUCTION,

AND DEATH."

-- CHANCES OF IMPLEMENTING BUSH\'S IDEAS.

TAHER AL-ADWAN, CHIEF EDITOR OF INDEPENDENT, MASS-

APPEAL ARABIC DAILY AL-ARAB AL-YAWM WROTE ON JUNE 26:

"I EXPECT THAT MOST ARAB STATES WILL ACCEPT BUSH\'S

IDEAS, WHETHER PUBLICLY OR IN SECRET, NOT BECAUSE THEY

AGREE WITH ANYTHING IN THEM, BUT OUT OF FEAR OF A

CONFRONTATION WITH WASHINGTON. IN REALITY, BUSH\'S

IDEAS MEET WITH SHARON\'S STRATEGY IN A VITAL POINT,

THAT OF CHANGING THE PALESTINIAN LEADERSHIP AND ALL

PALESTINIAN INSTITUTIONS. THIS AGREES WITH SHARON\'S

STRATEGY TO WITHDRAW ISRAEL\'S RECOGNITION OF THE

PALESTINIAN LEADERSHIP, WHICH CAME AFTER THE OSLO

ACCORD. IN ADDITION, THE PRESIDENT GAVE SHARON

ADDITIONAL TIME TO CONTINUE HIS WAR AGAINST THE

PALESTINIAN PEOPLE. AS FOR PALESTINIAN ORGANIZATIONS,

BUSH\'S DISMISSAL OF THEM WILL ONLY PUSH THEM TO FURTHER

RESISTANCE AND DAILY MARTYRDOM OPERATIONS, EVEN IF

ARAFAT CALLS ON THEM EVERY DAY TO STOP." GNEHM
