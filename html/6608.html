SUBJECT: JIM WALLER

1. As you know, the Sri Lankan account has been very

busy these past few months and promises to remain so for

a long period. I doubt Jim Waller realized when he took

over the Desk just how much work there would be! In the

event, he has nevertheless has performed wonderfully in

support of this Mission, and I want to bring his

outstanding work to your attention. Unfailingly polite

and self effacing, Jim Waller is incredibly hard

working, detail-oriented and fluent in his drafting,

whether preparing briefing memos for D or email reports

giving us in Colombo a sense of how things are unfolding

in Washington. In the hurly burly of our profession,

especially now that the whole Department is under siege

because of the war in Iraq, I wanted to take this

occasion to single out Jim Waller for my warmest praise

and thanks for a job extremely well done.

WILLS
