SUBJECT: SWEDISH AMBASSADOR EXPLAINS SWEDEN\'S AID

CUTBACK TO ZIMBABWE

CLASSIFIED BY CHARGE D\'AFFAIRES EARL M. IRVING FOR

REASONS 1.5 B/D.

1. (C) SUMMARY: RELATIONS BETWEEN ZIMBABWE AND SWEDEN

FELL TO AN ALL-TIME LOW DURING THE FIRST WEEK OF

FEBRUARY AS THE SWEDISH GOVERNMENT ANNOUNCED THAT IT

HAD SUSPENDED BILATERAL AID TO THE GOVERNMENT OF

ZIMBABWE. ZIMBABWEAN FOREIGN MINISTER STANISLAUS

MUDENGE RESPONDED ANGRILY, SAYING THAT HIS GOVERNMENT

WOULD REVIEW ITS RELATIONS WITH SWEDEN. THE

GOVERNMENT OF ZIMBABWE, THROUGH AN ANONYMOUS

SPOKESMAN, ALSO IMPLIED THAT THE SWEDISH EMBASSY MIGHT

NO LONGER BE WELCOME IN ZIMBABWE. AT A LUNCHEON ON

FEBRUARY 7, SWEDEN\'S AMBASSADOR TO ZIMBABWE, LENNARTH

HJELMAKER, CITED HIS GOVERNMENT\'S WISH TO MAKE A

STRONG STATEMENT OF ITS CONCERN WITH EVENTS IN

ZIMBABWE, COUPLED WITH BAD TIMING, AS THE REASON FOR

THE CURRENT TENSION BETWEEN SWEDEN AND ZIMBABWE.

AMBASSADOR HJELMAKER DID NOT THINK THAT HE OR HIS

STAFF WERE IN IMMINENT DANGER OF BEING DECLARED

PERSONAE NON GRATA, BUT CONCEDED THAT HE WAS WORKING

HARD TO MANAGE THE DELICATE SITUATION. END SUMMARY.

2. (C) THE FEBRUARY 1 ANNOUNCEMENT IN STOCKHOLM THAT

THE GOVERNMENT OF SWEDEN WAS SUSPENDING BILATERAL

DEVELOPMENT ASSISTANCE TO THE GOVERNMENT OF ZIMBABWE

DID NOT PLAY WELL IN HARARE. THE STATE-OWNED DAILY

NEWSPAPER "THE HERALD" DISTORTED THE FACTS OF THE

SWEDISH DECISION IN ITS FEBRUARY 2 STORY, CLAIMING

THAT "SWEDEN...HAS ANNOUNCED THAT IT IS SLASHING AID

TO THE GOVERNMENT AND WILL CHANNEL ABOUT 400 MILLION

ZIMBABWE DOLLARS TOWARDS OPPOSITION POLITICAL PARTIES

AND NON-GOVERNMENTAL ORGANIZATIONS." WHILE THIS IS

NOT TRUE (SWEDISH AID SUPPORTS HIV/AIDS PROGRAMS, NON-

PARTISAN DEMOCRACY PROGRAMS AND DISASTER RELIEF), IT

PROVIDED RIGHTEOUS HIGH GROUND FOR AN UNNAMED GOZ

SOURCE WHO WAS QUOTED IN THE "HERALD" REPORT. THE

SOURCE SAID "...EMBASSIES WITH NO BUSINESS WITH THE

GOVERNMENT AND INTENDING TO CONCENTRATE THEIR EFFORTS

ON BACKING OPPOSITION POLITICAL PARTIES AND NON-

GOVERNMENTAL ORGANIZATIONS, MEDDLING IN THE INTERNAL

POLITICS OF THE COUNTRY, HAVE NO REASON TO BE HERE...

THERE IS NO WAY THAT GOVERNMENT WILL TAKE KINDLY TO A

SITUATION WHERE AN EMBASSY BROUGHT INTO THE COUNTRY

FOR OFFICIAL STATE-TO-STATE INTERACTION IS ALLOWED TO

DE-OFFICIALIZE ITSELF AND BECOME CONDUITS OF FOREIGN

OFFICIAL SUPPORT TO BOTH THE OPPOSITION AND PARTISAN

NGOS CREATED BY THEIR OWN GOVERNMENTS. THEY ARE

BECOMING FEATURES OF SUBVERSION AND EXPECT STATE

BENEDICTION. THIS CANNOT BE..."

3. (U) FOREIGN MINISTER STANISLAUS MUDENGE ALSO

REACTED ANGRILY AND PUBLICLY, APPEARING ON STATE-

CONTROLLED ZBC TELEVISION ON FEBRUARY 5 TO CRITICIZE

THE SWEDISH DECISION. MUDENGE SAID THAT HE WOULD

REVIEW ZIMBABWE\'S RELATIONS WITH SWEDEN, IMPLYING THE

COUNTRIES.

4. (C) AT A LUNCHEON IN THE SWISS EMBASSY ON FEBRUARY 7,

AMBASSADOR HJELMAKER TOLD EMBOFF THAT PART OF THE

REASON FOR ZIMBABWE\'S ANGER WAS THAT THE ANNOUNCEMENT

OF THE AID SUSPENSION HAD BEEN BADLY HANDLED. HE SAID

THAT HIS GOVERNMENT WANTED TO MAKE A STRONG STATEMENT

ABOUT ITS CONCERN OVER ZIMBABWE\'S DEPARTURE FROM

DEMOCRATIC NORMS, BUT THAT BAD TIMING IN STOCKHOLM AND

HARARE MEANT THAT THE ANNOUNCEMENT TOOK ZIMBABWE\'S

FOREIGN MINISTRY BY SURPRISE. HJELMAKER SAID THAT

ZIMBABWE\'S AMBASSADOR TO SWEDEN HAD BEEN OUT OF THE

COUNTRY WHEN THE SWEDES DECIDED IN LATE JANUARY TO

SUSPEND BILATERAL AID. SWEDISH PROCEDURE IS THAT

GOVERNMENT DECISIONS ARE GAZETTED ON THE FIRST OF EACH

MONTH ("...JUST PART OF OUR OPEN DEMOCRACY..." SAID

HJELMAKER), SO THE DECISION WAS PUBLISHED BEFORE THE

ZIMBABWEAN AMBASSADOR COULD BE NOTIFIED. SIMILARLY,

FOREIGN MINISTER MUDENGE AND AMBASSADOR HJELMAKER WERE

BOTH ABSENT FROM HARARE DURING THE LAST WEEK OF

JANUARY, SO A SUBORDINATE AT THE SWEDISH EMBASSY ONLY

INFORMED THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE (THROUGH WHICH ALL

BILATERAL ASSISTANCE IS CHANNELED) OF THE SUSPENSION.

5. (C) THE SWEDES HAVE BEEN PARTICULARLY STALWART

SUPPORTERS OF ROBERT MUGABE\'S ZIMBABWE AND VERY GENEROUS

PROVIDERS OF DEVELOPMENT ASSISTANCE. GIVEN THAT

HISTORY, THEIR DECISION TO SUSPEND FUNDING OF GOVERNMENT

PROGRAMS HERE REPRESENTS A SIGNIFICANT DEEPENING OF

ZIMBABWE\'S ISOLATION FROM WESTERN DEMOCRACIES. SWEDEN\'S

LACK OF A COLONIAL HISTORY IN AFRICA, OR OF OTHER

IMPERIALISTIC CREDENTIALS, MAKES IT MORE DIFFICULT FOR

THE GOVERNMENT OF ZIMBABWE TO DISMISS THE SUSPENSION OF

AID AS PART OF THE "BRITISH-IMPERIALIST PLOT" TO RUIN

ZIMBABWE. NONETHELESS, THE UNNAMED GOZ SOURCE DID TELL

"THE HERALD" THAT "THE SWEDES ARE NOW INCREASINGLY

REDUCING THEMSELVES TO AN APPENDAGE OF WHITEHALL."

6. (C) COMMENT: BECAUSE OF THEIR LONG HISTORY OF

SUPPORT FOR ZIMBABWE AND THEIR TRADITION OF

NEUTRALITY, THE SWEDES ARE UNLIKELY TO BE THROWN OUT

OF ZIMBABWE. THIS EPISODE DOES, HOWEVER, SUGGEST THE

NEED FOR CAREFULLY CHOREOGRAPHED NOTIFICATION IF THE

UNITED STATES EVER NEEDS TO DELIVER BAD NEWS TO THE
