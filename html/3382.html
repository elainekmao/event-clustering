SUBJECT: JORDAN: MEDIA REACTION ON GAZA BOMBING RAID

SUMMARY

JORDANIAN DAILIES ON JULY 25 CONTINUED TO BE DOMINATED

BY THE RAID ON GAZA. SEMI-OFFICIAL INFLUENTIAL ARABIC

DAILY AL-RAI REPORTED CONSERVATIVELY THE SECURITY

COUNCIL SESSION, WHILE OTHER DAILIES CARRIED PHOTOS OF

SLAIN CHILDREN BEING PULLED FROM UNDER THE RUBBLE,

UNDER THE HEADLINE: "AMERICAN OPPOSES ANY INTERNATIONAL

RESOLUTION REGARDING THE GAZA MASSACRE."

COLUMNS OF A NUMBER OF IMPORTANT DAILY EDITORIALISTS

DID NOT APPEAR TODAY. THOSE THAT DID ATTACKED THE

"MASSACRE" AND THE U.S. POSITION REGARDING IT, AND

DEBATED WHETHER THE CORRECT PALESTINIAN RESPONSE WOULD

BE REVENGE OR CALM.

NEWS REPORTS

THE CALL FOR A SECURITY COUNCIL EMERGENCY SESSION TO

DISCUSS THE GAZA RAID OCCUPIED THE LEAD SPACE ON ALL

JORDANIAN DAILIES ON JULY 25. WHILE AL-RAI REPORTED

CONSERVATIVELY: "EMERGENCY SESSION TO DISCUSS THE GAZA

MASSACRE," OTHER DAILIES CARRIED GRUESOME PHOTOS OF

SLAIN CHILDREN BEING PULLED FROM UNDER THE RUBBLE,

UNDER THE HEADLINE: "THE U.S. OPPOSES ANY INTERNATIONAL

RESOLUTION REGARDING THE MASSACRE."

CENTER-LEFT, INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR AND

INDEPENDENT, MASS-APPEAL ARABIC DAILY AL-ARAB AL-YAWM

REPORTED THE DEMONSTRATION THAT TOOK PLACE YESTERDAY

BEFORE THE UN BUILDING IN PROTEST AGAINST "THE MASSACRE

AND INTERNATIONAL SILENCE REGARDING IT."

EDITORIAL COMMENTARIES

-- TAKING THE ARAB MIND LIGHTLY

FORMER SECRETARY-GENERAL OF THE MINISTRY OF CULTURE

MOHAMMAD NAJI AMAYREH WROTE IN SEMI-OFFICIAL

INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-RAI ON JULY 25: "WORDS

ABOUT PEACE, SECURITY, AND STABILITY HAVE NOT BROUGHT

FOOD TO THE HUNGRY, NOR BROUGHT US PEACE NOR STABILITY.

TILL WHEN WILL WE HIDE BEHIND SLOGANS OF DEVELOPMENT,

STABILITY AND INVESTMENTS? EVEN IF WE WERE TO ACHIEVE

THOSE, THEY WILL ONLY BE DESTROYED BY THE ISRAELI

MILITARY MACHINE THAT IS SUPPORTED BY THE U.S. ARSENAL

AND NEW WORLD ORDER. THE F-16S THAT THAT BOMB

RESIDENTIAL AREAS IN PALESTINE ARE THE SAME THAT BOMB

IRAQ. BOTH KILL CHILDREN, WOMEN, AND OLD MEN. THE

DESTRUCTION OF THE ARAB WORLD AIMS AT ALL ARABS."

-- QUESTIONS ABOUT THE MASSACRE

DAILY COLUMNIST YASSER ZA\'ATREH WROTE IN CENTER-LEFT,

INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR ON JULY 25: "SHARON

DID NOT COMMIT THE MASSACRE BECAUSE HE WAS AFRAID OF

THE POLITICAL PROCESS AS SOME CLAIM, BECAUSE THE VISION

OF BUSH IS THE SAME AS THAT OF SHARON, WHOSE PROGRAM

SINCE ASSUMING POWER HAS NOT BEEN A SECRET.

FURTHERMORE, SHEIKH YASSIN\'S OFFER CAME IN ORDER TO

AVOID A CLASH WITH THE PNA, NOT TO GIVE A CHANCE TO THE

PEACE PROCESS, BECAUSE THE CONDITION THAT ISRAEL SHOULD

STOP KILLING PALESTINIAN CIVILIANS AND WITHDRAW FROM

PALESTINIAN CITIES WOULD NEVER BE ACCEPTED BY SHARON

AND BUSH. THE OBJECTIVE OF THE MASSACRE IS TO BREAK THE

PALESTINIAN AUTHORITY AND PEOPLE AND IMPOSE A

HUMILIATING SETTLEMENT ON THEM. THE CORRECT RESPONSE

TO SHARON\'S LOGIC IS TO ESCALATE RESISTANCE OPERATIONS

AND DEPRIVE HIM OF THE CHANCE TO BRAG THAT HE HAS

BROKEN THE PALESTINIAN WILL."

-- THE GAZA CRIME - THE HARDER OPTION

DAILY COLUMNIST URAIB AL-RANTAWI WROTE IN CENTER-LEFT,

INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR ON JULY 25: "THE

CHANGE IN INTERNATIONAL ATTITUDES TO ISRAEL CAME AT A

HIGH PRICE IN PALESTINIAN BLOOD, AND FOR THIS REASON

THE PALESTINIANS SHOULD NOT LET THIS BLOOD BE SHED IN

VAIN. THE MOST OBVIOUS RESPONSE WOULD BE REVENGE,

WHICH WOULD BE PERFECTLY LEGITIMATE. BUT THE HARDER

OPTION WOULD BE TO BEHAVE WISELY AND IN ACCORDANCE WITH

OUR INTERESTS. ANYONE WHO DOUBTS THIS SHOULD REMEMBER

THE CONSEQUENCES OF THE ASSASSINATION OF THE RACIST

LEADER REHAVAM ZEEVI." GNEHM
