1. (U) As per instructions reftel, we can verify the

legitimacy of the export of M60 tank components to the Jordan

Armed Forces (JAF) Royal Maintenance Corps (RMC). The

non-lethal parts/components will be used for routine

maintenance, salvage, and repair of the automotive track on

the JAF M60 tank fleet. The RMC King Hussein Main Workshops

has a tank track salvage and repair facility specifically for

this purpose.
