SUBJECT: ZIMBABWE TRIES TO MAINTAIN STATUS QUO IN DRC AS

IT GROWS SUSPICIOUS OF ITS ALLIES

REFTEL: HARARE 00349

CLASSIFIED BY CHARGE D\'AFFAIRES EARL M. IRVING FOR

REASONS: 1.5 (B) AND (D).

1. (C) ON JANUARY 23, CHARGE SPOKE WITH THE PERMANENT

SECRETARY IN ZIMBABWE\'S MINISTRY OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS,

SIPDIS

WILLARD CHIWEWE, ABOUT THE DRC. CHIWEWE REITERATED

CONFIDENTIAL

POINTS THAT SPEAKER MNANGAGWA HAD MADE TO US LAST WEEK

(REFTEL): 1) ZIMBABWE WILL NOT CHANGE ITS APPROACH TO

THE DRC GOVERNMENT, 2) IT WANTS CONGOLESE "SOCIETY" TO

REMAIN "INTEGRAL" TO THE EQUATION, AND 3) IT DOES NOT

WANT ANY OF THE DRC PLAYERS TO TAKE ADVANTAGE OF

KABILA\'S ASSASSINATION WITH NEW MILITARY ADVENTURES.

ZIMBABWE WILL BE STRESSING THESE POINTS IN ALL ITS

MEETINGS WITH THE OTHER PLAYERS IN THE COMING WEEKS,

CHIWEWE STATED. THE PERMSEC, WHO JUST RETURNED FROM SIX

WEEKS OF LEAVE ON JANUARY 22, WANTS TO WAIT UNTIL NEXT

WEEK TO MEET WITH US IN ORDER TO GET FULLY UP TO SPEED

ON DRC EVENTS.

2. (C) POLOFF SPOKE WITH THE MFA\'S ACTING

UNDERSECRETARY FOR AFRICA, MR. TAKAVARASHA, ON JANUARY

23. WHEN ASKED ABOUT THE PROPOSED LUSAKA SIGNATORIES

MEETING, TAKAVARASHA DEMURRED, SAYING THAT THE ALLIED

HEADS OF STATE WOULD LIKELY BE MEETING ON THE MARGINS OF

OR SHORTLY AFTER KABILA\'S FUNERAL. THE ACTING

UNDERSECRETARY SAID THAT ZIMBABWE NO LONGER FEELS

COMFORTABLE MEETING WITH ANY OF THE DRC PLAYERS,

INCLUDING ITS ALLIES, WITHOUT KNOWING WHO WAS BEHIND THE

ASSASSINATION OF KABILA. TAKAVARASHA SUGGESTED THAT A

MEANINGFUL CONTINUATION OF THE LUSAKA DIALOGUE COULD NOT

TAKE PLACE UNTIL THE INVESTIGATION INTO KABILA\'S MURDER

IS COMPLETED. WHEN QUERIED ABOUT THE POSSIBILITY OF A

MAPUTO III SUMMIT, THE UNDERSECRETARY SAID HE WAS

UNAWARE OF ANY PLANS FOR SUCH A MEETING, AND, IN ANY

CASE, DISMISSED THE SOUTH AFRICANS AS "OUTSIDERS" TO THE

DRC CONFLICT.

3. (C) COMMENT: ZIMBABWE\'S UNEASINESS ABOUT THE

SITUATION IN THE DRC IS BEGINNING TO SHOW. THE GOZ

LEADERSHIP\'S PARANOID OPTICS MAY BE FOCUSING ON ITS

ALLIES, PARTICULARLY ANGOLA, AS SPECULATION AND RUMOR

ABOUND ABOUT WHO IS RESPONSIBLE FOR KABILA\'S DEATH.

TAKAVARASHA\'S COMMENTS ARE WORRISOME, AS HE SEEMED MORE

CONCERNED ABOUT FINGERING KABILA\'S KILLER THAN USING THE

OPPORTUNITY OF A CHANGE OF DRC LEADERSHIP TO ADVANCE THE

LUSAKA PEACE PROCESS. WE SHOULD BE ABLE TO OBTAIN A

MORE DEFINITIVE READOUT OF ZIMBABWE\'S THINKING AFTER THE

KEY PLAYERS RETURN FROM KABILA\'S FUNERAL THIS WEEK. END

COMMENT.

IRVING
