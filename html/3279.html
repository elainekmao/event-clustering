SUBJECT: ISRAELI-PALESTINIAN "SECRET" TALKS IN ROME: AN

EXAMPLE OF TRACK II DIPLOMACY

CLASSIFIED BY: ACTING POL MC GARY ROBBINS FOR REASONS 1.5 (B) AND (D)

1. (U) SUMMARY: THE MAYOR OF ROME FACILITATED SEVERAL

CONFIDENTIAL

MEETINGS BETWEEN THE ISRAELI URI SAVIR AND THE PALESTINIAN

ABU ALA LAST YEAR IN ROME. THE ITALIAN GOVERNMENT WAS NOT

AWARE OF THESE MEETINGS BUT IS ALSO NOT UNDULY CONCERNED

ABOUT THEM. THESE MEETINGS, UNOFFICIAL IN NATURE, PRODUCED A

"ROME UNDERSTANDING", WHICH WAS EVENTUALLY REJECTED BY PM

SHARON. END SUMMARY.

2. (C) POLOFF DISCUSSED WITH OFER BAVLY, THE SPOKESMAN OF THE

EMBASSY OF ISRAEL IN ROME, THE NEWS THAT BROKE IN THE ITALIAN

PRESS JULY 8 OF "SECRET" TALKS LAST YEAR BETWEEN SAVIR AND

ABU ALA IN ROME. BAVLY SAID THE ITALIAN MEDIA OVERPLAYED THE

IMPORTANCE OF THE ROME MEETINGS. SAVIR AND ABU ALA WORKED

TOGETHER ON THE OSLO ACCORD AND ARE "GOOD FRIENDS", HE SAID.

THEY MEET FREQUENTLY IN DIFFERENT PLACES IN THE WORLD, NOT

JUST IN ROME. THEY BOTH ASKED MAYOR VELTRONI TO ARRANGE

"SECRET" MEETINGS BECAUSE THEY BELIEVE, BASED ON THEIR

EXPERIENCE IN OSLO, THAT TALKS ARE MOST PRODUCTIVE WHEN MEDIA

PRESSURE IS ABSENT. SAVIR IS A LONG-TIME FRIEND AND

CONSULTANT OF FM PERES. (NOTE: SAVIR BECAME DG OF THE ISRAELI

MFA AT THE AGE OF 39. HE LATER BECAME THE PRESIDENT OF THE

PERES CENTER FOR PEACE. HE IS NOW IN PRIVATE BUSINESS AND

DIVIDES HIS TIME BETWEEN ISRAEL AND ITALY. END NOTE.) EVEN

THOUGH PERES WAS AWARE OF THESE CONTACTS, STRESSED BAVLY,

SAVIR IS NOT AN ISRAELI GOVERNMENT OFFICIAL AND THUS ANY

MEETINGS HE HAS DO NOT CONSTITUTE AN OFFICIAL CONTACT WITH

THE GOVERNMENT OF ISRAEL. IMPLEMENTATION OF THE ROME

UNDERSTANDING, WHICH WAS TO BEGIN WITH A CEASE-FIRE FOLLOWED

BY MUTUAL RECOGNITION OF ISRAELI AND PALESTINIAN STATES AND

SUBSEQUENT BORDER NEGOTIATIONS, WAS REJECTED BY SHARON.

BAVLY ADDED THAT, CONTRARY TO WHAT SOME MAY EXPECT, THE

EXPOSURE OF THE SAVIR-ABU ALA ROME MEETINGS HAS RECEIVED

LITTLE COVERAGE IN THE ISRAELI PRESS.

3. (C) POLOFF ALSO SPOKE WITH PAOLO DIONISI, DEPUTY

DIPLOMATIC ADVISOR FOR PM AND ACTING FM BERLUSCONI. DIONISI

SAID THAT THE MEETINGS BETWEEN SAVIR AND ABU ALA WERE NOT

OFFICIAL IN NATURE AND THAT THE GOI WAS NEVER INFORMED OF

THEM. ROME, ADDED DIONISI, IS A NATURAL CHOICE FOR SUCH

MEETINGS GIVEN ITS GEOGRAPHICAL PROXIMITY TO THE MIDDLE EAST,

THE GOOD RELATIONS THAT THE MAYOR OF ROME HAS WITH BOTH SAVIR

AND ABU ALA, AND THE EASE WITH WHICH SUCH MEETINGS COULD GO

UNNOTICED IN A CITY THAT SERVES AS HOME AND TRANSIT POINT FOR

THOUSANDS OF DIPLOMATS AND PUBLIC FIGURES.

4. (C) COMMENT: THE "SECRET" TALKS IN ROME BETWEEN SAVIR AND

ABU ALA ARE AN EXAMPLE OF TRACK II DIPLOMACY BETWEEN TWO

PEOPLE WHO WORKED WELL TOGETHER IN OSLO AND ARE REPORTEDLY

CLOSE FRIENDS. THE MAYOR OF ROME PROTECTED THE "SECRECY" OF

THE TALKS TO HONOR SAVIR AND ABU ALA\'S WISHES, AND POSSIBLY

ALSO IN THE HOPE OF EVENTUALLY EARNING DOMESTIC AND

INTERNATIONAL RECOGNITION IF THE TALKS BORE FRUIT. END

COMMENT.

5. (SBU) BIO NOTE: ROME\'S MAYOR WATER VELTRONI IS A LONG-TERM

CENTER-LEFT POLITICIAN, AND SERVED AS DEPUTY PM IN THE PRODI

GOVERNMENT (1996). HE HAS EXTENSIVE INTERNATIONAL CONTACTS,

INCLUDING THE TWO PARTICIPANTS IN THE TALKS. HE CONTINUES TO

HOLD AMBITIONS FOR HIGH-LEVEL NATIONAL OFFICE, SHOULD ITALY\'S

CENTER-LEFT AGAIN BE IN A POSITION TO WIN THE CONFIDENCE OF

ITALY\'S ELECTORATE. END BIO NOTE.
