SUBJECT: CROATIAN WEEKLY LISTS ALLEGED B-H ARMY

ATROCITIES AGAINST BOSNIAN CROATS IN 1993

1. THE SEMI-INDEPENDENT SPLIT-BASED WEEKLY "NEDELJNA

DALMACIJA" ON JAN 5 PUBLISHED A LENGTHY PIECE BY

RESPECTED FREELANCER MARIO MARUSIC ON THE THREAT

FACED BY BOSNIAN CROATS FROM B-H ARMY OR ALLIED

FORCES. MARUSIC DECRIES THE PAUCITY OF ATTENTION TO

ATROCITIES SUFFERED BY BOSNIAN CROATS WHILE THE

WORLD\'S ATTENTION WAS RIVETED ON "AHMICI, STUPNI DOL,

AND THE OLD BRIDGE (IN MOSTAR). AT THE END OF THE

PIECE, MARUSIC INCLUDED A LIST OF "THE MAJOR"

UNCLASSIFIED

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 03 ZAGREB 00513 01 OF 03 041824Z

(ALLEGED) ATROCITIES COMMITTED BY B-H ARMED FORCES

AGAINST BOSNIAN CROATS IN 1993.

2. SOME OF THE INCIDENTS ARE WELL-KNOWN, SUCH AS

UZDOL AND THE KILLING OF THE TWO FRANCISCAN FRIARS AT

FOJNICA; OTHERS, SUCH AS THOSE CENTERED AROUND

KONJIC, HAVE LONG BEEN BEEN ALLEGED BUT NEVER

SUBSTANTIATED (OR, IN MOST CASES, EVEN INVESTIGATED,

SO FAR AS WE KNOW); AND STILL OTHERS WE HAD NOT

HERETOFORE HEARD ABOUT.

3. IN ATTEMPTING TO ASCERTAIN THE ORIGINAL SOURCES

OF MARUSIC\'S REPORT, WE LEARNED THAT MOST OR ALL OF

THE PRE-AUGUST 20 ALLEGED INCIDENTS WERE CHRONICLED

IN A "HERCEG-BOSNA" PUBLICATION OF AROUND THAT DATE

WHICH COVERED THE PERIOD FROM 1991 TO THE DATE OF

PUBLICATION. SOURCE MATERIAL FOR INCIDENTS ALLEGED

TO HAVE OCCURRED AFTER AUGUST 20 ALSO COMES FROM

"HERCEG-BOSNA," FROM TESTIMONIES FILED WITH ODPR

(OFFICE OF DISPLACE PERSONS AND REFUGEES) BY PERSONS

ALLEGEDLY WITNESSING THE EVENT.

4. BECAUSE OF THE ORIGINAL "HERCEG-BOSNA" SOURCING,

THE ATROCITIES LIST CANNOT BE CONSIDERED

SUBSTANTIATED. WE REPORT IT, HOWEVER, BECAUSE OF ITS

SPECIFICITY, BECAUSE WE KNOW THAT AT LEAST SOME OF

THESE INCIDENTS DID TAKE PLACE, AND BECAUSE MARUSIC,

WHO HAS A SOLID REPUTATION, CLAIMS TO HAVE PERSONALLY

GONE OVER THE ORIGINAL SOURCE MATERIALS (TESTIMONIES,

MAPS, ETC) FOR BOTH PRE AND POST AUGUST 20 ATROCITIES

AND FOUND THEM COMPELLING.
