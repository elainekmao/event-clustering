SUBJECT: ARAFAT RECEIVES SRV SUPPORT

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 HANOI 02139 301022Z

1. (U) CLASSIFIED BY ROBERT C. PORTER, CHARGE D\'AFFAIRES,

REASON 1.5(D).

2. (C) SUMMARY: A BRIEF VISIT TO HANOI BY YASSER ARAFAT

ON AUGUST 24 WAS APPARENTLY AIMED AT SHORING UP SUPPORT

FOR PALESTINIAN POSITIONS IN THE WAKE OF ESCALATING

VIOLENCE IN THE MIDDLE EAST. VIETNAM REAFFIRMED ITS

GENERAL SUPPORT AND EXPRESSED CONCERN OVER THE VIOLENCE,

BUT BROKE NO NEW GROUND. END SUMMARY.

3. (U) YASSER ARAFAT STOPPED IN HANOI ON AUGUST 24 AS

PART OF A SWING THROUGH ASIA, DESCRIBED AS AN EFFORT TO

GATHER SUPPORT FOR PALESTINIAN POSITIONS. HE APPARENTLY

HAD ONLY ONE OFFICIAL MEETING, WITH SRV PRIME MINISTER

PHAN VAN KHAI, WHO REAFFIRMED THE SRV\'S POSITION THAT THE

PARTY, STATE, AND VIETNAMESE PEOPLE WILL CONTINUE THEIR

LONG-STANDING SUPPORT FOR THE PALESTINIAN POSITIONS,

INCLUDING THE RIGHT TO HAVE AN INDEPENDENT STATE.

ACCORDING TO LOCAL PRESS COVERAGE, KHAI ALSO CONFIRMED

SRV SUPPORT FOR UN RESOLUTIONS 242 AND 338 AND EXPRESSED

CONCERN OVER THE ESCALATING VIOLENCE. VIETNAM

TELEVISION, IN ITS AUGUST 24 NEWS SUMMARY, CRITICIZED THE

BUSH ADMINISTRATION FOR ITS SUPPORT OF ISRAEL.

4. (C) MINISTRY OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS WESTERN AFRICA AND

ASIA DEPARTMENT DEPUTY DIRECTOR DOAN NGOC BOI DESCRIBED

ARAFAT\'S DISCUSSIONS WITH THE PRIME MINISTER AS FRIENDLY

BUT DID NOT OFFER MANY DETAILS. HE NOTED THAT THE SRV\'S

SUPPORT FOR ARAFAT AND THE PALESTINIAN PEOPLE IS LONG-

STANDING. BOI COMMENTED THAT THE SRV IS ESPECIALLY

CONCERNED ABOUT ISRAEL\'S USE OF HEAVY WEAPONS IN CIVILIAN

AREAS UNDER THE PRETEXT OF SECURITY. HE STATED THAT THE

PALESTINIANS WERE READY FOR PEACE AND IT WAS UP TO ISRAEL

TO MAKE ADDITIONAL CONCESSIONS. BOI ALSO CHARGED THAT

THE US WAS "BIASED" TOWARDS ISRAEL. HE ALSO NOTED THAT

ARAFAT SEEMED PESSIMISTIC AND CONCERNED AND HAD MENTIONED

THE NEED FOR INTERNATIONAL OBSERVERS.

5. (C) BOI ALSO DESCRIBED ISRAEL-VIETNAM RELATIONS AS

"NORMAL" AND SAID THAT NEGOTIATIONS FOR A BILATERAL TRADE

AGREEMENT ARE PROCEEDING. HE ADDED THAT HE DID NOT

EXPECT VIETNAM TO OPEN AN EMBASSY IN ISRAEL WITHIN THE

NEXT 5 YEARS, HOWEVER.

6. (C) ISRAELI AMBASSADOR AMIKAM LEVY TOLD POLOFF AUGUST

29 THAT THE ENTIRE ARAFAT VISIT OCCURRED AT THE HANOI

AIRPORT AND LASTED ONLY ABOUT ONE HOUR. HE ADDED THAT

ARAFAT HAD VISITED VIETNAM AT LEAST 10 TIMES IN THE PAST

AND KNOWS THAT HE WILL ALWAYS BE WARMLY RECEIVED HERE.

7. (C) COMMENT: ARAFAT\'S BRIEF VISIT TO HANOI APPEARS

NOT TO HAVE BROKEN ANY NEW GROUND BUT ONLY FURTHER

DEMONSTRATED THE LONG-STANDING SYMPATHY AND GOOD

RELATIONS BETWEEN THE SRV AND THE PALESTINIANS.

END COMMENT

PORTER
