SUBJECT: AMBASSADOR\'S LUNCHEON WITH PRESIDENT CIAMPI

CLASSIFIED BY: AMBASSADOR MEL SEMBLER FOR REASONS 1.5 (B) AND (D)

1. (C) SUMMARY: IN A PRIVATE LUNCHEON, ITALIAN PRESIDENT

CIAMPI EXPRESSED HIS DEEP CONCERN OVER A DETERIORATING

SITUATION BETWEEN ISRAEL AND THE PALESTINIANS. HE MADE A

DETAILED ARGUMENT THAT ITALY CAN AND MUST BE BOTH PRO EUROPE

AND PRO US, WITH WHICH I AGREED. CIAMPI WOULD VERY MUCH LIKE

TO VISIT WASHINGTON FOR A MEETING WITH PRESIDENT BUSH; I

STRONGLY SUPPORT THIS. END SUMMARY.

2. (U) ITALIAN PRESIDENT CARLO AZEGLIA CIAMPI INVITED ME TO A

PRIVATE LUNCHEON FEB 12. TO OUR KNOWLEDGE, IT WAS THE FIRST

TIME HE HAS EXTENDED SUCH AN INVITATION TO ANY AMBASSADOR.

THE DCM AND ANTONIO PURI PURINI, THE PRESIDENT\'S DIPLOMATIC

ADVISOR, ALSO ATTENDED, ALONG WITH PRESIDENCY SECRETARY

GENERAL GIFUNI.

3. (C) CIAMPI DESCRIBED ITALY\'S RELATIONS WITH THE US AS

"FUNDAMENTAL". HOWEVER GOOD THE RELATIONS WITH THE US ARE,

ADDED CIAMPI, THEY MUST GO, IN LARGE PART, VIA THE EU. THIS

SAID, ITALY\'S RELATIONS WITH THE EU AND WITH THE US ARE NOT

IN COMPETITION. I SAID THAT THE US UNDERSTOOD THE IMPORTANCE

OF A STRONG EUROPE AND SUPPORTED IT. DURING LAST WEEK\'S

VISIT TO ROME, NATO AMB. NICK BURNS UNDERLINED HOW MUCH WE

APPRECIATED THE LEADERSHIP ITALY IS BRINGING TO NATO, AND THE

ACTIVE ROLE ITALY PLAYS IN THE BALKANS AND IN AFGHANISTAN.

ITALY\'S RELATIONS WITH THE EU DO NOT THREATEN ITS TIES WITH

THE US.

4. (C) CIAMPI DWELT ON ITALY\'S SPECIAL INTEREST IN ITS

IMMEDIATE NEIGHBORS IN THE BALKANS, THE MIDDLE EAST AND NORTH

AFRICA. SOUND WESTERN INVESTMENT AND ASSISTANCE IN THOSE

REGIONS NOT ONLY REDUCES THE PRESSURE FOR EMIGRATION TO

EUROPE, BUT ALSO UNDERMINES THE INFLUENCE OF FUNDAMENTALISM.

HE NOTED THAT HE RECENTLY HAD TOLD BOTH KOSOVO LEADER RUGOVA

AND MONTENEGRIN PRESIDENT DJUKANOVIC THAT ITALY OPPOSES

INDEPENDENCE FOR THOSE ENTITIES. A NEW CROP OF BALKAN

"MINI-STATES" IS IN NO ONE\'S INTEREST; THEY WOULD BE

OFFSHOOTS OF THE "MALIGNANT SEED OF NATIONALISM."

5. (C) ON THE MIDDLE EAST, CIAMPI EXPRESSED HIS DEEP CONCERN

OVER THE DETERIORATING SITUATION IN GENERAL AND SHARON\'S

APPROACH IN PARTICULAR. I EMPHASIZED THAT PRESIDENT BUSH IS

DIRECTLY INVOLVED IN THE SITUATION, AND THAT HE WOULD LIKE

THE EU AND THE US TO SPEAK WITH A SINGLE VOICE ON THE MIDDLE

EAST: THE VIOLENCE MUST STOP; AND THEN THE PEACE PROCESS CAN

RESTART. CIAMPI SAID THAT THE US IS THE ONLY POWER ABLE OF

BRINGING BOTH PARTIES TOGETHER, BUT ADMITTED THAT IT IS VERY

DIFFICULT TO DO SO UNDER THE CURRENT ATMOSPHERE OF VIOLENCE.

6. (C) CIAMPI, NOW IN HIS THIRD YEAR AS PRESIDENT, SAID THAT

THE ONLY OFFICIAL VISIT HE HAD EVER MADE TO WASHINGTON WAS IN

SEPTEMBER 1993 WHEN HE WAS PRIME MINISTER. PURI PURINI SAID

THAT CIAMPI WOULD LIKE TO BE ABLE TO AGREE ASAP ON A DATE FOR

A VISIT TO WASHINGTON, IDEALLY THIS FALL. I REPLIED THAT

SURELY IT WOULD NOT BE POSSIBLE TO HAVE A STATE VISIT, BUT AN

OPPORTUNITY TO HAVE A SUBSTANTIVE DISCUSSION WITH PRESIDENT

BUSH WOULD BE VALUABLE. CIAMPI SAID IT WAS NOT IMPORTANT

THAT IT BE A STATE VISIT, AS LONG AS HE COULD HAVE A

SUBSTANTIVE DISCUSSION WITH THE PRESIDENT, AS THEY DID LAST

JULY IN ROME. HE ADDED THAT HE VERY MUCH APPRECIATES THE

PRESIDENT\'S "HUMANITY, COMPASSION, AND STRAIGHTFORWARD

APPROACH".

7. (C) COMMENT: CIAMPI REMAINS FAR AND AWAY THE MOST POPULAR

POLITICIAN IN ITALY (ACCORDING TO SOME POLLS, HE IS MORE

POPULAR THAN SUCH VENERABLE INSTITUTIONS AS THE CARABINIERI

AND THE CHURCH). HE IS RESPECTED NOT ONLY AS THE ARCHITECT

OF ITALY\'S ECONOMIC TRANSFORMATION IN THE 1990S, BUT ALSO FOR

THE CRUCIAL ROLE HE PLAYS IN STABILIZING ITALY\'S DOMESTIC AND

FOREIGN POLICIES. THAT THE HEAD OF STATE OF A MAJOR AND

STEADFAST NATO ALLY HAS NOT YET BEEN INVITED TO WASHINGTON IS

AN ANOMALY THAT SHOULD BE CORRECTED. I STRONGLY SUPPORT A

VISIT TO WASHINGTON THIS FALL AS A RECOGNITION OF ITALY\'S

STRONGLY SUPPORTIVE ROLE IN THE WAR ON TERRORISM, AND AS A

VALUABLE OPPORTUNITY FOR THE PRESIDENT TO ENGAGE WITH A KEY

OPINION SHAPER IN ITALY. END COMMENT.
