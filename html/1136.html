SUBJECT: KENYANS REFUSE TO ISSUE TRANSIT VISAS FOR CUBAN

DOCTORS

REF: NAIROBI 4914

CLASSIFIED BY AMBASSADOR TOM MCDONALD FOR REASONS 1.5

B/D.

1. (C) ON JUNE 13, AMBASSADOR MCDONALD SPOKE WITH

AMBASSADOR DR. HUKKA WARIO, HIGH COMMISSIONER OF KENYA

TO ZIMBABWE, TO SECURE THE LATTER\'S ASSISTANCE IN

ISSUING TRANSIT VISAS FOR CUBAN DOCTORS LEONEL CORDOVA

RODRIGUEZ AND NORIS DEL CARMEN PENA MARTINEZ. OVER THE

JUNE 10-11 WEEKEND HIGH COMMISSIONER WARIO HAD ASSURED

AMBASSADOR MCDONALD THAT THE VISA WOULD NOT BE A

PROBLEM, AND THAT IT WAS HIS OPINION THAT THE CUBANS

WOULD BE ABLE TO BOARD A KENYA AIRWAYS FLIGHT FOR

NAIROBI WITHOUT VISAS. THE KENYAN VISAS WILL COST

US50.00 EACH.

2. (C) AMBASSADOR WARIO TOLD AMBASSADOR MCDONALD THAT

AS OF 11:00 A.M. JUNE 13, HE HAD RECEIVED NO REPEAT NO

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 HARARE 03162 131211Z

INSTRUCTION FROM HIS FOREIGN MINISTRY REGARDING THE CASE

OF THE CUBAN DOCTORS (REFTEL). "THERE IS NOTHING I CAN

DO," HE SHRUGGED. MOREOVER, CONTRADICTING HIS EARLIER

COMMENTS, THE KENYAN HIGH COMMISSIONER NOW REQUIRES

DOCTORS CORDOVA AND PENA TO BE PRESENT WHEN APPLYING FOR

THEIR KENYAN VISA. THE TWO DOCTORS CONTINUE TO BE HELD

BY ZIMBABWEAN IMMIGRATION AUTHORITIES AT GOROMONZI

DETENTION CENTER.

3. (C) ACTION REQUESTED FOR NAIROBI: POST WOULD

APPRECIATE EMBASSY NAIROBI CONVEYING THE SENSE OF HIGH

COMMISSIONER WARIO\'S RESPONSE WITH THE RESPECT TO THE

CUBANS TO APPROPRIATE KENYAN FOREIGN MINISTRY OFFICIALS.

IF THE KENYAN FOREIGN MINISTRY AGREES TO INSTRUCT ITS

HIGH COMMISSIONER IN HARARE IN WRITING, POST WOULD

APPRECIATE A COPY OF THAT INSTRUCTION TO PRESENT TO THE

KENYAN HIGH COMMISSION WHEN WE AGAIN ATTEMPT TO APPLY

FOR VISAS FOR THE CUBAN DOCTORS.

4. (C) ACTION REQUESTED FOR DEPARTMENT: IF THE GOK

CANNOT GET ITS ACT TOGETHER IN THE NEXT 24 HOURS, POST

WOULD APPRECIATE AUTHORIZATION TO APPROACH THE HIGH

COMMISSION OF CANADA. WE PROPOSE THAT CANADA ALLOW THE

CUBAN DOCTORS TO STAY A SHORT PERIOD OF TIME IN THEIR

TERRITORY IN ORDER TO COMPLETE THEIR UNITED STATES

IMMIGRATION PROCESSING REQUIREMENTS.

MCDONALD
