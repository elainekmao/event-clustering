INTRODUCTION AND SUMMARY

------------------------

1. (LOU) THIS IS ONE IN A SERIES OF REPORTS ON

SPECIFIC AND GROSS HUMAN RIGHTS ABUSES IN BOSNIA AND

HERCEGOVINA (B-H). ON OCTOBER 27, THE EMBASSY

RECEIVED AN EIGHT-PAGE LETTER IN CROATIAN, ADDRESSED

TO PRESIDENT BUSH, FROM SAID MUMINOVIC, A MUSLIM

REFUGEE FROM BRCKO, BOSNIA. IN THE LETTER, MUMINOVIC

DESCRIBED IN DETAIL HIS INTERNMENT IN THE BRCKO CAMP

IN NORTHERN BOSNIA. HE WROTE THAT HE HAD BEEN

INTERNED FOR SEVERAL DAYS BEGINNING MAY 5. BELOW IS

AN INFORMAL TRANSLATION OF THE SIGNIFICANT PARTS OF

MUMINOVIC\'S LETTER. HE DESCRIBES HIS DETENTION AND

HOW HE WITNESSED FOUR EXECUTIONS. HE APPEALS FOR THE

U.S. TO "DO SOMETHING." END INTRODUCTION AND SUMMARY.

MR. PRESIDENT OF THE USA:

SINCE I AM AN EYEWITNESS, I WANT TO GIVE MY STATEMENT

ABOUT THE MASSACRE IN BRCKO...

THE YUGOSLAV ARMY OCCUPIED THE GREATER PART OF BRCKO

ON MAY 1, 1992. THE CITIZENS WERE UNPREPARED FOR THE

SERBIAN OCCUPATION BECAUSE YUGO ARMY REPRESENTATIVES

HAD PROMISED OVER THE RADIO SECURITY AND PEACE JUST

THREE DAYS BEFORE THE OCCUPATION. I WAS AT HOME WITH

MY WIFE AND TWO UNDERAGE DAUGHTERS.

FOR FIVE DAYS WE WERE CLOSED INSIDE OUR HOME,

LISTENING TO THE SHOOTINGS AND THE GRENADES WITH

WHICH THE CITY WAS BEING SYSTEMATICALLY DESTROYED.

HIDDEN BEHIND OUR WINDOWS, WE WATCHED THE ARRIVAL OF

TANKS AND BUSES FULL OF BLOOD-THIRSTY CHETNIKS FROM

NEIGHBORING BIJELJINA, WHICH WAS ALREADY OCCUPIED,

AND WHERE 1,000 MUSLIMS HAD BEEN KILLED.

ON MAY 5 A REPRESENTATIVE OF THE YUGO ARMY IN A RADIO

BROADCAST INSTRUCTED THE CITIZENS IN MY PART OF THE

TOWN TO GO TO THE ARMY BARRACKS, FROM WHERE THE YUGO

ARMY WOULD ORGANIZE AN EVACUATION TO A SAFER PLACE.

KNEW THAT THE SERBS HAD SPECIAL GROUPS USED TO

MASSACRE CIVILIANS. THESE GROUPS OF LUNATICS AND

PSYCHOPATHS GO FROM APARTMENT TO APARTMENT, CELLAR TO

CELLAR, AND, HIDDEN BEHIND THICK WALLS, COMMIT CRIMES

AGAINST THE POWERLESS CIVILIANS. UNDER THESE

CIRCUMSTANCES, GOING TO THE YUGO ARMY BARRACKS SEEMED

LIKE A BETTER CHANCE TO LEAVE THIS TOWN IN WHICH ONE

COULD ALREADY SMELL DEATH.

UPON OUR ARRIVAL AT THE BARRACKS, WE REALIZED WE WERE

ALL IN A TRAP BECAUSE THERE, TOGETHER WITH THE

REGULAR ARMY, WERE CHETNIKS AND OTHER SERBIAN REFUSE.

THEY OFFERED TO GIVE US RIFLES IF WE WOULD FIGHT

AGAINST OUR OWN PEOPLE FOR THE SERBIAN CAUSE. THE

MUSLIMS AND CROATS SILENTLY REFUSED. OUR WIVES AND

CHILDREN WERE PUT ON A BUS AND WERE TAKEN TO AN

UNKNOWN DESTINATION.

WE NOTICED, FROM THE PUDDLES OF BLOOD ON THE FLOOR,

COUPLE HUNDRED OF US WHO WERE LOCKED UP, THE LONG

USUAL WEAPONS IN THEIR BELTS, THESE UNIFORMED PEOPLE

ALSO CARRIED BIG KITCHEN KNIVES. WE KNEW THEIR

PURPOSE AND EXPECTED THE WORST.

THE TORTURE STARTED WITH ORAL THREATS. THE SERBS

WOULD SING SONGS DEDICATED TO DRAZA MIHAJLOVIC, THE

HIM IN THE BACK WITH A MACHINE GUN.

LOOKING AT THE HOLES IN HIS BACK, MY WHOLE LIFE

PASSED IN FRONT OF MY EYES. I THOUGHT I WOULD NEVER

SEE EITHER MY CHILDREN OR MY FAMILY AGAIN AND THAT

ALL THAT WAS HAPPENING TO ME WAS DEFINITELY NOT A

DREAM BUT A HORRID REALITY. MY EYES CLOSED, I LOST

CONSCIOUSNESS, AND MY BODY CRUMBLED ONTO PIECES OF

GLASS. MY FAINTING AWAKENED A BIT OF HUMANITY IN THE

SERBS\' LEADER. HE ORDERED THEM TO BRING ME BACK TO

THE HALL, WHERE I COULD REST A LITTLE. ONE OF THE

SERBS TOOK SATISFACTION IN REMOVING MY GLASSES AND

BREAKING THEM.

THE OTHER THREE MEN WERE KILLED IN THE MOST CRUEL

MANNER. FIRST THEY WERE BEATEN IN AN ANOTHER ROOM

AND LEFT TO RECOVER A LITTLE. THEN THEY WERE TAKEN

TO A COURTYARD WHERE WE HEARD THE WORST SOUNDS THAT A

HUMAN THROAT CAN PRODUCE. WE HEARD THE DULL SLASHES

MEN WERE HELD BY THEIR LEGS AND BEATEN WHILE AGAINST

THE WALL OF THE BUILDING WHERE WE WERE IMPRISONED.

THE NEXT AFTERNOON, FOUR OF US MANAGED TO GET SOME

SYMPATHY FROM ONE OF THE SERBIAN POLICEMEN. HE LET

US GET ON A BUS GOING TO BIJELJINA, ON WHICH THERE

WAS A GROUP OF WOMEN AND CHILDREN. FROM THERE I

LUCKILY MANAGED TO ESCAPE, EVEN FROM BIJELJINA, WHERE

THE SERBIAN AUTHORITIES STILL TAKE PEOPLE FROM THEIR

HOMES, USUALLY DURING THE NIGHT, WHO NEVER RETURN. I

MANAGED TO GET TO THE FREE PART OF CROATIA THROUGH

HUNGARY AND SLOVENIA.

ALL THAT I HAVE WRITTEN HERE CAN BE CONFIRMED BY

THREE OTHER WITNESSES WHO ALSO MANAGED TO ESCAPE FROM

THAT HELL. I THINK WE WERE LUCKY THAT WE WENT

THROUGH ALL THIS IN THE FIRST DAYS OF THE SERBIAN

OCCUPATION, WHILE THE SERBIAN KILLING MACHINERY WAS

NOT SO WELL DEVELOPED YET. I DO NOT KNOW THE DESTINY

OF THE OTHERS WHO REMAINED THERE. A FEW GYPSIES

ACCEPTED THE SERBIAN OFFER TO JOIN THEIR ARMED FORCES

AND THUS SAVED THEIR NECKS FROM THE CHETNIKS...

I AM NOT SUCH A GREAT WITNESS SINCE I WITNESSED ONLY

FOUR KILLINGS, OF WHICH THREE INDIVIDUALS I KNEW

PERSONALLY. THAT IS MORE THAN ENOUGH FOR ME TO

DISCOVER EVIL AND HELL.
