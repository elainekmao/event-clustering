SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION ON MIDDLE EAST

Summary

-- Lead story in all papers today, June 19, highlights

reports about yesterday\'s suicide bus bombing in

Jerusalem. Headlines refer to it as "the Jerusalem

operation". Reports also focus on Israel\'s decision

to retaliate in Palestinian Authority areas.

Editorial commentaries continue to criticize Israel\'s

occupation and its aggression against the Palestinian

people. One columnist today holds the United States

"fully responsible" for the destruction and

assassinations taking place in the Palestinian

territories.

Editorial Commentary

-- "Giving Sharon the excuse!"

Daily columnist Yaser Za\'atreh writes on the op-ed

page of center-left, influential Arabic daily Al-

Dustour (06/19): "They said that the heroic Jerusalem

operation is going to give Sharon the excuse he needs

to escalate his aggressions against the Palestinians

people. Yet, the Palestinians wonder when did Sharon

stop those aggressions in the first place. Giving in

to Sharon\'s security and then political conditions is

the only guaranteed way to stop the aggressions

against the Palestinian people. However, it is a way

that is not acceptable to the principles, values,

dignity and pride know to all free people. The heroic

Jerusalem operation and operations before it fall

within the category of resistance, combating

occupation and convincing the whole world that ending

the occupation is the only solution. The Palestinian

people and their leadership are being humiliated, and

there is no response to that other than continuing the

resistance."

-- "Will the Israeli ever learn the lesson?"

Daily columnist Jamil Nimri writes on the back page of

independent, mass-appeal Arabic daily Al-Arab Al-Yawm

906/19): "Yesterday\'s operation in Jerusalem says it

all about the effectiveness of the dividing wall that

Sharon is building. Insisting on dealing with the

Palestinian people with brutality, oppression and

humiliation has only succeeded in entrenching the

anger and the rage and the willingness to die, so that

more and more young men ready for martyrdom

operations."

-- "The American green light"

Daily columnist Fahd Fanek writes on the back page of

semi-official, influential Arabic daily Al-Ra\'i

(06/19): "Bush is the one asking the Arabs and the

Palestinians to bear their responsibilities so that

his vision of the Palestinian state can come into

reality. Israel is never asked to do anything. It is

doing more than its share. Even more than that,

Sharon is a man of peace according to Bush, and Arafat

is a terrorist. This sums up America\'s vision and its

policies towards this region. Saying that American

gives Sharon the green light to act is actually

belittling American\'s role in the events of the

Israeli aggression. In fact, American is not only

allowing Sharon to do what he sees fit, but is also

instigating him, arming him, financing him and

providing him with a moral and legal cover in order to

protect him from the United Nations and the world.

America bears full responsibility for what is

happening in the West Bank."
