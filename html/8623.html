SUBJECT: DUTCH RESPONSE ON IAEA SAFEGUARDS BUDGET INCREASE

REF: SECSTATE 150262

(SBU) PolOff delivered reftel demarche to MFA\'s

Nonproliferation Director Paul Wilke on June 5. While the

Dutch MFA supports the safeguards budget increase, their

Ministry of Finance does not. Accordingly, the GONL is not

in a position to demarche other countries at this time. If

the IAEA approves the budget increase, this may improve the

MFA\'s leverage with the Ministry of Finance.
