SENSITIVE BUT UNCLASSIFIED; PLEASE HANDLE ACCORDINGLY

1. (SBU) In response to letter delivered APRIL 16 as

requested in REF B, recipient Dr. Muwaffak Haddadin, Director

General, Arab Company for Drug Industries and Medical

Appliances (ACIDMA) asked that we pass along his response on

behalf of the company addressed to OFAC\'s David Mills.

Haddadin told us that he presented the letter to the

company\'s Board of Directors, and subsequently the Board

voted to resubmit the request. Original will be sent via

pouch to OFAC. Body of the letter follows:

Begin text:

Thank you for your letter FAC NO. IQ-210714 dated March 10,

2003, which was handed to me by Mr. Gregory G. Garramone,

Economic Officer, Embassy of the United States of America in

Amman, Jordan.

In the light of the new developments in Iraq, and the new

position of the American Government to lift the sanctions on

Iraq, the Arab Company for Drug Industries and Medical

Appliances (ACIDMA) would like to repeat its request for a

specific license to unblock funds in the amount of US

$4,464,726.50 from the blocked account of Rafidain Bank at

the Bank of New York as per its letter to the U.S. Department

of Treasury No.3/2/249 dated January 20, 2003 (copy attached

for easy reference).

Thanking you for your consideration.

Sincerely,

Dr. Muwaffak J. Haddadin

ACDIMA/Director General

End text.
