1. AS THE COUNTDOWN TO ZIMBABWE\'S PRESIDENTIAL POLL

COMES TO A CLOSE, THIS ELECTION CONTINUES TO DOMINATE

EDITORIAL COLUMNS IN MOST MAINSTREAM NEWSPAPERS.

SAMPLES OF THE EDITORIALS FOLLOW:

2. UNDER HEADLINE "THE CHOICE IS SIMPLE: BETWEEN A

FIST AND A PALM," THE MARCH 8 EDITION OF THE

INDEPENDENT WEEKLY THE "ZIMBABWE INDEPENDENT" COMMENTS:

"THE CHOICE THIS WEEKEND COULDN\'T BE MORE SIMPLE:

BETWEEN THE FIST ON THE ONE HAND AND THE OUTSTRETCHED

PALM ON THE OTHER. BETWEEN VIOLENCE AND PEACE. THIS

IS A CHOICE BETWEEN TWO DIAMETRICALLY OPPOSED SYSTEMS.

BETWEEN A BLIND AND MALIGNANT NATIONALISM ROOTED IN THE

PAST, AND A FUTURE FULL OF PROMISE BASED ON DEMOCRACY

AND RESPECT FOR HUMAN RIGHTS. MAKE NO MISTAKE, SHOULD

PRESIDENT ROBERT MUGABE WIN ANOTHER SIX-YEAR TERM

ZIMBABWEANS WILL BE DEPRIVED OF THEIR HOPE FOR

SOMETHING BETTER. . ."

3. UNDER HEADLINE "REPORTS OF RIGGING MERE

FABRICATIONS," THE MARCH 8 EDITION OF THE GOVERNMENT-

CONTROLLED DAILY "THE HERALD" COMMENTS:

".ALREADY, THE OPPOSITION MDC AND ITS WESTERN MASTERS

HAVE LAUNCHED A MASSIVE PROPAGANDA BLITZ THAT THIS

WEEKEND\'S PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION WILL BE A FRAUD. . .

THAT IS TO BE EXPECTED, FOR IT IS PART OF THEIR AGENDA

TO CRITICIZE ANYTHING THAT IS NOT IN THEIR FAVOR. . .

WE APPEAL TO ALL REGISTERED VOTERS TO TURN UP IN THEIR

THOUSANDS AND CAST THEIR VOTES TOMORROW AND SUNDAY.

THEY SHOULD IGNORE WHAT THE OTHER FORCES ARE SAYING.

THEY ARE ONLY BENT ON DISTURBING ZIMBABWE\'S PEACE.

ZIMBABWEANS NEED TO GIVE AN EMPHATIC NO TO THOSE WHO

WANT TO REVERSE THE GAINS OF INDEPENDENCE AND TO RE-

COLONIZE THE COUNTRY. WE ARE SURE THAT WHEN ALL THIS

HULLABALOO IS OVER, ZIMBABWEANS WILL HAVE BEEN DECLARED

THE WINNER. WHAT ZIMBABWEANS DESPERATELY NEED IS PEACE

AND CALM."

4. AFTER RUNNING AN ARTICLE ACCUSING THE UNITED

STATES AND BRITAIN OF MASTERMINDING "A MAJOR MILITARY

OFFENSIVE" AGAINST THE MUGABE ADMINISTRATION IF IT

REMAINS IN POWER AFTER THE PRESIDENTIAL POLL, THE

BULAWAYO GOVERNMENT-CONTROLLED DAILY "CHRONICLE"

DEDICATED ITS MARCH 8 EDITORIAL TO CALLING ON

ZIMBABWEANS, ESPECIALLY THOSE LIVING IN MATABELELAND,

TO RESIST BEING PLUNGED INTO ANOTHER ARMED CONFLICT.

UNDER HEADLINE "SAY NO TO WAR," THE PAPER COMMENTS:

". . .FORMER COLONIAL MASTERS BRITAIN AND THEIR RACIST

COUSINS, THE UNITED STATES, ARE PREPARING A MILITARY

ATTACK ON ZIMBABWE. ONCE AGAIN MATABELELAND IS BEING

USED AS THE SCAPEGOAT. THE DISTURBANCES IN THE EARLY

1980S. . .ARE BEING USED AS THE \'REASON\' FOR THE

INSURGENCY. TO PLAN MILITARY ACTION BASED ON FLIMSY

\'EVIDENCE\' AND A HISTORY THAT ALL CONCERNED ARE TRYING

TO RESOLVE AMICABLY IS HYPOCRISY AT ITS WORST. WE

DEMAND THAT BRITAIN AND THE U.S. COME CLEAN ON THIS ONE

FOR ZIMBABWEANS WILL NOT JUST LOOK WHILE THEY ATTACK

US. . . BRITAIN AND THE U.S. ARE BACK TO THEIR EVIL
