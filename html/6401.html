SUBJECT: ATLANTIC SUMMIT: DEMARCHE TO JORDAN

REF: STATE 69421

Classified By: A/DCM Doug Silliman for reasons 1.5 (B)(D)

1. (C) In the absence of the King, Prime Minister, and

Foreign Minister (all in Abu Dhabi), A/DCM March 16 conveyed

ref points on the Atlantic Summit to Ali al-Ayed, Personal

Assistant to Foreign Minister Marwan Muasher, urging that the

GOJ support publicly the Atlantic Summit\'s forthcoming

statement on Iraq and the future of the Iraqi people.

Al-Ayed responded that the GOJ would read the statement

carefully to see whether Jordan could associate itself with

it.
