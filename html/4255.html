START MEMO NOMINATING-JUSTIFICATION:

Ms. Ismail, an accountant by profession, represents the

Ceylon Chamber of Commerce (CCC), which is the oldest

and apex chamber in Sri Lanka. Ms. Ismail has been

with the CCC for eight years, held many positions and

assumed the post of Senior Assistant Secretary-

General/Financial Controller in 1998.

Other than the function as a Financial Controller, Ms.

Ismail has been involved in conceptualizing and

implementing many programs. Ms. Ismail is actively

involved in the Industrial Association and many

services related activities of the CCC. She also

handles the taxation, finance, banking, capital markets

and insurance, and business ethics and governance sub-

committees of the CCC.

Ms. Ismail has excellent future prospects. She is

tipped to become the Secretary-General of the CCC very

soon and will be the first time a woman who will head a

prominent chamber in Sri Lanka. Ms. Ismail has no

previous training or work experience in the U.S. The

IV program on U.S. Financial System will provide a good

opportunity for Ms. Ismail to sharpen her skills and

gain wider exposure to the world economy to serve the

business community and act a catalyst of economic

growth.

END MEMO NOMINATING-JUSTIFICATION:
