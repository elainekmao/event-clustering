SUBJECT: OFFICIAL GREETINGS - HOLY SEE

1. AN OFFICIAL GREETING IS SENT ANNUALLY TO THE HOLY SEE

ON THE OCCASION OF THE ANNIVERSARY OF THE ELECTION OF THE

REIGNING PONTIFF. THIS TAKES THE PLACE OF THE NATIONAL DAY

GREETING. FOR THE PONTIFICATE OF JOHN PAUL II THE DATE IS

OCTOBER 16. NOTE: THIS DATE WILL CHANGE WITH THE ELECTION OF

HIS SUCCESSOR. END NOTE.

2. NOTE: THE POPE\'S BIRTHDAY AND OTHER OCCASIONS DO NOT

CALL FOR AN OFFICIAL GREETING. THE AMBASSADOR TO THE HOLY

SEE MAY WISH TO SEND GREETINGS TO THE POPE ON OTHER

OCCASIONS AS A PERSONAL COURTESY. FORMER PRESIDENT CLINTON,

ALONG WITH MANY OTHER WORLD LEADERS, SENT A MESSAGE TO POPE
