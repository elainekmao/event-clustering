Classified By: H. EGGLESTON. REASONS 1.5 (B, D)

1. (S/NF) This is the fifth request for imagery support for the

Royal Nepalese Army (RNA) to conduct offensive operations against

Maoist rebels.

2. (S/NF) This request is the direct result of a large-scale attack

on GON personnel in Achham District. RNA anticipates that Maoist

insurgents are moving to the area indicated by the following

coordinates:

- 81 degrees 10 min 30 sec to 81 degrees 30 min 00 sec East

- and 29 degrees 05 min 00 sec to 29 degrees 12 min 30 sec North.

- Refer to Map Sheet Numbers 2981-13B and D, 2981-14 A and C.

3. (S/NF) The Maoist attacks inflicted heavy RNA, police and civilian

casualties (see septel). Initial, but necessarily sketchy, reports

indicate that the attacks were carried out by one thousand-plus

Maoist cadre and an additional several hundred load bearers. An

unconfirmed report indicates that the Maoists used mortars, captured

from the RNA in November, in this attack.
