SUBJECT: ANTI-TRAFFICKING PROJECT FUNDING REQUEST

1. SUMMARY AND INTRODUCTION: POST SUBMITS THE FOLLOWING

PROJECT PROPOSAL FOR FUNDING CONSIDERATION. WE RECEIVED THIS

PROPOSAL FROM CARITAS BRINDISI, THE LOCAL BRANCH OF THE

VATICAN\'S MAJOR HUMANITARIAN RELIEF AND DEVELOPMENT NGO.

CARITAS BRINDISI HAS A WELL-ESTABLISHED RELATIONSHIP WITH

THE REPARATION SISTER OF MARY, THE PROJECT\'S PROTAGONISTS.

POST INTENDS TO WORK WITH CARITAS TO SUPPORT THIS NEW ANTI-

TRAFFICKING EDUCATION PROGRAM. WE ARE AWARE THAT ECA IS NOT

ABLE TO FUND THE PURCHASE OF THE 4X4 VEHICLE MENTIONED IN

THE PROPOSAL. HOWEVER, WE HAVE LEFT IT IN THE PROJECT\'S

OVERALL BUDGET (TO BE FUNDED BY SOMEONE ELSE - THE

AUSTRALIAN EMBASSY HAS INDICATED INTEREST IN SUPPORTING THE

PROJECT AND WE WILL FOLLOW UP ON THIS WITH THEM. THE

REPARATION SERVANT SISTERS OF MARY HAVE WORKED FOR TEN YEARS

IN ALBANIA WITH AT RISK WOMEN AND ARE FULLY VERSED IN

ALBANIAN SOCIAL DYNAMICS. THEY SEEK USG FUNDING TO CREATE A

PROJECT IN ADDITION TO THEIR CURRENT WORK THAT WILL FOCUS ON

ANTI-TRAFFICKING EDUCATION AND COUNSELING FOR POTENTIAL

VICTIMS. END SUMMARY AND INTRODUCTION.

2. "SLAVES NEVER AGAIN" IS A MULTI-FACETED ANTI-

TRAFFICKING FRAMEWORK THAT ESTABLISHES ACTIVITY AND TRAINING

CENTERS, A WOMEN\'S MEDICAL CLINIC AND OTHER INITIATIVES

WHERE WOMEN ARE THE PROTAGONISTS. IT ALSO TARGETS ALBANIAN

MEN TO ENCOURAGE A MORE POSITIVE DISPOSITION TOWARD ALBANIAN

WOMEN. THE PROJECT SUPERVISOR IS SISTER BARBARA PAVAN, KISHA

KATOLIKE, PUSI I MEZINIT, VLORA. TEL. 011.355.632.3861 FAX.

011.355.632.5681 E-MAIL: ELISA-SMR@AUL.SANX.NET

3. STAFF AND TRAINING: THE 20 MEMBER TEAM INCLUDES THE

SISTERS AND IS COMPOSED OF 12 TEACHERS, 5 SOCIAL WORKERS, A

DOCTOR, 2 NURSES, PLUS A NUMBER OF ACTIVITY COORDINATORS.

FURTHER TRAINING WILL GIVE THEM WOMEN\'S DEVELOPMENT SKILLS,

INCLUDING SOCIAL PSYCHOLOGY, THE PHENOMENOLOGY OF

PROSTITUTION, AND INTERVENTION SKILLS. TRAINING WILL BE

PROVIDED BY EXPERTS BASED IN TIRANA (FOR EXAMPLE, THOSE

WORKING WITH THE IOM) OR ABROAD AND LAST APPROXIMATELY 30

DAYS. INITIAL TRAINING WILL BE FOLLOWED BY A WEEKLONG

INTENSIVE COURSE FOCUSED ON ANTI-TRAFFICKING COUNSELING

SKILLS AND STRATEGIES. ASSESSMENT AND EVALUATION OF

INDIVIDUAL SKILLS WILL TAKE PLACE EVERY 40 DAYS. SALARIES

ARE BASED ON ALBANIAN SCALES AND REFLECT EMPLOYMENT OF

LOCALLY HIRED PART-TIME STAFF. THE NUNS RECEIVE A MINIMAL

STIPEND, WHICH IS INCLUDED IN THE OVERALL SALARY COSTS. ALL

STAFF INVOLVED IN THE PROJECT SPEAK ALBANIAN.

4. PREVENTION MODULE EDUCATION: IN ADDITION TO COUNSELING,

EDUCATION FOR PREVENTION IS A PRIORITY AT THIS STAGE OF

ANTI-TRAFFICKING EFFORTS. THE SISTERS WILL CAPITALIZE ON

THEIR EXISTING CONTACTS WITH 3500 PLUS YOUNG PEOPLE. IN

VLORA, THE SISTERS HAVE ACCESS TO 7 JUNIOR HIGH AND HIGH

SCHOOLS. THEY ALSO HAVE CONTACTS IN A NEWLY ESTABLISHED

OUTER SUBURB (FUSCH AVIACIONE) HOUSING POOR FAMILIES NEWLY

ARRIVED FROM SOUTHERN ALBANIA AND THE MOUNTAIN REGION, WHO

MOVED TO VLORA IN SEARCH OF WORK. THE SISTERS ARE WORKING

WITH MUNICIPAL AUTHORITIES TO ESTABLISH SCHOOLS AND BASIC

SERVICES. THEY HAVE SET UP A SMALL EDUCATION CENTER TO SERVE

A LARGE GROUP OF GIRLS WHO HAVE NO SOCIAL LIFE AND LITTLE

EDUCATION, AND THUS PERFECT TARGETS FOR TRAFFICKERS.

5. EDUCATION WILL FOCUS ON THE RIGHTS OF MINORS AND WOMEN

AS OUTLINED IN THE UNIVERSAL DECLARATION ON HUMAN RIGHTS.

THE COURSES WILL PRESENT INFORMATION, HELP THE STUDENTS

ASSIMILATE IT AND ASSIST THEM IN APPLYING CONCEPTS TO THEIR

EVERYDAY SITUATIONS. THE PROJECT WILL SEEK THE PROFESSIONAL

ASSISTANCE OF PEOPLE

ALREADY WORKING IN THE ANTI-TRAFFICKING

FIELD (E.G. VERA LESKO, DIRECTOR OF THE ANTI-TRAFFICKING NGO

"VATRA"). THE SISTERS RECOGNIZE THAT MESSAGES AND

INFORMATION MUST ACKNOWLEDGE THE REALITIES OF ALBANIAN LIFE.

THE SISTERS WILL USE THEIR UNDERSTANDING OF THE "CULTURAL

CODES" AND THE SOCIOLOGY OF THE ALBANIAN FAMILY TO TAILOR AN

APPROPRIATE PROGRAM INCLUDING PSYCHOLOGICAL, MORAL AND

SOCIOLOGICAL STRATEGIES.

6. EDUCATIONAL MATERIAL WILL BE PURCHASED OR PRINTED. THIS

INCLUDES FLYERS AND POSTERS, BOOKS, AND ARTICLES. THE

LATTER TO MAKE UP A SMALL NEWSPAPER TO LAUNCH A NETWORK OF

INFORMATION AND IDEAS. THE TEAM WILL PROMOTE LOCAL MEDIA

INVOLVEMENT AND PRODUCE BROADCASTS TO EDUCATE YOUNG PEOPLE

AND SENSITIZE ADULTS.
