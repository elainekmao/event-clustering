SUBJECT: PDAS RIES VISIT: ECONOMIC PRIORITIES FOR ITALY\'S

EU PRESIDENCY

CLASSIFIED BY: CHARGE D\'AFFAIRS EMIL SKODON FOR REASONS 1.5 (B, D)

1. (C) SUMMARY. GOI OFFICIALS BELIEVE THAT AS EU PRESIDENT,

ITALY\'S TOUGHEST ECONOMIC CHALLENGE WILL BE TO HELP RESTORE

CONSUMER AND INVESTOR CONFIDENCE IN THE MARKETS. A CREDIBLE

MESSAGE BY THE ITALIAN PRESIDENCY AND COMMITMENT BY ALL EU

MEMBERS TO JUMP-START EURO-ZONE ECONOMIES ARE NEEDED TO WIN

BACK CONFIDENCE, MFA AND FINANCE MINISTRY OFFICIALS TOLD

VISITING PRINCIPAL DEPUTY ASSISTANT SECRETARY RIES DURING A

MAY 19TH ROUNDTABLE DINNER DISCUSSION. THE GOI ALSO WILL

SEEK A STRONG US/EU COMMON STATEMENT IN SUPPORT OF THE DOHA

TRADE ROUND PRIOR TO THE CANCUN MEETING, FOLLOWED BY A

SUCCESSFUL MEETING ITSELF. ALSO DISCUSSED WERE THE

POSSIBILITY OF REVITALIZING THE TRANSATLANTIC BUSINESS

DIALOGUE, ITALY\'S E-GOVERNMENT PLANS, TRADE ISSUES AND THE

MID-TERM REVIEW OF THE COMMON AGRICULTURAL PROGRAM. END

SUMMARY.

3. (C) THE CONTINUED RECESSION, THE RISING EURO (AND

PARTICULARLY ITS IMPACT ON EUROPEAN EXPORTERS), AND INCREASED

UNEMPLOYMENT, ALL HAVE LEFT EUROPEAN CONSUMERS AND INVESTORS

JITTERY ABOUT THE MARKETS, COMMENTED MAGLIANO AND

BINI-SMAGHI. ONE OF ITALY\'S KEY TASKS AS THE EU PRESIDENT

AND A G7 COUNTRY WILL BE TO MAKE A CREDIBLE STATEMENT AND

TAKE REAL STEPS THAT WILL HELP RESTORE CONFIDENCE, ARGUED

MAGLIANO. NOTING THAT ATMOSPHERICS AT THE G7 FINANCE

MINISTERS MEETING WERE POSITIVE, WITH NO VISIBLE LINGERING

EFFECTS OF IRAQ, BINI-SMAGHI ASSERTED THAT WE SHOULD TAKE

THIS AS A MESSAGE AND FOCUS ON ECONOMIC ISSUES DURING OUR

PRESIDENCY TO HELP RESTORE U.S./EU RELATIONS TO GOOD HEALTH.

4. (C) AT THE SAME TIME, THERE ARE NO SHORTAGES OF CONCERNS,

BINI-SMAGHI CONTINUED. THE DOLLAR\'S FUTURE VALUE IS THE

GREAT UNKNOWN. AT THE END OF LAST WEEK\'S MEETING, G7 FINANCE

MINISTRY OFFICIALS THOUGHT THE U.S. STILL WOULD DEFEND A

STRONG DOLLAR. ALL WERE SURPRISED BY THE WEEKEND\'S PRESS

REPORTS THAT THE U.S. ADMINISTRATION HAD BACKTRACKED FROM

THIS POSITION AND WOULD "LET THE DOLLAR FALL." THE

"AMBIGUITY" OF THE U.S. POSITION, AND THE FLUCTUATIONS OF THE

DOLLAR\'S VALUE VIS-A-VIS THE EURO, WORRY EUROPEAN CONSUMERS

AND EXPORTERS, BINI-SMAGHI CONTENDED. EUROPEAN EXPORTERS

AND JOBS THAT DEPEND ON EXPORTS STAND TO LOSE THE MOST. HE

ALSO OBSERVED THAT WIDESPREAD FLUCTUATIONS HAVE ONLY OCCURRED

IN THE DOLLAR/EURO VALUES. ASIAN CURRENCIES ON THE WHOLE

HAVE MAINTAINED MORE EQUILIBRIUM WITH THE DOLLAR. TO

COMPOUND MATTERS, MANY ASIAN INVESTORS ARE DIVERSIFYING THEIR

FINANCIAL ASSETS TO INCLUDE EURO-DENOMINATED ASSETS, WHICH

PUTS EVEN MORE UPWARD PRESSURE ON THE EURO.

5. (C) MAGLIANO AND BINI-SMAGHI AGREED THAT ITALY, A G7

COUNTRY HOLDING THE EU PRESIDENCY, HAS A PARTICULAR

OBLIGATION TO REASSURE CONSUMERS AND INVESTORS. (COMMENT:

THE LAST G7 COUNTRY THAT HELD THE EU PRESIDENCY WAS FRANCE

THREE YEARS AGO, WHICH WAS AT THE TAIL END OF ECONOMIC BOOM

YEARS. END COMMENT.) WHEN QUERIED ABOUT SPECIFIC PROPOSALS

UNDER THE PRESIDENCY, BINI-SMAGHI, KEEPING THE GOI\'S CARDS

FAIRLY CLOSE TO THE CHEST, MENTIONED VAGUE PLANS ABOUT

INFRASTRUCTURE INVESTMENT AND A WRY AND WEARY ACKNOWLEDGMENT

OF THE NEED FOR PENSION REFORM. HE CONCEDED THAT A STRONGER

EURO MIGHT FORCE THE HAND OF GOVERNMENTS TO ADOPT SOME MAJOR

STRUCTURAL REFORMS.

GOI OFFICIALS ARE CONCERNED ABOUT THE "WORST-CASE" ECONOMIC

SCENARIO UNDER THE ITALIAN PRESIDENCY: A BREAKDOWN IN THE

DOHA TRADE ROUND, AN UNCONTROLLED FREE-FALL OF THE U.S.

DOLLAR WITH A DOLLAR/EURO EXCHANGE RATE OF LEVELS UP TO

$1.40/E1, AND UNEMPLOYMENT IN EUROPE EXACERBATED BY A STRONG

EURO. ALTHOUGH NONE OF THESE IS SPECIFICALLY UNDER ITALY\'S

CONTROL, THE GOI DOES NOT WANT TO BE IN THE CHAIR IF EUROPE\'S

ECONOMY TRULY STALLS.

DEFENSE SPENDING AND STABILITY PACT

-----------------------------------

7. (C) ASKED ABOUT NEWS REPORTS EARLIER THAT DAY THAT GERMAN,

ITALIAN AND FRENCH DEFENSE MINISTERS ARE ADVOCATING THAT

DEFENSE EXPENDITURES BE EXCLUDED FROM STABILITY PACT

REQUIREMENTS, ALLOWING THE BIG THREE TO INCREASE DEFENSE

EXPENDITURES, BINI-SMAGHI POLITELY SCOFFED AS THIS PURPORTED

AGREEMENT, CALLING IT A "PRIVATE INITIATIVE." "THEY CAN SAY

WHAT THEY LIKE," HE SHRUGGED, BUT STRESSED THAT FINANCE

MINISTERS, NOT DEFENSE MINISTERS, ARE THE ONES OBLIGED TO

FULFILL STABILITY PACT REQUIREMENTS. COMMENT: THE COMMISSION

HAS RULED OUT EASING THE MAASTRICHT CRITERIA BY EXCLUDING

DEFENSE FROM OVERALL EXPENDITURES. EARLIER THIS MONTH,

DIRECTOR GENERAL ARRIGO SADUN OF THE FINANCE MINISTRY TOLD US

THAT FINANCE MINISTER TREMONTI IS LOOKING AT CREATIVE WAYS TO

FINANCE INCREASED DEFENSE EXPENDITURES, WITHOUT VIOLATING

PAGE 03 ROME 02294 02 OF 03 230857Z

STABILITY PACT COMMITMENTS. DURING THE 2002 BUDGET DEBATE IN

ITALY, TREMONTI PROPOSED SIGNIFICANT CUTS IN DEFENSE SPENDING

TO MEET STABILITY PACT REQUIREMENTS. HE BACKTRACKED,

HOWEVER, AFTER INTENSE PRESSURE FROM DEFENSE MINISTER MARTINO

AND LATER, FROM PRIME MINISTER BERLUSCONI, WHO HAD PROMISED

NATO SYG ROBERTSON THAT ITALY WOULD IMPROVE ITS MILITARY

CAPABILITIES. END COMMENT.

ECONOMIC ASSISTANCE

-------------------

8. (SBU) IN RESPONSE TO PDAS RIES\' SUGGESTION THAT THE US AND

EU SHOULD DO MORE TO JOIN EFFORTS ON ECONOMIC ASSISTANCE TO

THIRD COUNTRIES, MAGLIANO CONCURRED, NOTING THAT JOINT

PROJECTS COULD HELP BRIDGE THE CURRENT GAP BETWEEN THE U.S.

AND THE EU. HE SPECIFICALLY POINTED TO THE CASE OF IRAQI

RECONSTRUCTION PROJECTS.

9. (SBU) STEFANO GATTI, DIPLOMATIC ADVISOR TO THE MINISTER OF

TECHNOLOGY AND INNOVATION, NOTED THAT ITALY SEES E-GOVERNMENT

AS A TOOL FOR ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT FOR LESS-DEVELOPED

COUNTRIES. ONE OF THE FIRST EVENTS OF ITALY\'S EU PRESIDENCY

WILL BE AN E-GOVERNMENT CONFERENCE, BRINGING TOGETHER THE EU

MEMBER STATES, MULTILATERAL DEVELOPMENT BANKS, THE UNITED

NATIONS, AND SELECTED INVITED GOVERNMENTS. THE GOI RECENTLY

LAUNCHED TWO PROJECTS IN JORDAN ON E-PROCUREMENT AND

E-ACCOUNTING AND WILL SOON HAVE ON-LINE PROJECTS IN NIGERIA,

MOROCCO AND ALBANIA. REGARDING THE LATTER, PDAS REMARKED

THAT ANTI-CORRUPTION EFFORTS IN THE BALKANS ARE ONE OF THE

USG\'S TOP PRIORITIES AND THAT PERHAPS, THE U.S. AND ITALY, AS

EU PRESIDENT, COULD WORK TOGETHER.

TRADE, CAP REFORM, AND THE TABD

-------------------------------

10. (C) THE GOI SUPPORTS A STRONG STATEMENT BY G7 COUNTRIES,

AT THE JUNE 1-2 SUMMIT IN EVIAN, IN SUPPORT OF THE PRINCIPLES

OF THE DOHA TRADE ROUND. THIS WILL HELP CLEAR THE WAY FOR A

SIMILAR U.S./EU STATEMENT, WHICH ITALY WILL PRESS FOR UNDER

ITS PRESIDENCY. SUCH A STATEMENT IS A WIN-WIN FOR ALL

PARTIES, MAGLIANO ARGUED. TRADE DG TETI STRESSED THAT THE

GOI HOPED THE ITALIAN EU PRESIDENCY WOULD BE A TIME OF

COOLING OFF, NON-CONFRONTATION, AND A WILLINGNESS TO SEARCH

FOR COMMON GROUND BY BOTH SIDES OF THE ATLANTIC. IN THIS

CONTEXT, HE SAID THE GOI WOULD BE PREPARED TO TAKE A MORE

LIMITED AND "PRAGMATIC APPROACH" TO GEOGRAPHIC INDICATIONS

(ITS TOP DOHA PRIORITY). HE PREDICTED THAT PROGRESS ON CAP

REFORM IN THE NEAR FUTURE WOULD HAVE A POSITIVE IMPACT ON THE

EU\'S POSITION ON AGRICULTURE WITHIN THE WTO NEGOTIATIONS.

THERE IS TO BE ANY CHANCE OF A SUCCESSFUL AGRICULTURAL

AGREEMENT AT THE SEPTEMBER WTO MINISTERIAL IN CANCUN. TWO

ISSUES - DECOUPLING OF FARMER INCOME SUPPORT PAYMENTS FROM

PRODUCTION DECISIONS AND CONTINUATION OF THE DAIRY QUOTAS -

WERE IDENTIFIED AS THE LARGEST STUMBLING BLOCKS FOR AG

MINISTERS TO CONTEND WITH. COMMENT: AS USEU ALREADY HAS

REPORTED, THE AG COUNCIL\'S DEFINITION OF DECOUPLING IS FLUID

AT BEST. TETI\'S COMMENT THAT THE FRENCH ARE NO LONGER OPPOSED

TO PARTIAL DECOUPLING AND DE LUTIO\'S REMARKS THAT ITALY\'S

THREE FARMER ORGANIZATIONS ARE NOW UNITED IN THEIR SUPPORT

FOR PARTIAL DECOUPLING SIGNALS TO US THAT THE COUNCIL HAS

INDEED MOVED SOME DISTANCE FROM THE COMMISSION\'S ORIGINAL

PROPOSAL. NEITHER DE LUTIO OR TETI COULD GIVE US THE AG

COUNCIL\'S WORKING DEFINITION OF PARTIAL DECOUPLING. END

COMMENT

12. (SBU) BOTH AT THE DINNER AND LUNCH EARLIER THAT DAY,

ITALIAN OFFICIALS (SOMEWHAT UNEXPECTEDLY) RAISED THE

TRANSATLANTIC BUSINESS DIALOGUE AS A POSSIBLE MECHANISM FOR

IMPROVING US-EU RELATIONS GENERALLY. THEY LISTENED WITH

INTEREST TO PDAS RIES\' RECOUNTING OF THE HISTORY OF THE TABD

AND CURRENT COMMERCE DEPARTMENT THINKING ON HOW IT MAY BE

REINVIGORATED. THE ITALIANS EXPRESSED AN INTEREST IN KEEPING

IN TOUCH ON THE TABD.
