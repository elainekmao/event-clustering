SUBJECT: ENERGIZING ASEAN IN THE ARF

REF: A) STATE 227617; B) STATE 222865

1. THIS IS AN ACTION MESSAGE. SEE PARA SIX.

2. POLOFF PRESENTED REF A POINTS TO MFA ASEAN DEPARTMENT

EXPERT PHAM MINH THU ON 8 DECEMBER. THU WAS NOT PREPARED TO

RESPOND TO POLOFF\'S QUESTIONS IN MORE THAN GENERALITIES. HE

SAID OVERALL ASEAN AGREES WITH ARF VIEWS ON ENHANCING THE

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 02 HANOI 02665 081040Z

POSITION OF THE ARF CHAIR, WITH THE AIM OF STRENGTHENING

ASEAN\'S ROLE IN THE ARF. ANY ENHANCEMENTS, HOWEVER, MUST BE

UNDERTAKEN ACCORDING TO ALREADY-AGREED GUIDING PRINCIPLES OF

THE ARF.

3. ON MOVEMENT TOWARD PREVENTIVE DIPLOMACY, THU REPEATED

THE WELL-WORN MANTRA THAT PROGRESS MUST MOVE AT A PACE

COMFORTABLE TO ALL. IT IS IMPORTANT TO REMEMBER, THU NOTED,

THAT THE ARF IS, FIRST AND FOREMOST, A FORUM FOR DIALOGUE ON

POLITICAL AND SECURITY ISSUES, THE MAIN PURPOSE OF WHICH IS

TO BUILD MUTUAL CONFIDENCE. THE MAJORITY OF ARF MEMBERS

AGREE THAT THE ARF NEEDS TO MOVE FORWARD, BUT THE UNRESOLVED

ISSUE REMAINS HOW TO ACHIEVE PROGRESS WITHOUT UPSETTING

MUTUAL CONFIDENCE LEVELS, WHICH THU DESCRIBED AS STILL

INADEQUATE.

4. THU OFFERED THAT ALL ARF MEMBERS HAVE BEEN ASKED TO

SUBMIT THEIR VIEWS ON THE ROLE OF THE ARF CHAIR, THE

REGISTRATION OF EXPERTS AND EMINENT PERSONS, AND STEPS

TOWARD PREVENTIVE DIPLOMACY TO THE ARF CHAIR BY 15 JANUARY.

THU EXPECTED HIS DEPARTMENT TO HAVE MORE CONCRETE COMMENTS

TO OFFER IN LATE JANUARY. EMBASSY WILL REVISIT REFTEL

ISSUES WITH THE MFA AT THAT TIME.

5. COMMENT: THIS MEETING FOLLOWED A WELL-ESTABLISHED

PATTERN IN DISCUSSIONS WITH THE VIETNAMESE ON THE ARF.

DESPITE VIETNAM\'S OSTENSIBLE LEADERSHIP OF THE ARF THIS

YEAR, ANY CONSENSUS TO MOVE FORWARD MORE VIGOROUSLY ON ARF

OBJECTIVES WILL PROBABLY FIND VIETNAM AT THE TAIL END OF THE

QUEUE, BEING DRAGGED. END COMMENT.

6. ACTION REQUEST: PER REFTELS A AND B ON INVITING

VIETNAMESE AND OTHER OFFICIALS TO OBSERVE MULTILATERAL

ORGANIZATIONS, EMBASSY WEIGHED IN WHEN DEPARTMENT FIRST

RAISED THE IDEA SOME MONTHS AGO. OUR VIEW STILL IS THAT IT

COULD POTENTIALLY BE VERY BENEFICIAL, AS LONG AS IT IS

OFFERED IN A WAY WHICH DOES NOT MAKE VIETNAM FEEL SINGLED

OUT FOR REMEDIAL TREATMENT. IN ORDER TO GET A FIRMER FIX ON

WHETHER THE IDEA IS PRACTICAL, EMBASSY WOULD LIKE TO FLOAT

THE IDEA IN MORE DETAIL WITH MFA COUNTERPARTS. WE REQUEST

POINTS WHICH WE MAY USE WITH THE GVN.
