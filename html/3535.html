SUBJECT: SIGNING OF NEPAL-ILO AGREEMENT ON US-FUNDED

CHILD LABOR PROJECT

1. Nepal and the ILO signed an agreement August 20 on

the implementation of an ILO/IPEC time-bound project to

eliminate the worst forms of child labor. USDOL has

funded the project with more than USD 5 Million. DCM

and LabOff attended the signing ceremony.

2. Talks on the terms of the agreement had dragged on

for more than nine months. Finance Ministry officials

attempted to insert a sentence into the agreement that

would allow the Auditor General of Nepal to audit the

project\'s accounts at any time. The ILO objected,

arguing that as a member state the GON would have to

accept established UN audit procedures. Post made

repeated representations, pressing the Finance Ministry

to expedite the negotiations. In the end the GON

dropped its demand that the objectionable provision be

included.

3. Post will continue to work with the ILO and Nepal\'s

line ministries to ensure the successful implementation

of DOL-funded initiatives.
