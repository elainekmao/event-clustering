SUBJECT: ITALYS LOCAL ELECTION SNOOZE

1. CONSULATES NAPLES, MILAN, AND FLORENCE CONTRIBUTED TO

THIS CABLE.

2. SUMMARY: ITALYS LOCAL ELECTIONS WERE OVERSHADOWED BY

ATTENTION TO THE NATO-RUSSIA SUMMIT IN ROME. ELECTIONS

WENT SMOOTHLY, WITH INCUMBENTS OR INCUMBENT GOVERNING

COALITIONS LARGELY RETURNED TO OFFICE. THERE ARE NO

NATIONAL-LEVEL GENERALIZATIONS TO BE MADE FROM THE

AGGREGATE OF HUNDREDS OF ELECTIONS DOMINATED BY LOCAL

ISSUES. END SUMMARY.

3. ITALIANS VOTED MAY 26-27 IN LOCAL ELECTIONS TO CHOOSE

TEN PROVINCIAL PRESIDENTS AND COUNCILS, 794 MAYORS, AND 749

CITY COUNCILS. (NOTE: ITALY IS DIVIDED INTO TWENTY

REGIONS, AKIN TO U.S. STATES, WHICH ARE FURTHER DIVIDED

D

INTO 100 PROVINCES, SIMILAR TO COUNTIES.) VOTER TURNOUT,

AT 75.8%, WAS SLIGHTLY LOWER THAN THE 76.4% TURNOUT AT

PREVIOUS LOCAL ELECTIONS. VOTING WAS EXPANDED IN FEBRUARY

TO OCCUR OVER TWO DAYS, IN A MOVE THE GOVERNMENT INTENDED

TO STREAMLINE VOTING PROCEDURES AND INCREASE VOTER

PARTICIPATION. (LAST MAY\'S NATIONAL ELECTIONS WERE MARRED

BY PROTESTS IN MANY CITIES AS POLLING STATIONS BECAME

OVERCROWDED AND WERE HELD OPEN HOURS BEYOND THE OFFICIAL

CLOSING TIME. ADDITIONALLY, ABSTENTION HAS GROWN IN ITALY,

IN PART DUE TO APATHY, BUT ALSO BECAUSE MANY ITALIANS GO

AWAY FOR THE WEEK-END AND ONLY RETURN HOME LATE SUNDAY

NIGHT.)

4. THE ELECTIONS ATTRACTED LITTLE NATIONAL OR PARTY

ATTENTION, WITH NONE OF ITALYS MAJOR CITIES OR MORE

SIGNIFICANT PROVINCES INVOLVED. LOCAL ISSUES PREVAILED,

WITH LITTLE RESORT TO NATIONAL THEMES SUCH AS CONFLICT OF

INTEREST OR JUDICIAL REFORM, AND CANDIDATES PERSONALITIES

OFTEN HELD GREATER SWAY THAN THE ISSUES. WHILE IN SOME

CASES LOCAL RACES WERE HOTLY CONTESTED, THESE DID NOT SPARK

REGGIO CALABRIA, ON THE TOE OF ITALYS BOOT, SAW THE ONLY

TURNOVER IN THE FIRST ROUND. BOTH THE INCUMBENT CENTER-

LEFT PROVINCIAL PRESIDENT AND MAYOR WERE DEFEATED BY THEIR

CENTER-RIGHT OPPONENTS.

5. COMMENT: ELECTION RESULTS WERE LARGELY PREDICTABLE ON

THE BASIS OF PAST VOTING. EVEN THE REGGIO CALABRIA UPSET

IS NOT OVERLY SIGNIFICANT; THE CALABRIA REGION VOTED

CENTER-RIGHT IN LAST YEARS GENERAL ELECTION, AND IT WAS

NOT SURPRISING THAT ONE OF ITS PROVINCES WOULD FOLLOW SUIT.

AS ELABORATED REFTEL, THERE WERE REGIONAL NUANCES. IN

NORTHERN ITALY, ALSO, FORZA ITALIA\'S PERFORMANCE WAS NOT AS

AS

STRONG AS SOME OBSERVERS EXPECTED FOR REASONS ATTRIBUTED

VARIOUSLY TO SILVIO BERLUSCONIS DECISION NOT TO CAMPAIGN

PERSONALLY, THE "WEAKNESS" OF THE CANDIDATES, OR THE

FRUSTRATION ON THE PART OF ECONOMIC SECTORS WHICH SUPPORTED

THE CENTER-RIGHT IN LAST YEAR\'S ELECTIONS. NONETHELESS,

EVEN FEW PARTY REPRESENTATIVES HAVE TRIED TO IMPUTE

SIGNIFICANCE TO THE INDIVIDUAL GAINS AND LOSSES; THE WHOLE

EVENT WAS OVERSHADOWED BY THE NATO-RUSSIA SUMMIT. SOME

OBSERVERS HAVE CREDITED THE CENTER-RIGHT WITH HAVING HAD

MORE TO LOSE FROM THE ELECTION AND THUS WITH A MORE
