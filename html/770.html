1. (C) - ENTIRE TEXT.

2. UNCRO DEPUTY DIRECTOR OF POLITICAL OPERATIONS UK COL.

WILLIAMS PULLED DCM ASIDE SEPTEMBER 13 AT A BRIEFING FOR

THE NEW UN HUMANITARIAN CRISIS CELL, SET UP IN RESPONSE

TO THE PROBLEMS OF CIVILIAN POPULATIONS IN CROATIA AND

BOSNIA. WILLIAMS POINTED OUT THAT HV AND HVO GAINS OF

THE PAST FEW DAYS HAVE CREATED A LARGE AREA IN WESTERN

BOSNIA THAT IS EFFECTIVELY DEVOID OF UN OBSERVER

PRESENCE, AND WHICH MAY BECOME THE SOURCE OF NEW LARGE

NUMBERS OF REFUGEES.

3. THE FRONT, ACCORDING TO GOC CLAIMS, NOW RUNS ALONG A

LINE FROM TITOV DRVAR EAST INCLUDING SIPOVO NEARLY TO

JAJCE. BOSNIAN SERB LEADER KARADZIC CONFIRMED ON

TELEVISION SEPTEMBER 12 THAT SIPOVO WAS OCCUPIED.

ACCORDING TO WILLIAMS, UNCRO OBSERVERS ARE NOT ALLOWED

NORTH OF THE LIVNO-PROZOR ROAD FROM THE SOUTH, AND ARE

DENIED ACCESS TO BOSNIA FROM THEIR FOUR OBSERVATION POSTS

LOCATED IN FORMER SECTORS NORTH AND SOUTH OF CROATIA.

UNPROFOR FORCES DO NOT HAVE ACCESS FROM THE EAST.

4. THIS MEANS THERE ARE NO UN OBSERVERS PRESENT IN A

LARGE SWATH OF TERRITORY. WILLIAMS ESTIMATED THAT 20,000

NEW REFUGEES MAY SHORTLY EMERGE FROM JAJCE AND SAID THAT

PAST CROATIAN ACTIONS IN SECTORS NORTH AND SOUTH CAUSE

FEARS THAT HOSTILE ACTS AGAINST CIVILIANS WILL TAKE PLACE

IN THE NEWLY \'REINTEGRATED\' AREAS OF BOSNIA AS WELL.

WILLIAMS SPECULATED THAT THE CROATS MAY CONTINUE TOWARDS

BANJA LUKA AND CAUSE A ROUT OF SERBS IN WESTERN BOSNIA.

5. SERBS REMAINING IN CROATIA CONTINUE TO HAVE SERIOUS

HUMAN RIGHTS PROBLEMS IN SECTORS NORTH AND SOUTH. THE UN

PROVIDED A FULL SUMMARY LISTING INCIDENTS OF KILLINGS AND

HOUSE BURNINGS IN THE KRAJINA REGION TO ZAGREB DIPLOMATS

SEPTEMBER 13 (EMBASSY WILL FAX TO EUR/SCE). NEARLY 800

SERBS REMAIN CONFINEO IN THE UN HEADQUARTERS COMPOUND IN

KNIN, AS A SERIES OF AGREEMENTS RELATING TO THEIR LEAVING

FOR SERBIA (MINUS SEVERAL -- FIRST 65, NOW 32 -- OF THEIR

NUMBER CHARGED WITH WAR CRIMES) RAN INTO SNAGS FROM

CROATIAN AUTHORITIES.

6. COMMENT; THE UN NEEDS TO BE ALLOWED TO ENTER THE

NEWLY-TAKEN AREAS SOONEST TO DOCUMENT WHAT IS HAPPENING

TO THE CIVILIAN POPULATION THERE.
