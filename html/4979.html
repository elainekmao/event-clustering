SUBJECT: EXTRANCHECK: PRELICENSE CHECK CSI MIDDLE EAST

REF(S): USDOC O6224; APPLICATION NO. D302748

1. ON 17 DEC COMMERCIAL OFFICER MARY O\'BRIEN MET WITH EMAD

BITAR OF CSI MIDDLE EAST AND COR CORBEEK OF EMERSON EUROPE

REGARDING PRELICENSE CHECK RQUESTED IN REFTEL.

2. EMERSON PROCESS MANAGEMENT, FORMERLY EMERSON ELECTRIC,

HAS RECENTLY PURCHASED COMPUTATIONAL SYSTEMS, INC, (CSI) OF

KNOXVILLE, TN. (WWW.CSIMEANSRELIABILITY.COM) CSI IS KNOWN TO

COMMOFF AS A DISTRIBUTOR OF THERMAL IMAGING EQUIPMENT,

INCLUDING FLIR, IN SOUTHEAST ASIA.

3. EARLIER IN THE DAY, COMMOFF O\'BRIEN MET WITH MR. BITAR

AT MEOS REGARDING ANOTHER PRELICENSE CHECK (D302543) THAT

ALSO INVOLVED CSI. DURING THAT MEETING, MR. BITAR OF CSI

SAID HE WAS UNAWARE OF ANOTHER ORDER AND PERHAPS HIS BOSS

HAD ORDERED IT WITHOUT HIS KNOWLEDGE. ARRANGEMENTS WERE

MADE TO MEET AT THE PREMISES OF EMERSON/CSI LATER THAT DAY.

4. EMERSON AND CSI MIDDLE EAST SHARE OFFICE SPACE IN THE

JEBEL ALI FREE ZONE. COMOFF MET WITH BITAR AND HIS MANAGER,

COR CORBEEK. CORBEEK EXPLAINED THE CAMERA WOULD BE USED IN

TRAINING CLASSES AT EMERSON\'S FACILITY FOR PEOPLE FROM

AROUND THE GULF REGION, NORTH AFRICA, AND SOUTH ASIA. COMOFF

VISITED A CLASS IN SESSION THAT INCLUDED REPRESENTATIVES

FROM MOROCCO, UAE, AND KUWAIT, AS WELL AS A LARGE WELL

EQUIPPED STOREROOM WHERE CSI/EMERSON KEEP A VARIETY OF

INSTRUMENTS FOR TRAINING PURPOSES.

5. WHEN ASKED WHO PLACED THE ORDER ON CSI EUROPE, CORBEEK

SAID IT WAS BITAR, WHO HAD EARLIER SAID HE WAS UNAWARE OF

THE TRANSACTION. WHEN ASKED FOR A COPY THE PURCHASE ORDER

CSI MIDDLE EAST HAD SENT TO CSI EUROPE, BITAR SAID HE WOULD

HAVE TO LOOK FOR IT AND FAX IT TO THE FCS OFFICE. THE

PURCHASE ORDER IS DATED 12/19 TWO DAYS AFTER THE VISIT AND

WAS FAXED TO THE FCS OFFICE ON 12/22. IT ALSO APPEARS THE

PURCHASE ORDER WAS PLACED ON CSI IN THE U.S. RATHER THAN

ELECTROPHYSICS BECAUSE IT SAYS EX WORKS KNOXVILLE WHICH IS

WHERE CSI IS HEADQUARTERED.

6. ON 12/23, COMMOFF RECEIVED A LETTER FROM MR. BITAR

WHEREIN HE FIRST SAYS THE ORDER WAS FOR AN UNNAMED CUSTOMER

IN RAS AL KHAIMAH. COMOFF HAS ATTEMPTED TO CONTACT CSI TO

CLARIFY THE IDENTITY OF THE CUSTOMER IN RAS AL KHAIMAH BUT

IT APPEARS THE CSI IS CLOSED DURING THIS HOLIDAY WEEK. THE

LETTER THEN GOES ON TO SAY THE UNIT WILL BE KEPT IN BELGIUM

AND USED IN TRAINING IN A VARIETY OF PLACES INCLUDING THE

MIDDLE EAST. NO MENTION WAS MADE OF THIS DURING THE MEETING

AT CSI/EMERSON. THE LETTER AND PURCHASE ORDER DOCUMENTS

HAVE BEEN IMAGED AND CAN BE EMAILED FOR EA AND INTERAGENCY

REVIEW.

7. CONTACTS MADE BY OEE HQ (MICHELLE ZAGER) TO THE

APPLICANT, ELECTROPHYSICS, INDICATE THEY HAVE NO

DOCUMENTATION OF THIS ORDER, STATING THEY ARE ACTING AS A

MIDDLEMAN BETWEEN RAYTHEON AND CSI. ELECTROPHYSICS SAYS CSI

BELGIUM WILL BE THE OWNER OF THE CAMERA AND WILL SEND IT TO

DISTRIBUTORS AS NEEDED AND THEN HAVE THE DISTRIBUTORS RETURN

THE CAMERA TO BELGIUM WHEN NOT IN USE.

8. NOTE THAT THE SALE OF A CAMERA TO MEOS WAS SUPPORTED BY

A BXA-711 BUT THAT NO BXA-711 APPEARS TO HAVE BEEN COMPLETED

BY CSI MIDDLE EAST IN THIS CASE. PERHAPS ELECTROPHYSICS

CONSIDERS THIS AN EXPORT FOR DEMONSTRATION; HOWEVER, IT IS

NOT CLEAR WHETHER DEMONSTRATION INCLUDES TRAINING IN THE USE

AND OPERATION OF THE CAMERA AND ACCOMPANYING SOFTWARE.

9. CSI MIDDLE EAST IS THE SOLE PRESENCE OF CSI IN THIS

PART OF THE WORLD. THEY ARE A LEGITIMATE COMPANY WITH A

LEGITIMATE NEED FOR THIS PRODUCT, ALTHOUGH THEY WERE

SOMEWHAT EVASIVE IN THEIR ANSWERS.

10. THE MAIN FOCUS OF THIS CHECK THOUGH REVEALS THAT THE

OWNER, CONSIGNEE AND PARTY FIRST RESPONSIBLE FOR COMPLIANCE

WITH ANY LICENSE CONDITIONS WOULD BE CSI EUROPE. COMOFF

SUGGESTS THAT CSI EUROPE AS WELL AS CSI MIDDLE EAST BE

REQUIRED TO AGREE IN ADVANCE TO ANY LICENSE CONDITIONS,

INCLUDING A SECURITY PLAN AND PROHIBITIONS ON TRAINING

PERSONNEL FOR EMBARGOED DESTINATIONS.
