SUMMARY

-------

1. (C) POLOFFS, REFCOORD, AND DART VISITED BIHAC AUGUST

11 TO ASSESS HUMANITARIAN SITUATION AND DISCUSS

POLITICAL/MILITARY DEVELOPMENTS IN THE WAKE OF THE GOC

OFFENSIVE TO RETAKE "RSK" TERRITORY. UN OFFICIALS

COMPLAINED ABOUT THE INADEQUACY OF UN SAFE AREA POLICY

WHICH ONLY PROTECTS BIHAC TOWN, WHILE THE ENTIRE POCKET IS

IN RANGE OF SERB GUNS. THEY DISCUSSED THE REFUGEE AND

DISPLACED PERSON PROBLEM; CURRENTLY THERE ARE ABOUT 40,000

DP\'S IN THE BIHAC POCKET, AND PROBABLY BETWEEN 15-20,000

REFUGEES LOYAL TO FIKRET ABDIC (AND POSSIBLY ALSO

INCLUDING "RSK" SERBS) ON THE ROAD NEAR THE VILLAGE OF

KRSTINJA IN CROATIA. THE UN ASSESSES THAT A FULLY ARMED V

CORPS WILL BE ON THE OFFENSIVE AGAIN IN ONE TO TWO WEEKS

UNPROFOR VIEWS ON THE HUMANITARIAN SITUATION...

--------------------------------------------- --

3. (C) POLOFFS MET WITH THE DANISH UN COMMANDER, COL.

JASPER HELSOE (PROTECT), AND CIVIL AFFAIRS OFFICER RUS

BALLANTYNE (PROTECT) AT BANBAT HQ IN CORALICI. THEY

COMPLAINED THAT THE UN SAFE AREA POLICY WAS GROSSLY

INADEQUATE IN THE CASE OF BIHAC, WHERE ONLY THE TOWN WAS

PROTECTED, BUT THE ENTIRE POCKET WAS IN RANGE OF SERB

GUNS. THEY NOTED THAT THE POWER STATION AND WATER

TREATMENT PLANT ARE BOTH OUTSIDE OF THE SAFE AREA AND WERE

FREQUENTLY TARGETED

4. (U) BOTH STRESSED THAT IT WAS ALL THEY COULD DO TO

MANAGE THE VARIOUS FLOWS OF DISPLACED PERSONS IN THE

CANTON THAT HAVE OCCURRED ALMOST CONTINUOUSLY OVER THE

LAST FOUR YEAR. THE SMALL SIZE OF THE BIHAC POCKET MEANT

THAT ANY TIME SHELLING STARTED, LARGE NUMBERS OF PEOPLE

WOULD FLEE, USUALLY TO BIHAC TOWN, SEVERELY TAXING LIMITED

SERVICES AND SUPPLIES. THEY ESTIMATED THAT 20 PERCENT OF

THE AREA\'S POPULATION FLED FROM ABDIC CONTROLLED TERRITORY

TOWARD THE SOUTH. ABDIC\'S "REPUBLIC OF WESTERN BOSNIA"

ALSO CONTAINED ABOUT 20 PERCENT OF THE AVAILABLE FARM

PROVIDE SEEDS FOR THE NEXT PLANTING.

AND THE NEXT MILITARY MOVES

---------------------------

7. (C) UN OFFICIALS IN CORALICI NOTED THAT AS A RESULT OF

THE HV DEFEAT OF THE "ARSK," THE PRESSURE ON BIHAC\'S

NORTHERN BORDER WAS RELIEVED AND V CORPS COULD REDIRECT

ABOUT 10-12,000 TROOPS TO THE FRONT LINE WITH THE BSA.

ACCORDING TO THE UN, V CORPS NOW HAS A "LIFETIME" SUPPLY

OF AMMUNITION (BALLANTYNE NOTED THAT ON ONE OCCASION HE

SAW TEN 10 TON TRUCKS MOVING SOUTH CARRYING AMMO AND

WEAPONS FOR V CORPS), AT LEAST FOUR TANKS, APC\'S, AND

ARTILLERY TAKEN FROM ABDIC\'S "REPUBLIC OF WESTERN BOSNIA."

V CORPS IS EXPECTED TO MOVE SOUTH AND EAST AND EXPECTS TO

TAKE PRIJEDOR. THE UN CITED RUMORS THAT THE HVO AND V

CORPS HAVE ALREADY LINKED UP AT KULEN VAKUF, ABOUT 30

KILOMETERS SOUTHEAST OF BIHAC ON THE CROATIAN BORDER.

GOVERNOR VELADZIC, PRESIDENT OF THE CANTON GOVERNMENT

FERIZOVIC, AND CANTON PARLIAMENT PRESIDENT ADEM BORIC.

ALL THREE WERE EUPHORIC THAT THE CANTON HAD FINALLY BEEN

10. (U) VELADZIC BELIEVED THAT THE PERFECT OPPORTUNITY

NOW EXISTED TO START UP "REAL" LIFE AGAIN. THE ENCLAVE

WANTED TO STOP ITS DEPENDENCY ON HUMANITARIAN HELP AS SOON

AS POSSIBLE. HE URGED THAT ASSISTANCE BE DIRECTED TOWARDS

RESTARTING INDUSTRIES AND ALLOWING PEOPLE TO EARN A

LIVELIHOOD, SO THEY WOULD NO LONGER BE DEPENDENT ON AID.

--------------------------------------

POLITICAL SITUATION IN BIHAC AND CAZIN

--------------------------------------

11. (U) CANTON PRESIDENT FERIZOVIC STRESSED THAT,

ALTHOUGH NINETY PERCENT MUSLIM, THE CANTON WELCOMED ALL

NATIONALITIES. HE STRESSED THAT A GENERAL AMNESTY WOULD

BE GRANTED FOR EVERYONE WHO FOUGHT AGAINST THE GOVERNMENT,

BUT WAR CRIMINALS (PARTICULARLY ABDIC) WOULD BE PURSUED.

FERIZOVIC SAID THAT THERE IS EXTREMELY GOOD COOPERATION

WITH THE CROATIAN POPULATION WHICH COULD BE AN EXAMPLE FOR

THE REST OF BOSNIA. (THIS POINT WAS MENTIONED EARLIER BY

BALLANTYNE, WHO NOTED THERE IS AN HVO BRIGADE WITH V

CORPS.) IT WAS PROUDLY NOTED THAT THE CANTONAL GOVERNMENT

WAS FORMED IN 15 DAYS AFTER THE MAY 27 MEETING OF THE

LOCAL PARLIAMENT. LOCAL GOVERNMENT MINISTERS COME FROM

BOTH THE DOMINANT MUSLIM SDA AND ALSO THE CROAT HDZ.

12. (U) FERIZOVIC SAID THAT AT LEAST IN BIHAC, THE

VILLAGE SCENES

--------------

13. (U) DURING OUR DRIVE THROUGH THE POCKET WE SAW LIFE

RETURNING TO SOME SEMBLANCE OF NORMALITY. A NUMBER OF

PEOPLE WERE OUT AND ABOUT EVEN IN THE TOWNS, SUNBATHING,

AND PLAYING IN THE RIVERS. THE BIHAC AUTHORITIES AND V

CORPS ESCORT SHOWED US OUTLYING VILLAGES DESTROYED BY THE

"ARSK." IN ZAVALJE, A CROATIAN VILLAGE, ONE OLD WOMAN HAD

RETURNED THAT SAME DAY TO CHECK ON HER PROPERTY. IT HAD

BEEN LIBERATED ONLY DAYS BEFORE. IN THE MUSLIM VILLAGE OF

SOKOLAC, LIBERATED BY THE ABH A FEW MONTHS BEFORE, WE MET

AN ELDERLY MAN WHO RETURNED TO HIS BURNED OUT HOUSE AND

PROPERTY EVERY SO OFTEN TO FIX SMALL DETAILS IN HIS HOUSE

AND TO CUT HIS LAWN.
