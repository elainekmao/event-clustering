SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION ZIMBABWE DEMOCRACY BILL; HARARE

1. UNDER HEADLINE "U. S. DEMOCRACY BILL DRACONIAN: IN

WHOSE INTEREST IS THE LEGISLATION?," THE AUGUST 7 EDITION

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 02 HARARE 02361 091342Z

OF THE GOVERNMENT-CONTROLLED DAILY \'THE HERALD\' CARRIED A

LENGTHY FEATURE ARTICLE THAT WAS CRITICAL OF THE BILL.

EXCERPTS FOLLOW:

2. ". . .IN WHOSE INTEREST WAS THE BILL PASSED. . .? IT

IS CLEAR THAT THE BILL HAS COME ABOUT AS A RESULT OF

PRESIDENT MUGABE\'S UNCOMPROMISING STANCE ON THE LAND

ISSUE, AND ANYTHING ELSE THAT IS SAID, INCLUDING

PERCEIVED VIOLATIONS OF HUMAN RIGHTS AND LACK OF RULE OF

LAW, IS JUST A SMOKE-SCREEN TO COVER THE REAL PURPOSE OF

THE BILL. THE CONTROVERSIAL PIECE OF LEGISLATION IS

MEANT TO PUNISH ZIMBABWE FOR DOING THE RIGHT THING FOR

ITS PEOPLE - REDISTRIBUTING LAND EQUALLY AMONG ALL

CITIZENS IRRESPECTIVE OF COLOR, RELIGION OR POLITICAL

AFFILIATION. . . ."
