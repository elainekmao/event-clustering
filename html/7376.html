1. (U) WE DELIVERED POINTS REF A TO APPROPRIATE GOI

OFFICES. RESPONSES FOLLOW BELOW.

ESDP

-----

2. (SBU) MFA BALKANS OFFICE DIRECTOR FERNANDO GENTILINI SAID

THAT THE GOI CONCURS WITH THE USG VIEW THAT THE EU NEEDED TO

MAKE A SUCCESS OF OPERATION CONCORDIA BEFORE FOCUSING ON A

POSSIBLE EU-LED OPERATION TO REPLACE SFOR IN BOSNIA.

3. (SBU) CURRENT GOI MILITARY PARTICIPATION IN OPERATION

CONCORDIA IS: 22 PERSONNEL ON THE GROUND IN MACEDONIA, 11

STAFF MEMBERS IN BRUSSELS AND 5 SUPPORT STAFF IN NAPLES.

GENTILINI SAID THAT THE GOI HAD NO PLANS TO ADJUST THESE

FIGURES FOR THE FORESEEABLE FUTURE.

CROATIA AND SERBIA

------------------

4. (SBU) GENTILINI WAS GRATEFUL FOR THE POINTS SUMMARIZING

USG VIEWS ON CROATIAN AND SERBIAN INTEGRATION. HE WAS

PREPARING BRIEFING PAPERS FOR AN APRIL 11 TROIKA MEETING IN

ZAGREB ON THE ZAGREB PROCESS/BALKAN INTEGRATION, AND SAID

THAT THE POINTS WOULD BE TAKEN INTO CONSIDERATION WHEN

PREPARING GOI POSITIONS.

5. (SBU) GENTILINI REITERATED THAT ONE OF THE PRIORITIES OF

THE GOI EU PRESIDENCY BEGINNING JULY 1 IS TO RE-FOCUS EU

ATTENTION ON THE LONG-TERM ACCESSION PROCESS FOR THE BALKANS.

STARTING WITH THE STABILIZATION AND ASSOCIATION AGREEMENT

(SAA), AND MOVING ON THROUGH FORMAL ACQUIS COMMUNAUTAIRE

NEGOTIATIONS, THE GOI BELIEVES THAT THE ACCESSION PROCESS IS

AN EFFECTIVE TOOL FOR ENCOURAGING ASPIRANT COUNTRIES TO

REFORM ECONOMIC AND POLITICAL INFRASTRUCTURES.

6. (SBU) PART OF THAT PROCESS, CONTINUED GENTILINI, WILL BE

THE REQUIREMENT FOR MORE "ROBUST" COOPERATION WITH THE ICTY

AND RETURNS OF REFUGEES AND DISPLACED PERSONS. HE SAID THAT

THE GOI WOULD CONTINUE TO EMPHASIZE THE NEED FOR PROGRESS IN

THESE AND OTHER CONDITIONS HIGHLIGHTED IN REF A. AT THE APRIL

14 GAERC AND THROUGHOUT THE ITALIAN PRESIDENCY.

BELARUS VISA BAN

----------------

OSCE OFFICE, THE GOI IS PLEASED THAT THE USG MODIFIED ITS

POSITION ON RESCINDING THE VISA BAN AND WANTS TO WORK WITH

THE EU ON COORDINATED STATEMENTS EXPRESSING CONCERN ABOUT THE

HUMAN RIGHTS SITUATION IN BELARUS. HE SAID THAT SUCH AN EU

STATEMENT WAS ALREADY BEING DRAFTED IN BRUSSELS IN

PREPARATION FOR THE GAERC, AND CONFIRMED THAT THE GOI WOULD

IRAN/NORTH KOREA/NON PROLIFERATION

NORTH KOREA) AND CHINA (FOR NORTH KOREA) IN THE PROCESS.

AS COLOMBIA,S GRADUATION FROM THE EU,S GSP PROGRAM, WITH

AMEDEO TETI, DIRECTOR GENERAL FOR COMMERCIAL AGREEMENTS,

MINISTRY OF PRODUCTIVE ACTIVITIES (FOREIGN TRADE). TETI TOLD

US THE GOI REMAINS CAUTIOUSLY OPTIMISTIC THAT THE UPCOMING

CANCUN WTO MINISTERIAL MEETING WILL BE SUCCESSFUL, BUT TETI

PREDICTED THAT FORWARD MOVEMENT ON THE AGRICULTURAL PROPOSAL

LIKELY WILL NOT OCCUR UNTIL ADDRESSED BY MINISTERS AT CANCUN.

THE GOI CONTINUES TO HOPE FOR RESOLUTION OF THE

TRIPS/MEDICINE ISSUE BEFORE CANCUN, AND BELIEVES THAT U.S.

MOVEMENT IS REQUIRED FOR SUCH A RESOLUTION. TETI SAID THE

GOI IS LARGELY SATISFIED WITH THE WAY THE EU SERVICES

PROPOSAL IS SHAPING UP, BUT SEVERAL ISSUES (NOTABLY THE

IMMIGRATION COMPONENT OF MODE IV) ARE PROBLEMATIC FOR SEVERAL

MEMBER STATES. HE DID NOT HAZARD A GUESS REGARDING A

POSSIBLE SUBMISSION DATE FOR THE EU,S PROPOSAL.

11. (U) ON THE ISSUE OF COLOMBIA,S GRADUATION FROM THE

EU,S GSP PROGRAM, TETI TOLD US THE GOI IS IN AGREEMENT WITH

THE SOLUTION THAT APPARENTLY WAS REACHED AT THE APRIL 9

COREPER MEETING. IN A SEPARATE CONVERSATION, THE MFA,S

MASSIMO GAIANI, OFFICE DIRECTOR IN THE DIRECTORATE GENERAL

FOR EUROPEAN INTEGRATION (WITH WHOM WE ALSO SHARED THE DDA

AND COLOMBIA GSP POINTS) TOLD US THE AGREED SOLUTION AT

COREPER WAS A GRADUAL GRADUATION, WITH 50 PERCENT OF THE

BENEFITS TERMINATING IN NOVEMBER 2003, AND THE REMAINING

BENEFITS ENDING SIX MONTHS AFTER THAT.
