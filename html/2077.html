SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION WAR AGAINST TERROR; HARARE

1. UNDER HEADLINE "HE WHO SHELTERS A THIEF DIES WITH THE

THIEF," THE OCTOBER 18 EDITION OF THE INDEPENDENT DAILY

"THE DAILY NEWS" CARRIED THE FOLLOWING OPINION PIECE BY

SAUL GWAKUBA NDLOVU UNDER HIS COLUMN "A VIEW FROM THE

MATOPOS":

". . .IT IS VERY STRANGE THAT SOME GOVERNMENTS ARE

CRITICIZING THE U.S.-BRITISH MILITARY ACTIONS AGAINST

AFGHANISTAN. WHAT ALTERNATIVES DOES THE WORLD, MINUS

AFGHANISTAN, HAVE EXCEPT TO ATTACK THOSE WHO GIVE

PROTECTION, COMFORT AND SUCCOR TO ARCHITECTS OF

INTERNATIONAL TERROR? .AN OLD AFRICAN TRUISM SAYS: HE

WHO SHELTERS A THIEF DIES WITH THE THIEF. THAT IS WHAT

IS HAPPENING IN AFGHANISTAN. .THE MODERN WORLD IS BASED

ON, FIRST AND FOREMOST, TOLERANCE OF OTHER PEOPLE\'S

CULTURE. AN INTOLERANT CULTURE HAS NO PLACE IN THE

UNITED NATIONS CHARTER, OR THE ISLAMIC NATIONS, OR THAT

OF THE AFRICAN UNION, OR THAT OF THE ORGANIZATION OF

AMERICAN STATES. IT HAS NO PLACE IN ISLAM,

CHRISTIANITY, BUDDHISM, SHAMANISM, SHINTOISM, AND

HINDUISM."

2. UNDER HEADLINE "THE GLOBALIZATION OF TERROR," THE

OCTOBER 18 EDITION OF THE INDEPENDENT WEEKLY "THE FINANCIAL

GAZETTE" CARRIED THE FOLLOWING ARTICLE BY SHIMON PERES,

ISRAEL\'S DEPUTY PRIME MINISTER AND MINISTER OF FOREIGN

AFFAIRS:

"THE ATTACK ON THE TWIN TOWERS AND THE PENTAGON IN THE

U. S. A. ON SEPTEMBER 11 UNCOVERED ANOTHER SIDE OF

GLOBALIZATION - GLOBALIZATION OF TERROR. .THE WORLD IS

MOVING FROM A POSITION OF NATIONAL STRATEGY TO A

POSITION OF GLOBAL STRATEGY - FROM BATTLES BETWEEN

ARMIES TO A FIGHT AGAINST DANGERS. FROM A WORLD OF

ENEMIES (NATIONALISTIC) TO A WORLD OF DANGERS (GLOBAL).

GLOBAL DANGER HAS NO BORDERS. IT CAN STRIKE ANYWHERE

AND AT ANY TIME. UNFETTERED BY HUMAN VALUES, THE

ATROCITIES IT PERPETRATES ARE INDISCRIMINATE, LIMITLESS,

SLAUGHTERING CIVILIANS AND INNOCENT PEOPLE. IT

DISSEMINATES HORROR, IT IS THE PERSONIFICATION OF

PRESENT-DAY SATAN. THERE IS NO ROOM FOR COMPROMISE

BETWEEN EVIL ACTIONS AND HUMAN BEHAVIOR. .DEMOCRACY

CANNOT, AND MUST NOT, DIVERT ITSELF OF ITS MORAL VALUES,

EVEN IN THE FACE OF SUCH A BITTER WAR. .THE NEW DANGER

IS GREAT AND TERRIBLE. IT CANNOT BE OVERCOME WITH EMPTY

WORDS OR THE THREAT OF A SINGLE SWORD. YET IT MUST BE

DEFEATED. AND WE SHALL PREVAIL"

3. UNDER HEADLINE "EXPANSIONISM AT THE HEART OF AMERICA\'S

WARS," THE OCTOBER 18 EDITION OF THE INDEPENDENT DAILY "THE

DAILY NEWS" CARRIED THE FOLLOWING ARTICLE BY FRANZ

SCHURMANN:

"AMERICA IS 225 YEARS OLD. FROM 1776 TO 2001, IT HAS

WAGED 11 MAJOR WARS. THE FIRST WAS THE REVOLUTIONARY

WAR AGAINST BRITAIN (1775 - 1781). THE 11TH IS THE

CURRENT WAR AGAINST AFGHANISTAN (2001). TEN OF THE WARS

INVOLVED FOREIGN ENEMIES. WHY SO MANY WARS? THE

SIMPLEST ANSWER IS: EXPANSIONISM. A MORE COMPLEX ANSWER

IS: EXPANSIONISM AND EMPIRE BUILDING. BUT 11 SEPTEMBER

2001 DISRUPTED THIS FLOW. ONCE AGAIN WE SEE THE GREAT

FEAR ARISING IN AMERICA, AS IN 1950. THEN, THE METAPHOR

FOR THE FEAR WAS \'COMMUNISM.\' NOW IT IS \'TERRORISM.\'
