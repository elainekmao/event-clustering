SUBJECT: ZIMBABWE REPORTEDLY WANTS CUBANS DOCTORS

RELEASED TO ANOTHER COUNTRY FOR PERMANENT RESETTLEMENT

REF: HARARE 3113

CLASSIFIED BY CONSUL THERESA A. HEBRON FOR REASONS 1.5

B/D.

1. (U) THIS IS AN ACTION REQUESTED MESSAGE; PLEASE SEE

PARAGRAPH 8.

2. (C) SUMMARY: UNHCR OFFICIALS ADU AND SALOMONS

CONTINUED IN THEIR QUEST TO SECURE THE RELEASE OF THE

CUBAN DOCTORS JUNE 12, AT ONE POINT TELLING THE INS

OFFICER TO LEAVE THEM TO WORK INDEPENDENTLY. THEY HAD

NO OBJECTION TO EMBOFFS SEEING THE CUBANS BUT SAID THEY

WOULD NOT HELP THEM GAIN ACCESS. AT THE SAME TIME

SALOMONS PROBED FOR PROPRIETARY INFORMATION ON WHAT THE

DOCTORS TOLD INS OFFICER VARSANI ABOUT THEIR REFOULEMENT

ORDEAL. THE UNHCR OFFICIALS TOLD US THAT THE DECISION TO

RELEASE THE CUBANS NOW RESTED WITH PRESIDENT MUGABE, AND

THAT THE ZIMBABWEANS WERE ASKING THAT THE CUBAN

DEFECTORS BE RESETTLED IN A COUNTRY OTHER THAN THE

UNITED STATES, AS HAD BEEN THE PRACTICE IN SUCH CASES IN

THE PAST. REGIONAL UNHCR REPRESENTATIVE BAJULAIYE MAY

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 HARARE 03160 01 OF 02 121733Z

COME TO HARARE TO TAKE OVER THE CASE, BUT DOWNPLAYS HIS

CHANCES OF ACHIEVING SUCCESS. MEANWHILE, SENIOR

GOVERNMENT OFFICIALS CONTINUED TO OUCK THE AMBASSAOOR\'S

ATTEMPTS TO CONTACT THEM, OR FEIGNEO IGNORANCE OF THE

MERITS OF THE CASE WHEN REACHED. IF WE SEE NO PROGRESS

IN THE NEXT COUPLE OF DAYS, POST ASKS THAT THE SECRETARY

REQUEST UNHCR HEAD OGATA TO INTERVENE WITH PRESIDENT

MUGABE TO SECURE THE CUBANS\' RELEASE. END SUMMARY.

3. (C) ON JUNE 12 UNHCR REPRESENTATIVES ADU AND SALOMONS

ADVISED INS OFFICER SANJEETA VARSANI THAT HER PRESENCE

WOULD BE AN IMPEDIMENT TO ANY MEETINGS HELD WITH GOZ

OFFICIALS CONCERNING THE CUBANS AND WOULD HINDER UNHCR\'S

IMPARTIALITY. SALOMONS, HOWEVER, ASKED VARSANI TO SHARE

INS INTERVIEW NOTES FROM THE JUNE 9 REFUGEE INTERVIEW

THAT SHE CONDUCTED WITH THE CUBANS, AS THIS INFORMATION

WOULD BE HELPFUL TO UNHCR IN THE MEETINGS WITH THE GOZ

OFFICIALS. SALOMONS ALSO STATED THAT HE SPECIFICALLY

WANTED INFORMATION REGARDING WHAT HAPPENED TO THE CUBANS

AFTER THEY WERE ABDUCTED FROM THE TEACHER\'S HOME ON JUNE

2 AND WHAT SOUTH AFRICAN OFFICIALS DID OR SAID WHEN THE

CUBANS ARRIVED IN JOHANNESBURG. SALOMONS INDICATED THAT

IF UNHCR COULD ESTABLISH THAT SOUTH AFRICAN OFFICIALS

ASSISTED THE GOZ IN THE ATTEMPTED REFOULEMENT OF THE

CUBANS, THEN UNHCR STOOD A BETTER CHANCE AT SECURING THE

CUBANS\' RELEASE INTO THEIR CUSTODY TODAY JUNE 12.

VARSANI DEMURRED AND SAID THAT SHE COULD NOT SHARE ANY

INFORMATION WITHOUT THE CONSENT OF HER INS SUPERIORS.

SALOMONS WENT ON TO STATE THAT THE PROCESS OF GETTING

THE CUBANS RELEASED MAY TAKE SEVERAL DAYS, AS THE FINAL

DECISION RESTED WITH PRESIDENT MUGABE. HE ALSO ADDED

THAT IN THE PAST, WHEN HIGH PROFILE CUBANS DEFECT, CUBA

TRIES TO SAVE FACE BY HAVING THEM RESETTLED IN ANY

COUNTRY EXCEPT THE UNITED STATES.

4. (C) CONSUL RECEIVED A TELEPHONE CALL FROM FORWARD

MADZIYA (STRICTLY PROTECT), THE TEACHER WITH WHOM THE

CUBANS HAD BEEN STAYING AFTER THEY REQUESTED ASYLUM.

VARSANI WENT TO VISIT MADZIYA AT THE SCHOOL WHERE HE

TEACHES IN GLEN NORAH, A HIGH-DENSITY AREA OUTSIDE OF

HARARE. AFTER CONFIRMING THAT VARSANI HAD THE CUBANS\'

BEST INTERESTS IN MIND AND THAT SHE KNEW WHERE CORDOVA

HAD HIDDEN THEIR PASSPORTS, MADZIYA TOOK VARSANI TO HIS

HOME AND HANDED OVER THEIR PASSPORTS, TELEPHONE DIARIES,

AND OTHER PERSONAL DOCUMENTS.

5. (C) ADU AND SALOMONS CONTINUE TO ARGUE THAT THE USG

SHOULD KEEP ITS DISTANCE FROM THE CASE AND ALLOW THE

CUBANS TO BE RESETTLED IN SOME OTHER COUNTRY, AS A

"FACE-SAVING MEASURE" FOR THE GOZ. SALOMONS ALSO TOLD

VARSANI THAT IF SHE ANO CONSUL WISHED TO VISIT THE

CUBANS, THAT PERMISSION WOULO HAVE TO BE SECURED WITHOUT

UNHCR ASSISTANCE. JAMES MCANULTY, POLITICAL REFUGEE

COOROINATOR AMEMBASSY NAIROBI, SAIO THAT PREETA LAW OF

THE UNHCR REGIONAL SERVICES CENTER NAIROBI ALSO

MENTIONEO THE GOZ\'S OSTENSIBLE OESIRE TO HAVE THE CUBANS

RESETTLEO ANYWHERE BUT IN THE U.S. MCNAULTY THOUGHT

THAT ONE POSSIBLE SOLUTION TO THE IMPASSE WOULO BE TO

ACKNOWLEOGE THAT THE CUBANS WOULO GO TO KENYA, WHILE

GLOSSING OVER THE FACT THAT THEY WOULD ALSO TRAVEL

ONWARO FOR RESETTLEMENT IN THE U.S. HOWEVER, THIS MAY

BE TOO TRANSPARENT A RUSE FOR THE ZIMBABWEAN GOVERNMENT,

AS WORO OF ONWARO PROCESSING HAS ALREAOY HIT THE PRESS

IN THE STATES.

6. (C) OLUSEYI BAJULAIYE, UNHCR REGIONAL

REPRESENTATIVE, TOLO LUSAKA DCM WHITEHEAO JUNE 12 THAT

HE MAY COME TO HARARE TO SORT THE PROBLEM OUT HIMSELF.

BAJULAIYE REITERATED THAT THE CASE WAS ON MUGABE\'S OESK

FOR DECISION. ALTHOUGH A SKILLED OIPLOMAT, BAJULAIYE

THINKS HE IS CURRENTLY OUT OF FAVOR WITH THE GOZ,

BECAUSE OF STATEMENTS HE MAOE ABOUT POSSIBLE REFUGEE

INFLUXES FROM ZIMBABWE DUE TO THE LAND CRISIS.

7. (C) MEANWHILE, AMBASSADOR MCDONALD SPOKE WITH THE

INFLUENTIAL SECRETARY OF THE CABINET, CHARLES UTETE, WHO

WAS CORDIAL ENOUGH BUT PROFESSED IGNORANCE OF THE MERITS

OF THE CASE. THE AMBASSADOR ALSO ATTEMPTED TO SPEAK

WITH HOME AFFAIRS MINISTER DUMISO DABENGWA AND OTHER

SENIOR GOVERNMENT OFFICIALS, ALL OF WHOM PROVED

UNAVAILABLE.

8. (C) ACTION REQUESTED: IF THE CUBAN DOCTORS ARE NOT

RELEASED WITHIN THE NEXT 48 HOURS POST RECOMMENDS THAT

THE SECRETARY PLACE A CALL TO UNHCR HEAD OGATA TO

REQUEST THAT OGATA SPEAK WITH PRESIDENT MUGABE TO SECURE

THE DOCTORS\' FREEDOM AND TRANSFER OF CUSTODY TO UNHCR.
