SUMMARY

-------

1. (C) ZANU-PF HAS ESCALATED ITS INTIMIDATION OF OPPOSITION MDC

LEADERS AND SUPPORTERS, AND IS MOVING TO ELIMINATE MANEUVERING

ROOM FOR ALL OTHER INDIVIDUALS AND INSTITUTIONS THAT MIGHT

THREATEN THE RULING PARTY\'S CONTINUED HOLD ON POWER. IN THE LAST

SEVERAL WEEKS, ZANU-PF OR THOSE ACTING ON ITS BEHALF HAVE RAIDED

THE MDC\'S OFFICES IN HARARE AND BULAWAYO; BEATEN SEVERAL

OPPOSITION MEMBERS OF PARLIAMENT AND PEOPLE CLOSE TO THEM;

DESTROYED THE HOUSE OF AN MDC MP; TARGETED MDC SUPPORTERS IN

TERROR CAMPAIGNS NATIONWIDE; AND INTENSIFIED REPRESSION OF

COMMERCIAL FARMERS. ALL OF THESE DEVELOPMENTS ARE PART OF THE

RULING PARTY\'S INCREASINGLY VICIOUS BUT WELL-COORDINATED STRATEGY

TO REMAIN IN POWER BEYOND THE 2002 PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION. IT IS

IMPORTANT THAT THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY DO ALL IT CAN TO HALT

THIS ONCE-PROMISING COUNTRY\'S SLIDE TOWARD TOTALITARIANISM. AN

IMPORTANT STARTING POINT IS TO MAKE THE UPCOMING ELECTION A FOCAL

POINT OF OUR DIPLOMATIC EFFORTS AND TO EMPHASIZE TO PRESIDENT

MUGABE AND HIS ADVISORS PRECISELY WHAT CONSEQUENCES THEY WILL FACE

IF THE ELECTION IS NOT FREE AND FAIR. END SUMMARY.

INCREASING REPRESSION OF COMMERCIAL FARMERS

-------------------------------------------

6. (C) THE SITUATION ON COMMERCIAL FARMS NATIONWIDE IS HEATING

UP, AS THE GOVERNMENT APPEARS INTENT ON BEATING FARMERS -- IN SOME

CASES, QUITE LITERALLY -- INTO SUBMISSION. DAVID HASLUCK,

EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR OF THE COMMERCIAL FARMERS UNION (CFU), TOLD

ECON/COMMERCIAL CHIEF ON JULY 9 THAT THE ORCHESTRATED CAMPAIGN OF

VIOLENCE AND INTIMIDATION TARGETING COMMERCIAL FARMERS HAS

INTENSIFIED IN RECENT WEEKS. ACCORDING TO HASLUCK, THE GOZ HAD

RECENTLY ORDERED ITS GANGSTER MILITIA OF WAR VETERANS AND ZANU-PF

SUPPORTERS (WHOSE TACTICS ARE BECOMING INDISTINGUISHABLE FROM

THOSE OF MUSSOLINI\'S BROWNSHIRTS) TO DELIVER A CLEAR MESSAGE TO

WHITE FARMERS THAT ANY RESISTANCE TO THE CHAOTIC LAND RESETTLEMENT

NEW CITIZENSHIP LAW

-------------------

12. (U) ON JULY 6, PRESIDENT MUGABE SIGNED INTO LAW AN AMENDMENT

TO THE CITIZENSHIP ACT OF ZIMBABWE INTENDED TO STRENGTHEN ZANU-

PF\'S PROSPECTS IN THE 2002 PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION. ACCORDING TO

THE IMPLEMENTING REGULATIONS, ZIMBABWEANS HOLDING DUAL CITIZENSHIP

ARE REQUIRED TO RENOUNCE THEIR FOREIGN CITIZENSHIP WITHIN SIX

MONTHS OF THE ENACTMENT DATE, IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE FOREIGN

COUNTRY\'S LAWS. PREVIOUSLY, ZIMBABWEANS HOLDING DUAL CITIZENSHIP

WERE ONLY REQUIRED TO RENOUNCE THEIR FOREIGN CITIZENSHIP UNDER

ZIMBABWEAN LAW, A RENUNCIATION WHICH SOME FOREIGN COUNTRIES DID

NOT RECOGNIZE.

13. (C) COMMENT: OBSERVERS AGREE THAT THE AMENDMENT IS AIMED

SOLELY AT DISENFRANCHISING WHITES, WHOM THE GOZ BELIEVES TO BE

OVERWHELMINGLY SUPPORTIVE OF THE MDC. IT IS UNCLEAR, HOWEVER, HOW

THE GOVERNMENT PLANS TO ENFORCE THE NEW PROVISION. WILL ALL WHITE

ZIMBABWEANS, FOR EXAMPLE, BE FORCED TO PROVE THAT THEY ARE NOT

CITIZENS OF ANOTHER COUNTRY WHEN THEY APPLY FOR RENEWAL OF THEIR
