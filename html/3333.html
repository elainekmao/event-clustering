1. (C) SUMMARY: THE GOI GENERALLY SUPPORTS OUR "FUTURE OF

IRAQ" WORKING GROUPS PROPOSAL

AND IS ESPECIALLY INTERESTED IN THE AREAS OF PUBLIC HEALTH

AND CULTURAL PRESERVATION.

WHILE ITALY VERY MUCH WANTS A DIFFERENT IRAQI GOVERNMENT IN

POWER, IT IS CONCERNED ABOUT

WHO WILL SUCCEED SADDAM. IN THE VIEW OF THE ITALIANS, EITHER

THE ISLAMISTS GAINING POWER

IN IRAQ OR THE DESTABILIZATION OF THE PERSIAN GULF REGION

WOULD BE AS BAD AS -- OR WORSE THAN --

SADDAM\'S CURRENT REGIME. END SUMMARY.

2. (C) POLOFF DELIVERED REF DEMARCHE TO LUIGI MACCOTTA, THE

MFA\'S OFFICE

DIRECTOR FOR THE PERSIAN GULF AND ROGUE STATES, AND EMANUELE

FARRUGIA,

DEPUTY OFFICE DIRECTOR AND DESK OFFICER FOR IRAQ. ON THE

PRIORITIES OF THE WORKING

GROUPS, FARRUGIA SUGGESTED THAT THE FOREIGN AND NATIONAL

SECURITY POLICY GROUP

COME FIRST TO ALLOW THE UNCONDITIONAL RETURN OF UN WEAPONS

INSPECTORS. ON

ITALY\'S CONTRIBUTION TO THE IRAQI PEOPLE, FARRUGIA EXPLAINED

THAT THE GOI

FOCUSES -- AND WILL LIKELY FOCUS -- ITS EFFORTS IN IRAQ ON

TWO DOMAINS: PUBLIC HEALTH AND CULTURAL

PRESERVATION. IT IS NOT EASY TO DEAL WITH THE BUREAUCRATIC

IRAQI GOVERNMENT

THAT CONSIDERS ANY NGO PART OF AN INTELLIGENCE SERVICE,

COMMENTED FARRUGIA.

THAT SAID, THE ITALIAN EXPERTS IN IRAQ HAVE DEVELOPED GOOD

CONTACTS IN ORDER TO

FACILITATE THEIR TASKS.

3. (C) AMONG EU COUNTRIES, ITALY HAS BEEN ONE OF IRAQ\'S MOST

IMPORTANT TRADING

PARTNERS FOR MANY YEARS. THIS CHANGED AFTER SEPTEMBER 11.

ITALY\'S EXPORTS TO

IRAQ FELL FROM 5% TO 1% OF THE OIL FOR FOOD PROGRAM ALLOWANCE

OVER THE PAST

YEAR, EXPLAINED FARRUGIA. THAT WAS THE ECONOMIC PRICE ITALY

PAID FOR ITS POLITICAL DECISION TO REDUCE OFFICIAL CONTACTS

WITH IRAQ, WHICH DID NOT TAKE A CLEAR STAND ON TERRORISM, HE

SAID.

4. (C) FARRUGIA STRESSED THAT A MILITARY ATTACK WOULD NOT BE

ITALY\'S FIRST OPTION FOR

DEALING WITH SADDAM, DUE IN PART TO CONCERNS ABOUT THE

STABILITY OF THE REGION.

ABSENT CAREFUL PLANNING, STRESSED FARRUGIA, THERE IS A REAL

POSSIBILITY

FOR THE DESTABILIZATION OF IRAQ AND THE REGION POST-SADDAM.

THE GOI IS "KEEN

ON SUPPORTING SECULAR REGIMES, EVEN IF THEY ARE DICTATORIAL

IN NATURE, OVER

CONFIDENTIAL

ISLAMIC REGIMES." THAT IS WHY THE GOI IS SUPPORTING THE

GOVERNMENTS IN TUNISIA

AND ALGERIA. IF THE SECULAR SADDAM REGIME FALLS, ARGUED

FARRUGIA, IRAQ

RUNS THE RISK OF BECOMING A RADICAL SHIITE SOCIETY. HE ADDED

THAT THE IRANIAN

RELIGIOUS CONSERVATIVES RELY HEAVILY ON THE IRAQIS IN EXILE

IN IRAN, WHO TEND

TO BE MOST RADICAL. AN IRAQ SHIITE STATE REPLACING SADDAM\'S

REGIME COULD

PRODUCE ANOTHER IRAN AND BLOCK THE WAY OF REFORM, STRESSED

FARRUGIA.

5. (C) THE KURDISH ISSUE IS SO IMPORTANT THAT IT OUGHT TO

HAVE ITS OWN WORKING

GROUP, ARGUED FARRUGIA. THE KURDS OUGHT TO ENJOY THEIR

CULTURAL AUTONOMY.

HOWEVER, THE KURDS COULD PLAY A MAJOR ROLE IN THE

STABILIZATION OF A POST-SADDAM

IRAQ. THEY ARE USUALLY SUNNIS AND SECULAR, AND THUS COULD

ENSURE THE

CONTINUITY OF A SECULAR IRAQ POST-SADDAM, STATED FARRUGIA.
