SUBJECT: U.S. GOALS FOR UNGA DELIVERED TO JORDAN

REF: STATE 146035

Classified By: Charge Gregory L. Berry. Reasons 1.5 (b,d)

1. (C) On July 31, Charge delivered reftel points (left as a

non-paper) on U.S. Goals at the 57th UNGA to Ali al-Ayed,

personal aide to Foreign Minister Muasher. Al-Ayed (who will

be part of the Jordanian delegation to the UNGA) promised to

share the non-paper with other relevant MFA officials, and

with FM Muasher when he returns from the U.S. this weekend.
