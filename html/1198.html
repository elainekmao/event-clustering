SUBJECT: OFFICIAL LAUNCH OF EARTH CHARTER IN THE HAGUE

1. ON JUNE 29, THE EARTH CHARTER COMMISSION HELD A

CONFERENCE IN THE HAGUE OFFICIALLY LAUNCHING THE EARTH

CHARTER CAMPAIGN. MIKHAIL GORBACHEV, A CO-CHAIR OF THE EARTH

CHARTER COMMISSION, ATTENDED THE LAUNCH AS DID HER MAJESTY

QUEEN BEATRIX OF THE NETHERLANDS, AND RUUD LUBBERS, FORMER

DUTCH PRIME MINISTER. MEMBERS OF THE DIPLOMATIC CORPS, THE

MEDIA, AND ACADEMIA ALSO ATTENDED.

2. THE EARTH CHARTER SETS FORTH PRINCIPLES CONCERNING

ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION, DEMOCRACY BUILDING, RIGHTS OF

INDIGENOUS PERSONS, PROTECTION OF ENDANGERED SPECIES,

NONVIOLENCE, AND SOCIAL JUSTICE. AN EARTH CHARTER WAS FIRST

PROPOSED AT THE RIO EARTH SUMMIT IN 1992, BUT A DOCUMENT WAS

NOT UNVEILED FOR CIRCULATION UNTIL MARCH 2000.

3. WITH THE LAUNCH OF THE CAMPAIGN, THE COMMISSION INTENDS

TO CIRCULATE THE EARTH CHARTER WORLDWIDE AND LOBBY FOR ITS

ENDORSEMENT BY THE UNITED NATIONS GENERAL ASSEMBLY IN 2002.

IT ALSO SEEKS THE USE OF THE EARTH CHARTER IN SCHOOLS AROUND

THE WORLD AS AN EDUCATIONAL RESOURCE.
