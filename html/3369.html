SUBJECT: JORDAN: MEDIA REACTION ON THE GAZA BOMBING

SUMMARY

JORDANIAN DAILIES ON JULY 24 WERE DOMINATED BY REPORTS

ON THE ISRAELI "MASSACRE" IN GAZA, AND REACTIONS TO IT.

REPORTS HIGHLIGHTED THE U.S. ADMINISTRATION\'S CRITICISM

OF THE RAID, AND ISRAEL\'S DESCRIPTION OF IT AS "A GREAT

SUCCESS."

EDITORIAL WRITERS RAGED AGAINST THE ISRAELI "MASSACRE."

ONE COMMENTARY SAID THAT THE "TRAGEDY IN GAZA MAY

ANSWER THE TRADITIONAL AMERICAN QUESTION: "WHY DO THEY

HATE US?" ANOTHER COLUMNIST CHARGED THAT: "SHARON AND

BUSH BEAR RESPONSIBILITY".

NEWS REPORTS

ALL JORDANIAN DAILIES ON JULY 24 LED WITH REPORTS OF

THE "INTERNATIONAL CONDEMNATION OF THE ISRAELI MASSACRE

IN GAZA." THE FRONT-PAGE REPORTS, WHICH INCLUDED

PHOTOS OF THE CHILDREN KILLED IN THE RAID, HIGHLIGHTED

JORDAN\'S CONDEMNATION OF THE RAID AND THE U.S.

ADMINISTRATION\'S CRITICISM OF IT.

IN SEPARATE STORIES, DAILIES REPORTED THAT: "THE

ISRAELI PRIME MINISTER EXPRESSED HIS SATISFACTION WITH

THE MASSACRE," WHICH HE DESCRIBED "A GREAT SUCCESS."

EDITORIAL COMMENTARIES

-- WHEN F-16S BOMB PEACE SETTLEMENT EFFORTS

COLUMNIST YASSER ABOU HILALEH WROTE IN SEMI-OFFICIAL

INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-RAI ON JULY 24: "ON THE EVE

OF THE CRIME HAMAS HAD DECLARED ITS READINESS TO

SUSPEND OPERATIONS BEYOND 1948 LINE, PALESTINIAN

OFFICIALS WERE SPEAKING WITH OPTIMISM, AND ARAB FOREIGN

MINISTERS RETURNED FROM WASHINGTON HAPPY. THERE WAS

HOPE OF A SETTLEMENT, BUT SHARON WANTED TO QUASH THIS

HOPE, NOT TO SAY THIS ILLUSION. THE ANGER GOES BEYOND

SHARON TO THE U.S. THAT GIVES HIM HIS F-16S, AND THAT

GIVES FALSE PROMISES TO THE PALESTINIANS. THE TRAGEDY

OF GAZA MAY ANSWER THE TRADITIONAL AMERICAN QUESTION:

`WHY DO THEY HATE US?\' IT ALSO RAISES MANY NEW

QUESTIONS ABOUT THE FUTURE OF THE SETTLEMENT, THE

REGION, AND THE WORLD."

-- REVENGE IS JUSTIFIED

TAHER AL-ADWAN, CHIEF EDITOR OF INDEPENDENT, MASS-

APPEAL ARABIC DAILY AL-ARAB AL-YAWM WROTE ON JULY 24:

"AT THE TIME OF WRITING, MORE THAN 12 HOURS AFTER

SHARON\'S LATEST MASSACRE IN GAZA, AND STILL THERE IS NO

REACTION FROM THE WHITE HOUSE. WHITE HOUSE REACTIONS

TO PALESTINIAN RESISTANCE AGAINST THE OCCUPATION DO NOT

TAKE MORE THAN 15 TO COME OUT, FULL OF DEROGATORY TERMS

AGAINST THE PALESTINIANS. THIS TIME WE WOULD NOT BE

SURPRISED TO HEAR IT JUSTIFY SHARON AS HAVING ACTED IN

SELF-DEFENSE. AND THE AMERICAN MEDIA WILL NOT SHOW IN

THE U.S. SHOTS OF PALESTINIAN FLESH TORN TO SHREDS BY

U.S. MADE AIRCRAFT. THE ONLY PERMISSIBLE FOOTAGE ON

THE PALESTINIAN-ISRAELI CONFLICT IS OF MARTYRDOM

BOMBERS. BUT NOW ALL THE PROMISES OF REVENGE EXPRESS

NOTHING BUT A LEGITIMATE RIGHT, AND SHARON AND BUSH

BEAR RESPONSIBILITY FOR ANY ESCALATION BECAUSE

EMPLOYING STATE OF THE ART TECHNOLOGY TO KILL CIVILIANS

IN THE MIDDLE OF THE NIGHT IS THE WORST FORM OF

TERRORISM."

-- A WAR CRIME IN GAZA

PROMINENT COLUMNIST URAIB AL-RANTAWI WROTE IN CENTER-

LEFT, INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR ON JULY 24:

"WASHINGTON, WHICH IS NORMALLY FASTER THAN TEL AVIV TO

CONDEMN ANY PALESTINIAN COMMANDO OPERATION, IS NOW

SILENT AS A TOMB. SOME MAY SEE THIS AS ENCOURAGING

SINCE THE TRACK RECORD OF BUSH IS TO DEFEND `ISRAEL\'S

LEGITIMATE RIGHT OF SELF-DEFENSE.\' AND KOFI ANNAN, WHO

COULD NOT FIND IN THE LEXICONS OF THE NUMEROUS

LANGUAGES THAT HE SPEAKS ADEQUATE TERMS OF CONDEMNATION

OF THE PALESTINIANS, IS NOW CONTENTED TO EXPRESS REGRET

THROUGH A SPOKESMAN. STILL, THE MOST DANGEROUS ASPECT

OF THIS OPERATION IS THAT IT CAME HOURS AFTER HAMAS

LEADER AHMAD YASSIN ACCEPTED TO SUSPEND MARTYRDOM

OPERATIONS IF ISRAEL ACCEPTS TO STOP TARGETING

PALESTINIAN CIVILIANS. ISRAEL CLEARLY WANTED TO

SABOTAGE EFFORTS AT A PEACE SETTLEMENT, BECAUSE SUCH A

SETTLEMENT WOULD IMPOSE ON IT COMMITMENTS THAT IT HAS

NO WISH OF HONORING." GNEHM
