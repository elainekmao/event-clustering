SUBJECT: STILL RELEVANT: ARABIC BOOKS AT THE SHARJAH

INTERNATIONAL BOOK FAIR

1. SUMMARY: THE PUBLIC AFFAIRS OFFICE IN ABU DHABI

RECENTLY PARTICIPATED IN THE SHARJAH INTERNATIONAL BOOK

FAIR, ONE OF POST\'S MAJOR ANNUAL CULTURAL ACTIVITIES.

PAO DISPLAYED AND SOLD MORE THAN 300 TITLES TO PRIVATE

INDIVIDUALS, OFFICIALS, AND REPRESENTATIVES FROM A WIDE

RANGE OF EDUCATIONAL AND CULTURAL INSTITUTIONS. OUR

PARTICIPATION REAFFIRMED THE USEFULNESS OF RBO ARABIC

BOOKS AS AN EFFECTIVE OUTREACH TOOL AND EXEMPLIFIED OUR

LONG-TERM CULTURAL AND EDUCATIONAL COMMITMENT TO THE

UAE. END SUMMARY.

2. THE PUBLIC AFFAIRS OFFICE IN ABU DHABI PARTICIPATED

IN THE TWENTY-FIRST SHARJAH INTERNATIONAL BOOK FAIR,

FROM DECEMBER 30, 2002, TO JANUARY 9, 2003. THE SHARJAH

BOOK FAIR REPRESENTS A MAJOR REGIONAL CULTURAL EVENT

THAT IS WIDELY ATTENDED BY SCHOLARS, ACADEMICS, AND

PUBLISHERS FROM THE UAE, GCC, AND OTHER MIDDLE EASTERN

COUNTRIES. THE U.S. EMBASSY BOOTH EXHIBITED MORE THAN

300 TITLES OF RBO BOOKS, ENGLISH TEACHING MATERIALS, AND

IIP MATERIALS. THE RULER OF SHARJAH, DR. SHEIKH SULTAN

BIN MUHAMMAD AL-QASSIMI, OFFICIALLY OPENED THE FAIR AND

SPENT TIME AT THE U.S. BOOTH DISCUSSING U.S. POLITICS

AND POST\'S MATERIALS WITH AMBASSADOR MARCELLE WAHBA, PAO

KATHERINE VAN DE VATE, AND OTHER EMBASSY STAFF. MAJOR

PURCHASERS INCLUDED THE SHARJAH CULTURAL DEPARTMENT, A

VARIETY OF PRIVATE SCHOOLS, PUBLIC LIBRARIES, AND

SHARJAH UNIVERSITY. HOWEVER, MORE THAN 150,000 PEOPLE,

40,000 OF THEM STUDENTS, ATTENDED THE BOOK FAIR.

3. THIS PROGRAM BUILDS ON POST\'S LONG-TERM COMMITMENT

TO CULTURAL AND EDUCATIONAL RELATIONS BETWEEN THE U.S.

AND THE UAE. WHILE OVERSHADOWED BY THE INCREASED

MOBILIZATION OF U.S. MILITARY AGAINST IRAQ, POST\'S

PARTICIPATION FOSTERED MUTUAL UNDERSTANDING BY GIVING

VISITORS AN OPPORTUNITY TO ACQUIRE CURRENT TITLES ON

U.S. CULTURE, POLITICS, AND GOVERNMENT IN ARABIC. THIS

PROGRAM FURTHER SUPPORTED MPP THEMES "DEMOCRACY AND U.S.

SOCIETY" AND "MUTUAL UNDERSTANDING." PARTICIPATION IN

THE SHARJAH BOOK FAIR ALSO ALLOWED US TO PRESENT U.S.

CULTURE AND VALUES TO A WIDE RANGE OF INTELLECTUALS,

MEMBERS OF THE MEDIA, AND STUDENTS, INCLUDING BOTH UAE

NATIONALS AND EXPATRIATE ARABS.

4. RESULT/IMPACT: EXCELLENT. IN SPITE OF THE INTENSE

POLITICAL ENVIRONMENT ASSOCIATED WITH THE CURRENT

MOBILIZATION OF U.S. TROOPS IN THE REGION, POST

SUCCEEDED IN OBTAINING MAXIMUM EXPOSURE FOR IIP

MATERIALS AND TRANSLATED AMERICAN PUBLICATIONS, SELLING

MORE THAN 500 BOOKS. THOUSANDS OF PEOPLE VISITED THE

BOOTH, INCLUDING SENIOR GOVERNMENT OFFICIALS, SHEIKHS,

INTELLECTUALS, AND FACULTY MEMBERS FROM SEVERAL

UNIVERSITIES AND COLLEGES. DUBAI TV CARRIED A 5-MINUTE

INTERVIEW WITH AMBASSADOR MARCELLE WAHBA WHO COMMENTED

ON THE IMPORTANCE OF POST\'S PARTICIPATION AND THE RBO

PROGRAM. DURING THE INTERVIEW, AMBASSADOR WAHBA

HIGHLIGHTED A RANGE OF IMPORTANT BOOKS, INCLUDING MANY

NEW TITLES.
