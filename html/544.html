1. CONFIDENTIAL - ENTIRE TEXT.

-------

SUMMARY

-------

2. A REGIME OF EXTRAORDINARY BRUTALITY HOLDS SWAY IN

SERBIAN-DOMINATED VUKOVAR AND ELSEWHERE IN THE SECTOR

EAST UNITED NATIONS PROTECTED AREA (UNPA) OF CROATIA.

CONFIDENTIAL

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 ZAGREB 01962 01 OF 08 091325Z

MURDERS, ASSAULTS, VIOLENT HARASSMENT, DESTRUCTION OF

CATHOLIC CHURCHES, AND ATTACKS ON MIXED MARRIAGES ARE

NOW COMMONPLACE. UNPROFOR REPS ARE WAGING AN UPHILL

STRUGGLE TO BRING TO TASK SERBIAN PERPETRATORS -- WHO

ARE INTEGRAL ELEMENTS OF THE SO-CALLED GOVERNMENT IN

THE AREA. THE ODDS ON UNPROFOR\'S SUCCEEDING ARE NOT

GREAT, WHICH IS WHY THE SECTOR EAST SITUATION IS AMONG

THE MOST OFT-CITED POTENTIAL CAUSES OF A FEARED LARGE-

SCALE FLARE UP OF SERBO-CROATIAN FIGHTING NEXT SPRING.

3. THIS REPORT IS BASED ON OBSERVATIONS AND

INTERVIEWS BY TWO EMBASSY OFFICERS DURING TRAVEL TO

SECTOR EAST OCTOBER 1-4 IN SUPPORT OF A CSCE

DELEGATION. SEPTEL REPORTS THEIR OBSERVATIONS ON

CONDITIONS IN SECTORS WEST AND SOUTH. END SUMMARY.

---------------------------------------

APPRECIATION OF THE SCOPE OF WANTON DESTRUCTION THAT

OCTOBER 4 EMBOFFS WERE GIVEN AN ESCORTED TOUR OF

VUKOVAR AND SEVERAL SURROUNDING TOWNS IN SECTOR EAST

BY UNPROFOR OFFICIALS. THE REMAINS OF THE CITY LOOK

LIKE BERLIN, CIRCA 1945. NEARLY EVERY RESIDENTIAL,

COMMERCIAL, CULTURAL, AND RELIGIOUS STRUCTURE HAS

BEEN GUTTED OR SEVERELY DAMAGED. GRAIN ELEVATORS,

CRANES IN THE PORT ALONG THE RIVER, FACTORIES, AND

5. THE POPULATION OF VUKOVAR TOWNSHIP HAS DWINDLED

TO 20,000 FROM A PRE-WAR 80,000 (OF WHOM AN ESTIMATED

58 PERCENT WERE CROATS). ONLY SOME TENS OF PEOPLE

WERE VISIBLE ON THE STREETS DURING THE HALF-HOUR TOUR

BY CAR. THERE WERE ONLY A FEW OTHER VEHICLES ON THE

ROADS DESPITE THE REPORTED AVAILABILITY OF GASOLINE

BROUGHT IN FROM SERBIA.

HUNGARIANS, SLOVAKS, AND CROATS LOSE JOBS AND HOMES

--------------------------------------------- ------

CONFIDENTIAL

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 ZAGREB 01962 02 OF 08 091325Z

6. IN THIS DISMAL SETTING, SERBIAN OFFICIALS ADD TO

THE MISERY THROUGH A SYSTEMATIC PROGRAM OF ETHNIC

CLEANSING AIMED AT DRIVING OUT ALL REMAINING CROATS,

HUNGARIANS, SLOVAKS, AND OTHER MINORITIES. ON AUGUST

31, 1992, SOME TWENTY NON-SERBS (MOSTLY SLOVAKS)

REPORTEDLY WERE DISMISSED FROM THE ILOK BRICK FACTORY

AND LOST, ALONG WITH THEIR JOBS, THEIR COMPANY-OWNED

HOUSING. SERBIAN REPLACEMENT WORKERS THEN MOVED IN

AND WERE HIRED AT THE BRICK FACTORY -- WHICH EMBOFFS

OBSERVED OPERATING AT A BRISK PACE.

7. UNPROFOR CIVILIAN POLICE ALSO TOLD EMBOFFS THAT

HUNGARIANS AND SLOVAKS ARE PRESSURED TO ACCEPT

MOBILIZATION INTO THE KRAJINA "ARMED FORCES." THOSE

WHO REFUSE ARE CLASSIFIED AS SUBVERSIVES AND HOUNDED

FROM THEIR JOBS AND HOMES.

9. THE SHAM COURT SYSTEM OF THE SO-CALLED KRAJINA

AUTHORITIES IN VUKOVAR APES WESTERN MODELS, WITH

COURTS OF THE FIRST INSTANCE, COURTS OF THE SECOND,

ETC. IN FACT, ACCORDING TO EXPERTS DIRECTLY FAMILIAR

WITH DAY-TO-DAY OPERATIONS, ALMOST ALL THE CASES IN

THE SERBIAN- CONTROLLED VUKOVAR REGION ARE BROUGHT

AGAINST CROATS AND OTHER MINORITIES. A JUDGE AND TWO

ASSISTANT JUDGES RUN THE PROCEEDINGS. NEITHER THE

PROSECUTOR NOR THE SUPPOSED DEFENSE ATTORNEY IS

ALLOWED TO PLAY A DIRECT ROLE. THE PRESIDING JUDGE

CONFIDENTIAL

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 ZAGREB 01962 03 OF 08 091325Z

ASKS ALL QUESTIONS. THE DEFENSE ATTORNEY MUST SUBMIT

ALL/ALL QUESTIONS IN WRITING TO THE JUDGE -- EVEN

THOSE HE WANTS TO ASK HIS OWN CLIENT. THE JUDGE MAY

OR MAY NOT ASK THE QUESTIONS SUBMITTED. IN ONE

TYPICAL CASE OBSERVED BY AN UNPROFOR CIVILIAN POLICE

OFFICER, A SIXTY-TWO YEAR OLD CROATIAN WOMAN WAS

ACCUSED OF HAVING A HANDGRENADE, GUN, AND SHOTGUN IN

HER HOME. IN A "QUICKY" TRIAL THE DEFENDANT WAS

FOUND GUILTY, SENTENCED TO TEN MONTHS IN JAIL, AND

FINED 6,000 DINARS. THE SO-CALLED DEFENSE LAWYER DID

ALMOST NOTHING.

---------------------------------

10. UNPROFOR CIVILIAN AUTHORITIES TOLD EMBOFFS THAT,

IN ANOTHER CASE, THEY INFORMED KRAJINA "OFFICIALS" OF

ACCUSATIONS AGAINST EIGHT INDIVIDUALS WHO HAD TRIED

TO FORCE 23 NON-SERBS THEY WERE EXPELLING TO CROSS A

KNOWN MINEFIELD AT THE UNPA FRONT LINE. THE UN POLICE

PREVENTED THE ACT FROM OCCURRING AND COLLECTED NAMES

OF 22 WITNESSES TO THE INCIDENT. NO ACTION HAS BEEN

TAKEN BY THE KRAJINA AUTHORITIES.

IMPLICATED (OR POSITIVELY IDENTIFIED) ARE OFTEN

MEMBERS OF THE SO-CALLED "SPECIAL POLICE" (SEE BELOW).

16. MEMBERS OF THE SO-CALLED "SPECIAL POLICE" ARE

ALSO BELIEVED TO BE DIRECTLY INVOLVED IN CRIMES

AGAINST THE CIVILIAN POPULATION. THESE FORCES ARE A

SUBTERFUGE DESIGNED TO AVOID THE LIMITS IN THE VANCE

PLAN AND WERE ORIGINALLY MEMBERS OF THE YUGOSLAV

NATIONAL ARMY (JNA). WHEN RESTRICTIONS WERE PLACED

ON THE JNA AND THEIR REMOVAL ORDERED, THEY BECAME

"TERRITORIAL DEFENSE." WHEN THE VANCE PLAN CALLED

FOR THEIR DEMOBILIZATION, THE SAME PEOPLE BECAME

"SPECIAL POLICE" OR "BORDER POLICE." THEIR EXACT

NUMBERS ARE A MATTER OF DISPUTE.

17. ACCORDING TO UN ESTIMATES, THE NUMBER OF SERBIAN

POLICE JUMPED FROM 150 TO 800 DURING THE TRANSITION

PERIOD AT THE SETTING UP OF THE UNPA. A "SPECIAL

POLICE" OF SOME 2,000 ALSO EXISTS AND OPERATES IN THE

NORTHERN PART OF THE UNPA. ON JULY 18, 1992, THE

SO-CALLED "REPUBLIC OF KRAJINA" ASSERTED ITS CONTROL

AND REFUSED TO PROVIDE ANY FURTHER INFORMATION ON THE

NUMBERS OF "POLICE." NO FURTHER SURRENDER OF WEAPONS

FROM PARAMILITARY GROUPS, AS CALLED FOR IN THE VANCE

PLAN, HAS TAKEN PLACE SINCE THAT DATE.

ARKAN AND OTHER PARAMILITARIES

------------------------------

19. THE NOTORIOUS SERBIAN TERRORIST ARKAN HAS BEEN

SEEN AGAIN IN THE UNPA. HE WAS REPORTEDLY FETED AS A

GUEST SPEAKER AT A RECENT PARTY IN THE REGION. THE

COMPOUND AND TRAINING COMPLEX AT ERDUT NEXT TO UN
