SUBJECT: NSC COALITION MEMBERS UPDATE DELIVERED: MFA

REF: STATE 89723

Classified By: Ambassador Edward W. Gnehm. Reasons 1.5 (b,d)

1. (S) In an April 8 meeting with FonMin Muasher that

covered a number of issues (septel), the Ambassador delivered

the April 7 NSC Coalition Members Update, as instructed

reftel. Muasher welcomed the information and said he would

look forward to receiving future updates as they become

available. As per the preferred mechanism to do so, Muasher

said he would prefer to get these updates by fax from the

Embassy. To that end, we recommend that upcoming NSC

Coalition Partners Updates be e-mailed to DCM Gregory Berry

(berrygl@state.gov), PolCouns Douglas Silliman

(sillimanda@state.gov), and PolOff Sam Kotis

(kotissd@state.gov).
