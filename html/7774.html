SUBJECT: CROATIAN GRUMBLING ABOUT TIRANA VENUE FOR ADRIATIC

CHARTER SIGNING

CLASSIFIED BY: AMBASSADOR LAWRENCE ROSSIN FOR REASONS 1.5 (B,D)

1. (C) THE DECISION TO SIGN THE ADRIATIC CHARTER IN TIRANA,

AND NOT IN WASHINGTON, LED TO GRUMBLING IN THE CROATIAN

FOREIGN MINISTRY AND SPECULATION IN THE PRESS HERE THAT

CROATIA WAS BEING PUNISHED FOR ITS REFUSAL TO SUPPORT OUR

APPROACH ON IRAQ. THE FOREIGN MINISTRY SOUGHT -- WITH NO

SUCCESS -- TO CONVINCE OTHER A3 COUNTRIES TO PUSH FOR A

CHANGE IN VENUE BACK TO WASHINGTON, EVEN IF IT WOULD HAVE

MEANT A DELAY. PICULA WOULD HAVE PREFERRED A HIGH-PROFILE

SIGNING IN WASHINGTON, WITH BILATERAL MEETINGS BEFORE AND

AFTER, TO VALIDATE THE GOC\'S PROGRESS TOWARD MEMBERSHIP IN

NATO AS THE COALITION ENTERS AN ELECTION SEASON. THE CHOICE

OF TIRANA AS A VENUE WILL ALSO HIGHLIGHT THE THINGS ALBANIA

IS DOING AND CROATIA IS NOT, LIKE SIGNING AN ARTICLE 98

AGREEMENT AND MAKING SIGNIFICANT CONTRIBUTIONS TO EFFORTS IN

IRAQ.

2. (C) THE CROATIAN PRESS, SPURRED ON BY MALICIOUS LEAKS

FROM THE DEFENSE MINISTRY, IS SPECULATING THAT THE U.S. CHOSE

TIRANA FOR THE SIGNING IN ORDER TO PUNISH THE RACAN

GOVERNMENT. POLITICAL COMMENTATORS CLAIM THAT THE U.S.

DECISION WAS PREDICATED BY CROATIA\'S REFUSAL TO BE NAMED AS A

MEMBER OF THE COALITION OF THE WILLING, BY CROATIA\'S REFUSAL

TO GRANT OVERFLIGHT CLEARANCE FOR MILITARY AIRCRAFT BOUND FOR

IRAQ, AND ALSO IN RESPONSE TO DEROGATORY REMARKS ABOUT

INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY REPRESENTATIVES (INCLUDING THE U.S.

AMBASSADOR) MADE RECENTLY BY DEPUTY PRIME MINISTER GORAN

GRANIC.

3. (C) THE GOC IS DOING ITS BEST TO TURN OFF SPECULATION

THAT THE TIRANA VENUE WAS CHOSEN AS "PUNISHMENT" FOR CROATIA.

CROATIA\'S MAP COORDINATOR EXPLAINED TO THE PRESS THE

SIGNIFICANCE OF SECRETARY POWELL\'S PERSONAL COMMITMENT TO THE

CHARTER AND POINTED OUT HOW DIFFICULT IT HAS BEEN FOR THE

SECRETARY TO FIND A TIME -- IN THE MIDDLE OF A KEY TRIP TO

SIPDIS

THE MIDEAST -- TO SIGN THE DOCUMENT. ANALYSTS BOTH IN AND

OUT OF GOVERNMENT WILL PROBABLY NOT BE CONVINCED. FOR MANY

OF THEM, CROATIA IS THE CENTER OF THE UNIVERSE, AND EVERY

WORLD EVENT WHICH AFFECTS CROATIA MUST HAVE SOMEHOW BEEN

BROUGHT ABOUT BY CROATIA\'S OWN ACTION.

ROSSIN
