SUBJECT: YOUR MAY 31 - JUNE 1 VISIT TO JORDAN

Classified By: Ambassador Edward W. Gnehm. Reasons 1.5 (b,d).

1. (C) Director Mueller: It was a pleasure speaking with you

last week regarding your upcoming visit to the region. As

you will recall, at that time I was not certain whether King

Abdullah or GID Director Sa\'ad Kheir would be in Jordan at

the end of the month or traveling abroad. I am happy to

report that the Palace has just informed us that the King and

Sa\'ad, along with PSD director Tahseen Shurdom, will be in

Amman May 31 - June 1 and available to meet with you on June

1. As we discussed, your visit will offer a timely

opportunity to review the excellent cooperation we have with

the Jordanians on a range of law enforcement and security

issues and to discuss ideas for most effectively working

together in the future.

2. (C) I would like to extend once again an invitation for

you to stay with me at the Residence, if that would be

convenient. I could accommodate one other senior member of

your staff as well. Due to space limitations, other members

of your delegation would spend the night at a nearby hotel.

Please let me know what your preferences are and we will plan

accordingly.

3. (C) Once again, I look forward to seeing you in Amman on

May 31 for what I am sure will be a productive dialogue with

the Jordanians.

Warm Regards,

Edward W. "Skip" Gnehm

Ambassador

GNEHM
