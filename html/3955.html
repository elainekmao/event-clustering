1. (C) ON OCTOBER 10 AT THE MFA, POLOFF MET WITH TIZIANA

MARINI, DEPUTY OFFICE DIRECTOR FOR SOUTHEAST ASIAN AFFAIRS,

TO DELIVER REF A DEMARCHE ON PROTECTING VIETNAMESE

MONTAGNARDS. AS HER PREDECESSOR RESPONDED TO THE PREVIOUS

DEMARCHE ON THIS ISSUE (REF B), MARINI SAID THAT THE GOI WAS

ACTING WITHIN EU POLICY. SHE ADDED, HOWEVER, THAT THE GOI

WAS CLOSELY MONITORING THE SITUATION AND WELL AWARE OF THE

NEED TO MAINTAIN PRESSURE ON THE GVN AND RGC.

2. (C) MARINI SHARED DETAILS ON BOTH THE EU\'S APPROACH AND

ITALY\'S POINT OF VIEW. IN APRIL, THE EU DEMARCHED THE RGC ON

THE NEED TO ABIDE BY THE GENEVA CONVENTIONS AND COOPERATE

WITH UNHCR. IN ADDITION, IT HAD DEMARCHED THE GVN ON THE

NEED TO IMPROVE THE ECONOMIC AND HUMAN RIGHTS SITUATION OF

CENTRAL HIGHLANDERS. SHE NOTED THAT THE VIETNAMESE

CONSTITUTION GUARANTEES FREEDOM OF RELIGION AS LONG AS

RELIGIOUS BELIEFS DO NOT INTERFERE WITH THE POLITICAL SYSTEM.

THE GVN REGARDS SOME PROTESTANT SECTS AMONG THE MONTAGNARDS

AS BEING COMMITTED TO THE OVERTHROW OF THE ESTABLISHED

POLITICAL ORDER.

3. (C) IN MAY, AN EU DELEGATION VISITED VIETNAM AND THE

CENTRAL HIGHLANDS, WHERE IT MET WITH PROVINCIAL AUTHORITIES.

WHILE THE EU HAS PROVIDED AID TO THAT AREA, IT IS STILL QUITE

UNDERDEVELOPED. THE GVN MADE A COMMITMENT TO THE DELEGATION

TO PRIORITIZE DEVELOPMENT OF RURAL AREAS WHERE ETHNIC

MINORITIES LIKE THE MONTAGNARDS LIVE. GVN OFFICIALS CONSIDER

THOSE WHO HAVE FLED THE CENTRAL HIGHLANDS FOR CAMBODIA TO BE

ECONOMIC MIGRANTS, NOT REFUGEES. WHILE NOT TAKING A POSITION

ON THE REASON FOR THE OUTFLOW OF VIETNAMESE MONTAGNARDS,

MARINI STATED THAT THE GOI TOOK THE GVN DEVELOPMENT
