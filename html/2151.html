SUBJECT: AFGHANISTAN\'S FUTURE - DUTCH QUESTIONS

REF: A. STATE 192153

B. TOKYO 7585

CLASSIFIED BY: POLCOUNS MARY E. DALY, REASONS 1.5 B/D

1. (U) THIS IS AN ACTION MESSAGE, CHARGE HAS SCHEDULED AN

APPOINTMENT WITH DUTCH DEPUTY POLITICAL SECRETARY GENERAL

HERMAN SCHAPER ON NOVEMBER 8 TO DISCUSS AFGHANISTAN\'S FUTURE

AND HE WOULD APPRECIATE TALKING POINTS TO ADDRESS DUTCH

QUESTIONS IN PARA 2 BELOW BY COB WASHINGTON TIME ON NOVEMBER

7.

2. (C) DURING MEETINGS ON NOVEMBER 5 TO DISCUSS REF A TALKING

POINTS ON AFGHANISTAN\'S FUTURE, SENIOR OFFICIALS IN THE DUTCH

MFA\'S SOUTH ASIA DEPARTMENT WERE LOOKING FOR A DUTCH ROLE IN

THE POLITICAL AND RECONSTRUCTION PROCESS, AS WELL AS MORE

INFORMATION ON U.S. VIEWS. WE WOULD APPRECIATE GUIDANCE ON

THE FOLLOWING POINTS IN ORDER TO MAXIMIZE THE EFFECTIVENESS

OF THE CHARGE\'S MEETING NOVEMBER 7:

-- THE DUTCH BELIEVE THAT THE AFGHAN SUPPORT GROUP SHOULD BE

THE FOCAL POINT FOR RECONSTRUCTION AND ASSISTANCE, RATHER

THAN ANY NEW ORGANIZATION. THEIR QUESTIONS: DOES THE U.S.

AGREE? IS THE JAPAN-HOSTED EVENT AT UNGA BEING DONE IN THE

CONTEXT OF THE AFGHAN SUPPORT GROUP? IF NOT, WHAT ROLE DOES

THE U.S. SEE FOR THE AFGHAN SUPPORT GROUP AND HOW WILL WE

ENSURE EFFICIENT USE OF AID?

-- THE DUTCH ASKED FOR U.S. VIEWS ON SRSG BRAHIMI\'S PROPOSAL

TO HOST A MEETING ON AFGHANISTAN\'S FUTURE IN MECCA, SAUDI

ARABIA. THE MFA\'S INITIAL, UNOFFICIAL REACTION IS THAT IT

WAS AN INTERESTING IDEA BECAUSE IT COULD LEND AN "ISLAMIST"

MANTLE TO A POLITICAL SOLUTION. WHAT ARE U.S. VIEWS?

-- WITH REGARD TO THE EFFORTS TO DEVELOP A POST-TALIBAN

GOVERNMENT, THE DUTCH INDICATED THAT THERE HAD TO BE A ROLE

FOR THE COUNTRIES THAT WOULD END UP FOOTING THE BILL. ONE

IDEA, NOT YET OFFICIAL DUTCH POLICY, IS TO PRESS THE U.S. TO

EXPAND THE 6-PLUS-2 MECHANISM TO INCLUDE ENTITITES SUCH AS

THE EU AND INDIA. THEY SAID THEY HAD NOT/NOT TALKED ABOUT

THIS YET WITH OTHER EU COUNTRIES. PLEASE ADVISE ON WHAT WE

CAN SAY TO THE DUTCH ABOUT THE ROLE THEY COULD PLAY IN THE

EFFORT TO DEVELOP A POST-TALIBAN GOVERNMENT.

FENDRICK
