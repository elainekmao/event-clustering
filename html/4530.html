SUBJECT: THE HOLY SEE AND THE EUROPEAN CONSTITUTION:

COUNTERING SECULARISM, NOT TURKEY\'S EUROPEAN AMBITIONS

SUMMARY

1. (SBU) ALTHOUGH THE HOLY SEE IS PRESSING HARD FOR THE

EUROPEAN CONSTITUTION TO RECOGNIZE THE CONTINENT\'S CHRISTIAN

HERITAGE, THIS DRIVE IS IN NO WAY INTENDED TO SUGGEST HOLY

SEE OPPOSITION TO TURKEY\'S EU MEMBERSHIP. ON THE CONTRARY,

THIS EFFORT PREDATES THE CURRENT DEBATE OVER TURKEY, AND THE

HOLY SEE SPECIFICALLY FAVORS CONSTITUTIONAL LANGUAGE THAT

WOULD ENSURE FREEDOM OF WORSHIP TO ALL FAITHS. IN PUSHING

FOR A "CHRISTIAN HERITAGE" REFERENCE AND OTHER PROVISIONS

RELATING TO RELIGION, THE HOLY SEE IS SEEKING A FOUNDATION

ON WHICH TO COUNTER THE INCREASINGLY DOMINANT SECULARISM

WITHIN EUROPE AND TO REINVIGORATE THE ROLE OF RELIGION IN

EUROPEAN CULTURE. THE HOLY SEE IN NO WAY SEEKS TO EXCLUDE

OTHER FAITHS OR PHILOSOPHIES, AND HAS TOLD US DIRECTLY THEY

HAVE NO PROBLEM WITH A PREDOMINANTLY ISLAMIC STATE SUCH AS

TURKEY BEING A MEMBER OF THE EUROPEAN UNION -- A DECISION IN

WHICH THEY HAVE NO DIRECT ROLE. END SUMMARY.

-------------------------------------

EUROPE\'S CHRISTIAN ROOTS IN ARID SOIL

-------------------------------------

2. (U) THE HOLY SEE IS PRESSING EUROPEANS HARD TO ENSURE

THAT THE PROPOSED EUROPEAN CONSTITUTION INCLUDES A REFERENCE

TO THE CONTINENT\'S CHRISTIAN HERITAGE, WHILE AT THE SAME

TIME MAKING CLEAR THAT JURIDICAL PROVISION SHOULD BE MADE TO

PROTECT ALL RELIGIOUS FAITHS. FROM THE POPE ON DOWN, THE

HOLY SEE HAS BEEN LOBBYING EUROPEAN POLICY MAKERS ACTIVELY

IN RECENT MONTHS. ACCORDING TO THE POPE, "CHRISTIANITY IS A

CENTRAL AND INHERENT ELEMENT" OF THE EUROPEAN IDENTITY, AND

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 03 VATICA 05753 01 OF 03 271518Z

"THE CHRISTIAN FAITH FORMED THE CULTURE OF THE CONTINENT."

FRAMING THE MOTIVATION BEHIND THE HOLY SEE\'S CURRENT FOCUS

ON THE EU CONSTITUTION, THE POPE TOLD THE DIPLOMATIC CORPS

IN HIS ANNUAL ADDRESS LAST JANUARY (REF A) THAT "THE

MARGINALIZATION OF RELIGIONS, WHICH HAVE CONTRIBUTED AND

CONTINUE TO CONTRIBUTE TO THE CULTURE AND HUMANITY OF WHICH

EUROPE IS LEGITIMATELY PROUD, STRIKES ME BOTH AS AN

INJUSTICE AND AN ERROR IN PERSPECTIVE." REFERRING TO THE

HOLY SEE\'S DESIRE FOR CONSTITUTIONAL RECOGNITION, HE ADDED:

"TO RECOGNIZE AN INDISPUTABLE HISTORICAL FACT IN NO WAY

MEANS TO DISREGARD THE MODERN DEMAND OF STATES TO HAVE AN

APPROPRIATE NON-CONFESSIONAL CHARACTER."

3. (U) THIS FALL, THE HOLY SEE HAS INTENSIFIED ITS OUTREACH

TO EU COUNTRIES WHEN IT CALLED IN ALL EUROPEAN AMBASSADORS

TO OUTLINE ITS PRIORITIES FOR THE FINAL CONSTITUTION. THESE

PRIORITIES ENCOMPASS REFERENCES TO FIVE FUNDAMENTAL VALUES:

CENTRALITY OF THE HUMAN PERSON, RELIGIOUS LIBERTY,

SOLIDARITY (THE SEARCH FOR THE COMMON GOOD), SUBSIDIARITY

AND PARTICIPATION, AND THE SHARING OF RESPONSIBILITY BETWEEN

THE CHURCHES AND THE EU.

4. (U) IN ADDITION, THE HOLY SEE IS ALSO SEEKING JURIDICAL

RECOGNITION OF THE CHURCHES AND RELIGIOUS COMMUNITIES WITHIN

THE CONSTITUTION. THEY NOTE THAT RELIGION, CHURCHES, AND

RELIGIOUS COMMUNITIES HAVE BEEN AFFECTED BY EU LEGISLATION

FOR DECADES, BUT THAT THERE ARE NO REFERENCES TO RELIGIONS

IN THE TREATY PROVISIONS OR ANNEXED PROTOCOLS. THEY POINT

OUT THAT ISSUES SUCH AS NON-DISCRIMINATION, LABOR LAW, DATA

PROTECTION, CULTURE, MEDIA LAW, CUSTOMS, FINANCES AND OTHER

AREAS ALL IMPACT ON THE CHURCH, AND THAT THE ABSENCE OF

JURIDICAL REFERENCES TO THEM WOULD CONSTITUTE A SERIOUS

VACUUM. CONSEQUENTLY, THE HOLY SEE IS SEEKING THROUGH THE

COMMISSION OF EPISCOPATES OF THE EU A PROVISION ASSURING THE

RIGHT TO SELF-DETERMINATION OF CHURCHES AND RELIGIOUS

COMMUNITIES IN THEIR TEACHING AND ORGANIZATION. THEY DO NOT

BELIEVE THAT RELIGIOUS FREEDOM OR CORPORATIVE SELF-

DETERMINATION IS GUARANTEED BY ARTICLE 10 OF THE CHARTER OF

FUNDAMENTAL RIGHTS OF THE EU OR ARTICLE 9 OF THE EUROPEAN

CONVENTION OF HUMAN RIGHTS. FINALLY, THE HOLY SEE WOULD

LIKE TO INCLUDE A PROVISION FOR CIVIL SOCIETY DIALOGUE AND

CONSULTATION SPECIFICALLY RELATING TO THE ROLE OF CHURCHES

AND RELIGIOUS COMMUNITIES.

5. (U) HAVING OUTLINED ITS GOALS, THE POPE HAS USED EVERY

OPPORTUNITY THIS FALL TO PRESS HIS CASE. IN HIS MEETINGS

WITH EU AMBASSADOR\'S HE HAS REPEATED HIS INSISTENCE THAT

EUROPE MUST BE FOUNDED ON "COMMON VALUES, TAKING INTO

ACCOUNT THE CHRISTIAN ROOTS OF THE DIFFERENT PEOPLE" WHO

LIVE ON THE CONTINENT. LIKEWISE, THE POPE\'S MEETING WITH

PRESIDENT OF THE EUROPEAN CONVENTION, VALERIE GISCARD

D\'ESTAING, FOCUSED ON THE CONCERNS OF EUROPE\'S CHRISTIAN

MAJORITY "TO SEE THEIR IDENTITY AND THEIR SPECIFIC

CONTRIBUTION TO THE LIFE OF EUROPEAN SOCIETY RESPECTED" AND

TO HAVE THE STATUS THEY ENJOY IN CERTAIN NATIONAL

-----------------------------------------

CHRISTIAN HERITAGE AS SEEN FROM THE CURIA

-----------------------------------------

6. (SBU) VATICAN DIRECTOR FOR EUROPEAN AFFAIRS MSGR. ETTORE

BALESTRERO EXPANDED ON THE HOLY SEE\'S VIEWS IN A NOVEMBER 18

MEETING WITH POLOFF. BALESTRERO ESTIMATED THAT ON A

CONTINENT OF ROUGHLY 700 MILLION PEOPLE, ABOUT 500 MILLION

WERE CHRISTIAN. (NOTE: THE VATICAN\'S STATISTICAL YEARBOOK OF

THE CHURCH COUNTS 702 MILLION POPULATION IN EUROPE WHICH

INCLUDES ALL OF THE OSCE COUNTRIES EXCEPT THE U.S., CANADA

AND TURKEY. END NOTE.) FURTHERMORE, BALESTRERO RECALLED,

CHRISTIANITY, UNLIKE OTHER FAITHS, WAS THE COMMON ELEMENT

THAT BOUND THE CLASSIC EUROPEAN CIVILIZATIONS OF GREECE AND

ROME TO MODERN EUROPE. IT WAS CHRISTIAN MONKS WHO HAD

PRESERVED EUROPE\'S KNOWLEDGE, AND JUDEO-CHRISTIAN ETHICS

THAT INFORM MODERN NOTIONS OF HUMAN RIGHTS AND JUSTICE. THE

HOLY SEE WAS SIMPLY SEEKING TO ENSURE THAT EUROPE\'S

CONSTITUTION RECOGNIZES THIS "REALITY" -- BUT WITHOUT ANY

NORMATIVE OR OPERATIONAL CONSEQUENCES. BALESTRERO SAID THE

HOLY SEE STILL SUPPORTS LANGUAGE IN A EUROPEAN CONSTITUTION

GUARANTEEING FREEDOM OF RELIGION, WITH IMPLEMENTATION

DEVOLVED TO THE NATION-STATES ACCORDING TO THE PRINCIPLE OF

SUBSIDIARITY. THE PRACTICAL EFFECT OF THIS, HE ADMITTED,

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 03 VATICA 05753 02 OF 03 271520Z

WOULD BE TO ENSHRINE THE PRIVILEGES AND RIGHTS OF THE

CATHOLIC CHURCH IN COUNTRIES WHERE CONCORDATS PROVIDE FOR

THEM, AND ALSO FOR THE ORTHODOX CHURCH IN COUNTRIES WHERE IT

PREDOMINATES.

7. (SBU) BALESTRERO ALSO NOTED A MISUNDERSTANDING OF THE

HOLY SEE\'S ROLE IN THIS DEBATE: THE IDEA FOR LANGUAGE IN

THE EUROPEAN CONSTITUTION ACKNOWLEDGING CHRISTIANITY WAS

DEVELOPED BY A MEETING OF THE COUNCIL OF EUROPEAN CHURCHES

(CEC - A PROTESTANT GROUP). ONLY LATER DID THE HOLY SEE

(AND GREEK ORTHODOX) JOIN THEIR VOICES TO IT. THE HOLY SEE,

BALESTRERO CONTINUED, VIEWS THIS LANGUAGE AS ENHANCING THE

IMPORTANCE OF ALL RELIGIONS IN EUROPE\'S DAILY LIFE.

-------------------------------------

A CHRISTIAN HERITAGE BUT ROOM FOR ALL

-------------------------------------

8. (SBU) CHALLENGED ON THE UNDENIABLE CONTRIBUTION TO

EUROPEAN CIVILIZATION FROM A JEWISH PRESENCE THAT PREDATES

THE CHRISTIAN ONE, AND THE HALF-MILLENNIUM LONG ISLAMIC

CONTROL OF IBERIA AND THE BALKANS, BALESTRERO SAID THE HOLY

SEE DOES NOT DENY THESE INFLUENCES, THOUGH THEY REGARD THE

CHRISTIAN INFLUENCE AS HISTORICALLY PREDOMINANT. MOREOVER,

THE HOLY SEE IS A CHRISTIAN ENTITY AND IS PUSHING FOR ITS

CHRISTIAN INTERESTS; IT HAS NO OBJECTION TO ANYONE ELSE

SEEKING TO INTERJECT LANGUAGE ON CONTRIBUTIONS FROM OTHER

SOURCES. HE SAID THE HOLY SEE HAD RECENTLY SHARED A TEXT OF

PROPOSED LANGUAGE WITH JEWISH LEADERS, WHO SUPPORTED IT.

MORE RECENTLY, DURING A VATICAN RADIO DISCUSSION ON THE

EUROPEAN CONSTITUTION, EUROPEAN PEOPLES PARTY PRESIDENT,

WILFRED MARTENS, NOTED THAT THE POPE IS PUSHING FOR LANGUAGE

SIMILAR TO THAT OF THE POLISH CONSTITUTION TO THE EFFECT

THAT JUDEO-CHRISTIAN NOTIONS OF HUMAN RIGHTS AND JUSTICE CAN

BE ARRIVED AT FROM BELIEF IN GOD OR OTHER SOURCES.

9. (U) THE POPE HIMSELF, WHILE AFFIRMING THE IMPORTANCE OF

EUROPEAN COMMUNITY RECOGNITION OF THE CONTINENT\'S CHRISTIAN

HERITAGE, CAUTIONED IN A SEPTEMBER 13 ADDRESS THAT THE

DEVELOPMENT OF A COMMON EUROPEAN IDENTITY SHOULD NOT BE USED

AS AN EXCUSE FOR HOSTILITY TOWARDS MIGRANTS AND REFUGEES. HE

ARGUED AGAINST ANY TENDENCY TOWARD "ISOLATION FROM THE REST

OF THE WORLD."

COMMENT

-------

11. (SBU) GISCARD D\'ESTAING\'S DECISION TO LAUNCH HIS

BROADSIDE AGAINST TURKISH MEMBERSHIP IN THE EU SHORTLY AFTER

HIS MEETING WITH THE POPE HAS UNNECESSARILY LED MANY TO VIEW

THE HOLY SEE AS INVOLVED IN WHAT IS A PURELY EUROPEAN

DOGFIGHT. AT NO TIME IN ANY OF OUR DISCUSSIONS OF THE HOLY

SEE\'S LONGSTANDING CONCERNS ABOUT THE SHAPE OF THE FUTURE EU

CONSTITUTION HAS TURKEY\'S EU MEMBERSHIP EVER ARISEN. THE

HOLY SEE\'S INTERESTS -- BOTH IN IMBEDDING REFERENCES TO KEY

VALUES AND ENSURING JURIDICAL PROTECTIONS -- PREDATE CURRENT

DISCUSSIONS ABOUT TURKEY\'S PLACE IN EUROPE. TO BE SURE,

THERE ARE MANY CATHOLIC INTELLECTUALS, SUCH AS ITALIAN

MINISTER FOR EU AFFAIRS, ROCCO BUTTIGLIONE, WHO QUESTION

WHETHER A MUSLIM TURKEY SHARES EUROPE\'S VALUE SYSTEM, WHICH

THEY REGARD AS DERIVED FROM JUDEO-CHRISTIAN TRADITIONS. BUT

EVEN BUTTIGLIONE IN A RECENT SPEECH HERE LEFT OPEN THE DOOR

THAT TURKEY COULD OVERCOME THESE DOUBTS BY BETTER PROTECTING

HUMAN RIGHTS AND STRENGTHENING ITS DEMOCRACY. NEVERTHELESS,

THE HOLY SEE REGARDS ITS CHALLENGE NOT AS ONE OF KEEPING THE

MUSLIM INFLUENCE OUT OF EUROPE, BUT OF REINVIGORATING ITS

CHRISTIAN INFLUENCES. AS THE POPE TOLD THE ITALIAN

PARLIAMENT, HE WANTS TO ENSURE THAT "THE NEW FOUNDATIONS OF

THE EUROPEAN \'COMMON HOUSE\' WILL NOT LACK THE \'CEMENT\' OF

THAT EXTRAORDINARY RELIGIOUS, CULTURAL AND CIVIL PATRIMONY

WHICH HAS GIVEN EUROPE ITS GREATNESS DOWN THE CENTURIES."
