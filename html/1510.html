SUBJECT: CONGRATULATORY LETTER FROM MINISTER OF FOREIGN

AFFAIRS MUDENGE TO SECRETARY OF STATE COLIN POWELL

1. FOLLOWING IS THE TEXT OF A CONGRATULATORY LETTER FROM

MINISTER OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS I.S.G. MUDENGE TO SECRETARY

OF STATE COLIN POWELL THAT THE MINISTRY OF FOREIGN

AFFAIRS DELIVERED ON FEBRUARY 5. POST WILL POUCH THE

ORIGINAL TO AF/S.

2. BEGIN TEXT:

30 JANUARY, 2001

THE HONORABLE MR. COLIN POWELL

UNITED STATES SECRETARY OF STATE

WASHINGTON D.C.

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 02 HARARE 00638 051304Z

UNITED STATES OF AMERICA

DEAR MR. SECRETARY

IT IS WITH GREAT PLEASURE THAT I EXTEND TO YOU WARM AND

SINCERE CONGRATULATIONS ON YOUR APPOINTMENT AS SECRETARY

OF STATE OF THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA. YOUR

NOMINATION AND SUBSEQUENT CONFIRMATION FOR THE POST

CLEARLY DEMONSTRATE THE TRUST AND CONFIDENCE PRESIDENT

GEORGE W BUSH AND CONGRESS HAVE REPOSED IN YOU.

I LOOK FORWARD TO WORKING CLOSELY WITH YOU TO FURTHER

STRENGTHEN AND BROADEN THE CORDIAL RELATIONS THAT EXIST

BETWEEN THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA AND ZIMBABWE. IT IS

FURTHER MY WISH TO CO-OPERATE WITH YOU AT INTERNATIONAL

FORA IN ADDRESSING THE UNIVERSAL DEMANDS FOR

INTERNATIONAL PEACE AND SECURITY AS WELL AS IN THE

PROMOTION OF A JUST, FAIR AND EQUITABLE INTERNATIONAL

SOCIAL AND ECONOMIC ORDER.

I WISH YOU GOOD HEALTH AND SUCCESS IN THE DISCHARGE OF

THE ONEROUS RESPONSIBILITIES OF THE OFFICE OF THE UNITED

STATES SECRETARY OF STATE.

PLEASE ACCEPT, MR. SECRETARY, THE ASSURANCE OF MY HIGHEST

CONSIDERATION.

/S/

I S G MUDENGE

MINISTER OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 03 HARARE 00638 051304Z

REPUBLIC OF ZIMBABWE

END TEXT.
