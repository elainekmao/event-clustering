SUBJECT: PROGRAM EVALUATION: U.S. SPEAKER FDNY CHIEF JOE

CALLAN, APRIL 18-20, 2002

1. DESCRIPTION OF ACTIVITY: PA ROME HOSTED A NEW YORK

FIREFIGHTER FOR TWO PROGRAMS, AN INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE

ON FIREFIGHTER SAFETY, ORGANIZED BY THE ITALIAN MINISTRY

OF THE INTERIOR, AND A CEREMONY AND PROGRAM IN

CIVITAVECCHIA, NEAR ROME, APRIL 18-20.

2. DATES: APRIL 18-20, FISCAL YEAR: FY 2002 QUARTER: 3RD

UNCLASSIFIED

3. JUSTIFICATION/OBJECTIVE: PA ROME REQUESTED SUPPORT

FOR PROGRAMMING A REPRESENTATIVE FROM THE NEW YORK CITY

FIRE DEPARTMENT AS A U.S. SPEAKER TO PARTICIPATE IN AN

ITALIAN MINISTRY OF INTERIOR CONFERENCE ON SAFETY FOR

FIREFIGHTERS, ENTITLED: "FIREFIGHTERS: ENSURING SAFE

CONDITIONS IN RESCUE OPERATIONS," HELD IN ROME, APRIL 18-

19, 2002. THIS WAS A FOLLOW UP TO PA ROME\'S SPEAKER

PROGRAM WITH LOUIE CACCHIOLI AND JIM CARNEY FROM FDNY,

DECEMBER 1-8, 2001. THE CONFERENCE BROUGHT TOGETHER

REPRESENTATIVES FROM ITALIAN AND INTERNATIONAL FIRE

DEPARTMENTS, RESCUE ORGANIZATIONS, AND OFFICIALS FROM

MINISTRIES OF INTERIOR AND FOREIGN AFFAIRS TO DISCUSS

RESCUE OPERATIONS AND THE RISKS FACED BY FIREFIGHTERS AND

RESCUE WORKERS FROM BOTH NATURAL DISASTERS AND ACTS OF

TERROR. ADDED TO THIS WAS AN OPPORTUNITY FOR FURTHER

PROGRAMMING IN CIVITAVECCHIA, A TOWN NEAR ROME.

4. MPP UMBRELLA THEMES: TERRORISM/COOPERATION ON GLOBAL

ISSUES

5. AUDIENCES REACHED: CIVIC LEADERS, MINISTRY OF INTERIOR

OFFICIALS, ACADEMICS, LOCAL GOVERNMENT, JOURNALISTS,

FIREFIGHTERS

6. RESULT/IMPACT: EXCELLENT. AT THE ROME CONFERENCE, U.S.

SPEAKER CALLAN DESCRIBED HIS EXPERIENCES AS HEAD OF

OPERATIONS IN THE NORTH TOWER DURING THE SEPTEMBER 11

ATTACKS, AND SPOKE OF LESSONS LEARNED AT GROUND ZERO FOR

THE NEW YORK FIREFIGHTERS. IN CIVITAVECCHIA, A CITY NEAR

ROME, HE MET WITH THE MAYOR AND PARTICIPATED IN A

CEREMONY IN FRONT OF A MEMORIAL PLAQUE DEDICATED TO THE

UNCLASSIFIED

VICTIMS OF THE SEPTEMBER ATTACKS. IN BOTH EVENTS, THE

ITALIANS RESPONDED WITH ADMIRATION FOR THE NY

FIREFIGHTERS AND EXPRESSED THEIR FRIENDSHIP AND

SOLIDARITY TO THE CITY OF NEW YORK AND TO THE UNITED

STATES. OVERALL, THE PROGRAM HELPED TO REINFORCE THE

NEED TO COMBAT INTERNATIONAL TERRORISM BY BRINGING HOME

TO THE ITALIAN AUDIENCES THE HUMAN COSTS OF TERRORIST

ATTACKS. FINALLY, CALLAN\'S PROGRAM RESONATED EVEN MORE

WITH ITALIANS WHEN ITALY\'S OWN TALLEST SKYSCRAPER,

LOCATED IN MILAN, WAS HIT BY A SMALL PRIVATE PLANE ON

APRIL 18, AND MANY AT FIRST FEARED THAT IT WAS A

TERRORIST INCIDENT.

7. NON USG SOURCES OF IN-COUNTRY FUNDING/IN-KIND SUPPORT:

THE MINISTRY OF INTERIOR HOSTED TWO DINNERS DURING THE

CONFERENCE, WHILE THE CITY OF CIVITAVECCHIA HOSTED A

LUNCH FOR CHIEF CALLAN.

8. QUALITY OF U.S. AND AGENCY OFFICE SUPPORT: GOOD.
