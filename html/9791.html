SUBJECT: ITALIANS ON PREPARATIONS FOR PARIS PSI MEETING

REF: STATE 213073

CLASSIFIED BY: A/POLITICAL MINISTER COUNSELOR GARY ROBBINS FOR REASONS

1.5(B)(D)

1. (C) ITALY\'S REPORT ON THE BRISBANE PROLIFERATION SECURITY

INITIATIVE MEETING HAS BEEN CIRCULATED TO CONCERNED AGENCIES

FOR COMMENT IN PREPARATION FOR THE SEPTEMBER PARIS MEETING,

ACCORDING TO PIERO SARDI, ACTING HEAD OF THE ITALIAN MFA

DISARMAMENT AND ARMS CONTROL OFFICE. SARDI SAID IT IS

UNLIKELY THE ITALIAN GOVERNMENT WILL RESPOND WITH FEEDBACK

UNTIL THE END OF AUGUST DUE TO EU PRESIDENCY DUTIES AND THE

ANNUAL SUMMER HOLIDAYS WHICH HAVE LEFT THEM SHORT-STAFFED.

ITALY\'S REPRESENTATIVE TO THE BRISBANE MEETING, FILIPPO

FORMICA, WILL NOT BE IN THE OFFICE UNTIL AUGUST 11. FORMICA

IS EXPECTED TO ATTEND THE PARIS MEETING AS WELL. POL-MIL

OFFICER CONVEYED REFTEL INFORMATION REGARDING USG PLANS FOR

THE UPCOMING MEETINGS, WHICH SARDI SAID HE WOULD PASS ON TO

FORMICA UPON HIS RETURN. SARDI SAID HE PLANS TO COORDINATE

WITH PARIS TO SCHEDULE THE NEXT MEETING IN THE SECOND WEEK OF

SEPTEMBER TO AVOID CONFLICTING WITH EU WORKING GROUP MEETINGS

IN BRUSSELS SEPTEMBER 1-5.

2. (C) A/POL MINISTER COUNSELOR ALSO RAISED REFTEL POINTS

WITH GIOVANNI BRAUZZI, DIRECTOR OF THE MFA NATO AFFAIRS

OFFICE. BRAUZZI SUGGESTED THAT, AS PSI MOVES FORWARD,

PLANNERS SHOULD MAKE FULL USE OF THE EXPERIENCE EUROMARFOR

HAS GAINED DOING MARITIME INTERCEPT OPERATIONS IN THE ARABIAN

SEA.
