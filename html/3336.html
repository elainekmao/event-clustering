SUMMARY

-------

1. UNHCR AND WFP SIGNED A REVISED MEMORANDUM ON JULY 9

AIMED AT FURTHER STRENGTHENING COOPERATION IN THE PROVISION

OF FOOD AID FOR REFUGEES AND RETURNEES WORLDWIDE. ON A

PILOT ONE-YEAR TRIAL BASIS, WFP WILL ASSUME FULL

RESPONSIBILITY FOR THE FINAL DISTRIBUTION OF THE BASIC FOOD

RATION IN FIVE REFUGEE PROGRAMS. UNHCR WILL FULLY INVOLVE

WFP IN THE PLANNING AND EXECUTION OF REFUGEE ENUMERATION,

REGISTRATION AND VERIFICATION EXERCISES FOR ACTUAL OR

POTENTIAL BENEFICIARIES OF FOOD AID AND RELATED NON-FOOD

ITEMS. FURTHER, WFP AND UNHCR WILL TAKE MEASURES TO ENSURE

THAT AT LEAST 80 PER CENT OF FOOD INPUTS ARE DIRECTLY

MANAGED BY THE ADULT FEMALE IN THE HOUSEHOLD. END SUMMARY.

2. RUUD LUBBERS, UN HIGH COMMISSIONER FOR REFUGEES (UNHCR)

PARTNERS UPON REQUEST. END NOTE.

COMMENT

-------

4. WHO CONTROLS THE FINAL DISTRIBUTION OF FOOD TO REFUGEES,

RETURNEES AND IDPS HAS BEEN HISTORICALLY A POINT OF UNHCR-

WFP CONTENTION. WHEN THEN HIGH COMMISSIONER OGATA ADDRESSED

THE WFP BOARD IN OCTOBER 1996 SHE AFFIRMED UNHCR\'S LEAD

POSITION: "I FEEL QUITE STRONGLY THAT RETAINING

RESPONSIBILITY FOR DISTRIBUTION, WHETHER OF FOOD OR NON-

FOOD ITEMS, IS ESSENTIAL FOR THE DISCHARGE OF MY MANDATE."

THIS MOU MOVES BEYOND THIS "BARRIER" (AND IN USMISSION\'S

VIEW WISELY SO) IN CONFIRMING WFP\'S LEAD IN FINAL FOOD AID

DISTRIBUTION IN FIVE REFUGEE SETTINGS ON A ONE-YEAR PILOT

BASIS. MOREOVER, "IF (AS THE UN PRESS RELEASE ON THE

SIGNING COMMENTED) THE NEW DIVISION OF LABOR PROVES MORE

EFFECTIVE, WFP WILL TAKE OVER THE TASK (I.E., FINAL FOOD

AID DISTRIBUTION IN ALL WFP/UNHCR PROGRAMS) WORLDWIDE."
