SUBJECT: NIGERIA: PARTICIPATION IN THE MARCH 18-22 UN

FINANCING FOR DEVELOPMENT CONFERENCE

REF: STATE 24465

1. Nigerian President Olusegun Obasanjo is now planning to

attend the UN Conference on Financing for Development. This

is a change in plans from just last week. Minister of

Finance Adamu Ciroma heads the delegation for the

Ministerial.

2. We have no information on side events planned by Nigeria

at Monterrey. We will meet with Special Advisor to the

President Dr. Obi Ezekwesili next week to discuss Nigeria\'s

participation.

3. President Obasanjo has been outspoken in his demands for

debt relief for Nigeria and may see this as a venue to

highlight debt relief as well as promote the New Partnership

for African Development (NEPAD). He may, however, be

amenable to the thrust of some of the new initiatives. We

will follow up with more detail after meeting Dr. Ezekwesili.
