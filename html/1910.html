1. (C) SUMMARY: THE VATICAN IS STRONGLY URGING U.S.

PRESENCE AT WCAR, AT LEAST AT THE WORKING LEVEL.

ACKNOWLEDGING U.S. CONCERNS, DELEGATION DEPUTY AND AMCIT

MONSIGNOR FRANK DEWANE PLEDGED VATICAN WILLINGNESS TO WORK

FOR COMPROMISES IN THE FINAL WCAR DOCUMENT. NONETHELESS,

MSGR. DEWANE TOLD POLOFF THAT WCAR CANNOT IGNORE A PATTERN

OF WHAT HE TERMED RACIAL DISCRIMINATION WITHIN ISRAEL,

ACCENTUATED SINCE THE BREAKDOWN OF THE PEACE PROCESS. END

SUMMARY.

2. (C) POLOFF MET 8/27 WITH VATICAN WCAR DELEGATION DEPUTY

AND AMCIT MONSIGNOR FRANK DEWANE. MSGR. DEWANE

ACKNOWLEDGED U.S. SENSITIVITIES ABOUT THE DURBAN CONFERENCE

WHICH HAD LED TO THE DECISION AGAINST ATTENDANCE BY THE

SECRETARY. HOWEVER, MSGR. DEWANE URGED SOME FORM OF U.S.

SIPDIS

ATTENDANCE, AT LEAST AT THE WORKING LEVEL. APART FROM THE

NEED FOR U.S. INPUT, MSGR. DEWANE ARGUED THAT THE U.S.,

DESPITE EVIDENT SHORTCOMINGS, REPRESENTED AN OVERALL

SUCCESS STORY IN THE STRUGGLE AGAINST RACISM THAT REMAINS A

GLOBAL MODEL. HE PLEDGED THAT IN DURBAN THE VATICAN WOULD

WORK WITH THE USG FOR COMPROMISES IN THE LANGUAGE OF THE

FINAL DOCUMENT.

3. (C) MSGR. DEWANE EXPRESSED UNDERSTANDING AND

APPRECIATION FOR THE STAUNCH U.S. DEFENSE AT WCAR OF

ISRAEL. HOWEVER, WCAR, HE INSISTED, COULD NOT IGNORE THE

FACT OF RACIAL DISCRIMINATION WITHIN ISRAEL, ESPECIALLY

GIVEN THE VOLATILE AND VIOLENT CLIMATE IN THE MIDDLE EAST

THAT HAS FOLLOWED THE BREAKDOWN OF THE PEACE PROCESS. A

PALESTINIAN IN ISRAEL SIMPLY DID NOT HAVE THE SAME RIGHTS

AS AN ISRAELI, ACCORDING TO MSGR. DEWANE. MSGR. DEWANE

CONFIDENTIAL

SINGLED OUT CURTAILED OF FREEDOM OF MOVEMENT FOR

PALESTINIANS, WHO ARE SUBJECT TO CHECKS AND CONTROLS THAT

HE LIKENED TO REPRESSIVE POLICE PRACTICES IN CHINA AND THE

ERSTWHILE SOVIET UNION. NONETHELESS, MSGR. DEWANE THOUGHT

IT WOULD BE TRAGIC IF WCAR BECAME A VEHICLE FOR ANTI-

SEMITIC PREJUDICE AND ATTACKS ON ZIONISM. THE VATICAN, HE

NOTED, HAD REITERATED ITS CONDEMNATION OF ANTI-SEMITISM IN

A PAMPHLET ON THE CHURCH AND RACISM, RE-RELEASED WITH A NEW

INTRODUCTION LAST WEEK IN ADVANCE OF WCAR.

4. (C) CRISIS IN THE MIDDLE EAST HAD RADICALLY ALTERED THE

WCAR AGENDA, OFFERED MSGR. DEWANE. ORIGINALLY, THE VATICAN

ANTICIPATED ONLY A "DEEP APOLOGY" RATHER THAN "REPARATIONS"

FROM DEVELOPED WESTERN NATIONS FOR THE ILLS OF COLNIALISM.

MSGR. DEWANE COMMENTED THAT THE VATICANHAS LATELY

DISCOVERED THE DIPLOMATIC VALUE OF ANAPOLOGY. HOWEVER,

THE VATICAN NOW FAVORS SOME FRM OF COMPENSATION RATHER

THAN ONLY A COST-FREE EA CULPA. DEBT RELIEF, IN

PARTICULAR, REMAINED ACONSIDERABLE CHALLENGE, WITH WESTERN

NATIONS, DEPITE SOME PROGRESS, STILL LARGELY SHIRKING

THEIR MORAL RESPONSIBILITY.

5. (C) COMMENTING ON THE PREPCOM MEETINGS IN GENEVA, MSGR.

DEWAYNE EXPRESSED FRUSTRATION AT THE TACTICS OF CERTAIN

DELEGATIONS, WHO EXPLOITED WCAR TO DEFLECT ATTENTION FROM

THEIR OWN DOMESTIC ABUSES AND HAD USED THE DELIBERATIONS TO

TO WEAKEN OTHER U.N. DOCUMENTS, SUCH AS THE CONVENTION ON

THE CHILD. MSGR. DEWANE REPEATEDLY FINGERED THE PAKISTANI

DELEGATION AS UNCOOPERATIVE, NONETHELESS CONCEDING ITS

EFFECTIVENESS IN DIVERTING WCAR FROM THE MALTREATMENT OF

CHRISTIANS IN PAKISTAN. THE VATICAN, MSGR. DEWANE NOTED,

HAD SUCCESSFULLY RETAINED LANGUAGE IN THE PREPCOM DOCUMENTS

TO PROTECT RELIGIOUS MINORITIES AGAINST DISCRIMINATION,

DESPITE INITIAL OPPOSITION FROM EU NEGOTIATORS AS WELL AS

THE PRC DELEGATION.

6. (C) COMMENT: MSGR. DEWANE LEFT A STRONG IMPRESSION OF

VATICAN DISAPPOINTMENT IN THE OUTCOME OF WCAR. WHILE

VATICAN SYMPATHIES LIE STRONGLY WITH THE PALESTINIAN

POPULATION WITHIN ISRAEL, THE HUE AND CRY AT WCAR OVER

ZIONISM AS RACISM REPRESENTS A LOST OPPORTUNITY FOR THE

VATICAN TO DRAW ATTENTION TO THE PLIGHT OF CHRISTIAN

MINORITIES ELSEWHERE, WHO OFTEN ARE ALSO ETHNIC/RACIAL

MINORITIES. VATICAN AMBIVALENCE, HOWEVER, DOES NOT

TRANSLATE INTO ALIGNMENT WITH U.S. INTERESTS. YET THERE IS

REASON TO BELIEVE THE VATICAN WOULD LIKE WCAR TO LOOK

BEYOND THE HORIZONS OF CONFLICT IN THE MIDDLE EAST.
