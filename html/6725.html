SUBJECT: TFIZ01: ITALY TO ESTABLISH IRAQ RECONSTRUCTION

TASK FORCE

REF: A. STATE 65142

B. ROME 1217

CLASSIFIED BY: ECON M/C SCOTT KILNER, REASONS 1.5 (B, D)

1. (C) IN A MARCH 21 MEETING WITH POL MINCOUNS AND ECONOFF,

ANTONIO BADINI, THE ITALIAN MFA\'S DIRECTOR GENERAL FOR THE

MIDDLE EAST, SAID THAT HE WOULD ESTABLISH AN INTER-AGENCY

TASK FORCE ON IRAQ RECONSTRUCTION FOLLOWING HIS TRIP THIS

WEEK TO WASHINGTON AND NEW YORK. BADINI HAS YET TO ESTABLISH

THE FINANCIAL AND HUMAN RESOURCE REQUIREMENTS FOR THE TASK

FORCE BUT IS LOOKING TO HIS MEETINGS IN THE U.S. AS A WAY TO

LEARN FROM THE USG\'S "METHODOLOGY" OF COORDINATING IRAQ

RECONSTRUCTION. BADINI DESCRIBED THE GOI\'S INTEREST IN BROAD

ENGAGEMENT OVER THE NEAR- AND MEDIUM-TERM TO INCLUDE

POST-CONFLICT SUPPORT, HUMANITARIAN ASSISTANCE AND EVENTUALLY

COMMERCIAL CONTRACTS.

2. (C) WHILE BADINI DID NOT SPECIFY AMOUNTS OF MONETARY

ASSISTANCE, HE DID MENTION THAT SPECIAL FINANCIAL MEASURES

WOULD BE TAKEN TO SECURE NECESSARY FUNDS. MEANWHILE, BADINI

WAS SUPPORTIVE REGARDING SUCH GOI CONTRIBUTIONS OF

SPECIALISTS IN BIOLOGICAL AND CHEMICAL WEAPONS AS MIGHT BE

NEEDED IN THE IMMEDIATE POST-WAR PHASE (REF A), SAYING "WE

SHOULD CONTRIBUTE TO THAT FOR SURE." BADINI ALSO AGREED ON

THE NEED TO BE ATTENTIVE TO TURKEY\'S PRIOR COMMITMENT TO

RESPECT THE TERRITORIAL INTEGRITY OF IRAQ. BADINI ALSO

SHARED HIS PARTICULAR INTEREST IN ENCOURAGING STRONGER USG

ENGAGEMENT WITH, AND REASSURANCES FOR, IRAN AND SYRIA

(REPORTED SEPTEL, REF B).

3. (C) COMMENT: BADINI CLEARLY WANTS TO USE HIS VISIT TO

WASHINGTON ON MARCH 24 AND 25 AND TO NEW YORK ON MARCH 26 AND

27 AS A POINT OF DEPARTURE FOR BUILDING THE GOI\'S IRAQ

RECONSTRUCTION TASK FORCE IN SUCH A WAY AS TO INTERFACE

EFFECTIVELY WITH USG AND UN ORGANIZATIONS. HE IS EAGER TO

LEARN FROM OUR OWN COORDINATION EFFORTS AND TO UNDERSTAND HOW

ITALY CAN HELP IN A RANGE OF AREAS FROM NON-COMBAT TROOPS TO

HUMANITARIAN ASSISTANCE TO SALVAGING FORMER COMMERCIAL

CONTRACTS THAT HAVE BEEN SUSPENDED IN RECENT YEARS. BADINI

MADE CLEAR THAT HE WANTED TO BE SURE THAT ITALIAN INTERESTS

WERE FAIRLY REPRESENTED IN THE POST-WAR PERIOD, ALBEIT IN A

WAY THAT IS CONSISTENT WITH IRAQ\'S POST-WAR DEVELOPMENT

NEEDS. END COMMENT.
