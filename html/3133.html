1. (C) SUMMARY: PLANS ARE AFOOT FOR TAIWAN PRESIDENT CHEN TO

TRANSIT ITALY THIS JULY ON TRAVEL FROM TAIPEI TO AFRICA,

ACCORDING TO TAIWAN EMBOFF, YET THE VATICAN IS NOT PUTTING

OUT THE RED CARPET. TAIWAN FM EUGENE Y.H. CHIEN MET MAY 30

CONFIDENTIAL

WITH VATICAN FM ARCHBISHOP JEAN-LOUIS TAURAN, YET REACHED NO

AGREEMENT ON VATICAN MEETINGS FOR PRESIDENT CHEN. BEHIND

CLOSED DOORS, THE VATICAN PROTESTED TAIWAN DOMESTIC POLICIES

THAT IT BELIEVES BLOCK CHURCH EXPANSION.

END SUMMARY.

1. (C)TAIWAN EMBOFF PETER TING TOLD POLOFF JUNE 5 THAT TAIWAN

FOREIGN MINISTER EUGENE Y.H. CHIEN\'S MAY 30 MEETING WITH

VATICAN FM JEAN-LOUIS TAURAN BROUGHT WHAT HE CALLED THORNY

BILATERAL ISSUES TO THE FORE. FM TAURAN LODGED A COMPLAINT

ABOUT TAIPEI\'S DELISTING OF DECEMBER 25 AS A TAIWAN NATIONAL

HOLIDAY (PREVIOUSLY JUSTIFIED TO COMMEMORATE THE PROMULGATION

OF THE 1936 CONSTITUTION.) TAURAN FURTHER PROTESTED TAIPEI

ZONING LAWS WHICH, THE VATICAN CLAIMS, IMPEDE CONSTRUCTION OF

NEW SEMINARIES. MOST IMPORTANTLY, THERE WAS NO VATICAN

AGREEMENT FOR MEETINGS WITH TAIWAN PRESIDENT CHEN SHUI-BIAN

SHOULD HE TRANSIT ITALY IN JULY.

2. (C) TAIWAN PRESIDENT CHEN IS TENTATIVELY PLANNING A VISIT

TO AFRICA IN JULY THAT WOULD INCLUDE TRANSIT TRAVEL THROUGH

INDIA AND ITALY, ACCORDING TO TAIWAN EMBOFF. TAIWAN, WHICH

ENJOYS DIPLOMATIC RELATIONS WITH THE VATICAN, WOULD NORMALLY

SEEK A VISIT, YET THE VATICAN, TING SAID, HAS NOT BEEN

ENCOURAGING. CONSEQUENTLY, TAIWAN WAS NOW THINKING IN TERMS

OF AN OVERNIGHT AIRPORT STOP AT MILAN AND A PRIVATE DINNER,

POSSIBLY WITH PROMINENT VATICAN OR GOI LEADERS, ACCORDING TO

TING.

3. TAIWAN FM CHIEN ALSO SAW FORMER VATICAN

DFM ARCHBISHOP CLAUDIO CELLI. CELLI, WHO AS DFM REACHED A

BREAKTHROUGH AGREEMENT WITH VIETNAM ON RELIGIOUS PRACTICE,

NOW HAS PRINCIPAL OVERSIGHT OF PRC-VATICAN DIALOG. TING SAID

CHIEN AND CELLI DISCUSSED THE STATUS OF VATICAN-PRC DIALOG --

STALLED UNTIL THE PRC RESOLVES SUCCESSION OF PARTY LEADERSHIP

-- AND LONG-TERM IMPLICATIONS FOR VATICAN-TAIWAN RELATIONS.

SINCE 1999 VARIOUS VATICAN OFFICIALS HAVE VOCALLY REITERATED

THE STATEMENT BY SECRETARY OF STATE (PRIME

MINISTER-EQUIVALENT) CARDINAL SODANO THAT THE VATICAN IS

READY TO DOWNGRADE TIES WITH TAIPEI OVERNIGHT IF BEIJING

AGREES TO DIPLOMATIC RELATIONS ON ACCEPTABLE TERMS. TING

ADMITTED TAIWAN INCREASINGLY VIEWED THIS VATICAN STANCE WAS

INSULTING, A MARKED DEPARTURE FROM THE GENERALLY CAUTIOUS AND

POSITIVE COMMENTARY THAT HIS MISSION RESERVES FOR THE

VATICAN, THE SOLE GOVERNMENT IN EUROPE TO RECOGNIZE TAIWAN.

4. COMMENT: TAIWAN LATELY HAS BEEN TESTING THE LIMITS OF

ITS VATICAN FRIENDSHIP AS WELL AS OF THE LATERAN ACCORDS, THE

ITALIAN-VATICAN AGREEMENT THAT INTER ALIA REQUIRES ITALY

GRANT TRANSIT VISAS FOR FOREIGN OFFICIALS ON VATICAN VISITS.

TAIWAN VP ANNETTE LU SWUNG THROUGH THE VATICAN IN MARCH FOR

WHAT THE VATICAN\'S CHINA DESK OFFICER, MSGR. ROTA-GRAZIOSI,

DISMISSED AS A MEANINGLESS CEREMONIAL MEETING WITH FM TAURAN.

FM CHIEN\'S MEETINGS ARE A PRELUDE TO AN ATTEMPTED

PRESIDENTIAL VISIT THAT THE VATICAN IS UNLIKELY TO GRANT.

THE VATICAN IS SUPPORTIVE OF ITS TAIWAN FRIENDS, YET WILL NOT

BE PARTY TO GRATUITOUS PROVOCATIONS OF THE PRC, PARTICULARLY

AMID THE RUN-UP TO THE PRC PARTY CONGRESS THIS FALL AND THE

VATICAN\'S EVIDENT HOPE TO KICK-START DIPLOMATIC NEGOTIATIONS

SOON THEREAFTER.
