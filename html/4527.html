SUBJECT: ARTICLE 98: JORDAN EMPOWERS ITS UN PERMREP TO

NEGOTIATE AN AGREEMENT WITH THE U.S.

REF: A. AMMAN 6463

B. AMMAN 6270

C. AMMAN 5626

Classified By: Amb. Edward W. Gnehm for reasons 1.5 (B) and (D)

1. (C) PolCouns on November 26 separately queried Ali

al-Ayed, personal assistant to Foreign Minister Muasher, and

Samer Naber, MFA Legal Advisor, about the status of GOJ

consideration of an Article 98 agreement with the U.S.

PolCouns reiterated our desire to complete an agreement with

Jordan as soon as possible, and recalled King Abdullah\'s

previous statements to senior USG officials and the

Ambassador that he wanted Jordan to conclude such an

agreement if it was compatible with Jordanian law and the

Jordanian constitution.

2. (C) Ayed and Naber both acknowledged the King\'s interest

in signing an Article 98 agreement with the U.S., and noted

that the King had empowered the Jordanian PermRep in New

York, Prince Zaid bin Raad, to negotiate with the U.S. They

both strongly recommended that contacts in New York continue

to move the process toward an Article 98 agreement.

3. (C) COMMENT: As we have previously reported, the locus

of GOJ action on Article 98 has moved from Amman to New York.

We understand that contacts with Prince Zaid continue. We

would appreciate any information that would allow us to be

helpful in pressing the GOJ.

GNEHM
