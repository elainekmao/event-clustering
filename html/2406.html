SUBJECT: UNRESREP PAINTS BLEAK PICTURE OF THE

SITUATION IN ZIMBABWE

THIS MESSAGE IS UNCLASSIFIED BUT SENSITIVE AND SHOULD BE

HANDLED ACCORDINGLY; NOT SUITABLE FOR INTERNET

DISSEMINATION.

1. (SBU) SUMMARY. AT THE INVITATION OF UNDP RESREP

VICTOR ANGELO, THE DONOR COMMUNITY HELD ITS FIRST

COORDINATING MEETING OF 2002 AT THE UNDP OFFICES.

ANGELO, WHO NORMALLY SEEKS TO INTERPRET EVENTS IN A

POSITIVE LIGHT, WAS PARTICULARLY DOWNBEAT. ON

ELECTIONS, HE SAID THAT THERE NO LONGER APPEARS TO BE

ANY ROLE FOR THE UN. ON THE LAND ISSUE, THE UNDP REPORT

REMAINS UNCLEARED ON THE UNSYG\'S DESK. IN THE MEANTIME,

THE AGRICULTURAL SECTOR OF ZIMBABWE IS LURCHING TOWARD

DISASTER. THE RELATED HUMANITARIAN RELIEF INITIATIVE IS

OFF THE GROUND BUT FAR BEHIND THE CURVE IN BRINGING IN

ADEQUATE FOOD STOCKS IN TIME TO AVOID SHORTAGES. ABOUT

THE ONLY GOOD NEWS WAS THAT, AFTER INITIAL DALLYING, GOZ

EXPERTS HAVE GIVEN AN 8,400 MT SHIPMENT OF U.S.-SOURCE

FORTIFIED CORN MEAL (FCM) IN DAR ES SALAAM A CLEAN BILL

OF PHYTOSANITARY HEALTH. END SUMMARY.

2. (U) AFTER INTRODUCING THE NEWLY ARRIVED UN

HUMANITARIAN AFFAIRS OFFICER AND DISTRIBUTING THE

UNSYG\'S STATEMENT ON ZIMBABWE, ANGELO LAUNCHED INTO A

DESCRIPTION OF WHERE THINGS STAND, FROM HIS PERSPECTIVE.

ELECTIONS

---------

3. (U) ANGELO HAD NO GOOD NEWS ON THIS FRONT. HE SAID

THAT THE CONTROVERSIAL AMENDMENTS TO THE ELECTORAL LAW

HAD BEEN FURTHER AMENDED BUT WERE STILL FLAWED. THE

DRAFT BILL WOULD BE REINTRODUCED AS SOON AS PARLIAMENT

CONVENED (AS HAS REPORTEDLY HAPPENED). THE REGISTRAR

GENERAL HAS PULLED BACK AN EARLIER REQUEST TO UNDP TO

SUPPLY TRANSLUCENT BALLOT BOXES. THE UN IS VERY

CONCERNED ABOUT THE LACK OF PLANNING FOR VOTER EDUCATION

IN RURAL AREAS, AS WELL AS TRAINING FOR CIVIL SERVANTS

THAT WILL PRESUMABLY SERVE AS ELECTION MONITORS. THE

NUMBER OF MOBILE VOTING STATIONS UNDER DISCUSSION WAS

WORRISOME, AND THE LACK OF OPPOSITION ACCESS TO PUBLIC

MEDIA IS UNCHANGED.

4. (SBU) ANGELO SAID THAT HE HAD STRESSED TO THE GOZ

INTERLOCUTORS THAT THERE WERE MEANINGFUL ELECTORAL

STANDARDS THE GOZ COULD ADOPT, INCLUDING THOSE OF SADC-

PF, AND THAT INTERNATIONAL OBSERVERS SHOULD BE INVITED

WELL IN ADVANCE OF VOTING. HE CONFIRMED THAT THE UN HAS

NOT YET BEEN INVITED TO OBSERVE AND ADDED THAT IT WAS

TOO LATE TO BECOME INVOLVED EVEN IF AN INVITATION WERE

FORTHCOMING. ON THE QUESTION OF NATIONAL OBSERVERS, HE

SAID THAT THE NUMBER 16,000 HAS BEEN BANDIED ABOUT. THE

KEY TO ACTUAL NUMBERS WOULD BE THE FEE CHARGED, SINCE

THE LAND ISSUE

THIS. ANGELO ADDED THAT HE EXPECTED THE REPORT TO COME

OUT AT THE END OF JANUARY. HE SAID THAT THE ONE FINDING

HE COULD SHARE WAS THAT THE PRESENT PROCESS OF LAND

REFORM IS NOT SUSTAINABLE -- IT MUST BE REVISITED. IF

IMPLEMENTED AS NOW PLANNED, THE "LAND REFORM" WOULD

REQUIRE GOZ FINANCING OF ZIMBABWE DOLLARS 80 BILLION

ANNUALLY OVER THE NEXT FIVE YEARS, A BUDGETARY

IMPOSSIBILITY.

SUBJECT: UNRESREP PAINTS BLEAK PICTURE OF THE

SITUATION IN ZIMBABWE

REPORTS IN THE GOZ PRESS OF HAVING SEIZED MORE THAN

30,000 MT OF MAIZE "A LIE." THE ACTUAL FIGURE WAS NEAR

10,000 MT, AS THE GRAIN MARKETING BOARD AGENTS HAD

DISCOVERED FAR LESS HOLDINGS ON COMMERCIAL FARMS THAN

ANTICIPATED. ANGELO SAID THAT THE GOZ HAD PUT A LEGAL

VENEER ON THE SEIZURES BY ISSUING VIRTUALLY UNNOTICED ON

DECEMBER 28 STATUTORY INSTRUMENT 387 OF 2001. THIS

REQUIRED ALL COMMERCIAL PRODUCERS OF MAIZE/WHEAT IN THE

NAME OF NATIONAL INTEREST TO DELIVER THESE CEREALS FOR

SALE TO THE GMB WITHIN FOURTEEN DAYS OF HARVESTING.

ANGELO SAID THAT THE GMB CLAIM OF HOLDING A 50,000 MT

STOCK WAS PROBABLY UNFOUNDED. THERE WAS PROBABLY ENOUGH

GRAIN FOR 15 DAYS OF TOTAL CONSUMPTION COUNTRYWIDE, NOT

COUNTING THE NEEDS OF LIVESTOCK.

7. (SBU) ANGELO SHARED SOME DRAMATIC FIGURES ON THE

DECLINE IN PRODUCTION. TOTAL MAIZE PRODUCTION FOR THIS

YEAR MIGHT BE LESS THAN ONE MILLION TONS, OR

APPROXIMATELY HALF OF ZIMBABWE\'S REQUIREMENT. DESPITE

OPTIMISTIC PREDICTIONS THAT TOBACCO PRODUCTION WOULD

REACH 150,000 MT, THE UN PROJECTS A 30-40% SHORTFALL AND

AN EVEN GREATER FOREX CRUNCH. BECAUSE GRAIN STOCKS

INTENDED FOR LIVESTOCK IS BEING SEIZED, FARMERS HAVE

PAGE 03 HARARE 00201 02 OF 03 221341Z

REDUCED THEIR HERDS BY AN ESTIMATED 40-45 PERCENT. THE

NUMBERS FOR POULTRY AND SWINE, WHICH CANNOT BE TURNED

OUT TO PASTURE, ARE EVEN STARKER. ANGELO SCOFFED AT

REPORTS THAT THE GOZ HAD BROKERED GRAIN PURCHASES IN THE

AMERICAS. HE SAID THEY ARE TRYING TO IMPORT 50,000 MT

FROM SOUTH AFRICA THANKS TO A BUSINESSMAN WITH CLOSE

LINKS TO MUGABE WHO HAS OFFERED TO FINANCE THE PURCHASE.

ANGELO SAID THAT THE BUSINESSMAN HAS NO EXPERIENCE IN

THIS AREA AND THAT THE LONG DELIVERY TIME -- THE

RAILWAYS ARE CONGESTED AND EACH TRUCK CAN HAUL ONLY A 35

MT LOAD -- MEANS THAT IT WILL TAKE 10 TO 15 WEEKS TO

DELIVER ANY APPRECIABLY AMOUNT.

8. (U) THE SITUATION FOR COMMERCIAL FARM WORKERS IS

ALSO WORSENING. ANGELO SAID THAT A MINIMUM OF 5,570

FAMILIES HAVE BEEN DISPOSSESSED SINCE THE BEGINNING OF

DECEMBER, WITH THE ACTUAL FIGURE POSSIBLY REACHING

10,000. MOST HAD BECOME UTTERLY DESTITUTE.

9. (SBU) ANGELO STRESSED THAT THE UN BELIEVES THERE IS

STILL TIME FOR THE RIGHT POLICIES TO YIELD A RAPID TURN

AROUND. THE AGRICULTURAL SECTOR ESPECIALLY WOULD REACT

RAPIDLY TO POLICY CHANGE. HE SAID THAT THE PRESENT

CROPPING SEASON WOULD BE A BUST, BUT EVEN THE NATIONAL

CATTLE HERD COULD BE RESTORED TO NORMAL LEVELS WITHIN

THREE YEARS IF THE RIGHT POLICIES ARE PUT IN PLACE. HE

CONCLUDED THAT IF THERE IS NO POLICY CHANGE, HOWEVER,

ECONOMIC COLLAPSE COULD OCCUR AS EARLY AS NEXT MAY.

10. (U) ANGELO SAID THAT THE WFP HAS BEGUN TO TAKE

"ANOTHER DONOR" (THE U.S.) HAS DELIVERED AN ADDITIONAL

ON UN TICKETS, HAD INSPECTED THE CORN MEAL AND GIVEN IT

A CLEAN BILL OF HEALTH. ANGELO EXPECTED AN IMPORT

FOOD WOULD IMMEDIATELY START MOVING TO LUSAKA BY RAIL,

THEN TRUCKED INTO ZIMBABWE FOR DISTRIBUTION. THE FIRST

DELIVERIES WERE EXPECTED IN MID-FEBRUARY. UNDP HAD

RECEIVED SOME MONEY AND OTHER PLEDGES, BUT THESE FELL

FAR SHORT OF THE APPEAL.

SUBJECT: UNRESREP PAINTS BLEAK PICTURE OF THE

SITUATION IN ZIMBABWE

PROVIDED ANY PROJECTION FOR THE 2002 WHEAT HARVEST, A

STAPLE FOODSTUFF IN URBAN AREAS. ANGELO REPLIED THAT

THE FIGURE WAS FUZZY, BUT THE REALITY WAS THAT THERE WAS

VIRTUALLY NO LAND PREPARATION TAKING PLACE ON COMMERCIAL

FARMS, THE MAJOR SOURCE OF WHEAT PRODUCTION.

-- (U) THE EU DELEGATE ASKED A) THE LOCATION OF

DISPLACED FARM WORKERS B) HOW LONG THE WFP FOOD STOCKS

WOULD LAST AND C) HOW SECURE WAS THE DISTRIBUTION.

ANGELO SAID THAT IT WAS HARD TO PINPOINT EXACTLY WHERE

ALL THE DISPOSSESSED FARM WORKERS WERE. THE WFP

REPRESENTATIVE SAID THAT WFP DISTRIBUTIONS WOULD COVER

TWO MONTHS OF ASSISTANCE TO THE MOST VULNERABLE ONLY.

HE ADMITTED THAT THE DISTRIBUTION WAS A TRICKY EXERCISE.

THE WFP HAD ORGANIZED WORKSHOPS TO TEACH IMPLEMENTING

PARTNERS HOW TO DETERMINE BENEFICIARY SELECTION. HE

EXPRESSED FULL CONFIDENCE THAT WFP IS FULLY IN CONTROL

OF THE DISTRIBUTION AND MONITORING PHASES.

-- (U) THE AUSTRALIAN HIGH COMMISSIONER NOTED THAT

JOURNALISTS FROM AUSTRALIAN PUBLIC RADIO HAD BEEN

REFUSED VISAS TO VISIT ZIMBABWE. HE READ OFF PLUMMETING

GRAIN PRODUCTION STATISTICS, SAID THAT IN CANBERRA\'S
