TAIWAN, THE VATICAN, AND THE PRC

--------------------------------

2. (C) THE VATICAN MAINTAINS OFFICIAL STATE-TO-

STATE RELATIONS WITH TAIWAN (THE REPUBLIC OF

CHINA), HAVING TRANSFERRED ITS EMBASSY (KNOWN AS

A "NUNCIATURE") FROM BEIJING TO TAIPEI IN THE

WAKE OF THE REVOLUTION. TAIWAN IN TURN MAINTAINS

AN EMBASSY TO THE VATICAN, PROVIDING THE ISLAND

NATION WITH A PRECIOUS EUROPEAN PLATFORM FROM

WHICH TO PURSUE A VIGOROUS DIPLOMATIC AGENDA.

THE VATICAN CONTINUES TO SIGNAL ITS READINESS TO

DOWNGRADE ITS TAIPEI MISSION AND ESTABLISH

OFFICIAL RELATIONS WITH THE PRC. THE VATICAN

AFFIRMS THAT THE MOTHBALLING OF ITS ORIGINAL

BEIJING NUNCIATURE WAS NEVER DEFINITIVE, ADDING

THAT IT HAS CONSISTENTLY PRACTICED A ONE-CHINA-

POLICY. THE VATICAN HAS A LOW-KEY PRESENCE IN

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 VATICA 03297 01 OF 02 201534Z

HONG KONG, AN OFFICE THAT IS A VESTIGE OF THE

HAND-OVER; IT CLAIMS TO BE POISED TO REOPEN IN

BEIJING QUOTE OVERNIGHT ENDQUOTE, A CLAIM THAT IS

LARGELY RHETORICAL. TAIWAN\'S REPRESENTATIVES TO

THE VATICAN ARE SANGUINE AT THIS PROSPECT OF A

DIPLOMATIC SEA CHANGE, YET THE TAIWAN EMBASSY IS

QUIETLY EXPLORING OTHER WAYS TO PRESERVE A

DIPLOMATIC PRESENCE IN ROME, FOR INSTANCE,

THROUGH ACCREDITATION TO THE SOVEREIGN MILITARY

ORDER OF MALTA, LOCATED IN ROME AND FORMALLY

RECOGNIZED BY 85 GOVERNMENTS, INCLUDING ITALY.

3. (C) VATICAN DISCUSSIONS WITH THE PRC REMAIN

UNOFFICIAL, IRREGULAR, AND OFTEN VITUPERATIVE.

THE STICKING POINT IS PRC UNWILLINGNESS TO

ACKNOWLEDGE VATICAN JURISDICTION OVER THE CHINESE

CATHOLIC COMMUNITY, INCLUDING VATICAN OVERSIGHT

OF THE TRAINING OF CLERGY AND THE APPOINTMENT OF

NEW BISHOPS. THE PRC TREATS VATICAN CLAIMS TO

ECCLESIASTICAL AUTHORITY IN CHINA AS TANTAMOUNT

TO FOREIGN INTERFERENCE IN INTERNAL SOVEREIGN

AFFAIRS. WHILE THE PRC PERMITS OCCASIONAL CHURCH

VISITS BY VATICAN ENVOYS, INCLUDING AT THE

CARDINAL LEVEL, AND ALLOWS LIMITED TRAINING

ABROAD FOR "PATRIOTIC" SEMINARIANS, THE RUMORS OF

AN EMERGING DIPLOMATIC RAPPROCHEMENT HAVE BEEN

GREATLY OVERSTATED, WITH THE VATICAN-PRC

RELATIONSHIP INCREASINGLY STRAINED. IN JANUARY

2000 THE PRC CROSSED A RUBICON OF SORTS WHEN THE

STATE-SPONSORED "CATHOLIC PATRIOTIC ASSOCIATION"

ORDAINED FIVE BISHOPS IN DEFIANCE OF A VATICAN

BAN. CONVERSELY, THE MASS BEATIFICATION OF 120

CHINESE MARTYRS AT THE VATICAN ON OCTOBER 1,

VATICAN MEDIA HAVE REPORTED THE RAMPANT

DESTRUCTION OF PLACES OF WORSHIP LACKING OFFICIAL

PERMITS. THE VATICAN HAS VOICED CONCERN THAT

BOTH THE CATHOLIC PATRIOTIC ASSOCIATION AND THE

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 VATICA 03297 02 OF 02 201534Z

UNDERGROUND CHURCH FACE A SHORTAGE OF CLERGY AS

WELL AS A LOOMING GENERATION GAP, WITH MOST

PRIESTS TRAINED BEFORE 1955 OR AFTER 1985 ON

ACCOUNT OF A PROLONGED INTERRUPTION OF OFFICIAL

ORDINATIONS. MOREOVER, A NUMBER OF ELDERLY

CLERGY REMAIN IN DETENTION, WITH THE VATICAN

OSTENSIBLY UNABLE TO DETERMINE THEIR WELFARE AND

WHEREABOUTS. THE VATICAN IS CAUTIOUS IN ITS

COMMENTARY ON THE PATRIOTIC ASSOCIATION AND THERE

ARE INDICATIONS THAT THE DIVISION BETWEEN THE

PATRIOTIC AND UNDERGROUND CATHOLIC COMMUNITIES IS

BLURRING. ACCORDING TO VATICAN SOURCES AND FIELD

REPORTING, A NUMBER OF CLERGY IN THE PATRIOTIC

CHURCH ARE IN TRUTH LOYAL TO THE VATICAN.

SIMILARLY, THE VATICAN WELCOMES VISITS BY

PATRIOTIC ASSOCIATION SEMINARIANS AS A CHANCE TO

SHAPE, EVEN IF INDIRECTLY, THEIR THEOLOGICAL

FORMATION.
