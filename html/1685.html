2. (C) PURSUANT TO REF C, CHARGE AND A/DCM MET IN THE

AFTERNOON OF 5/4 WITH SANT\'EGIDIO FOUNDER AND DIRECTOR

ANDREA RICCARDI TO DISCUSS HOW TO PUSH THE PACE OF THE

PROPOSED INTER-RELIGIOUS DIALOGUE FOR BOSNIA. RICCARDI\'S

ASSISTANT, CLAUDIO BETTI, HAD HEARD FROM CARDINAL PULJIC

(THE KEY INTERLOCUTOR IN THIS DIALOGUE) THAT PULJIC

SUPPORTS THE CONFERENCE BUT THAT THE EARLIEST HE CAN ATTEND

WOULD BE JUNE 7-8. SANT\'EGIDIO IS PUSHING AHEAD TO CONFIRM

THE OTHER INVITEES FOR THAT DATE. A/DCM REMINDED RICCARDI

THAT THE IMPENDING HDZ DECLARATION OF A SEPARATE ENTITY IN

BOSNIA-HERZEGOVINA HAS LENT SOME URGENCY TO THE CONFERENCE,

BUT RICCARDI INSISTED THAT TO HAVE A PRODUCTIVE DIALOGUE

PULJIC NEEDED TO BE FULLY ON BOARD, AND JUNE 7 IS THE BEST

THEY CAN DO.

3. (C) RICCARDI TOLD US THAT SANT\'EGIDIO WOULD IN THE

MEANTIME HAVE OTHER MEETINGS WITH THE CARDINAL IN WHICH

THEY WOULD PRESS HIM TO TAKE A MODERATE LINE VIS A VIS THE

HDZ GAMBIT. DON VICENZO PAGLIA WILL SEE PULJIC SOON FOR A

CONFIRMATION IN BOSNIA; RICCARDI SAID THAT DON VICENZO WILL

PRESS FOR MODERATION, AS WILL RICCARDI HIMSELF WHEN HE SEES

PULJIC DURING THE 5/23 CONSISTORY IN ROME. RICCARDI

BELIEVED THAT THE FACT THAT PULJIC ACCEPTED THE IDEA OF AN

OFF-SITE CONFERENCE TO DISCUSS INTERCOMMUNAL RECONCILIATION

INDICATES THAT PULJIC IS "LOOKING FOR A WAY OUT OF THE

4. (C) PER REF B, CHARGE CALLED UK AMBASSADOR PELLEW IN

THE MORNING OF 5/4 TO SET THE RECORD STRAIGHT THAT THE

PROJECT IS SANT\'EGIDIO\'S. (NOTE: POST HAS PUSHED

AGGRESSIVELY FOR THE CONFERENCE TO OCCUR ASAP, AS WELL AS

FOR CO-FUNDING OF THE CONFERENCE, AND HAS SECURED A PLEDGE

FROM THE AUSTRIAN EMBASSY HERE TO PROVIDE AIR TRANSPORT FOR

THE BOSNIANS AS WELL AS A PLEDGE FROM THE SWEDISH EMBASSY

(EU PRESIDENCY) TO CO-FUND THE REMAINDER OF THE EXPENSES.

BUT THE TIME HAS COME FOR SANT\'EGIDIO TO TAKE THE REINS.

END NOTE.) PELLEW (PLEASE PROTECT), PERHAPS REFLECTING

FRUSTRATION THAT HIS MISSION HAS BEEN OUT OF THE LOOP, TOLD

US SANT\'EGIDIO IS MORE EFFECTIVE WHEN GIVEN SUFFICIENT LEAD

TO DO THINGS IN THEIR OWN WAY (READ: SLOWLY AND WITH GREAT

DELIBERATION). HE WASN\'T CERTAIN THAT THEY WOULD BE ABLE

TO HAVE A SUCCESSFUL EVENT IF "PRESSURIZED" BY THE USG. WE

LATER ASSURED RICCARDI THAT WE SEE THIS AS A SANT\'EGIDIO

PROJECT, AND THAT WE WERE THERE TO HELP AS NEEDED. HE TOO

BELIEVED THAT WAS THE BEST APPROACH, AND SAID SANT\'EGIDIO

WOULD COORDINATE AMONG THE OTHER EMBASSIES INVOLVED. POST

WILL NOT HESITATE TO JUMP BACK IN, HOWEVER, IF SANT\'EGIDIO

LOOKS LIKE IT IS DROPPING THE BALL.

5. (SBU) RICCARDI SUGGESTED THAT WE SEE PULJIC WHEN HE IS

HERE -- WE CAN DO SO AND WOULD APPRECIATE GUIDANCE FROM

DEPARTMENT/EMBASSY SARAJEVO ON WHETHER TO DO SO AND LATEST

TALKING POINTS TO DEPLOY. MERANTE
