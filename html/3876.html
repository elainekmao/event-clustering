3. (C) AS DESCRIBED AT LENGTH IN REF B, BUI HAS

RECENTLY FACED A SHRILL MEDIA CAMPAIGN AGAINST HIM

PERSONALLY, MOSTLY FOR HIS ROLE IN THE US FILM "WE WERE

SOLDIERS" BUT ALSO FOR EARLIER ROLES IN INTERNATIONAL

FILMS "THREE SEASONS" AND "GREEN DRAGON." HE HAS BEEN

ACCUSED INFORMALLY OF "BETRAYING THE FATHERLAND," A

SERIOUS ACCUSATION IN VIETNAM, WHERE "PROTECTING

NATIONAL SOLIDARITY" IS A CONSTITUTIONAL REQUIREMENT OF

ALL CITIZENS. BUI\'S PASSPORT HAS BEEN TAKEN FROM HIM,

HE APPARENTLY HAS ALREADY BEEN BARRED FROM BEGINNING A

NEW FILM OVERSEAS IN NOVEMBER, AND THERE ARE EFFORTS TO

BAN HIM FROM ACTING -- HIS SOLE LIVELIHOOD -- FOR AT

LEAST THE NEXT FIVE YEARS. HE HAS BEEN HARASSED BY

NIGHTLY PHONE CALLS FROM SECURITY OFFICIALS, HAS BEEN

SUBJECT TO SURVEILLANCE WHEN HE LEAVES HIS HOME, AND

REPORTS INCREASING ISOLATION FROM HIS FORMER FRIENDS

AND NEIGHBORS. HE WAS QUESTIONED BY POLICE ON OCTOBER

2, AND HAS EXPRESSED FEAR OF IMMINENT DETENTION AND/OR
