SUBJECT: TFKU01: WAR OF THE EMBASSIES SITREP: TURKS THROW IN THE

TOWEL AS GENERATORS RACK UP ANOTHER VICTUM

1. TURKISH AMBASSADOR INFORMED US THIS MORNING, AUGUST 28, THAT HE

HAS BEEN FORCED TO CLOSE HIS EMBASSY. HE AND A REMAINING STAFF OF

11 WILL CLOSE THE DOORS TODAY AND DRIVE TO BAGHDAD, AND HOPEFULLY ON

TO ANKARA. AMBASSADOR RECEIVED CLOSURE ORDERS FROM ANKARA AT 0100

LKT. DECISION TO CLOSE IS RESULT OF GENERATOR FAILURE, AND

CONSEQUENT FOOD SPOILAGE, NOT/NOT A CHANGE IN POLICY. TURKS HAVE

BEEN AMONG THE TOUGHEST IN STANDING UP TO THE IRAQIS HERE, AND HAVE

BUILT MORE THAN ONE BACKBONE INTO A WEAKER EUROPEAN ALLY IN TOWN.

2. ASKED IF HE HAD COMMUNICATED HIS GOVERNMENT\'S DECISION TO LOCAL

IRAQI AUTHORITIES, AMBASSADOR DERISIVELY STATED "I AM NOT TALKING TO

THEM". HE WILL SIMPLY GET IN EMBASSY CARS AND GO, AND IS TOYING

WITH THE IDEA OF LEAVING WITH FULL FLAGS FLYING. HE EXPECTS TO

SPEND TUESDAY NIGHT IN BAGHDAD AND GO ON TO ANKARA AUGUST 29.

3. FOR BAGHDAD: TURKISH AMBASSADOR WILL CONTACT YOU UPON

ARRIVAL. PLEASE CONVEY WORD OF SAFE ARRIVAL TO US ASAP. TURKISH

AMBASSADOR IS A STALWART FRIEND AND WE ARE SURE HE WOULD GLADLY TAKE

ANYTHING FOR BAGHDAD HE CAN CARRY.

4. FOR ANKARA: TURK\'S COMMO IS UNCERTAIN. PLEASE INFORM GOT OF

CONVERSATION AND AMBASSADOR\'S PLANS.

5. COMMENT: THIS IS THE FOURTH EMBASSY WE KNOW OF THAT HAS

EXPERIENCED SEVERE GENERATOR PROBLEMS. UK HAVE APPARENTLY REPAIRED

THEIRS, FOR NOW. CANADIANS ARE WORKING ON FINDING A NEW BATTERY FOR

THEIRS. JAPANESE ALMOST HAD TO LEAVE BECAUSE OF THEIRS. WE SUSPECT

THERE ARE MANY OTHERS IN THE SAME FIX. IT WILL BE A SAD IRONY IF

SADDAM IS ABLE TO FORCE US ALL OUT NOT BY BROKEN WILL, BUT BROKEN

GENERATORS. END COMMENT.
