SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION: FALL OF TIKRIT SYRIA IS NEXT U.S.

MOTIVES FOR WAR

1. SUMMARY: THE FALL OF TIKRIT TO COALITION FORCES, THE IN

IRAQI CITIES, AND USG WARNINGS TO SYRIA DOMINATED COVERAGE IN UAE

MEDIA. AN "AL KHALEEJ" EDITORIAL ALLEGED THAT IRAQ WAS FIRST BUT

SYRIA AND OTHER COUNTRIES ARE NEXT. AN "AL ITTIHAD" EDITORIAL

COMMENTED ON SADDAM\'S TORTURE CHAMBERS BUT NOTED THAT THEY ARE

ONLY THE TIP OF THE ICEBERG. DR. ANWAR GHARGHASH IN "AL ITTIHAD"

DECLARED THAT THE WAR IS A HISTORIC OPPORTUNITY FOR ARABS TO

CHANGE THEIR RECENT HISTORY OF HYPOCRISY, CORRUPTION, OPPRESSION,

AND SETBACKS. IN A RARE OP-ED, UAE NATIONAL GULF NEWS EDITOR-IN-

CHIEF ABDUL HAMID AHMAD PROCLAIMED THAT AUTHORITARIAN ARAB

REGIMES MUST PURSUE DEMOCRATIC REFORMS AND STOP USING THE

PALESTINIANS AS AN EXCUSE TO JUSTIFY THE REPRESSION OF THEIR

PEOPLES. END SUMMARY.

2. COMMENTING ON THE ARAB CHANCE TO ELIMINATE THE BLACK STAINS

IN ITS HISTORY, UAE NATIONAL DR. ANWAR GARGASH WROTE IN SEMI-

OFFICIAL ABU DHABI-BASED ARABIC DAILY "AL-ITTIHAD" ON 4/14:

"... OUR ARAB WORLD IS FACED WITH A HISTORICAL CHANCE THAT

SYMBOLIZES THE END OF THE ARAB HISTORY AS WE KNOW IT - A HISTORY

OF HYPOCRISY, CORRUPTION, OPPRESSION, AND SETBACKS DURING RECENT

DECADES. IT IS TIME TO REPLACE THE VOID SLOGANS AND ILLUSIONS

THAT OCCURRED IN LOSING THE ARAB COMPASS, WITH A WORKING METHOD

TO SANCTIFY HUMAN RIGHTS AND TO GUARANTEE POLITICAL RIGHTS AND A

DECENT LIFE FOR EVERY ARAB."

3. DESCRIBING THE SITUATION IN IRAQ AS A "FIRE" THAT WILL SPREAD

TO OTHER ARAB COUNTRIES, ESPECIALLY SYRIA, SHARJAH-BASED PAN-ARAB

DAILY "AL-KHALEEJ" EDITORIALIZED 4/14:

"... EGYPTIAN PRESIDENT HOSNI MUBARAK\'S WARNING OF A CIVIL WAR IN

IRAQ SHOULD BE SERIOUSLY CONSIDERED AND DEALT WITH ... THE FIRE

THREATENING THE REGION, STARTING WITH THE ONE IN IRAQ, OPENS THE

DOOR TO FEARS THAT ARE NOT ELIMINATED WITH AMERICAN PROMISES AND

REASSURANCES THAT WASHINGTON SHOWERS (US) WITH DEMOCRACY,

DEVELOPMENT, AND MORE. THE EVIL COMPANIES AND ISRAELI INTERESTS

THAT CONTROL AMERICAN DECISIONS THAT REFUTE ALL RUMORS REGARDING

THIS SITUATION ..."

4. COMMENTING ON SADDAM\'S TREATMENT OF HIS PEOPLE, "AL ITTIHAD"

EDITORIALIZED 4/14:

"... WHAT THE WORLD SAW IN THE VAULTS, SECRET TUNNELS, AND

TORTURE EQUIPMENT IN THE HOUSES OF EVIL, IN ADDITION TO THE

GRAVES, GROUP GRAVES, AND THE REMAINS OF UNKNOWN PEOPLE, IS ONLY

THE TIP OF THE ICEBERG FLOATING ON THE SURFACE, MADE OF BODIES

AND THE SUFFERING OF MILLIONS OVER THE LAST 35 YEARS. THE SOULS

OF THE VICTIMS ARE STILL FLYING OVER WHERE THEY WERE KILLED, AND

THE ECHOES OF THE MOANS OF THE TORTURED STILL LOOM IN MANY

HOUSES."

5. NOTING THAT THE WORLD IS ASKING THE COALITION FORCES TO END

THE CHAOS IN IRAQ INSTEAD OF ENDING THEIR OCCUPATION, "AL-

KHALEEJ" EDITORIALIZED 4/13:

"... THE ORGANIZED CHAOS IN IRAQI CITIES DURING THE LAST COUPLE

OF DAYS IS ONLY BUT A DROP IN THE SEA OF THE LAW OF THE JUNGLE,

IN WHICH THE U.S. AND UK WANT IRAQ TO DROWN IN ORDER TO

STRENGTHEN THEIR OCCUPATION ..."

6. CRITICIZING THE ARAB POLICY TOWARDS IRAQ AND COMPARING IT TO

THE FRENCH POLICY, DUBAI-BASED BUSINESS-ORIENTED ARABIC DAILY "AL-

BAYAN" EDITORIALIZED 4/13:

"... THE ARAB LEAGUE AND ARAB COUNTRIES ARE REQUESTED TO MOVE

TOWARDS PROTECTING NOT ONLY IRAQ AND SYRIA, BUT ALSO ALL ARAB

COUNTRIES THAT MIGHT SOONER OR LATER FIND THEMSELVES AN AMERICAN

TARGET ..."

7. COMMENTING ON THE RECONSTRUCTION OF IRAQ, "AL-ITTIHAD"

EDITORIALIZED 4/13:

"... REBUILDING A NEW IRAQ AFTER ALL THIS DESTRUCTION WILL NOT

HAPPEN EXCEPT WITH THE HANDS OF ITS PEOPLE AND ON THE BASIS OF

COOPERATION BETWEEN ALL POWERS AND POLITICAL, SOCIAL, AND

CULTURAL GROUPS IN THE IRAQI COMMUNITY. THIS IS THE BATTLE FOR

IRAQIS INSIDE AND OUTSIDE IRAQ."

8. IN A CLOSING STATEMENT IN "AL BAYAN," SALIM WROTE:

"THE LOOTING THAT THE IRAQI CITIES ARE WITNESSING UNDER THE EYES

AND EARS OF THE AMERICAN OCCUPYING FORCES IS DONE BY ROBBERS AND

GANGS ... THIS REVEALS THE TRUTH ABOUT THE MOTTO OF LIBERATION

ESPOUSED BY WASHINGTON TO JUSTIFY ITS ATTACKS AGAINST IRAQ. THIS

IS A LIBERATION OF IRAQI CITIES FROM SECURITY AND LEAVES THE

THESE CITIES FOR ROBBERS TO LOOT, EXCEPT FOR THE MINISTRY OF

PETROLEUM AND OIL REFINERIES AND WELLS, WHICH HAVE THEIR OWN

LOOTERS WHO COME FROM OVERSEAS."

9. UNDER THE HEADLINE, "THE IRAQI TREMOR AND ITS KEY LESSON FOR

ALL," UAE NATIONAL EDITOR-IN-CHIEF ABDUL HAMID AHMAD WROTE IN AN

OP-ED IN EXPATRIATE-ORIENTED ENGLISH DAILY "GULF NEWS" ON 4/14:

"THE COLLAPSE OF THE IRAQI BA\'ATH PARTY HAS LONG BEEN ANTICIPATED

... THE REGIME SUPPRESSED FREEDOM AND DEMOCRACY IN ECONOMIC,

POLITICAL, AND SOCIAL FIELDS - IN FACT, ALL ASPECTS OF LIFE ...

THE IRAQI EXPERIENCE IS SIMILAR TO THE EXPERIENCE OF ALGERIA,

SYRIA, YEMEN, AND OTHER ARAB COUNTRIES RULED BY SUCH FORCES AND

NATIONAL PARTIES THAT ARE CHARACTERIZED BY TOTALITARIANISM AND

RULE BY ONE ... SINGLE PARTY MONOPOLY SUPPRESSES ALL TYPES OF

POLITICAL PARTICIPATION AND ONLY LEADS TO THE SUFFOCATION OF

PEOPLE, POLITICALLY AND SOCIALLY. THE (BA\'ATH) PARTY\'S ACTIONS

AND THE MONOPOLY OF POWER BY AN UNQUALIFIED CLIQUE - EXCEPT FOR

ITS INSATIABLE DESIRE TO TERRORISE IT OWN PEOPLE AND NEIGHBORS -

IS A STRONG LESSON THAT ARABS SHOULD LEARN FROM."

"... IT HAS BECOME CLEAR THE PALESTINIAN CAUSE IS JUST AN EXCUSE

FOR THESE REGIMES TO JUSTIFY THE REPRESSION OF THEIR PEOPLE AND

THEIR MONOPOLY OF POWER. IT IS AS IF PALESTINE CAN ONLY BE

LIBERATED THROUGH REPRESSION SIMPLY BECAUSE THESE REGIMES HAVE

CLAIMED DEMOCRACY WILL NOT ACHIEVE THIS GOAL, THIS DREAM."

"WE SHOULD NOT START LOOKING FOR EXPLANATIONS AS TO WHY IT

HAPPENED, BECAUSE DICTATORSHIPS CAN ONLY LEAD TO DESTRUCTION AS

WAS THE CASE IN IRAQ ... THE TIME HAS COME FOR US ARABS TO

REALIZE AND LEARN FROM WHAT HAS TAKEN PLACE IN IRAQ."
