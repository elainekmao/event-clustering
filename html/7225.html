SUBJECT: TFIZ01: KUWAIT OIL UPDATE -- APRIL 08, 2003

REF: KUWAIT 1280 AND PREVIOUS

Classified By: DCM Frank C. Urbancic for reasons 1.5 (B) and (D)

1. (U) Local press reports today quote Kuwait Undersecretary

for Oil Issa Al-Aoun as saying that Kuwait will continue to

produce 2.44 million barrels of oil a day until further

notice to meet shortages in the international oil market. He

also told reporters that Kuwait will start supplying 25,000

barrels per day of crude oil to Jordan. He added that the

Kuwaiti crude would have to be mixed with Saudi crude so it

could be processed in Jordan\'s refineries.

3.(C) Explanatory Comments:

Wafra Joint Operations refer to onshore concessions in

Saudi-Kuwait divided zone operated by Saudi Arabian Texaco

(SAT). R/B refers to the two producing reservoirs -- Ratawi

and Burgan.

KOC is Kuwait Oil Company

KNPC is Kuwait National Petroleum Corporation

KGOC is the Kuwaiti successor to Arabian Oil Company (AOC),

which previously operated a concession in the offshore

portions of the Saudi-Kuwait divided zone. AOC still

operates the fields under a service agreement.

KEC refers to Kuwait Export Crude, the country\'s export

blend.

Salt PTB refers to pounds of salt per thousand barrels of

crude oil.
