SUBJECT: JORDAN: SPECIAL MEDIA REACTION ON IRAQ

SUMMARY

FOR FOUR DAYS (FRIDAY -MONDAY JULY 12-15) JORDANIAN

DAILIES WERE DOMINATED BY OFFICIAL DENIALS (BY THE

PRIME MINISTER AND MINISTER OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS) OF

MEDIA REPORTS THAT JORDAN WOULD ALLOW THE U.S. TO USE

ITS TERRITORY TO LAUNCH AN ATTACK AGAINST IRAQ. ON

SUNDAY JULY 14, THE MAIN STORY WAS THE JORDANIAN

GOVERNMENT\'S STATEMENT ON FORMER CROWN PRINCE HASSAN\'S

PARTICIPATION IN THE MEETING OF IRAQI DISSIDENT

OFFICERS, WHICH DESCRIBED THE PRINCE\'S MOVE AS A

PERSONAL ACT THAT DOES NOT REFLECT JORDAN\'S POINT OF

VIEW, AND OF WHICH NEITHER THE LEADERSHIP NOR THE

GOVERNMENT HAD PRIOR KNOWLEDGE.

MANY EDITORIAL WRITERS DEVOTED THEIR COLUMNS TO

SUPPORTING JORDAN\'S OFFICIAL POSITION AND ATTACKING THE

MEDIA REPORTS OF JORDAN\'S PARTICIPATION IN AN ATTACK ON

IRAQ, WHICH THEY DESCRIBED AS A "VICIOUS MEDIA CAMPAIGN

AGAINST JORDAN" ORIGINATING IN THE U.S. ACCORDING TO

SOME WRITERS.

NEWS REPORTS

ON FRIDAY JULY 12, ALL JORDANIAN DAILIES LED WITH

REPORTS ON THE PRIME MINISTER\'S PRESS CONFERENCE, WHICH

HE CALLED FOR EXPRESSLY TO DENY MEDIA REPORTS THAT

JORDAN WOULD BE USED AS A BASE FOR AN ATTACK ON IRAQ.

ABUL RAGHEB STRESSED THAT THERE ARE NO U.S. TROOPS IN

JORDAN, AND THAT THERE IS NO UNDERSTANDING BETWEEN

JORDAN AND THE U.S. TO USE JORDANIAN TERRITORY OR

AIRSPACE FOR AN ATTACK ON IRAQ NOW OR IN THE FUTURE.

THE PRIME MINISTER ALSO INVITED JOURNALISTS TO VISIT

ANY JORDANIAN MILITARY BASES TO PROVE TO THEM THAT

THERE ARE NO U.S. TROOPS THERE. DAILIES ON THE SAME

DAY REPORTED THE SAME MESSAGE BEING GIVEN BY MINISTER

OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS MARWAN MU\'ASHER IN A SPEECH BEFORE

THE JORDANIAN MEDIA FORUM, A PRIVATE MEDIA

ORGANIZATION.

ON SATURDAY JULY 13, DAILIES REPORTED THE ASSERTION

THAT JORDAN WOULD NOT BE USED AS A BASE TO STRIKE IRAQ

NEITHER NOW NOR IN THE FUTURE, THIS TIME FROM THE

MINISTER OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS MU\'ASHER WHO WAS IN CAIRO

ATTENDING THE ARAB COORDINATION MEETING. DAILIES ALSO

REPORTED THE LONDON MEETING OF IRAQI DISSIDENT

OFFICERS, BUT FAILED TO MENTION THE PARTICIPATION OF

FORMER CROWN PRINCE HASSAN IN THAT MEETING.

ON SUNDAY JULY 14, THE MAIN NEWS ITEM WAS THE MINISTER

OF INFORMATION\'S STATEMENT THAT PRINCE HASSAN\'S

PARTICIPATION IN THE LONDON MEETING OF IRAQI DISSIDENT

OFFICERS "WAS A PERSONAL ACT THAT DOES NOT REFLECT

JORDAN\'S POINT OF VIEW." THE MINISTER ALSO EXPRESSED

"SURPRISE" AT THE PRINCE\'S BEHAVIOR, AND ASSERTED THAT

"NEITHER THE LEADERSHIP NOT THE GOVERNMENT HAD PRIOR

KNOWLEDGE OF IT." ON THE SAME SUBJECT, INDEPENDENT,

MASS-APPEAL ARABIC DAILY AL-ARAB AL-YAWM QUOTED PRINCE

HASSAN AS SAYING THAT "I HAVE COME IN MY PERSONAL

CAPACITY AND I AM NOT AN OFFICIAL OF THE JORDANIAN

GOVERNMENT."

ON MONDAY JULY 15 THE PRIME MINISTER AVAILED HIMSELF OF

HIS MEETING WITH ARAB LEAGUE SECRETARY-GENERAL AMRE

MUSSA TO REAFFIRM THAT: "JORDAN REJECTS MILITARY ACTION

AGAINST IRAQ," AND THAT "THERE ARE NO AMERICAN BASES IN

JORDAN."

EDITORIAL COMMENTARIES

-- JORDAN IS NOT THE LAUNCHING GROUND OF THE ATTACK

PROMINENT DAILY COLUMNIST FAHED AL-FANEK WROTE IN SEMI-

OFFICIAL INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-RAI ON JULY 12:

"JORDAN IS PROUD TO HAVE POLITICAL PLURALISM THAT

ENCOMPASSES AND ALLOWS FREE EXPRESSION TO MANY

POLITICAL POINTS OF VIEW. HOWEVER, THERE IS TOTAL

UNANIMITY OVER JORDAN\'S REJECTION OF MILITARY

AGGRESSION AGAINST IRAQ. HOW CAN THE U.S., WHICH

CLAIMS TO ADVISE US ON WAYS TO DEVELOP DEMOCRACY IN

JORDAN, ASK THE GOVERNMENT TO GO AGAINST THIS POPULAR

CONSENSUS AND TAKE PART IN ITS AGGRESSION AGAINST OUR

BROTHERS?"

-- THE MAGIC TURNED AGAINST THE MAGICIAN

PROMINENT DAILY COLUMNIST FAHED AL-FANEK WROTE IN SEMI-

OFFICIAL INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-RAI ON JULY 13:

"THERE IS NO DOUBT THAT THOSE WHO BEAT THE DRUMS OF WAR

IN THE PENTAGON WOULD LOVE FOR JORDAN TO TAKE PART IN

THE AGGRESSION AGAINST IRAQ; BUT THEY WANTED TO KNOW

JORDAN\'S ANSWER BEFORE POSING THE QUESTION, SO THEY

GAVE LEAKS TO THE NEWSPAPERS. THIS TRIAL BALLOON,

HOWEVER, BURST BEFORE TAKING OFF. WE WONDER IF THESE

MENDACIOUS AND MALIGNANT LEAKS WERE THE WORK OF THE

PENTAGON\'S OFFICE OF STRATEGIC MISINFORMATION."

-- THE MEDIA CAMPAIGN AGAINST JORDAN...DIMENSIONS AND

OBJECTIVE

DAILY COLUMNIST RAKAN MAJALI WROTE IN CENTER-LEFT,

INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR ON SATURDAY JULY

13: "MODERATE VOICES IN THE WEST WARN AGAINST THE

MILITARIZATION OF POLITICS AND THE MEDIA IN THE U.S. AS

IN A NEW ROME OR NAZISM IN MODERN GUISE. GOBLES

FOUNDED THE THEORY THAT SAID: `KEEP REPEATING THE LIE

UNTIL FINALLY PEOPLE BELIEVE IT.\' BUT THE INDUSTRY OF

LIES AND FABRICATED STORIES IN TODAY\'S MEDIA

IMPERIALISM HAS BECOME A REFINED ART. DURING THE PAST

FEW DAYS JORDAN FACED A VIRULENT MEDIA CAMPAIGN FULL OF

MENDACIOUS ALLEGATIONS THAT JORDAN WOULD BE A PARTNER

IN THE AGGRESSION AGAINST IRAQ WITH WHICH THE U.S. HAS

THREATENED SINCE THE PRESENT ADMINISTRATION REACHED

POWER. THE PROGRAMMED MEDIA PAY NO ATTENTION TO THE

JORDANIAN CATEGORICAL DENIALS, NOR DO THEY PAY ANY

ATTENTION TO THE TRUTH. INSTEAD THEY CONTINUE TO

REPEAT THEIR LIES WITH OBVIOUS ILL INTENT."

-- IRAQI OPPOSITION AND THE AMERICAN ALTERNATIVE

DAILY COLUMNIST YASSER ZA\'ATREH WROTE IN CENTER-LEFT,

INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR ON JULY 15: "THE

STORY IS NOT ABOUT SADDAM HUSSEIN AND HIS REGIME, AND

THE AMERICANS ARE NOT CONCERNED WITH THE SUFFERING OF

THE IRAQI PEOPLE. WASHINGTON NEEDS A VICTORY TO MAKE UP

FOR ITS FAILED WAR AGAINST WHAT IT CALLS TERRORISM, AND

IT NEEDS TO PLEASE THE ZIONIST LOBBY AND THE CHRISTIAN

REPUBLICANS. NOTHING WOULD PLEASE THESE TWO MORE THAT

STRIKING IRAQ. THE DANGER IS NOT LIMITED TO IRAQ. IT

THREATENS THE WHOLE REGION. BUT THE LEADERS OF IRAQI

OPPOSITION NEED URGENTLY TO RECONSIDER THEIR PRESENT

POLICIES BECAUSE THEY ARE NOT ASSISTING A WAR AGAINST

SADDAM HUSSEIN, BUT AGAINST THEIR NATION." GNEHM
