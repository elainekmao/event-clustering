SUBJECT: IRAQ/UNSC: DEMARCHE ON JORDANIAN FLIGHTS TO

BAGHDAD DELIVERED; GOJ SAYS FREQUENCY HAS DECREASED

REF: (A) STATE 141384 (B) STATE 112154

Classified By: Charge Gregory L. Berry. Reasons 1.5 (b,d).

1. (C) On July 29, Charge delivered ref A points regarding

the frequency and regularity of Royal Jordanian Airlines\'

Baghdad flights to Ali Al-Ayed, personal aide to Foreign

Minister Muasher. Al-Ayed said that, in response to our

earlier demarche, the number of flights per week had dropped,

and he characterized this as "a downward trend." (Note:

According to our count, there were four flights in each of

the last two weeks, significantly down from previous weeks.)

The Charge welcomed the reduction, but noted that flight

frequency had shown a tendency to creep up in the past. He

said we would need to stay alert to the issue.
