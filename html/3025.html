SUBJECT: JORDAN WELCOMES PRESIDENT\'S DECISION TO SEEK THE

SENATE\'S ADVICE AND CONSENT FOR RATIFICATION OF THE

U.S.-IAEA ADDITIONAL PROTOCOL

REF: STATE 100792

1. (U) On May 27, Poloff conveyed reftel information on the

President\'s May 9 decision to seek the Senate\'s advice and

consent to ratify the U.S.-IAEA Additional Protocol to Ayman

Aamiri, Deputy Director of the MFA\'s Department of

International and Regional Organizations. Aamiri said that

Jordan -- as one of the countries that have safeguard

agreements and Additional Protocols in force -- welcomed the

step, and noted the importance of U.S. support for the

success of the IAEA\'s activities.
