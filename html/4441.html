START MEMO NOMINATING-JUSTIFICATION:

Although Dr. Silva is the Head of the Department of

English at the University of Colombo, her interests

run beyond the field of English. She is the

consultant and editor of the Social Scientists\'

Association and also works closely with the Regional

Centre for Strategic Studies, and the International

Centre for Ethnic Studies. Through these reputed

organizations Dr. Silva is actively involved with

peace and conflict resolution, and women\'s rights.

Dr. Silva is young and has a long academic career

ahead of her. She also has all indications of

becoming a future leader in activities on peace,

conflict resolution and women\'s rights.

An exposure through the IV program on "Women as

Leaders in the Public and Private Sectors," would be

greatly beneficial to her and in return to the Sri

Lankan society.
