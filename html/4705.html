Classified by Pol-Mil Counselor Stuart Brown. Reasons 1.5 B

and D.

1.(S) On December 11, Operation Northern Watch POLAD

delivered to MFA Iraq Deskoff Ferhat Alkan ref A demarche on

cooperation between Turkish firm Yapa-Dis-Ticaret and Iraq.

Alkan said he would immediately forward the information to

the relevant GOT authorities and get back to us as soon as he

had additional information.

2. (S) Also on December 11, Econ Counselor raised ref A

information with Turkey\'s new Under Secretary for Foreign

Trade Tuncer Kayalar. Kayalar said Turkey would not allow any

items on the Wasenaar list to be exported to Iraq. Econ

Counselor responded that this was not sufficient; Turkey

should process all of its Iraq exports through the UN system.
