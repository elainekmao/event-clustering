1. (C) SUMMARY. IN ADDITION TO THE SUPREME COURT

DECISION STRIKING DOWN ELECTORAL LAW AMENDMENTS

(REFTEL), THERE HAVE BEEN A NUMBER OF LEGAL AND OTHER

ELECTORAL DEVELOPMENTS WITHIN THE PAST 48 HOURS. A HIGH

COURT JUDGEMENT ON DUAL NATIONALITY AND A SUPREME COURT

RULING ON VOTING BY CONSTITUENCIES HAVE CLARIFIED THESE

ISSUES, ALTHOUGH IT IS PROBABLY TOO LATE IN THE PROCESS

TO HAVE ANY PRACTICAL EFFECT. JUSTICE MINISTER

CHINAMASA CONFIRMED OUR ANALYSIS (REFTEL) THAT HE

INTENDS TO RUN THE ELECTIONS AS HE WISHES, COURT RULINGS

NOTWITHSTANDING. THE CONFUSION SURROUNDING THE

ELECTORAL PROCESS HAS BEEN FURTHER COMPLICATED BY THE

ELECTORAL SUPERVISORY COMMISSION\'S (ESC) FAILURE TO

RELEASE THE LIST OF POLLING PLACES, WHICH IN TURN WOULD

TRIGGER THE RELEASE OF THE NAMES OF POLLING AGENTS AND

PRESIDING OFFICERS. THE EMERGING "CHAOS" AROUND THE

ELECTORAL PROCESS IS BEING ORCHESTRATED BY THE GOZ TO

FAVOR ZANU-PF. END SUMMARY.

LEGAL DEVELOPMENTS

------------------

2. (C) A HIGH COURT RULING AND A SUPREME COURT RULING

HAVE CLARIFIED TWO ISSUES THAT HAVE DOGGED THE

ZIMBABWEAN POLITICAL CLIMATE FOR MONTHS. THE HIGH COURT

RULED PROVISIONALLY ON THE CONTROVERSIAL DUAL

NATIONALITY ISSUE, WHICH HAS FORCED THOUSANDS OF DUAL

NATIONAL ZIMBABWEANS TO RENOUNCE ONE OR THE OTHER OF

THEIR HOMELANDS. (THE OPPOSITION HAS REPEATEDLY CHARGED

THAT ZANU-PF ENGINEERED THIS INITIATIVE TO

DISENFRANCHISE SEVERAL MILLION POTENTIAL VOTERS WHO ARE

COMMERCIAL FARM WORKERS WITH ROOTS IN NEIGHBORING

COURTRIES, OR SETTLERS OF NON_AFRICAN ANCESTRY.) THE

HIGH COURT EXTENDED THE ALREADY PASSED DEADLINE FOR

RENUNCIATION BY SIX MONTHS, AN INTERIM RULING THAT

PRESUMABLY WOULD BE WELCOMED BY THE MDC. ON THE OTHER

HAND, THE SUPREME COURT OVERTURNED A HIGH COURT RULING

THAT COMPELLED THE REGISTRAR GENERAL (RG) TO COMPILE A

NATIONAL LIST AND PERMIT NATIONAL, RATHER THAN

CONSTITUENCY LEVEL, VOTING FOR THE PRESIDENTIAL

ELECTION. SCORE ONE FOR ZANU-PF.

CHINAMASA SET THE RECORD STRAIGHT

---------------------------------

3. (C) IN AN EXTENDED LIVE INTERVIEW ON ZBC TELEVISION

EVENING NEWS, JUSTICE MINISTER CHINAMASA CONFIRMED OURS

SUSPICIONS THAT HE WILL FORGE AHEAD ON ELECTORAL

PLANNING AS HE CHOOSES, AND DAMN THE COURTS. CHINAMASA

STATED, ALTHOUGH SOMEWHAT MORE OBLIQUELY, THAT THE GOZ

WILL EMPLOY PRESIDENTIAL DIRECTIVES TO RUN THE ELECTION

ALONG THE SAME LINES ENVISAGED BY THE AMENDED ELECTORAL

ACT THAT THE SUPREME COURT STRUCK DOWN.

THE CATCH 22 POLLING STATION CONUNDRUM

--------------------------------------

4. (C) IN A MEETING WITH INTERNATIONAL OBSERVERS, AS

REPORTED BY THE EU, THE DEPUTY HEAD OF THE ESC MORE OR

LESS STATED THAT THE ESC IS EMPOWERED BY LAW TO PUT

POLLING STATIONS WHERE THEY ARE NEEDED. BY LOGICAL

EXTENSION, THEY MAY ALSO REMOVE THEM FROM WHERE THEY ARE

NOT WANTED. HE WOULD NEITHER CONFIRM NOR DENY THAT

POLLING STATION NUMBERS WILL BE REDUCED IN MDC

STRONGHOLDS AND INCREASED IN ZANU-PF TERRITORY, BUT THIS

APPEARS TO BE THE CASE. IT IS THUS FAR IMPOSSIBLE TO

PREVENTED ANYONE FROM OBTAINING NAMES OF POLLING AGENTS

AND PRESIDING OFFICERS, EFFECTIVELY BLOCKING PROGRESS ON

PLANNING WHERE AND HOW TO DEPLOY INTERNATIONAL/LOCAL

MONITORS AND POLITICAL PARTY AGENTS ON THE VOTING DAYS.

COMMENT

-------

5. (C) WE ARE INCREASINGLY CONVINCED THAT THE RECENT

DEVELOPMENTS ARE INTEGRAL PARTS OF A CAREFULLY

ORCHESTRATED SCHEME TO OBSCURE THE ELECTIONS BENEATH A

VEIL OF DISORDER AND UNCERTAINTY. THE HIGH COURT RULING

ON DUAL NATIONALITY MEANS NOTHING, ONE WEEK AWAY FROM

THE VOTE, SINCE THE VOTER ROLLS ARE PRESUMABLY CLOSED

FOR THOSE PROBABLE MDC SUPPORTERS WHO HAVE THEORETICALLY

RECEIVED A JUDICIAL REPRIEVE FROM DISENFRANCHISEMENT.
