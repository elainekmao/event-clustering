2. (C) In delivering the Secretary\'s oral message

(refs A and B), Ambassador previewed ref C talking

points with UAE Minister of State for Foreign Affairs

Shaykh Hamdan bin Zayid Al-Nahyan on 4/13. The

Ambassador said we would be following up with UAEG

officials. Shaykh Hamdan responded that the UAEG

would take this and other requests for humanitarian

assistance to Iraq under consideration. Post will

follow-up with MFA officials, but anticipates

questions regarding the logistics of delivery and

distribution of the refined petroleum products to

Iraq. We would appreciate guidance on how to respond

to these questions, as well as any further information

available about our assessment of Iraq\'s domestic

petroleum needs -- especially in light of recent press

reports that Iraq\'s northern oil fields and associated

refineries have not been significantly damaged by

Coalition or Iraqi forces.
