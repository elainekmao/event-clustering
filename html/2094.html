SUBJECT: NIGERIA: OBASANJO VISIT TO WASHINGTON ON NOV 2

REF: STATE 184924

Classified by Ambassador Howard F. Jeter; Reasons 1.5 (b/d).

1. (C) On October 24, I orally delivered President Bush\'s

formal invitation to President Obasanjo to visit Washington

on November 2. Obasanjo accepted the invitation with

pleasure, and said a written acceptance would follow the next

day. I also told President Obasanjo that a meeting with

Secretary Powell had been scheduled. Asked if he desired

SIPDIS

meetings with Treasury Secretary O\'Neill and Energy Secretary

Abraham, if such meetings could be arranged, Obasanjo readily

agreed to the proposal.

2. (C) As far as we know now, President Obasanjo and his

party plan to arrive in Washington on November 2 at 0700.

Although the official delegation is still being decided, we

understand that it will be small and will almost certainly

include Foreign Minister Sule Lamido, National Security

Advisor General Aliyu Mohammed (rtd), State House Chief of

Protocol Ambassador Jonathan Coker, the President\'s Special

Assistant Adobe Obe and Chief Economic Advisor to the

President, Magnus Kpakol.

Jeter
