SUBJECT: HOLY SEE WOULD WELCOME CONTINUED PRESIDENTIAL PUSH

FOR GREATER RELIGIOUS FREEDOM IN CHINA

CLASSIFIED BY: CHARGE D\'AFFAIRES D. BRENT HARDT FOR REASON 1.5(B) AND (

D)

1. (C) SUMMARY: WHEN PRESIDENT BUSH MEETS WITH CHINESE

PRESIDENT JIANG OCTOBER 24 IN CRAWFORD, THE HOLY SEE WOULD

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 VATICA 05194 231549Z

WELCOME A CONTINUED STRONG PRESIDENTIAL PUSH FOR RELIGIOUS

FREEDOM IN CHINA, REINFORCING THE MESSAGE THE PRESIDENT

DELIVERED IN HIS LAST MEETING WITH JIANG. HOLY SEE FOREIGN

MINISTER ARCHBISHOP JEAN-LOUIS TAURAN TOLD CHARGE OCTOBER 22

THAT THE HOLY SEE GREATLY APPRECIATED THE PRESIDENT\'S EFFORTS

ON BEHALF OF RELIGIOUS FREEDOM AND THE POSITION OF THE

CATHOLIC CHURCH IN HIS LAST MEETING, AND BELIEVES A

REITERATION OF THE SAME POINTS AT THIS TIME WOULD BE HELPFUL.

AT THE SAME TIME, THE HOLY SEE IS NOT CONVINCED THAT THE

TIMING IS PROPITIOUS TO PRESS TOO HARD FOR SPECIFIC

CONCESSIONS FOR THE CATHOLIC CHURCH PRIOR TO THE COMMUNIST

PARTY CONGRESS, AS JIANG MAY BE LESS RECEPTIVE NOW THAN AFTER

THE CONGRESS. RATHER, THE HOLY SEE BELIEVES IT WOULD BE

USEFUL FOR PRESIDENT BUSH TO INSIST ON RELIGIOUS FREEDOM FOR

ALL FAITHS IN CHINA AND TO CONVEY THAT THE UNITED STATES

WOULD WELCOME NORMAL RELATIONS BETWEEN CHINA AND THE HOLY

SEE. END SUMMARY.

2. (C) ACCORDING TO FM TAURAN AND VATICAN CHINA DESK OFFICER

ROTA-GRAZIOSI, THE HOLY SEE BELIEVES THAT THE CATHOLIC CHURCH

IN CHINA WILL BENEFIT MOST THROUGH THE UNIVERSAL ADVANCEMENT

OF RELIGIOUS FREEDOM IN CHINA. THE HOLY SEE WOULD WELCOME

THE PRESIDENT\'S AFFIRMATION OF UNITED STATES\' SUPPORT FOR

NORMALIZATION OF RELATIONS BETWEEN THE HOLY SEE AND CHINA AND

FOR CHINESE GOODWILL GESTURES TOWARD THE CHURCH. SUCH

GESTURES COULD INCLUDE PERMISSION FOR A SENIOR HOLY SEE

REPRESENTATIVE TO VISIT A LOCAL CHINESE CHURCH OR AN EASING

OF THE RESTRICTIONS ON THE MANY BISHOPS AND PRIESTS BEING

HELD IN POLICE CUSTODY, HOUSE ARREST OR UNDER HEAVY

SURVEILLANCE. OVER THE LONGER TERM, THE HOLY SEE WOULD LIKE

TO SEE CHINESE AUTHORITIES PERMIT THE ORDINATION OF CHINESE

BISHOPS AND PRIESTS BY THE POPE AS WELL AS THE PILGRIMAGES

AND OTHER VISITS OF CATHOLIC AUTHORITIES TO THE HOLY SEE.

3. (C) THE KEY TO SINO-VATICAN RELATIONS AT THIS JUNCTURE,

THEY BELIEVE, IS FOR THE CHINESE GOVERNMENT TO BETTER

UNDERSTAND THE NATURE OF THE SPECIFIC LINK BETWEEN CATHOLICS

AND THE HOLY SEE. ACCORDING TO ROTA-GRAZIOZI, THE CHINESE

REMAIN GRIPPED WITH FEAR THAT CATHOLIC CHURCH MEMBERSHIP

WOULD THREATEN CHINESE CIVIL POWER. HE BELIEVED SUCH FEARS

COULD BE ALLAYED IF THE CHINESE BETTER UNDERSTOOD THAT

CATHOLIC FAITHFUL DO NOT ALWAYS FOLLOW THE HOLY SEE\'S

POSITIONS ON INTERNATIONAL SOCIAL ISSUES AND THAT PAPAL

AUTHORITY IS A SPIRITUAL, NOT POLITICAL, MATTER.
