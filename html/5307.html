SUBJECT: TURKISH CHOD STRESSES IMPORTANCE OF

DEFENSE-RELATED PROJECTS IN MEETING WITH CHAIRMAN MYERS

(U) Classified by Ambassador W. Robert Pearson. Reasons: 1.5

(B and D)

1. (C) During a January 20 meeting with visiting CJCS,

Turkish General Staff (TGS) CHOD Gen. Ozkok said that TGS

would concentrate on the ATAK helicopter and the tank

modernization programs after the conclusion of the three

"big" visits (i.e. CJCS, 1/23 UK CHOD and 1/24 SACEUR Gen.

Jones). Ozkok said that Turkey attaches great importance to

US weapons systems, noting that training and systems like

computers are easier. However, Turkey cannot afford the

current prices. Ozkok also said that Turkey could review its

configurations and specifications.

2. (C) Ozkok said that while the debate between the "cheap

but many" and the "expensive but few" continues in Turkey, at

the moment there is a sentiment in Turkey that defensive

weapons can defeat sophisticated offensive weapons.

3. (C) Ozkok said that he is also interested in the Joint

Strike Fighter (JSF) program and requested that Turkish

companies be considered.

4. (U) This message has been cleared by the Joint Staff.

PEARSON
