SUBJECT: Iraqi Charge phones Ambassador, saying he is

calling from embassy of "liberated Iraq"; Asks to meet

(U) Classified by Ambassador E. Ashley Wills.

Reasons 1.5 (B,D).

1. (U) This message contains an Action Request. Please

see Para 3.

2. (S/NF) The Ambassador received a phone call from

Iraqi Charge Wajdi Mardan Abbas this morning (April 10).

Abbas said he was calling from the "embassy of liberated

Iraq" and wanted to express his deep appreciation to the

U.S. for its role in Iraq. In doing this, he kept

repeating "thank you, thank you, thank you." Abbas

added that he wanted to come and meet the Ambassador as

soon as possible in order to discuss a "letter" sent by

a "friend" to the U.S. embassy. Abbas did not go into

the matter further. The Ambassador was non-committal in

his response.

3. (S/NF) ACTION REQUEST: Mission has no information

about the letter mentioned by Abbas. From what we

understand, Abbas is an Iraqi intelligence operative.

Clearly, he is in bail out mode given the recent events

in Baghdad. GSL contacts have also told us that he may

have recently requested political asylum in Sri Lanka,

but this is not confirmed. We also recently heard that

he had let his household staff go. We would appreciate

it if Washington could get back to us ASAP with

instructions on how to handle Abbas and his request to

meet. END ACTION REQUEST.

4. (U) Minimize considered.

WILLS
