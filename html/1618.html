SUBJECT: ZIMBABWE POLICE ARREST SUSPECT IN MARCH KILLING

OF SEVENTY-TWO YEAR OLD FEMALE COMMERCIAL FARMER

REF: HARARE 919

CLASSIFIED BY CHARGE D\' AFFAIRES EARL M. IRVING FOR

REASONS 1.5 (B/D).

1. (U) THE MARCH 31 EDITIONS OF BOTH THE GOZ-CONTROLLED

"THE HERALD" AND THE INDEPENDENT "THE DAILY NEWS"

REPORTED THAT THE ZIMBABWE POLICE HAVE ARRESTED A

SUSPECT IN THE MURDER OF GLORIA OLDS. OLDS, A 72-YEAR-

OLD WHITE FEMALE COMMERCIAL FARMER, WAS BRUTALLY

MURDERED BY AUTOMATIC WEAPONS FIRE IN AN AMBUSH AT HER

FARM GATE ON MARCH 4 (REFTEL). THE INDIVIDUAL ARRESTED,

ALBERT NCUBE, IS DESCRIBED AS A 47 YEAR OLD UNEMPLOYED

RESIDENT OF BULAWAYO, A LARGE CITY NEAR OLD\'S FARM.

2. (U) ACCORDING TO THE POLICE, NCUBE WAS ARRESTED ON

MARCH 15 WHEN HE WAS CAUGHT CARRYING BEEF AWAY FROM A

BULL THAT HAD BEEN KILLED WITH AN AK-47 RIFLE.

BALLISTIC TESTS INDICATED THAT SHELL CARTRIDGES FOUND

ALONGSIDE THE DEAD BULL MATCHED THOSE FOUND NEAR THE

BODY OF OLDS. THE PROSECUTOR IN THE CASE ALLEGES THAT

NCUBE, ALONG WITH ANOTHER MAN NOT IN POLICE CUSTODY,

KIDNAPPED OLDS\' FARM FOREMAN ON THE MORNING OF HER

KILLING, AND INTERROGATED HIM REGARDING SECURITY ON THE

OLDS\' FARM. THEY THEN ALLEGEDLY RELEASED HIM AFTER

THREATENING HIM WITH DEATH IF HE SAID ANYTHING ABOUT HIS

KIDNAPPING. THE PROSECUTOR ALLEGES THAT LATER THAT

MORNING, NCUBE AND THE ACCOMPLICE WENT TO THE OLDS\'

FARM, WEARING ZIMBABWE NATIONAL ARMY (ZNA) FATIGUES, AND

KILLED GLORIA OLDS.

3. (SBU) "THE HERALD" ARTICLE REPORTS THAT THE POLICE

HAVE EVIDENCE THAT NCUBE POSSESSED THE AK-47 RIFLE USED

TO KILL THE BULL, AND THAT HE WAS SUBSEQUENTLY

IDENTIFIED IN A POLICE LINEUP BY OLDS\' FARM FOREMAN.

THE POLICE ALSO ALLEGEDLY RECOVERED ZNA FATIGUES FROM

NCUBE\'S RESIDENCE. THE NEWSPAPER ARTICLES DO NOT

INDICATE WHAT POSSIBLE MOTIVE NCUBE MIGHT HAVE HAD FOR

KILLING OLDS, AND REPORT THAT HE HAS NOT BEEN ASKED TO

MAKE A PLEA. HE IS BEING HELD IN POLICE CUSTODY UNTIL

AN APRIL 19 HEARING. OUR ATTEMPT TO SECURE COMMENT ON

THE CASE DIRECTLY FROM THE POLICE WERE UNSUCCESSFUL.

CIVIL SOCIETY CONTACTS WE SPOKE TO ARE OF THE OPINION

THAT THE GOVERNMENT OF ZIMBABWE (GOZ) ALWAYS KNEW WHO

OLDS KILLERS WERE, AND THAT THIS ARREST REFLECTS AN

EFFORT BY THE GOZ TO SHOW THAT THERE IS RULE OF LAW IN

ZIMBABWE.

4. (C) COMMENT: WE ARE GOING TO TAKE A WAIT AND SEE

ATTITUDE REGARDING THE ARREST OF NCUBE IN THE OLDS

KILLING. THOUGH THE FIRST KILLING OF A WHITE FARMER

TOOK PLACE OVER A YEAR AGO, AND MRS. OLDS WAS NUMBER

EIGHT, THE ZIMBABWE POLICE HAD NOT ARRESTED SUSPECTS IN

ANY OF THESE HOMICIDES UNTIL THIS ONE, EVEN THOUGH THERE

WERE EYEWITNESSES TO SOME OF THE OTHER KILLINGS. THERE

HAVE ALSO BEEN VERY FEW ARRESTS AND NO TRIALS OF ANY

SUSPECTS FOR THE KILLINGS OF THE THIRTY-FIVE MDC

CONFIDENTIAL

SUPPORTERS MURDERED SINCE THE ELECTION VIOLENCE THAT

STARTED LAST APRIL. WE DO NOT YET KNOW WHETHER NCUBE

WAS A FORMER OR CURRENT MEMBER OF THE ZIMBABWE NATIONAL

ARMY, OR WHETHER HE IS A LIBERATION WAR VETERAN. WHAT

IS CLEAR IS THAT OLDS WAS ASSASSINATED IN A HAIL OF

AUTOMATIC WEAPONS FIRE AS SHE WENT TO OPEN THE GATE TO

HER FARM ON MARCH 4. THE MANNER OF HER KILLING

CONTINUES TO INDICATE A MOTIVE OTHER THAN A BOTCHED

ROBBERY ATTEMPT. END COMMENT.
