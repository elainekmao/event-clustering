SUBJECT: AMBASSADOR\'S LUNCH WITH MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS

SUMMARY

-------

1. (U) DURING HIS INITIAL MEETING WITH THE MINISTER OF

UNCLASSIFIED

COMMUNICATIONS, THE AMBASSADOR PROMOTED MOTOROLA\'S BID FOR

THE TENDER FOR A NATIONAL PUBLIC SAFETY DIGITAL MOBILE

COMMUNICATIONS SYSTEM, SOUGHT FAIR MARKET ACCESS FOR U.S.

FIRMS, AND OUTLINED THE U.S. MILITARY\'S NEED FOR RADIO AND

TELEVISION COMMUNICATIONS FREQUENCIES. END SUMMARY.

2. (U) MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, MAURIZIO GASPARRI,

HOSTED THE AMBASSADOR AT LUNCH THURSDAY, JANUARY 31.

UNDER-SECRETARY GIANCARLO INNOCENZI AND MIN. GIANFRANCO

GIORGOLO, THE DIPLOMATIC ADVISOR, DCM POPE, AND ECMIN DEAN

ALSO ATTENDED.

AFNS

----

3. (U) THE AMBASSADOR USED THE OPPORTUNITY TO RAISE THE

NEED OF THE US AMERICAN FORCES NETWORK SOUTH (AFNS) TO

PROVIDE RADIO AND TELEVISION COMMUNICATIONS ACCESS TO THE

U.S. MILITARY COMMUNITY IN ITALY (AROUND 65,000 PEOPLE) AS

NECESSARY FOR FORCE PROTECTION AND FOR MORALE. SINCE THE

ITALIAN MINISTRY OF DEFENSE WILL NOT EXTEND LOANER

FREQUENCIES BEYOND THE END OF 2002, DOD IS CONCERNED THAT WE

MAY LOSE OUR ABILITY TO COMMUNICATE WITH OFF-DUTY MILITARY

AND THEIR FAMILIES. THE MINISTER SAID THAT HE WOULD LOOK

INTO IT.

4. (SBU) ON FRIDAY, FEB. 1, ING. GIGANTINO FROM THE MINISTRY

OF COMMUNICATION AND THE INDIVIDUAL RESPONSIBLE FOR TRACKING

THE AFNS ISSUE, CALLED US SENDING STATE OFFICE AND THE

ECONOMIC MINISTER SEPARATELY TO SAY THAT THE MINISTRY WOULD

HAVE NO PROBLEMS WITH THE MINISTRY OF DEFENSE AND ITALIAN

UNCLASSIFIED

DEFENSE GENERAL STAFF (IDGS) EXTENDING THE LOAN OF THE

FREQUENCIES BEYOND THE END OF THE YEAR AS LONG AS THE MOD AND

IDGS AGREED. THE MINISTRY OF COMMUNICATION, HOWEVER, HAS NO

CIVILIAN FREQUENCIES THAT THEY COULD LEND. THE MINISTRY

WANTED TO KNOW WHAT THE U.S. PROPOSALS WERE FOR A SOLUTION

WHEN THE LOAN PERIOD EXPIRES AT THE END OF THE YEAR.

(COMMENT: DESPITE THE MINISTER\'S OPTIMISTIC VIEW THAT HE

COULD FIX THIS, THE FOLLOW-UP CALL INDICATES ESSENTIALLY NO

PROGRESS.)

FAIR MARKET ACCESS FOR U.S. FIRMS

---------------------------------

5. (U) THE AMBASSADOR ALSO EMPHASIZED THE NEED TO HAVE A

LEVEL PLAYING FIELD IN THE BUSINESS WORLD. ITALY AND THE

U.S. ARE IN AN INCREASINGLY GLOBAL MARKET PLACE AND THE RULES

NEED TO BE TRANSPARENT AND FAIR. IF IT TAKES ONLY TWO DAYS

FOR AN AMERICAN TO SWITCH TELEPHONE PROVIDERS, AND 12 DAYS IN

EUROPE, IT IS HARD TO UNDERSTAND WHY IT SHOULD 28-35 DAYS IN

ITALY. SUCH DELAYS LOOK LIKE A BARRIER TO THOSE COMPANIES

WHO WISH TO PROVIDE COMMERCIAL ALTERNATIVES TO THE MONOPOLY

PROVIDER.

6. (U) THE AMBASSADOR RAISED THE JOINT STRIKE FIGHTER AS AN

EXAMPLE WHERE THE U.S. WAS LOOKING FOR TOP QUALITY PARTNERS,

THOSE WHO PRODUCE TOP-NOTCH PRODUCTS. WE LOOK FORWARD TO

ITALY\'S BECOMING A PARTNER IN THE JSF. EVERYONE WOULD BE

ABLE TO COMPETE FOR CONTRACTS, AND THIS IS WHAT WE WOULD LIKE

TO SEE ON ITALY\'S SIDE.

MOTOROLA

7. (SBU) REINFORCING THE EMBASSY\'S ADVOCACY WITH ITALY FOR

MOTOROLA TO WIN AN ITALIAN INTERIOR MINISTRY TENDER FOR A

NATIONAL PUBLIC SAFETY DIGITAL MOBILE COMMUNICATIONS SYSTEM,

THE AMBASSADOR AGAIN EMPHASIZED THE NEED FOR \'BUYING THE

BEST\' AND \'MOTOROLA TECHNOLOGY IN THIS FIELD IS THE BEST\'.

THE MINISTER UNDERLINED THAT THIS IS NOT A MINISTRY OF

COMMUNICATIONS DECISION, BUT THAT HE UNDERSTOOD THE

AMBASSADOR\'S CONCERN THAT THE COMPETITIVE PROCESS BE OPEN.

THE AMBASSADOR REITERATED THAT A LEVEL PLAYING FIELD ALSO

REQUIRES AN OPENNESS IN TENDERING AND MAKING SELECTION

DECISIONS. (COMMENT: EVEN THOUGH THIS IS A MINISTRY OF

INTERIOR DECISION, WE BELIEVE THAT GASPARRI IS LIKELY TO BE A

KEY PLAYER IN THE SELECTION PROCESS.)

WIRELESS LOCAL LOOP

-------------------

8. (U) GASPARRI NOTED THAT THEY WERE HAVING A COMPETITION

FOR THE FIXED TELEPHONE WIRELESS LOCAL LOOP WHICH SHOULD

IMPROVE SERVICE TO THE CUSTOMER. IN MOBILE TELEPHONY THERE

ARE FOUR COMPETITORS AND SO SERVICE IS GOOD, BUT IN FIXED

TELEPHONY THERE IS A DOMINANT OPERATOR WHO CONTROLS THE

MARKET AND SO THERE IS LITTLE COMPETITION AND LESS SERVICE.

9. MAURIZIO GASPARRI IS ONE OF THE CLOSEST ADVISERS OF THE

NATIONAL ALLIANCE LEADER AND DEPUTY PM GIANFRANCO FINI.

GASPARRI WAS BORN IN ROME ON JULY 18, 1957, GRADUATED FROM

HIGH SCHOOL, AND WORKED AS A JOURNALIST UNTIL 1994. HE WAS

ELECTED TO THE CHAMBER OF DEPUTIES IN 1992 WITH THE RIGHTIST

ITALIAN SOCIAL MOVEMENT. IN THE CHAMBER HE HAS BEEN A MEMBER

OF COMMITTEES FOR PRODUCTIVE ACTIVITIES, DEFENSE AND JUSTICE.

HE WAS UNDER SECRETARY IN THE INTERIOR MINISTRY UNDER THE

FIRST BERLUSCONI GOVERNMENT. HE IS MARRIED AND HAS ONE

DAUGHTER. HE DOES NOT SPEAK MUCH ENGLISH, ALTHOUGH HE

APPEARS TO UNDERSTAND SOME.
