SUBJECT: INTERNATIONAL ASSISTANCE FOR ILO PROJECTS

REF: A) HANOI 1968; B) HANOI 71

IN RESPONSE TO WASHINGTON AGENCIES\' REQUEST, AND IN

COOPERATION WITH THE MINISTRY OF LABOR, INVALIDS AND

SOCIAL AFFAIRS (MOLISA), EMBASSY FORWARDS HEREWITH A

LIST OF LABOR ASSISTANCE PROJECTS IN VIETNAM IN WHICH

THE INTERNATIONAL LABOR ORGANIZATION (ILO) IS FULLY OR

PARTLY ENGAGED IN IMPLEMENTATION. THERE ARE SEVERAL

OTHER ONGOING LABOR PROJECTS FUNDED BY INTERNATIONAL

DONORS WHICH DO NOT INVOLVE ILO PARTICIPATION. WE WILL

CONTINU}IES IN THE LABOR SECTOR, BUT ALSO MAY HELP U.S.

DEPARTMENT OF LABOR INDENTIFY AREAS WHERE IT MAY LIKE

TO COOPERATE WITH THE GVN ON LABOR ISSUES.
