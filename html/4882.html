2. (C) SUMMARY. POLICE DETAINED DEMOCRACY AND ANTI-

CORRUPTION ACTIVISTS PHAM QUE DUONG AND TRAN KHUE ON

DECEMBER 28 AND 29, RESPECTIVELY, IN HO CHI MINH CITY.

THEIR CURRENT WHEREABOUTS AND CONDITION ARE UNKNOWN,

BUT THEY MAY NOW BE FACING PROSECUTION ON POTENTIALLY

SERIOUS CHARGES AFTER YEARS OF HIGHLY VISIBLE ACTIVISM.

END SUMMARY.

3. (C) PROMINENT ACTIVIST DR. NGUYEN THANH GIANG ON

DECEMBER 30 CONFIRMED TO POLOFF AN ANONYMOUS REPORT

RECEIVED BY EMBASSY ABOUT THESE DETENTIONS. DR. GIANG

SAID THAT KHUE\'S DAUGHTER HAD TELEPHONED HIM TO "WARN"

ABOUT THE DETENTIONS ON DECEMBER 29. ACCORDING TO HER,

DUONG WAS DETAINED ALONG WITH HIS WIFE, A FRIEND

(DESCRIBED BY DR. GIANG AS AN EMPLOYEE OF THE GVN\'S

CULTURAL PUBLISHING HOUSE), AND TWO WELL-WISHERS AT THE

MAIN TRAIN STATION IN HO CHI MINH CITY ON THE AFTERNOON

OF DECEMBER 28. THEY HAD GONE TO HO CHI MINH CITY A

FEW DAYS BEFORE TO SEE FELLOW ACTIVIST TRAN KHUE.

POLICE DETAINED ALL FIVE AS DUONG, WIFE, AND FRIEND

WERE ABOUT TO BOARD A TRAIN HOMEWARD. THE TWO WELL-

WISHERS, FROM HO CHI MINH CITY, WERE RELEASED AFTER A

FEW HOURS, ACCORDING TO KHUE\'S DAUGHTER.

4. (C) KHUE\'S DAUGHTER ADDED THAT POLICE HAD COME TO

TRAN KHUE\'S HOUSE ON THE AFTERNOON OF DECEMBER 29.

THEY DETAINED KHUE AND TOOK AWAY HIS COMPUTER AND

VARIOUS DOCUMENTS.

5. (C) DR. GIANG SPECULATED THAT THE DETENTIONS WERE

LINKED TO THE DECEMBER 20 SENTENCING OF NGUYEN KHAC

CONFIDENTIAL

TOAN (REFTEL) FOR ESPIONAGE. HE CLAIMED THAT A

DOCUMENT USED AT THE TRIAL HAD ALLEGED THAT KHUE AND

DUONG, AS WELL AS DR. GIANG AND FELLOW ACTIVIST HOANG

MINH CHINH, WERE CONTINUING TOAN\'S ACTIVITIES. HE

DECLINED TO ELABORATE ON HOW HE HAD LEARNED OF A

DOCUMENT USED AT TOAN\'S CLOSED TRIAL. DR. GIANG

CONFIRMED, HOWEVER, THAT NOTHING UNTOWARD HAD HAPPENED

RECENTLY TO HIM, CHINH, OR OTHER ACTIVISTS TO HIS

KNOWLEDGE.

6. (C) COMMENT: GVN SECURITY OFFICIALS ARE OFTEN

QUICK TO SEE CONSPIRACIES EVEN IN OTHERWISE INNOCENT

ACTIVITIES. THEY MAY HAVE JUDGED THAT DUONG\'S UNUSUAL

TRIP TO HO CHI MINH CITY TO VISIT KHUE HAD A SINISTER

MOTIVE, GIVEN THE PROMINENCE THESE TWO HAVE HAD IN THE

ACTIVIST COMMUNITY SINCE THE SEPTEMBER 2001 EFFORT THEY

SPEARHEADED TO SET UP NON-GOVERNMENT ANTI-CORRUPTION

WATCHDOG. DUONG\'S WIFE HAS NOT PREVIOUSLY BEEN KNOWN

TO EMBASSY AS A POLITICAL ACTIVIST; HER SIMULTANEOUS

DETENTION MAY JUST BE AN MPS EFFORT TO GAIN EXTRA

BARGAINING LEVERAGE OVER HER HUSBAND TO BEHAVE. THERE

IS, HOWEVER, CONCERN HERE THAT DUONG AND KHUE MAY NOW

HAVE PUSHED THE ENVELOPE ENOUGH THAT THE GVN WILL FEEL

OBLIGED TO PROSECUTE THEM UNDER POTENTIALLY SERIOUS

CHARGES.
