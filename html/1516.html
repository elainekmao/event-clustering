SUBJECT: DRC ALLIES HEADS OF STATE MEETING POSTPONED

REF: KINSHASA 671

CLASSIFIED BY CHARGE D\'AFFAIRES EARL M. IRVING FOR

REASONS: 1.5 (B) AND (D).

1. (C) MAXWELL CHIKOROWONDO, THE ZIMBABWE MFA\'S DRC

DESK OFFICER, INFORMED POLOFF FEBRUARY 8 THAT THE DRC

ALLIES HEADS OF STATE MEETING DUE TO TAKE PLACE IN

VICTORIA FALLS (REFTEL) HAS BEEN POSTPONED. NAMIBIAN

PRESIDENT NUJOMA WAS UNABLE TO ATTEND THE FEBRUARY 8

MEETING BECAUSE OF A SCHEDULING CONFLICT. CHIKOROWONDO

STATED THAT THE ALLIED GOVERNMENTS WERE CONSULTING WITH

EACH OTHER TO DETERMINE A MORE SUITABLE DATE FOR A

MEETING, WHICH HAS NOT YET BEEN DECIDED.

2. (C) THE DESK OFFICER ALSO STATED THAT THE JOINT

MILITARY COMMISSION MEETING WOULD LIKELY BE HELD

FEBRUARY 9 OR 10, THE POLITICAL COMMITTEE MEETING

FEBRUARY 11 OR 12, AND THE LUSAKA ALL-SIGNATORIES SUMMIT

FEBRUARY 13. CHIKOROWONDO WAS NOT AWARE OF ANY PLANNING

FOR A MAPUTO III SUMMIT. THE MFA OFFICIAL WAS SKEPTICAL

OF THE USEFULNESS OF THE MAPUTO PROCESS BECAUSE THERE IS

NO LEGAL MECHANISM OUTLINED IN LUSAKA FOR "OUTSIDERS"

(COMMENT: READ "SOUTH AFRICANS" END COMMENT) TO CONVENE

MEETINGS.
