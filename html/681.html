1. (C) SUMMARY HAMAS SPOKESMAN IN JORDAN IBRAHIM

GHAWSHAH REFRAINED FROM PRAISING THE OCTOBER 19 TEL

AVIV BOMBING BUT HAS PREDICTED THAT HAMAS WILL

CONTINUE TO UNDERTAKE SUCH OPERATIONS. DESPITE HIS

WILLINGNESS TO OBLIGE THE MEDIA WITH THE KIND OF

FLAMBOYANT RHETORIC THAT MAKES A GOOD STORY,

GHAWSHAH HAS CONSISTENTLY MAINTAINED THAT HAMAS IN

JORDAN IS NOT LINKED TO HAMAS IN THE OCCUPIED

TERRITORIES EXCEPT IN THE MOST SUPERFICIAL WAYS.

PLO "POLITICAL COUNSELOR" OMAR SHAKAA SEPARATELY

REEMPHASIZED HIS BELIEF THAT WHILE THE MUSLIM

BROTHERHOOD AND HAMAS IN JORDAN ARE ESSENTIALLY THE

SAME ORGANIZATION, THEY DO NOT CALL THE SHOTS IN THE

TERRITORIES. OTHER REJECTIONIST GROUPS IN JORDAN

HAVE REAFFIRMED THEIR SUPPORT OF JIHAD AS A METHOD

OF RETAKING PALESTINE AND HAVE CALLED ON JORDANIANS

AS WELL AS PALESTINIANS TO RESIST THE PEACE TREATY

WITH ISRAEL.

2. (C) IN THE ABSENCE OF FIRM EVIDENCE OF A LINK

BETWEEN GHAWSHAH AND HAMAS\' MILITARY WING, THE

JORDANIAN GOVERNMENT SEEMS PREPARED TO TOLERATE

RHETORIC THAT DOES NOT EXCEED THAT OF OTHER

REJECTIONISTS HERE. ARAFAT\'S RECENT ALLEGATIONS

THAT HAMAS IN JORDAN IS SUPPORTING AND IN SOME CASES

DIRECTING EVENTS IN THE OCCUPIED TERRITORIES ARE

FLIMSY AND IGNORE THE FACT THAT KING HUSSEIN STANDS

TO LOSE AS MUCH, IF NOT MORE, THAN ARAFAT IF THE

PALESTINIAN AUTHORITY IS OVERWHELMED BY ISLAMIC

RADICALS. END SUMMARY.

3. (U) HAMAS SPOKESMAN IN JORDAN IBRAHIM GHAWSHAH

HAS NOT PRAISED THE OCTOBER 19 BOMBING IN TEL AVIV,

BUT HE HAS DEFENDED RECENT HAMAS ATTACKS BY

ASSERTING THAT RESISTANCE OPERATIONS WILL CONTINUE

AGAINST THE ISRAELI OCCUPATION. GHAWSHAH MADE THE

FOLLOWING POINTS TO THE LATEST EDITION OF ENGLISH-

LANGUAGE WEEKLY "THE STAR" (FULL TEXT IN FBIS AMMAN

JN2310100694) AND REITERATED THEM IN STATEMENTS TO

-- "THE OBJECTIVE OF THE WEST JERUSALEM SHOOTING

LAST WEEK WAS TO SHOW THAT PALESTINIAN

KICKING."

-- "THE IMPORTANCE OF THIS OPERATION (THE WEST

JERUSALEM SHOOTING) WAS THAT IT TOOK PLACE IN

THE HEART OF THE SO-CALLED ISRAELI ENTITY IN

WEST JERUSALEM, NOT IN EAST JERUSALEM.

ISLAMISTS ARE NOT ONLY DEMANDING THE RETURN OF

-- "THIS OPERATION PROVED THE INSIGNIFICANCE OF

COMPETITION BETWEEN JORDAN AND THE PLO OVER THE

HOLY PLACES IN JERUSALEM. THE CAUSE IS NOT WHO

CONTROLS THE HOLY PLACES, BUT HOW TO REDEEM THE

CITY OF JERUSALEM, THE CAPITAL OF PALESTINE."

-- "THE KIDNAPPING OF ISRAELI CORPORAL WACHSMAN

WAS A POLITICAL ACT HAMAS WANTED TO BRING TO

THE FOREFRONT THE ISSUE OF 5000 PALESTINIAN

DETAINEES STILL HELD IN ISRAELI JAILS."

GHAWSHAH\'S POSITION AS SPOKESMAN

--------------------------------

5. (C) DESPITE JORDANIAN GOVERNMENT RESTRICTIONS

PLACED ON GHAWSHAH AND HIS COLLEAGUE MUHAMMAD AL-

NAZZAL LAST APRIL, LOCAL AND INTERNATIONAL MEDIA

CONTINUE TO SEEK OUT GHAWSHAH AS AN ACCESSIBLE

SPOKESMAN OF THE HAMAS MOVEMENT AND BECAUSE OF HIS

DEMONSTRATED WILLINGNESS TO PROVIDE SENSATIONAL

COMMENTARY AND REACTION TO EVENTS IN THE OCCUPIED

TERRITORIES. NEVERTHELESS, JOURNALISTIC SOURCES

REPORT THAT GHAWSHAH CONSISTENTLY DENIES THAT HAMAS

IN JORDAN HAS ANY CONTROL OVER OR DIRECT CONNECTION

TO EVENTS IN THE OCCUPIED TERRITORIES, THOUGH THESE

DISCLAIMERS RARELY APPEAR IN PRINT.

6. (C) ON THE DAY OF THE TEL AVIV BOMBING, GHAWSHAH

WAS BESIEGED BY REPORTERS LOOKING FOR STATEMENTS.

SOURCES STRESSED THAT GHAWSHAH FIRMLY REFUSED TO

ISSUE A WRITTEN STATEMENT ON THIS OCCASION AND STUCK

TO HIS USUAL PRACTICE OF COMMENTING VERBALLY. THEY

POINTED OUT THAT IN THE RARE INSTANCES HE DOES

COMMIT A STATEMENT TO PAPER, HE PREFERS TO RELEASE

THEM IN LEBANON OR SYRIA. (COMMENT: EMBASSY HAS

NOTED OVER THE PAST FEW MONTHS THAT FORMAL HAMAS

COMMUNIQUES RARELY ORIGINATE IN AMMAN. HOWEVER,

THIS DOES NOT EXPLAIN THE HAMAS STATEMENT FAXED TO

THE "JORDAN TIMES" THAT WAS CITED IN REF D. THE

"TIMES" ARTICLE NOTED THAT THE STATEMENT HAS "NO

DATELINE."

VIEW FROM THE PLO IN JORDAN

---------------------------

7. (C) PLO "POLITICAL COUNSELOR" IN AMMAN OMAR

SHAKAA ON OCTOBER 19 RESTATED TO POLOFF HIS BELIEF

THAT HAMAS IN JORDAN EXISTS AS A WING OF THE

JORDANIAN MUSLIM BROTHERHOOD. HE ASSERTED THAT

NEITHER HAMAS NOR THE BROTHERHOOD HERE EXERT ANY

CONTROL OVER HAMAS IN THE TERRITORIES. HAMAS ALSO

DOES NOT RECEIVE TRAINING OR MATERIAL FROM JORDAN.

JORDAN\'S HAMAS MOVEMENT THUS PRIMARILY MAKES RADICAL

STATEMENTS IN SUPPORT OF OPERATIONS PLANNED AND

CARRIED OUT BY HAMAS IN THE TERRITORIES WITHOUT

REFERENCE TO THE OFFICE HERE, SHAKAA DECLARED.

8. (C) AS HE HAS BEFORE, SHAKAA MADE IT CLEAR THAT

HE DID NOT BELIEVE THAT KING HUSSEIN OR ANY ELEMENT

OF THE JORDANIAN GOVERNMENT IS INVOLVED IN

SUPPORTING HAMAS OPERATIONS IN THE TERRITORIES.

SHAKAA BELIEVES THAT THE KING AND THE GOJ HAVE A

TACIT AGREEMENT WITH THE MUSLIM BROTHERHOOD THAT

ALLOWS HAMAS TO OPERATE HERE UNDER THE BROTHERHOOD\'S

UMBRELLA IN EXCHANGE FOR ASSURANCES THAT THE GROUP

WILL NOT SUPPORT OR UNDERTAKE VIOLENCE IN JORDAN.

-------

COMMENT

-------

9. (C) ALTHOUGH GHAWSHAH\'S WORDS TAKE ON ADDED

SIGNIFICANCE BECAUSE HE ACTS AS HAMAS\' "OFFICIAL

SPOKESMAN," THE SENTIMENTS HE EXPRESSES ARE SIMILAR

TO THOSE OF OTHER REJECTIONIST GROUPS IN JORDAN

WHICH HAVE ATTACKED THE JORDANIAN-ISRAELI ACCORD AND

LENT VERBAL SUPPORT TO ATTACKS IN THE OCCUPIED

TERRITORIES BY HAMAS\' MILITARY WING. THE PRACTICAL

EFFECT OF THE JORDANIAN GOVERNMENT\'S HANDLING OF

GHAWSHAH, IN THE ABSENCE OF COMPELLING EVIDENCE OF A

MORE DIRECT CONNECTION TO HAMAS\' MILITARY CELLS, IS

TO TOLERATE GHAWSHAH\'S STATEMENTS AS LONG AS THEY DO

NOT EXCEED STANDARD REJECTIONIST RHETORIC.

10. (C) ALLEGATIONS THAT THE KING AND JORDANIAN

SECURITY SERVICES HAVE HAD A ROLE IN THESE ATTACKS

ARE EVENLESS CREDIBLE AND WERE DEALT WITH IN REF C.

THE SUGGESTION THAT THE KING WOULD PLAY A DANGEROUS

GAME WITH HAMAS TO EMBARRASS ARAFAT AND DESTABILIZE

THE PA IGNORES THE FACT THAT THE KING HAS JUST AS

UC TO LOSE AS ARAFAT IF HAMAS BRINGS DOWN THE PA.

BOMBINGS AND ARRESTS INSIDE JORDAN THIS YEAR (SEE

REF E AND PREVIOUS REPORTING ON THE "ARAB AFGHANS")

BROUGHT HOME THE POINT THAT JORDAN HAS ITS OWN

RADICAL ISLAMIST UNDERGROUND, PART OF WHICH MANY

THINK IS A CLANDESTINE EXTENSION OF HAMAS.
