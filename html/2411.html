SUBJECT: COUNTER-TERRORISM: THE WORD IS GETTING OUT

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 02 HANOI 00149 230434Z

1. EVEN DURING AN EMBASSY-SPONSORED LANGUAGE IMMERSION

TRIP TO THE ANCIENT CAPITAL OF HUE JANUARY 19-22, THE

CAMPAIGN AGAINST INTERNATIONAL TERRORISM REMAINED A

SURPRISINGLY RECURRENT THEME. IN RANDOM AND INFORMAL

CONVERSATIONS WITH THREE DIFFERENT GROUPS OF CONSTRUCTION

WORKERS ENGAGED IN THE UNESCO-SPONSORED RESTORATION OF THE

IMPERIAL PALACE COMPOUND (WHERE THEY LIVE IN HUTS WITHOUT

READY ACCESS TO TELEVISION OR NEWSPAPERS), POL/C WAS

BOMBARDED WITH QUESTIONS LIKE "HOW IS THE WAR AGAINST

TERRORISM GOING," AND "WHERE IS BIN LADEN NOW?" OVER AND

OVER, WORKERS STRESSED THEIR STRONG OPPOSITION TO TERRORISM,

SUPPORT FOR THE US-LED CAMPAIGN, AND THE VIETNAMESE PEOPLE\'S

"HATRED" OF BIN LADEN AND OTHER TERRORISTS. ONE JOKED THAT

PERHAPS BIN LADEN WAS NOW HIDING IN CAMBODIA; WHEN POL/C

ASKED WHAT VIETNAM WOULD DO IF HE TRIED TO ENTER THIS

COUNTRY, OTHER WORKERS ROBUSTLY PROCLAIMED THAT THE GVN

WOULD ARREST HIM AND STOP HIM FROM CONTINUING HIS TERRORIST

ACTIONS.

2. VARIOUS WORKERS EXPRESSED SYMPATHY FOR THE VICTIMS OF

SEPTEMBER 11. AFTER VOICING THE STRONG DESIRE OF THE

VIETNAMESE PEOPLE FOR "PEACE," SOME WORKERS ADDED THAT THEY

UNDERSTOOD THAT THE INTERNATIONAL CAMPAIGN WAS NECESSARY IN

ORDER TO ACHIEVE THE LASTING PEACE THE PEOPLES OF THE WORLD

WANTED.

2. COMMENT: THIS NON-SCIENTIFIC SAMPLING OF WORKING CLASS

OPINION OBVIOUSLY CANNOT BE REPRESENTATIVE OF BROADER PUBLIC

OPINION. BUT IT IS STRIKING THAT, EVEN IN A COUNTRY WHERE

THE GOVERNMENT-CONTROLLED MEDIA HAS NOT BEEN VERY

GOTTEN OUT LOUD AND CLEAR: INTERNATIONAL TERRORISM IS A

MUST BE STOPPED; AND, THE INTERNATIONAL CAMPAIGN AGAINST
