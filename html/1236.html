SUBJECT: ZCTU CALLS FOR STAYAWAY AUGUST 2-4 TO PROTEST

CONTINUING POLITICAL VIOLENCE

KEEP THE PEACE AND AVOID CONFRONTATIONS WITH POLICE OR

RULING PARTY SUPPORTERS. PRESIDENT MUGABE HAS MADE NO

STATEMENT ABOUT THE STAYAWAY, WITH GOZ OFFICIALS DESCRIBING

THE EVENT SIMPLY AS "UNFORTUNATE," OR PART OF THE WIDER

CONSPIRACY BENT ON DISCREDITING THE RULING PARTY. IN THE

END, THOUGH, WE DO NOT EXPECT THE EVENT TO ACCOMPLISH MUCH;

THE POWER TO RESTORE THE RULE OF LAW ON THE OCCUPIED FARMS

AND IN THE HIGH-DENSITY SUBURBS RESTS WITH ONE MAN -- AND

MUGABE CONTINUES TO SHOW NO INTEREST IN DOING SO. END

COMMENT.
