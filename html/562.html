-------

SUMMARY

-------

1. ON COMPLETION OF THEIR VISIT TO MACEDONIA AND

CROATIA NOVEMBER 15, A HELSINKI COMMISSION DELEGATION

COMPRISED OF SEN. DENNIS DECONCINI AND REP. FRANK

MCCLOSKEY ISSUED A STATEMENT CALLING FOR: IMMEDIATE

RECOGNITION OF MACEDONIA; INCLUSION OF MACEDONIA IN

UNCLASSIFIED

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 03 ZAGREB 02405 01 OF 05 161626Z

CSCE, THE UN, AND OTHER INTERNATIONAL ORGANIZATIONS;

MORE INTENSIVE DIALOGUE BETWEEN SKOPJE AND ATHENS;

AND AN INTER-ETHNIC MACEDONIAN HUMAN RIGHTS

COMMISSION. THEY URGED EUROPE AND THE U.S. TO ASSUME

THEIR SHARE OF THE BOSNIAN REFUGEE BURDEN. THEY

CALLED FOR "MORE DIRECT INTERVENTION, INCLUDING

MILITARY INTERVENTION, IN BOSNIA-HERZEGOVINA BY THE

INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY, INCLUDING THE UNITED STATES,"

TERMING IT A "POLITICAL NECESSITY AND MORAL

OBLIGATION." AND THEY URGED THE IMMEDIATE ARREST OF

RADOVAN KARADJIC AND HIS ASSOCIATES AS A START TOWARD

BRINGING TO JUSTICE THOSE RESPONSIBLE FOR WAR CRIMES

AND GENOCIDE. END SUMMARY.

FOREIGN MINISTER OF MACEDONIA, OTHER GOVERNMENT

OFFICIALS, MEMBERS OF PARLIAMENT, REPRESENTATIVES OF

THE ALBANIAN COMMUNITY AND OTHERS. THE DELEGATION

ALSO VISITED A CAMP FOR REFUGEES, FLEEING THE HORRIBLE

BEFORE AND AFTER THE VISIT. WE ALSO HAD THE

OPPORTUNITY TO MEET WITH U.S. DIPLOMATS IN MACEDONIA,

AS WELL AS THE HEAD OF THE CSCE SPILLOVER MISSION

THERE.

"WE LEFT MACEDONIA CONVINCED THAT INTERNATIONAL

RECOGNITION OF THAT COUNTRY WOULD BE THE RIGUT THING

TO DO, AND THAT IT SHOULD BE DONE IMMEDIATELY. THE

UNITED STATES SHOULD ALSO MORE FORCEFULLY PRESS FOR

MACEDONIAN INCLUSION IN THE CSCE, THE UNITED NATIONS

AND OTHER INTERNATIONAL ORGANIZATIONS. WE WILL

RECOMMEND THIS TO THE STATE DEPARTMENT UPON OUR RETURN

TO WASHINGTON.

"WE BELIEVE THAT MACEDONIA DESERVES THE RECOGNITION IT

HAS REQUESTED FOR SEVERAL REASONS. FIRST, IT WOULD

HELP UNLOCK THE ECONOMIC STRANGLEHOLD ITS

INTERNATIONAL ISOLATION HAS PLACED ON MACEDONIA. WE

WERE DEEPLY DISTURBED, AS WE SAW MACEDONIA\'S FIRST

SNOW OF THE WINTER, BY THE INCREDIBLE SHORTAGE OF FUEL

FOR HEATING, TRANSPORTATION AND AGRICULTURAL

PRODUCTION. SECOND, RECOGNITION SEEMS TO BE A

NECESSARY FIRST STEP FOR THE COUNTRY TO MOVE FORWARD

IN BUILDING DEMOCRACY AND A MARKET-BASED ECONOMY.

THIRD, IT WOULD DISCOURAGE THE EXPANSION OF THE

YUGOSLAV CONFLICT INTO MACEDONIA THROUGH THE

INTERNATIONAL SUPPORT AND RESPECT WHICH RECOGNITION

IMPLIES, AS

WELL AS THE INTERNATIONAL STABILIZATION IT WOULD

PROMOTE. FINALLY, WE BELIEVE THAT MACEDONIA IS

"THIS IS CERTAINLY NOT TO SAY THAT WE DO NOT HAVE

CONCERNS REGARDING MACEDONIA. INDEED, SOME ASPECTS OF

MACEDONIAN SOCIETY HAVE SUCCUMBED TO THE DANGEROUS,

EXTREME NATIONALISM THAT IS UNFORTUNATELY ALL TOO

COMMON IN THE REGION. THIS NATIONALISM ENCOURAGES

DIVISION AND TENSION WITHIN THE COUNTRY\'S SMALL BUT

DIVERSE POPULATION. FOR EXAMPLE, WE HEARD REPORTS

THAT THE RIGHTS OF THOSE BELONGING TO MACEDONIA\'S

LARGE ETHNIC ALBANIAN POPULATION ARE FREQUENTLY

UNCLASSIFIED

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 03 ZAGREB 02405 03 OF 05 161629Z

VIOLATED, AND THAT THEY ARE GIVEN ONLY A NOMINAL ROLE

IN THE COUNTRY\'S POLITICAL AND ECONOMIC AFFAIRS,

MAKING THEM FEEL EXCLUDED FROM DECISIONS AFFECTING THE

COUNTRY\'S FUTURE. SIMILARLY, THIS NATIONALISM

ENCOURAGES AN INACCURATE HISTORICAL PERSPECTIVE THAT

WRONGLY AND NEEDLESSLY PLAYS ON THE SENSITIVITIES OF

MACEDONIA\'S NEIGHBORS, ESPECIALLY GREECE. THE NEW

MACEDONIAN FLAG IS BUT ONE EXAMPLE OF THIS. WE DO NOT

BELIEVE MOST MACEDONIANS HAVE FALLEN PREY TO THIS

VIRULENT NATIONALISM, BUT GREATER EFFORTS MUST BE

TAKEN TO COUNTER THIS TREND IN A DEMOCRATIC WAY AND TO

MAKE ALL THOSE LIVING IN MACEDONIA FEEL THAT THEY ARE

CITIZENS WITH EQUAL RIGHTS AND OPPORTUNITIES. ALL

LOSE IF MACEDONIA\'S ETHNIC GROUPS CHOOSE TO FIGHT

NATIONALISM WITH NATIONALISM. EVEN DURING THESE

DIFFICULT TIMES, REASON MUST BE ALLOWED TO PREVAIL.

"MOREOVER, GREECE IS A CLOSE FRIEND AND ALLY OF THE

UNITED STATES, WITH A LEGITIMATE AND UNDERSTANDABLE

CONCERN FOR DEVELOPMENTS IN THIS REGION, A POINT WE

EMPHASIZED IN OUR TALKS WITH GREEK OFFICIALS. WE

ENCOURAGE A MORE DIRECT AND INTENSIVE DIALOGUE BETWEEN

SKOPJE AND ATHENS, INCLUDING THROUGH CHANNELS OFFERED

BY THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITY, THAT WILL HOPEFULLY LEAD TO

A MUTUALLY AGREEABLE RESOLUTION OF DIFFERENCES BETWEEN

THEM IN THE VERY NEAR FUTURE. BOTH SIDES NEED TO TALK

AND COMPROMISE.

"THE INCLUSION OF MACEDONIA IN EUROPEAN AFFAIRS, AND

THE ACTIVE ENGAGEMENT OF MACEDONIA IN INTERNATIONAL

PROGRAMS PROMOTING DEMOCRATIC DEVELOPMENT COULD

IMPROVE THE SITUATION IN MACEDONIA SUBSTANTIALLY. WE

"IN THE END, INSTABILITY WILL REMAIN IN THE BALKANS AS

REPRESSION IN KOSOVO GO UNCHECKED AND THOSE

RESPONSIBLE FOR ATROCITIES GO UNPUNISHED. IN LIGHT OF

WHAT WE HAVE SEEN IN MACEDONIA AND CROATIA, UPON OUR

RETURN TO WASHINGTON, WE WILL CONTINUE WITH NEW VIGOR

OUR EFFORTS IN THE CONGRESS TO ENCOURAGE AN END TO

THESE TRAGIC DEVELOPMENTS AND TO EASE THE PLIGHT OF

THE HUNDREDS OF THOUSANDS OF REFUGEES AND DISPLACED

PERSONS LEFT IN THEIR WAKE.

"WE VIEW MORE DIRECT INTERVENTION, INCLUDING MILITARY

INTERVENTION, IN BOSNIA-HERZEGOVINA BY THE

INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY, INCLUDING THE UNITED STATES,

TO BE A POLITICAL NECESSITY AND MORAL OBLIGATION. WE

HAVE ALREADY PRESSED FOR GREATER ACTION, INCLUDING THE

LIMITED USE OF FORCE IF NECESSARY, TO BRING THIS WAR

TO AN END, AND WE WILL CONTINUE TO DO SO UPON OUR

RETURN. WE WILL ALSO PRESS FOR REAL ACTION TO BRING

THOSE PERSONALLY RESPONSIBLE FOR WAR CRIMES AND

GENOCIDE TO JUSTICE THROUGH AN INTERNATIONAL

TRIBUNAL. THEIR ACTIONS CANNOT GO UNPUNISHED. AS A

START, WE CALL FOR THE IMMEDIATE ARREST OF THE BOSNIAN

SERB LEADER RADOVAN KARADJIC AND HIS ASSOCIATES. WE

MUST FINALLY ADDRESS THE FUNDAMENTAL PROBLEM IN

BOSNIA-HERZEGOVINA THAT COULD SPREAD WAR THROUGHOUT
