SUBJECT: MFA BLAMES WEATHER FOR OCALAN\'S ISOLATION, CLAIMS

EFFORT TO RESOLVE PROBLEM.

REF: ADANA 44

Classified by Polcouns John Kunstadter; reasons 1.5 b and d.

1. (C) Further to Reftel, Poloff February 7 discussed the

lack of attorney/family access to jailed PKK/KADEK leader

Abdullah Ocalan with Kaan Esener, head of the MFA\'s

Department of Council of Europe Affairs. Esener claimed that

a series of mechanical failures and high seas have grounded

the ferry that shuttles visitors every Wednesday to Imrali

Island in the Sea of Marmara, where Ocalan is the only

prisoner. Ocalan last received visitors November 27. "None

of Ocalan\'s attorneys or relatives" had been denied

permission to visit. MFA is concerned about Ocalan\'s

isolation, and the strong reaction it has evoked in the

southeast. MFA officials have contacted their counterparts

in the Justice and Interior ministries and urged them to find

a way to arrange a visit as soon as possible.

2. (C) In response to a question, Esener said he is aware of

rumors that Ocalan is dead, but said Justice Ministry

officials have assured MFA that Ocalan is in good health.
