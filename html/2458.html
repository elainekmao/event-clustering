CLASSIFIED BY: AMBASSADOR NICHOLSON, REASONS 1.5 (B,D)

1. (C) FIDES, A VATICAN NEWS AGENCY, IS SET TO LAUNCH A

LENTEN APPEAL NEXT WEEK FOR THE FREEDOM OF MISSING OR

DETAINED UNDERGROUND CATHOLIC CLERGY. FATHER BERNARDO

CERVELLERA, FIDES DIRECTOR AND CHINA SPECIALIST, PROVIDED

AMBASSADOR AN ADVANCE LIST OF MORE THAN 30 NAMES IN ORDER TO

FOREWARN THE WHITE HOUSE BEFORE THE PRESIDENTIAL VISIT TO

CHINA. THE APPEAL WILL COMPRISE SOME 40 CLERGYMEN IN TOTAL,

WITH EACH INDIVIDUAL CLERIC LINKED TO A DAY OF LENT. (LENT

BEGINS FEBRUARY 13 AND LASTS 40 DAYS.) THE LIST (SEE PARA 5)

CONSISTS OF UNDERGROUND CATHOLIC BISHOPS AND PRIESTS WHO ARE

PERSECUTED BY PRC AUTHORITIES ON ACCOUNT OF THEIR REFUSAL TO

COLLABORATE WITH THE OFFICIAL "CATHOLIC PATRIOTIC

ASSOCIATION." THOUGH NOT SEEKING TO EMBARRASS THE PRESIDENT

DURING THE CHINA VISIT, FIDES WILL LIKELY CAPITALIZE ON MEDIA

ATTENTION IN ORDER TO HIGHLIGHT ISSUES OF RELIGIOUS FREEDOM

IN CHINA.

2. (C) FATHER CERVELLERA ASSERTED THAT HIS VATICAN SUPERIORS

HAVE APPROVED THIS LENTEN PRAYER INITIATIVE ON BEHALF OF THE

UNDERGROUND CHURCH. HE CLAIMED THE PERSONAL ENDORSEMENT OF

CARDINAL SEPE, THE HEAD OF DE PROPAGANDE FIDE, A.K.A. THE

CONGREGATION FOR THE EVANGELIZATION OF PEOPLES, WHICH ALSO

OVERSEES THE FIDES NEWS SERVICE. (NOTE: DE PROPAGANDE FIDE

IS THE VATICAN MINISTRY CHARGED WITH THE SPREAD OF

CATHOLICISM AND THE WELFARE OF FRONTIER CHURCH COMMUNITIES,

SUCH AS IN CHINA.) CERVELLERA ADDED HE IS ANGLING FOR A

PAPAL STATEMENT OF SUPPORT FOR THE FIDES INITIATIVE, PERHAPS

DURING THE POPE\'S WEEKLY STATEMENT AT THE SUNDAY ANGELUS

PRAYER. HOWEVER, NO PAPAL INTERVENTION IS PLANNED AT

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 VATICA 00579 01 OF 03 061054Z

PRESENT, HE ADMITTED.

3. (C) COMMENT: FATHER CERVELLERA IS SEEKING TO LINK THE

FIDES INITIATIVE TO THE FORTY DAYS OF LENT. HENCE, THE

EFFORT, STILL INCOMPLETE, TO MUSTER FORTY NAMES. HOWEVER,

THE PRESIDENT\'S VISIT, WHICH FALLS IN THE FIRST WEEK OF LENT,

IS CLEARLY IN THE FOREGROUND OF CERVELLERA\'S THINKING, EVERY

BIT AS MUCH AS THE LITURGICAL CALENDAR. CERVELLERA CONFIDED

TO THE AMBASSADOR THAT HE PERSONALLY GIVES GREAT CREDIT TO

THE INTERVENTION OF PRESIDENT BUSH FOR THE REDUCED SENTENCE

-- TWO YEARS RATHER THAN THE DEATH PENALTY -- METED OUT TO A

CHINESE CONVICTED OF ILLICIT TRAFFICKING (IN BIBLES).

MOREOVER, HE NOTED GRATEFULLY THE HUMAN RIGHTS APPEAL BY

AMBASSADOR RANDT IN BEIJING ON BEHALF OF BISHOP SU, AS WELL

AS OTHER NON-CATHOLIC RELIGIOUS LEADERS. CLEARLY, FATHER

CERVELLERA WOULD WELCOME A USG ACKNOWLEDGMENT OF THE PLIGHT

OF UNDERGROUND CATHOLIC CLERGY.

4. (U) BACKGROUND: FATHER CERVELLERA IS A COMMITTED VATICAN

ADVOCATE ON BEHALF OF THE UNDERGROUND CATHOLIC CHURCH. A

MISSIONARY PRIEST PREVIOUSLY POSTED TO HONG KONG, CERVELLERA

MAINTAINS EXTENSIVE CONTACTS WITHIN CHINA. UNDER HIS

DIRECTION, FIDES, A VATICAN INTERNET NEWS ORGANIZATION, HAS

BEEN PUSHING AN AGENDA OF RELIGIOUS FREEDOM, PARTICULARLY IN

REGARD TO CHINA.
