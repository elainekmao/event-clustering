--------------------------------------------- ----

UNHCR SUSPENDED CONVOY OPERATIONS AS A PRECAUTION

--------------------------------------------- ----

2. ON APR 24 - 25, UNHCR SUSPENDED NEARLY ALL

CONVOY OPERATIONS FROM BELGRADE, METKOVIC, AND

ZAGREB AS A SECURITY PRECAUTION. UNHCR FEARED THAT

ITS STAFF COULD BE THE TARGET OF SERB REPRISALS

FOLLOWING AN APR 24 ANNOUNCEMENT FROM THE UN WAR

CRIMES TRIBUNAL IN THE HAGUE, WHICH SINGLED OUT

SEVERAL HIGH LEVEL SERB OFFICIALS AS SUSPECTED WAR

CRIMINALS. THE BOSNIAN SERB LEADER, RADOVAN

KARADZIC, WAS ONE OF THE THREE BOSNIAN SERBS NAMED

BY JUDGE GOLDSTONE. ALTHOUGH THE INDIVIDUALS HAVE

NOT BEEN INDICTED, THE INVESTIGATION COULD LEAD TO

FORMAL CHARGES BY THE END OF THE YEAR.

--------------------------------------------- -------

HIGH-LEVEL WORKING GROUP IN ANCONA DISCUSSES AIRLIFT

--------------------------------------------- -------

UNCLASSIFIED

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 04 ZAGREB 01681 01 OF 03 280908Z

3. A HIGH-LEVEL WORKING GROUP MET IN ANCONA ON APR

21 TO DISCUSS THE RESUMPTION OF THE SARAJEVO

AIRLIFT. IRC/SARAJEVO LOGISTICS OFFICER ATTENDED

THE MEETING AND REPORTED THAT THE FOLLOWING THREE

CONDITIONS WOULD HAVE TO BE CONSIDERED, BUT NOT

NECESSARILY MET, BEFORE THE AIRLIFT COULD RESUME:

A) THE EXTENSION OF THE CEASEFIRE IN B-H; B) A

GUARANTEE THAT THE FLIGHTS WILL NOT BE INTERFERED

WITH IN ANY WAY INCLUDING IN-FLIGHT (TARGETING OF

THE AIRCRAFT), ON THE GROUND (OBSTRUCTION OF CARGO),

OR PASSENGER SELECTION/HARASSMENT; AND C) A RETURN

TO THE JULY 1992 SARAJEVO AIRPORT AGREEMENT.

4. NOTE: THE 1992 AIRPORT AGREEMENT STIPULATED

SEVERAL THINGS INCLUDING THE DEFINITION THAT AID IS

COMPRISED OF FOOD, MEDICAL SUPPLIES, AND

SHELTER/WINTER MATERIALS. THE AGREEMENT FURTHER

OUTLINES THAT ONLY CURSORY CHECKS SHOULD BE MADE ON

THE "BLUE ROUTES", AND NO CONFISCATING OF GOODS,

VEHICLES, OR PEOPLE. END NOTE.

5. DURING A RECENT TRIP TO CENTRAL B-H, DART

REPRESENTATIVES WERE TOLD BY IRC THAT THE PROPOSED

UNHCR FUNDING REQUEST FOR SCHOOL AND HEALTH CENTER

REHABILITATION WOULD NOT BE APPROVED. A FAX FROM

UNHCR/ZAGREB TO IRC INDICATED THAT "MRS.

OGATA (UN

HIGH COMMISSIONER FOR REFUGEES) HAS REDUCED THE

UNHCR BUDGET FOR BOSNIA, AND THUS THE IRC PROPOSAL

WOULD NOT BE FUNDED." WHEREAS UNHCR USED TO BE THE

BIGGEST CONTRIBUTOR TO IRC PROGRAMS, IRC/BOSNIA HEAD

OF OFFICE REPORTED THAT IRC NO LONGER HAS ANY AID

PROJECTS FUNDED BY UNHCR. FURTHERMORE, IRC\'S HEAD

OF OFFICE IS CONCERNED THAT THEIR OPERATIONS WILL BE

SERIOUSLY HAMPERED IF UNHCR CONTINUES TO RECALL THE

VEHICLES AND EQUIPMENT PROVIDED FOR IRC\'S USE.
