SUBJECT: VACCINE FOR CRITICAL COALITION PARTNERS: OFFER TO

JORDAN

REF: SECSTATE 41006

Classified By: PolCouns Doug Silliman for reasons 1.5 (B)(D)

1. (C) PolCouns February 16 made ref points on the USG\'s

offer to provide Jordan with a limited supply of smallpox and

anthrax vaccines to Ali al-Ayed, Personal Assistant to

Foreign Minister Marwan Muasher. Al-Ayed thanked that USG

for the offer, and said he would coordinate the GOJ\'s

response with the Jordanian Armed Forces (JAF), the Ministry

of Health, and Civil Defense personnel.

2. (C) DATT provided the same information separately to the

JAF on February 16. We will report when we have a

coordinated GOJ response.
