SUBJECT: GOI ASSISTANCE TO KOSOVO

REF: STATE 22128

MFA\'S DIRECTORATE FOR DEVELOPMENT AND COOPERATION REPORTS

THAT GOI BUDGETED ASSISTANCE FOR KOSOVO FOR CY 2002 IS 2.5

BILLION LIRE (APPX $1.2 MILLION). THIS MONEY IS EARMARKED

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 02 ROME 00816 151701Z

FOR TWO PROJECTS: 1.5 BILLION LIRE ($730,000) FOR THE

REFURBISHING OF THE HOSPITAL IN PEJA/PEC AND 1 BILLION LIRE

($470,000) FOR ASSISTANCE TO KOSOVO SCHOOLS. ADDITIONAL

POTENTIAL CONTRIBUTIONS FOR 2002 INCLUDE 4 BILLION LIRE ($1.8

MILLION) FOR THE TRAINING OF MEDICAL STAFF AT THE PEJA/PEC

HOSPITAL, 2.0 BILLION LIRE ($940,000) FOR THE FOSTERING OF

COMMUNICATION BETWEEN THE ALBANIAN AND ORTHODOX SERB

COMMUNITIES OF THE PEJA/PEC REGION, 1.8 BILLION LIRE

($845,000) FOR TRAINING OF MUNICIPAL EMPLOYEES, 2.0 BILLION

LIRE ($940,000) FOR EMPLOYMENT GENERATION, PARTICULARLY

EMPLOYMENT OF MINORITIES IN SMALL AND MEDIUM SIZE BUSINESSES,

1.8 BILLION LIRE FOR ($845,000) FOR RETRAINING OF KPC

MEMBERS, AND 2 BILLION LIRE ($940,000) FOR SOLID WASTE

TREATMENT. IF ALL THESE PROJECTS ARE REALIZED, ITALY\'S 2002

CONTRIBUTION WOULD TOTAL 16.1 BILLION LIRE ($7.7 MILLION).
