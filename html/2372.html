1. (C) SANT\'EGIDIO AFRICA EXPERT AND SPIRITUAL DIRECTOR

DON MATTEO ZUPPI TOLD CHARGE 01/08 THAT HE HAD SPOKEN AGAIN

WITH UNITA ROME REP COSTA ABOUT HIS PROPOSED THREE-MONTH

UNITA CEASEFIRE (REFTELS). ZUPPI SAID THAT COSTA HAD CALLED

HIM THE PREVIOUS EVENING TO REPORT SAVIMBI\'S RESPONSE THAT

UNITA WAS NOT CURRENTLY IN A POSITION TO PROPOSE A CEASEFIRE

BECAUSE IT WAS UNDER "FAIRLY SERIOUS" ATTACK FROM GOS FORCES.

ZUPPI WAS GRATIFIED THAT SAVIMBI HAD NOT REJECTED THE NOTION

OUT OF HAND, AND TOLD COSTA HE WAS AVAILABLE TO TALK TO

SAVIMBI AT HIS CONVENIENCE (NOTE: ZUPPI SAID HE AND SAVIMBI

HAVE EACH OTHER\'S CELL/SATELLITE PHONE NUMBERS, BUT ZUPPI

NEVER CALLS SAVIMBI DIRECTLY.)

2. (C) ZUPPI TOLD US HE SUGGESTED TO COSTA THAT SINCE A

CHRISTMAS CEASEFIRE WAS OBE, UNITA SHOULD CONSIDER PROPOSING

A CEASEFIRE TO COINCIDE WITH THE POPE\'S JANUARY 25, 2002, DAY

OF PRAYER FOR PEACE IN ASSISI. (NOTE: THE POPE HAS INVITED

ABOUT 300 INTERNATIONAL RELIGIOUS LEADERS TO THIS, THE SECOND

DAY OF ECUMENICAL PRAYER FOR PEACE SINCE 1986, IN RESPONSE TO

THE SEPTEMBER 11 ATTACKS AND THE THREAT TO ECUMENISM FROM A

PERCEIVED CLASH OF CIVILIZATIONS.) ZUPPI BELIEVES THAT THIS

MEETING WILL BE IMPORTANT ENOUGH TO GARNER SUBSTANTIAL

INTERNATIONAL PRESS ATTENTION AND THUS WOULD PROVIDE A GOOD

OPPORTUNITY TO UNITA TO OFFER THE GOS AND THE WORLD A STRONG

SIGNAL OF ITS SERIOUSNESS AT OPENING A SEARCH FOR PEACE.

ZUPPI ALSO CLARIFIED THAT IN HIS TALKS WITH SAVIMBI AND

COSTA, THEY HAD ACKNOWLEDGED THAT THE LUSAKA PROCESS WAS

MORIBUND BUT NOT YET DEAD AND THAT ANY UNITA/GOS TALKS WOULD

HAVE TO PICK UP WHERE THE LAST LUSAKA DISCUSSIONS LEFT OFF.
