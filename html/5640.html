SUBJECT: SWITZERLAND\'S PROPOSED CONFERENCE ON IRAQ: JORDAN

TO BE REPRESENTED BY MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOREIGN AFFAIRS

REF: STATE 35667

Classified By: A/DCM Doug Silliman. Reasons 1.5 (b,d).

1. (C) On February 9, A/PolCouns delivered reftel points on

Switzerland\'s proposed February 15-16 conference in Geneva to

coordinate assistance to a post-war Iraq to FM Muasher\'s

personal assistant Ali al-Ayed. Al-Ayed said that the GOJ

had already been informed of U.S. concerns and the reasons

behind our decision not to participate via its mission in

Geneva. He also confirmed that after internal discussion of

the matter, Jordan has decided it will be represented at the

conference by its Minister of State for Foreign Affairs,

Shaher Bak. He noted that that the Swiss conference falls on

the same dates as an Arab League Ministerial in Cairo and

that FM Muasher would be busy with the latter.
