1. (C) ON OCT 15 DCM BRIEFED PM BERLUSCONI DIPLOMATIC ADVISOR

CASTELLANETA ON USG CONCERNS REGARDING RUSSIAN POLICIES

TOWARD IRAQ AND IRAN, USING POINTS IN REF A, IN PREPARATION

FOR THE PM\'S OCT 16 MEETING WITH PRESIDENT PUTIN IN MOSCOW.

(NOTE: CASTELLANETA HAD REQUESTED THIS BRIEFING ON BEHALF OF

BERLUSCONI, SO THAT THE PM COULD BE AWARE OF ANY POINTS THE

USG RECOMMENDED HE EMPHASIZE TO PUTIN. END NOTE.)

CASTELLANETA, A FORMER GOI AMBASSADOR TO TEHRAN, WAS

ESPECIALLY INTERESTED IN USG POINTS REGARDING RUSSIAN

INVOLVEMENT IN IRANIAN COVERT AND OVERT NUCLEAR PROGRAMS AND

THE THREAT THAT POSED TO U.S.-RUSSIAN RELATIONS. REGARDING

IRAQ, HE COMMENTED THAT THE PM AGREED COMPLETELY WITH USG

POINTS ON THE NEED FOR A FIRM AND UNITED INTERNATIONAL EFFORT

TO PRESSURE SADDAM TO GIVE UP HIS WMD. CASTELLANETA SAID

THAT THE SOLE EVENT ON THE SCHEDULE FOR THE PM\'S ONE-DAY

MOSCOW TRIP WAS A LONG ONE-PLUS-ONE LUNCH WITH PUTIN.

CASTELLANETA EXPECTED HE WOULD BE THE PLUS-ONE ON THE ITALIAN

SIDE. CASTELLANETA ALSO MENTIONED THAT THERE WAS A SLIM

POSSIBILITY THAT THE PM\'S RETURN FLIGHT WOULD BE RE-ROUTED

THROUGH BERLIN THE EVENING OF OCT 16, IF/IF A MEETING COULD

BE SCHEDULED WITH THE GERMAN CHANCELLOR.

2. (C) ON OCT 10, DCM AND POL MC RAISED REF B POINTS ON IRAN

WITH PIETRO SEBASTIANI, DIPLOMATIC ADVISOR FOR PIERFERDINANDO

CASINI, PRESIDENT OF THE CHAMBER OF DEPUTIES. CASINI HAD

REQUESTED USG INPUT PRIOR TO HIS TRIP TO IRAN. THEY STRESSED

THAT, EVEN THOUGH CASINI WAS NOT LIKELY TO MEET IRANIAN

OFFICIALS DIRECTLY INVOLVED WITH WMD AND TERRORISM ISSUES, IT

WAS IMPORTANT TO CLARIFY PUBLICLY AND PRIVATELY HOW VITAL

SUCH ISSUES WERE IN BUILDING STRONG TIES WITH EUROPE. THE

IRANIAN PEOPLE AND GOVERNMENT SHOULD NOT EQUATE THE EU

ENGAGEMENT POLICY WITH TACIT ACCEPTANCE OF IRAN\'S WMD OR

TERRORISM RECORD.

3. (C) ON OCT 14, CASINI INFORMED THE AMBASSADOR THAT HE

WOULD GO TO IRAN OCT 31-NOV 4. AS THE DCM DID WITH

SEBASTIANI, THE AMBASSADOR OFFERED TO PROVIDE CASINI BOTH

PUBLIC DOCUMENTS AND INTELLIGENCE-DERIVED BRIEFINGS ON IRAN,

WHICH CASINI ACCEPTED. EMBASSY IS CURRENTLY WORKING TO

ARRANGE THOSE BRIEFINGS.
