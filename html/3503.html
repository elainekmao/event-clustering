SUBJECT: JORDAN ENTHUSIASTIC ABOUT WSSD WATER PARTNERSHIP

REF: SECSTATE 151258

(U) Jordan\'s Ministers of Planning and Water "enthusiastically

support" joining in a water partnership with the U.S. at the WSSD

(reftel). The Jordanian National Higher Committee on Sustainable

Development, headed by Minister of Planning Bassam Awadullah, is

anxious to get further details on such a partnership, prior to

the opening of WSSD.

(U) We understand that the Jordanian delegation will be led by

Minister of Planning Awadullah and will include Minister of Water

Hazim el-Nasser as well as the Director of the General

Corporation for Environmental Protection, the Secretary General

of the Ministry of Tourism, the Secretary General of the Ministry

of Social Affairs, the Secretary General of the Jordan Valley

Authority, the Deputy Mayor of the Greater Municipality of Amman,

and three NGO representatives. The King\'s attendance remains

unknown.
