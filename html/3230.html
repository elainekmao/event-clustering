SUBJECT: JORDAN MEDIA REACTION ON PRESIDENT\'S MIDDLE

EAST SPEECH.

SUMMARY

NEWS REPORTS IN JORDAN\'S DAILIES QUOTED THE PALESTINIAN

STATEMENTS THAT NO PALESTINIANS WOULD MEET SECRETARY

POWELL WITHOUT THE GREEN LIGHT TO DO SO FROM ARAFAT.

ASSABEEL, WEEKLY NEWSPAPER REFLECTING THE VIEWS OF THE

ISLAMIC ACTION FRONT, ADDRESSED RARE BUT POINTED

CRITICISM TO THE GOVERNMENT OF JORDAN FOR ITS:

"EXAGGERATED OFFICIAL WELCOME OF THE U.S. PRESIDENT\'S

WORST SPEECH SO FAR."

EDITORIAL COLUMNISTS CONTINUED THEIR CRITICISMS OF

PRESIDENT BUSH\'S SPEECH, ADDING CRITICISM OF SECRETARY

POWELL\'S CALL FOR A CHANGE OF LEADERSHIP.

NEWS REPORTS

JORDANIAN DAILIES ON JULY 2 HIGHLIGHTED THE PALESTINIAN

CHIEF NEGOTIATOR SA\'EB EREQAT\'S STATEMENT MADE IN CAIRO

THAT NO PALESTINIAN WOULD MEET SECRETARY POWELL UNLESS

ARAFAT GIVES HIM THE GREEN LIGHT TO DO SO. THE SAME

STORY, WHICH WAS VIRTUALLY IDENTICAL IN BOTH SEMI-

OFFICIAL INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-RAI AND CENTER-

LEFT, INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR, ALSO QUOTED

THE PALESTINIAN OFFICIAL STATEMENT REJECTING THE

"CAMPAIGN AGAINST PALESTINIAN INDEPENDENCE" AND THE

PALESTINIAN CALLS FOR CIVIL DISOBEDIENCE AGAINST THE

ISRAELI CIVIL AUTHORITY IF IT REPLACES THE PNA, AND THE

THREAT TO RETURN TO TOTAL MILITARY ACTION.

ARABIC-LANGUAGE WEEKLY ASSABEEL, GENERALLY REFLECTING

THE VIEWS OF THE ISLAMIC ACTION FRONT (THE POLITICAL

PARTY BASED ON THE MUSLIM BROTHERS MOVEMENT),

CRITICIZED THE JORDANIAN GOVERNMENT STRONGLY FOR ITS:

"EXAGGERATED OFFICIAL WELCOME OF THE U.S. PRESIDENT\'S

WORST SPEECH SO FAR." AMONG THE PEOPLE INVITED TO

COMMENT ON THE GOVERNMENT\'S REACTION WAS A FORMER

MINISTER OF CULTURE WHO EXPLAINED THE GOVERNMENT\'S

POSITION AS BEING INTENDED TO "WARD OFF HARM."

ASSABEEL WAS THE ONLY NEWSPAPER TO QUOTE THE STATEMENT

OF THE ISLAMIC ACTION FRONT THAT CRITICIZED THE

GOVERNMENT FOR "SUPPORTING A SPEECH THAT EQUATES

BETWEEN JIHAD AND TERRORISM" AND THE STATEMENT OF THE

MUSLIM BROTHERS, WHICH SAID THAT THE PRESIDENT\'S

SPEECH: "LEFT THE PALESTINIAN PEOPLE NO OPTION BUT

JIHAD."

EDITORIAL COMMENTARIES

-- BUSH\'S SPEECH AND IGNORING THE FACT OF THE

OCCUPATION

FORMER PARLIAMENTARY DEPUTY HAMADEH FARA\'NEH WROTE IN

SEMI-OFFICIAL INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-RAI ON JULY

2: "PRESIDENT GEORGE BUSH\'S SPEECH TURNED A BLIND EYE

TO THE ONE REASON THAT SPARKED OFF AND ACCUMULATED THE

CONTINUOUS AGGRESSION, CONFLICT, AND FRUSTRATION IN OUR

REGION, WHICH IS ISRAEL\'S OCCUPATION OF THE LAND THAT

BELONGS TO THE PALESTINIAN PEOPLE, WHO HAVE BEEN

MARGINALIZED FOR DECADES. PRESIDENT BUSH IS WORKING TO

ENSURE HIS PARTY\'S VICTORY IN THE NOVEMBER

CONGRESSIONAL ELECTIONS, AND TO SECURE A SECOND TERM

FOR HIMSELF . EVEN IF THIS IS AT THE EXPENSE OF THE

PALESTINIANS AND THEIR CONTINUED SUFFERING."

-- A COORDINATED AMERICAN ISRAELI AGGRESSION

THE LEADING COMMENTARY OF CENTER-LEFT, INFLUENTIAL

ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR READ ON JULY 2: "IT IS NO

LONGER A SECRET TO ANY OBSERVER OF THE MIDDLE EAST THAT

THE CONTINUOUS ISRAELI AGGRESSION AGAINST THE

PALESTINIAN PEOPLE, THE TRAVESTIES, DEATH, DESTRUCTION,

AND TERROR, ARE DONE WITH OPEN U.S. ENCOURAGEMENT AND

WITH UNRESERVED POLITICAL SUPPORT FROM THE

ADMINISTRATION THAT SEES THIS AGGRESSION AS SELF-

DEFENSE AND THAT SEES ARIEL SHARON AS A MAN OF PEACE.

WHAT IS HAPPENING IN THE PALESTINIAN TERRITORIES IS A

COORDINATED AMERICAN-ISRAELI AGGRESSION, BUT WE WARN

AGAINST GIVING FREE REIGN TO SHARON WHO IS EXPLOITING

UNLIMITED AMERICAN SUPPORT TO NURTURE EXTREMISM,

CONSOLIDATE THE OCCUPATION, DESTROY ANY CHANCE FOR

PEACE, AND PROBABLY DRAG THE REGION TO ANOTHER WAR."

-- A NEW DISASTER

DAILY COLUMNIST JAMIL AL-NIMRY WROTE IN INDEPENDENT,

MASS-APPEAL ARABIC DAILY AL-ARAB AL-YAWM ON JULY 2:

"COLIN POWELL DASHED THE HOPES OF ARAB OFFICIALS BY

ADOPTING THE WORST POSSIBLE INTERPRETATION OF PRESIDENT

BUSH\'S SPEECH. HE NOT ONLY RULED OUT THE PROSPECTS OF

AN INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE IN THE PRESENT, BUT ALSO

AFFIRMED THE PRECONDITION OF CHANGING THE PALESTINIAN

LEADERSHIP, WHICH WAS REEMPHASIZED CATEGORICALLY BY

RICE. SEEN FROM HERE, THERE IS NO AIM FOR THE AMERICAN

PERFORMANCE EXCEPT TO SUPPORT THE ISRAELI PROGRAM,

USING TALK ABOUT A PALESTINIAN STATE AS A WEAK AND

WORTHLESS COVER. IF THE OFFER WERE OF A PALESTINIAN

STATE AND AN INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE IN RETURN FOR

ARAFAT\'S RESIGNATION, WE WOULD BE THE FIRST TO CALL ON

HIM TO DO SO. BUT THIS IS NOT THE CASE. IT IS A REPEAT

OF THE SAGA OF IRAQ AND SADDAM, WHICH IS JUST A PRETEXT

FOR EXTENDING THE SIEGE INDEFINITELY." GNEHM
