SUBJECT: MEPI ROLLOUT PLAN

REFTEL: SECSTATE 162118

1. POST PLANS TO HIGHLIGHT SECRETARY POWELL\'S

ANNOUNCEMENT OF THE MEPI INITIATIVE WITH A SERIES OF

RELATED ACTIVITIES TARGETED FOR JORDAN\'S BUSINESS,

INTELLECTUAL AND NGO ACTIVISTS AS WELL AS LOCAL MEDIA.

2. IF POSSIBLE, WE WOULD BE READY TO CONDUCT A "PRE-

BRIEF" FOR A SELECTED GROUP OF SENIOR EDITORS

IMMEDIATELY PRIOR TO THE SPEECH, TO FAMILIARIZE THEM

WITH THE KEY CONCEPTS OF THE MEPI INITIATIVE. THIS

WOULD HELP TO ENSURE THAT THE CRITICAL INITIAL

REPORTING IS INFORMED AND BALANCED.

3. PA WILL MAKE SPEECH TEXTS AND RELATED MATERIALS

AVAILABLE VIA THE EMBASSY WEBSITE AND VIA DIRECT

DISTRIBUTION TO CONTACTS.

4. PA WILL REQUEST A DVC WITH A SENIOR US OFFICIAL TO

DISCUSS THE PRACTICAL POSSIBILITIES OF MEPI WITH

JORDANIAN NGOS, THINK TANKS, ACADEMICS, MEMBERS OF THE

BUSINESS COMMUNITY AND SELECTED JOURNALISTS.

5. SELECTED EMBASSY OFFICIALS WILL BE AVAILABLE FOR

"BACKGROUND BRIEFINGS" ON THE MEPI INITIATIVE.

6. PA WILL COORDINATE WITH AID AND OTHER APPROPRIATE

ORGANIZATIONS TO PREPARE FACT SHEETS ON CURRENT US

ASSISTANCE TO JORDAN AND HOW IT SUPPORTS MEPI\'S

OBJECTIVES, AND TO HIGHLIGHT EXISTING "SUCCESS STORIES"

RESULTING FROM ONGOING PARTNERSHIPS ON BUSINESS, MICRO-

FINANCE, E-GOVERNMENT AND INTERNET INITIATIVES PLUS

EDUCATIONAL EXCHANGES SUCH AS FULBRIGHT, AND RELATED

PROJECTS.

7. WE WILL SUGGEST ONE OR MORE PUBLIC EVENTS,

INCLUDING LOCAL PRESS, DURING DAS CHENEY\'S UPCOMING

VISIT TO AMMAN, IN WHICH MEPI CAN BE PUBLICIZED.
