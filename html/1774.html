SUBJECT: ECOWAS PARTICIPANTS IN ACRI EXERCISE IN DAKAR

Executive Secretary, General (ret.) Cheik Oumar Diarra and

Military Advisor, Colonel Dixon Dikio, will attend the

exercise. Mission requests forwarding of a fund cite as soon
