SUBJECT: TWELVE-YEAR SENTENCE FOR NGUYEN KHAC TOAN

REF: HANOI 3034

1. (U) "NHAN DAN" NEWSPAPER ON DECEMBER 21 REPORTED THE

PROSECUTION AND CONVICTION ON DECEMBER 20 UNDER ARTICLE 80,

SECTION C, CLAUSE 1 OF THE SRV CRIMINAL CODE OF NGUYEN KHAC

UNCLASSIFIED

TOAN ON THE CHARGE OF "SPYING." THE HANOI PEOPLE\'S COURT

HANDED DOWN A TWELVE-YEAR PRISON SENTENCE, TO BE FOLLOWED BY

THREE YEARS OF ADMINISTRATIVE DETENTION AT HOME.

2. (U) ACCORDING TO THE PRESS ACCOUNT, TOAN BETWEEN

NOVEMBER 2001 AND JANUARY 6, 2002 (NOTE: WHEN HE WAS

DETAINED IN HANOI END NOTE) HAD "RECEIVED DIRECTIONS AND

INFORMATION" FROM "REACTIONARY VIETNAMESE PEOPLE LIVING IN

FRANCE," HAD SPREAD "INCORRECT INFORMATION" ABOUT THE

"POLITICAL SITUATION OF OUR NATION," AND HAD "VILIFIED

CADRES OF THE PARTY AND STATE." THE "NHAN DAN" ACCOUNT

ADDED THAT TOAN HAD A PRIOR CONVICTION FOR "THEFT OF CIVIL

PROPERTY" AS WELL AS TWO OTHER SIMILAR "CASE HISTORIES."

3. (U) COMMENT: IT APPEARS THAT THE TRIAL ON DECEMBER 20

LASTED LESS THAN A DAY, AS EXPECTED (REFTEL), AND THAT THERE

WERE NO FOREIGN OBSERVERS PRESENT, DESPITE EMBASSY\'S

REPEATED EFFORTS. THE WILLINGNESS OF "NHAN DAN" TO

PUBLICIZE THE OUTCOME OF THE CASE IS SOMEWHAT UNUSUAL, BUT

ALSO SERVES TO ATTEMPT TO SCARE OFF OTHER WOULD-BE ACTIVISTS

WHO MAY HAVE LINKS TO FOREIGN INDIVIDUALS AND ORGANIZATIONS

WITH EVEN A REMOTELY ANTI-SRV AGENDA.

BURGHARDT
