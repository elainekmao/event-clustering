SUBJECT: HOA HAO MONK THREATENING IMMOLATION

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 02 HANOI 02924 01 OF 02 081012Z

1. (SBU) ON NOVEMBER 8, CONGEN POLOFF CONTACTED TWO

HOA HAO BUDDHIST ELDERS IN HCMC TO CONFIRM DETAILS

OF A NOVEMBER 6 PRESS RELEASE BY A U.S.-BASED HOA

HAO ORGANIZATION THAT A HOA HAO MONK WAS

THREATENING SELF-IMMOLATION. POLOFF CALLED TRAN

HUU DUYEN, A HOA HAO ELDER WHO WAS RELEASED FROM

PRISON UNDER A 1998 AMNESTY, BY TELEPHONE. POLOFF

ALSO CALLED LE QUANG LIEM (LQL), THE HOA HAO ELDER

CURRENTLY UNDER HOUSE ARREST FOR MEETING WITH A

GROUP IN HCMC WHOM POLICE BELIEVE WERE PREPARING

THEMSELVES FOR IMMOLATION. (NOTE: TWO DAYS AFTER

LQLQS ARREST, A FEMALE HOA HAO FOLLOWER AND MEMBER

OF LQLQS UNOFFICIAL HOA HAO CHURCH DID COMMIT SELF-

IMMOLATION. END NOTE.) BOTH DUYEN AND LQL

CONFIRMED THE STORY IN THE PRESS RELEASE AND ADDED

DETAILS OF THEIR OWN.

2. (SBU) VO THANH LIEM (ALSO KNOWN AS NAM LIEM) WHO

RESIDES IN CHO MOI DISTRICT OF THE MEKONG DELTA

PROVINCE OF AN GIANG, TURNED HIS HOME INTO A PAGODA

SEVERAL YEARS AGO, AND DOZENS OF HOA HAO FOLLOWERS

WORSHIP THERE ON A DAILY BASIS. ACCORDING TO DUYEN

AND LQL, ON NOVEMBER 1, NAM LIEM ORGANIZED A HOA

HAO PRAYER CEREMONY AT HIS HOME. WHAT HE DID

DIFFERENTLY ON THIS OCCASION THAN DURING PREVIOUS

PRAYER CEREMONIES WAS TO DISPLAY THE HOA HAO FLAG

AND HANG PICTURES OF THE HOA HAO FOUNDER IN HIS

PAGODA.

3. (SBU) ACCORDING TO LQL, POLICE CAME TO THE

PAGODA AND ORDERED NAM LIEM TO TAKE DOWN THE

OFFENDING FLAG AND PHOTOGRAPHS. WHEN NAM LIEM

REFUSED, THE POLICE ORDERED ALL THE PEOPLE WHO HAD

COME TO THE PAGODA TO WORSHIP TO RETURN TO THEIR

HOMES. ACCORDING TO DUYEN, POLICE RETURNED LATER

IN THE EVENING AND BROKE INTO THE PAGODA WITH THE

PURPOSE OF TAKING DOWN THE FLAG AND PHOTOS. WHILE

THE POLICE WERE INSIDE, NAM LIEM CLOSED THE DOOR

FROM THE OUTSIDE AND LOCKED THEM IN. AFTER SEVERAL

ATTEMPTS TO BREAK THE DOOR DOWN, THE POLICE HAD TO

SHOOT THE LOCK OFF TO GET OUT.

4. (SBU) FOR THE NEXT SEVERAL DAYS, THE POLICE

MAINTAINED A PRESENCE AT THE PAGODA AND DID NOT

ALLOW ANY HOA HAO FOLLOWERS TO ENTER. ACCORDING TO

LQL, NAM LIEM HAD SEVERAL HEATED EXCHANGES WITH THE

POLICE AND, ON NOVEMBER 6, CLIMBED UP A KNOTTY PINE

TREE BEHIND THE PAGODA CARRYING A 5-LITER CONTAINER

OF GASOLINE AND A KNIFE THREATENING TO KILL HIMSELF

IF THE POLICE DID NOT GO AWAY. BOTH LQL AND DUYEN

TOLD POLOFF THAT AS OF THE AFTERNOON OF NOVEMBER 8,

NAM LIEM WAS STILL UP IN THE TREE THREATENING TO

IMMOLATE HIMSELF. DUYEN ADDED THAT NAM LIEM HAD

CUT HIMSELF WITH THE KNIFE, BUT THAT THE INJURY WAS

MINOR. BOTH LQL AND DUYEN SAID DOZENS OF POLICE

ARE ON HAND - THEY ARE TRYING TO PERSUADE NAM LIEM

TO COME DOWN; THEY ARE ALSO KEEPING CURIOUS

ONLOOKERS AWAY FROM THE PAGODA AND TELLING THEM TO

GO HOME.

5. (U) FURTHER BACKGROUND: NAM LIEM WAS INVOLVED IN

A SIMILAR INCIDENT IN 1996. AT THAT TIME, HE HAD

REFUSED TO SIGN A DOCUMENT TO ALLOW LOCAL

AUTHORITIES TO TEAR DOWN A GATE HE HAD BUILT

WITHOUT PERMISSION AT THE ENTRANCEWAY LEADING FROM

THE VILLAGEQS MAIN ROAD TO HIS PAGODA. WHEN POLICE

CAME TO ARREST HIM, HE CLIMBED UP THE SAME TREE.

HE STAYED THERE FOR FOUR DAYS. WITNESSES SAID AT

THAT TIME THAT HUNDREDS OF POLICEMEN WERE NEEDED TO

KEEP SEVERAL THOUSAND SPECTATORS AWAY FROM THE

AREA. (NAM LIEMQS PAGODA IS IN A WOODED COMPOUND

LOCATED ABOUT 100 YARDS FROM THE MAIN ROAD SITUATED

IN THE MIDDLE OF SURROUNDING GREEN RICE PADDIES.)

6. (U) WHEN NAM LIEM CAME DOWN FROM THE TREE IN

1996, HE WAS TAKEN INTO CUSTODY, CHARGED WITH

INTERFERING WITH THE DUTIES OF A POLICE OFFICER,

AND SENTENCED TO TWO YEARS IN PRISON. HE IS FAMOUS

LOCALLY, KNOWN AS QTHE MAN WHO CLIMBED THE TREE.Q

7. (U) POLOFF SPENT A HALF DAY WITH NAM LIEM IN

JULY 1999, SEVERAL MONTHS AFTER HE HAD FINISHED HIS

PRISON SENTENCE. POLOFF FOUND HIM TO BE INTENSELY

SPIRITUAL AND EXTREMELY CHARISMATIC. AT ONE POINT

DURING THEIR MEETING, NAM LIEM SHOWED POLOFF A SCAR

ON THE SIDE OF HIS KNEE. HE SAID HE HAD BIT

HIMSELF THERE WHILE IN PRISON Q TO DEMONSTRATE TO

HIS JAILERS THAT HE WAS NOT AFRAID OF PAIN.

COMMENT

-------

8. (SBU) NAM LIEM IS NOT PART OF LQLQS RENEGADE HOA

HAO SECT. HE APPEARS TO HAVE HIS OWN FOLLOWING IN

AND AROUND AN GIANG PROVINCEQS CHO MOI DISTRICT.

POLOFF BELIEVES HE WOULD NOT HESITATE TO RESORT TO

IMMOLATION IF HE FELT IT NECESSARY. AS LONG AS

POLICE USE PERSUASION, RATHER THAN FORCE, HOWEVER,

THE THREATENED IMMOLATION MAY BE AVOIDED. THAT THE

STANDOFF IS NOW IN ITS THIRD DAY IS AN ENCOURAGING

SIGN THAT NEITHER SIDE IS RUSHING TO ACTION.
