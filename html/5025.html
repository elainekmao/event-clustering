SUBJECT: DEMARCHE TO JORDAN ON ABU ABBAS

REF: STATE 7054

Classified By:AMBASSADOR EDWARD W. GNEHM; REASONS: 1.5(B) AND (D)

1. (S) The Ambassador made the points in reftel to Foreign

Minister Muasher on the morning of January 9. Muasher said

he understood the importance of the issue and would talk

immediately to the Prime Minister and to GID Director Sa\'ad

Kheir.

2. (S/NF) Simultaneously, we raised the issue through

ORCA channels. GID Director Kheir told us he was aware of

reports about Abu Abbas\' travel and would inform us if

there was evidence that he might be transiting Amman.

Kheir said the Jordanians would "delay" Abu Abbas at the

airport if he arrived. In answer to our question, Kheir

said the GOJ had not heard from the Italian authorities.

GNEHM
