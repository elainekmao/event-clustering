DEMOCRACY IN CROATIA

-

1. CONFIDENTIAL - ENTIRE TEXT.

SUMMARY

-------

2. ALTHOUGH ARMED CONFLICT BETWEEN THE WARRING

FACTIONS IN CROATIA CONTINUES TO BE OF CONCERN TO

SERBS AND CROATS, BOTH SIDES (WITH THE ARRIVAL OF UN

PEACEKEEPING FORCES) HAVE NOW APPARENTLY BEGUN TO

FOCUS ON A NEW STRATEGY -- THE FORCED EVACUATION OF

RESIDENTS OF THE OPPOSITE NATIONALITY FROM AREAS

UNDER THE CONTROL OF THE OTHER. EACH SIDE HAS MADE

SPECIFIC AND DETAILED CHARGES AGAINST THE OTHER,

ACCUSING THE GUILTY PARTY OF THREATS, ABUSES,

EXPULSIONS AND KILLINGS. ALTHOUGH THE CROATIAN

CONFIDENTIAL

CONFIDENTIAL

GOVERNMENT CONTINUES TO VIEW HUMAN RIGHTS ISSUES AS

POLITICAL QUESTIONS, SOME OFFICIALS, INCLUDING TO

SOME DEGREE PRESIDENT TUDJMAN, ARE BEGINNING TO MAKE

A CONNECTION BETWEEN TOLERATION OF VIOLATIONS OF THE

HUMAN RIGHTS OF SERBS IN CROATIA WITH THE DECLINING

STATE OF DEMOCRACY IN THE REPUBLIC. THE PRIME

MINISTER OF GOVERNMENT HAS SPOKEN OUT STRONGLY

AGAINST SUCH ABUSES AND INSISTED THAT THE CROATIAN

GOVERNMENT MUST RENEW ITS EMPHASIS ON RESPECT FOR THE

RIGHTS OF CITIZENS, PUBLIC ORDER AND PEACE. TUDJMAN,

IN RECENT DAYS, HAS RECEIVED HUMAN RIGHTS DELEGATIONS

FROM HELSINKI WATCH AND THE EC, AND A DOMESTIC

AMNESTY COMMITTEE MADE UP OF SERBS AND OPPOSITION

POLITICAL FIGURES. IN SPITE OF HELSINKI WATCH\'S

STRONG CONDEMNATION OF HUMAN RIGHTS VIOLATIONS IN

CROATIA, TUDJMAN HELD A CORDIAL MEETING WITH THE

ORGANIZATION\'S REPRESENTATIVES AND PROVIDED A

WELL-CONSIDERED, DETAILED WRITTEN RESPONSE TO THEIR

FINDINGS. IN A COMPLETE CHANGE OF TONE, THE

GOVERNMENT\'S REPLY THANKED HELSINKI WATCH FOR ITS

INTEREST IN EVENTS IN CROATIA AND THE OBJECTIVE

EVALUATION OF ITS REPORT.

-

3. WESTERN PRESSURE ON THIS ISSUE HAS HAD SOME

SUCCESS, AT LEAST IN TERMS OF ATTITUDINAL SHIFTS BY

CROATIAN LEADERS. IT IS NOT CLEAR, HOWEVER, THAT

THIS HAS TRANSLATED INTO MORE FORCEFUL ACTION TO CURB

ABUSES. IF ANYTHING, HUMAN RIGHTS VIOLATIONS ON THE

CROAT SIDE MAY HAVE INCREASED IN RECENT WEEKS. WE

HAVE NOT SENSED A CONCURRENT UPSURGE IN

INVESTIGATIONS AND ARRESTS (THOUGH THE PICTURE ON

THE RULE OF LAW ON THE GROUND IS GREATLY RESTRICTED

4. WITH THE "HOT" WAR ABATING, DAMPENED BY THE

ARRIVAL OF UN PEACEKEEPING FORCES, THE FIGHT OVER

LAND (WITH ORDINARY CITIZENS AS THE COMBATANTS) IS

HEATING UP. EMBASSY BELGRADE AND CONGEN ZAGREB HAVE

REPORTED ON THE EFFORTS OF BOTH SIDES TO FORCE

CIVILIANS TO FLEE THEIR HOMES AND RESETTLE EITHER

CROATS OR SERBS IN THE ABANDONED PROPERTIES IN ORDER

TO REARRANGE THE ETHNIC MAKE-UP OF ENTIRE VILLAGES

AND TOWNS. EVEN IN THE LARGER CITIES, THERE HAVE

HAD LEFT, AT LEAST TEMPORARILY. HE POINTED TO THE

NON-PAYMENT OF PENSIONS TO RETIRED JNA OFFICERS

COMMUNITY) AS A SERIOUS MATTER WHICH COULD LEAD TO
