SUBJECT: Sauces/Fanta Beverage Base Classification;

Turkey Request Support for Candidacy at WCO

Ref: (A) State 111099 (B) State 111059

1. (U) Econoff presented U.S. positions on

classification of sauces and of Fanta beverage base

(reftels) to Ercan Saka, General Director, EU and

Foreign Relations Department, Turkish Customs

Undersecretariat, on May 6. Saka responded that Turkish

Customs would take these demarches into account in

formulating Turkey\'s position on these issues. However,

he stated that the EU Customs Union gave Turkey very

little flexibility to deviate from U.S. positions.

2. (SBU) Saka told Econoff that Turkey had put forward

the candidacy of Edip Aktas, currently a senior advisor

at the Customs U/S, for top position at the World

Customs Organization, competing against candidates from

France (seeking a second term) and the Netherlands.

Noting that he had raised Aktas\' candidacy with U.S.

Customs in the past, Saka asked for USG support in the

WCO election. Embassy will fax Aktas\' biographical

information to EB/TPP/MTA/MST.
