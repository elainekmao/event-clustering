SUBJECT: 2003-2004 SRI LANKA FULBRIGHT JUNIOR SCHOLAR

PROGRAM

1. THE UNITED STATES - SRI LANKA FULBRIGHT COMMISSION

(US-SLFC) RECEIVED A TOTAL OF 120 APPLICATIONS IN

RESPONSE TO ITS PUBLIC ANNOUNCEMENT OF THE SUBJECT

AWARDS PROGRAM. AFTER A PRELIMINARY INTERVIEW OF ALL

ELIGIBLE CANDIDATES BY THE COMMISSION\'S COMMITTEE ON

STUDY AND TRAINING, A SHORT LIST OF 19 CANDIDATES WAS

PRESENTED TO THE US-SLFC\'S BOARD OF DIRECTORS FOR A

FINAL INTERVIEW ON OCTOBER 16, 2002.

2. THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS FOUND THE 19 FINALISTS TO BE

AN EXCEPTIONAL GROUP OF CANDIDATES AND SELECTED SIX

INDIVIDUALS LISTED BELOW, FOR FURTHER PARTICIPATION IN

THE JUNIOR SCHOLAR FULBRIGHT AWARDS PROGRAM. AS PER

LAST YEAR, THE BOARD FOUND EACH OF THESE NOMINEES

WORTHY OF A FULBRIGHT AWARD. HOWEVER, DUE TO LIMITED

FUNDS THE BOARD SELECTED THREE PRINCIPAL AND THREE

ALTERNATE NOMINEES.

3. GIVEN BELOW IS A RANK-ORDER LISTING OF THE SLATE OF

SIX RECOMMENDED CANDIDATES FOR THE 2003-04 FULBRIGHT

JUNIOR SCHOLAR PROGRAM.
