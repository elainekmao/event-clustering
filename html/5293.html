SUMMARY

-------

1. (U) THE HOLY SEE, CATHOLIC DIOCESES WORLDWIDE,

AFFILIATED NGO\'S AND CATHOLIC RELIGIOUS ORDERS ARE

SIGNIFICANT, IF OFTEN OVERLOOKED, PLAYERS IN INTERNATIONAL

DEVELOPMENT AND HUMANITARIAN RELIEF EFFORTS. THE CATHOLIC

CHURCH IS IN EFFECT THE WORLD\'S LARGEST INTERNATIONAL

DEVELOPMENT AGENCY: SOME FOUR MILLION LAY PEOPLE, SISTERS,

MONKS, PRIESTS AND BISHOPS WORKING IN VARIOUS FORMS OF

DEVELOPMENT ACTIVITY MAKE A CONTRIBUTION WORTH BILLIONS OF

DOLLARS IN HOSPITALS, SCHOOLS, UNIVERSITIES, TECHNICAL,

AGRICULTURAL AND VOCATIONAL FACILITIES, AND SOCIAL

DEVELOPMENT CENTERS THROUGHOUT THE DEVELOPING WORLD. THIS

"COMMITMENT TO JUSTICE" IS DEEPLY ROOTED IN THE CHURCH\'S

SOCIAL DOCTRINE, WHICH HAS BEEN EXPANDED AND INTENSIFIED IN

THE POST-VATICAN II PERIOD WHEN THE CHURCH FIRST LINKED

WORLD PEACE TO HUMAN DEVELOPMENT. FOR THE HOLY SEE, THE

INHERENT AND INALIENABLE DIGNITY OF THE HUMAN PERSON FORMS

THE CORE OF ITS DEVELOPMENT POLICY; PEOPLE MATTER, AND THEIR

SPIRITUAL, SOCIAL AND PHYSICAL WELL-BEING IS AT THE HEART OF

THE HOLY SEE\'S APPROACH TO FOREIGN POLICY. END SUMMARY.

KEY PLAYERS IN DEVELOPMENT AND RELIEF

-------------------------------------

2. (U) THE HOLY SEE IS AN ACTIVE "HANDS ON" PLAYER IN

INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT WHOSE ROLE IS NOT LIMITED TO

TEACHING AND ENCOURAGING CHARITY. IN FACT, THE HOLY SEE

OPERATES A GLOBAL RELIEF AGENCY, IS PRESENT IN THE MOST

REMOTE AND BADLY AFFLICTED CRISES AREAS IN THE WORLD, AND

CARES FOR 25 PERCENT OF ALL HIV/AIDS VICTIMS WORLDWIDE. IN

SOME COUNTRIES, CATHOLIC AGENCIES ARE AMONG THE PRINCIPAL

DEVELOPMENT ORGANIZATIONS, PARTICULARLY IN THE FIELD OF

EDUCATION AND HEALTH CARE. THE HOLY SEE\'S DEVELOPMENT

AGENCIES AND AFFILIATED ORGANIZATIONS ARE OPEN TO PARTNERING

WITH THE USG, AND EMBASSY BELIEVES THERE IS SCOPE FOR CLOSER

COORDINATION IN INTERNATIONAL ORGANIZATIONS ON HUMAN AND

SOCIAL DEVELOPMENT PROJECTS. DESPITE SOME SKEPTICISM OF

COMMERCIAL MOTIVES BEHIND CERTAIN U.S. INITIATIVES AND

APPROACHES TO DEVELOPMENT, THE HOLY SEE\'S VIEWS ON

DEVELOPMENT HAVE MOVED CLOSER TO U.S. VIEWS IN RECENT TIMES

REGARDING THE NEED FOR RESPONSIBLE GOVERNMENT IN DEVELOPING

COUNTRIES (REFTEL). WE BELIEVE THE HOLY SEE WILL PROVE TO BE

AN INCREASINGLY USEFUL ALLY IN PROMOTING THESE VIEWS

WORLDWIDE.

3. (U) HOLY SEE CABINET-LEVEL DEVELOPMENT DEPARTMENTS

(DICASTRIES), WHICH FUNCTION AS THINK TANKS AND POLICY

MAKING AGENCIES, ARE INFLUENTIAL IN SHAPING A GLOBAL

CONSENSUS ON DEVELOPMENT STRATEGIES FOR A RANGE OF ISSUES

INCLUDING HUMAN RIGHTS, DEMOCRACY, MIGRATION, GLOBALIZATION,

AND HEALTH. THE CARITAS INTERNATIONALIS FEDERATION, WITH

MEMBERS IN 154 COUNTRIES, ENGAGES IN LOCAL AND INTERNATIONAL

PONTIFICAL COUNCIL FOR JUSTICE AND PEACE

----------------------------------------

5. (U) THE PONTIFICAL COUNCIL FOR JUSTICE AND PEACE (PCJP)

FOSTERS DEVELOPMENT OF NEEDY REGIONS AND PROMOTES SOCIAL

JUSTICE (SOCIAL EQUITY) INTERNATIONALLY. ITS BRIEF INCLUDES

LABOR ISSUES, INTERNATIONAL RELATIONS, DEVELOPMENT POLICY,

AND SOCIAL DEVELOPMENT. IT ALSO PROMOTES REFLECTION ON

ECONOMIC AND FINANCIAL SYSTEMS -- INCLUDING THE IMPACT OF

GLOBALIZATION -- PRODUCING IN 1999 FOR EXAMPLE, A REFLECTION

ON TRADE LIBERALIZATION IN PREPARATION FOR THE WTO

"MILLENNIUM ROUND." THE COUNCIL ALSO ADDRESSES

ENVIRONMENTAL AND NATURAL RESOURCES ISSUES. THE PCJP, OFTEN

ON BEHALF OF THE VATICAN FOREIGN MINISTRY, TREATS A BROAD

RANGE OF ISSUES RELATED TO WAR, DISARMAMENT AND THE ARMS

TRADE, INTERNATIONAL SECURITY, AND VIOLENCE IN ALL ITS

MANIFESTATIONS -- INCLUDING TERRORISM AND XENOPHOBIC

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 03 VATICA 00283 02 OF 05 241034Z

NATIONALISM. IT IS A FOCAL POINT FOR THE CHURCH\'S HUMAN

RIGHTS POLICY, DEVELOPING ITS PLACE IN CHURCH TEACHING,

ANALYZING ISSUES UNDER DISCUSSION IN INTERNATIONAL FORA,

AND PROVIDING ADVOCACY FOR VICTIMS OF HUMAN RIGHTS

VIOLATIONS.

6. (U) THE PCJP ALSO EXAMINES POLITICAL SYSTEMS AND THE

ROLE OF CATHOLICS IN POLITICS IN LIGHT OF THE CHURCH\'S

DESIRE TO WALK THE PHILOSOPHICAL TIGHTROPE BETWEEN RELIGIOUS

BELIEF AND SECULAR POLITICS. SINCE THE EARLY 1960\'S THE

HOLY SEE HAS JUDGED SOME POLITICAL SYSTEMS AS CLOSER TO

CHURCH THINKING THAN OTHERS: ATHEISTIC COMMUNISM HAS BEEN

FLATLY REJECTED, RIGHT-WING MILITARY REGIMES SEVERELY

CRITICIZED, AND LIBERAL CAPITALIST SYSTEMS TOLERATED BUT

MONITORED CLOSELY FOR INCLUSION OF SOCIAL WELFARE

PROVISIONS. A KEY ISSUE FOR THE COUNCIL IS HUMAN RIGHTS

WHICH IT TREATS IN THREE DISTINCT WAYS: DEVELOPING ITS PLACE

IN CHURCH TEACHING, ANALYSIS OF ISSUES UNDER DISCUSSION IN

INTERNATIONAL FORA, PROVIDING ADVOCACY FOR VICTIMS OF HUMAN

RIGHTS VIOLATIONS. PCJP OFFICIALS REPRESENT THE HOLY SEE AT

INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT, ENVIRONMENTAL OR DISARMAMENT

CONFERENCES. THE PROMOTION OF FORMER HOLY SEE PERMANENT

OBSERVER TO THE UN, ARCHBISHOP RENATO MARTINO, TO HEAD THE

PCJP AUGERS WELL FOR A MORE ACTIVIST PCJP AND FOR EVEN

CLOSER COOPERATION BETWEEN THE HOLY SEE AND THE USG ON MANY

DEVELOPMENT ISSUES. MARTINO LIVED IN THE U.S. FOR 18 YEARS

AND UNDERSTANDS US VALUES AND POLICY BETTER THAN ANY OTHER

NON-AMERICAN AT THE HOLY SEE. WHILE NOT ALWAYS IN AGREEMENT

WITH THE US, MARTINO HAS SPOKEN OUT IN FAVOR OF BIOTECH

FOODS TO AVERT STARVATION, ASSERTING THE ISSUE IS MORE

POLITICAL THAN SCIENTIFIC.

PREGNANT AND LACTATING MOTHERS AND CHILDREN UNDER FIVE.

COMMENT

-------

18. (SBU) THE HOLY SEE ITSELF APPEARS TO HAVE MADE A

PHILOSOPHICAL SHIFT IN RECENT YEARS ON ITS APPROACH TO

DEVELOPMENT. WHILE MANY POSITION PAPERS STILL CARRY MORE

THAN A HINT OF A DEVELOPMENT MESSAGE ROOTED IN THE HALCYON

DAYS OF LIBERATION THEOLOGY AND THE INTERNATIONAL

DEVELOPMENT THEORY OF THE LATE 1960S, RECENT STATEMENTS --

AT THE JOHANNESBURG SUMMIT ON SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT, FOR

EXAMPLE -- REFLECT A POSITION CLOSER TO THAT OF THE USG.

RECIPIENTS OF DEVELOPMENT AID ARE ASKED TO BECOME

PROTAGONISTS AND PARTNERS IN THEIR OWN DEVELOPMENT. CONCEPTS

SUCH AS TRANSPARENCY, GOOD GOVERNANCE, ACCOUNTABILITY AND

MARKET LIBERALIZATION NOW PROVIDE A COUNTERBALANCE TO

BLAMING "UNJUST STRUCTURES" OR "UNBRIDLED CAPITALISM" FOR

THE WORLD\'S WOES. THE POPE HAS REINFORCED THESE CONCEPTS IN

RECENT MESSAGES AND STATEMENTS, WHICH SUGGESTS THAT THE NEW

PERSPECTIVE WILL TRICKLE DOWN FROM THE DICASTERIES TO

DEVELOPMENT AGENCIES TO SHAPE THEIR POLICIES AND STRATEGIES.

GIVEN THE IMPORTANCE OF THE VATICAN\'S VOICE THROUGHOUT THE

DEVELOPING WORLD. EMBASSY BELIEVES USG DEVELOPMENT AGENCIES

SHOULD SEEK TO BROADEN CONTACTS WITH THE HOLY SEE TO BUILD

SUPPORT FOR OUR DEVELOPMENT POLICIES AND INITIATIVES AND TO

DEVELOP SYNERGIES WITH THE MANY VATICAN-RELATED DEVELOPMENT

AGENCIES. END COMMENT.
