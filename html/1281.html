SUMMARY

-------

1. (C) IN THEIR TWO-DAY RAID ON MDC OFFICES, POLICE AND

CIO AGENTS SEIZED PAPER FILES AND DISKETTES, AND

DOWNLOADED COMPUTER FILES. SEIZED DOCUMENTS INCLUDED THE

MDC\'S MEMBERSHIP LISTS, FUNDING SOURCES, STRATEGIES FOR

THE PARLIAMENTARY LEGAL CHALLENGES AND THE 2002

PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION, AND EVIDENCE GATHERED ON THE

MURDERS AND OTHER VIOLENCE COMMITTED DURING THE PRE-

ELECTION CAMPAIGN. ALL THE SEIZED INFORMATION WAS

RETURNED ON SEPTEMBER 16 ON THE INSTRUCTION OF A HIGH

COURT ORDER. MDC MP TENDAI BITI SPECULATED THAT THE

SERVING OF LEGAL PAPERS ON PRESIDENT MUGABE IN NEW YORK

HAD EMBARRASSED HIM AND LED HIM TO RETALIATE. HOME

AFFAIRS MINISTER JOHN NKOMO IS EXPECTED TO SPEAK TO

PARLIAMENT ABOUT THE RAIDS ON SEPTEMBER 19, AND BITI HAS

HEARD THAT NKOMO MAY LINK SENIOR MDC OFFICIALS TO ILLEGAL

ACTIVITIES, INCLUDING WEAPONS IMPORTATIONS.

2. (C) LAST WEEK\'S RAIDS WERE THE LATEST CHAPTER IN THE

GOZ\'S CAMPAIGN OF INTIMIDATION AGAINST THE MDC. IT IS

UNCLEAR HOW FAR THE GOVERNMENT PLANS TO GO, AND THE USG

NEEDS TO BE PREPARED WITH A STRONG RESPONSE IF IT BECOMES

CLEAR THAT THE GOZ IS TRYING TO ELIMINATE THE MDC AS A

VIABLE OPPOSITION PARTY. NKOMO\'S SEPTEMBER 19 SPEECH

SHOULD PROVIDE A GUIDE TO THE GOVERNMENT\'S INTENTIONS.

DOCUMENTS AND COMPUTER FILES SEIZED

-----------------------------------

3. (C) ACCORDING TO MDC ADVISOR CLIVE PUZEY, THE RAID ON

THE OFFICES OF THE OPPOSITION MDC CONCLUDED ON FRIDAY,

SEPTEMBER 15. HE STATED THAT POLICE, ACCOMPANIED BY

AGENTS OF THE CENTRAL INTELLIGENCE ORGANIZATION (CIO),

SEIZED ALL OF THE MDC\'S PAPER FILES AND COMPUTER

DISKETTES, AND DOWNLOADED ALL OF THE PARTY\'S COMPUTER

FILES. INCLUDED IN THE SEIZED INFORMATION, PUZEY SAID,

WERE THE PARTY\'S MEMBERSHIP LISTS, FINANCIAL RECORDS,

STRATEGIES FOR THE 2002 PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION AND FOR

THEIR LEGAL CHALLENGES RELATED TO THE JUNE PARLIAMENTARY

ELECTIONS, AND ALL THE EVIDENCE COLLECTED ON THE MURDERS

AND OTHER SIGNIFICANT VIOLENCE COMMITTED DURING THE PRE-

ELECTION PERIOD.

4. (C) LATE ON SEPTEMBER 15, POLOFF SPOKE WITH SEKAYI

HOLLAND, THE MDC\'S SECRETARY FOR INTERNATIONAL RELATIONS

AND UNSUCCESSFUL PARLIAMENTARY CANDIDATE. HOLLAND WAS

PRESENT AT THE MDC\'S EASTGATE OFFICE AND PROVIDED AN

ACCOUNT OF THE SECOND RAID BY POLICE AND CIO OFFICERS.

HOLLAND REPORTED THAT EARLY IN THE MORNING ON SEPTEMBER 15

THE POLICE ORDERED EVERYONE OUT OF THE BUILDING BUT, AS THE

MOST SENIOR MDC OFFICIAL PRESENT, HOLLAND REFUSED TO LEAVE.

SHE REPORTED THAT THE POLICE, ACCOMPANIED BY THE CIO,

PROCEEDED TO USE RAMMING RODS TO BREAK DOWN THE DOORS TO

SEVERAL OFFICES, INCLUDING THAT OF MDC PRESIDENT MORGAN

TSVANGIRAI. THE OFFICERS TOOK THE WHOLE DAY TO REMOVE

TRANSACTIONS AND THE MDC\'S FUNDING SOURCES. THEY SEIZED,

INTER ALIA, A LETTER FROM MORGAN TSVANGIRAI TO THE

INTERNATIONAL REPUBLICAN INSTITUTE (IRI) CONCERNING IRI\'S

WORKSHOPS FOR NEW MDC MPS, A LETTER THANKING A DUTCH NGO

FOR PROVIDING ASSISTANCE, AND LETTERS OF CONGRATULATIONS

TO THE MDC FROM A VARIETY OF ORGANIZATIONS SENT AFTER THE

PARLIAMENTARY ELECTIONS.

WHAT SPARKED IT

---------------

7. (C) WHEN ASKED WHY THE POLICE HAD SPENT TWO DAYS

REMOVING FILES, SEKAYI HOLLAND SAID ZANU-PF WAS ALARMED

BY A RESURGENCE OF ANTI-GOVERNMENT RALLIES IN

TRADITIONALLY STRONG ZANU-PF TERRITORY IN RURAL AREAS.

IN PARTICULAR, SHE SAID 15,000 PEOPLE HAD TURNED OUT FOR

AN MDC RALLY IN MBERENGWA EAST, MIDLANDS PROVINCE, THE

PREVIOUS WEEKEND TO CELEBRATE THE "REOPENING" OF THE

MBERENGWA DISTRICT TO MDC SUPPORTERS. MBERENGWA HAD BEEN

DECLARED A "NO-GO" AREA BY ZANU-PF SUPPORTERS DURING THE

PARLIAMENTARY ELECTION CAMPAIGN, AND HAD BEEN

PARTICULARLY HARD-HIT BY PRE-ELECTION VIOLENCE, ACCORDING

TO HOLLAND. (COMMENT: IT IS A STRETCH TO LINK THE RAID

OF THE MDC OFFICES IN HARARE TO AN MDC RALLY IN MIDLANDS.

HOWEVER, HOLLAND WAS CLEARLY FRIGHTENED BY ONGOING

THREATS AGAINST HER LIFE FROM ZANU-PF, AND THIS MAY HAVE

EXAGGERATED HER RESPONSE TO THE MDC RAIDS. END COMMENT.)

8 (C) TENDAI BITI SURMISED THAT THE RECENT SERVING OF

LEGAL PAPERS, INITIATED BY MDC MEMBERS, ON PRESIDENT

MUGABE IN NEW YORK LIKELY EMBARRASSED HIM AND LED HIM TO

RETALIATE. IN ADDITION, BITI CONTINUED, THE GOZ HAD

SERIOUSLY UNDERESTIMATED THE MDC\'S ABILITY TO PLAY AN

EFFECTIVE ROLE IN PARLIAMENT. FACED WITH A SINKING

ECONOMY AND REGULAR CRITICISMS FROM THE MDC, MUGABE IS

INCREASINGLY DESPERATE AND ONE OF HIS FEW OPTIONS IS TO

COME AFTER THE MDC WITH STRONG-ARM TACTICS. MUGABE, BITI

SAID, HAS ALWAYS USED VIOLENCE AND INTIMIDATION AS AN

EFFECTIVE TACTIC. NCUBE SURMISED THAT THE GOZ WAS FORCED

TO ACT OVERTLY AGAINST THE MDC BECAUSE THE CIO HAD BEEN

UNSUCCESSFUL AT INFILTRATING THE PARTY.

9. (C) BITI EXPRESSED CONCERN THAT THE GOZ MIGHT CLAMP

DOWN ON SENIOR MDC LEADERS IN THE COMING WEEK, AS THE GOZ

DID IN THE EARLY 1980\'S WHEN IT PROSECUTED SENIOR ZAPU

OFFICIALS ON TRUMPED-UP WEAPONS CHARGES. MINISTER OF

HOME AFFAIRS JOHN NKOMO IS SCHEDULED TO ADDRESS

PARLIAMENT ON SEPTEMBER 19 CONCERNING THE GRENADE ATTACK

AND THE POLICE/CIO RAIDS, AND THE MDC HAS HEARD HE MIGHT

LINK THE SENIOR-MOST MDC OFFICIALS TO ILLEGAL ACTIVITIES,

INCLUDING THE IMPORTATION OF WEAPONS. BITI INSISTED THAT

SUCH CHARGES ARE LUDICROUS, AS HE DOES NOT EVEN POSSESS A

GUN.

COMMENT

-------

11. (C) THE GOVERNMENT HAS HARASSED AND INTIMIDATED THE

MDC AT EVERY TURN SINCE ITS FOUNDING LAST YEAR. FACED

WITH THE MDC\'S RISING POPULARITY IN THE AFTERMATH OF

FEBRUARY\'S CONSTITUTIONAL REFERENDUM DEFEAT, AND THE

PROSPECT OF LOSING CONTROL OF PARLIAMENT, THE GOZ

UNLEASHED A CAMPAIGN OF TERROR FOCUSED ON MDC SUPPORTERS

AND CANDIDATES IN THE RUN UP TO THE JUNE PARLIAMENTARY

ELECTIONS. THE OCCUPATIONS BY SO-CALLED WAR VETERANS OF

COMMERCIAL FARMS, MOST OF WHOM ARE OWNED BY MDC

SUPPORTERS, WERE A KEY ELEMENT IN THE GOZ\'S ELECTION

STRATEGY.

MAY HAVE BEEN THE PRECIPITATING EVENT FOR THE GOVERNMENT

GAINING STRENGTH AND IS SUBJECTING THE GOZ TO CRITICISM,

CALLS FOR ACCOUNTABILITY, AND PUBLIC SCRUTINY TO WHICH IT

IS UNACCUSTOMED. THE OPPOSITION PARTY NOW POSES A VERY

REAL THREAT TO MUGABE\'S HOLD ON POWER (THE MDC WILL SWEEP

MUNICIPAL ELECTIONS EXPECTED LATER THIS YEAR AND LIKELY

WOULD WIN THE PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION IF IT WERE HELD TODAY

AND WERE HALF-WAY FAIR AND FREE), AND HE AND HIS CRONIES

WILL DO ALL THEY CAN TO MINIMIZE THAT THREAT. IN A SMALL

PRIVATE LUNCHEON ATTENDED BY FOUR GOVERNMENT MINISTERS

AND OTHER DIPLOMATS ON SEPTEMBER 15, THE AMBASSADOR ASKED

THE REASONS FOR THE GOZ ACTION AGAINST THE MDC. THE

MINISTERS WERE ALL SILENT, WITH NOT A SINGLE ONE

ATTEMPTING TO JUSTIFY THE RAID.

13. (C) IT IS APPARENT TO US NOW THAT THE GOZ (ZANU-PF)

NOT ONLY HAS NO INTENTION OF WORKING WITH THE MDC, BUT

WILL RESORT TO VIOLENCE AND INTIMIDATION TO TRY TO

RECOVER GROUND IT HAS LOST TO ITS UPSTART RIVAL. IT IS

UNCLEAR, THOUGH, HOW FAR THE GOVERNMENT INTENDS TO GO,

BUT THE POSSIBILITY THAT THE GOVERNMENT MAY TRY TO LINK

THE MDC LEADERSHIP TO ILLEGAL ACTIVITIES -- AND THUS

ARREST THEM -- IS EXTREMELY TROUBLING. AS BITI

MENTIONED, THE GOZ UNDERTOOK A SIMILAR APPROACH IN THE

1980\'S IN ELIMINATING ZAPU AS A VIABLE OPPOSITION PARTY.

WE HAVE KNOWN THE MDC LEADERS FOR QUITE SOME TIME AND

FIND IT EXTREMELY UNLIKELY THAT ANY OF THEM WOULD BE

INVOLVED IN IMPORTING WEAPONS.
