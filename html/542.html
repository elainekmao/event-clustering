SUMMARY

-------

1. THE PRESIDENTS OF CROATIA AND SERBIA WILL MEET

IN GENEVA TOMORROW (SEPTEMBER 30) TO DISCUSS

BILATERAL ISSUES. THE CROATIAN RED CROSS HAS

REGISTERED 26,110 MISSING PERSONS SINCE THE

BEGINNING OF THE WAR. ULTRA-RIGHTIST LEADER PARAGA

MAINTAINS THAT LARGE QUANTITIES OF ARMS FOUND DURING

A SEARCH OF HIS PARTY HEADQUARTERS SUNDAY WERE

B-H. LEADERS OF THE UNION OF SERBIAN PEOPLE

ANNOUNCED THAT SERBS IN SOME PARTS OF HERCEGOVINA

ARE COOPERATING WITH CROATS TO PREVENT CRIMES BY

SERBIAN EXTREMISTS. SERBIAN PLANES FIRED

GROUND-TO-AIR ROCKETS AND DROPPED CRATER BOMBS ON

SLAVONSKI BROD YESTERDAY. THE ORTHODOX BISHOP OF

BOSNIA TOLD INDEPENDENT BELGRADE RADIO STATION "B"

PLACE IN B-H. CROATIA\'S INTERIOR MINISTER HAS

DECLARED A WAR AGAINST CRIME IN THE UNSETTLED CITY

OF OSIJEK. END SUMMARY.

TUDJMAN-COSIC MEETING IN GENEVA

-------------------------------

2. THE ZAGREB MEDIA LED WITH THE REPORT THAT UN

PEACE ENVOY CYRUS VANCE HAD ANNOUNCED IN BELGRADE

YESTERDAY THAT A MEETING WILL TAKE PLACE IN GENEVA

SEPTEMBER 30 BETWEEN CROATIAN PRESIDENT TUDJMAN AND

"FRY" PRESIDENT COSIC.

BEGAN. IN SEPTEMBER ALONE, THE CROATIAN

ORGANIZATION SENT 97 REQUESTS TO THE INTERNATIONAL

RED CROSS ASKING THEM TO SEARCH FOR THE MISSING.

SERB-CROAT COOPERATION IN B-H

-----------------------------

5. THE EXECUTIVE COUNCIL OF THE UNION OF SERBIAN

PEOPLE ANNOUNCED THAT -- IN SOME PARTS OF

HERCEGOVINA, ESPECIALLY IN THE KONJIC DISTRICT --

ERBS AND CROATS ARE COOPERATING TO PREVENT CRIMES

BY SERB EXTREMISTS WHO ARE ACTIVE IN THIS AREA. THE

COUNCIL EXPRESSED GRATITUDE TO THE HVO (CROATIAN

DEFENSE COUNCIL), TO THE CATHOLIC CHURCH, AND TO THE

CROATIAN PEOPLE FOR THEIR PROTECTION OF INNOCENT

CITIZENS FROM ETHNIC CLEANSING. THE COUNCIL ALSO

NOTED THAT SOME SERBS HAVE VOLUNTARILY JOINED HVO

UNITS.

SLAVONSKI BROD ATTACKED BY AIR

------------------------------

6. ON SEPTEMBER 28, SERBIAN PLANES FIRED 12

AIR-TO-GROUND ROCKETS AND EIGHT CRATER BOMBS (250 KG

EACH) AT THE CENTER OF SLAVONSKI BROD. THE ATTACK

INJURED 11 PERSONS AND DAMAGED MANY RESIDENCES AND

INDUSTRIAL BUILDINGS. AFTER THE AIR ATTACK, SERBIAN

ARTILLERY SHELLED THE CITY AND SURROUNDING AREAS

FROM POSITIONS IN BOSNIA.
