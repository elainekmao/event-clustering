SUBJECT: GREECE/TERRORISM: DUTCH PM KOK PRESSES SIMITIS AT

FEIRA

REF: A. THE HAGUE 1801

B. STATE 113631

Classified by DCM Reed Fendrick, Reasons 1.5 b/d

1.(C) Prime Minister Kok\'s Foreign Policy Advisor Wim Geerts

told DCM on June 21 that, per ref A request, Kok pulled aside

Greek PM Simitis during the Feira Summit to voice his "strong

concern" about the recent murder of British Brigadier

Saunders. According to Geerts, Kok told Simitis that the

Dutch took the terrorist threat "extremely seriously" and

that Kok would expect Simitis to deliver on his pledges to

combat terrorism in Greece. Geerts wanted DCM to know that

the general statement on terrorism included in paragraph 51

of the concluding document of the Feira Summit, while not

referring directly to the Saunders murder, was crafted

specifically in response to the incident.

2.(C) Comment: While not taking sole credit for the Feira

language (he noted that British PM Blair and others had also

weighed in with Simitis), Geerts wanted the U.S. to be aware

of the strong Dutch efforts on Greek terrorism.
