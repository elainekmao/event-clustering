SUBJECT: TFIZ01: ITALY TO DEPLOY FIELD HOSPITAL TO BAGHDAD

IN 10 TO 15 DAYS

REF: ROME 1646

CLASSIFIED BY: A/DCM TOM COUNTRYMAN, REASONS 1.5 B, D.

1. (U) THIS CABLE CONTAINS ACTION REQUESTS. PLEASE SEE

PARAGRAPH 4.

2. (C) LATE APRIL 17 ECONOFF RECEIVED A READOUT ON THE LATEST

MEETING OF THE GOI IRAQ TASK FORCE MEETING FROM ITS DEPUTY

COORDINATOR, LUIGI MACCOTTA. MACCOTTA SAID THAT THE TASK

FORCE DECIDED TO DEPLOY WITHIN 10 TO 15 DAYS A MODULAR,

100-BED FIELD HOSPITAL PROVIDED BY THE ITALIAN RED CROSS.

THE HOSPITAL WILL BE STAFFED BY APPROXIMATELY 72 PEOPLE AND

BE COMPRISED OF 27 TENT UNITS COVERING A SURFACE AREA OF

BETWEEN 7 AND 10,000 SQUARE METERS. THE GOI INTENDS TO

DEPLOY THE HOSPITAL TO THE AREA BETWEEN THE AL NOUMAN GENERAL

HOSPITAL AND THE KHADIMIA PEDIATRIC HOSPITAL (NEAR SADDAM

CITY). THE GOI INTENDS FOR THE HOSPITAL TO BE SENT BY

ITALIAN MILITARY AIRCRAFT DIRECTLY TO BAGHDAD AND BE

PROTECTED BY A COMPLEMENT OF 50 TOSCANIA TROOPS (AIRBORNE

BRIGADE).

3. (C) ON APRIL 22, THE GOI WILL SEND A TECHNICAL TEAM TO

CONDUCT ASSESSMENTS IN IRAQ WITH THE PURPOSE OF PROPOSING

FUTURE PROJECTS FOR THE TASK FORCE TO UNDERTAKE. THE TEAM

WILL BE COMPRISED OF TWO MEDICAL EXPERTS, A CHEMICAL EXPERT,

AND THREE ENGINEERS SPECIALIZING, RESPECTIVELY, IN

INFRASTRUCTURE, ROADS, AND WATER/SANITATION. THIS TEAM WILL

BE SENT TO KUWAIT CITY ON ITS WAY TO IRAQ. COORDINATION WITH

ORHA AND DART IS BEING HANDLED BY ITALY\'S AMBASSADOR PARTI IN

KUWAIT.

4. (C) COMMENT AND ACTION REQUESTS. THE GOI TASK FORCE HAS

CLEARLY SEIZED ON OUR HOSPITAL REQUEST TO SWING INTO ACTION.

BY SENDING THEIR OWN EVALUATION TEAM, THE GOI IS SHOWING THAT

IT WILL NOT WAIT FOR INPUT ON NEEDS THAT IT CAN MOBILIZE TO

MEET. MACCOTTA SPECIFICALLY NOTED THAT THE 50 TROOPS BEING

SENT TO PROTECT THE HOSPITAL WOULD NEED TO COORDINATE WITH

AND BE COMPLEMENTED BY CENTCOM PATROLS IN THE ZONE WHERE THE

HOSPITAL WILL BE LOCATED. POST REQUESTS ANY GUIDANCE THAT

WOULD FACILITATE THIS COORDINATION OR OTHER LOGISTICAL

REQUIREMENTS FOR A SMOOTH DEPLOYMENT TO BAGHDAD WITHIN THE 10

DAY WINDOW NOTED. SPECIFICALLY, PLEASE CONFIRM THAT THE

GOI-PROPOSED LOCATION FOR THE FIELD HOSPITAL IS ACCEPTABLE TO

CENTCOM. END COMMENT AND ACTION REQUESTS.
