TAGS: PTER, KU

SUBJECT: TERRORISM: SEEKING HOST GOVERNMENT ASSISTANCE AGAINST

OSAMA BIN LADEN THREAT TO U.S. FORCES

REF: A) KUWAIT 3877, B) STATE 96941

(U) CLASSIFIED BY CHARGE. REASON 1.5 (B), (C) AND (D)

(S) CHARGE RAISED USG CONCERNS REGARDING OSAMA BIN LADEN (REF. B)

WITH INTERIOR UNDERSECRETARY LTG YOUSEF AL-KHORAFY, JUNE 7. CHARGE

NOTED THAT WE TAKE BIN LADEN\'S THREATS VERY SERIOUSLY. HE

EXPRESSED APPRECIATION FOR THE EXCELLENT FORCE PROTECTION SUPPORT

PROVIDED BY KUWAIT, PARTICULARLY THE ENHANCED SECURITY MEASURES AT

KUWAIT INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT (WHICH IS SEEING PARTICULARLY HEAVY

USE NOW AS MANY U.S. MILITARY PERSONNEL REDEPLOY). CHARGE NOTED

THAT MOST AIR TRANSPORTS CARRYING U.S. TROOPS USED FLIGHT PATHS TO

THE SOUTH OF THE AIRPORT -- HAD KUWAIT TAKEN ANY STEPS TO ADDRESS

A POSSIBLE TERRORIST THREAT IN THIS AREA? AL-KHORAFY STATED THAT

THE AREA SOUTH OF THE AIRPORT IS SECURE -- THE GOK IS FULLY AWARE

OF THE POTENTIAL THREAT AND HAS TAKEN MEASURES TO ADDRESS IT. THE

CHARGE ALSO RAISED ROUTE SECURITY FOR U.S. FORCES; AL-KHORAFY

REPLIED THAT BOTH KUWAITI AND U.S. FORCE PROTECTION MEASURES HAD

BEEN ENHANCED TO ENSURE THAT THE ROADS USED BY U.S. FORCES TO AND

FROM THE AIRPORT WERE SECURE. CHARGE AGAIN EXPRESSED OUR

APPRECIATION FOR THIS COOPERATION.

JEFFREY
