SUBJECT: CUBAN DOCTORS RELEASED TO THE CUSTODY OF UNHCR

REF: HARARE 3563

CLASSIFIED BY DCM EARL M. IRVING FOR REASONS 1.5 B/D.

1. (C) ON JULY 6 UNHCR REGIONAL REPRESENTATIVE OLUSEYI

BAJULAIYE, SPEAKING FROM LUSAKA, INFORMED DCM THAT CUBAN

DOCTORS LEONEL CORDOVA RODRIGUEZ AND NORIS DEL CARMEN

PENA MARTINEZ WERE RELEASED TO THE CUSTODY OF UNHCR ON

JULY 5. BAJULAIYE SAID THAT ZIMBABWEAN GOVERNMENT

OFFICIALS DID SO QUIETLY AND WITHOUT EXPLANATION. THE

PAIR ARE SAFE AND COMFORTABLE IN A HOUSE IN HARARE AND

PLAN TO TRAVEL TO SWEDEN ON JULY 7, ACCORDING TO THE

REGIONAL UNHCR REPRESENTATIVE. THEY WILL DEPART HARARE

ABOARD A BRITISH AIRWAYS FLIGHT ACCOMPANIED BY THE LOCAL

UNHCR REPRESENTATIVE, JOHN ADU. BAJULAIYE ASKED THAT WE

TREAT THIS INFORMATION AS CLOSE-HOLD UNTIL THE COUPLE

LEAVES HARARE. (NOTE: IRINA SCHOULGIN, FIRST SECRETARY

OF THE SWEDISH EMBASSY IN HARARE, CONFIRMED TO DCM ON

JULY 6 THAT HER GOVERNMENT IS EXPECTING THE COUPLE\'S

ARRIVAL AND ALREADY HAS PLACED SWEDISH VISAS IN THEIR

PASSPORTS. END NOTE.)

2. (SBU) ALSO ON JULY 6 WE RECEIVED VIA FAX FROM THE

OFFICE OF CONGRESSWOMAN ILEANA ROS-LEHTINEN (R-FLORIDA)

A COPY OF A PERSONAL LETTER TO DR. NORIS PENA FROM HER

RELATIVES IN MIAMI, WITH A REQUEST TO ASSIST IN

DELIVERY. WE FORWARDED A COPY OF THE LETTER TO UNHCR\'S

JOHN ADU THE SAME DAY.
