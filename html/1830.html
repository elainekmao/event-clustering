2. (C) SANT\'EGIDIO COMMUNITY SPIRITUAL DIRECTOR AND

SOMETIME AFRICAN-CONFLICT-MEDIATOR DON MATTEO ZUPPI ASKED

CHARGE AND A/DCM TO COFFEE 7/30 TO SEEK USG VIEWS AND

SUPPORT FOR PROPOSAL TO REINVIGORATE PROCESS FOR ANGOLA

PEACE. DON MATTEO SAID HE HAD BEEN APPROACHED BY AN

UNNAMED "GOOD CONTACT" IN UNITA WHO HAD SHARED A PROPOSAL

FOR STOPPING HOSTILITIES AND INITIATING MEDIATION. DON

MATTEO SAID THE UNITA OFFICIAL PROPOSED THAT SANT\'EGIDIO

HOST A SMALL "SECRET" MEETING OF UNITA AND GOA

REPRESENTATIVES AT SANT\'EGIDIO HEADQUARTERS IN ROME. THE

PURPOSE OF THIS MEETING WOULD BE TO ESTABLISH THE FRAMEWORK

FOR A CEASEFIRE, AFTER WHICH UNITA WOULD BE WILLING TO

ENTER INTO A BROADER MEDIATION FOR DISARMAMENT AND

RECONCILIATION. DON MATTEO ASKED FOR USG VIEWS, BUT

PARTICULARLY WANTED THE USG TO GAIN GOA BLESSING FOR THIS

INITIAL MEETING IN ROME. HE SAID THAT, WITH PRESIDENT DOS

SANTOS\' AGREEMENT, THE FIRST MEETING COULD TAKE PLACE AS

EARLY AS NEXT WEEK.

3. (C) COMMENT: AS LONG-TIME AFRICA WATCHERS KNOW,

SANT\'EGIDIO HAS A PROVEN BUT FADING TRACK RECORD OF

SUCCESSFUL CONFLICT MEDIATION IN AFRICA, HAVING FACILITATED

THE END TO MOZAMBIQUE\'S CIVIL WAR IN 1990-92. EVER SINCE,

SANT\'EGIDIO HAS SOUGHT, BUT FAILED, TO DUPLICATE THAT

SUCCESS BY HELPING RESOLVE SOME OTHER INTER-COMMUNAL

CONFLICT. DON MATTEO HIMSELF REMAINS VERY ACTIVE IN

AFRICAN AFFAIRS, MOST RECENTLY HAVING BEEN PROMOTED BY US

SPECIAL ENVOY WOLPE FOR A ROLE IN DRC CONFLICT MEDIATION.

POST HAS FOUND SANT\'EGIDIO TO BE A USEFUL INTERLOCUTOR --

THEY FREQUENTLY TRAVEL TO PLACES AND TALK WITH PEOPLE THAT

THE USG HAS LIMITED ABILITY TO REACH.
