SUBJECT: NIGERIA: LETTER FROM PRESIDENT OBASANJO TO

PRESIDENT BUSH ON ABACHA FUNDS

Classified by CDA Andrews; Reasons 1.5 (b).

1. (C) On 10 September, Embassy received the following letter

from President Obasanjo to President Bush (dated 5

September). Included with the letter are 26 pages of

attachments making up the forensic report referred to in the

letter.

Begin text:

5 September 2001

Dear President,

Thank you for the letter in which you gave assurances to

assist Nigeria in her Abacha loot recovery efforts.

A copy of the Forensic Accountant\'s report from Geneva-based

lawyer, Hauchman Monfrini & Bottge, on the bank documents

sent by the US Government, following Nigeria\'s request for

mutual assistance of 4 November 1999 is attached herewith as

requested.

From the content of the report, further investigation is

required in the US, if proceeds of the Abacha loot stashed in

US banks or moved through US banks to other destinations are

to be traced and recovered.

While I appreciate the US Government efforts at obtaining the

bank documents under reference, I, in anticipation, also

thank you for the expected detailed investigation that will

subsequently follow with a view to tracing, freezing and

recovering the loot.

Your sincerely,

/sgd/

Olusegun Obasanjo

2. (SBU) The original letter, along with attachments, is

being sent to the NSC by registered pouch with number 2584626.
