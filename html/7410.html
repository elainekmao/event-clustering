SUBJECT: TFIZ01: JORDAN WILL PROTECT IRAQI DIPLOMATIC

PROPERTY

REF: SECSTATE 93783

Classified By: PolCouns Doug Silliman for reasons 1.5 (B)(D)

1. (S) PolCouns on April 13 delivered ref points on the

protection of Iraqi diplomatic property to Ali al-Ayed,

personal assistant to Foreign Minister Marwan Muasher.

Al-Ayed said Jordan had already taken steps to protect the

Iraqi Embassy in Jordan even though no threat had yet arisen.

GOJ officials, al-Ayed said, have also stated publicly that

Jordan would protect Iraqi facilities in Jordan. He noted

that, aside from the three Iraqi Intelligence Service

officers expelled from Jordan several weeks ago, all of the

Iraqi diplomats accredited to Jordan, including the

Ambassador, remain in Amman and continue to occupy the Iraqi

embassy compound.
