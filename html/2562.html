SUBJECT: ECONOMICS AND TRADE LEAD PLACEMENTS IN FIRST

TWO WEEKS OF FEBRUARY

1. PAS Abuja and Lagos had 35 placements/coverages

in Nigerian media the first two weeks of February,

continuing a recent trend toward more placements and

more diversification by subject matter. The war on

terror was not the leading category for a change.

Economics and trade took the honors, thanks to a

number of local activities which involved the

Ambassador and/or USAID. A complete list of

placements and coverages follows:
