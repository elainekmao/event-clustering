OSIJEK MAYOR KRAMARIC THREATENS RESIGNATION

-------------------------------------------

2. THE YOUNG AND DYNAMIC MAYOR OF THE EMBATTLED

CROATIAN TOWN OF OSIJEK, ZLATKO KRAMARIC, COMPLAINED

TO A PRESS CONFERENCE THAT THE LOCAL MILITARY POLICE

ARE FAILING TO CRACK DOWN ON CRIMINAL ELEMENTS WHO

HAVE FOUND THEIR WAY INTO THE ARMY. HE CITED A

RECENT CAR BOMBING WHICH KILLED A YOUNG CROAT MAN AS

THE MOST RECENT EXAMPLE OF A GROWING LAWLESS TREND IN

THE TOWN. HE SAID THAT IF THE MILITARY POLICE WILL

NOT PREVENT CRIMES CARRIED OUT BY ARMED RENEGADES

DRESSED IN UNIFORMS, HE WILL BE OBLIGED TO SUBMIT HIS

RESIGNATION. KRAMARIC HAD ASKED SEVERAL TIMES THAT

THE MILITARY POLICE AND MINISTRY OF THE INTERIOR, SO

FAR WITHOUT RESULT, GET RID OF THOSE CRIMINALS WHO

ARE BURNING HOUSES, SHOPS, AND CARS. ACCORDING TO

THE MAYOR, THE ARMED FORCES DID NOT DO ENOUGH TO

PREVENT THE RECRUITMENT OF PEOPLE WITH AN UNSAVORY

BACKGROUND, AND THESE PERSONS NOW TAKE ADVANTAGE OF

THEIR UNIFORMS AND WEAPONS TO CARRY OUT CRIMINAL

ACTS.

MESIC IN LJUBUSKI CALLS FOR CROAT/B-H COOPERATION

--------------------------------------------- ----

4. STIPE MESIC, PRESIDENT OF THE EXECUTIVE COUNCIL

OF THE RULING HDZ (CROATIAN DEMOCRATIC UNION), WHICH

INCLUDES "OFF-SHORE" BRANCHES, VISITED LJUBUSKI

(HERCEGOVINA) TO DISCUSS THE RELATIONSHIP OF HDZ TO

OTHER PARTIES IN B-H. MESIC CALLED ON THE HDZ TO

IMPROVE ITS EFFORTS TO WORK WITH OTHER POLITICAL

GROUPS IN THE COUNTRY. HE LAMENTED THE RECENT ARMED

CLASH BEWTEEN THE "HVO" (CROATIAN DEFENSE COUNCIL)

UNITS AND THE "TO" (B-H TERRITORIAL DEFENSE) UNITS

WHICH RESULTED IN TEN INJURED. MESIC ALSO STOPPED IN

MEDJUGORJE WHERE HE MET WITH AMERICAN AND FRENCH

PILGRIMS WHO WERE AT THE CATHOLIC SHRINE TO CELEBRATE

THE ELEVENTH ANNIVERSARY OF THE APPEARANCE OF THE

HOLY VIRGIN.

UPDATE ON DUBROVNIK

-------------------

6. ACCORDING TO THE ZAGREB PRESS, DURING THE WEEKEND

JNA/SERB SHELLING RESULTED IN SIGNIPICANT DAMAGE TO

HISTORICAL BUILDINGS SUCH AS THE CHURCHES OP ST.

JOSEPH AND ST. VLAHO, THE ORTHODOX CHURCH, AS WELL AS

THE FRANCISCAN AND DOMINICAN MONASTERIES, PALACE

SPONZA, THE MUSICAL SCHOOL, AND MANY HOUSES ON

STRADUN (THE OLDEST STREET IN DUBROVNIK). EXPLOSIVES

PELL ALSO ON OTHER PARTS OP DUBROVNIK, PLOCE AND

PILE. THE MAYOR\'S ASSISTANT IN DUBROVNIK ON JUNE 23,

TOLD US THAT JNA/SERB PORCES SHELLED THE OUTSKIRTS OF

DUBROVNIK, RIJEKA DUBROVACKA, AND MOKOSICA ON JUNE

22. ACCORDING TO HIS INFORMATION, IT WAS QUIET THIS

MORNING IN THE DUBROVNIK AREA.

SLOVENIAN RTV DIRECTOR RESIGNED

-------------------------------

RADIO AND TV, JANEZ JEROVSEK, SUBMITTED HIS

RESIGNATION. ACCORDING TO JEROVSEK, THE INFLUENCE OF

"THE LEADING PARTY" (PRIME MINISTER DRNOVSEK\'S

LIBERAL DEMOCRATS) ON TV AND RADIO IS SO STRONG THAT

IT RENDERS "IMPOSSIBLE" REGULAR WORK. HE ACCUSED

DRNOVSEK\'S LIBERAL DEMOCRATS OF USING THE MASS MEDIA

TO START THEIR RE-ELECTION CAMPAIGN. RTV COUNCIL
