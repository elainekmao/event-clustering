27 BODIES FOUND IN KRIZANCEVO MASS GRAVE

----------------------------------------

1. ON JANUARY 6 UNPROFOR TROOPS WERE PERMITTED TO

EXHUME A MASS GRAVE NEAR KRIZANCEVO (NEAR VITEZ, CENTRAL

B-H). BRITISH UNPROFOR ELEMENTS INVESTIGATING THE

SITUATION THERE ON DECEMBER 29 WERE FORCED TO WITHDRAW

AFTER ENCOUNTERING HEAVY FIRE FROM B-H ARMY POSITIONS.

2. ON JANUARY 8, AFTER UNPROFOR DID GET ACCESS TO THE

UNCLASSIFIED

UNCLASSIFIED

SCENE, UNPROFOR REPRESENTATIVE SIMON GHILL GAVE THE

CROATIAN DEFENSE COUNCIL (HVO) A LIST OF NAMES PROVIDED

BY THE B-H ARMY FOR THE 27 BODIES FOUND IN THE GRAVE.

ALL 27 WERE LISTED BY THE B-H ARMY AS HVO FIGHTERS OF

THE VITEZ BRIGADE. THE HVO ASSERTS, HOWEVER, THAT MOST

OF THE BODIES EXHUMED WERE DRESSED AS CIVILIANS. SO

FAR, 15 OF THE BODIES HAVE BEEN POSITIVELY IDENTIFIED.

3. ACCORDING TO UNPROFOR, ANOTHER COMMON GRAVE NEAR THE

KRIZANCEVO SITE CONTAINS AT LEAST 8 BODIES, ABOUT WHICH

THE B-H ARMY SO FAR HAS PROVIDED NO INFORMATION. IN

ADDITION, HVO SOURCES SAY, THE B-H ARMY HAS REFUSED TO

PERMIT RETRIEVAL OF SOME 15-20 BODIES LYING FOR THE LAST

18 DAYS NEAR THE CONFRONTATION LINE. THE B-H ARMY

CLAIMS 74 CROATS IN ALL WERE KILLED DURING THE

PRE-CHRISTMAS B-H ARMY OFFENSIVE NEAR KRIZANCEVO.

CROATIAN CITIZENS REPORTEDLY MOBILIZED TO FIGHT IN B-H

--------------------------------------------- ---------

4. THE CROATIAN HELSINKI COMMITTEE (HHO) ISSUED A

STATEMENT DECEMBER 29 CLAIMING THAT IN THE WEEKS

PRECEDING THAT DATE SEVERAL THOUSAND CROATIAN CITIZENS

BORN IN B-H HAD BEEN DRAFTED AND SENT TO THE B-H FRONT.

THE HHO STATEMENT SAID MANY OF THE DRAFTEES ARE

STUDENTS, RETIRED OR EVEN HANDICAPPED PERSONS. THE HHO

SAYS THEY WERE STATIONED IN CROATIAN MILITARY BARRACKS

AND GIVEN UNIFORMS BEARING NO INSIGNIA. THE COMMITTEE

ALSO CLAIMS TO BE IN POSSESSION OF DOCUMENTS SHOWING

THAT SOLDIERS SERVING IN THE CROATIAN ARMY, AFTER

REFUSING TO BE TRANSFERRED TO B-H, WERE SUBJECTED TO

5. MINISTRY OF DEFENSE SPOKESMAN GENERAL DRAGO KRPINA

RESPONDED TO THE HHO CHARGES ON DECEMBER 31, SAYING

"NONE OF THE ALLEGATIONS IN THE HHO STATEMENT ARE

CORRECT." HE EXPLAINED THAT UNDER CROATIAN ARMY\'S

"SEVERAL THOUSAND" CROATIAN CITIZENS WERE MOBILIZED.

SPOKESMAN SAID, ADDING THAT HE DID NOT KNOW WHETHER

NATIVE BOSNIAN CROATS WOULD VOLUNTEER TO SERVE IN BOSNIA

"TO DEFEND THE HEARTHS OF THEIR FOREFATHERS."

7. A POWERFUL EXPLOSION EARLY DECEMBER 24 MORNING

SHATTERED THE CHRISTMAS CEASEFIRE CALM IN KARLOVAC,

COMPLETELY DESTROYING ONE WING OF THE ORTHODOX EPARCHY

RESIDENCE, PROPERTY OF THE SERBIAN ORTHODOX CHURCH, IN

THE CENTER OF THE CITY. SOME TEN SHOPS IN THE VICINITY

ALSO WERE DAMAGED, AS WELL AS THE ROOF OF A NEARBY

FRANCISCAN MONASTERY. POLICE SAY A TIME BOMB APPARENTLY

WAS THE CAUSE. THE RESIDENCE HAD BEEN VACATED AND

LOCKED SINCE SEPTEMBER 1991, WHEN MOST ORTHODOX CLERGY

LEFT CROATIA. A COMPLETE INVESTIGATION HAS BEEN ORDERED

BY CROATIAN AUTHORITIES.

8. CROATIAN ROMAN CATHOLIC CARDINAL FRANJO KUHARIC

IMMEDIATELY ISSUED A STRONG CONDEMNATION. "A CRIME HAS

BEEN COMMITTED AGAINST THE LAW AND AGAINST PEACE," HE

SAID. "RESOLUTELY AND WITH BITTERNESS WE PROTEST

AGAINST THIS CRIME, WHICH HAS TRAGIC CONSEQUENCES NOT

ONLY FOR THE EPARCHY BUT ALSO FOR CROATIA. DURING

CHRISTMAS, A CEASEFIRE WAS AGREED UPON IN THIS SENSITIVE

TERRITORY. THE BOMBING HAS VIOLATED THIS CEASEFIRE. WE

REQUEST A THOROUGH INVESTIGATION. WE CONDEMN EACH ACT

OF HATRED, VIOLENCE, AND REVENGE, BECAUSE IT IS AN

ATTACK ON DIVINE AND HUMAN LAWS. WHOEVER HAS COMMITTED

THIS ACT IS AN ENEMY OF MAN AND THE NATION."

9. KARLOVAC DISTRICT GOVERNOR JOSIP JAKOVCIC ALSO

CONDEMNED THE BOMBING AS A POLITICALLY-MOTIVATED

CRIMINAL ACT. JAKOVCIC SAID THAT THE PERPETRATORS "DID

CROATIA NO FAVOR -- JUST THE OPPOSITE." HE CALLED THE

10. ACCORDING TO KARLOVAC MILITARY CORPS COMMANDER

GENERAL PAVAO MILJEVAC, THE BOMBING DID GREAT HARM TO
