PHILIP MADANAT IS THE EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR OF AMMANNET RADIO,

THE FIRST INTERNET RADIO IN THE ARAB WORLD AND THE ONLY ONE

IN JORDAN. AMMANNET WAS ESTABLISHED IN 1999 WITH THE HELP

OF UNESCO, BUT HAS SINCE GONE INDEPENDENT AND HAS

COOPERATIVE RELATIONS WITH A NUMBER OF LOCAL ORGANIZATIONS

IN THE PRIVATE AND PUBLIC SECTORS IN JORDAN. AMMANNET

COVERS VARIOUS POLITICAL, ECONOMIC AND SOCIAL ISSUES BOTH

LOCALLY AND REGIONALLY. THE CONCEPT OF INTERNET RADIO

REMAINS NOVEL IN JORDAN, BUT ITS POTENTIAL IN EXPANDING THE

LIMITS IMPOSED ON PRESS FREEDOMS IN THE COUNTRY IS GRADUALLY

BEING NOTICED AND RECOGNIZED. MADANAT, A BUSINESSMAN

RUNNING A MEDIA ORGANIZATION, EXPRESSED AN UNDERSTANDING FOR

THE POSSIBILITY OF STRETCHING THE LIMITS OF FREEDOM OF

EXPRESSION AND PRESS FREEDOMS IN TERMS OF ADDRESSING ISSUES

OF GOVERNMENT TRANSPARENCY AND ACCOUNTABILITY. THIS IS

PARTICULARLY INTERESTING BECAUSE IT IS HAPPENING IN A REGION

WHERE CRITICISM OF GOVERNMENTS AND REGIMES IS NOT WELL-

APPRECIATED. MADANAT IS SPECIFICALLY INTERESTED IN MAKING

AMMANNET A CONDUIT FOR RAISING THE CEILING OF ACCOUNTABILITY

AND TRANSPARENCY, SPECIALLY IN THE FIELDS OF GOVERNMENT AND

BUSINESS IN JORDAN. WE BELIEVE THAT BY TAKING PART IN THIS

PROGRAM, MADANAT WILL BE ABLE TO APPLY THE KNOWLEDGE OF

INTERNET AND ELECTRONIC GRASS-ROOTS ORGANIZING TECHNIQUES TO

WIDEN THE SCOPE AND IMPACT OF AMMANNET\'S WORK IN JORDAN.
