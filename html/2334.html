SUBJECT: DISCUSSING TERRORISM IN NORTHEASTERN VIETNAM

REF: A. HANOI 3282 B. HANOI 3201 C. HANOI 2885

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 02 HANOI 03308 210910Z

1. DURING OFFICIAL TRAVEL IN TWO NORTHEASTERN PROVINCES --

HAI DUONG AND QUANG NINH -- DECEMBER 18-19 (SEPTEL), POL/C

DISTRIBUTED PAS-PRODUCED "RESPONSE TO TERROR" AND "NETWORK

OF TERROR" (AS WELL AS OTHER PAS MATERIALS ABOUT THE U.S.),

EXPRESSED APPRECIATION FOR GVN COOPERATION AGAINST

TERRORISM, AND HIGHLIGHTED THE IMPORTANCE OF FURTHER

STRENGTHENING INTERNATIONAL EFFORTS IN THE ONGOING CAMPAIGN

AGAINST TERRORISM. VICE CHAIRMAN OF THE HAI DUONG

PROVINCIAL PEOPLE\'S COMMITTEE BUI VAN XUYEN EXPRESSED

APPRECIATION FOR THE MATERIALS AND REITERATED VIETNAM\'S

OPPOSITION TO TERRORISM AND SUPPORT FOR EFFORTS TO COMBAT

IT. HE STRESSED THAT "EVERYONE IN THE WORLD" MUST COOPERATE

AGAINST TERRORISM AND THAT, IF "TERRORIST GROUPS CONTINUE TO

OPERATE, THERE WILL NEVER BE PEACE." AT THE SAME TIME, HE

NOTED THE CONCERN OF THE VIETNAMESE PEOPLE OVER POTENTIAL

COLLATERAL DAMAGE OR INJURY TO INNOCENT CIVILIANS.

2. VICE CHAIRMAN OF THE QUANG NINH PROVINCIAL PEOPLE\'S

COMMITTEE NGUYEN VAN MIEN CONDEMNED INTERNATIONAL TERRORISM

IN STRONG TERMS, WHILE CALLING FOR THE INTERNATIONAL

COMMUNITY TO RESPECT NATIONAL SOVEREIGNTY. HE EXPRESSED

CONCERN ABOUT THE POTENTIAL FOR USING THE CAMPAIGN AGAINST

TERRORISM TO ACHIEVE OTHER GOALS NOT DIRECTLY RELATED TO

COUNTER-TERRORISM, CITING THE SUFFERING OF VIETNAMESE

CIVILIANS IN WARTIME. POL/C EMPHASIZED THE CONCERTED

EFFORTS OF THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY TO MINIMIZE SUCH

DAMAGE OR INJURIES, AS WELL AS THE ONGOING EFFORTS TO

PROVIDED MUCH-NEEDED HUMANITARIAN ASSISTANCE.

3. BUI HUU THIEM, VICE CHAIRMAN OF MONG CAI TOWN ALONG THE

CHINESE BORDER, VIGOROUSLY EXPRESSED STRONG SUPPORT FOR THE

EFFORTS OF THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY TO COMBAT TERRORISM.

HE CLAIMED THAT MONG CAI RESIDENTS WERE SHARPLY AWARE OF THE

DANGERS OF INTERNATIONAL TERRORISM AS A RESULT OF EXTENSIVE

MEDIA COVERAGE AND CLAIMED THAT THEY "HATE BIN LADEN" AND

THE OTHERS IN HIS ORGANIZATION. HE NOTED THAT VIETNAM

PROVIDED NO SAFEHAVEN FOR TERRORISM AND PLEDGED THAT

"EVERYONE IS SAFE" HERE IN VIETNAM FROM THIS THREAT.

4. COMMENT: OFFICIAL INTERLOCUTORS IN THESE PROVINCES

APPEARED SOMEWHAT UNCERTAIN ABOUT THE EXTENT OF VIETNAM\'S

COOPERATION WITH THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY IN THE CAMPAIGN

AGAINST TERRORISM BUT CLEAR IN GENERAL TERMS ABOUT THE NEED

FOR INTERNATIONAL COOPERATION. THE CONCERN FOR CIVILIANS IS

A RECURRENT REFRAIN IN OFFICIAL GVN STATEMENTS AND REFLECTS

VIETNAM\'S OWN SPECIAL HISTORY WITH BOMBING CAMPAIGNS. WHILE

EMBASSY HAS BEEN DISAPPOINTED WITH THE SOMETIMES UNBALANCED

COVERAGE BY THE VIETNAMESE MEDIA OF THE MILITARY CAMPAIGN

AGAINST AL-QAEDA AND THE TALIBAN, IT APPEARS THAT, EVEN IN

THE PROVINCES, VIETNAMESE ARE GETTING THE MESSAGE THAT THIS

CAMPAIGN IS JUST AND NECESSARY AND THAT THEY WELCOME

COOPERATION TOWARD THIS EFFORT. PAS-PRODUCED MATERIALS WILL

BE VALUABLE IN DEEPENING THEIR UNDERSTANDING OF THE CURRENT

SITUATION AND US-LED EFFORTS.
