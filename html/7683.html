SUBJECT: AMBASSADOR DELIVERS DEMARCHE ON FLEEING IRAQI REGIME

OFFICIALS

REF: STATE 98747

1. (U) Classified by Ambassador M. Wahba, for reasons 1.5 (b)

and (d).

under cover of a personal letter addressed to MFA A/USEC for

Political Affairs Abdullah Rashid Al-Nuaimi and passed him the

list of wanted Iraqi regime officials. Based on comments from

other senior UAE officials, we have been assured that Iraqis

require visas for entry to the UAE and the UAEG has absolutely

no/no desire to allow former Iraqi regime officials to transit,

enter or reside in the Emirates.

both Abu Dhabi and Dubai.
