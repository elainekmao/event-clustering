SUBJECT: A/S SHATTUCK AND CROATIAN HUMAN RIGHTS

ACTIVISTS ASSESS "THE HUMAN RIGHTS CATASTROPHE" IN THE

FORMER YUGOSLAVIA

------

SUMMARY

-------

1. IN AN EXTRAORDINARILY INFORMATIVE AND SOMETIMES

EMOTIONAL JANUARY 7 MEETING HOSTED BY AMBASSADOR

GALBRAITH, HA A/S SHATTUCK AND LEADING HUMAN RIGHTS

SPOKESMEN IN CROATIA DISCUSSED WHAT A/S SHATTUCK CALLED

"THE HUMAN RIGHTS CATASTROPHE" IN THE FORMER

YUGOSLAVIA. IN A BROAD-RANGING CONVERSATION LASTINC

UNCLASSIFIED

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 03 ZAGREB 00176 01 OF 04 131752Z

MORE THAN AN HOUR, THE MEETING PARTICIPANTS ADDRESSED

THE ISSUES OF MISSING PERSONS, WAR CRIMES AND THE

HORRIFIC USE OF GENOCIDE AND ETHNIC CLEANSING AS

EFFECTIVE TOOLS OF CONQUEST AT THE END OF THE 20TH

CENTURY. A/S SHATTUCK UNDERSCORED THE U.S. COMMITMENT

TO A WAR CRIMES TRIBUNAL TO BRING RESPONSIBLE

INDIVIDUALS TO JUSTICE AND TO PRESSURE ALL SIDES IN THE

CONFLICT TO RESPECT BASIC HUMAN RIGHTS. END SUMMARY.

------------

PARTICIPANTS

------------

2. CROATIAN PARTICIPANTS INCLUDED MS. LJUBICA BUTULA,

PRESIDENT OF THE SOCIETY OF FAMILIES OF MISSING AND

IMPRISONED DEFENDERS OF CROATIA; DR. ZVONIMIR

SEPAROVIC, PRESIDENT OF THE ASSOCIATION FOR VICTIMOLOGY

AND GENOCIDE; PROFESSOR DRAGO ROKSANDIC, A UNIVERSITY

PROFESSOR AND SPOKESMAN FOR SERB MINORITY RIGHTS IN

CROATIA; DR. SLAVKO GOLDSTEIN, PUBLISHER AND VETERAN

HUMAN RIGHTS ACTIVIST; AND DR. SLOBODAN LANG, SPECIAL

ADVISOR ON HUMANITARIAN ISSUES TO THE PRESIDENT OF

CROATIA. A/S SHATTUCK AND AMBASSADOR GALBRAITH WERE

THE AMERICAN INTERLOCUTORS, AND THE EMBASSY CONSUL WAS

NOTETAKER. THE MEETING\'S DISCUSSION IS REPORTED HERE

BY TOPICS FOR CLARITY\'S SAKE.

3. A/S SHATTUCK SAID HE HAD COME TO CROATIA WITH UN

AMBASSADOR ALBRIGHT TO FOCUS ATTENTION ON THE "HUMAN

RIGHTS CATASTROPHE" UNFOLDING IN THE REGION, AND TO

UNDERSCORE AMERICAN COMMITMENT TO ESTABLISHMENT OF A

VIGOROUS INTERNATIONAL WAR CRIME TRIBUNAL. A/S

SHATTUCK INDICATED THE USG IS OFFERING USD 28 MILLION

TO FUND THE TRIBUNAL BECAUSE THE U.S. BELIEVES THAT ALL

ATROCITIES COME DOWN ULTIMATELY TO INDIVIDUAL

RESPONSIBILITY, AND INDIVIDUALS MUST BE HELD TO

ACCOUNT. THE DISCUSSION WAS THEN TURNED OVER TO THE

CROATIAN GUESTS FOR THEIR COMMENTS ON A RANGE OF

ISSUES, INCLUDING MISSING PERSONS, WAR CRIMES, THE

MEDIA AS A WEAPON AND THE USES OF GENOCIDE AND ETHNIC

CLEANSING AS TOOLS OF CONQUEST.

THE MEDIA AS WEAPON

-------------------

UNCLASSIFIED

UNCLASSIFIED

9. PROFESSOR ROKSANDIC SPOKE AT LENGTH ON THE ROLE OF

THE MEDIA AS A WEAPON OF WAR AND THE SIGNAL INSTIGATION

TO THIS ETHNIC CONFLICT. THE USE OF MASS MEDIA AT THE

OUTSET AND THROUGHOUT THE WARS IN CROATIA AND BOSNIA-

HERZEGOVINA TO FAN ETHNIC SUSPICIONS AND ANXIETIES INTO

ACTS OF VIOLENCE IS, IN ITSELF, A WAR CRIME, ROKANDIC

ARGUED. A/S SHATTUCK TOOK THE POINT FURTHER, ASKING IF

ROKANDIC WAS SUGGESTING A DELIBERATE EXPLOITATION OF

MEDIA BY SERB LEADER MILOSEVIC IN PARTICULAR, AND THAT

SUCH TACTICS SHOULD COME WITHIN THE AUTHORITY OF THE

WAR CRIMES TRIBUNAL. ROKSANDIC SAID THAT IT SHOULD BE

INVESTIGATED.

10. LANG AGREED AND EXPANDED ON THIS THEME.

ASSESSMENTS THAT THE CONFLICT IN FORMER YUGOSLAVIA WAS

AN INEVITABILITY OWING TO CERTAIN TRAITS AMONG "THE

BALKAN PRIMITIVES" ARE "RACIST AND DECEPTIVE, " LANG

SAID. THIS IDEA -- CIRCULATED WIDELY IN WESTERN MEDIA

AND AMONG POLICY ANALYSTS -- CONTRIBUTED TO

INTERNATIONAL INACTION IN THE CRISIS AND HENCE WAS

POSSIBLY FUELED BY SERB PROPAGANDA EFFORTS.
