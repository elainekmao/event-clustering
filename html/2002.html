SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION TERROR ATTACKS IN THE U. S.;

HARARE

1. THE INDEPENDENT DAILY "THE DAILY NEWS" DEDICATED

ITS SEPTEMBER 20 EDITORIAL TO THE SEPTEMBER 11

TERRORIST ATTACKS IN THE UNITED STATES. UNDER

HEADLINE "TARGETING ONE GROUP OF PEOPLE CAN BE

DANGEROUS," THE PAPER COMMENTS:

2. "PRESIDENT GEORGE W. BUSH ACTED QUICKLY TO DOUSE

THE

FLAMES OF ANTI-ISLAMIC HATRED IN THE AFTERMATH OF

THE 11 SEPTEMBER MASSACRES IN NEW YORK AND

WASHINGTON. INADVERTENTLY, BOTH BUSH AND HIS

SECRETARY OF STATE, COLIN POWELL, HAD LIT THE FUSE

WITH THEIR SELECTION OF THE SAUDI-BORN BILLIONAIRE

DISSIDENT, OSAMA BIN LADEN, AS THEIR PRIME

SUSPECT. THE MAN IS AN ARAB AND A MUSLIM, ALBEIT

ONE WHO BELIEVES IN A VERSION OF THE KORAN WHICH

ESPOUSES VIOLENCE AS PART OF THE WORSHIP OF ALLAH.

BUT THE WAVE OF ATTACKS ON MUSLIMS OR PEOPLE OF

MIDDLE EASTERN APPEARANCE IN THE U. S. A. IS VERY

DISTURBING, NOT ONLY FOR THE BUSH ADMINISTRATION,

BUT FOR THE WORLD AS WELL. . . THE PROBLEM FOR

THE BUSH ADMINISTRATION IS CLEAR. THE AMERICAN

PEOPLE ARE STILL ITCHING TO WREAK REVENGE ON

WHOEVER ATTACKED THE WORLD TRADE CENTER AND THE

PENTAGON. UNDER THIS TREMENDOUS PUBLIC PRESSURE,

THE ADMINISTRATION FELT IT HAD TO COME UP WITH

SOMETHING OR SOMEONE - AND OSAMA BIN LADEN, HELD

RESPONSIBLE FOR THE BOMBINGS OF U. S. EMBASSIES IN

NAIROBI AND DAR ES SALAAM IN 1998 - WAS IT.

NOBODY CAN BLAME THE U. S. GOVERNMENT FOR WANTING

TO PUNISH WHOEVER ATTACKED THEIR CAPITAL AND THEIR

CENTER OF INTERNATIONAL BUSINESS. A MOST HEINOUS

CRIME HAS BEEN COMMITTED AND PERPETRATORS MUST BE

FOUND AND PUNISHED, ONE WAY OR ANOTHER. BUT IN

THE HUNT FOR THE TERRORISTS, THE U. S. GOVERNMENT

MUST BE CAREFUL THAT IT DOES NO SQUANDER THE

GOODWILL AND SYMPATHY OF A WORLD AS OUTRAGED BY

THE ATROCITY AS THEY ARE."
