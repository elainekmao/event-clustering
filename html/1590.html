1. (C) POLOFF DELIVERED REFTEL DEMARCHE TO MSGR. LEO

BOCCARDI, VATICAN MFA DESK OFFICER FOR HUMAN RIGHTS ON

MARCH 15. MSGR. BOCCARDI REVIEWED THE POINTS FOR THE 57TH

COMMISSION ON HUMAN RIGHTS ENTHUSIASTICALLY. HE CLAIMED

NOTABLE CONGRUENCE BETWEEN MANY VATICAN AND USG POSITIONS,

PARTICULARLY ON THE CERD (COMMITTEE ON THE ELIMINATION OF

ALL FORMS OF RACIAL DISCRIMINATION) PROCESS, UNDERLINING

THE RISK OF INFRINGEMENT ON THE JUDICIAL SOVEREIGNTY OF

SIGNATORIES. MSGR. BOCCARDI ENDORSED THE WORLD CONFERENCE

AGAINST RACISM (WCAR) TO BE HELD IN DURBAN, OUTLINING A

PROACTIVE VATICAN STRATEGY IN THE LEAD-UP TO THE

CONFERENCE.

2. (C) VATICAN STRATEGY IN THE RUN-UP TO WCAR WILL FEATURE

PROMINENT PAPAL CONDEMNATIONS OF RACISM. THE VATICAN WCAR

PROGRAM WILL SEEK TO COMBAT RACISM THROUGH A) EDUCATION,

THROUGH WORKSHOPS IN CATHOLIC SCHOOLS; B) INCREASED INTER-

RELIGIOUS DIALOGUE; AND C) AN AGGRESSIVE DEFENSE OF

MINORITY GROUPS, WITH THE VATICAN PLEDGING TO PRESSURE

EUROPEAN GOVERNMENTS TO SAFEGUARD THE RIGHTS OF THE ROMA

MINORITY IN PARTICULAR.

3. (C) MSGR. BOCCARDI CONCURRED WITH USG CONCERN THAT

REGIONAL BLOCS MAY FRUSTRATE CONSENSUS ON CRUCIAL POINTS.

HE REFERRED TO A RECENT PROCLAMATION IN TEHRAN BY MUSLIM

MIDDLE EASTERN GOVERNMENTS THAT PALESTINE WAS BY DEFINITION

A MUSLIM NATION, AN ASSERTION THE VATICAN DEEMED

UNACCEPTABLE. HE WARNED OF A POSSIBLE EFFORT AT THE WCAR

TO PORTRAY PALESTINIAN HARDSHIPS AS RACIST (ANTI-

ARABIC/SEMITIC) IN ORIGIN.

4. (C) MSGR. BOCCARDI WARNED OF POSSIBLE VATICAN CONFLICT

AT DURBAN IF AN EFFORT WERE MADE TO ENSHRINE SEXUAL

ORIENTATION AS GROUNDS FOR DEFINITION OF A RACIAL MINORITY.

MSGR. BOCCARDI DECLARED THE VATICAN COULD ENDORSE LANGUAGE

ABHORRING DISCRIMINATION ON THE BASIS OF QUOTE SEX OR

GENDER ENDQUOTE; HOWEVER, SEXUAL ORIENTATION AS SUCH WAS AN

UNACCEPTABLE CATEGORY FOR THE VATICAN. ACCORDING TO MSGR.

BOCCARDI, THIS DEBATE WAS ADVANCED BY THE BRAZILIAN

DELEGATION AT A PREVIOUS CONFERENCE IN SANTIAGO. MSGR.

BOCCARDI WAS CAREFUL NOT TO CRITICIZE USG POSITIONS.

FURTHERMORE, HE VIGOROUSLY CONDEMNED DISCRIMINATION AND

VIOLENCE TOWARD LESBIAN OR GAY PERSONS. MERANTE
