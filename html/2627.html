SUMMARY

-------

1. (C) REGARDLESS OF THE OUTCOME, THE PERIOD PRECEDING ZIMBABWE\'S

MARCH 9-10 PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION HAS BEEN DEEPLY FLAWED. THE

GOZ\'S OPAQUE AND UNEVEN IMPLEMENATION OF ELECTORAL PROCEDURES

GIVES MAXIMUM ADVANTAGE TO THE RULING ZANU-PF, BY MINIMIZING

VOTER TURNOUT IN AREAS OF OPPOSITION STRENGTH AND COMPLICATING

OBSERVER EFFORTS. THE MERGER OF GOZ AND ZANU-PF STRUCTURES AND

FUNCTIONS, FROM DIRECT LOGISTICAL SUPPORT, TO CLEARLY BIASED

MEDIA CAMPAIGN COVERAGE, TO A SUBVERTED LEGAL REGIME, HAS

PROVIDED ADDITIONAL UNFAIR ADVANTAGE TO THE RULING PARTY.

2. (C) SUMMARY CONTINUED: FINALLY, CONTINUING VIOLENCE AND

WIDESPREAD INTIMIDATION AND HARASSMENT OF OPPOSITION LEADERS AND

SUPPORTERS BY ZANU-PF SUPPORTERS, AS WELL AS POLICE AND SOLDIERS,

HAVE SIGNIFICANTLY HAMPERED THE OPPOSITION\'S CAMPAIGN EFFORTS.

THE MDC COULD STILL EMERGE VICTORIOUS DESPITE THE SIGNIFICANTLY

UNEVEN PLAYING FIELD, BUT OVERCOMING ALL OF THE OBSTACLES PLACED

IN ITS WAY WILL REQUIRE A VERY LARGE VOTER TURNOUT. END SUMMARY.

-----------------------------------

MINIMIZING OPPOSITION VOTER TURNOUT

-----------------------------------

3. (C) GOZ INTIMIDATION AND LOGISTICAL MANIPULATION WILL MINIMIZE

TURNOUT IN URBAN AREAS, THE STRONGHOLD OF THE MDC. THE GOZ HAS

ENGAGED IN A COMPREHENSIVE CAMPAIGN TO DISENFRANCHISE, EITHER

EXPLICITLY OR LOGISTICALLY, GREAT NUMBERS OF POTENTIAL OPPOSITION

SUPPORTERS.

4. (C) INTIMIDATION EFFORTS BY GOZ/ZANU-PF:

-- SPECIFIC EXAMPLES OF VIOLENCE COUPLED WITH THE PROPAGATION OF

THE IDEA THAT ZANU-PF CAN AND WILL TRACK DOWN THOSE WHO VOTE FOR

THE MDC, BY SUCH MEANS AS HIDDEN CAMERAS AND TRACKING THROUGH THE

BALLOT SERIAL NUMBER.

-- HARASSMENT AT ROADBLOCKS AND IN NEIGHBORHOODS, DESIGNED TO

SHOW THE EXTENDED REACH OF WAR VETERANS AND YOUTH MILITIA.

-- MILITIA SEIZURE OF NATIONAL IDENTIFICATION CARDS FROM

SUSPECTED MDC SYMPATHIZERS, DEPRIVING THEM OF SUFFRAGE.

-- PROPAGATION OF THE IDEA THAT ZIMBABWE WILL EXPERIENCE CIVIL

WAR SHOULD THE MDC WIN. THE RECENT ZDF LEADER\'S STATEMENT THAT

THE DEFENSE FORCES WOULD ONLY ACCEPT AN INDEPENDENCE STRUGGLE

VETERAN AS PRESIDENT WAS A KEY PART OF THE STRATEGY.

-- HARASSMENT OF PRIVATE MEDIA, INCLUDING THE FIREBOMBING OF THE

INDEPENDENT "DAILY NEWS" AND THE ARREST OF ITS EDITOR, DESIGNED

TO SQUELCH COVERAGE CRITICAL OF PRESIDENT MUGABE OR ZANU-PF.

-- THE MURDER OF 31 MDC SUPPORTERS SINCE JANUARY 1, OUTLINING

THE POTENTIAL THREAT TO MDC SUPPORTERS OF VOICING THAT SUPPORT.

-- IMMUNITY FROM ARREST AND PROSECUTION FOR THOSE WHO COMMIT THE

VIOLENCE FURTHER SUBSTANTIATES THAT WAR VETERANS AND YOUTH

MILITIA HAVE BEEN GIVEN FREE REIN.

5. (C) MANIPULATION OF VOTING LOGISTICS:

-- THE SUPREME COURT AFFIRMED LAST WEEK THAT ZIMBABWEANS CAN

ONLY VOTE IN THEIR HOME CONSTITUENCIES. CIVIL SOCIETY AND

OPPOSITION MONITORS AND OBSERVERS WILL BE FORCED TO CHOOSE

BETWEEN OBSERVING TO HELP MINIMIZE POSSIBLE VOTE FRAUD, AND

STAYING IN THEIR HOME DISTRICT TO VOTE THEMSELVES.

-- MANY OF THE 80,000 CIVIL SERVANTS ASSIGNED DUTIES AS POLLING

OFFICERS WILL BE ASSIGNED OUTSIDE THEIR CONSTITUENCIES AND THUS

UNABLE TO VOTE.

-- POLICE AND SOLDIERS ARE ALLOWED TO USE ABSENTEE BALLOTS, BUT

REPORTEDLY HAVE BEEN FORCED TO FILL OUT BALLOTS IN THE PRESENCE

OF THEIR COMMANDING OFFICERS, WHO ARE PREDOMINANTLY SYMPATHETIC

TO THE RULING PARTY.

-- THE NUMBER OF POLLING STATIONS IN URBAN AREAS (MDC

STRONGHOLDS) DECREASED BY APPROXIMATELY HALF IN COMPARISON TO THE

2000 ELECTION. LINES AT THESE STATIONS WILL BE CORRESPONDINGLY

LONGER, POSSIBLY DISENFRANCHISING MANY.

-- IN RURAL AREAS, WHERE ZANU-PF SUPPORT IS STRONGEST, THE

NUMBERS HAVE GREATLY INCREASED.

USING STATE STRUCTURES FOR POLITICAL GAIN

-----------------------------------------

9. (C) THE GOZ, THROUGH OVERT COOPERATION WITH ZANU-PF STRUCTURES

AND ABUSE OF AN AUTHORITARIAN LEGAL REGIME, SEEKS TO KEEP THE

OPPOSITION OFF-BALANCE IN ITS CAMPAIGN EFFORTS. THE GOZ WORKS ON

BEHALF OF ZANU-PF IN THREE WAYS. FIRST, THE GOZ ALLOWS ZANU-PF

TO USE GOZ INFRASTRUCTURE IN CONDUCTING ITS ACTIVITY. SECOND,

STATE-SPONSORED MEDIA HAS BEEN EXTREMELY ONE-SIDED IN ITS

COVERAGE OF THE ELECTION, INCLUDING PROVIDING LENGTHY AND LURID

COVERAGE OF AN ALLEGED ASSASSINATION PLOT. THIRD, THE GOZ HAS

CREATED AN AUTHORITARIAN LEGAL ENVIRONMENT, WHICH LIMITS MDC

REDRESS.

CONCLUSION

----------

13. (C) REGARDLESS OF THE FINAL OUTCOME OF THESE ELECTIONS, THE

PROCESS HAS BEEN TAINTED SUCH THAT A FREE AND FAIR POLL CANNOT BE

ACHIEVED. THE ZANU-PF GOALS IN THE PRE-ELECTION PERIOD WERE

FOCUSED ON SUPPRESSING MDC VOTER TURNOUT TO THE EXTENT THAT ZANU-

PF SUPPORTERS WOULD INDEED OUTNUMBER THEM ON THE POLLING DAYS.

DURING THE 2000 ELECTIONS, ZANU-PF MILITIA CONDUCTED A CAMPAIGN

OF INTIMIDATION DURING THE PRE-ELECTION PERIOD, FOLLOWED BY A

RELATIVELY PEACEFUL POLL. THE GOZ THEN TRUMPETED REPORTS FROM

SHORT-TERM ELECTION-DAY OBSERVERS THAT THE PROCESS WAS CLEAN.

WE BELIEVE THAT THE GOZ AND ZANU-PF ARE FOLLOWING THAT SAME

SCRIPT IN THIS ELECTION.

14. (C) SHOULD THE ZIMBABWE ELECTORATE TURN OUT AT AN EXTREMELY

HIGH LEVEL, THE MDC COULD STILL POSSIBLY WIN THE ELECTION.

RESULTS, PARTICULARLY FROM THE URBAN MDC STRONGHOLDS, SHOULD BE

AVAILABLE BY TUESDAY AFTERNOON, MARCH 12. DELAYS BEYOND THAT

TIME WOULD BE A WORRYING DEVELOPMENT THAT WE BELIEVE COULD

INDICATE A GOZ ATTEMPT TO MANIPULATE THE COUNT.

15. (C) POST BELIEVES THAT THE OPERATIONAL POINTS DESCRIBED ABOVE

ARE FAR TOO WIDESPREAD AND ORGANIZED TO BE SPONTANEOUS

EXPRESSIONS OF ZANU-PF SUPPORT, AS THE GOZ CLAIMS. THESE YOUTH

MILITIAS, SUPPORTED BY STATE SECURITY FORCES, HAVE INSTILLED A
