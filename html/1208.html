SUBJECT: MUGABE SCHEDULED TO GO TO CONGO WEEK OF JULY 10

REF: HARARE 3629

CLASSIFIED BY AMBASSADOR TOM MCDONALD.

REASONS: 1.5 (B) AND (D).

(C) AMBASSADOR TELEPHONED JUSTICE MINISTER EMMERSON MNANGAGWA

JULY 7 TO ASK FOR AN UPDATE ON PLANS FOR A TRIP BY PRESIDENT

MUGABE TO THE CONGO (REFTEL). MNANGAGWA CONFIRMED THAT THE

PRESIDENT WILL TRAVEL TO LUBUMBASHI TO MEET WITH PRESIDENT

KABILA DURING THE WEEK OF JULY 10, MOST LIKELY ON JULY 11. HE

PROBABLY WILL BE ACCOMPANIED BY DEFENSE MINISTER MAHACHI AND

POSSIBLY BY FOREIGN MINISTER MUDENGE OR ANOTHER MFA OFFICIAL.

MNANGAGWA HIMSELF WILL NOT ACCOMPANY THE PRESIDENT, AS HE WILL

BE ATTENDING HIS DAUGHTER\'S UNIVERSITY GRADUATION IN LONDON.

MNANGAGWA CHARACTERIZED THE PLANNED MEETING AS A BROAD-RANGING

DISCUSSION BETWEEN THE TWO LEADERS REGARDING CONGO MATTERS.

MCDONALD
