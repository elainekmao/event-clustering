YOUR THOUGHTS ON THIS ISSUE AT YOUR EARLIEST

CONVENIENCE. WE NEED TO COME TO SOME CONSENSUS

2. DONORS INVOLVED IN THE UNDP/TECHNICAL SUPPORT UNIT

(TSU) LAND REFORM SUPPORT ACTIVITY, INCLUDING THE

U.S., NORWAY, SWEDEN, AND THE NETHERLANDS, MET ON

TUESDAY, SEPTEMBER 5 AT UNDP WITH ACTING UNDP RESIDENT

REPRESENTATIVE (RESREP) CHINWE TO DISCUSS THE STATUS

AND RECOMMENDED DISPOSITION OF THIS ACTIVITY GIVEN THE

CONTINUING ILLEGAL FARM OCCUPATIONS AND ASSOCIATED

PROBLEMS. NOTE: ALTHOUGH NOT A CONTRIBUTING DONOR TO

THIS SPECIFIC ACTIVITY, GIVEN THEIR CLOSE ASSOCIATION

WITH THIS PROGRAM, A WORLD BANK REPRESENTATIVE ALSO

ATTENDED THIS MEETING. END NOTE. SEE REFTEL A FOR A

FULLER DESCRIPTION OF THIS MULTI-DONOR LAND REFORM

SUPPORT PROJECT ACTIVITY.

3. PER REFTEL B, THIS ACTIVITY WAS OFFICIALLY \'FROZEN\'

(I.E., NO NEW FUNDING COMMITMENTS) AFTER DONOR AND

STATE DEPARTMENT CONSULTATIONS IN MARCH 2000. A

LETTER WAS SENT TO THE GOZ OULY REFLECTING THIS

DECISION. NOTE: TO DATE, WE HAVE RECEIVED NO

RESPONSE FROM THE GOZ TO THIS \'FREEZE\'

LETTER/DECISION. END NOTE. AS A RESULT, THE PROJECT

HAS ONLY BEEN PAYING SALARIES OF RECRUITED TSU STAFF

SINCE THAT TIME, AS NO ADDITIONAL MATERIALS, EQUIPMENT

OR OPERATING COSTS ARE PERMITTED UNDER THE SUSPENSION

OR FREEZE. ACCORDINGLY, EXACERBATED BY THE MORE

RECENT DRAMATIC AOVERSE TURN OF IN-COUNTRY EVENTS, TSU

STAFF HAVE BEEN ABLE TO ACCOMPLISH LITTLE OF THEIR

EXPECTED SCOPE OF WORK SINCE MARCH 2000. RESREP

CHINWE ALSO INFORMED THE MEETING THAT THERE WAS NEED

FOR A TIMELY DECISION IN THIS MATTER AS, GIVEN ITS

CURRENT LOCATION WITHIN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT

AND CABINET, THE TSU IS COMING UNDER INCREASING

PRESSURE FROM THE GOZ TO USE THEIR EQUIPMENT AND STAFF

IN SUPPORT OF THE GOVERNMENT\'S \'FAST-TRACK\' PROGRAM

WHICH THE DONORS DO NOT SUPPORT IN ITS CURRENT FORM.

4. CONSIDERING THESE CIRCUMSTANCES, AND THE CURRENT

BLEAK PROSPECTS FOR ANY RAPID IMPROVEMENT IN THIS

SITUATION, ALL DONORS TO THIS PROGRAM RECOMMEND THAT

IT IS BEST TO \'CUT OUR LOSSES\' AT THIS TIME, AND

TERMINATE OUR SUPPORT FOR THIS ACTIVITY.

5. LESS DRASTIC OPTIONS DISCUSSED (E.G., RETAINING

SOME MODIFIED SEMBLANCE OF THIS ACTIVITY FOR SUPPORT

OF ON-GOING TECHNICAL ASSISTANCE ACTIVITIES WITH

REMAINING PROJECT FUNDS, SUCH AS MONITORING OF ON-

GOING ZIMBABWE LAND REFORM ACTIVITIES/EVENTS,

CONSTRUCTIVE TECHNICAL STUDIES, AND/OR FACILITATION OF

A DONOR-GOVERNMENT DIALOGUE ON THIS SUBJECT) WERE

REJECTED DUE TO THE FORMAL NATURE OF THE AGREEMENT FOR

THIS ACTIVITY BETWEEN THE UNDP AND THE GOZ REQUIRING

THAT ANY/ALL SUCH MODIFIED USES OF PROJECT RESOURCES

WOULD HAVE TO APPROVED BY THE GOZ. UNDER THESE

CIRCUMSTANCES, AND IN THE INTEREST OF MAINTAINING A

DESIRED DEGREE OF AUTONOMY AND FLEXIBILITY VIS-A-VIS

THE GOVERNMENT\'S EXPRESSED PLANS AND ACTIONS ON THIS

SUBJECT, ALL PRESENT AGREED THAT IT WAS BEST TO

TERMINATE THIS ACTIVITY ALTOGETHER. THEREAFTER, ONCE

8. COMMENT: FOR THE SEVERAL REASONS STATED ABOVE,

EMBASSY BELIEVES THIS ACTIVITY TERMINATION DECISION IS

THE CORRECT ONE AT THIS TIME. ALL OTHER DONORS

PARTICIPATING IN THIS ACTIVITY CLEARLY SUPPORT THE

NECESSITY FOR THIS ACTION AT THIS TIME. IN THE

PRESENT CIRCUMSTANCES, AT BEST, WE ARE SIMPLY WASTING

MONEY BY CONTINUING TO FUND THE TSU; AND, AT WORST, WE

COULD SEE OUR RESOURCES CO-OPTED TO SUPPORT THE

GOVERNMENT\'S CURRENT ILLEGAL \'FAST-TRACK\' LAND REFORM

ACTIVITIES. IN ADDITION, ALTHOUGH A FINAL ACCOUNTING

IS YET TO BE COMPLETED, IT IS ESTIMATED THAT, AT MOST,

WE HAVE US$100,000 TO 150,000 OF U.S.G. FUNDS

REMAINING UNDER THIS ACTIVITY AT THIS TIME.

THEREFORE, EVEN IF WE OPTED TO \'GO IT ALONE\', WE COULD

ACCOMPLISH LITTLE ON OUR OWN WITHOUT THE CONTINUING

PARTICIPATION OF THE OTHER THREE DONORS TO THIS

ACTIVITY. FINALLY, IT IS NOTEO THAT THERE IS

CONSIDERABLE MERIT TO MAINTAINING A UNITED OONOR FRONT

ON THIS ISSUE. HOWEVER, AS THE TERMINATION LETTER TO
