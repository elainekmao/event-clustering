SUBJECT: STATE DEPT. HUMAN RIGHTS REPORT 2003

Nepal\'s both English and Nepali major newspapers

published excerpts from the State Department\'s Human

Rights Report 2003 on front pages on April 3. The

headlines message were "serious rights violation in

Nepal." One of the leading newspaper said: "The United

States Human Rights Report".highlights the poor human

rights record in Nepal, accusing both the government

security forces and the Maoists of human rights

abuses."
