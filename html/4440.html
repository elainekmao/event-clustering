START MEMO NOMINATING-JUSTIFICATION:

Ms. Cooray is an influential player in the Finance

Ministry, responsible for Fiscal Policy and Economic

Policy formulation and implementation. She has been

with the Central Bank for over 25 years holding

various positions. Currently she is one of the most

senior officers of the Central Ban k on release to

the Finance Ministry. As Director General Fiscal

Policy and Economic Affairs of the Finance Ministry,

she will be responsible for formulating and

implementing the proposed Fiscal Responsibility Act

(FRA) which will be one of the important pieces of

legislation of the current UNF government. The FRA

will govern overall public finance of Sri Lanka with

the aim of keeping the budget deficit within

targets. Along with this act Parliamentary Powers

will be increased to examine government activities

and prevent wastage of public funds. This exercise

has the support of the IMF. Therefore, the program

on Privatization and the U.S. Economy will be very

useful and relevant to Mrs. Cooray and the GSL.

Mrs. Cooray is an important player in the Finance

Ministry and one of our key contacts there. She is

in her early 50s but has several more working years

ahead of her. She has the potential and recognition

to assume even more responsible positions at the

Central Bank or the Ministry of Finance. Her

participation in this program will help strengthen

our ties.
