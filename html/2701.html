SUBJECT: HELPFUL GVN RESPONSE TO MONTAGNARD RESETTLEMENT:

"THEY CAN CHOOSE RESETTLEMENT OR REPATRIATION"

1. (U) ACCORDING TO THE MFA SPOKESPERSON AND A VIETNAM

NEWS AGENCY PRESS ARTICLE, MONTAGNARD "MIGRANTS" NOW IN

CAMBODIA CAN "CHOOSE" RESETTLEMENT IN A THIRD COUNTRY OR

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 02 HANOI 00762 010847Z

REPATRIATION. IN HER MARCH 31 STATEMENT, THE MFA

SPOKESPERSON SAID THAT ANY "MIGRANTS" WHO WISH TO RESETTLE

IN A THIRD COUNTRY WOULD BE "ASSISTED WITH THE NECESSARY

FORMALITIES, FOLLOWING PREVIOUS PATTERNS OF COOPERATION

BETWEEN THE UNHCR, VIETNAM AND OTHER RELATED COUNTRIES."

THOSE WHO WANT TO REPATRIATE TO VIETNAM WILL BE ALLOWED TO

DO SO UNDER A "COOPERATIVE PROCEDURE" BETWEEN VIETNAM,

CAMBODIA, AND THE UNHCR.

2. (U) THE MFA STATEMENT AND THE PRESS REPORTS DID NOT

SPECIFICALLY MENTION US INVOLVEMENT. RATHER THE FOCUS WAS

ON THE GVN AND RCG AGREEING TO GIVE THE "ILLEGAL MIGRANTS" A

CHOICE AND THAT RESETTLEMENT OR REPATRIATION SHOULD BE DONE

ON A HUMANITARIAN BASIS AND NOT BE POLITICIZED "OR USED AS A

TOOL TO UNDERMINE THE INTERESTS AND SECURITY OF VIETNAM AND

CAMBODIA."

3. (U) THE STATEMENT NOTED THAT HARSH CONDITIONS IN THE

CAMPS RESULTED IN SOME PEOPLE REPATRIATING ON THEIR OWN.

THE STATEMENT ALSO REITERATED CRITICISM OF THE UNHCR FOR

WITHDRAWING "UNILATERALLY" FROM THE TRIPARTITE AGREEMENT.

-------

COMMENT

-------

4. (SBU) THE GVN REACTION IS HELPFUL AND SHOULD CONTRIBUTE

TO THE SUCCESSFUL RESOLUTION OF A PROBLEM THAT HAS DAMAGED

VIETNAM\'S RELATIONS WITH BOTH THE U.S. AND CAMBODIA. A FACE-

SAVING MOVE, IT APPEARS THAT THE GVN IS PUTTING THE BEST

LIGHT POSSIBLE ON A SITUATION WHERE ITS ABILITY TO AFFECT
