2. (C) SUMMARY. AN EXPECTED CPV PLENUM IN JULY

SHOULD MAKE FINAL DECISIONS ON THE NEW GVN LEADERSHIP

LINE-UP, AND MAY ALSO BLESS PUNISHMENTS AGAINST SEVERAL

SENIOR OFFICIALS BELIEVED TO BE GUILTY OF CORRUPTION OR

OTHER WRONG-DOING. THESE BEHIND-THE-SCENES

DELIBERATIONS REMAIN DIFFICULT TO VERIFY, AND WE EXPECT

VARIOUS RUMORS WILL CONTINUE TO EMERGE UNTIL THE

DETAILS HAVE BEEN PINNED DOWN IN PRIVATE. END SUMMARY.

3. (C) ACCORDING TO LE THO BINH, CHIEF OF THE

EDITORIAL BOARD OF "COUNTRYSIDE TODAY," THE SIXTH

PLENUM OF THE NINTH PARTY CONGRESS OF THE COMMUNIST

PARTY OF VIETNAM (CPV) HAS BEEN DELAYED SEVERAL TIMES

DUE TO ONGOING INTERNAL DEBATES ABOUT THE NEW GVN

LEADERSHIP STRUCTURE THAT WILL BE VOTED UPON BY THE

11TH NATIONAL ASSEMBLY (NA) IN JULY (REF A). THE NA

MUST BY LAW MEET BY JULY 19 -- WITHIN TWO MONTHS FROM

THE MAY ELECTIONS -- AND SEVERAL NA SOURCES HAVE

SEPARATELY PREDICTED THE OPENING DATE WILL BE JULY 14.

4. (C) BINH, PREVIOUSLY A HARD-CHARGING INVESTIGATIVE

REPORTER WITH TUOI TRE NEWSPAPER, TOLD POLOFF ON JUNE

18 THAT THE PLENUM HAD INITIALLY BEEN PROPOSED FOR JUNE

15 OR 17, BUT WILL NOW MORE PROBABLY BEGIN O/A JULY 5.

BINH\'S CPV SOURCES HAVE CLAIMED THAT THE NINE OUT OF

THE FIFTEEN POLIBURO MEMBERS HAVE AGREED THAT PRIME

MINISTER PHAN VAN KHAI SHOULD REMAIN IN OFFICE FOR NOW,

A RUMOR THAT IS ALSO WIDELY CIRCULATING ELSEWHERE.

PRESIDENT TRAN DUC LUONG, DANANG PARTY CHIEF PHAN DIEN,

MASS MOBILIZATION CHIEF TRUONG QUANG DUOC, AND ECONOMIC

COMMISSION CHIEF TRUONG TAN SANG REPORTEDLY HAD

NONETHELESS EXPRESSED THEIR SUPPORT WITHIN THE

POLITBURO FOR DEPUTY PRIME MINISTER NGUYEN TAN DUNG TO

MOVE INTO THE TOP SPOT THIS SUMMER.

5. (C) MINISTER OF TRADE VU KHOAN IS STILL SEEN BY

BINH\'S CONTACTS (AND OTHERS) TO BE THE HANDS-ON

FAVORITE TO MOVE UP TO A DPM POSITION, MOST PROBABLY

THE FOREIGN AFFAIRS/TRADE SLOT NOW FILLED BY DPM NGUYEN

MANH CAM. THERE HAS BEEN SOME DISCUSSION THAT THE

NUMBER OF DPM SLOTS COULD DECLINE FROM FOUR TO THREE

WITH THE EXPECTED RETIREMENTS OF DPM CAM AND DPM NGUYEN

CONG TAN. FORMER MINISTER OF TRADE AND CURRENT NGHE AN

PROVINCIAL PARTY BOSS TRUONG DINH TUYEN IS RUMORED TO

BE A FAVORITE TO REPLACE VU KHOAN AT MOT, DESPITE

WISHES OF SOME SENIOR MOT OFFICIALS FOR AN IN-HOUSE

CANDIDATE AND SOME DISSATISFACTION WITH TUYEN\'S EARLIER

PERFORMANCE AT MOT (INCLUDING OVER BILATERAL TRADE

AGREEMENT NEGOTIATIONS).

6. (C) THE WIDELY-RUMORED RETIREMENT OF MINISTER OF

PUBLIC SECURITY LT. GENERAL LE MINH HUONG IS REPORTEDLY

RUNNING INTO DIFFICULTIES IN LIGHT OF ALLEGATIONS

AGAINST CURRENT VICE MINISTERS (PARA 8). SOME OF

BINH\'S CONTACTS HAVE SPECULATED THAT EXTENSION OF MPS\'

HUONG MIGHT "FOR BALANCE" NECESSITATE A SIMILAR

EXTENSION OF MINISTER OF DEFENSE LT. GENERAL PHAM VAN

TRA, WHO WAS RE-ELECTED TO THE NA IN MAY. (MOD

OFFICIALS HAVE SEPARATELY INDICATED THAT GENERAL TRA

WILL STILL BE IN PLACE THIS FALL TO ACCEPT THE

SECRETARY OF DEFENSE\'S RECIPROCAL INVITATION TO VISIT.)

9. (C) COMMENT: MUCH IS AT STAKE IN THE LEADERSHIP

SWEEPSTAKES, NOT ONLY IN TERMS OF PERSONAL FIEFDOMS BUT

ALSO IN CONVINCING THE PUBLIC THAT THE CPV AND GVN TAKE

SERIOUSLY THESE ALLEGATIONS OF CORRUPTION AND

MISCONDUCT AND ARE PREPARED TO MOVE AGAINST EVEN SENIOR

RANKING OFFICIALS. THE EXTENSIVE MEDIA COVERAGE GIVEN

TO THE NAM CAM CASE CANNOT BE FAVORABLE FOR THE

PROMOTION ASPIRATIONS OF HCMC PARTY CHIEF NGUYEN MINH

TRIET, ANOTHER FRONT-RUNNER AS EVENTUAL SUCCESSOR TO PM

KHAI. THE MEDIA CAMPAIGN MAY INDEED BE AN INDIRECT

POLITICAL ATTACK ON TRIET HIMSELF. WE EXPECT THE

RUMOR MILL TO CONTINUE TO FLOURISH IN THE RUN-UP TO THE

PARTY PLENUM AND NA OPENING SESSION. AT THE SAME TIME,

WE PREDICT A PUBLIC SHOW OF UNITY WITHIN THE CPV AND NA

ONCE THESE PERSONNEL AND PUNISHMENT DECISIONS ALIKE

HAVE BEEN MADE IN PRIVATE.
