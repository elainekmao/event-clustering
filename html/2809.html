SUBJECT: ENGAGING CATHOLIC CHURCH IN VENEZUELA

REF: STATE: 73097

CLASSIFIED BY: AMBASSADOR JIM NICHOLSON, REASON E.O. 12958 1.5 (B,D)

1. (C) PER REFTEL, AMBASSADOR MET WITH VATICAN FM EQUIVALENT

ARCHBISHOP JEAN LOUIS TAURAN EVENING OF APRIL 19 TO URGE THE

HOLY SEE TO TAKE AN ACTIVE AND APPROPRIATE ROLE IN SUPPORT OF

VENEZUELAN DEMOCRACY. TAURAN WAS WELL BRIEFED ON THE

SITUATION IN VENEZUELA. AS USUAL, HE REFERRED TO THE POPE\'S

STATEMENT ON THE SITUATION (PROMOTING RECONCILIATION AND

PEACE), GIVEN AT THE WEDNESDAY, APRIL 17 GENERAL AUDIENCE.

HE WAS GUARDEDLY POSITIVE ON THE CHURCH TALKING A PLACE IN

PRESIDENT CHAVEZ\' "NATIONAL DIALOGUE," BUT SAID THAT THE

POSITION OF THE CHURCH VIS A VIS CHAVEZ WAS UNCERTAIN, GIVEN

THE LATTER\'S HISTORICAL HOSTILITY TO THE CHURCH. AMBASSADOR

AGREED, RECALLING THAT CHAVEZ HAD CALLED THE CHURCH "A

CANCER" ON THE VENEZUELAN BODY POLITIC JUST A COUPLE OF

MONTHS AGO.

2. (C) TAURAN SAID THE CHURCH HAD TO BE CAREFUL, THAT EVEN

THOUGH TIME WAS SHORT, THEY WOULD TAKE A LOW PROFILE UNTIL

THE EARLY POST-COUP PERIOD PLAYS OUT. HE SAID CHAVEZ IS

TALKING RECONCILIATION NOW BUT, TAURAN AVERRED, CHAVEZ WAS

NOT AT ALL TRUSTWORTHY. THE HOLY SEE FEARS A NEGATIVE

REACTION TO THE FACT THAT THE CARDINAL ARCHBISHOP OF CARACAS,

VELASCO GARCIA, HAD SIGNED A DOCUMENT AT THE SATURDAY

INAUGURATION OF PRESIDENTIAL USURPER CARMONA.

3. (C) TAURAN COUNSELED THE USG TO "PRESSURE" THE MILITARY

AND BUSINESS CLASSES IN VENEZUELA TO SUPPORT THE NATIONAL

RECONCILIATION NOW THAT CHAVEZ WAS BACK IN POWER. HE

SPECULATED THAT SOME GENERALS WHO HAD SUPPORTED THE COUP,

HAVING SEEN THAT A CARMONA PRESIDENCY LOOKED LIKE IT WOULD

INVESTIGATE CORRUPTION SCANDALS IN VENEZUELA, DECIDED THAT

PERHAPS CHAVEZ WAS THE LESSER OF TWO EVILS. TAURAN DECRIED

THE CONTINUING PRESENCE OF "THOSE 15 CUBAN ADVISORS" AND

RECALLED THAT WHEN THE POPE MET FIDEL CASTRO IN 1998, FIDEL

HAD SAID THERE WAS A YOUNG NEW LEADER ON THE SCENE, CALLING

CHAVEZ "SPLENDID." TAURAN CLOSED BY ASSURING US THAT HE

WOULD SPEAK TO HIS NUNCIO OVER THE WEEKEND, AND WOULD STAY IN

CLOSE TOUCH AS WE GO FORWARD.
