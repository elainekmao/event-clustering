1. THE JANUARY 2 ANNOUNCEMENT IN THE CROATIAN PRESS OF

THE UPCOMING VISIT OF S/WCI AMBASSADOR PIERRE PROSPER

SPARKED A USEFUL RENEWAL OF THE PUBLIC DEBATE ON

CROATIA\'S STANDING WITH ICTY. PRESS COMMENTARIES WERE

MIXED. AT ONE END OF THE SPECTRUM WAS CRITICISM OF THE

GOVERNMENT FOR PUTTING INTEGRATION WITH NATO AND THE EU

AT RISK BY ITS "AUTISTIC" APPROACH TO COOPERATION. AT

THE OTHER WAS CRITICISM OF THE TRIBUNAL FOR ITS DELAY IN

SENDING A MEDICAL TEAM TO EXAMINE BOBETKO. THE AMCIT

ATTORNEY FOR FUGITIVE ICTY INDICTEE GENERAL ANTE

GOTOVINA RANTED THAT HIS CLIENT WILL NEVER SURRENDER

HIMSELF AND DERIDED THE U.S. POSITION ON ICTY AS

"HYPOCRITICAL" CONSIDERING THE USG REFUSAL TO JOIN THE

INTERNATIONAL CRIMINAL COURT. GOVERNMENT RESPONSES

GENERALLY WELCOMED THE VISIT AS AN OPPORTUNITY TO

EXPLAIN TO AMB. PROSPER HOW CROATIA IS COOPERATING WITH

THE TRIBUNAL. END SUMMARY.

2. THE CROATIAN PRESS ON JANUARY 2 REPORTED THAT S/WCI

AMBASSADOR PIERRE RICHARD PROSPER ANNOUNCED HIS

INTENTION TO VISIT BELGRADE, BANJA LUKA, SARAJEVO, AND

ZAGREB IN LATE JANUARY 2003. CROATIAN NEWS AGENCY HINA

ALSO REPORTED THAT AMBASSADOR PROSPER "ACCUSED THE THREE

COUNTRIES OF THE FAILURE TO COOPERATE WITH THE

INTERNATIONAL CRIMINAL TRIBUNAL FOR FORMER YUGOSLAVIA

(ICTY). ... IN CROATIA, THE U.S. OFFICIAL WILL INSIST

ON THE ARREST OF GENERALS JANKO BOBETKO AND ANTE

GOTOVINA. \'ALL KNOW WHERE BOBETKO IS AND HE SHOULD BE

ARRESTED,\' PROSPER SAID."

3. CROATIAN MEDIA ON JANUARY 3 REPORTED ON A STATEMENT

BY THE CROATIAN EMBASSY IN WASHINGTON WHICH STATED THAT

"CROATIA ENJOYS GOOD COOPERATION WITH THE U.S. SPECIAL

AMBASSADOR FOR WAR CRIMES, PIERRE RICHARD PROSPER, AND

DURING HIS IMPENDING VISIT TO ZAGREB, WHERE HE WILL URGE

THE ARREST OF GENERALS SUSPECTED OF WAR CRIMES, IT WILL

BRIEF HIM ABOUT COOPERATION WITH THE HAGUE TRIBUNAL AND

THE CASE OF GENERAL JANKO BOBETKO. PROSPER\'S VISIT TO

ZAGREB LATER THIS MONTH WILL BE AN OPPORTUNITY FOR

CROATIA\'S AUTHORITIES TO BRIEF HIM ABOUT COOPERATION

WITH THE U.N. WAR CRIMES TRIBUNAL, DEVELOPMENTS IN THE

REGION SINCE HIS LAST VISIT, AS WELL AS ABOUT THE

OUTCOME OF THE BOBETKO CASE."

4. IN AN ARTICLE BY ITS FOREIGN AFFAIRS CORRESPONDENT,

BRUNO LOPANDIC, ZAGREB-BASED GOVERNMENT-OWNED "VJESNIK"

ON JANUARY 3 REPORTED THAT CROATIAN DIPLOMATIC SOURCES

HAVE TOLD "VJESNIK" THAT "THERE IS NOTHING DRAMATIC IN

THE VISIT. AMBASSADOR PROSPER\'S VISIT WILL BE AN

OPPORTUNITY TO INFORM HIM PERSONALLY ABOUT THE CROATIAN

GOVERNMENT ACTIONS IN CONNECTION WITH COOPERATION WITH

THE HAGUE TRIBUNAL. CROATIA IS NOT REFUSING TO

COOPERATE WITH THE HAGUE TRIBUNAL AND WE WILL TELL

AMBASSADOR PROSPER THAT CLEARLY."

CORRESPONDENT, VLADO RAJIC, UNDER THE HEADLINE "WHY

ISN\'T CARLA DEL PONTE SENDING MEDICAL EXPERTS TO EXAMINE

GENERAL BOBETKO," IN WHICH HE COMMENTED ON ICTY\'S

FAILURE TO SEND A MEDICAL TEAM TO ZAGREB. IN THAT

CONTEXT, RAJIC ALSO COMMENTS ON AMBASSADOR PROSPER\'S

PROSPER VISIT TO ZAGREB SPARKS RENEWED DEBATE ON ICTY

ON AMBASSADOR PROSPER\'S ANNOUNCED VISIT. MISETIC STATES

THAT GOTOVINA WILL NEVER TURN HIMSELF IN TO THE HAGUE

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 02 ZAGREB 00014 02 OF 02 031500Z

TRIBUNAL, AND THAT HE HAS TOLD "CROATIAN AND FOREIGN

POLITICIANS AND DIPLOMATS THAT THEY HAVE TO LIVE WITH

THIS FACT. MISETIC REACTED TO STATEMENTS BY PIERRE

RICHARD PROSPER, SPECIAL AMERICAN AMBASSADOR FOR WAR

CRIMES, WHO IN AN INTERVIEW WITH CROATIAN RADIO-

TELEVISION AND HINA REQUESTED THE ARREST OF GENERALS

GOTOVINA AND BOBETKO. MISETIC ASSESSED THIS STATEMENT

AS HYPOCRITICAL. \'THE U.S. IS REFUSING TO JOIN THE

PERMANENT INTERNATIONAL WAR CRIMES TRIBUNAL (NOTE: WE

ASSUME HE MEANS THE INTERNATIONAL CRIMINAL COURT. END

NOTE.), BECAUSE SUCH TRIBUNALS ARE POLITICIZED, AND IT

DOESN\'T WANT ITS OFFICERS AND CITIZENS TO STAND TRIALS

AT SUCH TRIBUNALS. AT THE SAME TIME, PROSPER IS

REQUESTING THE ARREST OF A CROATIAN CITIZEN, GENERAL

GOTOVINA, WHO DOESN\'T ACKNOWLEDGE THE INDICTMENT BECAUSE

HE CONSIDERS IT TO BE POLITICIZED. THAT\'S

HYPOCRITICAL,\' COMMENTED ATTORNEY MISETIC, WHO LIVES IN

CHICAGO."

8. RIJEKA-BASED "NOVI LIST" ON JANUARY 3 CARRIED A

COMMENTARY BY DAMIR GRUBISA UNDER THE HEADLINE "RENEWED

INCREASE OF AUTISM IN CROATIA," IN WHICH HE TOO

COMMENTED ON AMBASSADOR PROSPER\'S ANNOUNCED VISIT.

GRUBISA COMMENTED: "THE LATEST, ANNOUNCEMENT OF THE

VISIT BY PIERRE RICHARD PROSPER, AMERICAN AMBASSADOR FOR

WAR CRIMES, AND HIS UNAMBIGUOUS CONDEMNATION OF CROATIA

BECAUSE OF ITS FAILURE TO EXTRADITE GENERALS BOBETKO AND

GOTOVINA, IS ANOTHER SERIOUS WARNING TO CROATIA THAT ITS

AUTISM WILL NOT TAKE IT TO THE WEST. ... THAT\'S WHY

PROSPER\'S VISIT IS VERY SIGNIFICANT: AMERICAN

AMBASSADOR IN ZAGREB HAS ALREADY CLEARLY ENOUGH, IN A
