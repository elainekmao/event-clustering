SUMMARY AND COMMENT

-------------------

2. SPEAKING JULY 13, CROATIAN FM GRANIC USED THE FALL

OF SREBRENICA TO REINFORCE THE POINT HE HAS BEEN MAKING

FOR SOME TIME, THAT BELGRADE IS RESPONSIBLE AND THE

INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY MUST EXERT STRONG PRESSURE ON

MILOSEVIC. OVERALL, GRANIC\'S MESSAGE WAS ONE OF

REASSURANCE THAT CROATIA REMAINED COMMITTED TO PEACEFUL

RESOLUTION OF THE REGION\'S PROBLEMS AND RESTRAINT IN

THE FACE OF PROVOCATION. BUT HE DID LAY DOWN MARKERS

CONCERNING UNCRO\'S NONPERFORMANCE OF ITS MANDATE AND

POSSIBLE SERB MILITARY THREATS TO BIHAC. REFERRING TO

EVIDENCE OF A SERB BUILDUP AROUND BIHAC, HE MADE CLEAR

THIS WOULD BE OF MUCH MORE DIRECT CONCERN TO CROATIA,

AND COULD LEAD TO A "WIDENING OF THE WAR," THOUGH HE

STOPPED SHORT OF PREDICTING CROATIAN MILITARY ACTION TO

PRESERVE BIHAC\'S "PROTECTED ZONE" STATUS. END SUMMARY

3. CROATIAN FM GRANIC CONVOKED AMBASSADORS/CHARGES OF

COUNTRIES JULY 13 FOR A REAOOUT OF THE CROATIAN VIEW OF

SITUATION ON THE GROUND

-----------------------

4. REVIEWING THE SITUATION IN SREBRENICA AND ZEPA,

GRANIC ADDED LITTLE TO WHAT THE UN HAS ALREADY PUT OUT.

HE USED THE FIGURE OF 55,000 FOR TOTAL REFUGEES AND

NOTED THAT MILITARY AGE MEN WERE BEING SEPARATED OUT

AND WOULD BE CHARGED WITH WAR CRIMES. HE CALLED THE

SERB ACTION IN CAPTURING THE "ONE TRULY DEMILITARIZED

ZONE" IN B-H "UNJUSTIFIED," CLAIMING THAT CHARGES THAT

MILITARY ATTACKS WERE BEING ORGANIZED FROM INSIDE

SREBRENICA WERE GROUNDLESS AND COULD BE DISPROVED BY

LOOKING AT THE LOW "RATIO OF WEAPONS" FOUND AMONG THE

PEOPLE THERE. GRANIC SAID THAT THE NEW REFUGEES WOULD

MOSTLY END UP ON FEDERATION TERRITORY, ESPECIALLY TUZLA

AND ZENICA, AND WOULD DESTABILIZE THE SITUATION THERE

BY FURTHER CROWOING OF AN ALREADY-REDUCED SPACE.

NOTING THERE HAD BEEN MORE FIGHTING AROUND ZEPA, HE

SAID CONFIDENCE THAT THE UN WOULD DO ANYTHING TO

PROTECT ZEPA WAS "ALMOST NIL."

5. GRANIC ARGUED THAT NEITHER KARADZIC NOR MLADIC

COULD DECIDE TO CONDUCT ACTIONS LIKE SREBRENICA WITHOUT

MILOSEVIC\'S OK. BELGRADE WAS ATTEMPTING TO IMPOSE A

SOLUTION BY FORCE; THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY WAS

"APPEASING" HIM -- HE STEPPED UP THE WAR, JUST A FEW

DAYS AFTER A RENEWAL OF LIMITED SANCTIONS RELIEF, AND

SUFFERED NO CONSEQUENCES.

6. THE GOC, SAID GRANIC, WOULD CONTINUE TO ADHERE TO

THE LETTER AND SPIRIT OF THE WASHINGTON AGREEMENT AND

THE CONTACT GROUP PLAN. IT WOULD NOT RESPOND TO

PROVOCATIONS, AND WOULD CONTINUE TO EXERCISE RESTRAINT.

"EVEN THE LATEST DEVELOPMENTS" WOULD NOT SWAY ZAGREB

FROM ITS PEACEFUL COURSE. EVENTS IN SREBRENICA WOULD

BIND CROATIA AND B-H MORE CLOSELY, AND CROATIA WOULD

CONTINUE ITS STRONG SUPPORT FOR BUILDING THE

FEDERATION. AS SOON AS THE LOGISTICS COULD BE WORKED

THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY

---------------------------

7. CONTINUING, GRANIC SAID CROATIA WANTED A REVIVAL OF

THE PEACE PROCESS AND TO THAT END, URGED THE

INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY TO SEEK RENEWED PROGRESS ON

PEACE THROUGH STRONG PRESSURE ON SERBIA; TO TAKE

IMMEDIATE STEPS TO ENSURE THAT UNCRO FULFILLED ITS

MANDATE; NOT TO LIFT SANCTIONS ON SERBIA UNTIL IT HAD

RECOMMITTED ITSELF TO THE PEACE PROCESS AND RECOGNIZED

BOTH B-H AND/AND CROATIA. THE GOVERNMENTS REPRESENTED

AT THE BRIEFING, HE SAID, SHOULD DO EVERYTHING POSSIBLE

TO ACTIVATE THE PEACE PROCESS, INCLUDING USE OF UNPF,

THE RAPID REACTION FORCE, AND PRESSURE ON MILOSEVIC.

8. SEEMING ALMOST AS AN AFTERTHOUGHT, GRANLC ADDED

THAT CROATIA WAS ESPECIALLY CONCERNED BY OBSERVED

"MASSING OF SERB FORCES" IN THE BIHAC POCKET,

ESPECIALLY NEAR SLUNJ. HE SAID THAT ANY SERB ACTION TO

TAKE BIHAC WOULD BE TAKEN "EXTREMELY SERIOUSLY." ASKED

LATER WHAT ACTION CROATIA WOULD TAKE IF THE SERBS

ATTACKED BIHAC, GRANIC SAID HE HOPED IT "WOULD NOT COME

TO THAT" BECAUSE SUCH ACTION WOULD BE DANGEROUS FOR THE

SERBS IN "SEVERAL WAYS." GRANIC SAID TUDJMAN\'S MESSAGE

WAS THAT IT WAS CRUCIAL TO "ACCELERATE THE PEACE

PROCESS," AND THIS WAS WHERE CROATIA\'S EFFORTS WOULD

CONCENTRATE. HE NOTED THE POSSIBILITY OF "INDIRECT

HELP," E.G., SUCH AS HVO ACTION IN LIVANSKO POLJE,

WHICH HAD "HELPED RELIEVE PRESSURE" ON THE BOSNIAN V

CORPS WHEN BIHAC WAS THREATENED AT THE END OF LAST

YEAR. (NOTE: AT ONE POINT GRANIC SAID THAT AN "ATTACK

ON BIHAC BY THE SERBS WOULD CERTAINLY WIDEN THE WAR"

BUT THIS WAS NOT TRANSLATED BY THE INTERPRETER.)
