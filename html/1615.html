SUBJECT: VATICAN CONDEMNS CLONING BUT PROTECTS THE CLONES

1. CHARGE AND POLITICAL ASSISTANT MET MARCH 23 WITH THE

POPE\'S THEOLOGIAN, FRENCH-BORN DOMINICAN MONK GEORGES

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 02 VATICA 01745 311607Z

COTTIER, TO DISCUSS THE CLONING OF HUMAN BEINGS. COTTIER

AFFIRMED THE VATICAN POSITION THAT CLONING IS IMMORAL IN

EVERY CIRCUMSTANCE. HOWEVER, HE STRESSED THAT HUMAN BEINGS

THAT RESULT FROM CLONING PROCEDURES MUST BE CONSIDERED EQUAL

TO ANY OTHER PERSON IN REGARD TO RIGHTS AND

RESPONSIBILITIES. (NOTE: TRANSLATED THEOLOGICALLY, FOR THE

VATICAN THESE CLONES WILL HAVE THE SAME IMMORTAL SOULS THAT

ALL HUMAN BEINGS POSSESS. END NOTE). COTTIER FIRMLY

DISMISSED THE POSSIBILITY OF A SUB-HUMAN RACE OF CLONES.

(NOTE: THE VATICAN ACCEPTS CLONING THERAPIES USING PLANTS

AND ANIMALS WHEN THIS DIRECTLY BENEFITS HUMAN PERSONS IN

TERMS OF FOOD AND HEALTH CARE, AS LONG AS ANIMAL RIGHTS AND

BIO-DIVERSITY ARE RESPECTED. END NOTE).

2. COTTIER ACCEPTED THE LICITY OF CLONING INDIVIDUAL BODY

PARTS FOR THERAPEUTIC PURPOSES. HE ALSO APPROVED OF THE USE

OF ANIMAL ORGANS FOR TRANSPLANTATION INTO HUMANS, IF PROPER

HEALTH SAFEGUARDS WERE FOLLOWED. COTTIER SAID THE USE OF

ADULT STEM CELLS IN HEALTH-CARE THERAPIES WAS LEGITIMATE. HE

CONDEMNED THE USE OF STEM CELLS FROM FETUSES DESTROYED BY

THE TECHNIQUE.

3. COMMENT: FATHER COTTIER EXPRESSED HIS APPRECIATION THAT

EMBASSY OFFICERS REPRESENTING THE USG HAD SHOWN FORESIGHTED

INTEREST IN HUMAN CLONING. HE OPINED THAT WHILE THE

TECHNIQUE SEEMED INEVITABLE, MOST GOVERNMENTS HAD FAILED TO

ANTICIPATE ITS MORAL, SOCIAL AND ECONOMIC IMPLICATIONS. END

COMMENT. MERANTE
