SUBJECT: PUERTO RICO REQUESTS FAO ASSOCIATE MEMBERSHIP

REF: (02) ROME 5470

1. SUMMARY: PUERTO RICO SEEKS TO REGAIN ASSOCIATE

MEMBER STATUS WITH THE UN FOOD AND AGRICULTURE

ORGANIZATION (FAO) AND HAS REPORTEDLY ASKED THE U.S.

SECRETARY OF STATE TO SUPPORT THE REQUEST. FAO

SIPDIS

CORRESPONDENCE TO THE SECRETARY OF STATE OF PUERTO RICO

(COPIED TO THE U.S. MISSION) CALLS ATTENTION TO PUERTO

RICO\'S OUTSTANDING ARREARS OF USD 75,424.80. END

SUMMARY.

2. REFTEL COVERED THE HISTORY OF PUERTO RICO\'S ASSOCIATE

MEMBERSHIP WITH FAO (NOW LAPSED). IN A LETTER DATED

APRIL 23, 2003 FROM DIRECTOR GENERAL JACQUES DIOUF TO

FERDINAND MERCADO (SECRETARY OF STATE OF PUERTO RICO),

COPIED TO THE U.S. MISSION, DIOUF WELCOMED THE NEWS THAT

"PUERTO RICO HAS REQUESTED THE SUPPORT OF THE U.S.

SECRETARY OF STATE" FOR ASSOCIATE MEMBERSHIP AND CALLS

SIPDIS

ATTENTION TO THE FACT THAT PUERTO RICO STILL HAS ARREARS

OF USD 75,424.80. (LETTER FAXED TO S.KOTOK, IO/EDA

4/29/03).

3. AS NOTED IN REFTEL, THE FAO CONSTITUTION ALLOWS FOR

ASSOCIATE MEMBERSHIP FOR TERRITORIES THAT ARE NOT

RESPONSIBLE FOR THE CONDUCT OF THEIR INTERNATIONAL

RELATIONS. THE MEMBER NATION HAVING AUTHORITY FOR THE

TERRITORY\'S INTERNATIONAL RELATIONS APPLIES ON THE

TERRITORY\'S BEHALF AND ASSUMES CERTAIN OBLIGATIONS (THE

MOST IMPORTANT BEING ASSUMING RESPONSIBILITY FOR ENSURING

THAT THE ASSOCIATE MEMBER FULFILLS ITS FINANCIAL

OBLIGATIONS.) HALL

NNNN
