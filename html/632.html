SUBJECT: TFSROL: THE MEDIA WAR: CROAT-MUSLIM

NAME-CALLING

1. (U) THIS EMBASSY CONTINUES TO NOTE THE ROLE OF THE

LOCAL MEDIA IN HEIGHTENING ETHNIC TENSIONS. GOVERNMENT

OWNED AND CONTROLLED CROATIAN TV (HTV) ON SATURDAY,

JAN. 29, BROADCAST TWO UNSCHEDULED DOCUMENTARIES

BACK-TO-BACK IMMEDIATELY AFTER THE EVENING NEWS. THE

FIRST WAS A TWENTY MINUTE FILM CALLED "THE ROAD TO

CONFIDENTIAL

CONFIDENTIAL

CENTRAL BOSNIA" AND THE SECOND WAS A LONGER FEATURE

CALLED "THE CHRONOLOGY OF MUSLIM WAR CRIMES AGAINST

CROATS IN B-H." THE EVENING MOVIE WAS DELAYED AN HOUR

IN ORDER TO AIR THESE DOCUMENTARIES. THE TV SPECIALS

WERE NOT ON THE DAILY TV SCHEDULE BUT HAD BEEN WELL

ADVERTISED DURING THE DAY ON HTV. AS THE TITLES

INDICATE, THEY FOCUSED ON MUSLIM MISDEEDS IN CENTRAL

B-H. BOTH DOCUMENTARIES APPEARED HASTILY PUT TOGETHER,

AND WERE POORLY PRODUCED. NEITHER WAS ACCOMPANIED BY A

NARRATED SCRIPT AND BOTH CONSISTED ALMOST EXCLUSIVELY

OF AMATEUR FOOTAGE AND TAPED INTERVIEWS WITH LOCAL

BOSNIAN CROATS DESCRIBING MUSLIM ATROCITIES. THERE WAS

LITTLE DOCUMENTATION PROVIDED AS TO THE TIME AND PLACE

OF THE EVENTS DESCRIBED.

2. (U) "THE ROAD TO CENTRAL BOSNIA" INCLUDED

INTERVIEWS WITH CROAT RESIDENTS OF CENTRAL BOSNIA (ALL

EXPRESSING FEAR OF MUSLIM FORCES) AND CROAT SOLDIERS.

THE OVERALL MESSAGE WAS "THE ENEMY IS COMING AND WE ARE

PREPARED." "THE CHRONOLOGY OF WAR CRIMES" WAS MORE

POWERFUL WITH GRAPHIC PICTURES OF CORPSES AND WEEPING

SURVIVORS, MOSTLY WOMEN. IN ALL CASES, MUSLIMS WERE

NAMED AS THE PERPETRATORS OF THE MASSACRES. MOST OF

THE FOOTAGE CLEARLY DATED FROM THE FALL OR BEFORE.

-

3. (C) ONE EMBARRASSED PROFESSIONAL TV JOURNALIST

COMPLAINED TO OUR PUBLIC AFFAIRS OFFICER THAT THIS LINE

OF TV PROGRAMMING WAS DIRECTED BY THE HEAD OF HTV,

ANTON VRDOLJAK. VRDOLJAK IS A HIGHLY UNPOPULAR, BUT

INFLUENTIAL, MEMBER OF THE RULING PARTY AND THE

CROATIAN PARLIAMENT. ACCORDING TO THE JOURNALIST, THE

PROGRAMMING CHIEF AT HTV WAS AFRAID OF VRDOLJAK AND

WOULD NEVER RESIST WHAT THE "BIG MAN" DIRECTED.

4. (U) THESE TV BROADCASTS CONTRAST TO THOSE IN THE

AUTUMN WHICH DEPICTED GRAPHICALLY (USING CNN FOOTAGE)

STARVING MUSLIMS IN THE HVO-RUN DETENTION CAMPS AND THE

HVO\'S DESTRUCTION OF THE OLD BRIDGE IN MOSTAR. HTV HAS

ALSO AIRED SHOWS WITH SURPRISINGLY CANDID CRITICISM

FROM OPPOSITION LEADERS ABOUT THE GOVERNMENT\'S B-H

POLICY. THUS, THESE CRUDE PROPAGANDIST EFFORTS

REPRESENT A BACKWARD STEP FOR HTV, AS WELL AS A SIGN OF

A DEGENERATING POLITICAL ENVIRONMENT.

5. (C) ANXIETY AMONG MUSLIM RESIDENTS OF ZAGREB WAS

EXPRESSED BY THE WIFE OF A LEADING MUSLIM HUMANITARIAN

AID WORKER WHO IS ALSO A PROFESSIONAL. SHE DECLINED AN

INVITATION FOR A PRESTIGIOUS USIA VISITORS\' PROGRAM

BECAUSE OF FEAR THAT HER EMPLOYER WOULD FIRE HER IF SHE

WAS ABSENT FROM HER JOB. OBVIOUSLY AGITATED AND
