SUBJECT: DEMARCHE ON IRANIAN PLANE SUSPECTED OF CARRYING

ARMS TO TERRORISTS DELIVERED TO JORDAN

REF: STATE 90218

Classified By: CDA GREGORY L. BERRY. REASONS 1.5 (B,C, and D).

1. (S) On May 11, upon receipt of NIACT precedence message,

poloff/duty officer passed reftel information regarding the

impending 5/12 flight of a suspected arms-laden Iranian cargo

plane to Ambassador Mohammed Shahankari, Director of Foreign

Minister Muasher\'s Private Office. Shahankari conferred with

Acting FonMin Shaher Bak, and then, in Poloff\'s presence,

instructed his MFA staff to immediately share the information

with the appropriate GOJ authorities. As the meeting

concluded, Shahankari told poloff "If we receive a request

for clearance from this flight, we will say no. Don\'t worry."
