SUBJECT: CGYOOL: CROATIAN AND BOSNIAN PRESIDENTS

- MEET TO IRON OUT DIFFERENCES OVER B-H

REF: (A) ZAGREB 2226 (NOTAL)

- (B) ZAGREB 2224

- (C) ZAGREB 2223 (NOTAL)

1. FOLLOWING A PUBLIC EXCHANGE OF RECRIMINATORY

LETTERS BY PRESIDENTS IZETBEGOVIC AND TUDJMAN (REFS A

AND B), THE TWO LEADERS MET IN ZAGREB ON SUNDAY,

NOV 1, TO ATTEMPT TO IRON OUT THEIR DIFFERENCES.

TELEVISION COVERAGE OF THE START OF THE MEETING

SHOWED B-H HDZ LEADER MATE BOBAN NEXT TO IZETBEGOVIC

ON THE BOSNIAN SIDE OF THE TABLE, ALONG WITH B-H

PRESIDENCY ADVISOR HAJRUDIN SOMUN AND SEFKO OMERBASIC,

PRESIDENT OF "MESIHATA FOR CROATIA AND SLOVENIA".

THE CROATIAN SIDE INCLUDED DEFMIN SUSAK, TUDJMAN CHEF

D\'CABINET JURE RADIC, AND GEN. SLOBODAN PRALJAK.

AFTER LENGTHY MEETINGS EXTENDING INTO SUNDAY EVENING,

THE TWO SIDES HAD REACHED AGREEMENT ON SEVERAL

SIGNIFICANT POINTS, WHICH WERE MADE PUBLIC NOV 2.

2. IZETBEGOVIC AND TUDJMAN CALLED FOR AN IMMEDIATE

END TO THE FIGHTING BETWEEN B-H AND HVO FORCES

"BECAUSE IT HARMS THE COMMON STRUGGLE AGAINST

SERBIAN/MONTENEGRIN EXTREMIST FORCES." THOSE GUILTY

OF INSTIGATING THE B-H/CROAT CONFLICT WERE TO BE

REMOVED AND PUNISHED. A JOINT COMMISSION IS TO BE

FORMED TO INVESTIGATE THE FACTS SURROUNDING THE

CONFLICT. THE TWO LEADERS AGAIN CALLED FOR THE

FORMATION OF A JOINT COMMAND FOR B-H AND HVO FORCES.

THEY ANNOUNCED THAT WORK WOULD BEGIN IMMEDIATELY IN

THE JOINT COMMISSION ON INTER-STATE AND MILITARY

COOPERATION, TO WHICH BOTH SIDES HAVE NOW NAMED THEIR

REPRESENTATIVES. IZETBEGOVIC PROMISED TO NOMINATE A

B-H AMBASSADOR TO CROATIA NEXT WEEK AND STRESSED THE

IMPORTANCE OF ESTABLISHING EMBASSIES TO HELP RESOLVE

PROBLEMS.

3. THE PRESIDENTS CONDEMNED STEPS TAKEN BY THE

SO-CALLED "REPUBLIC OF KRAJINA" IN CROATIA AND THE

SO-CALLED "SERBIAN REPUBLIC" IN B-H TO FORM A

POLITICAL UNION. THEY TERMED IT A NEW ACT OF

AGGRESSION AGAINST TWO SOVEREIGN AND INTERNATIONALLY

4. THE TWO PRESIDENTS SUPPORTED, IN PRINCIPLE, THE

DRAFT B-H CONSTITUTION UNVEILED LAST WEEK AT THE

GENEVA CONFERENCE. THEY INSISTED THAT THE FINAL TEXT

SHOULD ENSURE THE LEGITIMATE INTERESTS AND EQUALITY

OF THE MUSLIM AND CROATIAN PEOPLES IN B-H. FINALLY,

THEY PLEDGED TO MAKE A MAXIMUM EFFORT TO INCLUDE

LEGITIMATE REPRESENTATIVES OF THE SERBIAN PEOPLE IN

THE POLITICAL PROCESS OF FORMING A FUTURE B-H.

5. IZETBEGOVIC TOLD TUDJMAN THE B-H GOVERNMENT WAS

TO BE RESHUFFLED AND THAT THE PRIME MINISTER\'S

POSITION WOULD BE RESERVED FOR A BOSNIAN CROAT FROM

THE CROATIAN DEMOCRATIC UNION (HDZ). BOBAN FORMALLY

NOMINATED MIRO LASIC AS THE HDZ REPRESENTATIVE IN THE

B-H COLLECTIVE PRESIDENCY.

--------------------------------------------- ---------

6. FOLLOWING OCT 30 CONVERSATION WITH PRESIDENT

TUDJMAN REPORTED REF C, TUDJMAN MET WITH VANCE AND

OWEN, AFTER WHICH HE DECLARED, ONCE AGAIN, THAT "WE

RECOGNIZED BOSNIA-HERCEGOVINA AS A SOVEREIGN STATE.

THE PROBLEM OF CONFLICT THERE MUST BE RESOLVED

PEACEFULLY BY MUSLIMS AND CROATS THEMSELVES." HE

ADDED THAT "THE REPUBLIC OF CROATIA IS VITALLY

INTERESTED THAT PEACE IS ESTABLISHED IMMEDIATELY IN

THIS NEIGHBORING STATE."
