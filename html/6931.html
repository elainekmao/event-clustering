SUBJECT: EXTRANCHECK: PRE-LICENSE CHECK PROSPORTS

INTERNATIONAL

REF A)USDOC 00754 B) USDOC 01173; APPLICATION NOS.

D304672/D304689

1.REFTEL B WAS A FOLLOW UP TO A REQUEST FOR A PRE-LICENSE

CHECK TO DETERMINE THE SUITABILITY OF PROSPORTS

INTERNATIONAL, BOX 26723 DUBAI, UNITED ARAB EMIRATES AS A

RECIPIENT OF US ORIGIN GOODS OR TECHNICAL DATA. POST HAD NOT

RECEIVED REFTEL A, WHICH WAS FAXED TO POST ON MARCH 10,

2003.

2. ON MARCH 12, 2003, COMMERCIAL OFFICER MARY O\'BRIEN

CONDUCTED A PLC ON PROSPORTS INTERNATIONAL, LOCATED IN

DUBAI, TELEPHONE NUMBER 971-04-34-34-008, FAX 971-04-34-34-

007. COMMOFF MET WITH MR. JULIAN HOLMES, GENERAL MANAGER,

PROSPORTS INTERNATIONAL AND HIS ASSISTANT.

3. MR. HOLMES STATED THAT PROSPORTS INTERNATIONAL IS A

WHOLESALE COMPANY AND IS OWNED BY SHEIKH SAEED BIN MOHAMMED

AL MAKTOUM, A MEMBER OF THE RULING FAMILY IN DUBAI AND AN

OLYMPIC LEVEL MARKSMAN, WHO RECENTLY WON A GOLD MEDAL IN

COMPETITIVE SHOOTING AT THE ASIAN SHHOTING COMPETITION. MR.

HOLMES SAID THAT PROSPORTS INTERNATIONAL REPRESENTS SEVERAL

MAJOR COMPANIES, INCLUDING ADIDAS, ANTIGUA AND MOOSE APPAREL

FROM CANADA. MR. HOLMES SAID THAT PROSPORTS INTERNATIONAL IS

MOVING TO A NEW LOCATION ADJACENT TO WHERE THEIR WAREHOUSE

IS NOW LOCATED.

4. MR. HOLMES SAID THAT ACCORDING TO THEIR AGREEMENT WITH

BUSHNELL, PROSPORTS INTERNATIONAL IS THE EXCLUSIVE

DISTRIBUTOR FOR BUSHNELL FOR THE MIDDLE EAST. BUSHNELL OWNS

BOLLE SUNGLASSES AND SMITH ATHLETIC EYEWEAR, WHICH PROSPORTS

INTERNATIONAL DISTRIBUTES.

5. MR. HOLMES GAVE COMMOFF MARY O\'BRIEN A COPY OF PROSPORTS\'

TRADE LICENSE ISSUED ON 04/05/1995 FROM THE DEPARTMENT OF

ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT, GOVERNMENT OF DUBAI. THIS DOCUMENT CAN

BE IMAGED AND FORWARDED BY E-MAIL UPON REQUEST.

6. MR. HOLMES SAID THE RIFLESCOPES AND ACCESSORIES LISTED ON

THE EXPORT LICENSE APPLICATION WILL BE DISPLAYED AT THE

HUNTING SHOW SCHEDULUED FOR ABU DHABI THIS YEAR. BUSHNELL

PLANS TO PARTICIPATE IN THE SHOW AS AN EXHIBITOR. IN

ADDITION, MR. HOLMES HAS RECEIVED INQUIRIES FROM SOME RETAIL

SPORTING GOODS AND SHOOTING ESTABLISHMENTS IN THE UAE ABOUT

SELLING RIFLESCOPES. PROSPORTS INTERNATIONAL HAS NOT YET

ORDERED ANY SCOPES AND THINKS THAT THE NUMBERS ON THE

APPLICATION MAY BE OPTIMISTIC FOR THE TWO-YEAR LIFE OF THE

LICENSE. IT APPEARS BUSHNELL IS SEEKING THIS LICENSE IN

ANTICIPATION OF FUTURE ORDERS THROUGHOUT THE MIDDLE EAST

OVER THE TWO-YEAR LIFE OF THE LICENSE.

7. RECOMMENDATION: COMMOFF BELIEVES PROSPORTS INTERNATIONAL,

WHILE NOT AN EXPERIENCED IMPORTER/EXPORTER OF FIREARMS OR

ACCESSORIES, IS A RELIABLE RECIPIENT OF U.S. ORIGIN

COMMODITIES.
