SUBJECT: ZIMBABWEAN PRESIDENT MAKES NO DECISION ON

RELEASE OF CUBAN DOCTORS

REF: A) HARARE 3263

CLASSIFIED BY AMBASSADOR TOM MCDONALD FOR REASONS 1.5

B/D.

1. (C) ON JUNE 16 UNHCR ATTORNEY MICHIEL SALOMONS

REPORTED THAT HE HAD RECEIVED NO INFORMATION FROM THE

GOVERNMENT OF ZIMBABWE ON A POSSIBLE PRESIDENTIAL

DECISION TO RELEASE CUBANS DOCTORS LEONEL CORDOVA

RODRIGUEZ AND NORIS DEL CARMEN PENA MARTINEZ TO THE

CUSTODY OF THE UNHCR. UNHCR REGIONAL REPRESENTATIVE

OLUSEYI BAJULAIYE SEPARATELY INFORMED AMBASSADOR

MCDONALD THAT HE WOULD ASK UNHCR HEAD OGATA TO WRITE

PRESIDENT MUGABE ASKING HIM TO ARRANGE FOR THEIR EARLY

RELEASE. SALOMONS SAID HE WAS GOING TO MEET WITH THE

DUTCH AMBASSADOR ON THE MORNING OF JUNE 16 TO PURSUE THE

IDEA OF TEMPORARILY RESETTLING THE CUBANS IN THE

NETHERLANDS (REFTEL). HE ALSO INFORMED US THAT LOCAL

UNHCR REPRESENTATIVE JOHN ADU IS TO VISIT THE CUBANS ON

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 HARARE 03264 161115Z

THE SAME DAY.

2. (C) COMMENT: WE ARE CONVINCED THAT BAJULAIYE

SINCERELY WISHES TO REACH AN EARLY SOLUTION TO THE CASE

OF THE CUBANS DOCTORS THAT IS SATISFACTORY TO ALL. WE

ALSO DETECTED EXCESSIVE OPTIMISM ON HIS ORGANIZATION\'S

PART. BAJULAIYE IS A COMPETENT AND HARD-WORKING

DIPLOMAT, BUT HAS LITTLE TO SHOW FOR HIS EFFORTS TO

DATE. WE HAVE MADE OUR POSITION QUITE CLEAR, FORCEFULLY

AND OFTEN, ON A BILATERAL BASIS TO THE GOVERNMENT OF

ZIMBABWE AND HAVE EXHAUSTED THE POSSIBILITIES FOR

INTERVENTION. THE ZIMBABWEAN PRESIDENT IS NOT FOCUSED

ON THIS PROBLEM ON THE EVE OF AN ELECTION THAT MAY

DETERMINE HIS PARTY\'S FUTURE. INDEED, WE DOUBT THAT

MUGABE EVEN HEARD ABOUT THE CASE ON JUNE 15, PER REFTEL,

AS WE SAW HIS MOTORCADE ON THE STREETS HEADING TOWARD

HIS RESIDENCE AT THE TIME THE FOREIGN MINISTRY HAD

INFORMED THE UNHCR OFFICIALS THAT THE PRESIDENT WAS

SUPPOSED TO BE BRIEFED. END COMMENT.

3. (C) ACTION REQUESTED: WE THINK THAT MAXIMUM PRESSURE

NEEDS TO BE BROUGHT TO BEAR ON UNHCR IN GENEVA. WHILE

BAJULAIYE SAID THAT UNHCR HEAD OGATA WOULD WRITE

PRESIDENT MUGABE, WE THINK THAT A TELEPHONE CALL IS A

MORE POTENT INSTRUMENT. DEPARTMENT MAY WISH TO CONTACT

THE UNITED NATIONS HIGH COMMISSIONER FOR REFUGEES AGAIN

TO STRESS OUR DESIRE FOR HER PERSONAL INTERVENTION WITH

THE ZIMBABWEAN HEAD OF STATE IN THIS MATTER.

4. (C) ACTION REQUESTED CONTINUED: WE ALSO BELIEVE THAT

THE POWER OF THE MEDIA COULD BE BROUGHT TO BEAR ON THIS

CASE THROUGH A BACKGROUND BRIEFING FOR REPORTERS CHRIS

GAITHER AND/OR SANDRA MARQUEZ AT "THE MIAMI HERALD."

HERALD" TO FOCUS ITS ATTENTION ON THE GOVERNMENT OF

ZIMBABWE\'S UNILATERAL ABILITY TO RESOLVE THE CASE. THAT

ATTENTION, IN TURN, MAY HELP THE GOZ UNDERSTAND THAT

THIS IS AN IMPORTANT CASE, ONE THAT NEEDS TO BE RESOLVED

NOW.
