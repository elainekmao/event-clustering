SUBJECT: MTAG 05-96: GOI HALTS MISSILE-RELATED TRANSFER

BETWEEN ITALIAN FIRM AND IRAN (S)

REF: A. SECSTATE 94815

B. ROME 1566

C. ROME 1502

D. 02 STATE 35257

E. 02 STATE 13875

CLASSIFIED BY: A/ECMIN DAVID MULENEX REASONS 1.5 (D) AND (E)

1. (S) ECONOFFS MET APRIL 15 WITH GIANPAOLO SCARANTE, DEPUTY

DIPLOMATIC ADVISOR TO PM BERLUSCONI, TO RAISE CONTINUED

SERIOUS USG CONCERNS REGARDING THE EXPORT OF CLIMATIC

CHAMBERS FROM ITALY\'S MAZZALI SYSTEMS TO IRAN\'S SAFETY

EQUIPMENT PROCUREMENT INCORPORATED (REF A). SCARANTE WAS

VERY FAMILIAR WITH THE ISSUE, HAVING RECEIVED UK EMBASSY

OFFICIALS TWICE LAST WEEK ON THE CASE (REFS B AND C), TO WHOM

HE HAD CONFIRMED THE GOI\'S ISSUANCE OF AN EXPORT LICENSE.

SCARANTE TOLD US TODAY THAT ITALIAN CUSTOMS OFFICIALS HAD

HALTED THE SHIPMENT APRIL 14. HE FURTHER CONFIRMED THAT THE

GOI NOW IS CONSIDERING THE POSSIBLE REVOCATION OF THE EXPORT

LICENSE. WE THANKED SCARANTE FOR THE GOI ACTIONS, AND ASKED

THAT WE BE INFORMED OF ANY DEVELOPMENTS.

2. (S) WE ALSO PROVIDED REF A POINTS TO THE DIPLOMATIC

ADVISOR TO MINISTER OF PRODUCTIVE ACTIVITIES MARZANO (MARZANO

APPROVES EXPORT LICENSES, AND PRESUMABLY WILL BE THE OFFICIAL

TO APPROVE ANY REVOCATION), AND HAVE REQUESTED A FOLLOW-UP

MEETING TO SEEK ADDITIONAL DETAILS ON THE REVOCATION

PROCEDURE. WE ARE PROVIDING THE INFORMATION IN REFS D AND E

TO ALL RELEVANT GOI OFFICIALS IN ORDER TO BOLSTER THE CASE

FOR REVOCATION. SCARANTE INDICATED THAT THE GOI MIGHT REQUEST

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION FROM US TO FURTHER SUPPORT A

REVOCATION DECISION.

3. (S) EMBASSY WILL ALSO RAISE OUR CONCERNS WITH MFA DIRECTOR

GENERAL FOR ECONOMIC COOPERATION GIANDOMENICO MAGLIANO LATE

IN THE DAY APRIL 15, EXPRESSING OUR THANKS FOR FREEZING THE

SHIPMENT, AND ASKING FOR DETAILS ON THE REVOCATION PROCEDURE.
