SUBJECT: CUBAN DOCTORS TO FLY TO NAIROBI TO COMPLETE

U.S. TRAVEL PACKET ON JUNE 11

REF: (A) HARARE 3105 (NOTAL)

(B) NAIROBI 4808

CLASSIFIED BY DCM EARL M. IRVING FOR REASONS 1.5 (B) AND

(D).

1. (SBU) REGIONAL INS OFFICER SANJEETA VARSANI ARRIVED

IN HARARE FROM NAIROBI THE MORNING OF JUNE 9 AND WAS MET

BY CONSUL. THE PAIR WENT DIRECTLY TO UNCHR. JOHN ADU,

WHO INFORMED THEM THAT THE GOZ HAD NOT YET AUTHORIZED

THE MEETING WITH THE CUBANS, GREETED CONSUL AND VARSANI.

ADU THEN IMMEDIATELY REQUESTED AMBASSADOR MCDONALD\'S

ASSISTANCE IN GETTING ACCESS TO THE CUBANS. SEVERAL

TELEPHONE CALLS LATER, A MEETING WAS ARRANGED AND THE

TRIO, ACCOMPANIED BY ZIMBABWE IMMIGRATION, ARRIVED AT

GOROMONZI DETENTION CENTER TO INTERVIEW THE CUBANS.

2. (C) DOCTOR LEONEL CORDOVA RODRIGUEZ WAS INTERVIEWED

FIRST. CORDOVA DESCRIBED HOW ON JUNE 2 HE AND DOCTOR

NORIS DEL CARMEN PENA MARTINEZ WERE HUSTLED INTO A CAR

BY FOUR ZIMBABWEANS, TWO CIVILIANS AND TWO POLICEMEN

CARRYING RIFLES AND TAKEN TO IMMIGRATION HEADQUARTERS,

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 HARARE 03112 01 OF 02 101506Z

WHERE THEY REMAINED UNTIL THEY WERE TAKEN TO HARARE

INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT. CORDORVA REPEATEDLY ASKED TO SEE

UNHCR ATTORNEY MICHIEL SALOMONS, A REQUEST THAT WAS

DENIED AT EVERY TURN. CORDOVA SAID THAT HE SAW CUBAN

AMBASSADOR RODOLFO SARRACINO, CHIEF CONSUL JAVIER

VIAMONTES AND CUBAN MEDICAL TEAM LEADER DR. BUSTILLO

LURKING IN THE BACKGROUND. CORDOVA TOLD THEM, "YOU HAVE

WON, I WILL SEE YOU IN CUBA" BEFORE ENTERING THE PLANE.

CORDOVA MANAGED TO WRITE A NOTE, DESCRIBING HOW HE AND

PENA WERE BEING KIDNAPPED AND BEING RETURNED TO CUBA.

3. (C) THE CUBANS WERE KEPT FOR TWO DAYS IN CUSTODY OF

SOUTH AFRICAN IMMIGRATION AFTER THEY ARRIVED IN

JOHANNESBURG THE AFTERNOON OF JUNE 2. ON JUNE 4, SOUTH

AFRICAN IMMIGRATION OFFICIALS TOLD THE CUBANS THAT THEY

"MUST" RETURN TO CUBA, AS THEY HAD ENTERED THE COUNTRY

WITHOUT VISAS. ACCOMPANIED BY THE SAME TWO ZIMBABWEAN

HANDLERS AND NOW TWO SOUTH AFRICAN IMMIGRATION OFFICERS,

THE PAIR WERE ESCORTED TO AN AIR FRANCE FLIGHT BOUND FOR

HAVANA. THE GROUP WAS MET AT THE AIRPORT AND TAKEN TO

THE GATE BY THE CONSUL GENERAL OF THE CUBAN EMBASSY

PRETORIA AND BY DR. PABLO DIAZ, ALSO A CUBAN, WHO WAS

NOT UNIDENTIFIED. AT THIS POINT, DOCTORS CORDOVA AND

PENA STARTED SCREAMING AND THE AIR FRANCE CREW STOPPED

EVERYONE FROM ENTERING THE PLANE. CORDOVA EXPLAINED

WHAT WAS HAPPENING TO A SPANISH-SPEAKING FLIGHT

ATTENDANT, WHO TOLD EVERYONE TO GET OFF THE PLANE. THE

CUBANS AND TWO HANDLERS FROM SOUTH AFRICAN IMMIGRATION

BOARDED ANOTHER PLANE AND RETURNED TO HARARE THE SAME

DAY. UPON ARRIVAL IN HARARE THE CUBANS WERE TURNED OVER

TO ZIMBABWEAN IMMIGRATION AND TAKEN TO THE GOROMONZI

DETENTION CENTER.

4. (C) INS OFFICER VARSANI GRANTED THE CUBANS

CONDITIONAL REFUGEE STATUS, FINDING THAT THEY

ESTABLISHED A WELL-FOUNDED FEAR OF PERSECUTION ON

ACCOUNT OF THEIR POLITICAL OPINIONS IF RETURNED TO CUBA.

UPON THEIR RELEASE FROM THE GOROMONZI DETENTION CENTER,

THE TWO WILL TRAVEL TO NAIROBI ON JUNE 11 WITH VARSANI

IN ORDER TO COMPLETE THEIR TRAVEL PACKET FOR THE UNITED

STATES. WE EXPECT THEM TO FLY FROM NAIROBI TO THE

UNITED STATES BY THURSDAY, JUNE 15.

5. (C) IN THE LAST 24 HOURS BOTH THE LOCAL UNHCR

REPRESENTATIVE AND OFFICIALS OF THE ZIMBABWEAN MINISTRY

OF HOME AFFAIRS HAVE ATTEMPTED TO WASH OF THE CASE OF

THE CUBANS. THEY HAVE GIVEN CONSUL, INS OFFICER VARSANI

AND OTHER EMBASSY OFFICERS THE RUN-AROUND. WE ARE,

HOWEVER, CAUTIOUSLY OPTIMISTIC THAT WE WILL SECURE

CUSTODY OF DOCTORS CORDOVA AND PENA THE EVENING ON JUNE

10, BASED ON A CONVERSATION AMBASSADOR MCDONALD HAD WITH

MINISTER OF JUSTICE, LEGAL, AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS

EMMERSON MNANGAGWA, WHO UNDERTOOK TO EXPEDITE THE

TRANSFER PROCESS ON JUNE 10. THAT SAME DAY AMBASSADOR

MCDONALD ALSO SPOKE WITH THE KENYAN HIGH COMMISSIONER,
