SUBJECT: WHO PAYS THE PKK?

1. CONFIDENTIAL - ENTIRE TEXT.

2. SUMMARY: ETHNIC TURKISH WIFE OF ADANA AMCIT BUSINESSMAN

INFORMED ADANA POLOFF THAT HER BROTHER, OWNER OF A LARGE

RESTAURANT NEAR BURSA, WAS KIDNAPPED BY THE PKK AND TOLD TO MAKE

"FINANCIAL CONTRIBUTIONS." RELEASED UNHARMED THE NEXT DAY BUT

AWARE OF THE PKK\'S ABILITY TO EXACT REPRISALS, HE TOLD HIS WIFE

NOT TO TELL ANYONE, ESPECIALLY THE POLICE, ABOUT THE INCIDENT.

THE PKK\'S EXTORTION NETWORK NOW SEEMS TO EXTEND TO TURKISH

NATIONALS WITH FEW, IF ANY, TIES TO THE TROUBLED SOUTHEAST. END

SUMMARY.

3. AMCIT BUSINESSMAN IN ADANA, WHOSE WIFE IS AN ETHNIC TURK FROM

THE BURSA AREA OF NORTHWEST TURKEY, HOSTED A SMALL DINNER PARTY

ON SEPTEMBER 8. HIS WIFE WAS SOBBING AS SHE GREETED POLOFF AT

THE FRONT DOOR. SPEAKING IN A LOW VOICE SO THAT THE OTHER GUESTS

COULD NOT HEAR, SHE SAID THAT HER BROTHER HAD BEEN KIDNAPPED BY

THE PKK. HE OWNS A LARGE SEAFRONT RESTAURANT THAT FACES THE SEA

OF MARMARA AND HAS A PIER WHERE HIS YACHT AND OTHERS CAN DOCK.

4. THE HOSTESS\'S SISTER-IN-LAW HAD JUST CALLED TO PROVIDE THE

FOLLOWING DETAILS. ON THE MORNING OF SEPTEMBER 7, SHE AND HER

HUSBAND WERE GETTING INTO THE CAR OUTSIDE THEIR HOUSE. TWO WELL-

DRESSED MEN APPROACHED THEM. THEY TOLD HER THEY NEEDED TO SPEAK

TO HER HUSBAND PRIVATELY AND THAT NOTHING WOULD HAPPEN TO HIM

PROVIDED THAT SHE DID NOT GO TO THE AUTHORITIES. THEY THEN PUT

HER HUSBAND INTO THEIR CAR AND DROVE AWAY. SHE DID NOT CALL THE

POLICE.

5. THE NEXT MORNING HE WAS RETURNED TO HIS HOME UNHARMED. HE

HAD BEEN TAKEN BY TWO PKK OPERATIVES, HE SAID, AND DRIVEN TO A

LOCATION THAT HE COULD NOT (OR WOULD NOT) IDENTIFY. THEY TOLD

HIM THAT THEY KNEW HE WAS A RICH MAN AND THAT THEY EXPECTED HIM

TO MAKE APPROPRIATE CONTRIBUTIONS TO THE PKK. THEY CAUTIONED HIM

THAT NO HARM WOULD COME TO HIM OR HIS FAMILY IF HE COOPERATED AND

IF HE TOLD NO ONE OF THE APPROACH THEY HAD MADE TO HIM.

OTHERWISE, THEY TOLD HIM, THEY "KNEW WHERE HE LIVED." HIS WIFE

DID NOT SAY THAT HE HAD AGREED TO MAKE PAYMENTS, BUT THERE SEEMS

TO BE LITTLE DOUBT THAT HE DID SO. HE URGED HIS WIFE TO SAY

NOTHING ABOUT THE INCIDENT.

6. COMMENT: PKK EXTORTION HAS BEEN PART OF THE LANDSCAPE IN

SOUTHEAST TURKEY FOR A NUMBER OF YEARS. CONSULATE ADANA REPORTED

AS EARLY AS DECEMBER 1992 THE LIKELIHOOD THAT BUSINESSMEN AND

ELECTED OFFICIALS--EVEN MEMBERS OF THE NATIONAL COALITION\'S TRUE

PATH PARTY (DYP)--HAVE BEEN CONTRIBUTING, IN SOME CASES

UNWILLINGLY, TO THE ORGANIZATION\'S COFFERS. PKK COLLECTION

EFFORTS, AND RETRIBUTION FOR NON-PAYMENT, HAVE BECOME KNOWN IN

ADANA AS WELL. A LARGE ADANA LANDOWNER WAS TAKEN FROM HIS HOME

AND KILLED TWO YEARS AGO, APPARENTLY FOR REFUSING TO PAY THE PKK.

THE SITUATION OF THE RESTAURANTEUR NEAR BURSA IS A STRONG

INDICATION THAT THE TENTACLES OF THE PKK NOW EXTEND TO ETHNIC

TURKS WITH NO TIES EITHER TO THE SOUTHEAST OR TO THE INCREASINGLY

"KURDIFIED" ADANA-MERSIN REGION. IF HIS CASE IS NOT AN ISOLATED

ONE, IT IS YET ANOTHER INDICATION WHY THE PKK SEEMS NOT TO BE

CONSTRAINED BY A LACK OF FUNDS. END COMMENT.
