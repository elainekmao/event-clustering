SUBJECT: SYRIAN SUPPORT FOR PKK

REF: ANKARA 14197

1.(U) TURKISH SECURITY FORCES KILLED NINE PKK KURDISH

SEPARATIST TERRORISTS AND CAPTURED ONE NEAR MARDIN

TUESDAY WHILE THE TERRORISTS WERE ATTEMPTING TO ESCAPE

TO SYRIA. PRESS REPORTS CLAIM TURKISH GOVERNMENT

OFFICIALS HAVE CONCLUSIVE PROOF THAT PKK TERRORISTS ARE

BEING TRAINED IN SYRIAN CAMPS. IT IS ALSO CLAIMED

OFFICIALS KNOW WHERE THE CAMPS ARE AND WHO IS INVOLVED

IN THEIR OPERATION.

2.(C) COMMENT: THE GOT APPEARS DETERMINED TO TURN UP

THE HEAT ON SYRIAN SUPPORT FOR THE PKK. THIS LATEST

LEAK FOLLOWS BY ONE WEEK PRIME MINISTER OZAL\'S THREAT TO

CUT OFF SYRIAN WATER IF DAMASCUS CONTINUES TO BACK

SEPARATIST ACTIVITIES IN THE SOUTHEAST. END COMMENT.
