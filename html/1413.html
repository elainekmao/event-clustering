SUBJECT: ZIMBABWE LIKELY TO SUPPORT USG ON UN SCALES OF

ASSESSMENT REFORM

REF: (A) HARARE 6585, (B) STATE 218802

CLASSIFIED BY AMBASSADOR TOM MCDONALD FOR REASONS 1.5

B/D.

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 HARARE 06916 270831Z

1. (C) IN THE COURSE OF A TELEPHONE CONVERSATION ON

ANOTHER MATTER DECEMBER 7 WITH THE ZIMBABWEAN PERMANENT

REPRESENTATIVE TO THE UNITED NATIONS, TICHAONA JOKONYA,

I LEARNED THAT ZIMBABWE WOULD SUPPORT THE USG POSITION

ON UN SCALES OF REFORM. THE SENIOR ZIMBABWEAN DIPLOMAT

CLAIMED THAT HE HAD AUTONOMY TO VOTE AS HE PLEASED. HE

CONTINUED THAT ON MORE ORDINARY MATTERS ZIMBABWE FOLLOWS

THE G-77 OR NONALIGNED MOVEMENT. HOWEVER, ON IMPORTANT

QUESTIONS, SUCH AS UN SCALES OF ASSESSMENT REFORM, THE

BILATERAL RELATIONSHIP WITH THE UNITED STATES IS THE MOST

IMPORTANT FACTOR. JOKONYA EXPLAINED THAT HE HAD CONSULTED

WITH THE NAMIBIANS, WHO STILL HOLDS A SECURITY COUNCIL

SEAT FOR THE AFRICAN BLOC, AND REPORTED THAT NAMIBIA TOO

WOULD SUPPORT THE USG POSITION.

2. (C) COMMENT: I HAVE FOUND JOKONYA TO BE A RELIABLE

INTERLOCUTOR IN THE PAST. HIS SANGUINE ASSURANCES WOULD

APPEAR TO RUN COUNTER TO WHAT HIS HEADQUARTERS THINKS,

HOWEVER. IT AIN\'T OVER TILL THE FAT LADY SINGS, AND I

HOPE THIS ONE WILL PUT ON A VIRTUOSO PERFORMANCE FOR OUR

EARS WHEN THE TIME COMES. END COMMENT.

MCDONALD
