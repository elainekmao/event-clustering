SUBJECT: CONTINUED INDEPENDENCE OF ZIMBABWE LABOR

FEDERATION HINGES ON UPCOMING CONGRESS

REFS: A. HARARE 661, B. HARARE 612, C. HARARE 573,

D. 00 HARARE 3680

CLASSIFIED BY CHARGE D\' AFFAIRES EARL M. IRVING FOR

REASONS 1.5 (B/D).

1. (C) SUMMARY: THE CONTINUED INDEPENDENCE OF THE

ZIMBABWE CONGRESS OF TRADE UNIONS (ZCTU) FROM THE RULING

ZANU-PF PARTY, AND ITS SUPPORT FOR THE OPPOSITION

MOVEMENT FOR DEMOCRATIC CHANGE (MDC), HINGES ON THE

UPCOMING CONGRESS, ACCORDING TO SENIOR OFFICIALS OF THE

UMBRELLA LABOR ORGANIZATION. ZANU-PF IS ACTIVELY

ATTEMPTING TO BUY THE ELECTION OF A FRIENDLY LABOR

FEDERATION LEADERSHIP BY PAYING UNIONS\' BACK DUES TO THE

ZCTU. THE SUCCESS OF THESE EFFORTS WILL LIKELY DEPEND

ON WHETHER THE PRO-MDC FORCES WITHIN THE ZCTU ARE ABLE

TO PRESENT A UNIFIED SLATE OF CANDIDATES TO OPPOSE THOSE

BELIEVED TO BE SYMPATHETIC TO ZANU-PF. THE OUTCOME OF

THIS STRUGGLE WILL BE CRITICAL FOR THE FUTURE VIABILITY

OF THE MDC AS AN OPPOSITION PARTY, AND ITS CANDIDATE\'S

CHANCES IN THE UPCOMING PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION. END

SUMMARY.

2. (C) ON FEBRUARY 14 AND 15 LABOFF MET, RESPECTIVELY,

WITH GIFT CHIMANIKIRE AND ISAAC MATONGO TO DISCUSS THE

UPCOMING ZCTU CONGRESS, WHICH WILL BE HELD FEBRUARY 23-

24 IN MASVINGO. CHIMANIKIRE IS THE DEPUTY SECRETARY-

GENERAL OF THE MDC, A MEMBER OF THE ZCTU EXECUTIVE

COMMITTEE, AND SECRETARY-GENERAL OF THE 7,000-MEMBER

STRONG ZIMBABWE POST AND TELECOMMUNICATION WORKERS

UNION, ONE OF THE ZCTU\'S LARGER AND MORE INFLUENTIAL

UNIONS. MATONGO IS THE ACTING PRESIDENT OF THE ZCTU AND

NATIONAL CHAIRMAN OF THE MDC. BOTH MEN ARE KEY MEMBERS

OF THE MDC LEADERSHIP, AS WELL AS INFLUENTIAL MEMBERS OF

ORGANIZED LABOR IN ZIMBABWE, AND PLAYED CENTRAL ROLES IN

THE ZCTU\'S DECISION TO FORM THE MDC IN 1998.

THE ZCTU\'S INDEPENDENCE DEPENDS ON CONGRESS OUTCOME

--------------------------------------------- ------

3. (C) CHIMANIKIRE AND MATONGO WERE IN COMPLETE

AGREEMENT THAT THE FEBRUARY CONGRESS WILL BE CRUCIAL IN

DETERMINING WHETHER THE ZCTU REMAINS A TRULY INDEPENDENT

LABOR FEDERATION OR IS COOPTED BY ZANU-PF. MATONGO TOLD

LABOFF THAT ZANU-PF WAS INDEED PAYING AFFILIATED UNIONS\'

DUES ARREARAGES TO THE ZCTU IN ORDER TO INFLUENCE THEIR

VOTES AT THE UPCOMING CONGRESS. HE ADDED THAT HE DID

NOT BELIEVE THIS TACTIC WOULD BE SUCCESSFUL IN

INFLUENCING THE VOTES OF MOST OF THE UNIONS\'

REPRESENTATIVES BECAUSE THEY AND THEIR MEMBERSHIPS

REMAIN OVERWHELMINGLY PRO-MDC. CHIMANIKIRE STATED THAT

AT LEAST TWO UNION LEADERS ARE PLANNING TO REVEAL AT THE

CONGRESS THAT ZANU-PF HAD PAID THEIR UNION\'S BACK DUES.

4. (C) ACCORDING TO CHIMANIKIRE AND MATONGO, THERE IS NO

DOUBT THAT THE ACTING SECRETARY-GENERAL OF THE ZCTU,

ISIDORE ZINDOGA, IS A ZANU-PF PARTISAN. ZINDOGA, THEY

SAID, WAS AGAINST ZCTU STAYAWAYS THAT PROTESTED

GOVERNMENT OF ZIMBABWE (GOZ) POLICIES, AND OPPOSED THE

FORMATION OF THE MDC. IN ADDITION, HE HAS TRAVELED WITH

PRESIDENT MUGABE ON TRIPS OVERSEAS WITHOUT INFORMING THE

ZCTU GENERAL COUNCIL OF THE REASON FOR THESE TRIPS.

MOST RECENTLY, HE SIGNED AN AGREEMENT TO ENTER INTO A

TRIPARTITE SOCIAL CONTRACT WITH THE GOZ AND BUSINESS

WITHOUT THE APPROVAL OF THE ZCTU GENERAL COUNCIL. ON

FEBRUARY 10 THE ZCTU GENERAL COUNCIL ANNULLED THE

TRIPARTITE AGREEMENT ZINDOGA SIGNED. CHIMANIKIRE

MENTIONED THE WIDELY REPORTED SUSPICION THAT ZINDOGA IS

AN OPERATIVE OF THE GOZ CENTRAL INTELLIGENCE

ORGANIZATION (CIO), BUT OFFERED LITTLE EVIDENCE TO

SUBSTANTIATE HIS CLAIM. BOTH STATED THAT IF ZINDOGA IS

ELECTED TO HIS CURRENT POSITION, HE WOULD SEEK TO SEVER

ALL INFORMAL LINKAGES BETWEEN THE ZCTU AND THE MDC AND

PREVENT ITS SUPPORTING THE MDC IN ANY MANNER. MATONGO

ADDED THAT ZINDOGA IS THE HEAD OF A UNIFIED SLATE OF

PRO-ZANU-PF CANDIDATES THAT WILL BE NOMINATED AT THE

CONGRESS BY THEIR SUPPORTERS.

WILL THE PRO-MDC FORCES UNIFY BEHIND CANDIDATES?

5. (C) CHIMANIKIRE AND MATONGO WERE BOTH ADAMANT THAT

THE UNIONS AND THEIR MEMBERSHIPS REMAIN OVERWHELMINGLY

PRO-MDC. AT THE SAME TIME, HOWEVER, THEY EXPRESSED

CONCERN THAT IF THE PRO-MDC FORCES AT THE CONGRESS DID

NOT UNIFY BEHIND A SINGLE SLATE OF CANDIDATES, ZINDOGA

AND THE PRO-ZANU-PF SLATE COULD END UP BEING ELECTED.

CHIMANIKIRE AND MATONGO SAID A NUMBER OF QUALIFIED PRO-

MDC UNION LEADERS FEEL IT IS THEIR TIME TO ASSUME

LEADERSHIP POSITIONS IN THE ZCTU, COMPLICATING AGREEMENT

ON A SINGLE SLATE. THERE ARE THREE SUCH CANDIDATES FOR

EACH OF THE TWO MOST IMPORTANT POSITIONS IN THE ZCTU:

SECRETARY-GENERAL AND PRESIDENT. CHIMANIKIRE AND

SIPDIS

MATONGO BOTH STATED THAT THE MDC WAS WORKING

AGGRESSIVELY BEHIND THE SCENES, THROUGH PERSONAL

MEETINGS AND TELEPHONE CALLS TO THE DIFFERENT

CANDIDATES, TO SECURE AGREEMENT ON A SINGLE PRO-MDC

SLATE. MATONGO WAS CONFIDENT THAT SUCH AGREEMENT WOULD

BE REACHED BEFORE THE CONGRESS BEGINS, WHEREAS

CHIMANIKIRE BELIEVED THAT THE HORSE-TRADING INVOLVED MAY

MEAN THAT AGREEMENT WILL ONLY BE REACHED AT THE

CONGRESS. BOTH MEN AGREED THAT IF THE PRO-MDC FORCES

PRESENT A UNIFIED SLATE, THEY WILL BE ELECTED BY A

LANDSLIDE.

--------------------------------------------

THE DIRECTION OF THE ZCTU AFTER THE CONGRESS

--------------------------------------------

6. (C) IF THE PRO-ZANU-PF CANDIDATES ARE ELECTED,

CHIMANIKIRE AND MATONGO DOUBT THAT ZINDOGA WILL MAKE A

PUBLIC BREAK FROM THE MDC AND DECLARE THE ZCTU\'S

ALLEGIANCE TO ZANU-PF. RATHER, THEY BELIEVE HIS

STRATEGY WILL BE TO PREVENT THE ZCTU FROM UNDERTAKING

ANY ACTIVITIES THAT HE BELIEVES WILL AID THE MDC, SUCH

AS UNION EFFORTS AT VOTER REGISTRATION AND EDUCATION.

AT THE SAME TIME, HE WILL OPPOSE STAYAWAYS AND OTHER

ACTIONS PERCEIVED TO BE DIRECTED AT HARMING THE GOZ AND

ZANU-PF. HE WILL ALSO LIKELY PURSUE A SOCIAL CONTRACT

THAT THE GOZ AND ZANU-PF CAN TOUT AS A GREAT ADVANCE FOR

WORKERS\' INTERESTS.

7. (C) CHIMANIKIRE AND MATONGO WERE CLEAR ON WHAT WILL

OCCUR IF PRO-MDC CANDIDATES ARE ELECTED AT THE CONGRESS.

THE ZCTU LEADERSHIP WILL DECLARE THAT IT IS FORMALLY

INDEPENDENT AND AUTONOMOUS OF THE MDC, BUT IT WILL ALSO

STATE THAT THE LABOR FEDERATION CONTINUES TO SUPPORT THE

GOALS OF THE MDC. IN RECOGNITION OF ITS ROLE IN THE

FORMATION OF AND CONTINUING SUPPORT FOR THE MDC, IT WILL

ASK THE MDC TO ESTABLISH A FORMAL QUOTA WHEREBY ONE

QUARTER OF THE MDC\'S CANDIDATES FOR PARLIAMENT WOULD BE

WORKERS. THE ZCTU WILL ALSO AID THE MDC\'S ELECTION

STRATEGY BY ENGAGING IN ACTIVITIES SUCH AS VOTER

REGISTRATION, VOTER EDUCATION, GETTING OUT THE VOTE, AND

TRAINING OF ELECTION OBSERVERS. THESE STEPS WILL

ENHANCE THE CHANCES OF THE MDC CANDIDATE IN THE UPCOMING

PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION.

8. (C) COMMENT: THE UPCOMING ZCTU CONGRESS REMAINS A

CRITICAL EVENT IN ZIMBABWEAN POLITICS. PRESIDENT MUGABE

AND ZANU-PF WOULD CLEARLY LIKE TO CUT INTO ORGANIZED

LABOR\'S SUPPORT FOR THE MDC, AND THE MOST EFFECTIVE WAY

TO DO THIS WOULD BE TO SEVER THE INFORMAL CONNECTIONS

THAT EXIST BETWEEN THE ZCTU AND THE MDC. FOR THE MDC,

MAINTAINING THE ZCTU\'S SUPPORT IS CRITICAL TO BOTH ITS

CONTINUED EXISTENCE AS A VIABLE POLITICAL PARTY, AS WELL

AS ITS PRESIDENTIAL CANDIDATE\'S CHANCES IN 2002. IF THE

PRO-MDC FORCES ARE ABLE TO PRESENT A UNIFIED SLATE OF

CANDIDATES, WE BELIEVE IT LIKELY THAT THEY WILL BE

ELECTED, AND THE ZCTU WILL CONTINUE TO SUPPORT THE MDC.

IF THEY CANNOT UNIFY BEFORE THE VOTE AT THE CONGRESS,

MUGABE AND ZANU-PF ARE LIKELY TO SUCCEED AT BUYING THE

ELECTION OF PRO-ZANU-PF CANDIDATES. SUCH AN OUTCOME

WOULD GREATLY HINDER THE MDC\'S CHANCES IN THE UPCOMING

PRESIDENTIAL ELECTIONS. END COMMENT.
