SUBJECT: ANDORRA TO OPEN EMBASSY TO THE VATICAN

CLASSIFIED BY: AMBASSADOR JIM NICHOLSON FOR REASONS 1.5 (A) AND (D),

1.(C) ANDORRAN FOREIGN MINISTER JOLI MINOVES INVITED DCM TO

LUNCH FRIDAY, MARCH 1. MINOVES SAID EARLIER THAT DAY HE HAD

MET WITH BOTH VATICAN FOREIGN MINISTER TAURAN AND PRIME

MINISTER SODANO. MINOVES SAID THAT THE ONLY OUTSTANDING

BILATERAL ISSUE, THE SUCCESSION OF THE ARCHBISHOP OF URGELL

AND THUS THE CO-PRINCE OF ANDORRA, WAS RESOLVED WITH THE

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 VATICA 01176 061554Z

APPOINTMENT OF BISHOP VIVES SICILIA AS COADJUTOR WITH RIGHT

OF SUCCESSION.

2.(C) MINOVES VOLUNTEERED THAT ANDORRA PLANNED TO OPEN AN

EMBASSY TO THE VATICAN (AND CO-LOCATE IT WITH ONE TO ROME)

WITHIN THE NEXT YEAR. THIS WILL BE ANDORRA\'S EIGHTH (AND

NINTH) OVERSEAS EMBASSY. NICHOLSON
