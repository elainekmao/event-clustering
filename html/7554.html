SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION: DEMONSTRATIONS IN IRAQ U.S. THREATS TO

SYRIA RIYADH MEETING OF ARAB FOREIGN MINISTERS.

1. SUMMARY: ANTI-AMERICAN DEMONSTRATIONS IN BAGHDAD, SYRIA\'S

RESPONSE TO U.S. THREATS, AND THE ARAB FOREIGN MINISTER\'S MEETING

IN RIYADH DOMINATED NEWS COVERAGE IN UAE MEDIA. EDITORIALS

FOCUSED ON THE CREATION OF "AN IRAQ FOR IRAQIS." AN "AL BAYAN"

EDITORIAL ASSERTED THAT THE IRAQI PEOPLE WILL REFUSE ANY U.S.

MANDATE FOR IRAQ. AN "AL KHALEEJ" OP-ED BY DR. MOHAMMED EDREES

ALLEGED THAT U.S.\'S THREATS AGAINST SYRIA ARE PART OF ISRAEL\'S

AGENDA. END SUMMARY.

2. CLAIMING THAT IRAQ CAN ONLY BE FOR IRAQIS, SHARJAH-BASED PAN-

ARAB DAILY "AL-KHALEEJ" EDITORIALIZED 4/19:

"... WILL IRAQIS BE ALLOWED TO HAVE WHAT THEY ARE STRIVING FOR?

THIS IS THE MAIN QUESTION NOW, FOR WHICH THE ARABS MUST STRIVE,

BUT THIS WILL NOT BE POSSIBLE IF GEORGE BUSH AND TONY BLAIR HOLD

ON TO THEIR GOALS, WHICH REQUIRE A LONG OCCUPATION OF IRAQ ..."

3. UNDER THE HEADLINE "OCCUPATION OR LIBERATION?!," DUBAI-BASED

BUSINESS-ORIENTED ARABIC DAILY "AL-BAYAN" EDITORIALIZED 4/19:

"... THE IRAQI PEOPLE WILL REFUSE ANY AMERICAN MANDATE AND REFUSE

ANY GOVERNMENT THAT IS CONTROLLED BY THE U.S. THERE IS NOW AN

ARAB STANCE SUPPORTING THE DEMANDS OF THE IRAQI PEOPLE. WE HOPE

THAT THE U.S. WILL PROVE THAT ITS GOALS ARE NOT OCCUPATION OR

EXPANSION, AND THAT IT REALIZES THE IRAQI PEOPLE, ALONG WITH THE

ARAB WORLD, WILL NEVER ACCEPT FOREIGN OCCUPATION ..."

4. ALLEGING THAT THE U.S. THREATS AGAINST SYRIA ARE PART OF

ISRAEL\'S AGENDA, COLUMNIST DR. MOHAMMED EDREES WROTE IN "AL

KHALEEJ" ON 4/19:

"... SHARON\'S SPECIAL AGENDA REGARDING SYRIA AND PRESIDENT ASAD

WILL BE RELAYED BY THE U.S. SECRETARY OF STATE WHEN HE VISITS

DAMASCUS, OR WILL BE DELIVERED THROUGH DIPLOMATIC CHANNELS.

WHOEVER IS FOLLOWING UP ON THIS AGENDA SAYS THAT THE FUTURE OF

SYRIA DEPENDS ON ITS ACCEPTANCE OR REFUSAL OF THIS AGENDA.

SHARON THINKS THAT HE CAN IMPOSE HIS TERMS ON THE U.S.

ADMINISTRATION, AS MOST OF ITS HAWKS OWE LOYALTY TO ISRAEL BEFORE

THE U.S. AND HAVE CONVERTED THE U.S. ADMINISTRATION TO AN

\'ISRAELI MILITARY OPERATION ROOM.\'"

5. COMMENTING ON THE NEEDS OF THE IRAQI PEOPLE VERSUS THE

DOSSIERS OF THE IRAQI OPPOSITION, "AL KHALEEJ" EDITORIALIZED

4/18:

"... THE IRAQI PEOPLE WANT IRAQ, AND NOW AFTER THREE DECADES OF

OPPRESSION, THEY CRY OUT ONLY FOR IRAQ. AS FOR THE (IRAQI)

OPPOSITION, THEY WANT WHAT THE U.S. WANTS. THERE IS A BIG

DIFFERENCE BETWEEN AN IRAQ FOR IRAQIS AND IRAQ FOR THE U.S. WILL

THE OPPOSITION GET THEIR TRUE COMPASS, AND IS THAT THE COMPASS OF

THEIR COUNTRY AND NOTHING ELSE?"
