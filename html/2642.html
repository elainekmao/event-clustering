SUBJECT: ZIMBABWE: HOW MUGABE STOLE THE ELECTION;

WIDESPREAD RETRIBUTION UNDERWAY

REF: A) HARARE 661, B) HARARE 644

CLASSIFIED BY POLITICAL OFFICER TODD FAULK FOR REASONS:

1.5 (B) AND (D).

1. (C) SUMMARY: A WIDE VARIETY OF REPORTS OVER THE LAST

SEVERAL DAYS INDICATE THAT PRESIDENT MUGABE\'S RULING

PARTY ENGAGED IN MASSIVE FRAUD DURING THE MARCH 9-11

PRESIDENTIAL POLL, ENABLING HIM TO CLAIM VICTORY. MANY

OF THE NUMBERS DO NOT ADD UP, AND INFLATED RESULTS IN

THE MASHONALAND PROVINCES AND OTHER AREAS CORRELATE WITH

REPORTS OF POLICE AND ZANU-PF MILITIA DETAINING MDC

POLLING AGENTS. SINCE THE ELECTION, RULING PARTY

MILITIA, WAR VETERANS, AND FARM SQUATTERS HAVE ENGAGED

IN WIDESPREAD RETRIBUTION AGAINST MDC POLLING AGENTS AND

OTHER OPPOSITION SUPPORTERS; AT LEAST FIVE MDC

SUPPORTERS HAVE BEEN KILLED SINCE THE ELECTION RESULTS

WERE ANNOUNCED ON MARCH 13. END SUMMARY.

------------------------

THE NUMBERS DON\'T ADD UP

------------------------

2. (C) SERIOUS DOUBTS ABOUT THE LEGITIMACY OF THE

ELECTION RESULTS HAVE ARISEN SINCE REGISTRAR-GENERAL

TOBAIWA MUDEDE ANNOUNCED THEM ON MARCH 13. HIS FIGURES

ARE AT ODDS WITH THOSE RELEASED BY THE ELECTORAL

SUPERVISORY COMMISSION (ESC). ON MARCH 12, THE ESC

RELEASED PROVINCE-BY-PROVINCE FIGURES FOR THE NUMBER OF

VOTES CAST, TOTALING 3.17 MILLION VOTES. ON MARCH 13,

MUDEDE\'S FIGURES FOR VALID VOTES, RELEASED ON A

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 HARARE 00713 01 OF 04 191203Z

CONSTITUENCY-BY-CONSTITUENCY BASIS, ADD UP TO ONLY 3.03

MILLION, A DIFFERENCE OF 140,000 VOTES. THE NUMBER OF

SPOILED BALLOTS ADDS UP TO ABOUT 48,000. THE QUESTION

BECOMES: WHAT HAPPENED TO THE OTHER 92,000 VOTES

BETWEEN THE TIME THE ESC RELEASED ITS FIGURES AND THE RG

ANNOUNCED ITS RESULTS?

3. (C) IN A MARCH 18 "DAILY NEWS" ARTICLE, THE MDC\'S

DIRECTOR OF ELECTIONS, PAUL THEMBA-NYATHI, CLAIMED THAT

CONSTITUENCY FIGURES GIVEN TO THE MDC BY THE ESC ARE

STARKLY AT ODDS WITH THE OFFICIAL RESULTS. FOR EXAMPLE,

IN BUBI-UMGUZA DISTRICT IN MATABELELAND NORTH, THE ESC

SAID 46,993 PEOPLE VOTED, BUT THE RG SAID ONLY 29,340

VOTED, A DIFFERENCE OF 17,653. MOREOVER, MUGABE

OBTAINED NEARLY 10,000 MORE VOTES THAN ZANU-PF DID IN

THE 2000 PARLIAMENTARY ELECTION, GIVING HIM A

COMFORTABLE LEAD OF 4,500 VOTES OVER TSVANGIRAI. BUBI-

UMGUZA VOTED OVERWHELMINGLY FOR THE MDC IN 2000 (BY A 2-

TO-1 MARGIN). IT IS DIFFICULT TO IMAGINE THAT AN

NDEBELE DISTRICT OF SUCH PRO-MDC SYMPATHIES WOULD MAKE

SUCH AN ABOUT-FACE IN THE FACE OF STARVATION AND GREATER

ECONOMIC HARDSHIP. NEARBY TSHOLOTSHO DISTRICT

EXPERIENCED A SIMILAR REVERSAL OF FORTUNE BY GIVING

MUGABE 700 MORE VOTES THAN TSVANGIRAI.

4. (C) THE NUMBERS IN MANY DISTRICTS OF MASHONALAND AND

MASVINGO EQUALLY DEFY BELIEF. IN 24 CONSTITUENCIES IN

MASHONALAND EAST, CENTRAL, WEST AND MASVINGO, ZANU-PF\'S

TOTALS INCREASED BY 5,000 TO 10,000 VOTES PER

CONSTITUENCY OVER 2000, WHEREAS THE MDC\'S NUMBERS

REMAINED MOSTLY UNCHANGED OR DECLINED. SOME OF THE

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 04 HARARE 00713 01 OF 04 191203Z

INCREASES MAY BE "NATURAL," DUE TO GREATER NAME

RECOGNITION FOR MUGABE, ZANU-PF VOTER MOBILIZATION

DRIVES, AND MORE MOBILE POLLING STATIONS FOR RESETTLED

FARMERS. IN FACT, GREATER VOTER MOBILIZATION PLAYED A

ROLE IN THE INCREASES OF SEVERAL THOUSAND ZANU-PF VOTES

IN THE BINDURA, CHIKOMBA, AND BIKITA WEST BY-ELECTIONS

LAST YEAR. EACH OF THESE BY-ELECTIONS OCCURRED

INDIVIDUALLY AND ZANU-PF WAS ABLE TO FOCUS ITS RESOURCES

ON THEM. HOWEVER, THIS YEAR, WHEN CHIKOMBA SEES 10,000

MORE ZANU-PF VOTES AND FEWER THAN 250 MORE FOR THE MDC,

FOR EXAMPLE, VOTER MOBILIZATION IS NOT A LIKELY

EXPLANATION, ESPECIALLY WHEN THIS FEAT IS REPEATED IN AT

LEAST 40 OTHER CONSTITUENCIES NATIONWIDE AT THE SAME

TIME. IN REALITY, IT WOULD BE MUCH EASIER TO SWITCH OR

ADD ADDITIONAL BALLOT BOXES THAN TO GET AN ADDITIONAL

200,000 PEOPLE TO THE POLLS. IT SHOULD BE NOTED THAT

NOWHERE IN THE COUNTRY DID TSVANGIRAI SEE SUCH A WILD

INCREASE IN SUPPORT, EXCEPT IN CHIPINGE SOUTH WHERE THE

ZANU-NDONGA VOTERS THREW THEIR SUPPORT TO TSVANGIRAI

WHEN THEIR PARTY BOWED OUT OF THE RACE.

5. (C) WHEN INFLATED NUMBERS IN THE MASHONALAND AND

MASVINGO CONSTITUENCIES ARE CORRELATED WITH REPORTS OF

MDC POLLING AGENTS BEING FORCED TO FLEE DURING THE POLL,

IT BECOMES CLEAR THAT ZANU-PF HAD AMPLE OPPORTUNITY TO

STUFF THE BALLOT BOXES IN THOSE PROVINCES, AND SOME

DISTRICTS IN OTHER PROVINCES. IT SHOULD BE REMEMBERED

THAT THE RG WAS VERY LATE IN ANNOUNCING THE RESULTS FOR

MASHONALAND CENTRAL, THE PROVINCE WITH THE HIGHEST

NUMBER OF MUGABE VOTES OF ALL (REF A). ON MARCH 15, TWO

MDC POLLING AGENTS, PHAINOS JASI AND JACK KANKUNI

(PROTECT), APPROACHED US TO RELAY WHAT HAPPENED TO THEM

AS THEY OBSERVED THE ELECTION IN MOUNT DARWIN SOUTH AND

MUZARABANI CONSTITUENCIES, RESPECTIVELY (BOTH ARE IN

MASHONALAND CENTRAL). THE POLLING DAYS WENT SMOOTHLY,

BOTH SAID, BUT JUST AS THEY WERE ABOUT TO ACCOMPANY THE

BALLOT BOXES TO THE COUNTING CENTER ON THE MORNING OF

MARCH 11, A TRUCKLOAD OF WAR VETS AND YOUTH MILITIA

ARRIVED. IN MOUNT DARWIN SOUTH, ABOUT 20 YOUTHS CHASED

JASI TO A POLICE STATION, WHERE HE HID FOR SEVERAL HOURS

WHILE THE YOUTHS OUTSIDE DEMANDED "HIS HEAD." HE NEVER

SAW THE BALLOT BOX AGAIN AND EVENTUALLY ESCAPED TO

HARARE. JASI SAID ALL OF THE 24 OTHER MDC POLLING

AGENTS IN MOUNT DARWIN SOUTH WERE CHASED OFF AS WELL;

ONE OF THEM IS STILL MISSING. MOUNT DARWIN SOUTH SAW AN

INCREASE OF 8,500 VOTES FOR MUGABE AND A DROP OF 800 FOR

TSVANGIRAI.

6. (C) IN MUZARABANI, KANKUNI WAS NOT SO LUCKY. WHEN

HE WAS ABOUT TO ACCOMPANY THE BALLOT BOX FROM CHOBOMBO

VILLAGE TO THE COUNTING CENTER IN CENTENARY, A GROUP OF

WAR VETS INTERCEPTED HIM AND TOLD HIM TO GO TO THE

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 HARARE 00713 02 OF 04 191203Z

POLICE STATION IN CENTENARY. ABOUT 30 OTHER POLLING

AGENTS WERE SIMILARLY DIVERTED TO THE POLICE. SHORTLY

AFTER THEY ARRIVED, ABOUT 300 YOUTH AND WAR VETS

ATTACKED THEM WITH STONES AND AX HANDLES, KANKUNI SAID

(MUCH OF THE SKIN ON KANKUNI\'S FACE HAD BEEN SCRAPED

AWAY BY STONES). THE POLICE REFUSED TO INTERVENE, AND

ALL OF THE POLLING AGENTS FLED INTO THE BUSH. KANKUNI

SPENT A NIGHT IN A LOCAL SHELTER BEFORE MAKING HIS WAY

BACK TO HARARE. MUZURABANI SAW AN INCREASE OF 5,800

VOTES FOR MUGABE AND AN INCREASE OF 250 FOR TSVANGIRAI.

7. (C) THE DETENTION OR KIDNAPPING OF MANY OPPOSITION

POLLING AGENTS AND THE REFUSAL TO ACCREDIT MOST DOMESTIC

ELECTION OBSERVERS PREVENTED ADEQUATE COVERAGE OF MANY

RURAL AREAS, ESPECIALLY IN ZANU-PF STRONGHOLDS. AMNESTY

INTERNATIONAL REPORTED ON MARCH 14 THAT A TOTAL OF 1,400

PEOPLE WERE ARRESTED OR DETAINED OVER THE POLLING

WEEKEND; MANY WERE ELECTION OBSERVERS OR POLLING AGENTS.

THE ZIMBABWE REPUBLIC POLICE CLAIMED THAT 570 WERE

ARRESTED IN THE SAME PERIOD FOR VIOLATING THE PUBLIC

ORDER AND SECURITY ACT (ALL BUT TWO OF THEM FROM THE

MDC). LINDSAY CAMPBELL, A COMMERCIAL FARMER AND MDC

ACTIVIST NEAR MARONDERA IN MASHONALAND EAST, TOLD POLOFF

THAT TWO POLLING AGENTS AND FIVE OTHER ELECTION

OFFICIALS WERE DETAINED OR KIDNAPPED IN HER AREA OVER

THE POLLING WEEKEND; FOUR ARE STILL MISSING.

8. (U) THE MDC ALSO CLAIMS THAT THE GOVERNMENT LEFT THE

VOTERS ROLL IN A DELIBERATE SHAMBLES TO FACILITATE

ELECTORAL FRAUD. THE MDC COMMISSIONED AN INDEPENDENT

AUDIT OF THE INTERIM VOTERS ROLL THAT MUDEDE PROVIDED

THEM IN JANUARY (THE MDC WAS NEVER PROVIDED THE FINAL

ROLL). THE AUDIT FOUND THAT BETWEEN 30 AND 40 PERCENT

ON THE ROLL HAD MOVED OUT OF THEIR CONSTITUENCY OR DIED.

IN FACT, IN A RANDOM CHECK OF 500 VOTERS WHOSE DEATHS

9. (C) THE MDC CLAIMS THAT MEMBERS OF THE POLICE AND

CIO POSED AS MDC POLLING AGENTS OR OTHER ELECTION

OFFICIALS IN MANY AREAS. WE HAVE REASON TO BELIEVE THIS

INDEED OCCURRED. OUR TEAM THAT WAS DETAINED BY THE

CHINHOYI POLICE (REF B) REPORTED BEING QUESTIONED

AGGRESSIVELY BY THREE MEN IN THE POLICE STATION WEARING

ESC VESTS. WHEN ASKED WHY THE ESC REQUIRED THEIR

IDENTIFICATION AND WHAT THE ESC\'S ROLE WAS IN A POLICE

MATTER, THE THREE INITIALLY ADMITTED TO BEING POLICE

OFFICERS, BUT LATER DENIED IT. ALSO, WHEN ASKED WHO

THEIR SUPERIORS WERE, THE THREE INDICATED THE COMMANDING

OFFICER OF THE POLICE STATION.

11. (C) WAR VETERANS, ZANU-PF MILITIA, AND FARM

SQUATTERS ARE ENACTING WIDESPREAD RETRIBUTION AGAINST

COMMERCIAL FARMERS, THEIR WORKERS, POLLING AGENTS AND

OTHER MDC SUPPORTERS. SINCE MARCH 13, AT LEAST FIVE

OPPOSITION SUPPORTERS HAVE BEEN KILLED. FRANCES

LOVEMORE, THE ACTING DIRECTOR OF THE AMANI TRUST, A

WELL-RESPECTED HUMAN RIGHTS ORGANIZATION IN ZIMBABWE,

REPORTED TO POLOFF THAT HER OFFICE WAS OVERWHELMED OVER

THE WEEKEND WITH VICTIMS OF TORTURE AND DISPLACEMENT.

AT LEAST 84 FARM WORKERS AND 22 POLLING AGENTS SOUGHT

REFUGE WITH AMANI TRUST OVER THE WEEKEND. LOVEMORE ALSO

REPORTED A LARGE UPSWING IN THE NUMBER OF PEOPLE BEING

DETAINED BY ZANU-PF MILITIA, WHO APPARENTLY HAVE

REOPENED "RE-EDUCATION" CAMPS FOR WAYWARD MDC MEMBERS.

LOVEMORE STATED THAT AMANI TRUST PLANS TO ASK THE

INTERNATIONAL COMMITTEE OF THE RED CROSS (ICRC) IN

ZIMBABWE TO SET UP A REFUGEE CAMP; AMANI CAN NO LONGER

COPE WITH THE NUMBER THAT HAVE BEEN DISPLACED.
