SUBJECT: ZIMBABWE: 1994 ANNUAL TERRORISM REPORT

REF: A) STATE 286744 B) STATE 298865

1. FORMAT PER REFTEL:

(A) PROSECUTIONS INVOLVING INTERNATIONAL TERRORISM

AND/OR SIGNIFICANT ACTS OF DOMESTIC TERRORISM:

THERE WERE NO TERRORIST INCIDENTS OR TERRORIST

RELATED PROSECUTIONS IN ZIMBABWE IN 1994.

(B) EXTRADITION OF SUSPECTED TERRORISTS:

THERE WERE NO CASES INVOLVING EXTRADITION OF

SUSPECTED TERRORISTS IN ZIMBABWE IN 1994.

(C) IMPEDIMENTS TO PROSECUTION OR EXTRADITION OF

SUSPECTED TERRORISTS:

THE CURRENT ABSENCE OF EXTRADITION TREATIES WITH

THE US IS THE ONLY IDENTIFIABLE IMPEDIMENT TO

PROSECUTION OR EXTRADITION OF SUSPECTED TERRORISTS.

HOWEVER, FUNCTIONING (THOUGH UNRATIFIED) BORDER

REPATRIATION ARRANGEMENTS WITH ITS NEIGHBORS AND

OUR RELATIVELY SMOOTH BILATERAL NEGOTIATIONS WHICH

SHOULD RESULT IN A SIGNED EXTRADITION TREATY BY MAY

1995, INDICATE ZIMBABWE WOULD ACT RESPONSIBLY IN A

CASE INVOLVING INTERNATIONAL TERRORISM.

D. HOST COUNTRY RESPONSES OTHER THAN PROSECUTION:

ZIMBABWE OFFERED NO OFFICIAL STATEMENTS AND TOOK NO

SPECIFIC ACTION IN RESPONSE TO TERRORISM IN 1994.

E. MAJOR COUNTERTERRORISM EFFORTS IN 1994:

ZIMBABWE UNDERTOOK NO MAJOR COUNTERTERRORIST

EFFORTS THIS YEAR.

F. SUPPORT FOR INTERNATIONAL TERRORISM:

THOUGH ZIMBABWE MAINTAINS DIPLOMATIC RELATIONS WITH

THE SEVEN COUNTRIES IDENTIFIED AS SPONSORING

INTERNATIONAL TERRORISM (LIBYA, NORTH KOREA, IRAN

AND SUDAN MAINTAIN MISSIONS IN HARARE), THERE IS NO

EVIDENCE THE GOVERNMENT OF ZIMBABWE SUPPORTS OR

CONDONES TERRORIST ACTS PERPETRATED BY THESE

COUNTRIES.

G. PUBLIC STATEMENTS IN SUPPORT OF TERRORISM:

THE GOVERNMENT OF ZIMBABWE ISSUED NO STATEMENTS IN

SUPPORT OF TERRORIST ACTIVITIES IN 1994.

H. SIGNIFICANT CHANGES IN ATTITUDE TOWARDS

TERRORISM SINCE 1993:

IN RECENT YEARS THE GOVERNMENT OF ZIMBABWE HAS BEEN

GENERALLY COOPERATIVE IN ITS APPROACH TO MATTERS OF

INTERNATIONAL TERRORISM -- PARTICULARLY WHEN THERE

IS A PERCEIVED NATIONAL INTEREST. THERE IS NO

REASON TO BELIEVE ZIMBABWEAN ATTITUDE TOWARDS

TERRORISM HAS CHANGED IN THE PAST YEAR.
