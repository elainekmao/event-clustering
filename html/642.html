THE WITNESS

2. (LOU) SENAHID CAVALIC (STRICTLY PROTECT; WITNESS

STILL HAS RELATIVES BEING HELD IN BRCKO), HEREAFTER

REFERRED TO AS THE WITNESS, IS A BOSNIAN MUSLIM FROM THE

BRCKO MUNICIPALITY. HE WAS BORN FEBRUARY 21, 1958, AND

WAS A CIVILIAN DRIVER FOR THE BIMEX COMPANY AT THE TIME

OF HIS ARREST BY BOSNIAN SERB FORCES ON MAY 4, 1992.

---------------------

THE WITNESS\'S ACCOUNT

---------------------

3. (U) AT THE TIME OF HIS ARREST, THE WITNESS WAS

LOOKING FOR HIS WIFE AND CHILDREN, WHO HAD NOT RETURNED

TO HIS APARTMENT IN THE BRCKO "SETTLEMENT" OF MERAJE. HE

FOUND THEM IN A GROUP OF PEOPLE BEING ROUNDED UP BY SERB

SOLDIERS IN A CORNER OF THE SETTLEMENT. ALL THESE

PEOPLE, INCLUDING THE WITNESS, WERE TAKEN TO THE FRONT OF

THE HOSPITAL WHERE THE WOMEN AND CHILDREN WERE TAKEN AWAY

TO AN UNKNOWN DESTINATION IN BUSES. THE MEN WERE TAKEN

TO THE CELLAR WHERE THEY WERE ORDERED TO WRITE STATEMENTS

AS TO WHERE THEY HAD HIDDEN WEAPONS. THEY WERE BEATEN

ARMY BARRACKS. THEN, LATER IN THE EVENING, THEY WERE

1992.

4. (U) FROM MAY 9-11, 1992, THE WITNESS AND THREE OTHER

MEN HAD TO LOAD DEAD BODIES INTO THE FURNACE AT THE

HOSPITAL. THE BODIES HAD BEEN CUT UP AND PLACED INTO

50-KILOGRAM NYLON SACKS. THE WITNESS SAW BODY PARTS FROM

MEN, WOMEN, AND CHILDREN, MOST WITH CIVILIAN CLOTHING.

THE SACKS WERE BROUGHT TO THE HOSPITAL IN SMALL TRUCKS.

THE WITNESS PERSONALLY LOADED ABOUT 200 SUCH SACKS INTO

THE FURNACE DURING TWO DAYS AND TWO NIGHTS. WHEN HIS

GROUP LEFT THE HOSPITAL, THEY WERE REPLACED BY ANOTHER

GROUP OF FOUR. THE MEN WERE ALWAYS UNDER ARMED GUARD.

7. (U) THE WITNESS FREQUENTLY PASSED THE BIMEX SWINE

BREEDING FARM ON HIS WAY TO FARMS WHERE HE PERFORMED

FORCED LABOR. THERE WERE THREE LARGE PITS TO WHICH

BODIES WERE TAKEN IN A TAM TRUCK WITH LICENSE NUMBER

BC-519-15, DRIVEN BY ONE ACO STEVANOVIC. THE PITS WERE

ON THE WAY FROM BRCKO TO THE BIMEX FARM THREE KILOMETERS

EAST OF BRCKO. THE WITNESS GAVE THE FOLLOWING

INSTRUCTIONS FOR LOCATING THE SITE: DESCEND A HILL

TOWARD A BROOK AND STOP JUST BEFORE CROSSING THE BRIDGE.

ON THE RIGHT SIDE, OUTSIDE THE FENCED AREA OF THE FARM

AND ALONGSIDE THE BROOK, IS THE SITE. JUST BEYOND THE

SITE IS A GAS STATION THAT USED TO BE OWNED BY A MAN

NAMED MUSTRAFOVIC. THE WITNESS SAID THE PITS WERE AT

LEAST 15 METERS LONG.
