SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION WAR AGAINST TERROR; HARARE

1. MAINSTREAM NEWSPAPERS CONTINUE TO DEDICATE

SIGNIFICANT SPACE TO WIRE ARTICLES BY REUTERS, AGENCE

FRANCE PRESSE, AND THE ASSOCIATED PRESS CENTERING ON

THE U.S.-LED MILITARY STRIKES IN AFGHANISTAN. THE

NATIONAL STATE-RUN TELEVISION STATION, ZTV I, IS

CARRYING CABLE NEWS NETWORK FOOTAGE ON THE MILITARY

CAMPAIGN. THE NEWS CLIPS ARE BROADCAST DAILY DURING

ZTV\'S MAIN NEWS PROGRAMS IN ENGLISH AND THE TWO MAJOR

VERNACULAR LANGUAGES - SHONA AND NDEBELE. SIMILAR NEWS

BULLETINS ARE ALSO BEING AIRED ON ALL THE FOUR NATIONAL

STATE-CONTROLLED RADIO STATIONS.

2. MEANWHILE, MOST NEWSPAPERS CONTINUE TO PUBLISH

OPINION PIECES CENTERING ON THE WAR AGAINST TERROR.

SAMPLES OF THE ARTICLES FOLLOW:

3. UNDER HEADLINE "IMPERIAL AMERICA CHASES OWN

SHADOW: BY REFUSING TO ACCEPT REALITY, U. S. PROBLEMS

WILL MULTIPLY," THE OCTOBER 14 EDITION OF THE

GOVERNMENT-CONTROLLED WEEKLY "THE SUNDAY MAIL" CARRIED

THE FOLLOWING OPINION PIECE BY MWESIGA BAREGU:

". . . IF THE U. S. AND WEST IN GENERAL COULD VIEW

THE TRAGIC EVENTS OF SEPTEMBER 11 AS A WAKE-UP CALL. .

. AND USE THE OCCASION AS A MIRROR TO TAKE A KEEN LOOK

AT THEMSELVES THEN THE DEATHS OF THE VICTIMS WILL NOT

HAVE BEEN IN VAIN. . . IN THIS PIECE I WANT TO ARGUE

THAT AMERICA IS IN DENIAL WITH RESPECT TO A NUMBER OF

ILLS THAT AFFLICT IT. MOREOVER AMERICA LACKS THE

COURAGE TO ACKNOWLEDGE AND CONFRONT THESE ILLS BECAUSE.

. . ITS DENIAL ARISES FROM ITS SELF-LOVE AND A

PERSECUTION COMPLEX. . . AS TO THE WAR IN AFGHANISTAN

MY JURY IS STILL OUT. IT IS NOT AT ALL CLEAR WHAT THE

ANGLO-AMERICAN WAR AIMS ARE. WHAT IS BECOMING

4. UNDER HEADLINE "BEYOND BIN LADEN: A WIDER AGENDA;

UK, U.S. USE \'TERROR WAR\' AS OPPORTUNITY FOR

EXPANSIONISM," THE SAME PAPER CARRIED THE FOLLOWING

ARTICLE BY TAFATAONA MAHOSO:

"RESOLUTION 1373 IS THE SECURITY COUNCIL\'S RESPONSE

TO THE SEPTEMBER 11 ATTACKS ON NEW YORK AND WASHINGTON.

BUT THE DIPLOMATIC SOURCES IN HARARE SAID THE REAL

PROBLEM FOR THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY WAS THAT THE U.

S. AND UK NOW SAW THE RECENT TERRORIST ATTACKS ON NEW

YORK AND WASHINGTON AS YET ANOTHER OPPORTUNITY TO

ACQUIRE NEW MILITARY BASES IN CENTRAL ASIA AND TO

OVERTHROW GOVERNMENTS WHICH HAVE REFUSED TO TOE THE

ANGLO-AMERICAN LINE. . . THOSE WARNING THE WORLD

AGAINST THE BUSH-BLAIR ADVENTURE IN AFGHANISTAN ARE NOT

JUST CRITICIZING THE CHOICE OF RESPONSE TO TERRORISM IN

THIS CASE. THEY ARE OFFERING ALTERNATIVE RESPONSES,

WHICH INCLUDE: CALLING A SPECIAL SESSION OF THE GENERAL

ASSEMBLY OF THE U. N. TO CONSIDER THE LINK BETWEEN THE

DETERIORATION OF INTERNATIONAL RELATIONS AND THE

ESCALATION OF TERRORISM; CALLING FOR SPECIAL WORLD

FORUM OF RELIGIOUS LEADERS AND RELIGIOUS FAITHS IN

ORDER TO HARNESS THE POSITIVE INFLUENCE OF RELIGION IN

CONVENING A SPECIAL CONFERENCE FOR THOSE COUNTRIES AND

LEARNING TO DEAL WITH PARANOIA THAT ARISES FROM

6. THE OCTOBER 12 EDITION OF THE INDEPENDENT WEEKLY

"THE ZIMBABWE MIRROR" RAN THE FOLLOWING ARTICLE BY THE

"SCRUTATOR":

". . .ONLY THE MAD AND SENSELESS COULD HAVE REMAINED

UNTOUCHED BY THE TERROR AND HORROR OF 11 SEPTEMBER,

ESPECIALLY WHEN SO MANY INNOCENT LIVES WERE LOST IN

NEW YORK, WASHINGTON AND PITTSBURGH. BUT IT IS THE

REACTION OF THE U.S.A. AND ITS ALLIES THAT IS NOW

CAUSING THE SYMPATHY AND COMPASSION TO DISSIPATE SO

DANGEROUSLY. . . EVEN THE PRINCIPLES OF SOVEREIGNTY

AND INTERNATIONAL LAW ARE BEING BREACHED WITH

IMPUNITY; GONE, TOO, THE IDEA THAT INTERNATIONAL

PEACE AND SECURITY SHOULD BE SUSTAINED AND PROMOTED

UNDER THE AUSPICES OF THE U. N. SADLY, THE LATTER

IS NOW LARGELY FOR THE EXPEDIENT AND CONVENIENT USE

U. S., JAPAN AND EUROPE, AND THEN REBOUND TO 4.3

PERCENT IN 2002. BUT BECAUSE THE ATTACKS WILL DELAY
