SUMMIT FOR VATICAN DFM MIGLIORE BY NATO SPECIALISTS BRUCE

JACKSON AND JULIE FINLEY. JACKSON EXHORTED A PUBLIC PAPAL

ENDORSEMENT IN THE LEAD-UP TO PRAGUE.

VATICAN RUSSIA RELATIONS. RUSSIAN SAID THAT RECENT VATICAN

ACTIONS HAVE SET BACK RELATIONS &20-YEARS8.

-- ON MARCH 12, DCM FOLLOWED UP WITH MEETING WITH PASTOR OF

RUSSIAN ORTHODOX CHURCH IN ROME AND DE FACTO ORTHODOX REP TO

THE VATICAN WHO SAID THAT DIALOG, ON A POLITICAL LEVEL,

6. (C) POLITICAL SECTION MEETINGS:

-- POLOFFS MET 03/04 WITH SECRETARY OF THE CONGREGATION FOR

THE EVANGELIZATION OF PEOPLES, ARCHBISHOP ROBERT SARAH, A

GUINEAN, TO DISCUSS OBSTACLES TO THE CHURCH\'S EVANGELIZATION

AND RELIEF/DEVELOPMENT WORK IN AFRICA. SARAH, JUST BACK FROM

A VISIT TO THE DEMOCRATIC REPUBLIC OF THE CONGO, WAS WORRIED

ABOUT KILLINGS OF CATHOLIC CLERGY IN THE DRC.

-- POLOFF MET 03/07 WITH PONTIFICAL COUNCIL FOR

INTER-RELIGIOUS DIALOGUE AFRICA SPECIALIST DON DENIS CHIDI, A

NIGERIAN. CHIDI WAS MUCH MORE POSITIVE ABOUT THE CHURCH\'S

FUTURE IN AFRICA THAN THE EUROPEAN VATICAN INTERLOCUTORS POST

HAS DISCUSSED THIS WITH IN RECENT WEEKS. THE LATTER ARE

QUICKER TO PERCEIVE A CHALLENGE FROM FUNDAMENTALIST ISLAM

THROUGHOUT THE CONTINENT.
