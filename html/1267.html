1. (C) AS PART OF A SERIES OF CALLS BEING MADE ON

THE NEWLY APPOINTED CABINET, ON AUGUST 30 AMBASSADOR,

ECONOFF AND USAID OFFICER MET WITH DR. JOSEPH MADE,

CONFIDENTIAL

MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE, TO MAKE INTRODUCTIONS AND

DISCUSS LAND REFORM AND THE RESETTLEMENT PROGRAM.

ALSO ATTENDING THE MEETING WAS VINCET HUNGWE, ACTING

PERMANENT SECRETARY. MADE, A U.S. EDUCATED

TECHNOCRAT WHO LIVED IN THE STATES FOR 17 YEARS (MADE

EARNED HIS B.S., M.S AND PHD FROM THE UNIVERSITY OF

WISCONSIN AT MADISON AND KNOWS THE LAND TENUTE CENTER

THERE VERY WELL), DISCUSSED WITH US IN GENERAL TERMS

THE LAND ACQUISITION PROCESS GOING FORWARD AND

PROVIDED SOME ADDITIONAL DETAILS ON THE GOVERNMENT\'S

PLANS. HE TOLD US THAT A NEW OUTREACH PROGRAM TO THE

INVADERS/SQUATTERS BEGAN ON AUGUST 29, WITH TEAMS

MADE UP OF "THE POLICE, ARMY, THE GOVERNMENT, ETC"

(WE READ THE LATTER AS PARTY OFFICIALS AND/OR THE

CIO) FANNING OUT TO SPREAD THE WORD ON THE RELOCATION

EXERCISE AND THE HALT OF NEW INVASIONS. NO HARD

DATES OR A TIMETABLE WERE PROVIDED ON THE

RESETTLEMENT PROGRAM, AND ONLY VAGUE ASSURANCES WERE

GIVEN THAT COMMERCIAL FARMERS WILL NOT SUFFER OR BE

SHUT DOWN IN THE PROCESS.

2. (C) THE FACTS MINISTER MADE PROVIDED US ARE:

== THE POLICE WILL HALT NOW NEW INVASIONS AND

PROTECT PRIVATE PROPERTY AND/OR PERSONAL

ASSAULT/INJURY.

== THE PROGRAM OF TEAMS GOING OUT TO MEET WITH WAR

VETS AND SQUATTERS TO URGE RESTRAINT AND EXPLAIN THE

PROGRAM SHOULD HAVE STARTED WEEKS AGO. MADE DID NOT

KNOW HOW MANY TEAMS THERE WERE OR HOW LONG THE EFFORT

WOULD LAST.

== THE MINISTER WOULD ALSO NOT TELL US WHEN THE

RELOCATIONS OF INVADERS OFF NON-LISTED PROPERTIES

WOULD OCCUR. HE TOLD US THAT A MAXIMUM OF 20 PERCENT

OF THE ACQUIRED LAND WOULD GO TO WAR VETS, WITH THE

REMAINING 80 PERCENT DESTINED FOR PEASANTS.

== OF THE 5 MILLION HECTARES TO BE ACQUIRED, 2.1

MILLION HECTARES ARE TO BE PRIMARILY CROPPING LANDS

(35 PERCENT CROP ARABLE); WITH THE OTHER 2.9 MILLION

HA., PRIMARILY SUPPORTING LIVESTOCK (ONLY 10 PERCENT

CROP ARABLE). MADE EXPLAINED THAT THE LATTER WAS TO

ACCOMMODATE THE MANY COMMUNAL FARMERS WHO RUN SMALL

CATTLE HERDS AND WHO HAVE NO DESIRE TO RELOCATE.

THEY ONLY NEED LAND TO WHICH THEY CAN SEND STOCK FOR

GRAZING/FATTENING BEFORE SALE (WHAT THE GOVERNMENT

TERMS THE "THREE-TIERED MODEL" WITHIN EXISTING LAND

REFORM DOCUMENTATION.

== THE GOVERNMENT WILL CALL ON THE LANCASTER HOUSE

AGREEMENT TO MAKE THE U.K. PAY FOR THE ACQUIRED 5

MILLION HECTARES. IF FARMERS AND THE CFU CAN

PRIVATELY NEGOTIATE COMPENSATION FOR THE LAND FROM

DONORS, THAT IS ALL THE BETTER FROM HIS PERSPECTIVE.

== IN THE HISTORY OF LAND REFORM THROUGHOUT THE

WORLD, NO GOVERNMENT HAS EVER PUCHASED ON A WILLING

SELLER/BUYER BASIS AS MUCH LAND AS ZIMBABWE HAS SINCE

INDEPENDENCE. HE TOUTED THE FIGURE OF 3.8 MILLION

HECTARES PAID FOR BY THE GOZ IN THE FIRST 18 YEARS OF
