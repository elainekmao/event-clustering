SUBJECT: NIGERIA: PRESIDENTIAL ADVISOR FOR ENERGY REQUESTS

MEETING WITH USG COP-6 BIS DELEGATION

REF: (A) STATE 102061 (B) ABUJA 1530 (C) ABUJA 1733

1. This is an action cable. Please see para 5.

2. On June 22, Ambassador, DOE Energy Advisor, and

Econoff (notetaker) met with Special Advisor to the

President for Petroleum Affairs and Energy, Dr. Rilwanu

Lukman (Reftel B). Dr. Lukman commented that during

President Obasanjo\'s state visit to Washington in May,

Obasanjo promised President Bush that the GON would re-

evaluate the Kyoto Protocol issue.

3. (U) Dr. Lukman said that he will be a member of the

Nigerian delegation to the upcoming COP-6, and he would

like to meet with the U.S. delegation. This meeting

request is a follow-up to President Obasanjo\'s promise

to President Bush.

4. (U) Comment. Dr. Lukman specifically mentioned an

upcoming COP-6 meeting at the "Hague." We assume that he was

actually referring to the upcoming COP-6 bis meeting to be

held in Bonn, July 16 - 27. Within COP negotiations, the GON

has supported the Kyoto Protocol\'s emission reduction targets

and has pushed for assistance to developing countries, such

as technology transfers on a concessionary basis. (Reftel C

offers more detail on GON position.) It is somewhat

surprising that President Obasanjo and Dr. Lukman have

expressed a willingess to reconsider Nigeria\'s position;

such reconsideration will not be well received by other GON

agencies, such as the Ministry of Environment. End Comment.

5. (U) Action Request: Reftel A states that the USG intends

to send a delegation to COP-6 bis. Please advise if the

delegation would like to meet with Dr. Lukman. If so please

pass the contact details of the U.S. delegation to Post so

that we can inform Dr. Lukman\'s office.
