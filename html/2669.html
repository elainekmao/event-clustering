SUBJECT: NIGERIA: VP ATIKU ON MPRI AND AVID RECOVERY

Classified by Ambassador Howard F. Jeter; Reasons 1.5 (b) and

(d).

1. (C) Ambassador Jeter called on Vice President Atiku

Abubakar March 19. In addition to a wide range of economic

issues (septel), Ambassador stressed the need for a quick

decision on the FY02 MPRI payment; without that decision, the

program would be cut. On Operation Avid Recovery, Jeter said

the Operation should not only assist with the immediate UXO

problem in Ikeja, but also should be used to prevent future

disasters by providing a safety assessment of other

ammunition storage facilities in the country.

2. (C) The Vice President said that he personally could not

decide either issue. However, he committed to discuss EOD

assessments with the President once Obasanjo returns home.

The Vice President also said that he would work to ensure a

quick written response from the GON on the MPRI program.

Jeter
