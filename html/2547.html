SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION ZIM PRESIDENTIAL POLL; HARARE

1. WHILE THE INDEPENDENT WEEKLY "ZIMBABWE INDEPENDENT"

CONDEMNED THE RECENT ATTACKS ON ELECTION OBSERVERS IN

ZIMBABWE BY ALLEGED ZANU PF SUPPORTERS IN ITS MARCH 1

EDITORIAL, THE BULAWAYO-BASED GOVERNMENT-CONTROLLED

DAILY "CHRONICLE" SLAMMED PRESIDENT BUSH FOR ALLEGEDLY

USING ORGANIZATIONS LIKE THE U. S.-BASED "GENOCIDE

WATCH" TO FURTHER HIS POLITICAL INTERESTS. THIS BAD

TEMPERED EDITORIAL COMES IN THE WAKE OF AN ARTICLE,

BASED ON A RECENT "GENOCIDE WATCH" REPORT CARRIED IN THE

INDEPENDENT "FINANCIAL GAZETTE. ":EXCERPTS AS FOLLOWS:

2. UNDER HEADLINE "ATTACKS ON SADC OBSERVERS WERE

INEVITABLE," THE "ZIMBABWE INDEPENDENT" COMMENTS:

"WE VERY MUCH REGRET THE STAIN ON ZIMBABWEAN HOSPITALITY

THAT ATTACKS ON SOUTH AFRICAN AND SADC OBSERVERS

REPRESENT. BUT THEY WERE INEVITABLE. DESPITE PRESIDENT

MUGABE\'S PUBLIC COMMITMENT TO NON-VIOLENCE, HIS

SUPPORTERS CONTINUE TO ABDUCT, TORTURE, STONE, AND BEAT

UP OTHER ZIMBABWEANS WITH IMPUNITY. . .WE HOPE THAT

FOLLOWING THESE INCIDENTS, THE SOUTH AFRICANS AND THEIR

SADC COLLEAGUES WILL STOP DANCING AROUND ISSUES OF

ELECTORAL VIOLENCE. . . ."

3. UNDER HEADLINE "GENOCIDE EXISTS ONLY IN THEIR MINDS,"

THE "CHRONICLE" COMMENTS:

"PROPHETS OF DOOM FROM THE IMPERIALIST WORLD ARE ALREADY

PREDICTING A GLOOMY POST-ELECTION PERIOD OF ZIMBABWE.

THEY TELL US THERE WILL BE \'CIVIL UNREST,\' \'GENOCIDE,\'

AND EVERY POSSIBLE CURSE ON THE NATION, ALL BECAUSE THE

ZANU PF CANDIDATE IS POISED FOR A RESOUNDING VICTORY

COME 9 AND 10 MARCH. . .BUT MOST PEOPLE HAVE SEEN

THROUGH THIS CHARADE AND WILL VOTE FOR UPHOLDING OF OUR

SOVEREIGNTY AS A COUNTRY, HENCE THE PANIC BY THE UNITED

STATES, BRITAIN AND AUSTRALIA WHO ARE NOW SINGING THE

SANCTIONS BLUES. . .A U. S.-BASED ORGANIZATION WHICH

CALLS ITSELF \'GENOCIDE WATCH\' HAS ALREADY PREDICTED THAT

ZIMBABWE WILL \'SLIDE\' INTO \'GENOCIDE\' AND CALLED ON THE

U. S. AND THE EU TO PREPARE FOR A POSSIBLE \'MILITARY

INTERVENTION.\' THIS SOUNDS FAMILIAR. LIKE MANY OTHER

EGOCENTRIC RACISTS IN EUROPE, GAY GANGSTERS IN THE

U. S., MASQUERADING AS \'NGOS\' OR \'HUMAN RIGHTS\' BODIES,

WHEN IN FACT THEY ARE JUST GEORGE BUSH\'S RABBLE ROUSERS

AND LAP DOGS, ARE AT IT AGAIN. THE PROBLEMS WE NOW

HAVE, THAT HAVE ERUPTED INTO POLITICAL VIOLENCE, ARE

BEING SPONSORED AND MASTERMINDED BY SUCH BODIES. .

GEORGE W. BUSH IS TRYING TO BE A CHIP OFF THE OLD BLOCK.

HE HAS PROVED TO BE A WARMONGER AND WILL SLIDE US INTO

THE THIRD WORLD WAR IF THE WORLD DOES NOT STOP HIS

COWBOY POLITICS. . .\'GENOCIDE WATCH\' SHOULD CONCERN

ITSELF WITH THE MASSIVE GENOCIDE BUSH CAUSED IN

AFGHANISTAN WHERE HE KILLED INNOCENT CHILDREN AND

ORDINARY CIVILIANS."
