SUBJECT: VATICAN VIWS ON WCAR

1. (U) CARDINAL ROGER ETCHEGARAY, FORMER PRESIDENT OF THE

PONTIFICAL COUNCIL FOR JUSTICE AND PEACE AND MEMBER OF THE

U.N.\'S "EMINENT PERSONS" GROUP OF ADVISORS, SAID THE WCAR

MUST BE AWARE OF THE MODERN MANIFESTATIONS OF INEQUALITY.

FOLLOWING THE ROUND TABLE TALKS AT THE GENEVA OFFICES OF THE

UNHCR, THE CARDINAL STATED THAT "THE ANTIRACISM OF YESTERDAY

SEEMS UNSUITED TODAY AND NEEDS NOT ONLY TO REVIVE ITS

PERMANENT CONVICTIONS BUT ALSO TO RENEW ITS REASONING, EVEN

SOMETIMES TO CHANGE ITS TARGET." HE WARNED THAT NEW

PERMUTATIONS OF RACISM MIGHT BEST BE FOUGHT BY NEW METHODS.

2. (SBU) POLOFF AND POL INTERN MET AUGUST 2 WITH KISHORE

JAYABALAN, ASSISTANT TO FRANK DEWANE, UNDERSECRETARY OF THE

PONTIFICAL COUNCIL FOR JUSTICE AND PEACE, TO DISCUSS THE

HOLY SEE\'S VIEWS ON THE WORLD CONFERENCE ON RACISM. (NOTE:

DEWANE WAS THE VATICAN REPRESENTATIVE AT 1ST WEEK SESSION OF

WCAR IN GENEVA). THE HOLY SEE GENERALLY SHARES USG

POSITIONS ON CONTROVERSIAL AREAS OF THE WCAR DOCUMENTS,

SPECIFICALLY REPARATIONS FOR SLAVERY, ZIONISM AS RACISM

LANGUAGE, AND "ISLAMAPHOBIA."

3. (SBU) LIKE THE USG, THE HOLY SEE WOULD PREFER THE WCAR

TO BE A FORWARD LOOKING CONFERENCE, FOCUSING MORE ON

CONTEMPORARY PROBLEMS THAN APOLOGIES FOR WRONGS COMMITTED

GENERATIONS AGO. THEY WOULD RATHER DISCUSS MODERN

TRAFFICKING IN HUMAN BEINGS THAN REPARATIONS FOR THE SLAVE

TRADE FROM AFRICA - ESPECIALLY SINCE THIS IGNORES ALL OTHER

SLAVE TRADES, PAST AND PRESENT. IN ADDITION, THE VATICAN

HAD RESERVATIONS ON THE PARAGRAPHS DEALING WITH THE

HOLOCAUST. JAYABALAN DID SAY THAT THIS WAS A MORE TIMELY

AND RELEVANT ISSUE THAN SLAVERY REPARATIONS, AS THERE ARE

LIVING HOLOCAUST SURVIVORS.

4. (SBU) ACCORDING TO JAYABALAN, THE HOLY SEE ALSO

OBJECTED TO THE `ZIONISM AS RACISM\' LANGUAGE. CITING THE

EXAMPLES OF NORTHERN IRELAND AND THE BALKANS, HE SAID THEY

VIEW RELIGIOUS DISCRIMINATION AS SEPARATE AND DISTINCT FROM

DISCRIMINATION BASED ON ETHNICITY OR RACE, ALTHOUGH NO LESS

GRIEVOUS AN OFFENSE. A RELIGION IS A CHOICE OR A SYSTEM OF

BELIEFS, NOT AN INHERITED TRAIT.

5. (SBU) MR. JAYABALAN THOUGHT THE WCAR DOCUMENTS LACKED A

CLEAR AND CURRENT FOCUS. HE SAW THEM AS OVERLOADED WITH

INDIVIDUAL PET PROJECTS, WITH NO CLEAR FOCUS OR GOAL FOR THE

CONFERENCE AS A WHOLE. JAYABALAN BELIEVED THAT THESE

INDIVIDUAL ISSUES, SEXUAL ORIENTATION, FOR EXAMPLE, WOULD

NOT MAKE IT PAST THE PREPCON.

6. (SBU) COMMENT: WHILE THE HOLY SEE MAY SHARE USG VIEWS

ON MANY OF THE NEGATIVE ASPECTS OF THE WCAR, THEY TEND TO

LIMIT ACTIVE PARTICIPATION IN DEBATES TO TOPICS OF PARAMOUNT

IMPORTANCE TO THE VATICAN, SUCH AS ABORTION. THEY CAN,

HOWEVER, BE A USEFUL PARTNER BEHIND THE SCENES, ESPECIALLY

WITH COUNTRIES (E.G. LIBYA, IRAQ) WITH WHICH THE USG HAS

LITTLE LEVERAGE. END COMMENT. MERANTE
