SUBJECT: AFGHANISTAN\'S FUTURE - NEXT STEPS

REF: STATE 192153

1. (U) CLASSIFIED BY DCM GORDON R. OLSON, FOR

REASONS 1.5 (B) AND (D).

2. (S) POLOFF REVIEWED REFTEL POINTS ON THE

FUTURE OF AFGHANISTAN WITH MUHAMMAD ABDUL RAHIM

ABDUL JALIL, MFA DEPUTY DIRECTOR OF AMERICAN

AND EUROPEAN AFFAIRS ON NOVEMBER 7. SHE HANDED

OVER A COPY OF THE PRESIDENT\'S SPEECH TO THE

WARSAW CONFERENCE ON COMBATING TERRORISM.

ABDUL JALIL SAID HE HAD HEARD EXCERPTS OF THE

SPEECH ON AL-JAZEERA AND NOTED THAT THE

PRESIDENT HAD DELIVERED A FORCEFUL AND WELCOME

MESSAGE. ABDUL JALIL CONDEMNED THE 9/11

ATTACKS AND REITERATED THE UAE\'S STRONG

COMMITMENT TO THE ANTI-TERROR COALITION.

RECALLING THAT THE UAE HAD ITSELF BEEN A TARGET

OF TERRORIST ATTACKS IN THE 1970\'S AND 1980\'S

(THE FOREIGN MINISTER WAS ASSASSINATED AND

SEVERAL UAE DIPLOMATS ABROAD WERE TARGETED BY

ABU NIDAL), ABDUL JALIL DECLARED THAT WE NEEDED

TO STAND TOGETHER AGAINST THIS NEW THREAT. UBL

DID NOT SPEAK FOR THE MUSLIM PEOPLE, HE STATED,

MOST OF WHOM JUST WANT TO LIVE IN PEACE.

3. (C) NOTING USG HUMANITARIAN ASSISTANCE TO

THE AFGHAN PEOPLE, POLOFF LAUDED THE UAE\'S OWN

SUBSTANTIAL ASSISTANCE PROGRAM. SHE SAID THE

U.S. ANTICIPATED THAT A HUMANITARIAN ASSISTANCE

DONORS CONFERENCE WOULD BE HELD AND ENCOURAGED

UAE PARTICIPATION. GIVEN THE FACT THAT THE UAE

HAS HUMANITARIAN ASSISTANCE TEAMS ON THE GROUND

IN AFGHANISTAN -- WORKING THROUGH THE UAE RED

CRESCENT SOCIETY -- THE DONORS\' CONFERENCE

WOULD PROVIDE AN EXCELLENT OPPORTUNITY FOR THE

EMIRATIS TO SHARE THEIR KNOWLEDGE AND COMBINE

THEIR EFFORTS WITH OTHERS IN THE INTERNATIONAL

COMMUNITY. ABDUL JALIL AGREED THAT THE UAE

NEEDED TO BE PART OF ANY DONORS CONFERENCE AND

EXPRESSED CONCERN ABOUT CONDITIONS ON THE

GROUND IN AFGHANISTAN, PARTICULARLY IN ADVANCE

OF THE HARSH WINTER.

4. (C) TURNING TO AFGHANISTAN\'S FUTURE, POLOFF

NOTED THE DIFFICULTY OF FORGING A COALITION

AMONG AFGHANISTAN\'S DIVERSE ETHNIC GROUPS.

NEVERTHELESS, WE BELIEVED THAT A BROAD-BASED

GOVERNMENT NEEDS TO BE ESTABLISHED AND THAT, AT

ALL COSTS, WE SHOULD AVOID THE CREATION OF A

POWER VACUUM. TO THIS END, WE FULLY ENDORSED

THE EFFORTS OF UN SPECIAL REPRESENTATIVE

BRAHIMI. ABDUL JALIL, WHO SERVED UNTIL AUGUST

AS THE UAE\'S DCM IN TEHRAN, WONDERED ABOUT

IRANIAN DESIGNS AND HOPED THAT THE SIX PLUS TWO

MEETING IN NEW YORK WOULD BEAR FRUIT. THE

PASHTUNS -- WHO HE ESTIMATED COMPRISE 51% OF

THE AFGHAN POPULATION -- WILL NEED TO PLAY A

MAJOR ROLE IN ANY FUTURE GOVERNMENT, ABDUL

JALIL SURMISED.

5. (U) PESHAWAR MINIMIZE CONSIDERED.
