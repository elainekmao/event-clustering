SUBJECT: POLICY CHANGE TO CIRCULAR 175 AUTHORITY

AUTHORIZING LONGER-TERM STATUS OF FORCES AGREEMENT

REF: SECSTATE 71266

1. WE STRONGLY RECOMMEND AGAINST ANY PRE-RATIFICATION

NEGOTIATIONS AS OUTLINED IN REFTEL. OF THE CATEGORIES

OFFERED IN REFTEL, PARAGRAPH 8, WE CHOOSE CATEGORY D: NOT

FEASIBLE AT THIS TIME. AFTER THE CURRENT FOL AGREEMENT IS

RATIFIED, HOWEVER, WE WOULD RECOMMEND CATEGORY B: FEASIBLE

WITH SOME CHALLENGES. THESE CHALLENGES, WHILE FEW IN

NUMBER, WOULD NEVERTHELESS BE SIGNIFICANT OBSTACLES TO A

MORE EXTENSIVE AGREEMENT.

2. THE LANGUAGE PROPOSED IN REFTEL REGARDING AN EXPANDED,

LONGER-TERM STATUS OF FORCES AGREEMENT (SOFA) COVERING

PERSONNEL STATIONED IN ARUBA AND THE NETHERLANDS ANTILLES

RESEMBLES OUR OPENING PROPOSAL FOR THE SOFA-LIKE FORWARD

OPERATING LOCATION (FOL) AGREEMENT ESTABLISHING OUR PRESENCE

IN ARUBA AND CURACAO. WE BELIEVE THAT THE DUTCH RESPONSE TO

THE REFTEL PROPOSAL WILL MIRROR THAT GIVEN TO THE FOL

OPENING PROPOSAL. THIS RESPONSE FEATURED OBJECTIONS TO THE

LEVEL OF PRIVILEGES AND IMMUNITIES PROPOSED FOR PERSONNEL

AND THEIR DEPENDENTS AND THE EXTENT TO WHICH SUCH OPERATIONS

WOULD INVOLVE PART OF THE KINGDOM NOT UNDER DIRECT DUTCH

CONTROL. THERE WAS ALSO CONSIDERABLE UNEASE ABOUT THESE

LOCATIONS BEING USED TO INTERVENE MILITARILY IN THE AFFAIRS

OF ANOTHER COUNTRY. ALTHOUGH THE NETHERLANDS IS A MILITARY

ALLY, THE DUTCH ARE PARTICULARLY SENSITIVE ABOUT MILITARY

AGREEMENTS WITH THE U.S. IN THE CARIBBEAN AND LATIN AMERICA.

THIS SENSITIVITY IS EVEN GREATER AFTER A YEAR OF SOMETIMES

TENSE NEGOTIATION OVER THE FOL AGREEMENT.

3. AFTER EXTENSIVE NEGOTIATIONS SURROUNDING THESE ISSUES,

THE FOL AGREEMENT IS CURRENTLY AWAITING RATIFICATION BY THE

DUTCH LEGISLATURE. IT IS, HOWEVER, BEING PROVISIONALLY

APPLIED IN THE CARIBBEAN WITH THE OPTIMISTIC EXPECTATION

THAT IT WILL BE RATIFIED, BUT WE DO NOT EXPECT THIS TO OCCUR

IN THE NEAR FUTURE. IF THE PROPOSAL IN REFTEL WERE RAISED

WITH THE GON BEFORE RATIFICATION OF THE FOL AGREEMENT, THE

FOL AGREEMENT ITSELF MIGHT NOT BE RATIFIED BY THE GON DUE TO

THE RESURFACING OF THE OBJECTIONS ITEMIZED ABOVE. THIS

WOULD JEOPARDIZE THE ENTIRE OPERATION IN THE CARIBBEAN.

4. THE KINGDOM OF THE NETHERLANDS CONSISTS OF THREE

COUNTRIES: THE NETHERLANDS, THE NETHERLANDS ANTILLES, AND

ARUBA. ALTHOUGH THE NETHERLANDS ANTILLES AND ARUBA ARE SELF-

GOVERNING, THE NETHERLANDS RESERVES POWER OVER FOREIGN AND

DEFENSE POLICY. THEREFORE, ANY AUTHORIZATION AS PROPOSED IN

REFTEL WOULD REQUIRE THE AGREEMENT OF ALL THREE GOVERNMENTS.

5. THIS RESPONSE WAS COORDINATED WITH CONGEN CURACAO AS

REQUESTED IN REFTEL AND APPLIES TO THE GOVERNMENTS OF THE

DUTCH ANTILLES AND ARUBA.

SCHNEIDER
