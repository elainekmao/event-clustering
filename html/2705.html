SUBJECT: MEPP: POPE GRAVELY CONCERNED, HAS MESSAGE FOR

CLASSIFIED BY: AMBASSADOR JIM NICHOLSON FOR REASONS 1.5 (A) AND (D).

1. (C) VATICAN FOREIGN MINISTER TAURAN UNEXPECTEDLY CALLED

AMBASSADOR NICHOLSON THIS MORNING. H HAD JUST CONCLUDED A

MEETING WITH POPE JOHN PAU II WHO IS SHOCKED OVER REPORTED

DEATH OF A CATHLIC PRIEST IN BETHLEHEM. THE PRIEST WAS

REPORTEDY THE CHAPLAIN OF THE SISTERS OF SAINT BRIDGET AND

WAS KILLED BY ISRAELI FIRE. TAURAN SAID THAT THE POPE HAS

PERSONALLY REQUESTED THAT THE AMBASSADOR CONTACT PRESIDENT

CONTROLS THE MILITANTS. TAURAN ALSO RAISED TE NEED FOR THE

DETERIORATING SITUATION. IN ADDITION, TAURAN SAID THEY HAD

PLANNING TO DO, AND WOULD SHARE IT TOMORROW.

3. (C) ACTON REQUESTED: POST REQUESTS TALKING POINTS ON
