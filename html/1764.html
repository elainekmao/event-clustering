SUBJECT: TOLEDO AT THE HELM

CLASSIFIED BY AMBASSADOR JOHN R. HAMILTON FOR REASONS

1.5(B) AND 1.5(D).

1. (C) SUMMARY: ALEJANDRO TOLEDO WAS TENACIOUS IN HIS

STRUGGLE TO REACH THE PRESIDENCY AND WILL APPLY THE SAME

TENACITY TO SUCCEED IN OFFICE. HE COMES TO OFFICE WITH NO

GOVERNMENT OR MANAGEMENT EXPERIENCE, HOWEVER, AND HIS

PROPENSITY TO BE A ONE-MAN SHOW CAN BE A NAGGING

SHORTCOMING. NONETHELESS, IN OUR VIEW, HE WILL ULTIMATELY

EXERCISE SOUND JUDGMENT. HE FACES THE PRESSURE OF

EXORBITANT CAMPAIGN PROMISES, EXPECTATIONS THAT A VICTORY

FOR DEMOCRACY CAN SPEEDILY RESOLVE PERU\'S ILLS, AND

POLITICAL DEBTS. HE ALSO FACES DIFFICULT ECONOMIC

CONDITIONS. IT IS THE ECONOMY WHERE HE WILL FOCUS, WHILE

TRYING TO GIVE OTHER PRIORITIES, SUCH AS JUDICIAL AND

MILITARY REFORM, THEIR DUE. INTERNATIONALLY, HE WILL

PURSUE AN INDEPENDENT COURSE, WHICH NEVERTHELESS WILL BE IN

ACCORD WITH US MUCH OF THE TIME, AND BE LESS CONCERNED

ABOUT BEING IN LOCKSTEP WITH PERU\'S NEIGHBORS. WHETHER HIS

GOVERNMENT SUCCEEDS OR FAILS WILL ALSO BE SEEN AS A TEST

FOR DEMOCRACY. END SUMMARY.

2. (C) WHEN ALEJANDRO TOLEDO ASSUMES THE PRESIDENCY JULY

28, HE WILL BRING TO OFFICE A STRONGLY PERSONAL ONE-MAN

STYLE. DECISION-MAKING WILL RESIDE WITH HIM AS IT HAS

DURING THE CAMPAIGN. ALTHOUGH HE TALKS A GOOD GAME ABOUT

EMPOWERING HIS MINISTERS, TOLEDO HAS NOT PROVEN ABLE TO

DELEGATE AUTHORITY. A SOLITARY LEADER, HE DOES NOT EASILY

EXTEND HIMSELF OR HIS TRUST TO OTHERS. CLOSEST TO HIM IS

HIS WIFE, ELIANE KARP. NEGATIVE PERCEPTIONS OF HER

INFLUENCE HAVE LED THE TOLEDO CAMP TO LOWER HER PROFILE

TEMPORARILY, BUT HER INFLUENCE IS SIGNIFICANT AND WILL

LIKELY GROW.

3. (C) ALSO AFFECTING TOLEDO\'S PERSONAL STYLE IS HIS NEED

FOR AFFIRMATION, DOMESTIC AND INTERNATIONAL. AT HOME, THIS

CHARACTERISTIC, ACCORDING TO CRITICS, BRINGS HIM TO THE

BORDERS OF A MESSIANIC COMPLEX. AFTER THE VINDICATION OF

HIS ROLE IN RETURNING DEMOCRACY TO PERU, TOLEDO NOW SEES

HIMSELF AS SINGLE-HANDEDLY RESPONSIBLE FOR LIFTING PERU OUT

OF POVERTY OR, AT A MINIMUM, OUT OF ECONOMIC RECESSION.

THE TEST OF THE TOLEDO GOVERNMENT ON ECONOMIC ISSUES IS

ALSO THE TEST OF TOLEDO THE MAN - HIS PERSONAL SUCCESS OR

FAILURE BOUND UP WITH THAT OF HIS GOVERNMENT. ON THE

INTERNATIONAL SCENE, TOLEDO\'S NEED FOR APPROVAL INFLUENCES

HIS ATTITUDE TOWARDS THE USG. HE HARBORS SOME RESENTMENT,

FOR EXAMPLE, THAT WE DID NOT BACK HIM OPENLY WHEN HE

OPPOSED FUJIMORI AND SOUGHT INTERNATIONAL RECOGNITION. HE

WILL EXPECT TO BE TREATED ON EQUAL FOOTING WITH WORLD

LEADERS, INCLUDING WITH PRESIDENT BUSH, AND WILL USE THE

ATTENTION ACCORDED HIM BY OTHERS, REGARDLESS OF THE MERITS

OF THE COMPARISON, AS A BASELINE.

4. (C) THE POSITIVE SIDE OF TOLEDO\'S INDIVIDUALISM IS HIS

TENACITY AND HIS DETERMINATION TO SUCCEED. HE IS PROUD OF

BEING A FIGHTER, DEMONSTRATED IN HIS TWO-YEAR STRUGGLE TO

GET TO THE PRESIDENTIAL PALACE. HE COMES TO THE JOB,

HOWEVER, WITH NO EXPERIENCE AS AN ELECTED OFFICIAL OR IN

MANAGEMENT. THIS SHORTCOMING WILL MAKE THE DECISION-MAKING

PROCESS IN TOLEDO\'S GOVERNMENT APPEAR DYSFUNCTIONAL, BUT WE

BELIEVE TOLEDO\'S DECISIONS ON THE KEY ISSUES OF IMPORTANCE

TO THE U.S. WILL ULTIMATELY BE THE RIGHT ONES.

PRESSURES

=========

EXPECTATIONS HE HAS RAISED WITH HIS CAMPAIGN PROMISES.

WILL HELP HIM WEATHER MUCH OF THE CRITICISM, AT LEAST IN

THE FIRST THREE TO SIX MONTHS IN OFFICE. HE IS AWARE THAT

HE HAS TO BE PUBLICLY AGGRESSIVE IN REDUCING THOSE

EXPECTATIONS, AND HAS ALREADY BEGUN TO DO SO, SPEAKING OF

6. (C) THERE IS ALSO THE QUESTION OF WHAT HE MAY OWE

POLITICALLY TO HIS CAMPAIGN\'S SUPPORTERS. WITH ONLY

RUDIMENTARY CAMPAIGN FINANCING RULES, THERE IS LITTLE

INFORMATION ABOUT POSSIBLE FINANCIAL BACKERS AND THEIR

INTERESTS. WITH THE EXCEPTION OF THE ONE MILLION DOLLARS

GEORGE SOROS\' OPEN SOCIETY PROVIDED FOR THE JULY 2000 ANTI-

FUJIMORI PROTESTS (CUATRO SUYOS), ONLY MEDIA BUSINESSMAN

BARUCH IVCHER HAS BEEN IDENTIFIED AS A CONTRIBUTOR. THERE

ARE RUMORS THAT ANOTHER, SOMEWHAT UNSAVORY BUSINESSMAN,

LEON RUPP, HAS BEEN A MAJOR BACKER. RAUL DIEZ CANSECO, THE

FIRST VP FROM AN OLD, WEALTHY LIMA FAMILY, ALSO HELPED TO

BANKROLL TOLEDO\'S CAMPAIGN.

7. (C) TOLEDO\'S MOST PROMINENT POLITICAL DEBT IS TO

MORALIZING INDEPENDENT FRONT (FIM) LEADER FERNANDO OLIVERA.

FIM PLAYED THE CAMPAIGN ATTACK DOG AGAINST APRA\'S ALAN

GARCIA AND PLEDGED ITS ELEVEN LEGISLATORS TO BACK TOLEDO IN

THE CONGRESS. OLIVERA, HOWEVER, IS NOT VIEWED AS A

RELIABLE POLITICAL PARTNER (REFTEL). THE KEY FOR TOLEDO

WILL BE TO BALANCE ANY REWARDS FOR OLIVERA AGAINST

MAINTAINING COHESION IN HIS OWN PARTY\'S RANKS. WITHOUT THE

FIM, THE ONLY OTHER SUBSTANTIAL CONGRESSIONAL BLOC TOLEDO

HAS SUCCESSFULLY COURTED, TOLEDO\'S LEGISLATIVE AGENDA MAY

SUFFER.

YES, IT\'S THE ECONOMY

=====================

8. (C) TOLEDO WILL FOCUS HIS ENERGIES ON THE ECONOMY,

WHERE HE IS DESPERATE TO GET POSITIVE GROWTH FAST. HE HAS

STAKED MUCH ON REVITALIZING THE AGRICULTURE SECTOR,

GENERATING EMPLOYMENT AND BRINGING PERU OUT OF RECESSION.

HE HAS A SOLID, IF SOMEWHAT ECLECTIC, ECONOMIC TEAM AND IS

BANKING ON THE LYNCHPIN CABINET APPOINTMENT OF WELL KNOWN

INVESTMENT BANKER PEDRO PABLO KUCZYNSKI. HIS TWO VICE

PRESIDENTS, RAUL DIEZ-CANSECO AND DAVID WAISMAN, BRING

BUSINESS EXPERIENCE TO THE LEADERSHIP TEAM.

9. (C) THE ANTENNAE ARE OUT, HOWEVER, FOR POSSIBLE TURNING

POINTS IN HOW TOLEDO HANDLES ECONOMIC MATTERS WHEN THESE

ARE TOUGH ISSUES AND POTENTIALLY COMPETING ADVICE AND

INTERESTS. ONE FEAR IS THAT HE MAY RESORT SIMPLY TO HIS

OWN ECONOMICS EXPERTISE RATHER THAN RELYING ON OR LISTENING

TO ADVISORS. ANOTHER IS THE POSSIBILITY THAT HE MAY BEGIN

TO PURSUE POPULIST MEASURES IN RESPONSE TO SOCIO-ECONOMIC

PRESSURES AS HIS ECONOMIC RECOVERY PROGRAM EITHER TAKES

LONGER THAN EXPECTED OR DOES NOT MEET EXPECTATIONS.

10. (C) NON-DOMESTIC FACTORS, BEYOND TOLEDO\'S CONTROL,

WILL ALSO INFLUENCE HOW HE DOES ON THE ECONOMY. ON A

MACRO-LEVEL, A SLOW DOWN IN THE U.S. AND WORLD ECONOMIES

WILL HAVE A SIGNIFICANT EFFECT. IF THE U.S. ECONOMY

SLIDES, EVEN GOOD DECISIONS MAY NOT HAVE THE DESIRED

EFFECT. WORLD PRICES FOR PERU\'S COMMODITIES AND THE

POSSIBLE RETURN OF THE DESTRUCTIVE EL NINO WEATHER PATTERN

ARE ADDITIONAL FACTORS THAT COULD AFFECT PERU\'S GROWTH IN

THE COMING YEARS. MORE SPECIFICALLY, HOW THE ANDEAN TRADE

PREFERENCES ACT (ATPA) FARES IN THE U.S. CONGRESS WILL ALSO

11. (C) IN ADDITION TO THE ECONOMY, WE CAN EXPECT TOLEDO

TO DEVOTE ATTENTION TO JUDICIAL REFORM AND TO THE MILITARY.

THE INDEPENDENCE OF THE JUDICIARY HAS RECEIVED A STRONG

BOOST UNDER THE PANIAGUA GOVERNMENT AND IT IS LIKELY TO

REMAIN ON THE SAME TRACK UNDER TOLEDO. NEVERTHELESS,

ALTHOUGH JUDICIAL INDEPENDENCE HAS BEEN LARGELY RESTORED TO

THE STATUS QUO ANTE FUJIMORI, THE PERUVIAN JUDICIAL SYSTEM

REMAINS IN DIRE NEED OF RADICAL REFORM AND MODERNIZATION.

THE MILITARY, DISCREDITED FOR ITS CORRUPT LINKS TO FORMER

INTELLIGENCE ADVISOR VLADIMIRO MONTESINOS, FACES AN

UNSYMPATHETIC MASTER IN TOLEDO. HE HAS PUT THE ARMED

FORCES ON NOTICE ABOUT PURSUING CORRUPTION AND ABUSES,

FAVORS MILITARY BUDGET CUTS AND WILL PRESS FOR GREATER

CIVILIAN CONTROL.

BILATERAL RELATIONSHIP, HE WILL NONETHELESS SEEK TO ENSURE

HE MAKES HIS MARK. IN COUNTER NARCOTICS, FOR EXAMPLE,

TOLEDO WILL LIKELY SUPPORT COCA ERADICATION, BUT HE WILL

ALTERNATIVE DEVELOPMENT, TOLEDO WILL FAVOR ALTERNATIVE

CROPS, BUT HE MAY PROMOTE INTERNATIONAL PRICE SUPPORTS.

OVERALL, TOLEDO HAS INDICATED HE WILL TAKE A HARD LINE

AGAINST NARCOTICS TRAFFICKERS.

13. (C) UNLIKE THE INTERIM PANIAGUA GOVERNMENT, WHICH

FAVORED WORKING IN CONSENSUS WITH ITS NEIGHBORS REGARDLESS

OF DIFFERENCES, TOLEDO WILL PLAY A MORE INDEPENDENT ROLE IN

REGIONAL RELATIONS. LINKS WITH BRAZIL WILL NEED REPAIR

AFTER THE RECENT HISTORY OF BRAZILIAN FAVORING OF FUJIMORI

AND OF ONE OF TOLEDO\'S FIRST ROUND OPPONENTS, LOURDES

FLORES NANO. SIMILARLY, THERE IS NO LOVE LOST WITH

VENEZUELA\'S HUGO CHAVEZ, REGARDED BY TOLEDO AND MANY IN HIS

CAMP AS THE ANTI-DEMOCRAT. IN ADDITION, THERE IS THE

COMMON PERCEPTION THAT, IN PART THANKS TO CHAVEZ,

MONTESINOS HAS BEEN SUCCESSFUL IN REMAINING A FUGITIVE.
