(D)

1. THIS IS AN ACTION REQUEST. PLEASE SEE PARAGRAPH 5.

2. (C) THE DUTCH AMBASSADOR AND DCM IN ROME EXPRESSED CONCERN

TO EMB OFFICERS REGARDING POSSIBLE ISRAELI PARTICIPATION IN

JSF. THE DUTCH AMBASSADOR MENTIONED THE ISSUE AT THE

EMBASSY\'S JULY 4 RECEPTION, AND THE DUTCH DCM, WILLIAM VAN

EE, FOLLOWED UP THE NEXT DAY TO SEE IF OUR ITALIAN CONTACTS

HAD RAISED THE MATTER WITH US.

3. (C) VAN EE EXPLAINED THAT IN THE DUTCH VIEW, ISRAEL\'S

APPLICATION IS LARGELY POLITICALLY INSPIRED. GIVEN THE

POLITICAL SITUATION IN THE MIDDLE EAST, VAN EE SAID HIS

GOVERNMENT DOES NOT BELIEVE THIS IS THE TIME TO ENGAGE IN A

COOPERATIVE PROJECT WITH ISRAEL. IN ADDITION, ISRAEL\'S

PARTICIPATION WOULD CONFLICT WITH THE RESTRICTIVE EXPORT

POLICY OF THE DUTCH GOVERNMENT BASED ON CONCERNS ABOUT

TECHNOLOGY TRANSFERS, VAN EE SAID.

4. (C) VAN EE REPORTED THAT HE HAD MET WITH THE NEW DIRECTOR

OF THE MFA OFFICE OF ECONOMIC COOPERATION ON JULY 5 TO

DISCUSS DUTCH CONCERNS AND TO GAUGE ITALIAN REACTIONS. VAN

EE SAID HIS ITALIAN INTERLOCUTOR BELIEVED THAT ISRAEL HAS

BEEN ON THE LIST OF POTENTIAL PARTICIPANTS ALL ALONG, AND

THAT ISRAEL WAS INCLUDED AS SUCH IN THE ITALIAN MOU. VAN EE

ASKED HIM TO VERIFY THIS, AS THAT WOULD BE A MAJOR DIFFERENCE

FROM THE MOU SIGNED BY THE DUTCH.

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 ROME 03319 081456Z

5. (C) ACTION REQUEST. POST WOULD APPRECIATE GUIDANCE ON USG

REACTION TOWARD ISRAEL\'S APPLICATION TO PARTICIPATE IN JSF

AND CLARIFICATION AS TO WHETHER ISRAEL HAS BEEN IDENTIFIED IN

OTHER MOUS AS A POTENTIAL PARTICIPANT. WE UNDERSTAND THE

ISSUE IS BEING STUDIED BY RELEVANT OFFICES IN WASHINGTON. WE

HAVE NOT BEEN APPROACHED BY THE GOI BUT ANTICIPATE THAT WE

MAY BE NOW THAT THE DUTCH HAVE RAISED THE ISSUE.
