SUBJECT: GVN ACCEDES TO ADDITIONAL TERRORISM CONVENTIONS

1. (U) THIS IS AN ACTION REQUEST -- SEE PARA 6.

2. (U) IN A MEETING WITH POL/C ON AUGUST 26, DEPUTY

DIRECTOR GENERAL OF THE MFA\'S DEPARTMENT OF INTERNATIONAL

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 02 HANOI 02086 261022Z

LAW AND TREATIES NGUYEN TRUONG GIANG CONFIRMED THAT THE GVN

HAD NOW ACCEDED TO A TOTAL OF EIGHT INTERNATIONAL

CONVENTIONS AND PROTOCOLS RELATED TO COUNTER-TERRORISM

(REFTEL - NOTAL). IN ALL CASES, PRESIDENT TRAN DUC LUONG

SIGNED AS CHIEF OF STATE. NONE REQUIRED RATIFICATION FROM

THE NATIONAL ASSEMBLY, GIANG NOTED. HE PREDICTED FAVORABLE

DECISIONS ON AT LEAST TWO OTHER CONVENTIONS AND PROTOCOLS

WITHIN THE NEXT TWO MONTHS, AND THE FINAL TWO POTENTIALLY

LATER THIS YEAR.

3. (U) THE NEW LIST NOW INCLUDES:

-- CONVENTION ON THE PREVENTION AND PUNISHMENT OF CRIMES

AGAINST INTERNATIONALLY PROTECTED PERSONS INCLUDING

DIPLOMATIC AGENTS (SIGNED 4/4/02; ENTRY INTO FORCE 6/1/02);

-- CONVENTION FOR THE SUPPRESSION OF UNLAWFUL AGAINST THE

SAFETY OF MARITIME NAVIGATION (SIGNED 6/26/02; ENTRY INTO

FORCE 9/20/02);

-- PROTOCOL FOR THE SUPPRESSION OF UNLAWFUL ACTS AGAINST

THE SAFETY OF FIXED PLATFORMS LOCATED ON THE CONTINENTAL

SHELF (ALSO SIGNED 6/26/02; ENTRY INTO FORCE 9/20/02); AND,

-- CONVENTION FOR THE SUPPRESSION OF FINANCING OF TERRORISM

(SIGNED 8/20/02; GIANG DID NOT HAVE AVAILABLE DETAILS ABOUT

ITS ENTRY INTO FORCE DATE).

4. (U) GIANG CONFIRMED THAT THE MFA HAD NOTIFIED THE UN

SECURITY COUNCIL OF THESE ACTIONS IN COMPLIANCE WITH UNSCR

1373, AND ADDED THAT THE MFA EXPECTED THE UN TO NOTIFY

MEMBER STATES.

5. (U) POL/C STRONGLY WELCOMED THESE POSITIVE STEPS,

NOTING THAT THE U.S. HAD NOW RATIFIED AND BECOME A PARTY TO

ALL TWELVE CONVENTIONS. HE ENCOURAGED THE MFA TO BE EVEN

MORE FORTHCOMING WITH INFORMATION ABOUT ITS RATIFICATION

PROCESS, SO THAT U.S. LEADERS COULD PROVIDE APPROPRIATE

FEEDBACK TO THEIR VIETNAMESE COUNTERPARTS. DDG GIANG

REITERATED VIETNAM\'S "LONG-STANDING" COMMITMENT TO FIGHTING

TERRORISM AND ITS DETERMINATION TO CONTINUE COOPERATION WITH

THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY ON THIS ISSUE.

6. (U) ACTION REQUEST: DDG GIANG REQUESTED USG VIEWS ON

THE INDIAN INITIATIVE SEVERAL YEARS AGO FOR A COMPREHENSIVE

CONVENTION AGAINST TERRORISM. HE NOTED THAT THIS SEEMED

PARTICULARLY RELEVANT TO ONGOING INTERNATIONAL EFFORTS.
