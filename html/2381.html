SUBJECT: PAPAL ADDRESS FOCUSES ON VIOLENCE, RESPONSIBILITY

AND FORGIVENESS

1. IN HIS ANNUAL SPEECH TO THE DIPLOMATIC CORPS, POPE JOHN

PAUL II OFFERED HIS VERSION OF THE STATE OF THE WORLD,

FOCUSING ON AREAS TRADITIONALLY OF CONCERN TO THE CHURCH.

ESCHEWING FOR THE MOST PART HIS TRADITIONAL EXHORTATION FOR

DEVELOPED COUNTRIES TO DO MORE FOR THE LESS FORTUNATE, IT

STRUCK MANY THAT THE PONTIFF EXPRESSED A BALANCED AND

REALISTIC ASSESSMENT OF CURRENT CONDITIONS AND A GENTLE

PRESCRIPTION FOR THE FUTURE.

2. EARLY ON, HE OFFERED A COMMENTARY ON EU EXPANSION: HE

SUPPORTS IT AND INDEED ENCOURAGES MORE, BUT ASSERTS THAT

EVEN A SECULAR EUROPE MUST ALLOW RELIGIOUS VOICES TO HAVE A

PLACE IN EU PLANNING -- ESPECIALLY IN THE UPCOMING

DISCUSSIONS OF AN EU CONSTITUTION. HE NOTED PROGRESS IN

CYPRUS, KOSOVO AND SRI LANKA, AND CONGRATULATED BOTH THE

PEOPLES REPUBLIC, AND THE REPUBLIC, OF CHINA FOR TAKING

SEATS AT THE WTO.

3. ON THE ISRAEL/PALESTINIAN CONFLICT HE DECRIED "THE

INJUSTICE OF WHICH THE PALESTINIAN PEOPLE HAVE BEEN VICTIMS

FOR MORE THAN 50 YEARS." HE ADDED THAT ONLY A HALT TO THE

VIOLENCE AND "RESPECT FOR OTHERS AND THEIR LEGITIMATE

ASPIRATIONS, THE APPLICATION OF INTERNATIONAL LAW, THE

WITHDRAWAL FROM OCCUPIED TERRITORIES, AND INTERNATIONALLY

GUARANTEED STATUS FOR THE MOST HOLY PLACES IN JERUSALEM,.."

CAN BREAK THE CYCLE OF VIOLENCE TO WHICH THERE IS APPARENTLY

NO END. ALL OF THE ABOVE IS STANDARD VATICAN LANGUAGE -- AS

WAS HIS EXPRESSED HOPE THAT THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY BE

ACCEPTED BY ALL PARTIES TO TAKE A ROLE IN THE PEACE PROCESS.

(NOTE: WE READ THIS TO INCLUDE COUNTRIES BESIDES THE U.S. IN

THE PEACE PROCESS, TO INCLUDE INTERNATIONAL MONITORS.)

4. THE POPE REITERATED HIS STRONG SUPPORT FOR THE UNITED

STATES\' RIGHT TO "LEGITIMATE DEFENSE" AGAINST THE TERRORIST

ATTACKS OF 9/11. NOTING THE NEED ALSO TO CONSIDER

STRATEGIES TO COMBAT IT, ADDRESS CAUSAL FACTORS, AND BEGIN A

HEALING PROCESS, HE OFFERED AN IMPLICIT ENDORSEMENT OF OUR

CAMPAIGN BY SIMPLY URGING THE KARZAI GOVERNMENT TO MOVE

QUICKLY TO "PACIFY" ALL OF AFGHANISTAN\'S TERRITORY.

5. THE PONTIFF RESERVED HIS MOST HARSH AND BLUNT CRITICISM

FOR THOSE WHO WOULD COMMIT VIOLENCE IN THE NAME OF GOD. HE

CALLED THIS "BLASPHEMY AND A PERVERSION OF RELIGION." HE

FURTHER DECRIED TERRORISM "IN THE NAME OF GOD," AS A

"PROFANATION OF RELIGION."

6. TOWARDS THE END OF HIS TALK, THE PONTIFF OFFERED BRIEF

COMMENTS ON THE GLOBAL DEVELOPMENT GAP. UNLIKE IN PREVIOUS

ANNUAL ADDRESSES, IN WHICH HE SOUGHT TO STIMULATE THE NORTH

TO DO MORE FOR THE SOUTH, THIS TIME HE URGED THE POLITICAL

CLASSES -- IN AFRICA AND LATIN AMERICA ESPECIALLY -- TO SET

ASIDE THEIR GREED AND PARTISAN DIFFERENCE AND TAKE

RESPONSIBILITY FOR BETTERING THEIR OWN SITUATION.

7. IN CLOSING, THE PONTIFF OFFERED HIS INSIGHTS ON THE

NATURE OF GOD AND MAN; HE UNDERSCORED THAT, ALONE AMONG THE

MONOTHEISTIC RELIGIONS, CHRISTIANITY OFFERS TO THE WORLD

(AND TO ISLAM IN PARTICULAR) A FUNDAMENTAL TRUTH -- THAT TO

FORGIVE IS DIVINE. HE HOPED THAT THE JANUARY 24 DAY OF

PRAYER IN ASSISI WOULD BRING THE LEADERS OF THE WORLD\'S

RELIGIOUS LEADERS TOGETHER AROUND THE MESSAGE THAT RELIGION

SHOULD NOT, AND INDEED TODAY CANNOT, BE SPREAD BY FORCE OF

ARMS.
