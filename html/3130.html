SUBJECT: PRINCE HASSAN TRAVELING TO US JUNE 16-20

Classified By: AMBASSADOR EDWARD W. GNEHM FOR REASONS 1.5 (B) AND (D)

1. (C) Prince Hassan Bin Talal met with Ambassador Gnehm on

June 13 to discuss his upcoming travel to the U.S. The

Prince will arrive in New York on June 16 and is tentatively

scheduled to participate in a Fox television interview that

night. On June 17, Prince Hassan will help launch a joint

humanitarian initiative between Islamic countries and the

U.S. called "Partners in Humanities". Post is faxing a

letter directly to Senator Biden from the Prince inviting him

to attend the event. During the rest of his visit, Prince

Hassan plans to attend a World Conference on Religion and

Peace and has several scheduled media interviews.

2. (C) Prince Hassan has not requested any assistance during

his travel to the U.S.

Gnehm
