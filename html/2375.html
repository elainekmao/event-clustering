SUBJECT: MEDIA REPORT PARLIAMENT TO PASS TWO CONTROVERSIAL

BILLS TODAY; HARARE

ON JANUARY 9, THE RULING ZANU-PF PARTY USED ITS

PARLIAMENTARY MAJORITY TO SUSPEND PARLIAMENT\'S STANDING

ORDERS AND RULES TO ALLOW TWO CONTROVERSIAL BILLS - THE

ACCESS TO INFORMATION AND PROTECTION OF PRIVATE BILL AND

THE PUBLIC ORDER AND SECURITY BILL - TO BE TABLED BEFORE

THE HOUSE. ALTHOUGH MEMBERS OF PARLIAMENT FROM THE

OPPOSITION MOVEMENT FOR DEMOCRATIC CHANGE ARE FIERCELY

CHALLENGING THE BILLS AT EVERY TURN, ZANU-PF MPS ARE

EXPECTED TO USE THEIR SIMPLE MAJORITY IN THE HOUSE TO PASS

THE TWO BILLS BY COB TODAY (JANUARY 10).

SULLIVAN
