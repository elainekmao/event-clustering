GRANIC TOLD AMBASSADOR GALBRAITH THAT THE PLANNED

"LONG VACATION" TO AUSTRALIA BY THE "PRESIDENT" OF

THE SO-CALLED "REPUBLIC OF HERCEG-BOSNA," MATE BOBAN,

HAD BEEN CANCELLED BECAUSE BOBAN HAD BEEN DENIED A

VISA. WE LATER CONTACTED THE AUSTRALIAN EMBASSY IN

VIENNA AND WERE TOLD BY FIRST SECRETARY MARK HIGGIE

THAT BOBAN HAD BEEN DESIGNATED A "CONTROVERSIAL

VISITOR" BY THE MINISTER FOR IMMIGRATION AND ETHNIC

AFFAIRS ON THE BASIS THAT HIS VISIT "DID NOT MEET

PUBLIC INTEREST CRITERIA." SPECIFICALLY, HIGGIE SAID

THAT THE AUSTRALIAN GOVERNMENT FELT THAT BOBAN\'S

VISIT COULD POTENTIALLY UPSET ETHNIC SERBS AND

MUSLIMS FROM THE FORMER YUGOSLAVIA CURRENTLY RESIDING

IN AUSTRALIA.

3. HIGGIE STATED THAT VISAS HAD BEEN DENIED ON

SIMILAR GROUNDS TO DOBROSLAV PARAGA, PRESIDENT OF THE

CROATIAN PARTY OF RIGHTS, AND BOSNIAN SERB "VICE

PRESIDENT" BILJANA PLAVSIC. HE ADDED THAT THE VISA

TO BOBAN WAS DENIED, IN PART, TO BALACE THE EARLIER

REFUSAL OF PLAVSIC. HE ALSO COMMENTED THAT HAD BOBAN

APPLIED A YEAR EARLIER, BEFORE THE MUSLIM-CROAT

CONFLICT AND ENSUING VIOLATIONS OF HUMAN RIGHTS, HIS

APPLICATION MIGHT HAVE BEEN FAVORABLY RECEIVED.

4. ACTION REQUEST: GRANIC SAID THAT BOBAN STILL

HOPED TO VISIT THE U.S. FOR HIS "LONG VACATION." HE

ASKED, "OFF THE RECORD" WHETHER WE COULD TELL HIM

"OFF THE RECORD" WHETHER THE VISA APPLICATION WOULD

BE FAVORABLY RECEIVED. NOTE: BOBAN WAS GIVEN AN A-1

TDY VISA WITH 12 MONTHS VALIDITY LAST FEBRUARY TO

5. COMMENT: WHEN WE WERE FIRST INFORMED BY FOREIGN

MINISTER GRANIC THAT BOBAN WOULD BE TAKING A "LONG

VACATION," THE IMPLICATION WAS THAT THIS WAS PART OF

AN EFFORT TO GET HIM OUT OF THE WAY AND, COMBINED

WITH THE CREATION OF A NATIONAL SECURITY COUNCIL FOR

"HERCEG-BOSNA," PERHAPS PART OF AN EFFORT TO EASE HIM

OUT OF THE PICTURE ENTIRELY. WE ALSO NOTE THAT

FOREIGN MINISTER GRANIC WILL BE ACCOMPANIED BY

BOSNIAN CROAT LEADERS AKMADZIC AND TOMIC (WHOSE STARS

APPEAR TO BE RISING) AT THE TALKS IN VIENNA WITH B-H

PRIME MINISTER SALAJDZIC.

6. COMMENT CONTINUED: WE ARE WELL AWARE THAT BOBAN

MIGHT AT SOME POINT BE TRIED FOR WAR CRIMES ALTHOUGH

WE HAVE NO IDEA WHETHER AT THE PRESENT TIME

SUFFICIENT EVIDENCE EXISTS TO MAKE THE CASE AGAINST

HIM. WE BELIEVE THE SITUATION ON THE GROUND HERE

COULD BE IMPROVED IF ANOTHER "VACATION SITE" WERE

FOUND FOR MR. BOBAN. AUSTRALIA WAS AN IDEAL

DESTINATION, BEING AT LEAST 48 AIR HOURS AWAY AND

WITH A SUBSTANTIAL TIME DIFFERENCE BUT WE NOTE THAT

THERE ARE REMOTE PARTS OF THE U.S. AS WELL.
