QUOTE

1. THE PKK ATTEMPTED YESTERDAY TO EXPAND ITS TERROR

CAMPAIGN TO MALATYA.

2. TURKISH DAILY NEWSPAPERS REPORT THAT A SINGLE

TERRORIST, APPARENTLY INTENT ON STAGING A ONE-MAN

SUICIDE ATTACK ON SECURITY HEADQUARTERS, WAS FOILED

BEFORE HE COULD CARRY OUT HIS ATTACK WHEN HE DROPPED A

MAGAZINE FROM HIS KALASHNIKOV ON THE STREET. HAVING

LOST THE ELEMENT OF SURPRISE, HE RETREATED TO THE 6TH

FLOOR OF THE RAILWAY WORKERS\' UNION BUILDING ACROSS THE

STREET FROM THE SECURITY OFFICE, AND OPENED FIRE AT THE

SECURITY BUILDING.

3. DURING THE STANDOFF, WHICH LASTED FOUR HOURS, A

BUILDING GUARD WAS KILLED, AND A POLICEMAN AND A

PASSER-BY WERE WOUNDED. THE SIEGE ENDED WITH THE POLICE

KILLING THE TERRORIST, WHO WAS FOUND TO HAVE HAD A

KALASHNIKOV RIFLE, TWO PISTOLS, 150 ROUNDS OF

AMMUNITION, AND GRENADES WITH HIM.

4. MALATYA SECURITY OFFICIALS TOLD CONSULATE THAT THE

TERRORIST HAS NOT YET BEEN IDENTIFIED, BUT APPARENTLY

WAS OPERATING ALONE. NEVERTHELESS, SEVERAL OTHER

SUSPECTS HAVE BEEN ARRESTED AS POSSIBLE

CO-CONSPIRATORS.

5. COMMENT: MALATYA, WHICH IS RELATIVELY PROSPEROUS

COMPARED TO OTHER AREAS OF THE SOUTHEAST, HAS FOR SOME

TIME BEEN OUTSIDE THE AREA OF TERRORIST ACTIVITY. THIS

IS THE FIRST MAJOR OPERATION DIRECTED AGAINST MALATYA IN

OVER A YEAR. END COMMENT.
