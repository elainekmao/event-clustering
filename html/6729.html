SUBJECT: KUWAIT OIL UPDATE -- MARCH 24, 2003

REF: KUWAIT 1049 AND PREVIOUS

Classified By: DCM Frank C. Urbancic for reasons 1.5. (B) and (D)

1. (C) A detailed breakdown of Kuwait\'s oil production is not

yet available for today, but Embassy is told output from 0500

03/23/03 to 0500 03/24/03 reached 2.42 million barrels.

2. (C) Kuwaiti firefighters apparently put out one of the

fires burning in Iraq\'s Rumaila oilfield today, and they are

prepared to continue the effort. Embassy is working with

officials of the Kuwait Oil Company and the U.S. Army Corps

of Engineers (USACE) to ensure the KOC team is working in as

safe and secure an environment as possible. USACE (working

under the Office of Reconstruction and Humanitarian

Assistance) will also coordinate KOC efforts with those of

U.S. contractors to combat the blazes.

3. (C) A senior KOC official said that part of the

firefighting effort is the resumption of production from the

Ratqa field (which straddles the Kuwait Iraq border to become

the Rumaila field). By increasing production, pressure in

the reservoir will drop making the fires less dangerous.

Ratqa reportedly made 15,000 barrels today and will rise to

30,000 bpd by tomorrow.
