SUBJECT: (C) VATICAN SEES ARAFAT\'S MARGINALIZATION AS ROAD

TO RADICALIZATION AND MILITARY SOLUTION

CLASSIFIED BY: CLASSIFIED BY D. BRENT HARDT, CDA, REASON E.O. 12958 1.5

(B,D)

1. (C) SUMMARY: VATICAN DEPUTY FOREIGN MINISTER MIGLIORE

TOLD DCM JULY 12 THAT USG MARGINALIZATION OF ARAFAT WILL

LIKELY EMPOWER HAMAS AND THEREBY JUSTIFY ISRAEL PM SHARON\'S

PLAN TO SEEK A MILITARY SOLUTION. THE VATICAN BELIEVES

ARAFAT\'S PRESENCE CAN STILL LIMIT HAMAS\' APPEAL. ACCORDING TO

MIGLIORE, THE VATICAN SUPPORTS AN INTERNATIONAL PEACEKEEPING

FORCE FOR THE PALESTINIAN TERRITORIES, AND WOULD SEEK A SEAT

AT ANY INTERNATIONAL PEACE CONFERENCE FOR THE MIDDLE EAST.

END SUMMARY.

2. (C) DURING INCOMING DCM\'S JULY 12 COURTESY CALL ON

VATICAN DFM CELESTINO MIGLIORE, MIGLIORE RAISED HIS CONCERN

AT USG POLICY CALLING FOR NEW LEADERSHIP OF THE PALESTINIAN

AUTHORITY. MIGLIORE SAID THE ISRAEL/PALESTINIAN CONFLICT

REMAINS OF GREAT CONCERN TO THE HOLY SEE, WHICH WISHES TO SEE

AN INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE CONVENED AS SOON AS PRACTICABLE.

WHILE ACKNOWLEDGING ARAFAT\'S SHORTCOMINGS. HE AVERRED THAT

ARAFAT REMAINS A USEFUL RALLYING SYMBOL FOR PALESTINIANS. TO

MOVE HIM OUT NOW WOULD ONLY PAVE THE WAY FOR MORE RADICAL

ELEMENTS SUCH AS HAMAS, HE PREDICTED. DRAWING THE THREAD OUT

FURTHER, MIGLIORE SAID MARGINALIZING ARAFAT WOULD BE A "GIFT

TO SHARON," WHO WOULD THEN BE FREE TO PURSUE THE MILITARY

CAMPAIGN THAT THE HOLY SEE BELIEVES HE HAS WANTED ALL ALONG

-- AND SOMETHING THE HOLY SEE CATEGORICALLY REJECTS.

3. (C) MIGLIORE RECALLED THAT SECRETARY POWELL TOLD VATICAN

PM EQUIVALENT CARDINAL SODANO DURING THE PRESIDENT\'S JUNE

VATICAN VISIT THAT THERE WAS NO CURRENT PLANNING FOR A

CONFERENCE TO EXPLORE WAYS TO MOVE THE PEACE PROCESS FORWARD.

MIGLIORE UNDERSCORED THE IMPORTANCE OF DEVELOPING IDEAS FOR

MOVING THE PROCESS FORWARD. HE SAID THE HOLY SEE HAS ITS OWN

IDEAS, BUT NO PLAN AS SUCH, OTHER THAN ITS "CLEAR AND

SPECIFIC POLICY OF SUPPORT FOR INTERNATIONAL PROTECTION FOR

THE HOLY PLACES IN JERUSALEM." HE DID REMIND US OF THE HOLY

SEE\'S STANDING SUPPORT FOR AN INTERNATIONAL INTER-POSITIONAL

FORCE TO STOP THE VIOLENCE, AND NOW ITS STRONG INTEREST IN AN

OBSERVER ROLE IN ANY INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON THE MEPP.

4. (C) COMMENT: THE LEITMOTIF OF HOLY SEE FOREIGN POLICY

IS DEFENSE OF THE HUMAN PERSON THROUGH PEACE, DIALOGUE, AND

SOLIDARITY. THE VATICAN IS FEARFUL THAT THE PACE AND LEVEL

OF VIOLENCE IN ISRAEL AND THE PALESTINIAN TERRITORIES HAS

DULLED PEOPLES\' SENSES, AND THAT VIOLENCE WILL TURN WORSE

BEFORE IT EASES (IN FACT, FM TAURAN EXPRESSED THESE FEARS TO

US WHEN THE CURRENT INTIFADA BEGAN IN LATE 2000). ARAFAT, AS

DIFFICULT AS HE IS, HAS BEEN A REGULAR VISITOR AT THE HOLY

SEE AND IS SOMEONE THE VATICAN FEELS IT CAN WORK WITH TO

PROTECT THE CHRISTIAN HOLY SITES. THE VATICAN NOW SEES ITS

INTERESTS THREATENED EVER MORE BY A POSSIBLE TURN AMONG

PALESTINIANS TO ISLAMIC FUNDAMENTALIST LEADERSHIP IF ARAFAT

GOES.
