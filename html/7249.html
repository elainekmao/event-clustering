SUBJECT: TFI01: GOI APPROACHES EMBASSY RE SAFETY OF ITALIAN

JOURNALISTS IN BAGHDAD

CLASSIFIED BY: DEPUTY CHIEF OF MISSION EMIL SKODON FOR REASONS 1.5 (B)

AND (D).

1. (SBU) MFA SECRETARY GENERAL\'S CHIEF OF STAFF, ANTONIO

ZANARDI LANDI, PHONED DCM AT THE INSTRUCTION OF FOREIGN

MINISTER FRATTINI TO EXPRESS GOI CONCERNS ABOUT SAFETY OF THE

TWENTY ITALIAN JOURNALISTS AND SUPPORT STAFF IN BAGHDAD. THE

JOURNALISTS WERE AT THE HOTEL SHERATON AND THE PALESTINE

HOTEL. ZANARDI LANDI ASKED THAT THE EMBASSY CONVEY THESE

CONCERNS ASAP TO THE "APPROPRIATE U.S. AUTHORITIES."

2. (C) ZANARDI LANDI\'S APPROACH CAME IN THE WAKE OF

INTERNATIONAL MEDIA REPORTS THAT COALITION FORCES HAD FIRED

AT THE PALESTINE HOTEL AND CAUSED CASUALTIES AMONG FOREIGN

JOURNALISTS STAYING THERE. HE TOLD DCM OFF-THE-RECORD THAT

THE MFA AND PRIME MINISTER\'S OFFICE WERE UNDER STRONG

PRESSURE FROM ITALIAN JOURNALISTS ASSOCIATION AND OTHERS TO

"DO SOMETHING" TO HELP PROTECT ITALIAN NATIONALS AMONG THE

FOREIGN MEDIA IN BAGHDAD. HENCE THE APPROACH TO THE EMBASSY.

(EARLIER IN THE DAY, FOREIGN MINISTER FRATTINI, SPEAKING IN

BUDAPEST WHILE ON AN OFFICIAL VISIT TO HUNGARY, SAID THE MFA

WOULD SEEK TO VERIFY DETAILS OF THE PALESTINE HOTEL INCIDENT.

FRATTINI ADDED THAT "WE ALL KNOW VERY WELL THAT THE ALLIED

FORCES, WHOM WE SUPPORT, DO NOT MAKE A HABIT OF FIRING ON

JOURNALISTS" AND ADDED "IT IS OUR DUTY TO REMIND COALITION

FORCES TO BE PARTICULARLY ALERT WHEN OPERATING IN AREAS WHERE

THE PRESS IS PRESENT.")

3. (C) IN RESPONSE TO DCM\'S REQUESTS FOR MORE DETAIL, ZANARDI

LANDI CONFIRMED GOI UNDERSTANDING THAT ALL OF THE TWENTY

ITALIANS WERE STAYING AT ONE OR THE OTHER OF THE TWO

AFOREMENTIONED HOTELS. HE DID NOT KNOW EXACTLY HOW MANY WERE

AT EACH HOTEL. THE LAST CONTACT WITH THEM HAD BEEN IN THE

MORNING OF APRIL 8, ROME TIME. DCM ASKED THE JOURNALISTS\'

PREFERENCE: WERE THEY SEEKING SAFEHAVEN WITH U.S. FORCES IN

BAGHDAD, DID THEY WANT TO BE EVACUATED, OR DID THEY JUST WANT

THE HOTELS TO BE NO-FIRE ZONES? ZANARDI LANDI WAS NOT SURE,

ALTHOUGH HE EXPECTED THAT AT LEAST THE SEVEN ITALIANS WHO HAD

BEEN "ARRESTED" BY IRAQI AUTHORITIES AND WERE BEING DETAINED

AT THE PALESTINE HOTEL WOULD WANT TO DEPART. THE GOI HOPED

TO BE IN CONTACT WITH THE JOURNALISTS LATER TODAY AND WOULD

PUT THESE QUESTIONS TO THEM.

4. (C) SEPARATELY, EMBASSY ODC CHIEF RECEIVED A REQUEST FROM

ITALIAN MILITARY TO ADVISE RE FEASIBILITY OF IAF C-130J

EQUIPPED WITH CHAFF AND FLARES LANDING AT BAGHDAD

INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT TO EVACUATE ITALIANS AND OTHER

NATIONALS. THAT REQUEST BEING FORWARDED THROUGH MIL

CHANNELS. (COMMENT: THIS IS APPARENTLY ONLY A REQUEST FOR

INFORMATION FOR PLANNING PURPOSES AT THIS POINT AND DOES NOT

NECESSARILY PRESAGE AN IAF FLIGHT. END COMMENT.)

5. (C) ACTION REQUESTED: WE SHOULD RESPOND IN A TIMELY WAY

THAT HELPS THE GOI PREEMPT THE NEGATIVE STORIES ABOUT

"RECKLESS" COALITION FORCES SURE TO BE SPUN UP BY SOME

PORTIONS OF THE ITALIAN PRESS. PLEASE ADVISE APPROPRIATE

RESPONSE TO MFA.
