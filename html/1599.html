SUBJECT: ZIMBABWE DEMOCRACY AND ECONOMIC RECOVERY ACT

MIGHT PLAY INTO MUGABE\'S HANDS

REF: HARARE 1058

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 HARARE 01079 211013Z

CLASSIFIED BY CHARGE D\'AFFAIRES EARL IRVING. REASONS: 1.5

(B) AND (D).

MEDIA COVERAGE

--------------

1. (U) IN THE LAST SEVERAL DAYS, AS REPORTED REFTEL, TWO OF

ZIMBABWE\'S INDEPENDENT NEWSPAPERS HAVE DEVOTED PROMINENT

COVERAGE TO THE MARCH 8 INTRODUCTION IN THE U.S. SENATE OF

THE ZIMBABWE DEMOCRACY AND ECONOMIC RECOVERY ACT (ZDERA).

ALTHOUGH THE ACCOUNTS IN BOTH PAPERS WERE GENERALLY

FACTUAL, THEY BOTH EMPHASIZED THAT THE BILL SEEKS TO PUNISH

PRESIDENT MUGABE AND OTHER SENIOR OFFICIALS RESPONSIBLE FOR

THE POLITICAL VIOLENCE AND BREAKDOWN IN THE RULE OF LAW BY

IMPOSING TRAVEL RESTRICTIONS AND FREEZING THEIR ASSETS.

NEITHER ARTICLE CLARIFIED THAT THE CALLS FOR TRAVEL

RESTRICTIONS AND ASSET-FREEZING ARE CONTAINED IN THE NON-

BINDING SENSE OF CONGRESS SECTION. THE GOVERNMENT-

CONTROLLED PRINT AND BROADCAST MEDIA HAVE NOT TO DATE

CARRIED A SINGLE STORY ON THE ZDERA, BUT WE DO NOT EXPECT

THIS SILENCE TO LAST MUCH LONGER.

REACTIONS

---------

2. (C) THE GOVERNMENT HAS NOT RAISED WITH US DIRECTLY ITS

CONCERNS ABOUT THE ZDERA, BUT PROBABLY ONLY BECAUSE IT HAS

BEEN CONSUMED BY OTHER BUSINESS. IT IS ONLY A MATTER OF

TIME BEFORE THE GOZ FOCUSES ON THIS ISSUE AND CONVOKES THE

CHARGE TO CONVEY -- IN NO UNCERTAIN TERMS -- ITS CONCERNS

ABOUT THE PROPOSED LEGISLATION. IN THE COURSE OF A MEETING

ON ANOTHER TOPIC ON MARCH 20, THE CHIEF OF PROTOCOL OF THE

MINISTRY OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS SUGGESTED TO CHARGE THAT THE

ZIMBABWEAN GOVERNMENT WOULD CALL ON THE U.S. TO "BE FAIR"

WITH ZIMBABWE AND NOT SINGLE IT OUT FOR RUIN BY IMPOSING

ECONOMIC SANCTIONS. HE WONDERED WHY THE UNITED STATES,

VIEWED BY ZIMBABWE AS A FRIEND, SEEMED TO BE FOLLOWING

"BRITISH INSTRUCTIONS." A NUMBER OF OUR DIPLOMATIC

CONTACTS HERE, INCLUDING THE WORLD BANK, HAVE EXPRESSED AN

INTEREST IN THE LIKELY PROSPECTS OF THE ZDERA. CIVIL

SOCIETY GENERALLY WELCOMES IT AS A SIGN THAT THE

INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY GENERALLY -- AND THE UNITED STATES

SPECIFICALLY -- CARES ABOUT WHAT HAPPENS IN ZIMBABWE. IN

THE VIEW OF MANY OF OUR PRIVATE CONTACTS, ONLY

INTERNATIONAL PRESSURE OF THE KIND ENVISIONED BY THE ZDERA

CAN FORCE MUGABE AND HIS CRONIES TO RESTORE THE RULE OF

LAW.

COMMENT

-------

3. (C) WHILE WE STRONGLY AGREE THAT ONLY INTERNATIONAL AND

REGIONAL PRESSURE ON THE MUGABE REGIME WILL BE EFFECTIVE IN

HALTING ZIMBABWE\'S SLIDE TOWARD POLITICAL AND ECONOMIC

DESTRUCTION, WE WISH TO POINT OUT THAT THE ZDERA MIGHT HAVE

THE OPPOSITE EFFECT. MUGABE AND HIS CABINET ARE LIKELY TO

EMPLOY A TIME-TESTED TACTIC IN PORTRAYING THE BILL AS

ANOTHER UNJUST TOOL OF WESTERN, PRO-BRITISH INTERESTS, AND

PROBABLY LABEL IT AS RACIST INTERFERENCE. FM MUDENGE TOLD

FORMER AMBASSADOR MCDONALD LAST JULY THAT THE ZIMBABWE

DEMOCRACY ACT (WHICH DIED IN COMMITTEE IN THE HOUSE DURING

THE PREVIOUS CONGRESSIONAL SESSION) HAD SET THE GOZ ON A

WAR FOOTING AND THAT THEY WOULD FIGHT US ON IT WITH ALL

MEANS AT THEIR DISPOSAL. WE ANTICIPATE SIMILAR BLUSTERY

RHETORIC -- IN PUBLIC AND PRIVATE -- ONCE THE CURRENT BILL

BEGINS TO MOVE THROUGH THE SENATE. IN ADDITION, THE ZDERA

COULD WEAKEN THE GROWING INCLINATION WITHIN THE SOUTHERN

AFRICAN DEVELOPMENT COMMUNITY (SADC) -- PARTICULARLY SOUTH

AFRICA -- TO TAKE A TOUGHER LINE WITH ZIMBABWE, IF THEY

FEEL OBLIGATED TO RALLY AROUND MUGABE IN THE NAME OF

REGIONAL SOLIDARITY. THOUGH WE AT POST SHARE FULLY THE

CONCERNS EXPRESSED IN THE ZDERA, WE ARE DUTY-BOUND TO

PROVIDE OUR ASSESSMENT THAT THE BILL MAY BECOME, AT LEAST

IN THE NEAR TERM, ANOTHER PROPAGANDA TOOL FOR MUGABE IN HIS

EFFORT TO HOLD ONTO POWER BY WHATEVER MEANS NECESSARY. ONE

WAY TO MITIGATE THE GOZ\'S LIKELY DEMONIZATION OF THE UNITED

STATES IS FOR US TO CONDUCT A VERY PUBLIC EFFORT TO

COORDINATE OUR APPROACH TOWARD ZIMBABWE WITH THE COMMUNITY

OF NATIONS.
