SUBJECT: CIVIAR SUMMIT: UAE DECLINES PARTICIPATION

REF: STATE 6024

MINISTRY OF COMMUNICATIONS A/USEC MOHAMMED YAHYA AL-

SUWEIDI CONFIRMED TO ECONCHIEF 2/22 THAT THE UAE WOULD

NOT BE ATTENDING REFTEL SUMMIT. WITH AN OBLIGATION

TOWARDS THE END OF MARCH IN CANADA, NEITHER THE MINISTER

OF COMMUNICATIONS NOR THE UAE\'S FEDERAL CIVIL AVIATION

AUTHORITY, THE GCAA, WOULD BE ABLE TO SPARE STAFF TO

ATTEND AN EARLY MARCH SUMMIT IN WASHINGTON. WHILE THIS

IS THE UAEG\'S OFFICIAL EXPLANATION FOR NON-ATTENDANCE,

WE HAVE BEEN TOLD INFORMALLY THAT REGIONAL EVENTS MAY

ALSO HAVE FACTORED INTO THE DECISION.
