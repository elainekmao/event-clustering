SUPPORT FOR THESE CHANGES. END SUMMARY.

2. THE IDLI ASSEMBLY OF MEMBER STATES WILL MEET MARCH

20, 2002 IN ROME. MEMBERS WILL BE ASKED TO APPROVE TWO

CHANGES TO IDLI\'S "ESTABLISHMENT AGREEMENT" (FULL TEXT

AND MEETING AGENDA FAXED TO IO/EDA ROSE BERSTEIN

2/25/02):

1) CHANGE NAME TO INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT LAW

ORGANIZATION (VICE INSTITUTE) IN ORDER TO CORRECT COMMON

MISPERCEPTION THAT IDLI IS CONNECTED TO AN ACADEMIC

INSTITUTE.

2) PROVIDE A SPECIFIC MANDATE FOR RULE OF LAW AND GOOD

GOVERNANCE: WHEN IDLI WAS FOUNDED 18 YEARS AGO, ASSISTING

DEVELOPING COUNTRIES TO NEGOTIATE INTERNATIONAL

COMMERCIAL AGREEMENTS WAS ITS PRIMARY FUNCTION. WHILE

THIS REMAINS AN IMPORTANT PART OF IDLI\'S WORK, THE LEGAL

AND JUDICIAL REFORM AGENDA HAS PROVIDED NEW OPPORTUNITIES

TO WORK ON RULE OF LAW AND GOOD GOVERNANCE IN PURSUIT OF

SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT. IDLI\'S CURRENT WORK PROGRAM IS

VERY MUCH ENGAGED IN RULE OF LAW ISSUES. THE PRESENT

ESTABLISHMENT AGREEMENT IS BROAD ENOUGH TO ENCOMPASS WORK

ON SUCH MATTERS, BUT THE IDLI BOARD OF DIRECTORS FELT

THAT IDLI SHOULD HAVE A MORE SPECIFIC MANDATE IN ORDER TO

PROVIDE LEADERSHIP IN ITS FIELD. THE PROPOSED CHANGES TO

ARTICLE II (PURPOSES AND ACTIVITIES) MAKE THIS MANDATE

CLEAR.

3. THE CHANGE WILL COME INTO EFFECT UPON RECEIPT BY THE

DEPOSITARY OF NOTIFICATIONS BY A THREE-FOURTHS MAJORITY
