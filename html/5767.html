SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION: IRAQ

1. SUMMARY: UAE MEDIA EXTENSIVELY COVERED INTERNATIONAL

PROTESTS AND REACTION AGAINST A MILITARY STRIKE IN IRAQ. LOCAL

AND REGIONAL MEDIA CRITICIZED WHAT THEY CALLED A WEAK ARAB STANCE

TOWARDS THE CURRENT CRISIS. "AL ITTIHAD" CRITICIZES U.S. PLANS

TO CONTROL THE MIDDLE EAST, NOTING THIS IS NOT JUSTIFIED BY

INTERNATIONAL LAW. SIMILARLY, AN "AL BAYAN" EDITORIAL ARGUES THE

INCREASING INTERNATIONAL OPPOSITION TO A U.S.-LED ATTACK ON IRAQ

INDICATES A REJECTION OF U.S. EFFORTS TO DOMINATE THE REGIONAL

AND CONTINUE ITS VIOLATION OF INTERNATIONAL LAW. "AL KHALEEJ"

ARGUES THAT U.S. ISOLATION IS INCREASING DAILY AND PROVES THAT

THE WORLD WILL NOT BE A HOSTAGE TO THE CURRENT PLANS OF THE U.S.

ADMINISTRATION. ANOTHER "AL ITTIHAD" EDITORIAL ARGUES THAT THE

ARABS MUST SEIZE THIS OPPORTUNITY AND JOIN THE ORGANIZED

INTERNATIONAL EFFORTS TO FIND A PEACEFUL SOLUTION TO THE CRISIS

DRING THE UPCOMING REGIONAL SUMMIT. FINALLY, A SEPARATE "AL

KHALEEJ" EDITORIAL URGES ARABS TO SAY "NO TO WAR." END SUMMARY.

2. CRITICIZING U.S. PLANS FOR THE MIDDLE EAST, ABU DHABI-BASED

SEMI-GOVERNMENT "AL ITTIHAD" EDITORIALIZED ON 2/10:

"... THE AMERICAN GOALS EXCEED IRAQ. THE U.S. PLAYS A ROLE WHICH

CONFLICTS WITH THE INTERESTS OF THE PEOPLE IN THE REGION. WHO

GIVES THE U.S. THE RIGHT TO CONTROL COUNTRIES IN THE MIDDLE EAST

AND RE-ARRANGE THEM? WE FIRMLY SAY THAT THERE IS NO LAW THAT

JUSTIFIES WHAT IS HAPPENING. IT IS FAIR TO ASK WHERE IS YOUR

CLAIMED FOR DEMOCRACY WHEN YOU ARE PLANNING TO REARRANGE THE

MIDDLE EAST WITHOUT PAYING ATTENTION TO THE INTERESTS OF ITS

PEOPLE AND WHEN YOU DECLARE THAT THE TARGET OF THE REARRANGEMENT

IS TO STRENGTHEN YOUR OWN INTERESTS. AMID THE NOISE AND DRUMS OF

WAR, WE HEAR ABOUT THE INTENTION TO OCCUPY IRAQ AND STAY FOR A

LONG TIME AFTER TURNING IT INTO AN AMERICAN AND BRITISH COLONY.

THIS IS NOT ACCEPTED BY ANY ARAB OR MUSLIM. IRAQ IS NOT

AFGHANISTAN, AND IF THERE IS OCCUPATION, ARABS AND MUSLIMS WILL

HAVE ONE STANCE AND SAY ONE WORD ... WE STRESS THAT THERE IS

STILL A CHANCE FOR PEACE AND THE CRISIS COULD BE SOLVED

PEACEFULLY. WE MUST HEAR THE VOICE OF WISE PEOPLE IN FRANCE,

GERMANY, AND EUROPE CALLING FOR PEACE AND MORE PATIENCE BECAUSE

THE MISSION OF THE WMD INSPECTORS HAS NOT FAILED YET, AND IRAQ IS

COOPERATING WITH THEM ..."

3. ASSUMING THAT THE U.S. LACKS INTERNATIONAL SUPPORT FOR ITS

WAR ON IRAQ, DUBAI-BASED BUSINESS-ORIENTED "AL BAYAN"

EDITORIALIZED ON 2/10:

"... ONCE AGAIN THE U.S. DOES NOT HAVE INTERNATIONAL SUPPORT FOR

ITS PLANS IN IRAQ. IT WILL BE A DISASTER IF THE U.S. GOES TO WAR

ALONE, IGNORING THE U.N. THIS HAS ONE EXPLANATION ONLY, I.E.,

THAT THE U.S. IS PLAYING THE ROLE OF A NEW IMPERIAL POWER TRYING

TO DOMINATE OTHERS, AND CONSEQUENTLY VIOLATING INTERNATIONAL

LAWS."

4. PRAISING THE FRENCH-RUSSIAN-GERMAN INITIATIVE, "AL ITTIHAD"

EDITORIALIZED ON 2/12:

"... EFFORTS EXERTED BY FRANCE, RUSSIA, GERMANY, AND OTHER

EUROPEAN COUNTRIES MUST BE TREATED WELL AS THEY MAY LEAD TO GOOD

RESULTS. THEY ALSO MUST BE TREATED CAREFULLY AS SOME MAY

CONSIDER THEM AS A SHIELD TO PROTECT THE WRONG PEOPLE. HOWEVER,

THEY ARE A NEW CHANCE TO BE TAKEN SERIOUSLY EVEN IF THE THERE IS

LITTLE HOPE."

5. DESCRIBING THE CONFLICT INSIDE NATO AS A REAL CRISIS BETWEEN

WASHINGTON AND ITS ALLIES, SHARJAH-BASED PAN-ARAB "AL KHALEEJ"

EDITORIALIZED ON 2/13:

"... ARAB COUNTRIES MUST, IN THESE CRITICAL MOMENTS, TAKE THE

OPPORTUNITY TO SUPPORT THE FRENCH-GERMAN EFFORTS (FOR A PEACEFUL

RESOLUTION) AND BECOME A PRESSING FORCE TO STOP THE AMERICAN

DRIVE TOWARDS AN AGGRESSION BRINGING DISASTERS FOR ARABS IF IT

HAPPENS."

6. COMMENTING ON THE DISCUSSION INSIDE THE UNSC AND THE

WORLDWIDE DEMONSTRATIONS AGAINST A U.S.-LED WAR ON IRAQ, "AL

KHALEEJ" EDITORIALIZED ON 2/16:

"... THE U.S. ISOLATION INCREASED DURING THE LAST TWO DAYS. THE

WORLD PROVES THAT IT IS NOT A HOSTAGE TO THE AMERICAN

ADMINISTRATION AND ITS PLANS. IT ALSO PROVES THAT IT IS READY TO

RESIST THOSE PLANS. THE ARABS MUST JOIN THIS PARADE IN THEIR

NEXT SUMMIT."

7. IN ANOTHER EDITORIAL, "AL KHALEEJ" SAID ON 2/15:

"... THE U.S.-BRITISH-ISRAELI WAR AGAINST IRAQ WILL AFFECT ALL

ARABS. THUS, THE TITLE OF THE AWAITED REGIONAL SUMMIT MUST BE

\'NO TO WAR\' IN THE REGION."

8. ARGUING THAT THERE IS A FISSURE IN THE ALLIANCE OF WAR

SUPPORTERS, "AL ITTIHAD" EDITORIALIZED ON 2/16:

"... THE ARABS - WHO ARE EXPECTED TO HOLD THEIR SUMMIT NEXT WEEK

- MUST TAKE THE OPPORTUNITY AND JOIN THE ORGANIZED INTERNATIONAL

EFFORTS HOPING TO FIND A PEACEFUL SOLUTION FOR THE CRISIS,

PUTTING PRESSURE ON THE IRAQI REGIME TO COMPLY WITH THE U.N.

RESOLUTIONS, AND PLACING THE INTEREST OF THE IRAQI PEOPLE OVER

ANY PERSONAL INTERESTS."

9. CRITICIZING THE SILENCE OF ARABS, ABU DHABI-BASED PAN-ARAB

"AKHBAR AL ARAB" EDITORIALIZED ON 2/14:

"... UNDOUBTEDLY, THE ARABS WILL BE THE NEXT TARGET OF AN ATTACK

FOLLOWING THE OCCUPATION AND DESTRUCTION OF IRAQ. WASHINGTON IS

NOT KEEPING THIS A SECRET ANYMORE. IT TALKS IN PUBLIC ABOUT

REDRAWING THE MIDDLE EAST MAP IN A WAY TO SERVE AMERICAN AND THEN

ISRAELI INTERESTS."
