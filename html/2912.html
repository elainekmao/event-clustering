SUBJECT: JORDAN\'S THANKS ON WTO SUBSIDY ISSUE

REF: AMMAN 2040

Samer Tawil, the Secretary General of the Ministry of

Industry and Trade, called to express Jordan\'s appreciation

for the way the U.S. delegation in Geneva handled the issue

of Jordan\'s request that the WTO agree to an extension of

Jordan\'s tax break for corporate profits earned from exports.

Tawil said that the bilateral meetings between the U.S. and

Jordanian delegations had been very constructive. He

appreciated the chance to explain Jordan\'s point of view and

understood the broader considerations the U.S. delegation had

to take into account. Post adds its thanks for the efficient

handling of this issue.
