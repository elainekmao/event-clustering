SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION: IRAQ SECRETARIAL VISIT EXPLOSIONS IN

BAGHDAD

1. SUMMARY: SECRETARY RUMSFELD\'S VISIT TO THE UAE AND OTHER

COUNTRIES IN THE REGION, CIVILIAN DEATHS DUE TO AN ARMS DUMP

EXPLOSION IN BAGHDAD, AND ANTI-AMERICAN DEMONSTRATIONS IN IRAQ

DOMINATED COVERAGE IN UAE MEDIA. IN "AL BAYAN," UAE COLUMNIST

AYSHA SULTAN CRITICIZED ANY \'EXCUSES\' GIVEN FOR THE CRIMES

COMMITTED BY THE PREVIOUS IRAQI REGIME. IN "AL ITTIHAD," UAE

COLUMNIST DR. SHAMLAN AL EISSA CRITICIZED THE ATTITUDES OF A FEW

GULF COUNTRIES TOWARDS THE AMERICAN PRESENCE IN IRAQ. EDITOR-IN-

CHIEF OF "AL BAYAN," KHALED MOHAMMED AHMED, COMMENTED ON THE MANY

CONSEQUENCES OF THE WAR AND ITS EFFECTS ON NOT ONLY IRAQ BUT THE

ENTIRE ARAB WORLD. END SUMMARY.

2. U.S. SECRETARY OF DEFENSE DONALD RUMSFELD\'S VISIT TO THE GULF

STARTED WITH THE UAE AND WAS COVERED IN ALL ARABIC AND ENGLISH

NEWSPAPERS. EXCERPTS OF SECRETARY RUMSFELD\'S ABU DHABI TV

INTERVIEW WERE ALSO CARRIED IN UAE DAILIES. THE INTERVIEW WAS

BROADCAST IN ITS ENTIRETY DURING THE TWO-HOUR PROGRAM, "THE

TURNING POINT," ON ABU DHABI TV THE SAME NIGHT AND REBROADCAST

DURING MORNING NEWS PROGRAMMING.

3. CRITICIZING ANY EXCUSES FOR THE CRIMES COMMITTED BY THE

PREVIOUS IRAQI REGIME, UAE COLUMNIST AYSHA SULTAN WROTE IN DUBAI-

BASED BUSINESS-ORIENTED ARABIC DAILY "AL-BAYAN" ON 4/28:

"... HOW IS IT THAT SOME ARE STILL LOOKING FOR EXCUSES FOR THIS

REGIME, DESCRIBING IT AS PAN-ARABIST BECAUSE IT DID NOT ACQUIESCE

TO SIGNING ANY AGREEMENTS WITH ISRAEL AND IT OPPOSED THE U.S. AND

IT ... ? HOW COULD EXCUSES BE GIVEN TO THE MASSACRES, THE

INJUSTICE, AND THE TYRANNY? WHERE DID THE MILLIONS OF THE (MONEY

OF THE) IRAQI PEOPLE GO? AND THE OIL BILLIONS? WHY IS

EVERYTHING BEING GATHERED INTO BOXES AND BAGS BY THE AMERICANS,

AND TRANSFERRED TO UNKNOWN \'SECURE\' PLACES ABOUT WHICH ONLY THE

AMERICANS KNOW? WHY WEREN\'T THEY USED FOR THE BENEFIT OF THE

PEOPLE OF IRAQ? FOR THE DEVELOPMENT OF IRAQ? FOR REBUILDING

IRAQ? WHEN THE WATER PUMP INITIATED BY SHEIKH ZAYED BIN SULTAN

AL NAHYAN IN BASRA WAS READY WITHIN ONLY 36 HOURS, WHILE A WHOLE

REGIME WAS UNABLE TO PROVIDE THIS FOR 12 YEARS, LEAVING PEOPLE TO

DIE OF THIRST, THEN WHAT COULD BE SAID AFTERWARDS? WHAT DEFENSE

COULD BE ACCEPTABLE OR EVEN FORGIVEN?"

4. COMMENTING ON THE DOUBLE STANDARDS OF ARAB LEADERS ON THE

U.S. PRESENCE IN IRAQ, DR. ANWAR GHARGHASH WROTE IN ABU DHABI-

BASED SEMI-GOVERNMENT "AL ITTIHAD" ON 4/28:

"... THE REQUEST OF THE NEIGHBORING COUNTRIES FOR THE U.S. TO

SHORTEN ITS PRESENCE IN THE REGION SHOULD BE ACCOMPANIED BY INNER

INITIATIVES TO DEAL WITH THESE NEW ASPECTS. THESE INITIATIVES

SHOULD INCLUDE AN OPEN METHOD ASSERTING THE POLITICAL AND CIVIL

RIGHTS OF THE PEOPLE. THESE COUNTRIES SHOULD NOT REJECT THE U.S.

PRESENCE ON THE GROUNDS OF INDEPENDENCE, WHILE THEY DO NOT

RESPECT OTHER VALUES THAT ARE ESSENTIAL FOR AN ARAB PERSON ...

AND WHOEVER IS ASKING WASHINGTON TO LEAVE IRAQ CAN\'T CONTINUE HIS

ABUSIVE TREATMENT OF HIS OWN PEOPLE ..."

5. URGING NO ONE TO BELIEVE THAT THE U.S. OCCUPATOPM OF IRAQ IS

TO ESTABLISH DEMOCRACY, SHARJAH-BASED PAN-ARAB DAILY "AL-KHALEEJ"

EDITORIALIZED 4/27:

"... MAYBE HISTORY, BOTH RECENT AND ANCIENT HISTORY, CONFIRMS

THAT THE U.S. IS AND WAS NEVER A COUNTRY THAT RESPECTS DEMOCRACY

AND THE DECISIONS OF OTHER COUNTRIES. THROUGH ITS POLICY, THE

U.S. HAS BEEN GREATLY SUPPORTING TOTALITARIANISM AND

DICTATORSHIP. ALL ITS (U.S.) WARS IN THE WORLD WERE AIMED AT

DEFEATING THE PEOPLE AND CONFISCATING THEIR LIBERTY IN ORDER TO

ATTAIN ITS BENEFITS AS A GREATER COUNTRY. NOW THE U.S. IS

SEEKING TO ESTABLISH A NEW WORLD ORDER UNDER ITS OWN CONTROL TO

DOMINATE THE WORLD THROUGH POWER AND FORCE, NOT THROUGH LIBERTY

AND DEMOCRACY ..."

6. UNDER THE HEADLINE "BURGLARY IN IRAQ," "AL-BAYAN"

EDITORIALIZED 4/27:

"... WHAT WE ARE SEEING NOW CONFIRMS THAT THE U.S. IS SEEKING

TOTAL HEGEMONY IN IRAQ, OR SPECIFICALLY IN REGARDS TO ITS

ECONOMIC WEALTH. IT (THE U.S.) HAS ALREADY CHOSEN TWO FINANCIAL

OFFICIALS FROM THE DEPARTMENT OF THE TREASURY IN ORDER TO CONTROL

THE ECONOMY OF IRAQ, AND THEY ARE \'PETER MCPHERSON\' AND \'GEORGE

WOOLF.\' SO IT IS CLEAR WHO WILL BE SELLING IRAQI OIL AND WHO HAS

THE RIGHT FOR ITS DISPOSAL?!"

7. CRITICIZING THE ATTITUDES OF A FEW GULF COUNTRIES TOWARDS THE

AMERICAN PRESENCE IN IRAQ, DR. SHAMLAN AL EISSA WROTE IN "AL

ITTIHAD" ON 4/27:

"... MAYBE WE DO NOT HAVE THE SAME STANCE AS ISLAMIC IRAN AND

NATIONALISTIC SYRIA HAVE, BUT WE DO UNDERSTAND THEIR POINT OF

VIEW AND ACCEPT IT. WHAT WE DO NOT UNDERSTAND IS THE STANCE OF

SOME OF THE GULF COUNTRIES TOWARDS THE U.S. SOME OF THESE

COUNTRIES HAVE STRONG CONNECTIONS WITH THE U.S. IN ADDITION TO

HAVING U.S. MILITARY BASES FOR OVER 30 YEARS ..."

8. COMMENTING ON THE DILEMMA CONFRONTING THE ARAB WORLD

FOLLOWING THE WAR IN IRAQ, "AL-BAYAN" EDITORIALIZED 4/26:

"...THE TRUTH IS THAT THE ARABS ARE FACED WITH A REAL DILEMMA

BECAUSE OF AMERICAN CONTROL (OF THE REGION). ONE OF ITS

COUNTRIES (ARAB COUNTRIES) FELL UNDER AMERICAN OCCUPATION, SO IT

IS FAR FROM THE TRUTH THAT WASHINGTON MIGHT REPLAY THIS

INEXPENSIVE GAME IN ORDER TO OCCUPY ANOTHER ARAB COUNTRY..."

9. IN A CLOSING STATEMENT, SALIM WROTE IN "AL-KHALEEJ" ON 4/26:

"UNTIL NOW, THE U.S. HAS NOT BEEN ABLE TO PROVE THAT IRAQ HAS WMD

DURING THE ERA OF THE PREVIOUS REGIME, DESPITE THE FACT AND

DESPERATE INSPECTION EFFORTS CARRIED OUT BY U.S. TROOPS IN IRAQ.

WASHINGTON HAS TO PROVE THIS IN ORDER TO HAVE A MAJOR EXCUSE FOR

ITS ATTACK ON IRAQ, OTHERWISE, IT WILL LOSE ITS CREDIBILITY,

WHICH IT ALREADY LOST EVEN BEFORE ITS ATTACKS. WHAT IF THE BUSH

ADMINISTRATION PROVIDES PROOF BUT DOES NOT SUCCEED IN FINDING THE

WMD? NOTHING! WHO WILL THEN PUNISH THE U.S.?"

10. UNDER THE HEADLINE "THE CONSEQUENCES OF WAR," EDITOR-IN-

CHIEF KHALED MOHAMMED AHMED WROTE IN A FRONT-PAGE ARTICLE IN "AL

BAYAN" ON 4/26:

"... THE ARAB REGION WILL BE FACED WITH INTERESTING DEVELOPMENTS

THAT SWING BETWEEN TENSION AND APPEAL, BETWEEN ATTRACTION AND

INTIMIDATION, AND BETWEEN SHOCK AND AWE. SO ARE THE ARABS READY

FOR THIS? HAVE THEY BENEFITED FROM THE IRAQI LESSON, OR DO THEY

NEED MORE SHOCKS? NOW, AND THROUGH BAGHDAD, PASSES THE ROADMAP.

WILL IT ALLOW THE PALESTINIANS TO REACH THE END OF THE ROAD, OR

IS THE PATH STILL LADEN WITH THORNS EVEN AFTER THE FALL OF

BAGHDAD?"

11. UNDER THE HEADLINE, "FACING THE EARTHQUAKE AND ITS

AFTERSHOCKS," "AL KHALEEJ" EDITORIALIZED 4/25:

"... THERE IS A NEED FOR AN ACCURATE, TRANSPARENT AND THOROUGH

LOOK AT REALITY ... IN ADDITION, WE NEED TO TAKE INTO

CONSIDERATION THIS NATION\'S POTENTIAL AND CAPABILITIES THAT COULD

BE EMPLOYED IN THE RIGHT DIRECTION. THIS IS ONLY IF ARAB

COUNTRIES REALLY WANT TO DEVELOP IT THROUGH A COOPERATIVE ARAB

EFFORT TO THE LEVEL THAT MAKES THIS NATION PRODUCTIVE AND ABLE TO

GUIDE ITS OWN FUTURE ... WITHOUT THIS, THE CONSEQUENCES OF THE

EARTHQUAKE WILL DESTROY WHAT IS LEFT."
