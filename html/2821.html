SUBJECT: ITALY/MIDDLE EAST PEACE PROCESS: BERLUSCONI WITH

PERES AND SHAATH IN VALENCIA

CLASSIFIED BY: POLITICAL MINCOUNS THOMAS COUNTRYMAN, REASON 1.5 B, D

1. (C) ON APRIL 24, PM BERLUSCONI\'S SENIOR DIPLOMATIC

ADVISOR GIOVANNI CASTELLANETA TOLD EUR A/S ELIZABETH JONES

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 ROME 02023 241730Z

THAT THE PREVIOUS DAY\'S EU-MED MINISTERIAL IN VALENCIA HAD,

OBVIOUSLY, NOT BEEN SUCCESSFUL IN PROMOTING ISRAELI-ARABIC

DIALOGUE ON HALTING THE VIOLENCE. EMBASSY FOUND HIS READOUT

OF PRIME MINISTER (AND ACTING FOREIGN MINISTER) BERLUSCONI\'S

MEETINGS THERE WITH FM PERES AND PA OFFICIAL NABIL SHA\'ATH TO

BE OF INTEREST.

2. (C) ACCORDING TO CASTELLANETA, ISRAELI FM PERES HAD TOLD

PM BERLUSCONI THAT ISRAEL WOULD BE AGREEABLE TO AN

INTERNATIONAL PEACE CONFERENCE CONVOKED BY THE QUARTET. WHEN

PRESSED, CASTELLANETA CONFIRMED THAT PERES HAD EXPLICITLY

SAID QUARTET SPONSORSHIP, RATHER THAN INSISTING ON A

CONFERENCE SPONSORED BY THE US. HE SPECULATED THAT THERE IS

A SENSE OF "FATIGUE" IN THE ISRAELI GOVERNMENT NOW THAT THEY

HAVE ESTABLISHED A STRONGER MILITARY POSITION, AND THEREFORE

WOULD BE MORE AMENABLE TO NEGOTIATION.

3. (C) IN THE MEETING WITH PERES, BERLUSCONI OFFERED TO HOST

SUCH A MEETING IN ITALY, GIVEN THE GOI\'S PRESENTLY STRONG

RELATIONS WITH BOTH THE PALESTINIANS AND ISRAEL. (BEFORE

LEAVING VALENCIA, BERLUSCONI STATED TO THE PRESS ITALY\'S

WILLINGNESS TO HOST SUCH A CONFERENCE). LATER APRIL 24, LUCA

FERRARI, MFA OFFICE DIRECTOR FOR THE EASTERN MEDITERRANEAN,

SAID THAT ONE OF THE DIPLOMATIC ADVISORS TO PM SHARON HAD

"INFORMALLY" RAISED WITH THE ITALIAN EMBASSY IN TEL AVIV HIS

VIEW THAT ITALY WOULD BE A NATURAL SITE FOR SUCH A MEETING.

FERRARI SAID THE ADVISER\'S STATEMENT HAD BEEN VERY

HYPOTHETICAL AND CONDITIONAL, BUT WAS A CLEAR EXPRESSION OF

SHARON\'S WILLINGNESS TO COME TO ITALY.

4. (C) CASTELLANETA SAID THAT BERLUSCONI HAD BROACHED WITH

SHA\'ATH THE IDEA OF CREATING A "COUNCIL OF MINISTERS" TO TAKE

POLITICAL CONTROL OVER THE PALESTINIAN AREAS, WITH ARAFAT

OCCUPYING THE POSITION OF "HONORARY PRESIDENT." NOT

SURPRISINGLY, SHA\'ATH DID NOT WISH TO MAKE ANY COMMENT ON

THIS IDEA. FERRARI TOLD THE EMBASSY LATER APRIL 24 THAT

BERLUSCONI HAD AT LAST BROACHED WITH THE PALESTINIANS AN IDEA

THE MFA HAD PROPOSED TO HIM EARLIER THIS YEAR: THAT IT WAS

TIME FOR ARAFAT TO BEGIN DEVOLVING POWER TO A COMMITTEE OF

MINISTERS. IN THE GOI\'S VIEW, THE PALESTINIANS WOULD BE

BETTER SERVED IF ARAFAT BECAME, SOONER RATHER THAN LATER, A

"MERE PRESIDENT", CEREMONIAL IN THE ITALIAN SENSE RATHER THAN

POWERFUL IN THE FRENCH SENSE. ACCORDING TO FERRARI, ITALY

HAD NOT YET DISCUSSED THIS IDEA WITH EU PARTNERS, BUT HE

EXPECTED IT WOULD DO SO SOON.
