SUBJECT: STRENGTHENING GLOBAL SECURITY:

RIGHTSIZING THE IAEA SAFEGUARDS BUDGET

REF: a) STATE 13752 b) 02 Abu Dhabi 6747

1. The Ambassador presented ref A letter from the

Deputy Secretary to MFA Minstate Shaykh Hamdan Bin

Zayid Al-Nahyan during a 1/21 meeting with MFA

Assistant U/S for Political Affairs Abdullah Rashid

Al-Nuaimi. The Ambassador laid out the reasons for

our recommendation to upgrade the IAEA safeguards

budget and requested that the UAEG seriously

consider increasing their slice of the budget,

particularly in light of the fact that the UAE

recently signed a safeguards agreement with the

IAEA. Al-Nuaimi indicated that the matter would

likely be referred to the Ministry of Electricity

and Power, which has responsibility for IAEA-related

issues.
