SUBJECT: NIGERIA: ARMY MINISTER UPDATE ON OFR AND UNAMSIL

ISSUES

REF: A. ABUJA 2297

B. ABUJA 2218

SENSITIVE BUT UNCLASSIFIED - PROTECT ACCORDINGLY

1. (SBU) Army Minister 14 September called PolMilOff on OFR

and UNAMSIL issues discussed in recent weeks. Batagarawa

stated that he had spoken with Comptroller General of

Customs, who had agreed to give his clearance to the

container carrying some OFR items from Ghana stuck at the

land border. The Army Minister said he expected to have the

OFR MOI in his hands by Wednesday, 19 September. He said he

anticipated that the MOI could be signed shortly thereafter.

(He was clearly frustrated with the internal MOD handling of

the issue.)

2. (SBU) Finally, Batagarawa said he had instructed the

Nigerian Defense Advisor to the UN to work with USUN

personnel to do what is necessary to get the Nigerian

vehicles in Sierra Leone covered by the LOA.

3. (U) Freetown minimize considered.
