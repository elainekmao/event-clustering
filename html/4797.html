SUMMARY

---------

1. (S) THE CURRENT OIL FOR FOOD (OFF) DISTRIBUTION PROGRAM

IN IRAQ DELIVERS FOOD TO ALL IRAQIS. IN THE MONTH OF

NOVEMBER, 25.8 MILLION BENEFICIARIES RECEIVED A FOOD RATION

FROM 49,000 SMALL SHOPS THAT SERVE AS THE DISTRIBUTION

POINTS. WHOLE-GRAIN WHEAT IS IMPORTED AND MILLED INTO FLOUR

INSIDE IRAQ. THE U.N. WORLD FOOD PROGRAM (WFP) OVERSEES THE

DISTRIBUTIONS IN THE SOUTH/CENTRAL REGIONS AND PLAYS A MORE

INTEGRAL ROLE IN THE WHOLE PROCESS IN THE THREE NORTHERN

GOVERNORATES. UNDER THE OFF PROGRAM, WFP DOES NOT TENDER FOR

THE PURCHASE OF ANY FOOD, CONTRACTS NO OUTSIDE TRANSPORT, AND

INSIDE IRAQ, CONTRACTS TRANSPORT ONLY FOR THE THREE NORTHERN

GOVERNORATES, WORKS WITH NO NON-GOVERNMENTAL ORGANIZATIONS

(NGOS) FOR THE GENERAL DISTRIBUTION, DOES NOT ACTUALLY

CONDUCT ANY DISTRIBUTIONS--EVEN IN THE NORTH, AND DOES NOT

MANAGE THE FOOD PIPELINE. ALL THESE FACTORS IMPACT WFP\'S

ABILITY TO GEAR UP QUICKLY IF THE GOVERNMENT OF IRAQ\'S (GOI)

ROLE IN THE OFF SYSTEM COLLAPSED. WFP HAS VAST EXPERIENCE IN

ALL THESE AREAS, BUT IT WOULD TAKE TIME TO MOUNT SUCH AN

OPERATION. THE INITIAL WFP RATION, TARGETED TO THE MOST

VULNERABLE, WOULD CONSIST OF ITS STANDARD EMERGENCY RATION,

WHICH IS LESS DIVERSE THAN THE CURRENT OFF RATION. END

SUMMARY.

3. (U) THE CONTRACTS FOR ALL COMMODITIES ARE TENDERED AND

PURCHASED BY THE GOI, AFTER HAVING BEEN APPROVED BY THE

OFFICE OF THE IRAQ PROGRAM (OIP). ALL COMMODITIES ARE

IMPORTED. THE GOI ARRANGES FOR THE TRANSPORT OF ALL

COMMODITIES OUTSIDE THE COUNTRY TO THE LARGE TRANSIT

WAREHOUSES INSIDE IRAQ. FROM THE TRANSIT WAREHOUSES, THE

COMMODITIES ARE TAKEN TO THE VARIOUS GOVERNORATES\'

WAREHOUSES, I.E., THE EXTENDED DELIVERY POINTS (EDPS). AT

THE EDPS, THE COMMODITIES ARE TRANS-SHIPPED ONTO SMALLER

TRUCKS AND DELIVERED TO THE NUMEROUS FOOD AGENTS.

4. (U) IN THE NORTH, THE PROCEDURE IS VERY SIMILAR. THE

COMMODITIES ARE DELIVERED TO WFP-MANAGED TRANSIT WAREHOUSES

IN MOSUL AND KIRKUK. MOSUL AND KIRKUK ARE LOCATED IN THE

CENTRAL REGION BUT CLOSE TO THE BORDER WITH THE NORTHERN

GOVERNORATES. WFP, WORKING WITH THE LOCAL GOVERNMENTS OF THE

THREE NORTHERN GOVERNORATES, CALLS FORWARD THE FOOD TO THE

THREE EDP WAREHOUSES IN THE NORTH LOCATED IN SULAYMANIYAH,

DAHUK, AND ARBIL. FROM THERE, THE COMMODITIES ARE

TRANS-SHIPPED ONTO SHORT-HAUL TRUCKS AND DELIVERED TO THE

FOOD AGENTS (SMALL SHOPS) FOR FINAL DISTRIBUTION. WFP

CONTRACTS THE TRUCKING IN THE NORTH FOR THE EDP AND FOOD

AGENT DELIVERIES.

5. (U) ALL WHEAT IS IMPORTED AS WHOLE-GRAIN WHEAT AND IS

MILLED INTO FLOUR INSIDE IRAQ BEFORE BEING TRANSPORTED TO

WAREHOUSES. THERE ARE AT LEAST ONE TO TWO MILLS IN EACH

GOVERNORATE, AS WELL AS A VERY LARGE ONE IN PORT UMM QASR.

SOME OF THE BIGGER MILLS ARE ABLE TO MILL 500-600 MT PER DAY.

ALL WHEAT DESTINED FOR THE NORTH IS MILLED IN THE

SOUTHERN/CENTRAL REGIONS. (NOTE: THERE ARE MILLS IN THE

NORTH, HOWEVER THEY WOULD NEED TO BE REPAIRED BEFORE BECOMING

OPERATIONAL. END NOTE.) THE MILLS EITHER OPERATE ON

ELECTRICITY OR VIA GENERATOR. SILOS, AND SOMETIMES ALSO

WAREHOUSES, ARE SITUATED WITH THE MILLS.

6. (U) THE TOTAL TONNAGE DISTRIBUTED PER MONTH IN THE NORTH

IS 50,000-60,000 MT PER MONTH AND IN THE SOUTH/CENTRAL

REGIONS IS 400,000-420,000 MT PER MONTH. SINCE THE PROGRAM

BEGAN, A MONTHLY DISTRIBUTION HAS NEVER BEEN MISSED.

SOMETIMES ONE OR TWO COMMODITIES MAY BE ABSENT, BUT THEY ARE

PROVIDED RETROACTIVELY IN A SUBSEQUENT DISTRIBUTION.

-------------------

BENEFICIARY LISTS

-------------------

7. (C) IN THE ENTIRE COUNTRY, THE DISTRIBUTION IS DONE VIA

SMALL SHOPS BY THE FOOD AGENTS. THE FOOD AGENT RECEIVES EACH

MONTH THE LIST OF BENEFICIARIES. FOR THE CENTRAL/SOUTH, THE

GOI MAINTAINS THE DATABASE OF BENEFICIARIES; IN THE NORTH,

WFP MAINTAINS THE DATABASE. EACH BENEFICIARY RECEIVES A

RATION CARD THAT IS PRINTED BY THE GOI, HOWEVER IN THE NORTH

PAGE 04 ROME 06072 02 OF 04 191634Z

THE NAMES OF THE FAMILY MEMBERS ARE PUT ON THE CARDS BY WFP.

THE FOOD AGENT IS RESPONSIBLE FOR KEEPING RECORDS OF RECEIPT

FOR EACH MONTH\'S DISTRIBUTION, AND HE IS REQUIRED TO SEND IN

THE REPORT TO THE GOI. IF SOMEONE MOVES, HE/SHE MUST BE

TAKEN OFF ONE LIST BEFORE BEING ADDED TO A NEW LIST. IF A

BABY IS BORN, ONE MUST FILL OUT THE APPROPRIATE PAPERWORK TO

ADD A NEW FAMILY MEMBER. IT IS THE FOOD AGENT\'S

RESPONSIBILITY TO KNOW HIS BENEFICIARIES, WHICH ON AVERAGE

TOTAL 150 FAMILIES PER FOOD AGENT. BENEFICIARIES PAY

APPROXIMATELY 0.12 USD FOR THE MONTHLY RATION. (AVERAGE

INCOME IS ABOUT 3 USD PER MONTH.) IN THE SOUTH/CENTRAL

REGIONS, WFP MONITORS THE DISTRIBUTION ONLY.

8. (U) THE SAME DISTRIBUTION SYSTEM EXISTS IN THE NORTH,

BUT WFP PLAYS A MORE ACTIVE ROLE IN THE ENTIRE PROCESS. WFP

MAINTAINS THE DATABASE, THUS THE FOOD AGENTS RECEIVE THEIR

MONTHLY LISTS FROM WFP, HOWEVER THE LOCAL GOVERNORATES

ADMINISTRATION IS INVOLVED. THE GOI ALSO RECEIVES REPORTS

REGARDING THE DISTRIBUTIONS SINCE IT HANDLES THE PROCUREMENT.

ALSO IN THE NORTH, WFP CONDUCTS POST-DISTRIBUTION MONITORING

(PDM) IN COORDINATION WITH A GOVERNORATE REPRESENTATIVE. A

IF WFP WERE TO ASSUME RESPONSIBILITY

--------------------------------------

11. (S) WFP HAS PARTICULAR CONSIDERATIONS IF IT WERE TO TAKE

OVER TOTAL RESPONSIBILITY FOR FOOD DISTRIBUTIONS INSIDE

COUNTRY--EVEN IF THE BENEFICIARY NUMBERS WERE LOWER. UNDER

THE CURRENT OFF OPERATION, WFP DOES NO/NO TENDERING FOR FOOD

PURCHASES, CONTRACTS NO/NO OUTSIDE TRANSPORT AND INSIDE IRAQ,

CONTRACTS TRANSPORT ONLY FOR THE THREE NORTHERN GOVERNORATES,

WORKS WITH NO/NO NON-GOVERNMENTAL ORGANIZATIONS (NGOS) FOR

THE GENERAL DISTRIBUTION, DOES NOT ACTUALLY DO ANY

DISTRIBUTIONS--EVEN IN THE NORTH, AND DOES NOT MANAGE THE

FOOD PIPELINE. ALL THESE FACTORS IMPACT WFP\'S ABILITY TO

GEAR UP QUICKLY IF IT WERE NECESSARY TO ASSUME RESPONSIBILITY

FOR ANY OR ALL OF THESE TASKS, WHICH WOULD BE NECESSARY IF

THE GOI\'S ROLE IN THE OFF SYSTEM COLLAPSED. EVEN THOUGH WFP

HAS VAST EXPERIENCE IN ALL THESE AREAS, NONE OF THEM CAN BE

ACHIEVED OVERNIGHT.

12. (S) REF TEL RAISED SEVERAL CONCERNS OF WFP\'S THAT ARE

NECESSARY FOR ANY FOOD DISTRIBUTION OPERATION, I.E., ACCESS

TO SUFFICIENT TRUCKING CAPACITY WITHIN THE REGION, ACCESS TO

COUNTRY, ABILITY TO HIRE TRUCK DRIVERS AT AFFORDABLE RATES,

MAINTAIN THE FLEETS. IF WFP HAD TO RUN CONVOY TEAMS, AS IT

WFP RATION

-------------

15. (C) WFP FEELS THE MOST VULNERABLE ARE SITUATED IN THE

URBAN CENTERS, WHICH ALSO MIGHT BE THE AREAS MOST DIFFICULT

TO ACCESS. DURING THE INITIAL EMERGENCY PHASE, WFP WOULD

SUPPLY ITS STANDARD RATION COMPOSED OF A CEREAL (MOST LIKELY

WHEAT), PULSES, AND OIL FOR A TOTAL OF 2,100-2,400

SECRET

PAGE 03 ROME 06072 04 OF 04 191635Z

KILOCALORIES (KC) PER PERSON PER DAY. (2,400 KC FOR COLDER

CLIMATES PENDING FOOD AVAILABILITY). DUE TO THE FACTORS

LISTED ABOVE, WFP WOULD NOT, IN THE BEGINNING STAGES AT

LEAST, BE ABLE TO PROVIDE ALL THE ITEMS CURRENTLY PROVIDED IN

THE OFF RATION. THE SOAP AND DETERGENT WOULD HAVE TO BE

FUNDED BY ANOTHER ORGANIZATION, BUT PERHAPS COULD BE

PIGGY-BACKED ON THE SAME DISTRIBUTION MECHANISM, AS IT IS

NOW. AS THINGS FELL INTO PLACE, AND IF WFP HAD THE FUNDING

TO PURCHASE OTHER ITEMS, IT COULD LATER PROVIDE THE LARGER

RATION.

16. (S) IN THE INTIAL STAGE, THE EMERGENCY RATION WOULD BE

GIVEN ONLY TO THE MOST VULNERABLE, WHICH MIGHT BE CAUSE FOR

DISGRUNTLEMENT AMONG THE POPULATION ON TWO COUNTS: 1) SOME,

EVEN THOUGH VULNERABLE, MAY BE UNHAPPY WITH THE RATION SIZE

AND LACK OF DIVERSITY, AND 2) OTHERS MAY NOT LOOK KINDLY ON

THE CRITERIA DEVELOPED TO DETERMINE VULNERABILITY, AS THERE

IS NO QUALIFICATION CRITERIA NOW. THE CURRENT RATION HAS

BEEN INSTITUTIONALIZED; MANY DO NOT HAVE INCOMES; AND IF IT

WERE STOPPED "HUGE SWATHS OF THE POPULATION WOULD BE

AFFECTED," STATES WFP.

THE FUTURE SYSTEM?

--------------------

FULFILL THEIR FOOD NEEDS, A STAFF MEMBER CAUTIONED THAT THE

CURRENT SYSTEM IS ONE WAY IN WHICH THE GOVERNMENT EXERTS
