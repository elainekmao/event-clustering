SUBJECT: (C) UN/IRAQ: USING THE 12/18 SESSION TO CONDEMN

SADDAM\'S SPEECH

REF: USUN 3044

Classified By: AMBASSADOR RICHARD H. JONES; REASON 1.5 (B, D)

1. (C) Post concurs that the 12/18 UNSC session to receive

the report from Coordinator for Kuwaiti Property Yuli

Vorontsov provides a good opportunity for the USG to denounce

Saddam\'s December 7 speech addressed to the Kuwaitis, which

was billed as an apology but was in fact filled with insults,

threats and incitement to violence. We agree with the

sentiment expressed by the Kuwaiti Mission in New York

(reftel) that this is worthwhile whether or not a

condemnation of the speech makes it into the final press

statement.

JONES
