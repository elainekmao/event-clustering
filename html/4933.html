11. COMMENTS:

Product Data

A. Detailed Product Description: Applications are

invited from entrepreneurs interested in obtaining Test

and Development (T and D) licenses, to operate a

telecommunications system to test and develop third

generation (3G) cellular services.

B. Other information:

(a) Project proposals should be forwarded to

the Telecommunications Regulatory Commission of

Sri Lanka at the above address on or before

January 24, 2003.

Conditions applicable to a license are:

(1) The license would be for a period of one year and

with express statement of expiry of license at the end of

this period.

(2) Operations should be confined to the city limits of

Colombo within a 5 km radius. Only one base station would

be permitted and the power of such base station would be

appropriately restricted.

(3) Allocation of spectrum would be based on the minimum

required for testing based on bandwidth required for one

channel. This would be up to a maximum of 5 MHz of paired

spectrum or equivalent unpaired spectrum.

(4) All operators should open their service to all

interested application service providers on a non-

discriminatory basis. The operators would commit to

deploy all applications available for 3G services or

necessary for a 3G trial.

(5) Operators will not be permitted to levy tariffs from

customers for this service.
