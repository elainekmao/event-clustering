SUBJECT: PERES-BERLUSCONI MEETING: A READ-OUT

CLASSIFIED BY: POL MC TOM COUNTRYMAN FOR REASONS 1.5 (B) AND (D)

1. (C) POLOFF MET WITH THE SPOKESMAN OF THE EMBASSY OF ISRAEL

IN ROME TO OBTAIN A READ-OUT OF THE MAY 10 ROME MEETING

BETWEEN PERES AND BERLUSCONI. WE ALSO OBTAINED A READ-OUT OF

THE SAME MEETING FROM THE MFA. BOTH READ-OUTS WERE CONSISTENT.

-----------

THE QUARTET

-----------

2. (C) PERES STRESSED THE IMPORTANCE OF THE NEWLY FORMED

QUARTET. HE UNDERLINED THAT THE "HARMONIZATION" BETWEEN THE

FOUR, AND ESPECIALLY BETWEEN THE US AND THE EU, IS KEY TO

SOLVING THE CRISIS IN THE MIDDLE EAST. BERLUSCONI SAID HE

UNDERSTOOD AND APPRECIATED PERES\' MESSAGE ON THE NEED TO

COORDINATE EVEN MORE CLOSELY WITH THE US, THAT HE HAS AN

EXCELLENT RELATION WITH THE PRESIDENT, AND THAT HE PLANS TO

RAISE THE MIDDLE EAST ISSUE WITH BUSH ON MAY 27 IN ITALY.

--------------------------

ISRAEL\'S DEMANDS OF ARAFAT

--------------------------

3. (C) PERES STATED THAT THE ISRAELI GOVERNMENT DEMANDS A

100% EFFORT FROM ARAFAT TO STOP THE VIOLENCE AND TERROR

WITHOUT EXPECTING 100% RESULTS. IN ADDITION TO A CEASE-FIRE,

THE ISRAELI GOVERNMENT IS ASKING ARAFAT TO CREATE A CENTRAL

COMMAND OF ALL ARMED MILITIAS IN THE PALESTINIAN TERRITORIES

SO THAT THE ISRAELIS WOULD HAVE A SINGLE RATHER THAN MANY

COUNTERPARTS.

------------------

A PEACE CONFERENCE

------------------

4. (C) ONLY AFTER ISRAEL\'S ABOVE DEMANDS ARE MET, SAID PERES,

WOULD A PEACE CONFERENCE BE USEFUL. THIS CONFERENCE, HE

ADDED, OUGHT TO BE LED BY THE QUARTET TO ADDRESS LESS "THE

MEANS TOWARDS PEACE" AND MORE THE "CHARACTER" OF PEACE. SOME

ARAB STATES SHOULD PARTICIPATE IN THE CONFERENCE BUT NOT

SYRIA, WHICH, ACCORDING TO PERES, IS NOT "READY" YET. ALL

THE DIFFERENT PLANS OUGHT TO BE DISCUSSED: THE SAUDI PLAN,

THE TENET/MITCHELL PLAN, AND THE PERES/ABU ALAA DOCUMENT.

BERLUSCONI REPEATED HIS DESIRE TO HOST THE PEACE CONFERENCE

IN ROME. PERES APPROVED OF ROME AS THE CONFERENCE LOCATION

FOR ITS SUITABLE POLITICAL CLIMATE, ITS GEOGRAPHICAL

PROXIMITY, AND ITS EXCELLENT RELATIONS WITH BOTH SIDES. HE

ADDED HOWEVER THAT THE FINAL DECISION OF LOCATION BELONGS TO

THE US AND THAT THE GOI OUGHT TO DISCUSS IT DIRECTLY WITH THE

USG IF IT WANTS TO HOST THE CONFERENCE IN ROME. BOTH PERES

AND BERLUSCONI AGREED THAT THE CONFERENCE SHOULD TAKE PLACE

WITHIN TWO MONTHS.

-----------------------------

ISRAEL\'S VIEW OF ITALY\'S ROLE

-----------------------------

5. (C) WHEN ASKED HOW THE GOI COULD HELP THE SITUATION IN THE

MIDDLE EAST, PERES ANSWERED:

BY "HARMONIZING" THE EU/US EFFORTS, PURSUING AN ECONOMIC

ASSISTANCE PLAN FOR THE REGION, AND SUPPORTING THE IDEA OF A

PEACE CONFERENCE BY THE END OF JUNE.

6. (C) PERES SAID THAT THE PALESTINIANS HAVE A PRESIDENT BUT

NOT A LEADER IN ARAFAT. HE STRESSED THAT ISRAEL SUPPORTS UN

RESOLUTIONS 242 AND 338 BUT THAT A RETURN TO THE PRE 1967

BORDERS WAS OUT OF THE QUESTION BECAUSE OF ISRAEL\'S SECURITY.
