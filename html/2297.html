1. (C) AMBASSADOR PAID COURTESY CALL ON THE PRESIDENT OF

THE PONTIFICAL COUNCIL FOR INTER-RELIGIOUS DIALOG,

CARDINAL FRANCIS ARINZE. CARDINAL ARINZE IS ONE OF ONLY

TWO BLACK AFRICANS IN THE ROMAN CURIA (REF). THE

CARDINAL DISCUSSED THE COUNCIL\'S COORDINATION OF DIALOG

WITH ALL NON-CHRISTIAN RELIGIONS. THE ONLY EXCEPTION IS

DIALOG WITH THE JEWISH COMMUNITY, WHICH ALONG WITH NON-

CATHOLIC CHRISTAN DENOMINATIONS, IS CONDUCTED BY THE

COUNCIL FOR CHRISTAN UNITY. ARINZE FURTHER EXPLAINED

THAT THE COUNCIL GREW OUT OF THE SECOND VATICAN COUNCIL

IN WHICH IT WAS DECIDED TO TRY TO COLLABORATE WITH OTHER

RELIGIOUS BODIES TO FOSTER BETTER UNDERSTANDING BY

EMPHASIZING WHAT THEY HAVE IN COMMON, AND HOW THEY CAN

BETTER SERVE MANKIND JOINTLY.

2. (C) CARDINAL ARINZE\'S BIGGEST ACCOUNT IS DIALOG WITH

THE MUSLIMS. THE MUSLIM COMMUNITY, WITH 18% OF THE

WORLD\'S POPULATION, IS THE SECOND LARGEST WORLD RELIGION

AFTER CHRISTIANITY WHICH COMPOSES 33%. HE STATED THAT TWO

AREAS IN WHICH THE CATHOLIC CHURCH WISHES TO CHANGE

CURRENT MUSLIM THINKING IS THAT OF 1)EDUCATION AND

2)RELIGIOUS FREEDOM. THE VATICAN IS VERY CONCERNED ABOUT

THE MUSLIM RELIGIOUS EDUCATION SYSTEM WHICH PORTRAYS

CHRISTIANS AS THE "ENEMY"; AND WITH THE LACK OF RELIGIOUS

FREEDOM IN MANY MUSLIM COUNTRIES.

3. (C) THE CARDINAL USED THE OCCASION TO AGAIN EXPRESS

HIS CONDOLENCES FOR THE SEPTEMBER 11 ATTACK. HE SAID IT

WOULD BE A "DISASTER" IF THE CONCEPT OF HUNTINQTON\'S

"CLASH OF CIVILIZATIONS" WERE TO BECOME A REALITY. HE

WENT ON TO STATE, HOWEVER, THAT THE U.S. MUST REALIZE

THAT IF THE PALESTINIAN/ISRAELI CONFLICT WERE SETTLED AND

IF THE EMBARQO AQAINST IRAQ WERE LIFTED, MUCH OF THE

AQENDA OF THE FUNDAMENTALISTS WOULD "EVAPORATE". THE

AMBASSADOR EXPLAINED WHY IT IS NECESSARY TO KEEP THE

EMBARQO ON SADDAM HUSSEIN\'S REQIME. THE CARDINAL REPLIED

THAT EVEN IF THE CASE HAS MERIT, THE IMAQES IN WHICH THE

MUSLIM WORLD SEES ARE THOSE OF "DYING BABIES", DUE TO

HARD-HEARTED, MEAN AMERICANS.

4. (C) CARDINAL ARINZE SPOKE BRIEFLY ABOUT HIS COUNCIL\'S

ROLE IN ORGANIZING THE JANUARY 24 INTER-RELIGIOUS

CONFERENCE IN ASSISI. HE SAID THAT HE EXPECTED

APPROXIMATELY 50 U.S. RELIQIOUS LEADERS TO BE INVITED.

THE AMBASSADOR REQUESTED THAT HE SHARE THE INVITATION

LIST WITH THE EMBASSY AS SOON AS IT IS FINALIZED.

5. (C) COMMENT: THE JANUARY 24 CONFERENCE, ALTHOUGH

OSTENSIBLY A RELIGIOUS CONFERENCE, HAS THE POTENTIAL OF

PRODUCING POLITICAL STATEMENTS. IT IS LIKELY THAT ONE

SUCH STATEMENT WILL BE A CONDEMNATION OF THE EMBARQO IN

IRAQ. POST WILL MONITOR DEVELOPMENTS LEADINQ TO THE

CONFERENCE, AS WELL AS ATTEND IN AN OBSERVER CAPACITY.

NICHOLSON
