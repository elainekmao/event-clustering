SUBJECT: THREE MONTH ANNIVERSARY REMEMBRANCE EVENT OF

SEPTEMBER 11 ATTACKS

REF: SECSTATE 208711

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 02 VATICA 06350 071617Z

1. EMBASSY CONTACTED THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF VATICAN

CITY TO EXPLAIN THE IMPORTANCE OF A THREE-MONTH

COMMEMORATION OF THE SEPTEMBER 11 ATTACK. WE REQUESTED THAT

THE VATICAN HOLD AN EVENT IN REMEMBRANCE ON TUESDAY,

DECEMBER 11 AT 1446. THE VATICAN WILL CONFIRM LATER TODAY A

MEMORIAL SERVICE IN VATICAN CITY AT ST. PETER\'S BASILICA.

WE HAVE ALSO REQUESTED THAT CARDINAL SZOKA, THE PRESIDENT OF

THE PONTIFICAL COMMISSION OF VATICAN CITY, PRESIDE OVER THE

EVENT. WE HAVE ALSO REQUESTED OF THE RECTOR OF THE NORTH

AMERICAN COLLEGE THAT THE SEMINARIANS JOIN IN THE MASS AND

THAT BOTH THE PAPAL HYMN AND THE STAR SPANGLED BANNER BE

SUNG AT THE MASS.

2. WE HAVE ALSO BEEN IN CONTACT WITH VATICAN RADIO AND

REQUESTED THAT A REMEMBRANCE BE BROADCAST AT EXACTLY 1446 ON

TUESDAY, DECEMBER 11. NICHOLSON
