SUBJECT: AMBASSADOR CALLS ON DEAN OF THE COLLEGE OF

CARDINALS

(U) CLASSIFIED BY AMBASSADOR R. JAMES NICHOLSON FOR

REASONS 1.5 (B) (D).

1. (C) AMBASSADOR NICHOLSON PAID COURTESY CALL ON

CARDINAL BERNARDIN GANTIN, DEAN OF THE COLLEGE OF

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 VATICA 06036 231635Z

CARDINALS ON NOVEMBER 23. THE CARDINAL AGAIN

EXPRESSED HIS OUTRAGE AND CONDOLENCES FOR THE

SEPTEMBER 11 TRAGEDY. GANTIN ALSO SPOKE AT LENGTH ON

HIS CLOSE TIES WITH THE UNITED STATES, AS WELL AS HIS

HOPES TO VISIT IN DECEMBER IF HIS HEALTH PERMITS.

2. (C) CARDINAL GANTIN RAISED ONE ISSUE OF SUBSTANCE,

NIGERIA. HE ASKED THE AMBASSADOR IF HE HAS FOCUSED ON

NIGERIA. THE AMBASSADOR REPLIED THAT HE HAS NOT YET,

BUT EMBASSY VATICAN IS WORKING CLOSELY WITH

SANT\'EGIDIO ON A NUMBER OF PROJECTS IN AFRICA, AND WE

HOPE THAT SENATOR DANFORTH WILL TRAVEL TO THE VATICAN

IN DECEMBER TO DISCUSS SUDAN. CARDINAL GANTIN SAID

THAT HE RAISED NIGERIA FOR TWO REASONS. THE FIRST IS

THAT THE ONLY TWO BLACK AFRICAN CARDINALS IN THE ROMAN

CURIA ARE HE (FROM NEIGHBORING BENIN), AND CARDINAL

ARINZE FROM NIGERIA. THE SECOND REASON IS THAT HE IS

PERSONALLY VERY CONCERNED WITH THE NORTHERN NIGERIAN

ISLAMIC/CHRISTIAN VIOLENCE IN THE BENIN BORDER REGION.

3. (C) COMMENT: ALTHOUGH RETIRED FROM VATICAN

GOVERNMENT SERVICE, AS DEAN OF THE COLLEGE OF

CARDINALS, GANTIN COULD PRESIDE OVER THE DISCUSSIONS

AND VOTES OF THE COLLEGE OF CARDINALS IN THE EVENT OF

THE DEATH OF THE POPE. THUS, HE REMAINS AN INDIVIDUAL

OF CONSIDERABLE INFLUENCE IN THE VATICAN. IF THE

CARDINAL TRAVELS TO THE UNITED STATES IN DECEMBER,

POST RECOMMENDS HE MEET WITH APPROPRIATE OFFICIALS IN

THE BUREAU OF AFRICAN AFFAIRS TO DISCUSS HIS CONCERNS

WITH THE SITUATION IN NIGERIA.

NICHOLSON
