SUBJECT: SEPTEMBER 11 COMMEMORATION AMMAN

REF: STATE 163125

1. EMBASSY AMMAN WILL COMMEMORATE THE SEPTEMBER 11 TERROR

ATTACK ANNIVERSARY WITH IN-HOUSE AND OUTREACH ACTIVITIES

DESIGNED TO REMIND OUR JORDANIAN AUDIENCE OF THE TRAGIC

EVENTS BEHIND THE WAR AGAINST TERRORISM. FOLLOWING ARE OUR

MAJOR ACTIVITIES.

2. EMBASSY HAS COMMISSIONED THE PRODUCTION OF A THREE-

VOLUME COMMEMORATIVE BOOK SET OF AMERICAN LITERATURE,

READINGS IN AMERICAN DEMOCRACY, AND OTHER SELECTIONS OF

AMERICAN PROSE WHICH HAVE BEEN TRANSLATED INTO ARABIC BOUND

AS A BOXED SET. EMBASSY HEADS OF SECTION WILL DISTRIBUTE

THE COMMEMORATIVE BOOK SETS IN CALLS ON PRINCIPAL JORDANIAN

CONTACTS ON SEPTEMBER 11. CHARGE WILL DELIVER A SET OF THE

COMMEMORATIVE BOOKS TO FOREIGN MINISTER MUASHER SEPTEMBER 10

IN A BRIEF EVENT AT THE FOREIGN MINISTRY THAT WILL BE

COVERED BY THE JORDANIAN PRESS. PA WILL ISSUE A PRESS

RELEASE TO EXPLAIN THE MEANING AND SIGNIFICANCE OF THE BOOK

SETS.

3. POST WILL RUN AN EDITORIAL BY THE AMBASSADOR IN THE

THREE HIGHEST-CIRCULATION JORDANIAN DAILIES BASED ON THE

THEMES IN REFTEL. PUBLIC DIPLOMACY SECTION WILL ALSO

DISTRIBUTE IIP-PROVIDED MATERIALS RELATED TO SEPTEMBER 11 TO

CONTACTS IN THE PRESS, TARGETTED, IN PARTICULAR AT

JOURNALISTS PREPARING ARTICLES, SUPPLEMENTS AND NEWS

PROGRAMS RELATED TO THE SEPTEMBER 11 ANNIVERSARY.

4. POST HAS CREATED A SPECIAL "SEPTEMBER 11: ONE YEAR

LATER" WEBPAGE ON THE EMBASSY WEBSITE THAT WILL FEATURE

PHOTO GALLERIES, USG OFFICIAL STATEMENTS, IIP ELECTRONIC

JOURNALS, AND LINKS TO OTHER USG 9/11-RELATED WEBSITES.

5. IN HOUSE, ON SEPTEMBER 11, WE WILL HOLD A BRIEF

COMMEMORATIVE EVENT FOR THE EMBASSY COMMUNITY. JTV AND

JORDANIAN PRINT MEDIA PLAN TO SEND PHOTOGRAPHERS TO RECORD

THIS EVENT. WE FURTHER UNDERSTAND THAT JTV IS NEGOTIATING

TO SECURE RIGHTS TO AIR THE ROBERT DENIRO-HOSTED 9/11

SPECIAL FOR THE LOCAL JORDANIAN TELEVISION AUDIENCE.

BERRY
