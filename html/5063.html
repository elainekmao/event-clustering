SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION: MEPI, IRAQ, AND NORTH KOREA

1. SUMMARY: REGIONAL AND UAE MEDIA FOCUSED LARGELY ON A

PERCEIVED DOUBLE STANDARD IN U.S. POLICIES TOWARDS NORTH KOREA

AND IRAQ AS WELL AS THE MIDDLE EAST PARTNERSHIP INITIATIVE

(MEPI). AN "AKHBAR AL ARAB" EDITORIAL QUESTIONED WHY WASHINGTON

DOES NOT APPLY A STRONG STANCE TO NORTH KOREA SIMILAR TO WHAT IT

DOES ON IRAQ. CONVERSELY, AN "AL KHALEEJ" EDITORIAL ARGUED THAT

THE U.S. IS NOT REQUIRED TO START A WAR WITH NORTH KOREA, BUT IT

SHOULD HALT ITS MOVEMENT TO WAR WITH IRAQ. IN ANOTHER EDITORIAL,

"AL KHALEEJ" URGED IRAQ TO PRACTICE SELF-RESTRAINT. "AL BAYAN"

COMMENTED THAT THE U.S. DOES NOT REALIZE ITS POLICIES GENERATE

HOSTILITY WITH MOST PEOPLE AROUND THE GLOBE. COMMENTING ON U.S.

EFFORTS TO PROMOTE MEPI, QATARI WRITER DR. AHMED ABDUL MALAK

CONCLUDES THAT THE RELIGIOUS MAINSTREAM IN THE GULF WILL NOT

ALLOW THE SPREAD OF THESE VALUES. END SUMMARY.

2. COMMENTING ON A PERCEIVED DOUBLE STANDARD IN U.S. POLICIES

TOWARDS NORTH KOREA AND IRAQ, ABU DHABI-BASED PAN-ARAB "AKHBAR AL

ARAB" EDITORIALIZED ON 1/9:

"THE SUPPORTERS FOR WAR STILL PREFER A BUSH-SHARON BRINKMANSHIP

TYPE POLICY WITH IRAQ ... THE QUESTION IS WHY DOESN\'T THE BUSH-

SHARON ADMINISTRATION APPLY THE SAME SORT OF HOSTILE METHODS TO

NORTH KOREA, ESPECIALLY AFTER IT EXPELLED THE NUCLEAR INSPECTORS?

THE REASON IS CLEAR; NORTH KOREA IS STRONG AND UNITED, WHERE AS

IRAQ IS MILITARILY WEAK AND DESTROYED SOCIALLY DUE TO THE

SANCTIONS. IRAQ ALSO SUFFERS FROM THE GROWING MISTAKES OF THE

IRAQI LEADERSHIP. THE MOST IMPORTANT QUESTION IS WHAT WILL

HAPPEN IF THE RESULTS OF THE AMERICAN WAR ON IRAQ ARE NOT WHAT

BUSH-SHARON DESIRE?"

3. COMMENTING ON THE SAME SUBJECT, "AKHBAR AL ARAB"

EDITORIALIZED ON 1/11:

"... THE WAR ON IRAQ IS ONLY A MATTER OF TIME FOR WASHINGTON. IT

IS WAITING TO COMPLETE ITS MILITARY BUILD-UP AND END NEGOTIATIONS

WITH THE BIG POWERS ON EACH SHARE OF THE IRAQI PIE OR, SAY, THE

MORE DELICIOUS ARAB PIE. THE REAL INTENTIONS BEHIND THE WAR ARE

CLEAR NOW."

4. DISCUSSING AN AMERICAN-LED WAR ON IRAQ AND THE NEGOTIATIONS

WITH NORTH KOREA, SHARJAH-BASED PAN-ARAB "AL KHALEEJ"

EDITORIALIZED ON 1/11:

"... WHAT IS REQUIRED NOW IS NOT TO DECLARE WAR AGAINST THE U.S.

AS PYONG YANG HAS DONE. WHAT IS REQUIRED NOW IS TO STAND UP

HONESTLY AND SINCERELY TO STOP THE AMERICAN WAR ON ARABS WHICH IS

SEEN THROUGH ITS AGGRESSION AGAINST IRAQ AND THE INCREASING

ZIONIST AGGRESSION IN PALESTINE."

5. COMMENTING ON WHAT IT CALLED THE \'TRICKY\' STATEMENT OF HANS

BLIX ON THE RESULTS OF WMD INSPECTIONS, "AL KHALEEJ"

EDITORIALIZED ON 1/10:

"... IT (IRAQ) MUST ADOPT A POLICY OF SELF-RESTRAINT IN ORDER TO

PREVENT ANY RESPONCE TO THE PROVOCATIONS OF BUSH, BLAIR, BLIX, OR

THE WMD INSPECTORS. THE INCREASING MILITARY BUILD-UP (IN THE

GULF) BY WASHINGTON AND LONDON IS NOT TOURISM. IT IS FOR A VERY

CLEAR MISSION."

6. ARGUING THAT THE CONFLICT WITH IRAQ CLEARLY PROVIDES THE U.S.

IS AN INSANE, IMPERIAL POWER ABIDING ONLY BY THE LAW OF THE

JUNGLE, DUBAI-BASED BUSINESS-ORIENTED "AL BAYAN" EDITORIALIZED ON

1/11:

"... THROUGH THIS WAR, THE U.S. CONVEYS A MESSAGE TO THOSE WHO

DARE TO THINK OF COMPETING AGAINST IT. THE U.S. IS SAYING THAT

IT IS THE ONLY EMPIRE IN THE WORLD. ITS PLAN OF WORLD DOMINATION

HAS NO ROOM FOR PARTNERS. MOREOVER, EVERYBODY MUST CARRY OUT ITS

ORDERS OR BE PUNISHED THE SAME WAY AS IRAQ ... THE U.S. DOES NOT

REALIZE THAT THIS SITUATION WILL GENERATE MORE HOSTILITY WITH

PEOPLE AROUND THE GLOBE AND ONLY RESULT IN FURTHER ISOLATION."

7. NOTING THE NUMBER OF PEOPLE PROTESTING A WAR ON IRAQ IS

INCREASING, "AL BAYAN" EDITORIALIZED ON 1/10:

"... THE CIRCLE OF PEOPLE REFUSING A WAR IN IRAQ IS WIDENING IN

THE WEST. WILL WASHINGTON LISTEN AND RESPOND?"

8. COMMENTING ON MEPI, EGYPTIAN INTELLECTUAL SYED YASSIN ABU

EDITORIALIZED IN DHABI-BASED SEMI-OFFICIAL "AL ITTIHAD" ON 1/9:

"... IF THE U.S. TRIES TO PRACTICE SELF-CRITICISM - AS PER

RICHARD HASS\' CONFESSION OF THE U.S. BEING POLITICALLY

OPPORTUNIST - WILL IT BE ABLE TO WIDEN THE CIRCLE OF CRITICISM

AND CONFESS ITS TERRIBLE MISTAKE OF LIMITLESSLY SUPPORTING THE

RACIST ISRAEL. ENJOYING THE U.S. SUPPORT, ISRAEL ADOPTS A CLEAR

POLICY TO TERMINATE THE PALESTINIAN PEOPLE. IS THE U.S. READY TO

CONFESS ITS MISTAKE WHEN IT SAYS THAT ISRAEL IS THE OASIS OF

DEMOCRACY IN THE MIDDLE EAST."

9. COMMENTING ON THE AMERICAN PRESENCE IN THE GULF SINCE THE

PREVIOUS WAR WITH IRAQ, QATARI WRITER DR. AHMED ABDUL MALAK WROTE

IN "AL ITTIHAD" ON 1/9:

"... IF THIS PRESENCE IS JUSTIFIED WITH THE SUPPORT OF MANY

(ARAB) GOVERNMENTS, THERE WILL BE A CONFLICT WHEN THE AMERICAN

ADMINISTRATION STARTS FULFILLING ITS PROMISES IN THE REGION,

I.E., THE LIBERAL VALUES ANNOUNCED BY CONDOLEEZA RICE, AND THE

MIDDLE EAST PARTNERSHIP INITIATIVE ANNOUNCED BY COLIN POWELL.

THE RELIGIOUS STREAM IN THE REGION WILL NOT ALLOW THE ACCEPTANCE

OF THESE VALUES. IT WILL CONFRONT ANY GOVERNMENT IF IT DECLARES

ITS CLEAR ACCEPTANCE OF THOSE VALUES ... EVEN THE LIBERAL STREAM

WILL NOT AGREE WITH APPLYING THOSE VALUES - THOUGH IT BELIEVES IN

THEM - SIMPLY BECAUSE IT FEELS THAT THEY ARE AN AMERICAN GIFT

APPLIED THROUGH AMERICAN GUNS AND TANKS. THIS STREAM REJECTS THE

AMERICAN PRESENCE IN THE REGION AND MAY SACRIFICE ITS LIBERAL

VALUES IN RETURN FOR THE DEPARTURE OF AMERICAN FORCES ... IF THE

U.S. BETS ON A SUCCESSFUL DEAL BETWEEN ARABS AND ISRAEL VIA MEPI

AND THE VALUES OF DEMOCRACY, WE BELIEVE THAT IT WILL LOSE ITS BET

... THE U.S. WILL BE A VICTIM OF LOCAL CONTRADICTIONS IN THE

REGION, ESPECIALLY AFTER DEMOCRATIC CHANGES CAUSE PRO-U.S.

GOVERNMENTS TO CHANGE THEIR POLICIES AND STRIVE TO GUARANTEE

THEIR INDEPENDENCE FROM INTERFERENCE IN THEIR INTERNAL AFFAIRS.

FINALLY, ARAB COUNTRIES WILL REALIZE THE RESULTS OF A CHANGING

SYLLABUS, DEMOCRATIC VALUES, HUMAN RIGHTS, AND THE ROLE OF WOMAN

IN DEVELOPMENT. THESE ARE ALL BURNING ISSUES WHICH THE U.S. MAY

MAKE HOTTER."
