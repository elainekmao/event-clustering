SUBJECT: USING SOME OF IRAQ\'S FROZEN ASSETS IN ITALY

REF: A. ROME 1344

B. SECSTATE 126507

C. HQCOALITION 6101344

CLASSIFIED BY: ACTING POL MC JOHN BASS FOR REASONS 1.5 (B) AND (D)

1. (C) ON JUNE 12, LUIGI MACCOTTA, THE MFA\'S OFFICE DIRECTOR

FOR IRAQ, ASKED TO MEET WITH POL NEA WATCHER TO DISCUSS THE

COST OF MAINTAINING THE TWO "IRAQI" WARSHIPS KEPT IN THE

ITALIAN PORT OF LA SPEZIA. (NOTE: IN THE MID-1980S,

FINCANTIERI, A GOI-CONTROLLED SHIPYARD COMPANY, BUILT FOUR

LUPO FRIGATES (SMALL WARSHIPS) FOR THE SADDAM GOVERNMENT.

THESE FRIGATES WERE NEVER DELIVERED BECAUSE OF THE IRAQ/IRAN

WAR AND THEN THE UN EMBARGO (REF A). TWO OF THESE FRIGATES,

PARTIALLY PAID FOR BY THE IRAQIS, REMAIN AT LA SPEZIA. POST

HAS NO INFORMATION ON THE WHEREABOUTS OF THE OTHER TWO SHIPS.

END NOTE). THE COST OF MAINTAINING THESE TWO SHIPS,

ACCORDING TO MACCOTTA, IS $2 MILLION/YEAR, WHICH THE GOI HAS

BEEN PAYING SO FAR.

2. (C) SINCE THE GOI DOES NOT WISH TO CONTINUE TO PAY FOR THE

MAINTENANCE OF THESE IRAQI SHIPS, SAID MACCOTTA, THE MFA

SUBMITTED A PROPOSAL FOR POLITICAL APPROVAL BY WHICH ITALY

WOULD USE FROZEN IRAQI ASSETS TO PAY FOR THE MAINTENANCE OF

THE FRIGATES, AS WELL AS THE EXPENSES OF THE IRAQI INTEREST

SECTION IN ROME, IN ACCORDANCE WITH UN RESOLUTION 1483,

PARAGRAPH 23, SECTION B. MACCOTTA EXPECTED THIS PROPOSAL TO

BE APPROVED AT THE POLITICAL LEVEL SOON.

3. (C) ON THE TOPIC OF IRAQI CHIEFS OF MISSION RETURNING TO

THE MFA IN BAGHDAD (REF B), MACCOTTA SAID THAT THE IRAQI

CHARGE D\'AFFAIRES IN ITALY, FARIS ALI AL SHOKER, HAS NOT BEEN

ASKED TO RETURN TO THE IRAQI MFA (CONFIRMED BY REF C) SINCE

HE IS ONLY A FIRST SECRETARY AND IS NOT A CHIEF OF MISSION.

MACCOTTA SAID THAT AL SHOKER IS NOT "ONE OF THE BAD GUYS."

THE PLAN, SAID MACCOTTA, WOULD BE FOR THE GOI TO RELEASE

FUNDS FROM THE IRAQI FROZEN ASSETS TO AL SHOKER, WHO WOULD IN

TURN PAY FOR MAINTENANCE OF THE SHIPS AND THE EXPENSES OF THE

IRAQI INTEREST SECTION IN ROME.
