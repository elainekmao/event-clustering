SUBJECT: MUGABE\'S TRAVEL PLANS TO CONGO REMAIN

UNCONFIRMED; REPORTEDLY WILL TRAVEL JULY 12

REF: HARARE 3682

CLASSIFIED BY AMBASSADOR TOM MCDONALD.

REASONS: 1.5 (B) AND (D).

(S) FOLLOWING UP ON REPORTS THAT PRESIDENT MUGABE

PLANNED TO TRAVEL TO LUBUMBASHI THE WEEK OF JULY 10 FOR

A BILATERAL MEETING WITH PRESIDENT KABILA (REFTEL),

AMBASSADOR HAS TRIED WITHOUT SUCCESS TO CONFIRM THE

PRESIDENT\'S TRAVEL PLANS WITH SENIOR GOZ CONTACTS. THE

PRESIDENT\'S CHIEF SECRETARY, DR. CHARLES UTETE, WHEN

CALLED BY THE AMBASSADOR, WITH A LAUGH REFUSED TO

COMMENT ON THE PRESIDENT\'S PLANS. OTHER HIGH-LEVEL

CONTACTS ARE IN LOME FOR THE OAU CONFERENCE OR OTHERWISE

UNREACHABLE. A LOCAL DIPLOMAT TOLD ROAL JULY 11 THAT

MUGABE PLANS TO TRAVEL TO LUBUMBASHI ON JULY 12.

MCDONALD
