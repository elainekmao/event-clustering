1. (C) SUMMARY. ITALY\'S 2002 DEFENSE WHITE PAPER (WP)

PRESENTS THE DEFENSE INDUSTRIAL SECTOR AS A PILLAR OF ITALIAN

NATIONAL SECURITY. THE NATIONAL ARMAMENTS DIRECTOR HAS

OVERALL RESPONSIBILITY FOR DEFENSE PROCUREMENT AND

NEGOTIATING INTERNATIONAL DEFENSE INDUSTRIAL COOPERATION

ACCORDS. STATE-CONTROLLED COMPANIES AND A FEW LARGE PRIVATE

FIRMS DOMINATE THE DEFENSE SECTOR, ALTHOUGH A HANDFUL OF

HIGHLY SPECIALIZED NICHE CONTRACTORS ARE COMPETITIVE. THE

GOI SHOULD CONTINUE TO FUND PROGRAMS TO HELP INCREASE EXPORTS

AND MARKET COMPETITIVENESS, AND NEEDS TO SPEND MORE ON BASIC

R&D, ACCORDING TO THE WP. THE WP CALLS FOR GREATER

INTRA-EUROPEAN COORDINATION OF DEFENSE INDUSTRIAL POLICY, BUT

AT THE SAME TIME AFFIRMS THAT ITALY WILL CONTINUE TO RELY ON

THE U.S. FOR SOME KEY DEFENSE SYSTEMS. DEFENSE MINISTER

MARTINO IS COMMITTED TO RATIONALIZING ITALIAN DEFENSE

PROCUREMENT AND SPENDING SCARCE FINANCIAL RESOURCES ON

BOLSTERING ITALY\'S BATTLEFIELD CAPABILITIES. THE

COUNTERVAILING NEED TO PROTECT ITALY\'S INDUSTRIAL BASE AND

JOBS LIMITS HIS ROOM FOR MANEUVER, HOWEVER. END SUMMARY.

2. (U) THIS IS ONE IN A SERIES OF REPORTS ON ITALY\'S 2002

WHITE PAPER (WP) ON DEFENSE. THE WP SETS OUT THREE PILLARS

OF ITALIAN NATIONAL SECURITY: DEFENSE INDUSTRIAL POLICY,

FOREIGN POLICY, AND MILITARY POLICY. UNDER THE MINISTER OF

DEFENSE, THE NATIONAL ARMAMENTS DIRECTOR (NAD), CURRENTLY

ADMIRAL GIAMPAOLO DI PAOLA, IS RESPONSIBLE FOR DEFENSE

PROCUREMENT. THE PROCUREMENT BUDGET IS FUNDED THROUGH

MULTIPLE APPROPRIATIONS, INCLUDING THOSE FOR THE MINISTRY OF

PRODUCTIVE ACTIVITIES (MPA). IN ADDITION TO ACQUISITIONS,

WHICH ARE BASED ON PROGRAMS DEFINED BY THE CHOD, DI PAOLA

DIRECTS RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMENT AND HAS RESPONSIBILITY FOR

SOME 500 PERSONNEL IN THE NATIONAL ARMAMENTS DIRECTORATE. AS

THE ITALIAN NAD, DI PAOLA TAKES THE LEAD IN NEGOTIATING

BILATERAL AND MULTILATERAL ACCORDS ON DEFENSE INDUSTRIAL

COOPERATION.

3. (U) THE MAJOR STATE-CONTROLLED DEFENSE CONTRACTORS ARE

FINCANTIERI (SHIPBUILDING) AND FINMECCANICA (AEROSPACE,

HELICOPTERS, ELECTRONICS, ENERGY, AND TRANSPORT), WHICH

INCLUDES ALENIA DIFESA AND ALENIA AERONAUTICA, AND ALENIA

SPAZIO. THE LARGEST PRIVATE FIRMS ARE FIAT AVIO (VEHICLES,

PROPULSION SYSTEMS, AND ELECTRONICS); ELETTRONICA (ELECTRONIC

WARFARE, AVIONICS); MACCHI (TRAINING AIRCRAFT); BERETTA

(SMALL- AND MEDIUM-CALIBER WEAPONS); AND INTERMARINE (NAVAL

CONSTRUCTION). THE WP PUTS MARCONI (ELECTRONICS AND

COMMUNICATION) IN THIS CAMP, BUT FINMECCANICA IS NOW IN THE

PROCESS OF REABSORBING ITS FORMER SUBSIDIARY. TOGETHER,

THESE FIRMS ACCOUNT FOR 80 PERCENT OF ITALY\'S NATIONAL

DEFENSE REQUIREMENTS, PROVIDE APPROXIMATELY 30,000 JOBS, AND

EXPORT EQUIPMENT AND MATERIEL WORTH SOME 1.6 BILLION EUROS

ANNUALLY, ACCORDING TO THE WP. (COMMENT: A HANDFUL OF SMALL-

AND MEDIUM BUSINESSES HAVE DEVELOPED INTO SPECIALIZED

PROVIDERS OF ADVANCED TECHNOLOGY DEFENSE EQUIPMENT, SUCH AS

THE ROME-BASED SOFTWARE SYSTEMS ENGINEERING FIRM, DATAMAT.

THESE FIRMS HAVE TYPICALLY SERVED AS SUBCONTRACTORS TO

FINMECCANICA, BUT MORE THAN A DOZEN ARE COMPETING

INDEPENDENTLY FOR JSF CONTRACTS UNDER THE "BEST VALUE"

CONTRACT AWARD SYSTEM. END COMMENT.)

4. (U) THE GOVERNMENT SUPPORTS THE DEFENSE INDUSTRIAL

SECTOR THROUGH A JOINT PUBLIC GOVERNMENT-PRIVATE SECTOR

COMMITTEE CHAIRED BY THE DEFENSE MINISTER, ACCORDING TO THE

WP. WITHIN THE COMMITTEE, OFFICIALS FROM ACROSS THE GOI WORK

WITH REPRESENTATIVES FROM ACADEMIA AND THE HIGH-TECH SECTOR

(VIA THE ASSOCIATION FOR AEROSPACE DEFENSE INDUSTRIES) TO SET

OVERALL DEFENSE INDUSTRIAL POLICY, PROMOTE EXPORTS, AND

COORDINATE ASSISTANCE TO ITALIAN FIRMS COMPETING FOR

INTERNATIONAL ARMS CONTRACTS. THE MPA, WITH ITS SUBSTANTIAL

BUDGET FOR AIDING ITALIAN INDUSTRY, PLAYS A KEY ROLE ON THE

COMMITTEE. THE GOI HAS ALSO AIDED THE DEFENSE INDUSTRIAL

SECTOR\'S ORGANIZATIONAL AND FINANCIAL RESTRUCTURING WITH

INTERVENTION TO HELP FINANCE DEVELOPMENT, INCREASE

INTERNATIONAL COMPETITIVENESS AND MARKET ACCESS, AND BETTER

FOCUS COMPANIES ON END-USERS. BUILDING A STRONGER MILITARY

R&D BASE IS ANOTHER GOI PRIORITY, ACCORDING TO THE PAPER.

HOWEVER, IT ACKNOWLEDGES THAT EVEN THOUGH ANNUAL SPENDING HAS

INCREASED TO 130 MILLION EUROS, ITALY STILL APPROPRIATES MUCH

LESS THAN OTHER MAJOR EUROPEAN COUNTRIES.

5. (U) ACCORDING TO THE WP, ITALY HAS SIGNED 48 BILATERAL

DEFENSE INDUSTRIAL COOPERATION AGREEMENTS WITH COUNTRIES AS

DIVERSE AS FRANCE, GERMANY, RUSSIA, CHINA, AND SAUDI ARABIA.

COORDINATION OF DEFENSE INDUSTRIAL POLICY AT THE EUROPEAN

LEVEL -- THROUGH OCCAR, LETTERS OF INTENT, AND THE WEST

EUROPEAN ARMAMENTS GROUP -- BUT ACKNOWLEDGES THAT IT IS STILL

INCHOATE. THE WP ARGUES THAT JUST AS IT MAKES LITTLE SENSE TO

DO NATIONAL DEFENSE PLANNING WITHOUT FOCUSING ON

INTER-SERVICE OPERABILITY, THE INCREASING OVERLAP BETWEEN

ITALY\'S NEEDS AND THOSE OF ITS EUROPEAN PARTNERS MAKES

INTRA-EUROPEAN PLANNING ESSENTIAL. HOWEVER, THE GOI WANTS

THIS ACCOMPLISHED IN A WAY THAT COMPLEMENTS RATHER THAN

COMPETES WITH THE NATO PLANNING PROCESS.

6. (U) COMMERCIALLY, ITALIAN FIRMS ARE LOOKING FOR

OPPORTUNITIES TO PARTNER WITH THEIR EUROPEAN COUNTERPARTS

ALONG THE LINES OF THE APRIL 2001 AGREEMENT AMONG

FINMECCANICA, EADS, AND BAE SYSTEMS TO COOPERATE ON MISSILE

DEVELOPMENT, ACCORDING TO THE WP. (COMMENT: FRUSTRATED WITH

THEIR INABILITY TO STRIKE EQUITABLE ACCORDS WITH EUROPEAN

CONTRACTORS, ITALIAN DEFENSE CONTRACTORS ARE INCREASINGLY

LOOKING TO PARTNER WITH U.S. COMPANIES SUCH AS BOEING AND

LOCKHEED, WHICH ARE VALUED FOR THE ADVANCED R&D THEY BRING TO

BEAR ON PROJECTS, THE HIGH QUALITY OF PRODUCTION, AND THE

BENEFITS OF TECHNOLOGY TRANSFER. FOR EXAMPLE, BOEING AND

OTOMELARA/FINMECCANICA RECENTLY AGREED TO BEGIN CO-PRODUCTION

OF JOINT DIRECT ATTACK MUNITIONS IN MARCH 2003. END

COMMENT.) WHILE THE GOI WILL LOOK TO THESE ALLIANCES FOR

PROCUREMENT OPPORTUNITIES, THE WP ACKNOWLEDGES THAT ITALY

WILL CONTINUE TO RELY ON THE U.S. MARKET AS A KEY SOURCE FOR

DEFENSE EQUIPMENT AND TECHNOLOGY BECAUSE OF THE COMPETITIVE

ADVANTAGE HELD IN MANY SECTORS BY AMERICAN CONTRACTORS.

7. (C) COMMENT: MINDEF MARTINO IS COMMITTED IN PRINCIPLE

TO RATIONALIZING ITALIAN DEFENSE PROCUREMENT AND TO SPENDING

SCARCE FINANCIAL RESOURCES ON BOLSTERING ITALY\'S BATTLEFIELD

CAPABILITIES. RECENT ACTIONS (A400M WITHDRAWAL), AGREEMENTS

(JSF LEVEL II PARTNERSHIP), AND PURCHASES (BOEING 767

TANKERS) SUGGEST THAT ITALY IS INDEED MOVING IN THE RIGHT

DIRECTION. HOWEVER, ITALY IS OVERSTRETCHED IN TERMS OF

PROGRAMS THAT ARE ENTERING THE LATTER STAGES OF PRODUCTION,

SUCH AS THE EUROFIGHTER COMBAT AIRCRAFT, AND WILL HAVE TO

MAKE SOME HARD BUDGETARY CHOICES OVER THE NEXT 2-3 YEARS.

8. (C) COMMENT CONTINUED. PER REFTEL, MARTINO IS TRYING TO

SHIFT EVEN MORE OF THE PROCUREMENT BUDGET TO THE MPA AND

OTHER MINISTRIES. WHILE CURTAILING PARTICIPATION IN SOME

EUROPEAN COLLABORATIVE PROGRAMS MIGHT MAKE SENSE FROM A

COST-BENEFIT STANDPOINT, THIS RUNS COUNTER TO THE INTERESTS

OF ITALY\'S STATE-DOMINATED AEROSPACE AND DEFENSE INDUSTRY.

MOREOVER, WITH FIAT\'S AUTOMOBILE SECTOR IN DIRE STRAITS, THE

GOI WILL BE EVEN MORE AVERSE TO RISKING THE EROSION OF

ITALY\'S INDUSTRIAL BASE AND SACRIFICING JOBS FOR ECONOMIC

EFFICIENCY. IT ALSO MAY WELL BE A POLITICAL BRIDGE TOO FAR

FOR A GOVERNMENT THAT, DESPITE ITS PRO-U.S. BENT, STILL

CLAIMS SOME COMMITMENT TO BUILDING A COMMON EUROPEAN DEFENSE.

SEPTEL WILL FOCUS ON IDENTIFYING MORE PRECISELY THE TRENDS

WITHIN THE ITALIAN DEFENSE INDUSTRY, COMMERCIAL AND

GOVERNMENTAL ALLIANCE STRATEGY, AND THE NEXUS BETWEEN

DOMESTIC POLITICS, BUSINESS IMPERATIVES, AND DEFENSE

INDUSTRIAL POLICY. END COMMENT.
