SUBJECT: DUTCH RESPOND TO UK ALIEN SMUGGLING TRAGEDY

1. THE GON REACTED WITH BOTH WORDS AND ACTIONS TO THE JUNE

19 DISCOVERY IN THE UK OF 58 VICTIMS OF ALIEN SMUGGLING.

2. BECAUSE THE TRUCK IN WHICH THE VICTIMS WERE DISCOVERED IS

OF DUTCH REGISTRATION AND THE DRIVER IS DUTCH, THE GON

BECAME IMMEDIATELY INVOLVED. IT HAS SINCE ARRESTED BOTH THE

DUTCH FATHER OF THE DRIVER AND THE DUTCH OWNER OF THE

COMPANY THAT OWNS THE TRUCK. FURTHER INVESTIGATIONS BY THE

GON INTO THE DUTCH CONNECTION TO THE TRAGEDY ARE ONGOING.

3. PM WIM KOK DESCRIBED THE DEATHS AS DEEPLY TRAGIC AND

IMMEDIATELY CALLED FOR STRONGER PUNISHMENTS FOR ALIEN

SMUGGLERS. SINCE 1996, THE MAXIMUM PENALTY FOR ORGANIZED

ALIEN SMUGGLING HAS BEEN 8 YEARS IN PRISON. STATE SECRETARY

COHEN OF THE MINISTRY OF JUSTICE, HOWEVER, CALLED FOR CLOSER

POLICE COOPERATION IN EUROPE TO COMBAT SMUGGLING RATHER THAN

STRONGER PENALTIES.

4. DESPITE DUTCH EMPHASIS ON PUNISHING THOSE ARRESTED, THOSE

RESPONSIBLE MIGHT NOT BE PROSECUTABLE UNDER DUTCH LAW.

ALIEN SMUGGLING IS ONLY ILLEGAL IN THE NETHERLANDS IF THE

ACT OCCURRED IN THE NETHERLANDS OR THE PERSONS HAD BEEN

TRANSPORTED TO ANOTHER SIGNATORY COUNTRY OF THE SCHENGEN

TREATY. BECAUSE THE UK IS NOT A SIGNATORY COUNTRY AND THERE

IS NOT YET PROOF THAT ANY ACT TOOK PLACE WITHIN THE

BOUNDARIES OF THE NETHERLANDS, PROSECUTION MIGHT NOT BE

POSSIBLE HERE DESPITE THE INVOLVEMENT OF DUTCH NATIONALS, A

DUTCH VEHICLE, AND A DUTCH COMPANY. JUSTICE MINISTER

KORTHALS, HOWEVER, BELIEVES THAT IT IS POSSIBLE TO PROSECUTE

ALIEN SMUGGLERS UNDER DUTCH LAW FOR ACTS OF SMUGGLING

COMMITTED OUTSIDE THE NETHERLANDS IN CASES WHERE THE CRIME

IS ACTUALLY ORGANIZED AND PLANNED IN THE NETHERLANDS.

5. ALIEN SMUGGLING CONTINUES TO BE A SERIOUS PROBLEM IN THE

NETHERLANDS, AND COMBATING ALIEN SMUGGLING IS A HIGH

PRIORITY OF THE GON. IN 1993, ALIEN SMUGGLING FIRST BECAME

A CRIME. IN 1998, THE GON SET UP A SPECIALIZED TASK FORCE

TO COMBAT ALIEN SMUGGLING. AS A RESULT, THE NUMBER OF

PERSONS CONVICTED FOR ALIEN SMUGGLING HAS INCREASED SIX FOLD

FROM 16 TO 102 SINCE 1995. IN 1999 THERE WERE 221 ALIEN

SMUGGLING INVESTIGATIONS COMPARED TO 4 IN 1994.
