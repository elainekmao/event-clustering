SUBJECT: UNHCR LAWYER SKETCHES OUT POSSIBLE END GAME FOR

CUBAN DOCTORS

REF: HARARE 3162

CLASSIFIED BY DCM EARL M. IRVING FOR REASONS 1.5 B/D.

1. (C) A DOWNCAST MICHIEL SALOMONS (PROTECT) BRIEFED DCM

ON JUNE 13 ON WHAT HE VIEWS AS A WORSENING OUTLOOK FOR

THE RESOLUTION OF THE CASES OF CUBAN DOCTORS LEONEL

CORDOVA RODRIGUEZ AND NORIS DEL CARMEN PENA MARTINEZ.

SALOMONS, WHO CONTINUES TO GET THE RUNAROUND FROM

ZIMBABWEAN AUTHORITIES, ALSO SPECULATED THAT THE UNITED

NATIONS HIGH COMMISSIONER FOR REFUGEES (UNHCR) IN GENEVA

MIGHT DROP THE CASE. HE EXPLAINED THAT UNHCR HEAD OGATA

HAD VISITED HAVANA RECENTLY IN AN EFFORT TO SECURE

CUBA\'S ACCESSION TO THE 1951 CONVENTION CONCERNING

REFUGEES. MS. OGATA FOUND FIDEL CASTRO IMPRESSIVE,

ACCORDING TO SALOMONS. CASTRO IN TURN PROMISED MS.

OGATA "HUNDREDS" OF CUBAN DOCTORS TO ASSIST UNHCR, A

DANGLED ATTRACTION WHICH MAY NOW BE INFLUENCING MS.

CONFIDENTIAL

OGATA\'S PROFESSIONAL AND IMPARTIAL VIEW OF THE FATE OF

THE DOCTORS BEING HELD IN LOCAL DETENTION.

2. (C) SALOMONS ALSO TOLD US THAT HE HAD BEEN INFORMED

THAT THE QUESTION OF THE RELEASE OF THE CUBAN DOCTORS

COULD BE BROUGHT BEFORE THE CABINET IN ITS REGULAR

WEEKLY MEETING ON JUNE 13. IF THE CABINET DOES NOT ACT

ON IT TODAY, SALOMONS THOUGHT IT WOULD ONLY RETURN TO

THE ISSUE OF THE CUBANS\' PLIGHT AFTER THE ELECTIONS. HE

SPOKE OMINOUSLY OF A CUBAN TRANSPORT PLANE BEING MOVED

FROM GABON TO ZIMBABWE IMMINENTLY TO FORCIBLY REPATRIATE

THE PAIR TO CUBA.

3. (C) SALOMONS THOUGHT THAT IF THE U.S. REALLY WISHED

TO GRANT ASYLUM TO THE CUBANS, THEN IT SHOULD ENGAGE

DIRECTLY WITH PRESIDENT MUGABE, BECAUSE HE BELIEVED

CASTRO WAS ALREADY DOING SO. LEFT TO UNHCR, THE CASE

MIGHT GO TWO WAYS. ZIMBABWE WOULD GRANT TO THE UNHCR

"MANDATE STATUS", MEANING THE UN COULD RETURN THE PAIR

TO CUBA, RELYING ON THE CUBAN GOVERNMENT\'S PUBLIC

PROMISE THAT NO HARM WOULD BEFALL THEM AS THEY HAD

COMMITTED NO CRIME, OR, THE PAIR COULD BE SETTLED IN A

THIRD COUNTRY. EVEN THOUGH SALOMONS HAD ARGUED THAT THE

TWO CUBANS WERE BONA FIDE REFUGEES, HE THOUGHT THE USG

WOULD HAVE TO PRESS UNHCR HARD TO ESTABLISH THAT THEY

HAD A WELL-FOUNDED FEAR OF PERSECUTION IF RETURNED TO

THEIR HOMELAND. SALOMONS STATED THAT THE CUBANS HAD BEEN

MODEL CITIZENS IN CUBA AND THAT THEIR CLAIM FOR REFUGEE

STATUS ONLY AROSE AFTER THEY MADE ANTI-CASTRO REMARKS TO

THE NEWSPAPERS IN ZIMBABWE. FINALLY, SALOMONS PROPOSED A

"LOCAL SETTLEMENT", IN, WHICH THE CUBANS MIGHT BE

ALLOWED TO REMAIN IN ZIMBABWE NOT FOR TWO WEEKS, BUT FOR

PERHAPS A YEAR, AS HAD BEEN THE CASE WITH CUBAN DOCTORS

WHO DEFECTED IN VENEZUELA.

4. (C) IN WHAT WE VIEW AS A POSSIBLE FURTHER MUDDYING

OF THE WATERS, THE TEACHER (MADZIYA) WITH WHOM THE

CUBANS HAD BEEN STAYING TELEPHONED THE EMBASSY TODAY.

HE TOLD US THAT A POLICE SERGEANT CHIDHARARA AT

GOROMONZI DETECTION CENTER HAD BEEN IN TOUCH TO REQUEST

THAT MADZIYA TURN OVER THE CUBANS\' PASSPORTS, MONEY AND

ANY OTHER BELONGINGS IN HIS POSSESSION. HE DID NOT

STATE IF THE REASON BEHIND HIS REQUEST WAS AN IMPENDING

DEPARTURE FROM ZIMBABWE, OR A LIKELY EXTENDED STAY AS A

GUEST OF HIS FACILITY.

5. (C) COMMENT: SALOMONS CAME LATE TO THE REALIZATION

OF THE SERIOUSNESS OF THE CASE, AS EVIDENCED BY HIS

OFFHAND COMMENT TO INS OFFICER VARSANI THAT HE WAS

SURPRISED BY THE AMOUNT OF PUBLICITY THAT THE CUBANS\'

CASE HAS GENERATED. SALOMONS APPEARS UNABLE TO

ANTICIPATE OR DECIPHER THE POLITICAL ENDGAMES BEING

PLAYED BY THE GOVERNMENT, AND UNFORTUNATELY HIS

MALADROITNESS TRANSLATES INTO ANOTHER WEAK LINK IN THE

CHAIN. NEVERTHELESS, WE CANNOT RULE OUT THE POSSIBLE

SCENARIOS HE HAS OUTLINED, OR THE VALIDITY OF HIS
