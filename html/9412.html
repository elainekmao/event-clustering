SUBJECT: UAEG FORMALLY REQUESTS TIFA NEGOTIATIONS

1. The UAEG has formally notified us that it wants to

negotiate a Free Trade Agreement (TIFA) with the U.S. and

that, as a first step, it is prepared to negotiate a Trade

and Investment Framework Agreement with the U.S.

2. UAE Minister of State for Foreign Affairs Sheikh Hamdan

bin Zayed told the Ambassador that the UAEG is interested

in negotiating a TIFA with the U.S. He also said that he

has seen a copy of the U.S.-Bahrain TIFA and is circulating

it throughout UAE ministries. Ministry of Finance and

Industry Assistant Undersecretary Khalid Al-Bustani passed

Econchief a letter (dated June 16) from the Minister of

Finance and Industry Sheikh Hamdan Bin Rashid Al-Maktoum to

Treasury Secretary Snow expressing substantially the same

points. Al-Bustani told Econchief that the Ministry of

Finance had passed the letter to the MFA for transmission.

3. Action Request: Please advise as to next steps we should

take with the UAEG. The UAE is very interested in

negotiating both a TIFA and a free trade agreement. End

Action Request.
