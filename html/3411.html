SUBJECT: OLD AND NEW STYLE CHEMICAL MANUFACTURERS IN SICILY

REF: ROME 3390

1. SUMMARY. ON 24 AUGUST A/ECMIN VISITED THE PRIOLO

INDUSTRIAL AREA IN SOUTHERN SICILY TO MEET WITH DOW ITALIA

AND POLIMERI EUROPA, TOUR THEIR FACILITIES, AND ACQUIRE

GENERAL KNOWLEDGE OF THE COMPANIES TO PAVE THE WAY FOR

FUTURE DISCUSSIONS ON EU CHEMICALS POLICY. DOW IS THE

LARGEST CHEMICAL COMPANY OPERATING IN ITALY, AND POLIMERI

EUROPA IS THE LARGEST PRODUCER OF POLYETHYLENES IN EUROPE.

THE VERY DIFFERENT OPERATIONAL STRUCTURES AND STYLE OF THE

COMPANIES SUGGESTS THAT WE MAY FIND DIFFERENT KNOWLEDGE

ABOUT THE EU PROPOSALS; PE PLANTS FUNCTION AS PRODUCTION

UNITS WITH LITTLE KNOWLEDGE OF POLICY OR MANAGEMENT ISSUES,

WHEREAS DOW HAS INTEGRATED ITS PRODUCTION/RESEARCH

FACILITIES AND PERSONNEL INTO OVERALL CORPORATE MANAGEMENT.

END SUMMARY.

2. THE INDUSTRIAL SECTION OF PRIOLO WAS BUILT AS A SINGLE,

MULTIFUNCTIONAL COMPLEX IN THE 1950S BY THE GOI, THEN

PRIVATIZED IN THE EARLY 1960S. THE PRIOLO SITE, WHICH

OCCUPIES 900 HECTARES NORTH OF SYRACUSE, CONTAINS PLANTS

OWNED BY AGIP, ENICHEM, DOW, POLIMERI EUROPA, ERG, AND ESSO.

SOME OF THESE COMPANIES FUNCTION INTERDEPENDENTLY AT THE

PRIOLO SITE, TRANSPORTING PRODUCTS AND/OR INTERMEDIATES TO

OTHER COMPANIES IN THE VICINITY. THE PRIOLO PLANTS SHARE AN

EXTENSIVE SECURITY SYSTEM AND THE SAME SHIPYARD FOR

TRANSPORTATION OF MATERIALS. IN ADDITION, THEY ALL BELONG TO

CIPA, THE INDUSTRIAL CONSORTIUM FOR ENVIRONMENTAL

PROTECTION, AND SHARE A COMMON BIOLOGICAL PLANT FOR

TREATMENT OF WASTEWATER (IAS), WHICH IS THE LARGEST OF ITS

KIND IN EUROPE AND THE FIFTH LARGEST IN THE WORLD. IAS\'S

CURRENT GOAL IS TO RECYCLE THREE MILLION CUBIC METERS OF

WASTEWATER FROM INDUSTRY; ITS FUTURE GOAL WILL BE TEN

MILLION CUBIC METERS.

3. A/ECMIN FIRST MET WITH POLIMERI EUROPA (PE) PERSONNEL

FROM PRIOLO. PE, BASED IN MILAN, PRODUCES TWENTY DIFFERENT

LOW-DENSITY LINEAR POLYETHYLENE (LDLPE) PRODUCTS, AS WELL AS

PROPYLENE, CUMENE, AND AROMATICS SUCH AS BENZENE, ORTHO- AND

PARA- XYLENE, AND TOLUENE. PE HAS TWO OTHER SICILIAN PLANTS

AT GELA AND RAGUSA; BOTH PRODUCE LDLPES, AND GELA ALSO

PRODUCES SMALL AMOUNTS OF ETHYLENE AND PROPYLENE. THE PRIOLO

FACILITY IS THE LARGEST OF THE THREE IN TERMS OF BOTH SIZE

AND OUTPUT, OCCUPYING THREE MILLION SQUARE METERS OF LAND

AND PRODUCING 745 KILOTONS OF ETHYLENE, 440 KTN OF

PROPYLENE, 440 KTN OF BENZENE, AND 200 KTN OF LDLPE PER

YEAR. IT HAS 565 EMPLOYEES AND A STORAGE CAPACITY OF 98,000

CUBIC METERS. THE AROMATICS PLANT WAS INSTALLED FROM 1964-

1971, AND THE CRACKING PLANT IN 1981. THE EQUIPMENT IN THE

ETHYLENE PLANT, WHERE PE IS GRADUALLY EMPLOYING MORE

ADVANCED COMPUTERIZED CONTROLS AND MONITORING SYSTEMS, WAS

INSTALLED IN 1987. THERE IS NO PE RESEARCH UNIT AT THE

PRIOLO SITE; RESEARCH INTO AROMATICS AND CRACKING TAKES

PLACE AT A MILAN FUNCTIONARY.

4. PE RECEIVES MONOMERIC ETHYLENE AND PROPYLENE FROM ENICHEM

PRIOLO, WHICH CAN THEN BE SUBJECTED TO VINYL POLIMERIZATION/

ZIEGLER-NATTA CATALYSIS, CREATING A STRONG LINEAR

POLYETHYLENE. PE ALSO RECEIVES AROMATIC C-6 FRACTIONS FROM

ERG, HYDROGEN FUEL GAS FROM ESSO PRIOLO, AND TRANSPORTS

MONOMERIC ETHYLENE AND AMMONIA VIA PIPELINE TO THE PLANTS AT

GELA AND RAGUSA.

5. A/ECMIN JOINED NAPLES CONGEN CLYDE BISHOP AT THE DOW

ITALIA POLYURETHANES SITE TO MEET WITH DOW PERSONNEL FROM

PRIOLO AND MANAGEMENT FROM DOW\'S MILAN HEADQUARTERS. THIS

PARTICULAR PLANT WAS ACQUIRED FROM ENICHEM\'S POLYURETHANES

DIVISION IN 2001, WHICH HAD ACQUIRED IT FROM UNION CARBIDE

IN 1990. AT THIS TIME, DOW ALSO ACQUIRED OTHER ENICHEM

POLYURETHANES PLANTS, ALL EQUIPPED WITH RESEARCH FACILITIES,

IN BRINDISI, PORTO MARGHERA (NEAR VENICE), AND TERTRO,

BELGIUM. WITH THESE ACQUISITIONS, DOW IS NOW THE LARGEST

CHEMICAL COMPANY OPERATING IN ITALY. THE PRIOLO SITE\'S

PRINCIPAL MARKETS LIE IN NORTH AFRICA, TURKEY, AND ITALY.

THE PLANT, WHICH HAS 97 EMPLOYEES, PRODUCES ETHYL OXIDES

ARE USED FOR COOLING FLUIDS, PHARMACEUTICALS, OIL FIELDS,

COATINGS, AGRICULTURE, SHOE SOLES, AND AS RAW MATERIALS FOR

POLYURETHANE SYNTHESIS. WHEN MEETING WITH MANAGEMENT FROM

DOW MILAN, A/ECMIN SUGGESTED DISCUSSION OF SUSTAINABLE

DEVELOPMENT IN ADDITION TO PREVIOUS PLANS FOR A SEPTEMBER

MEETING ON EU CHEMICALS POLICY. DOW MILAN SEEMED EAGER AND

PREPARED TO DISCUSS AT A LATER DATE.
