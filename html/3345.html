SUBJECT: FRATTINI TO BE NAMED ITALY\'S NEW FOREIGN MINISTER

CLASSIFIED BY: ACTING DCM THOMAS COUNTRYMAN FOR REASONS 1.5 (B) AND (D)

1. (C) ON JULY 19, PAOLO DIONISI, DEPUTY DIPLOMATIC ADVISOR

TO PM AND ACTING FM BERLUSCONI, INFORMED CDA THAT BERLUSCONI

PLANS TO ANNOUNCE ITALY\'S NEW FM AT THE END OF THE JULY 24-26

CONFERENCE OF ITALIAN AMBASSADORS TO BE HELD IN ROME. THE

NEW FM, ACCORDING TO DIONISI, WILL BE FRANCO FRATTINI,

CURRENTLY MINISTER WITHOUT PORTFOLIO FOR PUBLIC

ADMINISTRATION AND THE INTELLIGENCE.

2. (C) THE NEW ITALIAN MFA WILL FOLLOW THE CANADIAN MODEL,

EXPLAINED DIONISI. IT WILL BE CALLED THE MINISTRY OF FOREIGN

AFFAIRS AND TRADE. HOWEVER, IT WILL HAVE ONLY ONE MINISTER,

FRATTINI, WHO NOT ONLY KNOWS PUBLIC ADMINISTRATION WELL BUT

IS ALSO VERY CLOSE TO BERLUSCONI AND HIS DIPLOMATIC ADVISORS.

ADOLFO URSO, CURRENTLY DEPUTY MINISTER FOR PRODUCTIVE

ACTIVITIES, WILL BECOME THE DEPUTY MINISTER AT MFA WITH

ESSENTIALLY THE SAME RESPONSIBILITY FOR PROMOTING FOREIGN

TRADE THAT HE CURRENTLY HAS. (NOTE: IN HIS MOVE FROM THE

MINISTRY OF PRODUCTIVE ACTIVITIES TO THE MFA, URSO WILL BRING

WITH HIM HIS ENTIRE ORGANIZATION, PREVIOUSLY THE MINISTRY OF

FOREIGN TRADE. END NOTE.) FRATTINI\'S CHIEF OF STAFF WILL BE

CESARE RAGAGLINI, CURRENTLY BERLUSCONI\'S DEPUTY DIPLOMATIC

ADVISOR. WITH FRATTINI AS FM, RELATIONS BETWEEN THE MFA AND

PALAZZO CHIGI (THE PM\'S OFFICE) WILL BE EXCELLENT, STRESSED

DIONISI. HE ADDED THAT PALAZZO CHIGI DIPLOMATIC STAFF WILL

EXPAND TO MORE CLOSELY RESEMBLE THE FUNCTIONS OF THE US

NATIONAL SECURITY COUNCIL, WITH GIOVANNI CASTELLANETA,

BERLUSCONI\'S DIPLOMATIC ADVISOR, REMAINING TO HEAD THE

EXPANDED ORGANIZATION.

3. (C) COMMENT: EVEN AFTER ANNOUNCING FRATTINI\'S APPOINTMENT,

WE EXPECT BERLUSCONI, WHO HAS BEEN ACTING FM FOR EIGHT

MONTHS, TO CONTINUE TO PLAY THE LEAD ROLE IN SHAPING ITALY\'S

FOREIGN POLICY. BERLUSCONI, IN CHOOSING A TRUSTED ADVISER

FROM HIS OWN FORZA ITALIA PARTY AS FM, SEEKS TO ENSURE THE

END OF SQUABBLES BETWEEN PALAZZO CHIGI AND THE MFA THAT ITALY

EXPERIENCED UNDER FM RUGGIERO, AND LESS FREQUENTLY SINCE

RUGGIERO\'S RESIGNATION IN JANUARY. NO DOUBT FRATTINI WILL BE

AN ACTIVE PARTICIPANT IN SHAPING ITALY\'S FOREIGN POLICY, BUT

HE WILL ALSO BE EXPECTED TO ENSURE THAT THE MFA EXECUTES

FOREIGN AND TRADE POLICY DESIGNED BY BERLUSCONI AND PALAZZO

CHIGI. END COMMENT.

4. (U) POST WILL FOLLOW UP WITH BIOGRAPHIC DETAIL ON

FRATTINI.
