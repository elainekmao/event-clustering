SUBJECT: ZIMBABWE MAKES NO DECISION ON REFUGEE STATUS,

BUT TRANSFERS CUBAN DOCTORS TO CUSTODY OF UNHCR

REF: HARARE 3084

CLASSIFIED BY AMBASSADOR TOM MCDONALD FOR REASONS 1.5

B/D.

1. (C) UNITED NATIONS RESIDENT COORDINATOR CARLOS LOPES

INFORMED AMBASSADOR MCDONALD LATE ON JUNE 8 THAT THE

ZIMBABWEAN GOVERNMENT REFUGEE COMMITTEE HAD DELIBERATED

ON THE CASE OF THE CUBAN DOCTORS LEONEL CORDOVA

RODRIGUEZ AND NORIS PENA MARTINEZ FOR THREE HOURS AND

TOOK NO FORMAL DECISION. INSTEAD, THE ZIMBABWEAN PANEL

DECIDED TO GRANT "TEMPORARY REFUGEE STATUS" TO THE PAIR,

RELEASED THEM FROM PRISON AND HANDED THEM OVER TO THE

CUSTODY OF THE UNHCR. THE CLEAR UNDERSTANDING, LOPES

STATED, WAS THAT THE CUBANS WOULD BE RESETTLED PROMPTLY

IN A THIRD COUNTRY. ACCORDING TO LOPES, LUSAKA-BASED

UNCHR ATTORNEY MICHAEL SALOMONS WAS NOW SCHEDULED TO

ARRIVE IN HARARE ON JUNE 10.

2. (C) UNHCR REPRESENTATIVE JOHN ADU TOLD CONSUL EARLIER

THAT THE TWO DOCTORS WERE IN HIS CUSTODY, BUT THAT HE

DID NOT BELIEVE THAT THEY WOULD BE SAFE IN THE

ZIMBABWEAN IMMIGRATION SERVICE\'S TRANSIT FACILITY.

THEREFORE, ADU SAID THAT TWO DOCTORS WOULD BE HOUSED IN

THE GOZ\'S REMAND FACILITIES. ADU TOLD CONSUL THAT

DOCTORS CORDOVA AND PENA WERE IN HIS OFFICE AND THAT HE

INTENDED TO ACCOMPANY THEM TO THE REMAND CENTERS TO

ASCERTAIN THE EXACT LOCATION OF EACH. DOCTOR CORDOVA IS

TO BE HOUSED AT A FACILITY IN DOWNTOWN HARARE, WHILE

DOCTOR PENA WILL STAY AT CHIKURUBI REMAND CENTER, ON THE

GROUNDS OF ZIMBABWE\'S ONE MAXIMUM-SECURITY PRISON, JUST

OUTSIDE OF HARARE.

3. (C) COMMENT: WE CANNOT RULE OUT SOMETHING GOING

WRONG IN THE COMING DAYS, WHETHER IT IS THE CUBANS

ATTEMPTING TO SPIRIT THE TWO OUT OF ZIMBABWE OR

PRESIDENT MUGABE RECONSIDERING AND DECIDING THAT THIS IS

NOT THE OUTCOME HE WANTED FOR THE CASE. THE SITUATION

ARGUES FOR QUICK PROCESSING OF THE DOCTORS AND THEIR

TRANSFER TO U.S. SOIL AS SOON AS POSSIBLE. END COMMENT.

MCDONALD
