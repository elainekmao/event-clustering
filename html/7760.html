SUBJECT: DEMARCHE TO JORDAN ON ROADMAP PRESENTATION

REF: STATE 111481

Classified By: A/DCM Doug Silliman for reasons 1.5 (B)(D)

1. (C) A/DCM May 1 presented a copy of the Roadmap to and

reviewed reftel points with Ali al-Ayed, Personal Assistant

to Foreign Minister Marwan Muasher. Ayed took careful notes

of the talking points, focusing especially on the actions

that the USG is asking Israel and the Palestinian Authority

to take. He said he would pass the Roadmap and points to

FonMin Muasher, currently in London. Ayed said that Jordan

is very pleased that the Roadmap has been presented to the

parties and released publicly, and reiterated Jordan\'s desire

to work with the U.S., the Quartet, the Palestinians, other

Arab partners, and Israel to move the process forward.
