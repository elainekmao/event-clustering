SUBJECT: KING ABDULLAH\'S JULY VISIT TO THE US

REF: 06/10/02 ZIADEH-SILLIMAN PHONE CALL

Classified By: AMBASSADOR GNEHM FOR REASONS 1.5 (B) AND (D)

1. (C) Post understands that the Jordanian Embassy in

Washington has been working to arrange meetings for King

Abdullah with the President and other senior officials at the

end of July. The Palace and Minister of Planning Bassem

Awadallah have queried the Ambassador on the status of the

King\'s request to see the President. The King will attend a

conference in Aspen 29-31 and has a commitment to be in Aspen

for at least one of those three days, but could potentially

travel to Washington for meetings on any of those days or

immediately following the conference.

2. (C) Embassy would appreciate readout on status of the

King\'s request for meetings at the White House.

Gnehm
