SUBJECT: CARDINAL OF GENOA:

"I WILL URGE RESTRAINT DURING G-8"

1. (C) CHARGE AND POLOFF MET WITH CARDINAL DIONIGI

TETTAMANZI OF GENOA IN ROME ON FRIDAY, JULY 6. TETTAMANZI

WAS IN ROME FOR MEETINGS WITH THE POPE, ITALIAN PRIME

MINISTER BERLUSCONI, AND ITALIAN INTERIOR MINISTER SCAEOLA

ON THE EVE OF THE G-8 SUMMIT. HE SAID THAT ALL ARE ASKING

HIM TO URGE RESTRAINT BY HIS MANY ANTI-GLOBALIZATION

FOLLOWERS.

2. (C) TETTAMANZI SAID THAT IF FACT, HE IS NOT ANTI-

GLOBALIZATION, RATHER HE ACCEPTS IT AS A FACT, BUT WISHES

THE WORLD LEADERS TO REALIZE THE NEED TO BETTER ADDRESS THE

CONCERNS OF THE POOR COUNTRIES. HE POINTED TO THE FACT

THAT IN HIS RECENTLY RELEASED BOOK "GLOBALIZATION: A

CHALLENGE", HE NOTES THE POSITIVE EFFECTS OF GLOBALIZATION

ON THIRD WORLD DEVELOPMENT. THAT SAID, HE WOULD BE

SUPPORTING PEACEFUL DEMONSTRATIONS TO HIGHLIGHT THE PLIGHT

OF THE WORLD\'S POOR. HE ASSURED US, HOWEVER, THAT THE

DEMONSTRATIONS, WHICH HE WILL BE INVOLVED IN, WOULD TAKE

PLACE BEFORE THE OPENING OF THE SUMMIT, AND AFTER ITS

CLOSE.

3. (C) COMMENT: EMBASSY BELIEVES TETTAMANZI WILL DO HIS

BEST TO AVOID PARTICIPATION OF HIS FOLLOWERS IN THE

DEMONSTRATIONS DURING THE SUMMIT. HE DID NOTE, HOWEVER,

THERE ARE ONE OR TWO CATHOLIC PRIESTS WHO HE DESCRIBES AS

"RADICAL" WHO WILL ACTIVELY TRY TO DISRUPT THE MEETINGS.

POST WILL ATTEMPT TO OBTAIN MORE INFORMATION ON THESE

ACTIVITIES. MERANTE
