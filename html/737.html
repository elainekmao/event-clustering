MARTIC STILL BOASTFUL, AGGRESSIVE

---------------------------------

-

2. WHILE WE HAVE HAD NO DIRECT CONTACT WITH "RSK

PRESIDENT" MILAN MARTIC SINCE THE EVENTS OF MAY 1-3,

UNSRSG AKASHI, WHO MET HIM ON MAY 16, REPORTED TO THE

AMBASSADOR THAT MARTIC\'S MOOD REMAINS "BAD," AND THAT

HE IS "OBSESSED" WITH SITUATION IN WESTERN SLAVONIA AND

THE QUESTION OF "NORMALIZATION" THERE.

3. BY MOST OTHER ACCOUNTS, TOO, THE BELLIGERENT MOOD

MARTIC EXHIBITED IMMEDIATELY AFTER MAY 1 IS ESSENTIALLY

UNCHANGED. THREE WESTERN JOURNALISTS WHO WERE

IN/AROUND KNIN MAY 13-14 ALL TOLD PAO THEY FOUND MARTIC

TO BE SEEMINGLY "CONFIDENT" AND MAINTAINING HIS

BLUSTER, EVIDENTLY UNCHASTENED BY THE CROATIAN SUCCESS

IN RECAPTURING WESTERN SLAVONIA. ONE OF THEM ALSO

THOUGHT THAT MARTIC\'S POPULAR SUPPORT, EVEN AMONG THE

EDUCATED ELITE, HAD GROWN AS A RESULT OF THE CROATIAN

ACTION MAY 1. EVEN MODERATES IN THE "RSK," THE

JOURNALIST SAID, HAVE COME TO BELIEVE THAT THE CROATS

CANNOT BE TRUSTED, AND THEY "ARE ANGRY THAT MIKELIC

BLAMED THE WHOLE MESS ON MARTIC."

4. COUNTERBALANCING THIS LATTER ASSERTION, HOWEVER,

ARE REPORTS THAT MARTIC\'S FAILURE TO "SAVE" SECTOR WEST

HAS WEAKENED HIS SUPPORT, ESPECIALLY AMONG THE RANK-

AND-FILE "RSK" MILITARY. ONE TV BROADCOAST REPORTEDLY

SHOWED MARTIC BLUSTERING BEFORE "RSK" MILITARY UNITS

AND BEING ANSWERED WITH SARCASTIC JEERS.

5. THERE ARE ALSO OTHER INDICATIONS THAT MARTIC\'S

POSITION IS LESS FIRM THAN IT WAS. UN DEPUTY CIVIL

AFFAIRS OFFICER FOR SECTOR SOUTH, SAIJIN ZHANG,

INDICATED HE THOUGHT RENEWED MILITARY ACTION BY THE

KRAJINA SERBS WAS UNLIKELY LARGELY BECAUSE THE "RSK"

LEADERSHIP WOULD BE "VERY BUSY INTERNALLY" FOR AT LEAST

A WHILE. BEFORE HIS DEPARTURE ON LEAVE MAY 14, ZHANG\'S

BOSS, JURIJ MIAKOTNYKH, WHOM WE CONSIDER GENERALLY A

MORE INFORMED SOURCE, TOLD A UN COLLEAGUE THAT HE DID

NOT FORESEE MARTIC\'S TAKING ANY NEW STEPS WITHIN "THE

NEXT TWO WEEKS" BUT THAT SOME "MONSTROUS" ALTERNATIVES

WERE UNDER CONSIDERATION. (COMMENT: AT THIS STAGE WE

BELIEVE THIS TELLS US MORE ABOUT MARTIC\'S STATE OF MIND

THAN ABOUT ANY SPECIFIC ACTIONS HE MAY TAKE, ESPECIALLY

TAKING INTO CONSIDERATION OUR COMMENTS BELOW.)

CELEKETIC RESIGNATION

---------------------

6. THE MAY 14 RESIGNATION OF THE "RSK" FORCE COMMANDER

CELEKETIC, A HARDLINER WHO WAS A CLOSE POLITICAL ALLY

OF MARTIC, ALSO SUGGESTS THAT MARTIC IS WEAKENED,

POSSIBLY VIS-A-VIS HIS DOMESTIC POLITICAL RIVALS AS A

RESULT OF HIS FAILURE TO DEFEND "RSK" TERRITORY IN

SECTOR WEST, AND ALMOST CERTAINLY VIS-A-VIS MILOSEVIC.

CELEKETIC\'S SUCCESSOR, GENERAL MILE MRKSIC (PRON: MURK-

SHEETCH), IS A MILOSEVIC CHOICE WHO COMES TO HIS NEW

POSITION FROM THE JNA STAFF IN BELGRADE. THE CHANGE

GIVES MILOSEVIC A VETO OVER MARTIC\'S MILITARY

ADVENTURISM. FURTHER, MRKSIC MAY OR MAY NOT PROVE TO

BE AS CLOSE A WORKING PARTNER FOR MARTIC AS CELEKETIC,

SO HIS APPOINTMENT TO SOME DEGREE REDUCES MARTIC\'S

ABILITY TO CARRY THE DAY IN THE "RSK\'S" SENIOR

POLICYMAKING COUNCILS. EVEN IF SACRIFICING CELEKETIC

WAS, AS ZHANG BELIEVES, "THE PRICE MARTIC HAD TO PAY TO

STAY," THIS TOO INDICATES THAT MARTIC WAS UNDER

PRESSURE.

7. MARTIC ALSO FACES INTERNATIONAL PRESSURE AS WELL.

VISITING OFFICIALS OF THE HAGUE WAR CRIMES TRIBUNAL

DISCUSSED WITH AMB. GALBRAITH ON MAY 16 THEIR VIEW THAT

MARTIC HAD LEFT HIMSELF WIDE OPEN TO WAR CRIMES CHARGES

WITH HIS STATEMENT IMMEDIATELY AFTER THE ROCKETING OF

ZAGREB ("WE BOMBARDED THEIR CITIES FOR REVENGE"). IN

THEIR VIEW, THIS STATEMENT OFFERED AN ALMOST "OPEN AND

SHUT CASE." PAO\'S JOURNALIST CONTACTS SAID MARTIC DID

NOT APPEAR SINCERE WHEN HE LATER TOLD THEM THAT "RSK"

ROCKETS WERE NOT INTENDED TO KILL CIVILIANS BUT WERE

AIMED AT THE MINISTRY OF DEFENSE (WHICH THEY CAME

NOWHERE NEAR). WE INTERPRET THAT MAY 15 STATEMENT AS A

CYNICAL ATTEMPT ON MARTIC\'S PART TO BACKTRACK FROM HIS

EARLIER BOAST, AND AN INDICATION THAT ME MAY HAVE

RECOGNIZED HE HAD GONE TOO FAR IN LEAVING HIMSELF OPEN

TO WAR CRIMES CHARGES.
