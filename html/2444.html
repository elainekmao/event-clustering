1. (U) SUMMARY: POPE JOHN PAUL II LED AN INTERFAITH APPEAL

FOR PEACE JANUARY 24 IN ASSISI, ITALY AT WHICH HE ISSUED A

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 VATICA 00521 01 OF 03 040621Z

RINGING CONDEMNATION OF VIOLENCE PERPETRATED IN THE NAME OF

RELIGION. MORE THAN 300 RELIGIOUS LEADERS ATTENDED FROM

AROUND THE WORLD TO SHOW SUPPORT FOR THE PAPAL CALL FOR

DIALOG AMONG RELIGIONS AND CULTURES. THE MEETING, WHICH THE

POPE CALLED IN DIRECT RESPONSE TO THE WORLD SITUATION

POST-SEPTEMBER, INCLUDED REPRESENTATIVES OF WESTERN AND

NON-WESTERN RELIGIONS. VATICAN-WATCHERS HIGHLIGHTED THE

STRONG ORTHODOX PRESENCE. KEY MUSLIM PARTICIPANTS NUMBERED

OVER THIRTY, INCLUDING A THEOLOGIAN FROM MECCA WHO SPOKE AT

LENGTH WITH POLOFF. THE ASSISI MEETING REPRESENTED THE

CULMINATION OF RECENT VATICAN EFFORTS TO COMBAT PERCEPTIONS

OF A CLASH OF CIVILIZATIONS. END SUMMARY.

2. (U) THE JANUARY 24 ASSISI "DAY OF PRAYER FOR PEACE IN THE

WORLD" WAS THE OCCASION FOR UNUSUAL ENCOUNTERS AND UNLIKELY

DIALOGUE. SHARING CENTER STAGE WERE ZOROASTRIAN AND CATHOLIC,

ANIMIST AND MUSLIM, BUDDHIST AND BAPTIST, JAINIST AND QUAKER,

HINDU AND ORTHODOX, AND MANY OTHERS FROM SCORES OF

DISTINCTIVE FAITH TRADITIONS. AN ATTEMPT TO BRIDGE RELIGIOUS

DIFFERENCES IN AN AFFIRMATION OF COMMON VALUES, THE ASSISI

MEETING REPRESENTED AN ECUMENICAL AND INTERFAITH CAPSTONE FOR

THE JOHN PAUL II PAPACY. THE POPE\'S OWN CONDEMNATION OF WAR,

TERRORISM, AND, MOST ESPECIALLY, VIOLENCE IN THE NAME OF

RELIGION, FURTHER REPRESENTED A VERY PERSONAL RESPONSE TO THE

GLOBAL CRISIS PRECIPITATED BY SEPTEMBER 11 (REF.

3. (U) THE POPE CONVOKED THE ASSISI GATHERING IN LATE

NOVEMBER 2001 IN AN URGENT APPEAL FOR PEACE IN A WORLD

WOUNDED BY WAR AND TERRORISM. THE VATICAN HAD LITTLE

LEAD-TIME TO MOUNT A COMPLEX OPERATION IN LOGISTICS AND

PROTOCOL. THE VATICAN HOWEVER IS A VETERAN HAND IN

ORCHESTRATING LARGE EVENTS, ESPECIALLY AFTER HANDLING THE

MILLIONS OF PILGRIMS DURING THE JUBILEE YEAR. MOREOVER,

THERE WERE HELPFUL PRECEDENTS IN ASSISI ITSELF, WHICH HAD

HOSTED LARGE INTERFAITH GATHERINGS IN 1982, 1986 AND 1993.

4. (U) IN 1982 POPE JOHN PAUL II LAUNCHED THE ASSISI

MEETINGS WITH A LARGE-SCALE COMMEMORATION OF THE 700TH

BIRTHDAY OF SAINT FRANCIS OF ASSISI, THE CELEBRATED UMBRIAN

SAINT OF POVERTY AND PEACE (WHO ALSO TRIED HIS HAND AT

DIPLOMACY WHEN HE TRAVELED TO THE MIDDLE EAST TO SEEK AN END

TO THE CRUSADES). IN 1986 THE POPE RETURNED TO ASSISI

FLANKED BY MYRIAD RELIGIOUS LEADERS IN AN APPEAL TO END THE

DANGEROUS DIVISIONS OF THE COLD WAR. IN 1993 JOHN PAUL II

CONVOKED A SMALLER GATHERING IN ASSISI TO ADDRESS THE CRISIS

IN THE BALKANS. THE 1986 MEETING IN PARTICULAR BECAME THE

TOUCHSTONE FOR SUBSEQUENT VATICAN EFFORTS IN INTERFAITH

RELIGIOUS AND POLITICAL DIALOGUE. OTHER INTERFAITH

GATHERINGS, NOTABLY UNDER THE AUSPICES OF THE COMMUNITY OF

SANT\'EGIDIO, INVARIABLY INVOKE THE SPIRIT OF THE 1986 MEETING

IN ASSISI.

CONSISTENTLY BEEN TEMPERED BY EFFORTS TO DEFUSE ANY RELIGIOUS

ASPECT TO THE CONFLICT. EVEN AS VATICAN OFFICIALS LAUDED

6. (SBU) WHILE THE POPE\'S OWN ASSISI PROCLAMATION "VIOLENCE

NEVER AGAIN! WAR NEVER AGAIN! TERRORISM NEVER AGAIN!"

DOMINATED THE HEADLINES, THERE WERE A NUMBER OF INTERVENTIONS

READ BY OR ON BEHALF OF LEADERS OF DIFFERENT FAITHS, CREEDS,

AND DENOMINATIONS. EACH STATEMENT HAD ITS OWN ACCENT AND

IDENTITY, WITH NO EVIDENT PLOYS FOR SECTARIAN OR ETHNIC

ADVANTAGE. SHEIK MOHAMMED AL-TANTAWI, HEAD OF CAIRO\'S

AL-AZHAR MOSQUE, WAS ABSENT, YET SENT A DECLARATION READ BY A

REPRESENTATIVE. THE STATEMENT, A COMMITMENT TO THE

IMPORTANCE OF DIALOGUE, DID RAISE EYEBROWS HOWEVER, LAUDING

POPE JOHN PAUL II AS A CHAMPION OF PALESTINIANS AND OMITTING

ANY MENTION OF JUDAISM OR THE RABBINICAL PRESENCE ON STAGE.

YET FOR THE VATICAN INDIVIDUAL STATEMENTS COUNTED LESS THAN

THE FACT AND WITNESS OF EACH LEADER\'S PRESENCE, NOTABLY THE

MUSLIM AND ORTHODOX REPRESENTATIVES. "THE VERY FACT THAT WE

ARE LISTENING TO ONE ANOTHER," THE POPE SAID, "IS A SIGN OF

PEACE."

THE SECOND FRONT: ADVANCING RAPPROCHEMENT WITH THE ORTHODOX.

--------------------------------------------- ------

7. (U) THE ASSISI ENCOUNTER INCLUDED A HOST OF ORTHODOX

PATRIARCHS, THUS MARKING A KEY STEP FORWARD IN THE VATICAN\'S

OUTREACH TO EASTERN CHRISTIANITY. THE ECUMENICAL PATRIARCH OF

CONSTANTINOPLE, BARTHOLOMEW, WHO HAS MET THE POPE SEVERAL

TIMES, LENT LEGITIMACY AMONG ORTHODOX CHRISTIANS TO THE

GATHERING. THE TRUE WATERSHED HOWEVER WAS THE PRESENCE OF

THE RUSSIAN ORTHODOX PATRIARCH\'S PERSONAL REPRESENTATIVE,

METROPOLITAN PITRIM. VATICAN WATCHERS WERE QUICK TO READ

INTO THE METROPOLITAN\'S PRESENCE HOPES OF A SOFTENING IN THE

RUSSIAN ORTHODOX PATRIARCH\'S HITHERTO STRENUOUS OPPOSITION TO

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 VATICA 00521 02 OF 03 040621Z

A PAPAL VISIT TO RUSSIA, REITERATED PUBLICLY ONLY DAYS

PREVIOUSLY. INDEED, FIDES, A VATICAN-BASED NEWS-SERVICE,

CALLED THE HIGH CALIBER OF ORTHODOX SUPPORT FOR THE ASSISI

MEETING "AN UNHOPED FOR STEP CLOSER FOR CHRISTIANS OF EAST

AND WEST, UNTHINKABLE ONLY MONTHS AGO."

ASSESSMENT: A COUP FOR THE VATICAN.

--------------------------------------------- ------

8. (U) THE ASSISI GATHERING GENERATED ALMOST ENTIRELY

POSITIVE PRESS FOR THE VATICAN. IT REINFORCED THE VATICAN\'S

CAREFULLY CULTIVATED IMAGE AS A MODERN AGENT OF DIALOG

BETWEEN CULTURES. IT PROMOTED THE POPE\'S UNIQUE CLAIM TO

SPEAK AS A UNIVERSALLY RECOGNIZED MORAL AUTHORITY. THE

VATICAN ITSELF ADVANCED ITS AGENDA OF GREATER RAPPROCHEMENT

WITH THE ORTHODOX CHURCHES, THOUGH A PAPAL TRIP TO RUSSIA

REMAINS FOR NOW A DISTANT POSSIBILITY. AT ASSISI THE VATICAN

ALSO TOOK THE OCCASION TO SHOWCASE THE ORIENTAL CHURCHES --

E.G. THE SYRO ORTHODOX PATRIARCHATE OF ANTIOCH, THE ARMENIAN

APOSTOLIC CHURCH, THE ARMENIAN CATHOLICOSATE OF CILICIA, ETC.

-- WHOSE RELIGIOUS LEADERS WERE A MEDIA SENSATION IN THEIR

STRIKING CLERICAL GARB. THEIR PRESENCE IN ASSISI WAS A

SALIENT REMINDER THAT THE OVERARCHING ISSUE FOR THE VATICAN

IN THE MIDDLE EAST AND OFTEN ELSEWHERE IS THE PROTECTION OF

IMPERILED CHRISTIAN MINORITIES. FINALLY, AND MOST IMPORTANT

IN THE NEAR TERM, THE ASSISI MEETING PROVIDED AN INTERFAITH

IMPRIMATUR TO THE VATICAN\'S POLITICAL AND RELIGIOUS STRATEGY

TO ENSURE THAT THE AFTERMATH OF SEPTEMBER 11 DOES NOT EVOLVE

INTO A WAR BETWEEN RELIGIOUS TRADITIONS.

9. IN A FITTING SYMBOL, JOHN PAUL II AND OVER 300 RELIGIOUS

LEADERS DEPARTED TOGETHER FOR ASSISI FROM THE VATICAN RAIL

STATION, ITS FIRST USE FOR PAPAL TRAVEL SINCE 1979. DUBBED

THE PEACE TRAIN, IT WAS A MEDIA METAPHOR FOR THE ASSISI

MEETING, EMBARKING THE RELIGIOUS LEADERSHIP ON A JOURNEY WITH

A COMMON PURPOSE. IN MANY RESPECTS, ASSISI WAS A FORUM FOR

POLITICS BY OTHER MEANS, NOT JUST FOR THE VATICAN BUT FOR

OTHER RELIGIOUS LEADERS, TOO, WHO WERE FURNISHED A READY-MADE

STAGE ON WHICH TO ATTEST THEIR COMMITMENT TO PEACE AND

DIALOG.

10. (SBU) WHILE THE FACT OF ISLAMIC TURNOUT AT ASSISI WAS

PROMISING, IT WAS LIMITED. SANT\'EGIDIO LEADERS LAMENTED TO

POLOFF THE DIFFICULTY IN DEVELOPING A BASE OF MUSLIM

INTERLOCUTORS OPEN TO THE MESSAGE OF DIALOG. MOREOVER,

INTERESTED MUSLIM RELIGIOUS LEADERS ARE NOT NECESSARILY

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 VATICA 00521 03 OF 03 040622Z

PREPARED TO ADOPT A PUBLIC STANCE IN FAVOR OF DIALOG.

10. (C) POLOFF RETURNED FROM ASSISI TO ROME IN THE COMPANY

OF A LONGSTANDING MULSIM COLLABORATOR OF SANT\'EGIDIO, A

FRANCOPHONE ALGERIAN PROFESSOR IN MECCA WHO PARTICIPATED

UNOFFICIALLY IN THE INTERFAITH GATHERING. MUHAMMED ESSLIMANI

(STRICTLY PROTECT) TOLD POLOFF THAT HE HAS BEEN WAGING AN

UPHILL BATTLE WITH FACULTY COLLEAGUES ON THE MERITS OF

OUTREACH TO THE NON-MUSLIM RELIGIOUS COMMUNITY. HOWEVER,

SEPTEMBER 11, HE SAID, HERALDED A SEA CHANGE. RELIGIOUS

LEADERS NOW INCREASINGLY RECOGNIZED THAT ISLAM HAD A DUTY TO

ADDRESS THE ATROCITIES COMMITTED IN ITS NAME AND TO COMBAT

WIDESPREAD IGNORANCE ABOUT NON-MUSLIMS. HE ADDED THAT SOME

FORMERLY CRITICAL COLLEAGUES, WHO THEMSELVES ESCHEWED ALL

CONTACT WITH NON-MUSLIMS, HAD NOW EXPRESSED NEWFOUND

APPRECIATION FOR HIS OWN PERSONAL OUTREACH. NONETHELESS,

THERE WAS GREAT IGNORANCE CONCERNING NON-MUSLIMS, A NEED FOR

EDUCATION, AND FOR CONTACT WITH NON-MUSLIM RELIGIOUS LEADERS

OF GOOD WILL. HE REGRETTED THAT THE FEW MUSLIM LEADERS WHO

PRESENT THEMSELVES AS SO-CALLED MODERATES LACK REAL

THEOLOGICAL FOUNDATIONS AND CREDIBILITY, HARSHLY DENOUNCING

THE MUFTI OF MARSEILLES IN PARTICULAR. IT WAS IMPERATIVE TO

DEVELOP A NEW RELIGIOUS GENERATION WITHIN THE ARAB WORLD, HE

SAID, EXPLAINING HIS DISCREET COLLABORATION WITH SANT\'EGIDIO

AND HIS UNOFFICIAL PRESENCE AT THE ASSISI MEETING.
