SUBJECT: UAEG WILL ENDORSE BAN ON ALL HUMAN CLONING

REF: ABU DHABI 3127

1. (U) Local press reported that after attending a

meeting last week in Paris organized by a special

committee affiliated with WHO and UNESCO to discuss

human cloning, Ministry of Health Assistant

Undersecretary for Curative Medicine Dr. Abdul Ghaffar

Al-Hawi stated that the UAE would support a ban on all

human cloning. The UAE\'s position would extend the

prohibition to all laboratory experiments related to

genetic research.

2. (SBU) Comment: Al-Hawi\'s public statement indicates

that the UAEG should support the Costa Rican

resolution in the UNGA, as the MFA told us in reftel.

End comment.
