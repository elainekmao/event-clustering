SUBJECT: ZIMBABWE PRESIDENT TO CONVENE DRC ALLIES

MEETING NEXT WEEK

REFTEL: SECSTATE 09311

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 HARARE 00368 190927Z

CLASSIFIED BY CHARGE D\'AFFAIRES EARL M. IRVING FOR

REASONS: 1.5 (B) AND (D).

1. (C) CHARGE D\'AFFAIRES, ACCOMPANIED BY POLOFF,

DELIVERED REFTEL DEMARCHE TO DR. NGONI CHIDEYA, THE

ACTING PERMANENT SECRETARY IN ZIMBABWE\'S MINISTRY OF

FOREIGN AFFAIRS, THE MORNING OF JANUARY 19. DR. CHIDEYA

HAD POSTPONED THE MEETING FROM JANUARY 18. THE CHARGE

EMPHASIZED THAT THE USG HAS SIMILARLY URGED THE

GOVERNMENTS OF UGANDA AND RWANDA TO REFRAIN FROM TAKING

MILITARY ADVANTAGE OF THE SITUATION IN THE DRC, AND

ENCOURAGED THE GOZ TO ENGAGE IN DIALOGUE WITH UGANDA AND

RWANDA.

2. (C) CHIDEYA RESPONDED BY SAYING THAT THE GOZ WILL

ISSUE A STATEMENT ON THE EVENTS IN THE DRC IN THE NEXT

FEW DAYS, IF NOT TODAY. "SOON AFTER" KABILA\'S STATE

FUNERAL ON JANUARY 23, PRESIDENT MUGABE WILL CONVENE A

HEADS OF STATE MEETING WITH THE DRC ALLIES, NAMELY

ANGOLA AND NAMIBIA, ACCORDING TO THE ACTING PERMSEC. IT

WAS UNCLEAR FROM CHIDEYA\'S ACCOUNT WHETHER JOSEPH KABILA

OR DRC REPRESENTATIVES WOULD BE INVITED TO THE ALLIES

MEETING. ONCE THAT MEETING IS CONCLUDED, MUGABE WILL

INVITE ALL THE LUSAKA SIGNATORIES TO A WIDER MEETING ON

THE WAY FORWARD IN THE DRC, CHIDEYA SAID.

3. (C) CHIDEYA FURTHER STATED THAT A DRC DELEGATION OF

25 PEOPLE, INCLUDING EDUCATION MINISTER ABDOULAYE

YERODIA NBOMBASI AND SOCIAL AFFAIRS MINISTER MOBOTO,

ARRIVED IN HARARE LAST NIGHT (JANUARY 18). JUSTICE

MINISTER KONGOLO AND INTERIOR MINISTER KUKUDJI REMAINED

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 HARARE 00368 190927Z

IN KINSHASA, CHIDEYA REPORTED. HEALTH MINISTER MAMBA

ACCOMPANIED KABILA\'S BODY TO HARARE ON JANUARY 17 AND IS

STILL IN TOWN, ACCORDING TO THE ACTING PERMANENT

SECRETARY. THE PURPOSE OF THE DELEGATION VISIT IS TO

SIPDIS

INFORM PRESIDENT MUGABE OF EXACTLY WHAT HAPPENED TO

KABILA, AND TO ASSIST IN RETURNING KABILA\'S BODY TO THE

DRC, THE ACTING PERMSEC SAID. THE ZIMBABWEAN MFA\'S

PROTOCOL OFFICE IS HELPING TO MAKE THE ARRANGEMENTS.

THE BODY IS DUE TO GO BACK TO KINSHASA TOMORROW, JANUARY

20, CHIDEYA CONTINUED. PRESIDENT MUGABE, ALONG WITH

OTHER HEADS OF STATE, IS LIKELY TO ATTEND KABILA\'S

FUNERAL NEXT WEEK, THE ACTING PERMSEC ADDED.

4. (C) COMMENT: ZIMBABWE\'S MFA WAS AN UNUSUAL HIVE OF

ACTIVITY WITH ITS ASSISTANCE OF THE DRC DELEGATION.

CHIDEYA APPEARED TO BE IN THE CENTER OF THE ACTIVITY--

UNCHARACTERISTIC FOR AN MFA OFFICIAL--AND EVEN RECEIVED

A PHONE CALL FROM DEFENSE MINISTER MAHACHI DURING OUR

MEETING. FOREIGN MINISTER MUDENGE RETURNED WITH MUGABE

FROM CAMEROON, BUT IS TRYING TO RESUME HIS INTERRUPTED

LEAVE, WE WERE TOLD. FURTHERMORE, YERODIA\'S PRESENCE IN

HARARE MAY SUGGEST THAT HE HAS BEEN PARTLY RESURRECTED

BY JOSEPH KABILA AND THAT HE IS SEEKING THE BLESSING OF

ZIMBABWE FOR A MORE PROMINENT ROLE IN THE NEW CONGOLESE

DISPENSATION. END COMMENT.
