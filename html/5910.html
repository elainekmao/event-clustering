SUBJECT: SECRETARY\'S ORAL MESSAGE ON IRAQ

ISSUES AND NAM DEMARCHE DELIVERED

REF: A) STATE 47832

B) STATE 47493

1. (U) Classified by Ambassador Marcelle M. Wahba

for reasons 1.5 (B) and (D).

2. (C) The Ambassador delivered Ref A oral message

from the Secretary and Ref B NAM demarche on Iraq to

MFA Minstate Shaykh Hamdan Bin Zayid AL-Nahyan on

2/22. Noting the upcoming Arab League, Islamic

Conference and NAM summits, the Ambassador

underscored the need to keep Iraq focused on full

compliance with UNSCR 1441. Hamdan assured that the

UAE would stand firm, but predicted that other

countries, particularly Syria, may seek statements

critical of the U.S. Syria\'s strong-arm tactics at

what Hamdan termed as last week\'s "disastrous" Arab

League ministerial in Cairo, had not been well-

received in many Arab capitals.

3. (C) Hamdan briefed that visiting PLO Secretary

General and Arafat Deputy Abu Mazen (Mahmoud Abbas)

understood from his recent visit to Moscow that the

Russians were "98 percent" resigned to the fact that

the USG would move militarily against Iraq. In Abu

Mazen\'s view, the Russians would not prevent the

attack (i.e. exercise their UNSC veto option).

4. (C) According to Hamdan, Fujairah Ruler Hamad Al-

Sharqi will represent the UAE at the NAM summit

while Dubai Ruler and UAE Vice President/Prime

Minister Shaykh Maktoum Bin Rashid Al-Maktoum will

head up the delegation to the proposed March 1 Arab

League summit.

WAHBA
