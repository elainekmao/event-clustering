MALDIVIAN AT GITMO

------------------

1. (C) Per our recent o/i on this issue, we received an

official request today from the Permanent Secretary of

the Maldivian MFA Ahmed Shaheed to obtain information

about the investigation of Ibrahim Fauzee, the Maldivian

national being held at Gitmo. Shaheed proposes sending

an official from the Maldivian Ministry of Defense to

meet with the relevant authorities of the USG. Our A/RSO

has told us that the FBI has a program to allow foreign

officials access to their nationals at Gitmo. He

provides the following two points of contact at the FBI

for the Guantanamo Bay Task Force: Bill Ebinger or Jeff

Harp at 202-324-7999. We are faxing a copy of the

Dipnote and letter from Shaheed to you. We know you are

working on this issue, but please provide us a reply we

can give to the Maldivians ASAP. (POL, RSO)

DEMINING PROGRAM

----------------

2. (SBU) DoD has moved forward on starting a

humanitarian demining training program with military and

civilian components by early next year. There has been

no apparent progress at the Department on this matter,

according to DoD. Can you check with PM/HDP on what the

status is with the HMA program? DoD states they need an

official message from Department in order to implement

the training program on schedule. If we don\'t get such a

message soon, we will be in an embarrassing situation

with the GSL. We have basically promised the program to

the GSL and announced it in press statements, which the

Department reviewed. (Note: Please pass this item to

PM/HDP Murf McCloy.) (POL, DAO)

OSLO

----

3. (SBU) Just want to call your attention to our fax

containing the GSL\'s "Oslo Paper." We have also gotten

word that the GSL is thinking of having the conference

put out an "Oslo Declaration." We have nothing further

yet on what that might contain. (FO)
