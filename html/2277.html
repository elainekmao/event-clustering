SUBJECT: MEDIA REPORT ZDERA; HARARE

1. THE GOVERNMENT-CONTROLLED DAILY "CHRONICLE"

DEDICATED ITS DECEMBER 8 EDITORIAL TO CONDEMN THE

ZIMBABWE DEMOCRACY AND ECONOMIC RECOVERY ACT (ZDERA) OF

2001. UNDER HEADLINE "DEMOCRACY BILL, A BULLYING

TACTIC," THE PAPER COMMENTS:

2. ". . .IT IS STRANGE THAT THERE SHOULD BE

ZIMBABWEANS CELEBRATING AMERICA\'S PASSING OF A SO-

CALLED DEMOCRACY BILL THAT IN FACT SEEKS TO CURTAIL

DEMOCRACY. IT IS AN OPEN SECRET THAT THE WORLD\'S

FORMER COLONIAL POWERS ALREADY HAVE A CANDIDATE IN MIND

AND THEY ARE ONLY WAITING FOR THE CONFIRMATION OF

RESULTS AT THE PRESIDENTIAL PLEBISCITE. WE ARE TOLD

THAT THE PURPOSE OF THE SO-CALLED ZDERA IS TO PRESSURE

PRESIDENT MUGABE INTO HOLDING FREE AND FAIR ELECTIONS

AND ESTABLISH LAND OWNERSHIP PROTECTION. THERE IS AN

INSINUATION HERE THAT IT IS A FOREGONE CONCLUSION THAT

THE PRESIDENTIAL ELECTIONS WILL NOT BE FREE AND FAIR.

WE SYMPATHIZE WITH THE IGNORANCE AND NAIVETY (SIC) OF

THE AMERICANS. IF THEY EVER BOTHERED TO READ

ZIMBABWE\'S HISTORY, THEY WOULD KNOW THAT THIS COUNTRY

HAS HELD SEVERAL ELECTIONS THAT WERE FREE AND FAIR.

ANYWAY, WHO ARE THE AMERICANS TO TEACH US ABOUT

CONDUCTING FREE AND FAIR ELECTIONS WHEN THEY THEMSELVES

FAILED DISMALLY TO CONDUCT PROPER PRESIDENTIAL

ELECTIONS EARLY THIS YEAR. FOR ALL WE KNOW THE

INCUMBENT PRESIDENT GEORGE W. BUSH IS IN POWER

FOLLOWING THE INTERVENTION OF THE COURTS IN THAT

COUNTRY. IS THIS WHAT YOU CALL DEMOCRACY? FAILURES

LIKE THE AMERICANS DO NOT QUALIFY TO LECTURE ZIMBABWE

ON DEMOCRACY. . . THE AMERICAN EVIL BILL IS AN ATTEMPT

TO RUN ZIMBABWE BY REMOTE CONTROL, AND WE SAY NO TO

THIS. WE ARE A SOVEREIGN STATE WHICH DOES NOT NEED
