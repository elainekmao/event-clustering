SUBJECT: ZIMBABWE PRESIDENT DILUTES INDEPENDENCE OF HIGH

COURT WITH NEW APPOINTMENTS

CLASSIFIED BY CHARGE D\'AFFAIRES EARL M. IRVING FOR

REASONS: 1.5 (B) AND (D).

1. (U) ON DECEMBER 21, PRESIDENT MUGABE EXPANDED THE

ZIMBABWE HIGH COURT BY SWEARING IN FOUR NEW JUSTICES.

THREE OF THE NEW JUSTICES HAVE CLEAR TIES TO THE RULING

ZANU-PF. RITA MAKARAU IS A FORMER NON-CONSTITUENCY MP

APPOINTED BY MUGABE AND SERVED IN THE LAST PARLIAMENT.

SHE WAS ALSO APPOINTED TO SERVE ON THE GOVERNMENT\'S

CONSTITUTIONAL COMMISSION, WHICH LAST YEAR HIJACKED THE

BROAD-BASED NATIONAL CONSTITUTIONAL ASSEMBLY\'S EFFORT TO

WRITE A NEW CONSTITUTION. CHARLES HUNGWE WAS THE FIRST

CHAIRMAN OF THE ZIMBABWE NATIONAL LIBERATION WAR

VETERANS\' ASSOCIATION FROM 1992 TO 1995, AND WAS A

SENIOR MAGISTRATE BEFORE HIS HIGH COURT APPOINTMENT. HE

IS CURRENTLY A MEMBER OF THE ELECTORAL SUPERVISORY

COMMISSION, AND IS EXPECTED TO RESIGN FROM THAT POST TO

TAKE UP HIS NEW POSITION. BEN HLATSHWAYO, ALSO A MEMBER

OF THE NOW-DEFUNCT CONSTITUTIONAL COMMISSION, HAS BEEN A

STAUNCH CRITIC OF THE NCA, AND HE IS CURRENTLY AN

EDITORIAL ADVISOR TO THE PRO-ZANU-PF "ZIMBABWE MIRROR."

ANNE-MARIE GOWORA COMES FROM A PRIVATE LAW FIRM, AND

ONCE WORKED FOR THE ATTORNEY-GENERAL\'S OFFICE. IT IS

UNCLEAR WHERE HER POLITICAL LEANINGS LIE. THE

APPOINTMENTS EXPAND THE HARARE HIGH COURT FROM 15 TO 19

JUSTICES. THE SIZE OF THE BULAWAYO HIGH COURT REMAINS

UNCHANGED AT FOUR JUSTICES.

2. (C) BRIAN RAFTOPOULOS, A RESEARCH FELLOW AT THE

UNIVERSITY OF ZIMBABWE, TOLD THE EMBASSY THAT MUGABE\'S

APPOINTEES ARE INDEED CLOSE TO THE RULING PARTY, BUT NOT

SO CLOSE THAT THEY CAN BE CALLED ZANU-PF PUPPETS.

MUGABE WANTS CREDIBLE AND COMPETENT PEOPLE ON THE COURT,

RAFTOPOULOS OFFERED, TO AT LEAST PRESERVE SOME

APPEARANCE OF JUDICIAL INDEPENDENCE. HIGH COURT

JUSTICESHIPS ARE NOT "STRAIGHTFORWARD PATRONAGE

POSITIONS," THE RESEARCH FELLOW CONTINUED, BECAUSE

JUSTICES EXERCISE A CONSIDERABLE AMOUNT OF AUTONOMY IN

THEIR RULINGS. THE JUDICIAL SERVICE COMMISSION, HEADED

BY THE STAUNCHLY INDEPENDENT SUPREME COURT CHIEF JUSTICE

ANTHONY GUBBAY, HAD TO APPROVE THE NOMINEES, WHICH ALSO

LENDS SOME CREDIBILITY TO THE APPOINTMENTS. (COMMENT:

RECENTLY APPOINTED TO THE JUDICIAL SERVICE COMMISSION

WERE TWO VERY PRO-ZANU-PF MEMBERS GODFREY CHIDIYASIKU--

THE HIGH COURT JUDGE PRESIDENT--AND CANAAN DUBE. IN

ANOTHER ATTEMPT AT VITIATING THE GATEKEEPER POINT OF THE

JUDICIARY\'S INDEPENDENCE, THE COMMISSION CONTRACT OF BOB

STUMBLES--OUR EMBASSY LAWYER, AND WIDELY RESPECTED IN

THE LEGAL FIELD--WAS NOT RENEWED BY MUGABE. END

COMMENT.) NONETHELESS, THE APPOINTMENTS REFLECT

MUGABE\'S ONGOING, LONG-TERM PLAN TO TURN THE JUDICIARY

INTO AN ARM OF GOVERNMENT MORE PLIABLE TO ZANU-PF\'S

WISHES, RAFTOPOULOS BELIEVES.

3. (C) COMMENT: MUGABE\'S EXPANSION OF THE HIGH COURT

WITH JUSTICES HAVING STRONG TIES TO THE RULING PARTY

UNDERMINES THE INDEPENDENCE OF THE JUDICIAL SYSTEM, IN

OUR VIEW. RAFTOPOULOS IS CORRECT IN THAT THE NEW

JUSTICES HAVE STRONG LEGAL CREDENTIALS, AND MUGABE\'S

ATTENTION TO THEIR CREDIBILITY SHOWS THAT THE PRESIDENT

STILL CARES ABOUT MAINTAINING A FACADE OF LEGALITY FOR

HIS PARTY\'S ACTIONS. HOWEVER, THE JUDGE PRESIDENT OF

THE HIGH COURT IS AN AFFIRMED ZANU-PF LOYALIST, AND,

WITH A LARGER BENCH MORE AMENABLE TO HIS PARTY\'S CAUSES,

HE WILL HAVE MORE OPTIONS IN ASSIGNING CASES WHEN THEY

COME BEFORE THE HIGH COURT. THE COURT HAS BEEN

ESPECIALLY ACTIVE IN ADJUDICATING CLAIMS OF COMMERCIAL

FARMERS DENIED DUE PROCESS IN THE SEIZURE OF THEIR LAND.

MUCH OF THIS ACTIVISM, IN THE GOVERNMENT\'S

INTERPRETATION, COULD BE STOPPED COLD WHEN THE COURT

RESUMES ITS SESSION NEXT MONTH. END COMMENT.
