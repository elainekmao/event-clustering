CROAT/SERB GORSKI KOTAR REGION PROVIDED BOTH A

HOPEFUL AND NEGATIVE PICTURE OF FUTURE RELATIONS

BETWEEN SERBS AND CROATS IN CROATIA. DIFFERING

WARTIME EXPERIENCES IN VRBOVSKO AND OGULIN, ONLY 30

KM APART, HAVE COLORED SERB/CROAT RELATIONSHIPS IN

THE TWO DISTRICTS FOR YEARS TO COME. IN VRBOVSKO,

WHILE TENSIONS WERE HIGH, ACTUAL FIGHTING NEVER TOOK

PLACE, AND WITH THE OVERALL CONFLICT ENDING IN

CROATIA, INTERETHNIC RELATIONS SEEM TO BE TAKING A

TURN FOR THE BETTER. IN OGULIN, WHERE SERBS FLED AND

WHERE THE FRONT LINE IS ONLY 15 KM AWAY, SITTING DOWN

AT THE SAME TABLE TOGETHER IS STILL A MAJOR TASK FOR

THE AREA\'S CROATS AND SERBS. THE VILLAGE OF JASENAK,

A PREDOMINANTLY SERB VILLAGE IN THE OGULIN DISTRICT,

IS THE SITE OF A CONTROVERSIAL DISPLACED PERSON

CENTER. BOTH THE EC AND THE UNHCR ARE ACTIVELY

ENGAGED THERE TO SEE THAT THE SENSITIVITIES OF ALL

SIDES ARE TAKEN INTO ACCOUNT. THE EC AND UNHCR WILL

NOT BE HERE FOREVER, HOWEVER, AND THE PROCESS OF

RECONCILIATION WILL BE DIFFICULT AT BEST. IN SOME

SITUATIONS, UNFORTUNATELY, RECONCILIATION SEEMS A

DISTANT DREAM IF NOT AN IMPOSSIBILITY. END SUMMARY.

VRBOVSKO: A MODUS VIVENDI

--------------------------

4. SITTING DOWN WITH SERB AND CROAT REPRESENTATIVES

IN VRBOVSKO PROVIDED SOME HOPE THAT ALL WAS NOT

LOST. AS WE DISCUSSED ISSUE AFTER THORNY ISSUE THAT

HAVE PLAGUED SERB/CROAT RELATIONS IN THE FORMER

YUGOSLAVIA, IT APPEARED TO US THAT THE TWO SERBS AND

THREE CROATS MEETING WITH US HAD BEEN OVER THIS

GROUND BEFORE. THE WARTIME EXPERIENCE IN THE

VRBOVSKO AREA WAS, WHILE NOT WITHOUT TENSION, LARGELY

DEVOID OF ANY CONFLICT. THERE WAS NEVER A JNA BASE

IN THE DISTRICT, OFTEN THE FOCUS OF INITIAL FIGHTING

IN OTHER PARTS OF CROATIA, SERB VILLAGERS NEVER SET

UP BARRICADES, AND THE FRONT LINE WAS FAR ENOUGH AWAY

TO KEEP THE DISTRICT FROM RECEIVING ANY SHELLING.

THIS COMBINATION OF FACTORS CONSPIRED TO ALLOW

REASONABLE SERBS AND CROATS TO PREVAIL IN VRBOVSKO.

-

5. TWO OTHER FACTORS WORKED TO HOLD THE AREA

TOGETHER. ONE WAS POLITICAL AFFILIATION. OF THE

FOUR LOCAL GOVERNMENT OFFICIALS WE MET WITH (2 SERBS

AND 2 CROATS), TWO (ONE EACH) WERE MEMBERS OF THE

PARTY FOR DEMOCRATIC CHANGES (SDP - FORMER

COMMUNISTS), ONE SERB JOINED THE SERBIAN DEMOCRATIC

PARTY (SDS) FOLLOWING THE APRIL 1990 ELECTIONS, AND

THE OTHER CROAT WAS NOT AFFILIATED WITH ANY PARTY.

PRESIDENT TUDJMAN\'S RULING CROATIAN DEMOCRATIC UNION

(HDZ) WAS NOT REPRESENTED IN THIS GROUP. IN

ADDITION, THOUGH IT IS NOT CLEAR HOW THIS WAS

VRBOVSKO: NOT WITHOUT ITS PROBLEMS

-----------------------------------

7. BOTH SIDES ADMITTED TO SERIOUS STRAINS IN THE

RELATIONSHIP THAT THEY BLAMED ON SMALL GROUPS OF

CROATIAN AND SERBIAN "EXTREMISTS". "EVERYONE IS

ARMED," SAID LEADERS OF BOTH COMMUNITIES, LEADING TO

THE PROMISE OF MUTUALLY ASSURED DESTRUCTION IN CASE

OF ANY BREAKDOWN IN THE CURRENT PEACE. SOME OF THE

CAUSES OF INSTABILITY IN THE DISTRICT OVER THE LAST

10 MONTHS INCLUDE THE FOLLOWING:

-

-- IN VRBOVSKO, PRIOR TO THE WAR, THERE WERE 18

POLICEMEN, HALF OF WHOM WERE SERBS. SINCE THE WAR,

THE TOTAL NUMBER HAS INCREASED TO 62, TEN PERCENT OF

WHOM ARE SERBS. WHILE SERBS HAVE NOT BEEN FIRED FROM

THE POLICE FORCE, THEY HAVE NOT BEEN HIRED EITHER,

CONTRARY TO A NATIONAL REGULATION WHICH REQUIRES

DISTRICTS TO HIRE BASED ON THE PROPORTION OF THE

POPULATION WHICH IDENTIFIES ITSELF WITH DIFFERENT

ETHNIC GROUPS.

NEW, IMMEDIATE INFLOWS OF DISPLACED ARE PLANNED.

14. IT IS OUR VIEW THAT EVEN IN AREAS WHERE SOME

EFFORTS TOWARD RECONCILIATION ARE UNDER WAY, ANY REAL

SUCCESS DEPENDS ON THE OVERALL CLIMATE AS DEFINED BY

ZAGREB. PROGRESS IN INTER-ETHNIC RELATIONS HAS BEEN

UNTIL THE WAR IS OVER AND THE UNPROFOR HAS DONE ITS
