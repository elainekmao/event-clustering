(U) Classified by DCM Robert Deutsch. Reasons: 1.5b and d.

1. (C) DDG/Mideast Safak Gokturk told Pol-MilCouns 1/21 that

Turkey\'s "Peace Initiative" meeting would go ahead as planned

on 1/23 in Istanbul. However, it will be at ministerial

rather than summit level. All Foreign Ministers have agreed

to attend.

2. (C) Gokturk was reluctant to characterize the meeting as

"preparatory" since the MFA was not sure whether it would be

the last such meeting. That would depend on what happened in

Istanbul.

3. (C) Regarding the declaration, Pol-MilCouns stressed that

our goal is to send the strongest possible signal to Saddam.

If the statement is "balanced" with criticism of the US, it

will be terribly counterproductive. Gokturk said that the

declaration had not been discussed with the other countries

but that Turkey would do its best to keep criticism of the US

out.

4. (C) Finally, Gokturk went over the points his boss DG

Burcuoglu had made with the DCM (reftel), adding that Iran

had pressed for Kuwaiti inclusion "for its own reasons."
