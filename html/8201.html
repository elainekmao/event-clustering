SUBJECT: COMPANY DISAPPEARS AFTER PARTS RETURNED: BLUE

LANTERN LEVEL 3: 05-870151

REF: A. ROME 541

B. STATE 11035

1. LTCOL DAMIANI OF THE ITALIAN DEFENSE MINISTRY (MOD)

INFORMED US THAT THE MINISTRY RETURNED THE ORDER OF C-47

HELICOPTER PARTS TO A.S.I. AVIATION SERVICES ITALIA SEVERAL

MONTHS AGO BECAUSE THEY WERE NOT SATISFIED. AFTER TRYING A

VARIETY OF CHANNELS, WE WERE UNABLE TO CONTACT THE COMPANY.

ON MAY 20, THE MOD CONFIRMED THAT THE ADDRESSES FOR A.S.I.

AVIATION SERVICES ITALIA WERE NO LONGER VALID. LTCOL DAMIANI

ADMITTED THE COMPANY APPEARS TO HAVE DISAPPEARED. WE HAVE NO

FURTHER INFORMATION ABOUT THE DISPOSITION OF THE PARTS.
