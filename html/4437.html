START MEMO NOMINATING-JUSTIFICATION:

Ms. Hassan is the key official of the Maldives

Monetary Authority (MMA) who has been attached to

MMA for over 20 years. She reports directly to the

Vice-Governor who is the Minister of State for

Treasury and the Governor who is the President of

Maldives. Ms. Hassan is one of the comparatively

young women leaders in the public sector in the

Republic of Maldives. Although Ms. Hassan has

visited the U.S. before, she has had a very limited

exposure visiting only Washington, D.C. for training

course at the IMF Institute five years ago.

Since active role of women in the Maldives society

is very essential for its economic and social

development, her participation in this project will

be beneficial to her and to the Maldivian women as a

whole. She will be able to play a greater role in

uplifting of status of women in the Maldives.
