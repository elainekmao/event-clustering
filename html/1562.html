SUBJECT: SEVENTY-TWO YEAR OLD FEMALE COMMERCIAL FARMER

KILLED AMBUSH STYLE IN ZIMBABWE

REFS: A. HARARE 898, HARARE 815, C. 00 HARARE 7065,

D. 00 HARARE 7013

CLASSIFIED BY CHARGE D\' AFFAIRES EARL M. IRVING FOR

REASONS 1.5 (B/D).

-------

SUMMARY

-------

1. (C) ON MARCH 4, A 72-YEAR-OLD WHITE FEMALE COMMERCIAL

FARMER, GLORIA OLDS, WAS BRUTALLY AND VICIOUSLY

ASSASSINATED BY AUTOMATIC WEAPONS FIRE IN AN AMBUSH ON

HER FARM. HER SON MARTIN WAS KILLED BY "WAR VETS" ON

HIS FARM, WHICH WAS NEXT TO GLORIA\'S, LAST QPRIL AND NO

ONE HAS YET BEEN ARRESTED. THE POLICE CLAIM THAT OLDS

APPEARED TO BE KILLED IN THE COURSE OF A VEHICLE ROBBERY

ON HER FARM. THE CFU REGIONAL PRESIDENT, MAC CRAWFORD,

DISPUTES THE POLICE THEORY, STATING THAT OLDS WAS SHOT

MULTIPLE TIMES WITH AUTOMATIC RIFLES, THE LAST FEW SHOTS

DONE AT CLOSE RANGE. CRAWFORD SPECQLATES THAT OLDS WAS

MURDERED IN COLD BLOOD IN ORDER TO FURTHER INTIMIDATE

ZIMBABWE\'S WHITE COMMERCIAL FARMERS. END SUMMARY.

SEVENTY-TWO YEAR OLD FARMER BRUTALLY MURDERED

---------------------------------------------

2. (C) BOTH THE GOVERNMENT-CONTROLLED AND INDEPENDENT

PRESS REPORTED IN THEIR MARCH 5 EDITIONS THAT ON MARCH

4, GLORIA OLDS, A 72-YEAR-OLD FEMALE WHITE COMMERCIAL

FARMER, WAS KILLED AMBUSH STYLE AS SHE WAS APPROACHING

THE SECURITY GATE TO HER FARM, LOCATED NEAR THE TOWN OF

NYAMANDLOVU, ABOUT ONE HOUR BY ROAD NORTHWEST OF

BULAWAYO. THE PRESS REPORTS SPECULATED THAT OLDS WAS

KILLED AT 6:30 A.M., SINCE THIS IS WHEN SHE REGULARLY

OPENED THE SECURITY GATES TO HER FARM. HER BODY WAS

DISCOVERED BY HER SON DAVID, WHO WENT TO THE FARM AT 9

A.M., AFTER FAILING TO REACH HIS MOTHER BY PHONE.

GLORIA OLDS WAS THE MOTHER OF COMMERCIAL FARMER MARTIN

OLDS, WHO WAS KILLED ON HIS FARM BY "WAR VETS" LAST

APRIL 18, ZIMBABWE\'S INDEPENDENCE DAY. A PICK-UP TRUCK

BELONGING TO GLORIA OLDS WAS TAKEN BY THE ASSASSINS

AFTER SHE WAS KILLED, LEADING THE POLICE TO SPECULATE

THAT ROBBERY WAS THE MOTIVE BEHIND HER KILLING.

HOWEVER, COMMERCIAL FARMERS UNION (CFU) OFFICIALS

DISPUTE THIS THEORY, STATING THAT MONEY AND OTHER

VALUABLES WERE NOT TAKEN FROM GLORIA OLDS\' FARMHOUSE,

EVEN THOUGH THERE IS EVIDENCE THAT THEY ENTERED THE

FARMHOUSE TO GET THE KEYS TO THE PICK-UP.

----------------------------------

KILLING WAS A COLD-BLOODED AMBUSH

----------------------------------

3. (C) ON MARCH 5, ECONOFF SPOKE WITH MAC CRAWFORD,

PRESIDENT OF THE MATABELELAND REGION OF THE CFU,

REGARDING THE KILLING OF GLORIA OLDS (OLDS\' FARM IS

LOCATED IN MATABELELAND NORTH.) CRAWFORD STATED THAT

THE KILLING OF OLDS WAS AN AMBUSH-STYLE ASSASSINATION,

SIMILAR TO THAT OF ANOTHER WHITE FARMER, HENRY ELSWORTH

(REFS C AND D), LAST DECEMBER. ACCORDING TO CRAWFORD,

OLDS WOULD GO OUT AND OPEN THE SECURITY GATE TO HER FARM

EACH DAY AT 6:30 A.M. THE PHYSICAL EVIDENCE AT THE

SCENE OF THE KILLING INDICATES THAT OLDS WAS ABOUT A

METER FROM THE GATE, WHEN SHE WAS FIRST SHOT. SHE THEN

TURNED, BLEEDING, IN AN ATTEMPT TO RETURN TO HER

FARMHOUSE. SHE WAS THEN SHOT AGAIN. THE ASSAILANTS

THEN SHOT THE LOCK OFF THE SECURITY GATE, SHOT THREE OF

OLDS\' DOGS, AND THEN APPROACHED OLDS\' BODY. HOVERING

OVER HER, THEY SHOT HER A FEW MORE TIMES AT CLOSE RANGE.

CRAWFORD SAID THAT FIFTEEN SPENT BLACK AND GOLD

CARTRIDGES FROM AK-47 RIFLES WERE FOUND AT THE SCENE OF

%GEUNKQIQTHE CRIME, INDICATING THAT AT LEAST TWO DIFFERENT

WEAPONS MAY HAVE BEEN USED. CRAWFORD ADDED THAT THE

CARTRIDGES WERE NEW, AS IF THEY HAD JUST COME OUT OF AN

ARMORY. WHEN ECONOFF ASKED HOW HE WAS SURE THAT THE

WEAPONS USED WERE AK-47S, AND THAT THE CARTRIDGES WERE

FROM TWO GUNS AND WERE NEW, CRAWFORD STATED THAT HE HAD

BEEN IN THE MILITARY, AND HE WAS FAMILIAR WITH SUCH

WEAPONRY.

4. (C) CRAWFORD DISPUTES THE POLICE THEORY THAT OLDS\'

KILLING WAS THE RESULT OF A ROBBERY. WHILE HER TOYOTA

HILUX 4X4 WAS TAKEN BY THE KILLERS, THEY DID NOT STEAL

OTHER VALUABLES FROM THE FARMHOUSE. THE KILLERS ENTERED

OLDS\' FARMHOUSE, AND TOOK HER PURSE CONTAINING THE PICK-

UP KEYS, AS WELL AS SOME CHILDRENS\' CLOTHES FROM A

CLOSET. HOWEVER, THEY DID NOT TAKE A PILE OF CASH THAT

WAS SITTING ON THE KITCHEN TABLE, MADE NO EFFORT TO OPEN

A SAFE THAT HAD THE KEYS IN THE DOOR, TOOK NONE OF THE

WEAPONS THAT OLDS KEPT IN HER BEDROOM, OR APPARENTLY ANY

OTHER VALUABLES. WHEN ASKED WHAT OTHER MOTIVE THERE

MIGHT BE FOR HER KILLING, CRAWFORD STATED THAT OLDS WAS

IN THE PROCESS OF EVICTING A FEMALE SHOPKEEPER WHO

FAILED TO PAY RENT FROM A BUILDING SHE OWNED. THE

SHOPKEEPER\'S HUSBAND IS A FORMER ZIMBABWE PEOPLES

REVOLUTIONARY ARMY (ZIPRA) FIGHTER FROM THE INDEPENDENCE

STRUGGLE. THERE HAD RECENTLY BEEN A DEMONSTRATION IN

NYMANDLOVU BY THE AFFIRMATIVE ACTION GROUP (AAG), A

PRO-ZANU-PF CIVIL SOCIETY GROUP THAT ARGUES FOR GEATER

BLACK OWNERSHIP OF COMPANIES, DIRECTED AGANST OLDS

BECAUSE OF THE SHOPKEEPER\'S PLIGHT. HOEVER, CRAWFORD

DID NOT BELIEVE THAT THIS SITUATIO WOULD HAVE LED

ANYONE TO BRUTALLY GUN DOWN MRS.OLDS IN COLD BLOOD AT

HER FARM.

5. (C)CRAWFORD SPECULATED THAT GLORIA OLDS WAS KILLED

O SEND A SIGNAL TO THE WHITE COMMERCIAL FARMING

CMMUNITY. HE BELIEVED THAT THE MURDER OF A 72-YEAROLD

FEMALE WIDOW WAS MEANT TO SHOW THAT EVEN ELDRLY OR

FEMALE FARMERS ARE NOT SAFE ON THEIR FARC. CRAWFORD

ALSO BELIEVED THAT OLDS WAS SINGLED UT FOR

ASSASSINATION BECAUSE SHE WAS THE MOTHER O MARTIN OLDS.

HER KILLING WOULD RE-IGNITE IN THEMINDS OF WHITE

FARMERS THE KILLINGS AND VIOLENCETHAT PREVAILED IN THE

PARLIAMENTARY ELECTION PEROD. CRAWFORD LINKED THIS

KILLING TO THE UPCOMIN PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION AND

GOVERNMENT PRESS-TOUTE DIVISIONS WITHIN THE CFU (REFS A

AND B).

6. C) ZANU-PF HAS BEGUN A CAMPAIGN BY "WAR VETS" IN

MATABELELAND, A STRONGHOLD OF THE OPPOSITION MOVEMNT

FOR DEMOCRATIC CHANGE (MDC), TO INTIMIDATE MD

SUPPORTERS. AS PART OF THIS CAMPAIGN, A GROUP O 15 TO

20 "WAR VETS" HAS BEEN GOING RECENTLY FRO FARM TO FARM

BEATING UP FARM WORKERS AND THREATNING FARMERS SO THAT

THEY DO NOT SUPPORT THE MDC IN THE UPCOMING PRESIDENTIAL

ELECTION. CRAWFORD ADDED THAT THE MATABELELAND REGION

OF THE CFU WAS ONE OF THE MOST UNITED REGIONS IN ITS

SUPPORT FOR THE CFU EXECUTIVE LEADERSHIP AND ITS

STRATEGY OF CHALLENGING THE GOZ "FAST-TRACK"

RESETTLEMENT PROGRAM THOUGH THE COURTS. AT THE RECENT

CFU COUNCIL MEETING IN HARARE, CRAWFORD REVEALED THAT IT

WAS THE MATABELELAND REGION OF THE CFU THAT UNITED THE

COUNCIL BEHIND THE EMBATTLED CFU EXECUTIVE, AND WARDED

CFU MEMBERS (REF A). CRAWFORD WAS ADAMANT THAT IT IS

OLDS WAS BRUTALLY KILLED.

COMMENT

-------

7. (C) WE ARE VERY SKEPTICAL THAT GLORIA OLDS WAS KILLED

IN THE COURSE OF A ROBBERY. ONE DOES NOT NEED TO SHOOT

A 72-YEAR-OLD UNARMED GRANDMOTHER MULTIPLE TIMES WITH

AUTOMATIC RIFLES IN ORDER TO TAKE HER VEHICLE. WE

SIMILARLY DOUBT THAT SHE WAS MURDERED AS A RESULT OF THE

COURT PROCEEDING TO EVICT THE SHOPKEEPER FROM A BUILDING

SHE OWNED. WHAT WE DO FIND CREDIBLE IS THAT SHE WAS

KILLED, IN A BRUTAL AND COLD-BLOODED MANNER, IN ORDER TO

SOW FURTHER FEAR IN THE WHITE COMMERCIAL FARMING

COMMUNITY. THIS COULD INCLUDE THE GOAL OF CAUSING MORE

FARMERS TO ABANDON THEIR FARMS AND LEAVE ZIMBABWE OUT OF

FEAR FOR THEIR LIVES AND FAMILY\'S SAFETY. IT COULD ALSO

AIM TO CREATE FURTHER DIVIDES IN THE CFU, LEADING TO ITS

ULTIMATE BREAK-UP. ONE THING THAT WE ARE FAIRLY SURE OF

IS THAT THIS KILLING WILL JOIN THE LIST OF THE OTHER SIX

MURDERED WHITE COMMERCIAL FARMERS WHOSE KILLERS HAVE
