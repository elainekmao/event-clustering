SUBJECT: JORDAN: MEDIA REACTION ON ARAB MINISTERS

WASHINGTON MEETING, IRAQ, JORDANIAN DETAINED IN DETROIT

SUMMARY

NEWS REPORTS IN JORDAN\'S DAILIES COVERED THE POSITIVE

NOTE EXPRESSED BY ISRAELIS AND PALESTINIANS AFTER THEIR

MEETING, WHICH WOULD BE RESUMED IN A WEEK.

EDITORIAL COMMENTARIES COMMENTED SKEPTICALLY ABOUT THE

OPTIMISM EXPRESSED BY ARAB MINISTERS OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS

AFTER THEIR WASHINGTON MEETINGS.

ALL DAILIES REPORTED THAT THE FAMILY OF OMAR AL-

SHISHANI, WHO WAS DETAINED IN DETROIT, AFFIRMED THAT

SHISHANI WOULD BE RELEASED ON WEDNESDAY, AND THAT THE

DETENTION WAS THE RESULT OF A CONFUSION OVER NAMES.

NEWS REPORTS

NEWS REPORTS IN JORDAN\'S DAILIES REPORTED THE POSITIVE

NOTE AFTER THE ISRAELI-PALESTINIAN TALKS, WHICH WOULD

BE RESUMED IN A WEEK, ACCORDING TO THE REPORTS. CENTER-

LEFT, INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR LED WITH A

REPORT THAT THE PNA FEARS A GENERAL FAMINE.

IN AN EXCLUSIVE STORY, CENTER-LEFT, INFLUENTIAL ARABIC

DAILY AL-DUSTOUR ATTRIBUTED TO THE MINISTER OF INDUSTRY

AND TRADE THE STATEMENT MADE UPON HIS RETURN FROM

BAGHDAD, THAT A JORDANIAN-IRAQI FREE TRADE AGREEMENT

WOULD BE SIGNED SOON.

EDITORIAL COMMENTARY

-- JOHNSON\'S LETTER AND BUSH\'S AFFIRMATIONS

PROMINENT DAILY COLUMNIST TARIQ MASARWAH WROTE IN SEMI-

OFFICIAL INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-RAI ON JULY 22:

"WE REMEMBER VIVIDLY THE LETTER FROM PRESIDENT LB

JOHNSON TO HIS MAJESTY THE LATE KING HUSSEIN, IN WHICH

HE SAID THAT THE ARAB COUNTRIES\' ACCEPTANCE OF LORD

CARADON\'S INITIATIVE, WHICH BECAME RESOLUTION 242,

WOULD ENABLE HIS ADMINISTRATION TO RESOLVE THE CONFLICT

WITHIN SIX MONTHS. HUNDREDS OF MONTHS LATER, WE RECALL

THIS TO THE ARAB MINISTERS WHO CAME BACK FROM

WASHINGTON BEARING THE AFFIRMATIONS OF PRESIDENT BUSH,

HIS OPTIMISM, AND HIS CLARITY THAT THE PALESTINIAN

STATE WOULD BE ESTABLISHED WITHIN THREE YEARS, PROVIDED

THAT THE COUNTRIES WHO HAD ACCEPTED LORD CARADON\'S

INITIATIVE WOULD ONLY ACCEPT TO END TERRORISM, END

CORRUPTION, AND PRODUCE AN ACCEPTABLE PALESTINIAN

LEADERSHIP. WE REMIND THESE MINISTERS THAT THEY ONLY

HAVE PRESIDENT BUSH\'S VERBAL ASSURANCES. THIS TIME

THERE IS NOT EVEN A LETTER. BUT WHAT IS THERE TO

PREVENT OPTIMISM?"

-- WILL DROPPING THE OPTION OF RESISTANCE RESTORE

LEGITIMATE RIGHTS?

DAILY COLUMNIST MAZEN AL-SAKET WROTE IN CENTER-LEFT,

INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR ON JULY 22:

"DEVELOPMENTS BETWEEN ISRAEL AND THE U.S. ON THE ONE

HAND AND EGYPT, SAUDI ARABIA, JORDAN, AND THE PNA ON

THE OTHER, ALL REVOLVE AROUND `ENDING THE VIOLENCE,\'

WHICH MEANS DROPPING THE PALESTINIANS OPTION OF

RESISTING THE OCCUPATION. IS IT SUFFICIENT FOR THE U.S.

TO DICTATE THIS POSITION TO ARAB MINISTERS WHILE

ISRAELI EXPANSION AND SETTLEMENT ACTIVITY CONTINUE?

WHAT GUARANTEES DO THE PARTICIPATING ARAB COUNTRIES

OFFER US THAT THE REQUIRED ISRAELI WITHDRAWAL WOULD

TAKE PLACE, AND THAT PALESTINIANS WOULD RECOVER THEIR

LEGITIMATE RIGHTS? WHAT ARE THE POSITIVE ELEMENTS THAT

THE MINISTERS TALK ABOUT, WHICH NO ONE OTHER THAN THEM

CAN SEE IN THE AMERICAN POSITION THAT IS TOTALLY BIASED

TO ISRAEL AND THAT CALLS FOR ENDING PALESTINIAN

NATIONAL REPRESENTATION?"

-- THE TRIPARTITE DELEGATION RETURNS OPTIMISTIC

DAILY COLUMNIST JAMIL AL-NIMRI WROTE IN SEMI-OFFICIAL

INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-RAI ON JULY 22: "THE MAIN

POINT SCORED BY THE DELEGATION IS THAT THEY MOVED BUSH

AWAY FROM HIS INSISTENCE ON REMOVING ARAFAT, IN FAVOR

OF A FORMULA WHEREBY HE WOULD HAVE DIMINISHED POWER.

AT THE SAME TIME, THERE WOULD BE AN AMERICAN-ARAB

MANDATE ON PALESTINIAN REFORMS. IF ALL GOES WELL, THIS

WOULD NOT LEAD TO A RESOLUTION OF THE CONFLICT, BUT IT

MAY PUT SHARON IN A CORNER AND BRING ABOUT A NEW

SITUATION. REACHING A POLITICAL SOLUTION WILL ALWAYS

REMAIN SUBJECT TO THE ISRAELI PEOPLE ACCEPTING SUCH AN

OPTION, WHICH ALSO MAY BE POSSIBLE AFTER A LONG PERIOD

OF CALM. THERE IS, AT LEAST, AN ELEMENT THAT SERVES

THE PALESTINIANS IN VIEW OF THE GREAT IMBALANCE OF

STANDARDS, WHICH IS THAT THE ISRAELI MILITARY OPTION

WILL NOT RESOLVE THE SITUATION." GNEHM
