SUBJECT: A U.S. COMPANY HELPS THE RESERVE BANK FINANCE AND

MANAGE ITS GOLD SALES

CLASSIFIED BY ECON/COMML CHIEF MARK PROKOP FOR REASONS 1.5

B&D

REF: HARARE 4808

1. (C) ON AUGUST 23 AMBASSADOR AND ECONOFF MET WITH CARL

PETERSON, EXECUTIVE VICE PRESIDENT, GERALD METALS INC., (A

STAMFORD CONNECTICUT-BASED PRECIOUS METALS DEALER AND

MERCHANT BANK) TO DISCUSS CONDITIONS IN ZIMBABWE, THE

STATUS AND CONSEQUENCES OF THE ZIMBABWE DEMOCRACY ACT, AND

TO LEARN MORE OF GERALD METALS\' DEALINGS AND RELATIONSHIP

WITH ZIMBABWE\'S RESERVE BANK. THE MEETING WAS THE FIRST

TIME WE HAD HEARD OF THE RELATIONSHIP BETWEEN THE TWO, AND

THIS MESSAGE PROVIDES DETAILS ON THE JOINT HISTORY.

2. (C) ACCORDING TO PETERSON THE RELATIONSHIP BEGAN WHEN

THE RESERVE BANK CHOSE GERALD METALS TO PROVIDE PRE-EXPORT

FINANCING OF GOLD SALES IN 1984 WHEN ZIMBABWE HAD A NEED

FOR FOREIGN CURRENCY CAUSED BY DROUGHT CONDITIONS. IN

1988, THE U.S. FIRM HELPED THE RESERVE BANK SETUP A GOLD

JEWELRY MANUFACTURING FACILITY TO BOOST THE REVENUE

RECEIVED FROM GOLD SALES. AT ITS PEAK, WE WERE TOLD, THE

PLANT EMPLOYED 2,000, THOUGH THAT IS NOW DOWN TO ABOUT

1,200. LAST YEAR THE WORKSHOP PRODUCED ABOUT 70,000

OUNCES OF GOLD JEWELRY (ALL SOLD IN THE U.S. MARKET), OR

ABOUT 8 PERCENT OF 1999 GOLD PRODUCTION. THE BUSINESSMAN

TOLD US THAT THEIR INTRODUCTION OF AN ISRAELI MANAGER WHO

OVERSAW STARTUP AND PRODUCTION FOR THE FIRST EIGHT YEARS

OF THE JEWELRY OPERATION WAS THE IMPETUS BEHIND THE

OPENING OF AN ISRAELI EMBASSY HERE ABOUT FIVE YEARS AGO.

WHEN WE POINTED OUT THAT THIS WAS THE FIRST WE HAD HEARD

OF SUCH AN OPERATION, HE EXPLAINED THAT THE RESERVE BANK

WAS VERY PROTECTIVE AND SECRETIVE ABOUT IT, AS THEY

REALIZED THAT KNOWLEDGE OF THE OPERATION WOULD BRING

ATTENTION, AND LIKELY, DEMANDS FOR A PIECE OF THE ACTION.

REGARDING THE RELATIONSHIP IN GENERAL BETWEEN GERALD

METALS AND THE RESERVE BANK PETERSON NOTED THAT, "I HAVE

ALWAYS FOUND THE RESERVE BANK TO BE SCRUPULOUSLY HONEST

AND TRUSTWORTHY, AND THEY HAVE ALWAYS PAID AND HONORED

THEIR COMMITMENTS COMPLETELY AND ON TIME." HE POINTED TO

THE EARLY PAYBACK OF A 12 MONTH USD 150 MILLION FACILITY

AS EVIDENCE OF THIS.

3. (C) DESCRIBING THIS TRANSACTION, PETERSON TOLD US THAT

IT WAS GERALD METALS THAT ARRANGED THE USD 150 MILLION

FORWARD GOLD SALE IN NOVEMBER LAST YEAR (HE TERMED IT PRE-

EXPORT FINANCING), AND EVEN THOUGH THIS WAS A 12 MONTH

CREDIT, IT HAD ALL BEEN PAID BACK BY LAST MONTH. HE WAS

UNWILLING TO SHARE DETAILS ON WHY THIS OCCURRED, GIVEN

ZIMBABWE\'S DIRE NEED FOR HARD CURRENCY, STATING ONLY THAT

DELIVERY OF BULLION TO NEW YORK HAD BEEN SUFFICIENT TO PAY

OFF THE LOAN. HE WAS ALSO UNWILLING TO PROVIDE DETAILS OF

THE CREDIT, SUCH AS THE INTEREST RATE AND PARTICIPANTS,

SAYING ONLY THAT A NUMBER OF EUROPEAN AND ONE JAPANESE

BANK HAD PARTICIPATED IN THE LENDING SYNDICATE. HE DID

TELL US THAT ALL OF THESE BANKS HAD RECENTLY EXPRESSED NO

INTEREST IN PARTICIPATING IN ANY NEW ZIMBABWE CREDITS AT

THIS TIME.

4. (C) PETERSON SAID THAT HIS FIRM\'S LATEST TRANSACTION

WAS A $10 MILLION PRE-EXPORT LINE OF CREDIT TO BE USED BY

THE RESERVE BANK TO FUND THE FOREIGN CURRENCY ACCOUNTS NOW

ABLE TO BE HELD BY GOLD PRODUCERS (REFTEL). HE STATED

THAT THE RESERVE BANK WOULD LIKE THIS INCREASED IN OCTOBER

OR NOVEMBER BY ANOTHER USD 10 TO 15 MILLION, BUT TO DO SO

HE WOULD NEED THE PARTICIPATION OF COMMERCIAL BANKS. "WE

HANDLED THE $10 MILLION OURSELVES", HE STATED, "BUT THAT\'S

ABOUT AS BIG AS WE, AS A PRIVATE COMPANY, CAN GO." ECONOFF

SHARED OUR VIEW OF THE VERY SERIOUS BALANCE OF PAYMENTS

SITUATION FACING ZIMBABWE, AND HE ADMITTED THAT NEW CREDIT

EXTENSION BY COMMERCIAL BANKS WOULD GROW MORE DIFFICULT IF

NOT IMPOSSIBLE TO SECURE AS THE YEAR WORE ON.

NEVERTHELESS, HE WANTED TO MAINTAIN THE EXCELLENT, AND

PROFITABLE, WORKING RELATIONSHIP GERALD METALS HAD

DEVELOPED WITH THE RESERVE BANK. IN CLOSING WE OFFERED

OUR ASSISTANCE GOING FORWARD, AND OUR APPRECIATION FOR THE

INFORMATION PETERSON SHARED WITH US.
