SUBJECT: UNHCR/VIETNAM RELATIONS: "NEVER SO BAD"

1. (SBU) UNHCR REGIONAL REPRESENTATIVE JAHANSHAH ASSADI

BRIEFED CHARGE AND POL/C JUNE 10 ABOUT HIS MEETING EARLIER

THAT DAY WITH THE MFA\'S NEW DIRECTOR GENERAL OF THE CONSULAR

DEPARTMENT, BUI DINH DINH. ACCORDING TO ASSADI, DINH SPENT

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 02 HANOI 01502 101049Z

ALMOST A HALF AN HOUR READING TALKING POINTS, EXPRESSING

"DISAPPOINTMENT" THAT THE UNHCR HAD "ABANDONED" THE

TRIPARTITE AGREEMENT UNDER "UNDUE INFLUENCE AND PRESSURE"

FROM THE US. DINH TOLD ASSADI THAT RELATIONS BETWEEN

VIETNAM AND UNHCR HAD "NEVER BEEN AS BAD AS NOW." HOWEVER,

DINH SUBSEQUENTLY SAID THAT THE MFA NOW WANTED TO "REBUILD

RELATIONS" WITH UNHCR.

2. (SBU) ASSADI NOTED TO CHARGE THAT HE HAD URGED THE MFA

TO LOOK TOWARD THE FUTURE AND NOT DWELL ON THE PAST, WHILE

REMINDING DG DINH OF SOME OF THE UNHELPFUL GVN ACTIONS THAT

HAD PRECIPITATED THE COLLAPSE OF THE TRIPARTITE AGREEMENT

PROCESS. UNHCR IS NOW EAGER TO BEGIN DISCUSSIONS ON MICRO-

PROJECTS TO ASSIST THE 200+ MONTAGNARD RETURNEES (ALMOST ALL

OF WHOM WERE "SPONTANEOUS") AND THEIR LOCAL COMMUNITIES.

ASSADI SAID THAT DG DINH HAD AGREED; THESE TALKS MAY BEGIN

DURING THE WEEK OF JUNE 17. UNHCR HOPES TO BUILD ON ITS

SUCCESSFUL EXPERIENCE WITH MICRO-PROJECTS UNDER THE EARLIER

COMPREHENSIVE PLAN OF ACTION, DURING WHICH UNHCR SPENT USD

14.7 MILLION ON ABOUT 700 PROJECTS. ASSADI SAID THAT HE

BELIEVES UNHCR WILL BE ABLE TO HAVE SUFFICIENT FUNDING FOR

THIS NEW PROGRAM, WHICH WOULD NECESSARILY ENTAIL ADEQUATE

ACCESS TO THE CENTRAL HIGHLANDS BY UNHCR STAFF. HE SAID

THAT HE HAD NOT YET REQUESTED A TRIP TO THE HIGHLANDS FOR

HIMSELF, BELIEVING THAT THE TIME WAS NOT YET PROPITIOUS.

3. (SBU) ASSADI EXPRESSED CONCERN OVER THE CONTINUED

CLOSURE OF THE BORDER BY THE RCG, NOTING THAT UNHCR HAD

CREDIBLE REPORTS OF AT LEAST 250 PEOPLE HAVING BEEN TURNED

BACK IN RECENT MONTHS. HE WAS UNSURE WHETHER CAMBODIAN

OFFICIALS HAD ACTED ALONE OR WITH VIETNAMESE COOPERATION.

UNHCR STAFF REMAINS IN RATTANAKIRI AND MAKES REGULAR TRIPS

TO MONDOLKIRI AS WELL, HE ADDED. HE URGED THAT THE

INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY CONSIDER SOON HOW BEST TO CONVINCE

THE RCG AGAIN TO RESPECT THE RIGHT OF FIRST ASYLUM. HE

REQUESTED USG SUPPORT FOR THIS PRINCIPLE.
