1. (C) THE GOI IS INTERESTED IN CONTINUING BILATERAL

DISCUSSIONS ON MISSILE DEFENSE BUT WANTS FURTHER DISCUSSIONS

WITHIN NATO TO DEVELOP FURTHER A COMMON CONCEPTUAL APPROACH

TO THE PROGRAM, ACCORDING TO MINISTER CARLO TREZZA, MFA

COORDINATOR FOR INTERNATIONAL SECURITY AFFAIRS. NATO

MEMBERS, AND THE GOI INTERNALLY, NEED TO CONSIDER THE

STRATEGIC IMPLICATIONS OF SWITCHING FROM THEATER MISSILE

DEFENSE (TMD), WITH ITS FOCUS ON PROTECTING DEPLOYED TROOPS,

TO A BROADER MISSILE DEFENSE PROGRAM AIMED AT PROTECTING

POPULATIONS AND TERRITORIES, TREZZA TOLD POL-MIL COUNSELOR

DURING AN OCTOBER 17 MEETING.

2. (C) ITALIAN EMBASSY OFFICIALS IN WASHINGTON HAVE RECENTLY

HAD GENERAL DISCUSSIONS WITH PENTAGON REPRESENTATIVES ABOUT

POSSIBLE CONCRETE ITALIAN PARTICIPATION IN THE MD PROGRAM,

TREZZA SAID. BUDGET CONSTRAINTS ARE A REAL PROBLEM BUT THE

GOI VIEWS MD AS A DEFENSE CAPABILITY ISSUE, ACCORDING TO

TREZZA. BOTH THE MFA AND THE MOD ARE PRESSURING THE FINANCE

MINISTRY TO PRESERVE FUNDING FOR CAPABILITY PRIORITIES, HE

NOTED. MORE DETAILS FROM THE USG ABOUT THE PROGRAM WOULD

MAKE IT EASIER FOR ITALY TO IDENTIFY SPECIFICALLY HOW IT CAN

CONTRIBUTE, TREZZA ADDED. THE GOI WOULD WELCOME FURTHER

BILATERAL DISCUSSIONS, HE SAID, BUT HE EMPHASIZED THE

IMPORTANCE OF WORKING WITHIN NATO AS WELL.

3. (C) THE GOI SUPPORTS INCLUDING A REFERENCE TO MD IN THE

PRAGUE SUMMIT COMMUNIQUE AS A POLITICAL ENDORSEMENT OF THE

PROGRAM, AND AGREES IN PRINCIPLE TO EXTENDING THE NATO TMD

FEASIBILTY STUDY TO COVER POPULATIONS AND TERRITORIES,

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 ROME 05117 211158Z

ACCORDING TO GIOVANNI BRAUZZI, MFA NATO AFFAIRS OFFICE

DIRECTOR. BUT ITALY NEEDS TO CLOSELY EVALUATE THE

CONSEQUENCES OF SHIFTING FROM TMD TO MD BEFORE THE GOI CAN

FULLY SUPPORT THE PROPOSAL, HE SAID. TO DO THIS, BRAUZZI

SAID, IT WOULD BE HELPFUL TO HAVE THE SPECIFIC TERMS OF

REFERENCE OF THE EXPANDED FEASIBILITY STUDY.

4. (C) BOTH TREZZA AND BRAUZZI EXPRESSED ITALY\'S STRONG

INTEREST IN THE INDUSTRIAL COMPONENT OF THE MD PROGRAM.

BRAUZZI SAID ITALY WOULD LIKE TO BE A HIGHER LEVEL PARTNER

BUT ADDED THAT THAT WOULD DEPEND ON THE TYPE OF INDUSTRIAL

INTERACTION AVAILABLE. TREZZA POINTED OUT THAT ITALIAN AND

US COMPANIES ARE ALREADY COORDINATING (A REFERENCE TO THE

ALENIA-BOEING MOU SIGNED IN JULY) BUT SAID THERE IS NO FORMAL

POLICY AT THE POLITICAL LEVEL BECAUSE OF THE NEED FOR FURTHER

DISCUSSIONS WITHIN NATO ON THE IMPACT OF THE SHIFT FROM TMD

TO MD. AS AN EXAMPLE, HE NOTED SOME NATO MEMBERS HAD

EXPRESSED DOUBTS ABOUT THE THREAT ASSESSMENT PRESENTED AT THE

OCT. 15 EWG/SPC MEETING.

5. (C) COMMENT. THE MD BRIEFINGS ITALIAN OFFICIALS HAVE

RECEIVED HAVE GENERATED SOME ENTHUSIASM FOR ITALIAN

PARTICIPATION IN THE PROPOSED MD FRAMEWORK. ITALY IS

PARTICULARLY INTERESTED IN INDUSTRIAL COOPERATION AS THE

PROGRAM DEVELOPS. BILATERALLY, THE GOI WOULD LIKE MORE

INFORMATION ABOUT THE SPECIFICS OF THE PROGRAM IN ORDER TO

IDENTIFY WHERE AND HOW ITALY CAN CONTRIBUTE. A USG TEAM

TAILORED TO ADDRESS INDUSTRIAL COOPERATION AND THE IMPACT OF

EXPANDING FROM TMD TO A BROADER PROGRAM IN PARTICULAR WOULD

HELP THE GOI DEVELOP TARGET HOW THEY CAN CONTRIBUTE. NATO

DISCUSSIONS ELABORATING THE ALLIANCE\'S VISION FOR THE MD
