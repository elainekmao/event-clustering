ARCHBISHOP SAYS MILOSEVIC ARREST IS A "SIGN FOR FUTURE"

--------------------------------------------- ----------

2. IN AN APRIL 4 INTERVIEW WITH VATICAN NEWS AGENCY

"FIDES," BELGRADE\'S NEW ARCHBISHOP, STANISLAV HOCEVAR,

DEEMED THE ARREST OF FORMER PRESIDENT MILOSEVIC "A SIGN OF

HOPE FOR THE FUTURE" AND THAT "YUGOSLAVIA IS ON THE RIGHT

PATH." THE 55 YEAR-OLD HOCEVAR HAS BEEN ARCHBISHOP "IN

WAITING" FOR THE LAST 12 MONTHS AND ASSUMED FULL CONTROL FOR

THE CHURCH IN THE YUGOSLAV CAPITAL WHEN THE POPE ACCEPTED

ARCHBISHOP FRANC PERKO\'S RESIGNATION FOR HEALTH REASONS ON

MARCH 31. SPEAKING TO "FIDES" HOCEVAR SAID MILOSEVIC\'S

ARREST AND THE FUTURE IDENTIFICATION AND TRIAL OF GOVERNMENT

AND MILITARY OFFICIALS RESPONSIBLE FOR WAR CRIMES IN THE

BALKANS ARE ONLY PART OF THE PROCESS OF RECOVERY.

-------------------------------

NO PAPAL TRIP TO CYPRUS FOR NOW

-------------------------------

3. VATICAN SPOKESMAN, JOAQUIN NAVARRO-VALLS, ANNOUNCED

APRIL 3 THAT POPE JOHN PAUL II WILL NOT STOP IN CYPRUS

DURING HIS UP-COMING TRAVEL TO GREECE, SYRIA AND MALTA.

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 03 VATICA 01907 071610Z

NAVARRO-VALLS SAID THE POPE HAD RECEIVED AN INVITATION TO

VISIT FROM THE CYPRIOT PRESIDENT. VATICAN WATCHER THEN

SPECULATED THE POPE COULD MAKE A BRIEF STOP ENROUTE TO

GREECE. WHILE THE PONTIFF HOPES TO ACCEPT THE OFFER "ONE

DAY," NAVARRO-VALLS SAID IT WAS A "TECHNICAL IMPOSSIBILITY"

TO INCLUDE A STOP ON THE ISLAND AS PART OF THE POPE\'S MAY 04

- 09 TRAVEL ITINERARY.

---------------------------

FULL STEAM AHEAD FOR EASTER

---------------------------

4. THE VATICAN ANNOUNCED APRIL 4 THAT THE INCREASINGLY

FRAIL POPE JOHN PAUL II WILL PRESIDE OVER ALL OF THE VATICAN

CITY EASTER WEEK EVENTS. THE EASTER LITURGICAL CEREMONIES

ARE THE MOST DEMANDING IN THE VATICAN\'S OFFICIAL CALENDAR.

THE 80 YEAR-OLD PONTIFF HAS RESUMED HIS REGULAR SCHEDULE

AFTER THE RIGORS OF THE 2000 HOLY YEAR, INCLUDING MEETINGS

WITH GROUPS OF BISHOPS FROM AROUND THE WORLD AND VISITS TO

ROMAN PARISHES. (NOTE: THE EASTER WEEK EVENTS ARE OFFICIAL

VATICAN CEREMONIES THAT THE DIPLOMATIC CORPS IS EXPECTED TO

ATTEND. END NOTE). THE POPE IS EXPECTED TO TAKE SOME TIME

OFF AFTER EASTER AT HIS RESIDENCE IN THE HILLS OUTSIDE OF

ROME. MERANTE
