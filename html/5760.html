SUBJECT: REINFORCING THE NPT: DEMARCHE TO JORDAN

REF: SECSTATE 42535

Classified By: PolCouns Doug Silliman for reasons 1.5 (B)(D)

1. (C) PolCouns February 16 discussed ref points on North

Korea and the NPT with Ali al-Ayed, Personal Assistant to

Foreign Minister Marwan Muasher. Al-Ayed responded that,

while Jordan has not been directly involved in the North

Korea dispute, it hopes that the current confrontation can be

resolved peacefully. He noted that, as important as North

Korea is, Jordan feels more threatened by nuclear weapons

programs in its neighborhood and hopes the USG will hold

Israel to the same standard it is applying to North Korea.
