SUBJECT: Demarche on High-Level Steel Meeting

REF: State 252310

Econoff met with Tevfik Mengu, Acting Director General

for Agreements at the Foreign Trade Undersecretariat, on

December 10 to deliver reftel demarche on our objectives

for the December 18-19 OECD High Level Group on Steel.

Mengu offered no concrete response to reftel points, but

said that the Foreign Trade Undersecretariat would

consider U.S. views in preparing for this meeting.
