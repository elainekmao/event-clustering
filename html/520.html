SUMMARY

-------

1. (C) IN A MAY 18 CONVERSATION WITH UNPROFOR

OFFICIAL MIKAEL MAGNUSSON, MAGNUSSON SAID THAT WHILE

CROATIAN FORCES WERE HOLDING TO AGREEMENTS IN SECTOR

EAST, THE JNA APPEARED TO BE BENDING THE AGREED

CONFIDENTIAL

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 ZAGREB 00780 01 OF 03 191250Z

TIMETABLE. CROATIAN ARMY AUTHORITIES WERE UPSET BY

REPORTS OF AN EXTENSION GRANTED TO THE JNA BY

UNPROFOR TO WITHDRAW JNA HEAVY ARTILLERY FROM SECTOR

EAST. MEANWHILE, CROATIAN VICE PRESIDENT MILAN

RAMLJAK COMPLAINED BITTERLY TO UN GENERAL NAMBIAR

ABOUT THE ALLEGED EXPULSION OF 98 CIVILIANS FROM

SECTOR EAST BY SERB FORCES AFTER UNPROFOR TOOK OVER

RESPONSIBILITY FOR THE REGION. FROM THE CROATIAN

PERSPECTIVE, THESE EVENTS CONTINUE TO PUT INTO

QUESTION THE ABILITY OF UNPROFOR TO CARRY OUT ITS .

MISSION. END SUMMARY.

UNPROFOR COMMENTS ON SECTOR EAST

--------------------------------

2. (C) WE QUERIED UNPROFOR ZAGREB SENIOR LIAISON

OFFICER FOR CIVILIAN AFFAIRS MIKAEL MAGNUSSON

(PROTECT) MAY 18 ON THE STATUS OF UN PEACEKEEPING

OPERATIONS IN SECTOR EAST (OSIJEK, VINKOVCI, VUKOVAR,

ETC.). IN FRANK AND ADMITTEDLY UNOFFICIAL

COMMENTARY, MAGNUSSON TOLD US THAT OPERATIONS IN THAT

SECTOR WERE PROCEEDING "STICKINGLY". WHILE CROATIAN

FORCES WERE ACTING IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE TIMETABLE

FOR WITHDRAWAL OF THEIR FORCES FROM THE REGION, THE

JNA, EXPLAINED MAGNUSSON, WAS APPARENTLY TRYING TO

"BEND" THE TIMETABLE. WHILE THE REASONS FOR JNA

RELUCTANCE TO WITHDRAW WERE NOT CLEAR, MAGNUSSON\'S

UNDERSTANDING (THOUGH HE COULD NOT CONFIRM THIS) WAS

THAT THE JNA HAD RECEIVED UNPROFOR PERMISSION TO

EXTEND THE DEADLINE BY WHICH HEAVY ARTILLERY WAS TO

BE WITHDRAWN FROM THE UNPA. THE JNA IN SECTOR EAST

THAT UNPA BY 1600 ON MAY 20. THE ORIGINAL DEADLINE

3. (C) MAGNUSSON NOTED THE CEASEFIRE VIOLATIONS THAT

HAD TAKEN PLACE OVER THE LAST FEW DAYS, PARTICULARLY

AROUND OSIJEK. UNPROFOR PATROLS WERE COVERING ALL OF

SECTOR EAST, HE EXPLAINED, AND RANDOM PATROLS WERE

BEING RUN IN CRISIS AREAS OUTSIDE THE UNPA. SO FAR,

SAID MAGNUSSON, UN PEACEKEEPERS WERE "TAKING NAMES,

BUT NOT KICKING ASS." THEY HAD FILED COMPLAINTS WITH

THE JNA CONCERNING THE SLOWNESS OF ITS WITHDRAWAL.

CROATIAN REACTION TO THE EXTENSION

----------------------------------

CONFIDENTIAL

4. (U) IMMEDIATE CROATIAN REACTION TO THE

UNPROFOR-GRANTED EXTENSION CAME FROM THE DEPUTY

CROATIAN COMMANDER OF THE OSIJEK OPERATIONAL ZONE

BRANIMIR GLAVAS. GLAVAS TOLD REPORTERS THAT CROATIAN

FORCES WERE OPPOSED TO AGREEMENTS IN WHICH THEY WERE

NOT CONSULTED (NOTE: REPORTEDLY, THE CROATIAN ARMY

WAS NOT INCLUDED IN THE DECISION-MAKING PROCESS

REGARDING THE EXTENSION). GLAVAS ADDED THAT THE ARMY

WOULD DO WHAT ITS GOVERNMENT ASKED IT TO DO BUT THAT

IT WOULD NOT BE NAIVE. HE CONTINUED BY SAYING THAT

"IF BY PEACEFUL MEANS, CROATIAN TERRITORY CANNOT BE

LIBERATED, THEN WE WILL THANK UNPROFOR AND SAY DEAR

SIRS, NOW IT IS FOR US TO SOLVE THIS ISSUE." GLAVAS

CONCLUDED THAT THROUGH ACTIONS SUCH AS THE EXTENSION,

UNPROFOR WAS LOSING ITS POLITICAL CREDIBILITY.

QUESTION OF A CRIME COMMITTED IN THE SECTOR WHICH IS

UNDER YOUR CONTROL. ACCORDING TO PARAGRAPH 12 OF

SECURITY COUNCIL RESOLUTION NO. 752, YOU TOOK OVER

RESPONSIBILITY FOR THIS SECTOR ON MAY 15, 1992 AND

THEREFORE YOU ARE DIRECTLY RESPONSIBLE FOR THE EXILE

AND SUFFERING OF THESE UNFORTUNATE PERSONS. THEY

EXPECTED YOUR PROTECTION, INSTEAD THEY WERE EXPOSED

TO THE RUDEST VIOLENCE.

-- IT IS DIFFICULT FOR ME TO BELIEVE THAT THE EXILE

WAS DONE WITH THE PROTECTION AND KNOWLEDGE OF

UNPROFOR. UNDOUBTEDLY, THIS IS A CASE OF FAILURE IN

THE EXECUTION OF THE PEACE OPERATION IN SECTOR "E"

-- UNPROFOR UNITS MUST URGENTLY ORGANIZE THE RETURN

COMMENT

-------

6. (C) AS WE NOTED IN REFTEL, CROATIA IS ANXIOUSLY

WATCHING THE RESULTS OF UNPROFOR\'S FIRST OPERATIONAL

SECTOR. AFTER THE WEEKEND SHELLING OF OSIJEK, THE

DECISION TO EXTEND THE JNA\'S TIMETABLE FOR WITHDRAWAL

AND THE ALLEGED SERBIAN EXPULSION OF CIVILIANS FROM

MIKLUSEVCI HAS FURTHER DEGRADED RESPECT FOR

UNPROFOR\'S CAPABILITIES IN THE CROATIAN GOVERNMENT,

ARMY AND AMONG THE POPULATION AS A WHOLE. WHILE

CROATIAN EXPECTATIONS MAY BE TOO HIGH, THEIR

PERCEPTIONS OF UNPROFOR FAIRNESS AND ABILITY TO DO

THE JOB WILL BE CRITICAL TO SUCCESS IN THE REMAINING

SECTORS DUE TO COME UNDER UN CONTROL. END COMMENT.
