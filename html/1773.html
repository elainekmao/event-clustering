SUBJECT: DONOR/GOVERNMENT DISCUSSION OF VIETNAM\'S 10 YEAR SOCIO-ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT STRATEGY

1. (SBU) SUMMARY: ON JUNE 14, KICKING OFF A SERIES OF

DONOR-GOVERNMENT CONSULTATIONS, UNDP SPONSORED A `HIGH-

LEVEL\' CONFERENCE TO DISCUSS ONE OF THE CENTRAL DOCUMENTS

APPROVED BY VIETNAM\'S COMMUNIST PARTY DURING THE NINTH PARTY

CONGRESS CONCLUDED IN APRIL. THE DOCUMENT, `THE 10 YEAR

SOCIO-ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT STRATEGY\', IN MANY WAYS MORE A

POLITICAL STATEMENT THAN A MODERN ECONOMIC STRATEGY,

NONETHELESS HELPS SET THE STAGE FOR THE COMING PERIOD.

UNFORTUNATELY, WHILE IT ACKNOWLEDGES THE ROLE OF THE PRIVATE

SECTOR - BOTH DOMESTIC AND FOREIGN-INVESTED - IT ALSO

PANDERS TO THE STATE SECTOR. THE PAPER RATHER PERFUNCTORILY

NOTES THE MANY COMMITMENTS TO INTERNATIONAL INTEGRATION THE

GVN HAS IN RECENT YEARS SIGNED ONTO, INCLUDING AFTA AND THE

U.S.-VIETNAM BILATERAL TRADE AGREEMENT. IT ALSO MENTIONS

THE GOAL OF `EVENTUAL\' ACCESSION TO THE WTO. DONORS

APPRECIATED THE OPPORTUNITY TO DISCUSS THE DOCUMENT AND

PROVIDE THE GOVERNMENT WITH SUGGESTIONS FOR CARRYING OUT THE

MEANINGFUL ELEMENTS OF THE STRATEGY.

2. (SBU) SUMMARY CONT\'D: AMBASSADOR PETERSON CRITICIZED THE

VESTIGIAL PLANNING NATURE INHERENT IN THE DOCUMENT AND

HIGHLIGHTED THE IMPORTANCE OF THE PRIVATE SECTOR IN JOB

CREATION. HE ALSO URGED THE GVN TO REASSESS THE CONCEPT

ARTICULATED IN THE STRATEGY OF THE STATE SECTOR `LEADING\' OR

`DOMINATING\' A CONSIDERABLE NUMBER OF `STRATEGIC\'

INDUSTRIES. HIS COMMENTS TURNED THE STAID CONFERENCE INTO A

SIGNIFICANT DEBATE REGARDING VIETNAM\'S FUTURE. OTHER

COMMENTS INCLUDED A SURPRISE INTERVENTION BY THE CHINESE

AMBASSADOR ABOUT THE NEED FOR GREATER COMPETITION IN THE

VIETNAMESE ECONOMY, INCLUDING ROBUST PARTICIPATION OF THE

PRIVATE SECTOR IN TELECOMMUNICATIONS. SEVERAL DONORS

HIGHLIGHTED THE NEED FOR DEVELOPING DEEPER LEGAL

INSTITUTIONS AND PROMOTING TRANSPARENCY AND PROCESS IN

DEVELOPING LAWS AND REGULATIONS, AS WELL AS THE PERVASIVE

NEED FOR PUBLIC ADMINISTRATION REFORM. THE U.S. DELEGATION

ALSO UNDERSCORED THE TRANSPARENCY REQUIREMENTS CONTAINED IN

THE U.S.-VIETNAM BTA (NOW BEFORE CONGRESS) AND URGED

ENFORCEMENT OF LAWS WHERE LAWS EXISTED AS IN THE AREA OF

INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY RIGHTS PROTECTION. THE UNDP LEGAL

REFORM ADVISER SUGGESTED THE GVN CONSIDER AMENDING ITS

CONSTITUTION TO CREATE A `PROFESSIONAL\' NATIONAL LEGISLATURE

IN VIEW OF VIETNAM\'S NEED TO INCREASE THE NUMBER AND QUALITY

OF NATIONAL LAWS IN COMING YEARS. END SUMMARY.

3. (SBU) IN AN `OFF-THE-RECORD\' MEETING ON JUNE 14, DONORS

AND THE GOVERNMENT MET IN HANOI TO DISCUSS VIETNAM\'S 10 YEAR

SOCIO-ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT STRATEGY (2001-2010), THE FIVE-

YEAR SOCIO-ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT PLAN (2001-2005), AND A

STRATEGY FOR PUBLIC ADMINISTRATIVE REFORM FOR THE PERIOD

2001-2010. (COPIES OF THESE DOCUMENTS WILL BE POUCHED TO

EAP/BCLTV.) FIRST DEPUTY PRIME MINISTER NGUYEN TAN DUNG AND

MINISTER OF PLANNING AND INVESTMENT TRAN XUAN GIA CHAIRED

THE SESSIONS FOR THE GOVERNMENT AND MANY OTHER SENIOR

VIETNAMESE ECONOMIC OFFICIALS ALSO PARTICIPATED.
