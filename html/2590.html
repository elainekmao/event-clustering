1. (C) SUMMARY. THE EU\'S POSITION ON AN ENHANCED IDA GRANT

PROGRAM IS CLEAR: SUCH A PROGRAM MUST BE VERY LIMITED IN

SCOPE, TARGETED FOR BASIC NEEDS (E.G. HEALTH AND EDUCATION),

AND TESTED OVER SEVERAL YEARS. LORENZO BINI SMAGHI, DIRECTOR

GENERAL FOR INTERNATIONAL FINANCIAL RELATIONS AT ITALY\'S

FINANCE MINISTRY, TOLD ECMIN THAT THE DIVERGENT VIEWS BETWEEN

THE U.S. AND EUROPE MAY NOT BE RECONCILABLE. FURTHER U.S.

INSISTENCE MAY PROMPT SOME MEMBER STATES TO RETRACT THEIR

SUPPORT FOR A BEEFED UP GRANT PROGRAM, AT ANY LEVEL, HE

WARNED. END SUMMARY.

2. (C) POST MET WITH BINI SMAGHI, ITALY\'S FINANCIAL SHERPA,

WHO OVERSEES ALL INTERNATIONAL FINANCIAL POLICY, TO DISCUSS

IDA AND OTHER FINANCIAL ISSUES. TREASURY U/S TAYLOR AND HE

HAVE BEEN IN FREQUENT CONTACT ON IDA GRANTS AND OTHER ISSUES.

BINI SMAGHI SAID THE EU, WHICH CONTRIBUTES NEARLY 50 PERCENT

TO IDA, HAS MET THE UNITED STATES PART WAY ON THE ISSUE OF

IDA GRANTS. THERE IS NO WAY THE U.S.- DESIRED TARGET LEVELS

FOR IDA GRANTS CAN BE REACHED IN SUCH A SHORT TIME, BINI

SMAGHI ARGUED. NO CERTAINTY EXISTS THAT AN ENHANCED PROGRAM,

AT THE LEVELS IDENTIFIED BY THE USG, WOULD BE EFFECTIVE,

ESPECIALLY IN THE ABSENCE OF STRONG, WELL-DEFINED AND

ENFORCED CONDITIONALITY. PREVIOUS LENDING POLICIES SHOULD

NOT BE ABANDONED OVERNIGHT, HE CONTENDED.

3. (C) THE EU\'S POSITION HAS NOT BEEN STATIC. "WE ARE NOW

WILLING TO CONSIDER FOR CERTAIN GROUPS OF COUNTRIES --

PERHAPS FOR THOSE WITH A PER CAPITA OF US ONE DOLLAR A DAY OR

LESS -- A GRANT PROGRAM OF 50 PERCENT, TARGETED FOR HEALTH

AND EDUCATION. IN POST-CONFLICT AND NATURAL DISASTER CASES, A

GRANT PROGRAM OF 100 PERCENT COULD EVEN BE CONSIDERED,"

STATED BINI SMAGHI. THIS SHOULD BE THE FRAMEWORK FOR AN

ENHANCED IDA GRANT PROGRAM. IF THE PROGRAM IS SUCCESSFUL, BY

MEASURES STILL TO BE DEFINED, THE LEVEL OF GRANTS COULD BE

INCREASED AT A FUTURE DATE, TO MIRROR THE LEVELS THE USG NOW

PROPOSES. BINI SMAGHI BELIEVES A MINIMUM OF THREE YEARS IS

REQUIRED TO TEST THE EFFECTIVENESS OF A RECONSTITUTED GRANT

PROGRAM.

4. (C) RATHER THAN INSISTING ON GREATER MOVEMENT BY EUROPEAN

GOVERNMENTS, THE USG SHOULD RECOGNIZE AND ACCEPT THE PROGRESS

MADE WITH EU MEMBER STATES. THE USG SHOULD NOT IGNORE THE

CONTENTIOUSNESS OF THIS ISSUE, AND THE SERIOUS OPPOSITION

FROM SEVERAL COUNTRIES, ESPECIALLY IN SCANDINAVIA, TO ANY

INCREASE IN GRANTS, BINI SMAGHI COMMENTED. THE USG\'S

RELENTLESS PRESSURE ALMOST CAUSED NORWAY, SWEDEN AND A FEW

OTHERS TO RETRACT THEIR SUPPORT FOR EVEN A LIMITED, TARGETED

50 PERCENT GRANT PROGRAM. BINI SMAGHI ASSERTED THAT IT WAS

ONLY BECAUSE ITALY, AS A G7 MEMBER, EXERTED PRESSURE ON

SEVERAL GOVERNMENTS THAT EU CONSENSUS KEPT FROM BREAKING

DOWN.

5. (C) THERE\'S A PERCEPTION AMONG EU MEMBERS THAT THE USG

DOES NOT UNDERSTAND THEIR ASSISTANCE PROGRAMS, BINI SMAGHI

REMARKED. GRANTS ARE THE PREFERRED MECHANISM FOR BILATERAL

ASSISTANCE PROGRAMS, AND EU GOVERNMENT CONTRIBUTIONS TO THE

UNDP. "SO, CLEARLY, WE ARE NOT OPPOSED TO GRANTS IN

PRINCIPLE," HE ARGUED. FOR EU GOVERNMENTS, HOWEVER, IT IS

IMPORTANT TO MAINTAIN THE APPROPRIATE DIVISION OF LABOR

BETWEEN THE WORLD BANK AND THE UNITED NATIONS, BETWEEN THE

INTERNATIONAL FINANCIAL INSTITUTIONS AND BILATERAL ASSISTANCE

PROGRAMS. MOREOVER, THE WORLD BANK DOES NOT HAVE THE

NECESSARY EXPERTISE TO ESTABLISH AND MONITOR THE IMPOSITION

OF EFFECTIVE CONDITIONALITY. (NOTE: MOST OF ITALY\'S BILATERAL

ASSISTANCE ALSO IS IN GRANT FORM. ITALY HAS, HOWEVER, A LOW

LEVEL OF OFFICIAL DEVELOPMENT ASSISTANCE, JUST .14 PERCENT OF

GDP IN 2001. THE GOI HAS MADE SOME NOISE ABOUT UPPING THIS

TO .7 PERCENT OF GDP BY 2006, BUT NO ROAD MAP TO THAT TARGET

YET EXISTS. END NOTE.)

6. (C) COMMENT: BINI SMAGHI IS CLEARLY PESSIMISTIC THAT THE

IMPASSE BETWEEN THE USG AND THE EU WILL BE BROKEN IF WE

CONTINUE HARD OVER ON OUR WAY. HE WAS WILLING TO HEAR OUR

ARGUMENTS, BUT DID NOT DEVIATE FROM THE EU COMMON POSITION.

ITALY WHOLEHEARTEDLY SHARES THE EU CONSENSUS AND A PHASED

APPROACH TO INCREASING THE PROPORTION OF GRANTS. THEY WILL

USE THEIR INFLUENCE, AS THEY HAVE ALREADY DEMONSTRATED, TO

KEEP THE EU CONSENSUS AT 50 PERCENT, WITH CONDITIONS FOR

CERTAIN COUNTRIES. THEY HAVE NO INTEREST IN BROKERING

ANYTHING BEYOND THIS. END COMMENT.
