SUBJECT: GOI OFFICIAL ON STRENGTH OF LTTE

REF: NEW DELHI 102

1. CONFIDENTIAL ENTIRE TEXT.

2. REFTEL (MFA UNDERSECRETARY FOR SRI LANKA\'S COM-

MENTS ON LATEST IPKF TROOP WITHDRAWAL) PRESENTS AN

INTERESTING SNAPSHOT OF CURRENT SOUTH BLOCK THINKING

ON SITUATION IN NORTHEASTERN PROVINCE.

3. ON THE ROLE OF LTTE, HOWEVER, THE WORKING-LEVEL

INDIAN HIGH COMMISSION OFFICIAL WHO DEALS MOST CLOSELY

WITH ALL ASPECTS OF INDIA\'S ROLE IN NORTHEASTERN

PROVINCE, DR. S. JAISHANKAR, PRESENTS A SOMEWHAT MORE

SOBER VIEW OF CURRENT STRENGTH OF THE LTTE. HE

SAYS THAT THE LTTE HAS BEEN OUTMANEUVERED IN THE

EAST AND IS NO LONGER OF MUCH IMPORTANCE THERE. BUT

HE TOLD POLCOUNS JAN 6 THAT THERE ARE PROBABLY STILL

2000-3000 TIGERS IN THE OLD NORTHERN PROVINCE, IN

WANNI AREA SOUTH OF ELEPHANT PASS, AND THAT THEY CAN

CONTINUE TO ELUDE IPKF. JAISHANKAR SAID THAT A STEADY

(ALBEIT SMALLER THAN PRE-JULY 1987) STREAM OF WEAPONS

STILL COME ACROSS THE PALK STRAIT TO THE TIGERS,

AND DENIED THAT THE SECURITY OFFICIALS IN TAMIL NADU

WERE CAPABLE OF MORE THAN MARGINALLY AFFECTING THIS

TRAFFIC, WHICH HE POINTED OUT, DREW ON TIES BETWEEN

FISHING/SMUGGLING COMMUNITIES WHICH DATE BACK A

HUNDRED YEARS. HE EXPRESSED SOME CONCERN ABOUT WHAT

WOULD HAPPEN TO SUPPORT FOR THE LTTE IN TAMIL NADU IF

THE DMK WERE TO WIN IN THE JANUARY 21 ELECTION.

4. THE INDIANS SEE THE FEB 15 GENERAL ELECTION HERE

AS A REAL TEST OF STRENGTH IN WHAT USED TO BE NORTHERN

PROVINCE, PARTICULARLY IN JAFFNA PENINSULA WHERE THE

LTTE CONTINUES TO EXERT SOME POLITICAL FORCE EVEN

THOUGH ABLE TO DO ALMOST NOTHING MILITARILY. JAISHANKAR

SAID THAT IPKF WILL GO ALL OUT TO HELP ENSURE A BIG

TURNOUT IN THAT ELECTION SINCE THE INDIANS EXPECT THE

LTTE TO CONTINUE TO SUPPORT A BOYCOTT OF THE POLLS.

HE IMPLIED THAT THE GOI WOULD VIEW A 65 PER CENT TURN-

OUT IN JAFFNA PENINSULA AS A REAL VICTORY FOR HIS SIDE

BUT REFUSED TO SPECULATE WHAT WOULD HAPPEN IF THE

TURNOUT WERE LOW.
