SUBJECT: LTTE ATTACKS SHIP CHARTERED BY US

COMPANY

REP: COLOMBO 2714

1. (U) CLASSIFIED BY: MICHAEL OWEN, ECONOMIC

COUNSELOR. REASON 1.5 (D)

2. (C) INITIAL REPORTS INDICATE THAT AT

APPROXIMATELY 0100 LOCAL TIME SEPTEMBER 9, THE

LIBERATION TIGERS OF TAMIL EELAM (LTTE)

ATTACKED THE MERCHANT VESSEL "CORDIALITY"

WHICH WAS ANCHORED OFF THE NORTHEAST COAST OF

SRI LANKA NEAR THE TOWN OF PULMODDAI,

APPROXIMATELY 30 MILES NORTH OF TRINCOMALEE.

UP TO 20 PEOPLE HAVE BEEN REPORTED KILLED,

WOUNDED OR MISSING IN THE ATTACK, INCLUDING

LOCAL WORKERS, FOREIGN CREWMEN (POSSIBLY

CHINESE), AND ARMED FORCES PERSONNEL GUARDING

THE SHIP. THERE WERE NO REPORTS OF AMCITS ON

BOARD. BY LATE MORNING OF SEPTEMBER 9, THE

SHIP\'S ENGINE ROOM WAS REPORTEDLY ON FIRE AND

THE SHIP IN DANGER OF SINKING. SRI LANKAN

NAVY PERSONNEL WERE ATTEMPTING TO EXTINGUISH

THE FIRE.

3. (C) THE M/V CORDIALITY IS REPORTEDLY

OWNED BY THE CHINA OCEAN SHIPPING COMPANY, A

CHINESE STATE-OWNED ENTERPRISE; IT WAS

REGISTERED IN PANAMA AND FLYING A PANAMANIAN

FLAG. THE SHIP WAS UNDER CHARTER BY THE U.S.

COMPANY "ACI CHEMICALS, INC." OF GREAT NECK,

NEW YORK, TO TRANSPORT 30,000 TONS OF

ILMENITE, A MINERAL SAND, FROM SRI LANKA TO

ROTTERDAM AND THEN, POSSIBLY, TO THE UNITED

STATES.

4. (C) COMMENT: THIS IS THE THIRD INCIDENT

INVOLVING CIVILIAN SHIPPING SINCE JULY 1 (SEE

REFTEL), BUT THE FIRST OCCASION WHEN A SHIP

SAILING UNDER U.S. CHARTER HAS BEEN ATTACKED.

IT IS DOUBTFUL THAT THE LTTE WOULD HAVE BEEN

AWARE THAT THERE WERE U.S. INTERESTS AT STAKE

WHEN ITS CADRES ATTACKED THE "CORDIALITY".

HOWEVER, IT HAS BECOME INCREASINGLY CLEAR THAT

THE LTTE IS WILLING TO ATTACK FOREIGN

INTERESTS IN ITS EFFORTS TO INTERDICT SHIPPING

TO THE NORTH. END COMMENT.
