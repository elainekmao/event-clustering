SUBJECT: NIGERIA CONTINUES TO COOPERATE IN EFFORT TO

IDENTIFY TERRORISTS\' ACCOUNTS

REF: STATE 44893

EconOff delivered Reftel information March 14 to Ernest Ebi,

Deputy Governor for International Operations at the Central

Bank of Nigeria. Ebi agreed to release the list of two (2)

entities in a new Call Circular, which will require all

Nigerian banks to identify accounts associated with these

terrorist entities. Ebi assured EconOff of Nigeria\'s full

and unwavering support for the U.S. global campaign against

terrorism.
