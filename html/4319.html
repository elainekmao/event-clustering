SUMMARY

1. (C) POPE JOHN PAUL II HAS SPOKEN OUT FORCEFULLY AND

CONSISTENTLY AGAINST ANTI-SEMITISM IN EUROPE AND AGAINST

SPECIFIC ANTI-SEMITIC ACTS. HE ACKNOWLEDGES THE CATHOLIC

CHURCH\'S SPECIAL RESPONSIBILITY TO COMBAT ANTI-SEMITISM -- IN

PART BASED ON THE CHURCH\'S SELF-ACKNOWLEDGED HISTORICAL

&SIN8 OF PROMOTING ANTI-JUDAISM (RELIGIOUS DISCRIMINATION)

IN EUROPE. THROUGHOUT HIS PONTIFICATE, THE POPE HAS SOUGHT TO

TURN THIS AWARENESS OF PAST ERRORS INTO A RESOLVE TO OVERCOME

THIS HISTORICAL ANTI-JUDAISM AND TO COMBAT EUROPEAN

ANTI-SEMITISM (ETHNIC DISCRIMINATION). TO THIS END, THE POPE

HAS RADICALLY TRANSFORMED THE HOLY SEE\'S RELATIONS WITH JEWS,

REDEFINING THE JEWISH RELIGION AS INTRINSIC TO CHRISTIANITY

AND PART OF A COMMON HERITAGE, WHILE ACTIVELY DEEPENING THE

CHURCH\'S DIALOGUE WITH JEWS. BUILDING ON THE SECOND VATICAN

COUNCIL\'S REAPPRAISAL OF CATHOLICISM\'S RELATIONSHIP TO

JUDAISM, THE HOLY SEE HAS TAKEN SYSTEMIC MEASURES TO ADDRESS

ITS ROLE IN HISTORICAL ANTI-SEMITISM AND TO CHANGE ITS

PRACTICE. ASIDE FROM PUBLIC AND DOCTRINAL STATEMENTS, IT

ACTIVELY PROMOTES AN AWARENESS OF THE ERROR OF ANTI-JUDAISM

AND ANTI-SEMITISM IN ITS MANY CATHOLIC EDUCATION PROGRAMS.

THESE TENETS ARE ALSO PROMOTED BY BISHOPS\' CONFERENCES IN

EUROPEAN COUNTRIES AND ARE PREACHED REGULARLY THROUGHOUT THE

CONTINENT. CATHOLIC LAY ORGANIZATIONS SUCH AS THE

SANT\'EGIDIO COMMUNITY ENGAGE IN FREQUENT DIALOGUE AND

EDUCATIONAL OUTREACH, WITH THE FULL SUPPORT OF THE HOLY SEE.

END SUMMARY.

JPII: AN ACTIVIST AGAINST ANTI-SEMITISM

----------------------------------------

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 VATICA 05545 01 OF 04 141510Z

2. (U) JOHN PAUL II\'S EFFORTS TO COMBAT ANTI-SEMITISM STEM IN

PART FROM HIS PERSONAL EXPERIENCES GROWING UP IN A TOWN WITH

A LARGE JEWISH POPULATION, DEFENDING JEWISH FRIENDS FROM

ANTI-SEMITIC ATTACKS, AND WITNESSING HIS FELLOW COUNTRYMEN

AND WOMEN BEING SHIPPED TO NAZI CONCENTRATION CAMPS. FROM

THE POPE\'S EARLY STUDIES HE CAME TO THE VIEW THAT

ANTI-SEMITES WERE ANTI-CHRISTIAN. SUBSEQUENTLY, HE DEVELOPED

A STRONG PERSONAL CONVICTION OF THE INHERENT DIGNITY OF ALL

HUMAN BEINGS; AND THREATS TO THIS DIGNITY HAVE ALMOST BECOME

PERSONAL "CAUSES" FOR THIS POPE. THE POPE HAS ASSERTED THAT

THE CHURCH MUST ATONE FOR THE HISTORICAL EXISTENCE OF

ANTI-JUDAISM IN CHRISTIAN EUROPE THAT AT LEAST IN PART

CREATED A FOUNDATION FOR THE RACIAL ANTI-SEMITISM OF THE

TWENTIETH CENTURY.

3. (U) IT IS THEREFORE NO COINCIDENCE THAT UNDER JOHN PAUL II

THE VATICAN HAS UNDERTAKEN NUMEROUS SYMBOLIC AND PRACTICAL

STEPS TO BUILD ON VATICAN II REFORMS AND REACH OUT TO JEWS --

MOST DRAMATICALLY WHEN THE POPE VISITED ROME\'S SYNAGOGUE IN

1986, AND JERUSALEM\'S WAILING WALL IN 2000. IN FACT, WHEN

POPE JOHN PAUL II STEPPED INTO ROME\'S SYNAGOGUE IN APRIL

1986, HE WAS THE FIRST POPE IN 1,900 YEARS TO TAKE THAT STEP.

THROUGHOUT HIS PONTIFICATE, HE HAS MERGED SUCH DRAMATIC,

SYMBOLIC STEPS, SUCH AS HIS VISIT TO AUSCHWITZ IN POLAND,

WITH A NEW CONCEPTUAL APPROACH TO CATHOLIC-JEWISH RELATIONS

EMBODIED IN AN ARRAY OF STATEMENTS AND EXHORTATIONS. THESE

STATEMENTS, WHICH BEGAN WITH THE SECOND VATICAN COUNCIL\'S

NOSTRA AETATE IN 1965 AND INCLUDE "WE REMEMBER: A REFLECTION

ON THE SHOAH," HAVE EMPHASIZED THE CHURCH\'S REJECTION OF ALL

FORMS OF PERSECUTION, ITS SHARED SPIRITUAL HERITAGE WITH

JUDAISM, AND A RECOGNITION OF AND REPENTANCE FOR PAST ERRORS.

WHAT THE HOLY SEE IS DOING

--------------------------

4. (U) THE CHURCH HAS ORGANIZED ITSELF SINCE VATICAN II TO

ENGAGE IN AN OPEN DIALOGUE WITH OTHER FAITHS, INCLUDING THE

ORTHODOX, BUT IN PARTICULAR, WITH THE JEWISH FAITH.

REFLECTING THE POPE\'S COMMITMENT, HE HAS PUT SOME OF HIS BEST

PEOPLE ON THIS TASK. GERMAN CARDINAL WALTER KASPER, ONE OF

THE VATICAN\'S MOST OPEN AND PERCEPTIVE INTERLOCUTORS, FOR

EXAMPLE, HEADS THE PONTIFICAL COUNCIL FOR CHRISTIAN UNITY,

WHICH INCLUDES RESPONSIBILITY FOR DIALOGUE WITH JEWS, AND IS

CHARGED WITH FOSTERING A BETTER MUTUAL UNDERSTANDING OF BOTH

RELIGIOUS TRADITIONS. KASPER SAID OCTOBER 18, IN REFERENCE

TO OPENING THE HOLOCAUST-ERA VATICAN ARCHIVES, THAT THE

CHURCH SHOULD NOT BE AFRAID TO CONFRONT ITS PAST. MORE

RECENTLY, ON NOVEMBER 6 HE OFFERED A THREE-POINT ACTION PLAN

FOR THE ON-GOING FIGHT AGAINST ANTI-SEMITISM FOCUSING ON

REVISING RELIGIOUS AND EDUCATIONAL TEXTS, INCREASING PERSONAL

ENCOUNTERS WITH JEWISH PEOPLE, AND STUDYING JEWISH HISTORY.

5. (U) BEYOND KASPER\'S DIALOGUE, THE HOLY SEE IS HIGHLY

ATTUNED TO ANY INCIDENT THAT MIGHT SMACK OF RESURGENT

ANTI-SEMITISM. FOR EXAMPLE, THE POPE QUICKLY SENT A MESSAGE

WHEN IT SEEMED JEWISH GRAVES IN ROME\'S VERANO CEMETERY HAD

BEEN DESECRATED EARLIER THIS YEAR (REF A). THE POPE\'S

LEADERSHIP IN THIS REGARD IS REFLECTED IN VIRTUALLY ALL OF

THE EUROPEAN CATHOLIC BISHOPS\' CONFERENCES, WHICH HAVE EITHER

ISSUED DOCUMENTS OR MADE STATEMENTS IN THE MEDIA CONDEMNING

ANTI-SEMITISM GENERALLY AND SPECIFIC ANTI-SEMITIC ACTS.

EUROPEAN BISHOPS CONFERENCES WILL CONTINUE TO SPEAK OUT

AGAINST ANTI-SEMITIC ATTACKS, AS THE FRENCH BISHOPS DID WHEN

SYNAGOGUES IN FRANCE WERE DESECRATED IN APRIL THIS YEAR.

6. (U) AT THE OSCE, VATICAN FOREIGN POLICY ACTIVELY

SUPPORTS AND PROMOTES RELIGIOUS FREEDOM AND TOLERANCE IN

EUROPE. WHILE DEFENDING ITS OWN INTERESTS AND POSITION, IT

EMBRACES THE CAUSES OF ALL OTHER RELIGIONS FOR MUTUAL

BENEFIT. THE HOLY SEE\'S REPRESENTATIVE IS WORKING WITH US TO

ENSURE RELIGIOUS DISCRIMINATION OR ANTI-SEMITISM ARE

CONDEMNED BY THE OSCE, AND THAT THE ORGANIZATION PUTS

PRESSURE ON OFFENDING GOVERNMENTS TO AMEND LEGISLATION AND

PRACTICES.

7. (U) THE RECENT ESTABLISHMENT OF THE INSTITUTE FOR

INTER-RELIGIOUS STUDIES AT THE PONTIFICAL GREGORIAN

UNIVERSITY AND THE OPENING OF THE LIBRARY FOR STUDIES IN

JUDAISM RUN BY THE SISTERS OF SION REFLECTS THE CATHOLIC

CHURCH\'S CONTINUING COMMITMENT TO PROMOTING UNDERSTANDING OF

NON-CHRISTIAN RELIGIONS, ESPECIALLY JUDAISM. WE EXPECT MORE

CATHOLIC INSTITUTES OF HIGHER LEARNING TO ESTABLISH PROGRAMS

TO STUDY JUDAISM IN THE YEARS AHEAD. EDUCATION AT ALL LEVELS

WILL CONTINUE TO BE A KEY COMPONENT OF THE HOLY SEE\'S EFFORTS

TO COUNTER ANTI-SEMITISM. IN 1985, THE HOLY SEE RELEASED

"NOTES ON THE CORRECT WAY TO PRESENT THE JEWS AND JUDAISM IN

PREACHING AND CATECHESIS," A PRIMER DESIGNED TO PUT AN

DEFINITIVE END TO TEACHING OF JEWISH CULPABILITY IN CHRIST\'S

DEATH. CENTURIES OF MISUNDERSTANDING WILL NEVERTHELESS TAKE

TIME TO CORRECT, BUT THE HOLY SEE\'S EDUCATIONAL EFFORTS ARE

CONTRIBUTING TO THAT PROCESS.

8. (SBU) BEYOND THE VATICAN WALLS BUT WORKING CLOSELY WITH

AND CONFLICT RESOLUTION AROUND THE WORLD) HAS MAINTAINED A

TWENTY-YEAR DIALOGUE WITH JUDAISM. ON THE OCTOBER 16

ANNIVERSARY OF THE DEPORTATION OF ROME\'S JEWS TO NAZI

EXTERMINATION CAMPS, SANT\'EGIDIO FOUNDER ANDREA RICCARDI

EMPHASIZED THAT JEWS ARE AN ESSENTIAL COMPONENT OF ROME\'S

SOLIDARITY WITH THE JEWS AND NEVER AGAIN TO ABANDON THEM.

RICCARDI, A RESPECTED HISTORIAN, MENTIONED A NUMBER OF

ANTI-SEMITIC INCIDENTS THAT HAVE PEPPERED ROME\'S RECENT PAST.

RICCARDI ALSO REPRESENTED THE SANT\'EGIDIO COMMUNITY AT A

MEMORIAL EVENT AT ROME\'S CITY HALL WHERE HE SPOKE ABOUT THE

IMPORTANCE OF KNOWING HISTORY IN FIGHT AGAINST ANTI-SEMITISM.

THE SANT\'EGIDIO COMMUNITY HOSTS THIS ANNUAL COMMEMORATION

AND CONDUCTS DURING THE YEAR A NUMBER OF EDUCATIONAL TRIPS

FOR ITALIAN CHILDREN TO THE ROME SYNAGOGUE AND JEWISH SCHOOL,

WITH THE FULL SUPPORT AND BACKING OF THE HOLY SEE ITSELF. IN

ADDITION, IT CONDUCTS AN IMPRESSIVE ANNUAL INTER-RELIGIOUS

DIALOGUE ENCOUNTER WITH ACTIVE HOLY SEE AND JEWISH

PARTICIPATION AIMED AT FOSTERING UNDERSTANDING AND

EMPHASIZING COMMON VALUES OF DIVERSE FAITHS IN THE SPIRIT OF

THE POPE\'S 1986 ASSISI INTER-FAITH INITIATIVE.

11. (C) IN A NOVEMBER 13 CONVERSATION WITH THE AMBASSADOR,

RICCARDO DI SEGNI, CHIEF RABBI OF ROME, PRAISED THE HOLY SEE

FOR ITS ROLE IN DENYING ANTI-SEMITES A THEOLOGICAL BASIS FOR

THEIR PREJUDICE. HE SAID THAT THE VATICAN II REFORMS HAVE

"CUT THE TREE AT ITS ROOTS -- BUT THE ROOTS ARE STILL THERE,

WAITING FOR WATER." IN THAT REGARD HE BELIEVED THAT POPE

JOHN PAUL\'S HIGHLY SYMBOLIC GESTURES SUCH AS THE 1986 VISIT

TO THE ROME SYNAGOGUE HAD DONE MUCH TO EDUCATE THE ITALIAN

AND EUROPEAN PUBLIC. REGARDING THE SCOPE OF THE

ANTI-SEMITISM PROBLEM, DE SEGNI MAINTAINED THAT EACH COUNTRY

HAS TO BE ANALYZED IN ITS OWN RIGHT. HE AGREED THAT MANY

RECENT ANTI-SEMITIC ACTS OF VIOLENCE, PARTICULARLY THOSE IN

FRANCE, RESULTED LARGELY FROM FRUSTRATIONS OF DISAFFECTED

IMMIGRANT YOUTHS FROM NORTH AFRICA AND THE MIDDLE EAST. SUCH

ATTACKS, HE SAID, WERE UNKNOWN IN ITALY. THE FRENCH

PHENOMENON WITH ITS MIDDLE EAST LINKS, HE BELIEVED, WAS

DIFFERENT FROM THE HISTORIC ANTI-SEMITISM THAT STILL EXISTS

IN EUROPE. CITING LOCAL SOCCER HOOLIGANS\' USE OF THE WORD

"JEW" AS AN EPITHET, DE SEGNI INDICATED THAT ITALIAN

AUTHORITIES NEEDED TO WORK HARDER BOTH TO EDUCATE THE PUBLIC

AND CREATE A MORE JUST ECONOMIC ORDER, SO THAT MARGINALIZED

PEOPLE DO NOT MISTAKENLY TARGET JEWS TO EXPRESS THEIR

DISAFFECTION.

12. (C) OUR ISRAELI EMBASSY CONTACTS ALSO WELCOMED THE POPE\'S

PERSONAL COMMITMENT TO COMBATING ANTI-SEMITISM AND HIS MANY

IMPORTANT SYMBOLIC ACTS DURING HIS PAPACY. THEY NOTED THAT

THE COMMITMENT TO COMBAT ANTI-SEMITISM WAS INCORPORATED IN

THE "FUNDAMENTAL ACT" AGREEMENT THAT FORMED THE BASIS OF

DIPLOMATIC RELATIONS BETWEEN THE HOLY SEE AND ISRAEL, AND WAS

THUS BOTH A LEGAL AND MORAL COMMITMENT FOR THE HOLY SEE. THE

ISRAELI EMBASSY HAS IN RECENT TIMES FOUND THE HOLY SEE SLOWER

TO RAISE ITS VOICE IN RESPONSE TO SPECIFIC ACTS AND RELUCTANT

TO REPEAT ITS CONDEMNATIONS OF ANTI-SEMITISM OUT OF FEAR THAT

SUCH STATEMENTS WOULD BE INTERPRETED IN THE ARAB WORLD AS

SUPPORT FOR THE ISRAELI GOVERNMENTS POLICIES IN THE OCCUPIED

TERRITORIES. THE ISRAELI EMBASSY CONTINUES TO EMPHASIZE THE

IMPORTANCE OF KEEPING THESE ISSUES SEPARATE, AND RECENTLY

FOUND SUPPORT FROM CARDINAL ROGER ETCHEGARAY, WHO PUBLICLY

STATED THAT CRITICISM OF ISRAELI POLICIES MUST NEVER SLIP

INTO REDUCED VIGILANCE AGAINST ANTI-SEMITISM. THE EMBASSY,

FOR ITS PART, IS WORKING WITH THE HOLY SEE TO DEVELOP

EDUCATIONAL AND EXCHANGE PROGRAMS AND TO SUPPORT THE STUDY OF

JUDAISM AT CATHOLIC UNIVERSITIES. DESPITE THESE EFFORTS,

ISRAEL\'S RELATIONS WITH THE HOLY SEE ARE CURRENTLY COOL AS A

RESULT OF THE GOVERNMENT\'S HARD-LINE TACTICS IN THE WEST BANK

AND GAZA.

FROM THE CURIA\'S PERSPECTIVE, THE SPIRAL OF VIOLENCE IN

ISRAEL AND THE OCCUPIED TERRITORIES AND THE RESULTANT EXODUS

OF THE REGION\'S CHRISTIANS, IS A MATTER OF PRIMARY CONCERN

AND A SUBJECT OF REPEATED PAPAL INTERVENTIONS.

COMMENT

-------

13. (SBU) WE CAN CONTINUE TO COUNT ON THE HOLY SEE -- AND

THIS POPE IN PARTICULAR -- TO BE A VITAL PARTNER IN COMBATING

ANTI-SEMITISM IN EUROPE TODAY. DESPITE OCCASIONAL

DIFFICULTIES AND LINGERING DIFFERENCES BETWEEN THE CATHOLIC

CHURCH AND JUDAISM, WE CAN CONTINUE TO COUNT ON THE HOLY SEE

TO SPEAK OUT, TEACH, PROVIDE MORAL AND SPIRITUAL GUIDANCE,

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 04 VATICA 05545 03 OF 04 141511Z

AND KEEP PRESSURE ON EUROPEAN GOVERNMENTS TO COUNTER SIGNS OF

RESURGENT ANTI-SEMITISM. FOR EXAMPLE, THE HOLY SEE IS MORE

THAN WILLING TO USE BOTH ITS VOICE AND VOTE ON SUCH MORAL

ISSUES AS ANTI-SEMITISM AT EUROPEAN ORGANIZATIONS SUCH AS THE

OSCE. POST WILL CONTINUE TO WORK WITH THE HOLY SEE TO

HIGHLIGHT THE NEED FOR VIGILANCE AGAINST RESURGENT

ANTI-SEMITISM, NO MATTER WHAT ITS ORIGIN. LAST WEEK, FOR

EXAMPLE, THE AMBASSADOR BEGAN COORDINATING WITH HOLY SEE

COM\'S TO ENSURE THAT THE ANTI-SEMITISM THEME WILL BE PART OF

THE DIPLOMATIC CORPS\' ANNUAL GREETING TO THE POPE, WHICH

SHAPES THE POPE\'S ANNUAL DIPLOMATIC CORPS ADDRESS. IN

ADDITION, THE AMBASSADOR MEETS REGULARLY WITH ANTI-DEFAMATION

LEAGUE PRESIDENT ABRAHAM FOXMAN AND RELEVANT VATICAN

OFFICIALS TO KEEP THIS ISSUE IN THE FOREFRONT OF OUR AGENDA

AND TO HIGHLIGHT U.S. CONCERNS. LATER THIS MONTH, THE

AMBASSADOR WILL BE RECEIVING THE "NOSTRA AETATE" AWARD IN NEW

YORK FROM THE CENTER FOR JEWISH-CHRISTIAN RELATIONS AT SACRED

HEART UNIVERSITY, REFLECTING HIS COMMITMENT TO COMBATING

ANTI-SEMITISM. WE ALSO CONTINUE TO SUPPORT AND ACTIVELY

PARTICIPATE IN SANT EGIDIO\'S INTERFAITH ACTIVITIES AIMED AT

DEEPENING THE CATHOLIC-JEWISH DIALOGUE.
