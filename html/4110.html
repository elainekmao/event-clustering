SUBJECT: PRISON BEATINGS

1. (U) SUMMARY AND COMMENT: ACCORDING TO UNUSUAL MEDIA

REPORTS, HOANG TIEN PRISON IN THE NORTHERN PROVINCE OF HAI

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 02 HANOI 02607 290740Z

DUONG (WHICH EMBOFFS VISITED IN SEPTEMBER -- REF B), HAS

EXPERIENCED THREE SERIOUS INCIDENTS INVOLVING GUARDS AND

PRISONERS BETWEEN LATE AUGUST AND MID-SEPTEMBER. IT REMAINS

A MYSTERY WHY A FEW ELEMENTS OF THE MEDIA HAVE PUBLICIZED

THESE INCIDENTS AND THE SUBSEQUENT INVESTIGATION BY THE

SUPREME PEOPLE\'S PROCURACY, AS WELL AS WHY THE GVN SELECTED

THIS PARTICULAR PRISON FOR THE FIRST-EVER VISIT TO A

VIETNAMESE PRISON BY A US EMBASSY POLITICAL OFFICER. END

SUMMARY AND COMMENT.

2. (U) ON THE MORNING OF AUGUST 26, TWO HOANG TIEN GUARDS

STABBED INMATE NGUYEN TAI LONG, AFTER HE ASKED FOR A

TEMPORARY REST FROM DAILY LABOR DUE TO FATIGUE RELATED TO

HIS AIDS INFECTION. LATER THAT DAY, AFTER HIS CONDITION

DETERIORATED, LONG WAS SENT TO A LOCAL MEDICAL CLINIC, AND

THEN TO THE PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL, WHERE HE RECOVERED.

3. (U) ON SEPTEMBER 10, INMATE PHAM VAN DZUNG DIED AT

12:30 P.M., AFTER TWO GUARDS HAD STABBED HIM REPEATEDLY,

TIED HIM UP, AND LEFT HIM EXPOSED TO THE SUN FOR TWO HOURS

ON THE DECK OF A BARGE IN 34 CELSIUS (OVER 100 DEGREE

FAHRENHEIT) HEAT. DZUNG HAD BEGUN A SIX-YEAR SENTENCE IN

MAY 2002. THE GUARDS\' PUNISHMENT CAME AFTER DZUNG HAD TWICE

ASKED FOR PERMISSION TO REST FROM HIS LABOR THAT DAY OF

UNLOADING ROCKS FROM THE BARGE ON A RIVER NEAR THE PRISON.

HE DIED ON THE WAY TO THE DISTRICT HOSPITAL.

4. (U) ON THE AFTERNOON OF SEPTEMBER 22, THE DEAD BODY OF

INMATE DUONG VAN THU WAS FOUND HANGING FROM A TREE IN THE

CENTER OF THE PRISON. THU WAS SERVING A SENTENCE OF FOUR

AND A HALF YEARS FOR NARCOTICS TRAFFICKING. THU REPORTEDLY

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 03 HANOI 02607 290740Z

COMMITTED SUICIDE BECAUSE HE WAS UPSET THAT HIS FAMILY HAD

NOT PAID THE FINES UNDER HIS "BRACKET PENALTIES," WHICH

MIGHT HAVE QUALIFIED HIM FOR A SPECIAL AMNESTY ON THE

OCCASION OF THE VIETNAMESE NATIONAL DAY. THE MEDIA AND

INVESTIGATORS HAVE RAISED QUESTIONS ABOUT HOW HE WAS ABLE TO

HANG HIMSELF IN SUCH A PUBLIC SITE, AND HOW HE HAD EVER

LEARNED ABOUT HIS "POSSIBLE" ELIGIBILITY FOR AMNESTY, WHICH

IS SUPPOSED TO BE CLASSIFIED INFORMATION.

5. (U) FOLLOWING THE DEATHS OF DZUNG AND THU, OFFICIALS

FROM THE SUPREME PEOPLE\'S PROCURACY CONDUCTED AN

INVESTIGATION AT HOANG TIEN; ONLY THEN DID THEY LEARN ABOUT

THE AUGUST INCIDENT, ACCORDING TO MEDIA. ONE OFFICIAL FROM

THE SUPREME PEOPLE\'S PROCURACY, IN COMMENTS TO LOCAL

REPORTERS, ADMITTED SYSTEMATIC BEATINGS AND OVERTAXING OF

INMATES AT HOANG TIEN PRISON, WHICH HAD PREVIOUSLY BEEN

LISTED AMONG THE MOST MERITORIOUS PRISONS UNDER THE

MANAGEMENT OF THE MINISTRY OF PUBLIC SECURITY.
