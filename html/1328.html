SUBJECT: SULTAN BIN ZAYID: "MUST THE U.S. BE TOUGHER

THAN THE ISRAELIS ON PALESTINE?"

1. (U) CLASSIFIED BY THEODORE H. KATTOUF, AMBASSADOR,

REASON 1.5 (B) AND (D).

2. (C) SUMMARY: A FRIENDLY BUT CLEARLY FRUSTRATED SULTAN BIN

ZAYID, UAE DEPUTY PRIME MINISTER AND SECOND SON OF PRESIDENT

ZAYID BIN SULTAN, CONVEYED TO AMBASSADOR IN FRANK, DELIBERATE

TERMS OCTOBER 29 HIS OWN "AND MANY OTHERS\'" DEEP DISAPPOINTMENT

THAT THE U.S., IN ITS "UNCRITICAL SUPPORT" FOR ISRAEL AND HARSH

CRITICISMS OF THE PALESTINIANS, HAD BETRAYED ITS HISTORICAL

REPUTATION AS A CHAMPION OF FAIRNESS, JUSTICE AND THE

INDEPENDENCE OF OPPRESSED PEOPLES. THE ARABS, HE SAID, NO LONGER

HAD ANY EXPECTATIONS THAT THE U.S. WOULD SERVE AS AN HONEST

BROKER FOR PEACE, AND THIS COULD HAVE RAMIFICATIONS FOR LARGER

U.S.-ARAB RELATIONS, "WHICH WAS NOT GOOD FOR EITHER OF US."

OFFERING HIS CONDOLENCES FOR THE LOSS OF LIFE RESULTING FROM THE

"COWARDLY" ATTACK ON THE USS COLE, SULTAN WARNED THAT EXTREMISTS

WOULD CONTINUE TO EXPLOIT TENSIONS IN THE REGION TO FURTHER THEIR

OWN AGENDAS. IN A RARE COMMENTARY ON LEBANON, SULTAN LABELED

SYRIAN POLICY THERE "DISASTROUS," AND SAID THE UAE WOULD BE THE

FIRST TO APPLAUD THE WITHDRAWAL OF SYRIAN TROOPS FROM LEBANESE

SOIL. SULTAN\'S UNVARNISHED VIEWS NO DOUBT REFLECT HIS FATHER\'S

THINKING, ALTHOUGH SULTAN SAID HE HAD AVOIDED RAISING THE CURRENT

SITUATION IN THE OCCUPIED TERRITORIES IN RECENT DISCUSSIONS WITH

HIS RECUPERATING FATHER SO AS NOT TO UPSET ZAYID. THE MEETING

WAS GIVEN EXTENSIVE MEDIA COVERAGE THAT EVENING AND IN THE

FOLLOWING DAY\'S PRESS, UNDER THE HEADLINE "THE UAE URGES

WASHINGTON TO BE HONEST AND DO WHAT IS RIGHT." END SUMMARY.

-------------------------------

MORE ZIONIST THAN THE ISRAELIS?

-------------------------------

3. (C) MEETING WITH THE AMBASSADOR, WHO WAS ACCOMPANIED BY THE

DCM, ON OCTOBER 29 AT HIS SEASIDE PALACE, UAE DEPUTY PRIME

MINISTER SULTAN BIN ZAYID -- RENOWNED FOR HIS FRANKNESS -- WASTED

LITTLE TIME WITH CONVENTIONAL FORMALITIES BEFORE LAUNCHING INTO A

CRITIQUE OF THE U.S. RESPONSE TO RECENT VIOLENCE IN THE OCCUPIED

TERRITORIES. SPEAKING QUIETLY (NOTE: IN TYPICAL BEDOUIN FASHION

WHEN EXPRESSING DISAPPOINTMENT TO A FRIEND. END NOTE.) AND IN

ARABIC, SULTAN ASKED WHY IT WAS THAT THE U.S. APPEARED TO BE

TAKING A TOUGHER POLITICAL STANCE AGAINST THE PALESTINIANS IN THE

CURRENT SITUATION THAN EVEN ISRAEL? STRESSING THAT THIS WAS NOT

JUST HIS OPINION, BUT WHAT HE WAS HEARING IN THE MAJLISES AS

WELL, SULTAN SAID THERE WAS GREAT DISAPPOINTMENT THROUGHOUT THE

ARAB WORLD IN THE U.S. FAILURE TO ACT AS AN HONEST BROKER.

MOREOVER, THE ARABS WERE BLAMING THE U.S. MORE THAN ISRAEL FOR

THE CRISIS BECAUSE IT WAS IN A POSITION TO DO SOMETHING AND DID

NOT. "THE ISRAELIS ARE FIRING U.S. WEAPONS -- APACHE HELICOPTER

GUNSHIPS AND M16 RIFLES -- AGAINST UNARMED PALESTINIAN CHILDREN,"

SULTAN SAID; "THE WORLD HAD WITNESSED SCENES OF BEASTLY BEHAVIOR

ON THE PART OF THE ISRAELIS, AND YET THE U.S. CRITICIZES THE

ARABS." THE ARABS HAD EXPECTED MUCH MORE OF THE U.S., GIVEN ITS

HISTORY OF STANDING UP FOR JUSTICE, HUMAN RIGHTS AND FAIR

TREATMENT, AND ITS OWN SUCCESSFUL FIGHT FOR INDEPENDENCE AGAINST

AN OPPRESSOR NATION. THE PALESTINIANS HAD BEEN LIVING UNDER ONE

FORM OF OCCUPATION OR ANOTHER FOR YEARS. THEY WERE LOSING ALL

HOPE. ALL THEY WERE SEEKING WAS THEIR INDEPENDENCE. THE ARABS

WOULD ACCEPT THE ISRAELIS AND THEIR NEED FOR SECURITY IF THEY, IN

TURN, WOULD ACCEPT BOUNDARIES AND THE SOVEREIGNTY OF OTHERS.

THEY COULD NOT RELY FOREVER ON THEIR NUCLEAR ARSENALS TO PROTECT

THEM; THEY WOULD HAVE TO MAKE PEACE WITH THEIR ARAB NEIGHBORS.

BUT, SULTAN CONTINUED, EVEN THE WHITE HOUSE COULD NOT OFFER A

BALANCED ASSESSMENT OF THE CONFLICT, AND SO THE ARABS NO LONGER

HAD EXPECTATIONS OF AMERICAN FAIRNESS.

4. (C) WHILE ACKNOWLEDGING THE TRAGIC ASPECTS OF THE CURRENT

CRISIS AND OFFERING HIS SYMPATHIES TO THE INNOCENT VICTIMS OF

VIOLENCE, THE AMBASSADOR URGED SULTAN TO TAKE THE LONGER VIEW.

ALL PARTIES HAD EVOLVED IN THE PROCESS OF SEEKING PEACE, AND MUCH

HAD BEEN ACCOMPLISHED OVER THE YEARS, IN PARTICULAR, FOLLOWING

THE MADRID CONFERENCE. RABIN AND PERES RECOGNIZED THE GROWING

THREAT IRAN AND IRAQ POSED AND DECIDED TO MAKE PEACE WITH THEIR

NEIGHBORS AND DEAL WITH THE PLO. ALTHOUGH ONCE SHUNNED, ARAFAT

HAD VISITED PRESIDENT CLINTON AT THE WHITE HOUSE ON NUMEROUS

OCCASIONS AND WAS TREATED AS A FULL PARTNER IN THE SEARCH FOR

PEACE. THE REASON THINGS HAD NOW FLARED UP SO BADLY WAS

PRECISELY BECAUSE OF THE PROGRESS MADE AT CAMP DAVID II,

PARTICULARLY THE REQUIREMENT TO DEAL WITH JERUSALEM AND ITS HOLY

PLACES. THE EXTREMISTS WERE WORRIED. BUT IT WOULD BE WRONG TO

FORGET OR THROW AWAY ALL THE PROGRESS MADE OVER THE PAST DECADE

BECAUSE OF THE TRAGIC EVENTS OF THE PAST MONTH. WHAT WAS NOW

IMPORTANT WAS THAT THE VIOLENCE CEASE SO THAT PASSIONS COULD

COOL. THE U.S. WOULD REMAIN ENGAGED IN THE SEARCH FOR PEACE NO

MATTER WHO WON THE UPCOMING U.S. ELECTIONS, AS THIS WAS IN THE

NATIONAL INTEREST.

--------------------------------------------- --

THE CONSEQUENCES FOR LARGER U.S.-ARAB RELATIONS

--------------------------------------------- --

5. (C) SULTAN AGREED THAT "THIS WILL PASS (I.E. THE CRISIS); IT

IS LIFE," WHILE EXPRESSING HIS HOPE THAT IT DID NOT GET WORSE.

OTHERWISE, HE SAID, THERE WOULD BE SERIOUS CONSEQUENCES FOR U.S.-

ARAB RELATIONS. IT WOULD BE DIFFICULT FOR MODERATE ARAB LEADERS

TO DEFEND CONTINUED CLOSE TIES TO THE U.S. OR TO DEFLECT

ACCUSATIONS THAT THE U.S. IS MANIPULATING THEM UNLESS THE U.S. IS

SEEN TO BE FAIR ON ISSUES IMPORTANT TO THE ARABS. OFFERING HIS

CONDOLENCES FOR THE DEATH OF THE 17 SAILORS FOLLOWING THE

"COWARDLY" ATTACK ON THE USS COLE, SULTAN SUGGESTED A LINKAGE

BETWEEN SUCH ACTS OF TERRORISM AND THE ISRAELI-PALESTINIAN

CONFLICT. IN THIS CONTEXT, SULTAN REMARKED THAT THE U.S. SEEMED

TO FORGET THAT ISRAEL HAD "CREATED" HAMAS AND SUPPORTED HIZBOLLAH

AS COUNTERWEIGHTS TO THE PLO. NOW THESE EXTREMIST GROUPS WERE

OUT OF CONTROL, PRESENTING A THREAT TO ALL CONCERNED. YET WHEN

THEY ATTACK, SULTAN SUGGESTED, THE REAL VICTIM IS U.S.-ARAB

RELATIONS.

------------------------------------------

BUT ISRAEL NOT THE ONLY OCCUPYING POWER...

------------------------------------------

6. (C) EXPANDING ON HIS THEME OF ISRAELI ATTEMPTS TO DOMINATE

AND POLARIZE -- RATHER THAN LIVE WITH -- HER NEIGHBORS (AND

ALLUDING TO PERCEIVED ISRAELI EFFORTS TO CANTONIZE THE WEST

BANK), SULTAN DESCRIBED ISRAELI EFFORTS TO "DIVIDE AND CONTROL"

LEBANON THROUGH THE CREATION OF A SERIES OF SECTARIAN CANTONS.

"THIS WAS BEN GURION\'S AND SUBSEQUENTLY SHARON\'S PLAN," HE SAID,

"AND IT DIDN\'T WORK." THE AMBASSADOR IMPLIED THAT SYRIA HAD

CONTRIBUTED ITS SHARE TO LEBANON\'S TRAVAILS, AIDED AND ABETTED BY

CERTAIN LEBANESE POLITICIANS WHO CONTINUED TO LOOK TOWARD

DAMASCUS FOR DECISIONS EVEN ON TRIVAL MATTERS. SULTAN READILY

AGREED THAT SYRIA\'S POLICIES IN LEBANON WERE "DISASTROUS," AND

EFFECTIVELY JUST ANOTHER FORM OF OCCUPATION. THERE WOULD BE

BROAD ARAB SUPPORT FOR SYRIA\'S WITHDRAWAL OF ITS MILITARY TROOPS

FROM LEBANON AND "THE UAE WOULD HEAD THE LIST." "I KNOW THIS,"

SULTAN EMPHASIZED, "AND CAN SPEAK FOR MY COUNTRY\'S POSITION."

COMMENT

-------

7. (C) THE TELEVISION CAMERAS AND CREW THAT AWAITED THE

AMBASSADOR\'S ARRIVAL SIGNALED SULTAN\'S INTENTION TO USE THIS

MEETING AS MUCH TO MOLLIFY AN ANGRY POPULACE AS TO PASS A MESSAGE

TO THE U.S. INDEED, THE MEETING WAS GIVEN PROMINENT COVERAGE ON

THE EVENING NEWS AND IN THE FOLLOWING MORNING\'S PRESS, WITH AN

UNUSUAL AMOUNT OF DETAIL. OPTICS ASIDE, SULTAN\'S MESSAGE WAS

SINCERE AND REFLECTS WHAT WE ARE HEARING FROM A BROAD RANGE OF

CONTACTS.

KATTOUF
