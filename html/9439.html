SUBJECT: UAE WOMEN\'S CONFERENCE

1. Sheikha Fatima bint Mubarak, the President of the UAE

General Women\'s Union and the wife of the President of the

UAE, has invited National Security Advisor Dr. Condoleezza

Rice and NEA Deputy Assistant Secretary Elizabeth Cheney to

attend the "First Economic Businesswomen Forum" in Abu

Dhabi scheduled for October 7 to October 10, 2003. The

forum is cosponsored by the Arab League Council for Arab

Business Women, the General Women\'s Union, and the Abu

Dhabi Chamber of Commerce and Industry. The topic of the

forum is "Projects for Businesswomen: Reality and

Ambitions," and it is designed to encourage further

participation by Arab women in the economies of their

respective countries.

2. We understand that this forum replaces a long planned

conference, scheduled for October 5-6, hosted by the Abu

Dhabi World Trade Center and the Abu Dhabi Businesswomen\'s

Council, on "Arab Women in the 21st Century: First Economic

and Political Conference." The organizers have told Post

that, given the conflicting time schedule between the two

conferences, they have cancelled the October 5-6

conference.

3. According to the preliminary schedule provided by the

Women\'s Union, the conference will begin with a reception

followed by a dinner on October 6. Work sessions are

scheduled for October 7 and 8 covering issues relating to

investment and women in business. A trip to UAE President

Sheikh Zayed\'s nature reserve Sir Bani Yas Island is

scheduled for October 9. A trip to Dubai - with shopping

and dinner - is scheduled for October 10.

4. Post will pouch letters of invitation and a copy of the

schedule to the Department. It is possible that some

details may change. The Ambassador will engage with the

conference arrangers in the next week in order to obtain

more details. A copy of the preliminary schedule will be

faxed to NEA/ARP.
