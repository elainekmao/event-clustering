SUBJECT: JORDAN: MEDIA REACTION ON SITUATION IN THE

OCCUPIED TERRITORIES, THE FUTURE OF ARAB-AMERICAN

RELATIONS

SUMMARY

THE MAIN NEWS REPORTS IN JORDAN\'S DAILIES WERE STILL

DEVELOPMENTS IN THE OCCUPIED TERRITORIES. THE

PRINCIPAL ELEMENTS WERE THE END OF THE SIEGE ON

ARAFAT\'S HEADQUARTERS, ISRAEL\'S REJECTION OF THE U.N.

FACT-FINDING MISSION, AND ARAB CALLS TO EVOKE THE 7TH

ARTICLE OF THE UN CHARTER.

A NUMBER OF EDITORIAL COLUMNISTS PRAISED THE KING\'S

ROLE IN UNDERTAKING HIS FORTHCOMING VISIT TO THE U.S.

OTHERS COMMENTED GLOOMILY ON FUTURE ARAB-AMERICAN

RELATIONS IN LIGHT OF THE U.S. POSITION ON THE MIDDLE

EAST CONFLICT.

NEWS REPORTS

LEAD STORIES IN JORDAN\'S DAILIES ON MAY 2 CONCENTRATED

ON "THE START OF THE IMPLEMENTATION OF ENDING THE SIEGE

ON ARAFAT," AND THE TRANSFER OF "ZE\'EVI\'S KILLERS TO

JERICHO."

DAILIES ALSO REPORTED THE COLLAPSE OF THE FACT-FINDING

MISSION, UNDER HEADLINES THAT ANNOUNCED: "SECURITY

COUNCIL TO DEBATE THE ISRAEL\'S REJECTION OF THE FACT-

FINDING MISSION, ARAB DRAFT RESOLUTION CALLING TO EVOKE

ARTICLE 7."

DAILIES ALSO QUOTED AGENCY REPORTS ABOUT A "U.S.-SAUDI

STRATEGY TO PRESSURE ISRAEL INTO ENDING THE CURRENT

CRISIS.

EDITORIAL COMMENTARIES

-- THE KING\'S VISIT TO WASHINGTON

PROMINENT DAILY COLUMNIST URAIB AL-RANTAWI WROTE IN

CENTER-LEFT, INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR ON MAY

2: "HIS MAJESTY KING ABDULLAH STARTS AN IMPORTANT VISIT

TO THE U.S. IN WHICH HE ADDRESSES AMERICAN PUBLIC

OPINION AS WELL AS DECISION MAKING INSTITUTIONS, THINK

TANKS, AND THE MEDIA. HIS DISCOURSE WILL HIGHLIGHT

THREE POINTS: FIRST, THAT THE OSLO AGREEMENTS AND

PHILOSOPHY HAVE COMPLETED THEIR FUNCTIONS, AND THAT

THERE IS NO USE IN SECURITY ARRANGEMENTS WITHOUT A

POLITICAL DIMENSION, OR TRANSITIONAL AGREEMENTS WITHOUT

THE FINAL SETTLEMENT. SECOND, THAT WASHINGTON STANDS

AS THE ONLY DECISION-MAKING CENTER THAT REMAINS

CONFUSED BETWEEN THE NEO-CONSERVATIVE TREND THAT PUSHES

FOR MORE FLAGRANT BIAS IN FAVOR OF THE HEBREW STATE,

AND THE STATE DEPARTMENT THAT IS BEGINNING TO REALIZE

THE CONSEQUENCES OF SUCH A POLICY. THIRD, THE NEED TO

RESTORE THE PNA AND ITS LEADER AFTER THE CAMPAIGNS THAT

AIMED TO MARGINALIZE AND DESTROY THEM. THIS DISCOURSE

AND THE FIELD ACTIVITY THAT WILL FOLLOW WILL BE THE

QUIET RESPONSE TO THE BOISTEROUS CLAMOR THAT SURROUNDS

US WITHOUT HAVING ANY VISIBLE CONSEQUENCE."

-- SHOULD WE LOSE HOPE IN AN HONEST AMERICAN MEDIATION?

FORMER NEWS DIRECTOR IN JORDAN TELEVISION YAQOUB JABER

WROTE IN CENTER-LEFT, INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-

DUSTOUR ON MAY 2: "I FEAR THAT THE PALESTINIANS HAVE

LOST HOPE OF AN HONEST AMERICAN MEDIATION THAT MAY

PRODUCE A JUST SETTLEMENT OF THEIR STRUGGLE WITH

ISRAEL. AN HONEST MEDIATION WOULD EXERT PRESSURE ON

ISRAEL THAT OCCUPIES THE LAND, KILLS AND MUTILATES THE

PEOPLE, AND REJECTS PEACE. BUT WHAT WE HAVE SEEN FROM

THE BUSH ADMINISTRATION IS THAT IT NOT CONTENTED WITH

FLAGRANT SUPPORT FOR ISRAEL AND ITS BLOODY CRIMES IN

PALESTINIAN TOWNS AND VILLAGES, BUT ALSO TOTAL ENMITY

FOR PALESTINIANS AND THEIR LEGITIMATE LEADERSHIP. AND

WE WOULD BE MISTAKEN TO THINK THAT THE PRESENT

ADMINISTRATION IS MORE BIASED FOR ISRAEL THAT ITS

PREDECESSORS. BUT WE SHOULD NOT REACH THE POINT OF

DESPAIR. OUR OBJECTIVE SHOULD BE TO REACH AMERICAN

PUBLIC OPINION THAT MAY HAVE BEGUN TO BE AROUSED BY

ISRAEL\'S MOST RECENT CRIMES AND STARTED TO FEEL ITS WAY

IN THE TOTAL OBSCURITY IMPOSED ON IT BY ZIONIST

PROPAGANDA."

-- THE MORE IMPORTANT BATTLE HAS BEGUN

DAILY COLUMNIST KHALIL AL-SAWAHIRI WROTE IN CENTER-

LEFT, INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR ON MAY 2:

"THE SIGHT OF THE YOUTH IN JENIN CAMP, AFTER SURVIVING

THE MASSACRE, AS THEY TROD ON CASES OF AID BEARING THE

U.S. FLAG SENT WAVES OF PRIDE IN OUR SOULS THAT ARE

SUNK IN SORROW AND FRUSTRATION. [THE PALESTINIANS]

HAVE RETURNED TO AMERICA, THE FULL PARTNER IN THE

CRIME, ITS CRUMBS OF ASSISTANCE THAT ARE SOAKED WITH

THE BLOOD OF INNOCENT PALESTINIAN CHILDREN. AND LIKE

THEM, ARABS REFUSE TO CONTINUE FINANCING THE BARBARIC

AMERICAN ONSLAUGHT BY BOYCOTTING AMERICAN PRODUCTS. I

WAS AMAZED TO HEAR MY SEVEN YEAR-OLD GRANDSON TALK

ABOUT THIS BOYCOTT, FOR HE KNEW EXACTLY THE NUMBER AND

TRADEMARKS OF THE PRODUCTS THAT HE AND OTHERS OF HIS

GENERATION HAVE BOYCOTTED THROUGH THEIR CONVICTION THAT

EVERY PENNY SPENT ON THEM WILL RETURN AGAINST US AS

BULLETS AND BOMBS. AMERICA MAY NOT BE CONCERNED WITH

THIS BOYCOTT, SINCE IT HAS LOTS OF OTHER MARKETS, BUT

THE CRUCIAL POINT IS THAT ARAB AWARENESS HAS BEEN

RESTORED. THE BOYCOTT IS NOT A MERE REFUSAL TO

CONSUME, BUT A POLITICAL AND MORAL COMMITMENT THAT IS

GROWING EVERY DAY, AND IT IS THE START OF THE

CONFRONTATION AND ARAB SELF-DEFENSE AGAINST TOTAL

SURRENDER TO AMERICA OR ANYONE ELSE. AMERICA WILL

DISCOVER WHAT A QUAGMIRE IT HAS DRAGGED ITSELF IN BY

ITS SHAMEFUL SUPPORT OF THE ISRAELI RACIST AND BARBARIC

AGGRESSION." GNEHM
