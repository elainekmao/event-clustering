SUBJECT: TFI01: UPDATE ON ITALIAN JOURNALISTS IN BAGHDAD

CLASSIFIED BY: DEPUTY CHIEF OF MISSION EMIL SKODON FOR REASONS 1.5 (B)

1. (SBU) EARLY ON APRIL 9 MFA SECRETARY GENERAL\'S CHIEF OF

STAFF, ANTONIO ZANARDI LANDI, SPOKE TO DCM TO FOLLOW UP

PREVIOUS EVENING\'S APPROACH RE SAFETY OF ITALIAN JOURNALISTS

IN BAGHDAD (REFTEL). DCM ADVISED THAT WE HAD PASSED ON GOI

CONCERNS TO DEPARTMENT AND TO CENTCOM, AND REAFFIRMED THAT

U.S. FORCES WERE DOING ALL THEY COULD TO AVOID CIVILIAN

CASUALTIES. DRAWING ON BACKGROUND RECEIVED FROM TASK FORCE,

DCM ALSO TOLD ZANARDI LANDI THAT THE SITUATION IN BAGHDAD WAS

VERY FLUID AS OF DAYBREAK IN IRAQ, AND IT WOULD BE DANGEROUS

FOR ALL CONCERNED FOR U.S. FORCES TO TRY AND EVACUATE

JOURNALISTS FROM THE PALESTINE OR SHERATON HOTELS. THAT

SAID, THE SITUATION ON THE GROUND WAS CHANGING RAPIDLY TODAY

AND CENTCOM WOULD ADJUST AS THE ENVIRONMENT EVOLVED.

2. (C) ZANARDI LANDI SAID THAT GOI HAD MANAGED TO REACH THE

ITALIAN JOURNALISTS IN BAGHDAD AND HAD ASKED THEIR

INTENTIONS, AS REQUESTED BY DCM. THE SEVEN WHO HAD BEEN

ARRESTED BY IRAQI OFFICIALS AND DETAINED IN THE PALESTINE

HOTEL ADVISED THAT IRAQI AUTHORITIES HAD PROMISED TO RETURN

THEIR PASSPORTS AND RELEASE THEM ON APRIL 9, AND ONCE THAT

HAPPENED THEY WISHED TO DEPART BAGHDAD. THE OTHER THIRTEEN

ITALIAN MEDIA REPS AT THE PALESTINE AND SHERATON HOTELS

PLANNED TO STAY TO COVER THE BATTLE. DCM ASKED ZANARDI LANDI

FOR NAMES AND MEDIA AFFILIATIONS OF THE SEVEN WHO HAD BEEN

ARRESTED AND ALSO FOR THE OTHER ITALIAN JOURNALISTS IN

CONFIDENTIAL

BAGHDAD, WHICH HE AGREED TO PROVIDE.

3. (C) LATER IN THE DAY, WHILE ACCOMPANYING U/S BOLTON TO A

CALL ON MFA SECGEN BALDOCCI, DCM ASKED BALDOCCI FOR AN UPDATE

ON THE JOURNALISTS. BALDOCCI SAID THE IRAQIS HAD RETURNED

PASSPORTS TO THE SEVEN ARRESTEES, AS PROMISED, AND INDICATED

THEY WERE FREE TO LEAVE. GIVEN THE DAY\'S FAST-MOVING EVENTS,

HOWEVER, THEY HAD DECIDED TO FOLLOW THEIR JOURNALISTIC

INSTINCTS AND REMAIN IN BAGHDAD. SHORTLY THEREAFTER, EMBASSY

ODC CHIEF RECEIVED A CALL FROM GOI MILITARY CONTACTS TO SAY

THAT THERE WAS NO NEED TO PURSUE THE PREVIOUS DAY\'S REQUEST

FOR DATA ON THE FEASIBILITY OF AN IAF C-130J LANDING AT

BAGHDAD INTERNATIONAL TO EVACUATE THE JOURNALISTS.

4. (C) WHILE THE ABOVE CONVERSATIONS WERE TAKING PLACE,

ZANARDI LANDI FAXED US THE DETAILS OF ALL ITALIAN JOURNALISTS

CURRENTLY IN IRAQ. ALTHOUGH THE ITALIANS DESIRE TO ESCAPE

THE FIGHTING IN BAGHDAD HAS APPARENTLY BEEN OVERTAKEN BY THE

DAY\'S EVENTS, WE REPEAT BELOW THEIR IDENTIFYING DETAILS AS

GIVEN US BY ZANARDI LANDI IN CASE THEY MAY BE OF ANY USE TO
