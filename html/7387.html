SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION: CHAOS IN BAGHDAD LOOTING IN IRAQ

SYRIA\'S NEXT

1. SUMMARY: THE FALL OF BAGHDAD TO COALITION FORCES AND LOOTING

IN IRAQI CITIES DOMINATED COVERAGE IN UAE MEDIA. PRESIDENT

BUSH\'S AND PRIME MINISTER BLAIR\'S STATEMENTS TO THE IRAQI PEOPLE

RECEIVED POSITIVE COVERAGE IN BOTH PRINT AND ELECTRONIC MEDIA.

UAE MEDIA DEVOTED EXTENSIVE COMMENTARY TO THE ROLE OF COALITION

FORCES IN POST-WAR IRAQ BUT ALLEGED SYRIA IS THE NEXT U.S.

TARGET. AN "AL KHALEEJ" EDITORIAL DESCRIBED THE IMAGES OF

LOOTING IS A DELIBERATE "DISTORTION OF THE IMAGE OF IRAQ AND ITS

PEOPLE." AN "AL BAYAN" EDITORIAL CRITICIZED U.S. TROOPS FOR

THEIR FAILURE TO PROVIDE SECURITY IN BAGHDAD. IN "AL BAYAN," UAE

COLUMNIST AYSHA SULTAN CONDEMNED COALITION FORCES FOR THEIR

LACKADAISICAL ATTITUDE TOWARDS THE ANARCHY IN BAGHDAD. IN A RARE

EDITORIAL, ABDUL HAMID AHMAD, GULF NEWS EDITOR-IN-CHIEF, LAMENTS

THE CIRCUMSTANCES IN WHICH IRAQIS HAVE FOUND THEMSELVES. END

SUMMARY.

2. DESCRIBING THE SITUATION IN IRAQ AS A DELIBERATE "DISTORTION

OF THE IMAGE OF IRAQ AND ITS PEOPLE," SHARJAH-BASED PAN-ARAB

DAILY "AL-KHALEEJ" EDITORIALIZED 4/12:

"... THE HAPPINESS REGARDING THE DOWNFALL OF THE REGIME IS ONE

THING, BUT THE LOOTING OF THE GOVERNMENT INSTITUTES, MINISTRIES,

HOSPITALS, AND EDUCATIONAL FOUNDATIONS SUCH AS UNIVERSITIES IS

SOMETHING ELSE COMPLETELY DIFFERENT AND WHICH HAS A VERY

DANGEROUS CONSEQUENCE FOR THE FUTURE OF IRAQ, PARALLEL TO THE

DANGERS OF ITS OCCUPATION. THERE IS DELIBERATE DISTORTION OF THE

IMAGE OF IRAQ AND ITS PEOPLE ... WHAT IRAQ IS FACING NOW, THE

CHAOS AND LOOTING, SERVES ONLY AMERICAN INTERESTS, INCLUDING THE

INTERESTS OF THE ZIONIST ENTITY ..."

3. UNDER THE HEADLINE "WHO WILL SAVE BAGHDAD?," DUBAI-BASED

BUSINESS-ORIENTED ARABIC DAILY "AL-BAYAN" EDITORIALIZED 4/12:

"... WHAT WE ARE SEEING IN BAGHDAD AND ELSEWHERE IN IRAQI CITIES

MAKES OUR HEARTS BLEED. THE FOREIGN TROOPS ALONE BEAR

RESPONSIBILITY FOR WHAT IS HAPPENING, AND IT IS AS IF THEY WANT

TO DESTROY EVERYTHING AND TO DISPLAY THE ARAB PEOPLE IN IRAQ IN A

DISTORTED PICTURE. AND THE ARABS (SORRY TO SAY) ARE STILL

WATCHING BAGHDAD. ISN\'T IT TIME TO DO SOMETHING TO SAVE THEIR

OWN IMAGES?"

4. CRITICIZING THE ATTITUDE OF THE COALITION FORCES IN IRAQ, UAE

COLUMNIST AYSHA SULTAN WROTE IN "AL BAYAN" ON 4/12:

"... WHAT IS OCCURRING IS CRUEL AND BRUTAL ON THE SOUL AND THE

EYES AND REPRESENTS AN EPISODE IN THE SERIES OF INVASIONS. EVEN

WHEN THE MONGOLS INVADED BAGHDAD, THEY THEMSELVES LOOTED, RAPED,

MASSACRED, DESTROYED GOVERNMENT PROPERTY, AND WENT INTO MOSQUES,

HOUSES, AND LIBRARIES THAT WERE EMPTIED OF ALL THEIR BOOKS AND

THROWN IN THE RIVERS OF IRAQ! TODAY, AMERICANS ARE NOT DOING

THAT WITH THEIR OWN HANDS, SO THEY WON\'T BE ACCUSED OF BEING

THIEVES, SINCE THEY CAME AS \'PROPHETS\' TO THIS REGION IN ORDER TO

SAVE IT FROM THE DARKNESS OF DICTATORS AND TAKE IT INTO THE LIGHT

OF DEMOCRACY AND FREEDOM. THE AMERICANS LEFT THE POOR AND HUNGRY

TO DESTROY THE ENTIRE COUNTRY\'S INFRASTRUCTURE, ITS CIVILIZATION,

AND THE IMAGE OF ITS GREAT PEOPLE, WHILE THEY OBSERVE AND LEAVE

THE WORLD TO WATCH THIS CRISIS!"

5. COMMENTING ON THE LOOTING IN IRAQ, COLUMNIST ABU KHALDOUN

WROTE IN "AL KHALEEJ" ON 4/12:

"... I DON\'T THINK PRESIDENT BUSH SHOULD BE HAPPY WHEN HE WATCHES

A FEW IRAQIS CELEBRATE THE INVASION OF U.S. FORCES, SINCE THESE

ARE ONLY A COUPLE HUNDRED PEOPLE AND ARE A GROUP OF THIEVES. THE

PERSON WHO LOOTS HIS NEIGHBOR OR GOVERNMENT CAN\'T HELP BUT ACT

LIKE THIS, AS HE CELEBRATES THE HIGHWAY ROBBERS WHO GAVE HIM AN

OPPORTUNITY TO STEAL. AS FOR THE DISCRETE IRAQI PERSON, HE JUST

CLOSED HIS HOUSE DOOR SO HE WOULDN\'T SEE THE INVADERS IN HIS

LAND."

6. CLAIMING THAT IRAQ IS ONLY FIRST U.S. TARGET IN THE REGION,

"AL KHALEEJ" EDITORIALIZED 4/11:

"... WHEN THE U.S. THREW ITS FIRST BOMB UNDER ITS MOTTO FOR THE

\'LIBERATION\' OF IRAQ, IT WAS A NEW ERA OF OCCUPATION. IT WAS THE

FIRST BOMB AGAINST ALL ARABS AND THEIR RIGHTS AND IT WON\'T BE THE

LAST. BAGHDAD IS ONLY THE FIRST PIECE IN AN CHAIN OF ARAB

COUNTRIES THAT WILL FALL. CHOOSING IRAQ AS THE FIRST TARGET WAS

NOT A RANDOM STEP SINCE IT IS THE BIGGEST, RICHEST, AND MOST

POWERFUL COUNTRY IN THE MIDDLE EAST ..."

7. IN A RARE FRONT-PAGE EDITORIAL UNDER THE HEADLINE "IRAQ FOR

IRAQIS," GULF NEWS EDITOR-IN-CHIEF ABDUL HAMID AHMAD WROTE ON

4/10:

"THIS IS A HEART-BREAKING MOMENT FOR ANY ARAB SEEING MARINES

ROAMING THE STREETS OF BAGHDAD, THE CAPITAL OF CALIPH AL RASHID

... AS MUCH AS AN ARAB IS PLEASED TO SEE HIS IRAQI BROTHERS FREE

FROM THE YOKE OF DICTATORSHIP WHICH HUMILIATED AND TORTURED THEM

FOR MORE THAN 20 YEARS, HIS HEART BREAKS FROM DEPRESSION AND PAIN

SEEING THIS SAD END TO BAGHDAD, THE CAPITAL OF IRAQ, COLLAPSING

UNDER THE CHAINS OF MARINES\' TANKS. THIS IS A TOUGH, SAD AND

PAINFUL MOMENT WITH OUR FEELINGS LEAVING US SPEECHLESS ... WE

WISHED THAT THIS END WOULD HAVE COME AT THE HANDS OF HIS OWN

PEOPLE, NOT AT THE HANDS OF THE MARINES. TODAY HE (SADDAM)

BROUGHT THEM TO THE CAPITAL OF IRAQ, BAGHDAD OF AL RASHID AND AL

MUTANABBI. WE HAVE NO RELIEF BUT IN SEEING IRAQIS REGAIN THEIR

FREEDOM AND RISING TO BUILD THEIR COUNTRY AND GOVERN THEMSELVES,

COOPERATING WITH ARABS IN PEACE AND JUST-LOVING PEOPLES TO FIGHT

TYRANNY, ILLNESS, HUNGER, AND IGNORANCE AND OTHER FOES OF

HUMANKIND.
