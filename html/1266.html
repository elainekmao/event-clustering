1. (C) SUMMARY: ON AUGUST 29 THE IMF RESREP, ROBERT

FRANCO, AND THE VISITING IMF ARTICLE IV REVIEW TEAM

LEADER, PAULO NEUHAUS, MET WITH THE AMBASSADOR, USAID

DIRECTOR, AND ECON AND POLOFF TO DISCUSS ECONOMIC AND

POLITCAL CONDITIONS IN ZIMBABWE, POST ELECTION

POLICIES AND LEADERSHIP, AND SHARE INFORMATION AND

INSIGHTS. NEUHAUS\' PRELIMINARY ASSESSMENT OF

CONDITIONS IN ZIMBABWE, AT LEAST FROM A POLICY AND

PLANNING STANDPOINT IS, IN HIS OWN WORDS, "DISMAL;

THERE ARE NO HARD PLANS OR DETAILS ON HOW TO DEAL

WITH THE MANY CRISES FACING THE COUNTRY." HAVING

ONLY ARRIVED ON SUNDAY, AUGUST 27, THE TEAM LEADER

HAD SO FAR JUST MET WITH THE MINISTER OF FINANCE,

SIMBBA MAKONI, AND THE RESERVE BANK GOVERNOR, LEONARD

TSUMBA. NEUHAUS SOUGHT OUR FEEDBACK, WHICH WE

SIPDIS

PROVIDED HIM, ON KEY ISSUES SUCH AS THE ABILITY AND

FREEDOM OF THE TECHNOCRATS TO FUNCTION, ON THE AREAS

AND PROSPECTS OF GOZ SPENDING CUTS, AND WHERE LAND

REFORM WAS HEADED. END SUMMARY.

2. (C) SOMEWHAT FRUSTRATED, NEUHAUS TOLD US THAT THE

ANSWERS HE HAD RECEIVED FROM BOTH THE FINANCE

MINISTER AND THE CENTRAL BANK GOVERNOR WERE

DISAPPOINTING AND INADEQUATE. "I SEE NO HARD PLANS

FROM EITHER OF THEM OF HOW A HOST OF PROBLEMS ARE TO

BE SOLVED OR THINGS TURNED AROUND," HE STATED. "THE

FINANCE MINSTER CANNOT TELL US WHAT THE FISCAL

DEFICIT FOR THE YEAR IS GOING TO BE OR HOW IT WILL BE

FINANCED. ON HOW MUCH PRIVATIZATION CAN EARN AND

WHEN, I ALSO GOT NO ANSWERS. THE GUIDELINES FOR THE

2001 BUDGET, WHICH IS IN PREPARATION NOW, ARE ALSO

VERY VAGUE," HE CONTINUED, "AND THIS IS DISTURBING."

WHEN I ASKED WHY THE DEVALUATION STOPPED AT 50, THE

ONLY ANSWER I GOT WAS THAT IT WAS AN ACCEPTABLE

"PSYCHOLOGICAL" LEVEL. IN ANSWER TO THE AMBASSADOR\'S

QUERY ON THE EXCHNAGE RATE, NEUHAUS REPLIED THE RATE

SHOULD HAVE BEEN ALLOWED TO SLIDE TO 65 OR 70, AS

ONLY THEN COULD AN EQUILIBRIUM BE TESTED.

3. (C) COMMENTING ON THE RESERVE BANK (RBZ), NEUHAUS

SAID THAT IT IS "CLEARLY, TOTALLY SPACED OUT, AND ANY

LIMITS ON MONETARY GROWTH HAVE CLEARLY BEEN

SUSPENDED. THEY COULD ONLY TELL ME THAT THEY WILL

STOP LENDING TO THE GOVERNMENT WHEN THEY HIT THE

STAUTORY LIMITS." "WHERE IS THAT LIMIT?" HE QUERIED

OUT LOUD, CLEARLY DISAPPOINTED WITH THE SUSPENSION OF

DISCIPLINE AT THE CENTRAL BANK. BROAD MONEY SUPPLY

GROWTH WAS SURGING AT 70 PERCENT AS OF MID-AUGUST, HE

AND THE RESREP OBSERVED, AND THE INFLATION

CONSQUENCES WILL BE TERRIBLE. THE IMF TEAM LEADER

TOLD US THAT ZIMBABWE CURRENTLY HAD NO PLANS TO

5. (C) THE TEAM LEDER\'S OBSERVATIONS AND

CONCLUSIONS AFTER ONLY 48HOURS IN THE COUNTRY IS

NOT, IN OUR OPINION, A POPITIOUS START TO THE REVIEW

OF ZIMBABWE. HIS HIHLIGHTED AREAS OF CONCERN MATCH

THOSE WE HAVE REORTED ON AND WHICH WE TRACK CLOSELY.
