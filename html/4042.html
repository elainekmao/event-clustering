SUBJECT: THE POPE AND THE ROMANIAN PATRIARCH: REINFORCING

BRIDGES TO ORTHODOX EUROPE

-------

SUMMARY

-------

1. (U) IN THE VATICAN, WHERE GESTURES AND SYMBOLS CARRY

WEIGHT, POPE JOHN PAUL II PULLED OUT ALL THE STOPS DURING

ROMANIAN ORTHODOX PATRIARCH TEOCTIST\'S OCTOBER 7-13 VISIT TO

VATICAN CITY AND THE CITY OF ROME. IN A SERIES OF EVENTS

CULMINATING IN A SPECIAL MASS IN ST. PETER\'S, THE PATRIARCH

WAS ACCORDED THE HIGHEST OF HONORS PEPPERED WITH WARM

FRATERNAL EMBRACES. THE VISIT WAS REMARKABLY SIMILAR IN

POMP AND PROTOCOL TO A PAPAL VISIT ABROAD, AND WAS INTENDED

TO RECIPROCATE THE WARM RECEPTION THE POPE RECEIVED IN

ROMANIA IN 1999. WHILE THE POPE AND THE PATRIARCH ARE GOOD

FRIENDS, THE HEARTY WELCOME TEOCTIST RECEIVED IS A CLEAR

SIGN TO THE REST OF EUROPE\'S ORTHODOX COMMUNITY THAT THE

HOLY SEE INTENDS TO CONTINUE ITS VIGOROUS PURSUIT OF CLOSER

RELIGIOUS AND GEOPOLITICAL TIES TO ORTHODOX EUROPE IN ORDER

TO FURTHER POPE JOHN PAUL II\'S LONG-HELD VISION OF EUROPE

EXTENDING FROM THE ATLANTIC TO THE URAL MOUNTAINS. END

SUMMARY.

--------------------------

ROLLING OUT THE RED CARPET

--------------------------

2. (U) PATRIARCH TEOCTIST\'S "UNOFFICIAL" INTRODUCTION TO

VATICAN CITY STATE BY THE POPE OCCURRED BEFORE AN AUDIENCE

OF SOME 250,000 PILGRIMS ATTENDING THE CANONIZATION OF THE

FOUNDER OF OPUS DEI. THE HOLY SEE USED THE OCCASION TO

HONOR TEOCTIST\'S STATUS AS A NATIONAL ORTHODOX LEADER --

THROUGHOUT HIS VISIT HE WAS SEATED ON THE SAME LEVEL AS THE

POPE IN AN IDENTICAL CHAIR. PATRIARCH TEOCTIST\'S SCHEDULE

INCLUDED VISITS TO ROME\'S MAJOR BASILICAS. IN EACH HE WAS

ACCORDED THE HIGHEST OF HONORS. THE PATRIARCH ALSO VISITED

THE SANT\'EGIDIO COMMUNITY WHERE HE SPOKE OF CONSTRUCTING A

CHRISTIAN EUROPE AND ON THE PERSECUTION SUFFERED BY ORTHODOX

CHRISTIANS AT THE HANDS OF THE COMMUNISTS. PATRIARCH

GRATITUDE TO THE SANT\'EGIDIO COMMUNITY FOR ITS INTERNATIONAL

EFFORTS TO PROMOTE DIALOGUE BETWEEN THE WORLD\'S PEOPLES.

OVERCOMING DIFFERENCES: PROPERTY AND PROSELYTISM

--------------------------------------------- ----

3. (U) IN A COMMON DECLARATION ISSUED OCTOBER 12, POPE JOHN

PAUL AND PATRIARCH TEOCTIST CALLED FOR CLOSER RELIGIOUS TIES

AND URGED ROMANIA\'S CHRISTIANS TO SETTLE THEIR DISPUTES

AMICABLY. (COMMENT: CATHOLICS IN ROMANIA ARE SEEKING

RESTITUTION OF PROPERTIES CONFISCATED DURING THE COMMUNIST

ERA AND HANDED OVER TO ORTHODOX CHRISTIANS. END COMMENT).

ALSO AT ISSUE ARE CONVERSIONS TO CATHOLICISM AMONG NON-

BELIEVERS WITH AN ORTHODOX HERITAGE. THE ORTHODOX CHURCH

CONSIDERS SUCH NON-BELIEVERS TO BE THEIR NATURAL

CONSTITUENCY, WHILE THE CATHOLIC CHURCH VIEWS THEM AS OPEN

TO CONVERSION. THE ORTHODOX CHURCH IN ROMANIA, AS IN

RUSSIA, HAS MAJOR CONCERNS ABOUT LOSING CONSTITUENTS TO

CATHOLICISM THROUGH SUCH "CONFESSIONAL COMPETITION." THE

TWO LEADERS EFFECTIVELY SIDE-STEPPED THIS ISSUE BY CALLING

FOR COOPERATION RATHER THAN COMPETITION IN THEIR RESPECTIVE

MISSIONARY WORK AMONG NON-CHRISTIANS. THE POPE, HOWEVER,

COMMON CONCERNS: EU ENLARGEMENT AND EVANGELICALS

4. (U) THE LEADERS WERE IN FULL AGREEMENT ON THE NEED TO

ENSURE THAT EUROPEAN UNION EXPANSION, WITH ITS SECULAR

ORIENTATION, IS COMPLEMENTED BY RELIGIOUS VALUES AND

INCREASING RELIGIOUS UNITY. TO THIS END, THE POPE AND THE

PATRIARCH AGREED TO WORK TOGETHER TO GET THEIR ISSUES ON THE

EU\'S AGENDA. THESE INCLUDE THE SANCTITY OF LIFE, THE

DIGNITY AND FUNDAMENTAL RIGHTS OF THE HUMAN PERSON, JUSTICE

AND SOLIDARITY, PEACE AND RECONCILIATION, THE IMPORTANCE OF

THE FAMILY, AND PROTECTION OF THE ENVIRONMENT.

COMMENT

-------

6. (SBU) POPE JOHN PAUL II USED HIS PERSONAL FRIENDSHIP

WITH ROMANIAN PATRIARCH TEOCTIST, AS HE HAS PREVIOUSLY WITH

AN ARMENIAN ORTHODOX LEADER, TO BOLSTER ECUMENICAL RELATIONS

WITH THE ORTHODOX CHURCH. IN THE FACE OF PERCEIVED RUSSIAN

ORTHODOX INTRANSIGENCE AND INCREASING PRESSURE FROM RUSSIAN

AUTHORITIES ON THE CATHOLIC CHURCH IN RUSSIA, SUCH BRIDGE-

BUILDING PROVIDES A USEFUL COUNTERPOINT FOR THE POPE TO

DEMONSTRATE THE POSSIBILITIES OF CATHOLIC-ORTHODOX DIALOGUE.

AS LONG AS THIS POPE REMAINS IN OFFICE, THIS DIALOGUE WILL

REMAIN A PRIORITY. IN FACT, THE POPE SUGGESTED THAT THIS

DIALOGUE BE GIVEN AN INSTITUTIONAL STRUCTURE THAT WOULD

PROVIDE FOR A MORE REGULAR ORTHODOX-CATHOLIC EXCHANGE. THE

HOLY SEE AND ORTHODOX CHURCHES IN GENERAL VIGOROUSLY PROMOTE

EUROPE\'S HISTORICALLY CHRISTIAN HERITAGE IN THE CONTEXT OF

EU EXPANSION TO FORMERLY COMMUNIST STATES. THE WELCOME

TEOCTIST RECEIVED IN ROME IS PART OF A BROAD VATICAN

STRATEGY TO CEMENT ECCLESIASTICAL ALLIANCES TO MATCH THE

SECULAR ONES CENTERED ON BRUSSELS AND STRASBOURG, AND ALSO

TO SHOW THE RUSSIAN ORTHODOX THEY HAVE NOTHING TO FEAR FROM

THIS POPE AND FROM CATHOLICISM.
