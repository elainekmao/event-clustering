1. (SBU) FM FRATTINI WILL CALL THE SECRETARY EARLY AFTERNOON

EDT FEB. 14 TO DEBRIEF THE SECRETARY ON FRATTINI\'S MEETING

WITH TAREQ AZIZ IN ROME FEB. 14 (REF A). THE FOLLOWING IS A

SUMMARY OF AZIZ\'S TRIP/STATEMENTS IN ROME SO FAR.

---------------

AZIZ STATEMENTS

---------------

2. (U) EMBASSY SEES NOTHING NEW OR INNOVATIVE IN AZIZ\'S

STATEMENTS SO FAR. HIS RECURRENT THEMES:

-- "WE (IRAQIS) ARE COOPERATING WITH THE UNMOVIC AND IAEA

INSPECTORS, WE ARE DOING OUR BEST."

-- IRAQ\'S MISSILES ARE "WITHIN THE RANGE LIMITS THAT WERE

DECIDED BY THE UN. OUR (IRAQ\'S) MISSILES ARE NOT DANGEROUS.

THE PROBLEM IS THAT IRAQ\'S MISSILES HAVE A VERY SHORT RANGE

AND DO NOT HAVE A GUIDING SYSTEM. THIS IS WHY THEY LAND 5 OR

10 KILOMETERS FROM THE TARGET AND THEY ARE THEREFORE NOT VERY

DANGEROUS."

-- "ALL THOSE WHO BELIEVE IN PEACE AND IN JUSTICE, ALL THOSE

WHO BELIEVE IN GOD, CHRISTIANS AND MUSLIMS, ARE AGAINST THIS

AGGRESSION AND CONTINUE TO MAKE EVERY EFFORT TO PREVENT IT."

-------------

GOI OFFICIALS

-------------

3. (U) PM BERLUSCONI\'S COALITION GOVERNMENT HAS GRANTED AZIZ

ONLY TWO MEETINGS SO FAR: ONE WITH THE LOMBARDY REGIONAL

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 03 ROME 00619 150254Z

PRESIDENT FORMIGONI (REF B) FEB. 13 AND THE OTHER WITH FM

FRATTINI (REFS A AND C) FEB. 14. IN A PRESS CONFERENCE

FOLLOWING HIS MEETING WITH FORMIGONI, AZIZ SAID:"IF WE GIVE

THE INSPECTORS MORE TIME, THE TRUTH WILL BE EVIDENT, BECAUSE

THEY WILL NOT BE ABLE TO FIND WEAPONS OF MASS DESTRUCTION,

SINCE THEY DON\'T EXIST. THEIR (THE AMERICANS) REAL INTENT

HAS A NAME: IT IS OIL AND DOMINATION."

IN THE PRESS CONFERENCE AFTER HIS MEETING WITH AZIZ,

FORMIGONI SAID:

-- "THE INSPECTORS MUST BE ASKED TO GIVE CLEAR ANSWERS, BUT

THEY MUST ALSO BE GIVEN TIME TO GET CLEAR ANSWERS."

-- TAREQ AZIZ "DID NOT COME EMPTY-HANDED. HE CAME WITH

PROPOSALS AND HYPOTHESES, WHICH I CANNOT TALK ABOUT, FOR

FAIRNESS\' SAKE. BUT I GOT THE DISTINCT IMPRESSION THAT THE

SITUATION IS IN MOTION."

-- FORMIGONI NOTED AN "AVAILABILITY ON THE IRAQI GOVERNMENT\'S

PART TO COOPERATE WITH THE UN INSPECTORS. "I THINK GIVING

THE INSPECTORS TIME TO WORK IS RIGHT AND PROPER. THE IRAQI

GOVERNMENT\'S AVAILABILITY IS IMPORTANT, IN ORDER TO GET FROM

THE INSPECTORS ALL THE NECESSARY CLARITY THAT WE ASK OF

THEM," SAID FORMIGONI.

4. (SBU) EMBASSY FINDS IT SIGNIFICANT THAT ROCCO BUTTIGLIONE,

MINISTER WITHOUT PORTFOLIO FOR EU POLICIES, CONSIDERED VERY

CLOSE TO POPE JOHN PAUL II, REFUSED TO MEET WITH TAREQ AZIZ

THIS TIME. BUTTIGLIONE HAD MET WITH AZIZ SEVERAL TIMES IN

THE PAST. HE WAS INVITED BY AZIZ TO BAGHDAD IN AUGUST, BUT

DECLINED THE INVITATION. BUTTIGLIONE SAID HE WANTED THE GOI

TO SPEAK WITH ONE VOICE ON IRAQ.

THE OPPOSITION

--------------

5. (U) IN PRESS STATEMENTS AFTER HIS MEETING FEB. 13 WITH THE

OLIVE TREE (OPPOSITION) LEADERS, AZIZ SAID THAT "THE FACT

THAT THE US DOES NOT CONSIDER THE WORK OF THE UN INSPECTORS

TO BE IMPORTANT SHOWS THAT IT ONLY WANTS TO DOMINATE THAT

AREA OF THE WORLD AND IT WANTS TO CONTROL IRAQ\'S OIL."

THE FOLLOWING IS AN INFORMAL TRANSLATION OF A STATEMENT BY

THE OLIVE TREE CHAMBER OF DEPUTIES GROUP LEADERS AT THE

CONCLUSION OF THEIR MEETING WITH AZIZ:

"DURING THE MEETING, WE REITERATED THE OLIVE TREE\'S POSITION

AGAINST WAR AND IN FAVOR OF MAKING EVERY EFFORT TO FIND A

PEACEFUL SOLUTION TO THE CRISIS. WE ALSO REITERATED THE NEED

THAT HUMAN RIGHTS AND FUNDAMENTAL FREEDOMS BE GUARANTEED IN

IRAQ, AS WELL AS THE BASIC RIGHTS FOR A MODERN DEMOCRACY,

COMPLIANCE WITH THE UN RESOLUTIONS, AND THE RECOGNITION OF

ALL THE NECESSARY WORKING CONDITIONS FOR THE UN INSPECTORS.

THESE ARE FUNDAMENTAL CONDITIONS TO ALLOW FOR THE IRAQI

POLITICAL SYSTEM TO ACQUIRE THE NECESSARY CREDIBILITY AND TO

MAKE IT POSSIBLE TO AVOID WAR."

SEMBLER
