SUBJECT: MFO: ITALY CANNOT PROVIDE REPLACEMENTS FOR US

MEDICAL FORCES

B. ROME 278

PAGE 02 ROME 00323 281702Z

CLASSIFIED BY: POLITICAL MINISTER-COUNSELOR THOMAS COUNTRYMAN FOR REASO

NS 1.5 (B) AND (D)

(C) ON JAN. 27, FRANCESCO TRUPIANO, THE MOD DIPLOMATIC

ADVISOR, REPORTED TO POLMIL OFFICER THAT ITALY DOES NOT

CURRENTLY HAVE THE CAPACITY TO SUPPORT THE USG REQUEST TO

PROVIDE REPLACEMENTS FOR US MEDICAL FORCES IN THE MFO (REF

A.) TRUPIANO STRESSED THAT THE GOI STUDIED THE USG REQUEST

VERY CAREFULLY. HOWEVER, TRUPIANO SAID THAT THE GOI, FOR THE

TIME BEING, IS UNABLE TO SUPPORT OUR MFO REQUEST SINCE THE

GOI:

-- ALREADY COMMITS 10,000 PERSONNEL TO INTERNATIONAL

PEACEKEEPING AND HUMANITARIAN MISSIONS, MOSTLY IN THE BALKANS

AND THE MIDDLE EAST.

-- IS COMMITTING 1,000 ALPINI TROOPS TO OEF.

-- NEEDS TO RESERVE SOME OF ITS FORCES AND ASSETS FOR

NATIONAL CONTINGENCIES RANGING FROM DISASTER RELIEF TO

HOMELAND SECURITY.
