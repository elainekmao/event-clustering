2. (C) SUMMARY: THERE IS NO NEW INFORMATION SUGGESTING

AN IMMINENT PAPAL DEMISE, BUT THE POPE\'S HEALTH

APPEARS TO CONTINUE ITS DECLINE. POST OFFERS SOME

RECOMMENDATIONS ON THE USG\'S ACTIONS SURROUNDING THE

PAPAL FUNERAL AND ELECTION OF A NEW POPE. THE USG

WOULD HAVE NINE DAYS FROM THE DEATH OF THE POPE TO

FORM AND SEND A PRESIDENTIAL DELEGATION TO THE FUNERAL

MASS IN THE VATICAN AND POSSIBLY THE POPE\'S NATIVE

POLAND. END SUMMARY.

3. (C) ALTHOUGH POST HAS NO NEW INFORMATION ABOUT THE

POPE\'S HEALTH THAT IMPELS THE TIMING OF THIS CABLE,

THE POPE\'S STATE OF HEALTH IS WELL-KNOWN AND THE

PROSPECT OF A PAPAL FUNERAL AND A SUBSEQUENT ELECTION

ARE ON THE NEARER RATHER THAN THE FARTHER HORIZON.

POST WISHES THE DEPARTMENT NOT TO BE SURPRISED BY

PROTOCOL URGENCIES ON SHORT NOTICE. AFTER THE DEATH

OF A POPE, THERE IS A PERIOD OF MOURNING OF NINE DAYS

BEFORE THE FUNERAL MASS, WHICH WOULD BE ATTENDED BY

HEADS OF STATE OR THEIR REPRESENTATIVES. THE PROCESS

FOR ELECTING THE NEW POPE WILL BEGIN AFTER BURIAL.

---------------------------

BACKGROUND: THE INTERREGNUM

---------------------------

4. (C) WHEN THE POPE DIES, THE PREFECT OF THE PAPAL

HOUSEHOLD (CURRENTLY AMERICAN BISHOP JAMES HARVEY)

INFORMS THE CARDINAL CHAMBERLAIN (CURRENTLY SPANISH

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 VATICA 02295 01 OF 04 270100Z

CARDINAL EDUARDO MARTINEZ SOMALO), WHO MUST VERIFY THE

DEATH, ARRANGE FOR A DEATH CERTIFICATE AND INFORM THE

CARDINAL-VICAR OF ROME (CURRENTLY ITALIAN CARDINAL

CAMILLO RUINI). THE CARDINAL-VICAR MAKES THE DEATH

PUBLIC; VATICAN RADIO THEN BROADCASTS THE NEWS. THE

VATICAN PRESS OFFICE WOULD PROBABLY HOLD A PRESS

CONFERENCE THAT DAY. THE CARDINAL-CHAMBERLAIN THEN

LOCKS AND SEALS THE POPE\'S PRIVATE APARTMENTS. THE

CARDINAL CHAMBERLAIN, ON BEHALF OF THE COLLEGE OF

CARDINALS ASSUMES RESPONSIBILITY "AD INTERIM" FOR

CHURCH GOVERNMENT, INCLUDING THE FUNERAL AND BURIAL OF

THE POPE AND THE ELECTION OF HIS SUCCESSOR. THREE

CARDINAL ASSISTANTS, SELECTED BY LOT FROM AMONG

CARDINALS ALREADY IN ROME, HELP THE CHAMBERLAIN. THE

HEADS OF VATICAN GOVERNMENT ORGANIZATIONS, WITH SOME

EXCEPTIONS SUCH AS THE CHAMBERLAIN HIMSELF ARE

SUSPENDED FROM EXERCISING THEIR AUTHORITY. THE PAPAL

FUNERAL RITES CONTINUE FOR NINE CONSECUTIVE DAYS.

---------------------------------

TWO FUNERALS? AND AN INAUGURATION

---------------------------------

5. (C) POST RECOMMENDS THAT THE PRESIDENT ATTEND THE

PONTIFF\'S FUNERAL SERVICES IN THE VATICAN. IT IS VERY

POSSIBLE THAT THE POPE\'S WILL DIRECTS THAT HE BE

BURIED IN HIS NATIVE POLAND, A REQUEST THAT THE

VATICAN WOULD HONOR, WE BELIEVE. THE WHITE HOUSE

SHOULD THUS CONSIDER THAT THERE MAY BE A NEED FOR

ANOTHER PRESIDENTIAL DELEGATION FOR BURIAL SERVICES IN

POLAND. IN ADDITION, WE RECOMMEND A HIGH-LEVEL

--------------------------

THE CONCLAVE: PREPARATIONS

--------------------------

7. (C) CARDINALS WILL ARRIVE IN ROME FROM THE WORLD

OVER; THEY WILL HAVE NO PROBLEM WITH HOTEL SPACE, AS

THEY WILL STAY IN THE DOMUS SANTA MARTA FACILITY,

INSIDE THE VATICAN AND REBUILT RECENTLY FOR THEIR USE.

PREVIOUS CONCLAVES INVOLVED THE CARDINALS STAYING IN

CRAMPED ROOMS NEAR THE SISTINE CHAPEL. EVEN WHILE THE

FUNERAL RITES ARE GOING ON, THE CARDINALS MEET IN

"GENERAL CONGREGATIONS" ORGANIZED BY THE CHAMBERLAIN.

AT THIS TIME THE CARDINALS WOULD LIKELY BEGIN

INFORMALLY TO DISCUSS CANDIDATES FOR THE PAPACY.

--------------------

THE CONCLAVE: VOTING

--------------------

8. (C) THOSE CARDINALS WHO ARE ELIGIBLE TO VOTE MUST

BEGIN TO DO SO NO EARLIER THAN FIFTEEN AND NO LATER

THAN TWENTY DAYS AFTER THE DEATH OF THE POPE. NEWS

ORGANIZATIONS HAVE ALREADY OPTIONED CHOICE LOCATIONS

TO STAKE OUT THE VOTING. THE CONCLAVE PROPER BEGINS

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 VATICA 02295 02 OF 04 270101Z

WITH A MORNING MASS IN ST PETER\'S BASILICA, AFTER

WHICH THE CARDINALS GO TO THE SISTINE CHAPEL, WHERE

THEY TAKE AN OATH TO FOLLOW THE ELECTION RULES

(CHANGED BY POPE JOHN PAUL II). THEN THEY CAST THE

FIRST VOTE.

---------------------

HOW LONG IT WILL LAST

----------------------

9. (C) THERE IS NO LIMIT ON HOW LONG THE VOTING MAY

GO ON, BUT IN PRACTICE, IT LIKELY WILL BE A MATTER OF

DAYS. NONE OF THE TEN CONCLAVES SINCE 1831 LASTED

MORE THAN FOUR DAYS. WHEN A CANDIDATE RECEIVES THE

REQUISITE MAJORITY (WHICH VARIES ACCORDING TO HOW MANY

TIMES VOTES HAVE BEEN CAST), THE DEAN OF THE COLLEGE

OF CARDINALS FORMALLY ASKS THE WINNER IF HE ACCEPTS

ELECTION AND BY WHAT NAME HE WISHES TO BE CALLED. THE

CONCLAVE IS THEN OFFICIALLY OVER.

------------

SMOKE RISING

------------

10. (C) ORDINARILY, THE FIRST INDICATION TO THE

OUTSIDE WORLD THAT A NEW POPE HAS BEEN ELECTED COMES

WHEN A PLUME OF WHITE SMOKE RISES FROM THE SISTINE

CHAPEL AS THE LAST BALLOTS ARE BURNT. (FOR BALLOTS

THAT DO NOT RESULT IN THE NEEDED MAJORITY, A CHEMICAL

MIXED WITH THE BALLOTS GIVES THEM A DARK APPEARANCE

WHEN THEY ARE BURNT.) NEWS ORGANIZATIONS WITH PRIME

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 04 VATICA 02295 02 OF 04 270101Z

LOCATIONS WILL BE VIDEOTAPING SMOKE RISING FROM THE

SISTINE CHAPEL. THESE ORGANIZATIONS WITH THEIR ROUND-

THE-CLOCK STAKEOUTS WILL FURTHER EXACERBATE THE HOTEL

ROOM SHORTAGE WE PREDICT FOR ROME. THE MEDIA WILL

WAIT TO CAPTURE ON TAPE THE SENIOR CARDINAL DEACON

PROCLAIMING THE ELECTION ON THE MAIN BALCONY FROM ST

PETERS, OVERLOOKING THE SQUARE WHEN THE NEW POPE

APPEARS TO IMPART HIS BLESSING.

NO CORONATION

--------------

11. (C) POPE JOHN PAUL I (WHOSE REIGN LASTED 33 DAYS)

DISCONTINUED THE ELABORATE CORONATION CEREMONY

PRESCRIBED BY HIS PREDECESSORS. THE NEXT POPE\'S

ELECTION, LIKE THOSE OF JOHN PAUL I AND JOHN PAUL II

IN 1978, SHOULD BE COMPLETED AND CELEBRATED BY TWO

EVENTS. FIRST, THERE WILL MOST LIKELY BE A PUBLIC

"SOLEMN CEREMONY OF INAUGURATION" OF THE NEW PAPACY.

SHORTLY THEREAFTER, THE NEW POPE WOULD TAKE FORMAL

POSSESSION OF HIS PATRIARCHAL BASILICA, WHICH IS ST

JOHN LATERAN, THE "CATHEDRAL" OF ROME, IN A CEREMONY

INVOLVING A PONTIFICAL MASS THERE. THE VATICAN WOULD

NOT EXPECT A HEAD OF STATE TO ATTEND THESE MASSES IF

S/HE ATTENDED THE FUNERAL, BUT A DELEGATION FROM THE

HEAD OF STATE WOULD BE EXPECTED TO ATTEND. EMBASSY

VATICAN SUGGESTS A TEN-PERSON DELEGATION.

HOUSING FOR A PRESIDENTIAL DELEGATION

-------------------------------------

12. (C) WE RECOMMEND THAT THE WHITE HOUSE CONSIDER

THE RESIDENCE OF THE U.S. AMBASSADOR TO THE HOLY SEE

FOR THE PRESIDENT\'S USE DURING HIS STAY. THE

AMBASSADOR\'S RESIDENCE HAS TWO EXTRA BEDROOMS, WITH

ONE OTHER ROOM USABLE AS A SLEEPING ROOM. THE

RESIDENCE HAS A 24-HOUR GUARD AND GATED ENTRY. THE

RESIDENCE HAS A TOTAL OF 1400 SQUARE METERS (15,246

SQUARE FEET).

13. (C) IF THE PRESIDENT DOES NOT STAY AT THE

RESIDENCE, WE SUGGEST HOTELS WHICH ARE CLOSE TO THE

VATICAN, DUE TO PROJECTED EXTREME TRAFFIC CONGESTION:

- CAVALIERE HILTON - 4.6 KLOMETERS FROM THE VATICAN -

312 ROOMS TOTAL. THERE ARE 17 SUITES IN ALL, TWO OF

WHICH ARE "PRESIDENTIAL" AND ONE "ROYAL."

- HOLIDAY INN ST PETER\'S - 4.5 KM FROM THE VATICAN)-

300 ROOMS, AND 25 EXECUTIVE ROOMS.

- HOTEL MINERVA - (2.5 M FROM THE VATICAN)- WITH 118

ROOMS AND 16 SUITES.

- HOTEL AMBASCIATORI - 4 KM FROM THE VATICAN -WITH

150 ROOMS AND 8 SUITES.

- EXCELSIOR HOTEL- 4 KM FROM THE VATICAN - WITH 318

-------------------------------------

GETTING A HOTEL ROOM WILL NOT BE EASY

-------------------------------------

14. (SBU) FOLLOWING MEDIA REPORTS OVER THE PAST

SEVERAL YEARS AND CULMINATING IN RECENT ARTICLES,

MAJOR NEWS ORGANIZATIONS ARE PLANNING FOR PERSON-

INTENSIVE OPERATIONS, WITH ROUND-THE-CLOCK STAKEOUTS

OF THE VATICAN. THEY WILL NEED HUNDREDS OF HOTEL

ROOMS. IT WILL BE CRITICAL FOR THE U.S. DELEGATION TO

MAKE HOTEL RESERVATIONS IMMEDIATELY AFTER LEARNING OF

THE POPE\'S DEATH. IF THE SECRETARY OF STATE COMES FOR

ONE OF THE MASSES, HOTEL SPACE WILL BE EVEN MORE IN

DEMAND.

15. (SBU) IN ADDITION TO THE MEDIA HORDES THAT WILL

DESCEND ON ROME, MOST NATIONS WILL SEND TOP LEVEL

DELEGATIONS WITH MANY HEADS OF STATE. DUE TO A

COMBINATION OF FACTORS, THIS POPE\'S FUNERAL MAY

ATTRACT THE LARGEST NUMBER OF OFFICIAL VISITORS FOR

ANY PAPAL FUNERAL IN HISTORY. OVER TWENTY YEARS OF

HIGH-PROFILE INVOLVEMENT IN FOREIGN AFFAIRS, THE MOST

FOREIGN TRAVEL OF ANY POPE IN HISTORY, THE GREAT

NUMBER OF PERSONAL MEETINGS BETWEEN THIS POPE AND

EXISTING HEADS OF STATE OR GOVERNMENT - ALL THESE

ARGUE FOR THE HIGHEST LEVEL OF GOVERNMENTAL

REPRESENTATION, ACCOMPANIED BY THE INEVITABLE

ENTOURAGES.

16. (C) FOR EXAMPLE, AT THE FUNERAL MASS FOR JOHN

PAUL I, WHOSE 33 DAYS AS POPE HARDLY GAVE HIM THE TIME

TO BUILD UP THE RESPECT AND GOODWILL ENJOYED BY HIS

SUCCESSOR, APPROXIMATELY 47 COUNTRIES SENT

DELEGATIONS. (MANY OTHER NATIONS SENT THEIR RESIDENT

AMBASSADORS) 117 MISSIONS (NATIONAL AND INTERNATIONAL)

WERE REPRESENTED. 121 MISSIONS SENT REPRESENTATIVES

FOR THE MASS CELEBRATING THE BEGINNING OF JOHN PAUL

II\'S MINISTRY AS PONTIFF A FEW WEEKS LATER. 109

MISSIONS SENT REPRESENTATIVES TO THE MASS FOR THE

BEGINNING OF JOHN PAUL I\'S PAPACY, WITH 110 MISSIONS

REPRESENTED AT THE FUNERAL MASS FOR PAUL VI. SINCE

THE 1979 ROUND OF FUNERAL AND ELECTION MASSES, THE

VATICAN HAS ESTABLISHED DIPLOMATIC RELATIONS WITH EVEN

MORE NATIONS. THERE WILL CLEARLY BE A HEAVY INFLUX OF

FOREIGN DIGNITARIES AND THEIR ENTOURAGES, NOT TO

MENTION ITALIAN OFFICIALS FROM ALL OVER THE COUNTRY

AND HORDES FROM POLAND.

ADMINISTRATIVE SUPPORT

----------------------

18. (C) EMBASSY VATICAN AND EMBASSY ROME ARE WELL-

STAFFED TO PROVIDE VISIT SUPPORT FOR A VISIT OF THIS

TYPE, INCLUDING A SECRET SERVICE OFFICE. EMBASSY

VATICAN WOULD HAVE OVERALL VISIT CONTROL ON THE

GROUND, BUT WOULD HAVE EXTENSIVE ADMINISTRATIVE

SUPPORT FROM EMBASSY ROME.

19. (U) EMBASSIES ROME AND VATICAN WOULD MAKE

VEHICLES AVAILABLE FOR THE VISIT. THERE ARE A NUMBER

OF LARGE RENTAL VEHICLE COMPANIES (HERTZ, EUROPCAR,

ETC.) IN ROME THAT COULD IN THE AGGREGATE SUPPLY THE

REST OF THE VEHICLES NEEDED.
