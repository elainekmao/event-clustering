SUBJECT: U.S. DIPLOMAT ASSASSINATED IN AMMAN 10/28: SITREP 1

Classified By: Ambassador Edward W. Gnehm. Reasons 1.5 (b,d).

1. (C) Between 0705 and 0715 this morning (October 28), an

individual approached USAID employee Larry Foley as he was

entering his vehicle to depart his residence and fired

multiple shots at close range. Assailant immediately fled

the scene on foot. Mr. Foley appears to have died instantly.

2. (C) The RSO and other Embassy personnel, as well as the

PSD, including PSD national commander Shurdom, reacted

immediately. EAC is underway.

3. (U) We will update department as additional facts become

available.

GNEHM
