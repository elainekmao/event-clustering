SUBJECT: KUWAIT\'S PROPOSED INTERNATIONAL CONFORMITY

CERTIFICATION PROGRAM (ICCP)

REF: STATE 16459

Post has requested meetings for the Ambassador with Salah

Khorshid, Kuwait\'s Minister of Commerce and Industry, and

separately with Shaykh Dr. Mohammed Al-Sabah, Kuwait\'s

Minister of State for Foreign Affairs, to discuss reftel

points and to request that Kuwait postpone the date of

implementation of its proposed ICCP. On January 20, Post

delivered reftel points as a non-paper to Khalid Al-Fahed,

Director of Standards and Metrology at Kuwait\'s Public

Authority for Industry, and requested that Al-Fahed share

this information with appropriate GOK officials. Post will

report responses on this issue.

JONES
