SUBJECT: OPCW ITALIANS CONCERNED ABOUT LINKAGE TO

CONTRIBUTION PAYMENTS

REF: A. A) STATE 53938

B. B) STATE 51363

CLASSIFIED BY: POLMIN COUNSELOR TOM COUNTRYMAN FOR REASONS 1.5(B), (D)

1. (C) ITALY DOUBTS THE REMOVAL OF DG BUSTANI WILL BE SETTLED

BY THE OPCW EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE AND EXPECTS IT WILL HAVE TO

BE RESOLVED AT THE STATES PARTIES MEETING THIS FALL,

ACCORDING TO MFA ARMS CONTROL AND DISARMAMENT OFFICE DIRECTOR

ALESSANDRO CEVESE. CEVESE MET WITH POL-MIL OFFICER JUST

AFTER TALKING WITH ITALY\'S OPCW REPRESENTATIVE ON MARCH 21.

ITALY CONTINUES TO SUPPORT THE NO CONFIDENCE MOTION, CEVESE

SAID, BUT THE PROCESS HAS BEEN VERY DIVISIVE FOR THE

EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE AND COULD END UP BEING COSTLY AS WELL.

2. (C) CEVESE MENTIONED ONE THEORY CURRENTLY CIRCULATING THAT

THE U.S. EFFORT TO REMOVE BUSTANI IS AN ATTEMPT TO WEAKEN THE

OPCW IN ORDER TO STRENGTHEN THE AUSTRALIA GROUP. WHILE ITALY

RECOGNIZES THAT BUSTANI\'S REMOVAL IS SOUGHT AS A MEANS OF

RESTORING THE OPCW\'S VIABILITY, SOME COUNTRIES SUSPECT THE

U.S. HAS OTHER MOTIVES, CEVESE COMMENTED. RUMOURS THAT THE

U.S. MIGHT NOT PAY ITS CONTRIBUTION OR MIGHT PRESSURE THE

ORGANIZATION TO CEASE OPERATIONS FOR SEVERAL MONTHS IF

BUSTANI IS NOT REMOVED UNDERMINE U.S. STATEMENTS THAT ITS

OBJECTIVE IS TO STRENGTHN THE OPCW, HE SAID. THE GOI WOULD

BE VERY CONCERNED IF THE U.S. IS CONSIDERING EITHER OF THESE

OPTIONS, ACCORDING TO CEVESE. SUCH ACTIONS WOULD FURTHER

DELAY DECISIONS ON RESOLVING THE FINANCIAL CRISIS FACING THE

ORGANIZATION. ITALY AGREES WITH THE U.S. THAT BUSTANI\'S

RESIGNATION IS NECESSARY, CEVESE SAID, BUT LINKING HIS

REMOVAL TO CONTRIBUTIONS PAYMENTS OR THE OPCW OPERATION WOULD

BE A SERIOUS MISTAKE. HE MADE IT CLEAR THAT ITALY INTENDS TO

PAY ITS CONTRIBUTION WHETHER OR NOT BUSTANI IS REMOVED.

POL-MIL OFFICER, DRAWING FROM REFS A, B AND OTHERS,

EMPHASIZED THE U.S. COMMITMENT TO THE CWC AND THE OPCW, AND

STRESSED THAT STRENGTHENING THE ORGANIZATION IS THE ONLY

MOTIVE BEHIND U.S. ACTIONS.

3. (C) COMMENT. THE GOI SUPPORTS A NO CONFIDENCE MOTION

AGAINST DG BUSTANI, IN PART BECAUSE IT AGREES BUSTANI HAS

FAILED TO REPAIR THE DAMAGE DONE TO THE ORGANIZATION BUT ALSO

BECAUSE IT RECOGNIZES THAT BUSTANI HAS LOST THE CONFIDENCE OF

THE U.S. THAT SAID, THE ITALIANS WOULD HAVE PREFERRED

BROADER CONSULTATIONS WITH OPCW MEMBERS BEFORE THE U.S.

MOUNTED THE EFFORT TO REMOVE BUSTANI. THE GOI\'S PARAMOUNT
