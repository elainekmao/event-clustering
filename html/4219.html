SUBJECT: NIGERIA: MOD DANJUMA SAYS SERTI OFR BATTALION

WILL SOON ROTATE TO SIERRA LEONE

CLASSIFIED BY AMBASSADOR HOWARD F. JETER; REASONS 1.5

(B) AND (D).

1. (C) On two separate occasions within the past

several weeks, Minister of Defense T.Y. Danjuma has

assured me that the OFR trained battalion currently in

Serti will be rotated out to participate in UNAMSIL.

Danjuma said that he has reiterated the order for the

rotation and that there had been no internal army

resistance to his orders.

2. (SBU) The battalion, according to Danjuma, was in

its final stage of departure preparations and would be

leaving for Sierra Leone very soon.
