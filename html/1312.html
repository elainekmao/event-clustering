SUBJECT: BLEAK WORLD BANK ASSESSMENT OF PROSPECTS FOR

GOVERNMENT OF ZIMBABWE IMPLEMENTING SOUND ECONOMIC

POLICIES

CLASSIFIED BY AMB MCDONALD. REASONS 1.5 (B) AND (D).

1. (C) SUMMARY: DESK OFFICER, IN HARARE ON AN

ORIENTATION TRIP, AND ECONOFF MET WITH ROGIER VAN DEN

BRINK, WORLD BANK DEPUTY RESIDENT REPRESENTATIVE AND

SENIOR ECONOMIST IN HARARE ON OCTOBER 5 TO OBTAIN HIS

PERSPECTIVE ON THE ECONOMIC SITUATION IN ZIMBABWE. VAN

DEN BRINK MADE CLEAR DURING THE MEETING THAT THE

GOVERNMENT OF ZIMBABWE (GOZ) WAS NOT TAKING THE STEPS

NECESSARY TO RESOLVE THE COUNTRY\'S SERIOUS ECONOMIC

PROBLEMS. JUST THIS WEEK, ZIMBABWE WAS PLACED IN NON-

ACCRUAL STATUS BY THE WORLD BANK FOR NOT MAKING PAYMENTS

FOR SIX MONTHS. THIS STATUS PUTS ZIMBABWE AT THE BOTTOM

RANKING OF COUNTRIES WITH THE WORLD BANK, INCLUDING

LIBERIA, IRAQ, AND BURMA. VAN DEN BRINK DOES NOT EXPECT

ANY DRAMATIC CHANGES IN ECONOMIC POLICIES FROM THE GOZ,

BECAUSE TOP GOZ OFFICIALS VIEW ZIMBABWE\'S ECONOMIC

PROBLEMS AS LARGELY BEING THE RESULT OF A BRITISH

CONSPIRACY. END SUMMARY.

---------------------------------

ALL WORLD BANK PROJECTS SUSPENDED

---------------------------------

2. (C) ROGIER VAN DEN BRINK, THE WORLD BANK\'S DEPUTY

RESIDENT REPRESENTATIVE IN HARARE, TOLD DESK OFFICER AND

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 HARARE 05677 01 OF 02 091255Z

ECONOFF ON OCTOBER 5 THAT ALL EIGHT OF THE WORLD BANK\'S

PROJECTS IN ZIMBABWE HAVE BEEN SUSPENDED BECAUSE OF NON-

PAYMENT, AND ZIMBABWE HAS ENTERED INTO NON-ACCRUAL

STATUS. THIS PLACES ZIMBABWE AT THE BOTTOM OF THE WORLD

BANK RATING SYSTEM FOR FINANCIAL WORTHINESS, IN THE

COMPANY OF STATES SUCH AS LIBERIA, IRAQ, AND BURMA, AND

MEANS THAT ZIMBABWE IS NOW LARGELY ISOLATED FROM

INTERNATIONAL FINANCING. VAN DEN BRINK SAID HIS OFFICE

WAS NOT CURRENTLY PURSUING NEW STRATEGIES TO HELP THE

GOZ OUT OF ITS CURRENT DIRE ECONOMIC PLIGHT, AS THE GOZ

KNOWS THE ECONOMIC ACTIONS THAT NEED TO BE TAKEN, BUT IS

NOT TAKING THEM.

----------------------

DOING EVERYTHING WRONG

----------------------

3. (C) VAN DEN BRINK STATED THAT THE GOZ MILLENIUM

ECONOMIC RECOVERY PLAN WAS NOT WELL THOUGHT OUT, AND

THAT ACTIONS ARE BEING TAKEN BY THE GOZ FOR POLITICAL

REASONS THAT ARE FURTHER DAMAGING THE ECONOMY. THESE

INCLUDE THE FAILURE TO MOVE FORWARD ON PRIVATIZATION OF

PARASTATALS, THE MUSHROOMING GOVERNMENT DEFICIT, AND

ARTIFICIALLY SUPPRESSING THE INTEREST RATE TO SPUR

ECONOMIC GROWTH. HE INSISTED THAT THE MINISTRY OF

FINANCE ECONOMIC TEAM KNOWS WHAT NEEDS TO BE DONE FOR A

SOUND ECONOMIC PROGRAM, BUT APPARENTLY IS BEING

PREVENTED FROM TAKING CRUCIAL STEPS BY OTHER MINISTERS

AND PRESIDENT MUGABE.

DEVALUATION OF THE ZIM DOLLAR

-----------------------------

4. (C) AN EXAMPLE OF COUNTERPRODUCTIVE ACTIONS BEING

TAKEN BY THE GOZ IS THE CRACKDOWN ON THE PARALLEL

CURRENCY MARKET. VAN DEN BRINK STATED THAT RIGHT AFTER

THE GOZ DEVALUED THE ZIM DOLLAR FROM 36:1 TO 50:1, THE

GOZ CRACKED DOWN ON THE PARALLEL MARKET, WHERE THE ZIM

DOLLAR HAD BEEN TRADING AT BETWEEN 63:1 AND 65:1 AND

WHERE 70 TO 80 PERCENT OF ALL FOREIGN CURRENCY

TRANSACTIONS WERE OCCURRING. ACCORDING TO VAN DEN

BRINK, THIS ACTION RESULTED IN AN INCREASE IN THE

VALUATION OF THE ZIM DOLLAR FOR THE MAJORITY OF

INDIVIDUALS AND COMPANIES INVOLVED IN FOREIGN CURRENCY

EXCHANGE WHO HAD BEEN USING THE PARALLEL MARKET, AS THEY

NOW HAD TO GET THE ZIM DOLLAR AT THE OFFICIAL RATE OF

50:1 VERSUS THE PRIOR PARALLEL MARKET RATE OF 63-65:1.

HE STATED THAT THE MDC PLAN FOR ECONOMIC REFORM IS "MORE

IMF THAN THE IMF\'S PLAN", BUT HE QUESTIONED WHETHER THE

MDC WOULD HAVE THE POLITICAL SUPPORT TO IMPLEMENT IT.

VAN DEN BRINK BELIEVES THAT THE WHITE ZIMBABWEAN

BUSINESS COMMUNITY THAT SUPPORTS THE MDC, AND RECOGNIZES

THE NEED FOR MAJOR STRUCTURAL CHANGES IN THE ECONOMY,

HAS NO REAL POLITICAL POWER. THE MDC\'S REAL POWER IS

STILL TO BE FOUND IN THE LABOR UNION MOVEMENT FROM WHICH

IT EMERGED, AND LABOR LIKELY WOULD OPPOSE MUCH OF THE

MDC\'S CURRENT ECONOMIC PLAN WERE THE PARTY TO GAIN

POWER.

----------------------

ANTI-MUGABE CONSPIRACY

----------------------

6. (C) VAN DEN BRINK STATED THAT PRESIDENT MUGABE AND

MANY GOZ MINISTERS BELIEVE THAT ZIMBABWE\'S CURRENT

ECONOMIC PROBLEMS AND THE FAILURE OF THE IMF AND WORLD

BANK TO PROVIDE NEEDED FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE ARE ALL PART

OF A BRITISH CONSPIRACY TO FORCE MUGABE TO RESIGN. VAN

DEN BRINK BELIEVES THAT IN THIS POLITICAL CLIMATE IT IS

IMPORTANT THAT THE USG BE CAREFUL IN ITS STATEMENTS ON

ZIMBABWE SO AS NOT TO GIVE THE GOZ FODDER FOR USING IT

AS A SCAPEGOAT FOR ITS OWN ECONOMIC PROBLEMS. HE ALSO

CAUTIONED THAT THE MDC HAS TO BE CAREFUL IN WHAT IT

SAYS, BECAUSE HE BELIEVES THAT MUGABE AND THE GOZ ARE

LOOKING FOR AN EXCUSE TO LAUNCH A VIOLENT CRACKDOWN ON

THE MDC OR TO IMPOSE MARTIAL LAW.

7. (C) COMMENT: THE RESREP\'S COMMENTS ON THE FUNDAMENTAL

STRUCTURAL PROBLEMS OF THE ZIMBABWEAN ECONOMY WERE RIGHT

ON TARGET AND HIS ANALYSIS OF THE COUNTERPRODCUTIVE

RESULTS OF SOME OF THE GOZ\'S ECONOMIC RECOVERY PLAN

ACTIONS WERE INSIGHTFUL. IT IS CLEAR THAT THE GOZ IS

FAILING TO ADDRESS THE FUNDAMENTAL STRUCTURAL PROBLEMS

OF THE ECONOMY, AND AS A RESULT THE ECONOMIC SITUATION

IS LIKELY TO CONTINUE TO WORSEN IN THE NEAR TERM. THE

FACT THAT ZIMBABWE HAS NOW FALLEN TO THE BOTTOM OF THE

WORLD BANK RATING SYSTEM, IN THE SAME CATEGORY AS

LIBERIA, IRAQ, AND BURMA, IS COMPELLING EVIDENCE OF HOW

BAD THE GOZ\'S ECONOMIC MISMANAGEMENT HAS BECOME. END

COMMENT.
