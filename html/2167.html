SUBJECT: NIGERIA: AF COUNTERTERRORISM CONVENTIONS

REF: STATE 180306

We delivered reftel points to MFA Director of West African

Affairs Olukorede Willoughby. Willoughby reiterated that

Nigeria, based on President Obasanjo\'s personal initiative,

wanted to do all it all it could to fight terrorism.

However, he said the Ministry would take a serious look at

the treaties still unsigned or unratified and let us know

what action the Ministry would recommend the GON take.
