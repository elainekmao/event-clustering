SUBJECT: KUWAITIS ARGUE TAPE OFFERS PROOF OF BIN LADEN\'S

GUILT

REF: KUWAIT 3757

1. TWO INFLUENTIAL KUWAITIS WROTE COLUMNS RECENTLY ARGUING

THAT THE TAPE OF BIN LADEN RELEASED BY THE DEFENSE

DEPARTMENT CONSTITUTED UNDENIABLE EVIDENCE OF HIS

RESPONSIBILITY FOR THE SEPTEMBER 11 ATTACKS. A MEMBER OF

THE MUSLIM BROTHERHOOD AND WELL-KNOWN COLUMNIST, FAISAL AL-

ZAMEL, STATED THAT BIN LADEN HAD "FULLY CONVICTED HIMSELF IN

THE VIDEOTAPE." SAMI AL-NUSF, THE MEDIA ADVISOR TO ACTING

PRIME MINISTER AND FOREIGN MINISTER SHAYKH SABAH, RIDICULED

BIN LADEN AND HIS DREAM-BASED ACTIVITIES. BELOW ARE KEY

EXCERPTS FROM THE COLUMNS.

--------------------

"THE BIN LADEN TAPE"

--------------------

2. THE KUWAITI DAILY "AL-ANBA" PUBLISHED DECEMBER 19 A

COLUMN BY ISLAMIST FAISAL AL-ZAMEL. HE ARGUED, "OSAMA BIN

LADEN HAS FULLY CONVICTED HIMSELF IN THE VIDEOTAPE.

THEREFORE, THE CREDIBILITY OF THE TAPE SHOULD NOT BE DOUBTED

BECAUSE IT IS AS CLEAR AS DAYLIGHT."

-----------------------

"THE DEAD IN TORA BORA"

-----------------------

3. AL-ANBA ALSO PUBLISHED DECEMBER 19 A COLUMN BY SAMI

ABDUL-LATIF AL-NUSF, THE MEDIA ADVISOR TO SHAYKH SABAH. AL-

NUSF ARGUED: "WE WONDERED IN THE PAST ABOUT THE BASIS UPON

WHICH BIN LADEN BASED HIS WAR AND THE ENSUING VICTORY HE

EXPECTED. THE ANSWER CAME IN THE VIDEOTAPE WHERE IT BECAME

EVIDENT THAT THIS OPERATION WAS BASED ON THE DREAMS SEEN BY

BIN LADEN\'S FOLLOWERS. IS THIS THE ISLAMIC PROJECT FOR THE

AGE OF COMPUTERS, SPACE, AND GENETIC ENGINEERING? IS THIS

THE ISLAMIC PROJECT SEEN BY THESE \'DISSIDENTS\' (ARABIC TERM

USED TO REFER TO THE DISSIDENTS DURING THE PROPHET MOHAMED\'S

ERA) AS THE ANSWER FOR HUMANITY\'S DILEMMAS AND THE

CHALLENGES OF THIS NEW AGE?"
