SUBJECT: NUCLEAR PHYSICS LAB SHUT DOWN FOR ALLEGED

POLLUTION

1. ON MAY 30, THE PRESS REPORTED THAT THE NATIONAL

INSTITUTE OF NUCLEAR PHYSICS (INFN) GRAN SASSO LABORATORY

WAS SHUT DOWN FOR ENVIRONMENTAL POLLUTION. IN PARTICULAR,

THE MAGISTRATE DECIDED TO KEEP THE HALL C SEALED, WHERE THE

BOREXINO EXPERIMENT IS AND FROM WHERE PSEUDOCUMENE LEAKED

INTO A RIVER LAST AUGUST. NOTE. THE LEAK WAS DUE TO A

"HUMAN MISTAKE" - ACTUALLY TO LACK OF PERSONNEL BECAUSE OF

THE SUMMER HOLIDAY - NOT A TO DAMAGED OR UNSAFE FILTERS.

END NOTE.

2. HOWEVER, ON MAY 29 INFN DECIDED TO SUSPEND "ALL RESEARCH

ACTIVITIES WHICH IMPLY THE USE OF ANY KIND OF LIQUID". INFN

PRESIDENT IAROCCI HAS ASKED THE COMPETENT AUTHORITY TO

URGENTLY INSPECT ALL THE LABS AND FIX WHATEVER NEEDS TO BE

REPAIRED SO THAT THE RESEARCH ACTIVITY COULD START AGAIN AS

SOON AS POSSIBLE. ACCORDING TO IAROCCI\'S ASSISTANT, THIS IS

THE ONLY WAY TO STOP THE CONTINUOUS ATTACKS AND ACCUSATIONS

FROM SEVERAL GREEN NGO\'S. SKODON
