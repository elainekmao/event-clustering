SUBJECT: THE MUSLIM BROTHERHOOD: THE AFGHANISTAN CONNECTION

REF: A) STATE 291823, B) DUSHANBE 1176

1. CONFIDENTIAL - ENTIRE TEXT.

2. INTRODUCTION/SUMMARY: WE KNOW LITTLE OF INDIGENOUS

MUSLIM BROTHERHOOD ORGANIZATIONS IN AFGHANISTAN. ON THE

OTHER HAND THERE IS A LONG HISTORY OF NON-AFGHAN MUSLIM

BROTHERHOOD ORGANIZATIONS ("IKHWAN") OPERATING

IN AFGHANISTAN USUALLY IN CONJUNCTION WITH ONE OR MORE OF THE

FUNDAMENTALIST AFGHAN TANZIMS. WE OFFER THE FOLLOWING WITH THE

CAVEAT THAT IT LACKS THE DETAIL WHICH SHOULD BE AVAILABLE

ELSEWHERE. END INTRODUCTION/SUMMARY.

TRENDS IN IKHWANI ACTIVITY IN AFGHANISTAN

-----------------------------------------

3. THE FOLLOWING REFLECTS OUR UNDERSTANDING OF RECENT

TRENDS IN IKHWAN ACTIVITY IN AFGHANISTAN. WE BELIEVE

THAT THERE IS AN ETHNIC DIVISION OF IKHWANIS IN

AFGHANISTAN WITH PASHTUNS COALESCING

AROUND GULBUDDIN AND TAJIKS UNITING BEHIND AHMED SHAH MASOUD.

EVER SINCE THE MURDER OF OVER 30 SHURA NAZAR COMMANDERS BY

GULBUDDIN\'S COMMANDERS IN 1989, THERE HAS BEEN A MOVEMENT OF

HEZBI ISLAMI - GULBUDDIN COMMANDERS WHO ARE ETHNIC TAJIKS

TOWARD AHMED SHAH MASOUD. THIS TREND ACCELERATED IN 1992-93 WITH

THE GROWING CONFRONTATION BETWEEN GULBUDDIN AND MASOUD. THE

RESULT HAS BEEN GROWING INFLUENCE OF THE IKHWANI WING WITHIN

MASOUD\'S FOLLOWERS IN SHURA NAZAR AND MORE GENERALLY WITHIN

JAMIAT-I ISLAMI.

4. AS REF B SOUGHT TO ELABORATE, THERE APPEARS TO BE

SIGNIFICANT DIVISION WITHIN THE MUSLIM BROTHERHOOD AND

WITHIN THE INTERNATIONAL ISLAMIC FUNDAMENTALIST NETWORK

GENERALLY OVER THE QUESTION OF WHETHER TO SUPPORT GULBUDDIN

OR AHMED SHAH MASOUD.

IKHWANI AND OTHER FUNDAMENTALIST ISLAMIC ACTIVITY

IN AFGHANISTAN

--------------------------------------------- -----

(GAMA AL ISLAMIA)

5. GAMA AL ISLAMIA AND GAMA AL JIHAD HAVE HAD SENIOR PARTY

FIGURES IN AFGHANISTAN SINCE THE EARLY-MID 1980\'S. GAMA

AL JIHAD HAS IN THE PAST HAD TIES TO (AND FINANCIAL SUPPORT

FROM) THE ARAB FUNDAMENTALIST FINANCIER ASMANA BIN LADIN.

GAMA AL ISLAMIA WAS LONG UNDER THE DIRECTION/INFLUENCE OF

TWO INDIVIDUALS WHO FUNCTIONED AS COMMANDERS AS WELL AS

PARTY CADRE: THESE WERE MOHAMMED SHAUQI ISLAMBULLI (WHOSE

BROTHER ORCHESTRATED THE SADAT ASSASSINATION) AND TALUT

FUAD KASSEM. ACCORDING TO ARAB JOURNALISTS BASED IN

PESHAWAR, THE TWO GAMA AL ISLAMIA LEADERS HAD GOOD

REPUTATIONS AS COMMANDERS AND LED FORCES OF APPROXIMATELY

300.

6. GAMA AL ISLAMIA WAS INVOLVED IN THE FAILED 1989

OFFENSIVE AT JALALABAD, PLANNED AND ORGANIZED BY

THE ISID. GAMA AL ISLAMIA WAS ALSO INVOLVED IN THE

ULTIMATELY SUCCESSFUL 1992 DRIVE ON KHOST. GAMA AL ISLAMIA

WAS TIGHTLY ORGANIZED ON A CELL-LIKE BASIS AND SHUNNED

PUBLICITY. MUCH OF THE LITTLE WE KNOW OF THIS ORGANIZATION\'S

OPERATIONS SUGGESTS IT TENDED TO CONFINE ITSELF TO

NANGARHAR AND PAKTIA PROVINCES (THOUGH THIS MAY REFLECT

THE LIMITED RANGE OF KNOWLEDGE OF OUR SOURCES).

7. OUR INFORMATION SUGGESTS THAT TALUT WENT TO SUDAN

IN 1992, AS RECENTLY AS FEBRUARY 1993, ISLAMABULI WAS

IN JALALABAD WHERE (IN FEBRUARY) HE GAVE AN INTERVIEW

TO A LONDON-BASED ARAB NEWSPAPER.

8. ANOTHER KEY FIGURE IN THE GAMA AL ISLAMIA IS

SHEIK OMAR ABDUL RAHMAN, WHOSE ALLEGED INVOLVEMENT IN THE

WORLD TRADE CENTER BOMBING BROUGHT GAMA AL ISLAMIA

INTERNATIONAL ATTENTION. SHEIK OMAR OPERATED OUT OF

PESHAWAR IN 1988 AND IN 1990 -- AND POSSIBLY IN INTERVENING

PERIODS.

(HADIMAT ISLAMI)

9. HADIMAT ISLAMI, ALSO KNOWN AS THE "SERVICE OFFICE,"

WAS THE FIRST INTERNATIONALIST ISLAMIC OFFICE ESTABLISHED

TO ASSIST THE AFGHAN JIHAD. ITS PRINCIPAL LEADER

WAS ABDULLAH AZAM, WHO WAS KILLED IN A CAR BOMB

ASSASSINATION IN PESHAWAR IN 1991. THE SERVICE BUREAU,

WHICH WORKED WITH BUT MAY NOT HAVE BEEN UNDER THE

CONTROL OF THE MUSLIM BROTHERHOOD, OPERATED REST HOUSES

IN THE PESHAWAR AREA FOR COURIERS AND FIGHTERS

TRAVELLING TO AND FROM AFGHANISTAN.

COMMENT:

10. THE PUTATIVE CONCORDAT BETWEEN GENERAL DOSTUM\'S NORTHERN

ALLIANCE AND JAMIAT-I ISLAMI (AND SHURA NAZAR) AGAINST GULBUDDIN

POSES A DILEMMA FOR TAJIK IKHWANI\'S WHO MUST CHOOSE BETWEEN THEIR

ETHNIC KIN AND THEIR IDEOLOGICAL BROTHERS -- THE IKHWANI\'S WITH

GULBUDDIN. MAKING THE CHOICE EVEN MORE DIFFICULT IS THE

BACKGROUND OF EX-NAJIBULLAH LEADER DOSTUM, WHOSE DEVOTION TO ISLAM

IS UNDERSTANDABLY SUSPECT AND WHO IS SURROUNDED BY MANY WATTAN

PARTY CADRE WHO SOUGHT HIS PROTECTION AFTER NAJIBULLAH\'S FALL.
