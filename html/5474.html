SUBJECT: CENTRAL BANK GOVERNOR CONFIRMS STATUS OF RAFIDAIN BANK IN UAE

REF: TREASURY DTG 221921Z JAN 03

1. (U) Classified by Charge D\'Affaires Thomas Williams for reasons 1.5

(b) and (d).

2. (C) In a meeting with Charge on February 3, UAE Central Bank

Governor Sultan Al- Suweidi confirmed that a single branch of Rafidain

Bank operates in Abu Dhabi, but its activities are very limited and

closely scrutinized by the Central Bank. According to the Governor, the

bank does not conduct retail banking operations, but rather manages a

static asset portfolio of USD $3-4 million (excluding an approximate

USD $11 million in reserves required by the Central Bank).

follows: P.O. Box 2727, Abu Dhabi, phone numbers +971 (2)

6335882/6335883/6310302/6326996 and 6323231 (manager\'s line).
