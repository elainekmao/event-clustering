SUBJECT: GOI\'S REACTION TO SENDING A COORDINATED U.S. AND

EU MESSAGE TO ISRAEL AND THE PALESTINIANS

REF: A) STATE 23988 B) ROME 489

CLASSIFIED BY: POL MC TOM COUNTRYMAN. REASON:1.5 (B)(D)

1. (C) ON THE EVE OF THE EU FOREIGN MINISTERS MEETING IN

SPAIN, POL MC DELIVERED THE POINTS IN REF A TO LUCA

FERRARI, THE MFA OFFICE DIRECTOR FOR THE MIDDLE EAST.

FERRARI FULLY AGREED WITH THE US MESSAGE AND STRESSED THAT

THE MFA IS CONVINCED THAT THE ONLY MESSAGE ARAFAT NEEDS TO

HEAR RIGHT NOW IS TO END THE VIOLENCE.

2. (C) HOWEVER, FERRARI PREDICTED THAT MOST EU PARTNERS

WILL THINK DIFFERENTLY. HE BELIEVED THAT FRANCE, GERMANY,

AND SPAIN, TOGETHER WITH SOLANA, HAVE FORMED A BLOC WHICH

WOULD PUSH FOR THE FRENCH PROPOSAL OF ELECTIONS IN

PALESTINIAN TERRITORIES. (NOTE: FERRARI COMPARED THIS BLOC

TO A COUPLE HAVING MARITAL PROBLEMS THAT DECIDES TO HAVE A

BABY TO SOLVE ITS PROBLEMS. END NOTE).

3. (C) THE ITALIAN MFA HAD MIXED FEELINGS ABOUT THE FRENCH

PROPOSAL, SAID FERRARI. ON ONE HAND, IT COULD POSITIVELY

CHANGE THE DYNAMICS OF THE PALESTINIAN POLITICAL SYSTEM.

ON THE OTHER HAND, FERRARI KNEW THAT IT WOULD NOT BE

FEASIBLE TO ORGANIZE AND CARRY OUT ELECTIONS IN THE

PALESTINIAN TERRITORIES UNTIL THE SECURITY SITUATION HAD

STABILIZED

4. (C) AT THE FEB 8 MEETING IN SPAIN, FERRARI PREDICTED

THAT ONLY THE UK WAS LIKELY TO SPEAK UP AGAINST THE FRENCH

PROPOSAL. OTHER EU STATES WOULD BE RELUCTANT TO DO SO

SINCE THEY HAD NO COUNTER PROPOSALS. AS FOR ITALY, FERRARI

COULD NOT PREDICT WHAT PM AND ACTING FM BERLUSCONI WOULD DO

SINCE HE FACED THE SAME DILEMMA AS OTHER EU STATES OF

WANTING THE EU TO DO SOMETHING TO HELP BUT NOT HAVING A

CONCRETE SOLUTION TO OFFER. (NOTE: EMBASSY ALSO SHARED

POINTS IN REF A WITH THE PM\'S OFFICE BUT DID NOT HAVE A

CHANCE TO DISCUSS THEM DIRECTLY. END NOTE).

5. (C) FERRARI FINALLY PREDICTED THAT THE FEB 8 MEETING
