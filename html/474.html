2. BEGIN TEXT. ON 6 NOVEMBER, AN EC MONITORING

TEAM VISITED THE GRUBISNO POLJE AREA TO INVESTIGATE

A REPORT BY THE PRESIDENCY OF THE SFR YUGOSLAVIA

3. THE TEAM WERE ESCORTED BY THE PERSONNEL FROM THE

CROATIANS HAD BEEN KILLED (16 IN THE VILLAGES

DONE BY SERBIAN IRREGULARS (CHETNIKS) MADE UP FROM

AMONG THE LOCAL SERBIAN VILLAGERS. WITH THE ADVANCE

OF THE CNG FORCES, THE SERBIAN FORCES, SUPPORTED BY

YPA UNITS, HAD WITHDRAWN TOGETHER WITH MOST OF THE

LOCAL SERBIAN POPULACE FROM THE VILLAGES CONCERNED.

SUBSEQUENTLY VISITED THE VILLAGES OF VELIKA

PERATOVICA, D RASENICA AND IVANOVO SELO; THEY ALSO
