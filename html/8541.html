SUBJECT: DEMARCHE ON STROVILIA DELIVERED

REF: SECSTATE 152362

(U) Classified by DCM Robert S. Deutsch. Reason: 1.5 (b,d).

1. (SBU) We presented reftel demarche to Ethem Barkan Oz,

first secretary in Cyprus Directorate General, who undertook

to convey USG concerns to Cyprus DG Ertugrul Apakan.

2. (C) Implying that he did not understand why the USG was

making the demarche, Oz denied vehemently that Turkish

Cypriots are breaking any pre-existing agreement with the UN

or Cypriot government. Oz explained that the Turkish

Cypriots had only occupied the single -- and abandoned, he

claimed -- home in Strovilia to help facilitate the flow of

people between the two sides. Further, Oz argued that unless

the Turkish Cypriots remain in the house, they will not be

able to keep the gate open. By lodging complaints with

UNFICYP about Strovilia, Oz asserted, the Greek Cypriots are

spoiling the current positive atmosphere on the island.
