SUBJECT: ALEMANNO TO SEMBLER: LESS OPEN ON BIOTECH

REF: A. ROME 1457

B. ROME 1697

C. ROME 1726

CLASSIFIED BY: AMBASSADOR SEMBLER FOR REASONS 1.5 (B AND D)

1. (SBU) SUMMARY. AMBASSADOR MET MAY 17 WITH ITALIAN

MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE ALEMANNO TO DELIVER SECRETARY

VENEMAN\'S LETTER ON ALEMANNO\'S BIOTECH WHITE PAPER AND LAY

THE GROUNDWORK FOR THE MAY 27 MEETING BETWEEN PRESIDENT

BUSH AND PRIME MINISTER BERLUSCONI. ALEMANNO INDICATED

THAT HIS POSITION ON AGRICULTURAL BIOTECHNOLOGY IS LESS

OPEN TODAY THAN WHEN HE AND THE AMBASSADOR MET LAST

JANUARY. THERE APPEARS TO BE A SMALL OPPORTUNITY FOR

COLLABORATIVE BIOTECH RESEARCH. THE EMBASSY WILL CONTINUE

TO WORK WITH MINAG STAFF TO SECURE ALEMANNO\'S SUPPORT FOR

AN AGREEMENT ON COLLABORATIVE BIOTECH RESEARCH THAT COULD

FREE OTHER MINISTRIES TO WORK MORE ACTIVELY WITH US.

2. (C) COMMENT. AS PREVIOUSLY REPORTED, ALEMANNO

CONTINUES TO AVOID AN OPEN AND HONEST DEBATE ON THE MERITS

OF AGRICULTURAL BIOTECHNOLOGY. HE CONTINUES TO TRY TO

SECURE A PRE-DETERMINED OUTCOME BEFORE OTHER COMPETING

POINTS OF VIEW WITHIN THE GOI CAN EFFECTIVELY WEIGH-IN.

VARIOUS AND CONTRADICTORY MOTIVES ARE ASCRIBED TO

ALEMANNO\'S POSITION ON BIOTECH, FROM A ROMANTIC VIEW OF

ITALIAN AGRICULTURE, TO PANDERING TO CONSUMER SENTIMENTS

AND POPULIST POLITICS. WHATEVER HIS UNDERLYING

CONVICTIONS, WE ARE NOT GOING TO GET ANYWHERE ARGUING THE

MERITS OF BIOTECHNOLOGY WITH HIM. RATHER, THE WAY TO ROLL

BACK HIS MOMENTUM IS BY CONTINUING TO HELP OTHER VOICES

WITHIN THE AN PARTY, BERLUSCONI\'S BROADER COALITION, AND

ITALIAN SOCIETY WHO WANT TO TALK THROUGH THE ISSUES ON THE

MERITS. SUCH A COALITION OF LIKE-MINDED, OR AT LEAST

OPEN-MINDED, ADVOCATES OF BIOTECHNOLOGY AND SCIENCE-BASED

DECISION MAKING CAN PROVIDE A COUNTERWEIGHT TO THE

COALITION OF STRANGE BEDFELLOWS THAT ALEMANNO HAS BEEN

RIDING TO THIS POINT. PM BERLUSCONI AND DEPUTY PM FINI CAN

ALSO MODERATE ALEMANNO\'S INTENTIONS, BUT IF, AND ONLY IF,

THEY ARE CONVINCED THEY NEED TO FOCUS THEIR ATTENTION ON

THE ISSUE. END COMMENT AND SUMMARY.

MAY 17 TO DELIVER SECRETARY OF AGRICULTURE VENEMAN\'S LETTER

THE MAY 27 MEETING BETWEEN PRESIDENT BUSH AND PRIME

MINISTER BERLUSCONI. AMBASSADOR SEMBLER EMPHASIZED THE

SECRETARY\'S CONCERNS THAT THE ALEMANNO PAPER IS UNBALANCED

TAKEN PLACE IN ITALY. HE EXPLAINED THAT ITALY\'S LARGEST

AND STRONGEST FARMERS\' ORGANIZATION, COLDIRETTI, HAS MADE

CLEAR ITS OPPOSITION TO BIOTECHNOLOGY AND HAS "CEMENTED A

STRONG FRONT WITH ALL OF ITALY\'S CONSUMER ORGANIZATIONS AND

ENVIRONMENTAL GROUPS". THEIR STRENGTH WAS "CLEARLY SHOWN"

IN THE DEMONSTRATION THEY HELD IN FRONT OF MONSANTO\'S

FACILITY IN LODI ON APRIL 20, ALEMANNO ASSERTED. ACCORDING

TO ALEMANNO, COLDIRETTI HAS ALSO ACCUSED THE MINAG OF BEING

TOO SOFT ON GMO-USE IN ITALY.

6. (C) COMMENT. ALEMANNO\'S ANALYSIS IS CLEARLY

SELF-SERVING. FIRST, ACCORDING TO MONSANTO, THE

DEMONSTRATION WAS PEACEFUL WITH A FEW TRACTORS AND PERHAPS

30-40 PEOPLE. BY ALL ACCOUNTS, THE PRESS COVERAGE WAS FAR

MORE DAMAGING TO MONSANTO THAN THE PROTEST ITSELF. SECOND,

WE STILL NEED TO BE CONVINCED THAT ALEMANNO AND COLDIRETTI

HAVE ALL ITALIAN CONSUMER GROUPS IN THEIR CORNER. THOSE

THAT ATTENDED THE EMBASSY\'S FEBRUARY 14 PROGRAM WITH

TUSKEGEE UNIVERSITY PROFESSOR C.S. PRAKASH DEMONSTRATED

OPEN-MINDEDNESS AND EVEN WARMED TO THE SUBJECT OF

BIOTECHNOLOGY. END COMMENT.

7. (SBU) ACKNOWLEDGING THAT THERE IS NO SCIENTIFIC

EVIDENCE WHICH INDICATES THAT BIOTECH PRODUCTS ON THE

MARKET ARE HARMFUL TO HUMAN HEALTH, ALEMANNO EXPLAINED THAT

ITALIAN CONSUMERS BELIEVE BIOTECHNOLOGY IS INCOMPATIBLE

WITH QUALITY FOOD. IN ITALY, WHERE THE REPUTATION OF

PAGE 03 ROME 02551 02 OF 03 241547Z

ITALIAN PRODUCTS FOR QUALITY IS OF PARAMOUNT IMPORTANCE, IT

WOULD NOT BE COMMERCIALLY VIABLE TO DEVELOP BIOTECH CROPS.

ALEMANNO ADDED THAT BECAUSE OF THE LIMITED QUANTITY OF LAND

AVAILABLE FOR AGRICULTURAL PRODUCTION, IT WOULD BE

IMPOSSIBLE TO GUARANTEE THE SEPARATION OF GMO-FREE

PRODUCTION, IF PLANTED ALONGSIDE GMO CROPS. THE LATTER

CONCERN IS THE BASIS FOR ITALY\'S ESPECIALLY CAUTIOUS

APPROACH TO BIOTECHNOLOGY. HE REPEATED THAT ITALY\'S

CONCERN WITH BIOTECHNOLOGY WAS BASED ON COMMERCIAL, NOT

HEALTH, FACTORS. IN CONCLUSION, ALEMANNO STATED THAT THE

PURPOSE OF THE WHITE PAPER IS TO IDENTIFY THE PROBLEMS

SURROUNDING BIOTECHNOLOGY IN ORDER THAT THE COUNCIL OF

MINISTERS OR THE PARLIAMENT COULD HAVE AN OPEN AND FAIR

DEBATE ON THE ISSUE. HE PROCLAIMED THAT IF THE WHITE PAPER

IS PERCEIVED AS BIASED, HE IS PREPARED TO MODIFY IT.

8. (SBU) THE U.S. SIDE EXPLAINED THAT THERE IS NO

CONCLUSIVE STUDY WHICH SUPPORTS THE ASSUMPTION THAT THE

CULTIVATION OF BIOTECH CROPS CANNOT CO-EXIST WITH

CONVENTIONAL CROPS. TO THE CONTRARY, SIMILAR CHALLENGES

HAVE BEEN OVERCOME IN ORDER TO ENSURE THE PURITY OF

PLANTING SEEDS. THE ANSWER TO ALEMANNO\'S QUESTION LIES IN

RESEARCH, SOMETHING THE UNITED STATES AND ITALY MIGHT WORK

ON TOGETHER. THE U.S. SIDE ALSO CHALLENGED ALEMANNO\'S

ASSERTION THAT BIOTECHNOLOGY IS INCOMPATIBLE WITH QUALITY

AGRICULTURE. ITALIAN SCIENTISTS HAVE ALREADY DEVELOPED NEW

PLANT VARIETIES WHICH DEMONSTRATE THE POTENTIAL FOR

BIOTECHNOLOGY TO IMPROVE THE PRODUCTION, QUALITY, AND

SUSTAINABILITY OF TRADITIONAL CROPS SUCH AS THE SAN MARZANO

TOMATO AND CARNAROLI RICE.

9. (SBU) ALEMANNO WAS QUICK TO DENY THAT HE PERSONALLY

BELIEVES GMO\'S ARE INCONSISTENT WITH QUALITY ITALIAN

AGRICULTURE. RATHER, THIS WAS THE PREVAILING PERCEPTION

AMONG ITALIAN CONSUMERS. RETURNING TO THE IDEA OF A

COOPERATIVE AGREEMENT ON BIOTECHNOLOGY RESEARCH, ALEMANNO

ENCOURAGED THE EMBASSY TO WORK WITH PROF. FERNANADO ROMANO,

THE NEWLY APPOINTED CHAIR OF THE MINISTER\'S BIOTECH

COMMITTEE. ROMANO IS ALSO CHAIR OF THE NATIONAL INSTITUTE

ON NUTRITION. ALEMANNO SAID HE WOULD ASK ROMANO TO CONTACT

THE EMBASSY EARLY THE WEEK OF MAY 20.

10. (SBU) AMBASSADOR SEMBLER REPEATED THE INTEREST OF THE

UNITED STATES TO MOVE QUICKLY IN ADVANCE OF PRESIDENT

BUSH\'S VISIT. ALEMANNO IN RESPONSE CONFIRMED THAT PM

BERLUSCONI, DEPUTY PM FINI, AND HE ALL WANTED TO AVOID A

DISPUTE OVER BIOTECH THAT IMPINGES ON U.S.-ITALIAN

RELATIONS. HE SUGGESTED THAT IF GMO-FREE PRODUCTION COULD

TAKE PLACE ALONGSIDE BIOTECH CROPS "WITHOUT CONTAMINATION"

THE ISSUE COULD BE EASILY RESOLVED.

12. (C) ALEMANNO IS NOT NEARLY SO OPEN-MINDED TO

BIOTECHNOLOGY AS HE ATTEMPTED TO PORTRAY HIMSELF TO THE

AMBASSADOR. HIS PUBLIC STATEMENTS AND SPEECHES MORE

CLEARLY REFLECT HIS VIEWS ON THE SUBJECT. THE LATEST

EXAMPLE WAS ON MAY 21 WHEN ALEMANNO, SPEAKING TO A NAPLES

CONFERENCE ON WINE, SAID THAT HE WAS PERSONALLY CONVINCED

THAT IT IS A GOOD IDEA TO WED "GMO-FREE" TO QUALITY FOOD.

WHAT IS ALSO CLEAR IS THAT HE\'S RIDING A WAVE OF SUPPORT

FROM THE MARRIAGE OF CONVENIENCE BETWEEN COLDIRETTI

(FORMERLY ASSOCIATED WITH THE CHRISTIAN DEMOCRATS), THE

GREENS, ANTI-GLOBAL GROUPS, AND SOME CONSUMER

ORGANIZATIONS. ARE THEY AS FORMIDABLE AS HE SAYS? WE THINK

NOT, BUT THEY CAST A MENACING SHADOW. THEY\'VE HAD SUCCESS

SIMPLY BY VIRTUE OF THE LACK OF ANY REAL COMPETING VISION

ON BIOTECHNOLOGY, ESPECIALLY FROM POLITICAL LEADERS.

13.(C) WE SHOULD NOT UNDERESTIMATE ALEMANNO AND HIS

DETERMINATION TO ADVANCE HIS POLITICAL CAREER. ALTHOUGH HE

KNOWS NEITHER AGRICULTURE NOR SCIENCE (HIS SPECIALTY AND

POLITICAL HOBBY HORSE IN THE PAST HAS BEEN LABOR), HE KNOWS

POLITICS FROM THE GRASSROOTS TO THE CONSIGLIO. HE HAS USED

HIS HOME TIES TO THE PUGLIA REGION AND PARTY CONNECTIONS TO

LINE UP SUPPORT FOR HIS WHITE PAPER AMONG REGIONAL

AGRICULTURE COMMISSIONERS, AND TO CUT A DEAL WITH SOME

NATIONAL ALLIANCE (AN) MEMBERS AND FORMER COMMUNIST REGIONS

TO SUPPORT HIS NO-GMO VIEWS (WITH ONLY THE REGION OF

LOMBARDIA DISSENTING, AND STRONGLY). HE RECENTLY DISPLAYED

HIS AMPLE POLITICAL SKILLS AT THE AN CONGRESS IN BOLOGNA;

HIS ONE HOUR SPEECH IN A FIFTEEN MINUTE SLOT GALVANIZED THE

PARTY FAITHFUL. HIS MOST TRUSTED STAFF AT MINAG FEEL AT

LIBERTY TO ROUTINELY ENGAGE IN VERBAL ATTACKS ON OPPONENTS

WITHIN THE GOI\'S NATIONAL BIOTECH COMMITTEES WHO DARE TO

CRITICIZE THE WHITE PAPER.

14. (C) WE SUSPECT THAT BEHIND CLOSED DOORS ALEMANNO SEEKS

TO SWAY GOVERNMENT POLICY WITH THE SPECTER OF POPULAR

PROTESTS. WE QUESTION THE VIABILITY OF SUCH A STRATEGY,

BUT NONETHELESS WE CAN\'T AFFORD TO LET HIM PROCEED

UNIMPEDED. AT PRESENT THE ONLY COUNTERWEIGHT WE SEE TO

ALEMANNO\'S STRATEGY IS TO CONVINCE PM BERLUSCONI AND DEPUTY

PM FINI THAT ADOPTING ALEMANNO\'S PAPER UNCHANGED (WHETHER

OUT OF FEAR OF POTENTIAL DEMONSTRATION OR A DESIRE TO AVOID

INTERNAL CONFLICT ON THE ISSUE) WOULD CREATE A SUBSTANTIAL

IMPEDIMENT TO U.S.-ITALIAN RELATIONS. THE UPCOMING VISITS

OF THE PRESIDENT, SECRETARY VENEMAN, U/S LARSON AND OTHERS

TO ROME PROVIDE PERFECT OPPORTUNITIES TO ADVANCE OUR

INTERESTS. END ANALYSIS AND COMMENT.
