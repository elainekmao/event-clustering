SUBJECT: POLICY RECOMMENDATIONS UNTIL ELECTIONS:

KEEP THE PRESSURE ON AND LEAVE ROOM FOR MISTAKES

REF: (A)STATE 197909, (B)HARARE 3303

CLASSIFIED BY JOSEPH G. SULLIVAN FOR REASONS 1.5 B/D

1. (C) SUMMARY: THE MUGABE GOVERNMENT HAS INTENSIFIED

ITS VIOLENCE AND EFFORTS TO DOMINATE ELECTION PROCEDURES

AND HAS NOT BEEN DETERRED BY THREATS OF SANCTIONS. HARSH

METHODS, NOTWITHSTANDING, ZANU-PF IS STILL VERY HARD-

PRESSED BY A RESILIENT MDC OPPOSITION, BOLSTERED BY

ZIMBABWE\'S CONTINUING ECONOMIC DECLINE. UNDER DOMESTIC

AND INTERNATIONAL PRESSURE, THE GOZ HAS MADE MANY

MISTAKES AND COULD MAKE MORE. WE RECOMMEND LAYING DOWN

CLEAR MARKERS OF WHAT IS NECESSARY FOR US TO CONFER

LEGITIMACY UPON THE ELECTION RESULT AND LEVERAGING KEY

AFRICANS INTO PRESSING FOR SUCH FREE AND FAIR

CONDITIONS. IF THE GOZ FAILS TO DELIVER A CREDIBLE

ELECTION AND DEPRIVES THE OPPOSITION OF A CHANCE OF

VICTORY, THE US, EU, AND THE COMMONWEALTH SHOULD REACT

QUICKLY TO DECLARE THE PROCESS UNFREE AND UNFAIR.

SUPPORT OF KEY AFRICANS, PARTICULARLY THE SOUTH AFRICANS

AND THE NIGERIANS, FOR OUR ACTIONS WILL BE CRITICAL.

2. (C) IN THE MONTHS AHEAD, TONE WILL BE IMPORTANT. WE

WANT TO BE FIRM IN DEFENDING THE HUMAN AND CIVIL RIGHTS

OF THE ZIMBABWEAN PEOPLE, BUT WISH TO DO IT IN A WAY

THAT PUTS US ON THE SIDE OF THE PEOPLE AND AVOIDS THE

IMAGE OF BIG POWER DICTATION. WE ALSO WANT TO AVOID

CONDEMNATION FOR ITS OWN SAKE AND MAKE CLEAR REPEATEDLY

WHAT STEPS WE SEEK FROM THE GOZ. THIS TONE MAINTAINS

THE PRESSURE FOR CHANGE AND MAKES GAINING AFRICAN

SUPPORT EASIER. WE ALSO INTEND TO KEEP PUBLICIZING OUR

OWN EFFORTS ON BEHALF OF THE ZIMBABWEAN PEOPLE IN THE

AREAS OF HIV/AIDS AND IN SELF-HELP PROJECTS TO MAKE IT

CLEAR THAT OUR DIFFICULTIES ARE WITH THE GOZ AND NOT THE

PEOPLE. END SUMMARY

3. (C) PRESIDENT ROBERT MUGABE AND HIS ZANU-PF

GOVERNMENT HAVE RECENTLY INTENSIFIED THEIR VIOLENCE AND

INTIMIDATION OF OPPOSITION AND INDEPENDENT MEDIA AND

THEIR EFFORTS TO CONTROL AND MANIPULATE THE 2002

PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION PROCEDURES. THEY HAVE NOT BEEN

DETERRED BY THE THREAT OF COMMONWEALTH, EU, US OR OTHER

SANCTIONS, AND EVEN LESS BY THE ECONOMIC AND OTHER HARM

THEY ARE CAUSING THEIR COUNTRY IN THEIR ALL-OUT EFFORT

TO STAY IN POWER. THE GOVERNMENT HAS NOT HESITATED TO

LAMBASTE ANY AND EVERY FOREIGN GOVERNMENT OR ZIMBABWEAN

WHO GETS IN THEIR WAY AND HAVE FEW FRIENDS LEFT OUTSIDE

THEIR TIGHT CIRCLE OF INSIDERS.

4. (C) FOR THE SAME REASON THE THREAT OF SANCTIONS HAS

NOT AFFECTED THE MUGABE GOVERNMENT\'S ACTIONS, THE

IMPOSITION OF SANCTIONS IS UNLIKELY TO DETER IT FROM THE

WIN-AT-ALL-COSTS STRATEGY. PRESIDENT MUGABE AND HIS

CLOSEST ADVISERS OBVIOUSLY CARE LITTLE ABOUT LOSS OF

ASSISTANCE TO THE NATION AND APPEAR TO BELIEVE THAT

THERE IS NO DAMAGE THAT CAN COME FROM PERSONALIZED

FINANCIAL OR TRAVEL SANCTIONS WHICH EQUATES TO MUGABE\'S

LOSS OF THE PRESIDENCY. ON THE OTHER HAND, WHAT DOES

MATTER TO PRESIDENT MUGABE IS THE INTERNATIONAL AND

DOMESTIC LEGITIMACY, WHICH COMES FROM AN ELECTION

VICTORY. FOR THAT REASON, WE BELIEVE THE EFFORT TO LAY

DOWN A CLEAR MARKER OF WHAT ELECTION STANDARDS ARE

NECESSARY (REFS A AND B) TO OBTAIN INTERNATIONAL

LEGITIMACY AND THE DOMESTIC LEGITIMACY THIS HELPS CONFER

IS AN IMPORTANT EXERCISE.

5. (C) THE ABOVE NOTWITHSTANDING, THE ZIMBABWEAN

GOVERNMENT MAY NOT RESPOND POSITIVELY TO MEET

INTERNATIONAL STANDARDS, ASSUMING PERHAPS THAT

PROTECTION BY FELLOW AFRICAN STATES, SOME COSMETIC

OBSERVATION NEAR THE END OF THE ELECTIONS AND POST-

ELECTION SIGNALS OF OPENNESS WILL WIPE AWAY UNPLEASANT

MEMORIES. MUGABE AND HIS INNER CIRCLE APPEAR TO

BELIEVE THAT THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY WILL HAVE TO

DEAL WITH A GOZ, WHICH RESULTS FROM AN ELECTION

CARRIED OUT ON ZANU-PF TERMS.

8. (C) WHILE THE GOZ HAS FOR THE MOST PART REFRAINED FROM

EVEN PARANOIDS HAVE ENEMIES

---------------------------

9. (C) WHILE ZANU-PF DOES HAVE MANY ENEMIES, FOREIGN AND

DOMESTIC, ITS ACTIONS ARE THOSE OF A PARANOID FLAILING

ABOUT AT REAL AND IMAGINED ENEMIES AT HOME AND ABROAD.

IN ITS FLAILING, THE GOZ IS BUYING ITSELF A NEW ENEMY OR

MORE PER DAY. THE ARREST OF THE DAILY NEWS EDITOR WHO

IS ABOUT TO WIN THE COMMITTEE FOR THE PROTECTION OF

JOURNALISTS\' AWARD IS A CASE IN POINT. AND ALL THE

GOVERNMENT\'S ACTIONS TO DATE HAVE BOUGHT IT ONLY A

STATISTICAL DEAD HEAT IN THE LATEST PUBLIC OPINION POLL

OF UNCERTAIN RELIABILITY.

10.(C) CONTINUED PRESSURE INSIDE AND OUTSIDE ZIMBABWE

HAS ALREADY INDUCED GOZ MISTAKES AND COULD LEAD TO

FURTHER MISCALCULATIONS AND ERRORS. ON THE EVE OF A

UNDP MISSION TO ASSESS COMPLIANCE WITH THE ABUJA ACCORD

ON LAND REFORM, THE GOVERNMENT BROKE THE ABUJA AGREEMENT

FLAGRANTLY BY LEGISLATING THE CONFISCATION OF ALL

DESIGNATED LANDS AND SHORT-CIRCUITING ALL COURT APPEALS.

IN ORDER TO DISPENSE WITH ITS OBLIGATIONS UNDER THE

NIGERIAN-BROKERED AGREEMENT, THE GOZ MIGHT EVENTUALLY

BREAK WITH THE ABUJA PROCESS, UNDOUBTEDLY BLAMING THE UK

FOR ITS ACTIONS. MASSIVE RESORT TO FORCE, TARGETED

ASSASSINATIONS OF OPPOSITION FIGURES OR DECLARATION OF A

STATE OF EMERGENCY ARE ALSO POSSIBILITIES, ANY OF WHICH

WOULD RAISE MAJOR LEGITIMACY ISSUES IN ZIMBABWE AND THE

REGION.

KEEP THE PRESSURE ON AND LEAVE ROOM FOR MISTAKES

EXCUSE FOR ABORTING ELECTIONS WHICH THE MDC STILL HAS A

REASONABLE CHANCE OF WINNING. MOREOVER, SADC, THE

COMMONWEALTH AND OTHER AFRICANS, I.E. THE NIGERIANS,

WOULD LIKELY WIND UP SIDING WITH THE ZIMBABWEANS IN A

SANCTIONS DISPUTE WITH THE "DEVELOPED WORLD." INSTEAD,

WE RECOMMEND LAYING DOWN CLEAR MARKERS OF WHAT IS

NECESSARY FOR US TO CONFER LEGITIMACY UPON THE ELECTION

RESULT AND SEEKING TO LEVERAGE KEY AFRICANS INTO

PRESSING FOR SUCH FREE AND FAIR CONDITIONS. (THE

ZIMBABWE DEMOCRACY ACT, IF PASSED BY CONGRESS, SHOULD BE

PRESENTED AS A WARNING AND AN OPPORTUNITY FOR THE GOZ TO

CHOOSE BETWEEN ITS INCENTIVES AND ITS SANCTIONS.) IF

THE GOZ FAILS TO DELIVER A CREDIBLE ELECTION PROCESS AND

THE OPPOSITION IS DEPRIVED OF A FAIR CHANCE AT VICTORY

BY THESE METHODS, THE US, THE EU AND THE COMMONWEALTH

NEED TO BE PREPARED TO REACT STRONGLY AND QUICKLY AFTER

THE ELECTION TO DECLARE THE PROCESS UNFREE AND UNFAIR.

IT WILL ALSO BE CRITICAL TO HAVE KEY AFRICANS,

PARTICULARLY SOUTH AFRICA AND NIGERIA ALONGSIDE US IN

THIS DECLARATION. WHILE SOME MIGHT CAUTION THAT SUCH A

DECLARATION WILL SET OFF AN INTERNAL CRISIS, WE BELIEVE

THE ABSENCE OF A CLEAN AND CREDIBLE RESULT INTERNALLY

WILL PROVOKE AN INTERNAL CRISIS ON ITS OWN.

TONE IS IMPORTANT

-----------------

12. (C) WE WANT TO BE FIRM IN DEFENDING THE PEOPLE OF

ZIMBABWE, THEIR HUMAN RIGHTS, MEDIA FREEDOM AND RIGHT TO

FREE AND FAIR ELECTIONS IN THE MONTHS AHEAD. IT IS

ALMOST AS IMPORTANT THAT WE MAINTAIN THE IMAGE OF

DEFENDER OF JUSTICE AND AVOID THE IMAGE OF BIG POWER

DICTATION. IT IS ALSO IMPORTANT THAT WE CONTINUE TO

PRESS FOR CHANGE IN GOVERNMENT BEHAVIOR RATHER THAN

THE ABOVE TONE IS IMPORTANT FOR GAINING AFRICAN SUPPORT

PUBLICIZING PUBLIC ACTIVITIES IN SUPPORT OF THE

BEHALF OF THE PEOPLE.
