SUBJECT: COVERAGE OF EASTERN SLAVONIA BASIC AGREEMENT

IN CROATIAN MEDIA -- REFLECTING THE AMERICAN VIEW

1. BECAUSE OF SOME SKEPTICISM WITHIN THE CROATIAN MEDIA

AND PUBLIC OVER THE BASIC AGREEMENT ON EASTERN SLAVONIA,

UNCLASSIFIED

UNCLASSIFIED

WE INVITED FOUR COLUMNISTS FROM CROATIA\'S MAIN

NEWSPAPERS FOR A SESSION WITH AMBASSADOR GALBRAITH IN

THE HOPES OF GETTING OUR VIEWS OUT. THE FIRST FRUITS OF

THIS DISCUSSION APPEARED TODAY (NOVEMBER 16) IN ONE OF

THOSE PAPERS, VECERNJI LIST, UNDER THE HEADLINE

"SKELETON WITHOUT THE FLESH: AMERICA\'S VIEW OF THE

AGREEMENT ON EASTERN SLAVONIA."

2. FOLLOWING ARE SIGNIFICANT EXCERPTS FROM THAT COLUMN,

BY FOREIGN AFFAIRS WRITER MARINA SERIC:

(BEGIN TEXT)

THE BASICS OF THE AGREEMENT ON EASTERN SLAVONIA CAN BE

UNDERSTOOD TO BE A SKELETON TO WHICH THE "FLESH" STILL NEEDS

TO BE ATTACHED. IT IS AN OPPORTUNITY FOR EVERYTHING TO GO

SMOOTHLY, FOR THOSE WHO ARE ALREADY (LIVING) THERE TO STAY

AND FOR THOSE WHO WERE EXPELLED TO RETURN, AND FOR (EASTERN

SLAVONIA) TO BE A MULTI-ETHNIC COMMUNITY AGAIN. THE

TRANSITIONAL AUTHORITY WILL BE A NEUTRAL FORCE THAT WILL BE

RESPONSIBLE FOR ATTACHING THE FLESH TO THE SKELETON AND FOR

EVERYTHING GOING SMOOTHLY. THIS COULD SUMMARIZE THE

THOUGHTS ON THE BASIC AGREEMENT ABOUT EASTERN SLAVONIA FROM

THE MOUTH OF THE AMERICAN AMBASSADOR PETER GALBRAITH. . . .

"THIS IS THE PLACE WHERE THE WAR BEGAN. THEREFORE, IT IS

LOGICAL THAT ITS END BEGIN THERE. THAT REGION NEEDS TO

REMAIN A MULTI-ETHNIC COMMUNITY. THIS AGREEMENT IS A BASIS

FOR THE INITIATIVES OF BOTH SIDES, WHICH IS IMPORTANT FOR

ITS REALIZATION. THE TRANSITIONAL PERIOD EXISTS BECAUSE IT

IS NECESSARY FOR THE PRESENT LOCAL POPULATION TO PREPARE

ITSELF FOR NORMAL LIFE WITHIN CROATIA," EXPLAINS GALBRAITH.

THE U.S. HAS APPROACHED THIS PROBLEM FROM THE PERSPECTIVE OF

ITS OWN MULTI-ETHNICITY. THEREFORE ONE OF THE BASIC IDEAS IS

THAT AN AREA THAT USED TO BE MULTI-ETHNIC IN THE PAST REMAIN

MULTI-ETHNIC IN THE FUTURE. "THERE IS STILL THE POSSIBILITY

HERE FOR THOSE WHO ARE THERE TO REMAIN, AT THE SAME TIME FOR

THOSE WHO WERE EXPELLED TO RETURN, AND FOR THE

REESTABLISHMENT OF CONFIDENCE BETWEEN THESE PEOPLES. THAT

WOULD BE AN EXCELLENT EXAMPLE FOR BOSNIA," CLAIMS GALBRAITH,

WHO IS CONVINCED THAT THERE IS NO POSSIBILITY THAT THE

AGREEMENT WILL FALL APART AND (WHO) BELIEVES THAT IT IS IN

THE INTERESTS OF ALL THAT THE TRANSITION PERIOD "PASS

SMOOTHLY, WITHOUT A MASSIVE EXODUS OF THE SERB POPULATION

AND WITHOUT INCITING NEW EMOTIONS FOR WAR."

HE ALSO CONSIDERS THIS AGREEMENT TO BE A SIGN OF THE

MATURITY OF THE SERB NEGOTIATING TEAM, WHICH, UNLIKE THE

KNIN LEADERSHIP, UNDERSTOOD THAT THEY WERE IN A DIFFICULT

SITUATION. (SERB DELEGATION HEAD) MILAN MILANOVIC WAS

DESCRIBED AS AN INFLUENTIAL MAN WHO OPERATED IN THE

BACKGROUND. "THERE WEREN\'T ANY ARGUMENTS WITHIN THE SERB

DELEGATION, AS THERE WERE IN KNIN, WHOSE LEADERSHIP SHOWED

ITSELF TO BE TOTALLY IRRESPONSIBLE. THESE (PEOPLE) HAVE

LEARNED SOMETHING AND ACCEPTED RESPONSIBILITY. GREAT CREDIT

MUST GO TO (CROATIAN DELEGATION CHIEF HRVOJE) SARINIC AND

HIS TEAM. IT WAS A MATTER OF INTELLIGENT AND ELEGANT

COMPROMISES," CONCLUDED THE AMBASSADOR.

ASKED WHETHER THIS AGREEMENT AND THE ONE BETWEEN PRESIDENTS

TUDJMAN AND IZETBEGOVIC IN DAYTON WERE ONLY MASKS FOR THE

(LACK OF) SUCCESS OF NEGOTIATIONS ON BOSNIA-HERCEGOVINA,

SUCCESSES ARE SIGNS OF RESOLVE TO FIND A SOLUTION, AND THAT

THE LIFTING OF THE OTHER "WALL" -- SANCTIONS ON SERBIA --

BEHIND WHICH LIES THE POSSIBILITY OF SERBIA\'S ACCEPTANCE
