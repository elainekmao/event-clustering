SUBJECT: JERUSALEM PATRIARCH PLEAS FOR U.S. INTERVENTION

CLASSIFIED BY: AMBASSADOR JIM NICHOLSON, REASON E.O. 12958 1.5 (D).

1. (U) THIS IS AN ACTION REQUEST, PLEASE SEE LAST PARA.

2. (C) SUMMARY: AT THE SUGGESTION OF THE POPE, CATHOLIC

PATRIARCH OF JERUSALEM MICHEL SABBAH MADE AN URGENT PLEA TO

AMBASSADOR OCTOBER 10 FOR U.S. HELP TO STOP THE VIOLENCE,

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 VATICA 04991 01 OF 03 141419Z

AMELIORATE THE INCREASINGLY DESPERATE PLIGHT OF THE

PALESTINIAN PEOPLE, AND MOVE THE PARTIES TOWARD MEANINGFUL

DIALOGUE. SABBAH HAD MET WITH THE POPE THE DAY BEFORE TO

CONVEY HIS CONCERNS ABOUT THE SITUATION IN JERUSALEM AND THE

PALESTINIAN TERRITORIES. ACCORDING TO SABBAH, BOTH THE

PALESTINIAN AND ISRAELI PEOPLE INCREASINGLY RECOGNIZED THAT

THE CURRENT CYCLE OF VIOLENCE SERVED NO PURPOSE, BUT THEY

NEEDED TO BE "SAVED" FROM THEIR CURRENT BLOODY IMPASSE BY

OUTSIDE PRESSURE. ONLY U.S. INTERVENTION, HE INSISTED, COULD

MOVE THE ISRAELI GOVERNMENT FROM ITS CURRENT POLICY OF

REPRESSION. SABBAH, WHO PARTICIPATES IN THE "ALEXANDRIA

GROUP" -- AN INTER-FAITH RELIGIOUS FORUM ENCOMPASSING

CHRISTIAN, JEWISH, AND MUSLIM LEADERS -- ASSERTED THAT THIS

GROUP HAD CONTACTS WITH HAMAS AND THE PALESTINIAN AUTHORITY,

AND COULD SECURE A CESSATION IN TERRORIST ATTACKS OF "MORE

THAN A MONTH" IF ONLY THE ISRAELI GOVERNMENT WOULD OFFER A

"REAL PROMISE" OF EASING THE PALESTINIANS\' SUFFERING. SABBAH

CONDEMNED PALESTINIAN TERRORISM AND "HATE SPEECH", BUT SAID

IT WAS A MANIFESTATION OF PALESTINIAN DESPERATION THAT WOULD

STOP ONLY WHEN THE OCCUPATION AND "WAR" ENDED. SEEING NO

SIGNS THAT THE SHARON GOVERNMENT WAS INTERESTED IN PEACE,

SABBAH ASKED THE AMBASSADOR TO CONVEY TO THE PRESIDENT HIS

URGENT PLEA FOR INTERVENTION FROM THE BUSH ADMINISTRATION TO

SECURE SOME MEANINGFUL CONCESSION FROM THE GOI THAT WOULD

GIVE THE ALEXANDRIA GROUP THE LEVERAGE IT NEEDED TO SECURE A

CESSATION OF VIOLENCE FROM THE ARAB SIDE AND SET IN PLACE THE

BEGINNINGS OF REAL PEACE TALKS LEADING TO AN END TO ISRAELI

OCCUPATION. END SUMMARY.

3. (C) AMBASSADOR RECEIVED A CALL FROM VATICAN FOREIGN

MINISTER EQUIVALENT ARCHBISHOP JEAN-LOUIS TAURAN OCTOBER 10,

WHO CONVEYED THE POPE\'S REQUEST THAT THE AMBASSADOR MEET THAT

DAY WITH VISITING PATRIARCH OF JERUSALEM MICHEL SABBAH, WHO

WAS IN ROME TO SEE THE POPE ABOUT THE DETERIORATING SITUATION

IN THE HOLY LANDS. MEETING WITH THE AMBASSADOR AND STAFF,

SABBAH AND THE VICAR OF JERUSALEM MADE AN URGENT, ALMOST

DESPERATE PLEA FOR UNITED STATES INTERVENTION TO STOP THE

VIOLENCE AND END THE UNTENABLE SITUATION FOR THE PALESTINIANS

IN THE HOLY LANDS. HE SAID THAT THE CURFEW FORBADE THEIR

MOVEMENT 24 HOURS PER DAY AND DEPRIVED THEM OF BASIC HUMAN

NECESSITIES. HE FURTHER SAID THAT BOTH THE PALESTINIAN AND

ISRAELI PEOPLE NEEDED "PROTECTION" AND "SALVATION" IN THE

FORM OF A U.S. OR UN MILITARY PRESENCE THAT WOULD PROVIDE

SECURITY FOR ALL. SABBAH ASSERTED THAT THE ARAB WORLD WAS

READY FOR PEACE, THE PALESTINIAN WORLD WAS READY, AND THE

TIME WAS RIGHT FOR A MOVE TO PEACE. "THERE IS NO REASON FOR

THIS WAR TO GO ON," HE INSISTED. UNFORTUNATELY, IT APPEARED

TO HIM THAT THE SHARON GOVERNMENT HAD "NO INTEREST IN PEACE."

SABBAH SAID THAT EVENTUALLY THE ISRAELI GOVERNMENT WOULD

HAVE TO COME TO TERMS WITH THE PALESTINIAN PEOPLE (AND RETURN

TO THE 1967 BOUNDARIES), AND THAT SINCE PEOPLE WERE DYING

EVERY DAY, IT WAS POINTLESS NOT TO MOVE IN THIS DIRECTION NOW

AND SAVE MANY LIVES.

4. (C) SABBAH SKETCHED A GRIM PICTURE OF PALESTINIAN DAILY

LIFE UNDER SIEGE AND CURFEW, WITH SCHOOLS CLOSED, LIMITED

MOVEMENT POSSIBLE ONLY FOR THREE HOURS THREE TIMES A WEEK, NO

ECONOMIC LIFE, AND NO PROSPECTS FOR IMPROVEMENTS. HARSH

ISRAELI REPRESSION, SABBAH NOTED, HAS NOT RESULTED IN GREATER

SECURITY FOR THE ISRAELI PEOPLE. ON THE CONTRARY, "EVEN THE

ISRAELIS" WERE SUFFERING, HE SAID. WITH PALESTINIANS LIVING

UNDER SUCH DESPERATION, SABBAH SAID IT WAS UNDERSTANDABLE

THAT SOME WOULD RESORT TO "IMMORAL ACTS" LIKE SUICIDE

BOMBING. HE CONDEMNED TERRORIST ACTS AS "MORALLY WRONG AND

STRATEGICALLY WRONG," BUT ADDED THAT MOST PALESTINIANS CLAIM

"AS LONG AS THE ISRAELIS ARE IN OUR TERRITORY MAKING WAR ON

US, WE WILL MAKE WAR ON THEM." HE LAMENTED THAT PALESTINIANS

WERE CONDEMNED FOR SUICIDE BOMBINGS, WHILE ISRAEL WAS ABLE TO

KILL INNOCENT CIVILIANS AND DESTROY PROPERTY BY TRADITIONAL

MILITARY MEANS THAT EARNED LESS APPROBATION FROM THE

INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY.

ISRAELI GOVERNMENT BROUGHT IT TO AN END AND BEGAN A NEW

REITERATED HIS REQUEST THAT WE CONVEY TO THE PRESIDENT HIS

URGENT PLEA FOR AN INTERVENTION WITH SHARON TO CONVINCE HIM

TO EASE THE SITUATION IN THE PALESTINIAN TERRITORIES AND JOIN

IN STEPS TOWARD A LASTING CEASEFIRE. SABBAH NOTED THAT HE

AND THE OTHER RELIGIOUS LEADERS OF JERUSALEM HAD PREVIOUSLY

REQUESTED A MEETING WITH THE PRESIDENT, AND HOPED TO BE ABLE

TO PRESENT THEIR CONCERNS DIRECTLY IN THE NEAR FUTURE.

AMBASSADOR PROMISED TO CONVEY SABBAH\'S COMMENTS DIRECTLY TO

WASHINGTON.

6. (C) AMBASSADOR ASKED SABBAH ABOUT THE STATUS OF HIS OWN

EFFORTS TO ESTABLISH A DIALOGUE WITH THE ISRAELI GOVERNMENT.

SABBAH SAID HE HAS MET WITH DEPUTY FOREIGN MINISTER MELCHIOR,

THE ONLY GOI OFFICIAL WHO REGULARLY SPOKE WITH HIM, BUT THAT

MELCHIOR MAKES CLEAR THAT HE CANNOT SPEAK ON BEHALF OF THE

GOVERNMENT, AND GENERALLY PROMISES ONLY TO CONVEY SABBAH\'S

CONCERNS. HE SAID HE HAS HAD A REQUEST IN TO MEET DIRECTLY

WITH PM SHARON FOR SEVERAL MONTHS, BUT THE PM\'S OFFICE HAS

NOT RESPONDED. HE BELIEVED SHARON WAS NOT INTERESTED IN

PEACE, AND WAS BENT ON EXPELLING ALL BUT A FEW THOUSAND

PALESTINIANS FROM THE WEST BANK IN ORDER TO CREATE A GREATER

ISRAEL.

ALEXANDRIA GROUP: CEASEFIRE POSSIBLE WITH ISRAELI COMMITMENT

--------------------------------------------- ---------------

7. (C) SABBAH TOLD US THAT THE "ALEXANDRIA GROUP" OF

RELIGIOUS LEADERS FROM THE MUSLIN, JEWISH AND CHRISTIAN

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 VATICA 04991 02 OF 03 141420Z

FAITHS CREATED LAST JANUARY MAINTAINS GOOD CONTACTS WITH

HAMAS AND THE PALESTINIAN AUTHORITY. SABBAH SAID HE HAD

ASKED HAMAS WHAT IT WOULD TAKE TO STOP THE VIOLENCE AND THE

REPLY WAS THAT AS LONG AS THE OCCUPATION CONTINUED, HAMAS WAS

AT "WAR"; WHEN THE OCCUPATION CEASED, THE WAR WOULD BE OVER.

HE SAID THAT AFTER THEY HAD SPOKEN WITH SHEIK YASSIM, HAMAS

HAD REFRAINED FROM ATTACKS FOR OVER FOUR WEEKS. DURING THAT

PERIOD, HE SAID, THE ISRAELI SIDE HAD CONTINUED ITS

OCCUPATION AND ASSASSINATION POLICY, AND SO THE TERROR

ATTACKS WERE RENEWED.

8. (C) PRESSED BY THE AMBASSADOR ABOUT CURRENT DIFFICULTIES

IN ESTABLISHING AN IMPROVED SECURITY SITUATION WITHOUT

PALESTINIAN SUICIDE ATTACKS, SABBAH INSISTED THAT THE

ALEXANDRIA GROUP COULD USE ITS GOOD OFFICES TO OBTAIN A REAL

CESSATION IN THE TERRORIST ATTACKS, FROM HAMAS, THE PA, AND

POTENTIALLY EVEN HIZBOLLAH, FOR "A MONTH." BUT THEY COULD DO

SO ONLY IF THEY HAD SOMETHING CONCRETE FROM THE ISRAELI SIDE

TO TAKE TO THE TABLE. AT AMBASSADOR\'S QUERY AS TO WHAT WOULD

TYPE OF STEPS WOULD BE NEEDED FROM THE ISRAELI SIDE TO

ACHIEVE SUCH A CESSATION OF ATTACKS, SABBAH CONCEDED THAT

SOMETHING AS SIMPLE AS A REAL PLEDGE FROM THE GOI TO EASE THE

CURFEWS FOR A WEEK COULD CREATE A FOUNDATION ON WHICH TO

BUILD, FOLLOWED BY A RETURN TO "A MORE NORMAL LIFE."

FOLLOWING UP ON SABBAH\'S INDICATION THAT THE ALEXANDRIA GROUP

WOULD BE MEETING UNDER THE AUSPICES OF THE ARCHBISHOP OF

CANTERBURY OCTOBER 24-25 AT LAMBETH HOUSE IN LONDON,

AMBASSADOR URGED SABBAH TO DEVELOP HIS PROPOSAL WITHIN THE

ALEXANDRIA GROUP. WHILE CAUTIONING THAT IT WOULD BE A

DIFFICULT TASK TO SET THE ALEXANDRIA GROUP OF RELIGIOUS

LEADERS ON A POLITICAL PATH, HE EXPRESSED A WILLINGNESS TO

EXPLORE WAYS IN WHICH THE GROUP COULD PROMOTE A CESSATION OF

VIOLENCE AND CREATE SOME SPACE FOR A RESTORATION OF DIALOGUE.

PALESTINIAN LEADERSHIP AND REFORM

---------------------------------

9. (C) RESPONDING TO U.S. CONCERNS ABOUT THE IMPORTANCE OF

POLITICAL REFORM AND RESPONSIBLE LEADERSHIP WITHIN THE

PALESTINIAN TERRITORIES, SABBAH DISMISSED THESE AS "SECONDARY

CONCERNS." "EVEN WITH THE BEST ADMINISTRATION, FREE FROM

CORRUPTION, VIOLENCE WILL STILL BE THERE IF NOTHING IS DONE

ABOUT THE OCCUPATION," HE OBSERVED. ON THE LEADERSHIP ISSUE,

SABBAH CAUTIONED THAT U.S. AND ISRAELI PRESSURE TO GET RID OF

ARAFAT WILL HAVE THE OPPOSITE EFFECT. "THE MORE YOU SAY THAT

ARAFAT IS IRRELEVANT, THE MORE RELEVANT HE BECOMES." WHEREAS

IF THE U.S. AND ISRAEL JUST IGNORED HIM AND LET THE

PALESTINIAN PEOPLE TAKE CARE OF IT, "YOU WILL GET THE

LEADERSHIP YOU WANT," HE ADDED. HE DESCRIBED ARAFAT AS

"PRISONER," WHO COULD DO NOTHING, AND TO WHOM NO ONE LISTENS,

BUT WHO GROWS IN POPULARITY THE MORE HE IS HUMILIATED.

COMMENT

10. (C) SABBAH\'S MEETING WITH THE POPE AND THE POPE\'S

RECOMMENDATION FOR A MEETING WITH THE AMBASSADOR REFLECT THE

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 VATICA 04991 03 OF 03 141420Z

POPE\'S INCREASING CONCERN FOR THE SITUATION IN THE HOLY LANDS

AND CONSISTENT OPPOSITION TO HEAVY HANDED ISRAELI REPRESSIVE

MEASURES. WHILE WE ARE CONFIDENT SABBAH MAINTAINS TIES TO

BOTH HAMAS AND THE PA, AND MAY BE ABLE TO PLAY A USEFUL ROLE

IN CONSTRAINING ATTACKS, WE LEAVE IT TO WASHINGTON, TEL AVIV

AND JERUSALEM TO ASSESS WHETHER THESE TIES WOULD BE STRONG

ENOUGH TO HELP ACHIEVE A CESSATION OF SUICIDE ATTACKS, AS HE

MAINTAINS. GIVEN THE CURRENT IMPASSE, HOWEVER, WE BELIEVE

THAT INTER-FAITH RELIGIOUS EFFORTS SUCH AS THAT OF THE

ALEXANDRIA GROUP CAN CONTRIBUTE TO THE CREATION OF SOME

DIALOGUE IN A SITUATION WHERE VERY LITTLE EXISTS. IN ANY

EVENT, SABBAH\'S DESPERATE PLEA FOR GREATER U.S. ENGAGEMENT --

A PLEA ECHOED IN EVERY MEETING WITH SENIOR HOLY SEE OFFICIALS

-- REFLECTS A STRONGLY HELD VIEW HERE THAT IN THE ABSENCE OF

GREATER U.S. PRESSURE ON SHARON, THERE IS NO HOPE FOR AN END

TO THE TRAGIC CYCLE OF VIOLENCE IN THE MIDDLE EAST. SABBAH,

A PALESTINIAN NATIONALIST AND A CATHOLIC CLERGYMAN,

UNDERSCORED TO THE AMBASSADOR HIS CONDEMNATION BOTH OF

TERRORISM AND THE HATE SPEECH THAT EXISTS IN PALESTINIAN

MEDIA AND SCHOOLS. HE SAID HE PREACHES TO HIS OWN FLOCK TO

"LOVE THE ISRAELIS, EVEN THOSE WHO DRIVE TANKS OVER YOUR

HOUSES." THIS WAS "HARD TO DO," HE ADDED, "BUT IT IS THE

CHRISTIAN THING TO DO." ACTION REQUEST: EMBASSY IS PREPARED

TO CONTINUE TO ENGAGE WITH THE HOLY SEE ON THIS TRACK IF THE

DEPARTMENT BELIEVES IT WILL BE HELPFUL IN ENCOURAGING

DIALOGUE AND REDUCING VIOLENCE. PLEASE ADVISE IF FOLLOWUP

ACTION IS REQUIRED. POST WOULD ALSO BE INTERESTED IN ANY

READOUT OF THE LONDON MEETING OF THE ALEXANDRIA GROUP.

NICHOLSON
