SUBJECT: MAOIST ATROCITIES AGAINST CIVILIANS ON THE INCREASE

REF: (A) KATHMANDU 0604; (B) KATHMANDU 0450;

(C) KATHMANDU 0446

---------

SUMMARY

---------

1. (U) MAOIST ACTIVITIES HAVE TAKEN A MORE VIOLENT TURN, WITH

ATROCITIES PERPETRATED AGAINST HELPLESS CIVILIANS ESCALATING BOTH

IN NUMBER AND SEVERITY. REPORTS OF THE MAOISTS DECAPITATING

VICTIMS OR TORTURING THEM TO DEATH WITH KNIVES IN DISTRICTS

ACROSS THE COUNTRY HAVE BECOME A HEART-RENDING COMMONPLACE IN

NEPAL\'S MEDIA. OVER THE PAST TWO WEEKS ALONE, AS DETAILED BELOW,

THE MAOISTS HAVE BRUTALLY SLAIN CIVILIANS IN EACH OF NEPAL\'S FIVE

DEVELOPMENT REGIONS. LOCAL HUMAN RIGHTS GROUPS HAVE ATTEMPTED TO

SHINE LIGHT ON THIS MOST HORRIBLE ASPECT OF THE INSURGENCY, BUT

SOME OF THEIR INTERNATIONAL COUNTERPARTS ARE TO DATE APPARENTLY

LESS MOVED BY THE DOCUMENTED HUMAN RIGHTS ABUSES OF THE MAOISTS.

----------------------

IN THE EASTERN REGION

----------------------

2. (SBU) ON MARCH 21 MAOISTS ABDUCTED LEKNATH GAUTAM, A TEACHER

AND MEMBER OF THE LOCAL CHAPTER OF AMNESTY INTERNATIONAL, FROM

HIS HOME IN PANCHTHAR DISTRICT. HIS BODY WAS LATER FOUND NEAR AN

ABANDONED POLICE POST THAT HAD BEEN DESTROYED IN A PREVIOUS

MAOIST ATTACK. THE LOCAL NGO HUMAN RIGHTS AND PEACE SOCIETY

(HURPES) ISSUED A PRESS STATEMENT CONDEMNING THE KILLING. HURPES

MEMBER KRISHNA PAHADI ALSO TOLD POLOFF THAT THE NGO HAS

TEMPORARILY SUSPENDED A PROGRAM OF "PEACE" DRAMAS IN VARIOUS

DISTRICTS AFTER RECEIVING THREATS FROM MAOIST SYMPATHIZERS. THE

NGO HAS ALREADY HELD SUCH DRAMATIZATIONS IN 16 DIFFERENT

DISTRICTS, DRAWING CROWDS OF UP TO 2,000 IN SOME LOCATIONS.

----------------------

IN THE CENTRAL REGION

----------------------

3. (U) A GROUP OF MAOISTS KILLED BHOLA SHAH TELI, AN ACTIVE

MEMBER OF THE RULING NEPALI CONGRESS AT KARUNIYA IN RAUTAHAT

DISTRICT ON THE NIGHT OF MARCH 22. NEPALI CONGRESS ACTIVISTS

HAVE BEEN FREQUENT TARGETS OF MAOIST VIOLENCE, ESPECIALLY IN

OUTLYING AREAS.

----------------------

IN THE WESTERN REGION

----------------------

4. (U) MAOISTS ATTACKED AND BRUTALLY KILLED RAM MANI GYAWALI,

CHAIRMAN OF KERUNG VILLAGE DEVELOPMENT COMMITTEE (VDC),

ARGHAKHANCHI DISTRICT, WESTERN NEPAL, ON THE EVENING OF MARCH 12.

AFTER GYAWALI\'S HANDS AND LEGS WERE TIED WITH A ROPE, HE WAS

SLASHED WITH A KNIFE UNTIL HE DIED. TRAUMATIZED FAMILY MEMBERS

STATED THAT THEY WERE DETAINED BY THE MAOISTS WITHIN EARSHOT OF

THE MURDER, THROUGHOUT THE TWO AND A HALF HOUR ORDEAL. GYAWALI,

WHO HAD RELOCATED TO KATHMANDU AFTER RECEIVING DEATH THREATS, HAD

TEMPORARILY RETURNED TO THE VDC TO FOLLOW PROGRESS IN AN

ELECTRIFICATION PROGRAM IN HIS HOME VILLAGE. THE VICTIM\'S SISTER-

IN-LAW, A PROGRAM SPECIALIST AT USAID KATHMANDU, CONFIRMED THE

NEWS OF HIS VICIOUS SLAYING.

5. (U) THE INSURGENTS ALSO ATTACKED AND WOUNDED HARI DHAKAL, A

TEACHER IN SUNWAL VDC OF NAWALPARASI DISTRICT, MARHC 14.

ACCORDING TO ASSISTANT CHIEF DISTRICT OFFICER (CDO) OF

NAWALPARASI, DHAKAL WAS SHOT IN THE HEAD, AND IS IN CRITICAL

CONDITION. A GROUP OF MAOISTS SET FIRE TO THE HOUSE OF DEVI

PRASAD NEUPANE, VICE CHAIRMAN OF CHANDRESWORE VDC OF LAMJUNG

DISTRICT ON MARCH 15. NEUPANE IS A LOCAL LEADER OF THE NEPALI

CONGRESS PARTY. THE DISTRICT POLICE OFFICE IN GORKHA CONFIRMED

THAT MAOISTS KILLED YAM KUMARI ALE OF MANKAMANA VDC OF GORKHA

DISTRICT ON MARCH 20. SHE HAD EARLIER SURRENDERED TO THE

DISTRICT ADMINISTRATION OFFICE AND DISSOCIATED HERSELF FROM

MAOIST ACTIVITY ABOUT TWO MONTHS BEFORE. IN KASKI DISTRICT, TWO

FEMALE FAMILY MEMBERS OF DEVENDRA THAKURI, A MEMBER OF THE ROYAL

NEPALESE ARMY, WERE KILLED AFTER MAOISTS BOMBED HIS HOUSE IN

THUKMI THE NIGHT OF MARCH 26.

-------------------------

IN THE MID-WESTERN REGION

--------------------------

6. (U) IN DANG DISTRICT, A GROUP OF MAOIST INSURGENTS BEHEADED A

BUS OWNER IN KAVRE VDC ON MARCH 14. HIS BODY WAS RECOVERED NEAR

THE PREMISES OF THE LOCAL POST OFFICE, ACCORDING TO DANG\'S

ASSISTANT CDO. MAOISTS MURDERED LAL BAHADUR GHARTI, WARD

CHAIRMAN OF SAHIGA VDC IN DANG DISTRICT, ON MARCH 15. DANG\'S CDO

DESCRIBED THE KILLING AS MERCILESS, NOTING THE DECEASED HAD

SEVERAL KNIFE CUTS ON HIS NECK AND BODY. DANG DISTRICT POLICE

AND CDO ALSO REPORT MAOISTS DECAPITATED SHANKAR GHARTI OF

TRIBHUVAN MUNICIPALITY, DANG DISTRICT ON MARCH 19. A GROUP OF

ARMED MAOISTS SHOT DEAD GANESH BAHADUR SHAHI, A DISTRICT CHAIRMAN

OF NEPAL TRADE UNION CONGRESS, SURKHET DISTRICT, ON MARCH 27.

-----------------

IN THE FAR-WEST

----------------

7. (U) MAOISTS KILLED PREM BAHADUR BISTA, VICE CHAIRMAN OF

MANILEKH VDC IN DADELHURA DISTRICT OF THE FAR WESTERN HILL

REGION, HACKING HIM TO DEATH WITH A KHUKURI KNIFE ON MARCH 14,

THE SPOKESMAN OF THE MINISTRY OF DEFENSE CONFIRMED. THE LOCAL

PRESS ALSO REPORTED THAT MAOISTS STABBED TO DEATH VDC CHAIRMAN

PADAM SINGH BADAL AND UDAYA SINGH DHAMI OF HIKILA VDC, DARCHULA

DISTRICT, ON MARCH 16. THIS REPORT COULD NOT BE CONFIRMED THROUGH

THE DISTRICT ADMINISTRATION, AS TELEPHONE CONNECTIONS TO THAT

REMOTE AREA HAVE BEEN DISRUPTED.

--------

COMMENT

-------

8. (SBU) MAOISTS HAVE TORTURED AND MURDERED HELPLESS CIVILIANS

FROM THE BEGINNING OF THE INSURGENCY IN FEBRUARY 1996. THE

MAOISTS\' CAMPAIGN OF INTENSIFYING BRUTALITY TOWARD INNOCENT

CIVILIANS REVEALS AS SPURIOUS THEIR CLAIM TO REPRESENT THE BEST

INTERESTS OF THE COMMON PEOPLE. THE TIMING OF THE MAOIST CALL

FOR A GENERAL STRIKE (REFTEL A), COINCIDING WITH THE ALL-

IMPORTANT NATIONAL SECONDARY SCHOOL EXAMS, REINFORCES THIS

CONCLUSION. SUBODH RAJ PYAKUREL, GENERAL SECRETARY OF THE LOCAL

HUMAN RIGHTS ORGANIZATION INSEC, DESCRIBED THE IMPACT OF THE

STRIKE AS PARTICULARLY HARMFUL, SAYING "THE ATTEMPT TO DISTURB

THE EXAMINATION WITH WHICH THE FUTURE OF THOUSANDS OF STUDENTS IS

LINKED, IS A CRIME BIGGER THAN MURDER." APPARENTLY UNABLE TO WIN

SUPPORT FOR THEIR MOVEMENT THROUGH PERSUASION, THE MAOISTS ARE

TURNING TO TERROR AND INTIMIDATION TO QUASH POPULAR OPPOSITION.

WHILE MANY LOCAL HUMAN RIGHTS GROUPS PUBLICIZE AND PROTEST SUCH

ATROCITIES, WE ARE CONCERNED BY THE COMPARATIVE LACK OF

COMMENSURATE COVERAGE GIVEN MAOIST ABUSES BY INTERNATIONAL HUMAN

RIGHTS GROUPS.
