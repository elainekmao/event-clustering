ACTING CHIEF JUSTICE SWORN IN

-----------------------------

1. (U) IN A LONG-EXPECTED MOVE, PRESIDENT MUGABE SWORE IN

HIGH COURT JUDGE PRESIDENT GODFREY CHIDYAUSIKU AS ACTING

CHIEF JUSTICE OF THE ZIMBABWEAN SUPREME COURT ON MARCH 13.

CHIDYAUSIKU WILL SERVE IN AN ACTING CAPACITY AT LEAST UNTIL

THE FORMAL RETIREMENT OF CHIEF JUSTICE ANTHONY GUBBAY,

CURRENTLY ON LEAVE UNTIL JUNE 30. THE CEREMONY AT STATE

HOUSE WAS ATTENDED BY GUBBAY\'S FOUR COLLEAGUES ON THE BENCH

-- BUT NOT GUBBAY HIMSELF -- JUDGES FROM THE HIGH COURT,

JUSTICE MINISTER PATRICK CHINAMASA, OTHER CABINET

MINISTERS, AND ATTORNEY-GENERAL ANDREW CHIGOVERA. THE

EVENT RECEIVED PROMINENT COVERAGE IN THE GOVERNMENT-

CONTROLLED PRINT AND BROADCAST MEDIA.

COMMENT

-------

2. (C) THE GOVERNMENT\'S INTENTION TO REPLACE GUBBAY WITH

THE PRO-ZANU-PF CHIDYAUSIKU HAS LONG BEEN AN OPEN SECRET,

AND THE CHIEF JUSTICE\'S MARCH 2 AGREEMENT WITH THE GOZ

CONCERNING HIS EARLY RETIREMENT (REF A) MADE THIS POSSIBLE.

TWO PRIMARY QUESTIONS NOW REMAIN UNANSWERED: WILL

CHIDYAUSIKU, DURING HIS THREE-AND-A-HALF MONTHS AS ACTING

CHIEF JUSTICE, BE ABLE TO HELP DISLODGE HIS FOUR NEW

CONFIDENTIAL

COLLEAGUES, AND WILL HE REPLACE GUBBAY PERMANENTLY WHEN THE

CHIEF JUSTICE FORMALLY RETIRES ON JUNE 30?

3. (C) THE FIRST QUESTION IS OPEN TO INTERPRETATION, AS

NEITHER THE CONSTITUTION NOR HISTORICAL PRECEDENT ADDRESSES

THE POWERS EXERCISED BY AN ACTING CHIEF JUSTICE WHILE THE

CHIEF JUSTICE IS COMPETENT AND REMAINS IN THE COUNTRY.

GUBBAY\'S VIEW, AS ELABORATED ON MARCH 10 TO VISITING AF

A/PDAS MARK BELLAMY, WAS THAT ONLY GUBBAY -- UNTIL HIS

RETIREMENT -- HAS THE AUTHORITY TO RECOMMEND TO THE

PRESIDENT CONVOCATION OF A TRIBUNAL TO INVESTIGATE THE

EXPULSION OF JUDGES FROM THE BENCH. STERNFORD MOYO,

PRESIDENT OF THE LAW SOCIETY OF ZIMBABWE, AGREED WITH

GUBBAY, TELLING US ON MARCH 14 THAT, DURING THIS INTERIM

PERIOD, CHIDYAUSIKU\'S RECOMMENDING ESTABLISHMENT OF A

TRIBUNAL ON HIS OWN -- WITHOUT CONSULTING GUBBAY -- WOULD

BE UNCONSTITUTIONAL. PRESIDENT MUGABE AND HIS ADVISORS

LIKELY WILL TAKE THE OPPOSITE VIEW, AS THEY ARE ANXIOUS TO

REPLACE THE COURT\'S INDEPENDENT-MINDED JURISTS WITH JUDGES

MORE WILLING TO GIVE THE GOVERNMENT A PASS ON THE RULE OF

LAW.

4. (C) GUBBAY SURMISED TO BELLAMY THAT, SINCE THE TRIBUNAL

ROUTE IS SO FRAUGHT WITH CONSTITUTIONAL PITFALLS AND SINCE

CHIDYAUSIKU CANNOT ALONE CARRY ZANU-PF\'S WATER, MUGABE

LIKELY WOULD TAKE THE EASIER -- AND LEGAL -- PATH OF SIMPLY

PACKING THE COURT WITH ADDITIONAL, POLITICALLY SYMPATHETIC

JUDGES. WE HAVE HEARD THAT JUSTICE MINISTER PATRICK

CHINAMASA HAS QUIETLY APPROACHED SEVERAL BLACK LAWYERS

ABOUT ACCEPTING JUDICIAL APPOINTMENTS, ALTHOUGH IT IS

UNCLEAR FOR WHICH COURT HE WAS RECRUITING. GUBBAY TOLD

BELLAMY HE FULLY EXPECTS CHIDYAUSIKU TO BE NAMED AS HIS

PERMANENT REPLACEMENT BEGINNING JULY 1. STERNFORD MOYO,

HOWEVER, SEPARATELY TOLD THE ACTING AF P/DAS THAT HE AND

OTHER MEMBERS OF THE JUDICIAL SERVICES COMMISSION --

CHARGED BY THE CONSTITUTION WITH ADVISING THE PRESIDENT ON

JUDICIAL NOMINEES -- WERE WORKING TO TRY TO BLOCK

CHIDYAUSIKU\'S ASCENSION TO THE APEX OF ZIMBABWE\'S

JUDICIARY.

5. (C) WE SUSPECT THE GOZ IS EAGER TO INSTALL A PLIANT

SUPREME COURT AS SOON AS POSSIBLE, AS THE COURT MIGHT SOON

CONSIDER SEVERAL CONSTITUTIONAL CASES THAT COULD HAVE A

DIRECT IMPACT ON THE RULING PARTY\'S POLITICAL PROGRAM. THE

HIGH COURT, FOR INSTANCE, IS CURRENTLY HEARING CHALLENGES

TO THE RESULTS IN 37 CONSTITUENCIES IN LAST JUNE\'S

PARLIAMENTARY ELECTIONS. A DECISION BY THE HIGH COURT TO

INVALIDATE THE RESULTS IN ANY OF THOSE CONSTITUENCIES --

AND TO CALL FOR NEW ELECTIONS TO FILL THE SEATS -- WOULD

WITHOUT DOUBT BE APPEALED TO THE SUPREME COURT. IN

ADDITION, THE MDC PLANS TO CHALLENGE THE CONSTITUTIONALITY

OF THE REPRESSIVE COLONIAL-ERA LAW AND ORDER MAINTENANCE

ACT (LOMA), WHICH THE GOZ HAS USED TO CRACK DOWN ON LEADING

OPPOSITION FIGURES, INCLUDING MDC PRESIDENT MORGAN

TSVANGIRAI AND HIS VICE-PRESIDENT GIBSON SIBANDA. CHIEF
