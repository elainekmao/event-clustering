SUBJECT: ITALY WON\'T FORCE IRAN WTO ACCESSION ISSUE

CLASSIFIED BY: CLASSIFIED BY ECOUNS KAREN MILLIKEN; REASON 1.5 B/D.

1. (C) SUMMARY AND COMMENT. ITALY STRONGLY DISAGREES WITH

USG POLICY ON IRAN\'S POTENTIAL ACCESSION TO THE WORLD TRADE

ORGANIZATION BUT DOES NOT INTEND TO PUSH THE EU TO FORCE THE

ISSUE AT THE MAY 13 WTO GENERAL COUNCIL MEETING OR

THEREAFTER, ACCORDING TO MFA\'S WTO COORDINATOR. ALTHOUGH THE

ITALIANS WILL NOT MAKE AN ISSUE OF IRAN\'S WTO AMBITIONS,

NEITHER CAN WE EXPECT THEM TO CARRY REF A\'S SUGGESTED MESSAGE

TYING CLOSER ECONOMIC TIES TO IMPROVED PERFORMANCE ON THE

LISTED ISSUES. ON THE CONTRARY, WE CAN COUNT ON THEM TO

COMMISERATE WITH THE IRANIANS ABOUT PERCEIVED USG OBSTINACY

ON WTO AS BENEFITING CONSERVATIVES AT THE EXPENSE OF

REFORMERS. END SUMMARY AND COMMENT

2. (C) ITALY ACCEPTS REF A\'S STRAIGHTFORWARD MESSAGE THAT WE

ARE NOT OPEN TO DISCUSSION OF IRANIAN ACCESSION TO THE WTO

AND, CONSEQUENTLY, ITALY SEES THE MATTER AS A NON-ISSUE FOR

THE MAY 13 WTO GENERAL COUNCIL MEETING, MAURIZIO ZANINI,

MFA\'S WTO COORDINATOR, TOLD ECONOFF ON APRIL 17. ECONOFF

ASKED WHETHER ITALY, LONG A FACILITATOR OF IRAN\'S CONTACTS

WITH THE WEST, WOULD PRESS THE EU TO SPONSOR IRAN IN GENEVA.

ZANINI REPLIED THAT ITALY PICKS VERY CAREFULLY THE ISSUES ON

WHICH IT LOOKS TO LEAD THE EU AND, IN THE TRADE AREA, ITALY\'S

SINGLE PROACTIVE ISSUE IS INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY PROTECTION

FOR GEOGRAPHICALLY DESIGNATED PRODUCTS. HE SAID ITALY WILL

NOT SPEND ITS WTO CAPITAL IN THE EU ON IRANIAN ACCESSION.

3. (C) ALTHOUGH CONCEDING THE ESSENTIAL POINT OF OUR

DEMARCHE, ZANINI LAMBASTED THE USG POSITION. HE WANTED TO BE

SURE THE USG UNDERSTANDS THAT SUCCESS IN BLOCKING CONSENSUS

ON AN ACCESSION PROCESS FOR IRAN IS NOT TANTAMOUNT TO ITALY

OR THE EU ADOPTING WHAT HE SEES AS MISGUIDED U.S. POLICY. HE

SAID ITALY, ON THE CONTRARY, "SUPPORTS EVERYONE FOR WTO

ACCESSION. THE WTO\'S MOST COMPELLING ASPECT IS ITS

INCLUSIVENESS. WHEN THE ISRAELIS SPEAK, FOR EXAMPLE, THE

ARABS DON\'T WALK OUT." THE U.S., HE INSISTED, IS ASKING THE

WRONG QUESTION. FOR ZANINI, THE QUESTION ISN\'T WHETHER

IRAN\'S POLITICAL CONDUCT WARRANTS WTO MEMBERSHIP BUT WHETHER

THE WORLD IS BETTER OFF WITH IRAN, NOTWITHSTANDING ITS

POLITICS, SUBJECT TO INTERNATIONAL TRADE RULES. HE BELIEVES

IRAN\'S EVENTUAL WTO MEMBERSHIP WOULD HELP STABILIZE THE WORLD

TRADING SYSTEM AND, ESPECIALLY GIVEN MAJOR ITALIAN

INVESTMENTS IN IRAN, HE WOULD LOOK NO FURTHER FOR

JUSTIFICATION FOR LAUNCHING AN ACCESSION PROCESS. IF THE USG

WANTS TO LOOK FURTHER, HE SUGGESTED, WE MIGHT CONSIDER

WHETHER ENHANCED STABILITY IN THE TRADING SYSTEM MIGHT NOT

FOSTER ENHANCED POLITICAL STABILITY AS WELL.

4. (C) ECHOING COMMENTS MADE BY MFA ACTING GULF STATES OFFICE

DIRECTOR EMANUELE FARRUGGIA TO POLOFF AND ECONOFF, ALSO ON

APRIL 17, ZANINI SAID THE DISCUSSION OF WTO ACCESSION FOR

IRAN IS, IN ANY EVENT, LARGELY SYMBOLIC. MFA SEES THE

IRANIAN ECONOMY AS YEARS AWAY FROM A SERIOUS WTO RUN AND

FALLING EVEN FURTHER BEHIND AS WITNESSED BY IRAN\'S RECENT

FAILURE TO ENACT EVEN RUDIMENTARY FOREIGN INVESTMENT

INCENTIVES (SEE ALSO REF B). NOTING THAT OUR DEMARCHE MAKES

NO MENTION OF THE OBVIOUS ECONOMIC BASIS FOR DENYING IRAN WTO

ACCESSION, FARRUGGIA SAID ITALY IS PRESSING IRAN HARD FOR

BOTH ECONOMIC AND POLITICAL REFORM BUT DOES NOT SEE ONE

EFFORT AS LEVERAGING THE OTHER.

5. (C) IN RESPONSE, EMBOFFS PRESENTED REF A POINTS,

EMPHASIZING THAT A LINKAGE BETWEEN CESSATION OF IRAN\'S

OBJECTIONABLE BEHAVIOR AND ECONOMIC COOPERATION COULD GIVE

IRANIAN REFORMERS BOTH A MEANS AND A MOTIVE TO AFFECT THE

BEHAVIOR OF CONSERVATIVES. ECONOMIC COOPERATION UNLINKED TO

POLITICAL BEHAVIOR, WE CONTINUED, COULD LEAVE CONSERVATIVES

CONVINCED THAT IRAN\'S POLITICAL CONDUCT WAS OF NO CONCERN TO

WESTERN ECONOMIES. FARRUGGIA ARGUED THE OPPOSITE POINT --

THAT WITHHOLDING ECONOMIC COOPERATION WOULD BOLSTER THE

CONSERVATIVES\' CONTENTION THAT IRANIAN RAPPROCHEMENT WITH THE

WEST IS ONE-SIDED.

6. (C) FARRUGGIA COMMENTED ON TWO OF REF A\'S POINTS IN

PARTICULAR -- IRAN\'S EVIDENT NUCLEAR AMBITIONS AND ITS

SUPPORT FOR VIOLENT OPPOSITION TO THE MEPP. HE SAID IRAN HAS

SEEN A NUCLEAR WEAPON CAPACITY AS AN ESSENTIAL NATIONAL

INTEREST EVER SINCE THE SHAH\'S REGIME. HE FEELS THAT ANY

EFFORT, THROUGH THE NONPROLIFERATION TREATY OR OTHERWISE, TO

DISSUADE IRAN FROM PURSUIT OF NUCLEAR WEAPONS MUST TAKE INTO

ACCOUNT THE VISCERAL PLACE THESE WEAPONS OCCUPY IN THE

IRANIAN NATIONAL PSYCHE. ON THE OTHER HAND, HIS FREQUENT

CONTACTS WITH IRANIAN OFFICIALS HAS NOT LEFT HIM WITH A SENSE

THAT SUPPORT FOR PALESTINIAN NATIONALISM IS SIMILARLY CENTRAL

TO THEM.

7. (C) COMMENT. ITALY GENERALLY SAVORS ITS OCCASIONAL ROLE

AS WASHINGTON-TEHRAN MESSAGE CARRIER, MOST RECENTLY EVIDENT

WHEN ITALY CONVEYED IRAN\'S CONCERN ABOUT ITS PROFILE IN DOD\'S

NUCLEAR POSTURE REVIEW. THE ITALIANS, HOWEVER, DRAW THE

LINE AT ADOPTING AS THEIR OWN THE USG-SUGGESTED LINKAGE

BETWEEN POLITICAL REFORM AND ECONOMIC COOPERATION. THE

ITALIAN ENERGY GIANT ENI, BY ITSELF, HAS TEN BILLION DOLLARS

AT RISK IN IRAN AND THE GOVERNMENT OF ITALY, ENI\'S BIGGEST

SHAREHOLDER, ISN\'T ABOUT TO MAKE IT MORE AT RISK. MFA WILL

CONTINUE TO PUSH IRAN ON WMD, BALLISTIC MISSILES, SUPPORT FOR

TERRORISM, AND THE REST, BUT ECONOMIC RELATIONS WILL CONTINUE

ALONG A DIFFERENT PATH. IN PARTICULAR, ITALY HAS LONG

SUPPORTED IRAN FOR WTO ACCESSION AND WILL CONTINUE TO DO SO,

ALBEIT NOT TO THE POINT OF PROVOKING AN EU CONFRONTATION WITH

THE U.S. WE SHOULD EXPECT, MOREOVER, THAT THE ITALIANS WILL

COMMISERATE FULLY WITH INEVITABLE IRANIAN COMPLAINTS THAT USG

OBSTINACY ON WTO ACCESSION PLAYS INTO THE HANDS OF THOSE IN

IRAN WHO OPPOSE REFORM.
