SUBJECT: MINISTRY OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS DENIES ANY

ZIMBABWEAN EMBASSY IN TRIPOLI

REFTEL: STATE 73268

A MINISTRY OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS CONTACT TOLD US ON APRIL

25 THAT THE GOVERNMENT OF ZIMBABWE HAS NO DIPLOMATIC

REPRESENTATION IN TRIPOLI AND THAT HE WAS UNAWARE OF ANY

PLANS TO ESTABLISH ANY. THIS INFORMATION IS CONSISTENT

WITH WHAT WE WERE INFORMED BY THE MFA IN JANUARY

(REFTEL).
