1. SECRET - ENTIRE TEXT.

-

2. SUMMARY: MOHTASHEMI IS CONTINUING HIS DIRECT ATTACK

ON RAFSANJANI THROUGH THE FIVE-YEAR ECONOMIC PLAN. HE

STRESSES THE NEED TO RELY ON FAITH AND MARTYRDOM AND TO

REJECT TECHNOLOGY AND DIPLOMACY AS WAYS OF DEALING WITH

THE WEST. HE COUPLES THIS TO DEMANDS THAT "DEATH TO

AMERICA" REMAIN A BASIC PRINCIPLE OF THE REVOLUTION.

ARDABILI ADDS FURTHER ATTACKS, AND BOTH ARE REINVIGORATING

THE CALLS FOR RUSHDIE\'S DEATH AND LINKING ANY WATERING

DOWN OF THIS POLICY WITH APPEASEMENT OF THE U.S. AND U.K.

WITH RAFSANJANI HAVING TO DEFEND HIMSELF ON THE ECONOMY

AND THE PRINCIPLES OF THE REVOLUTION IT IS UNLIKELY

THAT HE WOULD FIND THE MOMENT PROPITIOUS TO FIGHT ON A

SECOND FRONT AND TRY TO IMPROVE RELATIONS WITH THE U.S.

IN OUR VIEW, THIS IS THE CURRENT REALITY OF IRANIAN

POLITICS. THE RECENT TEHRAN TIMES EDITORIAL (REF D)

CALLING FOR RELEASE OF THE HOSTAGES HAS NEITHER BEEN

PICKED UP BY THE PERSIAN-LANGUAGE PRESS NOR ECHOED BY

RAFSANJANI. WE DOUBT IT IS A SERIOUS INDICATOR OF

POLICY CHANGE. END SUMMARY.

-

3. IN AN EXTENSIVE INTERVIEW RUN IN KEYHAN HAVAI ON

FEBRUARY 7 (REF A), MAJLIS MEMBER AND FORMER INTERIOR

MINISTER MOHTASHEMI NEVER MENTIONS RAFSANJANI BY NAME

BUT LAYS DOWN CHALLENGES TO HIS PROGRAM. MOHTASHEMI

BEGINS BY DENYING THAT ANY REEVALUATION OF THE

REVOLUTION IS NECESSARY; RATHER, IT IS BACKSLIDING FROM

REVOLUTIONARY PRINCIPLES WHICH HAS CAUSED IRAN\'S

WEAKNESS. HE SAYS THAT WHEN IRAN DEPENDED ON HEZBOLLAH

AND MARTYRDOM IT SUCCEEDED; WHEN IT RELIED ON TECHNOLOGY

AND DIPLOMACY, IT FAILED.

-

4. WITHOUT MENTIONING THE FIVE-YEAR PLAN DIRECTLY,

HE ATTACKS ITS BASIC DIRECTIONS, REPEATING CRITICISMS

MADE DURING THE MAJLIS DEBATES. ONE OF HIS TARGETS IS

THE PRIVATE SECTOR. "TODAY WE ARE AGAIN MOVING IN THE

WRONG DIRECTION." PROFITS ARE BEING DIRECTED TO THE

PRIVATE SECTOR. "AS A RESULT, THE PROFITS WILL ONCE

AGAIN GO TO THE POCKETS OF A SPECIAL GROUP OF PEOPLE.

THE MASSES CANNOT BENEFIT FROM THIS PROGRAM."

MOHTASHEMI WILL PROBABLY FIND SUPPORT FOR THIS ATTACK

SINCE PROFITEERS ARE BENEFITING FROM RAFSANJANI\'S FREE

MARKET POLICIES.

-

5. ANOTHER TARGET IS THE PLAN\'S EMPHASIS ON INDUSTRIALI-

ZATION. MOHTASHEMI ARGUES THIS WILL ONLY MAKE IRAN

DEPENDENT ON FOREIGNERS FOR PARTS AND MATERIALS. THIS

IS A STRONG THEME IN A NATION WHICH BELIEVES OVER-

WHELMINGLY THAT THE HISTORY OF THE LAST 100 YEARS IS ONE

OF SUFFERING FROM FOREIGN DOMINATION, FIRST BRITISH AND

THEN AMERICAN. MOHTASHEMI CALLS FOR A BASIC

REORIENTATION FROM FAVORING INDUSTRY TO FAVORING

AGRICULTURE AND SAYS THIS WAS KHOMEINI\'S COMMAND.

-

6. HE DEALS AT LENGTH WITH THE NEED FOR HOSTILITY

TO AMERICA. "DEATH TO AMERICA" IS A PRINCIPLE OF THE

IMAM. SLOGANS ARE BASIC TO THE REVOLUTION. IF THE

SLOGAN OF DEATH TO AMERICAN IS REMOVED, THE PEOPLE WILL

NOT RECOGNIZE THEIR ENEMY. ANIMOSITY AND HATE MUST FIND

A PERMANENT PLACE IN PEOPLE\'S HEARTS. WITHOUT

QUALIFICATION HE REJECTS RELATIONS WITH THE UNITED STATES.

-

7. MOHTASHEMI CRITICIZES THE AUTHORITIES FOR WEAKNESS

IN IMPLEMENTING THE DEATH DECREE ON SALMAN RUSHDIE,

THROUGH LACK OF DIRECT ACTION AND INADEQUATE THEOLOGICAL

DEFENSE OF THE DECREES THROUGHOUT THE WORLD. THE

RUSHDIE THEME IS PICKED UP AGAIN IN A FEBRUARY 23 SERMON

OF MUSAVI-ARDABILI (REF B), WHO ADDS, "THE ISSUE

CONCERNS AMERICA; THE ISSUE CONCERNS ENGLAND. IT IS

ABOUT ATTEMPTS TO DELIVER BLOWS AT THE REVOLUTION."

-

8. ARDABILI\'S SERMON IS ALSO NOTEWORTHY FOR ITS

COMPLETE ABSENCE OF MENTION OF RAFSANJANI AND KHAMENEI

ON AN OCCASION WHEN PRAISE OF THE RULERS IS TRADITIONAL.

ARDABILI DOES FIND ANOTHER WAY OF INDIRECTLY ATTACKING

RAFSANJANI IN DISCUSSING THE THREAT TO IRAN IMPLICIT

IN THE BREAKUP OF THE COMMUNIST EMPIRE. HE STATES THAT

THE WEST IS HOPEFUL OF DEPOLARIZING THE WORLD, "THE

WOLVES HAVE SHARPENED THEIR CLAWS, GLOBAL ECONOMIC CENTERS

HAVE DRAWN UP CERTAIN PLANS." THIS CONTRASTS WITH

RSFSANJANI\'S FEBRUARY 20 PRESENTATION OF THE BUDGET BILL

(REF C), IN WHICH HE SAW DANGERS IN THE CHANGES IN EUROPE

BUT ALSO A POSSIBILITY OF POSITIVE RESULTS FOR NATIONS

"LIKE US WHO WISH TO BE INDEPENDENT." ARDABILI IS UNMOVED

BY RAFSANJANI\'S CAUTION AGAINST "ONE-SIDED JUDGMENT."

-

9. THESE PUBLIC DECLARATIONS BY SUPPORTERS OF THE MOST

EXTREME POSITIONS OF THE LATE AYATOLLAH LAY DOWN AN

EXTENSIVE CHALLENGE TO RAFSANJANI. THEY SUGGEST THAT

PASSAGE BY THE MAJLIS OF THE ECONOMIC PLAN WILL NOT GIVE

HIM AN EASIER ROAD THAN HE HAS HAD. THE BUREAUCRATIC

DISORGANIZATION OF IRAN IS LIKELY TO PROVIDE AMPLE

FAILURES IN THE PLAN\'S AMBITIOUS GOALS. RAFSANJANI

MUST EXPECT THAT EVERY SETBACK WILL BE UTILIZED BY

HIS OPPOSITION TO PROCLAIM THAT THE "MASSES" ARE NOT

BEING AIDED AND THAT IRAN IS SINKING DEEPER INTO FOREIGN

DEPENDENCE. WITH SUCH A BATTLE ON HIS HANDS, IT IS MOST

UNLIKELY THAT RAFSANJANI WILL SEE THIS AS A SENSIBLE TIME

TO UNDERTAKE ANY WARMING TO THE UNITED STATES -- A

SECOND, AND RELATED, AREA ON WHICH THE HARDLINERS HAVE

STAKED OUT THEIR OPPOSITION AND WRAPPED THEMSELVES IN

THE BANNER OF THE DEPARTED IMAM.

-

10. THE ENGLISH-LANGUAGE TEHRAN TIMES EDITORIAL OF

FEBRUARY 22 (REF D) CALLING FOR THE RELEASE OF THE

HOSTAGES IN LEBANON DOES NOT STRIKE US AS A SIGNIFICANT

BASIS FOR ALTERNATIVE ANALYSIS. THE PAPER IS PRIMARILY

A VEHICLE FOR SPEAKING TO THE WEST, NOT TO THE FORCES

THAT MAKE POLICY IN IRAN. THE EDITORIAL, AS FAR AS WE

ARE AWARE, HAS NEITHER BEEN PICKED UP BY THE PERSIAN

PRESS NOR DISCUSSED IN PUBLIC STATEMENTS. RAFSANJANI\'S

COMMENTS ON THE FOREIGN SITUATION BEFORE THE MAJLIS

(REF C) LIKEWISE MAKE NO MENTION OF THE HOSTAGES. WHAT

HE DOES DO IS TO REJECT EXPLICITLY COMPROMISE WITH THE

WEST, ALTHOUGH HE NOTES THAT IRAN COULD IMPROVE ITS

ECONOMIC CONDITION IF "WE WERE PREPARED TO COMPROMISE

WITH THE ARROGANT POWERS OF THE WORLD."

-

11. IRANIANS ARE ADEPT AT GOING IN ONE DIRECTION WHILE

KEEPING OTHERS OFF BALANCE BY THROWING OFF TANTALIZING

HINTS OF DIFFERENT POSSIBILITIES IN FORA WHICH HOLD NO

ACCOUNTABILITY FOR IRANIAN POLICY MAKERS. WE BELIEVE

THE TEHRAN TIMES EDITORIAL IS ANOTHER SUCH RED HERRING.

THE PUBLIC CHALLENGES OF MOHTASHEMI AND ARDABILI

REPRESENT THE REALITY WITH WHICH RAFSANJANI WILL HAVE

TO COPE FOR SOME TIME TO COME.

WALKER
