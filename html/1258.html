SUBJECT: VIEW FROM ZIMBABWE ON DROC ISSUES DISCUSSED IN

MAPUTO

CLASSIFIED BY AMB. TOM MCDONALD. REASONS: 1.5 (B) AND

(D).

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 HARARE 04749 251442Z

1. (C) AMBASSADOR MCDONALD SPOKE TO FORMER INDUSTRY AND

COMMERCE MINISTER NATHAN SHAMUYARIRA ABOUT PRESIDENT

MUGABE\'S CONGOLESE DISCUSSIONS WITH REGIONAL LEADERS IN

MAPUTO AUGUST 20-23. SHAMUYARIRA, WHO EARLIER IN HIS

CAREER WAS FOREIGN MINISTER AND IS STILL IN CLOSE

COUNSEL WITH MUGABE, PROVIDED A BRIEF READOUT OF

MUGABE\'S ONE-ON-ONE MEETING (WITH NO NOTETAKERS) WITH

UGANDAN PRESIDENT MUSEVENI AND OTHERS. SHAMUYARIRA, WHO

ACCOMPANIED THE PRESIDENT TO MAPUTO BUT WAS NOT PRESENT

AT ANY OF THE RELEVANT MEETINGS, CONVEYED MUGABE\'S VIEW

THAT PRESIDENT KABILA IS NOW BEING MORE FLEXIBLE AND

WILL HELP GET THE LUSAKA ACCORDS BACK ON TRACK.

APPARENTLY, KABILA WILL PERMIT THE MONUC DEPLOYMENT IN

CONGOLESE TERRITORY AS THE FIRST CONCRETE STEP IN

REVIVING THE ACCORDS. ADDITIONALLY, POSITIVE ACTION BY

RWANDA AND UGANDA COULD BRING REBEL OFFENSIVES TO AN

END. SHAMUYARIRA FURTHER REPORTED THAT THE ROLE OF

MASIRE AS FACILITATOR IS STILL A POINT OF CONTENTION, SO

OTHER LEADERS WERE BEING CONSIDERED, INCLUDING NELSON

MANDELA. HE THOUGHT THAT KABILA WAS OPEN TO MANDELA

ASSUMING THE FACILITATOR ROLE AND THAT THE FORMER

PRESIDENT WAS ALSO INTERESTED.

2. (C) COMMENT: SHAMUYARIRA\'S BELIEF (AND PRESUMABLY

MUGABE\'S) THAT KABILA WILL TAKE THESE NECESSARY STEPS TO

REVIVE LUSAKA IS SOMEWHAT OVERLY OPTIMISTIC AT THIS

POINT. GIVEN KABILA\'S OPEN AND WELL KNOWN DISLIKE OF

MANDELA, IT IS HARD FOR US TO THINK THAT THIS IS A REAL

POSSIBILITY. HOWEVER, MUGABE\'S CONTINUING STRONG FOCUS

ON SEEKING A PEACEFUL SOLUTION TO THE DROC WAR

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 HARARE 04749 251442Z

UNDERSCORES HIS DESIRE TO WITHDRAW THE TROOP DEPLOYMENT

THAT HIS COUNTRY CANNOT AFFORD.

3. (C) COMMENT CONTINUED: PROCEDURALLY, IT IS

INTERESTING TO NOTE THAT NO ONE FROM THE FOREIGN

MINISTRY WAS INVOLVED IN THESE DISCUSSIONS IN MAPUTO AND

NO ONE AT MFA KNEW ANYTHING OF SUBSTANCE ABOUT THEM WHEN

WE INQUIRED TODAY. IT SOUNDS LIKE SHAMUYARIRA WILL BE

PLAYING THE ROLE OF SPECIAL ADVISOR ON SEVERAL IMPORTANT

FOREIGN POLICY ISSUES. THE FORMER FOREIGN MINISTER MAY

NOW FUNCTION LIKE SPEAKER OF PARLIAMENT EMMERSON

MNANGAGWA ON SUCH WEIGHTY MATTERS. HE TOLD THE

AMBASSADOR THAT HE IS ACCOMPANYING MUGABE ON HIS TRIP TO

LIBYA, WHICH BEGINS ON AUGUST 27 OR 28. SHAMUYARIRA

AGREED TO GIVE THE AMBASSADOR A READOUT ON THESE ISSUES

WHEN HE RETURNS FROM TRIPOLI LATER NEXT WEEK.

MCDONALD
