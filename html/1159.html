SUBJECT: CUBAN DOCTORS SHUFFLED TO ANOTHER IMMIGRATION

HOLDING FACILITY

REF: A). STATE 115930 (NOTAL)

B). HARARE 3264

CLASSIFIED BY DEPUTY CHIEF OF MISSION EARL M. IRVING FOR

REASONS 1.5 B/D.

1. (C) UNHCR REGIONAL REPRESENTATIVE OLUSEYI BAJULAIYE

TOLD AMBASSADOR MCDONALD JUNE 17 THAT CUBAN DOCTORS

LEONEL CORDOVA RODRIGUEZ AND NORIS DEL CARMEN PENA

MARTINEZ HAD BEEN TRANSFERRED FROM GOROMONZI REMAND

CENTER TO ANOTHER, UNDISCLOSED (TO US) LOCATION WITHIN

HARARE, STILL UNDER THE CONTROL OF THE ZIMBABWEAN

IMMIGRATION AUTHORITIES. BAJULAIYE SAID THAT HE AND

LOCAL UNHCR REPRESENTATIVE JOHN ADU INSPECTED THE

LOCATION PRIOR TO THE DOCTORS\' TRANSFER AND SATISFIED

THEMSELVES THAT IT WAS FIT FOR HABITATION. IN ADDITION,

THE UNHCR OFFICIAL HAD NO NEWS AS OF JUNE 18 ON PROGRESS

ON A DECISION BY EITHER THE ZIMBABWEAN GOVERNMENT OR

PRESIDENT MUGABE ON THE DISPOSITION OF THE DOCTORS. HE

ALSO COULD SHED NO LIGHT ON PROGRESS TO IDENTIFY A THIRD

COUNTRY WILLING TO ACCEPT THE CUBANS IF RELEASE IS

FINALLY GIVEN THE GREEN LIGHT. (COMMENT: IN SHORT, AS

OF THIS WRITING FORWARD MOVEMENT HERE ON THE CASE

REMAINS STALLED. END COMMENT).
