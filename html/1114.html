SUBJECT: ESTABLISHING A PERFORMANCE-BASED STANDARD FOR

GAS CONNECTOR HOSES

REF: USDOC 00884

1. POST CONTACTED THE DUTCH MINISTRY OF ECONOMIC AFFAIRS

AND CONVEYED U.S. GOVERNMENT\'S CONCERN ABOUT THE

POTENTIAL DEVELOPMENT OF DESIGN-BASED STANDARDS OVER

PERFORMANCE-BASED STANDARDS BY THE CEN TECHNICAL

COMMITTEE.

2. THE SPOKESMAN OF THE DUTCH MINISTRY OF ECONOMIC

AFFAIRS STATED THAT HE IS FAMILIAR WITH CASE OF THE

DORMONT GAS CONNECTOR HOSES. IN THIS PARTICULAR CASE, HE

ADVISED THAT DORMONT SHOULD AGAIN MAKE AN EFFORT TO

PRESENT ITS POSITION FOR THE CEN TECHNICAL COMMITTEE. HE

ALSO ADVISED THAT DORMONT CAN OBTAIN CE MARKING FOR ITS

GAS CONNECTORS. HE RAISED THE STANDARDS ISSUE AT THE LAST

MEETING IN MAY ALTHOUGH IT WAS NOT ON THE AGENDA.
