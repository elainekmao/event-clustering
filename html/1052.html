SUBJECT: MPINGA: MNANGAGWA PLAYS BOTH ENDS AGAINST THE

MIDDLE

CLASSIFIED BY AMBASSADOR TOM MCDONALD FOR REASONS 1.5

(B) AND (D).

1. (C) MINISTER OF JUSTICE, LEGAL AND PARLIAMENTARY

AFFAIRS EMMERSON MNANGAGWA TOLD ME APRIL 14 THAT HE HAS

KNOWN SOUTH AFRICAN/CONGOLESE BUSINESSMAN KALAA MPINGA

FOR FOUR YEARS. MPINGA, WHOSE AGE HE GUESSED TO BE 38-

40, IS THE NEPHEW OF THE DEMOCRATIC REPUBLIC OF THE CONGO\'S

MINISTER OF STATE FOR PETROLEUM, PIERRE-VICTOR MPOYO,

AND IS ORIGINALLY FROM KASAI ORIENTALE PROVINCE.

ACCORDING TO MNANGAGWA, MPINGA IS A "POLITICAL INFANT,"

WHO CLAIMS TO BE THE HEAD OF 600 CONGOLESE INTELLECTUALS

AND PROFESSIONALS IN THE DIASPORA (IN EUROPE AND CANADA)

WHO CONSTITUTE HIS "BODY POLITIC." MPINGA, WHO LAST

CALLED ON MNANGAGWA "ABOUT TWO MONTHS AGO," CLAIMS

THAT HE IS IN CONTACT WITH JEAN-PIERRE BEMBA, WHOM HE

DESCRIBES AS "UNRELIABLE, UNPREDICTABLE, AND A MAN

OF CHAOS."

2. (C) MPINGA ASKED MNANGAGWA WHAT INTERESTS ZIMBABWE

HAD IN THE CONGO, TO WHICH, AFTER A LENGTHY PREFACE ON

THE REASON FOR ZIMBABWE\'S INTERVENTION IN THE DROC, THE

MINISTER REPLIED THE COSLEG COMPANY, WHICH HE

CHARACTERIZED AS A JOINT VENTURE BETWEEN THE "ECONOMIC

ARMS" OF THE ZIMBABWEAN AND CONGOLESE ARMIES. THE

ZIMBABWEAN MINISTER CAUTIONED HIM THAT THOSE ECONOMIC

INTERESTS DID NOT AFFECT "OUR STAYING OR LEAVING; WE ARE

ANXIOUS TO LEAVE." MPINGA THEN REPORTEDLY TOLD

MNANGAGWA THAT HIS GROUP HAD ANALYZED THE CURRENT

SITUATION AND CONCLUDED THAT ZIMBABWE\'S INTERESTS IN THE

CONGO SHOULD BE HONORED.

3. (C) MNANGAGWA EXPRESSED CURIOSITY THAT MPINGA, WHOM

HE DESCRIBED AS AN "EMPLOYEE" IN CONTRAST TO MY

REFERRING TO HIM AS A "BUSINESSMAN" BECAUSE BUSINESSMEN

PAY SALARIES, SEEMED TO HAVE FEW PROFESSIONAL

OBLIGATIONS AND WAS ABLE TO MOVE AROUND SO FREELY.

ACCORDING TO THE ZIMBABWEAN MINISTER, MPINGA REVEALED

THAT ANGLO-AMERICAN, HIS EMPLOYER, PUT A PLANE AT HIS

DISPOSAL AND GAVE HIM AN UNLIMITED EXPENSE ACCOUNT.

MPINGA REPORTED THAT HE HAD HELD DISCUSSIONS WITH BOTH

THE REBELS AND THE GOVERNMENT. ACCORDING TO MNANGAGWA,

MPINGA\'S PLAN IS TO CREATE A NEW NATIONAL LEADERSHIP

THAT WOULD EXCLUDE BOTH KABILA AND BEMBA, OF WHICH HE,

MPINGA, WOULD BE PART. THE ZIMBABWEAN MINISTER TOLD ME

HE WARNED MPINGA NOT ENTERTAIN THE VIEW OF SEEING THE

ZIMBABWEAN GOVERNMENT GOING AGAINST KABILA. RATHER, HE

COUNSELED MPINGA TO SEEK UNITY, BECAUSE BY EXCLUDING KEY

FIGURES HE WON\'T GET FAR. HE ADVISED MPINGA TO FORM A

PARTY AND CONTEST ELECTIONS, AND NOTED THAT ZIMBABWE

WOULD ACCEPT THEIR RESULTS, EVEN IF KABILA WERE REMOVED

BY THEM. ON THAT POINT, MNANGAGWA THOUGHT THAT IF AN

ELECTION WERE HELD IN THE CONGO TODAY, KABILA WOULD WIN,

TAKING FIVE KEY PROVINCES, ALTHOUGH HE WOULD FACE

SUBSTANTIAL OPPOSITION. NEVERTHELESS, "WE ADVISE

[KABILA] HOW TO STAY IN OFFICE," THE JUSTICE MINISTER

JOKED.

4. (C) MNANGAGWA THEN TOLD ME THAT HE HAD NOT REVEALED

TO KABILA THAT MPINGA IS AGAINST HIM, BUT RATHER

INFORMED THE CONGOLESE PRESIDENT THAT THE YOUNGER MAN

WAS LIKE A UNIVERSITY STUDENT, FULL OF "WILD DREAMS,"

AND READY TO RIOT AGAINST ANYTHING. TO THE ZIMBABWEAN

MINISTER\'S KNOWLEDGE, MPINGA HAS MET KABILA 2-3 TIMES

AND ONLY HIMSELF IN THE ZIMBABWEAN GOVERNMENT. MPINGA

PLANNED TO RETURN TO ZIMBABWE IN EARLY MAY TO RESUME HIS

QUEST TO SECURE SUPPORT FOR HIS PRESIDENTIAL AMBITIONS,

ACCORDING TO MNANGAGWA. THE ZIMBABWEAN MINISTER

EXPRESSED SUSPICION OF ANGLO AMERICAN\'S CLAIM, AS VOICED

BY MPINGA, THAT IT SUPPORTS A MIDDLE FORCE TO BRING

5. (C) COMMENT: IN OUR JUDGMENT MNANGAGWA KEEPS HIS

DOOR OPEN TO MPINGA BECAUSE HE DOES NOT VIEW HIM AS A

THREAT TO THE INTERESTS OF ZIMBABWE OR HIS OWN. BY

CONTINUING TO RECEIVE HIM, HE KEEPS MPINGA VISIBLE AND

MORE EASY TO MONITOR. IF MPINGA SOME DAY AMOUNTS TO

SOMETHING IN THE CONGO (NOT AN IMPOSSIBILITY GIVEN HIS

LINEAGE), MNANGAGWA WILL HAVE THE INSIDE TRACK TO HIM,

MUCH AS HE DOES TO KABILA TODAY. IF HE DOES NOT, NO

GREAT LOSS. IN SUM, MNANGAGWA IS DOING THE POLITICALLY

PRUDENT THING WITH MPINGA, BUT, TO THE EXTENT WE CAN

DETERMINE, NOT PLAYING BALL.
