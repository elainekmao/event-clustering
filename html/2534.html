SUBJECT: NIGERIA: NO CONTACT WITH THAI GOVERNMENT

REF: STATE 29641

1. Embassy Abuja contacted the Federal Ministry of Health and

the National Action Committee on HIV/AIDS (NACA) in regards

to reftel information. According to officials from both

agencies, the Thai Government has not contacted the GON.

With reftel information, NACA officials sent an email to the

Director of Research and Development at the Thai Government

Pharmaceutical Organization requesting information about the

proposed equipment transfer, but have not heard back from the

GOT.

2. Separately, the Government of Brazil reportedly has been

in communication with the GON regarding the provision of

generic anti-retrovirals. Six months ago, Brazil canceled a

visit to Nigeria by its Minister of Health at the last minute.
