SUBJECT: IRAQ: URGENT TURKEY-US-IRAQI OPPOSITION MEETING

REF: STATE 61835

Classified by Ambassador W. Robert Pearson. Reasons 1.5 b

and d.

1. (C) DCM conveyed to MFA Director General for Middle

Eastern Affairs Tahsin Burcuoglu on March 8 reftel urgent

request for Turkey to consider a meeting in the very near

future of the US, Turkey and the Iraqi opposition, including

the Iraqi Turkmen Front. Burcuolgu said he had been

expecting us to make this approach based on Amb. Logoglu\'s

recent meeting with Amb. Khalilzad and the March 7 phone call

between VPOTUS and PM Gul. Burcuoglu asked about specifics

regarding the timing, format, agenda and Iraqi participation

beyond the KDP, PUK and ITF that the US had in mind. DCM

replied that we did not have specific details on these points

apart from the content of reftel, that we would welcome

Turkey\'s views, and that we would seek more details from

Washington. Burcuoglu said that format, agenda and

composition of the group would be important to Turkey.

2. (C) Burcuoglu noted that Turkey was aware that the

Constitutional Monarchists, INA, INM and the Arab tribes were

not happy about the Salahadeen opposition meeting and had not

attended it. He wondered whether we intended to include

those groups. He said he would try to reach Undersecretary

Ziyal and Deputy Undersecretary Tuygan, and convey the US

points to them for their consideration.

3. (C) He also told DCM that Amb. Khalilzad mentioned to Amb.

Logoglu that Turkish officers had been given access to

detained ITF security officer Izzat. Burcuoglu said this was

not the case, according to all GOT information. He asked

that we ensure Washington was aware that Turkish officers had

not yet had access to Izzat.
