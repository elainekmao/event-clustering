SUBJECT: CIVAIR HUSHKITS: ITALY ON OUR SIDE.

PAGE 02 ROME 03010 191526Z

1. POST INFORMED VARIOUS CIVIL AVIATION OFFICIALS OF THE

USG\'S DECISION TO LIFT THE ARTICLE 84 CASE AGAINST ALL EU

MEMBER STATES WITH THE EXCEPTION OF BELGIUM (REFTEL). ALDO

SANSONE, HEAD OF INTERNATIONAL DIVISION OF THE ITALIAN

MINISTRY OF INFRASTRUCTURE AND TRANSPORT, ALFREDO ROMA,

PRESIDENT OF THE ITALIAN CIVIL AVIATION AUTHORITY (ENAC),

AND ALESSIO QUARANTA, CHIEF OF ENAC\'S INTERNATIONAL

RELATIONS OFFICE, ALL CONFIRMED THAT NO ACTION SIMILAR TO

THE BELGIUM DECREE IS BEING CONTEMPLATED IN ITALY. THEY

EXPRESSED SURPRISE AT THE BELGIUM DECREE AND REITERATED

ITALY\'S COMMITMENT TO THE RECENTLY PASSED EU DIRECTIVE ON

OPERATING RESTRICTIONS WHICH ENDORSES A BALANCED APPROACH

TO AIRPORT NOISE MANAGEMENT.
