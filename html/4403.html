SUBJECT: WTO ACCESSION FOR ARMENIA: DEMARCHE TO TURKEY

REF: STATE 236110

Classified by Economic Counselor Scot Marciel. Reasons:

1.5(b,d).

1. (C) EconCouns delivered reftel demarche to MFA

Department Head Oya Karakas Novmeber 20. Karakas said the

MFA was awaiting word from Azerbaijan on the results of

ongoing Azeri efforts to persuade the WTO Secretariat to

accept "minor" changes to the Armenian letter. Karakas said

she expected to hear the results of the Geneva discussions

later today. She refused to predict whether the GOT would

block adoption of Armenia\'s accession package November 21 in

the absence of an agreement on the text.

2. (C) EconCouns reiterated USG view that a compromise

solution that enabled the accession to move forward was in

everyone\'s interest, and that a Turkish decision to block

accession would carry political costs to Turkey. Karakas

acknowledged the point, and again expressed hope that a

solution would be found today.
