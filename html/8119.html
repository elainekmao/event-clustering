SUBJECT: UAEG BRIEFED ON NEW USG COMMERCIAL REMOTE

SENSING SPACE POLICY

REF: STATE 126680

On 17 March, Poloff briefed MFA Legal Affairs Deputy

Director Yacub Al-Hosani on the new USG Commercial

Remote Sensing Space Policy, and advised that the

Department in the future will conduct an invitational

briefing for representatives from certain Embassies,

including the UAE Embassy. Al-Hosani responded that

he would forward the background information and fact

sheet to the UAE Ambassador in preparation for that

meeting.
