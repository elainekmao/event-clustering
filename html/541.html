SUBJECT: HUMAN RIGHTS ABUSES IN BOSNIA-HERCEGOVINA - REPORTED RAPES OF YOUNG MUSLIM WOMEN

-

1. A TWO-PAGE ARTICLE IN THE SEPT 25 EDITION OF THE

ZAGREB WEEKLY "GLOBUS" REPORTS THAT A GROUP OF FORTY

YOUNG WOMEN FROM THE MUSLIM-POPULATED TOWN OF BREZOVO

POLJE, NORTH OF SARAJEVO, WERE BRUTALIZED AND

REPEATEDLY RAPED IN MAY OF THIS YEAR BY SERBIAN

SOLDIERS. THE WOMEN, NINE OF WHOM ARE SHOWN IN A

GROUP PHOTOGRAPH WITH THEIR MOTHERS AND SEVERAL OF

THE MOST HEAVILY QUOTED OF WHOM ARE SHOWN IN

INDIVIDUAL PHOTOS, ARE NOW IN A DISPLACED PERSONS

CAMP IN TUZLA. THERE A "GLOBUS" JOURNALIST

INTERVIEWED THEM, ALONG WITH DR. MELIKA KREITMAYER,

IDENTIFIED AS THE HEAD OF A GROUP OF GYNECOLOGISTS

SENT BY THE UN TO B-H TO INVESTIGATE REPORTED CASES

OF "BODILY ATTACKS" ON THE PART OF SERBS.

2. THE ARTICLE DESCRIBES THE VIOLENCE DONE TO

MIRSADA DIDOVIC (23 YEARS OF AGE), ALMIRA AJANOVIC

(18), JASMINA FERIC (20), SEVLETA AJANOVIC (18), TWO

UNCLASSIFIED

MINORS IDENTIFIED AS MEJRA (17) AND ZIBA (17), AND

NUMEROUS OTHERS WHO WOULD NOT ALLOW THEIR NAMES TO BE

REVEALED. THE WOMEN INTERVIEWED GAVE SIMILAR

ACCOUNTS OF HOW SERBIAN SOLDIERS HAD ENTERED THEIR

TOWN IN MAY, FORCED THEIR WAY INTO MUSLIM HOMES,

TAKEN THE MOST ATTRACTIVE YOUNG WOMEN FROM THEIR

FAMILIES, AND JAILED THEM. GROUPS OF SERBIAN

SOLDIERS REPORTEDLY THEN RAPED THEM OVER A PERIOD OF

SEVERAL DAYS. ONE VICTIM, ALMIRA AJANOVIC, WAS

REPORTEDLY GANG RAPED IN FRONT OF HER FATHER, WHO WAS

HIMSELF GAGGED AND BOUND BY WIRE. ANOTHER, JASMINA

FERIC, REPORTEDLY WITNESSED THE SLITTING OF HER

FATHER\'S THROAT AND THE SHOOTING OF HER MOTHER BEFORE

SHE WAS GANG RAPED BY NINE SOLDIERS FOR THREE DAYS.

-

3. THROUGH QUOTES FROM DR. KREITMAYER AND SEVERAL OF

THE VICTIMS, WHO IN TURN QUOTED THEIR ATTACKERS, THE

ARTICLE ARGUES THAT GROSS VIOLENCE AGAINST AND

HUMILIATION OF MUSLIM WOMEN WAS NOT A HAPPENSTANCE OF

WAR OR MERELY THE ISOLATED ACTS OF CRAZED SOLDIERS.

RATHER, IT WAS SUPPOSEDLY PART OF A DELIBERATE SERBIAN

STRATEGY TO SOW TERROR IN THE MUSLIM POPULATION.

4. DR. KREITMAYER IS REPORTED TO HAVE PHYSICALLY

EXAMINED THE WOMEN, SOME OF WHOM HAD BECOME PREGNANT

AND ABORTED.
