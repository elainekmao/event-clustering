1. (SBU) THIS IS AN ACTION MESSAGE. CARDINAL SODANO,

VATICAN PM EQUIVALENT, ON 01/08/02 PASSED CHARGE A LETTER

VERBALE ADDRESSED TO THE AMBASSADOR (FAXED TO EUR/WE) IN

WHICH HE REQUESTED THE INTERVENTION OF THIS EMBASSY TO

EXPEDITE THE UNBLOCKING BY OFAC OF VATICAN OFFICIAL FUNDS.

THESE FUNDS WERE DESTINED TO SUPPORT THE HOLY SEE\'S EMBASSY

(NUNCIATURE) IN HAVANA. SODANO ALSO ASKED POST TO PASS TO

THE DEPARTMENT OF THE TREASURY A NOTE VERBALE REQUESTING THE

UNBLOCKING OF THE FUNDS. ACTION REQUESTED: PLEASE PASS THIS

INFORMATION TO OFAC ASAP; PLEASE PROVIDE GUIDANCE FOR

RESPONDING BEFORE THURSDAY, JANUARY 10, WHEN AMBASSADOR WILL

SEE SODANO AT AN OFFICIAL FUNCTION.

2. (SBU) THE INFORMATION POST HAS ON THIS ISSUE ALL COMES

FROM A PACKET OF CORRESPONDENCE BETWEEN THE ARCHDIOCESE OF

NEW YORK AND OFAC (ALL FAXED TO EUR/WE). THE BASIC FACTS

ALLEGED ARE THAT THE ARCHDIOCESE ATTEMPTED ON JULY 5, 2001

TO TRANSFER A PORTION OF THE PROCEEDS OF A VATICAN

INVESTMENT TO THE ACCOUNT OF THE HAVANA NUNCIATURE AT THE

ISTITUTO PER LE OPERE RELIGIOSE (IOR -- THE VATICAN\'S

OFFICIAL BANK). AT THAT POINT OFAC BLOCKED THE TRANSFER.

THE NUNCIATURE APPLIED FOR A LICENSE BY JULY 7, 2001. BY

SEPTEMBER 25, 2001 OFAC AND THE ARCHDIOCESE HAD EXCHANGED

CORRESPONDENCE DETAILING PARTICULARS OF THE PROPOSED IOR

TRANSACTION. BY JANUARY 5,2002, WITH NO APPARENT ACTION ON

THE PART OF OFAC, CARDINAL SODANO SENT THE NOTE AND LETTER

REFERRED TO ABOVE.

3. (SBU) COMMENT: THIS TRANSACTION HAS RISEN TO THE LEVEL

OF A FOREIGN POLICY ISSUE WITH THE LETTER FROM THE PRIME

MINISTER TO THE AMBASSADOR. WE NOTE THAT ON SEVERAL LEVELS

THIS ISSUE IS A NEURALGIC ONE FOR THE HOLY SEE -- FIRST

BECAUSE THE MONEY IS PART OF THE VATICAN\'S OFFICIAL, INDEED

DIPLOMATIC ACCOUNTS. SECOND, BECAUSE IT HAS BEEN BLOCKED AS

PART OF A SANCTIONS REGIME THAT THE HOLY SEE HAS RESOLUTELY

OPPOSED SINCE ITS INCEPTION. THIRD, BECAUSE THE IOR HAS IN

THE LAST YEAR BEEN SUBJECT TO A NUMBER OF LEGAL PROCEEDINGS

IN U.S. COURTS IN A MANNER WHICH SODANO PERSONALLY HAS

ASSERTED VIOLATE THE HOLY SEE\'S INTERNATIONAL SOVEREIGN

RIGHTS. SODANO WILL ENCOUNTER THE AMBASSADOR AT THE ANNUAL

DIPLOMATIC CORPS DINNER THIS COMING THURSDAY; THIS ISSUE IS

EXACTLY THE SORT OF ISSUE HE HAS SEIZED UPON IN THE PAST TO

PILLORY THE USG FOR ITS ALLEGED LACK OF RESPECT FOR VATICAN

SOVEREIGNTY. END COMMENT.
