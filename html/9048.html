SUBJECT: JORDAN SUPPORTS INDIAN PARTICIPATION IN IRAQI

STABILIZATION FORCE

REF: STATE 181085

Classified By: PolCouns Doug Silliman for reasons 1.5 (B)(D)

1. (C) Ambassador and PolCouns June 26 delivered to Foreign

Minister Marwan Muasher ref points asking that Jordan

encourage Indian participation in an Iraqi stabilization

force. Muasher noted that Jordan and India had already

agreed to adopt jointly a small number of hospitals in Iraq.

He said Jordan recognizes the need for an international

stabilization force in Iraq and would support Indian

participation in that force. He noted, however, that Jordan

would not contribute forces to an Iraqi stabilization force,

preferring to contribute to the rebuilding of Iraq in other

ways.
