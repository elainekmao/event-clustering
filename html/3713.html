SUBJECT: SEPTEMBER 11 MEETING OF EMBASSY AMMAN\'S HOMELAND

SECURITY WORKING GROUP

Classified By: CDA Gregory L. Berry for reasons 1.5 (B), (C) and (D)

1. (S/NF) The Charge on September 11 chaired a meeting of

Embassy Amman\'s Homeland Security Working Group (HSWG).

A/DCM, and DAO, LEGATT, ORCA, RSO, POL, ECON, CONS, and ADMIN

agency/section chiefs participated in the meeting.

2. (S) We intend to use the meetings of the

newly-constituted HSWG to complement the coordinative and

operational functions of the Emergency Action Committee, the

Terrorism Financing Coordination Group, the Visas Viper

Committee, and other Embassy groups concerned wholly or in

part with homeland security.
