SUBJECT: CYPRUS MIL EXERCISES: TURKS HAVE NO EXERCISE PLANS

REF: A. ANKARA 2145

B. NICOSIA 603

C. STATE 81500

(U) Classified by Pol-Mil Counselor Stuart Brown. Reasons

1.5 (b) and (d).

1. (C) On April 7, in response to Post\'s follow up on Ref A,

acting MFA Cyprus Department Head Barkan Oz contacted

polmiloff with an update on Turkey\'s intentions regarding the

spring Toros I exercise in Cyprus. Oz said that he had

confirmed with the Turkish General Staff that Turkey has "no

planned exercise" in Cyprus this spring. He added that the

only planned Turkish exercise on the island would be in the

fall, but that it was still too early in the year for

decisions on it now. He reiterated that Turkey would mirror

whatever the Greek/Greek Cypriot side did. He said that if

Alexandros/Olympos were to take place, the GOT would

"evaluate it and take a corresponding position."

2. (C) Comment: The Turks do not seem aware that Alexandros

took place on February 3-7 as a command post exercise with no

troops deployed (Ref B), nor did we seek to inform them.

Inasmuch as the Greek/Greek Cypriot side considers its spring

exercises concluded, we can expect the Turks to stick to not

having plans for Toros I. End Comment.

PEARSON
