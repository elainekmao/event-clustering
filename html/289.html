SUBJECT: "TURKISH ISLAMIC JIHAD ARMY"

1. CONFIDENTIAL - ENTIRE TEXT.

2. EMBASSY RECEIVED THE FOLLOWING DECLARATION IN TURKISH

VIA THE TURKISH POSTAL SYSTEM TODAY, JULY 18. THIS IS

THE FIRST WE HAVE HEARD OF THE "TURKISH ISLAMIC JIHAD

ARMY." THE DECLARATION WAS PRINTED ON HIGH QUALITY

PAPER, IN SEVERAL COLORS; AN OBVIOUSLY EXPENSIVE JOB.

THE TURKISH USED STRUCK OUR FSN\'S AND TURKISH SPEAKING

EMBOFFS AS CONTRIVED AND ARCHAIC, PROBABLY INDICATING IT

WAS DRAFTED BY A NON-NATIVE SPEAKER.

3. SO FAR AS WE ARE AWARE, SIMILAR COPIES HAVE NOT BEEN

MAILED TO OTHER WESTERN EMBASSIES IN ANKARA. WE HAVE

PASSED THE ORIGINAL TO TURKISH INTELLIGENCE AUTHORITIES

AND GIVEN COPIES TO THE POLICE AND TO THE MFA\'S COUNTER

TERRORISM OFFICE. ALL POSTS IN TURKEY HAVE BEEN

MAINTAINING AN ALERT SECURITY POSTURE BECAUSE OF EVENTS

IN IRAN OVER THE PAST FEW MONTHS. NEVERTHELESS, WE

BELIEVE NOW IS A GOOD TIME TO REVIEW ALL SECURITY

PROCEDURES AND TO REMIND ALL STAFF OF THE NEED TO

EXERCISE VIGILANCE.

4. INFORMAL EMBASSY TRANSLATION OF THE DECLARATION

FOLLOWS:

FOR THE VICTORY OF HIGH ISLAM, WE HAD SWORN TO GIVE THE

HYPOCRITES AND INFIDELS TO ISLAM WHO ATTACK OUR HOLY

RELIGION THE LESSON THEY DESERVE

WE, THE PRIVATE SOLDIERS IN THE TURKISH ISLAMIC JIHAD

ARMY, WANT TO STATE THAT WE ARE READY TO GIVE THE

NECESSARY PENALTY TO THE SERVANTS OF THE INFIDELS OF

ISLAM IN OUR COUNTRY WHO HAVE DEMONSTRATED THEIR

ARROGANCE BY CALLING THE HOLY DAY, FRIDAY, "BLACK

FRIDAY," AND CALLING THE DRESSES OF THE BELIEVERS "BLACK

CHADOR," AND TERMING MUSLIMS "BLACK HEADS."

WE ARE ALWAYS READY TO EXECUTE THE ORDER AND KILL ALL

EUROPEAN INFIDELS TO ISLAM, INCLUDING SALMAN RUSHDIE,

WHO DARED TO ATTACK THE HOLY ISLAM RELIGION.

WE WANT TO RECALL FOR THOSE WHO HAVE SOLD THEIR

CONSCIENCE TO THE WEST\'S SLAVE MARKET; THE ONES WHO ARE

THE ENEMIES OF RELIGION, THE NATION, HONOUR AND HONESTY;

THE ONES WHO ARE SERVANTS AND FANATICS THE FOLLOWING FACT:

ACCEPTING THAT GOD IS UNIQUE AND, BEING GOD, KNOWING

THAT MOHAMMED IS HIS MAN AND HIS PROPHET IS A MATTER OF

BELIEF. BUT, BELIEVING THIS AND ACCEPTING ITS

DISCIPLINE IS CALLED "SHARIAT," AND STRUGGLING FOR THIS

RULE IS THE BEST WAY OF WORSHIPPING. DYING FOR ALLAH IS

A REQUIREMENT FOR OUR RELIGIOUS PRESENCE.

THE COMING OF ANY PRO-SALMAN RUSHDIE PEOPLE TO TURKEY -

WHICH WAS THE HOME OF THE CALIPHATE FOR CENTURIES AND

WHICH HAD BEATEN THE CRUSADERS - WILL NOT EVER BE

ALLOWED. INFIDELS WHO PRAISE SALMAN RUSHDIE AND ENTER

THE MOTHERLAND, WHICH HAS BEEN WASHED WITH THE BLOOD OF

MANY MUSLIMS, WILL BE KILLED.

TURKISH ISLAMIC JIHAD ARMY

ABRAMOWITZ
