SUBJECT: MFA REQUESTS SMALLPOX/ANTHRAX VACCINES

REF: A. 02 STATE 256691

B. 02 STATE 256701

(U) Classified by DCM Robert Deutsch for reasons 1.5 (B) and

(D)

(C) Polmiloff delivered refs A and B to MFA Iraq desk officer

Ferhat Alkan on December 13 and subsequently learned that the

information had been passed to MFA\'s International Political

Organizations Department. Meeting with Econoff on February

4, Feza Ozturk, MFA Department Head for International

Political Organizations, provided the GOT\'s response to ref B

offer of access to U.S. smallpox and anthrax vaccines.

Ozturk stated that the GOT would like to receive vaccines for

30,000 persons for smallpox and 70,000 persons for anthrax.

The GOT would dedicate vaccine for 20,000 military personnel

for both smallpox and anthrax from these amounts, and would

also be interested in receiving a civilian/military expert

team to discuss the vaccines. Ozturk also requested

confirmation that there would be no charge to Turkey for the

vaccines.
