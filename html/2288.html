SUBJECT: BIOGRAPHICAL INFORMATION FOR CLAUDIO GUGEROTTI -

NEW VATICAN AMBASSADOR TO ARMENIA AND GEORGIA

1. THE POPE HAS APPOINTED ITALIAN-BORN VATICAN OFFICIAL,

MONSIGNOR CLAUDIO GUGEROTTI, AS NUNCIO (AMBASSADOR) IN

ARMENIA AND GEORGIA. GUGEROTTI IS CURRENTLY UNDER-SECRETARY

UNCLASSIFIED

(NUMBER THREE) AT THE PONTIFICAL COUNCIL FOR THE EASTERN

CHURCHES. HE WAS BORN IN VERONA, ITALY IN 1955 AND ORDAINED

A PRIEST IN 1982. MONSIGNOR GUGEROTTI HOLDS A DOCTORAL

DEGREE IN ORIENTAL LANGUAGES AND LITERATURE AND A MASTERS

DEGREE IN LITURGY. HE HAS TAUGHT PATRISTICS, THEOLOGY AND

EASTERN RITE LITURGY AT UNIVERSITY LEVEL. GUGEROTTI ENTERED

THE VATICAN\'S CIVIL SERVICE IN 1985, WORKING EXCLUSIVELY

SINCE THEN FOR THE CONGREGATION FOR THE EASTERN CHURCH. HE

BECAME UNDER-SECRETARY IN 1997. AS WELL AS HIS NATIVE

ITALIAN, GUGEROTTI SPEAKS FRENCH, ENGLISH, LATIN, GREEK,

CLASSICAL AND MODERN ARMENIAN, AND KURDISH. MONSIGNOR

GUGEROTTI WILL BE ORDAINED AN ARCHBISHOP PRIOR TO TAKING UP

HIS APPOINTMENT.

2. COMMENT: GUGEROTTI IS A RISING STAR IN THE VATICAN

CIVIL SERVICE. HE IS A PROTQGQ OF THE FORMER PREFECT OF THE

CONGREGATION FOR THE EASTERN CHURCHES, CARDINAL ACHILLE

SILVESTRINI. GUGEROTTI IS URBANE AND EXTREMELY INTELLIGENT,

SPEAKING HIS ENGLISH WITH A MARKED BRITISH ACCENT. HE HAS

TRAVELED OFTEN TO EASTERN EUROPE AND THE NEAR EAST IN THE

SERVICE OF THE VATICAN AND IS A WELL-KNOWN FIGURE IN

ARMENIAN AND GEORGIAN CHURCH CIRCLES, BOTH CATHOLIC AND

ORTHODOX. IT IS UNUSUAL FOR A NON-CAREER VATICAN DIPLOMAT TO

BE APPOINTED AS NUNCIO IN SUCH A STRATEGIC GEOGRAPHICAL

AREA. GUGEROTTI AT 46 IS ALSO VERY YOUNG, ANOTHER TESTIMONY

TO THE ESTEEM IN WHICH BOTH THE POPE AND SENIOR VATICAN

OFFICIALS HOLD HIM. WITH THE CONSTANTLY EXPRESSED VATICAN

HOPE FOR UNITY BETWEEN ROME AND THE ORTHODOX CHURCHES,

GUGEROTTI\'S APPOINTMENT AS AMBASSADOR TO ARMENIA IN

PARTICULAR AUGURS WELL FOR BRIGHTER, IF NOT POSITIVELY

EFFERVESCENT ECUMENICAL RELATIONS WITH THE CONSEQUENT
