START MEMO NOMINATING-JUSTIFICATION:

Mr. Ramathasan, an engineer by profession, is an

entrepreneur who has won the Entrepreneur of the Year

Award in the small industry category for 1997 awarded

by the Federation of Chambers of Commerce and Industry

of Sri Lanka. Mr. Ramathasan is one of the most

prominent construction contractors in Jaffna

specialized in construction of infrastructure

facilities, buildings, roads and bridges, and water

sewerage systems in the North and the East. It is

admirable his commitment to remain and develop an area

torn apart by the ethnic war in the country.

Mr. Ramathasan is the Vice President of the Jaffna

Chamber of Commerce and Industry that is making a

significant contribution in promoting economic growth

through creating links with the other provinces to

provide new opportunities for the people in the north

to integrate into mainstream economic activity. The

Jaffna Chamber is one of the new regional chambers in

the country and has a pivotal role to play in economic

development in the north. Mr. Ramathasan was

instrumental in forming the chamber and is the live

wire in providing leadership to the chamber. He was

key to the successful fact finding mission on USAID

concluded in June this year to plan out our economic

growth assistance to the north and is going to be an

important player in implementing USIAD activities soon.

Mr. Ramathasan has no previous exposure in the U.S.

Thus, the candidate\'s participation in the IV program

on Entrepreneurship in the U.S. represents an excellent

opportunity to broaden his perspective to provide

leadership to the business community to re-enter

mainstream economic activity, expand chamber activities

in the Northern Province.
