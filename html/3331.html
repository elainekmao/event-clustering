SUBJECT: JORDAN MEDIA REACTION ON IRAQ, THE

QUADRIPARTITE COMMITTEE

SUMMARY

JORDANIAN DAILIES ON JULY 18 REPORTED THAT KING

ABDULLAH WOULD MEET WITH PRESIDENTS CHIRAC AND BUSH IN

TWO WEEKS, THE PROGRESS OF THE QUADRIPARTITE MEETING IN

WASHINGTON, AND PRIME MINISTER TONY BLAIR\'S COMMENT

THAT MILITARY ACTION AGAINST IRAQ WOULD NOT REQUIRE A

NEW UN RESOLUTION.

EDITORIAL WRITERS COMMENTED ON IRAQ AND THE

QUADRIPARTITE MEETING

NEWS REPORTS

JORDANIAN DAILIES ON JULY 18 ALL REPORTED THAT KING

ABDULLAH WOULD MEET WITH FRENCH PRESIDENT JACQUES

CHIRAC AND PRESIDENT BUSH IN THE NEXT FORTNIGHT.

ON THE QUADRIPARTITE COMMITTEE MEETING, DAILIES

REPORTED PRESIDENT BUSH\'S COMMENT THAT PROGRESS HAS

BEEN MADE, THAT THE COMMITTEE SUPPORTS THE

ESTABLISHMENT OF A PALESTINIAN STATE, THAT IT IGNORED

THE CALL TO MARGINALIZE ARAFAT, AND THAT A TEAM OF

EXPERTS UNDER AMERICAN LEADERSHIP WILL HELP REFORM THE

PALESTINIAN SECURITY SYSTEM. FOREIGN MINISTERS MUASHER

AND MAHER OF JORDAN AND EGYPT WERE QUOTED AS SAYING

THAT PRESIDENT BUSH\'S POSITION REGARDING ARAFAT WOULD

NOT HANDICAP PEACE.

CENTER-LEFT, INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR GAVE

FRONT-PAGE COVERAGE TO BRITISH PRIME MINISTER\'S REMARKS

THAT MILITARY ACTION AGAINST IRAQ DOES NOT REQUIRE A

NEW UN RESOLUTION.

EDITORIAL COMMENTARIES

-- FROM DEFENSE TO ATTACK

PROMINENT JORDANIAN COLUMNIST FAHED AL-FANEK WROTE IN

SEMI-OFFICIAL INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-RAI ON JULY

18: "THE COMPATIBILITY BETWEEN THE REPORTS ON JORDAN\'S

ALLEGED COLLUSION IN THE AMERICAN AGGRESSION AGAINST

IRAQ DOES NOT INDICATE THEIR TRUTH, RATHER THAT THEY

CAME FROM THE SAME SOURCE. THIS MISINFORMATION

CAMPAIGN PUT JORDAN ON THE DEFENSIVE. BUT NOW IS THE

TIME TO MOVE FROM DEFENSE TO OFFENSE. THE FOREIGN

AGGRESSION BEING PLANNED AGAINST IRAQ IS NOT PLANNED

AGAINST SADDAM HUSSEIN ONLY, AS THE SCHOOL OF THOUGHT

THAT DERIVES ITS WISDOM FROM THE CIA CLAIMS. IT IS AN

AGGRESSION AGAINST THE STATE AND PEOPLE OF IRAQ, AND

AFTER IRAQ WOULD COME THE TURN OF SYRIA, LEBANON,

SUDAN, LIBYA, YEMEN, AND SO ON. NEUTRALITY IN THE FACE

OF THIS AGGRESSION DOES NOT FULFILL THE AMBITIONS OF

JORDAN. WE SHOULD REJECT THE AGGRESSION IN EVERY

LEGITIMATE WAY POSSIBLE. OTHERWISE, WHAT IS THE

MEETING OF ARAB NATIONAL SECURITY AND THE ARAB TREATIES

OF MUTUAL DEFENSE?"

-- THE OTHER FACE OF THE QUADRIPARTITE MEETING

DAILY COLUMNIST RAKAN AL-MAJALI WROTE IN CENTER-LEFT,

INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR ON JULY 18: "IT IS

VERY CLEAR THAT THERE ARE OBJECTIVES TO THE

INTERNATIONAL QUADRIPARTITE MEETING CONTRARY TO THOSE

THAT ARE DECLARED. THE MEETINGS CONCENTRATED ON THE

AMERICAN CAPRICIOUSNESS AND INTRANSIGENCE REGARDING

ARAFAT, IGNORING THE SUPPOSEDLY MAIN TOPIC OF

DISCUSSION, WHICH IS TO RESOLVE THE CONFLICT AND END

THE TRAGEDY IN THE OCCUPIED PALESTINIAN TERRITORIES. IT

HAS BECOME CLEAR THAT ALL PAST INITIATIVES AND PROMISES

WERE ILLUSORY, AND PRETEXTS WERE FOUND TO POSTPONE

INDEFINITELY. SIMILARLY, THE TALK ABOUT REFORMING THE

PNA AND CHANGING ARAFAT IS PART OF THE OLD SCENARIO

WHICH HAS ONE OBJECTIVE: TO CREATE ILLUSIONS THROUGH

FALSE PROMISES AND COVER UP FOR THE LACK OF ANY

INTENTION TO PROVIDE A REAL SOLUTION."

-- THE MEETINGS OF NEW YORK AND WASHINGTON

PROMINENT DAILY COLUMNIST URAIB AL-RANTAWI WROTE IN

CENTER-LEFT, INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR ON

JULY 18: "WASHINGTON FAILED TO PERSUADE THE

QUADRIPARTITE COMMITTEE TO EXILE ARAFAT, BUT IT

SUCCEEDED IN A MORE IMPORTANT PURSUIT, IMPOSING THE

AMERICAN VISION OF ADDRESSING DIFFERENT TRACKS IN

SUCCESSION, NOT IN PARALLEL. WHEN SECURITY BECOMES THE

PREREQUISITE FOR STARTING TO FULFILL ISRAEL\'S

OBLIGATIONS, IT MEANS GIVING THE FUTURE OF THE PEACE

PROCESS IN THE HAND OF A PARTY THAT REJECTS PEACE ON

IDEOLOGICAL GROUNDS. EITHER WAY, THE MEETINGS SHED SOME

LIGHT ON WHAT IS POSSIBLE INTERNATIONALLY AT THE

PRESENT STAGE, AND THIS CONSOLIDATES OUR PESSIMISTIC

ASSESSMENT THAT THE VACUUM IN THE REGION WILL CONTINUE

UNTIL NEXT SPRING AT LEAST." GNEHM
