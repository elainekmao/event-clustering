SUBJECT: UAE NO LONGER REPRESENTS LIBYAN INTERESTS

REF: STATE 130344

In response to ref A request, the UAE Government

confirmed per UAE Diplomatic Note No. 1/3/7-814 dated

16 June that the UAE does not represent Libyan

interests in the U.S. The diplomatic note also stated

that the UAE Embassy advised the USG by UAE Diplomatic

Note No. 1/3/7/26 dated 1 March 2002 that it ceased

representing Libyan interests in the U.S. on 30

January 2002.
