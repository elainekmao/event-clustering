SUBJECT: ZIMBABWE-US RELATIONS TAKE A BEATING IN

GOVERNMENT MEDIA

1. PERHAPS A BIT SLOW OUT OF THE GATE, GOVERNMENT OF

ZIMBABWE MEDIA AND INFORMATION RESOURCES ARE NOW FULLY

ENGAGED IN CRITICISM OF THE ZIMBABWE DEMOCRACY AND

ECONOMIC RECOVERY ACT. SOME OF THAT CRITICISM IS BASED

ON DELIBERATE DISTORTION OF THE BILL. GOVERNMENT-OWNED

ELECTRONIC MEDIA HAVE ALSO MANIPULATED REPORTING ON THE

VISIT OF ASSISTANT SECRETARY KANSTEINER IN ORDER TO

PORTRAY THE BILL AND THE U.S. AS BULLYING ZIMBABWE. TO

ITS CREDIT, HOWEVER, THE GOVERNMENT-OWNED DAILY "THE

HERALD" DID PUBLISH THE ENTIRE BILL AND ACCOMPANYING

HIRC REPORT.

2. AMONG THE MEANS USED BY GOVERNMENT-OWNED MEDIA TO

DISTORT ZDERA ARE:

- CONSISTENTLY CALLING IT THE "ZIMBABWE SANCTIONS

BILL" RATHER THAN BY ITS CORRECT NAME.

- COMPLETE LACK OF COVERAGE OF THE INCENTIVES ZDERA

OFFERS.

- AD HOMINEM ATTACKS AGAINST THE BILL\'S SPONSORS.

- CHARACTERIZATION OF THE BILL AS "RACIST"

LEGISLATION, FAILING TO ACKNOWLEDGE THE CRITICAL ROLE

THE CONGRESSIONAL BLACK CAUCUS PLAYED IN ITS PASSAGE.

- DELIBERATE MANIPULATION OF STATEMENTS BY ASSISTANT

SECRETARY KANSTEINER TO GIVE THE IMPRESSION THAT THE

UNITED STATES DEMANDED THAT THE SADC PARLIAMENTARY

FORUM OBSERVE ZIMBABWE\'S PRESIDENTIAL ELECTIONS.

- CITING CAPTIVE AND UNNAMED SOURCES FOR STATEMENTS

THAT ZDERA WILL "STARVE" OR "STRANGLE" THE PEOPLE OF

ZIMBABWE.

- HIGHLIGHTING AND REPEATING COMMENTS BY MEMBERS OF

THE SADC MINISTERIAL TEAM CURRENTLY IN HARARE THAT SADC

- UNCONFIRMED AND ALMOST CERTAINLY EXAGGERATED

REPORTS OF ANTI-ZDERA DEMONSTRATIONS BY CITIZENS IN

3. IN ADDITION TO THE SORT OF "NEWS REPORTING"

OUTLINED ABOVE, GOVERNMENT-OWNED PRINT MEDIA ARE ALSO

INDULGING THEIR POLITICAL BOSSES WITH LENGTHY OPINION

PIECES DENOUNCING ZDERA AND THE U.S. WE OFFER A FEW

HIGHLIGHTS.

COME TO THE CONCLUSION THAT THE REAL OBJECTIVES OF

THE BILL ARE REVEALED NOT BY ITS STATED INTENTIONS

BUT BY ITS CONTRADICTIONS. IN ITS OPENING

PARAGRAPH, THE BILL SAYS ITS INTENTIONS ARE \'TO

PROVIDE FOR A TRANSITION TO DEMOCRACY AND TO

PROMOTE ECONOMIC RECOVERY IN ZIMBABWE.\' REALLY!

ARE WE NOW TO BELIEVE THAT AFTER HOLDING REGULAR

ELECTIONS IN THE YEARS 1980, 1985, 1990, 1995,

1996, AND 2000, ZIMBABWE HAS SOMEHOW STOPPED BEING

A DEMOCRACY? AND IF THE AMERICAN GOVERNMENT IS

KEEN TO PROMOTE THE \'TRANSITION TO DEMOCRACY,\' WHY

HAS IT NOT DRAFTED AN APPROPRIATE BILL FOR SOMALIA,

THE DEMOCRATIC REPUBLIC OF CONGO AND THE SUDAN.?

IT IS A MEASURE OF THE ARROGANCE OF THE PEOPLE THAT

CRAFTED THIS BILL THAT THEY THINK ZIMBABWE\'S

6. WE ARE CONFIDENT THAT MORE OF THIS SORT OF

REPORTING AND COMMENTARY LIES AHEAD. WE EXPECT, FOR

EXAMPLE, THAT ZDERA WILL BE THE FOCUS OF CONSIDERABLE

ATTENTION AND CRITICISM AT THE ZANU-PF NATIONAL

CONFERENCE SCHEDULED FOR DECEMBER 13-15 IN VICTORIA

FALLS.

7. THROUGH PRESS CONFERENCES, INTERVIEWS,

DISTRIBUTION OF INFORMATION AND STEADY CONTACT WORK

WITH EDITORS AND REPORTERS, WE ARE DOING EVERYTHING WE

CAN TO ENSURE THAT THE PEOPLE OF ZIMBABWE HAVE ACCESS

TO ACCURATE INFORMATION ABOUT U.S. POLICY. OUR

UNWILLINGNESS TO LIE AND OUR LACK OF ACCESS TO THE

ELECTRONIC MEDIA IN ZIMBABWE JUST MAKE THE CHALLENGE OF

GETTING OUT OUR STORY ALL THAT MUCH MORE INTERESTING.
