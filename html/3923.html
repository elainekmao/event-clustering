1. (U) GUIDANCE REQUEST. SEE PARA 3.

2. (C) POL MINISTER COUNSELOR USED THE OPPORTUNITY OF HIS

COURTESY CALL ON THE NEW DIRECTOR GENERAL (A/S EQUIVALENT)

FOR EUROPEAN AFFAIRS PAOLO PUCCI TO UNDERLINE U.S. CONCERNS

SECRET

PAGE 02 ROME 04912 081516Z

OVER REPORTS (REFTEL) THAT UKRAINE PRESIDENT KUCHMA HAS

APPROVED THE SALE OF KOLCHUGA PASSIVE DETECTION SYSTEMS TO

IRAQ. (PUCCI\'S COMMENTS ON TURKEY/EU ACCESSION REPORTED

SEPTEL). PUCCI SAID THAT KUCHMA\'S SENIOR ADVISOR OREL HAD

BEEN IN ROME THE PREVIOUS WEEK AND HAD INFORMED THE GOI THAT

WHILE THE GOU HAD BEEN TALKING TO IRAQ, THE SUBJECT OF THE

KOLCHUGA RADAR SYSTEMS HAD NOT COME UP. HOWEVER, RECOGNIZING

CONCERNS IN THE U.S. AND OTHER WESTERN COUNTRIES, OREL SAID

THAT UKRAINE WAS READY TO BREAK OFF ALL TALKS WITH THE IRAQIS.

3 (S) PUCCI SAID THAT PRESIDENT KUCHMA HAS PLANNED, AND THE

GOI HAS APPROVED, A STATE VISIT TO ITALY AT THE END OF

NOVEMBER. THE AGENDA CALLS FOR KUCHMA TO MEET WITH PRESIDENT

CIAMPI, AND POSSIBLY WITH PM BERLUSCONI. PUCCI SAID THAT THE

GOI WANTED THE VISIT TO BE A SUCCESS, BUT HAS WARNED THE GOU

THAT THE KOLCHUGA ISSUE THREATENED TO TARNISH THE VISIT. IF

WASHINGTON WOULD LIKE US TO ASK THE GOI TO CANCEL THIS VISIT,

OR TO ASK IT TO CONDITION IT ON GOU BEHAVIOR IN THE WEEKS TO

COME, WE SHOULD GO IN AS EARLY AS POSSIBLE.
