INTRODUCTION

------------

1. (U) THIS IS ONE IN A SERIES OF INTERVIEWS WITH WAR

CRIMES VICTIMS CONDUCTED IN LATE 1993 IN MAOCA, BOSNIA-

HERZEGOVINA BY AN OFDA (DART/ZAGREB) FIELD REPRESENTATIVE

AND AN AMCIT INTERNATIONAL RESCUE COMMITTEE (IRC)

REPRESENTATIVE WHILE THEY WERE IN THE AREA ASSESSING

HUMANITARIAN ASSISTANCE NEEDS. THIS REPORT, LIKE THE

OTHERS, NAMES VICTIMS AND, IN SOME CASES, PERPETRATORS

AND THE DETAILS OF SPECIFIC CRIMES.

WITNESS INFORMATION

-------------------

2. (LOU) THE WITNESS, MRS. HADZIRA HAMZIC (STRICTLY

PROTECT) WAS BORN ON APRIL 25, 1937. HER SONS MEHO AND

MEVLA) AND SEVEN GRANDCHILDREN (MIRNES, MIRNESA, MEDINA,

SABINA, MIRSAD, ZAHID AND MIRELA) ARE MENTIONED BELOW;

THEIR NAMES SHOULD ALSO BE STRICTLY PROTECTED. MRS.

HER INTERVIEW, MRS. HAMZIC LIVED AT IVE ANDRICA B.B., C/O

HAMDIJA BAJRAMOVIC, IN TUZLA.

THE WITNESS\'S ACCOUNT

---------------------

3. (U) THE WITNESS SAID THAT ON MAY 20, 1992, SEVERAL

"CHETNIKS" (SERBIAN SOLDIERS) SURROUNDED THE WITNESS\'S

HOUSE AND THE NEARBY HOUSES OF TWO OF HER SONS. SHE, HER

TWO SONS, HER THREE DAUGHTERS-IN-LAW AND HER SEVEN

GRANDCHILDREN WERE GATHERED TOGETHER IN ONE ROOM. ALL OF

THEIR GOLD WAS TAKEN FROM THEIR BODIES AND THEIR HOUSES

WERE SEARCHED. AFTER THAT, THEY WERE ALL TAKEN ACROSS THE

RIVER ON FOOT TO THE VILLAGE OF SALIHOVICI. THERE THEY

WERE TAKEN TO A LARGE MULTI-STOREY HOUSE OWNED BY "DZUFA"

(NFI). THEY WERE SEARCHED THERE AND ADDITIONAL GOLD

OBJECTS, WHICH THEY HAD SECRETED ABOUT THEIR PERSONS, WERE

TAKEN.

4. (U) THE WITNESS ESTIMATED THAT SEVERAL HUNDRED MEN,

WOMEN, AND CHILDREN WERE BEING KEPT IN THE HOUSE. THE MEN

WERE KEPT SEPARATELY, IN A VERY CROWDED PANTRY; THEY WERE

GIVEN NO FOOD FOR THE FIRST THREE DAYS. OCCASIONALLY, THE

WITNESS SAID, A PIECE OF BREAD WOULD BE THROWN IN THE ROOM

WHERE THE WOMEN WERE AND THEY WERE BEATEN IF THEY TRIED TO

PICK IT UP.

5. (U) THE WITNESS SAID "CHETNIKS" WOULD COME TO THE

HOUSE AT NIGHT AND TAKE WOMEN INDIVIDUALLY TO ANOTHER

HOUSE IN THE SAME VILLAGE. SHE HERSELF WAS TAKEN ON TWO

OCCASIONS BY TWO SOLDIERS EACH TIME. SHE WAS RAPED BY

BOTH MEN ON EACH OCCASION. THEY WORE REGULAR "CHETNIK"

UNIFORMS. THEIR NAMES WERE VLAJKICA OBRENOVIC, "TIHOMIR"

(NFI), AND "ZORAN" (NFI). THE FOURTH WAS CALLED BY THE
