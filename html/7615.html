SUBJECT: TFIZ01: HEAD OF BENI HASSAN TRIBE IN JORDAN SAYS

BENI HASSAN IN IRAQ WISH TO COOPERATE WITH THE COALITION

Classified By: PolCouns Doug Silliman for reasons 1.5 (B) and (D)

1. (S) Sheikh Nawwaf al-Eitan, head of the Beni Hassan

tribe in Jordan, contacted PolCouns April 17 to say that the

head of the Beni Hassan tribe in Iraq would like to cooperate

with Coalition forces. Sheikh Nawwaf said that Sheikh Hatem

al-Hassan, leader of the Beni Hassan in Iraq, called this

week on his Thuraya phone and said that he wanted to get in

touch with Coalition representatives, but did not know how.

He said the Beni Hassan want to cooperate in rebuilding Iraq

in any way possible. Sheikh Nawwaf said that the Beni Hassan

had many members who had served and could again serve in the

Iraqi military, police forces, and fire/civil defense

brigades. In addition, the Beni Hassan would like to

participate in the new political process in Iraq.

2. (S) Sheikh Nawwaf provided the following names and

contact numbers for Beni Hassan leaders in Iraq.

Sheikh Hatem al-Hassan, leader of the Beni Hassan in Iraq,

Thuraya phone number 00-88-21-65-21-31-023. Sheikh Hatem

lives in an-Najaf province near Kufa in an area called Abu

Hallan.

Dr. Rahim Hatem al-Hassan, son of Sheikh Hatem and the Deputy

President of the Bar Association

Dr. Ali Hatem al-Hassan, son of Sheikh Hatem and professor at

a university in Baghdad

3. (SBU) According to Sheikh Nawwaf, the Beni Hassan of

Iraq number 1.5 million and live mostly in the area of

south-central Iraq near an-Najaf and Karbala. Although the

Beni Hassan of Jordan are Sunni, he said that nearly all Beni

Hassan in Iraq are Shia.
