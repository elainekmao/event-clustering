1. (C) POLOFF MET WITH FADUMA FARAH AIDID, THE SISTER OF

"SOMALI PRESIDENT" HUSSEIN MOHAMED AIDID AND DAUGHTER OF THE

ASSASSINATED WARLORD GENERAL AIDID FEBRUARY 20. HUSSEIN

AIDID IS THE HEAD OF THE SOMALI RECONCILIATION AND

RESTORATION COUNCIL (SRRC) FACTION BUT HAS NO REAL CLAIM TO

BEING PRESIDENT OF SOMALIA. EXPRESSING EXTREME DISTRUST OF

ITALIAN AND EUROPEAN INTENTIONS, MS. AIDID SOUGHT A "NEW

PARTNERSHIP" BETWEEN SOMALIA AND THE UNITED STATES.

ACCORDING TO THE MFA, MS. AIDID HAS A NUMBER OF BEEFS AGAINST

THE GOI, PRINCIPALLY THAT SHE IS UNABLE TO ACCESS THE SOMALI

EMBASSY AND ITS BANK ACCOUNT. SHE MADE A NUMBER OF SERIOUS

AND INCREDIBLE ALLEGATIONS AGAINST THE GOI, INCLUDING THAT

THE GOI HAD STOLEN SOMALI GOVERNMENT PROPERTY, PERMITTED ARMS

TO BE SMUGGLED INTO SOMALIA, AND SENT ASSASSINS TO KILL HER

BROTHER. PER REFCON INTEREST, POST\'S MEETING WITH AIDID IS

FULLY TRANSMITTED FOR THE RECORD. END SUMMARY.

2. (SBU) PRESENTING A NUMBER OF DOCUMENTS TO BOLSTER HER

CLAIM TO REPRESENT THE GOVERNMENT OF SOMALIA AS ITS

AMBASSADOR, FADUMA FARAH AIDID REQUESTED A MEETING WITH THE

AMBASSADOR "IN ORDER TO REESTABLISH NORMAL DIPLOMATIC,

POLITICAL, AND ECONOMIC RELATIONS BETWEEN SOMALIA AND THE

UNITED STATES." THE REQUEST WAS SIGNED BY AN ITALIAN NAMED

FRANCO CANNATA\', IDENTIFIED AS GENERAL COUNSELOR FOR SPECIAL

AFFAIRS OF THE SOMALI REPUBLIC. ON FEBRUARY 20, AIDID AND

CANNATA MET WITH POLOFF AT THE U.S. EMBASSY. THEY ARRIVED AN

HOUR LATE, AND MS. AIDID ADDRESSED POLOFF IN THE FAMILIAR

VOICE (IN ITALIAN). HER RAMBLING, ACCUSATORY REMARKS LACKED

IN DIPLOMATIC FINESSE AND CREDIBILITY.

3. (C) MS. AIDID IS THE DAUGHTER OF ASSASSINATED WARLORD

GENERAL AIDID AND SISTER OF "SOMALI PRESIDENT" HUSSEIN

HIMSELF INSIDE SOMALIA AS THE HEAD OF THE ETHIOPIAN-ALLIED

SRRC, NOT CHIEF OF STATE. HIS ONLY CLAIM TO BEING

"PRESIDENT" IS PHYSICAL POSSESSION OF THE PRESIDENTIAL

PALACE. IMPERIALE DISMISSED HIM AS NOT EVEN A PARTICULARLY

INFLUENTIAL WARLORD. STILL MORE ODDLY, HUSSEIN HAD RECENTLY

BYPASSED HIS SISTER AND CANNATA TO SEND A MESSAGE TO THE GOI

TO CONSULT WITH THE GOI REGARDING THE UPCOMING NAIROBI PEACE

ETHIOPIA, THE FIRST ENCOURAGING ETHIOPIA TO PARTICIPATE AND

THE SECOND RECOGNIZING ITS INFLUENCE OVER CERTAIN

PARTIES--READ SRRC--AND ASKING IT TO CONVINCE THEM TO

PARTICIPATE.)

THE EMBASSY AS A CARETAKER AND GUARDIAN OF ITS SECRET FILES.

HER ACCESS THE EMBASSY, SINCE SHE WAS NO LONGER AN ACCREDITED

REMAINING IN THE STATE TERRITORY WITHOUT APPLYING FOR THE

RESIDENCE PERMIT WITHIN THE PRESCRIBED TERM;

MS. AIDID, DAUGHTER OF THE FORMER PRESIDENT OF THE SOMALI

REPUBLIC, GENERAL AIDID, KILLED IN 1996, AND SISTER OF THE

IN THE DEPORTATION ORDER, IT IS STATED THAT MS. AIDID &IS NO

LONGER A STAFF MEMBER8 OF THE SOMALI MISSION TO ITALY;

SUCH A STATEMENT IS NOT SUPPORTED BY ANY DOCUMENTARY EVIDENCE

AND IS CONTRADICTED BY THE FACT THAT MS. AIDID IS THE HOLDER

OF A DIPLOMATIC VISA VALID UNTIL 2001 AND OF A NOTE OF

ACCREDITATION RELEASED BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE SOMALI

REPUBLIC;

THE TRIBUNAL OF VITERBO, THEREFORE, HAS INVALIDATED THE

DEPORTATION ORDER BY JUDICIAL RULING ON JUNE 28, 2000;

THE GENERAL COUNSELOR FOR SPECIAL AFFAIRS AT THE EMBASSY OF

THE SOMALI REPUBLIC TO ITALY, MR. FRANCO CANNATA,, IN A

COMPLAINT SENT TO THE REPUBLIC PUBLIC PROSECUTOR\'S OFFICE HAS

POINTED TO THE FACT THAT THE EMBASSY BUILDING HAS BEEN

ILLEGALLY TAKEN OVER BY PERSONS UNRELATED TO THE DIPLOMATIC

MISSION, NAMELY BY EXPONENTS OF THE REGIME OF SIAD BARRE,

OVERTHROWN IN 1991, WHO ACTUALLY CONTINUE TO PERFORM

FUNCTIONS THAT ARE NOT RECOGNIZED AS THEIR OWN BY EITHER THE

LEGITIMATE SOMALI GOVERNMENT OR THE INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY;

THE ITALIAN GOVERNMENT HAS NOT INTERVENED, SO FAR, TO PUT AN

END TO THIS SITUATION AND HAS, INSTEAD, INCREASED THE GENERAL

CONFUSION IN THAT IT DOES NOT RECOGNIZE THE REPRESENTATIVES

OF THE LEGITIMATE GOVERNMENT OF MOGADISHU, SO THAT THE SOMALI

EMBASSY TO ITALY DOES NOT EXIST AT PRESENT;
