SUBJECT: COMMUNITY OF DEMOCRACIES: JORDAN WILL BE

REPRESENTED BY AMBASSADOR

REF: STATE 206278

1. (U) On October 21, Ayman Aamiri, Deputy Director of the

MFA\'s IO Department informed poloff that, due to other

scheduling requirements, FonMin Muasher regretted that he

would not be able to attend the Community of Democracies

Ministerial in Seoul, November 10-12. The Jordanian

delegation will be led, Aamiri said, by Jordan\'s Ambassador

in Tokyo, Samir Naouri.
