Econoff met with Nilgun Altindag, Director General for

Customs, and Ahmet Ilguz, Customs Expert, on November 7

to deliver the U.S. position on customs classification

of Eggo Waffles, the Avid Media Composer 1000, and

Multifunctional Digital Machines (reftels) to be

discussed at the upcoming Harmonized System Committee.

Ilguz responded that he agreed with the U.S. positions

on the Avid Media Composer and Multifunctional Digital

Machines, but that, as a candidate country, Turkey would

coordinate its position with that of the EU. Customs

said that it disagreed with our position on Eggo

Waffles, as they do not meet the water content criteria

in the Explanatory Notes. Given the complete absence of

Turkish waffle production, Customs told us that Turkey

does not have a strong view on this issue and was open

to classifying Eggo Waffles as waffles if the

Explanatory Notes are amended.
