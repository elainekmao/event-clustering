SUBJECT: MEETING WITH THICH THAI HOA

RECEIVED INVITATIONS ABOUT ONE MONTH BEFORE THE SUMMIT

FROM VIETNAM\'S UN MISSION. THEY THEN WENT THROUGH

FORMAL GVN APPLICATION PROCEDURES FOR APPROVAL OF

TRAVEL FOR RELIGIOUS FIGURES. END NOTE).

7. (SBU) COMMENT: GIVEN THE DATE HOA RECEIVED THE

INVITATION (AUGUST 14) AND THE DATE HE FILED HIS

APPLICATION (AUGUST 21), IT IS DIFFICULT TO EXPECT HE

COULD HAVE RECEIVED A PASSPORT AND STILL BEEN ABLE TO

MAKE THE AUGUST 28-31 SESSION EVEN WERE HIS CASE NOT

CONTROVERSIAL BECAUSE OF HIS RELIGIOUS AFFILIATION. GVN

RELIGIOUS AUTHORITIES IN HANOI ARE FAMILIAR WITH HOA.

A FEW DAYS BEFORE POLOFF\'S VISIT, EMBASSY SPOKE WITH

NGUYEN THE DOANH, DIRECTOR OF THE DEPARTMENT FOR

INTERNATIONAL RELATIONS OF THE VIETNAM GOVERNMENT

COMMITTEE FOR RELIGIOUS AFFAIRS. DOANH DENIED THAT HOA

HAD BEEN REFUSED A PASSPORT, WHICH IS TECHNICALLY TRUE,

SINCE NO FORMAL REFUSAL WAS EVER ISSUED. END COMMENT.

8. (SBU) THE PROVINCIAL COMMITTEE ASKED HOA TO STATE

WHETHER HE IS A MEMBER OF THE VIETNAM BUDDHIST SANGHA

(VBS). (NOTE: NO RELIGIOUS ORGANIZATION IS LEGAL IN

VIETNAM UNLESS IT IS REGISTERED WITH THE GOVERNMENT.

THE VBS, "OFFICIAL" UMBRELLA ORGANIZATION FOR

VIETNAMESE BUDDHISTS, IS THE ONLY ONE WHICH THE

GOVERNMENT HAS ALLOWED TO REGISTER. END NOTE.) HOA

SAID HE REPLIED THAT HE IS NOT A MEMBER OF THE VBS, HE

IS A MEMBER OF THE UNIFIED BUDDHIST CHURCH OF VIETNAM

(UBCV), AN ORGANIZATION WHICH THE GVN DOES NOT ALLOW TO

REGISTER. SINCE THAT TIME, HOA SAID, HE HAS HEARD

NOTHING FROM THE PROVINCIAL AUTHORITY OR ANY OTHER GVN

OFFICE ABOUT HIS REQUEST FOR A PASSPORT.

9. (SBU) ON AUGUST 25, HOA SAID, HE SENT AN E-MAIL TO

SIR BAWA JAIN, CHAIRMAN OF THE MILLENNIUM SUMMIT,

EXPLAINING THAT THE GOVERNMENT HAD NOT YET ISSUED A

PASSPORT AND ASKING JAIN TO INTERVENE. HE ALSO SENT

JAIN A COPY OF THE SPEECH HE INTENDED TO DELIVER TO THE

SUMMIT. (HOA PROVIDED POLOFF WITH COPIES OF BOTH

DOCUMENTS. WE DO NOT KNOW, AND HOA DID NOT STATE,

WHETHER JAIN RECEIVED HIS E-MAIL, OR REPLIED). ON

SEPTEMBER 2, HOA SENT A LETTER OF PROTEST TO NINE HIGH-

RANKING VIETNAMESE GOVERNMENT AND PARTY OFFICIALS,

INCLUDING GENERAL SECRETARY LE KHA PHIEU, PRESIDENT

TRAN DUC LUONG, PRIME MINISTER PHAN VAN KHAI, AND

NATIONAL ASSEMBLY SPEAKER NONG DUC MANH. COPIES WERE

SENT TO NUMEROUS PROVINCIAL AND GOVERNMENT RELIGIOUS

AFFAIRS OFFICIALS. THERE HAS BEEN NO RESPONSE OF ANY

KIND TO THIS LETTER, SAID HOA.

10. (SBU) HOA SAID HE HAD BEEN INTERVIEWED IN RECENT

DAYS ABOUT THE CASE BY PHONE BY THE VOICE OF AMERICA

AND RADIO FREE ASIA. HE SAID AN INTERVIEWER FROM THE

VOA\'S ASIA DIVISION ALSO TRIED TO INTERVIEW HIM

SEPTEMBER 7, BUT THE CALL WAS INTERRUPTED AND THE

INTERVIEW NEVER TOOK PLACE. HOA BELIEVES GOVERNMENT

SECURITY FORCES OBSTRUCTED THE CALL. HE BELIEVES

SECURITY FORCES HAVE ALSO SOMETIMES JAMMED HIS FAX

MACHINE. (NOTE: IN VIETNAM, THE VAGARIES OF

INTERNATIONAL PHONE AND FAX SERVICE FREQUENTLY HINDER

COMMUNICATION. END NOTE).
