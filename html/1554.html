SUBJECT: UN SPECIAL ENVOY MORJANE SPEAKS AT SADC

PEACEKEEPING CENTER IN ZIMBABWE

CLASSIFIED BY CHARGE D\'AFFAIRES EARL M. IRVING FOR

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 HARARE 00882 020725Z

REASONS: 1.5 (B) AND (D).

1. (C) ON MARCH 1, UNSG SPECIAL ENJOY MORJANE DELIVERED

A SPEECH ON THE DRC TO THE SADC REGIONAL PEACEKEEPING

TRAINING CENTER (RPTC) IN HARARE. MORJANE\'S VISIT WAS

ARRANGED BY UNITAR-GENEVA AND SPONSORED BY THE RPTC.

THE RPTC\'S MANAGING DIRECTOR, PETER VOETMANN, PROVIDED A

READOUT TO POLOFF ON MORJANE\'S SPEECH, WHICH WAS

ATTENDED BY A NUMBER OF ZIMBABWE MILITARY OFFICERS AND

REPRESENTATIVES FROM THE ZIMBABWEAN MINISTRIES OF

DEFENSE AND FOREIGN AFFAIRS.

2. (C) ACCORDING TO VOETMANN, MORJANE WAS VERY

OPTIMISTIC ABOUT THE DRC PEACE PROCESS AND PROGRESS

BEING MADE IN MONUC DEPLOYMENT. MORJANE OUTLINED

OBSTACLES TO THE DEPLOYMENT OF PEACEKEEPERS,

PARTICULARLY POOR INFRASTRUCTURE AND DENSELY FORESTED

TERRAIN IN THE CONGO. MORJANE CALLED FOR THE USE OF

SMALLER, COMPANY-SIZED UNITS OPERATING MORE

INDEPENDENTLY THAN NORMAL MILITARY UNITS. AN ARMORED

INFANTRY BATTALION SIMPLY WOULD NOT BE EFFECTIVE IN THE

CONGOLESE ENVIRONMENT, THE ENVOY ARGUED. HE ALSO STATED

THAT MORE SOPHISTICATED EQUIPMENT, SUCH AS HELICOPTERS,

WOULD BE NEEDED FOR EFFECTIVE MONITORING.

3. (C) MORJANE ALSO FLOATED THE IDEA THAT THE SADC

ALLIES SHOULD CONSIDER "CHANGING THEIR BERETS TO BLUE"

IN THE DRC. HOWEVER, THE CONSENT OF ALL THE LUSAKA

PARTIES WOULD BE NECESSARY, INCLUDING PEOPLE ON ALL

LEVELS, FROM THE SOLDIERS ON UP. THE ENVOY STATED THAT

MORE ROBUST RULES OF ENGAGEMENT WOULD HAVE TO BE DEVISED

AS WELL, ACCORDING TO VOETMANN. VOETMANN COMMENTED THAT

MORJANE\'S IDEA WOULD REQUIRE A "SUBSTANTIAL" COMMITMENT

FROM THE UN, HOWEVER.

4. (C) COMMENT: MORJANE\'S PROPOSAL TO CONVERT SADC

TROOPS ALREADY IN THE DRC TO UN PEACEKEEPERS IS AN

INTERESTING IDEA THAT PROBABLY HAS NOT RECEIVED WIDE

CONSIDERATION HERE. AS VOETMANN SAID, HOWEVER, IT WOULD

REQUIRE A MAJOR FINANCIAL COMMITMENT THAT NEITHER THE UN

NOR THE BELLIGERENTS MAY BE PREPARED TO MAKE YET. END

COMMENT.
