1. SUMMARY. CONFAGRICOLTURA, ONE OF ITALY\'S

LEADING FARMER ORGANIZATIONS, TOLD AG

MINCOUNSELOR THAT IT HAS A COMMITMENT FROM

AGMINISTER ALEMANNO TO INTRODUCE A TOLERANCE

OF 0.3-0.5 PERCENT FOR ADVENTITIOUS PRESENCE

IN CORN AND SOYBEAN SEEDS FOR PLANTING SHOULD

THE ITALIAN SEED CERTIFICATION ASSOCIATION

(ENSE) CONCLUDE THERE ARE INADEQUATE SUPPLIES

OF GM-FREE CONVENTIONAL CORN AND SOYBEAN

SEEDS AVAILABLE TO MEET ITALIAN NEEDS.

CONFAGRICOLTURA CAUTIONED THAT RIVAL FARM

ORGANIZATION, COLDIRETTI, IS STRONGLY AGAINST

ANY TOLERANCE LEVEL FOR ADVENTITIOUS PRESENCE

AND WAS SEEKING TO CONVINCE ALEMANNO THAT

ADEQUATE SUPPLIES OF GMO-FREE CONVENTIONAL

SEEDS EXIST. CONFAGRICOLTURA BELIEVES THE

PROSPECTS FOR ALEMANNO IMPLEMENTING A

TOLERANCE OF 0.3-0.5 PERCENT ARE PRETTY GOOD,

PROVIDED THAT THE MAJOR SEED SUPPLING

COMPANIES (I.E. PIONEER, MONSANTO, AND

SYNGENTA) REMAIN UNIFIED AND REAFFIRM THAT

THEY CANNOT GUARANTEE ZERO ADVENTITIOUS

PRESENCE IN THEIR CONVENTIONAL SEEDS.

CONFAGRICOLTURA ALSO PUBLISHED A PRESS

RELEASE TODAY STATING ITS OPPOSITION TO ANY

MEETING WITH CONFAGRICOLTURA

2. AGMIN COUNSELOR, AG ATTACHE MURPHY, AND

SENIOR AGRICULTURAL SPECIALIST MET JAN.9 WITH

THE PRESIDENT AND SENIOR LEADERS OF

CONFAGRICOLTURA, ONE OF THE THREE MAJOR

ITALIAN FARMERS ORGANIZATIONS. AGMIN

COUNSELOR LEARNED THAT ITALIAN MINISTER OF

AGRICULTURE GIANNI ALEMANNO CONSULTED WITH

ALL THREE ORGANIZATIONS, COFAGRICOLTURA,

COLDIRETTI, AND CONFEDERAZIONE ITALIANA

AGRICOLTORI, BEFORE ISSUING THE DECEMBER 28,

2001 DECREE (SEE REFTELS). ACCORDING TO

CONFAGRICOLTURA, ALEMANNO WAS PREPARED AT

THAT TIME TO IMPLEMENT A TOLERANCE FOR

ADVENTITIOUS PRESENCE OF 0.3-0.5 PERCENT FOR

CORN AND SOYBEAN SEEDS. HOWEVER, COLDIRETTI,

THE LARGEST OF ITALY\'S THREE FARMER

ORGANIZATIONS, EXPRESSED VEHEMENT OPPOSITION,

FORCING ALEMANNO TO TAKE A MORE CIRCUITOUS

TACK IN ORDER TO GARNER SUFFICIENT POLITICAL

COVER. ALEMANNO THUS AGREED WITH THE THREE

FARM LEADERS FOR THE MINAG\'S SEED AGENCY,

ENSE, TO DETERMINE WHETHER THERE ARE

SUFFICIENT SUPPLIES OF CONVENTIONAL SEEDS

AVAILABLE WITH ZERO ADVENTITIOUS PRESENCE.

3. THE DEC. 28 DECREE SURPRISED

CONFAGRICOLTURA IN THAT THEIR DISCUSSION WITH

ALEMANNO DID NOT INCLUDE THE POSSIBILITY OF

LOWER STANDARDS FOR GERMINATION RATES. THE

PUBLISHED VERSION ADDS THE CLAUSE RELAXING

STANDARDS, A LIKELY CONCESSION TO COLDIRETTI

AND OTHERS, BUT CONFAGRICOLTURA DISCOUNTS

THIS AS A LIKELY SCENARIO.

4. CONFAGRICOLTURA WAS RELATIVELY CONFIDENT

THAT ALEMANNO WOULD IMPLEMENT A TOLERANCE FOR

ADVENTITIOUS PRESENCE WITH ONE CAVEAT-- THE

MAJOR SEED SUPPLIERS MUST REMAIN UNITED AND

REAFFIRM THEY CANNOT GUARANTEE ZERO PRESENCE

FOR GMOS IN THEIR CONVENTIONAL SEEDS. SHOULD

ENSE CONCLUDE THAT SUFFICIENT SUPPLIES OF

GMO-FREE CONVENTIONAL SEED EXIST, COLDIRETTI

IS LIKELY TO WIN THE DAY. CONFAGRICOLTURA

SOUGHT ASSURANCES THAT PIONEER AND THE OTHER

SUPPLIERS WOULD MAKE CLEAR THEIR INABILITY TO

GUARANTEE ZERO ADVENTITIOUS PRESENCE. (FYI,

EMBASSY HAS SHARED CONFAGRICOLTURA\'S CONCERNS

AND RECOMMENDATION WITH PIONEER AND OTHER

SUPPLIERS.) CONFAGRICOLTURA ACKNOWLEDGED THAT

5. CONFAGRICOLTURA ALSO INDICATED THAT IF

ALEMANNO IMPLEMENTS A TOLERANCE OF

ADVENTITIOUS PRESENCE IT WOULD LIKELY ENTAIL

A 0.3 PERCENT TOLERANCE FOR CORN SEED AND A

0.5 PERCENT TOLERANCE FOR SOYBEAN SEED.

CONFAGRICOLTURA UNDERSTANDS THAT THERE WOULD

BE NO MENTION OF SPECIFIC EVENTS (APPROVED

VS. UNAPPROVED). CORN SEED SUPPLIES ARE OF

PARTICULAR CONCERN TO CONFAGRICOLTURA.

SOYBEAN SEEDS ARE OF LESSER CONCERN GIVEN

SOYBEAN PLANTINGS IN ITALY NEXT SEASON ARE

EXPECTED TO DECLINE TO LESS THAN 200,000

HECTARES BECAUSE OF LOWER PRICES.

CDA MEETING WITH PM DIPLOMATIC ADVISOR

CASTELLENETTA

6. THE CHARGE, IN A CALL ON PM DIPLOMATIC

ADVISOR CASTELLENETTA, RAISED THE TALKING

POINTS IN REFTEL (ROME 68). HE STRESSED AS

WELL THAT WE ARE APPRECIATIVE OF THE INITIAL

STEPS TAKEN BY THE PM\'S OFFICE TO REOPEN

DISCUSSION ON AGRICULTURAL BIOTECHNOLOGIES

AND THE PM\'S DIRECTION THAT PROFESSOR

LEONARDO SANTI SHOULD EXPLORE WITH US AN

AGREEMENT ON BIOTECH COOPERATION SIMILAR TO

THE ONE WE CONCLUDED LAST SUMMER ON CLIMATE

CHANGE RESEARCH. THE CHARGE ALSO MENTIONED

THAT WE HAD RECEIVED THE ENVIRONMENT

MINISTRY\'S REQUEST TO CONSIDER AN INFORMAL

BILATERAL DISCUSSION OF GMOS IN NEW YORK AT

THE END OF JANUARY.

7. CASTELLANETA, WHO WAS INSTRUMENTAL IN

ACHIEVING THE CLIMATE CHANGE RESEARCH

ACCORD, KNEW ABOUT AND WAS POSITIVE TOWARD

PROF. SANTI\'S APPROACH TO THE EMBASSY. HE

DID NOT SEEM TO BE AWARE OF THE ENVIRONMENT

MINISTRY\'S PROPOSAL BUT LIKED IT.

8. CHARGE CONTINUED THAT, DESPITE THESE

POSITIVE SIGNS, THE USG IS PUZZLED AT AND

CONCERNED ABOUT AG MINISTER ALEMANNO\'S

DECEMBER 28 DIRECTIVE. HANDING CASTELLANETA

A COPY, THE CHARGE NOTED THAT OUR READING OF

THE DECREE INDICATES THAT IT WOULD ERECT NEW

BARRIERS TO GMOS. THIS WOULD NOT BE IN

KEEPING WITH DISCUSSIONS BETWEEN PRESIDENT

BUSH AND PM BERLUSCONI AND OTHER EXCHANGES WE

HAVE HAD WITH MUCH OF THE GOI. CASTELLANETA

SUGGESTED THAT PERHAPS ALEMANNO WAS TRYING TO

DEMONSTRATE THAT IT IS NOT POSSIBLE TO GET

DOWN TO ZERO PERCENT GMO CONTENT IN SEEDS.
