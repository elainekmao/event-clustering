SUBJECT: LEANER POLITBURO AND CENTRAL COMMITTEE TO BE UNVEILED AT WEEKEND COMMUNIST PARTY CONGRESS

REF: A) HANOI 905 B) HANOI 638

1. (U) CLASSIFIED BY POLITICAL COUNSELOR JAMES BRUNO;

REASONS 1.5 (B) AND (D).

2. (C) SUMMARY: FOUR NEW POLITBURO MEMBERS -- REPORTEDLY

INCLUDING MASS MOBILIZATION HEAD DUOC AND FORMER LABOR

MINISTER HOAN -- ARE EXPECTED TO JOIN A SLIMMED-DOWN, 15-

MEMBER LEADERSHIP TO BE UNVEILED AT THE NINTH PARTY

CONGRESS, WHICH OPENS APRIL 19. ON BALANCE, THE NEW

POLITBURO, EXPECTED TO BE HEADED BY ASSEMBLY SPEAKER MANH,

SHOULD BE MORE SUPPORTIVE OF ECONOMIC REFORM. SEVEN MOSTLY

OLDER MEMBERS ARE EXPECTED TO RETIRE, INCLUDING GENERAL

SECRETARY PHIEU, FATHERLAND FRONT LEADER DUYET, AND

PEOPLE\'S ARMY POLITICAL COMMISSAR NGAN. IN A SURPRISE,

SEVERAL SOURCES SAY DEFENSE MINISTER TRA, RECENTLY

DISCIPLINED BY THE CENTRAL COMMITTEE, WILL RETAIN HIS SEAT

AS THE LONE MILITARY MAN IN THE BODY. THE LATEST REPORTS

WE HEAR ARE THAT PM KHAI WILL REMAIN IN PLACE, PERHAPS FOR

ANOTHER YEAR. HCMC PARTY CHIEF TRIET, POPULAR AMONG HIS

CENTRAL COMMITTEE PEERS, IS SAID BY SEVERAL SOURCES TO BE

HIS HEIR APPARENT. OTHERS SAY FIRST DEPUTY PM DUNG WILL

RETAIN HIS DEPUTY PRIME MINISTER SLOT FOR THE TIME BEING.

WE EXPECT THE LEANER, 150-MEMBER CENTRAL COMMITTEE ALSO TO

BE MARGINALLY MORE ASSERTIVE WITH THE POLITBURO AND

PARTY CONGRESS TO APPROVE NEW POLITBURO

---------------------------------------

3. (C) THE 1,168 VOTING DELEGATES TO THE NINTH COMMUNIST

PARTY CONGRESS (TWO OTHERS ARE ILL) WILL FORMALLY MEET

APRIL 19-22 IN BA DINH HALL TO APPROVE THE FOUR SEMINAL

POLITICAL AND ECONOMIC DOCUMENTS OF THE COMMUNIST PARTY

(CPV) AND TO APPROVE THE NEW LEADERSHIP LINEUP. AS CITED

REF A, GENERAL SECRETARY LE KHA PHIEU APPEARS HIGHLY LIKELY

TO BE REPLACED BY NATIONAL ASSEMBLY SPEAKER NONG DUC MANH,

ACCORDING TO A NUMBER OF SOURCES. SEVERAL SOURCES REPORT

MANH, INITIALLY RELUCTANT TO TAKE THE SUPREME POST, AGREED

TO DO SO AFTER REQUESTS FROM CPV ELDERS AND WINNING A

STRONG VOTE OF SUPPORT FROM THE DELEGATES. AT THE SAME

TIME, OTHER REPORTS CONTINUE TO CIRCULATE THAT CPV

ORGANIZATIONAL AFFAIRS CHIEF NGUYEN VAN AN WAS CONSIDERED

ON THE LAST DAY OF THE PRE-CONGRESS FOR THE TOP SLOT. IN

ANY EVENT, THE PROGRAM FOR THE CONGRESS NOTABLY OMITS THE

NAME OF THE GENERAL SECRETARY AT THE END OF THE CONGRESS,

WHILE LISTING PHIEU AT THE BEGINNING. (COMMENT: WE EXPECT

MANH TO BE UNVEILED THE AFTERNOON OF APRIL 22 WHEN THE

RESULTS OF THE APRIL 21 CONGRESS ELECTIONS ARE FORMALLY

ANNOUNCED, BUT THIS OPAQUE LEADERSHIP STRUCTURE HAS

SURPRISED US BEFORE, SO WE\'LL WAIT FOR THE SOLOIST IN THIS

CPV OPERA TO FINISH HER LAST ARIA BEFORE PRONOUNCING WITH

CERTAINTY. END COMMENT.)

4. (C) THE CONVENTIONAL WISDOM AMONG CPV-CONNECTED SOURCES

IS THAT FOUR NEW MEMBERS ARE EXPECTED TO JOIN A SLIMMED-

DOWN, 15-MEMBER POLITBURO. WE HEAR DIFFERENT NAMES FROM

DIFFERENT SOURCES. HEAD OF THE CPV\'S MASS MOBILIZATION

ORGANIZATION, TRUONG QUANG DUOC, AND FORMER LABOR MINISTER

NGUYEN DINH HOAN ARE COMMON TO MOST LISTS. BOTH SEEM

PLAUSIBLE CHOICES. THE HEAD OF MASS MOBILIZATION

TRADITIONALLY HAS BEEN A POLITBURO MEMBER, AND THIS IS THE

POST PHAM THE DUYET PREVIOUSLY HELD. GIVEN UNREST IN THE

CENTRAL HIGHLANDS AND THE NEED TO RALLY POPULAR SUPPORT FOR

CPV POSITIONS AROUND THE COUNTRY, THIS MAKES SENSE.

LIKEWISE, WE HAVE HEARD FROM A CLOSE WORKING CONTACT

(STRICTLY PROTECT) AT THE LABOR MINISTRY, WHO CONSIDERS

HOAN HIS MENTOR, THAT HE BELIEVES HE MAY BE MOVING ON TO

WORK WITH HOAN WHEN HE MOVES UP TO THE POLITBURO.

(COMMENT: WE KNOW LITTLE ABOUT DUOC BUT HIS CURRENT

PORTFOLIO IS HIGHLY FOCUSED ON IDEOLOGICAL AND CPV

ORGANIZATIONAL MATTERS. HOAN IS AN ADVOCATE OF MORE RAPID

ECONOMIC AND SOCIAL REFORM, AND IS SEEN BY A FORMER DEPUTY

AS A PROGRESSIVE. END COMMENT)

5. (C) THE OTHER TWO POLITBURO SLOTS ARE MORE MUDDLED. ONE

CPV SOURCE SAYS CULTURE AND INFORMATION MINISTER NGUYEN

KHOA DIEM AND THE DEPUTY HEAD OF THE CPV CONTROL

COMMISSION, LE HONG ANH, ARE THE OTHER TWO TAPPED FOR THE

ELITE BODY. HOWEVER, EARLIER IN THE WEEK WE HEARD THAT

TRADE MINISTER VU KHOAN AND DEPUTY PM KHIEM WERE SLATED FOR

THIS PROMOTION. (COMMENT: RUMORS CIRCULATE THAT VU KHOAN

IS ALMOST CERTAIN TO TAKE THE DEPUTY PRIME MINISTER DUTIES

OF RETIRING NGUYEN MANH CAM, WHO IS ALSO A POLITBURO

MEMBER. THE QUESTION IS WHETHER KHOAN WILL TAKE OVER

EITHER THE POLITBURO SEAT AND POSSIBLY THE FOREIGN MINISTER

POST. END COMMENT.)

6. (C) SEVEN MOSTLY OLDER POLITBURO MEMBERS ARE EXPECTED TO

STEP DOWN. WE BELIEVE THEY WILL BE CPV GENERAL SECRETARY

PHIEU, FATHERLAND FRONT HEAD DUYET, PEOPLE\'S ARMY POLITICAL

COMMISSAR GEN. PHAM THANH NGAN (WHO REPORTEDLY WILL FALL

WITH HIS MENTOR PHIEU), DEPUTY PM CAM, HO CHI MINH

POLITICAL ACADEMY DIRECTOR NGUYEN DUC BINH, CPV IDEOLOGICAL

AFFAIRS DIRECTOR LE XUAN TUNG, AND CPV CONTROL COMMISSION

CHAIRWOMAN NGUYEN THI XUAN MY. WE HAVE LONG BELIEVED CAM,

BINH, AND TUNG WERE RIPE TO STEP DOWN. PHIEU\'S AND DUYET\'S

BATTLES TO RETAIN THEIR POSTS OVER THE PAST MONTHS HAVE

BEEN WELL DOCUMENTED. LIKEWISE, MY WAS STRONGLY CRITICIZED

FOR HER POOR MANAGEMENT OF THE CRITICISM/SELF-CRITICISM

CAMPAIGN, BUT THE CONVENTIONAL WISDOM UNTIL THIS PAST WEEK

WAS THE CPV WOULD NOT WANT TO REMOVE THE ONLY WOMAN EVER

APPOINTED TO THE POLITBURO.

7. (C) THE MOST INTERESTING NAME ON THIS LIST, NGAN, IS

CLOSELY TIED TO THE MOST INTERESTING OMISSION, TRA. GEN.

NGAN HAS LONG BEEN SEEN AS ONE OF THE POTENTIAL "COMERS"

AMONG THE POLITBURO, BUT IT HAS BEEN CLEAR HIS RAPID RECENT

RISE WAS CLOSELY TIED TO PHIEU\'S. HE FOLLOWED PHIEU AS

POLITICAL COMMISSAR OF THE ARMED FORCES, AND OVER THE PAST

YEAR SEVERAL MILITARY SOURCES SUGGESTED HE WAS DESTINED FOR

HIGHER THINGS. CONVERSELY, THE AGING DEFENSE MINISTER TRA

WAS SHARPLY RAPPED ON THE KNUCKLES BY THE CENTRAL COMMITTEE

ONLY WEEKS AGO, IN PART FOR THE VIOLATIONS OF VIETNAMESE

AIRSPACE BY VIETNAMESE-AMERICAN SELF-DESCRIBED "FREEDOM

FIGHTER" LY TONG. THAT REPRIMAND SEEMED TO FORESHADOW HIS

LIKELY RETIREMENT FROM HIS GOVERNMENT AND POLITBURO POSTS.

WE SUSPECT TRA MAY HAVE BEEN RETAINED EITHER AS A SOP TO

PHIEU LOYALISTS, OR AS PART OF OTHER BARGAINING WITH

MILITARY SUPPORTERS. STRIKINGLY, IF THE CURRENT FORECASTED

LINE-UP HOLDS, TRA WOULD BE THE ONLY MILITARY

REPRESENTATIVE IN THE POLITBURO.

KHAI REPORTED TO STAY ON AS PRIME MINISTER

------------------------------------------

8. (C) CONTRARY TO REPORTS IN REF A, THE LATEST REPORTS WE

HEAR ARE THAT PRIME MINISTER PHAN VAN KHAI WILL REMAIN IN

PLACE FOR THE TIME BEING, PERHAPS FOR ANOTHER YEAR. HCMC

PARTY CHIEF NGUYEN MINH TRIET, WHO POLLED SIGNIFICANT

SUPPORT AMONG HIS CENTRAL COMMITTEE PEERS IN SEVERAL

BALLOTS OVER THE PAST TWO WEEKS, APPARENTLY IS SEEN BY MANY

AS HEIR APPARENT. HOWEVER, FOR THE TIME BEING, IT APPEARS

FIRST DEPUTY PM NGUYEN TAN DUNG WILL RETAIN HIS SLOT AND

THUS A PLAUSIBLE CLAIM AS PM-IN-WAITING. (COMMENT: TRIET,

9. (SBU) SOURCES SUGGEST THE NEW CENTRAL COMMITTEE WILL

HAVE 150 MEMBERS (VERSUS 170 FOR THAT ELECTED IN 1996).

CPV BRIEFERS SAID APRIL 18 THE CPV WOULD EMPHASIZE "QUALITY

TOLD THE PRESS THE CPV WOULD BRING BACK A SECRETARIAT STAFF

THAT WOULD RUN THE DAY-TO-DAY AFFAIRS OF THE POLITBURO.

COMMENT

-------

10. (C) IF THE FORECASTED LINE-UPS ARE CONFIRMED APRIL 22,

THE NEW LEANER LEADERSHIP BODIES OF THE CPV LOOK TO BE

MODESTLY MORE SUPPORTIVE OF INCREASING THE PACE OF ECONOMIC

REFORM. NOTABLE IS THAT MOST OF THE IDEOLOGICAL DEADWOOD

OF THE SEPTUAGENARIANS IN THE POLITBURO WILL BE RETIRED.

SOME OF THE YOUNGER MEMBERS LIKELY WILL PROVIDE MORE VIGOR

IN PRESSING THE REFORM PROGRAM. THAT SAID, THIS IS A

COLLECTIVE LEADERSHIP, AND THE SCREEDS AGAINST THE U.S. IN

THE PRESS AND THE REFLEXIVE HARPING ON THE DANGERS OF

"PEACEFUL EVOLUTION" INDICATE REFORM CHANGES WILL BE

GRADUAL AND MEASURED. MANH IS A COALITION BUILDER, AND

THOUGH PERHAPS LESS WELL-REPRESENTED IN THE TOP RANK, THE

MILITARY, PUBLIC SECURITY AND CPV IDEOLOGUES STILL COMMAND

IMPORTANT SEATS AT THE TABLE. END COMMENT.
