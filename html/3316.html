SUBJECT: ACTIVIST CHARGED WITH "SPY ACTIVITIES"

1. (U) CLASSIFIED BY POL/C SCOTT BELLARD, REASON

1.5(D)

2. (C) THE MINISTRY OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS (MFA)

CONFIDENTIAL

INTERNATIONAL ORGANIZATIONS DEPARTMENT REPLIED BY FAX

ON JULY 15 TO EMBASSY\'S INFORMAL APRIL 3 REQUEST FOR

INFORMATION ABOUT THE STATUS OF DR. PHAM HONG SON.

(PRESS REPORTS HAD CLAIMED SON HAD BEEN ARRESTED AFTER

HAVING TRANSLATED A DOCUMENT ENTITLED "WHAT IS

DEMOCRACY" FROM THE U.S. EMBASSY WEBSITE.) ACCORDING

TO THE REPLY (TEXT IN PARA 5), DR. SON IS ACCUSED OF

"SPY ACTIVITIES" -- INCLUDING TAKING MONEY FROM ANTI-

SRV GROUPS OVERSEAS IN EXCHANGE FOR STATE SECRETS --

AND IS UNDER FOUR MONTHS OF "TEMPORARY DETENTION"

WHILE THE GVN INVESTIGATES HIM.

3. (C) POLOFF CALLED SON\'S WIFE, VU THUY HA, ON JULY

15 TO INFORM HER OF THE MFA\'S RESPONSE AND ASKED HER

HOW MUCH SHE ALREADY KNEW OF ITS CONTENTS. SHE

REPLIED THAT SHE AWARE OF MOST OF THE POINTS

MENTIONED, BUT SAID THAT SHE WAS NOT SURE THAT SHE

UNDERSTOOD ALL OF THE DETAILS. HOWEVER, SHE DENIED

THAT HER HUSBAND HAD COLLECTED INFORMATION AGAINST THE

STATE OR THAT HE HAD TAKEN MONEY TO DO ANYTHING

AGAINST THE STATE, EMPHASIZING THAT HER HUSBAND IS "AN

HONEST MAN." SHE SAID THE WAS LOOKING FORWARD TO THE

JULY 27 EXPIRATION OF DR. SON\'S TEMPORARY DETENTION

AND NOTED THAT THE AUTHORITIES WOULD HAVE TO MAKE A

DECISION ABOUT HIM BY THEN. MRS. HA REQUESTED

EMBASSY\'S ASSISTANCE "TO PUSH FOR A POSITIVE OUTCOME"

FOR HER HUSBAND.

4. (C) COMMENT: THE CHARGE AGAINST DR. SON IS QUITE

SERIOUS, BUT IT DOES NOT APPEAR TO BE RELATED TO HIS

TRANSLATION OF PAS DOCUMENTS AVAILABLE ON THE

INTERNET, OR TO THE LETTERS HE POSTED ON THE INTERNET

ABOUT POLICE TREATMENT OF ACTIVISTS, INCLUDING

COMPANY IN VIETNAM, PRESUMABLY WOULD HAVE HAD

INFORMATION ABOUT A VIETNAMESE CITIZEN CURRENTLY UNDER

INVESTIGATION. END COMMENT.

5. (U) SUBJECT: INFORMATION ON THE CASE OF PHAM

HONG SON

THE DEPARTMENT OF INTERNATIONAL ORGANIZATIONS, MOFA

WOULD LIKE TO FORWARD YOU THE INFORMATION THAT WE HAVE

JUST RECEIVED FROM THE MINISTRY OF PUBLIC SECURITY

CONCERNING THE CASE OF PHAM HONG SON AS FOLLOWS:

DURING THE RECENT TIME, SON OFTEN GOT IN TOUCH WITH

THOSE WORKING FOR HOSTILE ANTI-VIETNAM ORGANIZATIONS.

HE RECEIVED INSTRUCTIONS FROM THESE ORGANIZATIONS TO

"BUILD THE FORCES AND WAIT FOR THE RIGHT OPPORTUNITY."

HE PERSONALLY REQUESTED OVERSEAS SUPPORT

"SUBSTANTIALLY AND FINANCIALLY IN ORDER TO UNDERMINE

AND OVERTHROW THE STATE OF VIETNAM." COUNTED AS FAR

CONFIDENTIAL

AS LATE 2001, SON HAS COLLECTED AND PROVIDED

INFORMATION CONTAINING STATE SECRETS FOR OVERSEAS

ORGANIZATION TO USE, PROPAGATE, DISTORT AND UNDERMINE

THE STATE OF VIETNAM. SON REPEATEDLY RECEIVED MONEY

FROM OVERSEAS HOSTILE ORGANIZATIONS TO TRANSFER

HOSTILE SUBJECTS IN THE COUNTRY (NOTE: MEANING

UNCLEAR. END NOTE.). WITH THOSE ACTIVITIES, HE HAS

VIOLATED ARTICLE 89, PARAGRAPH 1C OF THE PENAL CODE OF

VIETNAM FOR "PROVIDING OR COLLECTING TO PROVIDE

INFORMATION CONTAINING STATE SECRETS TO FOREIGN

SUBJECT; COLLECTING, PROVIDING OTHER INFORMATION AND

MATERIALS AIMING AT GIVING FOREIGNERS A MEANS TO USE

AGAINST THE STATE OF VIETNAM." ON 27TH MARCH, 2002,

THE INVESTIGATION AND SECURITY AGENCY, MINISTRY OF

PUBLIC SECURITY ISSUED THE ORDER TO ARREST SON. ON

2ND APRIL, 2002, SON WAS PROSECUTED ON CHARGE (NOTE:

PROBABLE MEANING: CHARGED. END NOTE.) OF SPY

ACTIVITIES AND WAS PUT UNDER 4 MONTHS TEMPORARY

DETENTION (WITH THE APPROVAL OF THE PEOPLE\'S SUPREME

PROCURACY) IN THE INVESTIGATING DURATION.

SON IS NOW OBSERVING HIS TEMPORARY DETENTION AND

ENJOYING ALL THE LEGAL PROCEDURES APPLIED FOR

DEFENDANTS. (END MFA TEXT.)
