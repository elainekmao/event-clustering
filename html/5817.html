SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION: IRAQ

1. SUMMARY: DEVELOPMENTS RELATED TO IRAQ CONTINUED TO GENERATE

EXTENSIVE COVERAGE IN THE UAE\'S MEDIA. EDITORIALS LAUDED THE

FRENCH AND GERMAN POSITIONS AND URGED THE ARABS TO SUPPORT THESE

POSITIONS IN FACE OF U.S. DETERMINATION FOR A WAR AGAINST IRAQ.

THE EDITORIALS CRITICIZED ARABS\' FAILURE TO AGREE ON AN EMERGENCY

ARAB SUMMIT AT THIS CRUCIAL TIME. HOWEVER, MANY COLUMNISTS

BELIEVE THAT A WAR AGAINST IRAQ WILL BE LAUNCHED WHETHER IRAQ

COOPERATES WITH THE UN INSPECTORS OR NOT. END SUMMARY.

2. SHARJAH-BASED ARABIC PAN-ARAB DAILY "AL-KHALEEJ"

EDITORIALIZED ON 2/18:

"THE HUGE DEMONSTRATIONS THE WORLD WITNESSED LAST SATURDAY

REJECTING AN AMERICAN (LED) WAR AGAINST IRAQ HAVE RESTORED THE

HUMAN FACE OF OUR WORLD ... ALTHOUGH BRITISH OFFICIALS ARE AFRAID

OF THE MOVEMENTS IN THE STREET, WHICH SUPPORT JACK STRAW\'S

REMARKS THAT BRITAIN CANNOT TAKE PART IN A WAR WITHOUT POPULAR

SUPPORT, THOSE WHO BLOW THE TRUMPETS OF WAR ARE PERSISTENT,

WHETHER IN LONDON OR WASHINGTON. WASHINGTON WILL NOT BE CONFINED

UNLESS IT SNATCHES A NEW RESOLUTION FROM THE UN TO MAINTAIN A

COVER FOR ITS AGGRESSION... UNLESS THE FRENCH, GERMAN, RUSSIAN

AND CHINESE POSITIONS ARE BACKED WITH A STRONGER AND PRACTICAL

ARAB STANCE, THE AMERICAN-BRITISH DECEPTION IS EXPECTED TO

SUCCEED. AFTER ALL, NO ONE IN THE WORLD WILL BE MORE INTERESTED

IN ARAB INTERESTS THAN THE ARABS THEMSELVES."

3. UNDER THE HEADLINE "AN ARAB EMERGENCY SUMMIT IS A MUST," ABU

DHABI-BASED PAN-ARAB DAILY "AKHBAR AL-ARAB" EDITORIALIZED ON

2/18:

"... THE ARAB FOREIGN MINISTERS\' FAILURE TO DECIDE A DATE FOR AN

EMERGENCY SUMMIT IS A REFLECTION AND AN EXTENSION OF THE ARABS\'

SITUATION, WHICH COULD BE EXTREMELY DIFFICULT TO ELIMINATE IN 10

YEARS. NEVERTHELESS, EFFORTS SHOULD CONTINUE TO CONVENE SUCH A

SUMMIT SO THAT THE ARABS DISCUSS THE EXCEPTIONAL DEVELOPMENTS

FACING THEM. WE BELIEVE A WAR AGAINST IRAQ IS A GRAVE

DEVELOPMENT THAT WOULD DEEPLY SHAKE THE ENTIRE ARAB FOUNDATION."

4. COMMENTING ON REMARKS MADE BY BRITISH PREMIER TONY BLAIR ON A

LEGAL COVER FOR THE UN SECURITY COUNCIL TO DECIDE FOR WAR, ABU

DHABI-BASED SEMI-OFFICIAL ARABIC DAILY "AL-ITTIHAD" EDITORIALIZED

ON 2/18:

"... IT SEEMS THAT THE BRITISH PRIME MINISTER DOES NOT KNOW THAT

THE UN AUTHORITY AND THE INTERNATIONAL LEGALITY FROM WHICH HE

SEEKS ASSISTANCE HAS BEEN HUMILIATED AND DESPISED BY A RACIST AND

TERRORIST ENTITY ... BRITAIN PLANTED ITS SEEDS IN THE REGION AND

AGAINST WHICH DOZENS OF RESOLUTIONS HAVE BEEN ISSUED, NONE OF

THEM WAS IMPLEMENTED..."

5. DR. AISHA AL-MARRI WROTE IN HER COLUMN IN "AL-ITTIHAD" ON

2/19:

"THE INTENDED WAR AGAINST IRAQ IS A DARK COMEDY AND A MAZE WHERE

ALL ROADS WILL LEAD TO THE \'OPTION\' OF WAR. IF IRAQ COOPERATES

WITH THE INSPECTORS AND REVEALS NEW INFORMATION REGARDING HIS

ARSENAL, IT IMPLIES THAT HE HAS BEEN HIDING THEM FOR A LONG TIME,

WHICH SUPPORTS U.S. CLAIMS AND BOOSTS THE OPTION FOR WAR. BUT,

IF IRAQ DOES NOT REVEAL ANY NEW INFORMATION, IT MEANS THAT IT

STILL SHOULD HAVE BEEN DISCLOSED... THIS MAZE CAN ONLY LEAD TO

THE OPTION OF WAR..."
