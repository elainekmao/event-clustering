SUBJECT: NIGERIA: BIOGRAPHICAL INFORMATION ON MINISTER OF

COMMERCE NGELELE

REF: COLEMAN/KRZYWDA EMAIL 12/17/02

Classified by CDA Timothy D. Andrews; Reason 1.5 (d).

1. (C) Precious Ngelele is a native of Rivers State. A former

Abuja Poloff believes Ngelele is a U.S. citizen. We know he

lived in the United States for many years before returning to

Nigeria in 1999. Poloff reports Ngelele is divorced or

separated from a U.S.-citizen spouse, with whom he had

several children. Ngelele currently lives in Abuja with a

women from a South-South ethnic group who is not permitted to

marry because of her position in a royal family. (Note:

Ngelele\'s Nigerian colleagues may not be aware that he is a

U.S. citizen. The 1999 Constitution of the Republic of

Nigeria requires that ministers and other GON officials hold

Nigerian citizenship only. End Note.)

2. (C) At one point Ngelele may have supported President

Obasanjo\'s top opponent for the 1999 PDP presidential

nomination, Alex Ekueme, but in the end he backed Obasanjo

and after the election was named Minister of State for Water

Resources. Post has been unable to obtain additional

information on Minister Ngelele\'s pre-1999 educational or

professional background.

3. (C) The former Poloff reports that Ngelele has a low-key

personality and is well disposed to the United States.
