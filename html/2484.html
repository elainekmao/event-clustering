SUBJECT: NEPAL DEPORTS PAKISTAN EMBASSY STAFF CAUGHT WITH

COUNTERFEIT U.S. CURRENCY.

REF: KATHMANDU 24

Nepal Foreign Ministry and Police officials confirmed to

the Embassy that Siraj Ahmed Siraj, the Pakistan Embassy

employee arrested in Kathmandu January 3 with over USD

9000 in counterfeit U.S. currency (Reftel), was deported

to his home country February 8. A police investigation

concluded that Siraj, whose position as support staff at

the Embassy did not afford him diplomatic immunity, had

conducted illegal money transactions. The Foreign

Ministry spokesperson told us that Nepal has asked

Pakistan to take necessary legal action against Siraj and

to report back to Kathmandu.
