SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED ON POSSIBLE HIZBALLAH RESUPPLY

FLIGHT

REF: SECSTATE 244129

Classified By: AMBASSADOR EDWARD W. GNEHM. REASONS: 1.5 (B), (C) AND

(D).

1. (S) On December 1, PolCouns and PolOff delivered message

in reftel regarding a possible forthcoming Hizballah resupply

flight to Ali Ayad, personal assistant to Foreign Minister

Marwan Muasher. PolCouns urged Jordan to inform the USG if

it receives such an overflight request, and to deny the

request. Ayad said he understood that we consider this to be

a priority issue, and will convey our concern to appropriate

GOJ officials.

GNEHM
