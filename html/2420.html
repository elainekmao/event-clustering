SUBJECT: WASSENAAR HOS: ITALY STILL MUMBLING BUT COMING

AROUND

REF: (A) MCBRIDE-VANSON 1/24 E-MAIL E (B) VIENNA 92

CLASSIFIED BY: ECMIN MARGARET DEAN; REASON 1.5 B/D.

1. (SBU) UGO DE MOHR, MFA\'S NONPROLIFERATION COORDINATOR,

GAVE HIS VIEW OF THE STATE OF PLAY OF THE WASSENAAR HEAD OF

SECRETARIAT ISSUE DURING A JANUARY 24 MEETING WITH ECONOFF.

SIPDIS

HE SEES, OR AT LEAST COUCHES, THE SITUATION IN PROCEDURAL

TERMS: THE DECEMBER PLENARY HAVING ENDED WITHOUT SELECTION OF

A HOS, THE PROCESS HAS TO START OVER; THE UKRAINE CHAIR

SHOULD CALL FOR NOMINATIONS; THERE SHOULD BE AN EXTRAORDINARY

SESSION TO ELECT A SUCCESSOR, PERHAPS IN MAY.

2. (SBU) DE MOHR SAID ITALY WOULD NOT FIELD A CANDIDATE FOR

THE JOB. ON THE SWEDISH CANDIDACY, HE DOESN\'T SEE ANY

QUESTION OF BLOCKAGE. IF THE SWEDE IS THE ONLY CANDIDATE, HE

EXPLAINED, ITALY WOULD NOT OPPOSE HIM. ON THE OTHER HAND,

HE HAS ALREADY HEARD RUMORS THAT ANOTHER CANDIDATE (HE

THOUGHT MAYBE A SLOVAK) WOULD JUMP INTO THE RACE WHEN

NOMINATIONS ARE OPENED. IF THERE IS ANOTHER CANDIDACY, HE

SAID WOULD LIKELY VIEW IT IN THE CONTEXT OF "THE POLICY OF

GEOGRAPHIC ROTATION. AFTER ALL WE CAN\'T HAVE SUCH A POLICY

ON MONDAY AND THEN SAY ON WEDNESDAY IT NO LONGER APPLIES."

(NOTE. DURING THE CONTROVERSY OVER LAURIOLA\'S CANDIDACY FOR

A NEW HOS TERM, DE MOHR INSISTED THAT SELECTION OF A SWEDE

WOULD VIOLATE THE PRINCIPLE BECAUSE A SWEDE WAS ACTING HOS

BEFORE LAURIOLA. END NOTE.)

3. (C) COMMENT. ALTHOUGH DE MOHR WAS HIS USUAL COOPERATIVE

SELF ON THE SEVERAL OTHER ITEMS ON YESTERDAY\'S MEETING

AGENDA, HE MADE CLEAR THAT THE LAURIOLA EPISODE STILL

RANKLES. WHEN THE GLOVES CAME OFF DURING THAT DECEMBER

CONFRONTATION, DE MOHR UNDERSTOOD CLEARLY THAT OUR OPPOSITION

TO LAURIOLA DID NOT STEM SOLELY FROM A USG PREFERENCE FOR THE

ROTATION OF MULTILATERAL ORGANIZATION LEADERSHIP. HIS

ELEVATION OF THAT PREFERENCE TO AN OSTENSIBLE WASSENNAR

PRINCIPLE AND INDICATION THAT ITALY MAY TROT IT OUT IN

COUNTERATTACK TO THE SWEDISH CANDIDACY CLEARLY SHOWS HIS

BITTERNESS. SINCE DE MOHR RUNS A ONE-OFFICER SHOP, WE DON\'T

YET KNOW THE EXTENT TO WHICH THE GOI SHARES HIS PIQUE.

ALTHOUGH DE MOHR FAVORS A DELIBERATE CALL FOR NOMINATIONS AND

A VOTE NEXT SPRING, A CLEAR INDICATION THAT NO OTHER

CANDIDATES, SLOVAK OR OTHERWISE, ARE IN THE OFFING MAY HELP

US CONVINCE THE GOI, AT A PAYGRADE ABOVE DE MOHR\'S, TO ALLOW

ACCELERATION OF THE ELECTION PROCESS.
