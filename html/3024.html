SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION ON MIDDLE EAST

Summary

-- Lead stories today, May 27, continue to focus on

developments in the Palestinian-Israeli conflict and

the U.S. involvement. Other stories highlight

President Bush\'s visit to Europe and his remarks

there.

Editorial Commentary

-- "Bush looks at region with one eye"

Center-left, influential Arabic daily Al-Dustour

(05/27) editorializes: "President George Bush wastes

no chances to launch harsh criticisms of the

Palestinian Authority and its leader Yaser Arafat. It

is as if the entire crisis of this region is revolving

around the one man, without even the drop of a hint of

Israel\'s responsibility and its occupation of the

Palestinian lands for the past 35 years. It seems

that President Bush, launching his criticisms of the

Authority every chance he gets, did not get the news

about Israel\'s recurrent invasions of Palestinians

towns and villages, nor the news about Israel\'s

killings of men, women and children, nor the news

about the Likud\'s decision to reject the Palestinian

State. It is double standards that the Bush

administration is following. It is the American-

Israeli war against the Palestinian people, their

leadership, their cause and their rights. It is the

ultimate arrogance and meddling in other people\'s

affairs."
