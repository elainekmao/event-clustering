SUBJECT: POPE GIVES HIS BLESSING TO EURO CURRENCY

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 02 VATICA 00024 021816Z

1. POPE JOHN PAUL II HAS "BLESSED" THE ARRIVAL OF THE EURO

CURRENCY CALLING IT AN HISTORIC STEP. IN A JANUARY 1 ADDRESS

IN VATICAN CITY, THE POPE SAID HE WISHED PEACE AND

PROSPERITY ON THE COUNTRIES OF THE EUROPEAN UNION. HE SAID,

"I HOPE THAT IT (THE EURO) PROMOTES THE COMPLETE DEVELOPMENT

OF THE CITIZENS OF THE VARIOUS COUNTRIES."

2. VATICAN CITY STATE HAS CHOSEN TO ADOPT THE EURO AS ITS

CURRENCY. A 1930 ACCORD WITH ITALY, CONFIRMED IN 1998 BY THE

EUROPEAN UNION\'S MONETARY COMMITTEE, ALLOWS IT TO COIN EUROS

BUT NOT TO PRINT BANK NOTES. THE ITALIAN TREASURY\'S MINT IS

CHARGED WITH PRODUCING THE EURO COINS WHICH, SIMILAR TO

OTHER EUROPEAN STATES WITH A MONARCHY, WILL BEAR THE IMAGE

OF POPE JOHN PAUL II AS SOVEREIGN HEAD OF VATICAN CITY

STATE. WHILE THE EURO BECAME LEGAL TENDER ON JANUARY 1 2002,

BECAUSE OF TECHNICAL PROBLEMS THE VATICAN EDITION OF THE

COINS WILL REPORTEDLY ONLY BECOME AVAILABLE IN FEBRUARY. IN

THE MEANTIME, COINS AND BANK NOTES OF OTHER EUROPEAN

COUNTRIES MAY BE USED IN VATICAN CITY.

3. COMMENT: THE INTRODUCTION OF THE EURO IS ACTIVELY

SUPPORTED BY THE VATICAN BUT FOR POLITICAL RATHER THAN

ECONOMIC REASONS. THE VATICAN\'S FINANCE MINISTER, CARDINAL

SERGIO SEBASTIANI HAS PREVIOUSLY SAID THAT THE EURO CAN BE

SEEN AS A KIND OF GUIDING STAR FOR EUROPE "SO THAT IT CAN

OVERCOME THE BARRIERS OF PAST DIVISIONS AND BECOME A

STIMULUS FOR GREATER UNITY AMONG THE NATIONS." THROUGHOUT

HIS PONTIFICATE POPE JOHN PAUL II HAS CONSISTENTLY PREACHED

HIS VISION OF A CHRISTIAN EUROPE STRETCHING FROM THE

ATLANTIC COAST TO THE URAL MOUNTAINS. HE IS LIKELY TO REGARD

THE INTRODUCTION OF EURO NOTES AND COINS AS ANOTHER
