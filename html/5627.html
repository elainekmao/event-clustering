SUBJECT: Request for a DVC Series on the Arts

REF: AMMAN 05768

1. Summary: Post would like to request IIP\'s support in

organizing a new series of DVCs on the Arts for FY 2003.

The series will be conducted in coordination with the newly

established College of Arts and Design at the University of

Jordan and will serve to educate and introduce the students

at the college to alternative approaches to music, visual

art and theater. The initiation of the college is a

milestone for the exchange of ideas and culture between

Jordan and the U.S. and will broaden Post\'s contact with

youth and the next generation of Jordanian artists and

musicians.

2. PAS recommends the DVC series consist of eight programs

to be covered in two months, a program every two weeks. The

university has already selected speakers for five of the

eight programs, the contact information of which is below.

Each DVC will last 75 minutes and will be conducted at the

University of Jordan.
