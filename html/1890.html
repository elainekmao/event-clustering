1. (C) SUMMARY. UNDP RESIDENT REPRESENTATIVE VICTOR

ANGELO MET WITH A LARGE SEGMENT OF THE DONOR COMMUNITY

ON AUGUST 20 AT THE FORMER\'S INVITATION TO DISCUSS

DEVELOPMENTS ON THE FOOD AID FRONT. THE GROUP FOCUSED

IN SEQUENCE ON THE DIMENSIONS OF THE CRISIS,

ESTABLISHMENT OF A HUMANITARIAN SECRETARIAT WITHIN UNDP,

AND POSSIBLE PROGRAM ACTIVITIES. ANGELO DESCRIBED HIGH-

LEVEL GOZ RESISTANCE TO REQUESTING HUMANITARIAN

ASSISTANCE DESPITE INCREASINGLY RELIABLE, BUT SO FAR

INCONSISTENT, PROJECTIONS THAT THERE WILL BE FOOD

SHORTAGES BY EARLY NEXT YEAR. HE EXPRESSED CONCERN THAT

CURRENT CONDITIONS ON COMMERCIAL FARMS COULD FURTHER

AGGRAVATE THE SITUATION. ANGELO SAID THAT HE PLANS TO

ESTABLISH IMMEDIATELY A RELIEF AND RECOVERY UNIT, WITH

OR WITHOUT GOZ APPROVAL THAT IN FACT WOULD BE A UN

PLANNING AND ASSESSMENT MECHANISM. ON THE PROGRAMMATIC

FRONT, MOST DONORS ARE RELUCTANT TO COMMIT SPECIFIC

RESOURCES UNTIL VIABLE DELIVERY MECHANISMS HAVE BEEN

IDENTIFIED AND A MORE QUANTITATIVE PICTURE OF THE GRAIN

SHORTAGE AND THE POTENTIAL FINANCIAL RESOURCES TO PAY

FOR IT HAVE BEEN EXPLORED. WHILE WE SHARE ANGELO\'S

VIEWS THAT A SHORTAGE IS NEARLY CERTAIN, WE HAVE MANY

RESERVATIONS AT THIS POINT ABOUT THE BEST WAY TO ADDRESS

IT. WE INTEND TO GET A BETTER HANDLE OF THESE ISSUES

WITH A SENSE OF URGENCY. END SUMMARY.

2. (C) ANGELO AND A TEAM OF UN OFFICIALS KICKED OFF THE

MEETING WITH DUTCH, GERMAN, DFID, SWEDE, NORWEGIAN,

JAPANESE, FRENCH, DANISH, AND U.S. REPRESENTATIVES BY

REVIEWING THE FOOD CRISIS. ANGELO CHARACTERIZED THE

SITUATION AS "EXTREMELY DIFFICULT" DUE TO WHAT HE

DESCRIBED AS THE GOZ POSITION. HE ASSERTED THAT

WORKING-LEVEL OFFICIALS IN RELEVANT LINE MINISTRIES

UNDERSTAND THERE IS A DIFFICULT "HUMANITARIAN" SITUATION

AHEAD AND HAVE AGREED IN PRINCIPLE ON A DOCUMENT AND A

DRAFT LETTER TO THE UN REQUESTING EXTERNAL ASSISTANCE.

HOWEVER, THEY HAVE NOT YET SUCCEEDED IN GETTING THE

GREEN LIGHT FROM THE MINISTERIAL LEVEL, WHICH THEY CLAIM

IS UNWILLING TO TALK OF HUMANITARIAN NEED FOR

"POLITICAL" REASONS.

3. (U) THE LATEST FIGURES FROM THE GRAIN MARKETING

BOARD (GMB) AND STRATEGIC GRAIN RESERVE (SGR) INDICATE

THAT SHORTAGES LIE AHEAD. ANGELO SAID THAT GMB HAS ONLY

87 METRIC TONS OF WHEAT ON HAND, WITH THE REMAINDER

(APPROXIMATELY 40,500 MT) IN PRIVATE HANDS. ANGELO

ESTIMATED THIS AS ABOUT SIX WEEKS OF SUPPLY. MAIZE IS

SIMILARLY PROBLEMATIC, SINCE BOTH SMALL HOLDERS AND

COMMERCIAL FARMERS ARE HOLDING BACK THEIR STOCKS FROM

THE MARKET. (THIS COULD BE A WHOLLY WISE BUSINESS

DECISION IN THE CURRENT ECONOMIC SITUATION OF RAMPANT

INFLATION WHERE COMMODITIES ARE WORTH MORE THAN

7. (C) ANGELO ADMITTED THAT THERE HAS BEEN NO PROGRESS

IN LAUNCHING A PROPOSED UNDP HUMANITARIAN SECRETARIAT,

ORIGINALLY EXPECTED TO COMMENCE OPERATIONS IN AUGUST,

BECAUSE THE GOZ HAS NOT GIVEN UNDP A GREEN LIGHT. (THE

SWEDES, DUTCH AND US HAVE AGREED TO PROVIDE FUNDS FOR

THIS INITIATIVE.) ANGELO SAID THAT HE MAY HAVE NO

OPTION BUT TO MOVE AHEAD AND ESTABLISH THIS

UNILATERALLY, BRINGING IT IN UNDER THE GOZ\'S RADAR USING

THE RUBRIC OF A "RELIEF AND RECOVERY UNIT". ABSENT GOZ

APPROVAL OR TERMS OF REFERENCE, HE WOULD BILL THIS AS AN

INTEGRAL PART OF UNDP PROGRAMMING.

8. (C) ANGELO SAID THAT THIS APPROACH (ALSO A TROJAN

HORSE) WOULD PERMIT EXPEDITIOUS PLANNING. NONETHELESS,

IT WOULD REQUIRE COSMETIC PROGRAMS -- CHILD SURVIVAL

NEED ASSESSMENT, ETC. -- BEHIND WHICH IT COULD BEGIN

ASSESSMENT AND PLANNING FOR FOOD DELIVERY MECHANISMS.

SEVERAL OF THE DONORS RIGHTLY BALKED AT THIS. THE DFID

REPRESENTATIVE OBSERVED THAT IT MIGHT BE EASIER TO DO A

NUMBER OF SMALL REGIONAL BILATERAL PROGRAMS UNDER THE

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 04 HARARE 02480 02 OF 03 221423Z

RADAR RATHER THAN A LARGER, MULTILATERAL EFFORT. THE

SWEDES AGREED, POINTING OUT THAT THE FOOD SHORTAGES

COULD PROVE TO CONFIRM INITIAL ASSUMPTIONS THAT THEY

WILL BE LOCALIZED. THE DFID TEAM ALSO REJECTED OUT OF

HAND RECENT GOZ SUGGESTIONS THAT A REGIONAL APPROACH BE

TAKEN, SINCE IN DFID\'S VIEW THIS WOULD PERMIT THE GOZ TO

SANITIZE GRANT COMMODITIES THROUGH AN UPRIGHT NEIGHBOR

SUCH AS BOTSWANA BEFORE LAYING HANDS ON THE CEREALS FOR

GOVERNMENT PROGRAMS.

9. (C) OTHER PARTICIPANTS UNDERLINED THAT THE LACK OF

WFP EXPERTISE ON THE GROUND COULD BE A SERIOUS

IMPEDIMENT TO AN INDEPENDENT AND ACCURATE ASSESSMENT.

ANGELO SAID THAT FOR THE MOMENT HE WAS TAPPING WFP

PERSONNEL FROM ZAMBIA AND MOZAMBIQUE TO BRIDGE THE GAP.

THERE WAS A FINAL CONSENSUS AMONG THE DONORS THAT NONE

OF THEM FAVOR A RELIEF AND RECOVERY UNIT THAT AT THIS

TIME GOES BEYOND AN ASSESSMENT, MONITORING AND

COORDINATION ROLE. WITH THE EXCEPTION OF THE SWEDES,

EVERYONE WANTED TO SEE WHAT FOOD DELIVERY MECHANISMS

COULD BE IDENTIFIED BEFORE COMMITTING RESOURCES.

10. (C) THERE WAS GENERAL AGREEMENT THAT A DOMESTIC

SHORTFALL OF CEREALS IS A CERTAINTY, BUT THERE WAS NO

CONCLUSIVE AGREEMENT ON HOW TO HANDLE THIS OR TO WHAT

THE EXTENT OF EXTERNAL NON-COMMERCIAL ASSISTANCE MIGHT

BE REQUIRED. FOR ITS PART, THE GOZ INPUT INTO THE MOST

RECENT SADC FOOD EARLY WARNING SYSTEM REPORT INDICATES

THAT THE GOZ PLANS TO PURCHASE COMMERCIALLY 544,000 MT

OF CEREAL AND RESTORE THE SGR TO A CARRYOVER SURPLUS OF

500,000 MT OF MAIZE AND 200,000 MT OF WHEAT. THE WFP IS

STILL IN THE WATCHING PHASE (REFTEL) AND THE UNDP HERE

FINDS ITSELF FLANKED BY A RECALCITRANT AND POLITICIZED

GOZ ON ONE SIDE, AND SKEPTICAL DONORS ON THE OTHER.

11. (C) GIVEN THE WELTER OF CONFLICTING FIGURES AND

APPROACHES, ADDITIONAL INFORMATION/PLANNING WILL BE

NECESSARY BEFORE A COHERENT BILATERAL OR MULTILATERAL

USG PROPOSAL CAN BE PLACED ON THE TABLE. THE MISSION

WILL CONSULT WIDELY AND PROVIDE INPUT AS URGENTLY AS

POSSIBLE. WE ARE ALSO CONCERNED THAT ZIMBABWE\'S

EVOLVING ECONOMIC INSTABILITY, DRIVEN BY A STEEPLY

DECLINING ZD AND UNREALISTIC EXCHANGE RATES, WILL HAVE A

HEAVY AND MEASURABLE IMPACT ON THE FUTURE FOOD SITUATION

AND THE GOZ\'S ABILITY TO PURCHASE GRAIN COMMERCIALLY,

BOTH DOGMATICALLY AND FOR IMPORTS. THUS FAR THE GOZ

DOES NOT SEEM TO BE TAKING THIS INTO ACCOUNT IN ITS

CALCULATIONS.

10. (C) THERE WAS GENERAL AGREEMENT THAT A DOMESTIC

SHORTFALL OF CEREALS IS A CERTAINTY, BUT THERE WAS NO

CONCLUSIVE AGREEMENT ON HOW TO HANDLE THIS OR TO WHAT

THE EXTENT OF EXTERNAL NON-COMMERCIAL ASSISTANCE MIGHT

BE REQUIRED. FOR ITS PART, THE GOZ INPUT INTO THE MOST

RECENT SADC FOOD EARLY WARNING SYSTEM REPORT INDICATES

THAT THE GOZ PLANS TO PURCHASE COMMERCIALLY 544,000 MT

OF CEREAL AND RESTORE THE SGR TO A CARRYOVER SURPLUS OF

500,000 MT OF MAIZE AND 200,000 MT OF WHEAT. THE WFP IS

STILL IN THE WATCHING PHASE (REFTEL) AND THE UNDP HERE

FINDS ITSELF FLANKED BY A RECALCITRANT AND POLITICIZED

GOZ ON ONE SIDE, AND SKEPTICAL DONORS ON THE OTHER.

11. (C) GIVEN THE WELTER OF CONFLICTING FIGURES AND

APPROACHES, ADDITIONAL INFORMATION/PLANNING WILL BE

NECESSARY BEFORE A COHERENT BILATERAL OR MULTILATERAL

USG PROPOSAL CAN BE PLACED ON THE TABLE. THE MISSION

WILL CONSULT WIDELY AND PROVIDE INPUT AS URGENTLY AS

POSSIBLE. WE ARE ALSO CONCERNED THAT ZIMBABWE\'S

EVOLVING ECONOMIC INSTABILITY, DRIVEN BY A STEEPLY

DECLINING ZD AND UNREALISTIC EXCHANGE RATES, WILL HAVE A

HEAVY AND MEASURABLE IMPACT ON THE FUTURE FOOD SITUATION

AND THE GOZ\'S ABILITY TO PURCHASE GRAIN COMMERCIALLY,

BOTH DOGMATICALLY AND FOR IMPORTS. THUS FAR THE GOZ

DOES NOT SEEM TO BE TAKING THIS INTO ACCOUNT IN ITS

CALCULATIONS.
