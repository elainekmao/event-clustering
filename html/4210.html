SUBJECT: HOLY SEE NOMINATES NEW VATICAN PERMANENT OBSERVER

TO THE U.N.: BIOGRAPHICAL INFORMATION ON CELESTINO MIGLIORE

REFS: A) VATICAN 4398, B) VATICAN 4388

1. (U) POPE JOHN PAUL II HAS APPOINTED UNDERSECRETARY FOR

RELATIONS WITH STATES (DEPUTY FOREIGN MINISTER) MONSIGNOR

UNCLASSIFIED

CELESTINO MIGLIORE TO BE THE HOLY SEE\'S PERMANENT OBSERVER

AT THE UNITED NATIONS IN NEW YORK. MIGLIORE REPLACES

ARCHBISHOP RENATO MARTINO WHO RETURNS TO ROME TO TAKE UP A

SENIOR CURIA POSITION. MIGLIORE WILL BE ORDAINED AS AN

ARCHBISHOP BEFORE TAKING UP HIS POSTING TO NEW YORK.

2. (U) CELESTINO MIGLIORE WAS BORN IN CUNEO, IN PIEDMONT

PROVINCE, IN THE NORTH OF ITALY IN 1950. AFTER HIS

ORDINATION AS A CATHOLIC PRIEST HE TOOK UP GRADUATE STUDIES

IN CANON LAW AND DIPLOMACY AND ENTERED THE HOLY SEE\'S

DIPLOMATIC SERVICE IN 1980. MIGLIORE SERVED AT HOLY SEE

NUNCIATURES IN ANGOLA, THE U.S., EGYPT, AND POLAND. IN 1992

HE WAS APPOINTED AS THE HOLY SEE\'S PERMANENT OBSERVER TO THE

COUNCIL OF EUROPE IN STRASBOURG. HE RETURNED TO ROME IN

1995 UPON HIS APPOINTMENT AS UNDERSECRETARY OF THE SECTION

FOR RELATIONS WITH STATES (AT THE VATICAN\'S SECRETARIAT OF

STARE. HE SPEAKS ITALIAN, ENGLISH, FRENCH, SPANISH AND

PORTUGUESE.

3. (SBU) MIGLIORE HAS BEEN ONE OF POST\'S MOST OPEN,

ARTICULATE, AND FRIENDLY INTERLOCUTORS. MORE SO THAN MANY

OTHER VATICAN OFFICIALS, HE HAS BEEN WILLING TO ACT TO

RESOLVE PROBLEMS OR ADVANCE POLICY, SOMETIMES DEFYING THE

HOLY SEE\'S TRADITIONAL CAUTION. HE WAS, FOR EXAMPLE,

INSTANTLY AND POSITIVELY DISPOSED TO WORKING WITH POST AND

OTHER DIPLOMATIC MISSIONS TO ORGANIZE AND HOST AN

INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON TRAFFICKING IN PERSONS WHEN

OTHERS IN THE HOLY SEE WERE MORE HESITANT. THIS "CAN DO"

ATTITUDE SHOULD STAND HIM WELL IN HIS UPCOMING

RESPONSIBILITIES IN THE U.N. THIS IS NOT TO SAY, HOWEVER,

THAT MIGLIORE THINKS LIKE AN AMERICAN. HE IS ITALIAN, AND

SEES THE WORLD THROUGH A EUROPEAN FILTER, WHICH HAS LED HIM

AT TIMES TO BE CRITICAL OF OR SKEPTICAL TOWARD ASPECTS OF

U.S. POLICIES. FOR EXAMPLE, IN AN EARLY OCTOBER 2001

DISCUSSION WITH POLOFF ABOUT THE U.S. RESPONSE TO THE

ATTACKS OF SEPTEMBER 11, MIGLIORE POINTEDLY NOTED THAT

"AFTER ALL, BIN LADEN IS A U.S. CREATION" (NOTE: REFERRING

TO U.S. ASSISTANCE TO ISLAMIC MUJAHADIN IN AFGHANISTAN IN

THE 1980\'S.) LIKEWISE, IN DISCUSSIONS ABOUT BIOTECH FOODS,

HE HAS ASSERTED THE VIEW THAT U.S. POLICY IN ZAMBIA IS

DRIVEN BY ECONOMIC, RATHER THAN HUMANITARIAN, INTERESTS.

4. (SBU) MIGLIORE\'S VIEWS ON IRAQ AND THE MIDDLE EAST ARE

REPORTED IN DETAIL IN REFTELS. MIGLIORE HAS MADE CLEAR THAT

HE IS "NO FAN OF IRAQ," BUT DOES NOT YET BELIEVE THE U.S.

HAS YET MADE THE CASE FOR PREEMPTIVE MILITARY ACTION.

5. (SBU) MIGLIORE, AS DEPUTY FOREIGN MINISTER EQUIVALENT AT

THE VATICAN, NECESSARILY COVERED A GLOBAL RANGE OF FOREIGN

POLICY ISSUES. WHETHER BY CHOICE OR CIRCUMSTANCE, IN THE

LAST YEAR HE HAS TAKEN A PARTICULAR INTEREST IN A FEW AREAS:

THE HUMANITARIAN CRISIS IN NORTH KOREA (HE HAS TRAVELED

THERE FIVE TIMES IN RECENT YEARS), THE CHURCH\'S POSITION IN

RUSSIA WHERE HE RECENTLY TRAVELED TO DISCUSS GOR\'S REFUSAL

OF VISAS TO CATHOLIC CLERGY, AND THE CHURCH\'S DIFFICULT

RELATIONS WITH VIETNAM. ALL IN ALL, MIGLIORE CAN BE COUNTED

ON TO BE AN OPEN, SOMETIMES UNVARNISHED, BUT ALSO CAREFUL

AND DELIBERATE INTERLOCUTOR AT THE U.N. HE CERTAINLY ENJOYS

THE CONFIDENCE OF FOREIGN MINISTER TAURAN AND THE POPE

HIMSELF.

6. (U) ARCHBISHOP-DESIGNATE MIGLIORE WILL STEP INTO THE

SHOES OF THE ABLE RENATO MARTINO, THE VATICAN\'S

REPRESENTATIVE AT THE U.N. FOR THE LAST 16 YEARS. IT WAS

MARTINO\'S LEADERSHIP THAT STEERED THE HOLY SEE DELEGATIONS

TO SELF-PROCLAIMED SUCCESS AT U.N. CONFERENCES IN RIO DE

JANEIRO IN 1992, CAIRO IN 1994, BEIJING IN 1995 AND THE MORE

RECENT 2002 CONFERENCE ON SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT IN

JOHANNESBURG, SOUTH AFRICA. MARTINO\'S 40 YEARS IN THE

SERVICE OF VATICAN DIPLOMACY, IN PLACES AS FAR APART AS

SINGAPORE AND LEBANON, PROVIDED HIM WITH A UNIQUE

PREPARATION FOR HIS TIME AT THE U.N. MARTINO WAS CONSTANTLY

IN THE NEWS, BOTH SECULAR AND ECCLESIASTICAL. HE ALSO SPENT

A GREAT DEAL OF TIME PROMOTING THE HOLY SEE\'S DIPLOMATIC

INITIATIVES AT THE U.N. DURING VISITS ABROAD. IN FACT,

ARCHBISHOP MARTINO\'S EFFORTS HAVE TURNED THE PERMANENT

OBSERVER POST INTO A KEY VENUE FOR THE HOLY SEE TO PROJECT

ITS MESSAGE. MIGLIORE INHERITS THE PRESTIGE, BUT ALSO THE

RESPONSIBILITY OF THE POST. THE FACT THAT POPE JOHN PAUL

CHOSE MIGLIORE TO BE THE HOLY SEE\' PERMANENT OBSERVER AT THE

U.N. SHOWS THE HOLY SEE WANTS ITS BEST DIPLOMATIC HAND

PLAYED AT THIS KEY INTERNATIONAL TABLE.
