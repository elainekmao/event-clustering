SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION EU TRAVEL RESTRICTIONS; HARARE

1. Stung by the recent detention and deportation of

former Bulawayo mayor Joshua Malinga at Gatwick Airport

(London) and Sweden\'s refusal of visas to four ZANU-PF

women legislators, an editorial in the August 5 edition of

the government-controlled daily "The Herald" calls on the

government of Zimbabwe to hit back. Under headline "Zim

should take retaliatory steps" the paper comments:

2. ". . .It is now time for Zimbabwe to consider

implementing the eye for an eye rule. . .The

international community should not cry foul when

government puts in place measures that will in turn

prohibit the movement of some Westerners to

Zimbabwe. . .The government should now take

retaliatory steps, which should include the

withdrawal of passports from political

opponents. . . ."
