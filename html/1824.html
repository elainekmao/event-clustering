RATIFICATION OF COMPREHENSIVE NUCLEAR TEST BAN TREATY

--------------------------------------------- --------

2. ARCHBISHOP RENATO MARTINO, PERMANENT OBSERVER OF THE

HOLY SEE TO THE UNITED NATIONS, HAS DELIVERED THE HOLY SEE\'S

INSTRUMENT OF RATIFICATION OF THE COMPREHENSIVE NUCLEAR TEST

BAN TREATY TO THE UN HEADQUARTERS IN NEW YORK. THE TREATY

WAS ADOPTED BY THE UNGA ON SEPTEMBER 10, 1996, AND SIGNED BY

THE HOLY SEE ON SEPTEMBER 24 OF THE SAME YEAR. ARCHBISHOP

MARTINO EXPRESSED THE VATICAN\'S FIRM SUPPORT FOR ENDING

ILLICIT TRAFFIC IN SMALL ARMS, THE CONVENTION FOR THE TOTAL

ELIMINATION OF LAND MINES, THE CHEMICAL WEAPONS CONVENTION

AND THE BIOLOGICAL WEAPONS TREATY. WHILE THE HOLY SEE HAS

PERMANENT OBSERVER STATUS IN THE UNITED NATIONS, IT HAS FULL

(VOTING) MEMBERSHIP IN THE INTERNATIONAL ATOMIC ENERGY

COMMISSION.

VATICAN SAYS WHITE HOUSE PUT "SPIN" ON STEM CELL REMARKS

--------------------------------------------- -----------

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 03 VATICA 03976 01 OF 02 282136Z

3. VATICAN SPOKESMAN, JOAQUIN NAVARRO-VALLS HAS ACCUSED

UNNAMED WHITE HOUSE OFFICIALS OF TRYING TO PUT "A SPIN" ON

THE POPE\'S WORDS TO PRESIDENT BUSH. WHEN POPE JOHN PAUL II

MET THE PRESIDENT JULY 23 HE CONDEMNED THE "CREATION FOR

RESEARCH PURPOSES OF HUMAN EMBRYOS." NAVARRO-VALLS SAID JULY

25 THAT THE POPE WAS NOT OPENING THE POSSIBILITY OF AN

ETHICAL USE AND DESTRUCTION OF EMBRYOS CREATED FOR OTHER

PURPOSES. BUSH ADMINISTRATION OFFICIALS WERE CITED AS SAYING

THE POPE\'S WORDS, WHILE CLEARLY REFERRING TO EMBRYOS CREATED

FOR RESEARCH, MIGHT NOT COVER OTHER EMBRYOS, SUCH AS THOSE

CREATED FOR IN VITRO FERTILIZATION BUT NOT IMPLANTED IN A

UTERUS. NAVARRO-VALLS STATED THAT THE DELIBERATE DESTRUCTION

OF A HUMAN EMBRYO - NO MATTER HOW OR WHY IT WAS CREATED - IS

MORALLY UNACCEPTABLE, EVEN IF THE AIM IS TO HELP OTHERS.

(NOTE. WHEN A VATICAN-BASED JOURNALIST ACCUSED NAVARRO-VALLS

OF PUTTING HIS OWN "SPIN" ON THE POPE\'S SPEECH, HE RETORTED,

"NO, IT IS NOT SPIN, IT IS THE TRUTH." END NOTE).

VATICAN NEWS AGENCY: OLYMPICS OPPORTUNITY TO ENGAGE CHINA

--------------------------------------------- ------------

4. THE DIRECTOR OF VATICAN MISSIONARY NEWS AGENCY "FIDES,"

FATHER BERNARDO CERVELLERA, SUPPORTS BEIJING HOSTING THE

2008 OLYMPIC GAMES. IN A JULY 13 STATEMENT, CERVELLERA SAID

REFUSING TO GIVE CHINA THE GAMES WOULD HAVE BEEN

UNPRODUCTIVE AND HYPOCRITICAL. HE POINTED OUT THAT AUSTRALIA

HAD BEEN AWARDED THE 2000 OLYMPICS DESPITE A LONG HISTORY OF

HUMAN RIGHTS VIOLATIONS AGAINST ITS ABORIGINAL POPULATION.

CERVELLERA SAID THE CHINESE AUTHORITIES WOULD PROBABLY USE

THE GAMES "AS A SORT OF DRUG TO SOFTEN THE HARDSHIPS OF THE

UNCLASSIFIED

COMING YEARS" AS IT FACES ECONOMIC DIFFICULTIES. HOWEVER,

ACCORDING TO CERVELLERA, A TIME OF HARDSHIP AND STRUGGLE IS

NOT WHEN A NATION SHOULD BE IGNORED - RATHER IT IS A TIME TO

ENGAGE IT. "THE ALTERNATIVE IS ISOLATION, WHICH WOULD ALLOW

THE REGIME TO COMPLETELY SUFFOCATE ALL DISSIDENCE," HE SAID.

THE PRIEST ADDED THAT WHILE CHINA WAS LIKELY TO INCREASE

SECURITY MEASURES DURING THE GAMES, THERE IS NO WAY IT CAN

PREVENT INTERACTION BETWEEN CHINESE CITIZENS AND THE TENS OF

THOUSANDS OF ATHLETES AND TOURISTS WHO WILL TRAVEL TO

BEIJING FOR THE OLYMPICS.

POPE TO VISIT ARMENIA AND KAZAKHSTAN IN SEPTEMBER

--------------------------------------------- ---

6. THE VATICAN PRESS OFFICE HAS CONFIRMED POPE JOHN PAUL

II\'S SEPTEMBER 22-27 VISIT TO ARMENIA AND KAZAKHSTAN. THE

LEADER OF THE ARMENIAN APOSTOLIC CHURCH, CATHOLICOS KAREKIN

II OF ETCHMIADZIN, INVITED THE POPE TO JOIN IN CELEBRATIONS

IN HONOR OF THE 1,700TH ANNIVERSARY OF CHRISTIANITY IN THE

NATION. THE VISIT WILL INCLUDE CEREMONIES WITH MINORITY

CATHOLIC COMMUNITIES AS WELL AS IMPORTANT ECUMENICAL EVENTS.

(NOTE. THE VATICAN HAS A WARM RELATIONSHIP WITH THE ARMENIAN

APOSTOLIC CHURCH (ONE OF SIX INDEPENDENT ORIENTAL ORTHODOX

CHURCHES) AND SEVERAL DOCTRINAL DIFFERENCES HAVE ALREADY

BEEN RESOLVED. MANY ECUMENISTS REGARD THE DIALOGUE BETWEEN

THE VATICAN AND THE ARMENIAN APOSTOLIC CHURCH AS A MODEL FOR

NEGOTIATIONS WITH OTHER ORTHODOX CHURCHES. MUCH OF THE

SUCCESS IS ATTRIBUTABLE TO THE CLOSE FRIENDSHIP BETWEEN POPE

JOHN PAUL II AND THE LATE CATHOLICOS, KAREKIN I. END NOTE).

ITALY WILL NOT EXTRADITE RWANDAN PRIEST: VATICAN PRESSURE?

--------------------------------------------- -------------

7. THE ITALIAN GOVERNMENT WILL NOT EXTRADITE A RWANDAN

CATHOLIC PRIEST WANTED ON GENOCIDE CHARGES BY THE

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 03 VATICA 03976 02 OF 02 282137Z

INTERNATIONAL WAR CRIMES TRIBUNAL. FATHER ATHANASE SEROMBA,

ACCUSED OF ORDERING HIS CHURCH TO BE BULLDOZED, CRUSHING AND

KILLING UP TO 2500 PARISHIONERS, HAS BEEN LIVING IN FLORENCE

UNDER THE NAME ANASTASIO SUMBA BURA. ALTHOUGH ITALIAN

AUTHORITIES SAY THAT AN AD HOC DECREE IS REQUIRED FOR

COOPERATION WITH THE ICTR, SOURCES OF "THE SUNDAY TIMES"

CITE DISCREET PRESSURE FROM THE VATICAN AS THE REAL REASON

FOR THE DELAY. A STATEMENT FROM THE ITALIAN BISHOPS

CONFERENCE SAID SEROMBA SHOULD BE GIVEN THE BENEFIT OF THE

DOUBT, AND THAT EVENTS IN RWANDA DURING THE GENOCIDE ERA

WERE SHROUDED IN CONFUSION. (NOTE: THE VATICAN BELIEVES AN

ANTI-CATHOLIC PREJUDICE LIES BEHIND EFFORTS TO PROSECUTE

PRIESTS AND NUNS FOR ALLEGED GENOCIDE CRIMES. END NOTE).
