SUBJECT: MFA REQUESTS BILAT BETWEEN SECRETARY POWELL AND FM

GUL AT WORLD ECONOMIC FORUM MEETING

1. (U) Classified by Deputy Chief of Mission Robert Deutsch.

Reasons 1.5 (b) and (d).

2. (C) Embassy Ankara understands that Secretary Powell will

attend the June 21-23 World Economic Forum meeting in Jordan.

We received a call on June 16 from the acting DDG for the

Americas at the MFA, who requested a bilat for FM Gul with

Secretary Powell, schedule permitting, at the meeting in

SIPDIS

Jordan.

3. (C) If the Secretary\'s schedule permits, we recommend a

bilat with FM Gul. This is a good opportunity for the two to

discuss Iraq and Middle East policy, other essential policy

areas, and U.S.-Turkey cooperation on these issues.
