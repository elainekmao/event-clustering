SUBJECT: PRINCE HASSAN TRAVELING TO US OCTOBER 3-8

Classified By: CDA GREGORY L. BERRY FOR REASONS 1.5 (B) AND (D)

1. (C) The office of Prince Hassan Bin Talal has notified us

that Prince Hassan will be traveling to the U.S. October 3-8.

The Prince will arrive in New York from Jordan on a private

or GOJ aircraft on October 3 to participate in a meeting of

the International Crisis Group. The Prince and his party will

reside at the Waldorf Astoria Hotel. On the morning of

October 7, the Prince will travel to Washington D.C. to attend

a meeting of the Nuclear Threat Initiative. He and his party

will reside at the Four Seasons Hotel. They will be departing

Washington D.C. on October 8 for Canada.

2. (C) Prince Hassan has requested assistance in customs and

immigration formalities on arrival and departure, as well as a

security detail for his time in New York and Washington D.C.

Post will forward additional information on the Prince\'s

itinerary and flight and aircraft information as it becomes

available.
