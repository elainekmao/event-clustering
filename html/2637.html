SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION U. S. IMPLICATED IN PLAN TO

OUST MUGABE; HARARE

1. THE LEAD STORY IN THE MARCH 16 EDITION OF THE

BULAWAYO-BASED GOVERNMENT-CONTROLLED DAILY "CHRONICLE"

ASSERTS THAT THE UNITED STATES SUPPORTS A "SECRET"

PLAN TO OUST PRESIDENT ROBERT MUGABE. THE ARTICLE,

HEADLINED "MASS STAYAWAY PLANNED: BRITAIN AND U.S.

PLAN TO OUST PRESIDENT MUGABE," ACCUSES THE U.S. AND

BRITAIN OF "PLANNING AND DIRECTING" WORK STOPPAGES AS

"AN EXCUSE" TO LAUNCH "A MILITARY INTERVENTION AGAINST

ZIMBABWE." EXCERPTS OF ARTICLE, AUTHORED BY "SPECIAL

PROJECTS EDITOR" INNOCENT MADONKO, FOLLOW:

2. "LACK OF STRUCTURE HAS FORCED THE OPPOSITION

MOVEMENT FOR DEMOCRATIC CHANGE (MDC) PARTY TO ENLIST

SERVICES OF THE HEAVILY WESTERN-FUNDED NATIONAL

CONSTITUTIONAL ASSEMBLY (NCA) AND THE ZIMBABWE

CONGRESS OF TRADE UNIONS (ZCTU) TO MOBILIZE WORKERS

FOR MASS ACTION FOLLOWING DEFEAT OF ITS LEADER, MR.

MORGAN TSVANGIRAI BY PRESIDENT MUGABE, \'CHRONICLE\'

LEARNT THIS WEEK. THE MASS ACTIONS, BEING PLANNED AND

DIRECTED BY THE U.S. AND BRITISH GOVERNMENTS WILL BE

USED AS \'AN EXCUSE\' TO LAUNCH A MILITARY INTERVENTION

AGAINST ZIMBABWE. THE TERMS \'CIVIC SOCIETY\' AND

\'LABOR\' ARE BEING BANDIED ABOUT AS THE WEST

ACCELERATES PLANS TO OUST THE LEGITIMATELY ELECTED

(MR.) MUGABE. . . MUGABE CREAMED MR. TSVANGIRAI IN

THE JUST-ENDED ELECTION ON 9 AND 10 MARCH. MOST

OBSERVERS EXCEPT NORWAY HAVE DECLARED THE POLLS FREE

AND FAIR. THE U.S. HAS CONDEMNED THE POLLS AS

\'FLAWED\' SETTING THE STAGE FOR THE PLANNED MILITARY

INTERVENTION. BUT THE BIGGEST HITCH OF THE PLAN IS

THE FACT THAT MDC HAS NO GRASSROOTS STRUCTURES HENCE
