1. (C) WITH FM TAURAN OUT OF TOWN, CHARGE DELIVERED REF A

POINTS ON THE PRESIDENT\'S UNGA ADDRESS TO VATICAN DFM

CELESTINO MILGIORE, EMPHASIZING THE PRESIDENT\'S FOCUS ON THE

UN\'S ROLE IN ADVANCING HUMAN DIGNITY, AND THE CHALLENGE TO

THAT DIGNITY AND TO THE UN POSED BY SADDAM\'S DECADE OF

DEFIANCE OF UN RESOLUTIONS. MIGLIORE WELCOMED THE

PRESIDENT\'S DECISION TO WORK WITH THE UN AS WE GO FORWARD.

HE EXPRESSED SOME DISCOMFORT WITH THE SPEECH\'S IMPLICATION

THAT THE U.S. WOULD ACT WITH OR WITHOUT THE UN. NOTING THAT

FOREIGN MINISTER TAURAN HAD JUST OUTLINED THE HOLY SEE\'S

POSITION ON CONFLICT IN IRAQ IN A SEPTEMBER 11 INTERVIEW (REF

B), AND THAT TAURAN WOULD BE CALLING IN THE DIPLOMATIC CORPS

SEPTEMBER 16 TO OUTLINE FURTHER THE HOLY SEE\'S VIEWS,

MIGLIORE DID NOT PROVIDE A MORE DETAILED REACTION.

2. (C) MIGLIORE DID SAY THAT A RECENT ARTICLE BY UK CARDINAL

CORMAC MURPHY-O\'CONNOR (WHICH CAN BE READ AT:

HTTP://WWW.CATHOLIC-EW.ORG.UK) WAS BEING WIDELY READ WITHIN

THE VATICAN, AND CONSIDERED AS A "BALANCED AND WISE"

PERSPECTIVE. MIGLIORE SAID THE HOLY SEE SHARES

MURPHY-O\'CONNOR\'S VIEW THAT MILITARY ACTION WOULD BE

DIFFICULT TO JUSTIFY IN THE ABSENCE OF MORE CONVINCING

ANSWERS TO QUESTIONS INCLUDING:

-- WHETHER THE PURPOSE OF MILITARY ACTION IS TO NEUTRALIZE A

THREAT OR EFFECT A CHANGE IN REGIME;

-- WHETHER MILITARY ACTION WILL STABILIZE OR DESTABILIZE THE

REGION;

-- WHETHER IT WILL DELAY OR ADVANCE THE MIDDLE EAST PEACE

PROCESS;

-- WHETHER THE UN SECURITY COUNCIL AND EU WILL ENDORSE IT;

AND

-- THE IMPLICATIONS FOR THE BROADER STRUCTURE OF

INTERNATIONAL LAW.

IN ADDITION, MIGLIORE ASSERTED THAT THERE NEEDED TO BE MORE

THINKING ABOUT A POST-SADDAM "END-STATE." (POST HAS

PREVIOUSLY PROVIDED MIGLIORE WITH BACKGROUND ON OUR EFFORTS

WITH IRAQI OPPOSITION GROUPS.) HE QUERIED WHAT THE

INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY COULD DO TO ENSURE THAT A CONFLICT

WOULD NOT RESULT IN EVER-GREATER SUFFERING.

3. (C) CHARGE PROVIDED MIGLIORE WITH A COPY OF THE

BACKGROUND PAPER, "A DECADE OF DECEPTION AND DEFIANCE,"

STRESSING THAT IT LAYS OUT A DETAILED, IMPARTIAL, AND FACTUAL

CASE ON THE NATURE OF IRAQI DEFIANCE OF THE UNITED NATIONS

AND ITS THREATS TO ITS PEOPLE, THE REGION AND THE WORLD. HE

URGED THE HOLY SEE TO REVIEW THIS IN DETAIL AS IT FURTHER

EVALUATES THE PRESIDENT\'S SPEECH AND THE WAY FORWARD ON IRAQ.

4. (C) RESPONDING TO CHARGE\'S COMMENT THAT THERE HAS BEEN

MUCH PUBLIC DISCUSSION RECENTLY IN VATICAN CIRCLES OF THE

TENETS OF JUST WAR, MIGLIORE SAID THE HOLY SEE IS MOVING AWAY

FROM REFERRING TO "JUST WAR" IN FAVOR OF DISCUSSING "CRITERIA

FOR THE ETHICAL PROSECUTION OF CONFLICTS AMONG STATES."

5. COMMENT: MIGLIORE REITERATED HIS VIEW (REF C) THAT THE

HOLY SEE RECOGNIZES THE PROBLEMS POSED BY SADDAM, AND HE WAS

CLEARLY GRATIFIED THAT THE U.S. HAD DECIDED TO ENGAGE THROUGH

THE UNITED NATIONS. THE DEBATE WITHIN THE HOLY SEE NOW IS
