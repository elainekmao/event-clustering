SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED TO JORDAN ON PROPOSED

AMENDMENTS TO 1988 SUA CONVENTION AND PROTOCOL

REF: A. A. SECSTATE 242866

B. B. WILCOX-SILLIMAN EMAIL 11/28/02

1. (U) On December 1, PolCouns and PolOff delivered message

in ref A regarding the Suppression of Unlawful Acts (SUA)

convention and its protocol to Samer Naber, Legal Adviser to

the Foreign Ministry. PolCouns also passed the full text of

proposed USG changes to the SUA per ref B.

2. (SBU) Naber noted that he has researched conventions and

treaties to which the GOJ is a party and does not know why

the GOJ signed, but has yet to ratify, the SUA. "I see no

reason why we shouldn\'t ratify it," he said. Regarding the

proposed USG amendments to the SUA, Naber said both he and

the Ministry of Transport will study and "positively consider

them."

3. (U) We plan to follow up with Naber on this matter.
