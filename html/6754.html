SUBJECT: UNESCO: Mission conveys points on US reentry;

Maldivian government welcomes decision

Refs: (A) State 75379

- (B) Colombo 393

- (C) State 54900

1. Per Ref B, Mission conveyed the points on the United

States\' decision to seek reentry into UNESCO and our

candidacy for the executive board to the Sri Lankan and

Maldivian governments. The Maldivian government has

replied that it welcomes the decision by the U.S. to

rejoin UNESCO and looks forward to working closely with

us matters of mutual interest. Mission has had no

response from the GSL as of yet on this matter.

2. Minimize considered.
