SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION: IRAQ ESCALATION IN THE TERRITORIES

1. SUMMARY: ESCALATION OF TENSIONS IN IRAQ AND THE WEST BANK

AND GAZA AND DEVELOPMENTS IN IRAQ CONTINUED TO GENERATE PROMINENT

COVERAGE IN PRINT AND ELECTRONIC MEDIA. AN "AL KHALEEJ"

EDITORIAL NOTES THAT THE U.S. OCCUPATION OF IRAQ AND THE ISRAELI

OCCUPATION OF PALESTINE ARE SIMILAR IN STYLE AND OBJECTIVES. AN

"AL ITTIHAD" EDITORIAL COMMENTS THAT THE ESCALATION OF THE

SITUATION IN IRAQ IS DUE TO THE DELAY IN HANDING OVER IRAQ TO ITS

OWN PEOPLE. END SUMMARY.

2. UNDER THE HEADLINE "LIBERATION TURNS INTO OCCUPATION,"

SHARJAH-BASED PAN-ARAB DAILY EDITORIALIZED 6/18:

"...THE GAME HAS BEGUN TO REVEAL ITS REALITY, AND THE IRAQIS HAVE

STARTED TO REALIZE THAT THOSE WHO CAME TO LIBERATE THEM, HAVE

ACTUALLY COME TO ACHIEVE THEIR OWN AMBITIONS AND SCHEMES USING

MEANS THAT ARE NO LESS UGLY AND HARSH THAN WHAT THEY FACED DURING

THE YEARS OF TYRANNY. THE U.S. TROOPS HAVE BEGUN TO SEEK THEIR

OWN SECURITY AND THAT OF THE IRAQIS, AND THE AMERICAN GOVERNOR IS

NOT INTERESTED IN HOW TO SECURE A GOOD LIFE FOR THE IRAQIS BUT IN

HOW TO DIVIDE THE PROFITS FOR AMERICAN AND WESTERN COMPANIES..."

3. COMMENTING ON THE SIMILARITIES OF THE U.S. OCCUPATION OF IRAQ

TO THE ISRAELI OCCUPATION OF PALESTINE, "AL KHALEEJ"

EDITORIALIZED 6/15:

"THERE IS A GREAT DEAL OF SIMILARITY BETWEEN THE AMERICAN

OCCUPATION OF IRAQ AND THE ISRAELI OCCUPATION OF PALESTINE IN

STYLE AS WELL AS IN OBJECTIVES... IN PALESTINE AS WELL AS IN

IRAQ, OCCUPATION IMPOSES ITSELF, AND THE PEOPLE\'S WILL IS TRYING

TO EMPHASIZE ITS RIGHT TO RESISTANCE TO GET THEIR LIBERTY BACK.

IF BOTH OCCUPATIONS COMPLETE ONE ANOTHER AND ARE CONNECTED IN

GOALS AND STRATEGIES, THEN THE RESISTANCE OF BOTH PEOPLE IS

UNITED IN OBJECTIVES AS WELL."

4. NOTING THE CONTINUOUS ESCALATION OF THE TENSION IN THE

SECURITY SITUATION IN IRAQ RECENTLY, ABU DHABI-BASED SEMI-

GOVERNMENT "AL ITTIHAD" EDITORIALIZED 6/15:

"... THE CONTINUOUS ESCALATION, AS AN INDICATOR OF MILITARY AND

SECURITY TENSION IN IRAQ DURING THE LAST FEW DAYS, WOULD NOT HAVE

TAKEN PLACE...HAD THE COALITION TROOPS HANDED THE AUTHORITY OVER

TO THE IRAQIS TO RULE THEMSELVES. THE OBSTRUCTION OF THE

POLITICAL PROCESS IN IRAQ SIGNALS MORE TROUBLES FOR THE COALITION

TROOPS AND MORE SUFFERING FOR THE IRAQIS UNLESS THE COALITION

HAND OVER THE PROCESS TO THE IRAQIS AS EARLY AS POSSIBLE. THERE

IS NO DOUBT THAT THE BROTHERS AND FRIENDS OF THE IRAQI PEOPLE

WILL REDOUBLE THEIR EFFORTS TO HELP THE IRAQI PEOPLE OVERCOME THE

POST-WAR CRISIS FOLLOWING THEIR SUCCESS IN OVERCOMING THE

OBSTACLE OF THE PREVIOUS REGIME, WHICH WAS \'THE MOTHER OF ALL

OBSTACLES\'."

5. UNDER THE HEADLINE "SHARON\'S WAR AGAINST PEACE," DUBAI-BASED

BUSINESS-ORIENTED ARABIC DAILY "AL BAYAN" EDITORIALIZED 6/14:

"... THE BEGINNING OF SHARON\'S COMPREHENSIVE TERRORIST WAR

AGAINST THE PALESTINIAN PEOPLE CAME TO ABORT ANY HOPE FOR

PEACEFUL NEGOTIATIONS. IN FACT, THE WAR ASSASSINATED THE HOPE OF

PEACE BY PUSHING THE PALESTINIANS TOWARDS A HUGE WALL OF DESPAIR

AND DISAPPOINTMENT... IF THE ARABS DO NOT MOVE TO SAVE THE

PALESTINIAN PEOPLE, THEN THEIR TURN WILL COME SOON, ACCORDING TO

A PUBLIC AGENDA THAT AIMS TO MAKE ISRAEL THE ONLY DOMINANT

REGIONAL PLAYER."
