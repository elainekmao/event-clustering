SUBJECT: CHINESE ASYLUM-SEEKERS IN FRENCH EMBASSY IN HANOI

REF: BEIJING 11645

1. (U) CLASSIFIED BY DCM DOUGLAS G. HARTER, REASON

1.5(B,D) .

2. (U) THIS IS AN ACTION CABLE. SEE PARAS 8-11.

3. (S) LATE AFTERNOON NOVEMBER 24 FRENCH AMBASSADOR

INFORMED U.S. AMBASSADOR THAT TWO CHINESE NATIONALS,

SUN WEIBANG AND JIANG JINGSHI, WERE UNDER PROTECTIVE

CUSTODY AT THE FRENCH EMBASSY. ACCORDING TO THE

FRENCH, THE ULTIMATE INTENTION OF THE TWO INDIVIDUALS

IS TO SEEK POLITICAL ASYLUM IN THE U.S.. SUN TOLD THE

FRENCH HE IS A DISSIDENT WHO WAS ARRESTED DURING THE

TIANANMEN SQUARE TURMOIL IN 1989 AND WAS IMPRISONED

FOR 10 YEARS. THE OTHER MAN WAS IDENTIFIED ONLY AS

SUN\'S TRAVELLING COMPANION.

4. (S) THE FRENCH EMBASSY CONDUCTED INITIAL

INTERVIEWS WITH SUN AND JIANG NOV 24 AND 25 AND PASSED

INFORMATION LOCALLY TO U.S. EMBASSY. SUN REPORTEDLY

TOLD THE FRENCH HE WAS "APPROVED" FOR ASYLUM IN THE

U.S. WITHIN THE PAST FEW WEEKS, BUT WHEN HE WENT TO

THE U.S. EMBASSY IN BEIJING TO RECEIVE THE NECESSARY

AUTHORIZATIONS AND DOCUMENTATION FOR ENTRY INTO THE

U.S., CHINESE SECURITY OFFICIALS REMOVED HIM FROM THE

WAITING LINE (PRESUMABLY THE CONSULAR WAITING LINE)

AND ESCORTED HIM AWAY. AUTHORITIES TOLD SUN HE WOULD

NOT BE ALLOWED TO EXIT CHINA TO TRAVEL TO THE U.S.. A

FEW DAYS LATER, SUN TRAVELLED BY TRAIN TO THE SINO-

VIETNAMESE BORDER AND THERE JOINEO A CHINESE TOUR

GROUP, ALLOWING HIM TO OBTAIN A BORDER PASS VALIO FOR

FOUR DAYS -- WHICH EXPIRED NOV 26. SUN SAIO THAT

JIANG IS SIMPLY AN ACQUAINTANCE WHO TRAVELLED WITH

HIM. NEITHER PERSON IS IN POSSESSION OF A PRC

PASSPORT. (NOTE: IT IS SIMPLE ANO FAIRLY COMMON FOR

CHINESE TO CROSS INTO VIETNAM ON LIMITED BOROER PASSES

FOR TOURISM OR TRAOE. WHILE SUN\'S ACCOUNT IS PLAUSIBLE

ON THIS SCORE, HIS ASSOCIATION WITH JIANG -- ANO

JIANG\'S OSTENSIBLE BASIS FOR AN ASYLUM REQUEST -- IS

MURKIER. END NOTE)

5. (S) SUN REQUESTEO PERMISSION FROM THE FRENCH

EMBASSY TO TRAVEL TO FRANCE TO ATTEND A LITERARY

CONFERENCE WHICH IS APPARENTLY SCHEDULED IN PARIS.

FROM THERE, SUN AND JIANG PLANNED TO APPROACH THE U.S.

EMBASSY IN PARIS WITH A REQUEST FOR POLITTCAL ASYLUM.

SUN ALSO CLAIMED THAT A COLLEAGUE, WHOSE NAME THE

FRENCH RENDERED IN WRITING AS LI YUCON (POSSIBLE

ALTERNATES LI YUCUN OR LI YUKAN), WAS CURRENTLY

RESIDING IN THE U.S. EMBASSY IN BEIJING (PRESUMABLY

WITH A SIMILAR ASYLUM REQUEST).

6. (C) INFORMATION FROM VARIOUS PRESS AND HUMAN-

RIGHTS ORGANIZATIONS TENOS TO SUBSTANTIATE SUN\'S CLAIM

TO DISSIDENT STATUS, IF HIS IDENTITY CAN BE

SUBSTANTIATED (OR IF NOT SUGGESTS HE AT LEAST DID HIS

HOMEWORK). SUN WEIBANG WAS EDITOR OF A DISSIDENT

DEMOCRATIC JOURNAL ENTITLED "HAILANG HUA" (SEA-WAVE

FLOWER) IN 1979 AND POSTED BIG-CHARACTER POSTERS

DURING THE 1979 DEMOCRACY MOVEMENT. HE WAS ARRESTED

AND SENTENCED TO ONE YEAR IN PRISON IN 1981 FOR THESE

ACTIVITIES. IN 1989, SUN REPORTEDLY RAN A SMALL

RESTAURANT THAT PROVIDED FREE MEALS TO STUDENT

PROTESTORS DURING THE DEMONSTRATIONS THAT CULMINATED

IN THE TIANANMEN SQUARE MASSACRE. HE WAS ARRESTED ANO

SENTENCEO TO 12 YEARS IN PRISON; HE WAS PAROLEO IN

FEBRUARY 1999. IN OCTOBER 1999, AFTER ALLEGEDLY

VISITING THE FAMILY OF DISSIDENT DEMOCRACY PARTY

FOUNDER XU WENLI, SUN WAS REPORTED TO HAVE BEEN RE-

7. (S) ON THE OTHER HANO, INFORMATION PROVIDED BY THE

FRENCH THUS FAR STOPS FAR SHORT OF ESTABLISHING SUN\'S

IDENTITY POSITIVELY AND EXPLAINING THE PRESENCE/STATUS

OF JIANG JINGSHI. HANOI UNDERSTANDS U.S. EMBASSY

BEIJING DOES NOT POSSESS ANY INFORMATION TO CONFIRM OR

EXPLAIN SUN\'S CLAIMS THAT HE HAD BEEN IN CONTACT WITH

THE EMBASSY, AND THAT THE EMBASSY HAS NOT BEEN IN

TOUCH WITH LI YUCON.

8. (S)

ACTION REQUESTS: DEPARTMENT: EMBASSY WILL

KEEP ALL ADDRESSEES FULLY UP TO DATE ON ANY FURTHER

DEVELOPMENTS. AT THIS POINT IT IS UNCLEAR WHETHER THE

FRENCH WILL DECIDE TO ASSIST SUN ANO JIANG, OR TURN

THEM OUT IN THE STREET -- IN WHICH CASE THERE IS A

POSSIBILITY THEY MAY TURN UP HERE. IN THE LATTER

EVENTUALITY, EMBASSY WILL NEEO TO CONSIOER OPTIONS FOR

OEALING WITH AT LEAST SUN. REQUEST OEPARTMENT VIEWS

ON THIS CONTINGENCY. (NOTE: IN A MEETING WITH THE

U.S. AMBASSADOR NOV 24, FRENCH AMBASSAOOR IMPLIED HIS

CURRENT "INSTRUCTIONS" LIKELY INVOLVED TRYING TO GET

AT LEAST SUN OUT OF THE COUNTRY. AMBASSAOOR PETERSON

SUGGESTEO HE CONTACT THE UNHCR OFFICE IN BANGKOK TO

DETERMINE WHAT IT MIGHT DO TO HELP. END NOTE)

9. (S) BEIJING: REQUEST ANY FURTHER INFORMATION

EMBASSY MAY BECOME AWARE OF RELEVANT TO SUN, JIANG,

AND/OR LI.
