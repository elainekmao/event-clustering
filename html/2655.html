SUBJECT: FRG OFFERS KING ARMORED MERCEDES; SEEKS TO

COORDINATE VEHICLE\'S ARRIVAL BY AIRPLANE IN KABUL

REF: STATE 50444

CLASSIFIED BY: POL MINCOUNS TOM COUNTRYMAN, REASONS 1.5 (B)

AND (D).

1. (U) GENERAL WALI, SON-IN-LAW TO FORMER AFGHAN KING ZAHIR

SHAH, TELEPHONED POLOFF MARCH 20 WITH AN URGENT REQUEST THAT

THE USG FACILITATE (THE MORE ACCURATE TERM MAY BE "PROVIDE")

DELIVERY OF AN ARMORED MERCEDES BENZ S600L REPORTEDLY BEING

GIVEN TO THE KING BY THE FRG AND MERCEDES BENZ. ACCORDING TO

WALI, THE GERMANS WOULD LIKE TO HAVE THE VEHICLE AVAILABLE

FOR THE KING UPON HIS ARRIVAL IN KABUL. THE VEHICLE WEIGHS

4,150 KG. AND HAS "ALL SECURITY MEASURES INCORPORATED." (THE

AFGHANS ALSO USED THE TERM "BULLET-PROOF." WE ASSUME THIS IS

AN ARMORED VEHICLE.)

2. (U) WALI RECEIVED THE INFORMATION FROM DR. RAHMAN

ASHRAF, WHOM HE DESCRIBED AS AN AFGHAN-GERMAN GEOLOGY

PROFESSOR WHO REGULARLY SPEAKS TO THE AFGHANS ON BEHALF OF

THE GERMAN GOVERNMENT. WALI THEN HAD DR. ASHRAF TELEPHONE

POLOFF. ASHRAF CONFIRMED THE ABOVE AND SAID THE GERMANS

HOPED TO FLY THE VEHICLE INTO BAGRAM. ASHRAF ASSERTED THAT

EVERY DAY, TWO AIRCRAFT FLY FROM RAMSTEIN AFB TO BAGRAM; THE

GERMANS HOPED TO BE ABLE TO PUT THE VEHICLE ON ONE OF THESE

FLIGHTS -- ONE ARRIVING BEFORE THE KING DOES. (SEE PARA 4.)

HE SAID THE FACTORY WOULD DELIVER THE CAR TO RAMSTEIN FROM

STUTTGART AS EARLY AS "TODAY" (MARCH 20) OR THE MORNING OF

MARCH 21. ASHRAF SAID DR. ZALMAI RASSOUL, FORMER EXECUTIVE

SECRETARY TO THE KING AND CURRENT CIVIL AVIATION MINISTER IN

SIPDIS

THE AIA, RAISED THE MATTER WITH SPECIAL ENVOY KHALILZAD AND

WITH AIA CHAIRMAN KARZAI, WHO BOTH REPORTEDLY SUPPORT THE

GIFT. WALI URGED US TO COMMUNICATE THE URGENCY OF THE

REQUEST FOR TRANSPORTATION ASSISTANCE, AS WELL AS THE KING\'S

ENTHUSIASTIC ACCEPTANCE OF AND SUPPORT FOR THE GENEROUS OFFER.

3. (U) POLOFF ASKED ASHRAF FOR CONTACTS IN GERMANY,

PREFERABLY BOTH IN THE GERMAN GOVERNMENT AND AT MERCEDES

BENZ. ASHRAF CALLED BACK TO PROVIDE CONTACT INFORMATION FOR

A MR. MUELLER, WHO HE SAID IS THE MERCEDES DIRECTOR IN CHARGE

OF SECURITY VEHICLES. MUELLER CAN BE REACHED BY TELEPHONE AT

49-711-173-3103, IN HIS OFFICE IN STUTTGART. ASHRAF DID NOT

HAVE A GOVERNMENT CONTACT, BUT SAID MUELLER WAS "AWARE OF ALL

ARRANGEMENTS." DR. ASHRAF\'S CONTACT INFORMATION IS AS

FOLLOWS: 49-171-540-6283 (CELLULAR); 49-74-717-3274 (HOME);

49-70-7129-74039 (OFFICE, TUBINGEN UNIVERSITY, INSTITUTE FOR

GEOLOGY AND PALEONTOLOGY).

4. (C) COMMENT: GIVEN USG CONCERNS ABOUT THE KING\'S SAFETY

INSIDE AFGHANISTAN (REF), THIS OFFER MAY BE A USEFUL ONE,

ALTHOUGH WE ARE NOT IN A POSITION TO EVALUATE THE CAR\'S

PRACTICALITY ON AFGHAN TERRAIN. THE KING DEPARTS ROME MARCH

25, TO ARRIVE IN AFGHANISTAN MARCH 26, SO THERE IS LITTLE

TIME TO GET THE MERCEDES IN PLACE TO RECEIVE HIM ON THE
