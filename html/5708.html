SUBJECT: DELIVERY OF DEMARCHE ON POSSIBLE HIZBALLAH

RESUPPLY FLIGHT THROUGH JORDAN

REF: STATE 41059

Classified By: A/DCM Doug Silliman for reasons 1.5 (B) and (D)

1. (S/NF) A/DCM on February 13 contacted Ali al-Ayed,

Personal Assistant to Foreign Minister Marwan Muasher, and

relayed points from reftel on the possibility of an Iranian

Hizballah resupply flight through Jordanian airspace this

week. Ayed said he would pass the information to the

appropriate Jordanian authorities.

2. (S/NF) ORCA passed the same message to the GOJ through

its channels.
