CLASSIFIED BY CHARGE D\'AFFAIRES AUBREY HOOKS. REASONS: 1.5

(B) AND (D).

1. (C) THE CHARGE TOOK ADVANTAGE OF A JULY 3 MEETING ON ANOTHER

TOPIC WITH MFA DIRECTOR FOR EUROPE AND THE AMERICAS JONATHAN

WUTAWUNASHE TO DELIVER REFTEL TALKING POINTS. CHARGE EXPRESSED

STRONG USG CONCERN OVER APPARENT SUPPORT BY THE GDRC FOR EX-

FAR/INTERAHAMWE ATTACKS ON RWANDA. THE CHARGE NOTED THAT WE HAD

NO EVIDENCE TO INDICATE THAT THE GOVERNMENT OF ZIMBABWE WAS

SUPPORTING THESE NEGATIVE FORCES. HE URGED THE GOZ, HOWEVER, TO

USE ITS INFLUENCE TO CONVINCE PRESIDENT KABILA TO END SUPPORT FOR

THESE FORCES. THIS WAS A CRUCIAL ISSUE, THE CHARGE EMPHASIZED,

PARTICULARLY GIVEN THE CURRENTLY DELICATE STAGE OF LUSAKA

IMPLEMENTATION. WUTAWUNASHE PROVIDED NO SUBSTANTIVE COMMENT, BUT

UNDERTOOK TO CONVEY THE USG\'S CONCERNS TO HIS FOREIGN MINISTRY

COUNTERPART WITH RESPONSIBILITY FOR AFRICAN ISSUES.

2. (U) WE WILL SEEK ADDITIONAL OPPORTUNITIES TO RAISE REFTEL

POINTS WITH OTHER APPROPRIATE ZIMBABWEAN OFFICIALS.
