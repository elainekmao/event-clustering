SUBJECT: ITALY: EU DIGITAL VAT DIRECTIVE IMPLEMENTATION

REF: STATE 42548

(U) SENSITIVE BUT UNCLASSIFIED. PLEASE HANDLE

ACCORDINGLY. NOT FOR INTERNET DISTRIBUTION.

1. (U) THIS CABLE RESPONDS TO REFTEL REQUEST FOR

INFORMATION ON ITALY\'S IMPLEMENTATION OF THE EU DIGITAL

VAT DIRECTIVE. INFORMATION WAS OBTAINED FROM LEGISLATIVE

OFFICE OF THE ITALIAN MINISTRY FOR EU AFFAIRS.

2. (SBU) ITALY HAS NOT ENACTED THE EU DIGITAL VAT

DIRECTIVE. EU MINISTRY OFFICIALS DO NOT EXPECT ITALY TO

MEET THE JULY 2003 DEADLINE. THE DIRECTIVE IS NOT

LIKELY TO BE ENACTED BEFORE SEPTEMBER 2003. DISCUSSION

WILL BE LIMITED TO GOI ENTITIES, AND THERE WILL NOT BE

PUBLIC HEARINGS.

3. (SBU) OFFICIALS IN THE TAX POLICY DEPARTMENT OF THE

FINANCE MINISTRY ARE DEVELOPING A DRAFT. ACCODING TO

OUR EU MINISTRY CONTACTS, THE FINANCE MINISTRY IS

RELUCTANT TO PROVIDE INFORMATION OUTSIDE THE GOI. WE

HAD ALREADY APPROACHED THE FINANCE MINISTRY, BUT HAVE

NOT RECEIVED A RESPONSE.

4. (SBU) EU MINISTRY OFFICIALS OFFERED TO PROVIDE US

WITH ALL THE SUPPLEMENTAL INFORMATION WE REQUESTED ONCE

THE FINAL DRAFT REACHES THEIR OFFICES. THE EU MINISTRY

WILL BE RESPONSIBLE FOR IMPLEMENTING THE DIGITAL VAT

DIRECTIVE.
