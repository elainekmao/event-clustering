SUBJECT: MISSION ACTION ON TOBACCO

REF: STATE 14016

IN RESPONSE TO REFTEL, MISSION IS NOT AWARE OF ANY USG

ACTION OR USE OF APPROPRIATED FUNDS FOR THE PROMOTION OF

THE SALE OR EXPORT OF TOBACCO, OR RELATED PRODUCTS,

EITHER IN ZIMBABWE OR TO EXTERNAL MARKETS. POST DOES

DIALOGUE WITH COMMERCIAL FARMERS, MANY OF WHOM GROW

TOBACCO IN ADDITION TO OTHER CROPS. MISSION HAS ALSO NOT

TAKEN ANY SPECIFIC ACTIONS IN SUPPORT OF GOZ TOBACCO

CONTROL EFFORTS, WHICH ARE MINIMAL, ALTHOUGH PART OF OUR

PERSONNEL ROSTER INCLUDES A CDC SPECIALIST INVOLVED IN A

REGIONAL SMOKING-CESSATION PROGRAM.
