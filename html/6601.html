SUBJECT: GOI WILL DO WHAT IT CAN TO LIMIT IRAQI OFFICIAL

PRESENCE IN ITALY

CLASSIFIED BY: DCM EMIL SKODON. REASON:1.5 (B)(D)

1. (U) GUIDANCE REQUEST. SEE PARA 6.

2. (S) SUMMARY: ON MARCH 20, MFA CHIEF OF CABINET CESARE

RAGAGLINI SAID THAT THOSE INDIVIDUALS IN THE IRAQI INTEREST

SECTION IN ROME IDENTIFIED AS INTELLIGENCE OFFICERS COULD BE

EXPELLED IMMEDIATELY. HE WONDERED IF THERE WOULD BE SOME

UTILITY IN ALLOWING THE CHARGE\', WHO THE MFA BELIEVES IS NOT

WITH THE INTELLIGENCE SERVICE, TO REMAIN. RAGAGLINI ALSO

POINTED OUT THAT IRAQ\'S EMBASSY TO THE HOLY SEE, WHICH THE

GOI CANNOT LEGALLY CLOSE, WOULD STILL HAVE ACCESS TO BANK

ACCOUNTS AND OTHER GOI RESOURCES. END SUMMARY.

3. (S) DRAWING ON POINTS REFTEL, POLMIN UNDERLINED WITH

RAGAGLINI THE IMPORTANCE OF LIMITING THE OFFICIAL IRAQI

PRESENCE IN ITALY. POLMIN HIGHLIGHTED THE POTENTIAL USE OF

THE IRAQI INTEREST SECTION (UNDER SUDANESE DIPLOMATIC

PROTECTION) AS A BASE FOR TERRORIST OPERATIONS, AND THE

POSSIBILITY THAT OFFICIALS OF THE CURRENT GOVERNMENT WOULD

PLUNDER PROPERTY WHICH RIGHTLY BELONGS TO THE IRAQI PEOPLE

AND DESTROY DIPLOMATIC RECORDS SHOULD IT BECOME APPARENT THAT

THE REGIME IN BAGHDAD WAS ABOUT TO CRUMBLE.

4. (S) RAGAGLINI AGREED THAT THOSE INDIVIDUALS IN THE IRAQI

MISSION IDENTIFIED AS INTELLIGENCE OFFICERS COULD BE EXPELLED

IMMEDIATELY. WITH THE EXCEPTION OF THE CHARGE\', THAT WOULD

INCLUDE ALL IRAQI EMPLOYEES, INCLUDING THE DRIVER. RAGAGLINI

WONDERED IF THERE WOULD BE SOME UTILITY IN ALLOWING THE

CHARGE\' TO REMAIN, GIVEN THE FACT THAT THE MFA BELIEVES THAT

HE IS NOT/NOT AN INTELLIGENCE OFFICER. (COMMENT: RAGAGLINI,

WHO ONCE SERVED AS CHARGE IN BAGHDAD, APPEARS PERSONALLY

CONVINCED THAT THE CHARGE HERE, FORMER CHIEF OF STAFF TO

TARIQ AZIZ, IS NOT A PROFESSIONAL INTELLIGENCE OFFICER). HE

ALSO POINTED OUT THAT ALL OF THESE INDIVIDUALS, INCLUDING THE

CHARGE\', COULD REQUEST POLITICAL ASYLUM. RAGAGLINI SAID THAT

THE FINAL DECISION ON PNG AND INTEREST SECTION CLOSURE WOULD

BE TAKEN BY FM FRATTINI, WHO IS CURRENTLY IN BRUSSELS WITH

THE PM FOR THE EU COUNCIL.

5. (S) THE OTHER COMPLICATING FACTOR IS THE IRAQI EMBASSY

TO THE HOLY SEE, OVER WHICH THE GOI HAS LITTLE OR NO CONTROL.

THE GOI WOULD DO WHAT IT COULD, PROMISED RAGAGLINI, BUT THE

PRESENCE IN THE VATICAN MEANS THAT AS FAR AS ITALY IS

CONCERNED, CLOSING THE INTEREST SECTION IN ROME WOULD HAVE

LITTLE OR NO PRACTICAL EFFECT ON THE GOALS SPELLED OUT IN

REFTEL POINTS. THE EMBASSY TO THE VATICAN, WHICH IS

CO-LOCATED WITH THE BILATERAL DIPLOMATS, WOULD HAVE ACCESS TO

BANK ACCOUNTS AND OTHER RESOURCES, UNLESS THE GOI COULD PROVE

THAT THEY WERE TO BE USED FOR TERRORIST OR OTHER ILLEGAL

ACTIVITIES. RAGAGLINI WILL ASK ITALIAN INTELLIGENCE

SERVICES TO LOOK INTO THE NATURE OF AND AMOUNTS IN THESE

ACCOUNTS. THE ONE BENEFIT FROM THE EMBASSY TO THE HOLY SEE

REMAINING OPEN IS THAT IT COULD TAKE CARE OF THE CONSULAR

NEEDS OF IRAQI NATIONALS IN ITALY.

6. (S) FOR DEPARTMENT: WE NOTE THAT EMBASSY VATICAN WAS NOT

REQUESTED TO UNDERTAKE REFTEL DEMARCHE. EMBASSY VATICAN

BELIEVES THE HOLY SEE WOULD BE UNLIKELY TO CLOSE THE IRAQI

MISSION, GIVEN THE CONTINUED OPERATION OF THEIR OWN MISSION

IN BAGHDAD AND THEIR FEAR OF REPRISALS AGAINST CHALDEAN

CHRISTIANS IN IRAQ. IN LIGHT OF BOTH THE COMPLICATIONS NOTED

ABOVE AND THE LIKELY HOLY SEE ATTITUDE, WE WOULD ASK THE

DEPARTMENT TO REVIEW WHETHER AN APPROACH TO THE HOLY SEE TO

CLOSE THE IRAQI EMBASSY TO THE HOLY SEE WOULD BE ADVISABLE.

7. (U) THIS CABLE WAS CLEARED BY BOTH EMBASSY ROME AND

EMBASSY VATICAN
