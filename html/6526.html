SUBJECT: TFIZ01: JORDAN HUMANITARIAN UPDATE

REF: AMMAN 1622

1. According to UNHCR Emergency Coordinator Carlos

Maldonado, UNHCR today began erecting tents at the refugee

camp at Ruweished (also identified on USG maps as Ruwayshid

or H-4). Poor weather conditions (rain & sandstorms) are

hindering UNHCR\'s efforts, but Maldonado reports that the

camp is ready to receive limited population flows. All

necessary water/sanitation infrastructure is now in place.

UNHCR will be ready to receive its anticipated initial influx

of 5,000 refugees within the next 72 hours.

2. UNHCR has a team of monitors at the Jordan-Iraq border.

The monitors report that no Iraqi refugees have approached

the border, but that two carloads of Sudanese nationals

entered Jordan today. The Sudanese nationals reportedly had

arranged their evacuation from Iraq with the Sudanese embassy

in Jordan, and were given authorization by the GOJ to pass

directly to Amman. No other TCN movements were reported.
