SUBJECT: JORDAN: MEDIA REACTION ON KING\'S ASPEN SPEECH,

U.S. REGIONAL POLICY

SUMMARY

JORDANIAN DAILIES ON JULY 31 ALL LED WITH KING

ABDULLAH\'S SPEECH AT ASPEN, BUT NOT HIS MEETING WITH

ISRAELI FOREIGN MINISTER SHIMON PERES. REPORTS ALSO

INCLUDED THE PALESTINIAN "COMMANDO" OPERATIONS AND THE

ESTABLISHMENT OF AN OFFICE TO IMPROVE THE IMAGE OF THE

U.S. ABROAD.

MANY EDITORIAL WRITERS PRAISED THE KING\'S "UNEQUIVOCAL

DEFENSE OF THE NATION\'S INTERESTS," WHILE OTHERS DEALT

CRITICALLY WITH DIFFERENT ASPECTS OF U.S. POLICY IN THE

REGION.

NEWS REPORTS

ALL JORDANIAN DAILIES ON JULY 31 REPORTED PROMINENTLY

KING ABDULLAH\'S SPEECH AT ASPEN, BUT NOT HIS MEETING

WITH ISRAELI FOREIGN MINISTER SHIMON PERES. DAILIES

HIGHLIGHTED TWO ELEMENTS FROM THE SPEECH: THAT IT IS

TIME TO ESTABLISH JUSTICE AND PEACE FOR THE PALESTINIAN

AND ISRAELI PEOPLE, AND THAT THE ARABS\' CONFIDENCE IN

THE INFLUENCE OF THE U.S. IS LOW.

DAILIES ALSO REPORTED THE PALESTINIAN "COMMANDO"

OPERATIONS, IN WHICH "TWO MARTYRS" FELL.

SEMI-OFFICIAL INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-RAI WAS THE

ONLY DAILY TO QUOTE THE WASHINGTON POST\'S ARTICLE

CLAIMING THAT PRESIDENT BUSH HAS DECIDED TO OPEN AN

OFFICE "TO IMPROVE AMERICA\'S IMAGE ABROAD."

EDITORIAL COMMENTARIES

-- A VOICE OF MODERATION

THE LEADER COMMENTARY IN CENTRIST, INFLUENTIAL AMONG

THE ELITE ENGLISH DAILY JORDAN TIMES ON JULY 31 READ:

"DIALOGUE BETWEEN THE UN AND IRAQ IS THE KEY TO

RESOLVING THE CONFLICT WITH IRAQ. WAR MUST NOT BE AN

OPTION. HARDLINERS IN THE U.S. ADMINISTRATION WHO ARE

PROMOTING THE MILITARY APPROACH ARE OFFERING A RECIPE

FOR DISASTER. THE KING WILL BE PRESENTING SOLID VIEWS

ON HOW TO PEACEFULLY RESOLVE THE TWO MOST EXPLOSIVE

ISSUES IN THE REGION. THEY ARE ARGUMENTS THAT THE U.S.

PRESIDENT WOULD DO WELL TO TAKE VERY SERIOUSLY."

-- IRAN AND SYRIA NEXT

PROMINENT DAILY COLUMNIST URAIB AL-RANTAWI WROTE IN

CENTER-LEFT, INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR ON

JULY 31: "THE HAWKISH CURRENTS IN WASHINGTON AND TEL

AVIV NO DO NOT RULE OUT THE POSSIBILITY THAT SYRIA AND

IRAN MAY BE AT THE TOP OF THE AMERICAN/ ISRAELI WAR

AGENDA AFTER IRAQ. IN FACT, THERE IS ALREADY TALK

WITHIN THESE CURRENTS ABOUT A `MULTI-TRACK WAR.\' IT

WOULD APPEAR THAT WASHINGTON\'S HAWKS HAVE REACHED THE

CONCLUSION THAT ISRAEL\'S DIRECT ENTRY INTO FUTURE WARS

IS NO LONGER A BARRIER AGAINST THE DECISION TO WAGE

WAR. IF THIS PESSIMISTIC SCENARIO COMES TRUE THEN THE

RESHUFFLING OF CARDS TOWARDS CHAOS WILL BE COMPLETE AND

THE REGION WILL TRULY JUMP INTO THE UNKNOWN."

-- REFORMING THE AMERICAN "AUTHORITY"

COLUMNIST USAMA AL-SHARIF WROTE IN CENTER-LEFT,

INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR ON JULY 31: "THE

AMERICAN PRESS NOW WAILS ABOUT THE NEED FOR TOTAL

REFORM IN CONGRESS, THE WHITE HOUSE, AND WALL STREET

BEFORE THE ECONOMY COLLAPSES TOTALLY. BUT PRESIDENT

BUSH DOES NOT SEEM TO TAKE THESE DEMANDS SERIOUSLY

BECAUSE HE IS OBSESSED WITH FIGHTING TERRORISM AND

CONVERTING HIS COUNTRY INTO AN IMPREGNABLE FORTRESS. IN

FACT, HE IS BEHAVING LIKE A HIJACKER WHO IS PILOTING

HIS HIJACKED AIRPLANE DELIBERATELY TO CRASH IT AGAINST

THE INTERNATIONAL ECONOMY. INTERESTINGLY, ARAFAT CAN

TODAY VINDICATE HIMSELF BY ASKING MR. BUSH TO PUT HIS

OWN HOUSE IN ORDER BEFORE ADDRESSING ADVICE ABOUT

REFORMING THE PNA." GNEHM
