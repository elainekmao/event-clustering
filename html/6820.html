SUBJECT: TFIZ01: IRAQ: ENCOURAGING POSITIVE JORDANIAN

STATEMENT AT UNSC OPEN SESSION

REF: STATE 77740

Classified By: A/DCM Doug Silliman for reasons 1.5 (B)(D)

1. (C) A/DCM on March 26 covered ref points on the UNSC

open session on Iraq with Ali al-Ayed, personal assistant to

Foreign Minister Marwan Muasher. A/DCM asked that Jordan

make a positive statement regarding the conflict in Iraq.

Al-Ayed said that he did not think Jordan\'s UN PermRep would

be making any remarks at the session, but undertook to pass

the request to the Jordanian Mission to the UN.
