SUBJECT: AMBASSADOR TALKS BUSINESS AT AMCHAM LUNCHEON

1. SUMMARY: THE AMERICAN CHAMBER OF COMMERCE (AMCHAM) IN

ITALY HOSTED A JUNE 18 LUNCHEON FOR THE AMBASSADOR. IN HIS

REMARKS, THE AMBASSADOR ASKED AMCHAM MEMBERS TO BECOME MORE

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 02 ROME 03301 01 OF 02 051439Z

ACTIVE AND ASKED THAT THE AMCHAM AND ITS MEMBERS WORK

CLOSELY WITH THE EMBASSY. HE EMPHASIZED THE EMBASSY\'S

COMMITMENT TO SUPPORTING U.S. FIRMS, BUT ALSO NOTED THAT

LOBBYING IS A LEGITIMATE ACTIVITY FOR BUSINESS. AMCHAM

MEMBERS THANKED THE AMBASSADOR FOR HIS AND THE EMBASSY\'S

ONGOING EFFORTS TO ENSURE EQUAL, FAIR AND TRANSPARENT

TREATMENT FOR U.S. COMPANIES AND ALSO RECOGNIZED EMBASSY\'S

SUPPORT IN PROVIDING ACCESS AND CREATING A POSITIVE BUSINESS

ENVIRONMENT IN ITALY. AN ACTIVE DISCUSSION ENSUED REGARDING

OBSTACLES AND OPPORTUNITIES FOR U.S. FIRMS IN ITALY, AND HOW

THE AMCHAM CAN BEST ASSIST ITS MEMBERS. THIS MEETING WAS A

USEFUL EVENT IN A CONTINUING EFFORT TO IMPROVE OPPORTUNITIES

FOR U.S. FIRMS IN ITALY. END SUMMARY.

2. THE AMCHAM HOSTED A JUNE 18 LUNCHEON IN ROME IN HONOR OF

AMBASSADOR SEMBLER. PARTICIPANTS INCLUDED REPRESENTATIVES

FROM CATERPILLAR, MORGAN GUARANTY TRUST, PMG, UNION CARBIDE,

PHILLIP MORRIS, VERIZON, BOYDEN INT., PROCTER AND GAMBLE,

THE AMERICAN INTERNATIONAL CLUB OF ROME, MCDONALDS, PFIZER,

AND BOOZ-ALLEN. COMMERCIAL MINISTER COUNSELOR ERIC WEAVER,

ECONOMIC COUNSELOR AND ECONOMIC AND COMMERCIAL SPECIALISTS

ALSO PARTICIPATED.

3. AMBASSADOR SEMBLER UNDERSCORED THE IMPORTANCE OF AN

ACTIVE AMCHAM IN ORDER FOR U.S. COMPANIES TO DEVELOP AND

GROW IN LOCAL MARKETS. HE ENCOURAGED COMPANY EXECUTIVES TO

BECOME MORE ACTIVE IN AMCHAM ACTIVITIES, AND TOLD THEM SUCH

EFFORTS WILL PAY LONG-TERM DIVIDENDS FAR IN EXCESS TO THEIR

AMCHAM INVESTMENT OF TIME AND ENERGY. THE AMBASSADOR

STRESSED HIS COMMITMENT TO CORRECTING THE TRADE IMBALANCE

BETWEEN THE U.S. AND ITALY. HE OFFERED THE EMBASSY\'S SUPPORT

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 03 ROME 03301 01 OF 02 051439Z

TO U.S. FIRMS, AND URGED THEM TO WORK WITH THE EMBASSY TO

REDUCE BARRIERS TO U.S. TRADE AND INVESTMENT IN ITALY. HE

ALSO NOTED THAT LOBBYING, WHILE NOT COMMON AMONG ITALIAN

FIRMS, WAS A PERFECTLY LEGITIMATE ACTIVITY FOR FIRMS AND

ORGANIZATIONS TO PROTECT AND PROMOTE LEGITIMATE BUSINESS

INTERESTS.

4. MINISTER COUNSELOR WEAVER INVITED AMCHAM TO STAY ACTIVE

AND HIGHLIGHTED THE SUCCESSFUL COOPERATION BETWEEN THE U.S.

MISSION AND THE AMCHAM IN RECENT YEARS. HE NOTED THAT

WORKING TOGETHER HAS ENABLED U.S. BUSINESSES TO GROW,

CONSOLIDATE AND BE MORE SUCCESSFUL IN ITALY. THE ECONOMIC

COUNSELOR ECHOED THE AMBASSADOR IN OFFERING THE EMBASSY\'S

SUPPORT. SHE SPECIFICALLY INVITED AMCHAM MEMBERS TO COME TO

THE EMBASSY EARLY WHEN THEY HAVE VIEWS, CONCERNS OR SPECIFIC

INTEREST IN ITALIAN POLICIES/LEGISLATION, EU

POLICIES/DIRECTIVES OR INTERNATIONAL (WTO, OECD)

NEGOTIATIONS.

5. COMPANY REPRESENTATIVES ENDORSED THE CALL FOR CONTINUED

EMBASSY/AMCHAM COOPERATION AND EXPRESSED APPRECIATION FOR

THE EMBASSY\'S LONG-TERM CONTRIBUTION IN SUPPORTING U.S.

BUSINESS IN ITALY AND IN ENSURING EQUAL ACCESS AND A LEVEL

PLAYING FIELD. VERIZON REPRESENTATIVE MADE SPECIFIC

REFERENCE TO THE EMBASSY\'S EFFECTIVE INTERVENTION, WHICH

ASSURED THAT VERIZON COULD PARTICIPATE IN A LARGE PUBLIC

TENDER IN 2001. (NOTE: VERIZON WON THE CONTRACT).

6. AN ACTIVE DISCUSSION ENSUED, INVOLVING ALL PARTICIPANTS.

AMONG ISSUES DISCUSSED WERE:

-- BARRIERS TO U.S. TRADE AND INVESTMENT, INCLUDING THE

COMPLEX TAX SYSTEM, LABOR RIGIDITIES, THE DIFFICULT

BUREAUCRATIC OBSTACLES, AND THE INADEQUACIES OF THE ITALIAN

JUSTICE SYSTEM.

-- ITALY\'S DIFFERENT BUSINESS CULTURE, REQUIRING PERSONAL

TRUST AND LONG TERM RELATIONSHIPS.

-- LARGE NUMBER OF U.S. "CONSULTANTS" WHO COME TO ITALY,

WHEN THE NEED IS FOR ENTREPRENEURS, MANAGERS AND PRODUCTIVE

FIRMS.

-- IDEA OF FORMING INDUSTRY SUBGROUPS WITHIN AMCHAM TO

EXPLORE THE PROBLEMS/OPPORTUNITIES IN EACH SECTOR AND FOCUS

LOBBYING EFFORTS.

7. THE PROCTER AND GAMBLE REPRESENTATIVE NOTED THAT, WHILE

IN THE PAST THE FIRM\'S OFFICIALS HAD PREFERRED THAT IT BE

SEEN AS AN ITALIAN ENTITY (INSTEAD OF AS A BRANCH OPERATION

OF A U.S. MULTINATIONAL), THIS HAS CHANGED. THEY NOW

BELIEVE IT IS IN THEIR INTEREST TO GIVE GREATER VISIBILITY

TO THEIR U.S. OWNERSHIP AND ARE, THEREFORE, MORE WILLING TO

PARTICIPATE IN AMCHAM ACTIVITIES AND ENGAGE IN GREATER

DIALOGUE WITH THE EMBASSY. THE PROCTER AND GAMBLE

REPRESENTATIVE THOUGHT THAT OTHER COMPANIES ALSO MIGHT BE

THINKING ALONG THESE LINES.
