SUBJECT: JORDAN: MEDIA REACTION ON PRESIDENT\'S SPEECH,

OCCUPIED TERRITORIES

SUMMARY

NEWS REPORTS IN JORDAN\'S DAILIES LED WITH DEVELOPMENTS

IN THE OCCUPIED TERRITORIES AND THE PRESIDENT\'S CALL TO

CUT OFF AID FROM THE PNA PENDING REFORMS. COVERAGE OF

REACTIONS TO THE PRESIDENT\'S MIDDLE EAST SPEECH

REFLECTED POSITIVE COMMENTS FROM ARAB OFFICIALS, AND

CRITICISM FROM ALL OTHER QUARTERS, INCLUDING AMERICAN

AND BRITISH COMMENTATORS.

JORDANIAN EDITORIAL COLUMNISTS CONTINUED TO CRITICIZE

THE PRESIDENT\'S MIDDLE EAST SPEECH, CHARGING THAT IT

IGNORED THE ARAB INITIATIVE AND ARAB DIPLOMACY, AND

THAT IT HAS PUT ARAB MODERATES AND THE ISRAELI PEACE

CAMP IN A VERY EMBARRASSING POSITION.

NEWS REPORTS

JORDANIAN DAILIES THIS MORNING COVERED DEVELOPMENTS IN

THE WEST BANK WITH HEADLINES SUCH AS: "A MILLION

PALESTINIANS UNDER CURFEW" IN THE INDEPENDENT, MASS-

APPEAL ARABIC DAILY AL-ARAB AL-YAWM, AND: "PITCHED

BATTLES IN HEBRON AND LARGE WAVE OF ARRESTS IN THE WEST

BANK" IN THE SEMI-OFFICIAL, INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-

RAI. CENTER-LEFT, INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR

LED WITH THE HEADLINE: "BUSH: NO AID TO THE

PALESTINIANS WITHOUT REFORMS."

COVERAGE OF REACTIONS TO THE PRESIDENT\'S MIDDLE EAST

SPEECH VARIED BETWEEN POSITIVE PRONOUNCEMENTS BY ARAB

OFFICIALS, AND CRITICISM FROM OTHER SOURCES. THE

CONTRAST WAS MANIFEST IN TWO ADJACENT STORIES IN AL-

RAI. THE HEADLINE OF THE FIRST READ: "AMR MUSSA: BUSH

DID NOT CALL FOR GETTING RID OF ARAFAT" WHILE THE

HEADLINE NEXT TO IT READ: "EUROPEAN UNION AND CANADA

CRITICIZE BUSH\'S CALL FOR GETTING RID OF ARAFAT."

AL-DUSTOUR AND AL-ARAB AL-YAWM REPORTED ON THEIR FRONT

PAGES THE ARREST IN THE U.S. OF A JORDANIAN WHO HAD

SHARED RESIDENCE WITH TWO OF THE SEPTEMBER 11

HIJACKERS.

EDITORIAL COMMENTARY

-- BUSH IGNORES THE ARAB INITIATIVE

MOST WIDELY READ JORDANIAN COLUMNIST DR. FAHED AL-FANEK

WROTE IN SEMI-OFFICIAL, INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-RAI

ON JUNE 27: "THE REFORMS THAT BUSH ASKED EXCLUSIVELY OF

THE PALESTINIANS ARE CORRECT, AND IN FACT THEY ARE

DEMANDED BY THE PALESTINIAN PEOPLE. BUT IN REALITY

BUSH IS NOT CONCERNED WITH THEM BECAUSE THE U.S. IS IN

THE HABIT OF DEALING AND MAKING ALLIANCE WITH THE WORST

DICTATORIAL CORRUPT REGIMES. IN FACT, PRESIDENT BUSH

ASKED CERTAIN RULERS TO HELP IMPLEMENT POLITICAL AND

FISCAL REFORMS IN PALESTINE THAT THEY DO NOT CARRY OUT

IN THEIR OWN COUNTRIES. THE OTHER POINT IS THAT THE

LEADERS OF JORDAN, EGYPT AND SAUDI ARABIA MADE SEVERAL

VISITS TO WASHINGTON AND SPENT LONG HOURS WITH THE

PRESIDENT TO EXPLAIN TO HIM THE ARAB POINT OF VIEW, AND

THEY RETURNED OPTIMISTIC THINKING THAT THEY MANAGED TO

INFLUENCE HIS THINKING, ONLY TO DISCOVER THAT HE HAD

CLOSED HIS EYES AND EARS TO THEM BECAUSE HE IS

COMMITTED TO SHARON\'S VISION."

-- THE SIGNIFICANCE OF IGNORING THE ARAB INITIATIVE

DAILY COLUMNIST MAHMOUD AL-RIMAWI WROTE IN SEMI-

OFFICIAL, INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-RAI ON JUNE 27:

"DESPITE THE FREQUENT MEETINGS THAT PRESIDENT BUSH MADE

WITH ARAB LEADERS, AND WHAT THE WHITE HOUSE PUBLICIZED

ABOUT A POSITIVE SPIRIT IN THESE MEETINGS THAT WERE

HELD AT THE HIGHEST LEVELS, IT IS EVIDENT THAT THE

AMERICAN HAWKS, WHO SHOULD BETTER BE DESCRIBES AND THE

AMERICAN LIKUD, WERE DOING ALL THAT THEY COULD TO MAKE

THE ADMINISTRATION\'S VIEWS COMPATIBLE WITH THOSE OF THE

OCCUPATION GOVERNMENT IN TEL AVIV. THIS IS THE GREAT

DANGER OF THE SPEECH. ONE NEEDS TO BE AN IMBECILE TO

BELIEVE AND TRUST THAT THE CONSTRUCTION OF THE

PALESTINIAN STATE SHOULD START WITH THE DESTRUCTION OF

ITS NUCLEUS WHILE CLOSING THE DOOR AGAINST SERIOUS

NEGOTIATIONS ON THE SYRIAN AND LEBANESE TRACKS. ARABS

ARE NOW CALLED UPON TO MOBILIZE THEMSELVES TO END THIS

SAGA THAT USES THE PRETEXT OF FIGHTING TERRORISM TO

PREVENT PEACE AND ABET THE STATE TERRORISM THAT IS

BEING PRACTICED BY THE WAR CRIMINALS OF TEL AVIV."

-- ONE STEP FORWARD, TWO STEPS BACK

PROMINENT JORDANIAN COLUMNIST URAIB RANTAWI WROTE IN

CENTER-LEFT, INFLUENTIAL ARABIC DAILY AL-DUSTOUR ON

JUNE 27: THE TONGUE [THAT UTTERED THE SPEECH] BELONGED

TO PRESIDENT BUSH, BUT THE HAND [THAT WROTE IT] WAS

THAT OF SHARON, TO THE POINT THAT SHARON COULD EASILY

CLAIM COPYRIGHT OF THIS SPEECH. PRESIDENT BUSH\'S

SPEECH IS A PERSONAL DEFEAT FOR ARAFAT, AND A STAB FOR

ARAB DIPLOMACY. NOT ONLY BECAUSE IT IGNORED COMPLETELY

THE INITIATIVE OF PRICE ABDULLAH AND THE BEIRUT

DECLARATION, BUT ALSO BECAUSE IT ADOPTED THE ISRAELI

VERSION FROM A TO Z, AND REFUSED TO COMMIT ITSELF TO

ANY ARAB RIGHTS OF INTERESTS. THE NET RESULT IS THAT

BUSH HAS OPENED THE DOOR FOR ESCALATING THE CRISIS

RATHER THAN SOLVING IT. HE HAS GIVEN FRESH IMPETUS FOR

EXTREMISTS IN THE REGION, MADE THE LIFE OF ARAB

MODERATES EXTREMELY DIFFICULT, WEAKENED THE PNA IN

FAVOR OF ITS RIVALS, GIVEN SHARON AND THE RIGHT-WING

CAMP A NEW LEASE OF LIFE, AND PLACED THE ISRAELI PEACE

CAMP UNDER SIEGE."

-- WHERE IS THE ISRAELI PEACE PARTNER?

TAHER AL-ADWAN, CHIEF EDITOR OF INDEPENDENT, MASS-

APPEAL ARABIC DAILY AL-ARAB AL-YAWM WROTE ON JUNE 27:

"PRESIDENT BUSH\'S SPEECH CONTAINS MANY ERRORS, THE MOST

IMPORTANT OF WHICH WAS TO PORTRAY THE PALESTINIAN

LEADERSHIP AND ITS PRESIDENT, ARAFAT, AS UNFIT TO BE A

PARTNER IN MAKING PEACE, EVEN THOUGH ARAFAT HAS A NOBEL

PEACE PRIZE, AND HE WAS THE FIRST PALESTINIAN LEADER TO

SHAKE HANDS WITH ISRAELI LEADERS AND TO RECOGNIZE

ISRAEL. IF PRESIDENT BUSH WERE BALANCED, HE WOULD HAVE

CALLED FOR A CHANGE IN THE ISRAELI LEADERSHIP, BECAUSE

EVEN ISRAELIS ACKNOWLEDGE THAT SHARON WAS A VOICE

AGAINST EVERY PEACE TREATY WITH EVERY ARAB COUNTRY, AND

HE IS AN EXPERT OF CRIMINALITY, MASSACRES, AND

DESTRUCTION. BUT BUSH CHOSE NOT TO BE BALANCED, AND HIS

VIEWS WILL PASS AS DUST, THE SAME AS THE DUST RAISED BY

THE CHAINS OF SHARON\'S TANKS." GNEHM
