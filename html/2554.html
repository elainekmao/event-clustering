SUBJECT: SANT\'EGIDIO THANKS UNCLE SAM AND CHIDES

"HYPOCRITE" FIDEL.

REF: REF A: VATICAN 00445 REF B: VATICAN 00700 REF C:

VATICAN 00578.

CLASSIFIED BY: AMBASSADOR JIM NICHOLSON. REASONS 1.5 (B,D).

1. (U) ANDREA RICCARDI, FOUNDER OF THE SANT\'EGIDIO

COMMUNITY, HOSTED AMBASSADOR AND EMBOFFS 2/19 AT A DINNER IN

RECOGNITION OF THE ORGANIZATION\'S CLOSE COOPERATION WITH THE

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 02 VATICA 01152 051435Z

USG. RICCARDI EXPRESSED GRATITUDE FOR CONTINUING ECA/PD

GRANTS THAT SUPPORT SANT\'EGIDIO CONFLICT RESOLUTION PROJECTS

(REF A). HE FURTHER ENDORSED THE MAY 15-16 ANTI-TRAFFICKING

CONFERENCE (REF. B) THE EMBASSY IS PLANNING IN CONCERT WITH

THE HOLY SEE DIPLOMATIC CORPS AND THE VATICAN, AND OFFERED

SANT\'EGIDIO ASSISTANCE.

2. (C) SEPARATELY, RICCARDI SPOKE CRITICALLY TO AMBASSADOR

OF HIS 1999 MEETING IN CUBA WITH FIDEL CASTRO, A MARATHON

EVENING COLLOQUY THAT LASTED PAST 3 AM. RICCARDI HAD SHARP

WORDS FOR WHAT HE TERMED CASTRO\'S HYPOCRISY, PARTICULARLY ON

CAPITAL PUNISHMENT IN CUBA. HE DERIDED CASTRO\'S AVOWED

PERSONAL OPPOSITION TO CAPITAL PUNISHMENT COUPLED WITH HIS

INSISTENCE THAT HE WAS OBLIGATED TO UPHOLD THE LAW OF THE

STATE.

3. (C) DON MATTEO ZUPPI, IN HAVANA THIS JANUARY TO

PARTICIPATE IN AN OFF-SITE FARC-COLUMBIA GOVERNMENT ENCOUNTER

(REF C), REMARKED TO EMBOFFS THAT CASTRO HAD PULLED HIM

ASIDE AND UPBRAIDED THE SANT\'EGIDIO COMMUNITY AND ZUPPI

PERSONALLY FOR THE NGO\'S RELUCTANCE TO COLLABORATE WITH

HAVANA IN 1999. ACCORDING TO ZUPPI, CASTRO HAD SOUGHT TO

EXPLOIT SANT\'EGIDIO IN THE CONTEXT OF THE 1999 KOSOVO

CONFLICT BY DEPLOYING 1,000 PURPORTED HUMANITARIAN WORKERS TO

KOSOVO AND YUGOSLAVIA UNDER THE AUSPICES OF SANT\'EGIDIO.

4. (U) IN ASIDE TO DCM, DON MATTEO ZUPPI REQUESTED SUPPORT

FOR SANT\'EGIDIO\'S PROPOSED $5 MILLION HIV/AIDS PROJECT IN

MOZAMBIQUE. SANT\'EGIDIO HAS FORWARDED A PROPOSAL TO THE

GLOBAL FUND TO FIGHT HIV/AIDS. ZUPPI IS SEEKING GENERAL

EXPRESSIONS OF USG SUPPORT TO THE GLOBAL FUND\'S DIRECTORS.
