SUBJECT: PM BERLUSCONI WILL ALSO REMAIN FM BERLUSCONI "FOR

A FEW MORE MONTHS"

REF: A) ROME 03651 B) ROME 3600

1. (SBU) DESPITE PUBLIC AND PRIVATE MUSINGS FROM VARIOUS

SOURCES IN THE GOI (REPORTED REFTELS), PM BERLUSCONI WILL NOT

RELINQUISH HIS POST AS INTERIM FOREIGN MINISTER ANY TIME

SOON. REFTELS REPORTED THAT THE PM WAS LIKELY TO ANNOUNCE

SOON THE APPOINTMENT OF CURRENT MINISTER FOR PUBLIC

ADMINISTRATION AND SECURITY FRANCO FRATTINI AS FOREIGN

MINISTER.

2. (U) DURING HIS ADDRESS TO A CONFERENCE FOR ITALIAN

AMBASSADORS IN ROME ON JULY 24, HOWEVER, BERLUSCONI SAID THAT

HE WOULD KEEP THE MFA INTERIM POST "FOR A FEW MORE MONTHS, OR

MAYBE EVEN LONGER." HE EXPLAINED THAT HE IS THE HEAD OF A

COALITION GOVERNMENT, AND THAT "WITHIN THIS COALITION, THE

CONDITIONS TO APPOINT A NEW FOREIGN MINISTER HAVE NOT YET

RIPENED." BERLUSCONI ASSURED THE AMBASSADORS THAT HE WOULD

TRY TO BE "MORE PRESENT, MORE UP TO THE TASK, TO MEET YOUR

EXPECTATIONS." HE ALSO ADMITTED THAT WHEN THE TIME FINALLY

COMES TO GIVE UP THE MFA, HE WILL MISS THE JOB.

3. (SBU) COMMENT. IN CITING THE COALITION GOVERNMENT AS

THE REASON FOR DELAYING HIS DECISION ON A NEW FM, BERLUSCONI

PROBABLY HAD IN MIND THE FACT THAT GIVING FRATTINI THE MFA

WOULD MEAN THAT THE MOST IMPORTANT GOVERNMENT MINISTRIES

(FOREIGN AFFAIRS, DEFENSE, ECONOMY, AND INTERIOR) WOULD BE

RUN BY FORZA ITALIA (FI - BERLUSCONI\'S PARTY) MEMBERS. WE

UNDERSTAND THAT THE CHRISTIAN DEMOCRATS IN THE COALITION, IN

PARTICULAR, ARE PUSHING FOR MORE POSITIONS. THIS DOES NOT

BODE WELL FOR FRATTINI\'S CHANCES. THE RELATIVE STRENGTHS OF

THE PARTIES COMPRISING THE COALITION WILL NOT CHANGE OVER THE

NEXT FEW MONTHS. BARRING A MAJOR SHUFFLE IN THE CABINET

(SOMETHING BERLUSCONI HAS SAID HE IS RELUCTANT TO DO), IT MAY

BE THAT BERLUSCONI IS STALLING FOR TIME TO CAST HIS NET WIDER

IN THE SEARCH FOR A CAPABLE AND, FROM PARLIAMENT\'S

STANDPOINT, ACCEPTABLE FM.

4. (SBU) COMMENT CONTINUED. DESPITE BERLUSCONI\'S PROMISE

TO BE MORE VISIBLE AT THE MFA, IT WILL BECOME INCREASINGLY

DIFFICULT FOR HIM TO HANDLE BOTH THE PM AND FM JOBS

EFFECTIVELY. "A FEW MORE MONTHS" MEANS HE WILL RETAIN THE FM

POSITION AT LEAST UNTIL JUST BEFORE, OR THROUGH, THE NATO

SUMMIT IN PRAGUE. GIVEN THE DETAILS TO BE HAMMERED OUT ON

ENLARGEMENT IN THE RUN-UP TO PRAGUE, THE LACK OF A FULL TIME

FM COULD BE PROBLEMATIC FOR THE GOI. IN ADDITION, THE MFA

HAS STARTED PREPARATIONS FOR ITALY\'S EU PRESIDENCY IN JULY

2003. BERLUSCONI MAY FIND THAT THE BURDEN OF TWO CABINET

POSITIONS WILL FORCE HIM TO EXPEDITE HIS CHOICE FOR FM.

RESPECTED MEDIA ANALYSTS ARE INCREASINGLY SUGGESTING JUST

THIS.
