Political

---------

The Polish Ambassador to Kuwait, Voycheck Bozil, called

the Operations Center on April 7 at 1810 to request USG

assistance in locating two Polish journalists who were

apparently captured within an area approximately 100 km.

from Al-Hilla, or Hilla, Iraq. Ambassador Bozik said the

journalists rented a car from Kuwait, and claimed that

they had received permission from the US military to

enter Iraq. His sources also claimed that one of the

journalists escaped from his captors on April 7 at 2:30

p.m. The names of the journalists are as follows:

1. Jacek Kaczmarcek: Works for Polish Radio Number One

2. Marchin Sirlej: Works for Polish Television T4N

(Described as the Polish version of CNN). He can be

reached at his mobile number: 982-4459. He said that the

Polish MFA had formally contacted the State Department to

request assistance. This information was passed to PAO

and OMCK Vice Chief for action.
