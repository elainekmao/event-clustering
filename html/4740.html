1. (C) SUMMARY: ITALY CONTINUES TO OPPOSE CONFRONTATIONAL

CONFIDENTIAL

TACTICS TO OVERTURN LIBYA\'S CHAIRMANSHIP OF CHR-59 THAT COULD

DAMAGE THE COMMISSION\'S LONGER-TERM VIABILITY OR THAT LEAD

PIECEMEAL TO A FORCED VOTE ON THE MATTER. THE BERLUSCONI

GOVERNMENT\'S REFLEXIVE SUPPORT FOR U.S. POLICY HAS, IN THIS

NARROW CASE, BEEN TRUMPED BY AN UNYIELDING SUPPORT FOR THE UN

SYSTEM, AND ITS CONSENSUAL DECISION-MAKING, AND A BELIEF THAT

THEIR POLICY OF ENGAGING LIBYA IS SLOWLY MOVING QADAFFI IN

THE RIGHT DIRECTION. THE ITALIANS HAVE NO ILLUSIONS ABOUT

QADAFFI\'S REGIME AND WOULD WORK IN ADVANCE TO PREVENT MORE

PROMINENT LIBYAN POSITIONS IN THE UN SYSTEM. BUT THEY

BELIEVE THE MORE CEREMONIAL NATURE OF THE CHR CHAIRMANSHIP

MAKES IT AN APPROPRIATE POSITION TO TEST AND ENCOURAGE

QADAFFI\'S COMMITMENT TO REHABILITATING LIBYA\'S INTERNATIONAL

STANDING. THEY ARE NOT WILLING TO JEOPARDIZE\'S THE

COMMISSION\'S FUTURE EFFECTIVENESS -- AND THEIR OWN FUTURE

CANDIDACY -- THROUGH AN OPEN-ENDED SERIES OF CRITICAL WEOG

LETTERS AND STATEMENTS. END SUMMARY.

2. (C) THE ITALIAN GOVERNMENT IS PREPARED CONDITIONALLY TO

SUPPORT A WEOG LETTER OF CONCERN AND, PERHAPS, A GROUP

STATEMENT ON LIBYA\'S CHAIRMANSHIP, OFFICIALS AT PRIME

MINISTER\'S OFFICE AND THE MFA TOLD US DECEMBER 16. THEIR

LIKELY SUPPORT FOR A LETTER OR STATEMENT WOULD BE CONTINGENT

UPON:

-- THE OVERALL STRATEGY: ITALY WOULD NOT AGREE TO AN

OPEN-ENDED, PIECEMEAL APPROACH THAT PRODUCED A SERIES OF

AD-HOC MEASURES CULMINATING IN A JANUARY 20 VOTE IF THE

LIBYAN CANDIDATE WAS NOT REPLACED. SUCH AN ESCALATORY

APPROACH WOULD BE OVERLY CONFRONTATIONAL, REDUCING PROSPECTS

FOR ANY PRODUCTIVE WORK AT THIS YEAR\'S CHR AND ENDANGERING

CONFIDENTIAL

PAGE 03 ROME 06002 01 OF 02 161821Z

THE WEOG\'S EFFECTIVENESS IN FUTURE COMMISSIONS. ITALY COULD

ACCEPT A LETTER OR STATEMENT IN LIEU OF A VOTE: BUT NOT AS A

FIRST STEP THAT CULMINATED IN A VOTE.

-- THE TONE AND OBJECTIVES OF THE LETTER AND/OR STATEMENT:

IT SHOULD NOT BE OVERLY CRITICAL OF THE AFRICAN GROUP FOR

SELECTING LIBYA BUT SHOULD INSTEAD CONCENTRATE ON STATING

CLEARLY WEOG EXPECTATIONS AND RED LINES FOR LIBYA\'S

CHAIRMANSHIP.

IN RESPONSE, WE HAVE UNDERSCORED THE DANGER IN PULLING THE

PUNCH TOO MUCH; IN THAT SCENARIO, WE COULD END UP WITH BOTH A

WEAK OR DISUNIFIED EXPRESSION OF CONCERN (OUR LEAST-FAVORED

OUTCOME) AND A CONFRONTATIONAL VOTE, THE GOI\'S LEAST-FAVORED

OUTCOME.

3. (C) CONTACTS IN THE PRIME MINISTER\'S OFFICE TOLD US PM

BERLUSCONI WAS AWARE OF THE LIBYAN CHAIRMANSHIP ISSUE; THE PM

WANTED AN OUTCOME GENERALLY CONSISTENT WITH HIS POLICY OF

REALISTIC ENGAGEMENT WITH LIBYA REINFORCED BY HIS OCTOBER

VISIT TO TRIPOLI. DURING THAT VISIT, THE ITALIAN DELEGATION

(THOUGH NOT BERLUSCONI PERSONALLY) HAD DISCUSSED WITH THE

LIBYANS A RANGE OF ACTIVITIES -- INCLUDING THE CHR

CHAIRMANSHIP -- THAT COULD GRADUALLY REINTEGRATE LIBYA INTO

THE WIDER INTERNATIONAL COMMUNITY. AS A RESULT, ITALY WAS IN

THE DIFFICULT POSITION OF SUPPORTING BROAD WEOG EFFORTS TO

REGISTER CONCERNS ABOUT LIBYA\'S CHAIRMANSHIP WITHOUT

VIOLATING ITS BILATERAL COMMITMENT TO TRIPOLI, MADE BEFORE

THE U.S. EXPRESSED ITS STRONG OPPOSITION TO A LIBYAN CHAIR.

CONSEQUENTLY, WE LEARNED SEPARATELY FROM MAGHREB OFFICE

DIRECTOR BELLAVIA, ITALY WAS PURSUING A STATEMENT THAT

CREATED A FRAMEWORK AND OUTLINED WEOG EXPECTATIONS FOR THE

UPCOMING CHR SESSION RATHER THAN ONE THAT EXCESSIVELY

CRITICIZED THE AFRICA GROUP\'S SELECTION OF LIBYA.

4. (C) EVEN BEFORE THE LATEST DEVELOPMENTS IN GENEVA, WE HAD

BEEN PRESSING THE CASE AGAINST LIBYAN CHAIRMANSHIP AT

SUCCESSIVELY HIGHER LEVELS, STRESSING THE IMPORTANCE OF

CONVEYING WEOG CONCERNS PRIOR TO THE HOLIDAY RECESS. WITH

MINOR VARIATIONS, THE REPLY HAS BEEN THE SAME: SKEPTICISM

THAT THE AFRICA GROUP COULD BE PERSUADED TO NOMINATE ANOTHER

CHAIR MORE REPRESENTATIVE OF THE COMMISSION\'S IDEALS; AND

CONCERN ABOUT COLLATERAL DAMAGE TO THE NORMAL COMITY AMONG UN

REGIONAL GROUPS. THE LATTER CONCERN CLEARLY REVOLVES AROUND

ITALY\'S PROSPECTIVE TENURE ON (AND, PERHAPS, CHAIRMANSHIP OF)

THE 2004 SESSION OF THE COMMISSION.

5. (C) REINFORCING THE ITALIAN VIEW IS THE RELATIVE PAUCITY,

IN THEIR ANALYSIS, OF COMPELLING ALTERNATIVE CHAIRS WITHIN

THE AFRICA GROUP. MFA DIRECTOR GENERAL FOR NEA BADINI

COUNTERED DCM\'S DECEMBER 6 PITCH ON THE ISSUE BY OBSERVING

THAT LIBYA\'S HUMAN RIGHTS RECORD WAS NOT WORSE THAN ITS

MAGHREB NEIGHBORS OR, FOR THAT MATTER, THOSE OF MOST MEMBERS

OF THE AFRICA GROUP.

MAJOR U.S. INITIATIVE SINCE PM BERLUSCONI ENTERED OFFICE 18

MONTHS AGO (INCLUDING STEPPING ASIDE WITHIN THE WEOG TO ALLOW

THE U.S. TO REGAIN A SEAT IN THE 2003 SESSION OF THE HUMAN

RIGHTS COMMISSION). BUT THAT SUPPORT TAILS OFF WHEN THE

OVERALL EFFECTIVENESS OF THE UN SYSTEM OR, IN THIS CASE, ONE

OF ITS COMPONENTS, IS THREATENED (IN THEIR VIEW) BY AN OVERLY

SPECIFIC U.S. OBJECTIVE. THE ITALIAN GOVERNMENT WILL NOT

SUPPORT MEASURES WITHIN THE WEOG THAT ARE, IN THEIR VIEW,

LITTLE MORE THAN SPEED BUMPS ON THE ROAD TO A

"CONFRONTATIONAL" VOTE JANUARY 20. BUT THEY ARE PREPARED TO

EXPLORE LANGUAGE THAT PUTS THE WEOG ON RECORD, AND LIBYA AND

THE AFRICA GROUP ON NOTICE, REGARDING OUR CONCERNS AND

EXPECTATIONS FOR THE 2003 SESSION.
