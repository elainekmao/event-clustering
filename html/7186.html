SUBJECT: UAE INTERESTED IN JOINING IRAQ "CORE"

GROUP IN PRINCIPLE

REF: A) State 80809; B) ABU DHABI 1508

1. (U) Classified by Ambassador Marcelle M. Wahba

for reasons 1.5 (B) and (D).

2. (S) The Ambassador followed-up on reftel demarche

with MFA Minstate Shaykh Hamdan bin Zayid Al-Nahyan

on April 6 (other topics covered septel). Hamdan

indicated that, in principle, the UAE would be

interested in participating in the Core Group

process, given the Emiratis\' expertise in delivering

humanitarian and reconstruction assistance and its

contributions to the coalition. Shaykh Hamdan would

like more information about how the Core Group

expects to function and its composition. He noted

that the UAE would expect a role for the Iraqis

themselves. While the UAE would channel private,

unofficial (i.e. Red Crescent) humanitarian

assistance purely on a bilateral basis, Hamdan said

the UAEG would be available to offer advice, based

on the Emiratis\' knowledge of Iraq, and to

coordinate on a multilateral basis for official,

government-funded reconstruction efforts. He

carefully noted that we should not expect the UAEG

to make a major financial donation, but they would

certainly be a participant in the multilateral

effort.
