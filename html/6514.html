SUBJECT: MEDIA REACTION: PRESIDENTS BUSH\'S 3/18 ADDRESS, IRAQ,

AN AMERICAN DEATH IN ISRAEL, UAE FNC, AND ANTI-US DEMONSTRATIONS

1. SUMMARY: PRESIDENT BUSH\'S MARCH 18TH ADDRESS TO THE AMERICAN

PEOPLE GENERATED EXTENSIVE COVERAGE IN LOCAL AND REGIONAL PRINT

AND SATELLITE MEDIA. \'AL KHALEEJ\' CARRIED THE FULL TEXT OF THE

ADDRESS WHILE OTHER ENGLISH AND ARABIC DAILIES CARRIED LENGTHY

EXCERPTS. AN \'AKHABAR AL ARAB\' EDITORIAL SUGGESTS THAT THE BEST

SACRIFICE SADDAM COULD MAKE FOR HIS COUNTRY IS TO STEP DOWN.

OTHER EDITORIALS STRONGLY CRITICIZED THE U.S. DETERMINATION FOR

WAR, NOTING THAT IRAQ IS ONLY THE BEGINNING AND OTHER ARAB

COUNTRIES WILL BE NEXT. EDITORIALS AND NEWS ALSO FEATURED LOCAL

DEMONSTRATIONS, THE POSSIBILITY OF AN ELECTED UAE FEDERAL

NATIONAL COUNCIL (FNC) REPRESENTATIVES, AND THE KILLING OF AN

AMERICAN PEACE ACTIVIST, RACHEL CORRIE, IN ISRAEL. END SUMMARY.

2. ON 3/18, SEVERAL LOCAL ENGLISH AND ARABIC DAILIES REPORTED

WITH PHOTOS A DEMONSTRATION IN RAS AL KHAIMAH AGAINST U.S.

POLICIES IN THE REGION. COVERAGE ALSO INCLUDED AN ARTICLE ON

STUDENT PLANS TO SEND A 30-METER LONG \'PEACE BANNER\' TO THE WHITE

HOUSE.

3. DUBAI-BASED ENGLISH-LANGUAGE DAILY \'GULF NEWS\' ON 3/18 AND

SEVERAL LOCAL ARABIC DAILIES ON 3/19 CARRIED INSIDE-PAGE ARTICLES

CITING SHEIKH KHALED BIN SAQR AL QASIMI, RAS AL KHAIMAH (RAK)

CROWN PRINCE AND DEPUTY RULER, AS SAYING THAT A YEAR FROM NOW,

THE FEDERAL NATIONAL COUNCIL (FNC) MEMBERS OF RAK WOULD BE

ELECTED AND NOT APPOINTED.

4. CLAIMING IRAQ WILL BE A MODEL FOR WHOEVER DOES NOT ABIDE BY

U.S. POLICIES IN THE FUTURE, SHARJAH-BASED PAN-ARAB "AL KHALEEJ"

EDITORIALIZED ON 3/19:

"... DURING THIS CRUCIAL TIME, FROM THE ELIMINATION OF THE

INTERNATIONAL SYSTEM INITIATED SIXTY YEARS AGO AFTER WORLD WAR

II, SOMETHING THAT FELL WITH THE U.S. ADMINISTRATION DECISION IN

FOR A WAR ON IRAQ, TO A NEW AMERICAN SYSTEM, SOMETHING WASHINGTON

WOULD LIKE TO IMPOSE ON THE WORLD. WHAT IS GOING TO FACE IRAQ

DURING THIS WAR WILL BE THE \'IDEAL\' MODEL THAT WILL BE IMPOSED ON

ALL OPPONENTS, NAY-SAYERS, AND ALL PEOPLE REJECTING BRITISH-

AMERICAN POLICIES ..."

5. COMMENTING ON PRESIDENT BUSH\'S ULTIMATUM FOR WAR IN HIS

ADDRESS TO THE AMERICAN PEOPLE, ABU DHABI-BASED PAN-ARAB DAILY

"AKHBAR AL ARAB" EDITORIALIZED ON 3/19:

"... SACRIFICING FOR THE SAKE OF YOUR COUNTRY IS NOT ALWAYS BY

GOING INTO BATTLES AND BECOMING A MARTYR, IT COULD ALSO BE

REALIZED THROUGH SIMPLE SACRIFICES, SUCH AS A LEADER STEPPING

DOWN FROM THE PRESIDENCY. ISN\'T THIS A SACRIFICE A PRESIDENT CAN

OFFER IN RETURN FOR AVERTING UNIMAGINABLE SUFFERINGS AND

UNBELIEVABLE CONSEQUENCES FROM HIS OWN FELLOW CITIZENS? HE CAN

SPARE HIS PEOPLE A DEAR PRICE ..."

6. COMMENTING ON A RECENT DEBATE BETWEEN PRIME MINISTER TONY

BLAIR AND A NUMBER OF BRITISH WOMEN, KHALAF AHMED AL HABTOOR

EDITORIALIZED IN \'GULF NEWS\' ON 3/18:

"... IT WAS A SERIOUS AND CONSTRUCTIVE DEBATE, NOT AS SUPERFICIAL

AS WE ARE USED TO IN MANY OF THE SO-CALLED ARAB \'DEBATES\' ... I

ADMIT THAT MY ADMIRATION FOR THIS GREAT PEOPLE DOUBLED ... BUT I

ALSO HAVE TO ADMIT THAT I WAS OVERWHELMED WITH DISAPPOINTMENT

WHEN I REMEMBERED THE SITUATION OF DEMOCRACY IN THE ARAB WORLD.

WHEN I COMPARED THE INDIFFERENT AND BITTER ARAB STATUS TO THAT

DEBATE, I FELT ENGULFED WITH FRUSTRATION ..."

7. IN AN EDITORIAL ENTITLED \'NOTING HUMANE QUESTIONS IN AN

INHUMANE TIME,\' "AL KHALEEJ" COMMENTED ON 3/18:

"... WHY DID THE PEACE ACTIVIST RACHEL CORRIE LEAVE HER COUNTRY

AND COME TO PALESTINE TO TAKE A HUMAN STAND WITH THE PEOPLE OF

PALESTINE, WHO ARE SUBJECT TO DAILY ATTACKS BY THE WAR CRIMINAL

SHARON? WHY ARE OTHER PEOPLE LEAVING IRAQ, KUWAIT, SYRIA, AND

OTHER COUNTRIES IN THE REGION, LEAVING THEIR PEOPLE TO FACE THE

FIRE OF THOUSANDS OF MISSILES AND BOMBS, INCLUDING \'THE MOTHER OF

ALL BOMBS,\' WHICH WEIGHS AROUND TEN TONS? THESE ARE HUMANE

QUESTIONS IN AN INHUMAN TIME."

8. COMMENTING ON THE NOW-CLOSED DIPLOMATIC DOORS TO ANY SOLUTION

OF THE CRISIS IN IRAQ, ABU DHABI-BASED SEMI-GOVERNMENT "AL

ITTIHAD" EDITORIALIZED ON 3/18:

"... WILL SADDAM DO IT? WILL HE BE CONCERNED ABOUT THE INTERESTS

OF HIS PEOPLE AND NOT HIS OWN? WILL HE LISTEN TO THE VOICES OF

WISDOM AND REASON IN ORDER TO SAVE HIS PEOPLE WHO HAVE BEEN

SUFFERING FOR YEARS NOW, AND WHO ARE STILL DREAMING THAT THIS WAR

COULD BE AVOIDED? WILL SADDAM, AS HIS HABIT IN THE LAST TEN

YEARS, BACK OFF AT THE LAST MINUTE AND GRANT MILLIONS OF IRAQIS

THE HOPE THEY HAVE BEEN AWAITING SO LONG?"

9. COMMENTING ON THE DEATH OF AMERICAN PEACE ACTIVIST RACHEL

CORRIE, KILLED BY AN ISRAELI BULLDOZER, "AKHBAR AL ARAB"

EDITORIALIZED ON 3/18:

"... RACHEL IS UNIQUE. SHE CARRIED OUT A MISSION FILLED WITH

NOBLE MEANING TO PALESTINIANS, ARABS, AMERICANS, MOTHERS AND

FATHERS, AND ALSO TO ALL NATIONS OF THE WORLD ... SHE DESERVES TO

BE OFFICIALLY HONORED. SHE DESERVES TO BE REMEMBERED BY THE ARAB

LEAGUE BECAUSE SHE IS REALLY IN THE HEARTS OF ALL ARABS AS A MOST

NOBLE AND HONEST EMISSARY (FOR PEACE)."
