1. (C) SUMMARY: SEVERAL WHITE COMMERCIAL FARMERS AND

BUSINESSPEOPLE TOLD AMBASSADOR MCDONALD JUNE 5 OF THE

CONTINUING POLITICAL VIOLENCE ON THEIR OCCUPIED

PROPERTIES AND THE SURROUNDING AREAS, DURING PRIVATE

MEETINGS WITH THEM IN MASHONALAND WEST PROVINCE. A

BLACK MOVEMENT FOR DEMOCRATIC CHANGE (MDC) OFFICIAL ALSO

INFORMED THE AMBASSADOR ABOUT THE LEVEL OF POLITICAL

VIOLENCE IN THIS AREA, WHICH HE SAID WAS ORCHESTRATED BY

SENIOR OFFICIALS OF ZANU-PF AND CARRIED OUT BY HIRED

THUGS ON BEHALF OF THE RULING PARTY. THE BASIC MESSAGE

WAS THAT THE FARM OCCUPATIONS HAVE NOTING TO DO WITH

LAND REFORM, BUT MERELY PROVIDED A PRETEXT UNDER WHICH

TO MOVE "WAR VETERANS" ON TO COMMERCIAL AND COMMUNAL

LANDS IN ORDER TO TERRORIZE PEOPLE INTO SUPPORTING ZANU-

PF DURING THE RUN-UP TO THE JUNE PARLIAMENTARY ELECTION.

THE CONSENSUS WAS THAT IT WAS NOT POSSIBLE TO PURSUE

SERIOUS LAND REFORM DISCUSSIONS WITH THE GOVERNMENT

CONFIDENTIAL

UNTIL AFTER THE ELECTION. NEVERTHELESS, THESE CONTACTS

MAINTAIN THAT THE MDC CONTINUES TO HAVE BROAD SUPPORT IN

THESE COMMUNITIES AND WILL WIN A SIGNIFICANT NUMBER OF

SEATS. THE GOVERNMENT, HOWEVER, IS MOVING TO

DISENFRANCHISE PRIMARILY WHITE VOTERS BY OMITTING THEIR

NAMES FROM THE VOTERS\' ROLLS AND MISINFORMING THEM ABOUT

THEIR RIGHT TO CORRECT SUCH OMISSIONS. END SUMMARY.

2. (C) ON JUNE 5 AMBASSADOR MCDONALD, ACCOMPANIED BY

POLOFF AND THE RSO ASSISTANT, TRAVELED TO SOME OF

ZIMBABWE\'S MOST PRODUCTIVE COMMERCIAL FARMLAND, IN THE

MASHONALAND WEST PROVINCE IN THE TOWNS OF BANKET,

CHINOYI AND DOMA. MANY OF THESE FARMS ARE ON THE LIST

OF 804 PROPERTIES PROPOSED FOR IMMINENT GOVERNMENT

ACQUISITION (REFTEL A). THIS PROVINCE HAS ALSO SEEN

SOME OF THE MOST EXTREME POLITICAL VIOLENCE, INCLUDING

THE DEATH OF TWO MDC ORGANIZERS IN KARIBA TOWN, AND

BRUTALIZATION OF FARM WORKERS IN RAFFINGORA AND OTHER

AREAS (REFTEL B). AMBASSADOR MCDONALD MADE UNANNOUNCED

CALLS ON SEVERAL BUSINESS PEOPLE WHO WERE EAGER AND

PLEASED TO TALK ABOUT THE ONGOING POLITICAL VIOLENCE IN

THE AREA. IN BANKET WE STOPPED AT ITICON IRRIGATION

CONSULTANTS, WHICH SUPPLIES IRRIGATION EQUIPMENT TO MOST

OF THE COMMERCIAL FARMERS IN THE AREA. THE MANAGERS

WITH WHOM WE SPOKE SAID THERE HAD BEEN WIDESPREAD

POLITICAL INTIMIDATION IN THE AREA, PARTICULARLY THE

COMMUNITY OF RAFFINGORA. SIMILARLY, WE HEARD OF MORE

POLITICAL VIOLENCE IN OUR CONVERSATIONS WITH THE OWNER

OF THE BANKET FARMERS\' CENTER, A PROSPEROUS LOOKING FARM

EQUIPMENT SUPPLY COMPANY, WHO IS AN ADMITTED MDC

ORGANIZER AND FINANCIAL BACKER IN THAT COMMUNITY. YET

AGRICULTURAL EQUIPMENT FOR THE AREA\'S COMMERCIAL FARMS.
