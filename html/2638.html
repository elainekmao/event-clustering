SUBJECT: MARCH 10 TIBETAN DEMONSTRATOR RELEASED

REF: KATHMANDU 507

Classified By: A/DCM Michael S. Hoza

(C) The ethnic Tibetan activist detained at a March 10

demonstration outside the Chinese Embassy (Reftel) was

released by Nepal\'s police March 14. The man, who goes by

the single name Yonten, alleged that police beat him while

taking him into custody, but reported that he was not

mistreated while in jail.
