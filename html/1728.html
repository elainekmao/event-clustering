SUBJECT: NORTHERN IRAQ: ISLAMIC JIHAD PARTY FORMED AFTER

SPLIT WITHIN ISLAMIC UNION MOVEMENT OF KURDISTAN

(U) CLASSIFIED BY POL-MIL COUNSELOR STUART BROWN, REASONS

1.5 B/D.

1. (C) ACCORDING TO PATRIOTIC UNION OF KURDISTAN (PUK)

ANKARA REP BAHROS GALALI, ON MAY 30 A SPLIT IN THE ISLAMIC

UNION MOVEMENT OF KURDISTAN (IUMK) RESULTED IN MULLAH ALI

BAPIR FOUNDING HIS OWN GROUP WHICH HE HAS NAMED THE ISLAMIC

JIHAD PARTY. BAPIR IS A RIVAL OF IUKM LEADER MULLAH ALI

ABDULAZIZ. BOTH LEADERS ARE BASED IN THE HALABJA DISTRICT,

HAVE ARMED MILITIA UNDER THEIR COMMAND AND RECEIVE SUPPORT

FROM IRAN. WHILE THE HALABJA DISTRICT HAS FOR SOME TIME

BEEN CONTROLLED BY THE IUMK, ACCORDING TO GALALI, ISLAMIC

JIHAD ON THE NIGHT OF MAY 30 SUCCEEDED IN TAKING THE

VILLAGES OF KURMAL AND ANAB WITHOUT A FIGHT. GALALI

CONTACTED US WITH THIS INFORMATION ON MAY 31 ON PUK LEADER

JALAL TALABANI\'S INSTRUCTION.

2.(C) THE PUK IS CONCERNED THAT THE ISLAMIST SPLIT AND GRAB

OF TERRITORY MAY LEAD TO FIGHTING BETWEEN THE ARMED RIVAL

FACTIONS, THOUGH NONE IS YET REPORTED. TALABANI IS ALSO

WORRIED THAT IRAN MAY TRY TO USE THE EXISTENCE OF TWO

ISLAMIST FACTIONS HOLDING TERRITORY IN THE BORDER AREA AS A

PRETEXT TO CUT OFF TALABANI\'S ROAD ACCESS TO IRAN BY

ENCOURAGING ISLAMIC JIHAD TO TAKE THE AREA AROUND PINJUIN.

(THE TWO MAIN ROADS FROM THE PUK AREA INTO IRAN RUN THROUGH

PINJUIN AND HALABJA RESPECTIVELY.) IN A FURTHER

COMPLICATION FOR THE PUK, TWO PUK "MINISTERS" AND TWO

"DEPUTY MINISTERS" ARE ISLAMISTS FROM THE IUMK AND IT IS

NOT CLEAR WITH WHICH FACTION THEY WILL ALIGN THEMSELVES.

3. (C) AS IS OFTEN THE CASE WITH DEVELOPMENTS IN NORTHERN

IRAQ, OUR INFORMATION IS LIMITED AND HAS NOT BEEN CONFIRMED

BY ANY OTHER SOURCE. WE WILL REPORT ANY FURTHER DETAILS WE

ARE ABLE TO LEARN SEPTEL.
