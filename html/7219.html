SUBJECT: ICC ARTICLE 98: UAE CONSIDERING

EXCHANGE OF DIP NOTES

REF: Abu Dhabi 415

1. (U) Classified by Ambassador Marcelle M. Wahba

for reasons 1.5 (B) and (D).

2. (C) The Ambassador used the opportunity of an

April 6 meeting with MFA Minstate Shaykh Hamdan bin

Zayid Al-Nahyan to again urge forward movement on

the signing of an ICC Article 98 agreement. Hamdan

acknowledged the importance of dealing with this

issue, which had been on the agenda of last year\'s

inaugural Bilateral Strategic Dialogue. The

Ambassador laid out the option of formalizing the

agreement via an exchange of diplomatic notes, but

emphasized that this would need to be legally

binding. Hamdan promised to look into the matter

right away.

3. (C) MFA Deputy Legal Affairs Director Yacoub Al-

Hosani contacted Polchief on April 7 to report that

Hamdan had decided to pursue an exchange of dip

notes. He assured that this course of action would

be legally binding. Polchief faxed over the dip

note draft language in Arabic and English provided

by the Department. Al-Hosani briefed that a

committee has been formed to review the language; he

estimated that the UAE would need approximately

three to four weeks for the review process. We will

continue to follow-up with the MFA and report our

findings to the Department.

WAHBA
