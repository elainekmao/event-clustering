SUBJECT: GVN COOPERATION ON COUNTERTERRORISM

1. (U) In a meeting with Charge on March 13, Assistant

Foreign Minister Nguyen Duc Hung, concurrently Director of

the MFA\'s America\'s Department, said that the GVN had taken

prompt action on all USG requests for name checks on

potential terrorist links since 9/11, including reviewing

information for potential terrorist financial assets that

might have made their way into Vietnam\'s banking system and

checking immigration records. AFM Hung reported that the

Ministries of Public Security and Finance as well as the

State Bank performed the checks and that each of the

concerned GVN agencies had reported to the MFA that all

checks proved negative.

2. (U) AFM Hung emphasized to Charge that the GVN is

determined to "cooperate fully with the United States" and

would make sure that no terrorist is issued a visa. He

noted that, concerning counterterrorism, "your goal is our

goal." He pledged that Vietnam would continue to respond to

all such future requests for cooperation. PORTER
