SUBJECT: CHANGING CHAIRS IN ITALY

SUMMARY

-------

1. (U) THE GOVERNMENT OF ITALY, SPECIFICALLY THE MINISTRY OF

ECONOMY AND FINANCE, RECENTLY CHANGED ITS SENIOR LEADERSHIP

IN THREE OF THE CORPORATIONS IN WHICH IT HAS A MAJOR

INTEREST: ENI (ITALIAN HYDROCARBON AGENCY), ENEL (NATIONAL

ELECTRICITY COMPANY), AND FINMECCANICA (A GROUP OF DEFENSE,

AVIATION AND AEROSPACE INDUSTRY COMPANIES). THESE ARE THE

THREE LARGEST INDUSTRIAL GROUPS REMAINING CONTROLLED BY THE

GOI. THE APPOINTMENTS WERE WELL RECEIVED BY THE FINANCIAL

MARKETS WHICH PERCEIVED THEM AS A WELCOMED SIGN OF CONTINUITY.

FINMECCANICA

------------

2. (SBU) PIERFRANCESCO GUARGUAGLINI, FORMER PRESIDENT OF

FINCANTIERI (ITALIAN SHIPBUILDERS\' ASSOCIATION) IS THE NEWLY

APPOINTED CHAIRMAN OF FINMECCANCA. GUARGUALINI, BORN IN

1937, HOLDS A PH.D. IN ELECTRONIC ENGINEERING FROM THE

UNIVERSITY OF PENNSYLVANIA. IN ADDITION TO HARMONY WITH THE

GOVERNMENT, CONTINUITY AND MARKET CONFIDENCE, GUARGUAGLINI

MAY HAVE A MANDATE TO INCREASE INTERNATIONALIZATION OF THE

COMPANY. FINMECCANICA IS A PARTIALLY STATE-OWNED COMPANY

(37.4 PERCENT OWNED BY THE GOI) HOLDING THE MAJOR ITALIAN

AEROSPACE PLAYERS (ALENIA AERONAUTICA, ALENIA DIFESA, ALENIA

SPAZIO, 50 PERCENT OF ALENIA MARCONI SYSTEMS, AND 25 PERCENT

OF AERMACCHI). ALTHOUGH THERE IS SPECULATION ABOUT CONTINUED

PRIVATIZATION WHEN THE MARKETS STRENGTHEN, GUARGUAGLINI SAID,

AT A RECENT LUNCH WITH AMBASSADOR SEMBLER THAT THE GOVERNMENT

DOES NOT WISH TO PRIVATIZE ANY MORE OF FINMECCANICA, THUS

PROTECTING ITALY\'S STRATEGIC AND DEFENSE INDUSTRIES. HE

ALLOWED THAT THE GOVERNMENT MAY DECIDE TO SPLIT SOME OF THE

COMPANIES INTO CIVIL AND DEFENSE COMPONENTS AND PRIVATIZE THE

CIVILIAN ELEMENTS, BUT EVEN THIS HAS NOT BEEN DECIDED.

GUARGUAGLINI ALSO CONFIRMED RUMOR THAT FINMECCANICA WILL BE

EXPECTED TO TAKE OVER FINCANTIERI, THE CORPORATION HE

PREVIOUS HEADED. STILL OWNED 100 PERCENT BY THE GOI,

ALTHOUGH IT HAS BEEN SLATED TO BE PRIVATIZED, FINCANTIERI

EMPLOYS ROUGHLY 10,000, HAS A TURNOVER AROUND TWO BILLION

EUROS/YEAR, AND HAS SUBSTANTIAL ORDERS FOR ITS SPECIALTIES --

CRUISE AND NAVAL SHIPS. FINCANTIERI AND FINMECCANICA HAVE

TRADITIONALLY WORKED CLOSELY TOGETHER, AND COULD SHARE MORE

THAN FIVE BILLION EUROS IN CONSTRUCTION IF THE NAVY SPENDS

THIS ON A NEW CLASS OF FRIGATES. GUARGUAGLINI ALSO NOTED

THAT, ALTHOUGH FINMECCANICA HAD DROPPED ITS BID TO BUY

MARCONI COMMUNICATION FOR THE TIME BEING, THEY FULLY EXPECT

MARCONI TO BE WILLING TO PURSUE THE DEAL A LITTLE LATER.

3. (U) ROBERTO TESTORE IS THE NEWLY APPOINTED MANAGING

DIRECTOR AND DIRECTOR GENERAL OF FINMECCANICA. GIVEN THAT HE

WAS A FIAT EMPLOYEE (SINCE 1976) AND THE MANAGING DIRECTOR OF

FIAT AUTO (SINCE 1996), SOME THINK THAT HE MAY BE LOOKING TO

CONSOLIDATE FIAT\'S DEFENSE BUSINESS INTO FINMECCANICA. HIS

NEW RESPONSIBILITY HOWEVER FOCUSES ON THE GROUP\'S OPERATIONAL

MANAGEMENT AND ON THE INTERNAL REORGANIZATION OF THE

FINANCIAL STRUCTURE OF THE GROUP.

4. (SBU) COMMENT: SENIOR MANAGEMENT\'S VIEW ON PRIVATIZING

FINMECCANICA IS AT VARIANCE WITH WHAT WE HEAR ELSEWHERE. IT

IS POSSIBLE THAT, AFTER HAVING MET THE EU\'S MINIMAL

PRIVATIZATION REQUIREMENTS, THE GOI WILL NOT PRIVATIZE ANY

MORE OF FINMECCANICA. THERE IS ALSO THE VIEW THAT SELLING

OFF THE COMMERCIAL ELEMENTS LEAVES THE WEAKER COMPONENTS IN

GOI HANDS, WITH A NEED FOR LONG-TERM SUPPORT. MARCONI

COMMUNICATION IS NOT PRIZE, BUT IF THE GOVERNMENT IS LOOKING

TO \'PROTECT\' WEAK DEFENSE PLAYERS, MARCONI MAY EVENTUALLY BE

ASSIMILATED INTO FINMECCANICA. OUR VIEW ON FINMECCANICA\'S

ACQUIRING FIAT\'S DEFENSE INDUSTRIES IS THAT IT WOULD NOT LEAD

TO ANY NET SYNERGIES, BUT JUST BE TRADING ONE FORM OF

ENI

---

5. (U) THE GOI OWNS MORE THAN 30 PERCENT OF ENI WHICH IS

MORE NARROWLY FOCUSED ON ENERGY AND ENSURING ITALY\'S ENERGY

SECURITY. ENI ONLY HAD A PARTIAL CHANGE IN THE BOARD BECAUSE

VITTORIO MINCATO, THE MANAGING DIRECTOR, HAS BEEN

RECONFIRMED. MINCATO HAS ONLY BEEN IN PLACE FOR SEVERAL

YEARS, AND IS WIDELY REPUTED AS SHOWING STRONG LEADERSHIP.

HE WILL BE SUPPORTED BY A NEWLY NAMED CHAIRMAN, ROBERTO POLI,

WHO IS A WELL-KNOWN EXPERT IN CORPORATE FINANCING. FROM 1978

TO 1998 HE TAUGHT CORPORATE FINANCE AT CATHOLIC UNIVERSITY OF

MILAN. POLI HAS IN THE PAST BEEN CHAIRMAN OF PUBLITALIA OF

THE BERLUSCONI GROUP AND A MAIN CONSULTANT OF THE BERLUSCONI

FAMILY IN THE RESTORATION OF FININVEST, BERLUSCONI\'S ASSET

HOLDING COMPANY.

7. (SBU) PIERO GNUDI WILL BECOME THE PRESIDENT, AND LEAVE

HIS POSITION AS CHAIRMAN OF IRI (THE FORMER STATE HOLDING

COMPANY FOR GOVERNMENT ENTERPRISES), WHICH IS REACHING THE

END OF ITS NATURAL LIFE. GNUDI IS VIEWED AS A FAMILIAR FACE

AND GIVEN HIS EXPERIENCE AT IRI WITH LIQUIDATING ITALIAN

PARASTATALS HE SHOULD BE ABLE TO MOVE FORWARD WITH THE NEXT

STEP IN THE PRIVATIZATION OF ENEL WHEN THE TIME IS RIGHT.

8. (SBU) FERNANDO NAPOLETANO, NEWLY APPOINTED TO THE BOARD

OF ENEL, CONFIRMED IN HIS INTRODUCTORY MEETING WITH THE

AMBASSADOR REPORTS THAT MINISTER OF PRODUCTIVE ACTIVITIES

MARZANO HAS GIVEN ENEL THE MANDATE TO CONTINUE TO EXPAND

ABROAD IN ITS MAJOR SECTORS: ENERGY AND TELECOMMUNICATIONS.

THE GOVERNMENT ENVISIONS A MULTI-SECTOR CORPORATION ALONG THE

LINES OF A GE TYPE OF OPERATION. NAPOLETANO, THE HEAD OF

BOOZ, ALLEN, HAMILTON ITALIA, EMPHATICALLY SAID EXPANSION WAS

THE ONLY REASON HE HAD BEEN APPOINTED TO THE BOARD AND THE

ONLY REASON HE HAD ANY ADDED VALUE FOR ENEL.

COMMENT

-------

9. (SBU) WE DO NOT ANTICIPATE THAT THE CHANGES IN THE

LEADERSHIP OF THESE GROUPS WILL HAVE A MAJOR EFFECT FOR U.S.

CORPORATIONS. THE TOP MANAGERS ARE ALL CITED AS COMPETENT

AND AUTHORITATIVE IN THEIR RESPECTIVE FIELD. WHAT IS

HAPPENING HOWEVER IS THAT THIS GOVERNMENT IS STAMPING ITS OWN

BUSINESS ORIENTED STYLE ON THE LEADERSHIP. THE OUT-GOING

GOVERNMENT HAD SALTED SENIOR POSITIONS WITH CLOSE ALLIES;

WHAT IS INTERESTING ABOUT THESE CHANGES IS NOT THAT THEY

OCCURRED BUT THAT THEY TOOK SO LONG TO OCCUR. THIS
