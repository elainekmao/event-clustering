SUBJECT: NHAN DAN ON FATHER LY\'S SENTENCE

REF: A. HANOI 2884 B. HANOI 2789

1. (U) A SIGNED COMMENTARY UNDER THE HEADLINE OF "NO ONE

HAS THE RIGHT TO INTERFERE" IN NHAN DAN, THE LEADING PARTY

DAILY NEWSPAPER, ON NOVEMBER 13 DENOUNCED FOREIGN MEDIA

COVERAGE, ESPECIALLY BY RFI AND BBC, REGARDING THE 15 YEAR

UNCLASSIFIED

PAGE 02 HANOI 02962 140144Z

CUMULATIVE SENTENCES GIVEN TO FATHER NGUYEN VAN LY ON

OCTOBER 19. IT INCLUDED SOME QUOTES FROM INTERNATIONAL

COVERAGE SUCH AS "CALLING ON THE GOVERNMENT OF VIETNAM TO

RESPECT HUMAN RIGHTS AND FREEDOM OF RELIGION AND REJECT THE

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER 31/CP REGARDING ADMINISTRATIVE

DETENTION"

2. (U) THE COMMENTARY DESCRIBED THE "TRUTH" AS BEING THAT

FATHER LY HAD VIOLATED THE LAW BY "CAUSING DIVISIONS AMONG

RELIGIOUS BELIEVERS, WITH AN AFFECT ON ORDER, SECURITY AND

THE PEACEFUL LIFE OF THE PEOPLE," BY "TWISTING THE TRUTH"

AND BY "DAMAGING THE POLICY OF SOLIDARITY AMONG THE ENTIRE

POPULATION." IT REITERATED THE "CONSISTENT POLICY" OF THE

PARTY AND STATE WAS TO RESPECT FREEDOM OF BELIEF AND NON-

BELIEF, BUT WARNED THAT ANY INSTANCE OF VIOLATING THE LAW

WILL BE "DEALT WITH BY THE LAW."

3. (SBU) COMMENT: THE GVN CONTINUES TO TRY TO PORTRAY

FATHER LY\'S CASE AS A LAW AND ORDER ISSUE, RATHER THAN A

RELIGIOUS LIBERTY ISSUE. OFFICIAL SENSITIVITY TO EXTERNAL

CRITICISM IS APPARENT IN THE INCLUSION IN THIS SEMI-

DEFINITIVE COMMENTARY OF DIRECTLY CRITICAL COMMENTS MADE BY

OUTSIDE OBSERVERS; THEY ARE PROBABLY WELL AWARE THAT

INTERNATIONAL NEWS PROGRAMS ARE POPULAR AND FELT THEY NEEDED

TO REACT TO SUCH CRITICISM FACE-ON.
